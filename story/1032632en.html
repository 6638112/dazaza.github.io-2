Raspberry Pi Zero W 1080p IP摄像机

2020-11-01 22:40:27

Download and install image to your SD card. The procedures are same as Raspberry Pi standard image.

下载映像并将其安装到您的SD卡。操作步骤与Raspberry Pi标准图像相同。

Connect your PC Wi-Fi to Raspberry Pi Zero's Wi-FI hotspot. The default SSID/password is piipcam/12345678

将您的PC Wi-Fi连接到Raspberry Pi Zero的Wi-Fi热点。默认ssid/密码为piipcam/12345678。

Improve latancy: Open preferences of VLC. Find the Network caching in "Show All" -> "Inputs/Codecs". Change the value to 100. The latency of streaming will less than 500 ms.

改善延迟：打开VLC的首选项。在"；显示所有"；-&>&34；输入/编解码器"；中查找网络缓存。将该值更改为100。流式传输的延迟将小于500ms。
