Dropboxs Performance is degrading for years and I have enough of this
Dropboxs性能多年来一直在下降，我对此已经足够了

2021-01-22 00:42:05

Hello, I'm a Dropbox User since the first days in 2007.  It was a blast in school and university, but the desktop clients got worse and worse. I even dealt with the shoving in my face of some Dropbox features like the Microsoft Word button, the constant upselling screens in the Dropbox desktop client ... BUT now i can not take it anymore. My pretty fast Lenovo Thinkpad from 2018 now aches under the constant high load Dropbox Sync puts on it.

您好，自2007年第一天以来，我一直是Dropbox用户。这在学校和大学中都是爆炸性的增长，但是台式机客户端变得越来越糟。我什至处理了一些Dropbox功能，例如Microsoft Word按钮，Dropbox桌面客户端中不断增加的屏幕销售……但是现在我不能再忍受了。我从2018年开始使用的相当快的Lenovo Thinkpad现在在持续的高负载Dropbox Sync上感到疼痛。

I'm a professional software developer and i the past i used the Dropbox folder as my Workspace, it was my desaster backup, saved me a couple of times and it worked. Put light load on the system even on big projects in Jetbrains  IntelliJ IDEA, synced even the usesless debug folders in a reasonable time. Since the beginning of 2019 i have shutdown Dropbox on the systemstart and while i m working. Maybe after work is done i can start it (which takes longer and longer in the years) and painfully watch how it scans folders, the thinkpad turns into a jetplane and takes ages to sync. The same game on a new i7 8700k with plenty of ram, countless new Windows 10 fresh installs. Yes i use 550 GB of my 2000GB of storage and have 750.000 files in my Dropbox but it worked in the past, and this is not unreasonable for a Pro-User.

我是专业的软件开发人员，过去，我使用Dropbox文件夹作为我的工作区，这是我的灾难备份，为我节省了几次，并且可以正常工作。即使在Jetbrains IntelliJ IDEA中的大型项目上，也减轻了系统负担，并在合理的时间内同步了无用的调试文件夹。自2019年初以来，我在系统启动时以及正在工作时都关闭了Dropbox。也许在工作完成后，我可以启动它（这需要花费越来越长的时间），并且痛苦地看着它如何扫描文件夹，ThinkPad变成了喷气式飞机并且需要花很多时间才能同步。新的i7 8700k配备了相同的游戏，配备了很多ram，无数新安装的Windows 10。是的，我在2000GB的存储空间中使用了550 GB，在Dropbox中有750.000个文件，但是过去可以使用，对于专业用户而言，这并非不合理。

I subscribe to the Dropbox Tech Blog and now it clicked: You are compressing the files on the client with "Brotli", save bandwidth, storage space and money on your side, make it in theory faster to download (because no recompression has to take place), but this whole concept just crashes under its own overengineered weight! You named this " Broccoli: Syncing faster by syncing less" and state your "zero-crash policy" ... well i can make it crash 3 or 4 times a day. Conclusion:  The user experience sucks and the product does not deliver it's promised features. My yearly subscription is ending in one month and i will not renew my subscription. I'm testing Google One and Microsoft OneDrive and OneDrive looks pretty decent, not as polished as Dropbox ... but it syncs faster, doesn't crash and uses way less ressources.

我订阅了Dropbox Tech博客，现在单击了它：您正在使用＆＃34; Brotli＆＃34;在客户端上压缩文件，节省了带宽，存储空间和金钱，从理论上讲下载速度更快（因为无需进行重新压缩），但整个概念只是在自身过度设计的压力下崩溃了！您将其命名为＆＃34;西兰花：通过少同步来加快同步速度＆＃34;并说明您的＆＃34;零崩溃政策＆＃34; ...好吧，我可以让它每天崩溃3到4次。结论：用户体验糟透了，产品没有提供其承诺的功能。我的年度订阅将在一个月内结束，并且我不会续订。我正在测试Google One和Microsoft OneDrive，而OneDrive看起来还不错，不像Dropbox那样优美……但是它同步速度更快，不会崩溃，并且使用的资源更少。
