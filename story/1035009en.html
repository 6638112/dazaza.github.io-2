陈旧媒体博物馆

2020-11-14 09:00:44

The home for over 650 current and obsolete physical media formats, covering audio, video, film and data storage.

拥有650多种当前和过时的物理媒体格式，涵盖音频、视频、电影和数据存储。

The Museum preserves the memory of those objects that held our memories, and every format listed in the Museum is represented by at least one example in the collection.

博物馆保留了那些保存着我们记忆的物品的记忆，博物馆列出的每一种形式都至少有一个藏品代表了这一点。

Find out more about the collection, how it started in 2006, and the answers to some common questions.

了解更多关于这个集合的信息，它是如何在2006年开始的，以及一些常见问题的答案。

What makes a format obsolete? Read more about the criteria used by the Museum to provide dates for obsolescence, or visit the complete list of formats in the Museum by date of obsolescence.

是什么让一种格式过时了？阅读更多有关博物馆用来提供过时日期的标准的信息，或访问博物馆中按过时日期列出的完整格式列表。

How long will examples of media be readable before they degrade, and how easy is it to find the equipment to read them even if they aren't degraded? See the ratings for formats commonly found in archives and personal collections.

介质的例子在降级之前能读多久？即使它们没有降级，找到设备来读它们又有多容易呢？查看档案和个人收藏中常见格式的评级。
