A New Take on RSS
RSS的新观点

2020-12-11 07:50:01

If you subscribe to any feeds, then 50% of the articles in your regular Findkaemails will be sampled from those feeds.I added this because, as I mentioned,I'm going to start doing more manual curation to make sure that Findka has asteady stream of new, great essays. I also think it's a valuable feature foranyone who wants a little more control over their Findka recommendations.

如果您订阅了任何提要，那么常规的Findkaemails中的文章中有50％将从这些提要中抽取。我添加了这个原因是因为，正如我提到的那样，我将开始做更多的手动策划，以确保Findka不断有新的，出色的论文。我还认为，对于希望对其Findka建议进行更多控制的人来说，这是一个宝贵的功能。

How's this different from existing RSS aggregators? For one thing, since thisis built into Findka, any articles that you like will start to be recommendedto other users, too. But there's more.

这与现有的RSS聚合器有何不同？一方面，由于这是Findka内置的，因此您喜欢的任何文章也将开始被推荐给其他用户。但是还有更多。

Most RSS aggregators keep your feeds separate. Findka instead merges them intoa single feed using a bandit algorithm. If you've subscribed to threefeeds—A, B and C—Findka will start out picking articles from thefeeds uniformly. 1/3 of the articles will come from feed A, etc. As time goeson, Findka will adjust the distribution based on your usage data. If you neverclick on articles from feed A and you always click on articles from feed B,then Findka will show you fewer articles from A and more articles from B.

大多数RSS聚合器会将您的供稿分开。相反，Findka使用强盗算法将它们合并为一个供稿。如果您已订阅了A，B和C这三种Feed，Findka将开始从Feed中统一挑选商品。 1/3的文章将来自Feed A等。随着时间的流逝，Findka将根据您的使用情况数据调整分布。如果您从不单击提要A中的文章，而始终单击提要B中的文章，则Findka将向您显示更少的A文章和更多的B文章。

On top of that, the number of articles in your feed is controlled by you, notby the feeds you subscribe to.

最重要的是，提要中的文章数量由您控制，而不是由您订阅的提要控制。

The result is that using RSS now takes extremely little effort. You get asingle, small feed that improves itself automatically.

结果是，现在使用RSS只需很少的工作。您会得到一个单一的小型饲料，该饲料会自动改善。
