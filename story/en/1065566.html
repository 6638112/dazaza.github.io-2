Solana Labs Completes a $314.15M Private Token Sale Led by Andreessen Horowitz
Solana Labs完成了Andreessen Horowitz领导的314.15M私人令牌销售

2021-06-14 06:26:17

New resources will accelerate the deployment of market-ready applications focused on onboarding the next billion users into crypto

新资源将加速部署市场准备应用的应用程序，专注于将下一十亿用户换入加入Crypto

SAN FRANCISCO, JUNE 9, 2021 — Solana Labs announced today it has completed a private token sale of $314,159,265 led by Andreessen Horowitz and Polychain Capital with participation from 1kx, Alameda Research, Blockchange Ventures, CMS Holdings, Coinfund, CoinShares, Collab Currency, Memetic Capital, Multicoin Capital, ParaFi Capital, Sino Global Capital, Jump Trading, and select individual investors like Boys Noize.

2021年6月9日旧金山 - 索拉纳实验室今天宣布它已经完成了Andreessen Horowitz和Polychain Capital的私人令牌销售额为Andreessen Horowitz和Polychain Capital从1KX，Alameda Research，BlockChange Ventures，CMS Holdings，Coinfund，Coinshares，Collab货币，Memetic资本，多学资本资本，Parafi Capital，Sino Global Capital，Jump Trading，并选择像男孩的个人投资者。

With the new resources, Solana Labs will launch an incubation studio to accelerate the development of decentralized applications and platforms building on Solana, alongside a venture investing arm and trading desk dedicated to the Solana ecosystem.

凭借新的资源，索拉纳实验室将推出一个潜伏期的工作室，以加速分散的应用程序和平台在索拉纳的平台建设，以及专门为索拉纳生态系统的企业投资和交易台。

“Solana is build-ready today. After seeing explosive growth in global developer uptake, we are convinced that scalability and throughput in blockchain are now a solved problem. This is an inflection point akin to the moment that PC’s became widely accessible in the 1970’s,” said Anatoly Yakovenko, Solana Labs Co-Founder and CEO. “The next phase is onboarding a billion users. Solana was built from the ground up to accommodate this scale. With this funding, Solana Labs is now positioned to bring in the right partners and capital to build products and tooling to get there.”

“索拉纳今天正在建设。在全球开发人员摄取的爆炸性增长之后，我们相信，在区块链中的可扩展性和吞吐量现在是一个解决问题。这是一种拐点，类似于PC在1970年代广泛访问的那一刻，“Solana Labs联合创始人兼首席执行官Anatoly Yakovenko说。 “下一阶段是十亿用户的奴役。索拉纳由地下建造，以适应这种规模。通过这项资金，索拉纳实验室现已定位，以赋予合适的合作伙伴和资本来建立产品和工具到达那里。“

Since the launch of Mainnet Beta in March of 2020, the Solana ecosystem has attracted tens of thousands of developers to its network and broke world records for the most number of participants in a hackathon. Due to the Solana protocol’s innovative design, the network has become a prime breeding ground for developers seeking to build and deploy market-ready applications that are secure, fast, low-cost and energy efficient, all of which play critical roles in attracting the next billion users of blockchain and crypto technologies.

自2020年3月在2020年3月推出Mainnet Beta以来，Solana生态系统已经吸引了数万种开发人员到其网络，打破了哈克松最多的参与者的世界纪录。由于索拉纳议定书的创新设计，该网络已成为寻求建立和部署市场准备好的产品的开发人员的主要育种地面，这些应用程序确保安全，快速，低成本和节能，所有这些应用程序都在吸引下一个方面发挥着关键作用亿用户区块链和加密技术。

It is easy to imagine countless use cases for crypto as a technology, but building them into real products that millions of people use requires the existence of a high-performance blockchain. Solana is a next-generation blockchain that can meet that high bar.”

Ali Yahya，General Partner，Andreessen Horowitz：“下一波加密的创新浪潮将发生在应用层。很容易想象Crypto作为一种技术的无数用例，但将它们建立成数百万人使用的真实产品需要存在高性能区块链。索拉纳是一个可以满足那个高酒吧的下一代区块。“

It has allowed the Solana protocol the ability to power on-chain transactions at the speed of Nasdaq matching engines for processing trades, orders, amendments, and cancellations, and breaks new ground of what’s possible across DeFi innovations, exchanges, non-fungible tokens, marketplaces, payments networks, music sharing, gaming and more.

通过使用历史证明，索拉纳已经大大减少了困扰其他分散的区块链的拥堵和冗余。它允许Solana议定书能够以纳斯达克匹配引擎为加工，用于处理交易，订单，修正和取消的速度发电，并打破新的污染创新，交流，非娱乐标记，市场，付款网络，音乐共享，游戏等。

The new injection of funding will fuel Solana Labs with the resources necessary to further accelerate the development and launch of new DeFi, or Web3, projects across the ecosystem.
