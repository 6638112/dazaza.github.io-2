Facebook’s Libra to reportedly launch in January 2021 as USD stablecoin
据报道，Facebook的天秤座将在2021年1月作为美元稳定币推出

2020-11-28 14:15:17

The long-awaited digital currency Libra could finally see the light of day as soon as January 2021, according to a new report.

一份新的报告显示，期待已久的数字货币天秤座最早可以在2021年1月见到曙光。

Following more than a year of scrutiny from global financial regulators, Libra will launch in the form of a U.S. dollar-backed digital currency, the Financial Times reported on Nov. 27.

据《金融时报》 11月27日报道，经过全球金融监管机构一年多的审查，天秤座将以美元支持的数字货币形式发行。

Citing three people involved in the Libra project, the Financial Times wrote that the Libra Association plans will eventually add more fiat currencies to the basket of assets that back Libra’s value.

英国《金融时报》援引三名参与天秤座项目的人的话说，天秤座协会的计划最终将为支持天秤座价值的资产篮子增加更多法定货币。

Expected in January, the exact launch date is still not known and would depend on when the Libra Association receives regulatory approval by the Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority, or FINMA, to operate as a payments service.

确切的发布日期预计在1月，仍不知道，具体取决于天秤座协会何时获得瑞士金融市场监管局（FINMA）的监管批准以用作支付服务。

A spokesperson at the FINMA declined to comment to Cointelegraph regarding Libra’s potential launch in January 2021. The representative referred to an  announcement on Libra’s licensing process, stating, “In accordance with its usual practice, FINMA will neither provide public information on the status of the ongoing procedure nor speculate on when it may be complete.”

FINMA的发言人拒绝就天秤座2021年1月的潜在启动向Cointelegraph发表评论。该代表提到有关天秤座许可程序的公告，指出：“按照惯例，FINMA不会提供任何有关天秤座状态的公开信息。正在进行的程序，也不推测它什么时候可以完成。”

The Libra Association did not immediately respond to Cointelegraph’s request for further information.

天秤座协会没有立即回应Cointelegraph的进一步信息请求。

Initiated in June 2019, the Libra Association faced major regulatory scrutiny causing a number of member companies like PayPal and MasterCard to subsequently leave the project. The basket of currencies backing LIbra was originally to include  several fiat currencies including the U.S. dollar, euro, the Japanese yen, the British pound and the Singapore dollar.

天秤座协会于2019年6月成立，面临重大监管审查，导致PayPal和MasterCard等许多成员公司随后退出该项目。支持LIbra的一篮子货币最初包括几种法定货币，包括美元，欧元，日元，英镑和新加坡元。

According to the Financial Times, several Libra members believe that the appointment of HSBC legal chief Stuart Levey as chief executive was a turning point for the Libra project.

据英国《金融时报》报道，几位天秤座成员认为，任命汇丰银行法律总监斯图尔特·莱维（Stuart Levey）为首席执行官是天秤座项目的转折点。
