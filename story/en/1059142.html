Detroit VCs weigh in on fundraising and building startups in Michigan and the Midwest
底特律VCS在密歇根和中西部的筹款和建筑创业方

2021-04-19 22:42:09

TechCrunch just hosted a small virtual meetup with Detroit startups and venture capitals. Like the one we held last month in Miami, the event was a blast and featured a talk with two local VCs on what startups work in Detroit and how to raise money from local investors. In case you missed it, the video of this talk is below. Jonathon Triest from Ludlow Ventures and Patti Glaza from Invest Detroit Ventures spoke extensively on the wide-ranging types of startups that call Detroit home. They point to Bloomscape (houseplants) to StockX (sneakers) to the numerous medical and security startups in Detroit, nearby Ann Arbor, and the surrounding metro area. Both firms invest in early stage startups, but do so in radically different ways.

TechCrunch刚刚举办了一个小型虚拟聚会，与底特律的初创公司和风险投资。就像我们上个月在迈阿密举行的那个活动一样，这一事件是一个爆炸，并与两位本地VCS进行了谈话，就底特律有什么初创公司以及如何从当地投资者筹集资金。如果您错过了它，则此谈话的视频如下。乔纳森从Ludlow Ventures和Invest Detroit Ventures的Patti Glaza最大限度地讲了广泛的类型的初创公司，称为底特律家园。他们指向彭罗森斯景观（室内植物）到Stockx（运动鞋）到底特律，附近的Ann Arbor和周围地铁区的众多医疗和安全初创公司。两家公司投资于早期初期的初创公司，但以完全不同的方式这样做。

The 20-minute conversation covers the types of founders who can find success in Detroit and other Midwestern areas.

20分钟的谈话涵盖了可以在底特律和其他中西部地区找到成功的创始人的类型。
