Gala Games and Polyient Games Join Forces to Shatter Record with an $800K NFT Sale

Gala Games和Polyient Games联手以$ 800K的NFT销售额打破纪录

In the world of Mirandus, it’s good to be the King. Over the weekend, Gala Games and Polyient Games negotiated the terms of a sale, which was executed this morning on OpenSea, for the Citadel of the Sun for the upcoming fantasy MMORPG game Mirandus. The Citadel of the Sun is a 1/1 Legendary-tier NFT that comes with its own Kingdom inside of the World of Mirandus.

在Mirandus的世界里，成为国王是一件好事。在周末，Gala Games和Polyient Games协商了一项销售条款，该条款于今天上午在OpenSea上执行，用于即将到来的幻想MMORPG游戏Mirandus的太阳堡垒。太阳堡垒是1/1传奇级NFT，它在Mirandus世界内拥有自己的王国。

With the purchase, Polyient Games steps into the role of the main financial layer within the World of Mirandus. In Mirandus, the Polyient Banks will allow players to safely deposit and store in-game representations of their NFTs and in-game currencies.

通过购买，Polyient Games进入了Mirandus世界中主要财务层的角色。在Mirandus，Polyient Banks将允许玩家安全地存放和存储其NFT和游戏内货币的游戏内表示。

In Mirandus, every player will want a space to store valuables they do not have room for in their backpacks. If a player has a deed or building, then they will always have their own storage in-game. However, with a limited quantity of these NFTs available, users will need somewhere to place their valuables so as to not risk them when out adventuring — making the Polyient Banks an integral component of the player experience in the upcoming virtual world.

在Mirandus中，每个玩家都希望有一个空间来存储他们没有足够空间容纳背包的贵重物品。如果玩家有契约或建筑物，那么他们将始终在游戏中拥有自己的存储空间。但是，由于可用的NFT数量有限，用户将需要在某处放置贵重物品，以便在冒险旅行时不会冒风险—这使Polyient Banks在即将到来的虚拟世界中成为玩家体验不可或缺的组成部分。

Next week, an exclusive auction will begin on OpenSea, during which 10 Majestic-tier bank buildings will be auctioned for Ether ( ETH), Gala Token ( GALA), and Polyient Games Unity Token ( PGU). The banks themselves will connect with the Polyient DEX and Polyient’s upcoming vault system, allowing holders of the banks to benefit from Polyient’s innovative liquidity and yield farming solutions, all while providing key, decentralized in-game services to players. This auction will be the next step in the growing partnership between Gala Games and Polyient Games.

下周，一场独家拍卖将在OpenSea上开始，在此期间，将拍卖10座雄伟的银行大楼，分别是以太（ETH），Gala Token（GALA）和Polyient Games Unity Token（PGU）。银行本身将与Polyient DEX和即将推出的保管库系统连接，使银行持有人可以从Polyient的创新流动性和收益农业解决方案中受益，同时为玩家提供关键的分散式游戏内服务。这次拍卖将是Gala Games和Polyient Games之间不断发展的合作关系的下一步。

“Using the banks will not be a hard requirement,” says Game Designer Michael McCarthy, “However, to store your excess riches and gear, you will want a vault from a local bank. Building owners will have storage, but since there are a limited number of buildings in Mirandus most players will need to use the bank. The Polyient Vault system may even allow players to even rent out their items to others.”

游戏设计师迈克尔·麦卡锡（Michael McCarthy）表示：“使用银行并不是硬性要求，但是，要存储您多余的财富和装备，您需要从当地银行获得金库。建筑物拥有者将拥有存储空间，但是由于Mirandus中建筑物的数量有限，因此大多数玩家都需要使用银行。 Polyient Vault系统甚至可以允许玩家将自己的物品出租给他人。”

Gala Games has been releasing information about Mirandus on a weekly basis on their Mirandus Monday announcements. Recently, they partnered with the Brave Browser, to release a limited-edition Physical NFT on the Brave Store. These NFTs, which sold out quickly, come with an in-game Homestead NFT, of which only 1000 will exist.

Gala Games已在其Mirandus星期一公告中每周发布有关Mirandus的信息。最近，他们与Brave浏览器合作，在Brave商店中发布了限量版的Physical NFT。这些NFT很快售罄，带有游戏内置Homestead NFT，其中只有1000个存在。

“I am extremely excited about Mirandus,” says Gala Games CMO Jason Brink. “McCarthy is a legend in the game development industry and made some of my favorite games, including Arcanum and Farmville 2. I know Mirandus is going to be AMAZING.”

“我对Mirandus感到非常兴奋，” Gala Games首席营销官Jason Brink说。 “ McCarthy是游戏开发行业的传奇人物，制作了一些我最喜欢的游戏，包括Arcanum和Farmville2。我知道Mirandus将会令人赞叹。”

“Since launching Polyient Games in early 2020, we have been leading the charge to develop the NFT asset class as a true alternative to traditional cryptocurrencies,” says Brad Robertson, the Polyient CEO. “Our latest investment in the Gala Games token economy further underlines our commitment to establishing these new standards in the emerging digital economy.”

“自2020年初推出Polyient Games以来，我们一直领导开发NFT资产类别，以替代传统的加密货币，” Polyient首席执行官布拉德·罗伯森（Brad Robertson）说。 “我们对Gala Games令牌经济的最新投资进一步表明了我们致力于在新兴的数字经济中建立这些新标准的承诺。”

The sale, executed on OpenSea, was submitted to the Guinness Book of World Records for the largest single NFT transaction. Witnessed by prominent blockchain greats this record raises the bar for in-game NFTs moving forward.

该交易在OpenSea上执行，已提交给吉尼斯世界纪录大全，成为最大的一笔NFT交易。在著名的区块链巨匠的见证下，这一记录提高了游戏中NFT前进的门槛。

Gala Games, from one of the founders of Zynga and some of the creative minds behind FarmVille and Words With Friends, brings the next revolution in gaming. Working to decentralize the $148B industry, Gala Games is re-imagining gaming to benefit creators and players, alike.

来自Zynga的一位创始人以及FarmVille和Words With Friends背后的一些创造力的Gala Games带来了游戏的下一场革命。致力于分散$ 148B行业的权力，Gala Games正在重新想象游戏，以造福于创作者和玩家。

Polyient Games, a subsidiary of investment group Polyient, is a unique investment ecosystem focused on the NFT and decentralized finance (DeFi) markets. Polyient’s portfolio includes both equity and tokens from leading NFT and DeFi startups.

Polyient Games是投资集团Polyient的子公司，是一个专注于NFT和分散金融（DeFi）市场的独特投资生态系统。 Polyient的投资组合包括NFT和DeFi领先创业公司的股权和代币。
