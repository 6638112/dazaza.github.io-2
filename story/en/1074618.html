Dutch Foundation Seeks Consumer Damages Over Apple, Google App Payments
荷兰基金会寻求消费者对苹果、谷歌应用支付的损害赔偿

2022-02-22 09:45:09

AMSTERDAM, Feb 15 (Reuters) - (This Feb. 15 story corrects job title in paragraph 9)

阿姆斯特丹，2月15日（路透社）--（这篇2月15日的报道更正了第9段中的职位名称）

Apple (AAPL.O) and Google face a potential class action lawsuit in the Netherlands over app store charges, after a foundation headed by Dutch entrepreneur Alexander Klöpping began gathering claimants.

苹果公司（ApL.O）和谷歌在荷兰因App Store的指控而面临一场潜在的集体诉讼，此前荷兰企业家Alexander Kl•奥普的一个基金会开始收集索赔人。

The action, which still faces legal hurdles before any court hearing, reflects growing criticism over how much the two firms charge developers for payments on their app stores, while the Netherlands has become increasingly popular as a jurisdiction to pursue complaints against multinational firms.

这起诉讼在任何法庭听证会之前仍面临法律障碍，反映出对两家公司向开发商收取应用商店付款金额的批评越来越多，而荷兰作为起诉跨国公司的司法管辖区，已越来越受欢迎。

Klöpping is a co-founder of Blendle, a digital platform that enables users to buy individual news articles, which he sold in 2020. He told Reuters his determination to pursue the tech giants grew out of his experience at Blendle.

Klöping是Blendle的联合创始人，Blendle是一个数字平台，用户可以购买个人新闻文章，他在2020年出售了该平台。他告诉路透社，他追求科技巨头的决心源于他在Blendle的经历。

"The reason it's getting so much attention right now is that everyone feels in their gut that there's this imbalance of power when it comes to big tech companies."

"原因是'；现在引起如此多关注的是，每个人的内心都觉得'；说到大型科技公司，这就是权力的不平衡"

He said while developers have complained most about app store practices, costs are ultimately passed on to consumers.

他说，虽然开发者对应用商店的做法抱怨最多，但成本最终会转嫁到消费者身上。

Klöpping's App Store Claims Foundation is being represented by law firm Hausfeld, with funding from Fortress Investment Group.

克尔平和#39；AppStore索赔基金会由法律公司HaSfFD公司代表，由堡垒投资集团提供资金。

Apple did not reply to requests for comment. A spokesperson for Alphabet (GOOGL.O), which owns Google, had no immediate comment but said the company had reduced fees on Google Play.

苹果没有回复置评请求。拥有谷歌的Alphabet（Google.O）的发言人没有立即置评，但表示该公司已经降低了谷歌Play的费用。

At this stage in the Dutch class action proceedings -- which were codified in a 2020 law -- formal notice has been sent to Apple and Google.

在荷兰集体诉讼程序的现阶段，正式通知已发送给苹果和谷歌。荷兰集体诉讼程序已被编入2020年的一项法律。

Rob Okhuijsen, strategy director at Hausfeld, said the next step will be submitting evidence to the Amsterdam District Court in April. If a judge agrees, the court would then begin weighing the merits of the complaint.

豪斯菲尔德的战略主管罗伯·奥赫伊森说，下一步将于4月向阿姆斯特丹地区法院提交证据。如果法官同意，法院将开始权衡申诉的是非曲直。

CMS lawyer Bart-Adriaan de Ruijter, who is not involved in the case but specialises in corporate litigation, said the Netherlands is becoming a magnet for suits against multinationals. Recent instances include a $339 million action launched last month against Airbus and a ruling last year ordering Shell to cut its carbon emissions. read more
