Apple agrees to let Parler back on the App Store
Apple同意让Parler回到App Store上

2021-04-20 04:12:25

After removing Parler from the App Store back in January, Apple has reportedly decided to allow the app to come back. According to a new report from CNN, Apple has approved Parler’s latest attempt to return to the App Store follow improvements to the moderation system…

据报道，Apple决定允许应用程序回来的苹果公司删除帕勒。根据CNN的一份新报告，Apple已批准Parler的最新尝试返回App Store遵循适度系统的改进......

In January,  Apple had given Parler 24 hours’ notice to implement a content moderation system or else face removal from the App Store. The company resisted and therefore the app was removed as Apple had promised. Over the last few months, however, Apple had kept the door open to allowing Parler back into the App Store, based on whether it implemented a content moderation system.

1月份，Apple给了Parler 24小时通知，以实施内容审核系统，或者从App Store中删除脸部。该公司抵制，因此该应用已被删除，因为苹果公司承诺。然而，在过去的几个月里，Apple基于是否实施了内容泛化系统，让Apple保持了允许Parler回到App Store。

The report cites a letter that Apple sent to Sen. Mike Lee (R-UT) and Rep. Ken Buck (R-CO). In the letter, Apple says that Parler has implemented a system to better detect hate speech and other offensive content:

该报告引用了一封苹果寄给SEN.Mike Lee（R-UT）和代表的信函。肯巴斯（R-Co）。在这封信中，Apple表示，Parler已经实施了一个更好地检测仇恨言论和其他攻击内容的系统：

Apple has approved Parler’s return to the iOS app store following improvements the social media company made to better detect and moderate hate speech and incitement, according to a letter the iPhone maker sent to Congress on Monday.

根据iPhone制造商在星期一发给国会的一封信，Apple已批准了Parler返回IOS App Store。

Apple’s approval of Parler’s latest version comes after it rejected the company’s attempt to return to the App Store last month. At the time, Apple said that Parler had not made “sufficient” changes to warrant re-entry into the App Store.

Apple对Parler最新版本的批准来到了该公司上个月返回App Store的尝试之后。当时，Apple表示，Parler没有提出“足够的”变更，以便重新进入App Store。

Parler was also removed from the Google Play Store, and as of this article, there is no indication that Google has allowed Parler to come back. Parler was also dropped by Amazon Web Services, but it has since found alternative hosting solutions to stay online.

帕勒也被从Google Play商店中删除，as本文中，没有迹象表明谷歌让Parler回来。亚马逊Web服务也被删除，但它已经找到了替代托管解决方案来留在网上。

Parler is not yet available in the App Store again, but now that Apple has approved the latest version, Parler can release it at any time. “Apple anticipates that the updated Parler app will become available immediately upon Parler releasing it,” Apple wrote in the letter to Congress.

Parler尚未再次在App Store中使用，但现在Apple已批准最新版本，Parler可以随时释放它。 “Apple预计更新的Parler应用程序将在放置释放它时立即可用，”Apple在致国会信中写道。
