We welcome sessions on a wide range of topics relevant to trustworthy AI and the internet health movement, but all sessions must be participatory, accessible, and inclusive. You are welcome to submit more than one session proposal.

我们欢迎就与值得信赖的人工智能和互联网健康运动相关的广泛主题举行会议，但所有会议都必须是参与性、可访问性和包容性的。欢迎您提交多个期次提案。

这是你第一次向MozFest提交建议书吗？在我们的MozFest 101了解更多信息。

Participatory sessions engage and activate audience members from the start and gets them making and doing.

参与式会议从一开始就吸引和激活了观众，让他们开始行动起来。

Learn more about MozFest, the session curation process, and how to design a participatory session in this comprehensive Facilitator Guide.

在这本全面的主持人指南中，了解更多关于MozFest、会话策划过程以及如何设计参与式会话的信息。

Anyone may submit a session proposal for MozFest 2021. You don’t need any particular level of expertise to facilitate a session, as long as you have a great project, idea, or topic. However, if you are under the age of 16 your parent or guardian must complete the form for you.

任何人都可以提交2021年MozFest的会议提案。只要你有一个很棒的项目、想法或主题，你就不需要任何特定水平的专业知识来促成一次会议。但是，如果您未满16岁，您的父母或监护人必须为您填写表格。

Yes, for anyone who would like to submit using an audio or video recording, please email us at festival@mozilla.org and we will send you the full list of questions to answer.

是的，对于任何想要使用音频或视频记录提交的人，请发送电子邮件给我们，电子邮件地址是Festival@mozilla.org，我们将给您发送完整的问题列表来回答。

We have created 3 session formats so that we can understand what type of session you will deliver and understand how you see the participants interacting with your session.

我们创建了3种会话格式，以便我们能够了解您将提供哪种类型的会话，并了解您如何看待参与者与您的会话的互动。

Includes art exhibits, film & video, or podcasts. Available for asynchronous self-navigating experiences.  Participants can explore these at any time. No time limit.

包括艺术展览、电影和视频或播客。可用于异步自导航体验。所有参与者可以随时探索这些体验。没有时间限制。

Sessions focused on bringing the MozFest community together in a social format. This could include a fitness or yoga session, baking classes or a games evening. No time limit.

会议的重点是以社交的形式将Mozfest社区聚集在一起。这可能包括健身或瑜伽课程，烘焙课程或游戏之夜。没有时间限制。

Synchronous live sessions provides space for building shared understanding, comparing diverse experiences and divergent perspectives, sharing knowledge or surfacing collective vision. Time limit: 60 mins

同步现场会议为建立共同的理解、比较不同的经验和不同的观点、分享知识或提出集体愿景提供了空间。时间限制：60分钟。

Learning sessions participants can experience on their own schedule with the facilitator being accessible for open office hours or questions for a set period of time, as well as offline documentation. Time limit: 15 mins

学员可以按照自己的日程安排体验学习课程，授课人员可以在一段设定的时间内开放办公时间或提出问题，还可以查看离线文档。时间限制：15分钟。

Synchronous live sessions held that allow for the group to collaboratively work on a project, learn a new skill together or co-develop ideas, collectively problem solve. Time limit: 60 mins

举行同步的现场会议，允许团队协作处理一个项目，一起学习新技能，或者共同开发想法，共同解决问题。时间限制：60分钟。

For the Arts, Films and Podcasts and Social sessions you will see 0 minutes listed on the proposal form - if your session is accepted we will be in contact to confirm the timing.

对于艺术、电影、播客和社交课程，您将看到建议书表格上列出了0分钟-如果您的课程被接受，我们将与您联系以确认时间。

Participants at MozFest come with a wide range of backgrounds, ages, abilities, disciplines, and levels of expertise. The diversity of the MozFest audience is what makes the festival unique. You may have anywhere from 3 to 30 participants pre-register for your session. Anyone, of any ability level, is welcome to join the session during the event.

MozFest的参与者具有广泛的背景、年龄、能力、学科和专业水平。莫扎节观众的多样性是这个音乐节的独特之处。您可能有3到30名参与者预先注册您的课程。任何人，任何能力水平的人，都欢迎在活动期间参加会议。

MozFest 2021 will be a very different year for the festival. Sessions proposed this year will take place as part of our virtual festival and will be delivered using an online platform.

对于这个音乐节来说，2021年的MozFest将是非常不同的一年。今年提出的会议将作为我们虚拟节日的一部分，并将使用在线平台提供。

Pick the right Space for your session. The MozFest Weekend experience is organized into thematic sections called “Spaces”. Each Space is a collection of sessions, art installations, and workshops exploring issues and current topics relevant to the internet health movement. Your session proposal should relate and respond to the Space you select.

为您的会话选择合适的空间。MozFest周末体验被组织成称为“空间”的主题部分。每个空间是一个会议、艺术装置和研讨会的集合，探索与互联网健康运动相关的问题和当前主题。您的课程提案应与您选择的空间相关并做出回应。

You must attend one of the facilitator training sessions before the event. This is for everyone facilitating, whether brand new to the festival or someone who has previously led a session at the festival.

您必须在活动前参加其中一个辅导员培训课程。这是为每个人提供便利的，无论是全新的音乐节还是以前在电影节上主持过一次会议的人。

艺术节团队已经列出了5个小贴士，用来设计吸引人和包容的会议，这将给你和你的工作带来成功。

After submitting your proposal, your name, organisation, and session data will be displayed publicly using PreTalx (our open-source curation platform) as part of the curation process. Any sensitive information that you submit (such as your email address and accessibility requirements) are redacted from public view, and can only be seen by our programme team.

提交您的提案后，您的姓名、组织和会议数据将使用PreTalx(我们的开源策展平台)作为策展过程的一部分公开展示。您提交的任何敏感信息(如您的电子邮件地址和可访问性要求)都将从公众视线中编辑，并且只能由我们的计划团队查看。
