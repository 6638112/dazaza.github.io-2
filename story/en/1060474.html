In a statement, YouTube TV said it is negotiating “in good faith” with Roku to reach a new deal. “Unfortunately, Roku often engages in these types of tactics in their negotiations,” the statement said. “We’re disappointed that they chose to make baseless claims while we continue our ongoing negotiations. All of our work with them has been focused on ensuring a high quality and consistent experience for our viewers. We have made no requests to access user data or interfere with search results. We hope we can resolve this for the sake of our mutual users.”

在一份声明中，Youtube电视表示，它与Roku谈判“真诚”达成新政。 “不幸的是，Roku经常从事这些类型的策略在谈判中，”声明说。 “我们很失望，因为我们选择在继续进行持续的谈判时做出毫无根据的索赔。我们与他们的所有工作都集中在确保您的观众的高质量和一致的体验。我们没有请求访问用户数据或干扰搜索结果。我们希望我们能够为我们的共同用户解决这个问题。“

In its own lengthy statement, a Roku spokesperson blasted Google’s actions in detail. The tech giant is “attempting to use its YouTube monopoly position to force Roku into accepting predatory, anti-competitive and discriminatory terms that will directly harm Roku and our users.” The company has also sent an email to customers this morning expressing the concerns.

在自己的冗长声明中，一个roku发言人详细抨击了谷歌的行动。科技巨头是“试图利用其YouTube垄断地位，强迫罗湖接受将直接伤害Roku和我们的用户的掠夺性，反竞争和歧视性术语。”该公司本前还向客户发送了一封电子邮件，表达了担忧。

Roku is arguing that YouTube and Google are out to manipulate the user experience to siphon data and tilt search results in YouTube’s favor, among other complaints. It also maintains that Google could require Roku to spend money upgrading microchips or other equipment in order to accommodate YouTube TV.

Roku是争论YouTube和Google是为了操纵用户体验到Siphon数据，并在其他投诉中倾斜搜索结果。它还坚持认为，谷歌可能需要Roku才能花钱升级微芯片或其他设备，以适应YouTube电视。

The current agreement between the companies will expire in the next few days. While the Roku statement did not specify a date, this week will see April end and May begin, a turning of the calendar that matches with most distribution contract deadlines.

公司之间的本协议将在未来几天内到期。虽然Roku声明没有指定日期，但本周将看到4月结束，可能开始，与大多数分销合同截止日期的日历转动。

YouTube TV, a streaming bundle of linear channels which launched in 2017, arrived on Roku in 2018. The main YouTube app dates back years earlier on Roku, and the current agreement for it is understood to not be on the verge of expiration. Executives at Google corporate parent Alphabet said last year that YouTube TV had passed 3 million subscribers. Along with Hulu + Live TV and Sling TV, it is one of the leading packagers of TV via streaming.

YouTube电视台，2017年推出的一条线性频道的流束束，于2018年抵达Roku。主要的YouTube应用程序在Roku上返回几年，而目前的协议被理解为不在临时的临时。 Google Corporate父母的高管父母字母表上年表示YouTube电视已通过300万用户。除了Hulu +直播电视和吊带电视之外，它是电视的领先包装器之一，通过流式传输。

Financial terms are often at the root of distribution friction, but Roku says in the case of YouTube TV it is more concerned about the use of data than it is about money. The company insists it is not asking for “a single additional dollar in value” from YouTube in a new agreement.

财务术语往往是分布摩擦的根源，但Roku在YouTube电视的情况下说，更关注使用数据的使用而不是关于金钱。该公司坚持认为，在新协议中，它不会要求YouTube从YouTube询问“价值额外的美元。

Across Roku’s footprint, the YouTube TV subscriber population is fairly small, but the platform is prime real estate for any streaming purveyor. The company has had bare-knuckled negotiations with WarnerMedia for HBO Max, NBCUniversal for Peacock and with Fox Corp., with the battles increasingly spilling into public view. Along with Amazon Fire TV, Roku has become a primary gatekeeper for streaming in the U.S., with 51 million active accounts as of the end of 2020.

跨越Roku的足迹，YouTube电视订阅者人口相当小，但该平台是任何流媒体提供者的主要房地产。该公司与HBO Max，NBCuniversal的Warnermedia进行了赤裸裸的谈判，用于孔雀和Fox Corp.，争夺逐渐蔓延到公众视野中。与亚马逊火电视一起，Roku已成为美国，截至2020年底5100万活跃账户的主要门卫。

Google as a company has been the subject of growing scrutiny in the U.S. and Europe, a fact that Roku seized upon in its statement. Last fall, the U.S. Department of Justice and 11 state attorneys general filed a civil antitrust lawsuit. The complaint said it aims to stop Google from “unlawfully maintaining monopolies through anticompetitive and exclusionary practices in the search and search advertising markets.”

谷歌作为一家公司一直是美国和欧洲日益严重审查的主题，这是罗库在其发言中扣押的事实。去年秋天，美国司法部和11个国家律师将军提出了民事反垄断诉讼。投诉称，它旨在阻止谷歌从“通过检索和搜索广告市场的反竞争和排除措施”非法维护垄断。“

Given the legal action against Google, “it should come as no surprise that Google is now demanding unfair and anti-competitive terms that harm Roku’s users,” the statement said.

声明说，鉴于对谷歌的法律诉讼，“它应该毫不奇怪，谷歌现在要求危害Roku的用户的不公平和反竞争术语”。

“We believe consumers stand to benefit from Google and Roku reaching a fair agreement that preserves consumers access to YouTube TV, protects user data and promotes a competitive, free and open marketplace,” Roku’s statement concluded. “We are committed to trying to achieve that goal.”

“我们相信消费者掌握从谷歌和Roku达到公平的协议，保留消费者访问YouTube电视，保护用户数据并促进竞争，自由和开放的市场，”Roku的陈述结束。 “我们致力于努力实现这一目标。”
