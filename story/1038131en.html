Urban Explorers Reveal a Treasure Trove of Soviet Computing Power
城市探险者揭示了苏联计算能力的宝藏

2020-12-07 04:46:41

All the chances – I used to work for a while at the plant, making clones of PDP-11 and then VAX-11. The most beautiful thing was, that original DEC software was loading and running, and once I got distro of C language, I begun professional era, that is still valid and of value even today. Before that, we had to write MACRO-11 assembly for the machine, Cobol for the data processing applications. At the same time, I had second job, teaching kids how to use Apple ][ clones too. Very good time, where we were digging very fundamentals of computing. My kid wasn’t interested later in internals, they are mostly surface users of electronic gadgets nowadays. One shops, then consumes – incomparable.

所有的机会–我曾经在工厂工作了一段时间，先克隆了PDP-11，然后又克隆了VAX-11。 最美丽的是，原始的DEC软件正在加载并运行，一旦我获得了C语言的发行版，便开始了专业时代，即使在今天，它仍然是有效且有价值的。 在此之前，我们必须为机器编写MACRO-11组件，为数据处理应用编写Cobol。 同时，我有第二份工作，也教孩子们如何使用Apple] [克隆。 很好的时光，我们在这里研究了非常基础的计算。 后来，我的孩子对内部构造不再感兴趣，他们如今主要是电子产品的地面用户。 一商店，然后消费–无与伦比。
