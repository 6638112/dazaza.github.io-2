Covid-19: Cuba will request WHO approval for homegrown vaccine
Covid-19：古巴将请求世卫组织批准国产疫苗

2022-02-17 02:23:34

Cuban health officials have said that they will apply for World Health Organization approval for one of the country’s homegrown covid-19 vaccines, as they announced that they had secured funding to produce 200 million vaccine doses for low income countries.

古巴卫生官员2019冠状病毒疾病疫苗接种申请，他们将申请世界卫生组织批准的一种国产CVID-19疫苗，因为他们宣布他们已经获得资金，为低收入国家生产2亿种疫苗。

Cuba’s health regulator (CECMED) has approved the use of three of the country’s homegrown vaccines, which are exported to four countries, but none has been yet been approved by WHO. 1 That should change this year, said Rolando Pérez Rodríguez, director of science and innovation at Cuba’s state run pharmaceutical conglomerate, BioCubaFarma.

古巴卫生监管机构（CECMED）已批准使用该国三种国产疫苗，这些疫苗出口到四个国家，但尚未获得世卫组织的批准。1古巴国有制药集团BioCubaFarma的科学和创新主管罗兰多·佩雷斯·罗德里格斯说，今年应该会有所改变。

“We have formally requested the prequalification of one vaccine: Abdala,” Rodriguez told a press conference in Havana on 25 January. “We are in the final review of the documents, and it should be sent in the following days to the WHO.”

1月25日，罗德里格斯在哈瓦那的新闻发布会上说：“我们正式要求对一种疫苗进行资格预审：阿卜杜拉。”。“我们正在对这些文件进行最后审查，应该在接下来的几天内将其发送给世卫组织。”

The application for WHO approval of a second Cuban vaccine, Soberana 2, should be sent in the coming weeks and is also expected to gain authorisation for use this year, said Dagmar García Rivera at the Finlay Institute of Vaccines in Havana.

哈瓦那芬莱疫苗研究所的达格玛·加西亚·里维拉说，世卫组织批准第二种古巴疫苗Soberana 2的申请应在未来几周内发出，并有望在今年获得使用许可。

Cuba has regained control of the record rate of covid-19 infections seen last summer. Its rollout of Abdala, Soberana 2, and a booster vaccine, Soberana Plus, has kept the more infectious omicron variant under control after it reported 861 new daily infections per million population in August 2021, the highest in the world. 2 On 24 January, by contrast, Cuba reported 284 new cases per day per million people.

去年夏天，古巴2019冠状病毒疾病的记录率已经恢复。阿布达拉、索巴拉那2号和一种增强疫苗SoeSuraPlus的推出，在2021年8月报告了每百万人口中861个新的每日感染量之后，保持了更具感染力的OmiCon变异体的控制，这是世界上最高的。2相比之下，1月24日，古巴报告了每百万人每天284例新病例。

Abdala is 92.3% effective against symptomatic infection after three doses, and Soberana 2 is 92.4% effective after two doses and an additional dose of Soberana Plus, non-peer-reviewed studies have shown. 3 4 Cuba has other vaccine candidates in trials, including Soberana 1 and Mambisa—an intranasal vaccine—as well as one that targets the nascent omicron variant. 5

非同行评审的研究显示，三剂Abdala对症状性感染的有效率为92.3%，两剂和额外一剂Soberana Plus后，Soberana 2对症状性感染的有效率为92.4%。3.4古巴还有其他候选疫苗在试验中，包括Soberana 1和Mambisa鼻内疫苗，以及针对新生omicron变种的疫苗。5.

The island nation has fully vaccinated 86% of its population and administered 2.97 doses for every person, more than anywhere else in the world. 6

这个岛国已经为86%的人口接种了疫苗，并为每个人接种了2.97剂，比世界上任何地方都多。6.

Cuba exports its vaccines to Iran, Nicaragua, Venezuela, and Vietnam and is in discussion with more than 15 countries, most of which are low income nations, said Rodríguez. It recently donated covid-19 vaccines to Syria and to St Vincent and the Grenadines and is sharing knowledge and technical know-how with other countries to help them manufacture the vaccines locally.

罗德里格斯说，古巴向伊朗、尼加拉瓜、委内瑞拉和越南出口疫苗，并正在与15多个国家进行讨论，其中大多数是低收入国家。最近，2019冠状病毒疾病疫苗捐赠给叙利亚、圣文森特和Grenadines，并与其他国家分享知识和技术知识，以帮助他们在当地生产疫苗。

Rodríguez said, “We are open to any proposal that implies a greater impact of our vaccines on the world . . . [Lack of WHO approval] has not been an obstacle for countries interested in the Cuban vaccines.” He added that the vaccines had been sold at a “price of solidarity” comparable to the Covax scheme, which distributes vaccines to low and middle income countries. The country’s manufacturing capacity allows it to produce around 120 million vaccine doses a year. 7

罗德里格斯说，“我们对任何暗示我们的疫苗对世界产生更大影响的提议持开放态度……对对古巴疫苗感兴趣的国家来说，（缺乏世卫组织批准）并不是一个障碍。”他补充说，疫苗的销售价格与Covax计划相当，Covax计划向中低收入国家分发疫苗。该国的生产能力使其能够每年生产约1.2亿剂疫苗。7.

However, WHO approval is a requisite for Covax vaccines, which has prevented Cuban shots from being administered through the WHO co-led mechanism. “Perhaps when the authorisation is there, that will facilitate or accelerate distribution,” said Rodríguez.

然而，世卫组织的批准是新冠肺炎疫苗的必要条件，新冠肺炎疫苗阻止古巴疫苗通过世卫组织共同领导的机制接种。罗德里格斯说：“也许当授权存在时，这将促进或加速分销。”。

Preliminary studies show that the vaccines hold up well against omicron, said Ileana Morales Suárez, director of science and technology innovation at Cuba’s Ministry of Public Health.

古巴公共卫生部科技创新主任伊莱安娜·莫拉莱斯·苏亚雷斯说，初步研究表明，疫苗对奥米克龙有效。

“Those vaccinated with Soberana and Abdala have high percentages of seroconversion against the omicron variant,” she said. The results, which should be published this week, were “very encouraging and favourable.”

“接种Soberana和Abdala疫苗的人对omicron变种的血清转化率很高，”她说。结果“非常令人鼓舞和有利”，应该在本周公布
