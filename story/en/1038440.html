It’s time—after myriad ground tests, SpaceX will let its Starship fly free
是时候了。经过无数次地面测试之后，SpaceX将让其飞船自由飞行

2020-12-08 23:37:00

After multiple ground-based tests of the propellant tanks inside its Starship prototype, and firings of the vehicle's three Raptor engines, SpaceX says the large vehicle may fly on Tuesday from the company's launch site near Boca Chica Beach in Texas. The earliest the flight may begin is 11am ET (1600 UTC), but more likely it will not occur until some time during the afternoon hours. Weather conditions are nearly perfect, but there are still myriad technical issues with this new hardware that could prevent a flight today.

在对星际飞船原型机内的推进剂坦克进行了多次地面测试，并点燃了车辆的三架Raptor发动机后，SpaceX表示，这辆大型飞行器可能会在周二从公司位于Boca Chica海滩附近的发射场飞行在德州。最早的飞行时间可能是美国东部时间上午11点（格林尼治标准时间1600 UTC），但更有可能直到下午时段的某个时间才会开始。天气条件几乎是完美的，但是这种新硬件仍然存在许多技术问题，可能会阻止今天的飞行。

This is the first time SpaceX has attempted to fly Starship to a high altitude—the vehicle may fly as high as 12.5km. Previous tests have only gone up to about 150 meters and have not included the flaps, nose cone, and other features needed to control the vehicle's flight in the thin, upper atmosphere.

这是SpaceX首次尝试将“星舰”飞行到更高的高度，该飞行器可能飞行高达12.5公里。之前的测试仅进行了约150米的测试，并未包括襟翼，前鼻锥和其他控制车辆在稀薄的高空飞行所需的功能。

Because of the new flight profile, much could go wrong with the test. After ascending to its peak altitude, the vehicle will perform a flip maneuver to reorient itself for a return to Earth at a 70° angle of attack. Then, near the surface, Starship's three engines will re-ignite and the vehicle will land vertically, under powered flight.

由于采用了新的飞行配置文件，因此测试可能会出错。上升到峰值高度后，车辆将执行翻转操作以重新定向，使其以70°迎角返回地球。然后，在动力飞行下，星际飞船的三个引擎将在水面附近重新点火，并且车辆将垂直降落。

SpaceX founder and chief engineer Elon Musk has estimated that there is about a one-in-three chance of the vehicle landing safely. In talking to several current and former employees, this estimate seems about right. There are many unknowns with regard to this flight, and in lieu of testing them with further simulations, the company has chosen to just go ahead and fly.

SpaceX创始人兼首席工程师埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）估计，飞行器安全着陆的可能性约为三分之一。在与几名现任和前任雇员交谈时，这一估计似乎是正确的。关于这次飞行，还有许多未知数，为了代替通过进一步的模拟对其进行测试，该公司选择继续飞行。

It can afford to do this because the program is rich in hardware, and following an iterative design method. The vehicle on the pad in South Texas is "Serial Number 8," or SN8. This Starship prototype is the same size and shape of the final vehicle, although the company is still tinkering with the landing legs, flaps, and much, much more. SpaceX already has nearly completed construction of SN9; SN10 is perhaps a couple of weeks behind, and parts of several other vehicles are in work. With each vehicle, the welds get smoother and the manufacturing processes more precise. Lessons learned from SN8 will be incorporated into future vehicles.

之所以可以这样做，是因为该程序具有丰富的硬件，并且遵循迭代设计方法。南德克萨斯州垫上的车辆是＆＃34;序列号8＆＃34;或SN8。尽管该公司仍在修补着陆脚，襟翼等等，但该Starship原型具有与最终飞行器相同的尺寸和形状。 SpaceX已经几乎完成了SN9的构建； SN10可能要落后几周，其他几辆车的零件仍在工作。每辆车的焊接都变得更加平滑，制造过程也更加精确。从SN8获得的经验教训将被纳入未来的车辆中。

To reach orbit, Starship will need to be launched on a Super Heavy rocket. SpaceX has begun constructing a prototype version of this rocket in South Texas as well. If SN8 or SN9 flies successfully, it is possible that SpaceX may be able to attempt an orbital flight late next year with both the rocket and Starship upper stage. Within a couple of years, Starship may begin launching very large payloads into space, with human flights potentially following by the mid-2020s.

为了达到轨道，将需要在“超级重型”火箭上发射“星际飞船”。 SpaceX也已经开始在南德克萨斯州建造这种火箭的原型机。如果SN8或SN9成功飞行，SpaceX可能会在明年下半年尝试在火箭和星际飞船的上层进行轨道飞行。几年之内，星际飞船可能会开始向太空中发射非常大的有效载荷，而人类飞行可能会在2020年代中期完成。

But that is getting ahead of the game. Today, or later this week, is all about testing the prototype, with its novel Raptor engines, large, stainless steel fuel tanks, flaps, and the complex flight software written to control the large vehicle's flight through the atmosphere.

但这已经领先于游戏了。今天或本周晚些时候，将通过其新颖的Raptor发动机，大型不锈钢油箱，襟翼以及为控制大型车辆在大气中飞行而编写的复杂飞行软件，对原型进行测试。
