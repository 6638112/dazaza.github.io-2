YouTube在播放视频时遇到普遍的技术问题

2020-11-12 09:35:00

users in multiple regions around the world encountered issues in playing videos on the site Wednesday.

周三，全球多个地区的用户在该网站上播放视频时遇到了问题。

The site’s TeamYouTube account acknowledged the problems in a Twitter post. “If you’re having trouble watching videos on YouTube right now, you’re not alone — our team is aware of the issue and working on a fix. We’ll follow up here with any updates,” it tweeted at 7:23 p.m. ET.

该网站的TeamYouTube账号在推特上承认了这些问题。“如果你现在在YouTube上观看视频有问题，你并不孤单--我们的团队已经意识到了这个问题，并正在努力解决这个问题。”晚上7点23分，它在推特上写道：“如果有任何最新消息，我们会在这里跟进。”埃特。

According to monitoring site DownDetector, user-reported YouTube problems spiked at around 7 p.m. ET. The issues appeared most acute in the Northeast U.S., the U.K. and Western Europe, as well as in Mexico, Australia and South America. Around 95% of the issues reported were with watching videos, per DownDetector.

根据监测网站DownDetector的数据，用户报告的YouTube问题在晚上7点左右激增。埃特。这些问题似乎在美国东北部、英国和西欧以及墨西哥、澳大利亚和南美最为严重。根据DownDetector的数据，大约95%的报告问题与观看视频有关。

Users who tried to play videos on YouTube video encountered a spinning wheel, with videos failing to initiate playback (before sometimes starting after several minutes). YouTube was sporadically displaying error messages, including one that says “An error occurred. Please try again later.” For some users, YouTube’s homepage was not loading. Google’s YouTube TV subscription-television service also was down, according to user reports on social media.

试图在YouTube视频上播放视频的用户遇到了一个转轮，视频无法启动播放(有时会在几分钟后开始播放)。YouTube零星地显示错误消息，包括一条写着“发生错误”的消息。请稍后再试。“。对于一些用户来说，YouTube的主页没有加载。根据用户在社交媒体上的报告，谷歌的YouTube电视订阅电视服务也关闭了。

The problems were evident on desktop and mobile apps, as well as connected-TV devices including  Google’s Chromecast. YouTube ads also were not loading during the technical outage.

这些问题在桌面和移动应用程序以及包括谷歌Chromecast在内的联网电视设备上都很明显。在技术中断期间，YouTube广告也没有加载。

Extended outages are rare for YouTube, which boasts more than 2 billion monthly logged-in users and claims it streams more than 1 billion hours of video daily.

对于YouTube来说，长时间的停机是罕见的，它号称拥有超过20亿的月度登录用户，并声称每天播放超过10亿小时的视频。

In October 2018, YouTube experienced global access problems for approximately 90 minutes, with the issues also affecting YouTube TV and YouTube Music services.

2018年10月，YouTube经历了大约90分钟的全球访问问题，这些问题也影响了YouTube电视和YouTube音乐服务。
