从苹果的“再来一件事”活动中期待什么：首批苹果硅片驱动的Mac，MacOS Big Sur的发布日期，以及可能的AirTags，耳麦耳机

2020-11-10 09:19:13

Apple is expected to officially unveil its first computers powered by custom Arm-based silicon chips at its “One More Thing” event on Tuesday. The company is promising the new chips will deliver improved performance with less power consumption, and the switch also means Apple computers will support native iOS apps due to Apple’s A-series mobile and tablet chips sharing 64-bit Arm architecture.

苹果公司预计将在周二的“再来一件事”活动上正式发布其首批基于ARM硅芯片的定制电脑。该公司承诺，新芯片将以更低的功耗提供更高的性能，这也意味着苹果电脑将支持原生iOS应用程序，因为苹果公司的A系列移动和平板电脑芯片共享64位ARM架构。

The fact that Apple silicon-based computers are likely launching on Tuesday isn’t exactly a surprise; Apple said at WWDC in June it expected to ship the first Mac with an Apple-designed CPU by the end of the year. But naming the event after Steve Jobs’ iconic phrase indicates Apple thinks this will be a significant moment for the company — and hopefully these computers will be worth the wait.

苹果的硅基电脑可能会在周二发布，这一事实并不完全令人意外；苹果在6月份的WWDC上表示，预计将在今年年底前推出第一款搭载苹果设计的CPU的Mac电脑。但以史蒂夫·乔布斯这句标志性的话命名这场活动表明，苹果认为这将是公司的重要时刻--希望这些电脑值得等待。

There’s always the chance Apple will discuss other products as well. The company’s September and October events didn’t focus on just one product line; the September event featured both the Apple Watch and iPad, while the October event focused on the iPhone and the new HomePod mini. Rumors, as always, indicate Apple is working hard on lots of other products that may or may not see the light of day.

苹果也有可能会讨论其他产品。苹果公司9月和10月的活动并不只集中在一个产品线上；9月份的活动主要是Apple Watch和iPad，而10月份的活动主要是iPhone和新的HomePod mini。一如既往地，有传言称，苹果正在努力开发其他许多产品，这些产品可能会也可能不会面世。

The event kicks off at 1PM on Tuesday, so we won’t have to wait long to learn what Apple will be announcing. Here’s a rundown of what you can look out for.

发布会将于周二下午1点开始，所以我们用不了多久就能知道苹果将发布什么。以下是你可以注意的要点。

While Apple has only publicly promised to ship one Apple silicon-powered Mac computer by the end of the year, the company apparently plans to introduce more than one, according to Bloomberg. Apple is reportedly ramping up production of a new 13-inch MacBook Air, 13-inch MacBook Pro, and 16-inch MacBook Pro, and will show “at least” the two 13-inch laptops on Tuesday. Yet if you were hoping for a big design change to accompany the new internal architecture in the laptops, that’s not in the cards, according to Bloomberg.

据彭博社(Bloomberg)报道，虽然苹果只公开承诺在今年年底前发货一台苹果硅片驱动的Mac电脑，但该公司显然计划推出不止一台。据报道，苹果正在加紧生产新的13英寸MacBook Air、13英寸MacBook Pro和16英寸MacBook Pro，并将在周二“至少”展示这两款13英寸的笔记本电脑。然而，据彭博社报道，如果你希望笔记本电脑的新内部架构伴随着一个重大的设计变化，那是不可能的。

The processors in the new laptops will be “based on” the A14 chip found in the iPhone 12 lineup and the new iPad Air, Bloomberg reported. To help developers get a jump on porting their apps to the new chips, Apple has been offering a Mac mini Developer Transition Kit that’s powered by the A12Z, a variant of the A12 with an 8-core GPU that’s also found in the most recent iPad Pros released in March. Perhaps Apple will take a similar approach for the A14-based chip in the upcoming laptops.

据彭博社报道，新款笔记本电脑的处理器将“基于”iPhone 12系列和新iPad Air中的A14芯片。为了帮助开发者更快地将他们的应用移植到新的芯片上，苹果提供了一款Mac迷你开发者过渡套件，该套件由A12Z驱动，A12Z是A12的一个变种，配备了8核GPU，在3月份发布的最新iPad Pro中也可以找到这一点。也许苹果在即将推出的笔记本电脑中也会对基于A14的芯片采取类似的做法。

We found the A14 was quite capable in our reviews of those iOS devices, and presumably, Apple will show some impressive demos of the chip in the new laptops at the event to give us an idea of what its custom silicon will be capable of in computers, too. It was hard to tell just how fast the new chips will be when Apple first discussed them at WWDC, but it seems likely the company will go into a lot more detail about the processors’ performance on Tuesday.

我们发现A14在我们对这些iOS设备的评估中相当有能力，估计苹果将在发布会上展示一些令人印象深刻的新笔记本电脑中的芯片演示，让我们了解它的定制硅芯片在电脑中也能起到什么作用。当苹果在WWDC上第一次讨论这些新芯片时，很难说它们的速度会有多快，但看起来苹果公司可能会在周二更详细地介绍这些处理器的性能。

It also seems like a safe bet that Apple will talk more about Rosetta 2, which can automatically translate some existing Intel apps, and how developers can make their existing iOS and iPadOS apps run natively on Apple’s silicon-based Macs.

苹果似乎也会更多地谈论Rosetta 2，它可以自动翻译一些现有的英特尔应用程序，以及开发者如何让他们现有的iOS和iPadOS应用程序在苹果的硅基Mac电脑上本地运行，这似乎也是一个稳妥的押注。

If you’re hoping that Apple introduces a desktop Mac with Apple silicon on Tuesday, though, you might be out of luck, as Bloomberg’s report didn’t indicate that a new desktop will be announced at the event. Apple apparently does have a number of other Apple silicon-based Macs in the pipeline, though, including a redesigned iMac and a new Mac Pro.

不过，如果你希望苹果在周二推出搭载苹果硅片的台式机Mac，那你可能就大错特错了，因为彭博社的报道并没有暗示会在发布会上发布新的台式机。不过，苹果显然还有其他一些基于硅片的Mac正在筹备中，包括重新设计的iMac和新的Mac Pro。

If this is a Mac event as expected, then it seems likely that Apple will also give a release date for macOS Big Sur, the next major macOS update. Big Sur brings a huge visual overhaul to macOS, with things like new iOS-style icons for Apple apps and a translucent menu bar that blends into your wallpaper. There’s a dedicated control center to help you manage settings like Wi-Fi, display brightness, and volume, all in one place. And it’s not just visuals that got an overhaul — Big Sur has new system sounds, including the return of the Mac’s famous startup chime.

如果这是一场Mac发布会，那么苹果似乎也会给出MacOS Big Sur的发布日期，MacOS Big Sur是MacOS的下一次重大更新。Big Sur为MacOS带来了巨大的视觉变革，比如苹果应用程序的新iOS风格图标，以及与壁纸融为一体的半透明菜单栏。有一个专用的控制中心，可以帮助你在一个地方管理Wi-Fi、显示屏亮度和音量等设置。不仅仅是视觉效果有了翻新--Big Sur有了新的系统音效，包括Mac著名的启动钟声的回归。

Many Mac apps get updates as well. Safari in Big Sur has a customizable start screen, tab previews, and a privacy report feature that shows you what trackers the browser has blocked. Messages finally gets message effects, as well as features you might have already used in iOS 14, such as pinned replies and in-line conversations. Maps has a number of new features, too, including cycling directions and EV routing.

许多Mac应用程序也会获得更新。大苏尔的Safari有一个可定制的开始屏幕、标签预览和隐私报告功能，可以告诉你浏览器屏蔽了哪些追踪器。消息最终会获得消息效果，以及你可能已经在iOS 14中使用过的功能，例如固定回复和在线对话。地图也有许多新功能，包括骑行方向和电动汽车路线。

Although we’re expecting Tuesday’s event to be primarily about the Macs, Apple could certainly unveil non-Mac products.

尽管我们预计周二的发布会将主要围绕Mac电脑展开，但苹果肯定会推出非Mac产品。

Apple has long been rumored to be developing new high-end, over-ear headphones, so perhaps the company will finally unveil those on Tuesday. The headphones are rumored to have swappable ear pads and headbands, noise cancellation, Siri controls, and built-in touch controls. The headphones have recently run into some development difficulties, though, according to an October 26th Bloomberg report.

长期以来，一直有传言称，苹果正在开发新的高端耳机，因此，该公司可能最终会在周二发布这些耳机。据传，这款耳机有可交换的耳垫和头带，噪音消除，Siri控制，以及内置的触摸控制。然而，根据彭博社10月26日的一篇报道，这种耳机最近遇到了一些开发困难。

The headphones were apparently set to go into production “weeks ago,” but that milestone was pushed because the headband was too tight. Apple also reduced the size of the touch pads on the sides of the headphones, and the headphones may no longer have a replaceable headband, according to Bloomberg.

这款耳机显然是在“几周前”投入生产的，但这一里程碑被推迟了，因为头带太紧了。据彭博社(Bloomberg)报道，苹果还缩小了耳机侧面的触摸板尺寸，耳机可能不再有可更换的头带。

We’ve also been hearing rumors about Apple-made Tile-like trackers for quite awhile — references for the product appeared in iOS 13 beta code last June. The trackers, which could be called AirTags, are rumored to be small circular discs that could let you track things like a bag or your wallet via Apple’s Find My app. It’s expected that AirTags will have ultra-wideband technology to help with more precise tracking.

关于苹果制造的类似瓷砖的追踪器，我们也听到了很长一段时间的传言--去年6月，iOS13测试版代码中出现了对该产品的引用。据传，这种追踪器可以被称为AirTags，是一种小圆盘，可以让你通过苹果的Find My应用程序追踪包或钱包等东西。预计AirTags将拥有超宽带技术，以帮助进行更精确的跟踪。

Apple is also apparently working on a new Apple TV with a faster processor and an “upgraded” remote control that may not come out until next year, according to Bloomberg. Given that rumored release date, though, it doesn’t seem likely that Apple will announce it on Tuesday. And since Apple announced new Apple Watches and a new iPad Air in September and new iPhones in October, we probably won’t see new versions of those products on Tuesday, either.

据彭博社(Bloomberg)报道，苹果显然也在研发一款新的Apple TV，它的处理器速度更快，而且“升级”的遥控器可能要到明年才会发布。不过，考虑到传言中的发布日期，苹果似乎不太可能在周二宣布这一消息。而且，由于苹果在9月份发布了新的苹果手表和新的iPad Air，在10月份发布了新的iPhone，我们可能也不会在周二看到这些产品的新版本。
