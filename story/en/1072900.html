How can I tell if my SSID is in Google database?
如何判断我的SSID是否在谷歌数据库中？

2022-02-13 15:28:34

To improve Location services and estimate the location of a device, Google uses publicly broadcast Wi-Fi information from wireless access points and GPS, cell tower, and sensor data. This data is limited to information about the wireless access point itself, including its location.

为了改善定位服务并估计设备的位置，谷歌使用来自无线接入点、GPS、基站和传感器数据的公共广播Wi-Fi信息。这些数据仅限于有关无线接入点本身的信息，包括其位置。

To help apps like Google Maps work better, you can let Google's Location services use your Wi-Fi access point.

为了帮助像谷歌地图这样的应用程序更好地工作，你可以让谷歌'；s定位服务使用您的Wi-Fi接入点。

How do I opt my access point out of Google Location services? To opt out, change the SSID (name) of your Wi-Fi access point (your wireless network name) so that it ends with " _nomap." For example, if your SSID is " 12345," you would change it to " 12345_nomap."

我如何从谷歌定位服务中选择接入点？要选择退出，请更改Wi-Fi接入点（无线网络名称）的SSID（名称），使其以"结尾_诺玛" 例如，如果您的SSID为"；12345," 您可以将其更改为"；12345_nomap"

Make a physical connection between your access point and your computer with an Ethernet cable.

使用以太网电缆在接入点和计算机之间建立物理连接。

After you have the default gateway (it looks like 192.168.0.1), type it into the address bar of your web browser, like Chrome or Firefox. This step will take you to the control panel for your access point.

在你有了默认网关（看起来像192.168.0.1）之后，在你的网络浏览器的地址栏中输入它，比如Chrome或Firefox。此步骤将带您进入接入点的控制面板。

If asked to sign to your access point's controls, enter the right username and password. See the instructions that came with your access point.

如果被要求登录您的接入点'；s控件，输入正确的用户名和密码。请参阅接入点随附的说明。

After you’ve changed your SSID, re-establish a connection to the network using its new name. You can take this step from any devices using Wi-Fi, like your laptop or mobile phone. If your network is secured with a password, use the same password.

更改SSID后，使用新名称重新建立与网络的连接。您可以在任何使用Wi-Fi的设备上执行此步骤，如笔记本电脑或手机。如果您的网络有密码保护，请使用相同的密码。

To help ensure your changed SSID is submitted to Google quickly, open Google Maps on an Android device with Wi-Fi turned on. To establish a location fix near your Wi-Fi access point, tap My location .

为了帮助确保更改后的SSID快速提交给谷歌，请在打开Wi-Fi的Android设备上打开谷歌地图。要在Wi-Fi接入点附近建立定位，请点击“我的位置”。

Will my access point be removed from other Location services? Each Location service provider operates independently. Other providers can see the SSID method of opting out. We encourage other providers to respect the " _nomap" tag as a way to opt out from inclusion in a Location server.

我的接入点是否会从其他位置服务中删除？每个位置服务提供商都独立运营。其他提供商可以看到选择退出的SSID方法。我们鼓励其他供应商尊重"_nomap和#34；标记作为退出位置服务器的一种方式。

How do I secure my wireless network with strong password encryption? Separate from removing your access point from Google’s location services, you can find tips on how to secure your wireless network with strong password encryption on the Google Safety Center.

如何使用强密码加密保护无线网络？除了从谷歌的位置服务中移除接入点之外，你还可以在谷歌安全中心（Google Safety Center）上找到有关如何使用强密码加密保护无线网络的提示。
