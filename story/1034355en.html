苹果称MacOS Big Sur将于11月12日上市

2020-11-11 02:59:30

Apple says it’ll release macOS 11.0, dubbed Big Sur, on November 12th. The company first announced the new OS at its developer’s conference in June and released it as a public beta in August. This will be the first macOS to support Apple’s new M1 laptop chip. Once you download the new, free update, you’ll most likely immediately note the design and aesthetic changes, many taken from iOS.

苹果公司表示，将于11月12日发布名为Big Sur的MacOS 11.0。该公司在6月份的开发者大会上首次宣布了新的操作系统，并在8月份以公开测试版的形式发布了它。这将是第一款支持苹果新的M1笔记本电脑芯片的MacOS操作系统。一旦你下载了新的免费更新，你很可能会立即注意到设计和美学上的变化，其中许多变化来自iOS。

For one, Big Sur comes with a customizable Control Center where you can toggle brightness, Do Not Disturb, and other settings of your choice. It also includes a new notification center that’ll keep all of your notifications and widgets (also redesigned and available in the App Store) in one column, sort alerts by which are most recent, and group related notifications together. Both interfaces are translucent, just like iOS. Other changes include taller menu bars, font color changes based on the desktop background, and more translucent windows.

例如，Big Sur配备了一个可定制的控制中心，您可以在其中切换亮度、请勿打扰和其他您选择的设置。它还包括一个新的通知中心，它可以将你所有的通知和小工具(也是重新设计的，在App Store中可用)保存在一栏中，根据哪些是最新的警报进行排序，并将相关通知分组在一起。两个接口都是半透明的，就像IOS一样。其他变化包括更高的菜单栏、基于桌面背景的字体颜色更改以及更透明的窗口。

Some apps, like Mail and Photos, are also receiving design updates, and a new version of Maps for Mac is shipping along with the OS. That update features custom Guides, 360-degree location views, bicycle and electric-vehicle directions (which you can send directly to an iPhone), live updates for shared ETAs, congestion zones, and indoor maps.

邮件(Mail)和照片(Photos)等一些应用程序也在接收设计更新，新版Mac地图正在随操作系统一起发布。这一更新功能包括定制指南、360度位置视图、自行车和电动汽车方向(可以直接发送到iPhone)、共享ETA、拥堵区域和室内地图的实时更新。

In addition to design changes, Big Sur includes privacy-focused features, like an update to Safari called Privacy Report that lists cross-site trackers the browser has blocked over the last 30 days, as well as a password-monitoring tool through which Safari helps you upgrade to secure passwords if it detects that any of your saved passwords have been involved in a data breach. Big Sur’s App Store will also now include the types of data apps might collect and whether the data are shared with third parties for tracking.

除了设计上的改变，Big Sur还包括以隐私为重点的功能，比如Safari的更新名为隐私报告(Privacy Report)，它列出了浏览器在过去30天里屏蔽的跨站追踪器，还有一个密码监控工具，如果Safari检测到你保存的任何密码涉及数据泄露，它可以帮助你升级到安全密码。Big Sur的App Store现在还将包括应用程序可能收集的数据类型，以及这些数据是否被分享给第三方进行跟踪。

Finally, Apple also says Macs’ screens will turn on immediately when they’re opened, similarly to how the iPhone lights up when it’s picked up.

最后，苹果还表示，Mac电脑的屏幕在打开时会立即打开，就像iPhone在拿起时会亮起一样。
