E-commerce marketplace Wish falls after its public debut, dropping 8%+ to under $22 after pricing shares at $24
电子商务市场Wish在公开亮相后下跌，在将其股价定为24美元之后，股价下跌8％以上至22美元以下

2020-12-17 01:22:01

Wish is an online marketplace that features a variety of discounted goods, ranging from cheap home wares and apparel to electronics and toys.

Wish是一个在线市场，提供各种折扣商品，从廉价的家居用品和服装到电子产品和玩具，不一而足。

The opening trade was $22.75 per share, below its IPO pricing of $24 per share, which was at the high end of expectations.

开盘交易价格为每股22.75美元，低于其发行价每股24美元（处于预期的高端）。

The offering comes on the heels of successful IPOs from Airbnb and Doordash, both of which skyrocketed in their initial debuts.

此次发行是在Airbnb和Doordash成功进行首次公开​​募股之后的，这两家公司的首次公开募股都一飞冲天。

Shares of discount e-commerce marketplace Wish fell more than 9.3% in the company's market debut Wednesday.

折扣电商市场Wish的股票在周三上市时下跌了9.3％以上。

The opening trade was $22.75 per share, below its IPO pricing of $24 per share, which was at the high end of expectations. The offering raised $1.1 billion and gave the company an initial valuation of more than $14 billion. Private investors last valued the company at $11.2 billion.

开盘交易价格为每股22.75美元，低于其发行价每股24美元（处于预期的高端）。此次发行筹集了11亿美元，使该公司的初始估值超过140亿美元。私人投资者对公司的最新估值为112亿美元。

ContextLogic, Wish's parent company, filed to go public last month. Last week, the company estimated that it would price shares in the range of $22 to $24, and on Tuesday it priced at the top of that range.

Wish的母公司ContextLogic上个月申请公开上市。上周，该公司估计其股价将在22美元至24美元之间，而在周二，该公司的股价已达到该区间的最高价。

Wish, founded in 2010 by former  Google engineer Peter Szulczewski, is an online marketplace that features a variety of discounted goods, ranging from cheap home wares and apparel to electronics and toys. The app offers a slew of products for just a few dollars as a way to target low- to middle-income consumers with more affordable options than they can find on other sites, including  Amazon.

Wish由前Google工程师Peter Szulczewski于2010年创立，是一个在线市场，提供各种折扣商品，从廉价的家用商品和服装到电子产品和玩具。该应用程序仅需花费几美元就可以提供大量产品，以此来为中低收入消费者提供负担得起的选择，这是他们在包括亚马逊在内的其他网站上找不到的选择。

Wish is going public at a time when online sales are soaring, as the coronavirus pandemic has pushed more people to avoid making trips to physical stores in favor of shopping safely at home on their smartphone. The trend has led rival e-commerce companies like Amazon, Shopify and Etsy to be some of the biggest beneficiaries of the pandemic.

在线销售飙升之际，愿望就公开了，因为冠状病毒大流行促使更多人避免去实体店旅行，而赞成在智能手机上安全地在家购物。这种趋势使亚马逊，Shopify和Etsy等竞争对手的电子商务公司成为大流行的最大受益者。

The company's IPO prospectus revealed it's experiencing slower growth, from a much smaller base, than online retail counterparts like Amazon and Walmart. Wish reported $1.75 billion in revenue for the nine months ended Sept. 30, while it lost $176 million in the same period.

该公司的IPO招股说明书显示，与亚马逊和沃尔玛这样的在线零售同行相比，基数要小得多。截至9月30日的9个月，Wish的收入为17.5亿美元，而同期亏损了1.76亿美元。

The offering comes on the heels of successful IPOs from Airbnb and Doordash, both of which skyrocketed in their initial debuts. Wish's debut is likely to cap off what's already been a busy year for tech IPOs, particularly in the month of September, which saw a flood of software companies go public.

此次发行是在Airbnb和Doordash成功进行首次公开​​募股之后，这两家公司的首次公开募股都飞速上涨。 Wish的首次亮相可能会结束已经是技术IPO繁忙的一年，尤其是在9月份，那一年有大量软件公司上市。

ContextLogic is working with underwriters including Goldman Sachs and J.P. Morgan. Its stock trades on the Nasdaq under the ticker symbol "WISH."

ContextLogic正在与包括高盛（Goldman Sachs）和摩根大通（J.P. Morgan）在内的承销商合作。它的股票在纳斯达克交易，股票代码为＆＃34; WISH。
