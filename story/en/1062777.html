Analysis: the wallet used by DarkSide received a total of $17.5M in Bitcoin transactions since March, including a 75 BTC payment made by Colonial
分析：黑暗中使用的钱包在3月以来，总共收到比特币交易中的17.5亿美元，包括殖民地的75 BTC付款

2021-05-15 05:13:30

Elliptic clients can now use our transaction screening software to screen deposits for links to this high-profile incident

Elliptic客户端现在可以使用我们的交易筛选软件来筛选储存，以便与此高调事件的链接

Elliptic has identified the Bitcoin wallet used by the DarkSide ransomware group to receive ransom payments from its victims, based on our intelligence collection and analysis of blockchain transactions. This wallet received the 75 BTC payment made by Colonial Pipeline on May 8, following the crippling cyberattack on its operations - leading to widespread fuel shortages in the US.

椭圆型已确定暗边勒索瓶组使用的比特币钱包，根据我们的智力收集和对区间交易分析，从受害者获得赎金支付。这款钱包在5月8日之前收到了由殖民地管道制成的75 BTC支付，在其操作上的瘫痪网络中，导致美国广泛的燃料短缺。

The wallet has been active since 4th March 2021 and has received 57 payments from 21 different wallets. Some of these payments directly match ransoms known to have been paid to DarkSide by other victims, such as 78.29 BTC (worth $4.4 million) sent by chemical distribution company Brenntag on May 11.

钱包自2021年3月4日以来一直活跃，并从21个不同的钱包获得了57个付款。这些付款直接匹配了已知的赎金，被其他受害者支付给暗区，例如5月11日的化学配送公司Brenntag派出的78.29 BTC（价值440万美元）。

In total, the DarkSide wallet has received Bitcoin transactions since March with a total value of $17.5 million. Ransoms associated with previous attacks were paid to other wallets.

总共，黑暗的钱包自3月以来收到比特币交易，总价值为1750万美元。与以前的攻击相关的赎金被支付给其他钱包。

We can also use blockchain analysis to follow the money trail and determine where DarkSide is sending its ransomware proceeds, to launder them or convert them to cash.

我们还可以使用BlockChain分析来遵循金钱跟踪，并确定Darkside发送其赎金软件，以洗涤它们或将它们转换为现金。

It has been reported within the past hours that DarkSide itself has ceased operations and has had its funds seized - and indeed their wallet was emptied of the $5 million in Bitcoin it contained on Thursday afternoon.

在过去的几个小时内，Darkside本身已经停止了运营，并且已经抓住了它的资金 - 确实他们的钱包被列出了500万美元的比特币，它在星期四下午包含了500万美元。

But by tracing previous outflows from the wallet, we can gain insights into how DarkSide and its affiliates were laundering their previous proceeds. What we find is that 18% of the Bitcoin was sent to a small group of exchanges. This information will provide law enforcement with critical leads to identify the perpetrators of these attacks.

但是，通过从钱包中追踪以前的流出，我们可以深入了解暗侧面和其关联公司的洞察力如何洗涤他们之前的收益。我们发现的是，18％的比特币被派往一小组交易所。这些信息将提供执法，以批判导线确定这些攻击的肇事者。

An additional 4% has been sent to Hydra, the world’s largest darknet marketplace, servicing customers in Russia and neighboring countries. As we revealed in previous research, Hydra offers cash-out services alongside narcotics, hacking tools and fake IDs. These allow Bitcoin to be converted into gift vouchers, prepaid debit cards or cash Rubles. If you’re a Russian cybercriminal and you want to cash-out your crypto, then Hydra is an attractive option.

额外的4％已发送至世界上最大的DarkNet Marketplace，为俄罗斯和邻国提供客户的潮流。正如我们在以前的研究中透露，Hydra在毒品，黑客工具和假身份证中提供现金服务。这些允许比特币转换为礼品券，预付借记卡或现金卢布。如果你是俄罗斯网络犯罪分子，你想拿到你的加密，那么Hydra是一个有吸引力的选择。

By identifying this wallet, Elliptic’s clients, including financial institutions, crypto exchanges and fintechs will now be alerted to any client deposits that originate from the DarkSide wallet. By using our transaction and wallet screening tools they can ensure that DarkSide and other ransomware operators cannot cash-out or exchange their Bitcoin proceeds, disincentivizing this activity.

通过识别这个钱包，椭圆形的客户，包括金融机构，加密交流和金融金，现在将被警告到任何来自黑暗标岛的客户存款。通过使用我们的交易和钱包筛选工具，他们可以确保暗区和其他赎金软件运营商不能兑现或交换他们的比特币所得款项，验证这项活动。

Elliptic’s law enforcement clients can also use our software to trace these funds and seek to identify those responsible for these crippling cyber attacks.

Elliptic的执法客户还可以使用我们的软件来追踪这些资金，并寻求识别负责这些瘫痪的网络攻击的资金。
