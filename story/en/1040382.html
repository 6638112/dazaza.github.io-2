The Debian web updates its homepage and prepares for a major renewal
Debian网站会更新其主页，并准备进行重大更新

2020-12-18 15:43:47

Today, the Debian website displays a new homepage. Since the most recent web team sprintin March 2019, we have been working on renewing the structure, content, layoutand scripts that build the site.There has been work mainly in two areas: removing or updating obsolete content,and creating a new homepage which is more attractive to newcomers,and which also highlights the social aspect of the Debian projectin addition to the operating system we develop.

今天，Debian网站显示了一个新的主页。自2019年3月最新的Web团队冲刺以来，我们一直在努力更新构建网站的结构，内容，布局和脚本。主要工作在两个领域：删除或更新过时的内容以及创建新的主页对新来者更具吸引力，除了我们开发的操作系统之外，这也突出了Debian项目的社会方面。

Although this took longer than we would have liked,and we don't consider this new homepage final,we think it's a good first step towards a much better web site.

尽管花费的时间比我们希望的要长，并且我们不认为这个新的首页是最终的，但我们认为这是迈向更好的网站的第一步。

The web team will continue to work on restructuring the Debian website.We would like to appeal to the community for help, and are also consideringexternal assistance, since we're a small group, whose members are also involvedin other Debian teams. Some of the next steps we expect to walk areimprove the CSS, icons, and layout in general, and review of the content,to have a better structure.

网络团队将继续努力重组Debian网站。我们希望向社区寻求帮助，并考虑外部协助，因为我们是一个小组，其成员也参与了其他Debian团队。我们希望可以采取的一些下一步措施是对CSS，图标和布局进行总体改进，并对内容进行审查，以使结构更好。

If you would like to help, contact us. You can reply to the version of thisarticle (with some more details) published in our public mailing listor chat with us in the #debian-www IRC channel (at irc.debian.org).

如果您想提供帮助，请与我们联系。您可以回复发布在我们的公共邮件列表中的本文版本（还有更多详细信息），或者在＃debian-www IRC频道（在irc.debian.org上）与我们聊天。
