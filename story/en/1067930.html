Google and Jio Platforms announce JioPhone Next, an affordable Android smartphone
谷歌和jio平台宣布jiophone接下来，一个经济实惠的Android智能手机

2021-06-24 18:04:38

The Indian firm, which secured $4.5 billion investment from Google (and another $15.5 billion from Facebook and others) last year and shared plans to work on low-cost smartphones, said the JioPhone Next will help roughly 300 million users in India who are still on 2G network upgrade.

印度公司去年可获得谷歌（和其他155亿美元）的45亿美元的投资，并分享了在低成本智能手机上工作的计划，表示，Jiophone接下来将有助于大约3亿用户在印度仍在2G网络升级。

The phone, which is “powered by extremely optimized Android” operating system, will first launch in India on September 10 and will eventually be made available outside of the country.

这款手机是“由极其优化的Android”操作系统，将于9月10日首次推出印度，最终将在该国之外提供。

Jio Platforms, which serves 425 million subscribers, is positioned to add another 200 million in the next few years, said Mukesh Ambani, chairman of Reliance Industries, at its annual general meeting Thursday.

Mukesh Ambani（聘请42500万用户）的JIO平台定位在未来几年内，依赖于周四的年度股东周年大会。

The JioPhone Next will be the “most affordable smartphone” globally, claimed Ambani, though he didn’t reveal the price or the hardware specifications of the handset.

Jiophone接下来将是全球“最实惠的智能手机”，声称Ambani，虽然他没有透露手机的价格或硬件规格。

Google CEO Sundar Pichai said the company has also entered into a 5G cloud partnership with Jio Platforms. “It will help more than a billion Indians connect to a faster and better internet, support businesses in their digital transformation, and help Jio build new services in sectors like health, education and more — laying a foundation for the next phase of India’s digitization,” said Pichai.

谷歌首席执行官Sundar Pichai表示，该公司还与JIO平台进入了5G云合作伙伴关系。 “它将有助于超过十亿的印度人连接到一个更快，更好的互联网，支持他们的数字转型的企业，并帮助JIO在卫生，教育等行业中建立新的服务，为印度的下一阶段奠定基础， “Pichai说。

“As part of this collaboration, Reliance will also shift its core retail businesses to Google Cloud’s infrastructure. They will be able take advantage of Google’s AI and machine learning, e-commerce, and demand forecasting offerings. Harnessing the reliability and performance of Google Cloud will enable these businesses to scale up as needed to respond to customer demand,” he added.

“作为这一协作的一部分，依赖也将将其核心零售业务转移到谷歌云的基础架构。他们将能够利用谷歌的AI和机器学习，电子商务和需求预测产品。他补充说，利用谷歌云的可靠性和性能使这些企业能够根据需要扩展以响应客户需求。“

“We have worked closely with the Jio team on engineering and product development on useful voice-first features that enable these users to consume content and navigate the phone in their own language, deliver a great camera experience, and get the latest Android feature and security updates,” Google said in a statement.

“我们与JIO团队密切合作了关于工程和产品开发的有用的声音第一功能，使这些用户能够以自己的语言消耗内容并导航手机，提供很大的相机体验，并获得最新的Android功能和安全性更新，“谷歌在一份声明中表示。

Even as most smartphones that ship in India, the second largest market, are priced at $150 or less, customers looking for a smartphone priced under $100 are left with little choice. And that choice has further shrunk in recent years.

即使是印度的大多数智能手机，第二大市场的售价为150美元或更少，也留下了100美元的智能手机的客户留下了很少的选择。该选择近年来进一步缩减了。

Research firm Counterpoint told TechCrunch that the sub-$100 smartphones accounted for just 12% of the Indian smartphone market, down from 18% in 2019 and 24% in 2018. Sub-$50 smartphones represented just 0.3% of the entire market in 2020, down from 4.3% in 2018.

研究公司对比告诉TechCrunch，即100美元的智能手机占印度智能手机市场的12％，从2019年的18％下降，2018年的24％。50美元的智能手机在2020年占整个市场的0.3％，下降2018年的4.3％。

Smartphone makers are aware of this whitespace in the market, but have found it incredibly challenging to meet this demand. Some, including Jio Platforms, which has amassed over 425 million subscribers, earlier explored a range of feature phones to reach small cities and towns of India. Jio Platforms’ KaiOS-powered feature phone, called JioPhone, had amassed 100 million customers as of late February this year.

智能手机制造商意识到市场上的这种空格，但已经发现满足这一需求令人难以置信的挑战。其中一些，包括大约42500万用户的JIO平台，早些时候探索了一系列特色手机，以抵达印度小城镇和城镇。 JIO平台的Kaios-Powered Feature Phone，称为Jiophone，今年2月下旬拥有1亿客户。

In a recent report to clients, analysts at UBS said that after accounting the recent price surge of memory component, any smartphone priced at or under $50 is likely selling at cost.

在最近向客户报告中，瑞银的分析师表示，在考虑到最近的记忆组件的价格浪涌之后，任何智能手机在50美元的价格上可能以成本售价。

“While this move by Jio will accelerate 2G to 4G migration, we evaluated how interesting this space would be for other smartphone manufacturers, especially key players like Xiaomi. Xiaomi, the unit market leader in smartphones in India, is unlikely to follow up with a $50 smartphone, in our view,” they wrote in the report, obtained by TechCrunch.

“虽然JIO的这一举措将加速2G到4G迁移，但我们评估了这种空间对其他智能手机制造商的有趣程度，尤其是像小米这样的关键参与者。印度智能手机的单位市场领导者小米不太可能跟进50美元的智能手机，我们认为，“他们在报告中写道，由TechCrunch获得。

Google, too, has previously made several efforts — $100 Android One smartphones program in 2014 and low-resource intensive Android Go operating system in 2017 — to expand the reach of its handsets. The company has also  backed KaiOS, which powers popular feature phones.

谷歌此外，此前曾经做过几项努力 - 2014年的100美元Android一个智能手机课程和2017年的低资源密集型Android Go操作系统 - 扩大手机的触及范围。该公司还支持KaioS，为流行的功能手机提供了热门的功能。

The JioPhone Next is a “ momentous step in our Android mission for India, and is the first of many that our Android product and engineering teams will embark on in India,” Google said in a statement. “We are also actively expanding our engineering teams in India, as we continue to work on finding ways to answer the unique needs of India’s smartphone users.”

jiophone接下来是“印度的Android使命的重要步骤”，是我们的Android产品和工程团队将于印度开始的许多人，“谷歌在一份声明中表示。 “我们还积极扩展了印度的工程团队，因为我们继续努力寻找支持印度智能手机用户的独特需求的方法。”
