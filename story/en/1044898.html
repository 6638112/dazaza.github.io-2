The Dubai Lamp: The “World’s Most Efficient” LED Light Bulb
迪拜灯：“世界上最高效”的LED灯泡

2021-01-18 22:44:51

Back in October of 2016, the partnership of Dubai Municipality and Philips Lighting released their Dubai Lamp, a series of the “world’s most efficient commercially available” LED light bulbs. This past Sunday, May 7, two Dubai developers signed a deal for the installation of these Dubai Lamps for their upcoming projects.

早在2016年10月，迪拜市政府和飞利浦照明的合作伙伴发布了他们的迪拜灯，这是一系列“世界上最高效的商用” LED灯泡。在过去的5月7日星期日，两个迪拜开发商签署了一项为即将到来的项目安装这些迪拜灯的协议。

Dubai is a city-state located in the country of the United Arab Emirates (UAE). For a visual, the UAE is located at the tip of the Arabian Peninsula, neighboring Saudi Arabia and Omen. The partnership between Philips and Dubai Municipality began as an initiative by Dubai for the commitment of sustainability. It is Dubai’s goal to seek a 30% cut in their energy consumption by 2030, as well as a reduction of carbon emissions by 16% in the year 2021.

迪拜是一个城市国家，位于阿拉伯联合酋长国（UAE）的国家。为了获得视觉效果，阿联酋位于阿拉伯半岛的尖端，毗邻沙特阿拉伯和奥美。飞利浦与迪拜市政府之间的合作伙伴关系是迪拜为实现可持续性承诺而发起的一项举措。迪拜的目标是到2030年将能源消耗降低30％，并在2021年将碳排放量减少16％。

Through their partnership, Philips Lighting and Dubai Municipality created the Dubai Lamp, a light bulb that is said to decrease an electricity bill by 90%. According to a press release on Philips Lighting website, this is the world’s first commercially available light bulb that produces 200 lumen per watt. The Dubai Lamp comes in 4 different light bulb varieties:

通过双方的合作，飞利浦照明和迪拜市政府共同创建了迪拜灯，该灯泡据说可将电费减少90％。根据飞利浦照明网站上的新闻稿，这是世界上第一个商业灯泡，每瓦产生200流明。迪拜灯有4个不同的灯泡品种：

1 Watt, equivalent to 25W incandescent light bulb. Lifetime = 15,000 hours, or 15 years based on 1,000 hours a year.

1瓦，相当于25W白炽灯泡。寿命= 15,000小时，或15年（以每年1,000小时为基础）。

2 Watt, equivalent to 40W incandescent light bulb. Lifetime = 15,000 hours, or 15 years based on 1,000 hours a year.

2瓦，相当于40W白炽灯泡。寿命= 15,000小时，或15年（以每年1,000小时为基础）。

3 Watt, equivalent to 60W incandescent light bulb. Lifetime = 15,000 hours, or 15 years based on 1,000 hours a year.

3瓦，相当于60W白炽灯泡。寿命= 15,000小时，或15年（以每年1,000小时为基础）。

3 Watt Spot light bulb, equivalent to 35W or 50W halogen light bulb. Lifetime = 25,000 hours, or 25 years based on 1,000 hours a year.

3瓦点光源灯泡，相当于35W或50W卤素灯泡。寿命= 25,000小时，或25年（以每年1,000小时为基础）。

All 4 light bulbs are available in warm white tone and cool daylight, and last 15 times longer than your conventional light bulb. Even though it is said to be the “world’s most efficient commercially available” LED light bulb, the Dubai Lamp is applicable in households as well as businesses.

所有4个灯泡均提供暖白色调和凉爽的日光，使用寿命比传统灯泡长15倍。即使被称为“世界上最高效的商用LED灯泡”，迪拜灯也适用于家庭和企业。

Throughout this year alone, Philips Lighting and Dubai Municipality are preparing, for market-use, 2 million of these Dubai Lamps. That number is said to increase to 10 million by the year 2021.

仅在今年全年，飞利浦照明和迪拜市政当局就准备将200万台这种迪拜灯投入市场使用。到2021年，这个数字将增加到1000万。

The 4-year deal that was signed this past Sunday with Emaar and Dubai Properties Group to integrate the Dubai Lamps in their new facilities is said to include a study conducted by Dubai Municipality to monitor the energy savings that the Dubai Lamp is said to achieve.

据说上周日与Emaar和Dubai Properties Group签署了为期4年的协议，将Dubai Lamps整合到其新设施中，据说其中包括Dubai Municipality进行的一项研究，旨在监测Dubai Lamp据说实现的节能效果。

Developer Emaar is known for building the world’s tallest tower, the Burj Khalifa, as well as the Dubai Mall, one of the world’s largest shopping centers. Dubai Properties Group is a well-established real estate developer, known for numerous Dubai destinations including Jumeirah Beach Residences (JBR), Dubailand, Business Bay, and Culture Village, among other housing and retail spaces.

开发商埃玛尔（Emaar）以建造世界上最高的塔，哈利法塔（Burj Khalifa）以及世界上最大的购物中心之一迪拜购物中心而闻名。迪拜房地产集团是一家成熟的房地产开发商，以迪拜的许多目的地而闻名，包括卓美亚（Jumeirah）海滩住宅（JBR），迪拜乐园，商业湾和文化村以及其他住房和零售空间。
