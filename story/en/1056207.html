How Did Microsoft Make Flight Simulator Seem So Real?
微软如何让飞行模拟器似乎如此真实？

2021-04-05 11:21:36

Last August, Microsoft released the latest version of its Flight Simulator, extending the run of that franchise to 38 years and making it the longest-running product line in Microsoft’s history. Published by the technology giant’s Xbox Game Studios, the new Flight Simulator treats gamers to vastly greater detail and texture in both environment and aircraft, far better lighting, and much more realistic flight characteristics than in previous versions. The precise renderings of all 20 airplanes (which include the Boeing 787 Dreamliner, Cessna 172, and Beechcraft B350) and the particulars of individual airports are stunning.

去年8月，微软发布了其飞行模拟器的最新版本，将该特许经营权的运行扩展到38年，并使其成为微软历史上最长运行的产品线。由Technology Giant的Xbox Game Studios出版，新的飞行模拟器对待游戏玩家，在环境和飞机中都更加提高细节和质地，更好的照明，比以前的版本更具现实的飞行特性。所有20架飞机的精确渲染（包括波音787 Dreamliner，Cessna 172和Beechcraft B350）以及个别机场的细节令人惊叹。

Aircraft cockpits are functional down to the position of almost every switch. Air traffic (both actual and that of other users) is reproduced in real time, as is the weather: If Denver International Airport’s weather station is recording snow and FlightAware is showing 40 airplanes in the Denver airspace, then users will fly their simulated approach through a virtual snowstorm with 40 other aircraft mimicking those actually in the sky.

飞机驾驶舱功能下降到几乎每个开关的位置。空中交通（实际和其他用户）是实时转载的，就像天气一样一个虚拟暴风雪，其中40架飞机模仿其实在天空中的人。

One of the great joys of aviation is seeing Earth from above, so Flight Simulator reproduces the entire planet, from pole to pole, in high resolution. Stars appear as they would in real time, including the correct phase of the moon. The system even reproduces the effects of atmospheric refraction, which “notches” the rising or setting sun, causes stars to twinkle, and produces distortion generated by engine exhaust.

航空的一个巨大乐趣是从上面看到地球，因此飞行模拟器以高分辨率从杆从杆子再现整个行星。星星出现在实时，包括月球的正确阶段。该系统甚至再现了大气折射的效果，“凹口”的上升或夕阳，导致恒星闪烁，并产生由发动机排气产生的失真。

In this virtual world, users can adjust myriad criteria, including flight control sensitivity, time of day, weather conditions, and views of aircraft and surroundings.

在这个虚拟世界中，用户可以调整无数标准，包括飞行控制灵敏度，一天时间，天气状况和飞机和周围环境的景色。

Achieving this feat required coalescing and processing massive troves of data in disciplines ranging from aerodynamics to geodesy and photogrammetry to optics.

实现这一壮举需要聚结和加工在从空气动力学到大地测量的学科和摄影测量到光学器件的大规模数据。

Asobo Studio, a video game company based in Bordeaux, France, led the program’s development. Asobo worked with a host of specialized firms and organizations around the globe, including geospatial company Bing Maps, atmospheric data specialist Meteoblue, machine-learning system builder Blackshark.ai, FlightAware for real-time global air traffic, and many others. One reason I know how Microsoft achieved its verisimilitude is that I worked as a contractor on the project, researching airplanes and airports.

Asobo Studio，一家位于法国波尔多的视频游戏公司，领导了该计划的发展。 Asobo与全球各地的一系列专业公司和组织合作，包括地理空间公司Bing地图，大气数据专业Metooblue，机器学习系统建设者Blackshark.ai，用于实时全球空中交通，以及许多其他地区。我知道Microsoft如何实现其真实性的一个原因是我作为项目的承包商，研究飞机和机场。

To create Flight Simulator’s high fidelity, Asobo scanned actual airframes, inside and out, relying on an Artec Leo 3D scanner, which provides half-a-millimeter resolution. Gamers see renditions of these actual aircraft, down to scuff marks on windows and nicks on levers. Asobo also captured the full spectrum of an airplane’s interior and exterior acoustics, including the cockpit echoes caused by the pelting of rain or snow. The team carefully studied each airplane’s aerodynamics by simulating how the air interacts with 1,306 airplane surfaces and developed a flight control regime. “All aircraft have been reviewed with professional pilots who have many hours flying these aircraft or test pilots from the manufacturers to make sure they are controlled exactly like they should,” stated Asobo Studio founder and CEO Sebastian Wloch in a Flight Simulator “Discovery Series” episode about virtual aircraft aerodynamics. In other words, when sim-pilots try to yank-and-bank an A320, they will get the exact same response as if they were flying the real thing.

创建飞行模拟器的高保真度，Asobo扫描了实际的机身，内外，依赖于ARTEC LEO 3D扫描仪，提供半毫米分辨率。游戏玩家可以看到这些实际飞机的再现，下来穿上窗户和杠杆上的碎片标记。 Asobo还捕获了飞机的内部和外部声学的全部频谱，包括由雨或雪覆盖的驾驶舱回声。该团队通过模拟空气与1,306架飞机表面的互动并开发飞行控制制度来仔细研究每个飞机的空气动力学。 “所有飞机都经过审查的专业飞行员，他们从制造商那里飞行这些飞机或测试飞行员，以确保它们应该与他们应该完全相同，”Asobo Studio创始人和CEO Sebastian Wloch在飞行模拟器“Discovery系列”中。关于虚拟飞机空气动力学的剧集。换句话说，当SIM-PILOTS尝试yank-bank A320时，他们将获得完全相同的响应，好像他们飞过真实的东西。

Just as Asobo scanned individual aircraft, the team used high-resolution satellite imagery, aerial photography, and in many cases detailed photogrammetric data from Bing Maps to re-create the globe. The size of this data set, which is continuously updated and expanded, is now at three petabytes (and growing). Most of the data are vertical (looking straight down), but some are oblique, and all are in two dimensions. In order to present this information in three dimensions and ensure visual continuity, the Flight Simulator team decided to tap the services of Austria-based Blackshark.ai, a company with 50 computer specialists who developed proprietary machine-learning algorithms they fed into the Microsoft Azure cloud computing service. Initially helped along with human input, the Blackshark systems eventually learned to render and color all aspects of the planet, including determining the heights of trees (all 1.5 trillion of them), removing shadows of clouds from aerial imagery when appropriate, and ensuring accurate color. The end result is Earth, a planet rendered to a resolution of at least three feet, with the most populated 300,000 square miles rendered even finer, with a resolution of between three and 10 inches, including 58,000 square miles (mostly cities) rendered in such detail you can practically walk the streets.

就像asobo扫描的单独飞机一样，团队使用了高分辨率卫星图像，航拍和许多情况下的冰地图的摄影测量数据重新创建全球。此数据集的大小，该数据集是连续更新和扩展的，现在是三个petabytes（和成长）。大多数数据都是垂直的（直视上的垂直），但有些则是倾斜的，并且一切都是两个维度。为了以三维提供此信息并确保视觉连续性，飞行模拟器团队决定点击奥地利的BlackShark.ai的服务，这是一家拥有50个计算机专家的公司，他们开发了他们喂入Microsoft Azure的专有机器学习算法云计算服务。 Blackshark系统最初帮助与人类投入一起学习，最终学会了渲染和颜色地球的所有方面，包括确定树木的高度（所有1.5万亿），在适当时从空中图像中移除云的阴影，并确保精确的颜色。最终结果是地球，一个行星呈现为至少三英尺的分辨率，最具群体300,000平方英里甚至更精细，分辨率在三到10英寸之间，包括58,000平方英里（大多是城市）所呈现的细节你可以走上街头。

What would a flight simulator be without the sky? Meteoblue, a Switzerland-based graphical weather model pioneer, divided the Earth’s atmosphere from ground level to stratosphere into 250 million spatial compartments. “We do this by storing temperature, wind speed, humidity, pressure, rain, snow, and pretty much everything that defines the weather,” explains Mathias D. Müller, co-founder of Meteoblue. Its system coalesces massive amounts of historical and real-time weather data from ground stations and satellites and then re-creates the sky and all of its conditions at any given time based on algorithms that take into account molecular interaction. The system even renders how the weather interacts with aircraft, including icing and streaks of rainwater on a canopy.

飞行模拟器没有天空会是什么？瑞士图形天气模型先驱梅布鲁博士将地球的大气层从地面到平流层分成2.5亿空间。 “梅特博士联合创始人MathiasD.Müller解释说：”我们通过储存温度，风速，湿度，压力，雨，雪和几乎所有定义天气的所有内容来实现这一点。它的系统聚合了来自地站和卫星的大量历史和实时天气数据，然后在基于考虑分子相互作用的算法的任何时间重新创建天空和其所有条件。该系统甚至使天气如何与飞机互动，包括在树冠上的冰水和雨水条纹。

To handle air traffic, the system relies on aviation information company FlightAware to stream real-time data of aircraft in the sky using its continuously updated servers. What Microsoft Flight Simulator pilots virtually experience is actually occurring, from the pink coloration of a towering thunderstorm at sunset to a nearby airliner making an approach into an airport and (if desired) the nearby virtual aircraft of other Flight Simulator users. And this visualization, from within the cockpit to an external view, is rendered cinematically as if seen through a camera lens, with the experience streamed seamlessly from Microsoft’s servers.

为了处理空中交通，该系统依赖于航空信息公司飞行飞行，以使用其不断更新的服务器在天空中汇流飞机的实时数据。 Microsoft飞行模拟器飞行员几乎经验实际上发生了，从夕阳雷暴的粉红色着色到附近的速度到附近的班车，进入机场和（如果需要）其他飞行模拟器用户的附近虚拟飞机。从驾驶舱内到外部视图的这种可视化似乎通过摄像机镜头呈现出来，经验从微软的服务器无缝流动。

Flight Simulator attracted over two million players within weeks of its release—the game is already a classic in the making.

飞行模拟器在其释放后几周内吸引了超过200万名玩家 - 游戏已经是制作的经典。
