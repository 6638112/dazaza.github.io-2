Nuclear fusion tech developer General Fusion now has Shopify and Amazon founders backing it
核聚变技术开发商General Fusion现在拥有Shopify和Amazon创始人支持

2021-01-15 20:56:23

The size of the investment made by Lütke’s Thistledown Capital was not disclosed, but with the addition, General Fusion has the founders of the two biggest e-commerce companies in the Western world on its cap table.

吕特克（Lütke）的Thistledown Capital的投资规模没有透露，但除此之外，General Fusion还拥有西方世界最大的两家电子商务公司的创始人。

Jeff Bezos, the founder and chief executive of Amazon, first invested in the company nearly a decade ago and General Fusion has been steadily raising cash since that time. In 2019, the company hauled in $100 million. That capital commitment is part of a haul totaling at least, $192 million, according to Crunchbase although the real figure is likely higher.

亚马逊的创始人兼首席执行官杰夫·贝佐斯（Jeff Bezos）于十年前首次向该公司投资，自那时以来，General Fusion一直在稳步筹集现金。在2019年，该公司赚了1亿美元。根据Crunchbase的说法，这笔资本承诺至少是一笔总计1.92亿美元的大笔交易的一部分，尽管实际数字可能更高。

Indeed, General Fusion kept adding cash throughout 2020 as it looked to develop its demonstration fusion reactor.

确实，通用核聚变在寻求发展其示范性聚变反应堆的整个2020年一直在增加现金。

General Fusion’s process is based on technology called magnetized target fusion (MTF), first proposed by the U.S. Naval Research Lab and developed in the 1970s.

General Fusion的工艺基于称为磁化目标融合（MTF）的技术，该技术最早是由美国海军研究实验室提出并于1970年代开发的。

The process involves creating a magnetically confined moderately warm plasma of around 100 eV (roughly 50 times the photon energy of visible light) in a flux conserver (a shell that preserves the magnetic field). By rapidly compressing the flux conserver and the magnetic field inside of it surrounding the plasma, the plasma is superheated to a temperature that can initiate a fast fusion burn and create a fusion reaction, according to a 2017 description of the technology from General Fusion’s chief science officer and founder, Michel Laberge.

该过程涉及在通量保持器（保留磁场的壳体）中创建大约100 eV（约为可见光的光子能量的50倍）的磁场约束的适度温暖的等离子体。根据General Fusion首席科学2017年对技术的描述，通过快速压缩通量保持器及其周围围绕等离子体的磁场，等离子体被过热到可以引发快速聚变燃烧并产生聚变反应的温度。官兼创始人Michel Laberge。

The company uses a roughly three-meter sphere filled with molten lead-lithium that’s pumped to form a cavity. A pulse of magnetically confined plasma fuel is then injected into the cavity. Then, around the sphere, pistons create a pressure wave into the middle of the sphere, compressing the plasma to fusion conditions.

该公司使用大约三米长的球，里面充满了熔融的铅锂，然后将其抽成一个空腔。然后将电磁约束等离子体燃料脉冲注入腔中。然后，在球体周围，活塞在球体的中间产生压力波，将等离子体压缩到聚变状态。

Neutrons escaping from the fusion reaction are captured in the liquid metal, and the heat from that metal generates electricity via a steam turbine. A heat exchanger steam turbine produces the power and the steam is recycled to run the pistons.

从聚变反应中逸出的中子被捕获在液态金属中，而来自该金属的热量通过蒸汽轮机发电。换热器蒸汽轮机产生动力，蒸汽被回收以使活塞运转。

In recent years, both General Fusion and its main North American competitor Commonwealth Fusion Systems have made strides in getting their small-scale nuclear fusion technology ready for commercialization.

近年来，General Fusion及其主要的北美竞争对手Commonwealth Fusion Systems在使小型核聚变技术投入商业化方面取得了长足的进步。

In the past, the wry joke about fusion technologies was that they were always 10 years away, but now companies are looking at a four-year horizon to bring fusion to initial markets, if not the masses.

过去，关于融合技术的可笑的笑话是，它们始终相距10年，但是现在，公司正在考虑将融合技术带入初始市场（即使不是大众市场）的四年时间。

For its part, Commonwealth Fusion Systems is in the process of building a 10-ton magnet that has the magnetic force equivalent to 20 MRI machines. “After we get the magnet to work, we’ll be building a machine that will generate more power than it takes to run. We see that as the Kitty Hawk moment [for fusion],” said Bob Mumgaard, the chief executive of Commonwealth Fusion in an interview last year.

就其本身而言，联邦融合系统公司正在建造一个重达10吨的磁体，该磁体的磁力相当于20台MRI机器。 “在使磁铁工作之后，我们将制造一台机器，该机器将产生比运行所需的功率更多的功率。我们认为这是[融合]的“小鹰”时刻。”去年，英联邦融合执行长鲍勃·穆加德（Bob Mumgaard）说道。

Other startup companies are also racing to bring technologies to market and hit the 2025 timeline like the United Kingdom’s  Tokamak Energy.

其他初创公司也竞相将技术推向市场并赶上2025年时间表，例如英国的Tokamak Energy。

Like General Fusion, Commonwealth also has deep-pocketed backers including the Bill Gates-backed sustainable-technology-focused investor, Breakthrough Energy Ventures. In all, those investors have committed over $200 million to the company, which formally launched in 2018.

像General Fusion一样，英联邦也有实力雄厚的支持者，其中包括比尔·盖茨（Bill Gates）支持的以可持续技术为重点的投资者Breakthrough Energy Ventures。这些投资者总共向该公司承诺了超过2亿美元，该公司于2018年正式成立。

As these companies begin readying their technologies for market, governments are laying the groundwork to make it easier for them to commercialize.

随着这些公司开始为市场准备其技术，各国政府正在为使它们更易于商业化奠定基础。

At the end of last year, the Trump administration signed the COVID relief and omnibus appropriations bill that included an amendment to support the development of fusion energy in the U.S.

去年年底，特朗普政府签署了COVID救济和综合拨款法案，其中包括一项修正案，以支持美国聚变能的发展。

The new amendment directed the Department of Energy to carry out a fusion energy sciences research and development program; authorized DOE programs in inertial fusion energy and alternative concepts to find new ways forward for fusion power; reauthorized the INFUSE program to create public-private partnerships between national labs and fusion developers; and created a milestone-based development program to support companies not just through R&D, but into the construction of full-scale systems.

新的修正案指示能源部执行一项融合能源科学研究与开发计划；授权的美国能源部惯性聚变能方案和替代概念，以寻求新的聚变动力方法；重新授权INFUSE计划，以在国家实验室和核聚变开发商之间建立公私合作关系；并创建了一个基于里程碑的开发计划，以不仅通过研发为公司提供支持，而且还为全面系统的建设提供支持。

It’s this milestone program that was a cornerstone of the policy work that the Fusion Industry Association wanted to see in the U.S., according to a December statement from the organization.

根据该组织12月份的一份声明，正是这一里程碑式的计划，是Fusion Industry Association希望在美国看到的政策工作的基石。

By unlocking $325 million in financing over a five-year period, the U.S. government will actually double its research with matching contributions from the fusion industry. These demonstration facilities could go a long way toward accelerating the deployment of fusion technologies.

通过在五年时间内释放3.25亿美元的融资，美国政府实际上将其研究增加一倍，同时融合工业也做出了相应的贡献。这些示范设施可能会大大加快加速融合技术的部署。

Founded in 2019, Thistledown Capital was formed to invest in tech that can decarbonize industry. The firm, based in Ottawa, has already backed CarbonCure, a technology that captures carbon dioxide from the air.

Thistledown Capital成立于2019年，旨在投资可以使行业脱碳的技术。这家位于渥太华的公司已经支持了CarbonCure（一种从空气中捕获二氧化碳的技术）。

“ General  Fusion has a strong record of attracting funding support from some of the world’s most influential technology leaders,” said Greg Twinney, CFO,  General  Fusion, in a statement. “ Fusion is planet-saving technology, and we are proud to support the mission of Thistledown Capital in its pursuit for a greener tomorrow.”

General Fusion首席财务官Greg Twinney在一份声明中说：“ General Fusion在吸引一些世界上最具影响力的技术领导者方面一直保持着良好的记录。” “ Fusion是节省地球的技术，我们很荣幸能够支持Thistledown Capital的使命，以追求更绿色的明天。”
