Three Charged in Money Laundering Scheme Stealing $30MM from Safe Deposit Boxes
洗钱计划中的三次被控窃取30毫米的保险箱

2021-04-22 11:52:22

An indictment was unsealed earlier today in federal court in Brooklyn charging Val Cooper, Alex Levin and Garri Smith with money laundering conspiracy and conspiracy to violate the Travel Act in connection with their roles in a scheme to steal over $30 million in cash and other valuables from safe deposit boxes located at banks abroad, primarily in Eastern Europe.  The defendants were arrested this morning and will be arraigned this afternoon before United States Magistrate Judge Robert M. Levy.

今天早些时候在Brooklyn Charging Val Cooper，Alex Levin和Garri Smith在Frooklyn Conforgy和Garri Smith的联邦法院提出了起诉，与洗钱阴谋和阴谋违反了旅行法案，以与其在计划中窃取超过3000万美元的现金和其他贵重物品的角色保险箱位于国外银行，主要在东欧。被告今天早上被逮捕，并将在美国裁判法官罗伯特M.征收之前在今天下午进行。

Mark J. Lesko, Acting United States Attorney for the Eastern District of New York; William F. Sweeney, Jr., Assistant Director-in-Charge, Federal Bureau of Investigation, New York Field Office (FBI); Peter C. Fitzhugh, Special Agent-in-Charge, Homeland Security Investigations, New York Office (HSI); and Dermot F. Shea, Commissioner, New York City Police Department (NYPD), announced the arrests and charges.

Mark J. Lesko，纽约东区的美国律师; William F. Sweeney，Jr.，助理助理，联邦调查局，纽约州办事处（FBI）;彼得C.Fitzhugh，特别代理商，国土安全调查，纽约办事处（HSI）;纽约市警察局（NYPD）委员专员和德尔默特F. Shea宣布逮捕和收费。

“The defendants and their co-conspirators were allegedly part of a sophisticated network of criminals that used high-tech camera equipment to steal millions in cash and other valuables from victims who had tried to protect their property by using safe deposit boxes in foreign countries, and they then laundered the proceeds of their scheme through the United States financial system,” stated Acting United States Attorney Lesko.  “This Office will hold accountable anyone who uses our financial system to commit fraud and theft here or abroad.”  Mr. Lesko expressed his grateful appreciation to the European Union Agency for Law Enforcement Cooperation (Europol), and the governments of Azerbaijan, Latvia, Moldova, North Macedonia, Ukraine and Uzbekistan for their assistance in the investigation.

“被告及其共同承担者据称，据称是一部经过复杂的犯罪分子网络的一部分，这些犯罪分子是使用高科技摄像机设备窃取数百万的现金和其他贵重物品从国外使用保险箱保护其财产的受害者，然后，他们通过美国金融体系洗涤了他们的计划的收益，“Acting Statesey Lesko表示。 “这项办公室将持有责任的人，这些人在这里或国外使用金融系统犯下欺诈和盗窃。” Lesko先生对欧洲联盟执法合作局（Europol）和阿塞拜疆，拉脱维亚，摩尔多瓦，北部马其顿，乌克兰和乌兹别克斯坦的政府表示感谢欣赏欧洲联盟，以及在调查中的援助。

“The crimes we allege in this indictment read like something straight out of Hollywood fiction,” stated FBI Assistant Director-in-Charge Sweeney.  “The thieves used sophisticated tools to thwart security systems at foreign banks and tried to cover their tracks by laundering money through U.S. banks.  However, thanks to the outstanding work of our FBI Eurasian Organized Crime Task Force and our international partners, these criminals now face real federal charges and the possibility of real time in federal prison.”

“我们在这个起诉中声明的罪行就像直接出来的东西，”联邦调查局助理总干事斯明尼表示。 “盗贼利用先进的工具来阻止外国银行的安全系统，并试图通过通过美国银行洗钱来覆盖他们的曲目。然而，由于我们联邦调查局欧亚有组织犯罪工作队和我们的国际合作伙伴的杰出工作，这些罪犯现在面临着真正的联邦指控和联邦监狱实时的可能性。“

“Using state of the art technology, these modern-day bank robbers allegedly exploited small, unsophisticated banks to gain access to safety deposit boxes and looted $30 million in other people’s cash and valuables.  Cooper, Levin and Smith then used bank accounts located in the United States, to launder the proceeds of their illegal activities,” stated HSI Special Agent-in-Charge Fitzhugh.  “HSI is proud to work along-side our domestic and international law enforcement partners, including the United States Attorney’s Office, EDNY, and will utilize our unique and broad authorities to bring to justice those who engage in criminal activity for their own financial gain.  These defendants must now face our system of justice and answer to the allegations contained in the indictment.”

“使用现代技术的国家，这些现代银行劫匪据称，据称小型，不专业的银行进入安全存款盒，并在其他人的现金和贵重物品中抢劫3000万美元。 Cooper，Levin和Smith随后使用位于美国的银行账户，为其非法活动的收益，“HSI特别代理商菲尔兹特殊智能助手”。 “HSI很自豪地向上工作我们的国内和国际执法合作伙伴，包括美国律师的办公室，律师，并将利用我们独特和广泛的当局来伸展那些从事犯罪活动的人为自己的财务收益。这些被告现在必须面对我们的司法制度，并回答起诉书中所载的指控。“

As set forth in the indictment and court filings, between March 2015 and October 2019, the defendants and their co-conspirators allegedly stole over $30 million in cash and other valuables from safe deposit boxes at banks in multiple foreign countries, including the Ukraine, Russia, North Macedonia, Moldova, Latvia, Uzbekistan and Azerbaijan.  The co-conspirators targeted foreign banks that appeared to lack security features, including video surveillance cameras in certain areas.  After a bank was selected, they rented safe deposit boxes at the location by posing as customers.  The co-conspirators entered the safe deposit box rooms of the targeted banks and used sophisticated camera equipment, including borescopes that are typically used in medical procedures, to photograph the insides of locks of safe deposit boxes belonging to other individuals.  Another co-conspirator used these photographs to create duplicate keys, and then other co-conspirators used the duplicate keys to open the victim safe deposit boxes in order to steal the contents, including currency, gold bars, jewelry and other property.

截至2015年3月至2019年3月至2019年10月，据称在2015年3月至2019年至2019年10月期间，据称，被告及其共同承担者在包括乌克兰，俄罗斯的乌克兰的银行的安全保存箱中察到了超过3000万美元，北马其顿，摩尔多瓦，拉脱维亚，乌兹别克斯坦和阿塞拜疆。共同承担者针对外国银行，似乎缺乏安全功能，包括某些地区的视频监控摄像机。在申请银行后，他们在该地点租用了保险箱。共同领导者进入了目标银行的保险箱室，并使用了先进的相机设备，包括通常用于医疗程序的博社，拍摄属于其他个人的保险箱锁的内部。另一个Co-Conspirator使用这些照片来创建重复的键，然后其他共同剪辑器使用重复的键打开受害者保险箱，以窃取内容，包括货币，金栏，珠宝和其他财产。

Cooper, the leader of the network, directed others, received stolen property and used his bank accounts in the United States to launder proceeds from the scheme.  Levin used his bank accounts in the United States to purchase camera equipment used in some of the thefts and also to launder the proceeds.  Smith committed some of the safe deposit box thefts himself, flying from the United States to foreign countries to steal property from victim safe deposit boxes at the targeted banks.

Cooper是网络领导者，指导别人，收到被盗财产并在美国使用他的银行账户，从该计划中收益。 Levin在美国使用了他的银行账户来购买一些盗窃中使用的相机设备，也可以在收益中洗涤。史密斯致力于一些保险箱本人，从美国飞往国外，以窃取目标银行的受害者保险箱的财产。

Earlier today, agents executed a search warrant at, among other places, Cooper’s residence.  There, agents discovered, among other things, safe deposit box keys with no numbering on them, cash, jewelry and high-end handbags. Agents also searched a storage unit in Brooklyn controlled by Cooper and found a borescope and a safe deposit box lock.

今天早些时候，代理商在库珀的居住地等地方执行了一个搜索权证。在那里，代理商在其他事情中发现，保管箱钥匙，没有编号，现金，珠宝和高端手袋。代理商还在布鲁克林的存储单元中搜索了由Cooper控制的Brooklyn，并找到了一个Borescope和保险箱锁。

The charges in the indictment are allegations, and the defendants are presumed innocent unless and until proven guilty.  If convicted of money laundering conspiracy, the defendants face up to 20 years’ imprisonment.

起诉书中的收费是指控，除非并向被证明有罪，否则被告被毁灭无辜。如果被定罪的洗钱阴谋，被告面临长达20年的监禁。

This case is part of an Organized Crime Drug Enforcement Task Forces (OCDETF) operation.  OCDETF identifies, disrupts and dismantles the highest-level money launderers and transnational criminal organizations that threaten the United States by using a prosecutor-led, intelligence-driven, multi-agency approach that leverages the strengths of federal, state, and local law enforcement agencies against criminal networks.

本案是有组织犯罪药物执法工作队（OCDETF）行动的一部分。 OCDETF确定，扰乱和拆除了通过使用检察官主导，智力驱动的多机构的多机构方法来抑制和拆除威胁美国的最高级别的洗礼和跨国犯罪组织，这些方法利用联邦，州和地方执法机构的优势反对刑事网络。

The government’s case is being handled by the Office’s Organized Crime and Gangs Section.  Assistant United States Attorneys Keith D. Edelman, Andrey Spektor and Joy Lurinsky are in charge of the prosecution, assisted by Assistant United States Attorney Brian D. Morris of the Office’s Asset Forfeiture Unit.

政府的案件由办公室有组织的犯罪和帮派部分处理。助理美国律师Keith D. Edelman，Andrey Spektor和Joy Lurinsky负责检察机关，由美国助理助理Brian D.助理的资产没收单位助理。
