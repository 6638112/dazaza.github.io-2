第一次，星际飞船原型用三个引擎咆哮着起死回生

2020-10-20 17:59:20

SpaceX engineers achieved another milestone early Tuesday morning when the company's Starship vehicle roared to life for the first time with multiple Raptor engines.

周二清晨，SpaceX的工程师们实现了另一个里程碑，该公司的星际飞船首次配备了多个猛禽发动机，咆哮着起死回生。

At 3:13 am local time in South Texas, a Starship prototype dubbed SN8, or Serial Number 8, fired three Raptor engines for several seconds during a static fire test. Although there was no immediate confirmation from the company, the test at the company's Boca Chica launch site appeared to be successful.

在德克萨斯州南部当地时间凌晨3点13分，一架名为SN8或序列号8的星际飞船原型在一次静态射击测试中发射了三个猛禽发动机，持续了几秒钟。虽然没有立即得到该公司的确认，但在该公司的博卡奇卡发射场进行的测试似乎是成功的。

This was an important step toward preparing SN8 for a 15km test flight later this month, or in early November. Even as one team prepared to ignite the rocket during the wee hours on Tuesday—which tested its plumbing to handle chilled liquid oxygen and methane fuels, and the recent installation of three Raptor engines—another team assembled the nose cone that will go on top of SN8 in preparation for its flight.

这是为本月晚些时候或11月初进行15公里试飞做准备的重要一步。就在一个团队准备在周二凌晨点燃火箭-测试了处理冷冻液氧和甲烷燃料的管道，以及最近安装的三个猛禽发动机-另一个团队组装了将安装在SN8顶部的鼻锥，为它的飞行做准备。

Development of the SN8 vehicle has proceeded relatively quickly. SpaceX built the core of SN8 this fall, and moved it from its factory-beneath-tents in South Texas to the nearby launch site in early October. Between October 6 and 8 it underwent three "proof tests" to ensure the integrity of its large, stainless steel fuel tanks. It then completed the static fire test—a moment of truth for any launch vehicles—early on Tuesday.

SN8车辆的开发进展较快。SpaceX在今年秋天建造了SN8的核心，并于10月初将其从德克萨斯州南部的工厂帐篷下转移到附近的发射场。10月6日至8日期间，该公司进行了三次验证测试，以确保其大型不锈钢燃料箱的完整性。然后，它在周二早些时候完成了静态射击测试-对于任何运载火箭来说，这都是一个关键时刻。
