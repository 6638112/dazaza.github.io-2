Love Letters of Intent

2021-03-07 09:21:47

Sending a prospective seller a letter of intent is a bit like asking a girl out to the homecoming dance. If she says yes, you start to prepare for the big night. However, there is no guarantee she won’t ditch you for the varsity quarterback the night before. LOIs formalize the invite to start a deal process but are no guarantee the deal will go through.

向潜在卖家发送一封意图信有点像问一个女孩去他们的家庭舞蹈。如果她说是的，你开始为大夜做准备。但是，没有保证她不会在前一天晚上抛弃你的校舍四分卫。 Lois正式化邀请开始交易流程，但无法保证交易将通过。

Only put clauses into the LOI that are reasonably enforceable (from a time, cost and legal standpoint) and those which are going to increase your chances of finalizing the deal.  In the previous discussion, I mentioned that speed to close was my biggest asset. A complex, lengthy and overly aggressive set of terms in the LOI will likely force a drawn out process that could backfire. This does not mean that you should be sloppy when constructing your Letter of Intent, but know that many deal terms and closing conditions will become much more formalized as you move forward through due diligence and begin to structure the definitive deal documents (a.k.a The Contract).

只会将条款置于合理可执行的LOI（从一时间，成本和法律角度来看）以及那些将增加您完成交易机会的人。在以前的讨论中，我提到速度关闭是我最大的资产。在LOI中，一系列复杂，冗长和过于激进的术语，可能会强迫一个可能反馈的绘制过程。这并不意味着在构建您的意图时应该是邋，而是知道许多交易条款和结束条件将变得更加正式，因为您通过尽职调查前进并开始构建最终交易文件（AKA合同） 。

By the time you are crafting your Letter of Intent, you will likely have had at least a few discussions with the seller and a sense of basic business operations. Some sellers may request a formal NDA before sharing certain information. NDA’s are particularly common if you are acquiring a larger business or a business you already compete with. The founder of Hexadecimal was already incredibly transparent in public about the operations of his business, so much of this formality was unnecessary.

当您制定意向书时，您可能至少与卖方和基本业务感的讨论。在分享某些信息之前，一些卖方可以要求正式的NDA。如果您收购更大的业务或您已经竞争的商业，NDA是特别常见的。十六进制的创始人在公众方面已经令人难以置信地透明，关于他的业务的运营，这么多种形式是不必要的。

When navigating through this process, keep in mind that the legal framework is important but the end goal is to develop trust between buyer and seller. Trust will bring the deal across the finish line and prevent either party from getting cold feet.

在通过此过程导航时，请记住，法律框架很重要，但最终目标是在买方和卖方之间发展信任。信任将在终点线上带来交易，并防止任何一方变冷脚。

Like your invitation to the homecoming dance, make it clear that the LOI is non-binding. I am not a lawyer, but it should be obvious that in the event you find a hornets nest (or several) during due diligence you will want a way to cleanly back out of the arrangement.

就像你对家乡舞蹈的邀请一样，明确邮政局是不绑定的。我不是律师，但很明显，如果在尽职调查期间发现大黄蜂巢（或几个），您将需要一种彻底退回安排的方法。

Layout the offer in as clear of terms as possible. Again, remember that the LOI is a framework from which to move forward. You can always clarify or change agreed upon deal structure in the final deal documents (earn-outs, cash vs stock, closing conditions etc.).

尽可能清楚地布局报价。再次，请记住，LOI是向前移动的框架。您可以始终澄清或更改在最终交易文件中的交易结构（赚取现金，收盘，关闭条件等）。

In layman's terms: I was gonna pay cash, only buy assets (not equity), give both parties 30 days to close from contract signing and, release the whole payment at once. This is about as simple as an acquisition can be. I tried my best to outline what I believed to be the core assets needed to operate the business (see below). Knowing this was going to change I made it clear in the LOI that these terms were not definitive and subject to change per the definitive deal documents I would draft (more on those later).

在外行＆＃39;术语：我要支付现金，只购买资产（不是股权），让双方30天收到合同签约，并立即发布整个付款。这与获取一样简单。我尽力概述我认为是经营业务所需的核心资产（见下文）。知道这将改变我在LOI中明确说明这些术语并不明确，并且根据我将汇票的最终交易文件的变更可能会发生变化（更稍后的情况）。

The definitive deal docs would go on to outline additional items such as subscription information (Stripe account) and customer goodwill. Customer goodwill is a fancy term for the accounting value placed on a customer's willingness to continue paying you for services (e.g. your brand’s value).

最终的交易文件将继续概述其他项目，如订阅信息（Stripe帐户）和客户善意。客户善意是一个花哨的术语，适用于客户的会计价值＆＃39;愿意继续向您支付服务（例如您的品牌价值）。

With deal terms outlined, I had to come up with a reasonable set of diligence requirements. In larger, more complex acquisitions the due diligence requests can span thousands of detailed document requests; from sales collateral, to software licenses, to payroll history, and employment agreements.  Knowing this business was a one-person operation, I only needed a few dozen things to be confident I was not buying a pig with lipstick.

随着交易条款概述，我不得不提出合理的勤奋要求。更大，更复杂的收购，尽职调查请求可以涵盖数千个详细的文件请求;从销售抵押品，将软件许可证，工资历史以及就业协议。知道这项业务是一个人的操作，我只需要几十个事情来信心我没有用唇膏买猪。

Due diligence requests made in the LOI are never exhaustive and typically are only outlined at a high level (e.g. financial statements, sales contracts etc.). I wanted to make it more explicit as to what I would need to start the process and I knew that I could always request additional information if questions arose.

LOI中所做的尽职调查请求永远不会详尽无遗，通常只概述高水平（例如财务报表，销售合同等）。我想让它更明确到我需要开始这个过程，我知道如果问题出现问题，我可以始终要求其他信息。

Looking back, there was one mistake I did make in my LOI that luckily did not bite me the ass; I forgot to include a no-shop clause. Think of this like an engagement ring (except with legal repercussions) that prevents the seller from dating during the no-shop period (typically ranges from 30-90 days). Because the deal was so quick to close (about 12 days from LOI to closing), this mistake thankfully didn’t cause any issues.

回顾一下，我在我的厕所里做了一个错误，幸运的是不要咬我的屁股;我忘了包含一个禁石条款。考虑一下这就像一个订婚戒指（除了合法的反应之外），阻止卖方在挪用商店期间约会（通常为30-90天的范围）。因为这笔交易如此迅速关闭（从LOI到关闭约12天），但谢天谢地没有造成任何问题。

As one other parting piece of advice, one that I will make regarding all deal documents, please consult an attorney. If you don’t have legal counsel already in your rolodex of contacts, finding an attorney to review documents can be as simple as going to UpCounsel or Rocket Lawyer.

作为另一方面的咨询，我将在所有交易文件中进行，请咨询律师。如果您没有在您的Rolodex的联系人中有法律顾问，发现审查文件的律师可以像往北一样简单，就像往上货商一样简单。
