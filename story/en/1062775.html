Discord Rebranding
不和谐重塑

2021-05-15 05:12:24

Discord has become a place to call home to over 150 million monthly active users around the world to talk about everything from sneakers, to the deepest details of their favorite game soundtrack, to virtual car meetups and late-night study sessions.

Discord已成为在世界各地致电超过1.5亿超过1.5亿岁的人的地方，以谈论来自运动鞋的所有内容，到他们最喜欢的游戏原声的最深刻的细节，到虚拟汽车聚会和深夜学习会议。

Just under a year ago, we outlined the work we’ve done to welcome the world to Discord, no matter what your interests may be. To help, we also wanted to make our overall look and feel just as welcoming to the world as the ever-improving in-app experience and help everyone enjoy Discord just a bit more.

就在一年前，我们概述了我们所做的工作，以欢迎世界来说，无论你的兴趣是什么。为了帮助，我们还希望让我们的整体外观和感觉同样欢迎世界，因为持续改善的应用程序体验并帮助每个人都享受不和谐。

Our current logo has stuck around with us since the beginning of time itself… as in, since we first launched Discord in 2015. A lot of people aren’t aware that the little smiley icon in the Discord logo has a name: Clyde. Most people call him Discord, Mickey Mouse pants, a game controller, and of course, ugly. :(

自从时间开始以来，我们目前的徽标与我们陷入困境......就像我们在2015年首次发布不和谐一样。很多人都不知道Discord Logo中的小笑脸图标有名称：Clyde。大多数人称他是不和谐的米老鼠裤子，游戏控制器，当然是丑陋的。 :(

Most of the time, you’ll see old Clyde within a rounded rectangle chat bubble. Technically, being inside this bubble is Clyde’s “home”. Whether it’s on our website, within a profile icon on our social pages, or inside the app icon on your computer or mobile device, Clyde was always “in” something, like a hamster stuck in a rolling ball.

大多数时候，你会在圆形矩形聊天泡沫中看到旧克莱德。从技术上讲，在这个泡沫里面是克莱德的“家”。无论是在我们的网站上，在我们的社交页面上的配置文件图标中，还是在计算机或移动设备上的应用程序图标内，克莱德总是“在”某些东西中，就像仓鼠卡在滚球中。

Only the most eagle-eyed may notice, but the old logo was horizontally asymmetrical — something our Design Team’s been waiting years to fix. Small details such as “Clyde lives within a bubble” and “not symmetrical” can be deceivingly restrictive when creating new interface elements, art assets, and even merch items.

只有最具鹰眼的眼睛可能会注意到，但旧的徽标是水平不对称的 - 我们的设计团队等待多年来修复。在创建新的界面元素，艺术资产甚至Merch物品时，诸如“泡沫中的克莱德生活”和“不对称”等细节可以脱节限制。

So, we took a step back and thought to ourselves: How can we give Clyde a refresh without going too crazy? And what makes Clyde, well… Clyde? It took a while for us to land in a good place — people around the world are already familiar with the Discord logo, so we can’t have them wake up one day seeing something weird like a banana as our icon.

所以，我们又回来了，想到了自己：我们怎样才能给克莱德刷新而不会太疯狂？是什么让克莱德，嗯......克莱德？我们花了一段时间让我们落在一个好地方 - 世界各地的人已经熟悉了不和谐的标志，所以我们不能让他们醒来有一天看到像我们的图标一样奇怪的东西。

Below is a preview of all the ideas and experimentation we went through when determining how Clyde should look. We went from lots of circular shapes to what’s basically a :) and eventually refining the more squared-off shape that you’re seeing today.

下面是我们在确定Clyde如何看待时经历的所有想法和实验的预览。我们从大量的圆形形状到基本上是什么:)并最终改善了你今天看到的更平坦的形状。

We’ve also freed Clyde from being stuck in his bubble, and he’s very excited about it. In fact, you might even see Clyde smile. Or be a bit sad. Or sleepy. A good amount of fan art depicts Clyde with expressions, but now we’re officially letting Clyde feel what he needs to feel.

我们也释放了克莱德被困在他的泡沫中，他对此非常兴奋。事实上，你甚至可能看到克莱德的微笑。或者有点难过。或困倦。一定数量的粉丝艺术描绘了克莱德用表达式，但现在我们正式让克莱德感受到他需要的感受。

If you take a peek at the top of Clyde’s head, you may notice the old antennas look a bit more like shoulders now. We realized that sharp little antennas didn’t translate well when printed on different types of materials, or when printed on really small surfaces such as a pin.

如果你在克莱德的头顶偷看，你可能会注意到旧的天线现在看起来更像肩膀。我们意识到，当在不同类型的材料上印刷或在真正的小表面上打印时，尖锐的小天线在打印时没有良好翻译。

In the end, we believe changes to Clyde present him in a more friendly tone, reshaped to be more understandable whether smaller or larger, all while still keeping our pal looking unique and familiar to the most veteran of users.

最后，我们相信克莱德的变化将他以更友好的语气呈现出来，重塑以更加易懂，无论更小或更大，一切都在仍然保持我们的朋友看起来独特而熟悉最熟悉的用户。

All these smaller details, improvements, and new options add up to a fresh new look for years to come.

所有这些较小的细节，改进和新选择增加了多年来的新外观。

Alongside the refreshed Clyde, you’ll also see an updated Discord wordmark, which is a fancy way of saying “the font used to spell out ‘Discord’” — it’s a custom font based upon “Ginto” that we created to go with the new Clyde logo.

与刷新的克莱德一起，您也会看到一个更新的Discord Wordmark，这是一种奇特的方式，即“用于拼出'discord'的字体” - 这是我们创建的“Ginto”的自定义字体新的克莱德标志。

We explored hundreds of different fonts before landing on this very unique font face that we believe captures the playfulness of Discord. We even tweaked some elements of the font and letters so it’s easier to read than how it originally was.

在降落之前，我们探讨了数百种不同的字体，我们认为我们认为捕捉不和谐的乐趣。我们甚至调整了字体和字母的一些元素，所以它比最初是如何阅读的。

The biggest thing that we debated, changed, changed back, changed back again, agonized over, and might have even had serious anxiety about was whether we should keep the wordmark in all caps or in title case. Once something is finalized and the world sees, there’s no going back, and that includes how things are capitalized.

我们辩论的最大的事情，改变了，再次改变，再次改变，痛苦，可能会有严重的焦虑，这是我们是否应该把所有帽子或标题案件保持在所有帽子中。一旦某些东西最终确定，世界看到，就没有回去了，这包括如何资本化。

In the end, we decided that Title case — making sure it’s always capitalized like someone’s name — made our new wordmark more legible. Although you won’t often see the wordmark used on its own, it does look beautiful.

到底，我们决定了这个标题 - 确保它总是像某人的名字一样大写 - 使我们的新字标更清晰。虽然你不会经常看到自己使用的字标，但它看起来很漂亮。

Good ol’ Blurple: Just like Clyde, Blurple is in every corner of Discord. It’s not exactly blue, not exactly purple… but it’s definitely close to our hearts. All of our older colors were a bit on the pastel-y side, along with our cleverly-named shades of Grey including… “Greyple,” “Dark, but not black” and “Not quite black.”

好的ol'模糊：就像克莱德一样，模糊在不和谐的每个角落里。这不是蓝色，而不是完全紫色......但它绝对靠近我们的心。我们所有的旧颜色都在柔和的山坡上有点漂亮，以及我们聪明的灰色阴影，包括......“灰苹果”，“黑暗，但不是黑色”和“不是很黑”。

With a new logo comes a brighter, more saturated Blurple: below is our new Blurple, right alongside the new Clyde and our new wordmark so you can cohesively see the new look:

有了一个新的徽标，更明亮，更饱和的模糊：下面是我们的新模糊，与新的克莱德和我们的新歌唱镜头一起，所以你可以凝聚一体看新的外观：

Internally, we first used this new color “blurple_5_plus_adjustments,” but that didn’t quite roll off the tongue as well as Blurple did. The lighter color of Blurple will forever be known as “OG Blurple.”

在内部，我们首先使用这种新的颜色“blureple_5_plus_adjustments”，但这并没有完全脱落舌头以及模糊。模糊的较浅的颜色将永远被称为“OG模糊”。

We wanted to bring more energy to Discord for our entire color palette by brightening up the colors to be more bold and playful. Along with refining many of the existing colors with a brighter hue, we’re bringing a bit more variety to the color palette by introducing a new color: Red.

我们希望通过亮起颜色更加大胆和俏皮来带来更多的能量来表示整个颜色调色板。除了用更亮的色调炼制许多现有的颜色，通过引入新颜色，我们将对调色板带来更多态度：红色。

You might recognize some of these colors — they’re inspired by application features that you’re likely familiar with on Discord, such as Online, Away, and Do Not Disturb status messages. And while pink isn’t a direct correlation to an in-app color, it’s as equally playful and fun as the rest of ‘em.

您可能会认识到其中一些颜色 - 它们的灵感来自应用功能，您可能熟悉Discord，如在线，远离和请勿打扰状态信息。虽然粉红色不是与应用内颜色直接相关，但它与EM的其余部分一样俏皮和乐趣。

It’s a bit more lively. A little bit brighter. And hopefully, a bit more like home.

这有点活泼。有点亮。希望有点像家一样。

When we say our new palette is inspired by using Discord on the day-to-day, that doesn’t mean these colors are strictly for our website or blog.

当我们说我们的新调色板通过日常使用不和谐的启发时，这并不意味着这些颜色是严格的支持我们的网站或博客。

When you open up Discord next time, things might feel a bit brighter. We’ve got our logos and colors, along with some new typography and art on the login and registration pages.

当你下次打开Discord时，事情可能会感到更亮。我们已经有了我们的徽标和颜色，以及登录和注册页面上的一些新的排版和艺术。

Once you’re logged in, the changes will be more subtle. New Clyde will be welcoming you on the top-left, just as he always has, and all the colors will be just a bit bolder.

登录后，更改将更加微妙。新克莱德将在左上方欢迎你，就像他总是拥有一样，所有颜色都会有点大胆。

New typography will appear within certain headings throughout the app, but the majority of the headings and fonts will stay the same as they are today. As the year goes on, you may notice additional updates here and there, such as changes to illustrations in empty states and tooltips — we want to make sure they’re perfect first.

新的排版将在整个应用程序中出现在某些标题中，但大多数标题和字体将与今天保持相同。随着年度的情况下，您可能会注意到这里和那里的其他更新，例如空态和工具提示中的插图的更改 - 我们想先确保它们是完美的。

Discord has become a place for millions around the world to create art, to karaoke nights and sharing plant care tips, speculate about cryptocurrencies, and on occasion, accidentally fall asleep because you’ve been up too late on voice.

Discord已成为全球数百万营造艺术的地方，到卡拉OK夜晚和分享植物护理技巧，推测关于加密货币，并在偶尔睡着的是，因为你的声音太晚了。

When figuring out our new styles, we connected with over 26,000 members of the community to ask what Discord means to them. We got your feedback and direction on style, colors, and even the way we talk about Discord.

在找出我们的新风格时，我们与26,000多名社区成员联系，以问对他们来说意味着什么。我们得到了风格，颜色，甚至我们谈论不和谐的方式的反馈和方向。

Your feedback and guidance also helped us make sure our new colors are still readable in both Dark and Light themes. It’s your influence that helped guide us while making changes both big and small, and we’re so grateful to have such dedicated and creative members of our community.

您的反馈和指导还帮助我们确保我们的新颜色仍然可读在黑暗和光线上。这是您帮助指导我们的影响力，同时使更改大小，我们如此感谢我们的社区拥有这样的专用和创造性成员。

We’re super excited ( and just a tiny bit nervous) to unveil our new look today, on our 6th birthday. Around all our websites and social profiles, you’ll see a new phrase: Imagine a Place.

我们在我们的第6岁生日时，我们超级兴奋（只是一个小小的紧张）揭开我们的新看看。在所有网站和社交概况周围，您会看到一个新的短语：想象一个地方。

Imagine a Place is our new mantra that brings to life the fact that Discord means so many different things to so many people around the world. It’s a creation and collaboration with the community that we are proud of, and we hope you are too.

想象一下，一个地方是我们的新咒语，为生活带来了，这是一个不和谐对世界各地很多人的不同事物。与我们为其为荣的社区创造和合作，我们希望你也是。

After all, that new place you’re imagining on Discord? It’s what you make of it.

毕竟，那个新的地方你想象在不和谐上？这就是你所做的。

Today’s just the beginning. Head on over to our YouTube channel where we’ll be celebrating our birthday with style. We’d love it if you stopped by to celebrate our new look (and birthday!) with us.

今天只是一开始。前往我们的YouTube频道，我们将以风格庆祝我们的生日。如果你停下来庆祝我们的新外观（和生日！），我们就会喜欢它。

Our new look was crafted with your help, and we’d love your thoughts on how it turned out. You’re always welcome to shoot us a tweet at @discord. If your feedback doesn’t fit within a tweet, our feedback forum is always open.

我们的新外观是用你的帮助制作的，我们会喜欢你的想法。欢迎您在@Discord拍摄我们的推文。如果您的反馈不适合推文，我们的反馈论坛始终打开。

There’s a lot more plans on the horizon. We’re glad you’re with us on this journey. ❤

地平线上有很多计划。我们很高兴在这段旅途中与我们在一起。 ❤
