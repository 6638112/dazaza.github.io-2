Simple Bank Is Closing
简单银行即将关闭

2021-01-09 07:07:52

Online banker Simple, one of Portland’s most prominent tech businesses for the past decade, is shutting down as part of its parent bank’s own sale process.

作为过去十年波特兰最杰出的科技公司之一，网上银行Simple即将关闭，这是其母行自己的销售流程的一部分。

Simple notified employees of the pending closure Wednesday and told customers Thursday, calling it a “strategic decision.” Simple told clients it will transfer their accounts to BBVA but set no timetable for the change.

Simple于周三通知即将关闭的员工，并于周四告知客户，称其为“战略决策”。简单告诉客户，它将把他们的帐户转移到BBVA，但没有设置更改时间表。

In November, BBVA announced it would sell its U.S. business to PNC Financial Services Group for $11.6 billion.

BBVA在11月宣布，将以116亿美元的价格将其美国业务出售给PNC Financial Services Group。

“We’re focused on the things that make the most sense for the company’s future whether on a standalone basis or a potentially combined basis with PNC,” BBVA said in a written statement Thursday. BBVA is also shutting down two other financial technology services it owns, Azlo and Open Platform.

BBVA在周四的书面声明中说：“我们专注于对公司的未来最有意义的事情，无论是独立运作还是与PNC潜在合并。” BBVA还将关闭其拥有的另外两个金融技术服务，Azlo和Open Platform。

Founded in 2009, Simple sought to position itself as a contrast to the greedy financial institutions that triggered the Great Recession. It organized around a straightforward principle: “ We don’t suck.”

成立于2009年的Simple公司试图将自己定位为与引发大萧条的贪婪的金融机构形成鲜明对比的机构。它围绕一个简单的原则进行组织：“我们不吸吮。”

Simple moved from Brooklyn to Portland two years later amid a renaissance in Oregon technology entrepreneurship. It was instantly among the city’s most prominent tech businesses, promising a consumer-friendly, technologically savvy, no-fee approach to mobile online banking.

两年后，随着俄勒冈州技术企业家的复兴，Simple从布鲁克林搬到了波特兰。它立即成为该市最杰出的科技企业之一，并承诺以消费者友好，精通技术，无需付费的方式进行移动在线银行业务。

Former Google and Wells Fargo executive Peggy Mangot, now a partner with PayPal Ventures in San Francisco, wrote Thursday on Twitter that Simple “changed the industry” with its innovations.

谷歌和富国银行前高管佩吉·曼格特（Peggy Mangot）现在是旧金山PayPal Ventures的合伙人，周四在Twitter上写道，Simple凭借其创新“改变了行业”。

Simple, Mangot wrote, “showed us all what an elegant, transparent & intuitive consumer banking experience should look like.”

Mangot写道：“简单，向我们展示了一个优雅，透明和透明的环境。直观的消费者银行业务体验应该看起来像。”

The Portland company sold in 2014 to Spanish banker BBVA for $117 million and never found its footing under the new ownership. The company struggled to compete as larger banks adopted many of the convenient online banking features Simple helped pioneer.

这家波特兰公司于2014年以1.17亿美元的价格卖给了西班牙银行家BBVA，但从未在新所有权下立足。随着大型银行采用了许多简单的先行者便利的在线银行功能，该公司努力进行竞争。

The Portland company faltered as it tried to resolve a central tension over whether it was foremost a bank or a tech company. Simple laid off 10% of its staff in 2017 and replaced nearly its entire executive team. Nine months later, founding CEO Josh Reich quit.

这家波特兰公司在试图解决有关它是一家银行还是一家高科技公司的中央矛盾时步履蹒跚。 Simple在2017年解雇了10％的员工，并更换了几乎整个整个执行团队。九个月后，创始首席执行官乔希·赖希（Josh Reich）辞职。

“I suspect PNC is prioritizing their existing online banking tech,” Reich tweeted Thursday. “I’m saddened for the closure but happy for the journey and the lasting change that Simple has had on the global banking world. Customer experience matters. Great teams matter.”

“我怀疑PNC正在优先考虑他们现有的在线银行技术，” Reich周四发推文说。 “我为关闭感到难过，但为Simple所经历的旅程以及全球银行业的持久变革感到高兴。客户体验至关重要。优秀的团队至关重要。”

Simple has been among Portland’s largest tech employers. Its current staff numbers 220, according to BBVA, 80% of them in the Northwest.

Simple是波特兰最大的技术雇主之一。据BBVA称，其现有员工人数为220人，其中80％在西北地区。

BBVA didn’t announce details of Simple’s dissolution or the fate of its Southeast Portland office, at the east end of the Hawthorne Bridge.

BBVA并未宣布解散Simple的细节或霍桑大桥东端的东南波特兰办公室的命运。

“There continue to be many details to address as part of the entity’s wind down,” BBVA said in Thursday’s statement. “As we work through regulatory approval and integration plans, our longer-term staffing needs will become more clear.”

BBVA在周四的声明中表示：“作为该实体倒闭的一部分，仍有许多细节需要解决。” “随着我们通过监管部门的批准和整合计划，我们的长期人员需求将变得更加清晰。”

Simple embodied both the aspirations and disappointments of the Portland tech community in the years after the Great Recession. Capitalizing on mobile technology, social networking and cloud computing, Simple was among a new generation of hopeful Oregon startups that ended the Silicon Forest’s historic dependence on hardware manufacturing.

在大萧条后的几年里，简单体现了波特兰科技界的愿望和失望。利用移动技术，社交网络和云计算的优势，Simple是俄勒冈州一家充满希望的新一代初创公司之一，这些公司结束了Silicon Forest对硬件制造的历史依赖。

Others from that generation included Jive Software, Urban Airship (now Airship), Elemental Technologies, Puppet, Jama Software, Janrain and Act-On Software. They collectively attracted hundreds of millions of dollars in venture capital and hired thousands of people.

这一代的其他公司包括Jive软件，城市飞艇（现为飞艇），Elemental Technologies，Puppet，Jama软件，Janrain和Act-On软件。他们共同吸引了数亿美元的风险投资，并雇用了数千人。

All endured their own share of setbacks, though, and none grew into a really large company. Some still operate – Puppet says it hopes to go public this year – while others sold to larger tech businesses that retained or expanded their Portland sites.

但是，所有人都遭受了自己的挫折，没有一家成长为一个真正的大公司。一些公司仍在运营（Puppet说希望今年上市），而其他公司则卖给了保留或扩展其波特兰工厂的大型科技公司。

No big new tech company has emerged in Oregon since the 1990s, though, and the old ones are steadily vanishing. The $8 billion sale of Wilsonville-based Flir Systems, announced Monday, leaves just two publicly traded technology companies in Oregon.

但是，自1990年代以来，俄勒冈州没有一家大型的新科技公司出现，而旧的科技公司正在逐步消失。周一宣布以80亿美元的价格出售总部位于威尔逊维尔的Flir Systems，仅在俄勒冈州剩下两家公开交易的技术公司。

Simple’s dedicated customer service and a commitment to helping its clients save money, rather than spend it, differentiated it from larger, more establish banks and earned it a devoted following among customers.

Simple的专属客户服务以及致力于帮助客户省钱（而不是花钱）的承诺，使其与大型，成熟的银行区分开来，并在客户中赢得了忠实的追随者。

Online, there was an outpouring of disappointment from some of those clients on Thursday, and from financial technology leaders.

在网上，周四，其中一些客户以及金融技术领导者都感到失望。
