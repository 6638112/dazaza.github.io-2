Amazon Drops Plan To Track Mask Wearing by Drivers With Cameras
亚马逊下降计划跟踪带有相机的司机的面膜

2021-03-27 03:20:28

Amazon has scrapped a plan to use new high-tech surveillance cameras to verify whether its delivery drivers are wearing face masks on the job.

亚马逊已经报废了一个计划使用新的高科技监控摄像头来验证其交付司机是否戴上工作面部面具。

The internet retail giant is in the process of installing the cameras—which will use artificial intelligence to identify driver drowsiness, distracted driving and other risky behavior—in its vast fleet of Amazon-branded delivery vans in the U.S., a move Amazon says is aimed at improving safety. In a recent training video for drivers, Amazon listed one previously unreported form of driver behavior it planned to use the cameras to check for—“face mask compliance”—according to an image of the video viewed by The Information.
