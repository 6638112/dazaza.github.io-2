PC Life After Apple Silicon
苹果硅之后的PC寿命

2020-11-23 20:22:13

Apple Silicon, in its first incarnation as the M1 System-on-a Chip, combined with a new macOS version is about to expand Apple’s share of the PC market — at Intel’s expense.

苹果芯片公司将其第一代产品称为M1片上系统，并结合了新的macOS版本，这将扩大苹果公司在PC市场的份额，而这要以英特尔为代价。

The results are in. Apple Silicon Macs met the lofty promises made at Apple’s last June WWDC and repledged at the November 10th introduction of three new Macs powered by the new M1 chip. According to the reviews that have emerged since the embargo was lifted on November 17th, the new Macs do provide the promised combination of better performance and longer battery life. As Dieter Bohn noted in his review [as always, edits and emphasis mine]:

结果证明了事实。AppleSilicon Macs达到了苹果去年6月WWDC做出的崇高承诺，并在11月10日推出了三款采用新M1芯片的新Macs。根据自11月17日解除禁运以来出现的评论，新款Mac确实提供了更好的性能和更长的电池寿命。正如Dieter Bohn在他的评论中指出的[一如既往，编辑和重点是我的]：

“…just for a moment, enjoy: a tech company made a big promise that it could do a hard thing and then did that thing."

“……只是一会儿，享受一下：一家科技公司做出了一个大承诺，即可以先做一件困难的事情，然后再做那件事。”

You might also want to look at John Gruber’s extensive (4814 words!) and thoughtful piece at Daring Fireball. Titled The M1 Macs the article concludes thus:

您可能还想看看John Gruber在Daring Fireball上发表的内容丰富（4814个单词！）和周到的文章。因此，本文的标题为M1 Macs。

“Folks used to say that no one gets fired for buying IBM. Similarly, no one ever won by betting against Intel and the x86 architecture. Intel always won.

“人们曾经说过，没有人因为购买IBM而被解雇。同样，没有人通过押注英特尔和x86架构而获胜。英特尔始终赢了。

Until now, that is, when it’s clear they’ve lost. Apple’s two previous Mac architecture transitions were made from positions of desperation — they were jumping the Mac from sinking ships. This transition from Intel to Apple Silicon has clearly been made from a position of overwhelming strength.”

直到现在，也就是他们显然已经迷路了。苹果公司先前的两次Mac架构转换都是在绝望的情况下进行的–它们使Mac从沉没的船上跳了下来。从英特尔到Apple Silicon的这种过渡显然是出于压倒性优势。”

Indeed, the following graph from an AnandTech article shows us that in the past few years, Apple chips have made more rapid progress than Intel’s and have now taken the lead. [the line and arrows are mine]:

确实，下一篇AnandTech文章中的图表向我们表明，在过去的几年中，苹果芯片取得了比英特尔更快的进步，并且现在处于领先地位。 [线和箭头是我的]：

(The graph would be even more persuasive with power requirements figured in. All reviewers have noted that the M1 chips use significantly less power, thus extending battery life.)

（该图对于确定的功率要求将更具说服力。所有评论者都注意到，M1芯片使用的功率明显更少，从而延长了电池寿命。）

Cheers to Apple for making good on its promise and asserting itself in the PC world, but does it matter to Intel? Conventional wisdom tells that while the new Apple Silicon Macs will intrude on the edge of the PC market, the Santa Clara company’s x86 architecture, which commands more than 90% of desktops and laptops, and even more of the high-margin server market, will continue to dominate.

为Apple兑现诺言并在PC世界中树立自己而欢呼，但这对Intel来说有关系吗？传统观点告诉我们，虽然新的Apple Silicon Macs将会入侵PC市场的边缘，但Santa Clara公司的x86架构仍将占据90％的台式机和笔记本电脑，甚至更高利润的服务器市场。继续统治。

So does the M1 processor matter beyond the manicured confines of Apple’s Walled Garden? The answer might come from Microsoft.

那么，M1处理器是否超出了Apple围墙花园修剪整齐的范围？答案可能来自微软。

The Redmond company already has Windows 10 running on ARM silicon developed by Qualcomm. Despite an update this year, the combination hasn’t taken the world by storm, so why not go “whole Mac” and offer Windows 10 on M1? Microsoft’s key applications running on Apple hardware would deliver an experience that’s much more attractive than on an x86 laptop. As SR VP of Software Engineering Craig Federighi noted, it’s Microsoft’s decision:

这家位于雷德蒙德（Redmond）的公司已经安装了由高通（Qualcomm）开发的在ARM芯片上运行的Windows 10。尽管今年进行了更新，但合并并没有席卷全球，所以为什么不选择“全Mac”并在M1上提供Windows 10？在Apple硬件上运行的Microsoft关键应用程序将提供比x86笔记本电脑更具吸引力的体验。正如软件工程副总裁Craig Federighi指出的那样，这是Microsoft的决定：

“…for Windows running natively on the machine, “that’s really up to Microsoft,” he said. “We have the core technologies for them to do that, to run their ARM version of Windows, which in turn of course supports x86 user mode applications. But that’s a decision Microsoft has to make, to bring to license that technology for users to run on these Macs. But the Macs are certainly very capable of it.”

他说：“……对于本机上运行的Windows，这完全取决于Microsoft。” “我们拥有运行它们的ARM版本Windows所需的核心技术，而Windows当然又支持x86用户模式应用程序。但这是微软必须做出的决定，要使该技术获得许可，使用户可以在这些Mac上运行。但是Mac肯定有能力。”

I won’t presume to think for Microsoft, but it seems like an attractive proposition for a company that’s increasingly interested in its applications running everywhere on any hardware, whether locally or in the cloud. According to a Windows Central article, Microsoft Office support for M1 chips is “in the works” — or already there through emulation:

我不会为微软考虑，但对于一家公司来说，这似乎是一个诱人的主张，因为该公司对其在任何硬件（无论是本地还是云）上无处不在运行的应用程序越来越感兴趣。根据Windows Central的文章，Microsoft Office对M1芯片的支持“正在开发中”-或已经通过仿真实现了：

“Microsoft also wants to deliver the best experience across platforms, so it’s jumping on board the M1 train.”

“微软还希望在各个平台上提供最佳体验，因此它正在搭乘M1列车。”

With Microsoft apps running natively on M1 Macs, x86 PC makers such as HP and Dell will be competing with machines that are faster and have a better battery life. Their only rebuttal could be Windows 10 running on ARM silicon.

通过在M1 Mac上本机运行的Microsoft应用程序，惠普和戴尔等x86 PC制造商将与速度更快，电池寿命更长的计算机竞争。他们唯一的反驳可能是在ARM芯片上运行的Windows 10。

The ARM processor inside Microsoft’s Surface Pro X might not be as fast as the M1, but there’s a strong incentive for Qualcomm or AMD to partner with Microsoft and come up with an attractive ARM-based chip and offer it to today’s PC OEMs. With Windows 10 on ARM this would create a new generation of lighter, longer-running laptops that, in theory, would be able to compete with Apple Silicon Macs.

微软的Surface Pro X内的ARM处理器可能没有M1快，但是高通或AMD强烈地希望与微软合作，并提出一种有吸引力的基于ARM的芯片，并将其提供给当今的PC OEM。借助ARM上的Windows 10，这将创造出新一代的更轻便，运行时间更长的笔记本电脑，从理论上讲，它将能够与Apple Silicon Macs竞争。

In this scenario, Microsoft becomes the entity that controls Intel’s future in the PC market. While the M1 Macs might not make a dent in Intel’s PC business, Windows 10 running on competitive, inexpensive ARM processors would be an enormous blow…if Microsoft can pull it off.

在这种情况下，微软成为控制英特尔在PC市场上未来的实体。尽管M1 Macs可能不会在英特尔的PC业务中占便宜，但是运行Windows 10的竞争性，廉价的ARM处理器将是巨大的打击……如果微软能够实现这一目标。

Over the years, Microsoft’s adoption of ARM has been tentative. Windows RT, a version of Windows 8 running on 32-bit ARM hardware introduced in 2011, didn’t “take”. In 2016, Microsoft announced a partnership with Qualcomm to run 32-bit Windows software on ARM architecture hardware. In 2019, Microsoft shipped the Surface Pro X, followed by a 2020 version that promised to finally run native 64-bit Windows software.

多年来，微软采用ARM一直是尝试性的。 Windows RT是2011年推出的在32位ARM硬件上运行的Windows 8版本，并未“采用”。 2016年，微软宣布与高通公司合作，在ARM体系结构硬件上运行32位Windows软件。在2019年，微软发布了Surface Pro X，随后是2020版本，该版本承诺最终将运行本机64位Windows软件。

Contrast these hesitant, tepidly received steps with Apple’s firm move that combined hardware, macOS, and its own applications.

与这些犹豫不决的步骤形成鲜明对比的是，苹果公司坚定地采取了将硬件，macOS和自己的应用程序结合起来的举措。

Even if Microsoft were to forge partnerships that resulted in ARM-based PC processors, the “decentralized” command and control could result in a lot of confusion. As difficult as it is to move vertically integrated hardware and software to new silicon, the task is compounded when you’re trying to herd PC OEMs, processor makers, and software (Windows and applications).

即使微软与基于ARM的PC处理器结成伙伴关系，“分散的”命令和控制也可能导致很多混乱。将垂直集成的硬件和软件转移到新的芯片上虽然很困难，但是当您试图聚集PC OEM，处理器制造商和软件（Windows和应用程序）时，任务就变得复杂了。

One can see an uneasy, confused coexistence of x86 and various ARM-based products. I have no idea how Microsoft could bring order to a new ARM platform, certainly not with the success that let them reign for decades with x86 devices.

可以看到x86与各种基于ARM的产品并存的不安，困惑。我不知道微软如何将订单带到一个新的ARM平台上，当然不是让他们在x86设备上占据统治地位的成功。

This leaves us with two conclusions. First, moving to ARM-based PCs is a challenging proposition: Confusion if you do, less competitive PCs if you don’t.

这给我们两个结论。首先，转向基于ARM的PC是一个具有挑战性的命题：如果这样做，就会造成混乱，如果不这样做，则竞争会较弱。

Second, even if Macs don’t generate as much revenue as iPhones do, they are poised to easily expand Apple’s share of the laptop and desktop market — taken from Intel’s hide.

其次，即使Mac不能产生比iPhone更大的收入，它们也有望轻松扩大Apple在笔记本电脑和台式机市场的份额-这是取自Intel的。
