贝宝(PayPal)允许在其网络上购买、销售和购物加密货币

2020-10-21 20:24:53

LONDON (Reuters) - PayPal Holdings Inc PYPL.O joined the cryptocurrency market on Wednesday, allowing customers to buy, sell and hold bitcoin and other virtual coins using the U.S. digital payments company's online wallets.

伦敦(路透社)-美国数字支付公司PayPal Holdings Inc PYPL.O周三加入加密货币市场，允许客户使用这家美国数字支付公司的在线钱包买卖和持有比特币和其他虚拟币。

PayPal customers will also be able to use cryptocurrencies to shop at the 26 million merchants on its network starting in early 2021, the company said in a statement.

贝宝(PayPal)在一份声明中表示，从2021年初开始，贝宝客户还将能够使用加密货币在其网络上的2600万商家购物。

PayPal hopes the service will encourage global use of virtual coins and prepare its network for new digital currencies that may be developed by central banks and corporations, President and Chief Executive Dan Schulman said in an interview.

贝宝总裁兼首席执行长舒尔曼(Dan Schulman)在接受采访时说，贝宝希望这项服务将鼓励全球使用虚拟币，并为央行和企业可能开发的新数字货币网络做好准备。

“We are working with central banks and thinking of all forms of digital currencies and how PayPal can play a role,” he said.

他说：“我们正在与各国央行合作，考虑各种形式的数字货币，以及贝宝如何发挥作用。”

U.S. account holders will be able to buy, sell and hold cryptocurrencies in their PayPal wallets over the coming weeks, the company said. It plans to expand to Venmo and some countries in the first half of 2021.

该公司表示，未来几周，美国账户持有人将能够在贝宝(PayPal)钱包中买卖和持有加密货币。它计划在2021年上半年扩展到文莫和一些国家。

Other mainstream fintech companies, such as mobile payments provider Square Inc SQ.N and stock trading app firm Robinhood Markets Inc, allow users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies, but PayPal's launch is noteworthy given its vast reach.

其他主流金融科技公司，如移动支付提供商广场公司和股票交易应用公司罗宾汉市场公司，允许用户买卖加密货币，但贝宝的推出值得注意，因为它覆盖的范围很广。

The company, based in San Jose, California, has 346 million active accounts around the world and processed $222 billion in payments in the second quarter.

该公司总部位于加利福尼亚州圣何塞，在全球拥有3.46亿活跃账户，第二季度处理了2220亿美元的支付。

Cryptocurrencies tend to be volatile, making them attractive to speculators, but a lot less appealing to merchants and shoppers. Transactions have been slower and more costly than other mainstream payment systems.

加密货币往往波动性大，对投机者有吸引力，但对商家和购物者的吸引力要小得多。与其他主流支付系统相比，交易速度更慢，成本也更高。

Cryptocurrency payments on PayPal will be settled using fiat currencies, such as the U.S. dollar, meaning merchants will not receive payments in virtual coins, the company said.

PayPal表示，PayPal上的加密货币支付将使用美元等法定货币进行结算，这意味着商家将不会收到虚拟硬币支付。

Many central banks around the world have expressed their intention to develop digital versions of their currencies in the coming years, while Facebook Inc FB.O -led the creation of a cryptocurrency project called Libra in 2019. PayPal was a founding member but dropped out after a few months.

世界各地的许多央行都表示有意在未来几年开发其货币的数字版本，而Facebook Inc FB.O-在2019年牵头创建了一个名为Libra的加密货币项目。贝宝是创始成员之一，但几个月后就退出了。

PayPal, which has secured the first conditional cryptocurrency license from the New York State Department of Financial Services, will initially allow purchases of bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies called ethereum, bitcoin cash and litecoin, it said. It partners with Paxos Trust Company to offer the service.

贝宝(PayPal)已经从纽约州金融服务部(New York State Department Of Financial Services)获得了第一个有条件的加密货币牌照，该公司表示，最初将允许购买比特币和其他名为以太、比特币现金和Litecoin的加密货币。它与Paxos Trust Company合作提供这项服务。
