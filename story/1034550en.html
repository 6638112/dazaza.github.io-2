Fast.ai的Anki卡片

2020-11-11 23:32:02

Import cards using default import settings. Many cards utilize images - they should render assuming you have internet access.

使用默认导入设置导入卡片。许多卡片使用图像--假设你有互联网接入，它们应该会被渲染。

Each card has a link to aiquizzes at the bottom (there might be additional information available on aiquizzes, such as reference to the part of the lecture that discusses a given concept). Nonetheless, the information in the anki cards should be sufficient for review without accessing external resources.

每张卡片的底部都有一个问答题的链接(可能有关于问答题的其他信息，例如对讲座中讨论给定概念的部分的参考)。尽管如此，Anki卡片中的信息应该足以在不访问外部资源的情况下进行审查。
