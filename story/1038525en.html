Uber Gives Up on the Self-Driving Dream
优步放弃自我驾驶的梦想

2020-12-09 18:30:34

In 2015, then Uber CEO Travis Kalanick pulled off a bold talent raid when he poached some 40 roboticists from the National Robotics Engineering Center at Carnegie Mellon. The move reportedly left the world-class engineering university reeling, and it seemed to signal that the world’s hottest startup was on the cusp of making self-driving cars a reality.

2015年，Uber首席执行官特拉维斯·卡兰尼克（Travis Kalanick）从卡内基·梅隆大学国家机器人工程中心挖走了约40名机器人专家，展开了一次大胆的人才突袭。据报道，此举使世界一流的工程大学大为吃惊，这似乎表明世界上最炙手可热的初创公司正处于使无人驾驶汽车成为现实的风口浪尖上。

Now, that self-driving unit is no more, and the estimated timeline for robotaxi domination has extended well into this decade. Uber said Monday it would sell off the self-driving unit that was the result of that raid, the Pittsburgh-based Advanced Technologies Group. The 1,200-person unit will be acquired by the self-driving-tech developer Aurora. Uber will invest $400 million in Aurora as part of the deal, bringing Aurora’s valuation to $10 billion and tripling its workforce. Uber's current CEO, Dara Khosrowshahi, will also take a seat on Aurora’s board.

现在，该自动驾驶装置已经不复存在了，据估计，机器人控制的时间表已经延伸到了这个十年。优步周一表示，将出售由于这次袭击而导致的自动驾驶部门-总部位于匹兹堡的先进技术集团（Advanced Technologies Group）。自动驾驶技术开发商Aurora将收购可容纳1200人的单位。作为交易的一部分，Uber将向Aurora投资4亿美元，使Aurora的估值达到100亿美元，并使员工人数增加两倍。 Uber现任首席执行官Dara Khosrowshahi也将担任Aurora董事会成员。

The move continues the consolidation in self-driving technology, as the process of creating safe, secure autonomous vehicles continues to cost more and take longer than prognosticators once believed. Uber ATG lost $303 million between January and September of this year, according to financial filings, and the company has spent more than $1 billion in its five years of existence.

此举将继续巩固自动驾驶技术，因为制造安全可靠的自动驾驶汽车的过程将继续花费比预言家所认为的更高的成本和更长的时间。据财务文件显示，Uber ATG在今年1月至9月期间亏损了3.03亿美元，该公司成立5年以来已花费了超过10亿美元。

Aurora isn’t planning to build a self-driving car or truck itself; instead, it’s developing the complex software that will power autonomous vehicles. It has signed deals with carmakers including Hyundai, the electric vehicle company Byton, and Fiat Chrysler Automobiles. Through the Uber deal, Aurora likely gains another big partner: Toyota. The Japanese company invested $500 million in the Uber self-driving unit last year. Aurora is testing its technology in the Bay Area, Pittsburgh, and in Dallas. The company also has offices in Bozeman, Montana, the former home of the lidar company Blackmore, which it acquired in 2019.

Aurora并不打算自己制造自动驾驶汽车或卡车。相反，它正在开发可为自动驾驶汽车提供动力的复杂软件。它已经与包括现代，电动汽车公司拜顿和菲亚特克莱斯勒汽车公司在内的汽车制造商签署了交易。通过与优步的交易，极光可能会获得另一个大伙伴：丰田。这家日本公司去年向Uber自动驾驶部门投资了5亿美元。 Aurora正在匹兹堡湾区和达拉斯测试其技术。该公司还在蒙大拿州的Bozeman设有办事处，该公司于2019年收购了激光雷达公司Blackmore的前身。

The sale of ATG continues another trend, of Uber narrowing its scope and selling off parts of its business as it seeks profitability. The ride-hail company—which once hoped to be an “Amazon for transportation”—offloaded its micromobility unit Jump to Lime this summer, and it sold part of its trucking logistics business, Uber Freight, this fall. Uber is also reportedly in talks to sell off its autonomous air taxi business, Elevate. Uber “remains committed to commercializing self-driving transportation on the Uber network through industry partnerships,” spokesperson Sarah Abboud says.

ATG的出售延续了另一趋势，即Uber寻求盈利，从而缩小了范围并出售了部分业务。这家叫车服务公司曾经希望成为“运输的亚马逊”，今年夏天卸载了其微型运输部门Jump to Lime，并于今年秋天出售了部分卡车物流业务Uber Freight。据报道，Uber也正在商谈出售其自动空中出租车业务Elevate。优步发言人莎拉·阿布德（Sarah Abboud）表示，“优步仍然致力于通过行业合作伙伴关系将无人驾驶网络上的自动驾驶运输商业化”。

Uber’s self-driving efforts have been troubled. It was sued by Google sibling Waymo for trade-secret theft after acquiring another self-driving technology developer, Otto. After a buzzy few days of public trial in San Francisco, the two companies settled the case, with Uber promising to steer clear of Waymo’s tech—a serious setback for the Uber hardware team. Anthony Levandowski, the Uber self-driving head at the center of the trade secrets case, was later charged by federal prosecutors for his role in the scheme; after pleading guilty, he is now serving an 18-month prison sentence.

优步的自动驾驶工作遇到了麻烦。 Google兄弟姐妹Waymo在收购了另一家自动驾驶技术开发商Otto后，以商业秘密盗窃为由提起诉讼。在旧金山进行了数天的公开审判后，两家公司达成和解，Uber承诺避免使用Waymo的技术，这对Uber硬件团队是一个严重的挫折。优步（Uber）自动驾驶业务负责人安东尼·莱万多夫斯基（Anthony Levandowski）后来因其在该计划中的作用而被联邦检察官指控。在认罪后，他现在被判处18个月监禁。

In 2018, an Uber self-driving vehicle that was being tested struck and killed a woman in Tempe, Arizona. (The safety driver behind the wheel has since been charged with negligence.) The death—the self-driving industry’s first—led Uber to halt testing for months while it reevaluated its safety systems and program. (The company didn’t actually have an operational safety division at the time.) According to an investigation by the National Transportation Safety Board, Uber’s organizational failures were at least partly responsible for the woman’s death. Today, Uber is back on the road in a more limited capacity, testing in Pittsburgh and Washington, DC.

2018年，在亚利桑那州的坦佩，正在接受测试的Uber自动驾驶汽车撞死了一名妇女。 （自那时以来，方向盘后面的安全驾驶员被控过失。）自动驾驶行业的死亡是Uber的死亡，导致Uber在重新评估其安全系统和程序时暂停了几个月的测试。 （该公司当时实际上没有运营安全部门。）根据国家运输安全委员会的调查，Uber的组织失误至少部分原因是该名妇女的死亡。如今，Uber以更有限的容量重回道路，在匹兹堡和华盛顿特区进行测试。

It’s interesting, then, that Aurora cites Uber’s safety systems as a key part of the acquisition. A blog post from Aurora CEO Chris Urmson also praises Uber’s advances in software testing and its team’s experience in building for ride-hail. Aurora said this summer that it expects its first commercialization efforts will happen in trucking instead of ride-hail, but on Monday it said that autonomous vehicles powered by its Aurora Driver would one day run on Uber’s network.

有趣的是，Aurora引用了Uber的安全系统作为此次收购的关键部分。 Aurora首席执行官Chris Urmson的博客文章也赞扬Uber在软件测试方面的进步以及其团队在乘车建造方面的经验。 Aurora在今年夏天表示，它预计将在卡车而不是叫车方面进行首次商业化努力，但周一表示，由Aurora Driver驱动的自动驾驶汽车有一天将在Uber的网络上运行。

Aurora, founded in 2017, already has deep roots in the Uber robotics team: Its CTO, Drew Bagnell, was a founding member of ATG and was one of the Carnegie Mellon professors lured to Uber in 2015. Urmson is an industry vet who once led what’s now Waymo, when it was part of Google; Sterling Anderson, the company’s other cofounder, helped Tesla launch Autopilot.

Aurora成立于2017年，已经深深扎根于Uber机器人团队：其首席技术官Drew Bagnell是ATG的创始成员，也是2015年吸引Uber的Carnegie Mellon教授之一。Urmson是一位曾任职于行业的兽医Waymo是Google的一部分，现在是什么？该公司的另一位联合创始人斯特林·安德森（Sterling Anderson）帮助特斯拉推出了Autopilot。
