I noticed that Rust Foundation addressed this topic recently. They made the decision to not hire anyone directly,opting instead to try to help core contributors land employment at other companies:

我注意到Rust基础最近解决了这个话题。他们决定直接雇用任何人，而是试图帮助核心贡献者在其他公司的土地就业：

Why not hire folks? For one thing, we don’t think a Foundation would be able to pay themwhat they’re worth or support them in the way they should be supported. We would have tohire human resourcing people, managers, figure out peoples’ design goals and career path,and do all the other things that a good employer does, and that is not practical.

为什么不雇用人？对于一件事而言，我们不认为基础能够支付他们的价值或支持他们应该得到支持的方式。我们将举行人类资助人员，经理，弄清楚人民的设计目标和职业道路，做好雇主所做的所有其他事情，这并不实用。

I think it’s a wise move on their part. They have some major players on their board ofdirectors (Microsoft, Huawei, Amazon, Google), and have positioned Rust as a project thatthese large corporations, although competing in their respective markets, have a sort of“truce” because they mutually benefit from Rust, and are thus incentivized to donateemployee time to the project. This can scale to a large number of Rust contributors;they’ve basically decentralized compensation.

我认为这是一个明智的行动。他们在他们的董事会（微软，华为，亚马逊，谷歌）上有一些主要球员，并将RUDER作为一个项目作为一个项目，尽管在各自的市场竞争，但有一种“休战”，因为它们相互受益于生锈因此，并因此激励达到项目的时间。这可以扩展到大量的生锈贡献者;他们基本上分散赔偿。

However, Zig Software Foundation will never have big tech companies on the boardof directors. We are grateful to Pex, for example, for donating$5,000/month, even though they have no board seats. Our goal is to maintainindependence by keeping the mix of donations balanced among many parties.

但是，Zig软件基金会永远不会在董事会上拥有大型技术公司。例如，我们感谢PEEX捐赠5,000美元/月，即使他们没有董事会席位。我们的目标是通过保持许多缔约方之间的捐款组合来维护。

We have to be very careful to treat our contractors with respect andmake sure their needs are met. The quote above makes an excellentpoint that a good employer has the responsibility to meet various needs of their employees.

我们必须非常小心地对待我们的承包商，并确保他们的需求得到满足。上面的报价使得一个好雇主有责任满足员工的各种需求。

First and foremost it means we must compensate well. Right now we are paying 50 USD per hour,but I would like to see this raised to 75 within the next two years. Contractors do notreceive medical insurance or other employment perks, so the hourly wage must be higher toaccount for that.

首先，它意味着我们必须康复。现在我们每小时支付50美元，但我希望在未来两年内将此提升至75。承包商进行医疗保险或其他就业特权，因此每小时工资必须更高。

The main trick ZSF has up its sleeve to avoid hiring any managers or humanresourcing people, is to have a flat organization structure. By hiring contributorswho are self-directed and intrinsically motivated, compensating them well, and then giving them the freedom to work on what they choose, the need for managers is almostentirely eliminated. What’s left looks a lot like peers collaborating with each other, anddoes not itself consume a full-time role.

主要特技ZSF有袖子，以避免雇用任何经理或人类的人，是有一个平坦的组织结构。通过招聘贡献者是自我导向和本质上的动机，弥补它们，然后让他们自由地处理他们选择的东西，几乎擅自消除了对管理人员的需求。什么留下了很多像同行相互协作的很多，并且没有自己消耗全职角色。
