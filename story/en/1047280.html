Not just Facebook: Snap, Unity warn Apple’s tracking change threatens business
不只是Facebook：Unity Snap还警告苹果的追踪变更威胁业务

2021-02-06 21:00:28

Social media company Snap (which runs Snapchat) and game development software company Unity have joined Facebook in warning their investors that Apple's imminent ad-tracking change will negatively impact their businesses.

社交媒体公司Snap（运行Snapchat）和游戏开发软件公司Unity都加入了Facebook，警告投资者，苹果即将进行的广告跟踪更改将对其业务产生负面影响。

As previously reported, Apple plans to use the next iOS update (iOS 14.5, due out in early spring) to implement a requirement that all apps on the platform gain user opt in to track users with IDFA (ID for Advertisers) tags. IDFA tags are used to track what users do across multiple apps in order to target advertising more effectively.

正如先前报道的那样，Apple计划使用下一个iOS更新（iOS 14.5，将于春季初发布）实施一项要求，要求平台上的所有应用都可以吸引用户加入，以跟踪具有IDFA（广告客户ID）标签的用户。 IDFA标签用于跟踪用户在多个应用中的操作，以便更有效地定位广告。

Social media giant Facebook has told its own investors that the coming change to Apple's operating system could very negatively impact its advertising revenue, because this kind of tracking-based ad targeting is one of Facebook's main ingredients for success. It has also taken out full-page newspaper ads claiming that one effect of targeted advertising becoming less effective as a result of this change is that small and local businesses that rely on Facebook advertising will see higher costs to reach users, negatively impacting those businesses as well.

社交媒体巨头Facebook告诉自己的投资者，苹果操作系统即将发生的变化可能会对其广告收入产生非常不利的影响，因为这种基于跟踪的广告定位是Facebook成功的主要因素之一。它还删除了整版报纸广告，声称针对性广告由于这种变化而变得效果不佳的一个效果是，依赖Facebook广告的小型和本地企业将看到较高的到达用户费用，对这些企业产生负面影响。好。

Unity made a similar case, in a way. Unity runs a mobile app advertising platform, earning much of its revenue from game and app developers on platforms like iOS. The company's leadership said in yesterday's earnings call that the change will affect app and game developers' ability to efficiently gain new customers and optimize their lifetime value. The company predicts its revenues will fall $30 million in 2021 as a result of the change. However, that is estimated to be just 3 percent of the company's overall annual revenue.

在某种程度上，Unity提出了类似的案例。 Unity运行一个移动应用程序广告平台，其大部分收入来自iOS等平台上的游戏和应用程序开发人员。该公司的领导层在昨天的财报电话会议上表示，这一变化将影响应用程序和游戏开发商。有效吸引新客户并优化其终身价值的能力。该公司预测，由于这一变化，其收入将在2021年下降3000万美元。但是，据估计，这仅占公司整体年收入的3％。

For its part, Snap (which offers many sophisticated ad products) warned investors of the effects the change could have but tried to strike a more confident tone. "We admire Apple and believe they are trying to do the right thing for customers," said Chief Business Officer Jeremi Gorman. She said she believes Snap is well-positioned to prepare advertisers for the change, even as the potential impact was acknowledged.

就其本身而言，Snap（提供许多复杂的广告产品）向投资者警告了这一变化可能带来的影响，但试图打出更加自信的基调。 ＆＃34;佩服Apple，并相信他们正在努力为客户做正确的事，＆＃34;首席商务官Jeremi Gorman说。她说，她相信即使已经意识到潜在的影响，Snap也有充分的条件为广告客户做好准备以应对这种变化。

As a result of concern about the Apple change, Snap shares fell 7 percent in after-hours trading despite an otherwise positive business picture, including a 22 percent increase in daily active users year over year. The stock rose back above its previous levels during today's trading, however.

由于担心苹果公司的变化，尽管业务前景乐观，但Snap股价在盘后交易中下跌了7％，其中包括每日活跃用户同比增长22％。但是，该股在今天的交易中又回到了先前的水平。

When The Information reported that Facebook leadership was consulting with outside legal counsel for a potential lawsuit against Apple, it also said that many Facebook employees were opposed to the move because they did not see Facebook as "a compelling victim." While Snap and Unity have not orchestrated PR or legal battles against Apple like Facebook has, the public might find Snap and Unity more sympathetic. Snap is popular, but it does not dominate in the same way Facebook does, and it has been less embroiled in controversies over the spread of misinformation or privacy scandals. And Unity's stated cause for concern is that it will miss out on revenue because its customers—game and app developers big and small—will see their own revenue fall.

《信息报》报道称，Facebook领导层正在就可能针对苹果的诉讼向外部法律顾问咨询时，它还表示，许多Facebook员工反对这一举动，因为他们不认为Facebook是“令人信服的受害者”。 ;尽管Snap和Unity并未像Facebook那样精心策划针对苹果的公关或法律斗争，但公众可能会发现Snap和Unity更同情。 Snap很受欢迎，但它并没有以Facebook的方式独占and头，而且由于误传信息或隐私丑闻而引起的争议也较少。而且，Unity表示担忧的原因是，它将错失收入，因为它的客户（无论大小的游戏和应用开发人员）都将看到自己的收入下降。

Nonetheless, the companies plan to comply with Apple's required changes, and those changes are expected to roll out this spring. Apple has already updated its app review guidelines to reflect the new policy.

尽管如此，两家公司仍计划遵守Apple要求的更改，并且这些更改预计将在今年春季推出。 苹果已经更新了其应用审查指南，以反映新政策。
