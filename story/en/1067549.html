Facebook says it is working on visual search for Instagram shopping to help users find info on products in images, and Shops is coming to WhatsApp, Marketplace
Facebook表示，它正在研究Visual搜索Instagram购物，以帮助用户在图像中找到产品信息，而商店即将推出WhatsApp，市场

2021-06-23 04:26:55

Facebook is working on visual search technology for Instagram as it doubles down on shopping features throughout its platform. Speaking in a live audio room on Facebook, Mark Zuckerberg said the company is in the early stages of building camera-based search tools.

Facebook正在为Instagram开发Visual Search技术，因为它在整个平台上的购物特征下来。 Mark Zuckerberg表示，在Facebook上的现场音频室中说，该公司处于建立基于相机的搜索工具的早期阶段。

The feature would be similar to ones offered by Snapchat and Pinterest, which have both invested heavily into visual search. Zuckerberg outlined two potential use cases for the technology on Instagram. When browsing Instagram, visual search could surface similar products available elsewhere in the app, or it could allow users to find products using their cameras or images from their camera roll.

该功能与Snapchat和Pinterest提供的功能类似，这两者都投入了很多程度的视觉搜索。 Zuckerberg概述了Instagram上技术的两个潜在用例。在浏览Instagram时，可视化搜索可以在应用程序中其他地方的可用产品曲面，或者它可以允许用户使用摄像机或图像从相机滚动中找到产品。

“When you find something you'll be able to tap on it and find similar products that people across all of our shops are selling the moment that you see something that you like,” Zuckerberg said. “We think that visual search is going to be really helpful in making photos shoppable Instagram overall.”

“当你找到你的东西时，你能够点击它并找到类似的产品，我们所有商店的人们都在销售你喜欢的东西，”扎克伯格说。 “我们认为视觉搜索将非常有助于制作照片可购物的Instagram整体上。”

In addition to visual search for Instagram, Zuckerberg also announced that Facebook Shops will soon be arriving on Marketplace and WhatsApp. Facebook has already been integrating product catalogs into WhatsApp chats, but now businesses will be able to create storefronts for the chat app.

除了视觉搜索Instagram之外，Zuckerberg还宣布，Facebook商店很快将抵达市场和WhatsApp。 Facebook已经将产品目录集成到WhatsApp聊天中，但现在企业将能够为聊天应用程序创建店面。

The company is also working on “Shops ads,” which would allow businesses to target ads based on “people’s individual shopping preferences.” Eventually, facebook could further personalize these ads with special offers or promotions, Zuckerberg said.

该公司还在致力于“商店广告”，这将允许企业根据“人民个人购物偏好”来定位广告。 Zuckerberg说，Facebook可以通过特别优惠或促销，更加个性化这些广告。
