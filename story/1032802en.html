相对论太空的蒂姆·埃利斯将参加TC Sessions：Space 2020

2020-11-03 00:32:21

Relativity was founded in 2015 and since then has racked up a full slate of eager customers, making its Terran-1 rocket possibly the most pre-sold launch vehicle ever. And it’s not hard to see why. The 3D printing process that Ellis, with co-founder and CTO Jordan Noone, dreamed up while cutting their rocketry teeth at Blue Origin, is potentially revolutionary.

相对论成立于2015年，自那以来已经积累了一大批热切的客户，使其Terran-1火箭可能成为有史以来预售最多的运载火箭。不难看出其中的原因。埃利斯与联合创始人兼首席技术官乔丹·努恩(Jordan Noone)在蓝色起源(Blue Origin)切割火箭牙齿时梦想的3D打印过程可能是革命性的。

Launch vehicles are normally made up of thousands of parts, each of which must be manufactured, inspected, and fitted together individually. This not only makes building a rocket a long and complex process, but an expensive one, too.

运载火箭通常由数千个部件组成，每个部件都必须单独制造、检查和安装在一起。这不仅使建造火箭成为一个漫长而复杂的过程，而且也是一个昂贵的过程。

Relativity’s approach is to 3D print everything it possibly can in its enormous “Stargate” printers, reducing the total number of parts by orders of magnitude — while, at least according to pre-flight tests, not affecting the final product’s performance. In fact, by removing the magnification of minor manufacturing defects and human error in assembly, reliability and performance can be significantly improved. And if something goes wrong, well, you can break down the old one and print a new one in about two months, a fraction of the time it would take to build a new Delta IV or Falcon 9. Altogether it’s a very attractive proposition.

相对论的方法是在其巨大的“星门”打印机上尽可能地进行3D打印，将部件总数减少几个数量级-同时，至少根据飞行前的测试，不会影响最终产品的性能。事实上，通过消除组装中的微小制造缺陷和人为错误的放大，可以显著提高可靠性和性能。如果出了问题，你可以在大约两个月内拆解旧的并打印一份新的，这只是建造新的德尔塔4号或猎鹰9号所需时间的一小部分。总而言之，这是一个非常有吸引力的提议。

Of course, creating an entire new manufacturing process for rocketry from scratch is something of a major endeavor, but Relativity has done enough to convince both investors and customers that it is far from a mere science project. The company raised $35 million in 2018 and $140 million in 2019, and is preparing for its first orbital flight in 2021.

当然，从头开始为火箭创造一个全新的制造工艺是一项重大的努力，但相对论已经做了足够的工作，让投资者和客户相信，这远远不仅仅是一个科学项目。该公司在2018年和2019年分别筹集了3500万美元和1.4亿美元，并正在为2021年的首次轨道飞行做准备。

With next year shaping up to be the most important since the company was founded, Ellis will have a great deal to discuss, from the technology they use to the competition they’ll be going up against.

由于明年将是公司成立以来最重要的一年，埃利斯将有很多事情要讨论，从他们使用的技术到他们将面临的竞争。
