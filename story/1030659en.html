火狐：开源的明珠

2020-10-23 04:30:44

This feeling didn’t last. In 2016, I wrote In Memoriam - Mozilla, and in 2017, Firefox is on a slippery slope.Well, I was right, and Firefox (and Mozilla) have only become worse since. Thefuck-up culture is so ingrained in Mozilla in 2020 that it’s hard to see it evergetting better again.

这种感觉并没有持续多久。2016年，我用Memoriam-Mozilla写文章，到了2017年，Firefox陷入了滑坡。嗯，我是对的，从那以后，Firefox(和Mozilla)只会变得更糟。在2020年，这种他妈的文化在Mozilla中根深蒂固，很难再看到它变得更好了。

The most interesting things they’ve been involved in in the past few years areRust and Servo, and they fired most or all of their engineers involved in both.And, yesterday, Mozilla published a statement siding withGoogle on anti-trust, failing to disclose the fact that Google pays to keep theirlights on.

在过去的几年里，他们参与的最有趣的事情是Rust和Servo，他们解雇了这两家公司的大部分或全部工程师。昨天，Mozilla发表了一份站在谷歌一边的反垄断声明，没有披露谷歌付钱让他们继续运营的事实。

Is this the jewel of open source? No, not anymore. Firefox is the embarrassmentof open source, and it’s the only thing standing between Google and anall-encompassing monopoly over the web. Mozilla has divested from Firefox andstarted funnelling what money is left out of their engineering payroll and intotheir executive pockets. The web is dead, and its fetid corpse persists onlyas the layer of goop that Google scrapes between its servers and your screen.Anyone who still believes that Mozilla will save the web is a fool.

这是开源的明珠吗？不，不再是了。火狐是开源的耻辱，也是谷歌与万能的网络垄断之间的唯一障碍。Mozilla已经从Firefox剥离，并开始将他们的工程师工资单上剩下的钱转移到他们的高管口袋里。网络已经死了，它臭气熏天的身体只剩下谷歌在它的服务器和你的屏幕之间刮过的那层粘液，任何仍然相信Mozilla会拯救网络的人都是傻瓜。

As I have stated before, the scope of web browsers has beenincreasing at a reckless pace for years, to the point where it’s literallyimpossible to build a new web browser. We have no recourse left to preserve theweb. This is why I’m throwing my weight behind Gemini, a new protocolwhich is much simpler than the web, and which you can implement yourself in aweekend.

正如我以前说过的，多年来，网络浏览器的范围一直在以不计后果的速度增长，以至于从字面上讲，建造一个新的网络浏览器是不可能的。我们已经没有办法保护网络了。这就是我支持双子座的原因，双子座是一种比网络简单得多的新协议，你可以在周末实现它。

Forget about the web, it’s a lost cause. Let’s move on.

忘了网络吧，这是个失败的事业。我们继续吧。
