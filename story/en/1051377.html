The Sonos Roam is the smallest and least expensive Sonos speaker to date
迄今为止，Sonos Roam是最小，最便宜的Sonos演讲者

2021-03-10 23:53:06

The device will begin shipping on April 20 for $169, with preorders starting on Tuesday. While not inexpensive, that price makes the Roam the most affordable offering in the Sonos lineup.

该器件将于4月20日开始运输169美元，前从周二开始。虽然不便宜，但价格使漫游成为Sonos阵容中最实惠的产品。

The Roam is not the first Bluetooth speaker Sonos has created; that would be the Sonos Move, which the company launched in late 2019. At nearly 10 inches tall and 7 pounds, though, the Move was still best-suited in and around the home. The Roam, by contrast, appears much easier to take on the go. It measures 6.61x2.44x2.36 inches and weighs just under a pound. Its hard front grille, matte finish, and rounded corners resemble those of the Move, but the device comes in an elongated, gently triangular shape. It has an IP67 dust- and water-resistance rating, which means it can be submerged in up to a meter of water for 30 minutes. Raised controls for volume, playback, and mic muting sit on one end of the rod-like design, though the speaker can be used horizontally or vertically. The former orientation naturally angles the speaker upward, directing sound closer to the listener’s ears.

漫游不是第一个蓝牙扬声器SONOS创造的;这将是Sonos Move，该公司于2019年底推出。然而，近10英寸高，7磅，此举仍然最适合在家中。相比之下，漫游似乎更容易进入。它测量6.61x2.44x2.36英寸，重量恰好在一磅下。它的硬形格栅，哑光饰面和圆角类似于移动的圆角，但该器件有一个细长的，轻柔的三角形形状。它具有IP67防尘和防水等级，这意味着它可以浸没在一米的水中30分钟。升高的卷，播放和麦克风静音的控制坐在杆状设计的一端，尽管扬声器可以水平或垂直使用。前定向自然地将扬声器向上角度，将声音引导到更接近听众的耳朵。

Sonos revealed the Roam in a prerecorded presentation for the press, so we haven’t been able to listen to the device ourselves. Sonos is largely selling the Roam on promises of balanced, better-than-expected audio quality for the size, however, and says the device can supply enough volume to “fill a room.” Given the space constraints, we wouldn’t expect the Roam to sound as full or deeply detailed as a larger speaker like the Move or Sonos One. That said, like the Move, the Roam can stream audio over both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth (more specifically, 802.11ac Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 5.0). The greater bandwidth of the former should give it a noticeable quality advantage at home compared to many small speakers that can only stream over the latter. When you’re outside of Wi-Fi range, Sonos says the Roam will automatically re-pair with a remembered mobile device and switch to Bluetooth streaming.

Sonos在新闻界的预先展示中揭示了漫游，所以我们还没有能够自己倾听这个设备。然而，Sonos在很大程度上销售了对尺寸平衡，更好的音频质量的承诺的漫游，但是，该设备可以提供足够的音量“填充房间”。鉴于空间限制，我们不会指望漫游声音充分或深入详细，作为搬家或Sonos之类的更大的扬声器。也就是说，像移动一样，漫游可以通过Wi-Fi和蓝牙（更具体地说，802.11ac Wi-Fi和蓝牙5.0）来流传输音频。与许多只能在后者流流的小型扬声器相比，前者的更大的带宽应该在家里提供一个明显的质量优势。当您在Wi-Fi范围之外时，Sonos表示漫游者将与记住的移动设备自动重新搭配并切换到蓝牙流。

Beyond that, the Roam has a similar feature set to other devices in the Sonos lineup. It still works with the dozens of streaming services supported through the Sonos S2 app. It can be grouped with an existing Sonos system and paired with a second Roam speaker, though it’s not designed to be used as a wireless surround for a Sonos Beam or Arc soundbar. (We wouldn’t recommend doing that with such a small device anyway.) The Roam still supports voice commands via Alexa and the Google Assistant—a three-mic array is built into the device—while iOS users can directly beam audio to the Roam through AirPlay 2. Notably, the Roam makes it possible to stream Bluetooth audio to an existing Sonos system. Because the speaker can connect to Wi-Fi and Bluetooth simultaneously, you could feasibly start Bluetooth content on the Roam, group the Roam with a Sonos system over Wi-Fi, then listen to the same content across all of your speakers. (The Move could only stream over Wi-Fi or Bluetooth, not both at once.) To an extent, this lets current Sonos users get around the lack of Bluetooth audio support on most of the company’s devices—albeit for $169 extra.

除此之外，ROAM将与Sonos阵容中的其他设备设置有类似的功能。它仍然适用于通过Sonos S2应用程序支持的数十种流服务。它可以与现有的SONOS系统分组并与第二个漫游扬声器配对，但它不设计为SONOS梁或电弧声栏的无线环绕。 （无论如何，我们不建议使用这样的小型设备。）漫游仍然通过Alexa和Google助手 - 一个三麦克风数组内置于设备上的语音命令 - 虽然IOS用户可以直接将音频直接束到漫游通过Airplay 2.特别是，漫游可以将蓝牙音频流流将蓝牙音频流传送到现有的SONOS系统。由于扬声器可以同时连接到Wi-Fi和蓝牙，因此可以在ROAM上可行启动蓝牙内容，通过Wi-Fi将Roam与Sonos系统进行分组，然后在所有扬声器中收听相同的内容。 （此举只能通过Wi-Fi或蓝牙，而不是一次。）在某种程度上，这让当前的Sonos用户围绕大多数公司的设备缺乏蓝牙音频支持 - 尽管是169美元的额外费用。

The Roam also supports the Move’s “Automatic Trueplay” feature, which automatically adjusts the speaker’s EQ whenever the device is moved, calibrating its sound on the fly to better suit the acoustics of its location. This now works over Bluetooth in addition to Wi-Fi streaming, an addition Sonos says will be available on the Move as well. A new feature called “Sound Swap,” meanwhile, lets you hold the Roam’s play/pause button to automatically send whatever’s playing to the nearest speaker in a Sonos system—the idea being that you can move from one room to another without having to open up the app to switch speakers. Sonos says the Roam will get 10 hours of battery life per charge, with “up to 10 days” of life through a low-power mode that the device will immediately enter into when it’s not playing anything. (Whether this estimate applies to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth streaming is not clear, though—the former will presumably shorten the total play time.) The speaker can charge over a 15W USB-C port (a cable is included in the box) or any Qi-certified wireless charger. Sonos will sell a wireless charging pad specifically made for the Roam for $49.

ROAM还支持移动的“自动TruePlay”功能，每当设备移动时会自动调整扬声器的EQ，校准其声音，以更好地适合其位置的声学。这现在在蓝牙上工作除了Wi-Fi流外，还可以在移动上提供一个加法Sonos。一个新的功能，称为“声音交换”，同时，让您持有漫游的播放/暂停按钮，以自动发送到索斯系统中最近的扬声器的任何播放 - 这是您可以从一个房间从一个房间移动到另一个房间而无需打开将应用程序转换为切换扬声器。 Sonos说漫游将获得10小时的电池寿命，通过低功耗模式，使用低功耗模式，使用“最多10天”的电池寿命，当时该设备将立即进入它时，它不会播放任何东西。 （这估计是否适用于Wi-Fi或蓝牙流尚不清楚，尽管 - 前者会缩短总播放时间。）扬声器可以通过15W USB-C端口充电（盒子中包含电缆）或任何QI认证的无线充电器。 Sonos将以49美元的价格出售专门为漫游而专门制造的无线充电垫。

The Roam looks like Sonos’ first real step into outside-the-home audio. It advertises a deeper feature set than most portable speakers and could rope new buyers into Sonos’ family of devices, though at $169, it’s still pricey for its size. Whether it will be worth the premium will largely come down to sound quality, and that’s something we’ll have to test for ourselves. But for those who want a more premium travel (or shower) speaker, it could be appealing.

漫游看起来像Sonos的第一个真实的踏入家庭音频。它宣传了比大多数便携式扬声器更深入的功能，可以将新买家绳索进入Sonos'系列设备，虽然达到169美元，但它的大小仍然昂贵。是否值得保费在很大程度上归结为音质，这是我们必须为自己测试的东西。但对于那些想要更多优质旅行（或淋浴）扬声器的人来说，它可能会吸引人。
