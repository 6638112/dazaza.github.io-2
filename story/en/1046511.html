Apple's Tim Cook Criticizes Social Media Practices, Intensifying Facebook Conflict
苹果公司的蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）批评社交媒体行为，加剧了Facebook冲突

2021-01-29 04:18:23

(Reuters) - Apple Inc Chief Executive Officer Tim Cook on Thursday criticized polarization and misinformation on social media, intensifying a conflict between the iPhone maker and Facebook Inc.

路透社）-苹果公司首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）周四批评社交媒体上的两极分化和错误信息，加剧了iPhone制造商与Facebook Inc.之间的冲突。

In remarks delivered at the Computers, Privacy and Data Protection conference, Cook critiqued apps that he argued collect too much personal information and prioritize “conspiracy theories and violent incitement simply because of their high rates of engagement.”

在计算机，隐私和数据保护会议上发表的讲话中，库克批评了一些应用程序，他认为这些应用程序收集了太多的个人信息，并优先考虑“仅出于阴谋论和暴力煽动，因为他们的参与率很高。”

“At a moment of rampant disinformation and conspiracy theories juiced by algorithms, we can no longer turn a blind eye to a theory of technology that says all engagement is good engagement — the longer the better — and all with the goal of collecting as much data as possible,” Cook said.

“在算法充斥着虚假信息和阴谋论的时刻，我们不再对技术理论视而不见，该技术理论说，所有参与都是良好的参与-时间越长越好-所有这些都旨在收集尽可能多的数据库克说。

He did not name Facebook, but the two companies have been in a high-profile dispute. Apple is preparing to implement privacy notifications that many in the digital advertising industry believe will cause some users to decline to allow the use of ad-targeting tools.

他没有透露Facebook的名字，但是两家公司之间一直存在争议。苹果正准备实施隐私通知，许多数字广告行业人士认为这将导致某些用户拒绝使用广告定位工具。

Facebook has accused Apple of anticompetitive conduct because Apple has a growing catalog of paid apps and its own digital advertising business. Facebook Chief Executive Mark Zuckerberg on Wednesday said Apple has “every incentive to use their dominant platform position to interfere with how our apps and other apps work.”

Facebook指责苹果反竞争行为，因为苹果拥有越来越多的付费应用程序目录和自己的数字广告业务。 Facebook首席执行官马克·扎克伯格（Mark Zuckerberg）周三表示，苹果“有各种动机来利用其主导的平台地位来干扰我们的应用程序和其他应用程序的工作方式。”

Cook on Thursday criticized social media practices that he said undermine public trust in vaccines and encourage users to join extremist groups.

库克周四批评社交媒体的做法，他说这破坏了公众对疫苗的信任，并鼓励使用者加入极端主义团体。

“It is long past time to stop pretending that this approach doesn’t come with a cost — of polarization, of lost trust and, yes, of violence,” Cook said. “A social dilemma cannot be allowed to become a social catastrophe.”

库克说：“停止假装这种方法不会付出代价，已经过去了很长时间，两极分化，失去信任以及是暴力。” “不能让社会困境变成一场社会灾难。”

In response to Cook’s remarks, Facebook said in a statement that it believes “Apple is behaving anti-competitively by using their control of the App Store to benefit their bottom line at the expense of app developers and small businesses.”

为了回应库克的言论，Facebook在一份声明中表示，它相信“苹果通过利用对App Store的控制权来反竞争，从而损害了应用开发商和小型企业的利益。”
