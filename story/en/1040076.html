Bitcoin crosses $20K to reach an all-time high after doubling in the past three months
比特币在过去三个月中翻了一番后突破了2万美元，创历史新高

2020-12-16 22:47:47

After testing investor patience for three weeks, bitcoin has finally crossed above $20,000 to reach fresh all-time highs.

在测试了投资者三周的耐心之后，比特币最终突破了20,000美元，创下历史新高。

The number one cryptocurrency by market value jumped over the key psychological threshold during the early U.S. trading hours, surpassing the previous peak price of $19,920 recorded on Dec. 1. At the current price of $20,374, bitcoin is up 5.4% over 24 hours, according to CoinDesk’s Bitcoin Price Index (BPI).

市值排名第一的加密货币在美国早盘交易中就超过了关键的心理门槛，超过了12月1日创下的历史最高价19,920美元。根据目前的价格，即20,374美元，比特币在24小时内上涨了5.4％。到CoinDesk的比特币价格指数（BPI）。

Bitcoin’s value has doubled in the past three months and the institutional-led rally looks sustainable. Meanwhile, other prominent cryptocurrencies such as ethereum, litecoin and XRP are still down 58% to 88% from their respective lifetime highs reached three years ago.

在过去的三个月中，比特币的价值翻了一番，由机构主导的涨势看起来是可持续的。同时，其他著名的加密货币，如以太坊，莱特币和XRP，仍比三年前的历史最高水平下跌了58％至88％。

“When this [rally to near $20,000] happened in 2017, there was a real lack of products for the new converts to experience, whereas today there are endless uses, protocols, services across farming, lending, standard trading, etc,” Soravis Srinawakoon, CEO and co-founder of cross-chain data oracle Band Protocol told CoinDesk. “Therefore, we’d expect to see the new adopters hang around this time.”

Soravis Srinawakoon表示：“当[价格上涨至近20,000美元]发生在2017年时，确实确实缺乏为新的转换者提供体验的产品，而如今，却有无穷的用途，协议，跨农业的服务，贷款，标准交易等，”跨链数据甲骨文Band Protocol的首席执行官和联合创始人告诉CoinDesk。 “因此，我们希望新的采用者能够在这个时候徘徊。”

Breaking above $20,000, which represented a significant hurdle in the mindset of most traders, is entirely new ground for bitcoin and opens the doors for a climb to $100,000 over the course of 2021, according to some.

有人说，突破20,000美元以上，代表了大多数交易者的主要心态，这是比特币的全新领域，并为2021年期间升至100,000美元打开了大门。

That rise would bode well for other crypto sectors as well, including decentralized finance (DeFi), according to DversiFi’s CEO Ross Middleton.

DversiFi的首席执行官罗斯·米德尔顿（Ross Middleton）表示，这种上涨也将对其他加密行业（包括去中心化金融（DeFi））带来好兆头。

“Bitcoin profits are partially recycled back into other smaller tokens later in the bull cycle. In 2017 that was other blockchains such as Ripple, Litecoin and EOS,” said Middleton. “However, this time around, funds are likely to flow into the new crop of DeFi blue-chip projects, built on Ethereum.”

“比特币的利润在牛市周期的后期被部分回收回其他较小的代币。在2017年，还有其他区块链，例如Ripple，Litecoin和EOS。” Middleton说。 “但是，这一次，资金很可能流入以太坊为基础的新的DeFi蓝筹项目。”

DiversiFi’s CEO pointed to decentralized finance (DeF) protocols Aave, Compound, Synthetix, and Yearn Finance as his picks of where the capital could flow.

DiversiFi的首席执行官指出，去中心化金融（DeF）协议Aave，Compound，Synthetix和Yearn Finance是他选择资金流向的选择。

And while bitcoin is now up over 180% on a year-to-date basis, gold has added just over 22%. Bitcoin, often touted as digital gold, has decoupled from the yellow metal this quarter with a more than 80% rally. Meanwhile, gold has suffered a 1% drop, with investors  pulling money out of exchange-traded funds.

尽管从年初至今，比特币的涨幅已超过180％，但黄金的涨幅仅超过22％。经常被吹捧为数字黄金的比特币本季度与黄金分离，反弹超过80％。同时，黄金下跌了1％，投资者将资金从交易所交易基金中撤出。
