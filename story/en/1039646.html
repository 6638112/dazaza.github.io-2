Get your first look at a OnePlus 9 prototype
初探OnePlus 9原型

2020-12-15 02:51:54

2021 is fast approaching, and in the world of Android, that means a new Qualcomm chip and a new round of phone upgrades. OnePlus will be one of the earlier companies out of the gate with the OnePlus 9, a device that leaked pretty extensively at PhoneArena over the weekend.

2021年即将来临，在Android的世界中，这意味着新的高通芯片和新一轮的手机升级。 OnePlus将是最早推出OnePlus 9的公司之一，OnePlus 9周末在PhoneArena大量泄漏。

First up, PhoneArena has hands-on pictures with a prototype of the phone. The front design doesn't look that much different from the current OnePlus 8 Pro, with a hole punch camera and slim bezels all around. (Here's hoping we'll get an under-display camera with the OnePlus 9T later in the year.) PhoneArena says the display is flat—remember, this is the OnePlus 9, not the more expensive OnePlus 9 Pro, which will most likely have a curved screen.

首先，PhoneArena带有手机原型的动手图片。正面设计看起来与当前的OnePlus 8 Pro并没有太大区别，它具有打孔摄像头和纤巧的边框。 （这里希望我们可以在今年晚些时候购买配备OnePlus 9T的显示屏不足的摄像头。）PhoneArena表示显示屏是平坦的-请记住，这是OnePlus 9，而不是更贵的OnePlus 9 Pro ，它很可能具有弯曲的屏幕。

The back has a chunky, rectangular camera block with two big camera lenses and a third smaller lens. Next to the lenses is an LED flash and new "Ultrashot" branding. Also on the back is a strange logo in place of the OnePlus branding; the company apparently uses a fake logo on prototypes, similar to what we've seen on Google devices. PhoneArena also has a slew of specs for the device. The phone has a 120Hz, 6.55-inch 2400×1080 OLED display, the new Snapdragon 888 SoC, 8GB of RAM, 128GB of storage, and a 4,500mAh battery. Keep in mind that this is a prototype, so the specs could change. There will also probably be a OnePlus 9 Pro, which OnLeaks claims has a 6.7-inch display. It's a good bet the resolution is somewhere around 3168×1440, too, like the OnePlus 8 Pro. In the past, the OnePlus Pro model has enjoyed a refresh rate advantage over the base model, so does the OnePlus 9 having a 120Hz display mean the 9 Pro will hit 144Hz?

背面有一个矮胖的矩形摄像头，带有两个大摄像头镜头和一个第三小镜头。镜头旁边是一个LED闪光灯和新的＆lt; Ultrashot＆＃34;品牌推广。背面还有一个奇怪的徽标，代替了OnePlus品牌；该公司显然在原型上使用了伪造的徽标，类似于我们在Google设备上看到的徽标。 PhoneArena还具有许多有关该设备的规格。这款手机具有120Hz，6.55英寸的2400×1080 OLED显示屏，新的Snapdragon 888 SoC，8GB RAM，128GB存储空间和4,500mAh电池。请记住，这是一个原型，因此规格可能会更改。可能还会有一个OnePlus 9 Pro，OnLeaks声称它具有6.7英寸显示屏。可以肯定，分辨率也像OnePlus 8 Pro一样在3168×1440左右。过去，OnePlus Pro型号在基本型号上享有刷新率优势，那么具有120Hz显示屏的OnePlus 9是否意味着9 Pro将达到144Hz？

The rest of the phone has everything about where you would expect. There's an earpiece at the top and a volume rocker and mute switch on the side. The bottom houses a USB-C port, a speaker, and a SIM tray that looks like it has some amount of water resistance thanks to a rubber gasket.

电话的其余部分包含您期望的所有内容。顶部有一个听筒，侧面有一个音量摇杆和静音开关。底部装有一个USB-C端口，一个扬声器和一个SIM托盘，这似乎归功于它的橡胶垫圈，具有一定的防水性。
