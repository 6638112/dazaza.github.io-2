陆军参谋长马克·卡尔顿-史密斯爵士在错误的场地坠落后宣布失踪

2020-11-12 20:15:46

T he head of the British Army was declared “missing in action” after he was dropped in the wrong field in Salisbury, sparking a search.

这位英国陆军司令在索尔兹伯里被扔到了错误的地方，引发了搜救行动，随后他被宣布“在行动中失踪”。

Troops rushed to locate General Sir Mark Carleton-Smith, a former SAS commander, after he was dropped in the wrong field when visiting a 300 square mile training area on Tuesday evening.

周二晚间，前SAS指挥官马克·卡尔顿-史密斯爵士(Sir Mark Carleton-Smith)在参观一个300平方英里的训练区时被扔到了错误的场地，随后军队迅速找到了他。

General Carleton-Smith was brought in on a Wildcat helicopter to boost troops’ morale during a military exercise, reports The Sun.

据《太阳报》报道，卡尔顿-史密斯将军在一次军事演习中被带上一架野猫直升机，以鼓舞部队士气。

He jumped out of the helicopter but soon realised he was lost when there was nobody on the ground to meet him and the helicopter had already flown away.

他从直升机上跳了下来，但很快意识到他迷路了，因为地面上没有人来接他，直升机已经飞走了。
