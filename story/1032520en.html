肖恩·康纳利逝世，享年90岁。被誉为“众多中的佼佼者”詹姆斯·邦兹

2020-11-01 02:12:10

Sean Connery, the Scottish-born actor who rocketed to fame as James Bond and became one of the movies' most popular and enduring international stars, has died. He was 90.

因饰演詹姆斯·邦德一举成名并成为最受欢迎和经久不衰的国际明星之一的苏格兰出生的演员肖恩·康纳利去世。他90岁了。

Connery was an audience favorite for more than 40 years and one of the screen’s most reliable and distinctive leading men. Once pigeonholed as Ian Fleming’s sexy Agent 007, he went on to distinguish himself with a long and mature career in such films as “The Wind and the Lion,” “The Man Who Would Be King” and “Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade.”

康纳利40多年来一直是观众的最爱，也是银幕上最可靠、最有特色的男主角之一。他曾被归类为伊恩·弗莱明(Ian Fleming)饰演的性感特工007，随后他以漫长而成熟的职业生涯脱颖而出，出演了“风与狮子”(The Wind And The Lion)、“谁会成为国王的人”(The Man Who Will Will King)和“印第安纳·琼斯(Indiana Jones)与最后的十字军东征”(Indiana Jones and the L。

His turn as a tough Irish cop in Depression-era Chicago in Brian De Palma’s 1987 “The Untouchables” brought him a supporting actor Oscar.

在布莱恩·德·帕尔马(Brian De Palma)1987年执导的电影“贱民”中，他在大萧条时期的芝加哥饰演一名强硬的爱尔兰警察，为他带来了奥斯卡最佳男配角奖。

Even as he entered his seventh decade, Connery’s star power remained so strong that he was constantly in demand and handsomely remunerated. In 1999 he was selected People magazine’s Sexiest Man of the Century, and from his 007 days to 1999′s “Entrapment” opposite the much-younger Catherine Zeta-Jones, his screen roles more than justified the choice. Age seemed only to intensify his sex appeal and virility.

即使在他步入七十岁的时候，康纳利的明星影响力仍然如此强大，以至于他一直受到追捧，报酬也很丰厚。1999年，他被选为“人物”杂志评选的世纪最性感男人，从007天到1999年与年轻得多的凯瑟琳·泽塔-琼斯(Catherine Zeta-Jones)对手戏的“陷阱”，他的银幕角色充分证明了他的选择是合理的。年龄似乎只会增强他的性感和男子气概。

In his early career, his physique was his main asset as he modeled and picked up acting jobs where he could. In 1956 he landed the role of a battered prizefighter in the BBC production of “Requiem for a Heavyweight.” Good notices brought him to the attention of the entertainment community, and his first film was “No Road Back,” a B crime movie in 1956. He seemed doomed to play the hunk to aging leading ladies, as he did opposite Lana Turner in “Another Time, Another Place,” or roles that stressed his looks such as “Tarzan’s Great Adventure” in 1959.

在他早期的职业生涯中，他的体格是他的主要资产，因为他在可能的地方做模特和从事表演工作。1956年，他在BBC制作的“重量级安魂曲”中饰演一名饱经风霜的职业拳击手。良好的宣传使他引起了娱乐界的注意，他的第一部电影是1956年的B级犯罪电影“无路可走”。他似乎注定要在年迈的女主角面前扮演这个大块头，就像他在“另一个时间，另一个地方”中与拉娜·特纳(Lana Turner)对手戏一样，或者是在1959年的“人猿泰山大冒险”(Terzan‘s Great Advisence)等强调他外表的角色中。

It was easy to dismiss him in films like “Darby O’Gill and the Little People,” but his Count Vronsky to Claire Bloom’s Anna Karenina on the BBC brought him some respect and the kind of attention needed to raise him to the top of the Daily Express' poll of readers asked to suggest the ideal James Bond.

在“达比·奥吉尔和小人物”(Darby O‘Gill and the Little People)等电影中，人们很容易就会对他不屑一顾，但他在BBC上对克莱尔·布鲁姆(Claire Bloom)饰演的安娜·卡列尼娜(Anna Karenina)饰演的沃伦斯基伯爵(Count Vronsky)给他带来了一些尊重和关注，使他在“每日快报”(Daily Express)的读者推荐理想詹姆斯·邦德(James Bond)的民。

After an interview with producers Albert Broccoli and Harry Saltzman, he landed the role without a screen test, according to Saltzman. It was a controversial choice at the time, as Connery was an unknown outside Britain. But 1962′s “Dr. No,” the first of the Bond films, made him an international star.

据萨尔茨曼说，在采访了制片人阿尔伯特·布罗科利和哈里·萨尔茨曼后，他在没有经过试镜的情况下获得了这个角色。这在当时是一个有争议的选择，因为康纳利在英国以外是一个默默无闻的人。但1962年的“诺博士”，第一部邦德电影，使他成为国际明星。

His stature grew with the ever more popular sequels “From Russia With Love,” “Goldfinger” and “Thunderball,” which arrived over the next four years. Bond gave Connery a license to earn; he was paid only $30,000 for “Dr. No” but $400,000 for Alfred Hitchcock’s “Marnie” and was soon getting $750,000 a film.

随着在接下来的四年里陆续上映的更受欢迎的续集“有爱的俄罗斯”、“金手指”和“霹雳球”，他的声望也随之增长。邦德给了康纳利一个赚取许可证的机会；他的“诺博士”只有3万美元的片酬，但阿尔弗雷德·希区柯克的“玛妮”却有40万美元的片酬，并且很快就拿到了75万美元的片酬。

His initial efforts to break out of the Bond mold proved fruitless. Films like “A Fine Madness,” “Shalako” and “The Molly Maguires” were well-intentioned attempts that did nothing to shake Connery as Bond from the public consciousness. After 1967′s “You Only Live Twice,” he left the Bond franchise, but he was coaxed back for 1971′s “Diamonds Are Forever.” He looked old for the role, and the series seemed tired, so with that he left Bond behind -- though money would tempt him back once last time in 1983 for “Never Say Never Again.”

他最初试图打破邦德模式的努力被证明是徒劳的。像“美好的疯狂”、“沙拉科”和“莫莉·马奎尔一家”这样的电影都是善意的尝试，丝毫没有动摇康纳利作为邦德在公众意识中的地位。在1967年的“你只活着两次”之后，他离开了邦德专营权，但他在1971年的“钻石是永远的”中被哄骗回来。对于这个角色，他看起来老了，而且这个系列看起来很累，所以他把邦德抛在了身后--尽管金钱上一次诱惑他回来是在1983年，因为他“再也不会说永远不会再来了”。

He took a major misstep with sci-fier “Zardoz,” and his career seemed to be foundering.

他在科幻小说“扎尔多斯”中犯了一个重大错误，他的事业似乎要崩溃了。

But he bounced back in 1974 with a supporting role in “Murder on the Orient Express” and the following year with “The Wind and the Lion” and “The Man Who Would Be King,” two bold adventures featuring a mature, salt-and-pepper-bearded Connery. “Robin and Marian” (1976) opposite Audrey Hepburn was not a popular success, but critics embraced it, and the film cemented Connery’s reputation as a versatile, serious screen actor.

但1974年，他凭借“东方快车谋杀案”中的配角卷土重来，次年又凭借“风与狮”和“将会成为国王的人”，这两部大胆的冒险影片讲述了成熟的、留着胡椒盐胡子的康纳利的故事。与奥黛丽·赫本(Audrey Hepburn)合作的“罗宾与玛丽安”(1976)并不受欢迎，但评论家们欣然接受，这部电影巩固了康纳利作为一名多才多艺、严肃认真的银幕演员的声誉。

In the late ’70s there were more missteps such as “Meteor,” “A Bridge Too Far” and “Cuba.” But he scored in Terry Gilliam’s “Time Bandits.” It wasn’t until after his last Bond film that his standing as a box office star caught up to his critical reputation, thanks mostly to two huge worldwide hits: “Highlander” (which was not a big hit in the U.S.) and “The Name of the Rose” (which was also much more popular abroad).

七十年代末，“流星”、“一座桥太远”、“古巴”等失误较多。但他在特里·乔里安(Terry Gilliam)的“时光盗贼”中得分。直到他的上一部邦德电影之后，他作为票房明星的地位才赶上了他的评论界声誉，这主要要归功于两部全球大片：“海地人”(Highlander)(在美国并不是很受欢迎)。还有“玫瑰的名字”(在国外也很流行)。

The British Academy gave him a best actor award for “Name of the Rose,” and he received his Oscar for “The Untouchables.” After that he was an instant greenlight any time he agreed to take a role even if some of them, such as “The Presidio,” and “Family Business,” were not so hot.

英国学院给他颁发了最佳男演员奖，“玫瑰的名字”，他凭借“贱民”获得了奥斯卡奖。在那之后，每当他同意出演一个角色时，他都会立即开绿灯，即使其中一些电影，如“总统”和“家族企业”，并不是那么火爆。

Pairing Connery and Harrison Ford as father and son in the third “Indiana Jones” film was an inspired move, and the film grossed almost half a billion dollars worldwide.

康纳利和哈里森·福特在第三部“印第安纳·琼斯”电影中饰演父子，这是一个鼓舞人心的举动，这部电影在全球获得了近5亿美元的票房。

“The Hunt for Red October,” in which Connery played a defecting Soviet sub captain, was also a major hit in 1990.

康纳利在“追捕红色十月”中饰演一名叛逃的苏联潜艇船长，这部电影在1990年也是一部轰动一时的电影。

By the ’90s, he was so popular that his uncredited cameo as King Richard in “Robin Hood: Prince of Thieves” became one of the film’s highlights.

到了90年代，他变得如此受欢迎，以至于他在“罗宾汉：盗贼王子”中饰演理查德国王的不被认可的客串角色成为了这部电影的亮点之一。

He was still a force to contend with in the foreign market, as “Highlander 2,” “Medicine Man,” “Rising Sun,” “Just Cause” and “First Knight” proved over the next several years. His salary was regularly $5 million and above.

在接下来的几年里，他仍然是国外市场上一支需要与之抗衡的力量，正如“高地2”、“药师”、“旭日东升”、“正义事业”和“第一骑士”所证明的那样。他的薪水通常在500万美元以上。

One setback was a bout with throat cancer in the early ’90s, but Connery rebounded with a burst of activity. He starred with Nicolas Cage in 1996 actioner “The Rock,” playing a character that drew more than a little on his history as James Bond. In 2000, he essayed a very different role and received positive reviews for “Finding Forrester,” playing a reclusive writer who bonds with a young black basketball player who’s an aspiring scribe himself.

其中一个挫折是90年代初与喉癌的一场比赛，但康纳利随着活动的爆发而反弹。他与尼古拉斯·凯奇(Nicolas Cage)共同主演了1996年的动作片“岩石”(The Rock)，饰演的这个角色对他饰演的詹姆斯·邦德(James Bond)的历史有很大的吸引力。2000年，他尝试了一个非常不同的角色，并在“寻找福雷斯特”中获得了好评，饰演一位隐居的作家，与一名年轻的黑人篮球运动员建立了联系，而这名年轻的黑人篮球运动员自己也是一名有抱负的书记员。

Nevertheless, he continued with action roles well after his 70th birthday, playing the legendary adventurer Allan Quatermain in 2003′s “The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen.” He announced his retirement in 2005. He voiced a James Bond videogame the same year, and he subsequently did some other voice acting, playing the title character in the animated short “Sir Billi the Vet” and reprising the role in 2010 for “Sir Billi,” which he also exec produced.

然而，在他70岁生日之后，他继续扮演动作角色，在2003年的“非凡绅士联盟”中扮演传奇冒险家艾伦·夸特曼(Allan Quatermain)。他于2005年宣布退休。同年，他为詹姆斯·邦德(James Bond)视频游戏配音，随后又进行了一些其他配音表演，在动画短片“兽医比利爵士”(Sir Billi The Vet)中饰演主人公，并在2010年为他也是执行制片人的“比利爵士”(Sir Billi)重新出演了这个角色。

Thomas Sean Connery was born of Irish ancestry in the slums of Edinburgh. Poverty robbed him of an education, and by his teens he’d left school and was working as an unskilled laborer.

托马斯·肖恩·康纳利出生在爱丁堡贫民窟的爱尔兰血统。贫穷剥夺了他接受教育的机会，他在十几岁的时候离开了学校，成为一名非熟练工人。

At 17 he was drafted into the Royal Navy, but he was discharged three years later due to a serious case of ulcers.

17岁时，他被征召加入皇家海军，但三年后，由于一场严重的溃疡，他被退伍。

He returned to Edinburgh and worked a variety of jobs, including as a lifeguard. He took up bodybuilding and placed third in the 1950 Mr. Universe competition.

他回到爱丁堡，从事了各种工作，包括救生员。他开始健美，并在1950年的环球先生比赛中获得第三名。

After moving to London he learned of an opening in the chorus of “South Pacific.” He took a crash dancing and singing course and, surprisingly, landed the role, in which he stayed for 18 months. He was “hooked,” he said, but spent several years paying his dues in small repertory companies in and around London before anyone else became hooked on him.

搬到伦敦后，他得知“南太平洋”合唱团有一个空缺。他参加了一门快速舞蹈和歌唱课程，令人惊讶的是，他获得了这个角色，并在其中呆了18个月。他说，他是“迷上了”，但在其他人迷上他之前，他花了几年时间在伦敦及其周边的小型剧目剧目公司支付会费。

Connery was devoted to his native Scotland and used his stature to press for the re-establishment of a Scottish parliament. When the body reconvened in 1999, 296 years after its last meeting, Connery was invited to address the first session, where he was greeted with a thunderous ovation. The next year, when he was knighted by Queen Elizabeth -- an honor he called “one of the proudest days of my life” -- he asked that the investiture be performed in Edinburgh.

康纳利献身于他的祖国苏格兰，并利用他的地位推动苏格兰议会的重建。1999年，在上次会议296年后，当该机构重新召开会议时，康纳利被邀请在第一届会议上发表讲话，会上他受到了雷鸣般的掌声。第二年，当他被伊丽莎白女王授予爵士头衔时--他称这是“我一生中最自豪的日子之一”--他要求在爱丁堡举行授勋仪式。

Connery published his autobiography, “Being a Scot,” co-written with Murray Grigor, in 2008. Besides his knighthood and his Academy Award, he received many kudos over his long career, including the Kennedy Center Honors in 1999 and the American Film Institute’s lifetime achievement award in 2006.

2008年，康纳利与默里·格里戈尔(Murray Grigor)合著了他的自传《成为苏格兰人》(Being A Scot)。除了爵士头衔和奥斯卡奖，他在漫长的职业生涯中获得了许多赞誉，包括1999年的肯尼迪中心荣誉和2006年美国电影学会的终身成就奖。

Connery was married to actress Diane Cilento from 1962-73. The couple divorced in 1973 and Cilento died in 2011. Connery is survived by his second wife, painter Micheline Roquebrune, whom he married in 1975; his son by Cilento, actor Jason Connery; and a grandson from Jason’s marriage to actress Mia Sara.

康纳利于1962-73年与女演员黛安·西伦托(Diane Cilento)结婚。这对夫妇于1973年离婚，齐伦托于2011年去世。康纳利身后留下了他的第二任妻子、画家米歇尔·罗奎布卢恩(Micheline Roquebrune)，他们于1975年结婚；他与演员杰森·康纳利(Jason Connery)与西伦托(Cilento)的儿子；以及杰森与女演员米娅·萨拉(Mia Sara)婚姻中的孙子。
