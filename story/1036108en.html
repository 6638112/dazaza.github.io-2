The Oxford Vaccine Is 70% Effective, Cheaper and Easier To Store and Distribute
牛津疫苗的有效性为70％，更便宜，更易于存储和分发

2020-11-24 01:07:13

The coronavirus vaccine developed by the University of Oxford is highly effective at stopping people developing Covid-19 symptoms, a large trial shows.

一项大型试验显示，由牛津大学开发的冠状病毒疫苗在阻止人们出现Covid-19症状方面非常有效。

Interim data suggests 70% protection, but the researchers say the figure may be as high as 90% by tweaking the dose.

临时数据表明有70％的保护，但是研究人员说，通过调整剂量，这一数字可能高达90％。

The results will be seen as a triumph, but come after Pfizer and Moderna vaccines showed 95% protection.

该结果将被视为胜利，但在辉瑞和Moderna疫苗显示出95％的保护作用之后。

However, the Oxford jab is far cheaper, and is easier to store and get to every corner of the world than the other two.

但是，与其他两个相比，牛津戳戳要便宜得多，并且更易于存储和到达世界的每个角落。

So the vaccine will play a significant role in tackling the pandemic, if it is approved for use by regulators.

因此，如果获得监管机构的批准，该疫苗将在应对大流行中发挥重要作用。

"The announcement today takes us another step closer to the time when we can use vaccines to bring an end to the devastation caused by [the virus]," said the vaccine's architect, Prof Sarah Gilbert.

疫苗的建筑师莎拉·吉尔伯特（Sarah Gilbert）教授说：“今天的宣布使我们更加接近了可以使用疫苗来终结由（病毒）造成的破坏的时间。”

The UK government has pre-ordered 100 million doses of the Oxford vaccine, and AstraZeneca says it will make three billion doses for the world next year.

英国政府已经预购了1亿剂牛津疫苗，阿斯利康（AstraZeneca）表示，明年将为全球生产30亿剂疫苗。

Prime Minister Boris Johnson said it was "incredibly exciting news" and that while there were still safety checks to come, "these are fantastic results".

总理鲍里斯·约翰逊（Boris Johnson）说，这是“令人难以置信的令人振奋的消息”，尽管仍在进行安全检查，但“这些结果都很棒”。

The vaccine has been developed in around 10 months, a process that normally takes a decade.

该疫苗已在大约10个月的时间内开发完成，通常需要10年的时间。

There are two results from the trial of more than 20,000 volunteers in the UK and Brazil.

来自英国和巴西20,000多名志愿者的试验有两个结果。

Overall, there were 30 cases of Covid in people who had two doses of the vaccine and 101 cases in people who received a dummy injection. The researchers said it worked out at 70% protection, which is better than the seasonal flu jab.

总体而言，接种两剂疫苗的人中有30例Covid，接受虚拟注射的人中有101例。研究人员说，它的保护作用为70％，比季节性流感疫苗要好。

Prof Andrew Pollard, the trial's lead investigator, said he was "really pleased" with the results as "it means we have a vaccine for the world".

该试验的首席研究员安德鲁·波拉德教授表示，他对结果“感到非常满意”，因为“这意味着我们拥有面向全世界的疫苗”。

However, protection was 90% in an analysis of around 3,000 people on the trial who were given a half-sized first dose and a full-sized second dose.

但是，在该试验的大约3,000人中，对90％的患者进行了90％的保护，他们分别接受了一半大小的第一剂和完全大小的第二剂。

Prof Pollard said the finding was "intriguing" and would mean "we would have a lot more doses to distribute."

波拉德教授表示，这一发现“令人着迷”，这意味着“我们还有很多工作要做。

The analysis also suggested there was a reduction in the number of people being infected without developing symptoms, who are still thought to be able to spread the virus.

分析还表明，没有出现症状的被感染人数有所减少，但仍被认为能够传播这种病毒。

In the UK there are four million doses of the Oxford vaccine ready to go. But nothing can happen until the vaccine has been approved by regulators who will assess the vaccine's safety, effectiveness, and that it is manufactured to high standard. This process will happen in the coming weeks.

在英国，有400万剂牛津疫苗可供使用。但是，直到疫苗获得监管机构的批准之前，什么都不会发生。监管机构将评估疫苗的安全性，有效性以及高标准疫苗的生产。此过程将在未来几周内发生。

It is also unclear who will get this vaccine or the other vaccines the government has ordered.

还不清楚谁将获得这种疫苗或政府订购的其他疫苗。

However, the UK is preparing to press the go button on an unprecedented mass immunisation campaign that dwarfs either the annual flu or childhood vaccination programmes.

然而，英国正准备在史无前例的大规模免疫运动上按下“前进”按钮，这使年度流感或儿童免疫接种计划相形见war。

Care home residents and staff will be first in the queue, followed by healthcare workers and the over-80s. The plan is to then to work down through the age groups.

敬老院的居民和工作人员将排在首位，其次是医护人员和80多岁的老人。接下来的计划是逐步降低年龄段的人群。

It uses a completely different approach to the vaccines from Pfizer and Moderna, which inject part of the virus's genetic code into patients.

它对辉瑞和Moderna的疫苗使用了完全不同的方法，这些方法将部分病毒的遗传密码注入患者体内。

The Oxford vaccine is a genetically modified common cold virus that used to infect chimpanzees.

牛津疫苗是一种转基因的普通感冒病毒，用于感染黑猩猩。

It has been altered to stop it causing an infection in people and to carry the blueprints for part of the coronavirus, known as the spike protein.

已对其进行了更改，以阻止它引起人的感染并携带冠状病毒一部分的蓝图，即冠状蛋白。

Once these blueprints are inside the body they start producing the coronavirus' spike protein, which the immune system recognizes as a threat and tries to squash it.

一旦这些蓝图进入体内，它们便开始产生冠状病毒的突突蛋白，免疫系统将其识别为威胁并试图压制它。

When the immune system comes into contact with the virus for real, it will know what to do.

当免疫系统真正接触到病毒时，它将知道该怎么做。

One idea is the immune system rejects the vaccine, which is built around a common cold virus, if it is given in too big an initial dose.

一个想法是免疫系统会拒绝以普通感冒病毒为基础的疫苗，如果疫苗的初始剂量太大的话。

Or a low then high shot may be a better mimic of a coronavirus infection and lead to a better immune response.

或从低到高拍摄可能是冠状病毒感染的更好模仿，并导致更好的免疫反应。

After Pfizer and Moderna both produced vaccines delivering 95% protection from Covid-19, a figure of 70% is still highly effective, but will be seen by some as relatively disappointing.

辉瑞公司和Moderna公司都生产出对Covid-19疫苗提供95％保护的疫苗后，仍然有70％的疫苗是非常有效的，但是有些人会认为它令人失望。

But this is still a vaccine that can save lives from Covid-19 and is more effective than a seasonal flu jab.

但这仍然是可以拯救Covid-19生命的疫苗，并且比季节性流感疫苗更为有效。

It also has crucial advantages that make it easier to use. It can be stored at fridge temperature, which means it can be distributed to every corner of the world, unlike the Pfizer/BioNTech and Moderna vaccines, which need to be stored at much colder temperatures.

它还具有重要的优势，使其更易于使用。它可以在冰箱温度下存储，这意味着它可以分布在世界的每个角落，这与辉瑞/ BioNTech和Moderna疫苗不同，后者需要在更低的温度下存储。

The Oxford vaccine, at a price of around £3, also costs far less than Pfizer's (around £15) or Moderna's (£25) vaccines.

牛津疫苗的价格约为3英镑，其价格也远低于辉瑞（15英镑左右）或Moderna（25英镑）疫苗。

And the Oxford technology is more established, so the vaccine is easier to mass produce cheaply. AstraZeneca has also made a "no-profit pledge".

而且牛津技术更加成熟，因此这种疫苗更容易廉价地大量生产。阿斯利康还做出了“非盈利承诺”。

A vaccine is what we've spent the year waiting for and what lockdowns have bought time for.

疫苗是我们一年来一直在等待的东西，而锁定正是他们为之付出了时间的。

However, producing enough vaccine and then immunising tens of millions of people in the UK, and billions around the world, is still a gargantuan challenge.

但是，要生产足够的疫苗，然后为英国乃至全球数十亿人提供疫苗，仍然是巨大的挑战。

Life will not return to normal tomorrow, but the situation could improve dramatically as those most at risk are protected.

明天的生活将不会恢复正常，但由于受威胁最大的人受到保护，情况可能会大大改善。

Health Secretary Matt Hancock told BBC Breakfast we would be "something closer to normal" by the summer but "until we can get that vaccine rolled out, we all need to look after each other".

卫生部长马特·汉考克（Matt Hancock）告诉BBC早餐，到夏天我们将“接近正常水平”，但“直到我们能够推出这种疫苗之前，我们都需要互相照顾”。

Prof Peter Horby, from the University of Oxford but not involved in the trial, said: "This is very welcome news, we can clearly see the end of tunnel now. There were no Covid hospitalisations or deaths in people who got the Oxford vaccine."

来自牛津大学但未参与试验的彼得·霍比教授说：“这是非常可喜的消息，我们现在可以清楚地看到隧道的尽头。接种牛津疫苗的人中没有科维德住院或死亡。 ”

Dr Stephen Griffin, from the University of Leeds, said: "This is yet more excellent news and should be considered tremendously exciting. It has great potential to be delivered across the globe, achieving huge public health benefits.

利兹大学的斯蒂芬·格里芬博士说：“这是一个更加令人振奋的新闻，应该被认为是非常令人振奋的。它具有巨大的潜力，可以在全球推广，实现巨大的公共卫生效益。
