It took a year, but Gwyneth Paltrow figured out how to exploit the pandemic
花了一年时间，但格温妮丝·帕特洛（Gwyneth Paltrow）弄清楚了如何利用这种流行病

2021-02-20 02:58:23

Gwyneth Paltrow is at it again. Here's the scene for the perfect grift for our times:

格温妮丝·帕特洛（Gwyneth Paltrow）又来了。这是我们时代最完美的礼物的场景：

Tens of millions of people around the country have fallen ill with COVID-19. Nearly half a million have died. Given chronic testing shortages, millions more have likely been infected and never diagnosed. Some of those infected will develop long-term effects, suffering lingering symptoms for weeks to months—or maybe longer.

全国有成千上万人患COVID-19。近一百万人死亡。鉴于长期的测试短缺，可能有数百万人被感染并且从未被诊断出。其中一些被感染者会产生长期影响，症状持续数周至数月甚至更长。

Sometimes the symptoms appear to be direct extensions of the illness, such as lingering shortness of breath, cough, and/or chest pain. Other times, the symptoms may be more nondescript, such as fatigue and trouble concentrating, aka “brain fog.”

有时，症状似乎是疾病的直接延伸，例如持续的气短，咳嗽和/或胸痛。在其他时候，症状可能更难以描述，例如疲劳和注意力不集中，又称“脑雾”。

So far, it’s unclear how many people will suffer from “long COVID,” though it’s clear it can strike people regardless of whether they had mild, moderate, or severe disease. A project to track COVID symptoms in the UK estimates that  10 percent of those infected have symptoms beyond two weeks. A survey led by the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention found that  35 percent of symptomatic adults hadn’t returned to their prior state of health two to three weeks after testing positive. Looking just at previously healthy people aged 18-to-34 in that survey, CDC researchers found that 20 percent had prolonged symptoms. According to other studies, people who are hospitalized with COVID-19 may have a much higher risk of long COVID.

到目前为止，虽然有轻度，中度或重度疾病的人都能打人，但目前尚不清楚有多少人会遭受“长时间的COVID”痛苦。英国一项追踪COVID症状的项目估计，感染者中有10％的症状超过两周。由美国疾病控制与预防中心领导的一项调查发现，有症状的成年人中，有35％在测试呈阳性反应后两到三周没有恢复到先前的健康状态。疾病预防控制中心研究人员仅在调查中观察了以前健康的18至34岁人群，发现20％的患者症状延长。根据其他研究，住院COVID-19的人长期服用COVID的风险可能更高。

Regardless of the exact percentages, the absolute number of people with long-term effects is bound to be substantial, likely into the millions—just in the US. The virus has spread rampantly, so even a small slice is still a huge number of people. Researchers are bracing for a massive wave of prolonged illnesses in the wake of the pandemic. And they’re working swiftly to understand as much as they can about long COVID—what causes it, who gets it, and most importantly,  how to treat it.

不管确切的百分比如何，具有长期影响的绝对人数注定是巨大的，很可能就是数百万人（仅在美国）。该病毒猖spread蔓延，因此即使是一小部分仍然是大量的人。在大流行之后，研究人员正准备迎接大规模的长期疾病浪潮。他们正在迅速努力，以尽可能多地了解长的COVID-是什么原因导致的，谁获得了它，最重要的是如何治疗它。

But they have their work cut out for them. With some early reports suggesting nondescript symptoms like fatigue, it may be hard to untangle cases of long COVID from other health problems. And with the testing barriers in the US, there’s likely to be many people with long COVID who never tested positive for the virus to begin with, creating the challenge for doctors to retrospectively diagnose them.

但是他们为他们做了工作。早期的一些报告暗示了诸如疲劳之类的难以描述的症状，可能很难将长COVID的病例与其他健康问题区分开。由于存在美国的测试障碍，因此可能有很多人的COVID较长，但从一开始就从未对该病毒进行阳性测试，这给医生进行回顾性诊断带来了挑战。

So basically, there’s this enormous new patient population, which is currently so poorly identified and so poorly understood that it could include almost anyone with the vaguest of symptoms.

因此，基本上，这是一个庞大的新患者群体，目前，这些患者的识别力很差，理解程度也很差，几乎可以包括任何症状最模糊的人。

In a recent blog post, Paltrow revealed that she fell ill with COVID-19 “early on” in the pandemic and is now suffering from long COVID. Specifically, she says she has experienced fatigue and brain fog in aftermath of her infection. That’s very unfortunate and she has our sincere sympathies. Luckily, though, she seems to be managing her prolonged illness extremely well, staying physically active, social over zoom, focused on improving the look of her skin, and involved in creative business projects.

Paltrow在最近的博客文章中透露，她在大流行中“较早”感染了COVID-19，现在正遭受长期的COVID困扰。她说，特别是感染后，她经历了疲劳和脑雾。真不幸，她对我们表示诚挚的同情。幸运的是，她似乎可以很好地控制长期病，保持身体活跃，社交活跃，专注于改善皮肤外观并参与创新的商业项目。

In fact, she’s even found an evidence-free, but certainly effective treatment regimen for her long COVID. And she’d just love to tell you about it in a post loaded with products from her lifestyle e-commerce brand Goop and affiliate links.

实际上，她甚至为长期的COVID找到了无证据但肯定有效的治疗方案。而且，她只想在一篇帖子中告诉您有关该帖子的信息，帖子中包含她的生活方式电子商务品牌Goop的产品和会员链接。

To help develop the regimen, the actor-turned-peddler of exploding vagina candles consulted with a chiropractor (naturally). The chiropractor, Will Cole, set Paltrow on a part-fasting, part-keto dietary plan that’s somehow “plant-based,” except that it includes “fish and a few other meats.” To complement this “freewheeling” diet, Paltrow also gulps a $102 fist-full of vitamins and supplements, which get washed down with a $60 “detoxifying” powder mixed with water.

为了帮助制定治疗方案，爆炸阴道蜡烛的演员转变为小贩，向脊医咨询（自然）。脊医Will Cole将Paltrow设定为部分禁食，部分酮饮食计划，该计划某种程度上是“基于植物的”，但其中包括“鱼和其他一些肉类”。为了补充这种“随心所欲”的饮食，帕特洛还also了102美元一拳，里面装满了维生素和补品，然后用60美元的“排毒”粉和水混合即可冲净。

Even though Paltrow says that she felt “energized, healthier” before even starting the diet, Cole informed her that based on her “labs” she needs a lot of healing, which will take “longer than usual.” Because, of course, who even needs evidence of benefits in the short term when you can just sign yourself up indefinitely for a pricy, unproven, long-term treatment that will pay off… someday… probably?

即使Paltrow说她甚至在开始饮食之前都感到“精力充沛，更健康”，但Cole告诉她，根据她的“实验室”，她需要很多治疗，这将“比平时更长”。因为，当然，谁会在短期内需要利益证明，而您可以无限期地签约一次昂贵的，未经证实的长期治疗，这有一天……可能会有所回报？

In addition to Cole’s protocol, the head Gooper has found some methods of her own for easing her long-COVID symptoms. Despite battling fatigue, she says she has energy and has been exercising in the mornings, including doing a lot of hiking. To keep trekking, she endorses a $220 pair of hiking shoes, a $145 hiking tank top, and a $8,600 gold necklace. [Yes, you read that correctly.] Apparently, Paltrow has a thing for wearing necklaces, often several at once. “But for a hike, just this [$8,600] one is perfect,” she writes. When she’s not scaling mountains to combat her COVID fatigue, she focuses her foggy mind on making certain her skin looks good for her many Zoom meetings. She touts four products just for this part of her long-COVID regimen. They are a $500 infrared sauna blanket, a $98 oil, a $125 serum, and $125 overnight acid peel.

除了Cole的规程外，Gooper的负责人还找到了一些缓解长期COVID症状的方法。她说尽管尽力抗疲劳，但她仍然精力充沛，并且每天早晨都在锻炼，包括进行很多远足。为了保持徒步旅行，她赞同购买220美元的远足鞋，145美元的远足背心和8600美元的金项链。 [是的，您没看错。]显然，帕特洛有一件戴项链的东西，常常一次戴几条。她写道：“但是要加息，这笔8,600美元是完美的。”当她不打算翻山越岭以应对COVID疲劳时，她会把迷雾的注意力集中在确保自己的皮肤适合许多Zoom会议上。她为长期COVID疗程的这一部分宣传了四款产品。它们是500美元的红外线桑拿毯，98美元的油，125美元的血清和125美元的隔夜酸皮。

And after all that expensive exercising, skin care, and video conferences, Paltrow unwinds in a $125 “Goop University” t-shirt and a $32 mocktail served in a $112 glass.

在进行了所有昂贵的锻炼，皮肤护理和视频会议之后，帕特洛穿着一件125美元的“ Goop University” T恤放松身心，并用112美元的玻璃杯装着32美元的无酒精鸡尾酒。
