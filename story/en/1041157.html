SEC filed an emergency action against Silicon Sage Builders for securities fraud
SEC针对Silicon Sage Builders的证券欺诈行为采取了紧急措施

2020-12-22 06:38:20

The Securities and Exchange Commission today announced that it filed an emergency action against California-based real estate development company SiliconSage Builders LLC, aka Silicon Sage Builders, and its sole owner, Sanjeev Acharya, in connection with an alleged $119 million fraudulent offering.

美国证券交易委员会（Securities and Exchange Commission）今天宣布，它对加利福尼亚的房地产开发公司SiliconSage Builders LLC（又名Silicon Sage Builders）及其唯一所有者Sanjeev Acharya提起了紧急诉讼，涉及涉嫌1.19亿美元的欺诈性发行。

According to the SEC's complaint, Silicon Sage Builders and Acharya raised money from approximately 250 retail investors, most of whom were members of the Northern California South Asian community, by falsely describing Silicon Sage Builders' real estate business as profitable and promising investors exorbitant returns. In fact, as the complaint alleges, from 2016 to 2019, all but one of Silicon Sage Builders' projects had significant cost overruns and did not generate enough money to pay investors the promised returns.  Acharya, as alleged in the complaint, misled investors into believing the payments they received were derived from Silicon Sage Builders' profits when, in reality, Silicon Sage Builders and Acharya had used new investor funds to pay earlier investors. The complaint also alleges that Acharya misled investors as to the amount of money the company was attempting to raise and falsely told investors they could redeem their investments despite there being insufficient funds to meet redemption requests.

根据SEC的投诉，Silicon Sage Builders和Acharya通过虚假描述Silicon Sage Builders筹集了大约250个散户投资者的资金，其中大多数是北加利福尼亚南亚社区的成员。房地产业务作为有利可图且有前途的投资者获得高额回报。实际上，正如投诉所指，从2016年到2019年，除了一名Sage Builders＆＃39;这些项目有大量的成本超支，并且没有产生足够的资金来支付投资者承诺的回报。如投诉中所述，阿查里亚误导了投资者，使他们认为他们收到的付款是来自Silicon Sage Builders＆＃39;实际上，当Silicon Sage Builders和Acharya使用新的投资者资金来支付较早的投资者时，就会获利。投诉还称，Acharya公司试图筹集的资金误导了投资者，并错误地告诉投资者尽管资金不足以满足赎回要求，他们仍可以赎回投资。

"As we allege in our complaint, wrongdoers sometimes prey on the trust of members of their communities to raise funds for their fraudulent schemes," said Alka Patel, Associate Regional Director of the SEC's Los Angeles Regional Office. "Affinity frauds are particularly harmful to retail investors, and this case demonstrates the SEC's commitment to pursuing such schemes and protecting retail investors."

The SEC's complaint, filed in the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California, charges Silicon Sage Builders and Acharya with violating the antifraud provisions of the federal securities laws. The SEC seeks preliminary and permanent injunctions, the appointment of a receiver over Silicon Sage Builders, asset freezes, disgorgement with prejudgment interest, and financial penalties against the defendants, as well as an order prohibiting the destruction of documents and an accounting.

美国证券交易委员会（SEC）的投诉已提交给加利福尼亚北区的美国地方法院，指控硅贤者和Acharya违反了联邦证券法的反欺诈规定。 SEC寻求初步禁令和永久禁令，任命Silicon Sage Builders的接管人，资产冻结，带有判决前利息的赃物以及对被告的经济处罚，以及禁止销毁文件和会计的命令。

The SEC's investigation was conducted by Tamar Braz and Christopher Conte in the Los Angeles Regional Office and supervised by Marc Blau and Alka Patel. The SEC's litigation is being led by Michael Sew Hoy and Amy Longo.

SEC的调查是由洛杉矶地区办事处的Tamar Braz和Christopher Conte进行的，并由Marc Blau和Alka Patel监督。 SEC的诉讼由Michael Sew Hoy和Amy Longo牵头。
