Mystery illness puts 450 in hospital in Indian state of Andhra Pradesh
神秘疾病将450人送入印度安得拉邦医院

2020-12-08 12:34:22

One person has died and more than 450 have been admitted to hospital with a mystery illness in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh.

印度安得拉邦有一个人死亡，有450多名因神秘疾病住院的人。

Over the weekend hundreds of people in Eluru, a town in the southern state, collapsed and experienced seizures and nausea, and some reportedly foamed at the mouth.

周末，南部州埃卢鲁（Eluru）的一个小镇有成百上千的人倒下，发生癫痫发作和恶心，据报道有些人在口中起泡沫。

A 45-year-old man admitted to hospital with the symptoms had a heart attack and died.

一名因症状入院的45岁男子心脏病发作死亡。

Dolla Joshi Roy, a district surveillance officer, said 455 people had been taken to hospital and 200 had since been discharged. On Monday the chief minister of Andhra Pradesh, YS Jaganmohan Reddy, visited patients in hospital.

地区监视官员多拉·乔希·罗伊（Dolla Joshi Roy）说，有455人被送往医院，200人已出院。周一，安得拉邦首席部长YS Jaganmohan Reddy参观了医院的病人。

The mystery outbreak has increased the burden on the healthcare system in Andhra Pradesh, one of the states worst affected by coronavirus with 800,000 active cases.

这场神秘的暴发增加了安得拉邦的医疗系统负担，安得拉邦是受冠状病毒影响最严重的州之一，活跃病例达80万。

Specialists from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences have travelled to the state to investigate. Roy said tests were being done to try to determine the cause of the illness, including looking into possible food or water contamination or airborne poisoning. Some of those who collapsed complained of burning eyes.

全印度医学科学研究所的专家已前往该州进行调查。罗伊说，正在进行测试以试图确定疾病的原因，包括调查可能的食物或水污染或空气中毒。有些倒下的人抱怨眼睛灼伤。

Roy said: “Specialist doctors are still working on finding out the cause of this disease and we are waiting for results from the labs. It could be food or waterborne or airborne or some other condition. But we do know this was very localised, the people affected all lived in the same city.”

罗伊说：“专家医生仍在努力寻找这种疾病的病因，我们正在等待实验室的结果。它可能是食物，也可能是水传播的或空气传播的或其他某种情况。但是我们确实知道这是非常本地化的，受影响的人们都生活在同一城市。”

He added: “We tested everyone for Covid-19 and that came back negative, so we know it is not that.”

他补充说：“我们测试了每个人的Covid-19，但结果都是负面的，所以我们知道不是那样。”
