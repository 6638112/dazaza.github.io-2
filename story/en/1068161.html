Windows 11 Drops Skype As a Default App
Windows 11将Skype作为默认应用程序删除

2021-06-25 09:49:14

Today’s Windows 11 news is all about where Microsoft sees computing going over the next few years, but it’s just as much the story of how Skype has flourished and ebbed since its $8.5 billion acquisition a decade ago. Five years ago, Skype was the big name in internet calling and video, and Microsoft made it an “inbox app” for Windows 10 that was included at installation and launched at startup by default.

今天的Windows 11消息全部是关于微软看到未来几年的计算的地方，但它与十年前的85亿美元的收购自25亿美元以来，Skype在Skype蓬勃发展的故事中。五年前，Skype是互联网呼叫和视频中的大名称，Microsoft为Windows 10制作了一个包含在安装中的“收件箱应用程序”，默认情况下在启动时启动。

Now, after a pandemic year that has had more people using their PCs for voice and video than ever before, Skype was nowhere to be seen in the Windows 11 presentation or materials. Instead, Microsoft Teams gets a highlight spot in the new center-aligned taskbar and deep integration into Windows.

现在，经过大流行的一年，在使用他们的PC的大流行年度比以往任何时候都有更多的人，Skype无处可见在Windows 11演示文稿或材料中。相反，Microsoft团队在新中心对齐任务栏中获得了一个突出显示的位置，并将深度集成到Windows中。

“Skype is no longer an inbox app for new devices that run Windows 11.”

“Skype不再是运行Windows 11的新设备的收件箱应用程序。”

The future vision that Microsoft had for Skype everywhere has turned into a reality — but that reality made competitors Zoom and FaceTime into household names instead. Back in June, when Microsoft made Teams available for personal accounts, the company still paid lip service to Skype, saying, “For folks that just want a very purpose-built app, Skype is a great solution, and we support it and encourage it.”

Microsoft到处都是Skype的未来愿景变成了现实 - 但现实情况使竞争对手缩放和关注家庭名称。回到6月份，当微软制作个人账户的团队时，公司仍然支付给Skype的唇部服务，说：“对于只想要一个非常目的地的应用程序的人，Skype是一个很好的解决方案，我们支持它并鼓励它。“

But now, if you want to use Skype, you’re going to have to go find it in the Microsoft Store like any other app. A company spokesperson tells The Verge: “Skype is no longer an inbox app for new devices that run Windows 11. The Skype app is available to download through the Microsoft Store for free.”

但现在，如果你想使用Skype，你将不得不在Microsoft商店里找到它，就像任何其他应用程序一样。一家公司发言人讲述了边缘：“Skype不再是运行Windows 11的新设备的收件箱应用程序。Skype应用程序可免费下载Microsoft Store。”

You can see just how precipitously Skype has fallen over the last year when you consider which other apps Windows 11 will include out of the box; even Windows Terminal will be a pack-in. Skype joins OneNote, Paint 3D, and 3D Viewer as the apps that will no longer come with the OS.

当您考虑其中的其他应用程序11将包括开箱即用时，您可以看到过去一年的Skype如何倒下;即使是Windows终端也将是一个包装。 Skype加入OneNote，Paint 3D和3D查看器作为操作系统不再附带的应用程序。

Even adding one-click “Meet Now” features couldn’t help Skype compete early in the pandemic. The sudden push to go remote saw regular users choose powerful enterprise-grade chat apps, with giants like Microsoft and Google opening their doors to encourage the trend. Now, just like Google, Microsoft has a complicated array of conferencing tools to sort out, and Windows 11’s launch suggests it’ll cut the fat sooner rather than later.

甚至添加一键单击“同时满足”功能无法帮助Skype在大流行早期竞争。突然推动遥远的遥远的人常规用户选择强大的企业级聊天应用程序，巨头像微软一样，谷歌打开他们的门鼓励趋势。现在，就像谷歌一样，Microsoft有一个复杂的一系列会议工具来解决，而Windows 11的发射推出表明它会迅速削减脂肪而不是稍后。
