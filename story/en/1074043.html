Tandem Diabetes Care says one of its apps got FDA clearance to program insulin delivery for the company's pump, the first such app to receive approval
Tandem Diabetes Care表示，该公司的一个应用程序获得了FDA的批准，可以为该公司的泵提供胰岛素，这是第一个获得批准的此类应用程序

2022-02-19 06:34:33

The Food and Drug Administration cleared a smartphone app from Tandem Diabetes Care to program insulin delivery for its t:slim X2 insulin pump, the company announced Wednesday. It’s the first phone app for both iOS and Android to able to deliver insulin, the company said in a statement. Previously, delivery had to be handled through the pump itself.

该公司周三宣布，美国食品和药物管理局（Food and Drug Administration，简称FDA）批准了Tandem Diabetes Care的一款智能手机应用程序，为其t:slim X2胰岛素泵提供胰岛素。该公司在一份声明中表示，这是首款iOS和Android都能提供胰岛素的手机应用。以前，输送必须通过泵本身进行。

With this update, pump users will be able to program or cancel bolus doses of insulin, which are taken at mealtimes and are crucial in keeping blood glucose levels under control. “Giving a meal bolus is now the most common reason a person interacts with their pump, and the ability to do so using a smartphone app offers a convenient and discrete solution,” John Sheridan, president and CEO of Tandem Diabetes Care, said in a statement.

有了这一更新，泵用户将能够编程或取消胰岛素的剂量，这是在进餐时服用的，对控制血糖水平至关重要。Tandem Diabetes Care的总裁兼首席执行官约翰·谢里登（John Sheridan）在一份声明中说：“现在，一个人与泵互动的最常见原因是给药，而使用智能手机应用程序进行互动的能力提供了一个方便而离散的解决方案。”。

The change could be a big improvement for people who prefer not to have pumps out in pubic settings or attach them to undergarments like bras.

对于那些不喜欢在公共场合使用抽水机或将抽水机固定在内衣（如胸罩）上的人来说，这一改变可能是一个巨大的进步。

Tandem Diabetes Care is a leading diabetes device company based in California. Its t:connect mobile app already displays users’ blood sugar trends, history of insulin delivery, and any alerts and alarms. It’s available on iOS and Android in the United States.

Tandem Diabetes Care是加州领先的糖尿病设备公司。它的t:connect移动应用程序已经显示了用户的血糖趋势、胰岛素输送历史以及任何警报和警报。它在美国的iOS和安卓系统上可用。

Tandem said in the statement that it will launch the new bolus delivery update for select users this spring ahead of a wider launch this summer.

Tandem在声明中表示，它将在今春为部分用户推出新的bolus delivery更新，在今夏更广泛的发布之前。
