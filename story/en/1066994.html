Jump to navigation Jump to search Reminiscences of a Stock Operator is a 1923 roman à clef by American author Edwin Lefèvre. It is told in the first person by a character inspired by the life of stock trader Jesse Livermore up to that point. [1]

跳转到导航跳转以搜索股票运营商的回忆是美国作家EdwinLefèvre的1923年罗马à谱号。它在第一个人中被一个人的人物引导，这是股票交易嘉合杰西的生活的灵感到那一点。 [1]

The book remains in print ( ISBN  0471770884). In December 2009, Wiley published an annotated edition in hardcover, ISBN  0-470-48159-5, that bridges the gap between Lefèvre's fictionalized account and the actual people and places referred to in the book. It also includes a foreword by hedge fund manager Paul Tudor Jones. [2]

这本书仍在打印中（ISBN 0471770884）。 2009年12月，Wiley在Holdcover中发布了一个注释版，ISBN 0-470-48159-5，弥补了Lefèvre＆＃39;在图书中提到的实际人和地区之间的差距。它还包括对冲基金经理Paul Tudor Jones的前言。 [2]

1890-1910: Livermore was able to make easy money by taking advantage of the bid–ask spread on inactive stocks with leverage of 100-to-1 at bucket shops.

1890-1910：利沃罗能够利用在不活动股上的出价股票，杠杆队在铲斗商店杠杆率为100至1的展示来易于赚钱。

1910-1920: Livermore was a stock trader on the New York Stock Exchange, where he went bust over and over again because he used too much leverage.

1910-1920：Livermore是纽约证券交易所的股票交易商，在那里他一遍又一遍地爆发，因为他用了太多的杠杆。

1920s: Livermore engaged in market manipulation, charging fees of 25% of the market value of the manipulated stock. This was before the creation of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission in 1934.

20世纪20年代：利弗雷从事市场操纵，收费的25％的被操纵股票的市场价值。这是在1934年创建美国证券交易委员会之前。

In his 2008 book, The Age of Turbulence, Alan Greenspan called the book "a font of investing wisdom" and noted that quotes from the book such as "bulls and bears make money; pigs get slaughtered" are now adages. [3]

在2008年的书中，骚动的年龄，艾伦格林斯潘叫书＆＃34;一个投资智慧的字体。并注意到这本书的报价如＆＃34;公牛和熊赚钱;猪被屠杀＆＃34;现在是谚语。 [3]

A March 2005 article in Fortune listed it among "The Smartest Books We Know" about business. [4]

2005年3月的财富文章在＆＃34中列出了它;我们知道的最聪明的书籍和＃34;关于业务。 [4]

In Market Wizards by Jack D. Schwager, many investors, including Richard Dennis, quoted the book as a major source of material on stock trading.

在Jack D. Schwager的市场巫师中，许多投资者（包括Richard Dennis），称该书称为股票交易的主要材料来源。
