New EU Antitrust Frontier Emerges for Microsoft and Google: Spam Ads
微软和谷歌的欧盟反垄断新前沿：垃圾邮件广告

2022-02-22 04:16:47

A new claim that Microsoft and Google are gaming the online advertising market to the detriment of smaller rivals threatens to set up a new antitrust clash in Europe, according to previously unseen data.

根据之前未公开的数据，一项新的指控称，微软和谷歌正在网络广告市场上玩游戏，损害较小的竞争对手，这可能会在欧洲引发新的反垄断冲突。

The two U.S. giants appear to be flooding smaller search engine partners with spam ads and keeping some of the most valuable ads for themselves, according to data reviewed by POLITICO, in a move that draws parallels with the infamous  €2.4 billion Google Shopping case.

POLITICO审查的数据显示，这两家美国巨头似乎正在向规模较小的搜索引擎合作伙伴大量投放垃圾广告，并为自己保留一些最有价值的广告，此举与声名狼藉的24亿欧元谷歌购物案不谋而合。

While EU competition chief Margrethe Vestager's 2015 offensive against Google's abuses in the search market got the backing of the EU General Court in November, there are some who say that blind spots in the case have allowed for certain violations to continue — illustrated by Swedish price-comparison site PriceRunner's decision earlier this month to sue Google for €2.1 billion in damages.

欧盟竞争主管玛格丽特·维斯塔格'；2015年对谷歌的攻势'；去年11月，谷歌在搜索市场上的侵权行为得到了欧盟总法院的支持，一些人说，该案的盲点让某些侵权行为得以继续——瑞典价格比较网站PriceRunner'；谷歌本月早些时候决定起诉谷歌，要求赔偿21亿欧元的损失。

And now, according to the same data, both Google and its closest rival in the search engine space, Microsoft, are siphoning off so-called spam ads to smaller search engines that use their search results, as well as limiting the quantity of higher-value ads that appear on these partner search engines.

而现在，根据同样的数据，谷歌及其在搜索引擎领域最接近的竞争对手微软正在将所谓的垃圾广告转移到使用其搜索结果的小型搜索引擎上，并限制这些合作伙伴搜索引擎上出现的高价值广告的数量。

Spam ads are regarded as those that have little relevance to the original search, direct users to less reputable online sources and generate little value for the search engine.  In the long term, such ads may turn users away from using alternative search engines like Qwant, Ecosia and DuckDuckGo, and drive them back to the likes of Bing and Google, thereby negatively affecting the smaller players’ bottom lines.

垃圾邮件广告被认为与原始搜索几乎没有关联，将用户引向信誉较差的在线来源，对搜索引擎产生的价值也很小。从长远来看，这类广告可能会让用户放弃使用Qwant、Ecosia和DuckDuckGo等替代搜索引擎，并将他们带回必应（Bing）和谷歌（Google）等搜索引擎，从而对小玩家的底线产生负面影响。

The data, compiled by researchers working in the adtech space who wish to remain anonymous for fear of damaging commercial relations between Microsoft or Google and smaller search engines, indicates that the two internet giants fob off low-value and irrelevant ads to their downstream syndication partners — smaller search engines that rely on both gatekeepers’ huge web page indexes.

这些数据由在adtech空间工作的研究人员汇编而成，他们希望匿名，以免破坏微软或谷歌与较小搜索引擎之间的商业关系，这表明这两家互联网巨头向其下游联合合作伙伴兜售低价值和不相关的广告，这些合作伙伴是依赖两个守门人的巨大网页索引的小型搜索引擎。

Estimates say that Google, which has close to 90 percent share of the search engine market, has up to 600 billion web pages indexed. Bing, for its part, has a much lower share of the search engine market — just under 7 percent — but still indexes between 100 billion and 200 billion web pages, according to the New York Times.

据估计，在搜索引擎市场占有近90%份额的谷歌，有多达6000亿个网页被索引。据《纽约时报》报道，必应在搜索引擎市场的份额要低得多——略低于7%——但其网页索引仍在1000亿到2000亿之间。

Smaller search engines Qwant, Ecosia, DuckDuckGo and Lilo use results from Microsoft Bing. Startpage, another smaller search platform, relies on Google for its results. These smaller alternative engines often have contractual agreements with the larger so-called gatekeepers, meaning that they are supplied not only with search results but also with ads that appear at the top of search results pages.

较小的搜索引擎Qwant、Ecosia、DuckDuckGo和Lilo使用微软Bing的搜索结果。另一个较小的搜索平台Startpage依赖谷歌搜索结果。这些较小的替代引擎通常与较大的所谓把关人签订合同协议，这意味着它们不仅可以获得搜索结果，还可以获得出现在搜索结果页面顶部的广告。

The findings, according to Marc-André Rousseau, a lawyer and partner at German law firm Schalast, would mean that Microsoft and Google ensure that the smaller search engines “monetize worse,” and that the “user experience with their competitors’ search engine services becomes vastly different than with their own services by being cluttered with annoying ads, even though the user is theoretically searching in the same advertising ecosystem.”

根据德国Schalast律师事务所的律师兼合伙人马克·安德烈·卢梭的调查结果，这将意味着微软和谷歌确保较小的搜索引擎“更糟糕地盈利”，并且“由于充斥着恼人的广告，竞争对手的搜索引擎服务的用户体验与他们自己的服务的用户体验大不相同，尽管理论上用户是在同一个广告生态系统中搜索的。”

Similarities with the Google Shopping saga “cannot be ignored at a first glance,” he added, given that a dominant company may again be enacting certain "self-preferencing" practices.

他补充说，与谷歌购物传奇的相似之处“乍一看不可忽视”，因为一家占主导地位的公司可能会再次颁布某些"；自我偏好"；实践。

POLITICO came into possession of the data, which was circulated among select Brussels policymakers, and decided to put the findings to the test. The experiment involved several weeks of conducting searches using a range of European IP addresses and comparing the resulting ads across different platforms.

POLITICO获得了这些数据，这些数据在布鲁塞尔的一些决策者中传播，并决定对这些结果进行检验。该实验涉及数周的时间，使用一系列欧洲IP地址进行搜索，并比较不同平台上的广告结果。

When searching for certain generic keywords, there was a marked difference in quality between ads displayed on alternative platforms and those shown on gatekeeper platforms.

在搜索某些通用关键词时，在替代平台上显示的广告和在看门人平台上显示的广告在质量上存在显著差异。

Specific examples contained in POLITICO’s investigation, highlighted in the accompanying chart, include a query from an Italian IP address for the term "Calcio" (football) on Qwant, which uses the Bing index. The search on Qwant yields four irrelevant and unrelated ads; on Bing, no such ads appear.

POLITICO的调查中包含的具体例子（在随附的图表中突出显示）包括一个来自意大利IP地址的查询，其名称为"；电话会议"；（足球）在Qwant上，它使用Bing索引。Qwant上的搜索结果是四则无关和不相关的广告；在必应上，没有出现这样的广告。

A search for the term "news" from a German IP address using the Ecosia search platform yields several low-quality ad links, while the same search on Bing — which Ecosia uses as its index — contains no such ads.

搜索术语"；新闻"；使用Ecosia搜索平台从一个德国IP地址搜索，会产生几个低质量的广告链接，而在Bing上的同一搜索——Ecosia使用Bing作为其索引——不包含此类广告。

In terms of one of Google’s principal syndication partners — Startpage — a search from a Belgian IP address on the smaller platform for the term "Apple" yields three directly unrelated ads. A search on Google, meanwhile, displays a relevant and likely high-value advertisement, which takes the user directly to the Belgian Apple store.

就谷歌的主要联合合作伙伴之一Startpage而言，这是一个在较小平台上从比利时IP地址搜索术语"；苹果34；同时，谷歌上的搜索会显示一个相关且可能具有高价值的广告，用户可以直接进入比利时苹果商店。

Advertisers are willing to pay more for certain listings associated with queries that more often lead users to make a purchase. Examples in English include search inputs like "car insurance," "buy sofa" or "life insurance." Another dataset in the obtained documents pointed to Microsoft and Google hoarding such higher-value ads for themselves, preventing smaller engines from benefiting monetarily from them.

广告商愿意为某些与查询相关联的列表支付更多费用，这些查询通常会引导用户进行购买。英语中的例子包括"；汽车保险和#34"买沙发"；或"；人寿保险" 获得的文件中的另一个数据集指出，微软和谷歌为自己囤积了此类价值更高的广告，使较小的引擎无法从中获利。

The investigation demonstrated a trend toward showing product ads — advertisements displayed in a banner at the top of a search page after making a query — considerably more in searches on gatekeeper engines, rather than on smaller alternative platforms.

这项调查显示了一种趋势，即在进行查询后在搜索页面顶部的横幅上显示产品广告，在守门人引擎上的搜索中显示的广告要比在较小的替代平台上显示的广告多得多。

One example here could be a search from an Italian IP address for the phrase " comprare divano online" ("buy sofa online"), which displays no product ads or imagery on Ecosia, while the exact same search on Bing yields a more relevant banner of product ads at the top of the page, linking directly to online product pages.

这里的一个例子是从意大利IP地址搜索短语"；康帕雷·迪瓦诺在线"；（"；在线购买沙发"；），它在Ecosia上不显示产品广告或图像，而在Bing上进行完全相同的搜索会在页面顶部生成一个更相关的产品广告横幅，直接链接到在线产品页面。

Again, when replicated across different IP addresses for certain terms that are likely to lead a user to make a purchase, the trend was consistent. More high-value product ads appeared on gatekeeper searches than for queries on their syndication partners' sites.

同样，当在不同的IP地址上复制某些可能导致用户购买的条款时，趋势是一致的。看门人搜索中出现的高价值产品广告多于对其银团合作伙伴的查询#39；地点。

Google, for its part, said that it was the choice of advertisers to decide where they want their advertisements shown and that all ads signed up to the search partner network are eligible to appear both on Google's search results and the results of syndication partners. However, the company “has certain algorithms in place that put controls on the types of ads shown,” a Google spokesperson said.

谷歌则表示，广告商可以选择在哪里显示广告，所有注册搜索合作伙伴网络的广告都有资格在谷歌和#39；的搜索结果和辛迪加合作伙伴的结果。然而，谷歌的一位发言人说，该公司“有特定的算法，可以控制显示的广告类型”。

Alternative search engines, meanwhile, struck a cautious tone when shown the findings of POLITICO’s investigation.

与此同时，另类搜索引擎在展示POLITICO的调查结果时，语气谨慎。

DuckDuckGo said that it is “constantly working to improve the quality of its results.” And the CEO of Qwant, Raphaël Auphan, said that sources for Qwant’s ads come from both the Microsoft Advertising marketplace and the Qwant Advertising ad server, but agreed that “low-value ads negatively impact the user experience on alternative search engines like Qwant.”

DuckDuckGo表示，它正在“不断努力提高结果的质量”Qwant的首席执行官Raphaël Auphan表示，Qwant广告的来源既来自微软广告市场，也来自Qwant广告服务器，但他同意“低价值广告会对Qwant等替代搜索引擎的用户体验产生负面影响”

Qwant has started to study this area in more detail over recent months, Auphan added.

奥芬补充说，近几个月来，Qwant已经开始对这一领域进行更详细的研究。

Startpage confirmed that its ads are generated by the Google Ad Network from advertisers that bid on specific terms. The company also heralded its privacy-preserving approach to search — an argument that is often used to explain why lower-quality ads often appear on alternative search platforms and less so on gatekeeper searches, because users are tracked less across many of the smaller engines.

Startpage确认其广告是由谷歌广告网络从按特定条款出价的广告商那里生成的。该公司还宣布了其保护隐私的搜索方法——这一观点经常被用来解释为什么低质量的广告经常出现在替代搜索平台上，而在守门人搜索中则较少出现，因为在许多较小的引擎中，用户被跟踪的次数较少。

However, this argument fell flat when POLITICO conducted its experiment on terminal equipment that had never previously been used to access Bing or Google services, or alternative search platform services. The results consistently showed that more low-value spam appeared on smaller search engines than on gatekeeper engines. High-value product ads, on the other hand, showed up more often on gatekeeper search platforms.

然而，当POLITICO在终端设备上进行实验时，这一论点没有得到证实。此前，这些终端设备从未被用于访问Bing、谷歌服务或其他搜索平台服务。结果一致显示，在较小的搜索引擎上出现的低价值垃圾邮件比在守门人引擎上出现的要多。另一方面，高价值的产品广告更多地出现在守门人搜索平台上。

The findings invite comparisons with the Google Shopping case. In November 2021, the EU General Court upheld the European Commission’s €2.4 billion fine against Google for favoring its own comparison-shopping services over rivals' in search results.

这些发现让人联想到谷歌购物案例。2021年11月，欧盟总法院维持了欧盟委员会对谷歌的24亿欧元罚款，以支持其与竞争对手39的比较购物服务；在搜索结果中。

Rousseau said that it “seems not unlikely” that the newly discovered adtech practices could also violate EU competition rules, based on the precedent of the Google Shopping judgment.

卢梭表示，根据谷歌购物判决的先例，新发现的adtech行为也可能违反欧盟竞争规则，“似乎并非不可能”。

Thomas Höppner, a lawyer at Hausfeld who provided legal counsel to complainants in the Google Shopping case, said that in order to prove wrongdoing, it would be necessary to identify the incentives for the gatekeepers to siphon off spam ads to alternative search engines and hoard higher-value ads for themselves.

Hausfeld的律师托马斯·霍普纳（Thomas Höppner）曾为谷歌购物案的投诉人提供法律顾问，他说，为了证明存在不当行为，有必要确定把关人将垃圾广告转移到其他搜索引擎并为自己囤积高价值广告的动机。

Both Google and Bing know that the more growth alternative search platforms have “on the back of technology” originally developed by the gatekeepers, “the more the alternative search platforms’ brands will be recognized, the more people will turn there and the stronger they may get and the weaker [Google and Bing] could get," Höppner said.

谷歌和必应都知道，替代搜索平台在最初由守门人开发的“技术基础上”的增长越快，“替代搜索平台的品牌被认可的越多，转向那里的人就越多，他们可能变得越强大，而（谷歌和必应）可能变得越弱，"；霍普纳说。

In terms of abusive self-practicing activities, Rousseau noted the relevance of the EU’s draft Digital Markets Act — which outlines a series of dos and don’ts for some of the world’s largest gatekeeper platforms.

在滥用自我实践活动方面，卢梭指出了欧盟《数字市场法》草案的相关性——该法案概述了世界上一些最大的看门人平台的一系列应做和不应做的事情。

“It should be obvious that the unfair market practice of ad-pricing and quality regulation needs to be clearly addressed by the final DMA,” he said.

“很明显，广告定价和质量监管的不公平市场行为需要在最终的DMA中得到明确解决，”他说。

For the search engine Ecosia, which relies on searches originating from Bing-indexed web pages, European Parliament amendments to the DMA could be a game-changer. The amendments could allow smaller search players to access other ad marketplaces, and therefore potentially have more sway over what ads are shown on their searches.

对于搜索引擎Ecosia来说，欧洲议会对DMA的修正案可能会改变游戏规则。Ecosia依赖的搜索源于Bing索引的网页。修正案可能允许较小的搜索玩家访问其他广告市场，因此可能对搜索中显示的广告有更大的影响力。

“Such marketplaces need similar regulation and to provide access under fair, reasonable and nondiscriminatory terms,” an Ecosia official said.

“此类市场需要类似的监管，并以公平、合理和非歧视性的条件提供准入，”Ecosia的一名官员说。

More broadly, abuses of dominance in the online adtech ecosystem have been consistently hitting the headlines over recent months.

更广泛地说，近几个月来，滥用在线广告技术生态系统中的主导地位一直成为头条新闻。

In the now-infamous antitrust lawsuit led by Texas Attorney General Ken Paxton, Google has been accused of misleading advertisers and publishers by manipulating ad auctions to make more money, using privileged information only it had access to, as well as conspiring with Facebook to the disadvantage of publishers in the adtech world. Google strongly denies the claims.

在德克萨斯州总检察长肯·帕克斯顿（Ken Paxton）领导的这起如今臭名昭著的反垄断诉讼中，谷歌被指控通过操纵广告拍卖赚取更多利润、使用只有自己才能获得的特权信息，以及与Facebook合谋，对广告技术领域的出版商不利，从而误导了广告商和出版商。谷歌坚决否认这些说法。

In Brussels, the Commission last year opened up a formal antitrust investigation into Google's display advertising technology in the adtech supply chain — focusing on concerns that the company hoards user data, to the detriment of third parties.

在布鲁塞尔，欧盟委员会去年对谷歌展开了正式的反垄断调查；s展示adtech供应链中的广告技术——关注公司囤积用户数据，损害第三方利益的担忧。

And just last week, a new complaint was submitted to Commission regulators from a contingent of European publishers, hitting out at Google’s “stranglehold” over press publishers, claiming that the company has monopolized “virtually every step of the ad tech value chain.”

就在上周，一批欧洲出版商向委员会监管机构提交了一份新的投诉，猛烈抨击谷歌对新闻出版商的“扼杀”，声称该公司“几乎垄断了广告技术价值链的每一步”

“Less advertising revenue means press publishers have fewer resources to invest in news content and fulfill their socially important mission of informing the general public and holding those in power accountable,” the complaint, issued by the European Publishers Council, says.

“广告收入减少意味着新闻出版商在新闻内容上的投资和履行其社会重要使命方面的资源减少，这一使命是向公众提供信息，并让当权者承担责任，”欧洲出版商委员会发布的诉状说。
