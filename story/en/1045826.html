Wehe – Check Your ISP for Net Neutrality Violations
Wehe –检查您的ISP是否违反网络中立性

2021-01-23 00:48:05

Have you ever wondered if your Internet service provider is slowing down certain apps relative to others?Unfortunately, your mobile device and carriers currently give you little or no way to tell if this is the case.With Wehe, we give you a way to test if this is happening to the Internet traffic generated by popularapps.

您是否曾经想知道您的Internet服务提供商是否在降低某些应用程序相对于其他应用程序的速度？不幸的是，您的移动设备和运营商目前很少或根本无法告诉您这种情况。借助Wehe，我们为您提供了一种测试方法如果这是流行应用程序生成的Internet流量发生的情况。

Wehe uses your device to exchange Internet traffic recorded from real, popular apps like YouTube andSpotify---effectively making it look as if you are using those apps. As a result, if an Internet serviceprovider (ISP) tries to slow down YouTube, Wehe would see the same behavior. We then send the same app'sInternet traffic, but replacing the content with randomized bytes, which prevents the ISPs from classifying thetraffic as belonging to the app. Our hypothesis is that the randomized traffic will not cause an ISP to conductapplication-specific differentiation (e.g., throttling or blocking), but the original traffic will. We repeatthese tests several times to rule out noise from bad network conditions, and tell you at the end whether yourISP is giving different performance to an app's network traffic.

Wehe使用您的设备交换从真实的流行应用（例如YouTube和Spotify）记录的Internet流量-有效地使其看起来就像您在使用这些应用。结果，如果Internet服务提供商（ISP）试图降低YouTube的速度，Wehe将会看到相同的行为。然后，我们发送相同的应用程序Internet流量，但是用随机字节替换内容，这将阻止ISP将流量分类为属于该应用程序。我们的假设是，随机流量不会导致ISP进行特定于应用程序的区分（例如限制或阻塞），但原始流量会。我们多次重复这些测试以排除不良网络状况带来的噪音，最后告诉您ISP是否为应用程序的网络流量提供不同的性能。

For more details about our methodology, please check out our technical details page,written both for non-technical and technical audiences.

有关我们的方法的更多详细信息，请查看我们的技术详细信息页面，该页面是为非技术和技术受众编写的。

Check out our globalstatistics page to learn about detected net neutrality violations so far,and explore our interactive data visualizations. We also provide specific reports about our findings in the United States and France. Wehe currently works for both Android (4.4+) and iOS. 请访问我们的globalstatistics页面，以了解到目前为止检测到的违反网络中立性的行为，并探索我们的交互式数据可视化。我们还提供有关我们在美国和法国的调查结果的具体报告。 Wehe目前适用于Android（4.4+）和iOS。它是Beta版软件，因此存在一些局限性，感谢您的耐心配合，我们将为您解决问题提供帮助。要安装：如果您不使用Play商店，请单击此处下载APK。
