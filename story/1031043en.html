IMF会议：用于跨境支付的央行数字货币(CBDC)

2020-10-25 10:10:05

This panel of high-level policymakers will discuss the advent of new digital currencies and the future of cross-border payments. The focus will be on macro-financial effects on monetary policy, financial stability, capital flows, financial inclusion, and the international monetary system. To what extent, and how, should policymakers collaborate to maximize social benefits and limit costs and risks?

这一高层政策制定者小组将讨论新数字货币的出现以及跨境支付的未来。重点将放在宏观金融对货币政策、金融稳定、资本流动、金融包容性和国际货币体系的影响上。政策制定者应该在多大程度上以及如何合作，以实现社会效益最大化，并限制成本和风险？

Current shortcomings of cross-border payments, and new technological developments are an invitation to the private sector to offer innovative solutions. Which are some of the emerging solutions, and how might we benefit from them? The panel will also uncover what private entrepreneurs expect from policymakers and regulators. Can innovation be encouraged while preserving a level playing field, and ensuring interoperability of solutions?

目前跨境支付的缺点，以及新的技术发展，都邀请私营部门提供创新的解决方案。哪些是新兴的解决方案，我们将如何从中受益？该小组还将揭示私营企业家对政策制定者和监管机构的期望。能否在保持公平竞争环境和确保解决方案互操作性的同时鼓励创新？

Rapid advancements in technology are inducing policymakers to innovate, and to work with entrepreneurs. What is the role of the public sector versus the private sector in offering new payment solutions and improving cross-border payments? Can the public sector keep up with innovation? What are the types of solutions being investigated by the public sector, and will these entail an evolution or a revolution?

技术的快速进步正在引导政策制定者创新，并与企业家合作。与私营部门相比，公共部门在提供新的支付解决方案和改善跨境支付方面发挥了什么作用？公共部门能跟上创新吗？公共部门正在研究哪些类型的解决方案，这些解决方案将需要进化还是革命？

Moderator: Tommaso Mancini-Griffoli, Division Chief, Payments, Currencies and Infrastructure Division, Monetary and Capital Markets Department, International Monetary Fund

主持人：Tommaso Mancini-Griffoli，国际货币基金组织货币和资本市场部支付、货币和基础设施司司长。

Tara Rice, Head of Secretariat, Committee on Payments and Market Infrastructures, Bank of International Settlements

塔拉·赖斯(Tara Rice)，国际清算银行支付和市场基础设施委员会秘书处负责人。

Jennifer Lucier, Associate Director, Division of Reserve Bank Operations and Payment Systems, Federal Reserve Board

Jennifer Lucier，美联储理事会储备银行业务和支付系统司副主任。

The G20 requested that an international task force propose a roadmap of concrete actions to improve cross-border payments. The task force’s co-chairs will unveil this ambitious roadmap, and kick-off a public consultation with users, innovators, regulators, and policymakers to further refine the plan.

G20要求一个国际特别工作组提出改善跨境支付的具体行动路线图。特别工作组的联合主席将公布这一雄心勃勃的路线图，并启动与用户、创新者、监管机构和政策制定者的公众咨询，以进一步完善该计划。
