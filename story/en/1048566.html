Flexport is hiring product managers to simplify global commerce
Flexport正在招聘产品经理以简化全球贸易

2021-02-20 01:58:40

At the intersection of trade and the internet, Flexport engineering is disrupting the age-old freight forwarding industry. We're looking for innovative, passionate people to build the digital technologies that will make shipping around the world easier and more efficient.

在贸易和互联网的交汇处，Flexport工程正在颠覆古老的货运代理行业。我们正在寻找富有激情和创新精神的人们来构建数字技术，使世界各地的运输变得更加轻松和高效。

We're changing how people think about moving freight. It's exciting to be a pioneer, solving the hard problems.

我们正在改变人们对货运的看法。成为开拓者，解决棘手的问题真是令人兴奋。

Our engineers are taking on huge and unique challenges: to start with, our teams are creating a data model that represents all the complexities of logistics in a way that’s true to reality, yet easy to understand and access. Plus, our clients and the supply chains we support are global. So we don’t roll out our products on a small scale – they’re operating for the businesses we partner with around the world right away.

我们的工程师正面临着巨大而独特的挑战：首先，我们的团队正在创建一种数据模型，以一种既真实又易于理解和访问的方式代表物流的所有复杂性。另外，我们的客户和我们支持的供应链都是全球性的。因此，我们不会大规模推出我们的产品-它们正在为我们与世界各地合作伙伴的业务提供服务。

Flexport is growing very quickly, and so is our product -- we welcome questions and unique ideas. Our engineers are empowered to identify critical projects, strategically prioritize them, and execute them. You decide how to get us to where we want to go.Don’t know much about freight forwarding? Neither do most engineers when they start, but you’ll develop expertise by collaborating and learning with your team. Plus, because so much of our software is for our internal operations teams, you’ll often get to work directly with your users!

Flexport的发展非常迅速，我们的产品也是如此-我们欢迎提出问题和提出独特的想法。我们的工程师有权识别关键项目，从战略上对它们进行优先排序，然后执行它们。您决定如何将我们带到我们想去的地方。对货运代理了解不多？大多数工程师一开始都不做，但是您将通过与团队的协作和学习来发展专业知识。另外，由于我们的大量软件都供内部运营团队使用，因此您经常可以直接与用户合作！
