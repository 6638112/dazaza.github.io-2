Telegram launches a tool to let users import chat history from WhatsApp, Line, or KakaoTalk on Android and iOS
Telegram启动了一个工具，允许用户从Android和iOS上的WhatsApp，Line或KakaoTalk导入聊天记录

2021-01-29 03:28:09

This year has seen a spike in interest for secure messaging apps and one of our favorites, Telegram, is seizing the oppurtunity. With its latest update, Telegram is introducing the ability to import your chat history from other apps including WhatsApp.

今年，人们对安全消息传递应用程序的兴趣激增，我们最喜欢的应用之一Telegram抓住了机遇。随着最新的更新，Telegram引入了从其他应用程序（包括WhatsApp）导入聊天记录的功能。

In a blog post today, Telegram details what’s new in its app. Starting today, that includes the ability to export a conversation and all of its chat history from WhatsApp, Line, or KakaoTalk over to Telegram. Obviously, this is not an automatic process and will require some work from users, but it’s something that’s unique to Telegram and a whole lot better than just losing your message history entirely.

在今天的博客文章中，Telegram详细介绍了其应用中的新功能。从今天开始，这包括将对话及其所有聊天记录从WhatsApp，Line或KakaoTalk导出到Telegram的功能。显然，这不是一个自动过程，需要用户做一些工作，但这是Telegram特有的，并且比完全丢失消息历史记录要好得多。

This new feature is available on both Android and iOS and works pretty well. For WhatsApp, it uses the normal export process, with the ZIP file generated just being imported to Telegram. The process works as follows for WhatsApp.

这项新功能可在Android和iOS上使用，并且效果很好。对于WhatsApp，它使用正常的导出过程，生成的ZIP文件只是导入到Telegram中。 WhatsApp的过程如下。

To move a chat from WhatsApp on iOS, open the  Contact Info or  Group Info page in WhatsApp, tap  Export Chat, then choose Telegram in the Share menu.

要从iOS上的WhatsApp移动聊天，请在WhatsApp中打开``联系信息''或``群组信息''页面，点击``导出聊天''，然后在``共享''菜单中选择``电报''。

On Android, open a WhatsApp chat, tap ⋮  > More > Export Chat, then choose Telegram in the Share menu

在Android上，打开WhatsApp聊天，点击＆＆gt;更多＆gt;导出聊天，然后在共享菜单中选择电报

Bringing chat history over from WhatsApp isn’t the only new feature added to Telegram in this update, though. There are some new privacy enhancements including the ability to delete groups, secret chats, and call history for all parties at once. Voice Chats have also been enhanced with new volume controls and there’s also an improved audio player in this update. Other tweaks include a new reporting option for fake channels or groups, better accessibility with TalkBack and VoiceOver, and new greeting stickers for starting fresh conversations.

不过，从WhatsApp获取聊天记录并不是此更新中添加到Telegram的唯一新功能。有一些新的隐私增强功能，包括立即删除所有参与者的群组，秘密聊天和通话记录的功能。语音聊天功能也有了新的音量控制，并且在此更新中也改进了音频播放器。其他调整包括针对虚假频道或群组的新报告选项，通过TalkBack和VoiceOver的更好的可访问性，以及用于开始新对话的新问候贴。

For Android users, another notable change is better animations across the board. Telegram doesn’t list all of the changes, but they include new animations when “downloading files, playing music, or loading chats.” You can see a few below.

对于Android用户而言，另一个值得注意的变化是全面改善了动画效果。 Telegram并未列出所有更改，但是在“下载文件，播放音乐或进行聊天”时，它们包含了新的动画。您可以在下面看到一些。
