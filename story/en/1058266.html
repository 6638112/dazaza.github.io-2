The First AI-Based Robot Weed Killer
第一个基于AI的机器人杂草杀手

2021-04-14 12:50:05

The Carbon Robotics Autonomous Weeder leverages robotics, artificial intelligence (AI), and laser technology to safely and effectively drive through crop fields to identify, target and eliminate weeds.

碳机器人自主除草者利用机器人，人工智能（AI）和激光技术安全，有效地通过裁剪领域驾驶以识别，目标和消除杂草。

Unlike other weeding technologies, the robots utilize high-power lasers to eradicate weeds through thermal energy, without disturbing the soil. The automated robots allow farmers to use less herbicides and reduce labor to remove unwanted plants while improving the reliability and predictability of costs, crop yield and more.

与其他杂草技术不同，机器人利用高功率激光器通过热能来消除杂草，而不会扰乱土壤。自动机器人允许农民使用更少的除草剂并减少劳动力以消除不需要的工厂，同时提高成本的可靠性和可预测性，作物产量等。

New models for the 2022 growing season are available for pre-order. Carbon Robotics offers a leasing option, which makes the robots more accessible to smaller farms with less acreage. The company will continue to explore new robot models and capabilities to help farmers improve efficiency and reliability across a variety of tasks.

2022年生长季节的新车型可用于预订。碳机器人提供租赁选项，使机器人更少于面积的较小的农场。该公司将继续探索新的机器人模型和能力，以帮助农民提高各种任务的效率和可靠性。

“The potential with these new robots is the highest I’ve seen with any technology as a farmer. I expect the robots to go mainstream because of how effectively they address some of farming’s most critical issues, including the overuse of chemicals, process efficiency and labor. These robots work with a variety of crops, are autonomous and organic. The sky’s the limit.”

“这些新机器人的潜力是我以任何技术作为农民见过的最高版本。由于他们如何有效地解决一些农业最关键的问题，我希望机器人成为主流，包括过度使用化学品，过程效率和劳动力。这些机器人适用于各种作物，是自主和有机的。天空是极限。“

“AI and deep learning technology are creating efficiencies across a variety of industries and we’re excited to apply it to agriculture. Farmers, and others in the global food supply chain, are innovating now more than ever to keep the world fed. Our goal at Carbon Robotics is to create tools that address their most challenging problems, including weed management and elimination.”

“AI和深度学习技术正在跨各种行业创造效率，我们很高兴将其应用于农业。农民和全球粮食供应链中的其他人现在的创新就是以往任何时候都更加创新，以保持世界喂养。我们在碳机器人的目标是创建解决最具挑战性问题的工具，包括杂草管理和消除。“
