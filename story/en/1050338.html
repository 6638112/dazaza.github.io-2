So that you know, here’s what’s going on behind the scenes with Element Home; in practice you’re getting your own fully managed, dedicated server alongside your Element app. It means you have a notably faster service than when using a free public server - you’re saved the hassle of self-hosting and it’s guaranteed to be kept updated with the very latest and greatest Matrix hosting best practices.

众所周知，这是Element Home在幕后发生的事情；实际上，您将在Element应用程序旁边获得自己的完全托管的专用服务器。这意味着与使用免费的公共服务器相比，您拥有显着更快的服务-避免了自托管的麻烦，并且可以保证使用最新，最出色的Matrix托管最佳做法来保持更新。

On top of faster messaging, Element Home comes with five user accounts. It’s the ideal way for a family, or groups of friends, to get a super-quick professionally hosted version of Element. A new type of messenger

除了更快的消息传递速度之外，Element Home还具有五个用户帐户。这是家庭或一群朋友获得超级快速的专业托管版本的Element的理想方法。新型信使

Because Element is decentralised, the app itself (what you see) is separate from the Matrix hosting service behind it (what you don’t see; the movement of messages and where they are stored).

由于Element是分散式的，因此应用程序本身（您看到的内容）与其背后的Matrix托管服务（您看不到的内容；消息的移动以及它们的存储位置）是分开的。

That’s important because it lets users decide where their messages and data are kept. In owning that choice, you also get to own your data and messages rather than having them sucked up into the likes of Facebook Messenger, Signal, Telegram or WhatsApp.

这很重要，因为它使用户可以决定将其消息和数据保存在何处。拥有这种选择，您还可以拥有自己的数据和消息，而不是让它们陷入Facebook Messenger，Signal，Telegram或WhatsApp之类的境地。

Some people choose to host themselves, and that’s great. As it requires some technical knowledge, many others choose to use a free public hosting service such as Matrix.org to get up and running.

有些人选择托管自己，这很棒。由于需要一些技术知识，因此许多其他人选择使用免费的公共托管服务（例如Matrix.org）来启动和运行。

Element Home is a third option; the ability to pay for a fully managed, dedicated server (aka a ‘homeserver’). Being a dedicated server, it devotes itself to just a handful of users; and that’s why it’s so much quicker than a free public server that constantly juggles thousands of users.

Element Home是第三个选择；能够购买完全托管的专用服务器（又称“家庭服务器”）的费用。作为专用服务器，它仅致力于少数用户。这就是为什么它比免费的公共服务器要快得多的原因，因为它会不断欺骗数千名用户。

As you know, Element and Matrix is all about giving you choice. If you want to use the free version, that’s completely fine by us!

如您所知，Element和Matrix就是为您提供选择。如果您想使用免费版本，我们完全可以！
