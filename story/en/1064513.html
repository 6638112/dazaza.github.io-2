Fueling the Future: MQ-25 First to Conduct Unmanned Aerial Tanking
加油未来：MQ-25首先进行无人驾驶空中坦克

2021-06-09 20:51:18

This successful flight demonstrated that the MQ-25 Stingray can fulfill its tanker mission using the Navy’s standard probe-and-drogue aerial refueling method.

这次成功的航班表明，MQ-25 Stingray可以使用海军的标准探针和滴灌空中加油方法来实现其油轮任务。

“This flight lays the foundation for integration into the carrier environment, allowing for greater capability toward manned-unmanned teaming concepts,” said Rear Adm. Brian Corey who oversees the Program Executive Office for Unmanned Aviation and Strike Weapons. “MQ-25 will greatly increase the range and endurance of the future carrier air wing – equipping our aircraft carriers with additional assets well into the future.” During the flight, the receiver Navy F/A-18 Super Hornet approached the Boeing-owned MQ-25 T1 test asset, conducted a formation evaluation, wake survey, drogue tracking and then plugged with the unmanned aircraft. T1 then successfully transferred fuel from its Aerial Refueling Store (ARS) to the F/A-18. “This is our mission, an unmanned aircraft that frees our strike fighters from the tanker role, and provides the Carrier Air Wing with greater range, flexibility and capability,” said Capt. Chad Reed, program manager for the Navy’s Unmanned Carrier Aviation program office (PMA-268). “Seeing the MQ-25 fulfilling its primary tasking today, fueling an F/A-18, is a significant and exciting moment for the Navy and shows concrete progress toward realizing MQ-25’s capabilities for the fleet.” The test flight will provide important early data on airwake interactions, as well as guidance and control, Reed said. The team will analyze that data to determine if any adjustments are needed and make software updates early, with no impact to the program’s test schedule.  Testing with T1 will continue over the next several months to include flight envelope expansion, engine testing, and deck handling demonstrations aboard an aircraft carrier later this year. The MQ-25A Stingray will be the world’s first operational carrier-based unmanned aircraft and provide critical aerial refueling and intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance (ISR) capabilities that greatly expand the global reach, operational flexibility and lethality of the carrier air wing and carrier strike group. The MQ-25 is foundational to the Navy’s Unmanned Campaign Framework and is the first step toward a future fleet augmented by unmanned systems to pace the evolving challenges of the 21st century.

“这一航班将融入承运人环境的基础奠定了综合的基础，允许更大的能力对载人无人驾驶的合作概念，”后期ADM。Brian Corey负责监督无人驾驶和罢工武器的计划执行办公室。 “MQ-25将大大提高未来承运人空气翼的范围和耐力 - 将我们的航空母舰装备额外的资产融入未来。” 在航班期间，接收器海军F / A-18超级大黄蜂接近波音公司的MQ-25 T1测试资产，进行了形成评估，唤醒调查，滴度跟踪，然后插入无人驾驶飞机。然后，T1从其空中加油商店（ARS）成功转移到F / A-18。 “这是我们的使命，一个无人驾驶飞机，从油轮角色释放我们的罢工战士，并提供了更大范围，灵活性和能力的承运人空军，”海军的无人驾驶航空计划办公室的乍得雷德，计划经理（PMA-268）。 “看到今天履行其主要任务的MQ-25，为F / A-18推动，是海军的一个重要而激动人心的时刻，并显示了实现MQ-25为舰队的能力的具体进展。” 测试飞行将提供关于航空互动的重要早期数据，以及芦苇的指导和控制。该团队将分析数据以确定是否需要调整并提前进行软件更新，对该计划的测试计划没有影响。 与T1的测试将在未来几个月内继续，包括飞行信封扩展，发动机测试和今年晚些时候在飞机承运人上搬运演示。 MQ-25A Stingray将成为世界上第一个基于运营的载体无人机飞机，并提供关键的空中加油和智力，监测和侦察（ISR）能力，这大大扩大了运载空气翼和运营机罢工的全球范围，操作灵活性和致命性团体。 MQ-25是海军的无人驾驶框架的基础，是未来舰队增添了无人机的舰队的第一步，以加快21世纪的不断变化的挑战。
