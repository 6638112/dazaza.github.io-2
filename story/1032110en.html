制图员将古董地图转换为显示高程的3D地形图

2020-10-30 03:41:06

In the olden days, every motorist had a clunky road atlas and flimsy paper maps. Now, digitized maps of the world are at your fingertips. Since ancient times, maps have informed users of their geological surroundings. However, they are also a fascinating record of change in how humans understand the Earth. Building off this long tradition of mapmaking, cartographer and ortho-imaging specialist Sean Conway uses modern expertise to enhance vintage maps with accurate elevation data. An ongoing project, the maps are available as stunning prints that defy the eye to appear as three-dimensional reliefs.

在过去，每个驾车者都有一本笨重的道路地图册和脆弱的纸质地图。现在，数字化的世界地图触手可及。自古以来，地图就告诉用户他们的地质环境。然而，它们也是人类理解地球方式变化的迷人记录。地图师和正射成像专家肖恩·康威(Sean Conway)在这一悠久的地图制作传统的基础上，利用现代专业知识，用准确的高程数据增强了古董地图。这是一个正在进行的项目，这些地图可以作为令人惊叹的印刷品出售，这些印刷品看起来像三维浮雕，令人目瞪口呆。

Trained in geography and cartography, Conway started the vintage map relief series as a personal project. For each piece, he begins by choosing a specific vintage map from a wide variety available in public domain high-resolution online scans. Next, elevation data from scientific databases must be layered onto the map—think of it as pressing another map on top; they must line up. In order to add the properly scaled data, Conway must first georeference the older map. To georeference a map is to determine exactly what area it represents and what projection it depicts—meaning the scale, longitude, and latitude lines must match the modern mapped elevations. Some maps have been georeferenced already, including some by the U.S. government. Others are old and technically inaccurate. For such projects, Conway warps the elevation data to maintain the vintage map's form.

康威接受过地理和制图方面的培训，开始了作为个人项目的古董地图浮雕系列。对于每一件作品，他首先从公共领域高分辨率在线扫描提供的种类繁多的地图中选择一张特定的古董地图。下一步，来自科学数据库的高程数据必须分层到地图上--可以把它看作是在上面按下另一张地图；它们必须排成一排。为了添加适当缩放的数据，Conway必须首先对较旧的地图进行地理参考。对地图进行地理参考是为了准确确定它所代表的区域和所描绘的投影-这意味着比例、经度和纬度线必须与现代绘制的高程相匹配。一些地图已经进行了地理参考，包括美国政府的一些地图。其他的是陈旧的，从技术上讲是不准确的。对于这样的项目，康威会扭曲高程数据，以保持古董地图的形式。

After the vintage map is georeferenced, that data is entered into a geographic information system (GIS) software called ArcGIS Pro. In this software, cartographers can crunch data, as well as create 2D and 3D maps and models. Here Conway can process the elevation data within the georeferenced map, in essence aligning the modern data with the old map. For the final touches, the project migrates to Blender, an open-source 3D creative software. The end result is a map that retains all the vintage charm of the original, but with detailed modeling of elevation flawlessly superimposed. While many of the vintage maps were topological to begin, the hyperrealism of the 3D effect brings the geological features to life.

在对古董地图进行地理参考之后，该数据将被输入到名为ArcGIS Pro的地理信息系统(GIS)软件中。在这个软件中，制图员可以处理数据，以及创建2D和3D地图和模型。在这里，Conway可以处理地理参考地图中的高程数据，实质上是将现代数据与旧地图对齐。最后，该项目将迁移到Blender，这是一款开源的3D创意软件。最终的结果是一张地图，它保留了原始地图的所有复古魅力，但将海拔的详细建模完美地叠加在一起。虽然许多古董地图一开始就是拓扑地图，但3D效果的超现实主义使地质特征栩栩如生。

Since starting the project, Conway has completed many vintage relief maps—areas represented include Alaska, Switzerland, the Grand Canyon, Brazil, Crater Lake, and many more.  While Conway is pleased that people think the maps are art, he says, “I'm far happier to hear when people tell me that it helped them to understand or better visualize geologic or geographic properties of the earth…Or how the 3D maps help them understand physical processes. It's really interesting to see the geological color delineation in a map in addition to the 3D effect. You can really see where geological phenomena change simply by upthrust or exposed valleys.”

自开始该项目以来，康威已经完成了许多古董地形图-代表的地区包括阿拉斯加、瑞士、巴西大峡谷、火山口湖等。虽然康威很高兴人们认为这些地图是艺术，但他说：“当人们告诉我它帮助他们理解或更好地可视化地球的地质或地理特性时，我要高兴得多。”他说，“当人们告诉我这帮助他们理解或更好地可视化地球…的地质或地理特性时，我要高兴得多。或者3D地图如何帮助他们理解物理过程。除了3D效果外，还能在地图上看到地质颜色的勾勒，这真的很有趣。你真的可以看到地质现象在哪里发生了变化，仅仅是因为凸起或裸露的山谷。“。

Choosing vintage maps available online, Conway georeferences each map—lining it up with data points which can be entered in a geographic information system (GIS).

Once the vintage map is georeferenced, it can then be combined with detailed elevation data in 3D GIS software.

一旦对古董地图进行了地理参考，就可以在3DGIS软件中将其与详细的高程数据结合起来。

Adding finishing touches in 3D editing software Blender, the final vintage relief map is a stunning depiction of geology and geography.

在3D编辑软件Blender中添加了最后的点睛之笔，最终的复古地形图是对地质和地理的令人惊叹的描绘。

Conway began the project for fun; but due to growing interest in his work, he began offering gorgeous art prints on Muir Way.

康威开始这个项目是为了好玩，但由于人们对他的工作越来越感兴趣，他开始在缪尔路(Muir Way)上提供华丽的艺术版画。

Although his work is beautiful, Conway hopes it will also be educational and that viewers will see topography and geology in a new light.

尽管他的作品很漂亮，但康威希望它也能具有教育性，希望观众能从新的角度看待地形和地质。
