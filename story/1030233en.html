没有规则的榜样：影响者对儿童有害吗？

2020-10-21 10:54:56

Peter Jobes is a tech and business writer having worked with PA and clients like Tesco, and RAC. CMO at Solvid.

彼得·乔伯斯(Peter Jobes)是一名科技和商业作家，曾与PA以及乐购(Tesco)和RAC等客户合作过。索尔维德的首席营销官。

Social media is a digital space that has been expanding exponentially over time. Despite being a 21st Century development, its impact on the world - and business - has been seismic.

社交媒体是一个随着时间的推移呈指数级增长的数字空间。尽管这是21世纪的发展，但它对世界-以及商业-的影响是震撼性的。

While businesses have been busy utilizing social media as a means of advertising products and services, many users still choose to use platforms to simply consume the content of the people they follow.

虽然企业一直忙于利用社交媒体作为宣传产品和服务的手段，但许多用户仍然选择使用平台来简单地消费他们关注的人的内容。

Following public figures on social platforms is a huge trend that’s widely leveraged by brands looking for innovative ways to reach their target audience. This has given rise to the role of the influencer in the lives of millions of individuals.

在社交平台上关注公众人物是一种巨大的趋势，品牌们广泛利用这一趋势寻找创新的方式来接触目标受众。这使得影响者在数百万人的生活中扮演着重要角色。

Increasing numbers of people are choosing to adopt the lifestyle and products used by the social media influencers that they follow without looking deeper into what’s happening behind the scenes. Naturally, the users who are set to be most affected by the actions of influencers are young people who are likely to be more impressionable and easier to persuade.

越来越多的人选择采用他们追随的社交媒体影响力者使用的生活方式和产品，而不是更深入地了解幕后发生的事情。当然，受影响者行为影响最大的用户是年轻人，他们可能更容易受到影响，更容易说服。

As the chart above shows, there’s a high volume of influencers active on Instagram. With such a large amount of users with high numbers of followers, it can be extremely difficult for platforms to regulate the content that users see.

如上图所示，Instagram上活跃着大量有影响力的人。由于拥有如此多的用户和大量的追随者，平台可能很难监管用户看到的内容。

This means that young or more impressionable audiences may be exposed to not only amoral marketing campaigns but also dangerous activities, trends or beauty fads that are being condoned by the accounts that they follow.

这意味着，年轻或更易受影响的受众可能不仅会接触到不道德的营销活动，还会接触到危险的活动、趋势或美容时尚，这些活动、趋势或美容时尚正被他们关注的账户纵容。

With this in mind, it’s worth asking the question, are influencers damaging for children? Here’s a deeper look into the power that influencers hold online:

考虑到这一点，我们有必要问这样一个问题：影响者是否会对儿童造成损害？下面是对有影响力的人在网上拥有的权力的更深层次的了解：

While the chart above may seem like a marketer’s dream, it may come across as somewhat alarming for parents to see how much young people believe in the sincerity of influencers on social media sites like Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and Instagram.

虽然上面的图表可能看起来像是营销人员的梦想，但看到年轻人对Facebook、Twitter、YouTube和Instagram等社交媒体网站上有影响力的人的诚意有多么信任，这可能会让父母感到有些震惊。

When it comes to social media, seemingly real users are actively influencing children and young people in their purchasing habits. Instagram is a prime example of a platform where influential users can portray their life as perfect with flawless images that are capable of being digitally enhanced and filtered.

当谈到社交媒体时，看似真实的用户正在积极影响儿童和年轻人的购买习惯。Instagram是一个典型的平台，在这个平台上，有影响力的用户可以用能够进行数字增强和过滤的完美图片来描绘自己的生活。

The images posted to Instagram by influencers is key in building a high following, and so more users craving higher volumes of followers continually look to showcase themselves as an example of perfection.

有影响力的人在Instagram上发布的照片是建立高追随者的关键，因此更多渴望获得更多追随者的用户不断寻求展示自己，以此作为完美的榜样。

These highly curated caricatures of influencers are exactly what children are most fond of as it makes an unobtainable level of perfection appear achievable to them.

这些精心策划的对有影响力的人的漫画正是孩子们最喜欢的，因为它使他们似乎可以达到无法达到的完美水平。

Insights into the life of influencers have been inspiring to countless children across the world, and the lost pursuit of perfection can have an adverse effect on the mental health of young people.

对有影响力的人的生活的洞察一直鼓舞着世界各地无数的儿童，而迷失对完美的追求可能会对年轻人的心理健康产生不利影响。

In the UK, it was recently revealed that almost one-in-five children want to become a social media influencer themselves when they grow up.

在英国，最近有消息称，近五分之一的儿童希望长大后自己也能成为社交媒体的影响力人物。

Children, and especially teenagers, can develop a sense of identity when they compare themselves with their peers. This natural process can become distorted when they begin to make similar comparisons online with filtered images on social networks.

儿童，尤其是青少年，当他们与同龄人比较时，可以发展出一种认同感。当他们开始在网上与社交网络上过滤后的图片进行类似的比较时，这一自然过程可能会被扭曲。

According to a recent study published in Pediatrics, children who view images of influencers with unhealthy snacks are more likely to consume unhealthy foods as a result.

根据最近发表在《儿科学》上的一项研究，孩子们如果看到那些拿着不健康零食的有影响力的人的照片，就更有可能因此食用不健康的食物。

One of the largest problems with influencer culture stems from how aspirational the perception of an influencer can be. Children are finding motivation in the fame and brand affiliations of influencers online and many are actively attempting to build their own profiles online in a bid to reach similar levels of popularity.

有影响力的人文化最大的问题之一来自于对有影响力的人的看法有多有抱负。孩子们在网上有影响力的人的名气和品牌从属关系中找到了动力，许多人积极尝试在网上建立自己的个人资料，以期达到类似的受欢迎程度。

In attempting, and potentially failing on such a huge stage, the results could be extremely bad for the child’s self-esteem.

在如此巨大的舞台上尝试并有可能失败，结果可能会对孩子的自尊造成极坏的影响。

There are many children today who actively engage in influencer behaviour online. They continually look to create and post content in order to amass more likes and shares on online social platforms. This directly affects the self-esteem of teens and young adults today, who can now measure their success on quantifiable factors like comments and the virality of their posts online.

今天有很多孩子在网上积极参与有影响力的行为。他们不断地寻求创建和发布内容，以便在在线社交平台上积累更多的赞和分享。这直接影响到今天青少年和年轻人的自尊，他们现在可以通过评论和网上帖子的病毒式程度等可量化因素来衡量自己的成功。

This can carry harmful consequences due to the superficiality of the life that they’re trying to achieve in their bid for influencer-levels of popularity.

这可能会带来有害的后果，因为他们试图在争取影响力水平的过程中实现生活的肤浅。

Away from the lost appeal of the influencer in the eyes of impressionable young people, and the dire psychological effects they can have, there’s also a big problem emerging in the type of content that influential users post online.

除了在易受影响的年轻人眼中失去影响力，以及他们可能产生的可怕心理影响之外，有影响力的用户在网上发布的内容类型也出现了一个大问题。

Influencers are largely unregulated in the type of content they produce. Where, in the past, young people found appeal in television personalities and artists, they would only gain insights into their behaviour in the media and various publications that are typically well-regulated by governing bodies to avoid the condoning of the actions they report.

有影响力的人在他们制作的内容类型上很大程度上是不受监管的。过去，当年轻人发现电视名人和艺术家具有吸引力时，他们只会深入了解自己在媒体和各种出版物中的行为，这些媒体和出版物通常受到管理机构的良好监管，以避免他们报道的行为被纵容。

Today, influencers have a direct avenue of communication with young people - meaning they can preach directly to them without any knowledge of the damage they’re causing or regulator on hand to stop their content from being aired.

今天，有影响力的人有了与年轻人沟通的直接途径-这意味着他们可以直接向他们宣讲，而不需要知道他们造成的损害，也不需要手头的监管机构来阻止他们的内容播出。

As a consequence, influencers have run into plenty of controversies regarding promoting gambling to children, engaging in explicit trolling, condoning eating disorders, and encouraging extreme beauty treatments.

因此，在向儿童推广赌博、进行露骨的嬉戏、纵容饮食失调以及鼓励极端美容治疗等方面，有影响力的人遇到了大量的争议。

In a notable recent example of the dangers that social media users with influence hold to impressionable viewers, one TikTok user recorded herself using cotton wool buds to rub hydrogen peroxide on her teeth in order to make them appear whiter.

最近有一个引人注目的例子，说明了有影响力的社交媒体用户对易受影响的观众的危险，一名TikTok用户记录下了自己用棉花花蕾在牙齿上摩擦过氧化氢的过程，以便让牙齿看起来更白。

In a video that amassed over 15 million views, the user even said during filming “if you’re a dentist, don’t tell me this is wrong.” Subsequently, users who viewed the video opted to undertake the hazardous DIY treatment themselves and post the results.

在一段点击量超过1500万次的视频中，这位用户甚至在拍摄期间说：“如果你是牙医，不要告诉我这是错的。”随后，观看视频的用户选择自己进行危险的DIY治疗，并发布结果。

The video has caused concerned dentists to speak out. Talking to Health.com, Chris Strandberg said: “Prolonged bleaching with these high concentrations of hydrogen peroxide, especially when used multiple days in a row, can lead to highly irritated gums and sensitive teeth.

这段视频引起了忧心忡忡的牙医们的直言不讳。克里斯·斯特兰伯格在接受Health.com采访时说：“长时间使用这些高浓度的过氧化氢漂白，特别是连续使用多天，可能会导致牙龈高度刺激和牙齿敏感。”

Gum irritation can get severe if more bleaching is done when the gums are already irritated. Tooth sensitivity is usually temporary (24 hours or so), but significant bleaching can increase tooth sensitivity permanently with long term use.”

如果在牙龈已经受到刺激的情况下进行更多的漂白，牙龈刺激会变得严重。牙齿敏感通常是暂时的(24小时左右)，但如果长期使用，显著的漂白可以永久性地增加牙齿的敏感度。“。

The fact that social media users are advocating extreme and illegal teeth whitening measures rather than the use of approved products illustrates the eagerness that young people have in attempting to replicate the levels of perfection that are prevalent on Instagram and other networks.

社交媒体用户倡导极端和非法的牙齿美白措施，而不是使用获得批准的产品，这一事实表明，年轻人渴望复制Instagram和其他网络上流行的完美水平。

With the recent rise of TikTok adding to an already burgeoning list of networks it’s fair to say that social media and the influencers that have immersed themselves online aren’t going anywhere soon.

随着TikTok最近的崛起，加上已经在蓬勃发展的网络名单，公平地说，社交媒体和沉浸在网上的有影响力的人不会很快消失。

While this may not seem like a good thing in light of this article, it’s important to state that many influencers create safe spaces for young people to build a sense of community and friendship with like-minded followers.

虽然从这篇文章来看，这似乎不是一件好事，但重要的是要指出，许多有影响力的人为年轻人创造了安全的空间，以与志同道合的追随者建立社区感和友谊。

If you’re concerned about the negative effects that influencers may have on your children in terms of affiliate marketing and encouraging unhealthy behavior, it’s worth starting up a dialogue with your kids.

如果你担心有影响力的人可能会在联盟营销和鼓励不健康行为方面对你的孩子产生负面影响，那么与你的孩子开始对话是值得的。

By asking them simple questions about what’s really happening when an influencer markets a product, it can help create a strong impact.

通过问他们一些简单的问题，比如当有影响力的人推销产品时到底发生了什么，它可以帮助创造强大的影响力。

Ask your children what the influencer is trying to achieve by showcasing a product on their account - was it attempting to make audiences laugh?

问问你的孩子，有影响力的人试图通过为他们展示一款产品来实现什么目标-这是为了让观众发笑吗？

Question whether the influencer has presented all the information necessary to accompany the product, and ask how the adverts make your children feel.

询问有影响力的人是否提供了与产品配套的所有必要信息，并询问广告给您的孩子带来了什么感觉。

The same approach should be taken for hazardous beauty tips and the condoning of unhealthy behaviour. With a vast and expanding online social ecosystem, it’s difficult to uphold any form of regulation, but by helping young people to become more aware of the content they’re seeing, it’s possible to make life on social networks a much safer place for the future.

对有害的美容小贴士和纵容不健康行为也应该采取同样的方法。由于在线社交生态系统庞大且不断扩大，很难维持任何形式的监管，但通过帮助年轻人更多地意识到他们所看到的内容，有可能让社交网络上的生活对未来来说更加安全。
