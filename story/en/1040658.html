Microsoft: 2021 Is the Year Passwords Die
微软：2021年是年度密码死亡

2020-12-19 07:46:27

Microsoft has been a proponent of passwordless technology for quite some time, saying that it wants traditional and unsafe passwords to die. To that end, it has invested in various solutions over the past few years such as Windows Hello, Microsoft Authenticator, FIDO2 security keys, and a palm vein authentication system, among other things.

微软在很长一段时间以来一直是无密码技术的拥护者，它表示希望传统和不安全的密码消失。为此，它在过去几年中投资了各种解决方案，例如Windows Hello，Microsoft Authenticator，FIDO2安全密钥和掌纹认证系统。

Now, the company has highlighted the strides it made to kill off passwords in 2020, and has stated that it plans to make them a thing of the past for all its customers in 2021.

现在，该公司强调了其在2020年消除密码方面所取得的进步，并表示计划在2021年使所有客户的密码成为历史。

Microsoft noted that almost 80% of all cyberattacks target passwords, and one in 250 corporate accounts get compromised each month due to this. That said, the company is making an effort to transition people to passwordless solutions. In November 2019, 100 million people were using Microsoft's passwordless sign-in. This number grew to 150 million by May 2020, which goes to show how millions of people are ready to ditch passwords due to the inconvenience of remembering them, coupled with how insecure they can be.

微软指出，几乎所有网络攻击中的80％都以密码为目标，因此，每月有250个企业帐户中的一个受到威胁。也就是说，该公司正在努力使人们过渡到无密码解决方案。在2019年11月，有1亿人正在使用Microsoft的无密码登录。到2020年5月，这个数字已增加到1.5亿，这表明由于记住密码的不便以及密码的不安全性，数百万人准备抛弃密码。

Throughout 2020, Microsoft participated in various conferences to share its vision of a passwordless future and a Zero Trust environment. It also unveiled a preview of Azure Active Directory support for FIDO2 security keys in hybrid environments, as well as a new passwordless wizard via the Microsoft 365 Admin Center. The firm engaged with multiple security partners in the Microsoft Intelligent Security Association (MISA) to drive passwordless solutions too.

整个2020年，Microsoft参加了各种会议，以分享其对无密码未来​​和零信任环境的愿景。它还展示了混合环境中对FIDO2安全密钥的Azure Active Directory支持的预览，以及通过Microsoft 365管理中心提供的新的无密码向导。该公司还与Microsoft智能安全协会（MISA）中的多个安全合作伙伴合作，以推动无密码解决方案的发展。

Passwordless usage in Azure Active Directory is up by more than 50 percent for Windows Hello for Business, passwordless phone sign-in with Microsoft Authenticator, and FIDO2 security keys.

对于Windows Hello企业版，使用Microsoft Authenticator进行的无密码电话登录以及FIDO2安全密钥，Azure Active Directory中的无密码使用率提高了50％以上。

More than 150 million total passwordless users across Azure Active Directory and Microsoft consumer accounts.

跨Azure Active Directory和Microsoft消费者帐户的总数超过1.5亿无密码用户。

The number of consumers using Windows Hello to sign in to Windows 10 devices instead of a password grew to 84.7 percent from 69.4 percent in 2019.

使用Windows Hello而非密码登录Windows 10设备的消费者数量从2019年的69.4％增加到了84.7％。

2021 is the year in which Microsoft plans to make passwords obsolete for all its customers. It is currently developing new APIs and a UX for managing FIDO2 security keys, and is also aiming to deliver a "converged registration portal", where customers can manage their passwordless credentials. While it hopes that 2021 marks a return to the "old normal", the company has emphasized that going passwordless will make online lives significantly easier.

微软计划在2021年为所有客户淘汰密码。 它目前正在开发新的API和用于管理FIDO2安全密钥的UX，并且还旨在提供一个“融合的注册门户”，客户可以在其中管理其无密码凭证。 该公司希望2021年标志着重返“古老的常态”，但该公司强调，无密码将大大简化在线生活。
