iOS 14.4 and iPadOS 14.4 hit supported devices today
iOS 14.4和iPadOS 14.4现已成为受支持的设备

2021-01-28 23:12:57

Today, Apple began rolling out iOS 14.4 and iPadOS 14.4 to supported iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch devices. The company also pushed watchOS 7.3 to Apple Watches and tvOS 14.4 to Apple TVs.

今天，Apple开始将iOS 14.4和iPadOS 14.4推出到受支持的iPhone，iPad和iPod Touch设备。该公司还将watchOS 7.3推向Apple Watch，将tvOS 14.4推向Apple TV。

iOS 14.4/iPadOS 14.4 is a somewhat small feature update. New additions in the release notes include the ability to read smaller QR codes with the iPhone cameras, notifications to tell users "when the camera of your iPhone is unable to be verified as a new, genuine Apple camera," and a number of bug fixes. Option to classify Bluetooth device type in Settings for correct identification of headphones for audio notifications

iOS 14.4 / iPadOS 14.4是一个很小的功能更新。发行说明中的​​新增功能包括能够使用iPhone相机读取较小的QR码，在用户无法将iPhone的相机验证为新的正版Apple相机时通知用户的通知。以及许多错误修复。可在“设置”中对蓝牙设备类型进行分类的选项，以正确识别音频通知的耳机

Notifications for when the camera on your iPhone is unable to be verified as a new, genuine Apple camera in iPhone 12, iPhone 12 mini, iPhone 12 Pro and iPhone 12 Pro Max

当iPhone上的相机无法被验证为iPhone 12，iPhone 12 mini，iPhone 12 Pro和iPhone 12 Pro Max中新的正版Apple相机时的通知

Audio stories from the News app in CarPlay may not resume after being paused for spoken directions or Siri

在暂停语音提示或Siri后，CarPlay中“新闻”应用中的音频故事可能无法恢复

Enabling Switch Control in Accessibility may prevent phone calls from being answered from the Lock Screen

在辅助功能中启用开关控制可能会阻止从锁定屏幕接听电话

Besides features listed in the release notes for iOS 14.4, some users and developers say they have found other changes. According to MacRumors, one of those is "Find My Items," which enables third parties to use Apple's Find My APIs for their devices. There is also an update to handoff with the HomePod mini, whereby the iPhone now uses its U1 ultra-wideband chip to detect proximity to the HomePod mini to enable an improved experience.

除了iOS 14.4发行说明中列出的功能外，一些用户和开发人员还说他们发现了其他更改。据MacRumors称，其中之一是＆＃34;查找我的物品，＆＃34;这使第三方可以在其设备上使用Apple的“查找我的API”。 HomePod mini的交接也有所更新，iPhone现在使用其U1超宽带芯片来检测与HomePod mini的接近度，从而改善体验。

As noted, Apple also introduce new updates for watchOS and tvOS. The tvOS update is composed purely of bug fixes and optimizations, and Apple did not provide detailed release notes. This is not uncommon with tvOS updates, which tend to get a big push with each major annual release but relatively little in between.

如前所述，Apple还推出了watchOS和tvOS的新更新。 tvOS更新完全由错误修复和优化组成，Apple没有提供详细的发行说明。这在tvOS更新中并不少见，tvOS更新通常在每个主要的年度版本中都有很大的推动力，但是在这两个版本之间相对较少。

Unity watch face—inspired by the colors of the Pan-African Flag, the shapes change throughout the day as you move creating a face that's unique to you

Unity表盘-受泛非旗帜的颜色启发，随着您移动，形状全天都会变化，从而创建出您独有的表盘

Time to Walk for Apple Fitness+ subscribers—an audio experience in the Workout app where guests share inspiring stories as you walk

Apple Fitness +订户的步行时间-锻炼应用程序中的音频体验，客人可以边走边分享鼓舞人心的故事

ECG app on Apple Watch Series 4 or later in Japan, Mayotte, Philippines, and Thailand

日本，马约特岛，菲律宾和泰国的Apple Watch Series 4或更高版本上的ECG应用程序

Fix for an issue where Control Center and Notification Center may be unresponsive when Zoom is enabled

修复了启用缩放功能时控制中心和通知中心可能无响应的问题

Apple announced the Time to Walk feature earlier this week. This Fitness+ feature guides users through walking workouts while they listen to talks by celebrities such as Dolly Parton and Draymond Green. The Unity watch face's release coincides with the start of Black History Month (February). Today, Apple also announced a number of other Black History Month-related initiatives, including a special Apple Watch design, new editorial collections across various content services, and more.

苹果本周早些时候宣布了“步行时间”功能。 Fitness +的这项功能可指导用户在听Dolly Parton和Draymond Green等名人讲话时进行步行锻炼。 Unity表盘的发布恰逢“黑色历史月”（2月）的开始。今天，苹果还宣布了其他与“黑人历史月”相关的其他举措，包括特殊的Apple Watch设计，跨各种内容服务的新社论集等等。

All of these software updates should be rolling out to users of supported devices globally today.

所有这些软件更新都应该在今天向全球范围内的受支持设备用户推出。
