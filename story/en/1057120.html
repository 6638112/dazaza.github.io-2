Union seeking to represent Amazon workers in AL says 3,215 ballots were cast, 55% of those eligible; hundreds of ballots are being contested, mostly by Amazon
寻求代表Al的亚马逊工人表示，施放3,215名选票，55％符合条件; 数百名选票是争议的，主要是由亚马逊

2021-04-09 03:02:50

Vote counting is set to start in high-profile unionization bid at Amazon facility in Alabama

投票计数被设定为在阿拉巴马州亚马逊设施的高调联盟竞标中开始

The union seeking to represent workers at an Amazon warehouse in Alabama said late Wednesday that there were 3,215 ballots cast — or about 55 percent of the roughly 5,800 workers who were eligible to vote.

在阿拉巴马州的亚马逊仓库中寻求代表工人的工会表示，周三晚些时候举行了3,215名选票 - 或约5,800名有资格投票的工人的55％。

The ballots will be counted by hand starting Thursday afternoon in the National Labor Relations Board’s office in Birmingham, according to the Retail Wholesale and Department Store Union. Hundreds of ballots are being contested, mostly by Amazon, the union said.

据零售批发和百货商店联盟称，选票将由在伯明翰国家劳工关系委员会办公室周四下午开始。联盟表示，数百名选票是争议的，主要是亚马逊。

The vote counting will be shown on a videoconference call to a small number of outsiders, including journalists, in addition to representatives from the union and the company.

除了来自工会和公司的代表之外，投票计数将在视频会议上呼吁呼吁少数外人，包括记者。

Union elections are typically held in person, but the labor board determined that the election should be conducted by mail to minimize risks during the pandemic. The ballots were sent to workers in early February and were due at the agency before March 30. Since then, Amazon and the union have had a chance to challenge whether particular workers were eligible to vote.

联盟选举通常是亲自举行的，但劳工委员会确定应通过邮件进行选举，以尽量减少流行期间的风险。选票于2月初向工人发送给工人，在3月30日之前在原子机到期。从那时起，亚马逊和联盟有机会挑战特殊工人是否有资格投票。

When the public counting is done, the agency will announce the formal results if the margin of victory for one side is greater than the number of contested ballots.

如果公共计数完成后，如果一方的胜利率大于有争议的选票的数量，该机构将宣布正式成果。

If the margin is narrower, then it could take two to three weeks for the N.L.R.B. to hold a hearing to sort through the contested ballots and take evidence from both sides on whether they should be counted.

如果利润率较窄，那么N.L.R.B可能需要两到三周。持有听证会通过有争议的选票来排序，并从双方获取证据是否应该被计算。
