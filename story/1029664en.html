Baby Boomers, people born between 1946 and 1964, currently control ten times more wealth than millennials, whose 72 million workers make them the most represented group in the workforce. Although it's not unusual for younger age groups to have less money than their elders, the average Baby Boomer working in 1989 during their early 30s had quadruple the wealth of what millennials have at that same age today. Millennials, who are the median age of 32 today, control just 4.6 percent of U.S. wealth, far behind the 21 percent Boomers had at about that same age a generation before. These working young adults will need to quadruple their bank accounts in the next two years in order to match the financial share controlled by their parents during the late 1980s, when the median Boomer was 34 years old.

如今年龄中值为32岁的千禧一代，仅控制了美国4.6%的财富，远远低于上一代人在大约相同年龄时21%的财富。这些工作的年轻人将需要在未来两年内将他们的银行账户翻两番，才能达到他们父母在20世纪80年代末控制的财务份额，当时婴儿潮一代的中位数是34岁。

The glaring disparities highlighted in the report reveal the coronavirus pandemic has worsened many of the already wide financial divides. The 50 richest people in the U.S. have earned $340 billion in just the first half of 2020, pushing the total worth of these several dozen people to nearly $2 trillion in combined wealth.

彭博社(Bloomberg)周四发布的一份代际财富报告参考了美联储(Federal Reserve)的数据，显示美国最富有的50个人的财富相当于美国一半的人，即1.65亿人的财富。报告中强调的明显差距表明，冠状病毒大流行加剧了许多本已很大的财务鸿沟。仅在2020年上半年，美国最富有的50人就赚了3400亿美元，将这几十人的总财富推高至近2万亿美元。

One major factor for the wealth disparity is the overwhelming majority of Americans don't benefit from rising stock prices. The richest one percent of Americans own more than 50 percent of the equity in corporations and mutual fund shares, Bloomberg notes. Upper-middle class Americans have seen a 10 percent drop in their equity interest in companies, as the richest 10 percent of U.S. adults now hold 88 percent of all stock shares.

造成贫富差距的一个主要因素是，绝大多数美国人没有从不断上涨的股票价格中受益。彭博社指出，最富有的1%的美国人拥有超过50%的公司股权和共同基金股份。美国中上阶层在公司的股权下降了10%，因为最富有的10%的美国成年人现在持有所有股票的88%。

Millennials have been unable to catch up with Generation X members, adults born between 1965 and 1979, who surpassed the wealth of the older Silent Generation (ages 75 to 95) in 2018. Americans in Generation X have doubled their collective net worth since mid-2016.

千禧一代一直无法赶上X世代成员，即出生于1965年至1979年之间的成年人，后者在2018年超过了年长的沉默一代(75岁至95岁)的财富。自2016年年中以来，X世代的美国人的总净资产翻了一番。
