I've never been a big fan of holidays, nor do I think Thanksgiving should be a federal holiday, given its origins. However, in this time of long-overdue criticism in the technology press, I would like to appropriate "The Spirit of Thanksgiving" and focus on the technology companies who's business ethics align toward the benefit of us users, and who's products are well-priced (or free!) and *really great*, for lack of a better term. For reference, here's my own list so far: 1. Bandcamp 2. OBS! 3. Typora 4. GIMP! 5. NeoCities So! I would love it if you'd reflect on software/services you use (or have used in the past) in this form. Thank you!
