Atlassian将终止服务器产品的销售，数据中心价格将提高

2020-10-21 04:19:50

We recently announced we're making important changes to our self-managed offerings as we sharpen our focus as a cloud-first company. Learn what’s changing, when, and what you need to do next.

我们最近宣布，我们正在对自我管理的产品进行重大改革，以突出我们作为一家云优先公司的重点。了解什么在改变，何时改变，以及下一步需要做什么。

In order to focus on delivering a world-class cloud experience, we’re ending the sale and support of our server licenses.

为了专注于提供世界级的云体验，我们将终止服务器许可证的销售和支持。

On February 2, 2021 PT, we will stop selling new licenses for our server products and cease new feature development in our server product line.

太平洋时间2021年2月2日，我们将停止销售服务器产品的新许可证，并停止服务器产品线的新功能开发。

If you have an existing server license, you can continue to renew and receive maintenance and support for your licenses until February 2, 2024 PT.

如果您有现有的服务器许可证，您可以继续续订许可证，并获得许可证的维护和支持，直到太平洋时间2024年2月2日。

On February 2, 2021 PT, we will increase the price of maintenance for existing server licenses to ensure the continued security and maintenance of our server platform.

2021年2月2日，太平洋时间，我们将提高现有服务器许可证的维护价格，以更好地确保我们服务器平台的持续安全和维护。

Atlassian Data Center will remain a key element of our strategy. In addition to continued investment in new features, we’ll look to make using our cloud and Data Center products together easier for you and your users.

亚特兰大数据中心仍将是我们战略的关键要素。除了对新功能的持续投资外，我们还将寻求让您和您的用户更轻松地共同使用我们的云和数据中心产品。

To meet your need for better collaboration and increased insights, we’re including some of our most powerful Atlassian apps and new features with Data Center subscriptions.

为了满足您对更好的协作和更深入的见解的需求，我们正在通过数据中心订阅包括一些功能最强大的Atlassian应用程序和新功能。

Get higher SLAs, faster triage, and faster resolutions for your critical applications with Priority Support for Data Center customers.

优先为数据中心客户提供支持，为您的关键应用程序获得更高的SLA、更快的分类和更快的解决方案。

Recover quickly from outages and avoid losing build results with cold-standby based high availability and seamless restarts in Bamboo Data Center (coming soon).

通过竹子数据中心(即将推出)中基于冷备的高可用性和无缝重启，快速从停机中恢复并避免丢失构建结果。

On February 2, 2021 PT, we will increase the price of Data Center subscriptions to reflect the value of these investments.

太平洋时间2021年2月2日，我们将提高数据中心订用价格，以反映这些投资的价值。

Not sure where to begin? Our two-minute quiz will help point you down the right path.

不确定从哪里开始？我们两分钟的测验将帮助你走上正确的道路。

Find the end-to-end tools, resources, and support you need to plan your migration to cloud.

找到您计划迁移到云所需的端到端工具、资源和支持。

See what’s shipped recently and what’s on the way so you can plan for your move.

查看最近发货的物品和正在运送的物品，这样您就可以为搬家做计划了。

Anticipate having over 1,000 users in cloud? You may be eligible for an additional discount on your cloud subscription.

预计云中的用户将超过1,000人？您可能有资格获得云订阅的额外折扣。

Our trusted network of Partners can help you plan for, move to, and grow with Atlassian’s cloud products.

我们值得信赖的合作伙伴网络可以帮助您规划、迁移到Atlassian的云产品，并与之共同发展。

To help you continue the work that matters most, discounted cloud pricing is available for non-profit and academic organizations.

为了帮助您继续最重要的工作，我们为非营利性组织和学术组织提供折扣云定价。

Teamwork is key to any Open Source initiative, which is why Atlassian’s cloud software is free for Open Source projects.

团队合作是任何开源计划的关键，这就是为什么Atlassian的云软件对开源项目是免费的。

We’re committed to meeting your unique needs, and are working on FedRAMP certification for our cloud products.

我们致力于满足您的独特需求，并正在为我们的云产品进行FedRAMP认证。

Find the end-to-end tools, resources, and support you need to plan your migration to Data Center.

查找您计划迁移到数据中心所需的端到端工具、资源和支持。

Wondering what’s in the works? Get the latest on what’s planned for our Data Center offerings.

想知道有什么要做的吗？了解我们的数据中心产品计划的最新情况。

To make your transition easier, server customers who choose to move to Data Center are eligible for a loyalty discount.

为了简化您的过渡，选择迁移到数据中心的服务器客户有资格享受忠诚度折扣。

To help you continue the work that matters most, we offer discounted Data Center pricing for academic organizations.

为了帮助您继续最重要的工作，我们为学术组织提供数据中心折扣定价。

We’re committed to helping you get things done faster, easier, and with greater security.

我们致力于帮助您更快、更轻松、更安全地完成工作。
