What CentOS alternative distro should you choose?
您应该选择哪种CentOS替代发行版？

2021-02-18 07:20:27

In January 2014, CentOS announced it was officially joining with Red Hat while staying independent from Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) under a new CentOS governing board.

2014年1月，CentOS宣布正式加入Red Hat，同时在新的CentOS理事会的领导下独立于Red Hat Enterprise Linux（RHEL）。

However, in December 2020, Red Hat requisitioned that CentOS terminate the development of CentOS 8. They also announced that support would be shifting to a CentOS alternative rolling-release Linux distribution, midstream between the upstream development in Fedora and the downstream development for RHEL.

但是，在2020年12月，红帽要求CentOS终止CentOS 8的开发。他们还宣布将支持转移到CentOS替代滚动发行Linux发行版，介于Fedora的上游开发和RHEL的下游开发之间。

CentOS was and still is a very commonly used production-ready Linux server distribution. With Red Hat’s move, CentOS departs from being an extremely stable, community-supported, and RHEL compatible distro to an early rolling-release distro for RHEL, namely CentOS Stream.

CentOS曾经是并且仍然是非常常用的可用于生产环境的Linux服务器发行版。随着Red Hat的发展，CentOS已从一个非常稳定，受社区支持且与RHEL兼容的发行版转变为RHEL的早期滚动发行发行版，即CentOS Stream。

As such, many users, including myself, are looking for CentOS alternatives. A CentOS alternative should offer long-term support, and of equal importance, it should be very stable.

因此，包括我在内的许多用户都在寻找CentOS的替代品。 CentOS替代品应该提供长期支持，并且同等重要的是，它应该非常稳定。

In response to the termination of CentOS as we know it, CentOS founder, Gregory Kurtzer, created a successor to the original mission of CentOS, then shortly afterward, CloudLinux also announced a new CentOS clone. Moreover, there are additional alternatives.

为了响应我们所知的CentOS终止，CentOS创始人Gregory Kurtzer创建了CentOS原始任务的继任者，然后不久，CloudLinux也宣布了新的CentOS克隆。此外，还有其他选择。

As such, let’s have a look at viable CentOS alternatives and begin our research, discussion, and decision-making, before the December 31st, 2021 deadline.

因此，让我们看一下可行的CentOS替代方案，并在2021年12月31日截止日期之前开始我们的研究，讨论和决策。

Rocky Linux is a fork of CentOS led by the founder of the CentOS project. It is a community-enterprise operating system designed to be 100% compatible with RHEL. Rocky Linux aims to function as a downstream build as CentOS had done previously, building releases after they have been added by the upstream vendor, not before. The current ETA for the initial release is March 31, 2021.

Rocky Linux是CentOS项目的创始人领导的CentOS的分支。它是一个社区企业操作系统，旨在与RHEL 100％兼容。 Rocky Linux的目标是像CentOS以前那样充当下游构建，在上游供应商添加发行版本之后（而不是之前）构建发行版本。初始版本的当前ETA是2021年3月31日。

Pros: Led by the founder of the CentOS project, has the most buzz and community support thus far.

优点：在CentOS项目的创始人的带领下，迄今为止获得最多的关注和社区支持。

Cons: ETA of March 31st, 2021, for an initial release, will this new distro catch on and last another ten years?

缺点：ETA将于2021年3月31日首次发布，这个新发行版本会继续流行并持续十年吗？

AlmaLinux is the latest RHEL clone created by CloudLinux. This distro is another free OS, with $1M in annual sponsorship from CloudLinux, which has promised to “support future RHEL releases by updating AlmaLinux.” The expected release date is Q1, 2021.

AlmaLinux是CloudLinux创建的最新RHEL克隆。该发行版是另一个免费的操作系统，由CloudLinux每年赞助100万美元，后者已承诺“通过更新AlmaLinux支持将来的RHEL版本。”预计发布日期为2021年第一季度。

Pros: Led by Cloudlinux, whose distro is widely used by shared hosting providers. The popular cPanel & WHM supports installation on CentOS, RHEL, and CloudLinux OS. So if you run cPanel on CentOS, this may be of interest to you. cPanel recently made this statement: “We have made the commitment to support the RHEL fork by CloudLinux.”

优点：由Cloudlinux领导，其发行版被共享主机提供商广泛使用。流行的cPanel＆amp; WHM支持在CentOS，RHEL和CloudLinux OS上进行安装。因此，如果您在CentOS上运行cPanel，您可能会对此感兴趣。 cPanel最近发表了这样的声明：“我们已经承诺通过CloudLinux支持RHEL fork。”

Cons: Not pioneers of free software. Hopefully, they will keep the promise on their homepage – “Always free, always open source.” – and it does not devolve into a bunch of paid addons.

缺点：不是自由软件的先驱。希望他们能在其首页上兑现承诺-“始终免费，始终开源”。 –并且不会分解为一堆付费插件。

Oracle’s Linux distro is available free of charge, and they’ve created a conversion script to switch your CentOS Linux server over to Oracle Linux.  Oracle Linux claims to be 100% compatible with existing applications on your CentOS server. This Linux distribution also closely follows the RHEL release cycle using the Unbreakable Enterprise Kernel (UEK) produced by Oracle.

Oracle的Linux发行版是免费提供的，他们已经创建了一个转换脚本，可以将您的CentOS Linux服务器切换到Oracle Linux。 Oracle Linux声称与CentOS服务器上的现有应用程序100％兼容。此Linux发行版还使用Oracle生产的Unbreakable Enterprise Kernel（UEK）密切关注RHEL发行周期。

Pros: It’s from Oracle, stable. Features a custom-built and rigorously-tested Linux kernel. Tight integration with Oracle’s hardware and software products, including most database applications.

优点：来自Oracle，稳定。具有定制的，经过严格测试的Linux内核。与Oracle的硬件和软件产品（包括大多数数据库应用程序）紧密集成。

Cons: It’s from Oracle. Many in the industry still feel that Oracle has been infamous for bait and switch tactics. (For example, Oracle’s handling of Java.

缺点：来自Oracle。业内许多人仍然认为Oracle因诱饵和转换策略而臭名昭著。 （例如，Oracle处理Java。

Springdale Linux is another Linux distribution based on Red Hat Enterprise Linux. This distribution is developed by the Institute for Advanced Study and Princeton University. This project was started long before ​CentOS, or other projects were available. The latest version is 8.3, which was launched on 20 November 2020.

Springdale Linux是另一个基于Red Hat Enterprise Linux的Linux发行版。该分布由高级研究学院和普林斯顿大学开发。这个项目在CentOS或其他项目可用之前就已经开始了。最新版本是8.3，于2020年11月20日发布。

ClearOS Linux is based on CentOS and Red Hat Enterprise Linux. Designed for use in small and medium enterprises. This is an open-source Linux distribution available for free to the community.

ClearOS Linux基于CentOS和Red Hat Enterprise Linux。设计用于中小型企业。这是一个免费的Linux发行版，社区可以免费使用。

Amazon Linux 2 (AL2) is also a CentOS-based RHEL clone. The Amazon Linux 2 AMI is provided at no additional charge to Amazon EC2 users. As such, if you have CentOS servers on AWS, Amazon Linux 2 is worth checking out. AL2 offers long-term support and multiple virtual machine images for KVM, Microsoft Hyper-V, Oracle VM VirtualBox, and VMware ESXi virtualization platforms.

Amazon Linux 2（AL2）也是基于CentOS的RHEL克隆。向Amazon EC2用户免费提供了Amazon Linux 2 AMI。因此，如果您在AWS上拥有CentOS服务器，那么值得一试的是Amazon Linux 2。 AL2为KVM，Microsoft Hyper-V，Oracle VM VirtualBox和VMware ESXi虚拟化平台提供长期支持和多个虚拟机映像。

The official alternative to CentOS 8 is CentOS Stream. Read: Converting from CentOS Linux to CentOS Stream.

CentOS 8的正式替代品是CentOS Stream。阅读：从CentOS Linux转换为CentOS Stream。

Suppose you are tired of worrying about long-term support. Then it may be time to pull out your wallet. Red Hat Enterprise Linux (abbreviated to RHEL) is a Linux distribution developed by Red Hat for the commercial market. Red Hat Enterprise Linux 8 (code name: Ootpa) is based on Fedora 28, upstream Linux kernel 4.18, GCC 8.2, Glibc 2.28, systemd 239, GNOME 3.28, and the switch to Wayland. RHEL 8 was officially released on May 7, 2019.

假设您担心担心长期支持。然后可能是时候掏出你的钱包了。红帽企业版Linux（缩写为RHEL）是红帽为商业市场开发的Linux发行版。红帽企业版Linux 8（代号：Ootpa）基于Fedora 28，上游Linux内核4.18，GCC 8.2，Glibc 2.28，systemd 239，GNOME 3.28以及向Wayland的转换。 RHEL 8已于2019年5月7日正式发布。

Pros: The crème de la crème of Linux Enterprise distros. The source of all the above clones.

优点：Linux Enterprise发行版的鲜奶油。以上所有克隆的来源。

Cons: Not free, not cheap, from the same company that pulled the rug out from under CentOS.

缺点：不是免费的，也不便宜的，这是从CentOS之下拿出地毯的同一家公司获得的。

Debian – 20+ years of experience with over 60,000 packages. Ubuntu LTS server is based on Debian SID. OpenSUSE Leap – Focuses on creating usable open-source tools. SUSE Linux Enterprise – provides interoperability with Windows and other platforms. Ubuntu Server – Specific server edition, extremely popular with commercial support available. Also see – Choosing the Best Linux Server Distro.

Debian –超过20年的经验，拥有超过60,000个软件包。 Ubuntu LTS服务器基于Debian SID。 OpenSUSE Leap –专注于创建可用的开源工具。 SUSE Linux Enterprise –提供与Windows和其他平台的互操作性。 Ubuntu Server –特定的服务器版本，非常受商业支持。另请参阅-选择最佳Linux服务器发行版。

Right then, what CentOS alternative distro should you choose? So far, I’m leaning towards Rocky Linux for CentOS-only servers, to AlmaLinux for CentOS with cPanel/WHM, and to Amazon Linux 2 on AWS. However, I wanted to share these choices with you guys because a lot can change before December 31st, 2021.

此时，您应该选择哪个CentOS替代发行版？到目前为止，我倾向于使用Rocky Linux（仅用于CentOS的服务器），AlmaLinux（具有cPanel / WHM的CentOS）和AWS上的Amazon Linux 2。但是，我想与大家分享这些选择，因为在2021年12月31日之前会有很多变化。
