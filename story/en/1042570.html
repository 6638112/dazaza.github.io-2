Apple pulls iPhone app promoting secret parties during the Covid-19 pandemic
在Covid-19大流行期间，苹果取消了iPhone应用程序以促进秘密聚会

2020-12-31 00:16:37

An iOS app called Vybe Together that promoted private parties during the COVID-19 pandemic has been removed from the Apple App Store, had its account on TikTok banned, and scrubbed most of its online presence. The app’s creators told The Verge that Apple was the one to take it off the App Store.

一款名为Vybe Together的iOS应用程序在COVID-19大流行期间促进了私人聚会，该应用程序已从Apple App Store中删除，禁止在TikTok上使用其帐户，并清理了其大部分在线业务。该应用程序的创建者告诉The Verge，苹果公司是将其从App Store中删除的人。

Vybe Together billed itself on TikTok and its website as a place to organize and attend underground parties, using the tagline “Get your rebel on. Get your party on.” Organizers would have to approve everyone who wanted to attend, and those who got approved would receive the address two hours before the event. Most of these gatherings would have been illegal under current US pandemic restrictions — which may explain why the app required you to submit a profile for approval before you were allowed on.

Vybe Together使用TickTok及其网站上的收费作为组织和参加地下派对的地方，使用的标语是“让您的叛军继续前进。参加聚会。”组织者必须批准所有想参加的人，获得批准的人将在活动开始前两个小时收到地址。在目前的美国大流行限制下，大多数此类聚会都是非法的-这可以解释为什么该应用要求您在允许进入之前要求您提交个人资料以供批准。

Calling a phone number on Vybe Together’s site connected The Verge with someone who identified themselves as Albin, one of the app’s co-founders. They said that the app had a few thousand users, and that a few thousand additional applicants had requested access to it since the company started putting videos on TikTok. Applying involved submitting your Instagram handle and uploading pictures of you partying to the service, presumably to make sure that you weren’t someone who was going to spill the beans about the events.

拨打Vybe Together网站上的电话号码即可将The Verge与该应用的共同创始人之一Albin联系起来。他们说，该应用程序有数千名用户，自从该公司开始在TikTok上放视频以来，已有数千名申请人请求访问它。申请涉及提交您的Instagram句柄并上传您参加该服务的照片，以确保您不是要在事件上撒谎的人。

Vybe Together largely had a low profile before today, although it was mentioned in at least one EventBrite invitation to a New York party in September. It received wider exposure after The New York Times reporter Taylor Lorenz critically tweeted screenshots of the website and described it as an app for “finding and promoting COVID-unsafe large, indoor house parties.”

尽管在9月份至少有一个EventBrite邀请纽约派对的时候提到了Vybe Together，但是在很大程度上它在今天之前一直是低调的。在《纽约时报》记者泰勒·洛伦兹（Taylor Lorenz）严厉地发布了该网站的屏幕截图并将其描述为“查找和推广不使用COVID的不安全的大型室内房屋聚会”的应用之后，该网站获得了更大的曝光。

Vybe Together’s now-removed FAQ page acknowledged the danger of the pandemic, but it said the app was designed to promote “small gatherings” rather than “big parties.” (Smaller gatherings of people from different households can still spread the coronavirus, creating chains of infection that extend far beyond the original event, putting people who didn’t attend the party at risk.)

Vybe Together现在已删除的“常见问题”页面承认了这种大流行的危险，但表示该应用旨在促进“小聚会”而不是“大聚会”。 （来自不同家庭的人的较小聚会仍然可以传播冠状病毒，产生的感染链远远超出了最初的事件，使未参加聚会的人处于危险之中。）

In a statement to The Verge, a spokesperson denied that the app was intended to promote unsafe behavior. “Vybe Together was [a minimum viable product] designed to help other people organize small get-togethers in parks or apartments during COVID,” the spokesperson said. “We never hosted any large parties, and we made one over-the-top marketing video that left a wrong impression about our intentions, which has since been taken down. We do not condone large unsafe parties during a pandemic.”

在向The Verge的声明中，一位发言人否认该应用程序旨在宣传不安全行为。发言人说：“ Vybe Together是（一种最低可行的产品），旨在帮助他人在COVID期间在公园或公寓内组织小型聚会。” “我们从来没有举办过大型聚会，我们制作了一个营销视频，给我们的意图留下了错误的印象，此后就被删除了。在大流行期间，我们不会纵容大型不安全的政党。”

It’s unknown whether the app substantially contributed to people holding unsafe events, although it promoted “gatherings every weekend” — including an upcoming New Year’s Eve party — in a TikTok video. The Vybe Together app had only 25 ratings before being removed, and its Instagram page has under 1,000 followers. In a statement to The Verge, TikTok said the account on that platform had only 139 followers when it was removed. The app appears to have launched previously under the name Trendies, a name mentioned on its now largely empty Instagram page.

尽管该应用程序在TikTok视频中宣传了“每个周末聚会”（包括即将举行的除夕晚会），但该应用程序是否对参与不安全事件的人们有重大贡献，这一点尚不清楚。 Vybe Together应用在被删除之前只有25个评分，其Instagram页面上的关注者不到1000。 TikTok在给The Verge的声明中说，该平台上的帐户被删除后只有139个关注者。该应用程序以前曾以趋势（Trendies）的名称启动，该名称在其现在基本上为空的Instagram页面上提到。

Vybe Together’s website also advertised a “Vybe House” for the 2021 Zamna Festival music event in Tulum, Mexico. The house’s pictures correspond to an Airbnb listing for a rental called the Casa Remy Villa. However, the listing’s manager couldn’t confirm the existence of a “Vybe House” event to The Verge.

Vybe Together的网站还为2021年在墨西哥Tulum举办的Zamna音乐节做广告“ Vybe House”。这所房子的照片与Airbnb上列出的名为Casa Remy Villa的租金清单相对应。但是，列表的经理无法确认The Verge是否存在“ Vybe House”事件。

“We have rented the villa to some influencers,” said the owner, who said they had “no idea” if any renters were linked to the app. “But they know parties aren’t allowed of any kind as they are forbidden by authorities during [the] pandemic, with the risk of shutting down the property and a huge fine.” The Airbnb host also notes that the Zamna Festival was recently postponed to April — not the January dates listed on its website.

业主说：“我们已将别墅租给了一些有影响力的人。”他们说，他们“不知道”是否有人将租户链接到该应用程序。 “但他们知道，在大流行期间，当局禁止政党禁止任何形式的聚会，这有可能会关闭财产并处以巨额罚款。” Airbnb房东还指出，Zamna节最近被推迟到4月，而不是其网站上列出的1月。

Vybe Together’s Instagram account now contains a single text post. “Blown out of proportion by media,” it reads. “We DO NOT CONDONE LARGE GATHERINGS.”

Vybe Together的Instagram帐户现在包含一条文字信息。它写道：“被媒体夸大了。” “我们不容忍大型合成器。”

Update December 29th, 8:06PM ET : The app’s creators have now clarified that Apple was the one to pull the app from the App Store.

更新时间：美国东部时间12月29日，晚上8:06：该应用程序的创建者已经明确表示，苹果是将其从App Store中拉出的应用程序。
