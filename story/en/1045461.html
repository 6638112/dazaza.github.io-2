Kia Will Lead Apple Car Project Work Under Hyundai Motor, Report Says
起亚将在现代汽车的领导下领导苹果汽车项目工作

2021-01-21 07:54:41

Increasingly, it seems like the Apple Car is far from dead. Rather, it continues to find new life in a number of reports connecting Korean automaker Hyundai Motor to the project in a potential upcoming partnership announcement. However, it may not be the Hyundai brand that works on the Apple Car, but rather Kia.

苹果汽车似乎越来越远了。相反，它在可能将要宣布的合作伙伴关系公告中，将韩国汽车制造商现代汽车（Hyundai Motor）与该项目联系起来的许多报道中继续找到新的生命。但是，在Apple Car上使用的并不是现代品牌，而是起亚。

A report from Reuters, via Korean publication Edaily, said Hyundai Motor, the parent automaker of Hyundai and Kia, will task the Kia division with Apple Car work. The brand also said on Wednesday it's looking at cooperation with foreign firms surrounding self-driving and electric vehicles. Kia made no mention of Apple, though the comment follows the reported connections to the tech giant. Kia did not immediately return Roadshow's request for comment, nor did Apple. Hyundai in the past declined to comment on the speculation surrounding work on an Apple Car.

路透社通过韩国出版物《每日报》报道，现代和起亚的母公司现代汽车（Hyundai Motor）将负责起亚部门与苹果汽车的合作。该品牌周三还表示，正在与外国公司围绕自动驾驶和电动汽车展开合作。起亚没有提及苹果，尽管这一评论是在报道与该科技巨头的联系之后进行的。起亚没有立即返回Roadshow的置评请求，苹果也没有。现代汽车过去拒绝就围绕苹果汽车工作的猜测发表评论。

CNET's Apple Report newsletter delivers news, reviews and advice on iPhones, iPads, Macs and software.

CNET的Apple Report新闻简报在iPhone，iPad，Mac和软件上提供新闻，评论和建议。

Earlier this month, various reports pegged the Apple Car as alive and well inside the tech company and said we could see a final vehicle in 2024 at the earliest. A separate report said the company may show a prototype of the vehicle next year. Interestingly, one of the reports last month also named Kia's factory in Georgia as the rumored home for Apple Car production, which coincides with news of Kia's possible involvement in the latest round of rumors.

本月早些时候，各种报道都认为苹果汽车在科技公司内部还活着，并且说我们最早可能在2024年看到最终的汽车。另一份报告称，该公司可能会在明年展示该车的原型。有趣的是，上个月的一份报告还称，起亚在乔治亚州的工厂是苹果汽车生产的传闻所在地，这与起亚可能参与最新一轮传闻的消息相吻合。

Reportedly, Apple is hard at work on breakthrough EV battery technology and automated driving systems to help usher in a game changing car. Choosing an established automaker to handle final production seems simple, since Apple has zero history of building cars. If you need an example of how difficult it is to build cars, take a look at Tesla's relatively short history in the business.

据报道，苹果公司正在努力开发突破性的电动汽车电池技术和自动驾驶系统，以帮助迎接改变游戏规则的汽车。由于苹果的汽车制造历史为零，因此选择一家成熟的汽车制造商来进行最终生产似乎很简单。如果您需要举个例子来说明制造汽车有多困难，请看一下特斯拉在业务上相对较短的历史。

We'll have to wait and see if this Kia-Apple connection comes to life, so for now, continue shaking a little bit of salt with these rumors.

我们将不得不拭目以待，看看起亚与苹果之间的这种联系是否会栩栩如生，所以就目前而言，继续用这些谣言震撼一下。
