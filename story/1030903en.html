DoorDash正在投资一家餐厅，奥克兰的Burma Bites，首次表示，这笔投资将帮助它建立一种更持久的送货模式

2020-10-24 09:17:47

For the first time in the company’s history, food delivery app DoorDash has invested in a restaurant.

外卖应用DoorDash在该公司历史上首次投资了一家餐厅。

The new venture for DoorDash is a stake in Oakland’s Burma Bites, a spin-off of Burma Superstar that will open on Oct. 28 for takeout and delivery service. Burma Superstar has defined Burmese food in the Bay Area since opening in San Francisco in 1992, making tea leaf salad, with its intoxicating combination of fermented tea leaves, lemon and fish sauce, one of the most sought-after dishes in the region.

DoorDash的新合资企业是奥克兰Burma Bites的股份，Burma Bites是从Burma Superstar剥离出来的，将于10月28日开业，提供外卖和送货服务。自1992年在旧金山开业以来，缅甸超级明星一直在旧金山湾区定义缅甸食物，制作茶叶沙拉，其发酵茶叶、柠檬和鱼露的令人陶醉的组合，是该地区最受欢迎的菜肴之一。

DoorDash declined to specify how much money it is investing in Burma Bites, but Georgie Thomas, head of regional merchant partnerships at the San Francisco delivery company, said in an email that COVID-19 “has accelerated the need for merchants” to establish their online presence in the Bay Area. Thomas said investing in Burma Bites is a way for the company to take its mission of increasing delivery sales for restaurants during the pandemic one step further.

DoorDash拒绝具体说明它将向Burma Bites投资多少资金，但这家旧金山快递公司的地区商家合作主管乔治·托马斯(Georgie Thomas)在一封电子邮件中表示，新冠肺炎“加快了商家在旧金山湾区建立在线业务的需求”。托马斯说，投资Burma Bites是该公司将其使命-在大流行期间增加餐厅的送货销售额-更进一步的一种方式。

“Burma Bites is an example of working alongside a restaurant to find new ways for our partnerships to adapt to the new normal,” Thomas said. “We set out to take the authenticity and uniqueness of the Burma Superstar dine-in experience, and the success they’ve seen with delivery from their other locations, and build a more permanent delivery model for their business.”

托马斯说：“Burma Bites是一个与餐厅并肩工作，为我们的伙伴关系寻找适应新常态的新方法的例子。”我们开始利用缅甸超级明星就餐体验的真实性和独特性，以及他们在其他地点送货取得的成功，为他们的业务建立更持久的送货模式。“。

DoorDash was founded as Palo Alto Delivery in 2013, when it started delivering food around the Stanford campus. It has since become one of the largest delivery apps in the Bay Area. While Burma Bites is the first DoorDash investment in a standalone space for one restaurant, the company opened a commissary kitchen in Redwood City in 2019. The location, dubbed DoorDash Kitchens, has been occupied by Bay Area companies like Nation’s Giant Hamburgers, Humphry Slocombe, Rooster & Rice and the Halal Guys.

DoorDash成立于2013年，前身是Palo Alto Delivery，当时它开始在斯坦福校园周围递送食物。自那以后，它已经成为旧金山湾区最大的送货应用程序之一。虽然Burma Bites是DoorDash对一家餐厅的独立空间的第一笔投资，但该公司于2019年在红杉城开设了一家小卖部厨房。这家名为DoorDash Kitchens的餐厅一直被旧金山湾区的几家公司占据，比如Nation‘s Giant Hamburgers、Humphry Slocombe、Rooster&Amp；Rice和The Halal Guys。

Burma Bites was originally set to open in March, down the street from Burma Superstar on Telegraph Avenue, but the debut was delayed by the pandemic. Owner Desmond Tan sees Burma Bites as a way to ensure the longevity of a business that now includes seven Bay Area Burmese restaurants.

Burma Bites原定于3月份开业，位于电报大道上缅甸超级明星的街道上，但由于大流行而推迟了首次亮相。老板Desmond Tan认为Burma Bites是一种确保企业长盛不衰的方式，该企业现在包括七家旧金山湾区的缅甸餐厅。

Tan had envisioned Burma Bites as a way to help support the busy Burma Superstar kitchen. There were always crowds at the restaurant, before the pandemic, and for the constant stream of folks who couldn’t grab a table, Burma Bites was going to be nearby to offer some of the menu items that they could take home.

谭恩美曾设想将缅甸咬人作为帮助支持繁忙的缅甸超级明星厨房的一种方式。在疫情爆发之前，餐厅里总是人满为患，对于无法抢到桌子的源源不断的人来说，Burma Bites将在附近提供一些他们可以带回家的菜单项目。

Even with Alameda County allowing for indoor dining at 25% of normal capacity or 100 people, whichever is fewer, Tan said, customers are still primarily looking to order food to go and through delivery apps. Burma Bites will serve a delivery radius of 3 to 5 miles through DoorDash, reaching parts of surrounding neighborhoods including Berkeley, Alameda, and the Oakland hills.

Tan说，即使阿拉米达县允许室内用餐达到正常容量的25%或100人，以数量较少者为准，顾客仍然主要希望点餐和通过送货应用程序。Burma Bites将通过DoorDash提供3至5英里的投递半径，到达包括伯克利、阿拉米达和奥克兰山区在内的部分周边社区。

Having only delivery and takeout orders meant the menu for Burma Bites had to be tweaked. For example, it offers a small bites section that includes the spicy, sweet flavors of the restaurant’s mango chicken dish, stir-fried chicken with fresh mangoes, but repurposed as a glaze on chicken wings for $12. The same goes for the mohinga wings, also $12, which are tossed in a wok and coated with the same lemongrass, ginger and tumeric flavors that can be found in Burma Superstar’s mohinga noodle dish, which usually features fish.

只有送货和外卖订单意味着缅甸美食的菜单必须调整。例如，它提供了一个小口部分，包括餐厅的芒果鸡肉菜肴辛辣甜美的味道，即新鲜芒果炒鸡肉，但将其重新用作鸡翅上的釉料，售价12美元。莫辛加鸡翅也是如此，也是12美元，它被扔进锅里，涂上与缅甸超级明星(Burma Superstar)的莫辛加面条相同的柠檬草、生姜和肿瘤的味道，后者通常以鱼为特色。

Burma Bites will have some classics, like the tea leaf salad with peanuts, sunflower seeds, dried shrimp and tomatoes for $15 and the popular nan gyi dok, rice noodles with chicken coconut curry sauce, for $16. Everything on the menu is priced between $12 and $19.

Burma Bites将有一些经典菜肴，比如15美元的花生、葵花籽、虾干和西红柿茶叶沙拉，以及16美元的颇受欢迎的South Gyi dok，即鸡肉椰子咖喱酱米粉。菜单上的每样东西的价格都在12美元到19美元之间。

Tan said his team spent the past few months tinkering to improve the delivery component of the restaurant, such as creating bags with dimensions fitting the size of their to-go containers so the items don’t slide around during transport. The Burma Bites delivery bags also have adhesive at the top and a branded sticker that will let the customer know upon delivery whether their food was tampered with after being packaged and picked up from the restaurant.

谭恩美说，他的团队在过去的几个月里一直在修修补补，以改善餐厅的送货部分，比如创造尺寸与外卖容器大小相适应的袋子，这样物品在运输过程中就不会四处滑动。Burma Bites快递袋的顶部也有粘合剂和品牌贴纸，可以让顾客在送货时知道他们的食物在包装并从餐厅取走后是否被篡改了。

Tan said none of his businesses have had to shut down during the pandemic. While the industry is still trying to find its footing, he said he hopes the opening of his new restaurant can be symbolic of potentially better times in the near future.

Tan说，在大流行期间，他的企业没有一家不得不关闭。虽然该行业仍在努力寻找立足点，但他表示，他希望他的新餐厅的开业能象征着不久的将来可能会有更好的时期。

“I want people to be able to see this place, and times are hard, sure, but they can see this and know restaurants are trying to survive,” he said. “That we can make it.”

他说：“我希望人们能够看到这个地方，当然，日子很艰难，但他们可以看到这一点，知道餐馆正在努力生存。”“我们能做到。”
