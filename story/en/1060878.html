AMD’s upcoming flagship GPUs should be 3x faster than RX 6900XT
AMD即将到来的旗舰GPU应比RX 6900XT快3倍

2021-05-06 04:47:55

According to wccftech and well-known hardware leaker @KittyYYuko, AMD's next-generation Radeon graphics cards should be a massive upgrade—as much as three times faster than AMD's current flagship graphics cards.

根据WCCFTECH和众所周知的硬件泄漏机@Kittyyyuko，AMD＆＃39;下一代Radeon显卡应该是大量升级 - 比AMD＆＃39;目前的旗舰显卡的速度快三倍。

In this reply to RedGamingTech's Paul Eccleston, @KittyYYuko claims a 3x performance increase when upgrading from AMD's flagship Navi 21 (as found in current-gen RX 6800XT and RX 6900 XT cards) to Navi 31.

在此回复RedgamingTech＆＃39; S Paul Eccleston，@Kittyyyyuko声称从AMD＆＃39的旗舰Navi 21升级时增加了3倍的性能增加（如Current-Gen RX 6800XT和RX 6900 XT卡）到Navi 31。

RDNA3—the architecture beneath the new GPUs—will shift process-node size from 7nm down to 5nm, which tends to result in major performance gains and even larger performance-per-watt gains. The downshift in process node isn't enough to account for the claimed performance uplift by itself, though—that comes from making the GPU itself significantly bigger and meaner.

RDNA3-新GPUS下方的架构 - 将流程节点大小从7nm下降到5nm，这往往会导致主要的性能提升甚至更大的每瓦的性能增长。流程节点的下降频率是＆＃39; t足以解释所要求的性能提升本身，但这来自使GPU本身显着更大和易于拟成。

The current flagship, Navi 21, offers 80 compute units and 5,120 shading units. Navi 31 will reportedly double that, offering a whopping 160 CUs and over 10,000 shading units, delivered in two 80 CU chiplets on a single die. This is likely only practical due to the downshift in process size—cooling and power supply are major concerns in flagship GPUs, which tend to require as much of both as OEMs and individual builders can comfortably cram into a case.

目前的旗舰，Navi 21提供80个计算单元和5,120个阴影单元。据报道，Navi 31将据据报道，提供高达160 CUS和超过10,000个遮阳装置的两倍，在单一的模具上送入两个80 CU小芯片。这可能仅是由于工艺尺寸的降档，电源降低，电源是旗舰GPU中的主要问题，这往往需要尽可能多地作为OEM和个别建筑物可以舒适地克服案例。

RedGamingTech suggests that Navi 31's ray-tracing performance should be roughly on par with Nvidia's Ampere GPUs, but machine-learning tasks will still be handled via low-precision operations on the main GPU itself, rather than on dedicated hardware like Nvidia's Tensor Cores.

RedgamingTech建议Navi 31＆＃39; S光线跟踪性能应大致与NVIDIA＆＃39; S安培GPU相当，但机器学习任务仍将通过主GPU本身的低精度操作来处理，而不是在专用硬件如nvidia＆＃39; s张核心。

We expect RDNA 3 cards to become available sometime in late 2022 or early 2023.

我们预计RDNA 3牌将在2022年末或2023年初可用。
