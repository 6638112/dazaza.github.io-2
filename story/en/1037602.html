Discovery To Launch Streaming Service in January Starting at $4.99 Per Month
Discovery将于1月启动流媒体服务，每月$ 4.99起

2020-12-04 21:34:03

Discovery is the latest media company to jump into the ever more crowded streaming wars.

Discovery是最新的媒体公司，它卷入了越来越拥挤的流媒体战争。

It will launch its streaming service Discovery+ on Jan. 4, 2021. The service will include a $4.99 per month ad-supported tier and a $6.99 per month ad-free tier.

它将在2021年1月4日启动其流媒体服务Discovery +。该服务将包括每月4.99美元的广告支持级别和每月6.99美元的无广告级别。

The lower $4.99 tier costs the same as NBCUniversal-owned Peacock's premium tier with ads. The ad-free $6.99 tier is on par with what Disney+ costs. Both offerings are much less expensive than WarnerMedia's HBO Max, which costs $14.99 a month, and Netflix, which raised its standard plan to $13.99 a month in Oct.

较低的$ 4.99级别费用与NBCUniversal拥有的Peacock带有广告的高级级别相同。 $ 6.99美元的无广告费用与Disney +的费用相当。两种产品的价格都比华纳媒体公司的HBO Max便宜得多，后者每月的费用为14.99美元，而Netflix在10月将其标准计划提高到每月13.99美元。

Discovery is also partnering with Verizon, which will give 55 million customers up to 12 months of the ad-free Discovery+ plan for free, depending on their wireless plan with the carrier.

Discovery还与Verizon合作，Verizon将为5500万客户免费提供长达12个月的无广告Discovery +计划，具体取决于与运营商的无线计划。

The company owns networks like the Discovery Channel, famous for its annual Shark Week, as well as home improvement channel HGTV and Food Network, among others. Discovery says Discovery+ will launch with the most content of any new streaming service: 55,000 episodes from 2,500 shows.

该公司拥有以一年一度的“鲨鱼周”而闻名的探索频道，家庭装修频道HGTV和Food Network等网络。 Discovery表示Discovery +将以所有新流媒体服务中最多的内容启动：2,500个节目中的55,000集。

Discovery+ is also adding content from A&E, the History Channel and Lifetime in a new partnership. The service will feature original content from figures like Sir David Attenborough, chef Bobby Flay and comedian Kevin Hart as well.

Discovery +还通过新的合作伙伴关系添加了来自A＆amp; E，历史频道和Lifetime的内容。该服务将以David Attenborough爵士，厨师Bobby Flay和喜剧演员Kevin Hart等人物的原创内容为特色。

Discovery+ also signed advertising partners such as Boston Beer Company, Kraft Heinz, Lowe's and Toyota.

Discovery +还签署了广告合作伙伴，例如波士顿啤酒公司，卡夫亨氏，劳氏和丰田。

The company did not say exactly where Discovery+ will be available. Some competitors have had trouble negotiating with Amazon Fire and Roku, two of the larger streaming TV platforms. Peacock launched on Roku in Sept. after months of negotiations, while HBO Max only launched on Amazon in mid-Nov. and remains absent from Roku.

该公司没有确切说明在哪里可以找到Discovery +。一些竞争对手在与两个较大的流媒体电视平台Amazon Fire和Roku进行谈判时遇到了麻烦。经过几个月的谈判，孔雀在9月于Roku上线，而HBO Max仅在11月中旬在亚马逊上线。仍然不在Roku中。

Discovery is smaller and lesser known than its competitors which just launched their streaming services in the past year. In just one year, Disney+ has amassed 73.7 million subscribers, far exceeding the company's expectations for 60 to 90 million subscribers by 2024. NBCUniversal's Peacock, which launched this summer, has reached nearly 22 million sign-ups already. WarnerMedia reported more than 38 million domestic subscribers for HBO and HBOMax in October.

与过去一年才推出流媒体服务的竞争对手相比，Discovery的规模较小，知名度也较低。在仅仅一年的时间里，迪士尼+的订户已达到7370万，远远超出了公司到2024年的60到9000万订户的预期。今年夏天推出的NBCUniversal Peacock签约人数已接近2200万已经。华纳媒体报道，10月，HBO和HBOMax的国内用户超过3800万。

Discovery said it already has 5 million global paying subscribers across its existing streaming services. It believes the addressable market in the U.S. is at least 70 million homes and will continue to grow.

Discovery表示，其现有流媒体服务已拥有500万全球付费用户。它认为美国的可寻址市场至少有7,000万户家庭，并将继续增长。

Discovery will hope its programming and partnerships can set its service apart as it joins a crowded field of competitors with leaders pulling away from the rest of the pack.

Discovery希望它的编程和合作伙伴关系能够与众不同，因为它加入了一个拥挤的竞争者领域，而领导者则从其他人中脱颖而出。
