Samsung's SmartThings will support Google Nest devices starting in January 2021
三星的SmartThings将于2021年1月开始支持Google Nest设备

2020-12-09 00:53:19

Google and Samsung are bringing their smart home platforms together for the first time. Samsung’s SmartThings will support Google Nest devices starting in January 2021, building a bridge between one of the industry’s leading ecosystems for managing smart devices like cameras and thermostats with one of the leading companies developing those products.

谷歌和三星首次将其智能家居平台整合在一起。三星的SmartThings将于2021年1月开始支持Google Nest设备，在业界领先的生态系统之一与开发这些产品的领先公司之一之间架起一座桥梁，以管理相机和恒温器等智能设备。

Prior to this, owners of devices that qualified for the “Works With SmartThings” (WWST) certification had to use separate software to manage any Google Nest products they owned. Following the launch of the integration next month, all Nest products will be controllable from the SmartThings platform and will qualify as WWST devices.

在此之前，符合“使用SmartThings”（WWST）认证的设备的所有者必须使用单独的软件来管理他们拥有的任何Google Nest产品。在下个月启动集成之后，所有Nest产品都可以通过SmartThings平台进行控制，并且可以作为WWST设备使用。

The partnership further bolsters Samsung’s smart home platform, which is one of the leading hub makers for managing smart home devices on the same network. Samsung purchased SmartThings in 2014 for $200 million due to the company’s proficiency at helping smart home products communicate across various protocol standards.

该合作伙伴关系进一步加强了三星的智能家居平台，该平台是在同一网络上管理智能家居设备的领先集线器制造商之一。三星在2014年以2亿美元的价格收购了SmartThings，原因是该公司精通帮助智能家居产品跨各种协议标准进行通信的技术。

Since then, SmartThings has grown to support thousands of products as Samsung has pushed to integrate the platform with virtually every smart home brand on the market, with Google remaining a notable exception until today. Samsung says its platform now has more than 120 certified brands and more than 63 million active users.

此后，随着三星推动将该平台与市场上几乎所有智能家居品牌集成，SmartThings已发展为支持数千种产品，而Google直到今天仍是一个明显的例外。三星表示，其平台目前拥有120多个认证品牌，活跃用户超过6300万。

Samsung says its new Google partnership means Nest devices can be incorporated into SmartThings Scenes for setting up automated controls for multiple devices at the same time, and the integration will also mean Samsung TVs and its Family Hub fridge will support streaming from Nest cameras and other devices.

三星表示，其新的Google合作伙伴关系意味着可以将Nest设备整合到SmartThings Scenes中，以便同时为多个设备设置自动化控件，并且集成也意味着三星电视及其Family Hub冰箱将支持Nest相机和其他设备的流媒体播放。
