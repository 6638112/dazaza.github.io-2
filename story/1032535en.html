这一立场导致Coinbase约5%的员工选择接受遣散费方案离开公司。其中两名员工是Coinbase全球营销主管John Russ和Coinbase副总裁Dan Yoo。

招聘启事写道：“我们相信，我们有可能100%致力于一个包容的工作场所，在每个人都安全和属于的地方，尊重多样性(作为其中的一部分，努力根除并消除公司存在的任何不容忍或偏见)，同时保持对我们使命的高度专注。”“为此，我们已公开表态，Coinbase不会就我们建立更加开放的金融体系和扩大经济自由的使命范围以外的议题发表外部声明，同时也加倍致力于让公司成为所有员工的一个令人惊叹的工作场所，无论背景如何.”

The Washington Post’s Nitasha Tiku shed some light on caste-based discrimination in the tech ecosystem. Specifically, 30 female Indian engineers who are part of the Dalit caste and work for companies like Apple, Google, Microsoft and Cisco, say they have faced caste bias. As Tiku explains, those in the Dalit caste are part of the lowest rank castes within India’s social hierarchy.

华盛顿邮报的Nitasha Tiku揭示了科技生态系统中基于种姓的歧视。具体地说，30名为苹果、谷歌、微软和思科等公司工作的达利特种姓女性工程师表示，她们面临种姓偏见。正如Tiku解释的那样，达利特种姓的人是印度社会等级中等级最低的种姓的一部分。

Uber said it has received more than 8,500 demands for arbitration as a result of it  ditching delivery fees for Black-owned restaurants via Uber Eats.

优步表示，由于通过优步Eats放弃了Black所有的餐厅的送货费，它已经收到了超过8500项仲裁要求。

Uber Eats made this change in June, following racial justice protests around the police killing of George Floyd, an unarmed Black man. Uber Eats said it wanted to make it easier for customers to support Black-owned businesses in the U.S. and Canada.  To qualify, the restaurant must be a small or medium-sized business and, therefore, not part of a franchise. In contrast, delivery fees are still in place for other restaurants.

Uber Eats在6月份做出了这一改变，此前围绕警察杀害手无寸铁的黑人乔治·弗洛伊德(George Floyd)的种族正义抗议活动。优步Eats表示，它希望让顾客更容易支持布莱克在美国和加拿大的业务。要获得资格，这家餐厅必须是中小型企业，因此不是特许经营的一部分。与此形成对比的是，其他餐厅仍然收取送货费。

In one of these claims, viewed by TechCrunch, a customer says Uber Eats violates the Unruh civil Rights Act by “charging discriminatory delivery fees based on race (of the business owner).” That claim seeks $12,000 as well as a permanent injunction that would prevent Uber from continuing to offer free delivery from Black-owned restaurants.

在TechCrunch看到的其中一项索赔中，一名顾客表示，优步Eats“基于(企业主)的种族收取歧视性的送货费”，违反了“Unruh民权法案”。这一索赔要求获得1.2万美元，以及一项永久禁令，以阻止优步继续提供黑人拥有的餐厅的免费送货服务。

Uber driver claims rating system is racially biased Uber is no stranger to lawsuits, so this one shouldn’t come as a surprise. Uber is now facing a lawsuit regarding its customer ratings and how the company deactivates drivers whose ratings fall below a certain threshold. The suit alleges the system “constitues race discrimination, as it is widely recognized that customer evaluations of workers are frequently racially biased.”

优步司机声称评级系统是有种族偏见的，优步对诉讼并不陌生，所以这一次应该不会令人惊讶。优步现在正面临一场诉讼，涉及其客户评级，以及该公司如何停用评级低于一定门槛的司机。诉讼称，这一制度“构成了种族歧视，因为人们普遍认识到，客户对工人的评价经常带有种族偏见。”

In a statement to NPR, Uber called the suit “flimsy” and said “ridesharing has greatly reduced bias for both drivers and riders, who now have fairer, more equitable access to work and transportation than ever before.”

在给NPR的一份声明中，优步称这起诉讼“脆弱”，并表示“拼车大大减少了司机和乘客的偏见，他们现在比以往任何时候都有更公平、更公平的工作和交通机会。”

Yes on Prop 22 gets another $3.75 million influx of cash DoorDash put in an additional $3.75 million into the Yes on 22 campaign, according to a late contribution filing. Proposition 22 is the California ballot measure that aims to keep gig workers classified as independent contractors.

根据最近提交的一份捐款文件，22号提案上的YES又获得了375万美元的现金流入，DoorDash在22号提案的Yes On 22竞选活动中额外投入了375万美元。22号提案是加州的投票措施，旨在将零工工人归类为独立承包商。

The latest influx of cash brought Yes on 22’s total contributions north of $200 million. As of October 14, the campaign had raised $189 million. But thanks to a number of late contributions, the total put toward Yes on 22 comes out to about $202,955,106.38, or, $203 million.

最近的现金流入使22家公司的总捐款超过2亿美元。截至10月14日，这场活动已经筹集了1.89亿美元。但由于一些后期捐款，22号的赞成票总额约为202,955,106.38美元，或2.03亿美元。

Prop 22 hit the most-funded California ballot measure long ago, but it’s now surpassed the $200 million mark.

22号提案很久以前就达到了加州资金最多的投票措施，但现在它已经超过了2亿美元大关。
