Netflix announces four comedy specials to be hosted and produced by Dave Chappelle; the comedian's last special led to Netflix employee protests
Netflix宣布将由Dave Chappelle主持和制作四个喜剧特辑；这位喜剧演员的最后一个特别节目引发了Netflix员工的抗议

2022-02-20 13:15:20

The streamer announced Friday the release of four upcoming comedy specials executive produced and hosted by the controversial comic.

《流光报》周五宣布，四部即将上映的喜剧特辑将由这位备受争议的喜剧演员制作和主持。

The series of specials is dubbed Chappelle’s Home Team, and each will feature a different comic introduced by Chappelle.

这一系列的特辑被称为查佩尔的主队，每个特辑都将以查佩尔介绍的不同漫画为特色。

The first to be featured is Earthquake (real name Nathaniel Martin Stroman), and his special, called Earthquake: Legendary, premieres on Feb. 28. Another special will spotlight Donnell Rawlings. One similarity shared by all the featured comics is they’ve been in the stand-up game for at least 30 years.

第一个出现的是地震（真名纳撒尼尔·马丁·斯特罗曼），他的特别节目《地震：传奇》将于2月28日首映。另一个特别节目将聚焦唐奈尔·罗林斯。所有特色漫画都有一个共同点，那就是它们已经在单口相声游戏中至少有30年了。

“I’ve been doing this a long time and comedians like Quake and Donnell are not only friends but have inspired my own career,” Chappelle said. “Anyone in the comedy community knows these names and knows their time to shine is long overdue. I am proud to be a part of this moment.”

查佩尔说：“我这么做已经很长时间了，像奎克和唐纳这样的喜剧演员不仅是朋友，也启发了我自己的事业。”。“喜剧界的任何人都知道这些名字，都知道他们早就该大放异彩了。我很自豪能成为这一时刻的一部分。”

The specials are directed by Chappelle’s go-to helmer Stan Lathan and executive produced by Chappelle, Lathan, Earthquake, Rikki Hughes and Jermaine Smith.

这些特别节目由查佩尔的《走向赫尔默·斯坦·拉坦》导演，由查佩尔、拉坦、地震、里基·休斯和杰梅因·史密斯制作。

Chappelle is a hugely popular draw for the streamer. But his last Netflix special, October’s The Closer, drew considerable criticism from the media as well as from many of his fans for the comic’s lengthy discussion of the transgender movement, segments that were blasted as transphobic.

Chappelle是一款非常受欢迎的彩带。但他在Netflix的最后一个特辑，10月份的《The Closer》中，因该漫画对跨性别运动的冗长讨论而招致了媒体和许多粉丝的大量批评，这些片段被抨击为跨性别运动。

Netflix CEO Ted Sarandos defended the special amid uproar from some in the streamer’s own employee ranks and later admitted “I screwed up” by not being more sensitive to Netflix staffers. Still, Sarandos backed the program as “consistent with our comedy offering, it’s consistent with Dave Chappelle’s comedy brand and this is … one of those times when there’s something on Netflix that you’re not going to like.”

Netflix首席执行官特德·萨兰多斯（Ted Sarandos）在该公司员工队伍中的一些人的喧嚣声中为这一特殊做法进行了辩护，后来他承认“我搞砸了”，对Netflix员工不太敏感。尽管如此，Sarandos还是支持该计划，称其“与我们的喜剧产品一致，与Dave Chappelle的喜剧品牌一致，而且现在是……Netflix上有些东西你不会喜欢的时候之一。”

At the time, The Closer was billed as the last of a six-special package of Chappelle content for the streamer.

当时，《Closer》被称为“流光”六个特殊的Chappelle内容包中的最后一个。

Chappelle will also headline a performance at the Hollywood Bowl for Netflix’s Los Angeles-based comedy festival, Netflix is a Joke, which gets underway April 28.

查佩尔还将在4月28日上映的Netflix洛杉矶喜剧节《Netflix是一个笑话》的好莱坞碗上担任主角。
