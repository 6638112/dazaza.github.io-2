Car Design History – A data visualization of Italian car design
汽车设计历史 - 意大利汽车设计的数据可视化

2021-04-22 11:30:54

As a car-obsessed designer, I spend countless hours browsing the internet, reading anything I can find about car design icons. In doing so, I noticed that I often miss the context of a particular design. Who designed it and how did it relate to other cars of the time? It got me thinking, how cool would it be if you could see all of these cars throughout time with relevant information?

作为汽车痴迷的设计师，我花了无数的时间浏览互联网，阅读任何我可以找到关于汽车设计图标的东西。在这样做时，我注意到我经常错过特定设计的背景。谁设计了它以及它的时间是如何与其他汽车有关的？它让我在思考，如果你能在整个时间内看到所有这些汽车，有多酷的是有关相关信息的？

It turns out it is very difficult to get a good overview of all the cars designed by these studios. I have spent a couple of hours collecting information from various sources, but mainly Wikipedia, COACHBUILD.com, and carstyling.ru. Because I gathered all the data myself, I am sure I missed some models and some of the information may be incorrect. Don’t hesitate to send a quick message about that!

事实证明，很难获得这些工作室设计的所有汽车的良好概述。我花了几个小时从各种来源收集信息，而是主要是维基百科，Coachbuild.com和Carstyling.ru。因为我自己收集了所有数据，所以我相信我错过了一些模型，一些信息可能不正确。不要犹豫，发一条快速信息！

Next up, I’d like to add cars from 1975 - 1980 as there are some funky designs from that era!

接下来，我想从1975年到1980年添加汽车，因为那个时代有一些时髦的设计！
