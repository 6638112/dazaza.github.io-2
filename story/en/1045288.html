Aurora partners with truck maker PACCAR to develop self-driving big rig trucks “in the next several years”, as it closes the deal for Uber's self-driving unit
Aurora与卡车制造商PACCAR合作，“在未来几年内”开发自动驾驶大型钻机卡车，因为它完成了与Uber的自动驾驶部门的交易

2021-01-20 12:41:29

Truck-maker Paccar will work with autonomous vehicle startup Aurora Innovation Inc. to develop self-driving heavy-duty trucks, the latest in a growing series of deals aimed at bringing automation to the business that moves most of the freight in the U.S.

卡车制造商Paccar将与自动驾驶汽车初创公司Aurora Innovation Inc.合作开发自动驾驶重型卡车，这是一系列不断增长的交易中的最新一项，旨在为将美国大部分货物运送到业务中的业务带来自动化。

Aurora’s partnership with Bellevue, Wash.-based Paccar announced Tuesday will integrate the startup’s technology, a mixture of software and hardware that helps vehicles see and navigate the world, with the manufacturer’s autonomous vehicle platform technology. Paccar isn’t taking a stake in the Mountain View, Calif.-based company, whose backers include Amazon.com Inc.

Aurora与总部位于华盛顿州Bellevue的Paccar的合作伙伴关系将把该初创公司的技术与该制造商的自动驾驶汽车平台技术相结合，该技术将帮助车辆看到并导航世界。 Paccar并未持有这家位于加利福尼亚山景城的公司的股份，该公司的支持者包括Amazon.com Inc.。

Paccar and Aurora will work together to develop, test and prepare for sale autonomous versions of certain models of Paccar’s Peterbilt and Kenworth brand trucks, with the goal of bringing self-driving trucks to market “in the next several years,” the companies said.

两家公司表示，Paccar和Aurora将共同开发，测试和准备销售某些型号的Paccar的Peterbilt和Kenworth品牌卡车的自动版本，目标是“在未来几年内将自动驾驶卡车推向市场”。

“The most important part is reaching a level of safety and reliability,” Kyle Quinn, Paccar’s chief technology officer, said in an interview.

Paccar的首席技术官Kyle Quinn在接受采访时说：“最重要的部分是达到安全性和可靠性。”

The agreement follows similar partnerships announced last year between the trucking arm of Daimler and Waymo LLC and between Volkswagen AG trucking subsidiary Traton Group SE and autonomous vehicle startup TuSimple Inc., which also struck a deal in 2020 with U.S. truck-maker Navistar International to jointly develop self-driving trucks.

该协议是继戴姆勒卡车公司和Waymo LLC去年宣布的类似合作伙伴关系以及大众汽车集团卡车子公司Traton Group SE和自动驾驶汽车初创公司TuSimple Inc.之间宣布的类似合作伙伴关系之后的又一次合作伙伴关系，该合作伙伴还于2020年与美国卡车制造商Navistar International达成了一项交易开发自动驾驶卡车。

Using self-driving technology to haul freight could help trucking companies cut costs and shipping times now limited by rules that restrict truckers’ hours behind the wheel. Proponents say autonomous big rigs could also improve highway safety, although technical and regulatory challenges loom before self-driving trucks could be widely deployed.

使用自动驾驶技术拖运货物可以帮助卡车运输公司削减成本和缩短运输时间，而现在这些法规限制了卡车司机的开车时间。支持者说，自动驾驶大型钻机也可以改善高速公路安全性，尽管在自动驾驶卡车可以广泛部署之前，技术和法规方面的挑战就已隐现。

Aurora has been testing its autonomous vehicle hardware and software in prototypes using Paccar trucks, and last year in Texas began doing commercial trucking runs in the Dallas-Fort Worth region. The company is also pursuing other applications for its technology and has signed deals with auto makers Hyundai Motor and Fiat Chrysler Automobiles NV, which is now known as Stellantis NV after Fiat Chrysler combined with Peugeot maker PSA Group.

Aurora一直在使用Paccar卡车测试其自动驾驶汽车硬件和软件的原型，去年在德克萨斯州开始在达拉斯-沃思堡地区进行商业卡车运输。该公司还正在寻求技术的其他应用，并已与汽车制造商现代汽车和菲亚特克莱斯勒汽车公司（Fiat Chrysler Automobiles NV）签订了交易，在菲亚特克莱斯勒与标致汽车制造商PSA集团合并之后，该公司现在被称为Stellantis NV。

Last month Aurora said it would acquire Uber Technologies self-driving car unit, Advanced Technologies Group, or ATG, bringing the startup’s valuation to about $10 billion. That deal closed Tuesday, the company said.

上个月，Aurora表示将收购Uber Technologies无人驾驶汽车部门，Advanced Technologies Group或ATG，使该公司的估值达到约100亿美元。 该公司表示，该交易周二完成。
