IKEA to discontinue its annual catalog, ending a 70-year run
宜家终止年度目录，结束了长达70年的运营

2021-02-17 18:31:37

Since 285,000 copies of the 1951 edition circulated through southern Sweden, the release of the new IKEA catalog has become an annual event for lovers of the iconic Swedish furniture brand. At peak circulation in 2016, more than 200 million copies made their way to homes and stores across more than 50 international markets and 30 different languages.

自从1951年版的285,000册在瑞典南部发行以来，新的宜家目录的发布已成为标志性瑞典家具品牌爱好者的年度盛会。在2016年的发行量达到顶峰时，有超过2亿份拷贝进入了50多个国际市场和30种不同语言的家庭和商店。

Times change, however, and global companies hoping to stay relevant have little choice but to change with them. To that end, IKEA has announced that its catalog’s glorious run will come to an end, a decision the company arrived at within the past few months. The 2021 edition, which debuted in October, will be its last. At 40 million copies, its circulation is only about 20% of what it had been just five years earlier.

然而，时代在变化，而希望与时俱进的跨国公司别无选择，只能与时俱进。为此，宜家宣布其产品目录的辉煌运营将告一段落，这是该公司在过去几个月内达成的一项决定。 2021年版本将于10月首次亮相，它将是它的最后一部。它的发行量为4000万册，仅是五年前的20％。

Over the years, IKEA’s catalog served as map and vision board in equal measure. For many, there was a certain ritual associated with getting each year’s copy and envisioning what some of its items might look like in the home. With that sort of history, the company admits that the decision to discontinue the catalog was not made lightly.

多年来，宜家的产品目录同时充当着地图和愿景板的角色。对于许多人来说，获取每年的副本并设想其某些物品在家里看起来像是一种仪式。基于这样的历史，该公司承认终止该目录的决定并非轻描淡写。

“For both customers and co-workers, the IKEA Catalog is a publication that brings a lot of emotions, memories, and joy,” Konrad Grüss, managing director of IKEA franchise service provider Inter IKEA Systems B.V., told NPR. Ultimately, the company regarded the discontinuation as an “emotional but rational decision.”

宜家特许服务提供商Inter IKEA Systems B.V.的常务董事KonradGrüss告诉NPR：“《宜家目录》对顾客和同事来说都是一种情感，回忆和欢乐。”最终，该公司将停产视为“情感但理性的决定”。

In many respects, IKEA’s decision to discontinue its print catalog is a sign of these increasingly digital times. Everything from online shopping to social media and even augmented reality have come to replace print media as a source of inspiration, both in terms of IKEA’s business and commerce as a whole. A pandemic-induced shift to online furniture shopping likely factored into the catalog thought process as well: Reuters notes that IKEA’s online sales surged by 45% from September 2019 through August 2020. With IKEA’s website generating billions of hits and the IKEA Place augmented reality app garnering thousands of App Store reviews, there are simply other (arguably more cost-effective and sustainable) ways for the company to reach fans in search of inspiration.

从许多方面来看，宜家决定终止其印刷目录，这标志着数字时代的发展。从网上购物到社交媒体，甚至是增强现实，一切都已取代印刷媒体成为灵感的来源，无论是从宜家的商业还是整个商业角度而言。大流行引起的向在线家具购物的转变也可能会影响目录的思考过程：路透社指出，从2019年9月到2020年8月，宜家的在线销售量增长了45％。宜家的网站吸引了数十亿点击量，而宜家的增强现实应用程序公司获得了成千上万的App Store评论，公司还有其他（可以说更具成本效益和可持续发展）的方式来吸引粉丝以寻找灵感。

Though there won’t be a 2022 edition of the catalog, its legacy will live on. The online IKEA “museum” lets visitors browse every issue from 1951 onward, functioning as a one-of-a-kind time capsule for design lovers. According to NPR, IKEA also plans to release a catalog compendium book next fall for those who simply can’t live without physically flipping through those pages.

虽然不会有2022年的目录，但它的遗产将继续存在。在线的IKEA“博物馆”使游客可以浏览1951年以后的每期杂志，充当设计爱好者的独特时光胶囊。据NPR称，宜家还计划于明年秋天发布《目录汇编》书籍，以帮助那些无法亲身浏览这些页面的人。

So if you were planning on recycling your 2021 IKEA catalog soon, just know that it’s now a collector’s item worth holding on to. We won’t see a bulky collection of Swedish furniture items like it ever again.

因此，如果您打算尽快回收2021年宜家目录，请知道它现在是值得保留的收藏家物品。我们不会再有像瑞典这样庞大的家具收藏了。
