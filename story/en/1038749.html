Open source Internet-less IRC using LoRa, for disaster resilience
使用LoRa的开放源代码的无互联网IRC，以实现灾难恢复能力

2020-12-10 05:27:07

What if the internet and cell phone towers went down for more than a few hours - or during an emergency situation? No communication could lead to lost lives.

如果互联网和手机发射塔掉了几个小时以上-或在紧急情况下怎么办？任何交流都不会导致生命损失。

The end goal of the project is to have a widespread network able to handle low-bandwidth traffic (text, compressed images) for a large number of users, to fill gaps when the larger Internet is unavailable.

该项目的最终目标是拥有一个能够为大量用户处理低带宽流量（文本，压缩图像）的广泛网络，以在较大的Internet不可用时填补空白。

In the event of an emergency, the CellSol network, much like the Internet, can be used as a knowledge base, as well as a rally point, giving people a tool to use to coordinate and organize even if other communication systems go down. We intend to scale the design, with long-haul routing capabilities, so that regional networks can intercommunicate and interoperate, allowing for a wider breadth of use cases.

在紧急情况下，CellSol网络（类似于Internet）可以用作知识库和集会点，即使其他通信系统出现故障，也可以为人们提供一种用于协调和组织的工具。我们打算通过远程路由功能扩展设计规模，以便区域网络可以相互通信和互操作，从而允许更广泛的用例。

The overall design is a mesh network of LoRa devices, called “Pylons”that act as repeaters (extending the range of the network). Terminals (devices that users access the network with) also repeat packets, so that a networkmade up entirely of end users is possible.

总体设计是LoRa设备的网状网络，称为“塔”，可充当中继器（扩展了网络范围）。终端（用户用来访问网络的设备）也会重复发送数据包，从而使最终用户完全可以组成一个网络。

The two basic types of pylons are the ESP32 WiFi Pylon (a terminal device) and the Ardunio Repeater Pylon (a pure repeater, but can have bluetooth to use as a terminal).

两种主要的挂架类型是ESP32 WiFi挂架（终端设备）和Ardunio直放站挂架（纯中继器，但可以带有蓝牙用作终端）。

Pylons with more specialized uses, such as data repositories for local emergency resources (phone numbers, shelter locations, etc.) and knowledge bases (such as a Wikipedia mirror) are also intended to bedeveloped in the future, to add to the overall usefulness of the network.

未来还将开发具有更专门用途的塔架，例如本地应急资源的数据库（电话号码，避难所位置等）和知识库（例如Wikipedia镜像），以增加网络。
