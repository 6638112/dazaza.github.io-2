As most of you likely know, Librem 14 laptops are being shipped to customers. What you most likely don't know, unless you've been deep diving into threads on r/Purism and on the Purism forums, is that at least some of the Librem 14 hardware Purism is sending out appears to have been modified by somebody who is not Purism.
