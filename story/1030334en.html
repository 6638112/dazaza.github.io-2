TRIPS项目：显式数据图执行(EDGE)处理器

2020-10-22 08:48:29

The TRIPS project is developing a new class of technology-scalable, power efficient, high-performance microprocessor architectures called EDGE (Explicit Data Graph Execution) architectures. The first EDGE architecture is called TRIPS, and has been designed at the University of Texas at Austin. The TRIPS prototype will offer higher instruction-level concurrency, and thus higher potential performance, than current industrial processors, with no changes to the programming model. The TRIPS team is currently bringing-up first-silicon in the lab, and is developing the software needed to evaluate this new class of architectures on embedded, streaming, scientific, and desktop workloads. For more information, please send e-mail to cart@cs.utexas.edu.

TRIPS项目正在开发一类新的技术--可扩展、高能效、高性能的微处理器体系结构，称为EDGE(显式数据图执行)体系结构。第一个边缘架构称为TRIPS，由德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校设计。与当前的工业处理器相比，TRIPS原型将提供更高的指令级并发性，从而提供更高的潜在性能，而不会改变编程模型。TRIPS团队目前正在实验室开发First-Silicon，并正在开发针对嵌入式、流、科学和桌面工作负载评估这类新架构所需的软件。欲了解更多信息，请发送电子邮件至cart@cs.utexas.edu。
