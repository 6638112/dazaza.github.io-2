During WWDC 2020, Apple announced that they would be transitioning away from Intel and producing their own Apple Silicon chips for their Macs. The transition to all Mac computers running Apple Silicon chips would take two years.

在WWDC 2020期间，Apple宣布他们将远离英特尔并为其MAC生产自己的Apple Silicon芯片。运行Apple Silicon Chips的所有Mac计算机的过渡需要两年时间。

Fast forward to November 17, 2020, and Apple releases the MacBook Pro, MacBook Air, and Mac Mini models equipped with the M1 chip. The outside wasn’t drastically different; in fact; it was the same on the outside, the difference was the significant improvement in performance, cooling, and battery life. The M1 outperformed every Intel laptop within the same price range and performed similarly to the Mac Pro.

快进至2020年11月17日，Apple发布了配备M1芯片的MacBook Pro，MacBook Air和Mac Mini型号。外面没有彻底不同;实际上;外面是相同的，差异是性能，冷却和电池寿命的显着改善。 M1在同一价格范围内优于每个英特尔笔记本电脑，并与Mac Pro类似地执行。

When compared to previous models of the Mac, the battery life was significantly longer, and the cooling was exceptional. Because of Apple’s crazy optimization with the M1 chip, the new machines did not get hot like the Intel models, they did not use as much battery life because of the crazy optimization, and cooling was incredible because of the optimization. It had an eight- core CPU with four energy efficient cores and four performance cores. Depending on the programs that were running, it would use different cores.

与以前的Mac模型相比，电池寿命明显更长，冷却尤为卓越。由于Apple与M1芯片的疯狂优化，新机器没有像Intel型号一样热，因为由于疯狂的优化，他们没有使用尽可能多的电池寿命，并且由于优化而令人难以置信。它有一个八核CPU，具有四个能源有效的核心和四个性能核心。根据运行的程序，它将使用不同的核心。

This year, we’re expecting an M1X chip on the newer Macs along with more practicality. According to @dylandkit who’s an Apple analyst on Twitter states that we’re expecting an M1X chip Mac for the last quarter of 2021. One thing that is assured is that the M1X chip will deliver more performance than the current M1 chip. According to Macworld.co.uk, we can expect to see a 16-core-GPU compared to the eight cores on the MacBook Pro and Mac Mini, and the seven cores on the MacBook Air.

今年，我们预计较新的MAC上的M1X芯片以及更多的实用性。根据@Dylandkit谁是Twitter上的Apple分析师，我们期待了2021年的前一季度的M1X芯片Mac。保证的一件事是M1X芯片将提供比当前M1芯片更多的性能。根据MacWorld.co.uk的说法，与MacBook Pro和Mac Mini上的八个核心相比，我们可以期待一个16核-GPU，以及MacBook Air上的七个核心。

M1X is an extension of the M1 that will contain more thunderbolt channels, more cpu cores, more gpu cores, and greater power draw. The M1X will be featured in the Higher end Mac Mini, the Macbook Pro 14 and 16, and a higher end iMac.

M1x是M1的扩展，它将包含更多雷电通道，更多CPU核心，更多GPU核心和更大的功率绘制。 M1X将在高端Mac Mini，MacBook Pro 14和16中具有较高的IMAC。

More Thunderbolt ports can be expected on the new Macs. Current Macs only support two Thunderbolt ports, and more ports allow users to connect more Thunderbolt accessories without purchasing additional dongles or adapters.

在新MAC上可以预期更多Thunderbolt港口。当前MAC仅支持两个Thunderbolt端口，更多的端口允许用户连接更多的雷电配件而不购买额外的加密窗或适配器。

In conclusion, we’re expecting a faster processor, improved graphical performance due to increased GPU cores, and a larger 14” screen to replace the current 13” MacBook Pro.

总之，我们期待更快的处理器，提高了GPU内核增加的图形性能，以及更大的14英寸屏幕来更换当前的13“MacBook Pro。
