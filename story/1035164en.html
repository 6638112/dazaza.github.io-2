Go中的数据库比Rust中的数据库快6倍

2020-11-15 02:34:19

Yesterday, InfluxData released a new product called InfluxDB IOx written in Rust. That solution covers a few different cases compared to VictoriaMetrics but still can be used to store metrics. And as you know, we love to do benchmarks with other metrics storages ( influx, scylla, timescale).

昨天，InfluxData发布了一款名为InfluxDB IOx的新产品，它是用Rust编写的。与VictoriaMetrics相比，该解决方案涵盖了几种不同的情况，但仍可用于存储指标。正如您所知道的，我们喜欢使用其他指标存储(Inflx、Scylla、Timescale)进行基准测试。

We’re always curious about new solutions and approache s in the world of time series, and IOx caught our eye. We were impressed with the product design and introduction. And we wanted to compare to our product, so we did the simplest billy benchmark (see more details here) on a single instance with the following specs: Intel(R) Core(TM) i5–8265U CPU @ 1.60GHz 4 CPU, 32GB RAM, 1TB SSD

在时间序列的世界里，我们总是好奇新的解决方案和方法，IOx吸引了我们的眼球。产品的设计和介绍给我们留下了深刻的印象。我们想要与我们的产品进行比较，所以我们在单个实例上进行了最简单的BLY基准测试(请在此处查看更多详细信息)，其规格如下：英特尔(R)酷睿(TM)i5-8265U CPU@1.60 GHz 4 CPU、32 GB RAM、1 TB固态硬盘。

It is effortless to use, and we encourage the reader to make the test as well.

它使用起来毫不费力，我们也鼓励读者进行测试。

Starting from 1,005,343 rows/sec IOx ingesting performance dropped to 603,077 rows/sec and then remained stable/constant

从1,005,343行/秒开始，IOx接收性能降至603,077行/秒，然后保持稳定/恒定。
