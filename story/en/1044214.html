Pirate Bay Founder Thinks Parler's Inability To Stay Online Is 'Embarrassing'
海盗湾创始人认为Parler无法上网是“令人尴尬的”

2021-01-15 20:41:45

As one of the original co-founders of The Pirate Bay, Peter Sunde Kolmisoppi knows a little something about keeping controversial services online. Kolmisoppi and his colleagues spent decades battling a global coalition of corporations, governments, and law enforcement agencies intent on wiping the file sharing website from the face of the internet. Unsuccessfully.

作为The Pirate Bay的原始联合创始人之一，Peter Sunde Kolmisoppi对使有争议的服务保持在线状态有所了解。 Kolmisoppi和他的同事们花了数十年的时间与由公司，政府和执法机构组成的全球联盟进行斗争，这些联盟旨在从互联网上清除文件共享网站。不成功。

Kolmisoppi took to Twitter this week to share some thoughts on Parler’s recent deplatforming for failing to seriously police death threats and illegal content before and after the fatal Capitol riots.

科尔米索皮（Kolmisoppi）本周在Twitter上分享了一些关于Parler最近平台删除的想法，这些平台由于在致命的国会暴动之前和之后未能认真监控死亡威胁和非法内容。

“The Pirate Bay, the most censored website in the world, started by kids, run by people with problems with alcohol, drugs and money, still is up after almost two decades,” Kolmisoppi said. “Parlor and gab etc have all the money around but no skills or mindset. Embarrassing.”

Kolmisoppi说：“海盗湾是世界上受审查最多的网站，由儿童创立，由酗酒，吸毒和赚钱的人经营，但经过了近二十年的发展仍在继续。” “客厅和杂货店等都有钱，但没有技能或思维定势。不好意思。”

Parler, part of a growing echosphere for rightwing conspiracy theories, is primarily financed by hedge-fund billionaire Robert Mercer and his daughter Rebekah. But the company’s deep pockets haven’t been able to assuage the concerns of companies unwilling to do business with a toxic enabler of hate speech in the wake of last week’s violent riot. While Parler has found a new provider in the far-right enablers over at Epik, the service isn’t expected to get back online anytime soon, in large part because, according to CEO John Matze, companies with enough firepower to host the platform don’t want to be associated with bigotry.

帕勒（Parler）是右翼阴谋理论不断发展的回声圈的一部分，主要由对冲基金亿万富翁罗伯特·默瑟（Robert Mercer）和他的女儿瑞贝卡（Rebekah）资助。但是，在上周发生暴力骚乱之后，该公司财力雄厚，无法缓解公司不愿以有害的仇恨言论开展业务的担忧。尽管Parler在Epik的最右端的支持者中找到了新的提供商，但该服务预计不会很快恢复在线，这在很大程度上是因为首席执行官John Matze认为，拥有足够火力来托管该平台的公司并不愿意。不想与偏执狂相关联。

Platforming white supremacy and hate speech is a tougher proposition than serving users pirated copies of the Prince discography. But Kolmisoppi was quick to laugh at the fact that despite being backed by billionaires and parts of the US government, Parler didn’t seem remotely prepared for the justified firestorm it found itself at the center of.

与服务用户盗版Prince唱片相比，将白人至上和仇恨言论平台化是一个更艰难的主张。但是Kolmisoppi很快就笑出了这样一个事实：尽管得到了亿万富翁和美国政府一部分的支持，但Parler似乎并没有为在其中心发动的合理的暴风雨做好准备。

“The most ironic thing is that The Pirate Bay’s enemies include not just the US government but also many European and the Russian one,” he said. “Compared to gab/parlor which is supported by the current president of the US and probably liked by the Russian one too.”

他说：“最具有讽刺意味的是，海盗湾的敌人不仅包括美国政府，还包括许多欧洲和俄罗斯政府。” “与美国现任总统所支持的gab /客厅相比，俄罗斯人也可能喜欢。”

Over the years, Kolmisoppi and The Pirate Bay crew explored no limit of strategies to keep its servers operational and out of the reach of law enforcement and the entertainment industry, even when that meant hiding them in caves and submarines, or even using low-orbit drones to redirect users to hidden regional servers hosting torrent indexes and trackers.

多年来，Kolmisoppi和The Pirate Bay的工作人员一直在探索各种策略，以使其服务器保持运转并使其不受执法和娱乐业的影响，即使这意味着将其隐藏在洞穴和潜艇中，甚至使用低轨道无人机将用户重定向到托管种子索引和跟踪器的隐藏区域服务器。

The organizations shift to magnet links in 2012 helped make it more difficult to take offline. And despite numerous trials, fines, multi-country IP address blockades, police raids, and even a stretch in prison, the website he originally co-founded on a shoestring budget remains more or less operational. In contrast, Parler lost its primary cloud service provider and simply disappeared, seemingly incapable of any adaptation. Meanwhile, thanks to sloppy coding and substandard security standards, researchers are hard at work scraping publicly-available Parler data to help identify platform users who thought it might be fun to flirt with violent sedition on live television. “In all honesty, the reason we did The Pirate Bay was to bring freedom and take back control from a centralised system,” Kolmisoppi said. “The reason that Gab et al will fail is because they're just whining bitches that have only one ideology: egotism. Sharing is caring y'all.”

该组织在2012年转而使用磁链，这使得脱机更加困难。尽管经历了无数的审判，罚款，多国IP地址封锁，警察突袭，甚至是长期的监狱羁押，但他最初是靠很少的预算共同创建的网站仍然或多或少地在运作。相比之下，Parler失去了其主要的云服务提供商，并消失了，似乎无能为力。同时，由于草率的编码和不合标准的安全标准，研究人员正在努力收集公共可用的Parler数据，以帮助识别那些认为在直播电视上挑起暴力煽动性的平台用户的平台用户。 Kolmisoppi说：“说实话，我们做海盗湾的原因是要带来自由并从集中式系统中收回控制权。” “ Gab等人之所以失败，是因为他们只是抱怨那些只有一种意识形态的人：自负。分享是您的全部。”

In more recent years, Kolmoisoppi has moved on to fund Njalla, a privacy-centric domain name registration service. One he says was already asked to host Parler, and refused.

近年来，Kolmoisoppi已开始资助以隐私为中心的域名注册服务Njalla。他说一个已经被要求接待Parler，并拒绝了。

“Of course we wouldn't,” Kolmisoppi said. “We're pro human rights, which includes the right to not be killed by extreme right wing terrorists.”

“我们当然不会，”科密索皮说。 “我们是支持人权的人，其中包括不被极端右翼恐怖分子杀害的权利。”
