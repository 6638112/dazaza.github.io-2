Hawai'i could be 2nd state to legalize psychedelic mushrooms
夏威夷可能成为迷幻蘑菇合法化的第二州

2021-02-19 10:16:14

SB 738 would require the Department of Health (DOH) to set up treatment centers for the therapeutic administration of both substances, and create a review panel to assess the effects.

SB 738将要求卫生部（DOH）建立治疗中心以对这两种物质进行治疗性给药，并建立一个评估小组以评估其疗效。

Senator Stanley Chang (District 9 - Hawai‘i Kai, Kuli‘ou‘ou, Niu, ‘Aina Haina, Wai‘alae-Kahala and Diamond Head) has introduced a bill that would remove items from the list of Schedule I substances, legalizing psychedelic mushrooms.

参议员史丹利·张（Stanley Chang）（第9区-夏威夷凯，库里欧瓦，纽伊，艾娜·海娜，怀阿拉拉·卡哈拉和钻石头）提出了一项法案，该法案将从附表I物质清单中删除物品迷幻蘑菇。

The two substances are psilocybin and psilocyn.  SB 738  would also require the Department of Health (DOH) to set up treatment centers for the therapeutic administration of both substances, and create a review panel to assess the effects.

这两种物质是psilocybin和psilocyn。 SB 738还将要求卫生部（DOH）建立治疗中心以对这两种物质进行治疗性给药，并建立一个评估小组以评估其疗效。

“There is an increasing number of reputable studies that show how psilocybin and psilocyn can have promising results on people suffering from depression, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and addiction,” said Senator Chang. “People with these conditions need options and this bill would give them that while also ensuring that is stored and administered at licensed treatment facilities.”

Chang参议员说：“越来越多的著名研究表明psilocybin和psilocyn如何对患有抑郁症，创伤后应激障碍（PTSD）和成瘾的人产生令人鼓舞的结果。” “患有这些疾病的人需要选择，并且该法案将为他们提供保证，同时还要确保将其存储在有执照的治疗机构中并进行管理。”

Hawai'i would join Oregon as the second state to legalize psychedelic mushrooms if the measure is passed.

如果这项措施获得通过，夏威夷州将加入俄勒冈州，成为第二个使迷幻蘑菇合法化的州。
