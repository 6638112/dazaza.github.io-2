哈萨克斯坦，反其道而行之，将“Borat”拥护为尼斯

2020-10-27 00:56:53

In 2005, Dennis Keen, a high school junior in Los Angeles, was applying for a summer exchange program. After not much deliberation, he decided it would be punky and funny to forgo France and Spain and go to Kazakhstan. “People didn’t know where it was,” Mr. Keen said. “In Kazakhstan, there’s pre-Borat and post-Borat.”

2005年，洛杉矶高三学生丹尼斯·基恩正在申请一个暑期交换项目。在没有过多考虑之后，他决定放弃法国和西班牙，去哈萨克斯坦，这将是一件既朋克又有趣的事情。“人们不知道它在哪里，”基恩说。“在哈萨克斯坦，有前波拉特和后波拉特。”

He was referring, of course, to “Borat: Cultural Learnings of America for Make Benefit Glorious Nation of Kazakhstan,” the Sacha Baron Cohen comedy that hit theaters a year later, in 2006. In the movie, Mr. Cohen pretends to be a television reporter visiting America from the former Soviet republic, whose people supposedly drink horse urine, consider women property and celebrate an anti-Semitic version of the running of the bulls. (The bulls are replaced by Jews.) The authoritarian Kazakh government banned the film, threatened to sue Mr. Cohen and took out a four-page advertisement in this newspaper defending the country’s honor.

当然，他指的是一年后于2006年上映的萨沙·巴伦·科恩(Sacha Baron Cohen)喜剧“波拉特：美国为造福哈萨克斯坦光荣国家而获得的文化收益”(Borat：Culture Lcome of America for Make Benefits Nation of哈萨克斯坦)。在电影中，科恩假扮成一名来自前苏联共和国的电视记者访问美国，据说那里的人喝马尿，考虑妇女的财产，庆祝反犹版本的奔牛。(公牛被犹太人取代了。)。独裁的哈萨克斯坦政府禁止了这部电影，威胁要起诉科恩，并在本报上刊登了一则四页的广告，捍卫了国家的荣誉。

And so when Mr. Cohen released a trailer on Sept. 29 for a “Borat” sequel, which he developed in secret and which debuted on Friday on Amazon, the satirist was prepared for another fight with the Kazakh government.

因此，当科恩在9月9日发布预告片时。29为了他秘密开发的“Borat”续集，并于周五在亚马逊上首次亮相，这位讽刺作家准备与哈萨克斯坦政府进行另一场战斗。

It never came. “It was like, ‘Oh, again?’” said Kairat Sadvakassov, the deputy chairman of Kazakhstan’s tourism board, who has a master’s degree in tourism management from New York University. The board was determined to avoid overreacting and letting Mr. Cohen make it look foolish once again. “The decision was made to let it die its natural death and not respond,” Mr. Sadvakassov said.

它从来没有来过。“就像是，‘哦，又来了？’”拥有纽约大学旅游管理硕士学位的哈萨克斯坦旅游局副主席凯拉特·塞德卡索夫(Kairat SAdvakassov)说。董事会决心避免反应过度，让科恩再次让它看起来很愚蠢。“我们决定让它自然死亡，不作回应，”斯德瓦卡索夫说。

Then Mr. Keen, the former exchange student, got involved. After his time abroad, he went on to graduate school at Stanford, where he studied with a professor from Kazakhstan. Mr. Keen eventually moved to the country, married a local and started a business giving walking tours of Almaty, the country’s largest city. He now hosts a travel show on a state television channel. (“I’m kind of like the American Borat,” Mr. Keen said.)

然后，曾是交换生的基恩先生也参与了进来。在国外度过一段时间后，他继续在斯坦福大学研究生院学习，在那里他师从一位来自哈萨克斯坦的教授。基恩最终搬到了这个国家，嫁给了一位当地人，并开始了一项业务，提供该国最大城市阿拉木图的徒步旅行。他现在一家国家电视台主持一个旅游节目。(“我有点像美国的波拉特，”基恩说。)。

When Mr. Keen learned about the sequel, he thought that rather than ignore Mr. Cohen, Kazakhstan should embrace the Borat character’s catchphrase and turn it into the country’s tourism slogan: “Kazakhstan. Very nice!”

当基恩得知续集的消息时，他认为哈萨克斯坦应该接受波拉特这个角色的口号，把它变成该国的旅游口号：“哈萨克斯坦”，而不是忽视科恩。太好了！“。

It’s the kind of idea you get when you own a tourism company and a pandemic has annihilated global tourism. “I’ve had a lot of free time,” Mr. Keen said. “Also, I just had a baby. When he grows up, I don’t want him to be ashamed of Borat. I want him to say, ‘That’s when my dad started this whole fun project.’”

这是当你拥有一家旅游公司，而流行病已经摧毁了全球旅游业时，你会得到的那种想法。“我有很多空闲时间，”基恩先生说。“而且，我刚生了一个孩子。等他长大了，我不想让他以波拉特为耻。我想让他说，‘我爸爸就是从那时开始这个有趣的项目的。’“。

Two weeks ago, Mr. Keen and a friend, Yermek Utemissov, who helps foreign film companies arrange shoots in Kazakhstan, pitched the board of tourism. They got an immediate yes. The two worked pro bono to make four slickly produced, internet-friendly 12-second spots featuring people walking around Kazakhstan and observing that it’s “very nice.” In one, a man at a market drinks traditional fermented horse milk (not horse urine!) and says, “That’s actually very nice.”

两周前，基恩和帮助外国电影公司在哈萨克斯坦安排拍摄的朋友叶尔梅克·乌特米索夫(Yermek Utemissov)向旅游委员会推销。他们立刻得到了肯定的答复。两人无偿合作，制作了四个制作精美、适合互联网的12秒广告，特色是人们在哈萨克斯坦四处走动，并观察到那里“非常好”。在其中一个例子中，一名男子在市场上喝传统的发酵马奶(不是马尿！)。他说，“那真是太好了。”

The new movie, “Borat Subsequent Moviefilm: Delivery of Prodigious Bribe to American Regime for Make Benefit Once Glorious Nation of Kazakhstan,” doesn’t let up on stereotyping the nation. It starts with Borat doing hard labor in prison, explaining the fictional consequences of the original film in a voice-over: “Kazakhstan become laughing stocks around the world. Our exports of potassium and pubis plummet. Many brokers leapt from our tallest skyscrapers. Since Running of Jew had been canceled, all Kazakhstan had left was Holocaust Remembrance Day, where we commemorate our heroic soldiers who ran the camps.”

这部新电影“波拉特随后的电影：向美国政权提供巨额贿赂，以换取曾经光荣的哈萨克斯坦国家的利益”，并没有放松对这个国家的刻板印象。首先是波拉特在监狱里的苦役，在画外音中解释了原版电影的虚构后果：“哈萨克斯坦成了全世界的笑柄。我们的钾和耻骨出口量直线下降。许多经纪人从我们最高的摩天大楼上跳了下来。自从犹太人的逃亡被取消后，哈萨克斯坦只剩下大屠杀纪念日，在这个日子里，我们纪念我们管理营地的英雄战士。“。

But Mr. Utemissov said he wasn’t worried that his fellow citizens would get mad this time. “It’s a newer generation,” he said. “They’ve got Twitter, they’ve got Instagram, they’ve got Reddit, they know English, they know memes. They get it. They’re inside the media world. We’re looking at the same comedians, the same Kimmel show. Kazakhstan is globalized.”

但乌特米索夫说，他并不担心他的同胞这次会生气。“这是新一代，”他说。“他们有推特，他们有Instagram，他们有Reddit，他们懂英语，他们知道表情包。他们明白了。他们身处媒体界。我们看的是同样的喜剧演员，同样的Kimmel秀。哈萨克斯坦是全球化的。“。

Mr. Sadvakassov, the deputy chairman of the tourism board, hadn’t seen the movie before its premiere, but he said he wasn’t concerned, either. “In Covid times, when tourism spending is on hold, it was good to see the country mentioned in the media,” he said. “Not in the nicest way, but it’s good to be out there. We would love to work with Cohen, or maybe even have him film here.”

旅游局副主席斯德瓦卡索夫在该片首映前没有看过，但他说他也不担心。“在Covid时代，当旅游支出被搁置时，很高兴看到媒体提到这个国家，”他说。“虽然不是以最好的方式，但能站在那里感觉很好。我们很乐意与科恩合作，甚至可能让他在这里拍摄。“。

When Mr. Cohen learned that Kazakhstan had reversed itself and embraced his franchise, he offered a statement by email. “This is a comedy, and the Kazakhstan in the film has nothing to do with the real country,” he wrote. “I chose Kazakhstan because it was a place that almost nobody in the U.S. knew anything about, which allowed us to create a wild, comedic, fake world. The real Kazakhstan is a beautiful country with a modern, proud society — the opposite of Borat’s version.”

当科恩得知哈萨克斯坦已经改弦易辙，接受了他的特许经营权时，他通过电子邮件发表了一份声明。他写道：“这是一部喜剧，电影中的哈萨克斯坦与真实的国家没有任何关系。”“我选择哈萨克斯坦是因为它是一个几乎没有美国人知道的地方，这让我们创造了一个狂野的、滑稽的、虚假的世界。真正的哈萨克斯坦是一个美丽的国家，拥有一个现代的、引以为豪的社会--这与波拉特的说法正好相反。“
