Gas shortage: Gov. Cooper declares state of emergency for NC
天然气不足：甘娃宣布核武器的紧急状态

2021-05-11 12:29:40

In light of the recent cybersecurity attack that temporarily shut down one of the largest pipelines in the United States, Governor Roy Cooper on Monday signed an Executive Order declaring a state of emergency in North Carolina. The order alsotemporarily suspends motor vehicle fuel regulations to ensure adequate fuel supply supplies throughout the state. On May 7, 2021, the Colonial Pipeline system reported a ransomware cyberattack, resulting in a temporary shutdown of that line.
