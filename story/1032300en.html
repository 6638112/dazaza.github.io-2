巴西支付处理器Conductor筹集1.5亿美元，由Viking Global Investors牵头在拉丁美洲扩张

2020-10-30 23:41:39

SAO PAULO/NEW YORK (Reuters) - Brazilian payments firm Conductor has raised $150 million in a private funding round to expand its business in Latin America ahead of a possible stock market listing in the United States, Chief Executive Antonio Soares told Reuters.

圣保罗/纽约(路透社)-巴西支付公司Conductor首席执行官安东尼奥·苏亚雷斯告诉路透社，该公司已在一轮私人融资中筹集了1.5亿美元，以扩大其在拉美的业务，然后可能在美国上市。

The fundraising underscores the expectation that financial technology companies can grow as more individuals in the region access the banking system.

此次融资突显了人们的预期，即随着该地区更多个人进入银行系统，金融科技公司可以发展壮大。

Backed by investment firm Riverwood Capital and payments processor Visa Inc V.N , Conductor provides payments services and software to companies including fintech businesses and retailers. It has 30 million active users and processes 1.5 billion transactions per year, according to Conductor's website.

在投资公司Riverwood Capital和支付处理器Visa Inc.V.N的支持下，Conductor为包括金融科技企业和零售商在内的公司提供支付服务和软件。根据Conductor网站的数据，它拥有3000万活跃用户，每年处理15亿笔交易。

“The electronic payments industry in Latin America is some five to 10 years behind Brazil and regulation in the region is pursuing more competition,” Soares said in an interview.

苏亚雷斯在一次采访中说：“拉丁美洲的电子支付行业比巴西落后大约5到10年，该地区的监管机构正在寻求更多的竞争。”

The funding round is led by Viking Global Investors and also includes Sunley House Capital, an affiliate of Advent International.

这轮融资由维京全球投资者牵头，还包括Advent International的子公司Sunley House Capital。

Proceeds will be used to expand Conductor’s reach in Latin America, both through its own growth and by acquiring companies. Conductor started its international expansion this year and now has businesses in Mexico, Peru, Colombia, Argentina and Ecuador.

收益将用于扩大Conductor在拉丁美洲的影响力，无论是通过其自身的增长还是通过收购公司。Conductor今年开始进行国际扩张，目前在墨西哥、秘鲁、哥伦比亚、阿根廷和厄瓜多尔都有业务。

Much of Latin America’s population does not have a bank account, creating opportunities for non-traditional finance firms, Soares said. Around one-third of Brazilians do not have a bank account, one of the highest ratios in the world.

苏亚雷斯说，拉丁美洲的大部分人口没有银行账户，这为非传统金融公司创造了机会。大约三分之一的巴西人没有银行账户，这是世界上比例最高的国家之一。

Soares declined to disclose Conductor’s valuation following the round, but said Riverwood remains the controlling shareholder.

苏亚雷斯拒绝透露Conductor在这轮融资后的估值，但表示Riverwood仍是控股股东。

Soares billed the fundraising as probably the company’s last before it undertakes an initial public offering (IPO) in the United States.

苏亚雷斯称，此次融资可能是该公司在美国进行首次公开募股(IPO)之前的最后一次融资。

Should Conductor list on a U.S. exchange, it would follow in the footsteps of other Brazilian payments companies including StoneCo Ltd STNE.O and PagSeguro Digital Ltd PAGS.N . Both have performed well since listing in 2018, gaining 131% and 74%, respectively.

如果Conductor在美国交易所上市，它将追随其他巴西支付公司的脚步，包括StoneCo Ltd STNE.O和PagSeguro Digital Ltd PAGS.N。两者自2018年上市以来均表现不俗，分别上涨131%和74%。
