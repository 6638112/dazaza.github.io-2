Google Photos应用程序中的代码显示，Google将为Google One订户添加新的编辑功能和过滤器

2020-11-07 14:19:23

The primary reason that you may decide to have a Google One subscription is to get extra storage for Google Drive and other apps, but the service has other benefits. Google Photos has now begun to lock some of its editing features behind a Google One paywall for some.

你决定订阅Google One的主要原因可能是为Google Drive和其他应用程序获得额外的存储空间，但这项服务还有其他好处。谷歌照片(Google Photos)现在已经开始将一些编辑功能锁定在Google One的付费墙后面。

A little over a month ago, Google Photos got a redesigned editor, meant to coincide with the launch of the Pixel 5 and Pixel 4a 5G. This redesign made the editor’s tools a little easier to find and use, and it also enabled the launch of new Pixel-exclusive tools like Portrait Light.

一个多月前，谷歌照片(Google Photos)得到了一个重新设计的编辑器，这是为了配合Pixel 5和Pixel 4a 5G的发布。这种重新设计使得编辑器的工具更易于查找和使用，也使得新的Pixel专属工具(如肖像灯光)的推出成为可能。

It looks like Google has plans to bring more functionality to the photo and video editor, but it seems not all of these features will be available for free. Our APK Insight team spotted some new texts in the latest update to the Google Photos app that point to “extra editing features” that will be locked behind a Google One paywall.

谷歌似乎计划为照片和视频编辑器带来更多功能，但似乎并不是所有这些功能都是免费的。我们的APK Insight团队在Google Photos应用程序的最新更新中发现了一些新的文本，这些文本指向将被锁定在Google One付费墙后面的“额外编辑功能”。

As tipped to XDA’s Mishaal Rahman, some have already begun to see Google Photos lock some features like Color Pop behind a Google One membership.

据XDA的Mishaal Rahman透露，一些人已经开始看到Google Photos锁定了Google One会员资格背后的一些功能，比如Color Pop。

Overall, this is a fairly disappointing change as Color Pop has been available for free on photos taken in Portrait mode for a few years now, but now it’s locked behind a paywall. For what it’s worth, it seems that any level of Google One is enough to unlock the full suite of editing features, as the first screenshot specifically advertises the cheapest £1.59 plan.

总体而言，这是一个相当令人失望的变化，因为Colour Pop在肖像模式下拍摄的照片已经免费提供了几年，但现在它被锁定在付费墙后面。无论它的价值是什么，似乎任何级别的Google One都足以解锁整套编辑功能，因为第一张屏幕截图特别宣传了最便宜的1.59 GB套餐。

Update 4:00pm: Google has clarified to Droid Life that the “Color Pop” seen in the screenshot above is not the same Color Pop as available in Google Photos today. Right now in Google Photos, Color Pop is only available on photos taken in portrait mode, meaning there is depth information available, which is especially helpful in making the foreground of an image pop.

下午4点更新：谷歌已经向Droid Life澄清，上面截图中的“彩色弹出物”与今天谷歌照片中的“彩色弹出物”不是同一种颜色弹出物。目前在Google Photos中，Color Pop只在肖像模式下拍摄的照片上可用，这意味着有深度信息可用，这对使图像的前景流行特别有帮助。

The version of Color Pop that will be locked behind Google One will work on photos without depth information. Likely this version attempts to use machine learning to automatically differentiate the foreground from the background.

将被锁定在Google One后面的Color Pop版本将在没有深度信息的照片上运行。很可能这个版本试图使用机器学习来自动区分前景和背景。

More importantly, this means that Google Photos will not be putting an existing feature behind a Google One paywall. Instead, it seems Google intends to create new features to incentivize Google One subscribers. We’ve striked out the incorrect information above, accordingly.

更重要的是，这意味着Google Photos将不会把现有功能放在Google One付费墙后面。相反，谷歌似乎打算创造新的功能来激励Google One的订户。因此，我们删除了上面不正确的信息。

For the time being, it seems this Google One paywall for Google Photos is only being tested in a limited capacity, as we’ve yet to confirm the test on any of our devices and have not seen any reports from our readers. If you have these Google One limitations on your device, let us know in the comments what phone you have and what all is locked.

目前，Google One的Google Photos付费墙似乎只是在有限的容量下进行测试，因为我们还没有确认在我们的任何设备上进行测试，也没有看到读者的任何报告。如果你的设备上有Google One的这些限制，请在评论中告诉我们你有什么手机，什么都被锁定了。
