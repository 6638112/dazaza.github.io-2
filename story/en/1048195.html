Electricity over $900/MWh in most of Western US now
现在美国西部大部分地区的电价超过$ 900 / MWh

2021-02-18 10:53:16

The ISO wholesale power market prices electricity based on the cost of generating and delivering it from particular grid locations called nodes. One energy market runs the day before the energy is needed (day ahead market), while another one runs in real-time to balance last minute demand needs.

ISO批发电力市场根据从称为节点的特定电网位置产生和输送电力的成本对电力进行定价。一个能源市场在需要能源的前一天运行（比市场提前一天），而另一个能源市场实时运行以平衡最后一刻的需求需求。

Switch between the Day-ahead, Real-time pre-dispatch and Real-time dispatch locational marginal price (LMP) maps using the drop down menu at the top of the map.  Prices are shown in megawatt-hours ($/MWh).

使用地图顶部的下拉菜单，在提前，实时预调度和实时调度位置边际价格（LMP）地图之间切换。价格以兆瓦时（$ / MWh）显示。

Change between maps using the drop down menu on the top left corner of the map

使用地图左上角的下拉菜单在地图之间切换

Zoom in and out using the zoom menu in the lower right corner of the map or with your mouse controls

使用地图右下角的缩放菜单或使用鼠标控件进行放大和缩小
