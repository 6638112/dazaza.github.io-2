Netflix's Dominance Starts to Slow as Streaming Rivals Gain
随着流竞争对手的增益，Netflix的主导地位开始缓慢

2021-04-26 16:09:41

Netflix still rules the streaming universe. As of the end of March, it had 207.6 million total paying subscribers, with about 67 million in the United States, the company noted in an earnings report on Tuesday.

netflix仍然统治流宇宙。截至3月底，它有20760万总支付用户，在美国汇率约为6700万，周二收益报告。

But its main competitors — Disney+, HBO Max, Paramount+ and AppleTV+, as well as the old-guard streamers Amazon Prime Video and Hulu — have cut into Netflix’s share of viewers’ attention.

但它的主要竞争对手 - 迪士尼+，HBO Max，Paramount +和AppleTV +，以及旧护卫垃圾媒体亚马逊峰值视频和Hulu - 已切入Netflix的观众的关注。

The global demand for original Netflix programs, like “Bridgerton,” the much buzzed-about romance series from the super-producer Shonda Rhimes, has started to drop relative to similar offerings from newcomers, according to the data firm Parrot Analytics, which has developed a metric to rate not only the number of viewers for given shows, but their likelihood of attracting subscribers to a streaming service.

根据“Bridgerton”，与“Bridgerton”的全球需求，如“Bridgerton，”Bridgerton，关于Super-Pronda Shonda Rhimes的浪漫系列，这已经开始逐渐下降到新人的类似产品，根据数据公司鹦鹉分析，这已经开发出来度量标准不仅是给定节目的观众的数量，而且对吸引用户吸引到流服务的可能性。

In its latest rankings, Parrot reported that Netflix’s share of total demand — a measure of the popularity of its shows — was slightly above 50 percent for the first three months of the year, compared with 54 percent a year ago and 65 percent in the first quarter of 2019.

在其最新排名中，Parrot报道，Netflix的总需求份额 - 这一节目普及的衡量标准 - 年前三个月略高于50％，而略高于今年的前三个月以上，而不是每年54％，而第一次65％ 2019年季度。

That showed up in the numbers. For the first quarter of 2021, Netflix reported the addition of four million new customers, below the six million it had forecast. The company expects to add only one million new customers for this current quarter ending in June.

这在数字中出现了。在2021年的第一季度，Netflix报告增加了400万新客户，低于它预测的600万。该公司预计将为6月份的本季度增加100万新客户。

Netflix shares plummeted about 10 percent in after-hours trading on Tuesday, after the earnings announcement.

收入公告后，Netflix股价在周二的余额交易中暴跌了大约10％。

“Are we sure it’s not competition? Because obviously there’s a lot of new competition,” Reed Hastings, the company’s co-chief executive along with Ted Sarandos, said on the earnings call after the report. “It’s intensely competitive, but it always has been. We’ve been competing with Amazon Prime for 13 years, with Hulu for 14 years.” He added: “So there’s no real change that we can detect in the competitive environment.”

“我们确定它不竞争吗？由于显然存在很多新的竞争，“公司的共同行政长官和TED Sarandos公司的芦苇黑斯廷斯在报告结束后发表了。 “这是强烈的竞争力，但它一直都是。我们一直在竞争13年的亚马逊素数，拥有Hulu 14年。“他补充说：“所以我们无法在竞争环境中检测到真正的变化。”

Netflix pulled back on productions during the pandemic, which has now rippled into its release schedule. The company didn’t have any big returning series in the period.

Netflix在大流行期间退回了产品，现在已经波荡到其发布时间表中。该公司在此期间没有任何重大回报系列。

“We’ll get back to much steadier state in the back half of the year,” Mr. Sarandos said, citing the return of popular series like “The Witcher” and “You.”

“我们将在一年的后半部分回到多大的状态，”萨兰多斯先生说，抓住了像“巫师”和“你”这样的流行系列的回归。

Netflix also raised prices in October, increasing its standard plan by a dollar to $14 a month. It added an extra $2 to its premium tier, which now costs $18. The company typically increases its fees about every 18 months. It is also trying to clamp down on password sharing, long a common practice.

Netflix还在10月份提高了价格，将标准计划增加一美元至每月14美元。它增加了额外2美元的优质级，现在需要18美元。该公司通常每18个月增加其费用。它还试图钳位密码共享，漫长的常见做法。

Last year, in the same period, just as the pandemic was underway, the company added a record 15.7 million subscribers.

去年，在同一时期，正如大流行的那样，该公司增加了创纪录的1570万用户。

As much of the world went into lockdown, people turned to screens to while away the hours. Netflix recorded a jump in new sign-ups, leading to a record year of nearly 37 million additional customers. The company is unlikely to repeat that performance for 2021 as restaurants, stores, theaters and sports stadiums start opening up to full capacity across the country.

正如世界上的大多数人进入锁定，人们转向屏幕，距离几个小时。 Netflix记录了新注册的跳跃，导致近3700万客户的历史年。该公司不太可能重复2021年的绩效，作为餐馆，商店，剧院和体育场馆开始开放全国各地的全部能力。

But Netflix is an international business. The majority of its revenues now come from overseas, and it has banked its future growth on emerging markets such as India and Latin America. Those regions have had recent surges in coronavirus cases, prompting new lockdowns. Most of the world, including Europe, has not vaccinated its citizens as quickly as the United States.

但Netflix是一家国际业务。现在的大部分收入来自海外，它已经在印度和拉丁美洲等新兴市场的未来增长。这些地区最近在冠状病毒病例中飙升，促使新的锁定。在内的大多数世界，包括欧洲，并没有像美国那样快速地疫苗。

Netflix is still spending big. It spent $465 million to buy two sequels to the hit whodunit “Knives Out,” a price tag 50 percent higher than the first film’s gross receipts. It’s also 10 times what the film cost to produce. Hollywood lit up with chatter. Did Netflix overpay?

Netflix仍在很大。它花了4.65亿美元来购买两个续集到击中Whodunit“刀片”，价格标签比第一部电影总收入高50％。这也是电影成本生产的10倍。好莱坞用喋喋要紧了。 netflix多付了吗？

The film’s director, Rian Johnson, came up with the idea for the film, and he and his producing partner control the rights. The lucrative deal is in keeping with Netflix’s expensive courtship of Hollywood creators. It has nine-figure agreements with prolific television producers including Ms. Rhimes and Ryan Murphy, as well as the actor-producer Adam Sandler. Mr. Johnson could join their ranks by creating additional series and films for the company.

这部电影董事RIAN JOHNSON提出了这部电影的想法，他和他的制作合作伙伴控制了权利。利润丰厚的交易是与Netflix昂贵的好莱坞创作者求爱。它具有与多产电视生产商协议，包括Rhimes女士和Ryan Murphy女士以及演员制作人Adam Sandler。约翰逊先生可以通过为公司创建其他系列和电影来加入他们的行列。

Despite Netflix’s push into owning its own content, it recently entered into a distribution agreement with Sony Pictures Entertainment, the last major Hollywood studio not tied to any streaming business. Netflix will have rights to some Marvel franchises, including the Sony-controlled “Spider-Man,” and several offshoots based on the character.

尽管Netflix推入拥有自己的内容，但它最近与Sony Pictures Entertainment签订了分销协议，最后一个主要的好莱坞工作室没有与任何流媒体业务联系在一起。 Netflix将拥有一些奇迹特许经营权，包括索尼控制的“蜘蛛侠”，以及基于角色的几个分支。

The company reported profit of $1.7 billion on revenue of $7.16 billion for the first quarter. Investors were looking to $1.3 billion in profit on $7.1 billion in sales.

该公司据报道，第一季度收入为71.6亿美元的收入。投资者在71亿美元的销售额上营造了13亿美元。

In addition, the board of directors approved a $5 billion stock buyback plan, which should lower the number of available shares in circulation, potentially making them more valuable.

此外，董事会批准了50亿美元的股票回购计划，该计划应降低流通中可用股份的数量，可能使其更有价值。

Although competitors are gaining ground, Netflix is in its best financial shape of its history. It hit a milestone at the end of last year, when it said it would no longer look to borrow money to fund its content slate. Another way to look at it: Netflix finally became a truly profitable business after topping 200 million subscribers, each paying an average of $11 a month.

虽然竞争对手正在获得地面，但Netflix是其历史上最好的财务形态。它在去年年底击中了一个里程碑，当时它说它不再希望借钱来资助其内容石板。看它的另一种方式：Netflix最终成为一个真正的有利可图的业务，在最大的200万用户之后，每个人每月支付11美元。
