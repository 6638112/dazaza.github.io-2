Google announces updates to Meet, rolling out next month on desktop, including a new UI, improved presentation features, and background replacement
Google宣布会面的更新，下个月在桌面上推出，包括新的UI，改进的演示功能和背景更换

2021-04-22 11:56:50

Google is refreshing its Meet user interface next month for desktop and laptop users. The new Meet for web interface includes improvements to video feeds, how you view or present meetings, and the navigation bar. While some of Google’s changes are playing catch-up to what’s offered in Zoom or Microsoft Teams, they are much-needed additions.

Google正在刷新它在下个月迎接其符合桌面和笔记本电脑用户的用户界面。新的Web界面的新见面包括对视频馈送的改进，您如何查看或呈现会议以及导航栏。虽然谷歌的一些更改正在追赶到Zoom或Microsoft团队中提供的内容，但它们是急需的补充。

If you’re fed up of seeing your own face during calls, you’ll soon be able to minimize or hide it entirely. New options will also include the ability to place your own video feed in a tile in the grid of meeting participants, or a floating picture that can be resized and repositioned.

如果您在呼叫期间看到自己的脸部，您将很快能够最大限度地减少或完全隐藏。新选项还将包括在会议参与者网格中将自己的视频进料放置在会议参与者网格中，或可以调整大小和重新定位的浮动图片。

If you’re busy presenting, Google is also improving how content is pinned and unpinned. You’ll soon be able to quickly unpin a presentation tile, and it resizes into the same size as other participants so you can see more people on a call or figure out if people were impressed by your slick slide transitions. In the coming months, Google Meet will also let participants pin multiple video feeds during a call.

如果您忙于呈现，谷歌也在提高内容覆盖和取消划分的内容。您将很快能够快速取消播放瓷砖，而且它将与其他参与者相同的大小调整大小，以便您可以在呼叫中查看更多人，如果您的光滑幻灯片过渡留下了深刻的印象，您可以在呼叫或弄清楚。在未来几个月内，Google会议也将让参与者在呼叫中引脚多个视频源。

One of the most useful changes is improvements to the bottom bar of meetings to put all controls in a single place. That means chat, attachments, and the participants list are all at the bottom so there’s more space to see people and whatever is being presented during a call. Google is also moving the end call button away from the camera and microphone buttons so you don’t accidentally leave a call instead of mute.

最有用的更改之一是对会议底部的底部条形图的改进，以便在一个地方放置所有控制。这意味着聊天，附件，和参与者列表都在底部，所以有更多的空间来看人们以及在呼叫期间呈现的任何东西。谷歌也将距离相机和麦克风按钮远离相机和麦克风按钮，因此您不会意外留下电话而不是静音。

Alongside these UI improvements, Google is bringing its low-light mode from Meet on mobile over to the web version. This feature will automatically adjust your webcam video to make you more visible if you’re in a dark room. It will also adjust your video if you have too much light blasting in from a window behind you on a sunny day. This light adjustment feature will be available to all Meet users on the web in the coming weeks.

除了这些UI改进之外，谷歌正在将其低光模式从Mobile On Web版本带到Web版本。此功能将自动调整网络摄像头视频，如果您在黑暗的房间里，请使您更加明显。如果您在阳光灿烂的日子里有太多的窗户，它也会调整您的视频。在未来几周内，所有灯具调整功能将可用于网上的所有符合用户。

Google Workspace (paid) subscribers will also get access to a new autozoom feature in Google Meet in the coming months. This AI-powered feature will follow you and position you squarely in front of your camera if you move around. Google is also adding video background replacement to Meet in the coming weeks, allowing you to pick between a classroom, a party, and a forest to replace your background. More video backgrounds will be available in the future, too.

Google Workspace（已支付）订阅者还将在未来几个月内获得Google在Google中的新自动组织功能。如果您四处走动，此AI驱动功能将跟随您并在相机前方放置在相机前面。谷歌还在未来几周内添加视频背景更换，以便您在课堂，派对和森林之间挑选以取代您的背景。更多视频背景也将在未来使用。
