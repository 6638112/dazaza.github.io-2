Why did 11 days disappear in 1752?
为什么11天在1752年消失了？

2021-04-10 11:47:22

Augusta Genealogical Society People living in Britain, America and other English colonies went to sleep on the night of Sept. 2, 1752, and when they woke up the next morning it was Sept. 14, 1752. Because the people thought the government was trying to cheat them out of 11 days of their lives, there were riots in villages. Eleven days (Sept. 3-13) were cut from the calendar, deleting them forever. These days simply never existed – no births, no marriages, no deaths.

奥古斯塔族古社会 生活在英国，美国和其他英国殖民地的人们在9月2日，1752年的夜晚睡了，当时第二天早上醒来，它是1752年9月14日的9月14日。因为人民认为政府正试图欺骗他们在他们生命的11天中，村里有骚乱。从日历中切断了十一天（9月3日至13日），永远删除它们。这些天根本从未存在 - 没有出生，没有婚姻，没有死亡。

This was very confusing by itself, but added to this change was that New Year moved from March 25 to Jan. 1. Think how confusing this must have been to people used to thinking about a year running from March 25 to March 24, now they had to get used to the year running from Jan. 1 to Dec. 31. Imagine – a person could have been married on April 26, 1710 and died on Feb 2, 1710.

这本身非常令人困惑，但增加了这一变化，这是新的一年从3月25日到1月开始搬家。想想这一定令人困惑的是曾经思考过一年从3月25日到3月24日跑的人，现在他们不得不习惯从1月1日至12月到12月31日的年度习惯。想象力 - 一个人可以于1710年4月26日结婚，并于1710年2月2日去世。

Among the last countries in the world to accept that they were using an inaccurate calendar were the British. On the Julian calendar, named after Julius Caesar, the year would be 365 days and 6 hours long. That calendar was officially adopted in 325 A.D.

在世界上的最后一个国家，接受他们正在使用不准确的日历是英国人。在朱利安历法上，以朱利叶斯凯撒之后的命名，这一年将是365天和6个小时长。该日历在325 A.D中正式采用。

This Julian system, based on the movement of Earth around the sun, created a 365-day calendar year with a leap year every four years and a New Year’s date of Jan. 1. The system was widely adopted, but over time the New Year slipped to March 25 to correlate with the Christian holiday, Annunciation Day (March 25 is nine months prior to Christmas).

这个朱利安制度基于太阳周围地球的运动，创造了365天的日历年，每四年闰年和1月的新年日期。该系统被广泛采用，但随着时间的推移新的一年向3月25日滑倒，与基督教假期相关联，通告日（3月25日为圣诞节前九个月）。

As it became possible to measure the length of the solar year more accurately, astronomers found that the Julian system exceeded the solar year by 11 minutes, or 24 hours every 131 years, and three days every 400 years. This excess amounted to 10 days between 325 A.D. and 1582 A.D.

由于可以更准确地测量太阳年的长度，天文学家发现，朱利安系统每年超过太阳年超过太阳年，或每131岁，每400年3天。超过325 A.D.和1582 A.D之间的过量约为10天。

Several centuries later, Europe begins to realize that the Julian calendar system had not perfectly calculated leap years and had caused the calendar dates to become out of sync with celestial and religious events.

几个世纪以后，欧洲开始意识到朱利安日历系统并没有完全计算闰年，并导致日历日期与天体和宗教事件变得不同步。

In 1582, Pope Gregory XIII refined the Julian calendar mathematically to fix this mistake and created a new system that we now know as the Gregorian calendar. Most of the world jumped forward by 10 days on Oct. 5, 1582, restoring the vernal equinox to March 21.

1582年，教皇格雷戈里XIII在数学上改进了朱利安日历来解决这个错误，并创建了一个我们现在所知道的新系统作为格雷戈里亚日历。世界上大多数世界上大多数10天向前跃升了1582年的10天，恢复了3月21日的春季股份。

But England, unhappy with the Church of Rome, refused to go along with the new calendar. In the mid-18th century the difference had grown to 11 days. All British lands except Scotland, which changed its calendar 100 years before, now celebrated New Year's Day on Jan. 1.

但英格兰，对罗马教堂不满，拒绝与新日历一起去。在18世纪中叶，差异已经成长为11天。除苏格兰外，所有的英国土地都在苏格兰改变了100年前的日历，现在庆祝了新的一年＆＃39; 1月1日。

Between 1582 and 1752 BOTH calendars, old and new style, were used in Europe in different locations – causing some rather perplexing dates for future researchers.

在1582年至1752年间，在欧洲在不同地点使用的日历，旧的和新风格 - 导致未来研究人员的一些相当令人困惑的日期。

But changing calendars left everyone with a problem. Since the Gregorian calendar accounted more accurately for leap years, it was 11 days ahead of the Julian calendar by 1752. To correct this discrepancy and align all dates, 11 days had to be dropped when the switch was made.

但更换日历让每个人都有一个问题。由于格雷戈里亚日历更加准确地占闰年，因此在朱利安日历提前11天，以1752年提前11天。为纠正这种差异并对齐所有日期，在制作开关时必须丢弃11天。

Dec. 31, 1750, was followed by Jan. 1, 1750 (under the “Old Style” calendar, December was the 10th month and January the 11th) March 24, 1750, was followed by March 25, 1751 (March 25 was the first day of the “Old Style” year) Dec. 31, 1751, was followed by Jan. 1, 1752 (the switch from March 25 to Jan. 1 as the first day of the year) Sept. 2, 1752, was followed by Sept. 14, 1752 (drop of 11 days to conform to the Gregorian calendar) This means that Sept. 3-13 simply never existed for those in the Julian calendar world – including those in Great Britain and Colonial America.

1750年12月31日，后续1750年1月1日（根据“旧式”历史，12月份，十一月十一月十一月十一日），1750年3月24日，随后于1751年3月25日（3月25日） “旧式”年的第一天）于1751年12月31日，遵循于1752年1月1日（2013年3月25日至1月1日）作为一年中的第一天的交换机）9月2日，被关注截至1752年9月14日（符合GREGorian历法的11天下降）这意味着9月3日至13日，对于朱利安日历世界的人来说，从未存在过 - 包括英国和殖民地的人。

Someone born on Jan. 26, 1696, as an example, might have their birth date recorded as Jan. 26, 1696/97 or 96-97 to show that, according to the old system, that person was born in 1696 (since the new year had not yet started) but according to the new style, they were born in 1697 (since the new year started on Jan. 1). This practice is known as double dating.

1996年1月26日出生的某人，作为一个例子，可能会录制为1月26日，1696/97或96-97，以表明，根据旧系统，该人出生于1696年（自以来新的一年尚未开始），但根据新风格，他们出生于1697年（自2018年1月1日开始新的一年以来。这种做法被称为双倍约会。

Despite the official calendar, people in England and the colonies began to use the Gregorian system as early as the 16th century. Thus, many early colonial records include double dates, written as "12 February 1661/1662," indicating that, although it was officially 1661, some considered it to be 1662.

尽管官方日历，英格兰的人和殖民地早在16世纪开始使用Gregorian系统。因此，许多早期的殖民记录包括双重日期，写作＆＃34; 12月12日1661/1662年12月12日，＆＃34;表明，尽管它是正式的1661，但有些人认为这是1662年。

Genealogists, especially those just starting out on their quest for ancestors, need to double-check dates found in English-speaking countries between 1582 and 1752. Are these dates listed as O.S. (Old Style) or N.S. (New Style)? Is there a date listed as 1750/51? That means it would have probably been between Jan. 1 and March 24, which means that 1750 is the old-style notation and 1751, the new one. These double dates occur only in JANUARY, FEBRUARY and MARCH – never in any other months and never after 1752.

家谱学家，特别是那些刚刚开始追求祖先的人，需要在1582年和1752年的英语国家/地区发现的审查日期。这些日期列为O.S. （旧式）或N.S. （新风格）？有一个列为1750/51的日期吗？这意味着它可能在1月1日和3月24日之间，这意味着1750年是旧式符号和1751年，这是新的。这些双日期仅在1月，2月和3月出现 - 从未在任何其他几个月内，从未在1752年之后。
