这就是通过的过程：大多数垃圾邮件注册都会被检测到并被忽略，然后再进入数据库。

A huge number of spam emails I recieve in my personal inbox originate from@gmail.com, and often they arrive in my inbox unscathed (as opposed to going toJunk) because Gmail is considered a reputable mail provider. My colleagueestimates that between 15% and 25% of the spam emails sent to a mailing list headministrates comes from Gmail.

我在我的个人收件箱中收到的大量垃圾邮件都来自@ gmail.com，通常它们毫发无损地到达我的收件箱（与去垃圾邮件相反），因为Gmail被认为是信誉良好的邮件提供商。我的同事估计，发送到邮件列表的垃圾邮件中，有15％至25％的邮件中有头目来自Gmail。

One might argue that, because Gmail is the world’s largest email provider, it’snatural to expect that they would have the largest volume of spam simply becausethey have proportionally more users who might use it for spam. I would arguethat this instead tells us that they have the largest responsibility to curtailspam on their platform.

有人可能会争辩说，由于Gmail是世界上最大的电子邮件提供商，很自然地希望他们会收到最多的垃圾邮件，这仅仅是因为它们有成比例的用户可能会将其用作垃圾邮件。我认为这反而告诉我们，他们最大的责任是减少平台上的垃圾邮件。

I’ve forwarded many, many reports to abuse@gmail.com, but they’ve never followedup and the problem has not become any better. I have had half a mind to blockGmail registrations on sourcehut outright, but about 41% of all registrationsuse Gmail.

我已经将许多报告转发给了滥用@ gmail.com，但是他们从未跟进，问题也没有得到改善。我全心全意地阻止了Sourcehut上的Gmail注册，但是大约41％的注册使用Gmail。

It bears repeating that anyone with any level of technical expertise ought toknow better than to use Gmail. I usually recommend Migadu 1, but there are many options to choose from.If you’re worried about mail deliverability issues, don’t be — it’s moreor less a myth in $CURRENTYEAR. If you set up DKIMproperly and unlist your IP address from the DNSBLs (a simple process), then your mailswill get through.

值得一提的是，具有任何技术专门知识的任何人都应该比使用Gmail更了解。我通常会推荐Migadu 1，但有很多选项可供选择。如果您担心邮件的可传递性问题，请不要担心–它或多或少是$ CURRENTYEAR的神话。如果正确设置DKIM并从DNSBL中取消列出IP地址（一个简单的过程），则您的邮件将通过。

In case you’re wondering, the dis-award for second-worst goes to Amazon SES.They don’t register on sourcehut (it’s outgoing only, so that makes sense), butI see them often in my personal inbox. However, SES only appears at a rate ofabout a tenth of the gmail spam, and they appear to actually listen to my abusereports, so I can more or less forgive them for it.

万一您想知道，第二坏的建议是转到Amazon SES。它们不会在sourcehut上注册（仅在发送中，这样才有意义），但我经常在我的个人收件箱中看到它们。但是，SES的出现率仅为gmail垃圾邮件的十分之一，而且它们似乎实际上是在听我的滥用报告，因此我可以或多或少地原谅他们。
