B8ta closing another San Francisco store after armed robbery
B8ta在武装抢劫后关闭了另一家旧金山商店

2021-02-06 20:04:35

Just one week after making the decision to close its Union Square store, b8ta CEO Vibhu Norby announced via Twitter on Wednesday that the Hayes Valley store is now closed “indefinitely” as well due to a store manager having a gun pointed at him during a robbery.

在决定关闭其联合广场商店的一个星期后，b8ta首席执行官Vibhu Norby周三在Twitter上宣布，海耶斯谷商店现在也“无限期”关闭，原因是商店经理在抢劫期间将枪对准他。

"Our story is not exceptional," Norby said. "I think the city will say it's a b8ta-specific problem because we have a visible storefront and high-value merchandise, but everybody’s witnessing this."

He said since his story has been covered in the media, other Hayes Valley merchants have reached out to share stories of their break-ins. Norby said he won't reopen the store until he sees a month free of break-ins on the block.

他说，自从他的故事被媒体报道以来，其他海斯谷商人都伸出手来分享他们闯入的故事。诺比（Norby）说，直到看到一个月内没有任何闯入者，他才会重新开店。

The Union Square b8ta store closed last week because there were three muggings on the street it is on this year. “It doesn’t matter how expensive it is,” Norby told SFGATE in January. “We can’t send our employees into a store where there’s even a 2% chance they get mugged.”

联合广场（Union Square）的b8ta商店上周关闭，因为今年大街上有三个抢劫案。 “多贵无关紧要，”诺比在一月份告诉《金融时报》。 “我们不能将员工送进一家商店，那里甚至有2％的机会被抢劫。”

Norby began expressing his frustrations on Twitter in January to get the attention of the city. A single thread details each incident, from a video of a man breaking the glass at the Hayes Valley store to steal items to a man entering the store in broad daylight to run out with a $2,200 Devialet speaker. Now, that thread includes his announcement that a store manager at the Hayes Valley store had a gun pulled on him and so he has decided to close the store.

诺比（Norby）从一月份开始在Twitter上表达他的挫败感，以引起这座城市的关注。每个事件都由一个线索详细说明，从一个录像带中，一名男子在海斯谷商店偷了一块玻璃偷东西的视频到一名男子在光天化日之下进入商店，用了2,200美元的Devial​​et扬声器耗尽。现在，该话题包括他宣布海耶斯谷（Hayes Valley）商店的一位商店经理对他开枪的消息，因此他决定关闭该商店。

“It's with a heavy heart that we have made the decision to close our Hayes Valley SF store as well, indefinitely and immediately,” the tweet reads. “Our store manager had a gun pulled on him this afternoon and we were robbed. The team is shaken up but doing OK. (And yes, we had private security.)”

这则推文写道：“我们怀着沉重的决心，决定无限期和立即关闭我们的Hayes Valley SF商店。” “我们的商店经理今天下午拉了枪，我们被抢了。团队动摇了，但一切正常。 （是的，我们有私人保安。）”

While Norby said he was reluctant to hire private security for the store since it would cost 30% more than the store’s monthly rent, he chose to do it anyway after the increase in incidents in January. "I thought [hiring private security] would work," he said. "In retrospect, it obviously doesn't because you're not going to ask someone to escalate the situation."

尽管诺比说他不愿意为这家商店租用私人保安，因为它的价格比商店的月租金高出30％，但他还是选择在一月份事件增加后继续这样做。 ＆＃34;我认为[雇用私人保安]会起作用，＆＃34;他说。回想起来，这显然不是因为您不会要求某人升级情况。＆＃34;

B8ta has stores around the globe, and Norby told SFGATE last month that the crime in San Francisco is unlike anything the company experiences elsewhere. “This actually doesn’t happen anywhere else in the country,” he said. “We have 17 other stores, including three internationally. This just doesn’t happen anywhere else. It’s only here.”

B8ta在全球各地都有商店，诺比（Norby）上个月告诉《金融时报》（SFGATE），旧金山的犯罪与该公司在其他地方的经历不同。他说：“实际上，这在该国其他任何地方都不会发生。” “我们还有其他17家商店，其中包括3家国际商店。这只是在其他任何地方都不会发生。只是在这里。”
