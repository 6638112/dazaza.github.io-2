Delivering sound to people where they want it for VR, AR
向需要VR，AR的人传递声音

2020-12-09 20:57:11

What if a commercial audio speaker could function like an autozoom projector does for light, and you could deliver the sound people want where they want it? Chinmay Rajguru, from the University of Sussex, will discuss his research team's work creating a sound projector that can deliver spatial sound at a distance by forming a beam of audible sound.

如果商用音频扬声器的功能类似于自动变焦投影仪的照明功能，又可以将人们想要的声音传递到他们想要的地方，该怎么办？萨塞克斯大学（University of Sussex）的Chinmay Rajguru将讨论他的研究团队的工作，即创建一个声音投射器，该声音投射器可以通过形成一束可听见的声音来传递一定距离的空间声音。

The session, "A spatial sound delivery system for virtual and augmented reality," will take place Dec. 8 as part of the 179th Meeting of the Acoustical Society of America, which will be held virtually Dec. 7-10.

会话，即“用于虚拟现实和增强现实的空间声音传输系统”，该会议将于12月7日至12月7日至10日举行，这是美国声学学会第179次会议的一部分。

The researchers wanted to investigate an alternative to headphones for use in virtual and augmented reality simulations, where sound increasingly plays a central role. With headphones, however, users cannot interact directly with the outside world. The device they created consisted of a portable speaker, and two metamaterial lenses of different focal length arranged in a telescope.

研究人员希望研究在虚拟和增强现实模拟中使用的耳机替代品，而声音在其中扮演着越来越重要的角色。但是，使用耳机，用户无法直接与外界互动。他们创建的设备包括一个便携式扬声器和两个不同焦距的超材料透镜，它们布置在望远镜中。

"Our system tracks the user through the camera and sends sound to the user," said Rajguru. They delivered signals between 5 to 6 kilohertz and achieved a 20-centimeter accuracy delivering sound.

They benchmarked the performance of the system with measurements and a pilot study with human participants. They also connected the projector to a VR headset to show how it can be used to deliver messages in VR.

他们通过测量和对人类参与者的初步研究来对系统的性能进行基准测试。他们还将投影仪连接到VR耳机，以展示如何将其用于在VR中传递消息。

The researchers found large and empty rooms work best for their technology, because understanding the direction of sound can be more difficult in smaller rooms due to reverberation. Using tracking hardware, a Kinect which they borrowed from , they were able to cover a 4- by 4-meter space in the , which can be used to give the impression of covering even larger spaces in the VR simulation.

研究人员发现，大而空的房间最适合他们的技术，因为在较小的房间中由于混响而难以理解声音的方向。通过使用跟踪硬件（他们从中借用的Kinect），他们能够在内覆盖4 x 4米的空间，从而可以给人留下在VR模拟中覆盖更大空间的印象。

Delivering directional sound could have broad applications, such as at hospitals to deliver notifications in a specific ward without disturbing other patients or in airports providing to a section or a moving individual about the status of their flight.
