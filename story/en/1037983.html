Superkids must battle an alien invasion in We Can Be Heroes trailer
超级孩子必须在《我们可以成为英雄》预告片中与外星人入侵作斗争

2020-12-06 09:01:41

It's been 15 years since the premiere of The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl in 3D, Director Richard Rodriguez's fantasy adventure kids' film that was hyped as the movie event of 2005. It fell short of expectations, but Rodriguez clearly retained a deep love for this imaginary world—deep enough that he was keen to revisit it with the forthcoming Netflix standalone sequel,  We Can Be Heroes. And yes, the original Lavagirl, Taylor Dooley, makes an appearance.

导演理查德·罗德里格斯（Richard Rodriguez）的奇幻冒险孩子3D影片《鲨鱼少年》和《熔岩女孩》首映已经15年了。这部电影曾被誉为2005年的电影盛会。虽然没有达到预期，但罗德里格斯显然对这个虚构的世界保持着深厚的爱慕-深得足以让他渴望与即将上映的Netflix独立续集《我们可以成为英雄》重新审视。是的，最初的Lavagirl，泰勒·杜利（Taylor Dooley）出场了。

Rodriguez is an impressively versatile director, from his early Western/action films El Mariachi (1992), Desperado (1995), and From Dusk Till Dawn (1996), to the Spy Kids franchise and last year's science fiction blockbuster, Alita: Battle Angel. After the success of 2003's S py Kids 3D: Game Over, Rodriguez pitched another immersive kids' film, based on a story by his young son, Racer Max. It featured a young boy named Max, neglected by his parents and bullied at school, who creates an imaginary dream world in his journal, called Planet Drool. The inhabitants include Sharkboy (Taylor Lautner), son of a marine biologist, and Lavagirl (Dooley), who has a tendency to set things on fire—as well as "plughounds" and singing bubbles called LaLas. But when the school bully steals Max's dream journal, those dreams start to bleed into reality.

罗德里格斯（Rodriguez）是一位令人印象深刻的多才多艺的导演，从他的早期西方/动作片《玛利亚奇》（1992年），《绝望之城》（1995年）和《从黄昏到黎明》（1996年），到《间谍儿童》系列和去年的科幻大片，艾丽塔：战斗天使。在2003年的《间谍儿童3D：游戏结束》取得成功之后，罗德里格斯推出了另一款身临其境的儿童游戏。电影，根据他的小儿子Racer Max的故事改编。它的特色是一个名叫马克斯（Max）的小男孩，他的父母忽视了这个男孩，并在学校被欺负，他在他的日记中创建了一个虚构的梦想世界，名为《行星流口水》。其中的居民包括海洋生物学家的儿子鲨鱼（Taylor Lautner），以及倾向于纵火的Lavagirl（Dooley），以及＆＃34; plughounds＆＃34;。唱着LaLas的泡泡。但是当学校的恶霸偷走了麦克斯的梦想日记时，那些梦想开始流血变为现实。

The film received mostly negative reviews—it holds a dismal 19 percent fresh rating on Rotten Tomatoes—largely due to the bad 3D effects and storyline (it was the brainchild of a seven-year-old, after all). But The Adventures of Sharkboy and Lavagirl did manage to gross $72 million worldwide (double its budget), the visuals were impressive, and Lautner would go on to star in the Twilight franchise. (Due to scheduling conflicts, he was unable to appear in We Can Be Heroes.) Rodriguez also co-wrote a series of children's novels based on the characters. 这部电影大多受到负面评价-在烂番茄上的最新评级只有19％，这主要归功于糟糕的3D效果和故事情节（毕竟这是7岁孩子的创意）。但是《鲨鱼男孩和熔岩女孩历险记》在全球的票房收入达到了7200万美元（是其预算的两倍），视觉效果令人印象深刻，劳特纳将继续担任《暮光之城》系列的主角。 （由于时间安排上的冲突，他无法出现在《我们可以成为英雄》中。）罗德里格斯还根据人物角色合编了一系列儿童小说。现在他以《我们可以成为英雄》回到那个世界。根据官方前提：

When alien invaders kidnap Earth's superheroes, their kids are whisked away to a government safe house. But whip-smart tween Missy Moreno (Yaya Gosselin) will stop at nothing to rescue her superhero dad, Marcus Moreno (Pedro Pascal). Missy teams up with the rest of the superkids to escape their mysterious government babysitter, Ms. Granada (Priyanka Chopra Jonas). If they're going to save their parents, they'll have to work together by using their individual powers — from elasticity to time control to predicting the future — and form an out-of-this-world team.

当外星入侵者绑架地球的超级英雄时，他们的孩子被赶到政府的安全屋。但是鞭子聪明的补间小姐莫西·莫雷诺（Yaya Gosselin）会毫不动摇地营救她的超级英雄父亲马库斯·莫雷诺（Petro Pascal）。 Missy与其他超级孩子联手逃脱了他们神秘的政府保姆Granada女士（Priyanka Chopra Jonas）。如果他们要挽救父母，他们将不得不利用自己的力量（从弹性，时间控制到预测未来）共同努力，并组建一支世界末日的团队。

The trailer opens with a voiceover by Missy lauding the Heroics, adult superheroes (like her dad, Marcus) who protect the world in an era of increasing uncertainty. But then aliens attack, and "this was the day our heroes fell." Miracle Guy (Boyd Hollbrook), Tech-No (Christian Slater), Marcus, and the rest of the Heroics are captured, and Missy is whisked off to Heroics Headquarters for her protection, along with all the other super kids. They include Rewind (Isaiah Russell-Bailey), who can manipulate the flow of time, and Guppy (Vivien Lyra Blair), who is the child of Sharkboy and Lavagirl and inherited powers from both ("She's got shark strength!"). With the aliens likely coming for them next, the assembled super kids, led by Missy, decide to band together to rescue their parents and save the planet.

预告片的开头是Missy的配音，称赞Heroics，成年的超级英雄（像她的父亲Marcus），在不确定性日益增加的时代保护着世界。但是外星人发动了进攻，这是我们的英雄跌倒的日子。捕获了奇迹家伙（Boyd Hollbrook），科技人（克里斯蒂安·斯莱特），马库斯（Marcus）和其他英雄组织，而密西（Missy）则与其他所有超级孩子一起被送到英雄组织总部保护她。他们包括可以控制时间流逝的Rewind（以赛亚·罗素·贝利）和Guppy（Vivien Lyra Blair），这是Sharkboy和Lavagirl的孩子，并继承了两者的力量（她得到了鲨鱼的力量！＆＃34;）。随着外星人接下来可能会来找他们，由米西（Missy）领导的聚集的超级孩子决定联合起来营救他们的父母并拯救地球。

We Can Be Heroes debuts on Netflix on December 25, 2020. Will it be better than its predecessor? Who knows? What critics like, and what children like, are often different. At least it's not in 3D. And I'm sure parents of young children will be grateful to have some fresh, family-friendly holiday fare to amuse their pandemic-weary offspring.

《我们可以成为英雄》于2020年12月25日在Netflix上首次亮相。它会比其前身更好吗？谁知道？评论家喜欢什么，孩子喜欢什么，通常是不同的。至少不是3D模式。而且我敢肯定，年幼的孩子的父母将很高兴能有一些新鲜的，适合家庭的假期票价，以娱乐他们厌倦大流行的后代。
