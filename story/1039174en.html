Apple is full-steam ahead on replacing Qualcomm modems with its own
苹果正在全力以赴地替换自己的高通调制解调器

2020-12-12 05:15:21

As rumored many months ago, Apple's silicon ambitions don't end with replacing Intel CPUs with its own in Macs—it plans to ditch Qualcomm modems in favor of its own custom-designed chips for iPhones, according to Apple hardware tech lead Johny Srouji.

正如许多个月前的传闻所言，苹果的芯片野心并没有以在Mac中用自己的Mac取代英特尔CPU来结束–苹果计划放弃高通调制解调器，转而采用自己为iPhone设计的定制芯片。硬件技术负责人Johny Srouji。

Srouji confirmed the company's plans when speaking to employees during an internal town hall meeting, as reported by Bloomberg today. Apple acquired Intel's 5G smartphone modem business last summer. That acquisition of Intel's intellectual property and resources was key for Apple's new efforts. This year, we kicked off the development of our first internal cellular modem which will enable another key strategic transition... Long-term strategic investments like these are a critical part of enabling our products and making sure we have a rich pipeline of innovative technologies for our future.

据彭博社今天报道，Srouji在一次内部市政厅会议上与员工交谈时证实了公司的计划。苹果去年夏天收购了英特尔的5G智能手机调制解调器业务。收购英特尔的知识产权和资源是苹果新努力的关键。今年，我们开始了首款内部蜂窝调制解调器的开发，这将实现另一项关键的战略转型……此类长期战略投资是实现我们的产品并确保我们拥有大量创新技术的关键部分为了我们的未来。

Apple introduced 5G modems for the first time this year in its iPhone 12 lineup, but the phones use modems made by Qualcomm. When Apple completes its work on its own modems, it is likely to drop the Qualcomm modems from most or all of its phones. Qualcomm shares fell in value after the Bloomberg report ran.

苹果今年在iPhone 12系列中首次推出了5G调制解调器，但这些电话使用的是高通公司生产的调制解调器。当Apple在自己的调制解调器上完成其工作时，很可能会从其大部分或所有电话中删除高通调制解调器。彭博社发表报告后，高通股价下跌。

However, the report notes that "a 2019 patent agreement between Apple and Qualcomm includes a six-year licensing pact," and that "Qualcomm charges license fees to phone makers based on wireless patents it owns, regardless of whether they use its chips or not."

但是，该报告指出，苹果与高通之间的2019年专利协议包括一项为期六年的许可协议，高通公司会根据其拥有的无线专利向电话制造商收取许可费，无论他们是否使用其芯片。

In any case, this news is a confirmation of suspicions long held by close observers of Apple. As if the Intel modem business acquisition wasn't a big enough clue, Apple was also in 2018 visibly and publicly ramping up its staff in an office in San Diego that is located close to Qualcomm's offices there. As we noted when we covered Apple's hiring sprees two years ago, the San Diego office appears all but designed to poach skilled engineers from Qualcomm and the smaller San Diego-based firms in its orbit to work on modem technologies.

无论如何，这则新闻证实了苹果密切观察者长期以来的怀疑。似乎收购英特尔调制解调器业务的线索还不够大，苹果也在2018年公开地在圣地亚哥位于高通办事处附近的办公室增加了员工人数。正如我们在两年前报道苹果招聘信息时所指出的那样，圣地亚哥办事处看上去几乎是为了从高通和位于圣地亚哥的较小公司中挖走技术熟练的工程师而从事现代技术工作的。

In 2019, Apple confirmed plans to open a second major San Diego campus. Further, Apple has been listing numerous opportunities related to modems on its jobs portal for a couple of years now. The report today says that Apple has people working on the new modems in San Diego, Cupertino, and Europe. It is unknown when Apple might add these new modems to its phones, but we can easily speculate as to what its priorities might be. Our iPhone 12 review (and the reviews at other publications) pointed out that the new iPhones take a dramatic hit to battery life when browsing in 5G. Apple may hope that it can produce more efficient alternatives that are more tightly integrated with the other technologies in its phones, in one possible focus.

苹果在2019年确认了计划开设第二个圣地亚哥主要校区的计划。此外，两年来，苹果一直在其工作门户网站上列出与调制解调器相关的众多机会。今天的报告说，苹果公司在圣地亚哥，库比蒂诺和欧洲的新调制解调器工作人员。苹果何时将这些新的调制解调器添加到手机中尚不清楚，但我们可以很容易地推测出它的优先级是什么。我们的iPhone 12评论（以及其他出版物的评论）指出，当在5G中浏览时，新iPhone对电池寿命产生了巨大影响。苹果可能希望它能够生产出更高效的替代品，使其与手机中的其他技术更加紧密地集成在一起，这可能是一个重点。
