英特尔宣布将收购SigOpt，这是一家建立优化AI模型平台的初创公司；SigOpt将加入英特尔的机器学习性能部门

2020-10-30 11:46:16

Intel today announced it will acquire SigOpt, a San Francisco-based startup developing a platform to optimize AI software models. Terms of the deal, which is expected to close this quarter, weren’t disclosed, but Intel says it plans to use SigOpt’s technologies across its hardware products to accelerate, amplify, and scale AI software tools for developers.

英特尔今天宣布，它将收购总部位于旧金山的初创公司SigOpt，该公司开发一个优化人工智能软件模型的平台。预计将于本季度完成的这笔交易的条款没有披露，但英特尔表示，计划在其硬件产品中使用SigOpt的技术，为开发人员加速、扩大和扩展人工智能软件工具。

The acquisition comes as Intel responds to setbacks by doubling down on an AI silicon market it expects to be worth over $25 billion by 2024. Following a delay with its 10-nanometer processor hardware, Intel announced it would push back its 7-nanometer manufacturing process. In February, the company ended development on its Nervana AI model training technology after spending $2 billion acquiring chipmaker Habana Labs.

此次收购正值英特尔为应对挫折，在预计到2024年价值超过250亿美元的人工智能硅市场上加倍下注。在10纳米处理器硬件推迟后，英特尔宣布将推迟7纳米的制造工艺。今年2月，该公司在斥资20亿美元收购芯片制造商哈瓦那实验室(Habana Labs)后，结束了Nervana AI模型培训技术的开发。

SigOpt focuses on the software side of AI and machine learning. After completing his Ph.D. at Cornell University, CEO Scott Clark developed the framework that would become SigOpt’s product while working at Yelp, where he used it to optimize models and A/B tests. In 2014, Scott founded SigOpt with CTO Patrick Hayes, who had worked as a software engineer at BlackBerry, Foursquare, Bloomberg, and Facebook. The duo launched the company with the idea of bringing the technology to other fields.

SigOpt专注于人工智能和机器学习的软件方面。在康奈尔大学(Cornell University)获得博士学位后，首席执行官斯科特·克拉克(Scott Clark)在Yelp工作期间开发了这个框架，该框架后来成为SigOpt的产品，他在Yelp用它来优化模型和A/B测试。2014年，斯科特与首席技术官帕特里克·海耶斯(Patrick Hayes)共同创立了SigOpt，海耶斯曾在黑莓(BlackBerry)、Foursquare、彭博社(Bloomberg)和Facebook担任软件工程师。两人创办了这家公司，想法是将这项技术带到其他领域。

SigOpt’s platform and API optimizes hyperparameters — parameters whose values are used to control the AI model training process — to generate high-performing models in production. It supports 100 hyperparameters and 100 times parallelism, incorporating a range of features — including multimetric optimization, multitask optimization, and conditional parameters — and leveraging Bayesian and global optimization algorithms.

SigOpt的平台和API优化了超参数-其值用于控制AI模型培训过程的参数-以在生产中生成高性能的模型。它支持100个超参数和100倍并行，融合了一系列功能-包括多参数优化、多任务优化和条件参数-并利用贝叶斯和全局优化算法。

With a few lines of code, teams can embed SigOpt into workflows, regardless of the type of AI platform, model management solution, cloud infrastructure, client library, or model type. Tapping a library that can create up to tens of thousands of concurrent experiments, SigOpt helps parametize models and run optimization loops, tracking experiment metadata and providing visualizations in a web insights dashboard.

只需几行代码，团队就可以将SigOpt嵌入到工作流中，而无需考虑AI平台、模型管理解决方案、云基础架构、客户端库或模型类型。SigOpt利用一个可创建多达数万个并发实验的库，帮助将模型参数化并运行优化循环，跟踪实验元数据，并在Web Insight仪表板中提供可视化效果。

SigOpt claims to have worked with “leading” algorithmic traders and government organizations, as well as academic users like MIT; the University of California, Berkeley; Carnegie Mellon; and Stanford. The startup raised a total of $8.7 million in venture capital from investors that include Andreesen Horowitz, SV Angel, U.S intelligence agency affiliate In-Q-Tel, and Blumberg Capital.

SigOpt声称与“领先的”算法交易员和政府组织以及麻省理工学院、加州大学伯克利分校、卡内基梅隆大学和斯坦福大学等学术用户合作过。这家初创公司总共从投资者那里筹集了870万美元的风险资本，其中包括安德森·霍洛维茨(Andreesen Horowitz)、SV Angel、美国情报机构附属机构In-Q-tel和Blumberg Capital。

SigOpt’s team, including Clark and Hayes, will join Intel’s machine learning performance division post-acquisition. Intel says SigOpt’s software technologies will complement its existing AI software portfolio, which includes OpenVINO, a toolkit released in 2018 to help developers accelerate AI inference on edge and cloud devices.

SigOpt的团队，包括克拉克和海耶斯，将在收购后加入英特尔的机器学习性能部门。英特尔表示，SigOpt的软件技术将补充其现有的AI软件组合，其中包括2018年发布的帮助开发人员在边缘和云设备上加速AI推理的工具包OpenVINO。

Intel recently reported that its AI solutions drive “meaningful revenue” — totaling over $3.8 billion in 2019. In May, the company announced its global investment organization Intel Capital had committed a total of $132 million to 11 startups focused on AI translation services, health care automation, big data analytics, and chipset design. Intel Capital claims it is on track to invest between $300 million and $500 million in startups specializing in AI, with a particular focus on intelligent edge devices and network transformation.

英特尔最近报告称，其人工智能解决方案推动了“有意义的收入”-2019年总收入超过38亿美元。今年5月，该公司宣布其全球投资组织英特尔资本(Intel Capital)已承诺向11家专注于人工智能翻译服务、医疗自动化、大数据分析和芯片组设计的初创企业提供总计1.32亿美元的资金。英特尔资本(Intel Capital)声称，它正在向专门从事人工智能的初创公司投资3亿至5亿美元，重点是智能边缘设备和网络转型。
