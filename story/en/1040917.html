Major Design Changes Coming in Gnome 40, Out Next Year
明年将在Gnome 40中进行重大设计更改

2020-12-21 02:49:07

Big changes to the way GNOME Shell users open and manage apps and workspaces are coming in GNOME 40, due for release next spring.

GNOME 40中将对GNOME Shell用户打开和管理应用程序及工作区的方式进行重大更改，该方法将于明年春季发布。

Several major user experience and design changes are planned, all aiming to improve the form and function of the “Activities” overview, aka the on-demand workspace accessed by clicking the ‘ Activities‘ text button on the top bar in GNOME Shell.

已计划了几项主要的用户体验和设计更改，目的都是为了改进“活动”概述的形式和功能，即单击GNOME Shell顶部栏上的“活动”文本按钮访问按需工作区。

“Following months of design exploration and 6 separate user research exercises […] the GNOME Shell team has an updated design for the Activities Overview,” Allan Day, user experience designer at Red Hat and GNOME design lead, explains.

“在接下来的几个月的设计探索和6个单独的用户研究练习[…]中，GNOME Shell团队针对活动概述进行了更新的设计，” Red Hat和GNOME设计负责人的用户体验设计师Allan Day解释说。

Concept images of the proposed changes have been shared by the GNOME desktop team. These illustrate the shift in spatial positioning devs feel is required to deliver ‘intuitive navigation and orientation’.

GNOME桌面团队已共享提议的更改的概念图。这些说明了开发人员提供“直观导航和方向”所需的空间定位的转变。

An empty desktop is the first thing users see in a vanilla install of GNOME Shell — remember: Ubuntu ships a customised version. There are no visible cues, save for icons in the top bar, on where to launch apps or how to manage windows. A user has to “discover” the Activities button, which also houses the Dash, and work out the rest from there.

用户在原始安装的GNOME Shell中首先看到一个空桌面，请记住：Ubuntu提供了自定义版本。除了顶部栏中的图标，启动应用程序的位置或如何管理窗口之外，没有可见的提示。用户必须“发现”“活动”按钮，该按钮也包含“仪表板”，然后从那里计算出其余部分。

Instead of a blank desktop, GNOME 40 greets users with the Activities mode, which is replete with with (new) horizontal workspace switcher, (new) horizontal Dash (or favourites bar), plus visible search bar.

GNOME 40代替了空白的桌面，向用户提供了“活动”模式，该模式充满了（新）水平工作区切换器，（新）水平破折号（或收藏夹栏）以及可见的搜索栏。

When an app is opened (or a workspace clicked) the screen “zooms” in to put the user in front of their selected task/workspace. The Activities screen is accessible at any time, as now.

当打开一个应用程序（或单击一个工作区）时，屏幕将“放大”以将用户置于其所选任务​​/工作区的前面。与现在一样，可以随时访问“活动”屏幕。

Interaction remains as rich as it is now; users can drag and drop app windows between workspaces (and presumably create new workspaces on demand). Window titles will appear on mouse over.

互动仍然像现在这样丰富。用户可以在工作空间之间拖放应用程序窗口（并可以根据需要创建新的工作空间）。窗口标题将出现在鼠标悬停时。

The “Applications” grid is launched by clicking the grid icon in the Dash. But, like much of the UI, it now pages horizontally. GNOME devs have consciously created a navigational consistency that remain true throughout the whole of the app switching UI. Left/right to move, up/down to transition.

单击“仪表板”中的网格图标可启动“应用程序”网格。但是，像大多数UI一样，它现在可以水平页面。 GNOME开发人员有意识地创建了导航一致性，这种一致性在整个应用程序切换UI中始终保持不变。向左/向右移动，向上/向下移动。

Other functionality, such as as-you-type search, access to search providers, app reordering, and customisable app folders remains in tact.

其他功能（如您键入搜索，对搜索提供程序的访问，应用程序重新排序和可自定义的应用程序文件夹）保持不变。

I’m reassured to see the Dash remains present in these new mockups. GNOME’s design discussion has, in the past, suggested the dock was ripe for removal. Such a bold move to the UX would be controversial but users unhappy at it would, as now, be free to tailor the GNOME Shell experience to meet their own needs and preferences through extensions.

我很确定看到Dash仍然存在于这些新模型中。过去，GNOME的设计讨论表明，该基座已经可以拆卸。如此大胆地转向UX会引起争议，但用户对此不满意，如现在一样，可以通过扩展自由定制GNOME Shell体验以满足他们自己的需求和偏好。

That said there is some functionality missing: in mockups where applications are open you’ll notice that they are NOT presented in the Dash as running apps, i.e. with a status dot. Does this mean the Dash is now just becoming a favourites bar and not a way to manage running apps?

也就是说，缺少一些功能：在打开应用程序的模型中，您会注意到它们在Dash中并未作为正在运行的应用程序显示，即带有状态点。这是否意味着Dash现在正变成收藏夹栏，而不是管理正在运行的应用程序的方法？

System76 were apparently involved the research resulting in these GNOME 40 plan. This isn’t a surprise since System76 ship a modified version of GNOME Shell desktop in Pop OS — a distro that has won many plaudits for its alterations and add-ons, and for its power-user focused workflow.

System76显然参与了导致这些GNOME 40计划的研究。这并不奇怪，因为System76在Pop OS中发布了GNOME Shell桌面的修改版-该发行版因其更改和附加组件以及针对超级用户的工作流而赢得了许多赞誉。

GNOME 40 is due for release in March, 2021. The design changes seen here are aimed at that release but not guaranteed to ship. Things may, naturally, change as development and implementation takes place.

GNOME 40将于2021年3月发布。此处看到的设计更改是针对该版本的，但不能保证会交付。随着开发和实施的发生，事情自然会发生变化。

But if all goes to plan the redesigned Activities experience showcased here could feature in Ubuntu 21.04 ‘Hirsute Hippo’, due for release in mid April, 2021.

但是，如果一切都按计划进行，那么此处展示的经过重新设计的“活动”体验可能会出现在Ubuntu 21.04“ Hirsute Hippo”中，该版本将于2021年4月中旬发布。
