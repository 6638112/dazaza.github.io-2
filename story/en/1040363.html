Launching the 2020 Nim Community Survey

No matter whether you use Nim today, have used Nim previously, or never used Nim before;we want to know your opinions.Your feedback will help the Nim project understand its strengths andweaknesses, and to determine development priorities for the future.

It shouldn’t take you much longer than 5 minutes to complete this survey.Submissions will be accepted until around February 2021, depending on theresponse rates.If you have any questions or feedback, please don’t hesitateto get in touch with us via email at [email protected] or on the Nim Forum.
