Johnson and Johnson one-dose Covid vaccine has 66%-72% efficacy
强生一剂Covid疫苗的功效为66％-72％

2021-01-29 23:02:53

A fifth vaccine, made by the US company Johnson & Johnson, has shown efficacy against the coronavirus and could transform prospects for protecting both the UK and the rest of the world, because it needs only a single dose.

美国公司强生公司生产的第五种疫苗约翰逊（Johnson）已显示出抗冠状病毒的功效，并且可以改变保护英国和世界其他地区的前景，因为它只需要单剂即可。

The vaccine, made by the US giant’s subsidiary Janssen, based in Belgium, was trialled in 44,000 people in the US, South Africa and Brazil. The US has ordered 100m doses with an option for more. Further trials involving a second dose are taking place in the UK – and the British government has bought 30m doses. The EU has ordered 400m doses.

这种由美国巨人的子公司扬森（Janssen）制造的疫苗位于美国比利时，已在美国，南非和巴西的44,000人中进行了试验。美国已经订购了1亿剂，还有更多选择。英国正在进行涉及第二剂的进一步试验-英国政府已购买了3000万剂。欧盟已经订购了4亿剂。

The company said it had 72% efficacy in preventing Covid in the US but a lower rate of 66% was observed globally in the large trial conducted across three continents and against multiple variants. It gave people 85% protection against severe illness, even in South Africa which is struggling with a problematic variant of the virus. The vaccine gave 100% protection against hospitalisation and death, as do the other vaccines now approved.

该公司表示，它在美国预防Covid的功效为72％，但在横跨三大洲针对多种变体进行的大型试验中，全球的预防率只有66％。它为人们提供了85％的保护，使其免受严重疾病的侵害，即使是在南非，该国家都在与该病毒的问题变种作斗争。与目前批准的其他疫苗一样，该疫苗可100％保护其免受住院和死亡的伤害。

The results of the trials have been much anticipated, because the Janssen vaccine can be given as a single injection. That means stocks will go further, and particularly in lower-income countries an immunisation programme will be easier as there is no need for a recall after three or 12 weeks.

由于可以将Janssen疫苗作为单次注射使用，因此该试验的结果已广为期待。这意味着库存将进一步增加，尤其是在低收入国家，由于无需在三到十二周后进行召回，免疫程序将更加容易。

Results recently published from early trials showed the vaccine triggered a good immune response. Just one shot induced neutralising antibody titers in 96% and T-cell responses in at least 80% of vaccinated volunteers. Some trials are investigating whether a second booster dose confers a useful benefit.

早期试验最近发表的结果表明，该疫苗引发了良好的免疫反应。在至少80％的疫苗接种志愿者中，仅注射一针即可产生96％的中和抗体滴度和T细胞反应。一些试验正在研究第二次加强剂量是否可以带来有益的益处。

Janssen has agreed in principle to supply up to 500m doses to Covax, the UN initiative to get vaccines to lower-income countries. It is the second biggest order for Covax after the AstraZeneca vaccine.

扬森原则上已同意向联合国向低收入国家提供疫苗的倡议Covax提供多达5亿剂。这是继AstraZeneca疫苗之后Covax的第二大订单。

“The potential to significantly reduce the burden of severe disease, by providing an effective and well-tolerated vaccine with just one immunisation, is a critical component of the global public health response,” said Paul Stoffels, chief scientific officer of Johnson & Johnson.

强生公司首席科学官保罗·斯托菲尔斯说：“通过只进行一次免疫就可以提供有效且耐受良好的疫苗，从而显着减轻严重疾病负担的潜力，是全球公共卫生对策的关键组成部分。”约翰逊。

“A one-shot vaccine is considered by the World Health Organization to be the best option in pandemic settings, enhancing access, distribution and compliance … 85% efficacy in preventing severe Covid-19 disease and prevention of Covid-19-related medical interventions will potentially protect hundreds of millions of people from serious and fatal outcomes of Covid-19. It also offers the hope of helping ease the huge burden placed on healthcare systems and communities.”

“世界卫生组织认为一次注射疫苗是大流行情况下的最佳选择，可提高获取，分配和依从性……在预防严重的Covid-19疾病和预防Covid-19相关的医疗干预措施中，将有85％的功效。可能使数亿人免于Covid-19的严重和致命后果。它还提供了希望，以减轻医疗保健系统和社区的巨大负担。”

The vaccine is based on a J&J vaccine successfully used against Ebola. It is similar to the Oxford/AstraZeneca design, but delivers a protein from the spike of the coronavirus through a human common cold rather than chimp adenovirus.

该疫苗基于成功用于抗埃博拉病毒的强生疫苗。它类似于牛津/阿斯利康的设计，但通过人类普通感冒而不是黑猩猩腺病毒从冠状病毒的刺突中递送蛋白质。

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) in the US and the Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency in the UK have already been looking at the early data and will be expected to make a decision soon on the vaccine’s emergency authorisation.

美国食品药品监督管理局（FDA）和英国药品与保健产品监管局已经在研究早期数据，预计将很快就疫苗的紧急授权做出决定。

J&J plans to seek emergency-use authorisation from the FDA next week. Public health officials have been counting on the J&J vaccine to increase much-needed supply and simplify the US immunisation campaign.

强生计划下周寻求FDA的紧急使用授权。公共卫生官员一直指望J＆amp; J疫苗可以增加急需的供应并简化美国的免疫运动。

The US has an agreement to buy 100m doses of J&J’s vaccine for $1bn. The company said in a statement the vaccine would be ready immediately upon emergency approval but declined to specify how many doses. The US government also has the option of purchasing an additional 200m doses.

美国已同意以10亿美元的价格购买1亿剂强生疫苗。该公司在一份声明中表示，一旦获得紧急批准，该疫苗将立即准备就绪，但拒绝透露具体剂量。美国政府还可以选择另外购买2亿剂。

Three vaccines have so far been authorised by various regulators around the world: those from Pfizer/BioNTech, Moderna and Oxford/AstraZeneca. The European Medicines Agency is expected to approve the Oxford vaccine on Friday. A fourth vaccine, from Novavax, published results from last-stage trials on Thursday and will also now go forward for approval.

迄今为止，世界各地的各种监管机构已批准了三种疫苗：来自辉瑞/ BioNTech，Moderna和牛津/阿斯利康的疫苗。预计欧洲药品管理局将在周五批准牛津疫苗。 Novavax的第四种疫苗于周四发布了最后阶段试验的结果，现在也将继续审批。
