2021年第一季度获得了240万新的积极账户，表明大流行驱动的流动势头，它在2020年的结果浮现，已经放缓了。与此同时，该公司粉碎了分析师对Q1的期望，包括发布意外的利润。

The streaming platform reported revenue of $574.2 million, up 79% year over year, and net income of $76.3 million (or 54 cents per diluted share) — after previously telling investors it was expecting a loss for Q1.

流媒体平台报告了574.2百万美元的收入，同比增长79％，净收入为7.63亿美元（或每次摊薄股份54美分） - 在以前告诉投资者预计Q1亏损。

With the pickup of new active accounts, Roku in Q1 2021 had 53.6 million total, up 35% year over year but a slower pace than its quarterly growth rates last year (37%-43%).

随着新的积极账户的拾取，六六六季度的Roku总共有53.6亿，同比增长35％，但比去年季度增长速度较慢（37％-43％）。

Wall Street analysts on average expected Roku to report Q1 revenue of $490.6 million and a loss of 13 cents per share.

华尔街分析师平均预期的Roku报告了Q1收入为490.6百万美元，每股损失13美分。

“Though there will be difficult COVID-19-related comparisons in 2021, we believe that the shift to streaming is inevitable,” CEO Anthony Wood and CFO Steve Louden wrote in a letter to shareholders. “It will be global and will transform the way content is distributed and monetized.”

“虽然在2021年将存在困难的Covid-19相关比较，但我们认为转向流媒体是不可避免的，”首席执行官Anthony Wood和CFO Steve Louden在股东的一封信中写道。 “它将是全局的，将改变内容分布和货币化的方式。”

Roku’s Platform segment revenue — comprising ad sales and revenue-sharing deals with content partners — doubled in Q1, to $466.5 million. The company said streaming hours consumed on Roku devices increased by 1.4 billion hours over last quarter to 18.3 billion. In addition, Roku reported average revenue per user (ARPU) on a trailing 12-month basis hit $32.14 in Q1, up 32% year over year.

Roku的平台分部收入 - 包括与内容合作伙伴的广告销售和收入分摊交易 - Q1加倍，达到466.5万美元。该公司表示，在罗湖设备上消耗的媒体时间增加了14亿小时，比上一季度增加到183亿。此外，Roku报告了每位用户的平均收入（ARPU）在尾后12个月内达到32.14美元，同比增长32％。

Roku’s Q1 earnings report comes after a public escalation in its fight with Google. Roku pulled YouTube TV from its channel store on April 30, alleging that Google was making anticompetitive demands for Roku to continue distribution of the core YouTube app. Google denied it was seeking any preferential treatment and claimed it was Roku that tied discussions over YouTube TV distribution with the deal to carry YouTube.

Roku的Q1收益报告在与谷歌斗争的公开升级之后出现。 Roku于4月30日从其频道商店从您的频道店拉动YouTube电视，声称谷歌正在对Roku进行反竞争要求，继续分发核心Youtube应用程序。谷歌否认它正在寻求任何优惠待遇，并声称它是Roku，它与YouTube电视分销的讨论与携带YouTube交易绑定。

Roku’s stock price dropped more than 20% following the YouTube TV removal. In after-hours trading Thursday, shares were up as much as 7.5% on the strong Q1 earnings.

YouTube电视拆除后，Roku的股票价格下跌超过20％。在星期四的余额交易中，股票上涨高达7.5％的初期收益。

For the second quarter, Roku issued guidance of net revenue between $610 million and $620 million and net income of $10 million-$20 million.

第二季度，Roku在6.1亿美元至620万美元之间发出了净收入的指导，净收入为1000万美元。

The Roku Channel, the company’s free, ad-supported service that also provides access to live TV and SVOD, had another quarter of record growth, reaching U.S. households with an estimated 70 million people, more than doubling from Q1 2020. Another key stat: According to Roku, in the first three months of 2021, more than 85% of adults 18-49 who watched The Roku Channel were unduplicated with traditional TV.

Roku频道，该公司的免费广告服务，还提供了直播电视和SVOD的访问，还有一季度的历史增长，达到了美国家庭，估计有7000万人，从Q1 2020增加了一倍以上。另一个关键统计数据：根据Roku的说法，在2021年的前三个月，超过85％的成年人18-49人看着Roku频道没有传统电视。

To boost The Roku Channel’s momentum, the company has embarked on a strategy of acquiring and producing original content.

为了提升Roku频道的势头，该公司已经开始了获得和生产原始内容的策略。

The company bought the now-defunct Quibi content library in January — encompassing more than 75 original shows — which it is rebranding as “Roku Originals” and release them on its free-to-watch AVOD service soon. Roku Originals also will be the brand name for future original programming on The Roku Channel. And last month, Roku acquired This Old House Ventures, producer of “This Old House” and “Ask This Old House” TV shows, in a deal that included a library of more than 1,500 episodes.

该公司于1月份购买了现已过度的Quibi内容图书馆 - 包含超过75个原创节目 - 它是将其重新装修作为“Roku原件”并尽快释放它们的免费观看艾索服务。 Roku原件也将是未来原始编程的品牌名称在Roku频道上。上个月，Roku收购了这座古老的房屋企业，“这座老房子”的生产商和“问这个老房子”电视节目，其中包括超过1,500个剧集的图书馆。

“[W]e expect that our content investment will continue to be commensurate with the scale and growth trajectory of The Roku Channel,” Wood and Louden wrote.

“[W] E期望我们的内容投资将继续与Roku频道的规模和增长轨迹相称，”木头和响应写道。
