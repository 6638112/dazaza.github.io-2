致力于揭穿超自然现象的魔术师和舞台艺术家詹姆斯·兰迪逝世，享年92岁

2020-10-22 09:11:00

James Randi, the internationally acclaimed magician and escape artist, both praised and cursed for devoting much of his career to debunking all things paranormal — from spoon bending and water dowsing to spirit channeling and faith healing — died Oct. 20. He was 92.

享誉国际的魔术师和逃生艺术家詹姆斯·兰迪(James Randi)于10月20日去世，享年92岁。兰迪职业生涯的大部分时间都致力于揭穿一切超自然现象--从弯勺子和放水，到灵魂通灵和信仰治愈--他既受到了赞扬，也受到了诅咒。

His educational foundation announced the death but did not provide additional details. In recent years, he had been treated for cancer and heart ailments.

他的教育基金会宣布了他的死讯，但没有提供更多细节。近年来，他曾接受过癌症和心脏病的治疗。

An inveterate skeptic and bristly contrarian in his profession, Mr. Randi insisted that magic is based solely on earthly sleight of hand and visual trickery. He scorned fellow magicians who allowed or encouraged audiences to believe their work was rooted in extrasensory or paranormal powers.

兰迪先生是一个根深蒂固的怀疑论者，他坚持认为魔术完全是建立在世俗的手艺和视觉欺骗的基础上的，他是一个根深蒂固的怀疑论者和坚定的逆向投资者。他鄙视那些允许或鼓励观众相信他们的作品植根于超感官或超自然能力的魔术师同行。

In contrast, the bearded, gnomish Mr. Randi cheerfully described himself as a “liar” and “cheat” in mock recognition of his magician’s skills at duping people into thinking they had seen something inexplicable — such as a person appearing to be cut in half with a saw — when it was, in fact, the result of simple physical deception. He was equally dismissive of psychics, seers and soothsayers.

相比之下，留着胡子的侏儒兰迪愉快地称自己是一个“骗子”和“骗子”，嘲笑他的魔术师欺骗人们认为他们看到了令人费解的东西--比如一个人看起来像是被锯子切成两半--而实际上，这是简单的身体欺骗的结果。这是对他的魔术师技能的嘲弄，他欺骗了人们，让他们认为他们看到了令人费解的东西--比如一个人看起来被锯子切成了两半--而这实际上是简单的身体欺骗的结果。他对通灵师、预言家和占卜师同样不屑一顾。

“The difference between them and me,” Mr. Randi told the New York Times in 1981, “is that I admit that I’m a charlatan. They don’t. I don’t have time for things that go bump in the night.”

“他们和我的不同之处在于，”兰迪在1981年告诉“纽约时报”，“我承认我是个江湖骗子。他们不会的，我没有时间去管那些在夜里会颠簸起来的东西。“。

Still, he was always careful to describe himself as an investigator, not a debunker, and insisted he was always open to the possibility of supernatural phenomena but simply found no evidence of it after decades of research.

尽管如此，他总是小心翼翼地将自己描述为调查人员，而不是揭穿真相的人，并坚称自己一直对超自然现象的可能性持开放态度，但经过数十年的研究，他根本没有发现任何证据。

To put his money where his mouth was, Mr. Randi and the research organization he helped found in 1976, the Committee for the Scientific Investigation of Claims of the Paranormal, offered payouts ranging up to $1 million to anyone who could demonstrate a supernatural or paranormal phenomenon under mutually agreed, scientifically controlled conditions. While he had many takers, he said, none of them earned a cent.

为了兑现诺言，兰迪和他在1976年帮助创立的研究机构--超自然现象科学调查委员会(Committee For The Science Investigation Of The Aranormal)--向任何能够在双方同意的科学控制条件下证明超自然或超自然现象的人提供最高100万美元的奖励。他说，虽然他有很多人接受，但没有一个人赚到一分钱。

“Randi is the man,” Michael Shermer, editor of Skeptic magazine, told the Times in 2001. “He’s the pioneer of the skeptical movement.”

“兰迪就是那个人，”“怀疑论”杂志编辑迈克尔·谢尔默在2001年告诉“泰晤士报”。“他是怀疑运动的先驱。”

In stage appearances and on television, Mr. Randi — with his penetrating blue eyes and bushy eyebrows — duplicated mind-reading feats and other telepathic stunts with ordinary hand-and-eye trickery and exposed a number of popular faith healers as frauds.

在舞台和电视上露面时，兰迪用他那双锐利的蓝眼睛和浓密的眉毛，用普通的手眼戏法复制了读心术和其他心灵感应特技，并揭露了一些广受欢迎的信仰治疗师是骗子。

At a public healing session led by televangelist Peter Popoff in Detroit in 1985, Mr. Randi sent in a man disguised as a woman, whom Popoff promptly “cured” of uterine cancer. Mr. Randi and his staff also discovered that Popoff wore a miniaturized radio receiver in his ear by which his wife relayed personal information — home addresses, type of illness — that she covertly collected from unwitting audience members so Popoff could impress them with his specific knowledge before proceeding to “heal” them.

1985年，在底特律由电视布道者彼得·波波夫(Peter Popoff)主持的一次公共治疗会议上，兰迪先生派了一个伪装成女人的男人，波波夫很快就“治愈”了子宫癌。兰迪和他的工作人员还发现，波波夫耳朵里戴着一个微型无线电接收器，他的妻子通过这个接收器传达她从不知情的听众那里秘密收集的个人信息--家庭地址和疾病类型--这样波波夫就可以在继续“治愈”他们之前，用他的特殊知识给他们留下深刻印象。

For his efforts, Mr. Randi was condemned as an emissary of the devil, receiving hate mail and threats veiled in biblical references. He periodically wore a bulletproof vest. He was frequently sued for defamation and loss of income by those he pursued, but he claimed he never paid a dime in damages.

由于他的努力，兰迪被谴责为魔鬼的使者，他收到了隐藏在圣经引用中的仇恨邮件和威胁。他每隔一段时间就穿一件防弹衣。他经常被他追捕的人以诽谤和收入损失为由起诉，但他声称自己从未支付过一分钱的损害赔偿金。

“They call me a ‘dried-up old runt of a magician,’ ” he told the Associated Press in 1986. “If I’m in their audience, they say a representative of Satan is with us today and point to me. I bow and wave.”

1986年，他告诉美联社：“他们称我为‘干枯的老魔术师’。”“如果我在他们的听众中，他们说撒旦的代表今天和我们在一起，并指着我。我鞠躬挥手。“。

Most famously, he challenged Uri Geller, the charismatic Israeli psychic who took the United States by storm in the 1970s, stunning audiences with his mental spoon-bending act and other feats. Geller’s abilities underwent tests by scientists at the highly respected Stanford Research Institute, which reported that some information can be transferred “outside the range of known perceptual modalities” and warranted further study.

最著名的是，他挑战了富有魅力的以色列通灵者乌里·盖勒(Uri Geller)，后者在20世纪70年代席卷了美国，以其精神上的弯勺行为和其他壮举震惊了观众。盖勒的能力接受了备受尊敬的斯坦福研究所的科学家们的测试，该研究所报告称，一些信息可以转移到“已知知觉模态范围之外”，因此需要进一步研究。

Conversely, Mr. Randi, after observing a spoon-bending performance by Geller at a private demonstration for Time magazine editors in 1973, dismissed it as phony, saying he detected Geller inserting an already-bent spoon into the act.

相反，兰迪在1973年观察到盖勒在一次面向时代周刊编辑的私人示威中弯曲勺子的表演后，对此不屑一顾，认为这是假的，说他发现盖勒在表演中插入了一把已经弯曲的勺子。

Mr. Randi then duplicated the stunt with standard sleight of hand. The magazine wrote off Geller as “a questionable nightclub magician,” triggering a bitter, decades-long feud between the two men.

然后，兰迪用标准的手法模仿了这个特技。该杂志将盖勒描述为“一个可疑的夜总会魔术师”，引发了两人长达数十年的激烈争执。

They exchanged angry potshots on national television and went to court in a series of libel actions filed by Geller. The litigation cost him thousands in legal fees, Mr. Randi said, but, as with the TV faith healers, he never paid any damages.

他们在国家电视台上怒气冲冲地交锋，并在盖勒提起的一系列诽谤诉讼中告上了法庭。兰迪说，这场诉讼花费了他数千美元的法律费用，但就像电视上的信仰治疗师一样，他从未支付过任何损害赔偿。

“In my view,” he told the Miami Herald in 1993, “Geller brings a disgrace to the craft I practice. Worse than that, he warps the thinking of a young generation of forming minds. And that is unforgivable.”

“在我看来，”他在1993年告诉“迈阿密先驱报”，“盖勒给我的手艺带来了耻辱。更糟糕的是，他扭曲了正在形成思想的年轻一代的思维。这是不可原谅的。“。

Randall James Hamilton Zwinge was born in Toronto on Aug. 7, 1928. He was the eldest of three children of a telephone company executive.

兰德尔·詹姆斯·汉密尔顿·茨温格1928年8月7日出生于多伦多。他是一家电话公司高管的三个孩子中的老大。

A child prodigy with a reputed IQ of 168, he was shy and often lonely. Ravenously curious and bored by rote classroom learning, he sought refuge in the Toronto public library, becoming largely self-taught.

作为一个公认的智商为168的神童，他很害羞，经常感到孤独。贪婪的好奇心和对死记硬背的课堂学习感到厌倦，他在多伦多公共图书馆寻求庇护，基本上是自学成才。

At a young age, he developed a keen interest in magic, inspired by a performance of legendary magician Harry Blackstone Sr. At 17, he dropped out of high school just days before graduation, turned down several college scholarships and joined a traveling carnival as a junior magician.

在年轻的时候，他受到传奇魔术师老哈里·布莱克斯通(Harry Blackstone)表演的启发，对魔术产生了浓厚的兴趣。17岁时，他在毕业前几天高中辍学，拒绝了几份大学奖学金，并以初级魔术师的身份参加了一场旅行嘉年华。

He overcame a stammer and fear of speaking in public, affected a turban and goatee, and honed his illusionist skills under a series of stage names, including Zo-Ran, Prince Ibis, Telepath and the Great Randall. He developed an early distaste for reputed psychic powers, challenging a preacher pretending to read the contents of sealed envelopes.

他克服了口吃和对在公共场合讲话的恐惧，戴上了头巾和山羊胡子，并在一系列艺名下磨练了自己的魔术技能，包括佐兰、伊比斯王子、泰勒帕斯和伟大的兰德尔。他很早就对所谓的通灵能力产生了反感，向一名假装阅读密封信封内容的传教士发起了挑战。

After a stint at simulating clairvoyance, in which many people took his prophecies seriously — he correctly predicted the winner of baseball’s World Series in 1949, for example — he said he was unable to persuade believers that his powers were strictly terrestrial. He said he “couldn’t live that kind of lie” and returned to conventional magic as The Amazing Randi, his permanent stage name thereafter.

在模拟了一段时间的千里眼之后，许多人认真对待了他的预言-例如，他正确地预测了1949年棒球世界大赛(World Series)的获胜者-他说，他无法说服信徒，他的力量完全是地球上的。他说他“不能再活在那种谎言里了”，并回到了传统的魔术中，成为了“神奇的兰迪”，此后他的艺名就是“神奇的兰迪”。

He also became an escape artist in the manner of Harry Houdini, thrilling crowds by extricating himself from straitjackets, bank safes and jails. He escaped from a straitjacket while suspended upside down over Niagara Falls. He once held Guinness world records for surviving the longest time inside a block of ice (55 minutes) and for being sealed the longest in an underwater coffin (one hour and 44 minutes), breaking a record Houdini set shortly before his death in 1926.

他还像哈里·胡迪尼(Harry Houdini)那样成为一名逃生艺术家，通过从紧身衣、银行保险箱和监狱中逃脱，让人群兴奋不已。当他倒挂在尼亚加拉瀑布上空时，他从紧身衣中逃脱。他曾保持着在冰块中存活时间最长(55分钟)和在水下棺材中密封时间最长(1小时44分钟)的吉尼斯世界纪录，打破了胡迪尼在1926年去世前不久创造的纪录。

In the late 1950s and early 1960s, Mr. Randi’s many appearances on television made him a fixture of prime-time entertainment. In 1973, he toured with heavy metal rock star Alice Cooper as an executioner simulating the beheading of the singer at each performance. By contrast, in 1975 he was invited to the White House by President Gerald R. Ford and first lady Betty Ford to do a special show for children.

在20世纪50年代末和60年代初，兰迪多次在电视上露面，使他成为黄金时段娱乐的固定人选。1973年，他与重金属摇滚明星爱丽丝·库珀(Alice Cooper)一起巡回演出，饰演一名刽子手，模拟了这位歌手在每次演出中被斩首的过程。相比之下，1975年，杰拉尔德·R·福特(Gerald R.Ford)总统和第一夫人贝蒂·福特(Betty Ford)邀请他到白宫为儿童做一个特别节目。

By that time, Mr. Randi was already living permanently in the United States, first in Rumson, N.J., and then Plantation, Fla. He changed his legal name to James Randi and, in 1987, became a U.S. citizen.

到那时，兰迪已经永久居住在美国，先是在新泽西州的鲁姆森，然后是佛罗里达州的普兰特。他改名为詹姆斯·兰迪(James Randi)，并于1987年成为美国公民。

A sophisticated showman and inexhaustible self-promoter, he appeared a total of 32 times on “The Tonight Show Starring Johnny Carson.” He also performed on “The Oprah Winfrey Show,” “Larry King Live,” “Anderson Cooper 360” and other programs.

作为一名老练的表演者和无穷无尽的自我推广者，他总共在“今夜秀”中出现了32次，主演是约翰尼·卡森(Johnny Carson)。他还在“奥普拉·温弗瑞秀”、“拉里·金现场秀”、“安德森·库珀360”等节目中表演。

Mr. Randi toured the world extensively, wowing throngs with his standard magic shows but also challenging claims of paranormal and other inexplicable phenomena, from household poltergeists and Ouija boards to flying saucers and reports of ships and planes lost in the Bermuda Triangle. All, he found, were based on exaggerated stories, media hype or physical manipulation.

兰迪在世界各地进行了广泛的巡回演出，他的标准魔术表演让人群惊叹不已，但他也对超自然和其他令人费解的现象的说法提出了质疑，从家喻户晓的恶作剧和占卜板，到飞碟，以及关于船只和飞机在百慕大三角失踪的报道。他发现，所有这些都是基于夸大的故事、媒体的炒作或物理操纵。

Mr. Randi was the author or co-author of 10 books, many detailing his accounts of debunking paranormal and supernatural claims. Two focused on his disputes with Geller.

兰迪是10本书的作者或合著者，其中许多书详细描述了他对超自然和超自然说法的揭穿。其中两个集中在他与盖勒的纠纷上。

With the creation of his Committee for the Scientific Investigation of Claims of the Paranormal (later shortened to Committee for Skeptical Inquiry), Mr. Randi greatly expanded his research. To add heft to the committee, he recruited the public support of astronomer and cosmologist Carl Sagan and science fiction writer Isaac Asimov.

随着他的超自然声称科学调查委员会(后来缩写为怀疑调查委员会)的成立，兰迪先生极大地扩大了他的研究范围。为了增加委员会的份量，他招募了天文学家和宇宙学家卡尔·萨根(Carl Sagan)和科幻作家艾萨克·阿西莫夫(Isaac Asimov)的公众支持。

In 1986, Mr. Randi won a $272,000 “genius grant” from the John D. and Catherine T. MacArthur Foundation, much of which he spent on legal battles with Geller. A decade later, he founded the nonprofit James Randi Educational Foundation with a $2 million donation from an anonymous computer mogul to further his agenda: maintaining a library, publishing a newsletter and setting up media-savvy blogs and websites.

1986年，兰迪从约翰·D·麦克阿瑟和凯瑟琳·T·麦克阿瑟基金会(John D.and Catherine T.MacArthur Foundation)获得了27.2万美元的“天才拨款”，其中大部分花在了与盖勒的法律诉讼上。十年后，他用一位匿名电脑大亨捐赠的200万美元创立了非营利性的詹姆斯·兰迪教育基金会(James Randi Education Foundation)，以推进他的议程：维护图书馆，出版时事通讯，建立精通媒体的博客和网站。

In 2010, at age 81, Mr. Randi publicly announced he was gay. He had a long relationship with a Venezuelan-born artist, Deyvi Peña, who was arrested by federal agents in 2011 for passport fraud and for living under a false identity; he said he had been trying to avoid deportation to his home country, fearing homophobic persecution. Peña pleaded guilty in 2012 to passport fraud and served six months of house arrest. He and Mr. Randi were married in Washington in 2013.

2010年，81岁的兰迪公开宣布自己是同性恋。他与委内瑞拉出生的艺术家德伊维·培尼亚(Deyvi Peña)有着长期的关系，后者于2011年因护照欺诈和使用假身份生活而被联邦特工逮捕；他说，由于担心遭到仇视同性恋的迫害，他一直在努力避免被驱逐回自己的祖国。培尼亚在2012年承认犯有护照欺诈罪，并服刑6个月的软禁。他和兰迪于2013年在华盛顿结婚。

The following year, filmmakers Tyler Measom and Justin Weinstein released “ An Honest Liar,” a full-length documentary of Mr. Randi’s life.

次年，电影制作人Tyler Measom和Justin Weinstein发行了一部关于兰迪生平的长篇纪录片“诚实的骗子”。

Raised loosely in the Anglican Church tradition in Canada, Mr. Randi described himself later in life alternately as agnostic and atheist. In one of his many essays, he questioned the religion of his early years as pointedly as others, jabbing at biblical accounts of creation, the virgin birth and the miracles of Jesus.

兰迪从小在加拿大的圣公会传统中长大，他在晚年时而称自己为不可知论者，时而称自己是无神论者。在他众多散文中的一篇中，他像其他人一样尖锐地质疑他早年的宗教，抨击圣经中关于创造、童贞出生和耶稣奇迹的描述。

“ ‘The Wizard of Oz’ is more believable,” he wrote. “And more fun.”

“”绿野仙踪“更可信，”他写道。“而且更有趣。”
