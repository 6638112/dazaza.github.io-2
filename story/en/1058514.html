Amazon launches a $250M fund in India to invest in startups that focus on helping SMBs automate and digitize their operations
亚马逊在印度推出了250米的基金，投资初创公司，专注于帮助SMBS自动化和数字化运营

2021-04-16 00:03:23

The announcement comes at a time when the American e-commerce group, which has previously invested over $6.5 billion in its India business, faces heat from government bodies, and the small and medium-sized businesses that it purports to serve.

该公告是在美国电子商务集团在其印度业务投入超过65亿美元的美国电子商务集团，面临着政府机构的热量，以及其旨在提供服务的中小型企业。

Through the new venture fund, called Amazon Smbhav Venture Fund, Amazon said it wants to invest in startups that focus on helping small businesses come online, sell online, automate and digitize their operations, and expand to customers worldwide. The company didn’t share the lifecycle of the fund (that is, how long it plans to take to run through $250 million).

通过新的风险基金，称为亚马逊SMBHAV企业基金，亚马逊表示，它希望投资初创公司，专注于帮助小企业在线上网，在线销售，自动化和数字化其运营，并扩展全球客户。该公司没有分享基金的生命周期（即计划营业为2.5亿美元的计划）。

“Small and medium businesses are the engine and lifeblood of economies,” said Andy Jassy, incoming CEO of Amazon at a virtual event Thursday. “We are passionate about accelerating SMBs.”

“中小型企业是经济体的发动机和生命线，”Andy Jassy of Amazon于周四的虚拟活动。 “我们对加速SMBS充满热情。”

在#Amazonsmbhav的电力包装第1天，它一切都始于与@ajassy的良好对话。必须在亚马逊谈论他的24年，在规模上实现创新，以及新的2.5亿美元的亚马逊SMBHAV风险基金。 pic.twitter.com/hpigbucff0.

Amazon said it has led a $10 million investment round in M1xchange, a three-year-old Gurgaon-headquartered startup that operates an invoice discounting marketplace exchange for MSMEs. M1xchange connects MSMEs with banks and financiers — and vice versa — through the marketplace exchange and the MSME is able to assign its invoice dues to a bank or financier and receive financing at better rates; thereby, solving the payments challenge for MSMEs, the e-commerce group said. This is the first investment from the company’s Amazon Smbhav Venture Fund.

亚马逊表示，它在M1Xchange领导了1000万美元的投资，这是一个三岁的古尔冈 - 总部启动，运营了MSMES的发票折扣市场交换。 M1xchange与银行和金融家连接MSMES - 反之亦然 - 通过市场交流，MSME能够将其发票会费分配给银行或金融家，并以更好的价格获得融资;由此，电子商务集团表示，解决MSMES的支付挑战。这是公司亚马逊SMBHAV企业基金的第一个投资。

Agriculture and healthcare are two additional areas Amazon is focusing on with its new venture fund, but it said it is open to looking at tech startups from other sectors if their work intersects with SMBs.

农业和医疗保健是亚马逊的两个其他地区，亚马逊专注于新的风险投资基金，但它表示，如果他们的工作与SMB相交，那么在其他部门看待技术初创公司。

In the agri-tech sector, Amazon is looking to invest in Indian startups that are using technology to make agri-inputs more accessible to farmers, provide credit and insurance to farmers, reduce food wastage, and improve the quality of produce to consumers. In the healthcare sector, Amazon said it will invest in startups that are enabling healthcare providers to leverage telemedicine, e-diagnosis, AI powered treatment recommendations.

在农业科技部门，亚马逊正在寻求投资印度初创公司，这些初创公司正在利用技术使农民更易进入农民，为农民提供信贷和保险，减少食物浪费，提高消费者的生产质量。亚马逊表示，在医疗保健领域表示，它将投资启动，使医疗提供者能够利用远程医疗，电子诊断，AI供电的治疗建议。

The announcement was made at Amazon’s annual four-day event, called Smbhav (which means ‘possible’ in Hindi), that focuses on India-based SMBs. At the virtual event, Amazon also unveiled ‘Spotlight North East’, an initiative to bring 50,000 artisans, weavers and small businesses online from the eight states in the North East region of India by 2025 and to boost exports of key commodities like tea, spices and honey from the region.

该公告是在亚马逊的每年为期四天的活动中发布的，称为SMBHAV（这意味着“在印地语中可能”可能“，这侧重于基于印度的SMB。在虚拟事件中，亚马逊还揭开了“聚光灯东北角”，这是一项倡议，一项倡议将印度东北地区的八个国家在线携带50,000名工匠，编织者和小企业，并提高了茶叶，香料等主要商品的出口和该地区的蜂蜜。

In the first edition of Smbhav last year, Amazon announced it would be investing $1 billion to help digitize 10 million small and medium sized businesses. Amazon said earlier this month that it had created 300,000 jobs in India since January 2020, and enabled exports for Indian-made goods worth $3 billion.

在去年的第一版SMBHAV中，亚马逊宣布投资10亿美元，以帮助数字化1000万个中小型企业。亚马逊本月早些时候表示，自2020年1月以来，它在印度创造了300,000个就业岗位，并使印度商品的出口能够超过30亿美元。

The company said more than 50,000 offline retailers and neighborhood stores — called kirana locally — are using Amazon marketplace and about 250,000 new sellers have also joined the platform. The company said today it aims to onboard 1 million offline retailers and neighbourhood stores by 2025 through the Local Shops on Amazon program.

该公司表示，50,000多家离线零售商和邻里商店 - 叫Kirana当地 - 正在使用亚马逊市场，约有25万个新卖家也加入了平台。该公司今天表示，到2025年通过亚马逊计划的当地商店落后于100万个离线零售商和邻里商店。

Not far from Smbhav’s first event last year, which was attended by Amazon chief executive and founder Jeff Bezos, tens of thousands of protesters marched on the street and expressed their concerns about what they alleged was unfair practices employed by Amazon to crush them.

去年的SMBHAV第一届活动不远，由亚马逊首席执行官和创始人杰夫贝斯参加，成千上万的抗议者在街道上游行，并对他们所谓的是亚马逊雇用的不公平实践来粉碎它们来表示关切。

A similar protest was observed today, where traders called for government intervention at an event they named Asmbhav (Hindi for ‘impossible’). You can hear some of their stories here. It’s an ongoing challenge for Amazon, which has long struggled to stay out of controversy in India.

今天观察着类似的抗议活动，贸易商呼吁政府干预他们将其命名为Asmbhav的活动（印度为“不可能”）。你可以在这里听到一些故事。这对亚马逊进行了持续挑战，这长期以来一直在努力避免在印度争议。

The Confederation of All India Traders (CAIT) “demanded” serious action from the Indian government against Amazon following revelations made in a Reuters story. “For years, CAIT has been maintaining that Amazon has been circumventing FDI [Foreign Direct Investment] laws of India to conduct unfair and unethical trade,” it said.

印度交易员（CAIT）的联合会“要求”在路透社故事中发出的启示后对印度政府对亚马逊的严肃行动。 “多年来，CAIT一直在保持亚马逊旨在规避印度的外国直接投资[外国直接投资]法律，以进行不公平和不道德的贸易，”它说。

Several international technology giants including Google, Facebook, and Microsoft have invested in Indian startups in recent years. Amazon, too, has backed a number of firms including ride-hailing startup Shuttl, and consumer brand MyGlamm. Last month, it acquired retail startup Perpule for about $20 million.

几个国际技术巨头包括谷歌，Facebook和微软近年来投资印度初创公司。亚马逊，也支持了一些公司，包括乘车骑行的创业公司Shuttl，以及消费品牌Myglamm。上个月，它收购了零售启动突发，持续约2000万美元。
