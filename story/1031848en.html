印第安纳州加里将成为美国第一个生产智能手机、平板电脑的城市

2020-10-29 10:12:31

Steel may have been what gave life to the city of Gary last century, but it is technology that may reinvigorate the city today.

钢铁可能是上个世纪给加里市带来活力的东西，但今天可能让这座城市重新焕发活力的是技术。

Gary Mayor Jerome Prince welcomed officials from nanotechnology manufacturer and cellphone equipment maker Akyumen Industries to the Genesis Center on Tuesday to announce a new partnership that will bring the business to the city.

加里市长杰罗姆·普林斯(Jerome Prince)周二欢迎纳米技术制造商和手机设备制造商Akyumen Industries的官员来到创世中心，宣布建立新的合作伙伴关系，将业务带到该市。

The partnership will make Gary the first U.S. site where smart phones and tablets, and the materials needed to make them, are manufactured and produced, according to Jeffrey Hayzlett, chairmen of C-Suite Network, an Akyumen partner.

Akyumen合作伙伴C-Suite Network董事长杰弗里·海兹利特(Jeffrey Hayzlett)表示，这一合作关系将使Gary成为美国第一个生产智能手机和平板电脑以及制造智能手机和平板电脑所需材料的网站。

Prince said the partnership is an opportunity to reimagine Gary and its future. Akyumen plans to build a manufacturing facility on the Chase Street property once home to the Ivanhoe Gardens housing complex. The company also will use the Genesis Center for preproduction while the manufacturing site is under construction and ultimately for its U.S. headquarters.

普林斯说，这一合作伙伴关系是重新想象加里及其未来的机会。Akyumen计划在曾经是艾芬豪花园住宅区所在地的蔡斯街物业上建立一个制造工厂。该公司还将使用Genesis中心进行试生产，同时制造地点正在建设中，最终将用于其美国总部。

Aasim Saied, chairman and CEO of Akyumen, said the project will bring 500 to 2,000 jobs to the city in the first three to five years. He said the company expects U.S. cellphone carriers to have a product option with phones completely manufactured in the U.S. He predicts their phones will be in demand, making the potential for future growth exponential.

Akyumen董事长兼首席执行官Aasim Saied表示，该项目将在最初的三到五年内为该市带来500至2000个就业机会。他说，该公司预计美国手机运营商将拥有完全在美国制造的手机的产品选择。他预测，他们的手机将需求旺盛，使未来的增长潜力呈指数级增长。

' “We will be the very first American company to make 5G devices,” Saied said.

赛德说：“我们将成为第一家制造5G设备的美国公司。”

The company’s phones are unique in that they can also serve as a projector that will display what is on the phone on a screen or wall.

该公司的手机的独特之处在于，它们还可以作为投影仪，将手机上的内容显示在屏幕或墙上。

Saied said the company’s use of nanotechnology has helped make the product viable and allow it to manufacture components at a competitive rate with foreign manufacturers despite paying U.S. wages. Saied said he expects jobs at the company to start at about $35,000 a year and go up from there.

Saied说，该公司使用纳米技术有助于使该产品可行，并使其能够在支付美国工资的情况下以与外国制造商竞争的速度制造零部件。Saied说，他预计公司的工作岗位起薪约为每年3.5万美元，并从那里开始上升。

“What is more powerful than steel … nanotechnology,” Saied said. “Bringing nanotechnology will make Gary stronger.”

“还有什么比钢铁…更强大的呢？纳米技术，“赛德说。“引进纳米技术会让加里变得更强壮。”

Prince said the city will work with Work One to create training opportunities for city residents to prepare them for the type of work that will be done at the facility.

普林斯说，该市将与Work One合作，为城市居民创造培训机会，让他们为将在该设施完成的工作类型做好准备。

The mayor said negotiations are still in progress, but are far enough along to make the announcement. Akyumen initially will move into the Genesis Center while its plant on Chase Street is under construction. If all goes as planned, the company will start that process in about 90 days.

市长说，谈判仍在进行中，但已经取得了足够的进展，可以宣布这一消息。Akyumen最初将搬进创世中心，而其位于蔡斯街的工厂正在建设中。如果一切按计划进行，该公司将在大约90天内启动这一过程。

Saied said the company plans to invest more than $100 million in the project and will construct an approximately 200,000-square-foot plant that will be expandable. The plant is expected to be operational by spring 2022. At this time the project is fully funded, Saied said.

Saied表示，该公司计划在该项目中投资超过1亿美元，并将建造一个约20万平方英尺的工厂，该工厂将可扩建。该工厂预计将在2022年春季投入运营。赛义德说，目前该项目资金充足。

Saied said he chose to partner with Gary after meeting with the mayor and his team and saw how focused they were on helping the people of Gary.

Saied说，他在与市长和他的团队会面后选择了与Gary合作，并看到他们是多么专注于帮助Gary的人民。

“We like to work with people who are driven more than people who like to hide,” Saied said.

萨伊德说：“我们更喜欢与有动力的人合作，而不是喜欢躲藏的人。”
