美国政府预算以超过3万亿美元的赤字结束财年

2020-10-21 03:01:40

That was more than triple last year's shortfall of $984 billion and double the previous record of $1.4 trillion in 2009.

这是去年9840亿美元缺口的三倍多，是2009年创下的1.4万亿美元纪录的两倍。

Efforts to combat the coronavirus pandemic left the U.S. government submerged in red ink as its fiscal year came to a close.

抗击冠状病毒大流行的努力让美国政府在财政年度接近尾声时陷入赤字。

The final tally for the budget deficit in fiscal 2020 came to $3.13 trillion, more than triple last year's shortfall of $984 billion and double the previous record of $1.4 trillion in 2009, courtesy of a stimulus package passed that year to battle the financial crisis.

2020财年的预算赤字最终达到3.13万亿美元，是去年9840亿美元缺口的三倍多，是2009年创下的1.4万亿美元纪录的两倍，这要归功于当年通过的与金融危机作斗争的刺激方案。

Most of the damage to this year's budget came due to the CARES Act, a $2.2 trillion spending package that included extra unemployment compensation to workers displaced during the pandemic and forgivable loans to business as an incentive to retain workers.

今年预算的大部分损失来自CARE法案，这是一项2.2万亿美元的一揽子支出计划，其中包括向在大流行期间流离失所的工人提供额外的失业补偿，以及作为留住工人的激励向企业提供可免除的贷款。

Receipts for the year came to $3.42 trillion against outlays of $6.55 trillion, the biggest of which came during June when the government spent $1.1 trillion, according to the Treasury Department.

根据财政部的数据，今年的收入达到3.42万亿美元，而支出为6.55万亿美元，其中最大的一笔来自6月份，当时政府支出了1.1万亿美元。

The fiscal year ended with government debt at just under $27 trillion, all but $6 trillion of which is held by the public.

本财年结束时，政府债务略低于27万亿美元，除6万亿美元外，其余均由公众持有。

Tax collections came in at 1.61 trillion for the year, $203 billion less than estimated in the budget. Corporate tax collections missed the budget estimate by $51.8 billion while social insurance and retirement receipts were $2.1 billion below.

全年税收收入为1.61万亿美元，比预算中估计的少2030亿美元。公司税收入比预算估计低518亿美元，而社会保险和退休收入比预算低21亿美元。

Higher-than-expected outlays for the departments of agriculture, education and health and human services also contributed to the shortfall.

农业、教育和卫生部门以及公共服务部门的支出高于预期也是造成缺口的原因之一。

The cost to service all that debt for the year came to $522.8 billion, which actually was the lowest total since 2017. Low government bond yields, helped in part by the Federal Reserve, helped keep debt service costs lower.

今年偿还所有债务的成本为5228亿美元，实际上是自2017年以来的最低总额。较低的政府债券收益率，在一定程度上得益于美联储(Federal Reserve)，帮助保持了较低的偿债成本。
