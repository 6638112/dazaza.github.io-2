艺电报告第二季度营收为11.5亿美元，同比下降14%，超出预期，但预计下个季度将表现疲软，原因是大流行游戏业务可能减弱

2020-11-06 11:43:31

Electronic Arts reported financial results today that beat Wall Street’s expectations with revenue falling 14% to $1.15 billion for the second fiscal quarter (third calendar quarter) ended September 30.

艺电今日公布的财报超出华尔街预期，截至9月30日的第二财季(第三财季)营收下滑14%至11.5亿美元。

While revenues were down compared to a year ago, EA outperformed expectations in the quarter. But EA’s forecast for the third fiscal quarter (fourth calendar quarter) came in below analysts’ expectations. As a result, in after-hours trading, EA’s stock price is down 7.6% to $118.53 a share.

虽然收入与一年前相比有所下降，但艺电在本季度的表现好于预期。但艺电对第三财季(第四个日历季度)的预测低于分析师的预期。因此，在盘后交易中，艺电的股价下跌了7.6%，至每股118.53美元。

It raises some questions about how strong and lasting the pandemic bump for gaming will be. EA reiterated how tough it is to predict things during the pandemic and the launch of a new console cycle.

这引发了一些问题，即游戏大流行的冲击将会有多强和多长时间。EA重申，在流感大流行和新一轮游戏机周期的启动期间，预测事情是多么困难。

In a call with analysts, chief financial officer Blake Jorgensen said, “It is a world in which we have never been before. We want to be careful [with forecasts] because it is a world that we don’t have a lot of history in.”

首席财务官布莱克·乔根森(Blake Jorgensen)在与分析师的电话会议中表示：“这是一个我们从未涉足的世界。我们希望(对预测)保持谨慎，因为这是一个我们没有太多历史的世界。“。

EA reported earnings per share of 63 cents, compared to $2.89 a share a year ago. GAAP revenues were $1.15 billion, compared to $1.35 billion a year earlier. Analysts had expected earnings of 35 cents a share on revenue of $9.59 billion.

艺电公布每股收益为63美分，而去年同期为每股2.89美元。GAAP的收入为11.5亿美元，而去年同期为13.5亿美元。分析师此前预计营收为95.9亿美元，每股收益为0.35美元。

One reason that the revenues are lower this year is that FIFA 21 is launching in the third fiscal quarter (the fourth calendar quarter ending December 31) instead of one quarter earlier. But that’s probably why analysts were bullish on the fourth calendar quarter.

今年收入较低的一个原因是，FIFA 21将在第三财季(截至12月31日的第四个日历季度)推出，而不是在一个季度之前推出。但这可能就是分析师看好第四个日历季度的原因。

EA forecast earnings of 61 cents a share on revenue of $1.68 billion for the third fiscal quarter (fourth calendar quarter) ended December 31, and $3.15 a share on revenue of $5.63 billion for the year. Analysts had expected third fiscal quarter earnings per share of $1.21 on revenue of $2.36 billion, and $3.48 a share on revenue of $6 billion a year.

艺电预计截至12月31日的第三财季(第四日历季度)每股收益为0.61美元，营收为16.8亿美元，全年营收为56.3亿美元，每股收益为3.15美元。分析师此前预计，第三财季每股收益为1.21美元，营收为23.6亿美元，一年营收为60亿美元，每股收益为3.48美元。

The big game publisher released some big games during the quarter, including UFC 4, Madden NFL 21, Rocket Arena, and Star Wars: Squadrons.

这家大型游戏发行商在本季度发布了一些大型游戏，包括UFC 4、Madden NFL 21、火箭竞技场(Rocket Arena)和星球大战：中队(Star Wars：Squadrons)。

Madden NFL 21 has nearly 30% more players this year compared to a year ago. And life-to-date, FIFA 20 has reached nearly 35 million players on console and PC. And EA has launched more than 125 games and content packs on Steam.

与一年前相比，21岁的马登NFL今年的球员数量增加了近30%。到目前为止，FIFA 20已经有近3500万游戏机和个人电脑玩家。EA已经在Steam上推出了超过125款游戏和内容包。

EA will spend some of its extra cash — $2.6 billion — on a stock repurchase program and first-ever quarterly cash dividend of 17 cents a share.

艺电将把部分额外现金-26亿美元-用于股票回购计划，以及有史以来第一次每股17美分的季度现金股息。

“Thanks to the incredible work of our teams and everything they continue to do for players while working from home, we’re delivering more fan-favorite games, growing our leading live services, and engaging more players across more platforms than ever before,” said CEO Andrew Wilson in a statement. “Our business has grown significantly this year, and we are projecting continued expansion into FY22 and beyond.”

该公司首席执行官安德鲁·威尔逊在一份声明中表示：“感谢我们的团队所做的令人难以置信的工作，以及他们在家工作时继续为球员所做的一切，我们正在提供更多球迷喜爱的游戏，发展我们领先的直播服务，并在更多平台上吸引更多的球员，”首席执行官安德鲁·威尔逊(Andrew Wilson)在一份声明中表示。我们的业务今年有了显著增长，我们计划在22财年及以后继续扩张。“。

Wilson said in a call with analysts that EA’s network has grown to 330 million unique accounts, with tens of millions joining (they did not give a time frame for this). He said Apex Legends is having great success in Asia with every new season of content. EA just launched Season 7 of the battle royale game from Respawn Entertainment. Jorgensen said that Apex Legends has gone from zero to $1 billion in less than two years.

威尔逊在与分析师的电话会议中表示，艺电的网络已经发展到3.3亿个独立账户，有数千万人加入(他们没有给出具体的时间表)。他说，Apex Legends的每一季内容都在亚洲取得了巨大成功。EA刚刚推出了Respawn Entertainment出品的《王室战斗》第七季。约根森说，Apex Legends在不到两年的时间里从零增加到10亿美元。

EA Play has more than 6.5 million paid subscribers, and Wlson said the company believes it can double the subscriber base over the next 12 months. EA is also adding games to Microsoft Game Pass, and Jorgensen said EA is generating revenue from that deal.

EA Play拥有超过650万付费订户，沃尔森表示，该公司相信未来12个月可以将订户基数翻一番。艺电还在微软游戏通行证(Microsoft Game Pass)上添加游戏，约根森表示，艺电正从这笔交易中获得收入。

Jorgensen added, “We delivered a quarter well above our guidance, driven by our live services, particularly Madden and FIFA. This resulted in a new record trailing twelve-month cash flow of $2.04 billion. We are pleased to announce a new share repurchase program and also to initiate a dividend for the first time in EA history. We are on track to deliver strong growth this year and expect continued growth in fiscal 2022 and in the years to come. Apex Legends is on track to become our latest billion-dollar franchise by the end of the fiscal year. All of our studios continue to execute amazingly well and have enabled us to launch an industry-leading eight games since the beginning of the fiscal year, while continuing to deliver live services content and expand onto new platforms.”

约根森补充说：“在直播服务，特别是Madden和FIFA的推动下，我们提供的服务比我们的指导高出四分之一。这导致过去12个月的现金流达到20.4亿美元，创历史新高。我们很高兴宣布一项新的股票回购计划，并在EA历史上首次启动分红。我们今年有望实现强劲增长，预计2022财年和未来几年将继续增长。《巅峰传奇》有望在本财年结束前成为我们最新的10亿美元特许经营权。我们所有的制片厂继续表现出色，使我们得以自本财年开始以来推出业界领先的8款游戏，同时继续提供现场服务内容并扩展到新平台。“。

By December 31, EA expects to launch FIFA 21, Medal of Honor: Above and Beyond, Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit Remastered, and NHL 21. Medal of Honor: Above and Beyond will launch on Oculus VR in December. The next Battlefield game is set to launch in the holiday of 2021.

到12月31日，艺电预计将推出FIFA 21，荣誉勋章：Bever and Beyond，Need for Speed：Hight Purchase Remasted，以及NHL 21。荣誉勋章：Beyond and Beyond将于12月在Oculus VR上推出。下一场战场游戏将于2021年的假期推出。

EA said net bookings for the past 12 months was $5.58 billion, up 8% from a year ago. Net bookings for the quarter were $910 million, compared with $1.31 billion a year earlier.

艺电表示，过去12个月的净预订量为55.8亿美元，同比增长8%。该季度的净预订量为9.1亿美元，而去年同期为13.1亿美元。
