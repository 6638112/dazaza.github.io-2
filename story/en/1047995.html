Copado, a DevOps platform built for Salesforce, raises $96M Series B led by Insight partners and Salesforce Ventures, bringing its total funding to $117M
Copado是为Salesforce构建的DevOps平台，在由Insight合作伙伴和Salesforce Ventures领导的B轮融资中筹集了9600万美元，使其总资金达到1.17亿美元

2021-02-17 22:11:32

Copado, a developer operations (DevOps) platform built for Salesforce, has closed a $96 million series B round co-led by Insight Partners and Salesforce Ventures. This follows an initial $26 million series B investment Copado announced last summer.

Copado是为Salesforce构建的开发人员运营（DevOps）平台，已完成由Insight Partners和Salesforce Ventures共同领导的9600万美元B轮融资。在此之前，去年夏天宣布了对Copado的2600万美元的B轮投资。

The Salesforce ecosystem could be at least 4 times larger than Salesforce itself, according to some estimates, as myriad third parties build sizable businesses off the platform. In the past three weeks alone, we’ve seen OwnBackup raise $167.5 million at a $1.4 billion valuation to bring cloud data backups to Salesforce, while fledgling startup Scratchpad secured $13 million for a productivity workspace aimed at Salesforce-powered sales teams.

据一些估计，由于无数第三方在平台上建立了规模可观的业务，因此Salesforce生态系统可能至少比Salesforce本身大4倍。仅在过去的三周中，我们就看到OwnBackup以14亿美元的估值筹集了1.675亿美元，以将云数据备份带到Salesforce，而刚起步的创业公司Scratchpad则获得了1300万美元的资金，用于面向由Salesforce推动的销售团队的生产力工作区。

Salesforce, which is now a $230 billion company in its own right, may attribute much of its success over the past 20 years to its thriving partnership ecosystem, which Copado is playing a growing part in expanding.

Salesforce现在本身就是一家市值2300亿美元的公司，在过去20年中，其成功的大部分原因可能是其蓬勃发展的合作伙伴生态系统，而Copado在其扩展业务中发挥着越来越重要的作用。

While Salesforce helps with many of the processes associated with DevOps, such as hosting and managing infrastructure, Salesforce DevOps teams still have to manage their deployment pipeline, or the steps and environments they go through to get their Salesforce configuration and code into production.

尽管Salesforce可以帮助解决与DevOps相关的许多流程，例如托管和管理基础架构，但是Salesforce DevOps团队仍必须管理其部署管道，或者他们将Salesforce配置和代码投入生产所经历的步骤和环境。

Founded out of Madrid, Spain in 2013, Copado promises enterprises an integrated Salesforce-native platform that covers each part of the DevOps process, including agile planning, continuous delivery, automated testing, and compliance. “It automates how companies build software in the cloud at scale,” Copado CEO Ted Elliott told VentureBeat.

Copado于2013年在西班牙马德里成立，向企业提供一个集成的Salesforce本地平台，该平台涵盖DevOps流程的每个部分，包括敏捷计划，持续交付，自动化测试和合规性。 Copado首席执行官Ted Elliott对VentureBeat表示：“它可以自动实现公司在云端大规模构建软件的方式。”

Copado is ultimately about increasing product releases, reducing bugs, and enhancing companies’ return on their SaaS cloud spending.

Copado最终将致力于增加产品发布，减少错误并提高公司在SaaS云上的支出回报。

The broader DevOps market, which was pegged at $5.2 billion in 2018, has plenty of tools to help developer and operations teams function cohesively and ship quality software on schedule. But Copado is betting that Salesforce DevOps teams need custom-fit tools.

广阔的DevOps市场在2018年约为52亿美元，它拥有大量工具来帮助开发人员和运营团队紧密协作并按时交付高质量软件。但是Copado认为Salesforce DevOps团队需要定制工具。

“Low-code cloud platforms like Salesforce work very differently than traditional development platforms. This prevents developers from working with popular code-based DevOps solutions,” Elliott said. “Copado is the only DevOps platform that was purpose-built for the speed and technology of SaaS clouds.”

“低代码云平台（如Salesforce）的工作方式与传统开发平台完全不同。这阻止了开发人员使用流行的基于代码的DevOps解决方案。” Elliott说。 “ Copado是唯一专门为SaaS云的速度和技术而构建的DevOps平台。”

Multinational chemical company Linde used Copado’s platform to “move from manually intensive development processes to a fully automated and transparent delivery pipeline,” Elliott said, one that “aligns multiple teams on a single set of business goals.”

跨国化工公司Linde使用Copado的平台“从人工密集的开发流程转变为完全自动化且透明的交付流程，” Elliott说道，“该流程可以使多个团队实现同一组业务目标。”

Copado has now raised a total of $117 million since its inception. With its fresh cash injection, which included investments from Lead Edge Capital, ISAI Cap Venture, and Perpetual Investors, the company is now well-financed to double down on its existing Salesforce support while also expanding its horizons.

自成立以来，Copado现在总共筹集了1.17亿美元。通过其新的现金注入，其中包括Lead Edge Capital，ISAI Cap Venture和Perpetual Investors的投资，该公司现在财务状况良好，可以在支持现有Salesforce的同时加倍投入，同时扩大视野。

“Based on customer demand, Copado is increasingly being used to deliver multi-cloud projects,” Elliott said. “This investment allows Copado to continue to expand and support additional clouds and platforms outside of Salesforce as our customers build more sophisticated and integrated customer experiences.”

“基于客户需求，Copado越来越多地用于交付多云项目，” Elliott说。 “这项投资使Copado可以继续扩展和支持Salesforce之外的其他云和平台，因为我们的客户可以建立更复杂和集成的客户体验。”
