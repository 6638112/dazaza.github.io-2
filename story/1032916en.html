旅行者2号在无线电沉寂八个月后重新上线

2020-11-03 13:34:35

Australian antenna upgrade appears to have worked and should be ready to help with imminent Mars landings

澳大利亚天线升级似乎已经奏效，应该准备好帮助即将登陆的火星。

The long break in conversation was due to maintenance of the only Earthly antenna now capable of sending signals to the probe needed an overhaul.

对话的长时间中断是因为维护了现在唯一能够向探测器发送信号的地球天线，需要进行大修。

The antenna in question is named Deep Space Station 43 (DSS43) and is located just outside Canberra, Australia’s capital city. The facility’s 70 metre main dish is forty-eight years old and the only antenna of that size in the southern hemisphere. As Voyager 2 is heading southwards compared to Earth’s orbital plane, only a dish south of the equator can send the probe a sufficiently powerful signal.

正在讨论的天线被命名为深空站43(DSS43)，位于澳大利亚首都堪培拉郊外。该设施的70米长的主菜已经有48年的历史了，也是南半球唯一一个这样大小的天线。与地球轨道平面相比，旅行者2号正在向南飞行，只有赤道以南的碟子才能向探测器发送足够强大的信号。

NASA started a refresh of the dish’s electrical cabling, power supply and cooling systems in March. The maintenance also saw the installation of a new X-band frequency cone that improved the facility’s sensitivity. Doing so required a crane capable of hoisting the three-tonne cone to a height of 20 storeys. Here’s a vid of that effort.

美国国家航空航天局(NASA)在3月份开始更新碟子的电缆、电力供应和冷却系统。维修还安装了一个新的X波段频率锥，提高了设施的灵敏度。要做到这一点，需要一台能够将3吨重的锥体吊装到20层楼高的起重机。以下是这一努力的视频。

The cone was installed in early May. And NASA has now reveaked that on October 29th the new rig was used to send a command to Voyager 2, and the probe responded.

这个圆锥体是在5月初安装的。美国国家航空航天局现在透露，10月29日，新的钻机被用来向旅行者2号发送命令，探测器做出了回应。

Which is no mean feat given that Voyager 2 is more than 18,750,000,000 km from Earth and getting further away at a rate of about 60,000 km/h. The probe launched on August 20th 1977, but is still just seventeen light-hours away.

考虑到旅行者2号距离地球超过18.75亿公里，并以每小时约6万公里的速度更远，这是一个不容易的壮举。探测器于1977年8月20日发射，但距离地球仍只有17光小时。

The job of upgrading DSS43 is not over, but the success of the Voyager 2 chat has left astroboffins confident they’ll wrap things up in line with the planned February 2021 deadline.

DSS43的升级工作还没有结束，但旅行者2号聊天的成功让ASTROFING相信他们会按照计划的2021年2月的最后期限完成工作。

Which will be just in time for the arrival of the Perseverance Rover on Mars, another mission DSS43 will handle along with its normal duties of staying in touch with other space probes. ®

这将正好赶上毅力月球车抵达火星，DSS43将在执行与其他太空探测器保持联系的正常职责的同时处理另一项任务。®。
