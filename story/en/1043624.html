Tesla Passes Facebook To Become Fifth Most Valuable US Company
特斯拉超越Facebook成为美国第五大最有价值公司

2021-01-09 08:52:05

Tesla stock closed up more than 7%, bringing its market cap to $820 billion. Facebook, meanwhile, closed the day with a $763 billion market cap.

特斯拉股价收盘涨幅超过7％，市值达到8200亿美元。与此同时，Facebook当天的市值为7630亿美元。

The rise makes Elon Musk's automotive venture the fifth-biggest company in the large-cap benchmark when counting the share classes of Alphabet together. It now just trails Apple, Microsoft, Amazon and Alphabet.

在将Alphabet的股份类别一起计算时，该上升使埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）的汽车企业成为大盘基准中的第五大公司。现在，它仅次于苹果，微软，亚马逊和Alphabet。

While the mark is largely symbolic, it signifies Tesla's dramatic rise over just a short period of time. Tesla became the first  $100 billion publicly listed U.S. carmaker in January 2019. Just 10 months later, the company surpassed a $500 billion market cap. The stock gained more than 700% in 2020.

虽然该商标在很大程度上具有象征意义，但它标志着特斯拉在短时间内的戏剧性崛起。特斯拉于2019年1月成为首家市值达1000亿美元的美国汽车制造商。仅仅10个月后，该公司的市值就超过了5000亿美元。该股票在2020年上涨了700％以上。

Tesla joined the S&P 500 in December, wrapping up a strong year of performance. Led by Musk, the company has turned in five consecutive profitable quarters. Tesla delivered 499,550 vehicles in 2020, and is currently building new factories  in Austin, Texas, and  Brandenburg, Germany, among other efforts to grow its production and sales volume.

特斯拉（Tesla）于12月加入标准普尔500指数（S＆amp; P 500），见证了强劲的一年表现。在马斯克的带领下，该公司已经连续五个季度实现盈利。特斯拉在2020年交付了499,550辆汽车，目前正在努力在德克萨斯州奥斯汀和德国勃兰登堡建立新工厂，以提高产量和销量。

Tesla shares rose this week following a very bullish note from Morgan Stanley analyst Adam Jonas, and news of a Democratic majority in the Senate after the runoff elections in Georgia.

摩根士丹利（Morgan Stanley）分析师亚当·乔纳斯（Adam Jonas）非常看好美联储，以及在佐治亚州举行径流选举后参议院获得民主党多数票的消息后，特斯拉股价本周上涨。

Among other things, President-elect Joe Biden is aiming to create 1 million new jobs in the domestic auto industry, expand electric vehicle charging infrastructure across the U.S., and ramp up solar energy and other renewables here as part of his clean energy plan. It would ostensibly prove easier for him to advance these goals with a Democratic majority in the Senate. The Democrats sealed their majority in the chamber this week after the Georgia runoffs.

别的不说，总统当选人拜登的目标是在国内汽车行业中创造100万个新的就业机会，扩大电动汽车在美国境内的充电基础设施，在这里，斜升太阳能和其他可再生能源作为其清洁能源计划的一部分。从表面上看，如果他在参议院以民主党多数席位实现这些目标，将更容易实现。在佐治亚州决选之后，民主党人本周将多数人封入了众议院。

According to CNBC calculations, Thursday's increase in Tesla's share price pushed Musk past  Jeff Bezos, who had been the richest person since 2017 and is currently worth about $184 billion. Musk started 2020 worth about $27 billion, and was barely in the top 50 richest people.

根据CNBC的计算，周四特斯拉股价的上涨使马斯克超越了杰夫·贝佐斯（Jeff Bezos），后者自2017年以来一直是首富，目前身价约为1840亿美元。到2020年，马斯克的身家约为270亿美元，仅排在前50名最富有的人中。
