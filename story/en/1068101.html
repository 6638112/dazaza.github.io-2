Biden's New $1.2 Trillion Infrastructure Plan Includes $65 Billion for Universal Broadband
Biden的新增1.2万亿美元的基础设施计划包括650亿美元用于通用宽带

2021-06-25 04:23:26

President Joe Biden and Vice President Kamala Harris on Thursday agreed to a $1.2 trillion bipartisan infrastructure plan that includes building out high-speed universal broadband across the nation. The infrastructure framework will use two-thirds of the resources from Biden's proposed American Jobs Plan, and also includes clean transportation, clean water infrastructure, renewable energy infrastructure and climate change resilience.

Joe Biden总统和Kamala Harris副总裁周四同意了1.2万亿美元的两亿美元的基础设施计划，包括在全国范围内建立高速通用宽带。基础设施框架将使用来自Biden＆＃39;拟议的美国工作计划的三分之二的资源，还包括清洁的运输，清洁水基础设施，可再生能源基础设施和气候变化弹性。

Under the plan, $65 billion will be invested in broadband for all. It proposes state and local investment in broadband infrastructure as well as using the proceeds from 5G spectrum auctions.

根据该计划，将投资6.5亿美元的宽带。它提出了宽带基础设施的州和当地投资，以及使用5G频谱拍卖的收益。

It's a step backwards from the $2.25 trillion infrastructure plan previously proposed by Biden in March, which included $100 billion for broadband infrastructure. In March, Biden spoke about the digital divide, and how more than 30 million Americans have no access to broadband while those living in urban and suburban markets face broadband bills that are too expensive.

"Democracy requires compromise," the White House said in a fact sheet published Thursday. "The historic Bipartisan Infrastructure Framework will make life better for millions of Americans, create a generation of good-paying union jobs and economic growth and position the United States to win the 21st century."
