Output为音乐家、作曲家、制作人和声音设计师开发软件和虚拟乐器，由Summit Partners牵头筹集4500万美元的首轮融资

2020-10-24 22:05:21

Output, a tech company whose software has been used in songs by Drake, Coldplay, Justin Bieber and Rihanna and musical scores including Stranger Things, Game of Thrones and Black Panther – has raised a $45 million Series A investment from Summit Partners.

科技公司Output的软件已被德雷克(Drake)、酷玩乐队(Coldplay)、贾斯汀·比伯(Justin Bieber)和蕾哈娜(Rihanna)使用在歌曲中，音乐配乐包括Stranger Things、Game Of Thrones和Black Panther。Output从Summit Partners筹集了4500万美元的首轮投资。

The Los Angeles-based company was founded in 2013 by CEO Gregg Lehrman, an ASCAP-award winning composer who got his start under Hans Zimmer and later produced sounds for various music software platforms.

这家总部位于洛杉矶的公司于2013年由首席执行官格雷格·莱曼(Gregg Lehrman)创立，他是ASCAP获奖作曲家，在汉斯·齐默(Hans Zimmer)的领导下起步，后来为各种音乐软件平台制作声音。

Output develops software for musicians, composers, producers and sound designers, including virtual instruments and FX in addition to samples and loops available through its Arcade app. The company also makes studio furniture.

Output为音乐家、作曲家、制作人和声音设计师开发软件，除了通过其Arade应用程序提供的样本和循环外，还包括虚拟乐器和FX。该公司还生产工作室家具。

The company claims that its software is used by producers, songwriters and composers to “find inspiration and break through writer’s block”.

该公司声称其软件被制作人、词曲作者和作曲家用来“寻找灵感，突破作者的障碍”。

The artists using Outputs’s software range from Diplo and Zedd to Mike Dean, Lil Jon and many others.

使用Output软件的艺术家范围从Diplo和Zazd到Mike Dean，Lil Jon和许多其他人。

According to a press release announcing the new investment, Output’s flagship music creation application Arcade is used in 167 countries and was originally “intended to help music professionals turn a library of tens of thousands loops and sounds into tracks”.

根据一份宣布新投资的新闻稿，Output的旗舰音乐创作应用Arade在167个国家使用，最初的目的是“帮助音乐专业人士将一个拥有数万个循环和声音的库变成曲目”。

Output claims that more than 50% of Arcade sessions result in the writing of a new track and that the “average user locks in a track within fifteen minutes”.

Output声称超过50%的Arade会话会导致新曲目的写入，并且“平均用户在15分钟内锁定一个曲目”。

In addition, Output states that more than 420,000 musicians have been supported by the Arcade platform since 2018 and that the company has seen a 300% increase in monthly recurring revenue (MRR) in the last 12 months.

此外，Output表示，自2018年以来，已有超过42万名音乐家得到Arade平台的支持，在过去12个月中，该公司的每月经常性收入(MRR)增长了300%。

This minority investment represents Output’s first-ever fundraise and will support the company’s continued global growth and expansion of its digital production platform, including the addition of playable instruments and mobile applications in 2021.

这笔少数股权投资代表了Output有史以来的首次融资，并将支持该公司在全球范围内的持续增长和数字生产平台的扩张，包括在2021年增加可播放乐器和移动应用程序。

Output claims to be one of the first SaaS solutions at this scale to offer loops, samples and melodic content in one unified platform for music creation.

Output声称是这种规模的首批SaaS解决方案之一，在一个统一的音乐创作平台上提供循环、样本和旋律内容。

“Technology democratized the creation process for video and photos. It makes sense that technology adoption would transition to music making.”

“技术使视频和照片的创作过程民主化。技术的采用将过渡到音乐制作，这是有道理的。“。

“Technology democratized the creation process for video and photos. It makes sense that technology adoption would transition to music making,” said Lehrman, Output Founder and CEO.

“技术使视频和照片的创作过程民主化。Output创始人兼首席执行官雷曼表示：“技术采用将过渡到音乐制作，这是有道理的。

“We are working to inspire a new generation of music makers with a platform that brings the power of a studio to anyone, wherever they are. Summit’s deep experience in consumer technology, digital media and e-commerce make them an ideal partner to help accelerate our global impact.”

我们正在努力用一个平台激励新一代音乐制作人，这个平台可以把录音室的力量带给任何人，无论他们在哪里。顶峰公司在消费技术、数字媒体和电子商务方面的深厚经验使他们成为帮助加速我们的全球影响力的理想合作伙伴。“。

Andrew Collins, a Managing Director with Summit Partners who has joined the Output Board of Directors, added: “We believe Output is at the forefront of digital music creation, offering a truly elegant, professional-grade platform that is accessible to music makers at all levels.

已经加入Output董事会的Summit Partners总经理安德鲁·柯林斯(Andrew Collins)补充说：“我们相信，Output处于数字音乐创作的前沿，为各级音乐制作人提供了一个真正优雅的专业级平台。
