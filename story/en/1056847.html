57 years ago: System/360 Announcement
57年前：系统/ 360公告

The following is the text of an IBM Data Processing Division press release distributed on April 7, 1964.

以下是1964年4月7日分发的IBM数据处理司新闻稿的文本。

A new generation of electronic computing equipment was introduced today by International Business Machines Corporation.

国际商业机器公司今天推出了新一代电子计算设备。

IBM Board Chairman Thomas J. Watson Jr. called the event the most important product announcement in the company's history.

IBM董事会主席Thomas J. Watson Jr.呼吁该活动是公司最重要的产品公布＆＃39;历史。

It combines microelectronic technology, which makes possible operating speeds measured in billionths of a second, with significant advances in the concepts of computer organization.

它结合了微电子技术，这使得在二十一秒中测量的可能工作速度，具有计算机组织概念的显着进步。

"System/360 represents a sharp departure from concepts of the past in designing and building computers. It is the product of an international effort in IBM's laboratories and plants and is the first time IBM has redesigned the basic internal architecture of its computers in a decade. The result will be more computer productivity at lower cost than ever before. This is the beginning of a new generation - - not only of computers - - but of their application in business, science and government."

＆＃34; System / 360代表了在设计和建筑计算机中过去概念的急剧偏离。它是IBM＆＃39; L实验室和植物中的国际努力的产品，是IBM首次在十年内重新设计了其计算机的基本内部架构。结果将比以往更低的成本更低的计算机生产率。这是新一代的开始 - - 不仅是计算机 - 而是他们在商业，科学和政府中的应用。＆＃34;

More than 100,000 businessmen in 165 American cities today attended meetings at which System/360 was announced.

今天165名美国城市的10多万名商人参加了系统/ 360宣布的会议。

System/360 is a single system spanning the performance range of virtually all current IBM computers - - from the widely used 1401 to nearly twice that of the most powerful computer previously built by the company. It was developed to perform information handling jobs encompassing all types of applications.

System / 360是一个单一的系统，包括几乎所有当前IBM计算机的性能范围 - - 从广泛使用的1401到近两倍于公司以前由公司构建的最强大的计算机。它是开发的，以执行包含所有类型应用程序的信息处理作业。

System/360 includes in its central processors 19 combinations of graduated speed and memory capacity. Incorporated with these are more than 40 types of peripheral equipment which store information and enter it into and retrieve it from the computer. Built-in communications capability makes System/360 available to remote terminals, regardless of distance.

系统/ 360包括其中央处理器19渐变速度和存储容量的组合。包含这些内容超过40种类型的外围设备，存储信息并从计算机中输入并检索它。内置通信功能使系统/ 360可用于远程终端，无论距离如何。

The equipment is supported by programs which enable System/360 to schedule its own activities for non-stop computing that makes most efficient use of system capabilities.

设备支持的程序支持，该程序使系统/ 360安排自己的非停止计算活动，这使得最有效地利用系统功能。

Internal processing power of the largest System/360 configuration is approximately 50 times greater than that of the smallest. The system's machine cycle time - - basic pulse beat of a computer - - ranges from one millionth-of-a-second to only 200 billionths-of-a-second.

最大系统/ 360配置的内部处理能力大约比最小的50倍。系统＆＃39; S机器周期时间 - 计算机的基本脉冲跳动 - 范围从一百万到秒到2000亿个-2秒。

System/360 core storage memory capacity ranges from 8,000 characters of information to more than 8,000,000. Information storage devices linked to the system can store additional billions of characters of data and make them available for processing at varying speeds, depending on need.

系统/ 360核心存储内存容量范围从信息的8,000个字符到8,000,000。连接到系统的信息存储设备可以存储额外的数据字符，并根据需要，使其以不同的速度处理。

It is the balancing of these factors - - all available within a single system using one set of programming instructions - - that will make it possible for a user to select a configuration suited to his own requirements for both commercial and scientific computing. With the same type of input/output devices, a user can expand his System/360 to any point in its performance range, without reprogramming.

这是使用一组编程指令的单个系统中的这些因素的平衡 - 这将使用户可以选择适用于商业和科学计算的自己要求的配置。使用相同类型的输入/输出设备，用户可以将其系统/ 360扩展到其性能范围内的任何点，而无需重新编程。

Solid Logic Technology. Microelectronic circuits -- product of IBM's Solid Logic Technology -- make up the system's basic componentry. System/360 is the first commercially available data processing system whose design is based on the use of microminiaturized computer circuits. Called logic circuits because they carry and control the electrical impulses which represent information within a computer, these tiny devices operate at speeds ranging from 300 down to six billionths-of-a-second. Transistors and diodes mounted on the circuits are only 28 thousandths-of-an-inch square and are protected by a film of glass 60 millionths-of-an-inch thick.

实心逻辑技术。微电子电路 - IBM＆＃39; S固体逻辑技术的产品 - 构成系统＆＃39; s基本组件。系统/ 360是第一商业上可用的数据处理系统，其设计基于使用微型计算机电路的使用。被称为逻辑电路，因为它们携带和控制代表计算机内信息的电脉冲，这些微小的装置以300降至六十亿分钟的速度运行。安装在电路上的晶体管和二极管仅为28千分之二英寸的方形，并受6000百万分钟厚的玻璃薄膜保护。

Memory power. A hierarchy of memories within System/360 makes information in core storage available at varying speeds. Small local store memories operate in as little as 200 billionths-of-a-second. Control memories operate in as little as 250 billionths-of-a-second. Powerful main memories - - containing up to 524,000 characters of information - - range from 2.5 millionths-of-a-second down to one millionth-of-a-second. A key development provides 8,000,000 characters in bulk core storage - - each character available in eight millionths-of-a-second and each at the direct command of a computer programmer. This is over sixty times more directly addressable characters than were previously available in IBM computers. The computer's historic limitations on memory size are overcome by this development.

存储器电源。系统/ 360内的存储器层次结构在不同速度下提供核心存储中的信息。小型本地商店记忆在少至2000亿秒内运行。控制记忆在少至250亿秒的时间内运行。强大的主要回忆 - - 含有高达524,000个字符的信息 - - 范围为2.5百万分钟-秒到百万分之一的一百万。一个关键的开发提供批量核心存储中8,000,000个字符 - - 每个字符在八百百万分中可用，每个字符在一次计算机程序员的直接命令中。这超过IBM计算机以前可寻址的可寻址字符超过六十次。通过该开发克服了计算机＆＃39;对内存大小的历史限制是克服的。

Application versatility. The traditional distinction between computers for commercial and scientific use is eliminated in System/360. Users will be able to process both business and scientific problems, or a combination of the two, with equal effectiveness. This versatility is reinforced by the variety of peripheral equipment which is part of the system.

应用程序多功能性。在系统/ 360中消除了商业和科学用途计算机之间的传统区别。用户将能够处理业务和科学问题，或两者的组合，具有平等的有效性。这种多功能性由作为系统的一部分的外围设备而加强。

Communications capability. Built into System/360 is the ability to respond to inquiries and messages from remote locations at any time. Hundreds of terminal devices can communicate simultaneously with a system while the computer continues to process the basic job on which it is working. System/360 monthly rentals will range from $2,700 for a basic configuration to $115,000 for a typical large multisystem configuration. Comparable purchase prices range from $133,000 to $5,500,000. Deliveries of the small configurations of System/360 are scheduled to begin in the third quarter of 1965. Deliveries of the largest configurations are scheduled to begin in the first quarter of 1966.

通信能力。内置于系统/ 360是能够随时响应远程位置的询问和消息。数百个终端设备可以在计算机继续处理其工作的基本作业时与系统同时通信。系统/ 360每月租赁的范围从2,700美元的基本配置为115,000美元，以满足典型的大型多系统配置。可比的购买价格从133,000美元到5,500,000美元。 System / 360的小型配置的交付计划于1965年第三季度开始。最大配置的交货计划于1966年第一季度开始。
