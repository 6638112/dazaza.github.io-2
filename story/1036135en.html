SARS-CoV-2 concentrations and virus-laden aerosol distributions in outdoor air
室外空气中的SARS-CoV-2浓度和携带病毒的气溶胶分布

2020-11-24 05:19:36

The COVID-19 disease spread at different rates in the different countries and in different regions of the same country, as happened in Italy. Transmission by contact or at close range due to large respiratory droplets is widely accepted, however, the role of airborne transmission due to small respiratory droplets emitted by infected individuals (also asymptomatic) is controversial. It was suggested that outdoor airborne transmission could play a role in determining the differences observed in the spread rate. Concentrations of virus-laden aerosol are still poorly known and contrasting results are reported, especially for outdoor environments. Here we investigated outdoor concentrations and size distributions of virus-laden aerosol simultaneously collected during the pandemic, in May 2020, in northern (Veneto) and southern (Apulia) regions of Italy. The two regions exhibited significantly different prevalence of COVID-19. Genetic material of SARS-CoV-2 (RNA) was determined, using both real time RT-PCR and ddPCR, in air samples collected using PM 10 samplers and cascade impactors able to separate 12 size ranges from nanoparticles (diameter D < 0.056 µm) up to coarse particles (D > 18 µm). Air samples tested negative for the presence of SARS-CoV-2 at both sites, viral particles concentrations were <0.8 copies m −3 in PM 10 and <0.4 copies m −3 in each size range investigated. Outdoor air in residential and urban areas was generally not infectious and safe for the public in both northern and southern Italy, with the possible exclusion of very crowded sites. Therefore, it is likely that outdoor airborne transmission does not explain the difference in the spread of COVID-19 observed in the two Italian regions.

COVID-19疾病在意大利的不同国家和同一地区的不同地区以不同的速度传播。由于大的呼吸道飞沫通过接触传播或在近距离传播，已被广泛接受，但是，由于感染个体散发的小的呼吸道飞沫（也无症状）引起的空气传播传播作用是有争议的。有人认为，室外机载传播可在确定传播速度差异方面发挥作用。载有病毒的气雾剂的浓度仍然知之甚少，并且报道了相反的结果，尤其是在室外环境中。在这里，我们调查了2020年5月在意大利北部（威尼托）和南部（普利亚）大流行期间同时收集的室外病毒载气的浓度和大小分布。这两个区域表现出明显不同的COVID-19患病率。使用实时RT-PCR和ddPCR确定了SARS-CoV-2（RNA）的遗传物质，使用PM 10采样器和级联撞击器收集的空气样本能够从纳米颗粒中分离出12个尺寸范围（直径D 18 µm）。空气样品在两个部位均检测到SARS-CoV-2呈阴性，在所研究的每个尺寸范围内，病毒颗粒浓度在PM 10中分别为<0.8拷贝m -3和<0.4拷贝m -3。在意大利北部和南部，住宅和城市地区的室外空气通常对公众而言并不具有传染性和安全性，可能会排除非常拥挤的场所。因此，室外机载传播可能无法解释在两个意大利地区观察到的COVID-19传播差异。
