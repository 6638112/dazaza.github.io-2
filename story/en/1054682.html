Fishbrain, a Stockholm, Sweden-based sport fishing app, social network, and digital marketplace, raised $31M in funding.

Fishbrain，斯德哥尔摩，基于瑞典的运动钓鱼应用，社交网络和数字市场，筹集资金3100万美元。

Backers included Consensus Småbolagsfond and Adrigo Asset Management, as well as all main existing shareholders, including B Capital Group and SoftBank Ventures Asia.

支持者包括共识Småbolagsfond和Adrigo资产管理，以及所有主要现有股东，包括B Capital Group和Softbank Ventures Asia。

The company will use the funding to continue to scale its user base, solidify its position in Sweden and Norway, strengthen its engineering, product, marketing, and sales teams at its HQ and product hub in Stockholm, as well as through hiring in the US and throughout Europe.

该公司将利用资金继续扩大其用户基础，巩固其在瑞典和挪威的职位，加强其在斯德哥尔摩总部的工程，产品，营销和销售团队，以及在美国招聘在整个欧洲。

Led by Johan Attby, CEO and co-founder, Fishbrain is a mobile app, social network, and social commerce platform for fishing. With nearly 12 million registered users across the globe, the app helps create the best possible fishing experience by providing everyone – whether beginner or pro – with the tools, insights, support, and fishing gear to enjoy fishing. The app’s interactive map helps anglers find hot new fishing spots and see what other people are catching, with what bait and specifically where. Fishbrain’s in-app and on-web marketplace, Fishbrain Shop, allows users to find and purchase the best fishing gear from over 300 of the world’s most respected brands. Other features include Fish Species Recognition, marine mapping (provided by C-MAP), forecasts, bait recommendations and more.

FishBrain是由Johan Attby，CEO和联合创始人领导的是钓鱼的移动应用程序，社交网络和社会商务平台。全球近1200万注册用户，该应用程序通过提供所有人 - 无论是初学者还是临近的工具，见解，支持和渔具，都可以帮助创造最佳捕鱼体验。该应用程序的互动地图有助于钓鱼者找到热门的新钓鱼点，看看其他人正在捕捉到什么，以及诱饵和专门的地方。 FishBrain的应用内和网上市场，FishBrain店，允许用户从世界上超过300个最受尊敬的品牌中查找和购买最佳渔具。其他功能包括鱼类识别，海洋映射（由C-MAP提供），预测，诱饵建议等。
