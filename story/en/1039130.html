We’ve got our first real look at Loki as Disney drops lengthy teaser trailer
随着迪士尼丢下冗长的预告片，我们对Loki有了第一次真正的了解

2020-12-12 02:29:55

Among the many (many!) tidbits unveiled during Disney's Investor Day was a lengthy teaser trailer for Loki, the Marvel spinoff limited series featuring Tom Hiddleston's charismatic trickster demigod and (adoptive) brother to Thor, last seen in Avengers: Endgame. Marvel Studios had vowed to provide "hefty" budgets for its various limited series, like WandaVision (which also has a new trailer), and this Loki teaser definitely has the high cinematic production values to show for it. Marvel Studios announced the development of its various limited series for Disney+ in 2018, all featuring supporting characters from the films who were not given their own standalone films within the MCEU. Details have largely been kept tightly under wraps, but we do know that both Loki and WandaVision will tie into the forthcoming Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness. Fan favorite Loki has always been one of the most multi-layered, psychologically complex characters, so we're keen to see what the limited series has in store for him.

在迪士尼投资者日期间发布的许多花絮中，有很长的一段是为洛基（Loki）宣传的预告片，其中包括汤姆·希德勒斯顿（Tom Hiddleston）具有超凡魅力的骗子半神和（收养）兄弟（托尔）的漫威限量版在《复仇者联盟：残局》中。 Marvel Studios发誓要提供“沉重”的游戏。它的各种有限系列的预算，例如WandaVision（也有新的预告片），这款Loki宣传片肯定具有很高的电影制作价值。漫威影业（Marvel Studios）宣布将于2018年为迪士尼+开发各种限量系列，所有这些系列均以电影中的配角为特色，这些人物在MCEU中未获得独立电影的支持。大部分细节都被严格保密，但是我们知道，Loki和WandaVision都将与即将出版的《疯狂的宇宙》中的奇异博士联系在一起。粉丝最喜欢的Loki一直是最多层，心理上复杂的角色之一，因此我们非常希望看到限量系列为他准备的东西。

Loki's head writer, Michael Waldron ( Rick and Morty), talked a little bit about that in a podcast interview back in March. “I think it’s the struggle with identity, who you are, who you want to be. I’m really drawn to characters who are fighting for control,” said Waldron. “Certainly, you see that with Loki over the first ten years of movies, he’s out of control at pivotal parts of his life, he was adopted and everything, and that manifests itself through anger and spite towards his family.”

Loki的首席作家Michael Waldron（Rick和Morty）在三月份的播客采访中谈到了这一点。 “我认为这是与身份，你是谁，你想成为谁的斗争。我真的很喜欢为控制而战的角色，” Waldron说。 “当然，您会发现在Loki的前十年电影中，他在生活的各个关键部分都失去了控制，他被收养，一切都变得如此，这通过对家人的愤怒和恶意表现出来。”

A brief shot from Loki that aired during the Super Bowl this year showed him wearing a jumpsuit with the initials "TVA" stamped on it. Hardcore Marvel fans will recognize the acronym: it stands for Time Variance Authority, the so-called "custodians of chronology" in the MCU, who monitor violations to the timeline. Catch their attention by trying to change history, and you just might meet the wrong end of the Retroactive Cannon (Ret Con) and have your entire history deleted from the historical timeline.

洛基（Loki）在今年的超级碗比赛中播出的简短镜头显示，他身穿带有首字母＆＃34; TVA＆＃34;的连身裤。踩在上面。顽固的Marvel粉丝会认出这个缩写词：它代表时间差异管理局，即所谓的“时间管理者”。在MCU中，他们监视违反时间轴的行为。通过尝试更改历史记录来吸引他们的注意，您可能会遇到追溯大炮（Ret Con）的错误提示，并将整个历史记录从历史时间轴中删除。

Time travel will very likely play a significant role in Loki, and this teaser trailer largely confirms that. It opens with a flashback to a pivotal scene in Endgame, where 2012 Loki snags the tesseract containing the Space Stone and vanishes through a portal. We next see him in a barren desert, with some puzzled local denizens looking on. Cut to a scene with Owen Wilson's still-unidentified character in an elevator—he's definitely TVA, since Loki is wearing his prisoner's jumpsuit, and Wilson admits as much in the scene ("Time passes differently in the TVA").

时空旅行很可能在Loki中扮演重要角色，这部预告片在很大程度上证实了这一点。它以倒叙开始，回味到《残局》中的关键场景，2012年的洛基（Loki）抓住包含太空石的tesseract，并通过门户消失。接下来，我们在一个荒芜的沙漠中看到他，当地居民有些困惑。切入场景中的是欧文·威尔逊（Owen Wilson）仍不为人知的角色，坐在电梯里-他绝对是TVA，因为洛基（Loki）穿着他的囚犯连身衣，威尔逊（Wilson）在场景中也承认了这一点（＆＃ 34;时间在TVA中流逝的时间有所不同）。

Loki assumes he's going to be executed, but Wilson's character assures him he just wants to talk. Loki claims he doesn't like to talk, but, well, that's a lie. ("We both know you love to talk," Wilson's character snarks. "Talky talky... ") The rest of the trailer is a jumble of scenes without much of a narrative thread, but it does seem clear that the TVA will be forcing Loki to face up to the damage he has wrought.

洛基（Loki）假定他将被处决，但威尔逊（Wilson）的性格向他保证，他只想讲话。洛基（Loki）声称他不喜欢说话，但是，那真是个谎言。 （我们俩都知道您喜欢交谈，威尔逊的角色在偷偷摸摸。＆＃34;说话健谈...＆＃34;）其余的预告片是一堆混乱的场面大部分是叙述性的线索，但显然TVA将迫使Loki面对他所造成的损害。

Loki is currently slated for release on Disney+ on May 21, 2021. All in all, it looks like a terrific new adventure for the character, and we're counting the days till then.

Loki目前定于2021年5月21日在Disney +上发行。总而言之，这对角色来说似乎是一次了不起的新冒险，我们正在计算直到那时的日子。
