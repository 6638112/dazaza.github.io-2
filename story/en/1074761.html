PlanetScale increases plans to include billions of reads
PlanetScale增加了数十亿次阅读的计划

2022-02-25 07:55:44

Since the beginning of PlanetScale, our team has worked to provide our users with an incredibly powerful and fast serverless database at a reasonable price. In the past year, we’ve introduced our database branching feature, deploy requests, and better query statistics. We are just getting started and have some more game-changing features coming your way in March.

自PlanetScale推出以来，我们的团队一直致力于以合理的价格为用户提供功能强大、速度极快的无服务器数据库。在过去的一年中，我们引入了数据库分支功能、部署请求和更好的查询统计。我们才刚刚开始，三月份会有更多改变游戏规则的功能。

We’ve also heard your feedback about how our pricing is difficult to understand and hard for you to predict. Today we’re announcing a few changes that we think will make your life a lot easier. Starting March 1st we’ll be offering our customers up to 200x more reads across all pricing plans. Notably the Scaler plan now comes with 100 billion reads included for $29/month.

我们也听到了您对我们的定价难以理解和预测的反馈。今天，我们宣布一些我们认为会让你的生活更轻松的改变。从3月1日开始，我们将在所有定价计划中为客户提供高达200倍的阅读量。值得注意的是，Scaler计划现在包含1000亿次读取，每月29美元。

This will enable you to use our product without worrying whether you’re going to see a giant bill at the end of the month. See our pricing page for more details.

这将使您能够使用我们的产品，而不用担心月底是否会看到巨额账单。有关更多详细信息，请参阅我们的定价页面。

We’re excited to have you on board as we continue to build the best database on the planet.

我们很高兴您能加入我们，我们将继续建立地球上最好的数据库。
