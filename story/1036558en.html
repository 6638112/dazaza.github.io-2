Salesforce Possibly Acquiring Slack
Salesforce可能会获得松弛

2020-11-26 01:53:51

Shares of Slack jumped Wednesday following a Wall Street Journal report that Salesforce held talks to buy the company.

在《华尔街日报》（Wall Street Journal）报道说，Salesforce进行了收购该公司的谈判之后，Slack的股价周三上涨。

Shares of Slack rose as much as 32% Wednesday following a Wall Street Journal report that Salesforce held talks to buy the company.

据《华尔街日报》报道，Salesforce举行了收购公司的谈判，Slack的股票周三上涨了32％。

The deal would likely value Slack at more than its market cap of $17 billion, according to the report. However, Slack's market cap ballooned to more than $20 billion following the WSJ report.

根据这份报告，这笔交易可能会使Slack的市值超过其170亿美元的市值。然而，根据《华尔街日报》的报道，Slack的市值激增至超过200亿美元。

Salesforce has been on an acquisition spree, taking advantage of its market cap growth in recent years to buy growing companies. It acquired MuleSoft for $6.5 billion in 2018, the company's biggest deal ever at the time, to help connect cloud applications. The following year it spent more than twice that amount on Tableau, acquiring the data visualization company for $15.3 billion.

Salesforce一直在疯狂收购，利用近年来的市值增长来收购成长中的公司。它在2018年以65亿美元的价格收购了MuleSoft，以帮助连接云应用程序，这是该公司当时最大的一笔交易。次年，它在Tableau上花费了两倍多的资金，以153亿美元收购了数据可视化公司。

Slack would be one of the biggest software deals ever for the industry. It would rank among IBM's $34 billion purchase of Red Hat in 2019, Microsoft's $27 billion purchase of LinkedIn in 2016 and Facebook's $19 billion purchase of WhatsApp in 2014.

Slack将成为该行业有史以来最大的软件交易之一。 IBM将在2019年以340亿美元收购Red Hat，2016年以270亿美元收购LinkedIn以及2014年以190亿美元收购WhatsApp的交易中排名。

"This would be a game changer move for Benioff & Co. to further build out its collaboration engine and product footprint as cloud spending ramps across the enterprise," Wedbush Securities' Dan Ives told CNBC, referring to Salesforce CEO Marc Benioff.

Wedbush Securities的Dan Ives告诉Salesforce首席执行官马克·贝尼奥夫（Marc Benioff）表示：“这将使贝尼奥夫（Benioff＆Co.）改变游戏规则，进一步扩大其协作引擎和产品覆盖范围，因为云计算在企业中的支出将不断增加。”

A deal is not guaranteed and the talks could fall through, the Wall Street Journal reported.

据《华尔街日报》报道，不能保证达成交易，谈判可能会失败。

Shares of Microsoft, which competes with Slack through its Teams offering, also dipped slightly on the report.

通过其Teams产品与Slack竞争的Microsoft股价也略微下跌。

Microsoft already competes with Salesforce in software for tracking customers. Microsoft reportedly looked at buying Salesforce but no deal came about. Later, Microsoft launched the Teams communication app.

微软已经与Salesforce在跟踪客户的软件方面展开竞争。据报道，微软曾考虑购买Salesforce，但没有达成任何协议。后来，Microsoft启动了Teams通信应用程序。

"Our primary competitor is currently Microsoft Corporation," Slack said when it sought to become a public company in 2019. Microsoft has a large customer base it has been able to convert to Teams, and Salesforce could give Slack a similar benefit.

Slack在2019年寻求成为上市公司时说：“我们的主要竞争对手是Microsoft Corporation。” Microsoft具有庞大的客户群，已经能够转换为Teams，Salesforce可以为Slack提供类似的好处。

Slack last said in October 2019 that it had more than 12 million daily active users.

Slack上一次在2019年10月表示，它每天有超过1200万活跃用户。
