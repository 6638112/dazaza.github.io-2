Blade Runner 2099 Sequel Series Coming from Ridley Scott
《银翼杀手2099》续集系列来自雷德利·斯科特

2022-02-14 10:35:15

The replicants keep replicating:  Blade Runner 2099, a live-action sequel series to Denis Villeneuve’s stellar 2017 film B lade Runner 2049, which itself was a follow-up to Ridley Scott’s 1982 masterpiece  Blade Runner, is in the works at Amazon Studios. Deadlinereports that Scott will be executive producing, which the man himself had all but confirmed last November when he said the pilot had been written. Now we know the scribe responsible: Silka Luisa, who is currently serving as showrunner for the Starz series Shining Girls starring Elisabeth Moss. As you no doubt guessed from the title, the series is set 50 years after  Blade Runner 2049.

复制者们一直在复制：《刀锋杀手2099》，这是丹尼斯·维伦纽夫（Denis Villeneuve）2017年主演的电影《刀锋杀手2049》（B lade Runner 2049）的真人续集，也是雷德利·斯科特（Ridley Scott）1982年的杰作《刀锋杀手》（Blade Runner）的后续作品，亚马逊工作室正在制作中。Deadlines报道说斯科特将担任执行制片，去年11月，当他说飞行员已经写好时，他本人几乎已经证实了这一点。现在我们知道了负责的抄写员：西尔卡·路易莎，她目前担任伊丽莎白·莫斯主演的Starz系列《闪亮女孩》的主持人。毫无疑问，正如你从标题中猜到的，该系列设定在《银翼杀手2049》50年后。

Blade Runner 2099 is currently staffing its writer’s room, and there’s no casting news or date set for its release. Depending on the timeline, Scott may direct, though he already has a lot on his plate. His HBO Max series  Raised by Wolves is currently airing its second season, and Scott remains one of the busiest feature film directors in Hollywood, having released two buzzy flicks,  House of Gucci and  The Last Duel, last year. He’s also shepherding along a TV adaptation of  Alien written by  Fargo‘s Noah Hawley, and he hasn’t given up on  Gladiator 2.

Blade Runner 2099目前正在编剧室工作，目前还没有演员阵容新闻或发布日期。根据时间安排，斯科特可能会执导，尽管他已经有很多事情要做。他的HBO Max系列剧《狼养大》目前正在第二季播出，斯科特仍然是好莱坞最繁忙的故事片导演之一，去年他发行了两部热门电影《古奇之家》和《最后一次决斗》。他也在指导法戈的诺亚·霍利（Noah Hawley）改编的电视剧《外星人》，他并没有放弃《角斗士2》。

Last year, the  Blade Runner universe expanded to anime with Crunchyroll and Adult Swim’s series  Black Lotus.

去年，《银翼杀手》的范围扩大到了有Crunchyroll和成人游泳系列《黑莲花》的动画。
