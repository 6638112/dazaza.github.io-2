News Corp announces a multi-year, global partnership with Google to provide content from its news sites “in return for significant payments by Google”
新闻集团宣布与Google达成一项多年的全球合作伙伴关系，以从其新闻站点提供内容，“以换取Google支付大量款项”

2021-02-18 03:08:36

New York, NY (February 17, 2021) – News Corp announced today that it has agreed to an historic multi-year partnership with Google to provide trusted journalism from its news sites around the world in return for significant payments by Google.

纽约，纽约（2021年2月17日）–新闻集团今天宣布，它已与Google达成一项历史悠久的多年合作伙伴关系，以从其全球新闻站点提供可信赖的新闻，以换取Google的大量付款。

Among the News Corp publications joining Google News Showcase will be The Wall Street Journal, Barron’s, MarketWatch, and the New York Post; in the UK: The Times and The Sunday Times, and The Sun; and in Australia a range of news platforms, including The Australian, news.com.au, Sky News, and multiple metropolitan and local titles.

加入Google News Showcase的新闻集团出版物将包括《华尔街日报》，《巴伦周刊》，MarketWatch和《纽约邮报》。在英国：《泰晤士报》和《星期日泰晤士报》以及《太阳报》；在澳大利亚，还有一系列新闻平台，包括《澳大利亚人报》，news.com.au，《天空新闻》以及多个大都市和地方新闻。

The landmark three-year agreement also includes the development of a subscription platform, the sharing of ad revenue via Google’s ad technology services, the cultivation of audio journalism and meaningful investments in innovative video journalism by YouTube.

这项具有里程碑意义的为期三年的协议还包括开发订阅平台，通过Google的广告技术服务分享广告收入，培养音频新闻业以及YouTube对创新视频新闻业进行有意义的投资。

Robert Thomson, Chief Executive of News Corp, said that the deal would have “a positive impact on journalism around the globe as we have firmly established that there should be a premium for premium journalism.

新闻集团首席执行官罗伯特·汤姆森（Robert Thomson）表示，该交易将“对全球新闻业产生积极影响，因为我们坚定地认为优质新闻业应有溢价。

“I would like to thank Sundar Pichai and his team at Google who have shown a thoughtful commitment to journalism that will resonate in every country. This has been a passionate cause for our company for well over a decade and I am gratified that the terms of trade are changing, not just for News Corp, but for every publisher.

“我要感谢Sundar Pichai和他在Google的团队，他们对新闻业表现出了深思熟虑的承诺，这一承诺将在每个国家引起共鸣。十多年来，这一直是我们公司的一个热情事业。令我感到欣慰的是，贸易条件正在发生变化，不仅对于新闻集团，而且对于每个出版商而言。

“The deal simply would not have been possible without the fervent, unstinting support of Rupert and Lachlan Murdoch, and the News Corp Board. For many years, we were accused of tilting at tech windmills, but what was a solitary campaign, a quixotic quest, has become a movement, and both journalism and society will be enhanced.

“如果没有鲁珀特和拉克兰·默多克以及新闻集团董事会的坚定支持，这笔交易根本不可能实现。多年以来，我们一直被指控偏向高科技风车，但一场孤零零的运动，一项古怪的追求已成为一项运动，新闻业和社会都会得到改善。

“Particular thanks are certainly due to the Australian Competition and Consumer Commission’s Rod Sims and his able team, along with the Australian Prime Minister, Scott Morrison, and Treasurer Josh Frydenberg, who have stood firm for their country and for journalism.”

“尤其要感谢澳大利亚竞争与消费者委员会的Rod Sims及其干练的团队，以及澳大利亚总理斯科特·莫里森（Scott Morrison）和财务主管乔什·弗莱登伯格（Josh Frydenberg），他们为自己的国家和新闻事业坚定不移。”
