Nylas, an API platform that helps developers integrate features like email and calendar into apps, raises $120M Series C led by Tiger Global
Nylas，一个API平台，可帮助开发人员将功能集成在电子邮件和日历等应用程序中，培养了Tiger Global的120万系列C LED

2021-06-17 20:50:15

Nylas, an API (application programming interface) platform that helps businesses and developers integrate key productivity features such as email and calendar functionality into their apps, has raised $120 million in a series C round of funding led by Tiger Global Management.

Nylas，API（应用程序编程接口）平台，可帮助业务和开发人员将电子邮件和日历功能等关键生产率功能集成到其应用中，由Tiger Global Management领导的C系列资金筹集了1.2亿美元。

The raise comes amid a booming API economy, defined by how organizations are using APIs to improve their efficiency by tapping the wider digital ecosystem. Rather than consuming internal resources developing their own infrastructure from scratch, businesses can use purpose-built APIs created by third-parties to bring login authentication to their finance app, privacy to their data-hungry health care app, or customer service support to their ecommerce app.

在蓬勃发展的API经济中，加剧是通过如何通过点击更广泛的数字生态系统来提高其效率的API经济来实现的。企业可以使用由第三方创建的目的内部API来消费自己的基础架构，而不是消费内部资源，而是通过第三方创建的目的地API，以将登录身份验证给他们的财务应用程序，隐私到他们的数据饥饿的医疗保健应用程序，或者客户服务支持他们的电子商务应用程序。

Nylas offers APIs spanning email, calendar, contacts, and scheduling, and includes integrations with all the major services from the likes of Microsoft, Google, AOL, Yahoo, and IMAP. So rather than having to develop multiple integrations for all these different services, a resource-intensive endeavor that requires ongoing maintenance and management, a CRM software maker (for example) can use Nylas’s email API to bypass all these hurdles to let sales teams send and receive emails from any provider directly from the CRM.

Nylas提供了API跨越电子邮件，日历，联系人和调度，并包括与Microsoft，Google，AOL，雅虎和IMAP相似的所有主要服务的集成。因此，而不是必须为所有这些不同的服务开发多个集成，而是需要持续维护和管理的资源密集型努力，一个CRM软件制造商（例如）可以使用Nylas的电子邮件API来绕过所有这些障碍让销售团队发送和销售团队发送直接从CRM接收来自任何提供商的电子邮件。

Among its client base is cloud communications juggernaut Dialpad, which uses Nylas to offer its end-users full create, read, update, and delete (CRUD) functionality spanning email, calendar, and contacts. This ensures all data is constantly synchronized in both directions between Dialpad and the service provider accounts (e.g. Google or Microsoft).

在其客户端基础中是云通信Juggernaut Dialpad，它使用Nylas来提供其最终用户的完整创建，读取，更新和删除（CRUD）功能跨越电子邮件，日历和联系人。这确保了所有数据在拨号板和服务提供商帐户之间的两个方向上不断同步（例如Google或Microsoft）。

In the past year, Nylas has launched a new neural API, which stems from its first acquisition last March when it snapped up June.ai, an AI-enabled email client that automatically organizes a user’s inbox. As part of Nylas, the technology helps to aggregate, analyze, and process vast swathes of data gleaned from emails, calendars, and contacts, taking unstructured data and transforming it into something more usable.

在过去的一年里，尼腹已经推出了一个新的神经API，它在去年3月份的第一次收购中源于june.ai，它是一个可自动组织用户的收件箱的AI启用的电子邮件客户端。作为尼布斯的一部分，该技术有助于聚合，分析和处理从电子邮件，日历和联系人收集的巨大数据，采取非结构化数据并将其转换为更具可用的东西。

“The Nylas neural API takes unstructured data, and uses intelligence and machine-learning algorithms to trigger automations that can save the average person one full day’s worth of work each week,” Nylas cofounder and CEO Gleb Polyakov told VentureBeat.

“Nylas神经API采用非结构化数据，并使用智能和机器学习算法来触发每周一个全天的普通人的自动化，”尼布斯Cofounder和Ceo Gleb Polyakov告诉Venturebeat。

For example, “categorizer” distinguishes machine-to-human from human-to-human messages, while also detecting bounced emails and automated out-of-office responses.

例如，“分类程序”将机器到人类与人类消息区分开来，同时还检测到反弹电子邮件和自动外出响应。

“Customers use this feature to automatically pause sales cadences based on detecting out-of-office emails and restart them based on the dates found in the email,” Polyakov explained. “Customers also use the categorizer API to trigger business specific automations — for instance, ecommerce customers use a categorizer to detect a shipping confirmation related email, and send custom notifications to their customers.”

“客户使用此功能基于检测Out Off Out Out Out Emails自动暂停销售节奏，并根据电子邮件中的日期重新启动它们，”Polyakov解释说。 “客户还使用分类程序API来触发企业特定的自动化 - 例如，电子商务客户使用分类程序检测运输确认相关电子邮件，并向客户发送自定义通知。”

Elsewhere, the neural API can extrapolate key data found in email signatures to automatically update their address books, or even trigger workflows based on a job title change, while sentiment analytics helps users figure out whether an email was positive, negative, or neutral. And ‘clean conversations’ helps to filter out “non-essential” content from messages to get to the nuts and bolts of a conversation.

在其他地方，神经API可以推断电子邮件签名中找到的关键数据，以自动更新他们的地址簿，甚至根据职位变更触发工作流程，而情感分析可帮助用户弄清电子邮件是积极的，负面或中性的。和“清洁对话”有助于从消息中过滤掉“非必要”内容，以便到谈话的螺母和螺栓。

“Emails are often polluted and hard to read through since they contain past responses, signatures, legal language, and so on,” Polyakov said “The clean conversations API makes it easy for developers to access the most important part of any email — the body — via a single API call.”

“电子邮件通常污染且难以阅读，因为它们包含过去的响应，签名，法律语言等，”Polyakov表示“清洁对话API使开发人员可以轻松访问任何电子邮件的最重要部分 - 身体 - 通过单个API呼叫。“

Prior to now, Nylas had raised around $55 million, almost half of which came via its series B round last June. Its latest investment included a host of notable old and new investors, including Slack (via Slack Fund); Stripe cofounders Patrick and John Collison; Nest founder Tony Fadell; Klarna CEO Sebastian Siemiatkowski; Citi Ventures; 8VC; Round13 Capital; and Owl Rock Capital.

在现在之前，Nylas已经筹集了5500万美元，其中几乎一半通过其第6月通过其系列。其最新投资包括一系列名人的旧和新投资者，包括松弛（通过Slack Fund）;条纹Cofounders Patrick和John Collicon;巢创始人Tony Fadell; Klarna首席执行官Sebastian Siemiatkowski;花旗资企业; 8Vc;第13轮资本;和猫头鹰岩石资本。

With another $120 million in the bank, Nylas said that it plans to continue investing in its product including security, components, and automation, such as AI and ML, sentiment analysis, natural language processing (NLP), and more.

尼斯表示，在银行额外12 000万美元表示，它计划继续投资其产品，包括安全，组件和自动化，如AI和ML，情感分析，自然语言处理（NLP）等。

“There’s a lot of work a developer has to do to build modern software –everything from integrating backend APIs, mining and filtering data, orchestrating workflows, and building rich front-end user experiences,” Polyakov said. “We’re developing products and services across all of these areas to help developers ship value faster to their end customers.”

Polyakov表示，“已经开始建立现代软件，以建立现代软件，为建立现代软件进行建立现代软件，挖掘工作流程，协调工作流程和建立丰富的前端用户体验，”Polyakov表示。 “我们正在开发所有这些领域的产品和服务，以帮助开发人员销售价值更快到最终客户。”
