Europe’s Startup Unicorns
欧洲的初创独角兽

2020-11-24 00:41:10

Like it or not, startup ecosystems are partly defined by how big their unicorn herds are; in other words, by how many companies have hit the billion-dollar valuation mark.

不管喜欢与否，创业生态系统在一定程度上取决于其独角兽群的规模。换句话说，有多少家公司的估值达到了十亿美元。

In Europe, the unicorn count is steadily growing. In 2020 so far, at least 10 new unicorns have been ‘born’. There are plenty more fast-growing startups likely to join the list soon.

在欧洲，独角兽数量稳步增长。到2020年为止，至少有10个新的独角兽“诞生”了。还有更多快速成长的初创公司可能很快加入该名单。

Others will leave: Europe has also seen several IPOs this year, including The Hut Group (although Covid has slowed things here) — and is likely to see many more in 2021.

其他人将离开：今年欧洲也出现了几宗IPO，包括The Hut Group（尽管Covid放慢了这里的步伐）–并可能在2021年看到更多。

To help you keep track of it all, Sifted’s team has compiled a list of Europe’s unicorns using data from European Startups and our own research. We’ll update the list whenever a new company hits the billion-dollar valuation.

为了帮助您掌握所有情况，Sifted小组使用来自欧洲新兴企业的数据和我们自己的研究汇总了欧洲独角兽的清单。每当有新公司的估值达到十亿美元时，我们都会更新列表。

To make the list, companies must hit the following criteria: headquartered in Europe; founded in 2000 or later; private (not listed or acquired). Please let us know if we’ve missed any!

要列出该名单，公司必须符合以下条件：总部位于欧洲；成立于2000年或之后；私人（未列出或获得）。如果我们错过任何活动，请告诉我们！
