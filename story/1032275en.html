怪异的AI Yankovic：生成恶搞歌词

2020-10-30 21:54:51

Download PDF Abstract: Lyrics parody swaps one set of words that accompany a melody with a new setof words, preserving the number of syllables per line and the rhyme scheme.Lyrics parody generation is a challenge for controllable text generation. Weshow how a specialized sampling procedure, combined with backward textgeneration with XLNet can produce parody lyrics that reliably meet the syllableand rhyme scheme constraints.We introduce the Weird AI Yankovic system andprovide a case study evaluation. We conclude with societal implications ofneural lyric parody generation.

下载PDF摘要：歌词恶搞将伴随旋律的一组词与新的一组词互换，保留每行的音节数和押韵模式。歌词恶搞的生成是可控文本生成的挑战。本文介绍了一种特殊的采样过程，结合XLNet的反向文本生成，可以可靠地生成符合音节和押韵方案约束的恶搞歌词，介绍了Weird AI Yankovic系统，并提供了一个案例研究评估。最后，我们总结了神经性抒情恶搞生成的社会意义。
