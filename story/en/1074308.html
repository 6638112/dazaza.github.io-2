Atlassian founder launches bid for power company AGL to ‘accelerate coal exit’
Atlassian创始人发起竞购电力公司AGL以“加速煤炭出口”

2022-02-20 22:38:05

Tech billionaire Mike Cannon-Brookes has teamed up with international fund manager Brookfield to launch an audacious takeover bid for Australian energy giant AGL with the intention of setting stronger emissions-reduction targets and forcing the early closures of its remaining coal-fired power stations.

科技亿万富翁迈克·坎农·布鲁克斯（Mike Cannon Brookes）与国际基金经理布鲁克菲尔德（Brookfield）合作，对澳大利亚能源巨头AGL发起了大胆的收购要约，目的是设定更高的减排目标，并迫使其剩余的燃煤发电站提前关闭。

AGL is the nation’s largest and oldest energy utility and its power stations are Australia’s largest source of planet-heating greenhouses gases, accounting for about 8 per cent of national emissions.

AGL是澳大利亚最大、最古老的能源公司，其发电站是澳大利亚最大的温室气体源，约占全国排放量的8%。

If successful, Brookfield and Mr Cannon-Brookes, Australia’s third-richest person, would spend $10 billion above the purchase price of AGL to get coal out of its operations by as early as 2030, 15 years earlier than its current forecasts.

如果成功，布鲁克菲尔德和澳大利亚第三富豪坎农·布鲁克斯将最早在2030年花费比AGL收购价格高出100亿美元的资金将煤炭从其运营中撤出，比目前的预测早15年。

WWF’s Australia energy transition manager, Nicky Ison, described the proposal as “potentially game-changing for the Australian energy transition”.

世界自然基金会澳大利亚能源转型经理尼基·伊森（Nicky Ison）称该提议“可能会改变澳大利亚能源转型的局面”。

However, the Morrison government last week condemned as “bitterly disappointing” a decision by rival energy company, Origin, to get out of coal early by closing the Eraring power station seven years early, in 2025. The government warned that could increase power prices, a claim questioned by Kerry Schott, a former chair of the Commonwealth’s Energy Security Board, who said Eraring’s closure could be managed without causing price spikes.

然而，莫里森政府上周谴责了竞争对手能源公司Origin提前七年（2025年）关闭埃拉林发电厂，提前退出煤炭供应的决定，称其“非常令人失望”。联邦能源安全委员会前主席克里·肖特质疑政府警告说，这可能会提高电价，他说，埃拉林的关闭可以在不造成电价飙升的情况下得到控制。

Sources said the AGL proposal had offered only a 4 per cent premium on the company’s last closing price of $7.16 a share, and was not expected to be approved by the board, which believed it undervalued the business. AGL is valued at $4.71 billion, a two-decade low after suffering a massive share-price plunge in the past five years.

消息人士称，AGL的提议仅比该公司上一次每股7.16美元的收盘价溢价4%，预计不会得到董事会的批准，董事会认为该提议低估了该业务的价值。AGL的估值为47.1亿美元，是过去五年股价大幅下跌后的二十年低点。

Under the bid, Brookfield and Mr Cannon-Brookes have specified that AGL’s target for “net zero” emissions — requiring the company to remove as much carbon dioxide from the atmosphere as it emits — would be brought forward by 12 years, forcing it to fast-track its closure of coal-fired power stations by 2035, according to sources familiar with the offer but not authorised to speak publicly.

根据该投标书，布鲁克菲尔德和坎农·布鲁克斯先生已明确表示，AGL的“净零”排放目标——要求该公司从大气中排放尽可能多的二氧化碳——将提前12年，迫使其在2035年前快速关闭燃煤发电站，据知情人士透露，该提议并未获得公开发言的授权。

The prospect of earlier closures would continue the trend of private companies bringing forward the retirements of ageing coal-fired power plants despite the federal Coalition government’s insistence that the fossil fuel is crucial to ensure ongoing reliable and cheap electricity.

尽管联邦联合政府坚持认为化石燃料对确保持续可靠和廉价的电力至关重要，但提前关闭的前景将继续保持私营公司提前淘汰老化燃煤发电厂的趋势。

Federal Energy Minister Angus Taylor is expected to comment on Monday when details of the bid are released. He warned last week that Origin’s decision to close the Eraring generator up to seven years early would leave a “considerable gap” in reliable electricity generation.

联邦能源部长安格斯·泰勒（Angus Taylor）预计将在周一公布竞标细节时发表评论。他上周警告称，Origin提前七年关闭Eraring发电机的决定将在可靠发电方面留下“相当大的差距”。

AGL’s board held a meeting on Sunday evening to discuss the takeover proposal ahead of making an expected announcement to the stock market on Monday morning.

AGL董事会于周日晚间召开会议，讨论收购提议，并于周一上午向股市发布预期公告。

“Shareholders will be looking for a pretty big premium to give up control of AGL,” said one source familiar with the offer. However, as AGL is a volatile stock, the offer represents a 20 per cent premium on the volume-weighted average price.

一位知情人士表示：“股东将寻求相当大的溢价，以放弃对AGL的控制。”。然而，由于AGL是一只波动性较大的股票，该报价比成交量加权平均价格溢价20%。

AGL, representatives for Mr Cannon-Brookes and Brookfield all declined to comment on Sunday, though Mr Cannon-Brookes tweeted: “Might be an afternoon espresso kind of day.”

AGL、坎农·布鲁克斯和布鲁克菲尔德的代表都拒绝在周日发表评论，不过坎农·布鲁克斯在推特上写道：“可能是下午的浓缩咖啡日。”

The joint offer proposes to acquire the entire company including its retail division, which supplies 4.5 million customer accounts, and power generation fleet spanning coal, gas and renewable energy assets.

联合要约提议收购整个公司，包括其零售部门（该部门提供450万个客户账户）以及跨越煤炭、天然气和可再生能源资产的发电机组。

In the face of mounting investor pressure to act on climate change, AGL said earlier this month it would bring forward the closure of its Bayswater plant in New South Wales by two years, from 2035 to no later than 2033, while Victoria’s Loy Yang A plant would retire by 2045 instead of 2048.

面对越来越大的投资者压力，AGL在本月早些时候表示，将提前两年关闭其位于新南威尔士州的Bayswater工厂，从2035年到2033年，而维多利亚州的Loy Yang A工厂将在2045年而不是2048年退役。

That announcement disappointed climate advocates, who have been ramping up demands for AGL to exit coal much earlier. The United Nations has called for developed countries to remove coal from their power networks by 2030 in order to avert catastrophic levels of climate change.

这一宣布令气候倡导者感到失望，他们一直在要求AGL更早地退出煤炭行业。联合国呼吁发达国家在2030年之前从其电网中移除煤炭，以避免灾难性的气候变化。

Mr Cannon-Brookes is the co-founder of software developer Atlassian. He has an estimated fortune of about $20 billion and is an investor in renewable energy and a prominent advocate for stronger climate action. Toronto-listed Brookfield manages more than $US690 billion ($961 billion) in assets including real estate, infrastructure and energy. This year, it finalised an $18 billion takeover of Victoria’s electricity transmission network operator, AusNet – an acquisition that raises the prospect of competition concerns arising from its bid to buy AGL.

坎农·布鲁克斯是软件开发商Atlassian的联合创始人。据估计，他拥有约200亿美元的财富，是可再生能源的投资者，也是加强气候行动的著名倡导者。在多伦多上市的布鲁克菲尔德管理着超过6900亿美元（9610亿美元）的资产，包括房地产、基础设施和能源。今年，它以180亿美元的价格完成了对维多利亚州输电网络运营商AusNet的收购——这一收购引发了因其收购AGL而引发的竞争担忧。

The viability of coal-burning power stations which provide the bulk of Australia’s electricity needs has increasingly been in doubt as the rapid rise of cheaper-to-run renewable energy drives daytime wholesale power prices to levels where fossil fuels are struggling to compete. Volatile electricity prices across the east-coast energy grid helped push AGL to a $2 billion net loss in the 12 months to June 30.

提供澳大利亚大部分电力需求的燃煤发电站的可行性越来越受到怀疑，因为廉价可再生能源的快速增长将日间批发电价推高到化石燃料难以竞争的水平。在截至6月30日的12个月内，东海岸电网的电价波动促使AGL净亏损20亿美元。

While reducing coal’s power output would significantly cut emissions, AGL has echoed concerns across much of the industry that shutting coal-fired generators too soon without enough reliable power to support weather-dependant wind and solar energy could risk causing a “messy” energy transition threatening power supply and prices.

虽然减少煤炭的发电量将显著减少排放，但AGL也回应了业内许多人的担忧，即在没有足够可靠的电力支持依赖天气的风能和太阳能的情况下过早关闭燃煤发电机，可能会导致“混乱”的能源过渡，威胁电力供应和价格。

Renewable energy is continuing to increase and accounted for a record-high average of 34.9 per cent of generation in the final three months of 2021, but coal remains Australia’s dominant source of electricity.

可再生能源持续增加，并在2021年的最后三个月里占到了34.9%的历史平均水平，但煤炭仍然是澳大利亚的主要电力来源。

AGL’s Bayswater plant combined with its Liddel generator in NSW, which is due to close next year, generate 35 per cent of the state’s power. The Loy Yang A brown coal plant in the Latrobe Valley supplies 30 per cent of the Victorian grid.

AGL的Bayswater发电厂与新南威尔士州的Liddel发电机（将于明年关闭）相结合，产生了该州35%的电力。位于拉特罗布山谷的洛扬褐煤电厂为维多利亚电网提供了30%的电力。

As the shift to renewable energy squeezes coal further out of Australia’s electricity market, AGL last year declared the “winds of change” had swept the sector much faster than anticipated, prompting the company to embark on plans for a historic demerger with a new entity called Accel Energy to own its fleet of large coal- and gas-fired generators. The other entity, to be called AGL Australia, would hold its electricity, gas and telecommunications retailing business along with some cleaner generation assets.

随着向可再生能源的转变将煤炭进一步挤出澳大利亚的电力市场，AGL去年宣布，“变革之风”席卷该行业的速度远远快于预期，促使该公司开始实施一项历史性的分拆计划，由一家名为Accel Energy的新实体拥有其大型燃煤和燃气发电机组。另一家名为AGL Australia的实体将持有其电力、天然气和电信零售业务以及一些清洁能源资产。

If successful, the takeover proposal by Mr Cannon-Brookes and Brookfield would halt the company’s plans to demerge its business later this year.

如果成功，坎农·布鲁克斯和布鲁克菲尔德的收购提议将停止该公司今年晚些时候分拆业务的计划。

Origin Energy last week gave notice of its intention to retire the 2880-megawatt Eraring generator at Lake Macquarie in NSW up to seven years earlier than its scheduled exit in 2032 as the roll-out of cheaper wind and solar power hammered the plant’s economic viability.

Origin Energy上周发出通知，表示打算在2032年退出新南威尔士州麦格理湖2880兆瓦的Eraring发电机，比原计划提前七年，因为廉价风能和太阳能的推出打击了该电厂的经济可行性。
