Severely ill Covid-19 patients fully recovered after treatment with cancer drug
重症Covid-19重症患者在用抗癌药物治疗后完全康复

2021-02-06 21:31:20

Twenty-nine out of 30 moderate-to-severe COVID-19 patients who were administered a treatment developed by Tel Aviv Sourasky Medical Center (Ichilov Hospital) as part of a Phase I trial recovered from the disease and were released within three to five days, the hospital said Friday.

在30例中至重度COVID-19患者中，有29例接受了由特拉维夫索拉斯基医学中心（Ichilov医院）开发的治疗，这是I期临床试验的一部分，已从该疾病中康复，并在三至五天内被释放医院周五说。

The patients were given Prof. Nadir Arber’s EXO-CD24 COVID-19 treatment, which is based on CD24-enriched exosomes and is meant to fight the cytokine storm that is associated with many of the world’s COVID-19 deaths.

为患者提供了Nadir Arber教授的EXO-CD24 COVID-19治疗，该治疗基于富含CD24的外泌体，旨在对抗与世界上许多COVID-19死亡相关的细胞因子风暴。

A cytokine storm is when the immune system essentially goes into overdrive and begins attacking healthy cells. Exosomes are responsible for cell-to-cell communication. In this case, they deliver the CD24 protein to the lungs, which helps calm down the immune system.

细胞因子风暴是免疫系统实质上进入超速状态并开始攻击健康细胞的时候。外泌体负责细胞间的通讯。在这种情况下，它们将CD24蛋白输送到肺部，这有助于使免疫系统平静下来。

“This protein is located on the surface of cells and has a well-known and important role in regulating the immune system,” explained Dr. Shiran Shapira, who works in Arber’s lab.

“这种蛋白质位于细胞表面，在调节免疫系统中起着众所周知的重要作用，”在Arber实验室工作的Shiran Shapira博士解释说。

Arber has been researching exosomes for the better part of two decades. He said it took about six months from the time the idea of using this treatment in the battle against COVID-19 was raised until it was first tested in humans.

Arber在二十年来的大部分时间里一直在研究外泌体。他说，从提出在对抗COVID-19的战斗中使用这种治疗方法的想法直到首次在人体中进行测试，大约用了六个月的时间。

The treatment is inhaled once a day for a few minutes at a time for five days. It directly targets the lungs, the site of the storm, as opposed to other treatments that could be given systemically and hence cause severe side effects, Arber explained.

该疗法每天吸入一次，持续几分钟，持续五天。 Arber解释说，它直接针对肺部，即风暴的发生地点，而不是系统地给予其他治疗，因此会引起严重的副作用。

The hospital has appealed to the Health Ministry to move forward with further clinical trials. Once approved, the treatment can be tried on additional patients.

该医院已呼吁卫生部进一步开展临床试验。一旦获得批准，可以对其他患者进行治疗。

“This is an innovative treatment that can be produced quickly and efficiently at a low cost,” Arber explained. “Even if the vaccines do what they are supposed to, and even if no new mutations are produced, then still, in one way or another, coronavirus will remain with us.”

“这是一种创新的治疗方法，可以低成本快速高效地生产，” Arber解释说。 “即使疫苗按照他们的预期去做，即使没有新的突变产生，但冠状病毒仍将以一种或另一种方式保留在我们体内。”

The head of Sourasky, Prof. Ronni Gamzu hailed the treatment as “innovative and sophisticated” and said that the results of the Phase I trial “give us all an expression of confidence in the method.” He added that he would personally assist Arber in obtaining the approvals needed from the Health Ministry to further his research.

Sourasky的负责人Ronni Gamzu教授称这种治疗“创新而精致”，并说第一阶段试验的结果“使我们所有人都对该方法充满信心。”他补充说，他将亲自协助Arber获得卫生部进一步研究所需的批准。

Gazmu served as Israel’s coronavirus commissioner from August to November 2020. He said that the international understanding is that alongside vaccines “it is especially important to develop drugs to treat the disease.. "I am proud that Ichilov… is perhaps bringing a blue-and-white solution to a terrible global pandemic,” he concluded.

加兹穆（Gazmu）从2020年8月至2020年11月担任以色列的冠状病毒专员。他说，国际共识是，与疫苗一起“开发治疗这种疾病的药物尤为重要。...我为伊希洛夫（Ichilov）带来了解决可怕的全球大流行的蓝白解决方案。”他总结道。

This is not the first Israeli drug to show promise in the treatment of COVID-19.

这不是以色列首个在COVID-19治疗中显示出希望的药物。

Last year, Hadassah-University Medical Center reported similar results from the use of the drug Allocetra, which was developed by the Enlivex Company based on research by a Hadassah doctor, Prof. Dror Mevorach. Similar to EXO-CD24, Allocetra is meant to treat the overreaction of the immune system. Also, Israel's Pluristem Therapeutics has been investigating the use of its PLX-PAD placenta-derived cellular therapy, which the company has said it hopes will play a meaningful role in mitigating the tissue-damaging effects of COVID-19 on the lungs. The company is now engaged in clinical trials as well.

去年，哈达萨大学医学中心报告了使用药物阿洛西特拉的类似结果，该药物是由Enlivex公司根据哈达萨医生Dror Mevorach教授的研究开发的。类似于EXO-CD24，Allocetra旨在治疗免疫系统的过度反应。此外，以色列的Pluristem Therapeutics公司一直在研究其PLX-PAD胎盘来源的细胞疗法的使用，该公司表示，希望在减轻COVID-19的组织破坏作用中发挥有意义的作用。肺。该公司现在也从事临床试验。
