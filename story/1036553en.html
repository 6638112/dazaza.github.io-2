Diego Maradona, one of the greatest footballers of all time, dies aged 60
迭戈·马拉多纳（Diego Maradona）是有史以来最伟大的足球运动员之一，享年60岁

2020-11-26 01:52:23

Diego Maradona, regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, has died aged 60.

迭戈·马拉多纳被认为是有史以来最伟大的足球运动员之一，享年60岁。

The Argentinian, who had brain surgery this month, died of a heart attack, his lawyer said. The country declared three days of national mourning.

他的律师说，这个月进行脑外科手术的阿根廷人死于心脏病。该国宣布全国哀悼三天。

Maradona inspired Argentina to World Cup glory in 1986 when as captain he displayed a level of skill, creativity, strength and desire arguably not seen before or since. In the 2-1 quarter-final victory over England he also scored perhaps the greatest goal of all time, a match in which the forward also showed his darker, mischievous side with the infamous “ Hand of God”.

马拉多纳（Maradona）在1986年的世界杯上为阿根廷带来了荣耀，当时担任队长的他表现出了无可比拟的技能，创造力，力量和渴望。在2-1英格兰的四分之一决赛中，他也取得了有史以来最大的进球，这场比赛中前锋还用臭名昭著的“上帝之手”表现出了他更黑暗，调皮的一面。

Maradona also achieved success at club level, most notably with Napoli, whom he led to their first Serie A title in 1987. A second followed in 1990, alongside an Italian Cup in 1987 and a Uefa Cup in 1989, and such was the player’s impact at a club that previously had lived in the shadow of Italy’s northern powerhouses, particularly Juventus, Milan and Internazionale, that Napoli announced in 2000 they were retiring his No 10 shirt.

马拉多纳在俱乐部一级也取得了成功，最著名的是那不勒斯，他在1987年带领了他们的第一个意甲冠军。第二次是1990年，接着是1987年的意大利杯和1989年的欧洲联盟杯，这就是球员的影响力在一个曾经住在意大利北部强队，尤其是尤文图斯，米兰和国际米兰的阴影下的俱乐部，那不勒斯在2000年宣布他们将退役他的10号球衣。

It was during his seven years in Naples, however, that Maradona’s addiction to cocaine took grip. He was hit with a 15-month suspension for drug violations in 1991 – the year he left Napoli – and, three years later, was thrown out of the World Cup in the USA after testing positive for ephedrine.

然而，正是在他在那不勒斯（Naples）的7年中，马拉多纳（Maradona）对可卡因的依赖开始受到控制。 1991年-离开那不勒斯那年，他因涉嫌违禁药物而被停赛15个月，三年后，他的麻黄碱呈阳性反应，因此被淘汰出美国世界杯。

From there Maradona’s personal life spiralled out control and in 2000 and 2004 he was hospitalised for heart problems, the second time requiring the use of a respirator to breathe properly. The following year he underwent gastric-bypass surgery to help stem his obesity.

从那以后，马拉多纳的个人生活逐渐失去控制，在2000年和2004年，他因心脏病入院，这是第二次要求使用呼吸器来正常呼吸。次年，他接受了胃旁路手术以帮助阻止肥胖。

None of that, however, could overshadow his talents on the pitch which, at their peak, were almost supernatural.

然而，在巅峰时期几乎是超自然的球场上，这些都无法掩盖他的才华。

Born in Buenos Aires on 30 October 1960, Diego Armando Maradona was a child prodigy and having joined Los Cebollitas, a youth team of Argentinos Juniors, at the age of 10 he played a key role in them going on an incredible 136-game winning streak, which in turn led to him making his debut for the senior side just before his 16th birthday.

迭戈·阿曼多·马拉多纳（Diego Armando Maradona）于1960年生于布宜诺斯艾利斯，是一名神童，他加入了阿根廷青年青年队Los Cebollitas，10岁那年，他在他们取得令人难以置信的136连胜的过程中发挥了关键作用，这反过来导致他在16岁生日之前首次登台登台。

Shortly afterwards – on 27 February 1977 – Maradona made his debut for Argentina, coming on as a 65th-minute substitute in a friendly against Hungary at La Bombonera, the home of Boca Juniors, whom Maradona joined in 1981. He spent only one season at the club but in that time scored a stunning solo goal against archrivals River Plate and helped Boca to win the title.

此后不久-1977年2月27日-马拉多纳首次亮相阿根廷，以65分钟的替补身份在博卡青年队的故乡拉·邦博纳拉对阵匈牙利的友谊赛中出场，马拉多纳于1981年加入了马拉多纳。俱乐部，但那时候他击败了竞争对手河床队（River Plate）取得了令人惊叹的独奏进球，并帮助博卡赢得了冠军。

Given his rapid development and consistently commanding displays, it was no surprise Maradona was soon being courted by Europe’s biggest clubs and in 1982, having featured at his first World Cup, in Spain, he joined Barcelona for a then world record fee of £5m.

鉴于他的快速发展和始终如一的指挥能力，马拉多纳很快就被欧洲最大的俱乐部所吸引，并在1982年因参加了他在西班牙举行的第一届世界杯足球赛而以500万英镑的创世界纪录转会费加盟巴塞罗那。

He struggled to show his best form for the Catalans, however, partly because of the broken ankle he suffered in September 1983 from a tackle by the “Butcher of Bilbao”, Andoni Goicoechea. But Maradona eventually recovered and in 1984 joined Napoli. Two years later came the World Cup in Mexico and the moment he established himself as a genuine great.

然而，他努力地向加泰罗尼亚人展示自己的最佳状态，部分原因是他在1983年9月因“毕尔巴鄂屠夫” Andoni Goicoechea的钓具而受伤，脚踝受伤。但马拉多纳最终康复，并于1984年加入那不勒斯。两年后，墨西哥举行了世界杯，从那一刻起，他就确立了自己的实力。

Argentina’s captain played every minute of every game, scoring five goals and providing assists for five others. He was supreme throughout, the most dynamic and exciting player at the tournament, and took the breath away with his second goal against England at the Azteca Stadium, when he twisted past two players on the halfway line before bursting past another pair and, under pressure from Terry Butcher’s lunge, went around Peter Shilton before passing the ball into an empty net. It was a stunning display of skill allied to courage and it little wonder the strike is still refereed to by some as “the goal of the century.”

阿根廷队长每场比赛每一分钟都打进一球，打进5球，为另外5球提供助攻。在整个比赛中，他都是至高无上，最有活力和最激动人心的球员。在阿兹台克体育场对阵英格兰的第二个进球中，他喘不过气来，当时他在中途扭过两名球员，然后冲过另一对，并在压力下来自特里·布彻（Terry Butcher）的弓步，绕过彼得·席尔顿（Peter Shilton），然后将球传给了空网。这是与勇气相关的惊人技能展示，毫无疑问，罢工仍被某些人称为“本世纪的目标”。

Four minutes earlier Maradona scored his first of the game. Argentina’s No 10 clearly punched the ball past Shilton as the pair challenged for a high ball but, despite protests from Bobby Robson’s men, the goal stood. “ I’ll never forgive him,” Butcher said in 2008. “It’s not nice when you lose a World Cup quarter-final under those circumstances. It’s very hard to forgive and forget in the circumstances.”

四分钟前，马拉多纳打进了自己的第一场比赛。阿根廷10号手显然向希尔顿（Shilton）扑了过去，两人挑战高球，但尽管鲍比·罗布森（Bobby Robson）的男子提出抗议，但进球仍然存在。 “我永远不会原谅他，”布彻（Butcher）在2008年说。在这种情况下很难原谅和忘记。”

Maradona took part in two further World Cups – at Italia 90, when the team he captained finished as runners-up, and at USA 94, when he was sent home after playing against Nigeria in what proved to be his last appearance for his country. In total Maradona was capped 91 times, scoring 34 goals, the last against Greece in Argentina’s opening game at the 1994 World Cup.

马拉多纳还参加了另外两次世界杯比赛：在意大利队90时，他带领队获得亚军；在美国94时，他在与尼日利亚队比赛后被送回家，事实证明这是他最后一次出场。马拉多纳总共封顶91次，打入34球，这是1994年世界杯阿根廷对阵希腊的最后一场比赛。

At club level Maradona joined Sevilla following his departure from Napoli, before returning to his homeland to play for Newell’s Old Boys and Boca, where he retired in 1997.

在俱乐部级别，马拉多纳（Maradona）在离开那不勒斯（Napoli）后加入塞维利亚（Sevilla），然后返回家乡效力于纽维尔（Newell）的老男孩（Old Boys）和博卡（Boca），他于1997年退休。

Following his personal battles he managed Argentina for two years, taking them to the 2010 World Cup in South Africa, where a team containing Juan Sebastian Verón, Carlos Tevez and Lionel Messi reached the quarter-finals. A year later, and having left the national-team post, he took charge of the Dubai-based club Al-Wasl before going on to hold a number of positions, most recently manager of the Argentinian first division side Gimnasia y Esgrima.

在个人争斗之后，他管理了阿根廷两年，将他们带到了2010年南非世界杯，在那里，一支由胡安·塞巴斯蒂安·维隆，卡洛斯·特维斯和莱昂内尔·梅西组成的团队进入了八强。一年后，他离开了国家队的职位，负责了迪拜俱乐部Al-Wasl的工作，随后继续担任多个职位，最近是阿根廷第一分部球队Gimnasia y Esgrima的经理。

But it is for his playing exploits that Maradona will be best remembered. Having been named South American Footballer of the Year on five occasions, he was in 2000 named Fifa’s Player of the Century alongside Pelé following a combination of an internet poll and nominations from Fifa officials, coaches and players. Maradona won the popular vote.

但是马拉多纳将以其出色的表现而被人们铭记。在互联网民意测验以及国际足联官员，教练和球员的提名相结合后，他五次被评为年度南美足球先生，并与贝利一起在2000年被评为国际足联年度最佳球员。马拉多纳赢得了全民投票。
