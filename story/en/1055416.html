Robinhood says it will remove a confetti animation from its app, which appears upon making a first trade, after criticism that it “gamifies” investing
罗宾时代说它将从其应用程序中删除五彩纸屑动画，这是在批评它“赌博”投资之后发出第一展

2021-04-01 20:18:38

Robinhood Markets Inc. is eliminating digital confetti from its trading app, the latest sign that the popular brokerage is working to correct concerns from critics, as it moves forward with plans to go public.

Robinhood Markets Inc.从其交易应用程序中消除了数字纸屑，最新的迹象表明，流行经纪正在努力纠正批评者的疑虑，因为它向公众进行了前进。

Robinhood’s digital confetti, which historically has rained down on users’ screens to celebrate occasions such as making a first trade, has become a point of contention for the brokerage in recent months. Critics have pointed to the animation as a sign that the company “gamifies” investing. Robinhood repeatedly defended the design.

罗布尼的数字五彩纸屑，其中历史上下雨了解用户的屏幕，以庆祝诸如制作第一款交易的场合，已成为最近几个月的经纪人的争论点。批评者指出了动画作为公司“游戏”投资的标志。罗宾情绪反复捍卫设计。

Starting next week, however, the company plans to roll out new digital designs. Instead of confetti, users will see animations that the company hopes will acknowledge “investing milestones” while also providing customers with more information about the actions they are taking.

然而，下周开始，该公司计划推出新的数字设计。而不是五彩纸屑，用户将看到公司希望承认“投资里程碑”的动画，同时还为客户提供有关他们正在采取的行动的更多信息。

“The confetti is getting really misconstrued, and I think it’s actually doing the opposite of what we want,” said Madhu Muthukumar, senior director of product management at Robinhood. It “seems to have distracted from the goal of the app, which is to make regular people, everyday folks who have been kept out of the market, feel like they can participate and feel like they are being positively reinforced for taking steps in their financial life.”

“五彩纸屑正在真正误解了，我认为它实际上与我们想要的相反，”罗布赫的产品管理高级总监Madhu Muthukumar说。它“似乎已经从应用程序的目标分散了分散注意力，这是使普通人，一直被远离市场的日常人员，感觉他们可以参与并觉得它们被积极加强，以便在他们的财务中采取措施生活。”

“If the focus of a conversation is around confetti and not around these people taking these first steps…then we’ve done something that’s just off base,” Mr. Muthukumar added.

“如果谈话的焦点是五彩纸屑，而不是周围这些人在这些人中服用这些第一步......然后我们已经完成了一些刚刚离开的东西，”Muthukumar先生补充道。

Robinhood first introduced digital-confetti animation on its app in the early fall of 2016 and has featured it in advertising. But as the trading app has grown more popular since the start of the Covid-19 pandemic, the digital animation became a lightning rod for controversy. In a December complaint, Massachusetts securities regulators alleged that confetti was one of several strategies Robinhood used to incentivize customers to repeatedly engage with the app.

Robinhood首次在2016年初秋季介绍了其应用上的数字五彩纸屑动画，并以广告为特色。但随着贸易的应用程序自Covid-19大流行开始以来的贸易应用程序更受欢迎，数字动画成为争议的避雷针。在12月份投诉中，马萨诸塞州证券监管机构声称，五彩纸屑是用于激励客户反复聘用该应用的若干战略机器人之一。

The topic of confetti was raised again this year by Gary Gensler, President Biden’s pick to head the Securities and Exchange Commission, during testimony before the Senate Banking Committee. Mr. Gensler didn’t mention Robinhood specifically but questioned what it means when you have confetti “dropping, and you have behavioral prompts to get investors to do more transactions.”

拜登总统在参议院银行委员会之前，今年加里格兰·加里格勒今年再次举办了五彩纸屑的主题。 Gensller先生没有特别提到罗宾情绪，但质疑当你有五彩纸屑“掉落，并且你有行为提示让投资者做更多的交易时意味着什么。”

Similar to the way the app operates currently, investors will see the new digital designs when taking steps such as upgrading to Robinhood Gold, its premium service for which users pay a monthly fee. But now, the new digital animations will aim to be “more informational and [lay] the groundwork for what customers are to expect,” said Rich Bessel, head of design at Robinhood.

类似于应用程序目前运营的方式，投资者将在采取措施升级到抢劫金时的级别时，投资者将看到新的数字设计，其用户支付每月费用的高级服务。但是现在，新的数字动画旨在成为“更信息和[为客户预期的信息和[奠定基础，”罗宾斯设计负责人富百尔斯尔说。

“Confetti, while it’s acknowledging something, can also take away from the text and what’s literally on the screen,” Mr. Bessel said. “We feel customers, based on feedback, will spend slightly more time on the screen as a result of this and understand exactly what action they took and what resulted.”

“五彩纸屑，虽然它承认某些事情，但也可以带走文本和屏幕上的内容，”贝塞尔先生说。 “我们觉得客户基于反馈的客户将在屏幕上花费稍微花更多的时间，并因此究竟理解他们所采取的行动以及导致的动作。”

Robinhood has increasingly found itself in the spotlight after the “meme stock” frenzy earlier this year, when the brokerage came under sharp scrutiny for restricting customer purchases of stocks such as GameStop for several days.

罗宾时代越来越多地发现了在今年早些时候的“Meme股票”狂热之后的聚光灯下，当经纪人受到严格的审查时，用于限制客户购买股票，例如Gamestop几天。

In the weeks since, the company has worked to mend its reputation, as it continues with its plans for an initial public offering. The company recently said it has expanded live phone support after facing criticism over its customer service. Robinhood has also continued to boost its executive team with industry veterans.

在此几周以来，该公司已致力于修复其声誉，因为它继续其初次公开的计划。该公司最近表示，在面对客户服务批评后，它已经扩展了直播电话支持。罗宾时期也继续推动其执行团队与行业退伍军人。

Still, the company has also battled other criticisms, including that it enables inexperienced investors to make risky trades and that it has built a male-dominated following. Robinhood has disputed those claims, saying previously that only 13% of users who traded in any given month in 2020 made an options bet, which is typically considered to be a riskier trading strategy.

尽管如此，该公司还致力于其他批评，包括它使Inteperenced投资者能够使风险交易，并且它建立了一个男性主导的追随者。罗宾时代有争议的声明，此前，只有13％的人在2020年的任何一个月内交易的用户做出了选项投注，这通常被认为是一个冒险的交易策略。

Robinhood has also said that diversity among its users has grown. At an event sponsored by Dow Jones, Jacqueline Ortiz Ramsay, the company’s head of public-policy communications, said Wednesday that the number of female Robinhood customers rose 369% between February of last year and February 2021, with women now making up nearly 31% of active customers. Additionally, she shared, about 17% of Robinhood users are Hispanic, 10% are Asian and 9% are Black.

罗宾情绪还表示，其用户之间的多样性已经成长。该活动由Dow Jones赞助的杰克奎琳·拉姆斯·拉姆扬（Rabqueline Ortiz Ramsay）周三表示，在去年2月和2月2021年2月之间的女性速降客户的数量上升了369％，妇女占近31％积极的客户。此外，她分享了大约17％的罗宾斯用户是西班牙裔，10％是亚洲人，9％是黑色的。
