Dutch and Belgian police say they shut down Sky ECC, a messaging service allegedly popular with criminals, after infiltrating it in Feb, leading to 100+ arrests
荷兰和比利时警方称他们关闭了天空ECC，据称在2月渗透它后涉嫌犯罪分子的消息传递服务，导致100多次逮捕

2021-03-10 22:36:51

Law enforcement agencies from the Netherlands and Belgium have shut down today  Sky ECC, a company that provided a secure encrypted messaging platform to criminal organizations across the world.

来自荷兰和比利时的执法机构今日Sky ECC已关闭，该公司为全球犯罪组织提供了安全的加密消息平台。

Authorities said they infiltrated the platform in mid-February 2021 and have managed to intercept live messages exchanged through the company’s servers.

当局表示，他们于2021年2月中旬渗透了该平台，并设法拦截通过公司服务器交换的现场邮件。

Officials said they used the access they gained last month to collect information on Sky ECC customers and help prevent violent crimes, such as kidnappings, shootings, and murders.

官员表示，他们使用上个月获得的访问，以收集天空ECC客户的信息，并帮助防止暴力犯罪，如绑架，枪击和谋杀案。

In the Netherlands, Dutch investigators raided 75 homes and offices and made 30 arrests part of  Operation Argus.

在荷兰，荷兰调查人员袭击了75家住宅和办事处，并制定了30个逮捕了一部分的武器。

In Belgium, more than 1,500 police officers carried out 200 house searches and arrested 48 suspects part of  Operation A-Limit. According to local media, most of the suspects were arrested around the port city of Antwerp, a gateway for shipping drugs into and out of Europe, with investigators also seizing $1.2 million in drug trafficking money.

在比利时，超过1,500名警察开展了200个房屋搜查，并逮捕了48名嫌疑人的一部分持续限额。据当地媒体介绍，大多数嫌疑人周围围绕安特卫普港口逮捕，该城市为欧洲进出欧洲，调查人员也占用贩毒资金120万美元。

Dutch Police said Sky ECC launched last year after a similar police investigation shut down Encrochat, a company that provided a similar secure platform and encrypted phones to cybercriminals.

荷兰警方表示，天空ECC去年推出了类似的警察调查关闭加密，这是一个为网络犯罪分子提供类似的安全平台和加密电话的公司。

Officials said that many criminal gangs moved from Encrochat to Sky ECC, and the new company amassed more than 70,000 users by the end of 2020, with more than 11,000 located in the Netherlands.

官员表示，许多犯罪团伙从加密到天空ECC迁移，新公司在2020年底到达70,000多名用户，位于荷兰超过11,000人。

Sky ECC’s main product was its proprietary messaging mobile app that allowed gang members to exchange encrypted communications via a private global network of servers.

Sky ECC的主要产品是其专有的消息传递移动应用程序，允许帮派成员通过专用全局服务器交换加密通信。

Customers could install the app on Android, BlackBerry, and iPhone devices, and the app would prevent their data from being leaked to the underlying OS or other phone trackers. The app also included a panic button to erase all its content in case of emergency or an arrest.

客户可以在Android，BlackBerry和iPhone设备上安装应用程序，该应用程序将阻止其数据泄漏到底层操作系统或其他手机跟踪器。该应用程序还包括一个恐慌按钮，以删除紧急情况或逮捕的所有内容。

In a contest put up on its website last year, the company was so confident in its technology that it offered to pay  a $5 million prize to anyone who hacked its systems.

在去年的一场比赛中，该公司对其技术进行了充满信心，即它向那些攻击其系统的任何人支付500万奖金。

Belgian police began investigating the company after they seized a container with 2.8 tons of cocaine last year in Antwerp and found devices running the app on suspects they arrested.

比利时警方于去年在禁区缉获了2.8吨可卡因的集装箱后，比利时警察于去年在安特卫普上找到了在他们被捕的嫌疑人上运行了应用程序的设备。

Neither Dutch nor Belgian police did not say how they breached the company’s network beyond releasing a generic statement of gaining the ability to read live messages.

荷兰语和比利时警方都不会说他们如何违反公司的网络，因为释放了获得读取现场信息的能力的通用声明。

Sky ECC becomes the fourth major company that provided secure phones to criminal gangs dismantled in recent years. Previous cases include  Ennetcom,  PGP Safe,  Phantom Secure, and  EncroChat.

天体ECC成为近年来拆除犯罪团伙的安全手机的第四大公司。以前的案例包括ENNERCOM，PGP安全，幻像安全和加密。

Update, March 10: A Sky ECC spokesperson contested the accuracy of police statements in an email to Motherboard. Sky ECC claimed that law enforcement did not breach their platform, but a cloned system set up by an unauthorized party.

更新，3月10日：天空ECC发言人在电子邮件中取决于警察声明的准确性。天体ECC声称，执法部门没有违反他们的平台，而是由未经授权的派对设立的克隆系统。
