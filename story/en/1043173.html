It probably won't come as a surprise that Disney Plus smash hit series The Mandalorian has won the (unfortunate) title of most-pirated TV show of 2020 -- using popular torrenting site BitTorrent.

迪士尼Plus畅销单曲《曼达洛人》（Mandalorian）赢得了2020年最盗版电视节目的（不幸的）称号，这很令人惊讶，这是通过受欢迎的洪流网站BitTorrent获得的。

According to analysis from TorrentFreak ( via IndieWire), Game of Thrones was the most-pirated TV show seven years running. But the HBO series ended in 2019, leaving The Mandalorian to improve its ranking from third to No. 1 in 2020.
