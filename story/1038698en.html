GetAccept raises $20M Series B, led by Bessemer, to expand its sales platform for SMBs
GetAccept筹集了由Bessemer领导的2000万美元的B轮融资，以扩展其针对中小企业的销售平台

2020-12-10 02:54:16

Last year the funding was led by DN Capital, with participation from BootstrapLabs, Y Combinator and a number of Spotify’s early investors. This round brings GetAccept’s total financing raised to $30M. GetAccept competes with several separate tools, including well-financed solutions like DocSend, PandaDoc, Showpad, Highspot, DocuSign and Adobe Sign.

去年，这笔资金是由DN Capital牵头的，BootstrapLabs，Y Combinator和许多Spotify的早期投资者也参与其中。这一轮融资使GetAccept的总融资额达到了3000万美元。 GetAccept与几种单独的工具竞争，包括资金充足的解决方案，例如DocSend，PandaDoc，Showpad，Highspot，DocuSign和Adobe Sign。

Founded in 2015 by Swedish entrepreneurs and Y Combinator alumni Samir Smajic, Mathias Thulin, Jonas Blanck, and Carl Carell, GetAccept has expanded from 30 to now 100+ employees over the last 18 months with offices across US and EU countries.

GetAccept由瑞典企业家和Y Combinator校友Samir Smajic，Mathias Thulin，Jonas Blanck和Carl Carell于2015年创立，在过去的18个月中，GetAccept的员工人数从30名扩大到现在的100多名，并在美国和欧盟国家设有办事处。

Smajic said: “We believe in the power of relationships and want to bring personalized and engaging interactions back to the online sales process. We saw this digital sales shift and change in behavior back in 2015, which is why we founded GetAccept in the first place. The COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated and forced B2B buyers and sellers to go digital, which has placed digital sales models high up on the company agendas. We aim to be the online place where every B2B business happens, in a personal way.”

Smajic表示：“我们相信关系的力量，并希望将个性化和互动性的互动带回在线销售流程。早在2015年，我们就看到了这种数字化销售的转变和行为的变化，这就是为什么我们首先创建GetAccept的原因。 COVID-19大流行加速并迫使B2B买卖双方转向数字化，这将数字化销售模式置于公司议程的首位。我们的目标是成为每个B2B业务以个人方式发生的在线场所。”

Alex Ferrara, Partner at Bessemer Venture Partners commented : “ Bessemer Venture Partners is thrilled to back the ambitious GetAccept team and their vision to empower millions of SMBs to streamline and digitize their end-to-end sales processes,” said Alex Ferrara, Partner at Bessemer Venture Partners. “They have built a world-class product, prepared for business transactions that continue to shift permanently online at a rapid pace. We look forward to partnering with GetAccept on the journey ahead.”

Bessemer Venture Partners合伙人Alex Ferrara表示：“ Bessemer Venture Partners很高兴能够支持雄心勃勃的GetAccept团队，他们的愿景是授权数百万个SMB简化并数字化其端到端销售流程，” Bessemer Venture Partners。 “他们已经开发出了世界一流的产品，专为业务交易而准备，这些业务继续在网上永久快速地转移。我们期待着与GetAccept合作，继续前进。”
