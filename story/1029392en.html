Microsoft Kin

Jump to navigation Jump to search Kin was a short-lived mobile phone line from Microsoft designed for users of social networking. [1] Microsoft described the phones' target demographic as men and women between ages 15 and 30. [2] It was manufactured by Sharp Corporation [3] and sold through Verizon Wireless. [4]

Microsoft invested two years and about US$1 billion developing the Kin platform, [5] [6] beginning with their acquisition of Danger Incorporated. The Kin was based on Windows CE. [8]

微软投资了两年时间和大约10亿美元开发Kin平台，[5][6]从他们收购Danger公司开始。Kin基于Windows CE。[8]。

The Kin ONE and TWO went on the market on May 14, 2010. Within two months, Verizon stopped selling the phones because of poor sales. [9] Microsoft scrapped its planned European release, stopped promoting the devices, ceased production, and reassigned the Kin development team to other projects. [10]

Kin 1和Kin 2于2010年5月14日上市。不到两个月，由于销量不佳，Verizon停止销售这款手机。[9]微软放弃了在欧洲发布的计划，停止推广这些设备，停止生产，并将Kin开发团队重新分配到其他项目。[10]。

Microsoft updated its unsold Kin inventory with firmware that removed their social and web-based features, and in December 2010 offered these re-purposed units through Verizon stores as limited feature phones, the Kin ONEm and the TWOm. In January 2011, Microsoft shut down the kin.com website, which controlled most of the earlier phones' features.

微软用固件更新了未售出的Kin库存，删除了它们的社交和基于网络的功能，并在2010年12月通过Verizon商店将这些重新用途的产品作为有限功能手机、Kin ONEm和TWOm提供。2011年1月，微软关闭了Kin.com网站，该网站控制着早期手机的大部分功能。

The Kin TWOm was discontinued in August 2011; [11] unsold inventory could still be found for sale on deals sites as late as June 2013. [12]

Kin TWOm已于2011年8月停产；[11]直到2013年6月，仍可在交易网站上找到待售的未售出库存。[12]。

The Kin project was first known by the codename Project Pink, and began under direction of Microsoft executive J Allard. [13] In order to gain a head start, Microsoft acquired Danger Incorporated, which built the Danger Hiptop/T-Mobile Sidekick, in 2008 for a purchase price rumored to be around US$500 million. [14] [15] In September 2009, a ZDNet source reported that Project Pink would bring an entirely new software stack and services. [16] Some reports predicted that the new mobile phone platform would be based on the Zune media device. [17] The project was managed by Roz Ho, who coined the project name, "Pink", her favorite color. Engineers and project managers used the Twitter hash tag " #TMDP" to communicate in code with one another—the acronym expands to " Truly, Madly, Deeply (Pink)" for Roz Ho's favorite movie and the project name. Engineers were told they should feel passion for the project, "Truly, Madly, Deeply". When milestones were reached or there was a successful test, Roz Ho would cheer the team on by exclaiming "Truly, Madly, Deeply" in meetings or emails.

Kin项目最初的代号是“粉色项目”，并在微软高管J·阿拉德的指导下开始。[13]为了抢占先机，微软在2008年收购了Danger InCorporation，该公司建造了Danger Hiptop/T-Mobile伙伴，据传收购价约为5亿美元。[14][15]2009年9月，ZDNet的一位消息人士报道说，Project Pink将带来一个全新的软件堆栈和服务。[16]一些报道预测，新的移动电话平台将基于Zune媒体设备。[17]这个项目由Roz Ho管理，她创造了项目名称，粉红色，她最喜欢的颜色。工程师和项目经理使用Twitter的散列标签进行代码交流--这个首字母缩写扩展成了Roz Ho最喜欢的电影和项目名称的首字母缩写"；Madly，Deepth(Pink)&34；。工程师们被告知，他们应该真正地、疯狂地、深深地感受到对这个项目的热情。当达到里程碑或测试成功时，何鸿燊会在会议或电子邮件中真心实意地、疯狂地、深切地欢呼，为团队加油。

Kin was developed inside Microsoft's Premium Mobile Experiences (PMX) division by a group that included employees from Danger. [18] Handset manufacturers and network carriers were said to be initially enthusiastic about Kin, and vying with each other to be involved with the project. [13]

KIN是在微软高级移动体验(PMX)部门内由一个包括危险员工在内的小组开发的。[18]据说手机制造商和网络运营商最初对Kin很热情，并争先恐后地参与到这个项目中来。[13]。

According to Engadget, there was jealousy and rivalry in Microsoft's executive ranks, and Windows Phone Senior Vice President Andy Lees managed to wrest control of the Kin project away from Allard, and move it under his Windows Phone division. [13] Danger's Sidekick, the predecessor to Kin, was based on the Java programming language, but Engadget says that Andy Lees wanted Kin to run an in-house Microsoft operating system. [13] Microsoft planned to base Kin on Windows Phone. Due to delays with Windows Phone, however, the software instead had to be based directly upon Windows CE. [18]

根据Engadget的说法，微软的高管队伍中充满了嫉妒和竞争，Windows Phone高级副总裁安迪·李斯设法从阿拉德手中夺取了Kin项目的控制权，并将其转移到他的Windows Phone部门下。[13]Danger'；的伙伴，Kin的前身，是基于Java编程语言的，但是Engadget说Andy Lees想让Kin运行内部的微软操作系统。[13]微软计划将Kin建立在Windows Phone之上。然而，由于Windows Phone的延迟，该软件不得不直接基于Windows CE。[18]

Engadget claims that Andy Lees lacked enthusiasm for the Kin project. [13] Nonetheless, Microsoft spent a further two years developing the Kin until its release in 2010.

恩加吉特声称安迪·李斯对Kin项目缺乏热情。[13]尽管如此，微软又花了两年时间开发Kin，直到2010年发布。

The unveiling of Microsoft Kin began when the company sent out invitations to select reporters for a mystery event in San Francisco on April 12, 2010. The tagline on the invitation said "It's time to share". However, just hours later a source confirmed that the event was about Project Pink (the official name not having been announced yet). [20] The event was held in a night club called Mighty and featured a presentation given by Robbie Bach, president of Microsoft's Entertainment and Device division. [21]

微软Kin的揭幕始于2010年4月12日，该公司发出邀请，邀请他们为旧金山的一个神秘活动挑选记者。邀请函上的标语说，是时候分享了。然而，就在几个小时后，有消息人士证实，这一活动是关于“粉色计划”(官方名称尚未公布)。[20]这次活动在一家名为Might的夜总会举行，微软娱乐和设备部门总裁罗比·巴赫(Robbie Bach)发表了演讲。[21]。

Microsoft was criticized for an online video advertisement for the phone that depicted a male teenager putting a Kin under his shirt to photograph his naked chest. He was then shown sending the image to a female teenager. The Consumer Reports group described the advertisement as a "downright creepy sequence," suggesting that it promoted sexting. In response, Microsoft deleted the "inappropriate" portion of the video. [22]

微软因这款手机的在线视频广告而受到批评，广告中描绘了一名男性少年将一枚Kin放在衬衫下面拍摄他的裸胸。然后，他被拍到将图像发送给一名女青少年。消费者报告小组将这则广告描述为一个彻头彻尾的令人毛骨悚然的序列，暗示它推广性短信。作为回应，微软删除了视频中不恰当的部分。[22]。

Kin suffered from poor sales. Microsoft executives told The New York Times that they were dismayed that Verizon Wireless staff were not promoting the phones actively enough. [23] After only forty-eight days on the market, Microsoft discontinued the Kin line on June 30, 2010 [24] and by mid-July 2010 Verizon had returned all their unsold phones to Microsoft. [9] Its planned European release on UK carrier Vodafone was canceled. [25] [26]

金的销售业绩不佳。微软高管告诉纽约时报，他们对Verizon Wireless员工推销手机的力度不够感到失望。[23]上市仅48天后，微软于2010年6月30日[24]停止生产Kin系列，到2010年7月中旬，Verizon已将所有未售出的手机退还给微软。[9]它原计划在英国航空公司沃达丰(Vodafone)上在欧洲上映的计划被取消。[25][26]。

“The Kin was a mistake from Day One,” Rob Enderle, principal analyst of Enderle Group, told eWeek magazine. “The extra time they took to convert the Kin from the Sidekick platform to Windows CE made it about a year and a half late to market, and the merger likely added another year and a half. That’s 1.5 to 3 years late depending on when you start the clock.” [27]

Enderle Group首席分析师罗布·恩德尔(Rob Enderle)告诉eWeek杂志：“从第一天起，Kin就是一个错误。”“他们花了额外的时间将Kin从SideKey平台转换到Windows CE，这使得它晚了大约一年半的时间投放市场，合并可能又增加了一年半的时间。那就晚了1.5到3年，这取决于你什么时候开始计时。“[27]。

On November 18, 2010 Verizon Wireless's website confirmed that the Kin ONE and Kin TWO were back on the market with a reworked feature phone operating system, re-badged as Kin ONEm and Kin TWOm. Since the new phone did not use the kin.com website it did not require a data plan. The new version of the phone arrived at Verizon stores in December of that year. [28] Along with the name change and re-classification as feature phones rather than smartphones, the prices of the devices were slashed, with the Kin ONEm reduced from $50 to free, and the Kin TWOm reduced from $100 to $50, with a new two-year contract.

2010年11月18日，Verizon Wireless的网站证实，Kin One和Kin Two重新推出了重新设计的功能手机操作系统，重新命名为Kin ONEm和Kin TWOm。由于新手机没有使用kin.com网站，因此不需要数据计划。新版手机于当年12月在Verizon商店上市。[28]随着功能手机更名并被重新归类为功能手机而不是智能手机，设备的价格也大幅下调，Kin ONEm从50美元降至免费，Kin TWOm从100美元降至50美元，并签订了新的两年合同。

The new feature phone OS removed the web-based and social networking integration features such as Kin Loop, Kin Spot, and Kin Studio. [29] The newer "m" phones (identified by "m" next to the model number and two yellow dots on the corners) could be "downgraded" to the original firmware version via holding "r", "b", and "power" upon turning on the device (a procedure that also erases all the phone's stored data and settings).

新的功能手机操作系统去掉了基于网络和社交网络的集成功能，如Kin Loop、Kin Spot和Kin Studio。[29]较新的手机(由型号旁边的和角落上的两个黄色圆点标识)可以在开机时通过按住"；r&34；、"；b"；和##34；电源(该程序还会擦除手机的所有存储数据和设置)降级到原始固件版本。

The kin.com website was discontinued on January 2011, and all user pictures and other information stored on the website were deleted. Verizon offered a free trade-in to a 3G smartphone to all affected phone owners. [30] After the Kin website shut down, the original Kin ONE and TWO lost access to the same social features that were removed from the ONEm and TWOm, making them also essentially feature phones that did not require a data plan.

Kin.com网站于2011年1月停止运营，网站上存储的所有用户图片和其他信息都被删除。Verizon向所有受影响的手机用户提供3G智能手机的免费折价。[30]在Kin网站关闭后，最初的Kin One和Kin Two失去了使用ONEm和TWOm中删除的相同社交功能的权限，这使得它们基本上也以不需要数据计划的手机为特色。

Microsoft described Kin devices as "social phones", [4] straddling the feature phone and smartphone markets, with an emphasis on social networking and sharing of content, but without downloadable apps or games. [31]

微软将Kin设备描述为横跨功能手机和智能手机市场的社交手机[4]，强调社交网络和内容共享，但没有可下载的应用程序或游戏。[31][31]。

The home screen on Kin was called Loop, and served as an aggregator for social networking connections from Facebook, Twitter, Windows Live, and MySpace as well as web content from web feeds.

Kin的主屏幕被称为Loop，它是Facebook、Twitter、Windows Live和MySpace社交网络连接的聚合器，也是来自web feed的web内容的聚合器。

Commentators noted Loop's 15-minute delay for updates, which CNET's Ina Fried described as "odd". [32] PC World argued this delay was at odds with Microsoft's claim that the phone is "always-connected". [33] Users could not adjust this interval, [34] although updates could be manually triggered with an on-screen refresh button, or locking then unlocking the phone. [ citation needed] Microsoft cited battery life and immature social networking APIs as reasons for the delay; Engadget speculated that Microsoft may have been using the delayed messaging to encourage Verizon to offer lower-priced data plans, which would be attractive to the platform's teenage target audience. [35]

评论家们注意到环路的更新延迟了15分钟，CNET的艾娜·弗里德(Ina Fry)将其描述为奇数。[32]《个人电脑世界》辩称，这一延迟与微软宣称的这款手机总是联网的说法不符。[33]用户无法调整此时间间隔[34]，尽管可以使用屏幕上的刷新按钮手动触发更新，或者先锁定然后再解锁电话。[需要引用]微软称电池续航时间和不成熟的社交网络API是延迟的原因；Engadget推测，微软可能一直在利用延迟的消息传递来鼓励Verizon提供更低价格的数据套餐，这将吸引该平台的青少年目标受众。[35]。

Kin did not support uploading photos or videos to Twitter. [36] Retweets, direct messages, viewing single person updates, and opening Twitter links from the Loop were also not supported. [34]

KIN不支持将照片或视频上传到Twitter。[36]也不支持转发推文、直接发送消息、查看单人更新以及从Loop打开Twitter链接。[34][34]。

Spot was a feature that allowed users to add an e-mail address or telephone number to an SMS or MMS, or to attach content (e.g., text, URLs or photos) to emails. [34]

Spot是一项功能，允许用户将电子邮件地址或电话号码添加到SMS或MMS，或者将内容(例如，文本、URL或照片)附加到电子邮件。[34][34]。

The Spot was represented as an omnipresent colored dot near the bottom center of the screen. To add an address field, the user dragged a recipient's photo to the Spot, then clicked on the Spot, opening a page that allowed the user to create a new email, SMS or MMS. [34]

该点被表示为屏幕底部中心附近的一个无处不在的彩色圆点。为了添加地址字段，用户将收件人的照片拖到现场，然后点击现场，打开一个页面，允许用户创建新的电子邮件、短信或彩信。[34][34]。

The Spot could also be used to set up e-mail attachments, by the user dragging the content to the Spot, then dragged the recipient's photo to the Spot, after which an email could be sent containing those attachments. [34] However, Kin did not support attachments to non-e-mail media such as MMS and could not be used to send content to social media sites. [34]

Spot还可以用来设置电子邮件附件，方法是用户将内容拖到Spot，然后将收件人的照片拖到Spot，之后可以发送包含这些附件的电子邮件。[34]但是，Kin不支持非电子邮件媒体(如彩信)的附件，也不能用于向社交媒体网站发送内容。[34][34]。

Content from the Kin phone, such as photos, videos and messages, was automatically synchronized to a cloud service called Kin Studio, and would then be accessible via a browser on other devices. The Kin Studio website was written in Silverlight and its appearance was similar to the Kin UI, even including a Spot for sharing content. [37] Kin Studio was shut down in January 2011.

来自Kin手机的内容，如照片、视频和消息，会自动同步到名为Kin Studio的云服务上，然后可以通过其他设备上的浏览器进行访问。Kin Studio网站是用Silverlight编写的，外观与Kin UI相似，甚至还包括一个分享内容的地方。[37]Kin Studio于2011年1月关闭。

The Kin ONE had a five- megapixel camera with standard-definition video recording capabilities. The Kin TWO included an eight-megapixel camera with 720p video recording. Photos were automatically geocoded on the original phones, a feature disabled on the "m" phones. [38] There was no photo editing software for Kin. [39]

Kin One拥有500万像素的摄像头，具有标清视频录制功能。Kin Two包括一个800万像素的摄像头，可以拍摄720p的视频。照片会自动在原始手机上进行地理编码，这是一项在手机上禁用的功能。[38]Kin没有照片编辑软件。[39][font=宋体]。

For media, Kin devices synced with Zune desktop software. In addition, the phones were compatible with Zune Pass. Much like contemporaneous Zune devices, Kin phones could also stream music over a WiFi connection in addition to 3G on the carrier's network. [40] For Mac users, Microsoft, in collaboration with Mark/Space, provided a media syncing tool that pulled audio and video files from iTunes and photos from iPhoto. [37] Kin did not support playing video from sites such as YouTube or Hulu, although the devices could watch YouTube videos from the browser through WiFi or 3G. [41] [ clarification needed]

对于媒体，Kin设备与Zune桌面软件同步。此外，这些手机与Zune Pass兼容。就像同时代的Zune设备一样，Kin手机除了运营商网络上的3G之外，还可以通过WiFi连接流媒体音乐。[40]对于Mac用户，微软与Mark/Space合作，提供了一个媒体同步工具，可以从iTunes中提取音频和视频文件，从iPhoto中提取照片。[37]Kin不支持播放YouTube或Hulu等网站的视频，尽管这些设备可以通过WiFi或3G从浏览器观看YouTube视频。[41][需要澄清]。

Kin had no app store and no third-party apps could be installed on the phones. [41] PC World described this as "baffling". [33] Further, the web browser did not support Flash web applications, [42] and there were no games for the phones. [42] Microsoft had stated their intention to eventually merge the Kin and Windows Phone platforms, with downloadable apps then being available for the combined platform. [43]

Kin没有应用商店，也没有第三方应用可以安装在手机上。[41]《个人电脑世界》(PC World)形容这一点令人费解。[33]此外，网络浏览器不支持Flash网络应用程序，[42]而且手机也没有游戏。[42]微软曾表示，他们打算最终合并Kin和Windows Phone平台，届时可下载的应用程序就可以在合并后的平台上使用。[43][font=宋体]。

Contact lists could only be copied from another phone by Verizon store employees. There was no way for the consumer to do this by any known means ( over the air, via a memory or SIM card, wirelessly via Bluetooth and vCard, or via direct USB cable connection).

联系人列表只能由Verizon商店员工从另一部手机复制。消费者无法通过任何已知的方式(通过空中、通过存储器或SIM卡、通过蓝牙和vCard无线、或通过直接USB电缆连接)做到这一点。

Kin had no calendar or appointment application, [41] nor any ability to sync with Outlook calendar or Google Calendar. [36] Some commentators suggested that a social phone should be able to share a social events calendar. [36]

Kin没有日历或约会应用程序，[41]也没有与Outlook日历或Google Calendar同步的能力。[36]一些评论家建议，社交电话应该能够共享社交活动日历。[36][36]。

Kin was unable to Instant Message (IM), or use any IM client, [41] which was considered odd for a phone built for messaging and aimed at the youth market. [36] It was discovered that the ROM inside the phones contained the foundation for an IM system supporting AOL Instant Messenger, Windows Live Messenger, and Yahoo! Messenger, but it was never made operational. It was speculated that future revisions of the software would have enabled instant messaging. [44]

KIN无法使用即时消息(IM)，也无法使用任何IM客户端，[41]对于一款专为发信息而针对年轻人市场的手机来说，这被认为是很奇怪的。[36]人们发现，手机内部的ROM包含支持AOL Instant Messenger、Windows Live Messenger和Yahoo！的IM系统的基础。信使号，但它从未投入使用。据推测，该软件未来的修订版将启用即时通讯。[44][44]。

In the United States, cellular telephone provider Verizon Wireless sold the Kin phones with a voice plan starting at $39.99 per month. An optional Zune Pass cost $14.99 per month for music access. [45]

在美国，移动电话提供商Verizon Wireless出售Kin手机，并提供语音套餐，起价为每月39.99美元。可选的Zune通行证音乐访问每月收费14.99美元。[45][45]。

After the kin.com website was discontinued in January 2011, the original Kin ONE and Kin TWO did not require a data plan, although the optional Zune Pass was still available.

在kin.com网站于2011年1月停用后，最初的Kin One和Kin Two不需要数据计划，尽管可选的Zune Pass仍然可用。

The Microsoft Kin has been described as a "close cousin" to Windows Phone, [46] with shared user interface characteristics. According to Microsoft:

微软Kin被描述为Windows Phone的近亲，拥有共享的用户界面特征。据微软称：

"Both KIN and Windows Phone share common OS components, software and services. We will seek to align around a single platform for both products as well as consistent hardware specifications." [46]

KIN和Windows Phone共享共同的操作系统组件、软件和服务。我们将寻求使这两种产品的单一平台以及一致的硬件规格保持一致。"；[46]。

Microsoft said that the underlying fundamentals of Kin and Windows Phone were held together by similar core technologies. Both operating systems ran the same Silverlight platform, [46] with Microsoft's stated intention being to eventually merge Windows Phone with Kin. [43]

微软表示，Kin和Windows Phone的基本原理是由类似的核心技术维系在一起的。这两个操作系统都运行相同的Silverlight平台，[46]微软宣称其最终意图是将Windows Phone与Kin合并。[43][font=宋体]。

In November 2010, Microsoft and Verizon re-launched the Kin phones, re-labeling them as feature phones with no required data plan. The prices of the phones also were slashed. Where the Kin TWO was formerly $100 on a two-year contract, the new Kin TWOm was offered at $20 on contract. Similarly, the Kin ONE, formerly $50 on a contract, was now $0 on contract.

2010年11月，微软和Verizon重新推出了Kin手机，将它们重新贴上了没有必要数据计划的功能手机的标签。手机的价格也被大幅下调。Kin Two以前的两年合同价格是100美元，而新的Kin TWOm的合同价格是20美元。同样，Kin One以前的合同价格为50美元，现在合同价格为0美元。

The new firmware on the repurposed "m" phones removed several data-centric features of the operating system, including many of the devices' social elements such as the Loop home screen, the Spot, and access to the Kin Studio cloud storage site, which was shut down in January 2011. With the lack of a data plan requirement and lower prices up front, Microsoft and Verizon hoped to do what they originally planned: capture the teenage market.

重新调整用途的手机上的新固件去掉了该操作系统的几个以数据为中心的功能，包括许多设备的社交元素，如Loop主屏幕、Spot，以及访问Kin Studio云存储网站(该网站于2011年1月关闭)。由于缺乏数据计划要求和较低的前期价格，微软和Verizon希望做他们最初计划的事情：占领青少年市场。

One seemingly data-laden feature of the old Kin devices which remained available for the repurposed phones was Zune Pass, although it was now able to stream music only over Wi-Fi, even when customers had 3G data enabled, to conserve data. This was done to accommodate Verizon's new tiered data plans, which marked the end of unlimited data for users.

旧Kin设备的一个看似充满数据的功能仍然适用于重新调整用途的手机，那就是Zune pass，尽管它现在只能通过Wi-Fi播放音乐，即使客户启用了3G数据，以保存数据。这样做是为了适应Verizon新的分层数据计划，这标志着用户数据不受限制的时代结束了。

Users were also able to sync music, podcasts, TV shows, and movies to their Kin phones through the Zune software. Music could be downloaded directly to the phones as well, though only over Wi-Fi. Podcasts, TV shows, and movies could only be synced to the phone via the Zune Software on a Windows PC.

用户还可以通过Zune软件将音乐、播客、电视节目和电影同步到他们的Kin手机上。音乐也可以直接下载到手机上，尽管只能通过Wi-Fi。播客、电视节目和电影只能通过Windows PC上的Zune软件同步到手机。

The "m" series added a calendar and a calculator application, to address complaints that these were missing functions that even the most basic feature phone typically has. Although the calendar application had basic features such as timed alerts and reminders, as well as day, month, and year views, it could not be synchronized with Microsoft Outlook, Microsoft Exchange, or even Windows Live Hotmail.

该系列增加了日历和计算器应用程序，以解决人们的抱怨，即这些应用程序缺少即使是最基本的功能手机通常都有的功能。尽管日历应用程序具有定时警报和提醒以及日、月和年视图等基本功能，但它无法与Microsoft Outlook、Microsoft Exchange甚至Windows Live Hotmail同步。

Along with the discontinued social networking features, the phones no longer geocoded pictures. Other features missing on the original version continued to be absent, including disallowing Bluetooth access for file transfer and wireless printing.

随着社交网络功能的停止，手机不再对图片进行地理编码。原始版本缺少的其他功能仍然缺失，包括不允许通过蓝牙进行文件传输和无线打印。

The Kin TWOm was unable to forward previously sent messages. Text messages were sent in a chat style format, making it unable to single out a specific text message.

Kin TWOm无法转发以前发送的邮件。短信是以聊天风格的格式发送的，因此无法挑选出特定的短信。
