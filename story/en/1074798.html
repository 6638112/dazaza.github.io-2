Grata, a New York-based private company intelligence engine provider, raised $25M in Series A funding.

总部位于纽约的私营公司情报引擎提供商格拉塔在a系列融资中筹集了2500万美元。

The round, which brings total funding to $35m, was led by Craft Ventures with participation from Teamworthy Ventures, Altai Ventures, Eigen Ventures, Bling, Accomplice, and Touchdown Ventures as well as current and former M&A executives.

这轮融资总额达到3500万美元，由Craft Ventures牵头，Teamworthy Ventures、Altai Ventures、Eigen Ventures、Bling、Concimate和Touchdown Ventures以及当前和以前的M&；一位高管。

The company intends to use the funds to accelerate its business reach. Co-founded by CEO Andrew Bocskocsky and COO Nevin Raj, Grata provides comprehensive coverage of private companies, featuring information and nuanced contextual insights on millions of companies. Its product combines a web data acquisition engine that scans billions of websites and proprietary machine learning (ML) and natural language processing (NLP) models that intuitively process unstructured information into nuanced, contextual insights on private companies.

该公司打算利用这些资金加速其业务范围。Grata由首席执行官安德鲁·博茨科斯基（Andrew Bocskocksky）和首席运营官内文·拉吉（Nevin Raj）共同创立，全面报道私营公司，提供有关数百万公司的信息和微妙的背景见解。它的产品结合了一个扫描数十亿个网站的网络数据采集引擎，以及专有的机器学习（ML）和自然语言处理（NLP）模型，这些模型直观地将非结构化信息处理为对私营公司的细致的、上下文相关的见解。
