Pocketlang is a small (~3000 semicolons) and fastfunctional language written in C. It's syntactically similar to Ruby and it canbe learned within 15 minutes.Including the compiler, bytecode VM and runtime, it's a standalone executablewith zero external dependencies just as it's self descriptive name. The pocketlangVM can be embedded in another hosting program very easily.
