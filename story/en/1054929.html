The Curious Case of the Y Combinator Bookmarklet
Y组合书签的奇怪情况

2021-03-29 03:13:51

I frequent the /new page of Hacker News, usually to find interesting articlesthat were missed by the masses 1. In recent years I noticed that people weresubmitting “ Y Combinator Bookmarklet”at least several times a week. From time to time, this would get a small number of upvotes.

我经常常常黑客新闻的新页面，通常会发现群众错过了有趣的Articlesthat 1.近年来，我注意到人们每周至少多次加入“Y Combinator Bookmarklet”。从时刻起，这将得到少数的回升。

After a while I started wondering why people were submitting this so frequently.Reviewing the bookmarklet page, it suddenly became clear 2. Logged-in HN users were clicking thelink which would then submit the bookmarklet page itself to Hacker News. Atsome point these links were simply automatically marked dead whenever submitted.

经过一段时间我开始想知道为什么人们如此经常提交这一点。评价书名页面，它突然变得清晰了2.登录的HN用户点击了Thelink，然后将书签页本身提交给黑客新闻。 atsome point这些链接在提交时简单地自动标记死亡。

But, there is a better (and simple) solution! Just add javascript to preventthe default behavior. The yellow highlighted text below shows the change.

但是，有一个更好（简单）的解决方案！只需添加JavaScript以防止默认行为。下面的黄色突出显示的文字显示了更改。

This allows users to drag the bookmarklet to their toolbar while preventing themfrom accidentally submitting to HN. An ancillary benefit is that, due to how YCtitles their pages, it will no longer be known as “Y Combinator Bookmarklet.”

这允许用户将书签拖动到其工具栏，同时防止意外提交到HN。辅助益处是，由于如何yctitles页面，它将不再被称为“Y Combinator Bookmarklet”。

This seems to be a problem with HN. First, there should be a preferencewhere landing on /new is the default. Second – I’m not sure the solution –there should be a way to allow for long-form articles because they rarelyif ever make it to the front page. ↩︎

这似乎是HN的问题。首先，应该有一个偏好的地方登陆/新是默认的。第二 - 我不确定解决方案 - 应该是一种方法来允许长形文章，因为它们很少有人进入首页。 ↩︎
