The Czech Republic may be better known for beer, hockey and the sights of Prague, but its entrepreneurial community is as ambitious as any. Pipedrive is an EU-based CRM company with offices in eight countries, but it has a Czech co-founder in VP of Product Martin Henk, one of several founders to emerge from the ecosystem.

捷克共和国可能会为啤酒，曲棍球和布拉格景点而闻名，但其创业界和任何人都是雄心勃勃的。 Pipedrive是一家以八所国家的办事处为基于欧盟的CRM公司，但它在产品Martin Henk的VP中有一个捷克联合创始人，其中几个创始人之一被生态系统出现。

Then there was Integromat, which did not raise any external capital but sold for around 2.5 billion crowns ($114 million), making its seven Czech founders into multimillionaires. Prague’s Memsource is valued at approximately 1.3 billion crowns or $59 million. But this is just the tip of the iceberg.

然后有Integromat，没有筹集任何外部资本，但销售约25亿冠（11400万美元），使其七个捷克的创始人成为多元素。布拉格的Memsource值得约为13亿冠或5900万美元。但这只是冰山一角。

To unpack this rare gem of Europe’s startup scene, we spoke to eight area investors.

为了解压缩这一欧洲的初始创业板，我们谈到了八个地区投资者。

Among the trends they identified are startups in B2B, business automation processes, e-commerce, AI, SaaS and COVID-19-related solutions, as well as “smart” everything: factories, cities, offices, etc. Other themes included cybersecurity, AR/VR, remote work, and cybersecurity.

在他们确定的趋势中，在B2B，商业自动化流程，电子商务，AI，SaaS和Covid-19相关解决方案中的启动，以及“智能”一切：工厂，城市，办公室等其他主题包括网络安全， AR / VR，远程工作和网络安全。

Saturated areas included cryptocurrency, blockchain, fintech and martech. The people we spoke to said they see travel, dating apps and other businesses traditionally based on physical interaction as weaker segments. Still, new opportunities are popping up in remote work, psychedelics and wellness.

饱和区域包括加密电力，区块链，金融电气和Martech。我们讨论的人表示，他们将在传统上查看旅行，约会应用程序和其他企业，基于身体互动作为较弱的细分。尽管如此，新的机会突破了遥远的工作，迷幻和健康。

Respondents said they invest around 50% inside Czechia and 50% across Central and Eastern Europe, while some are more focused across CEE generally, with some percentage of the fund supporting startups that have scaled to the U.S.

受访者表示，他们在捷克地区的50％左右投入50％，跨越欧洲和东欧50％，而一些人则在CEE上更加专注于CEE，其中一些基金支持初创公司的初始股份已经扩大到美国。

Most said their investments hadn’t been significantly impacted by COVID-19, but future uncertainly is a concern. The advice is to “be frugal to accommodate to the new situation and roll on.”

大多数人表示，他们的投资没有受到Covid-19的显着影响，但未来的不确定是一个问题。建议是“节俭，以适应新的情况并滚动”。

As far as green shoots, COVID-19 has “played a role of an accelerator for innovation in many business areas and even e-government and other rigid/conservative industries,” said one. D2C startups have benefitted and “Zoom selling” now seems “totally plausible.”

至于绿笋，Covid-19已经“在许多商业领域甚至电子政务和其他刚性/保守派行业的创新中发挥了加速器的作用，” D2C初创公司受益，现在“缩放销售”似乎“完全合理”。

Are you seeing “green shoots” regarding revenue growth, retention or other momentum in your portfolio as they adapt to the pandemic? Yes, demand for call center tools like omnichannel solution mluvii.com, which works at the home office move up significantly.

您是否在对大流行适应流行时看到“绿色射击”是关于您的投资组合中的收入增长，保留或其他势头？是的，对呼叫中心工具的需求，如omnichannel解决方案mluvii.com，在家庭办公室工作明显增加。

What is a moment that has given you hope in the last month or so? This can be professional, personal or a mix of the two. At spring our country was “best in COVID” and now it is “worst in COVID.” Last spring thousands of people from the startup community helped and came up with brilliant ideas, apps and solutions but at the end most outcomes (like eRouška and https://koronavirus.mzcr.cz/en/) were screwed by slow or faulty decisions of government. Instead of hope I’m disappointed, but I believe that vaccination will help us to get life back on the track.

在上个月左右给你希望的时刻是什么？这可以是专业，个人或两者的混合。在春天，我国是“最适合的Covid”，现在它是“Covid中最糟糕的”。比赛春天来自初创公司的数千人帮助并提出了辉煌的想法，应用程序和解决方案，但最终的成果（如Erouška和https://koronavirus.mzcr.cz/en/）被慢或有缺陷的决定搞砸了政府。而不是希望我很失望，但我相信疫苗接种会帮助我们让生活回到轨道上。

Who are key startup people you see creating success locally, whether investors, founders or even other types of startup ecosystems roles like lawyers, designers, growth experts, etc. We’re trying to highlight the movers and shakers who outsiders might not know. Patrik Juránek from Startup Disrupt community.

谁是关键的初创人，您在本地创建成功，是否投资者，创始人甚至其他类型的创业生态系统，如律师，设计师，增长专家等我们正试图突出推动者和散步者可能不知道的搬运工。来自启动中的PatrikJuránek扰乱了社区。

Any other thoughts you want to share with TechCrunch readers? Prague is great and safe city for living — when you setup a branch in Prague you can attract people from all of the CEE region to move in.

What trends are you most excited about investing in, generally? Anything that helps businesses run smarter is something we would like to take a look at. More specifically we are interested in areas such as Internet of Things, smart factories, smart cities, smart office, cybersecurity, big data and AR/VR. And especially when there is some kind of hardware involved — that something we really love.

通常，你最兴奋的趋势是什么？任何帮助企业跑步更聪明的东西都是我们想看看的东西。更具体地说，我们对事物，智能工厂，智能城市，智能办公，网络安全，大数据和AR / VR等领域感兴趣。特别是当有某种硬件时 - 我们真正爱的东西。

Are there startups that you wish you would see in the industry but don’t? What are some overlooked opportunities right now? It would be great to see more startups focusing on hardware. Admittedly, creating hardware and scaling-up a hardware-focused business is always a bigger challenge, but the opportunities are so vast and many are yet untapped.

有初创公司，你希望你能在行业中看到但不是吗？现在有什么忽视的机会？很高兴看到更多的初创公司专注于硬件。不可否认，创建硬件和扩展功能，一个硬件的业务始终是一个更大的挑战，但机会如此庞大，许多人尚未开拓。

What are you looking for in your next investment, in general? Apart from the “obvious” aspects such as innovativeness, global potential, scalability, strong team and fit with our investment thesis, we look for founders who show great strategic thinking and execution skills, who really understand the market and their customers’ needs and listen to feedback.

您在下次投资中寻找什么？除了创新，全球潜力，可扩展性，强大的团队等“明显”方面，与我们的投资论证相处，我们寻找表现出伟大的战略思维和执行技能的创始人，他真正了解市场和客户的需求和倾听反馈。

Which areas are either oversaturated or would be too hard to compete in at this point for a new startup? What other types of products/services are you wary or concerned about? Considering our focus on B2B, we have better overview of this part of the economy. Lately, we have seen a huge number of startups using AI/ML for computer vision or natural language processing use cases creating very similar products, meaning it will be rather difficult for them to differentiate and outperform the rest of the competition. But that does not mean that a new revolutionary idea cannot appear.

哪个领域要么过分，或者太难在这一点上竞争新的启动？您对哪些其他类型的产品/服务有什么要求或担心？考虑到我们对B2B的关注，我们更好地概述了这部分经济。最近，我们已经看到了使用AI / ML的大量启动，用于计算机视觉或自然语言处理用例创建了非常相似的产品，这意味着它们将相当困难，以区分和优于竞争的其余部分。但这并不意味着一个新的革命性想法不能出现。

How much are you focused on investing in your local ecosystem versus other startup hubs (or everywhere) in general? More than 50%? Less? Our focus is on the Central European region — so far we have invested in the Czech Republic and Slovakia, but we are open to founders from other neighboring countries as well. The majority of our portfolio is located in the Brno/South Moravia region, where Y Soft is based. It is not an outcome of an intentional strategy, but just the reality of which startups interested us the most.

您在普遍上专注于投资当地生态系统（或各处）的范围内收费多少？超过50％？较少的？我们的重点是在中欧地区 - 到目前为止，我们已经投资于捷克共和国和斯洛伐克，但我们也对来自其他邻国的创始人开放。我们的大多数投资组合位于布尔诺/南摩拉拉维亚地区，Y软件是基于的。这不是一个故意战略的结果，而是初创公司的现实最为感兴趣。

Which industries in your city and region seem well positioned to thrive, or not, long term? What are companies you are excited about (your portfolio or not), which founders? Generally, the Czech startup ecosystem is getting more mature, especially thanks to serial entrepreneurs as well as more experienced first-time founders, and the developing business angel/VC ecosystem. It is hard to pick just one industry, as the spectrum of companies is very vast.

您所在城市和地区的哪些行业似乎很好地茁壮成长，长期茁壮成长？您对（您的投资组合或不是）兴奋的公司是什么？一般来说，捷克启动生态系统正在变得更加成熟，特别是由于串行企业家以及更有经验的首次创始人以及开发商业天使/ VC生态系统。很难选择一个行业，因为公司的谱非常浩大。

How should investors in other cities think about the overall investment climate and opportunities in your city? From the investors’ point of view, the Czech startup ecosystem can provide a lot of interesting opportunities, and especially for foreign investors the investments can be a “good value for money,” even though the VC ecosystem has become more competitive in the last years due to influx of new money. The seed and partly Series A segment can be seen as rather saturated, but there is a significant potential in the larger Series A or later-stage investments.

其他城市的投资者应该如何考虑城市的整体投资气候和机遇？从投资者的角度来看，捷克启动生态系统可以提供很多有趣的机会，特别是对于外国投资者来说，投资可能是“物有所值”，即使VC生态系统在过去几年变得更具竞争力由于新资金的涌入。种子和部分串联段可以看出相当饱和，但较大的阶段或后期投资中存在显着潜力。

Do you expect to see a surge in more founders coming from geographies outside major cities in the years to come, with startup hubs losing people due to the pandemic and lingering concerns, plus the attraction of remote work? The main Czech hubs, Prague and Brno, are probably not going to see their status weakened, as they are not only business centers, but also have the main universities where the talented people are and are hearts of the cultural life that is attractive to many. But we will see a shift toward remote woking, allowing founders to tap a wider talent pool.

您希望在多年来主要城市以外的地理位置出色的更多创始人中看到一个激增，因为初创公司因大流行和挥之不去的关切而失去了人们，加上遥远工作的吸引力？主要的捷克枢纽，布拉格和布尔诺，可能不会看到他们的地位削弱，因为它们不仅是商业中心，而且还有才华的主要大学，是许多人的文化生活中的心灵的心。但我们将看到遥远的扫荡的转变，让创始人挖掘更广泛的人才池。

Which industry segments that you invest in look weaker or more exposed to potential shifts in consumer and business behavior because of COVID-19? What are the opportunities startups may be able to tap into during these unprecedented times? We believe that after the shock caused by COVID-19 fades away, there will be more opportunities for the companies in segments we invest in, as the induced trends are only forcing businesses to run smarter. The trends most relevant to us will be those associated with accelerated digital transformation, changes in supply chains and evolution of workspaces.

哪些行业细分市场看起来越来越弱或更为暴露于由于Covid-19而导致消费者和商业行为的潜在转变？启动机会的机会是什么可以在这些前所未有的时间内进行挖掘？我们认为，在Covid-19逐渐消失后，在我们投资的段中将有更多机会，因为诱导趋势只是强迫企业越来越聪明的企业。与我们最相关的趋势将是与加速数字转型相关的趋势，供应链的变化和工作区的演变。

How has COVID-19 impacted your investment strategy? What are the biggest worries of the founders in your portfolio? What is your advice to startups in your portfolio right now? COVID-19 has not impacted our strategy. The only changes were on the tactical level, as for a certain period of time we shifted more capacities to portfolio support. Most of our founders had to deal with a negative impact on their sales funnel, as some customers postponed or cancelled the planned deals. Some of the founders had to deal with disruptions in the distribution channels, as some of their partners’ businesses were hit rather hard, and a small number of companies had to resolve issues with their supply chain. These challenges are still, to an extent, worries to our portfolio companies, as the economic development is still uncertain. To deal with the situation, cash flow became the main focus, together with more active communication with key business partners throughout the value chains.

Covid-19如何影响您的投资策略？您的投资组合中的创始人最大的担忧是什么？您现在在投资组合中启动的建议是什么？ Covid-19没有影响我们的战略。唯一的变化是在战术层面上，正如一定时间的时间，我们向投资组合支持转移了更多的能力。我们的大多数创始人必须处理对其销售漏斗的负面影响，因为一些客户推迟或取消计划优惠。一些创始人必须处理分销渠道中断，因为他们的一些合作伙伴的企业被击中了相当困难，并且少数公司不得不解决其供应链问题。随着经济发展仍然不确定，这些挑战仍然是对我们的投资组合企业的担忧。为了解决这种情况，现金流成为主要焦点，以及在整个价值链中与关键业务合作伙伴更积极的沟通。
