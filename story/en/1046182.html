The main application toolbar containsa dock for frequently accessed widget attributes and the icons for selectinggeometry management. Left to right: relief, border, anchor, pack side, foreground, background,font, font-size, font-style, text-alignment, grid manager, pack manager,and place manager. On the bottom from left to right are the status indicator,mode indicator and save status bar.

主应用程序工具栏包含一个用于频繁访问的小部件属性的停靠栏，以及用于选择几何管理的图标。从左到右：浮雕，边框，锚点，包边，前景，背景，字体，字体大小，字体样式，文本对齐，网格管理器，包管理器和位置管理器。从左到右的底部是状态指示灯，模式指示灯和保存状态栏。

The toolbar is contains icons for each supportedwidget. From here you can create toplevel        windows (that's a form for you VB people) and insert widgets. From leftto right and top to bottom: pointer for selecting widgets, toplevel, message,frame, canvas, button, entry, label, listbox, text, checkbutton, radiobutton,vertical scrollbar, horizontal scrollbar, vertical scale, horizontal scale,menubutton.

工具栏包含每个受支持小部件的图标。在这里您可以创建顶层窗口（这是VB人员的表单）并插入小部件。从左到右，从上到下：用于选择小部件的指针，顶层，消息，框架，画布，按钮，条目，标签，列表框，文本，复选按钮，单选按钮，垂直滚动条，水平滚动条，垂直比例尺，水平比例尺，菜单按钮。
