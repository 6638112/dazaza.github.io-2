Oil In the Ocean Photooxidizes Within Hours To Days, New Study Finds
海洋中的油在几小时内的海洋氧化，新的研究发现

2021-03-16 18:12:04

A new study led by scientists at the University of Miami (UM) Rosenstiel School of Marine and Atmospheric Science demonstrates that under realistic environmental conditions oil drifting in the ocean after the DWH oil spill photooxidized into persistent compounds within hours to days, instead over long periods of time as was thought during the 2010 Deepwater Horizon oil spill. This is the first model results to support the new paradigm of photooxidation that emerged from laboratory research. After an oil , oil droplets on the ocean surface can be transformed by a weathering process known as photooxidation, which results in the degradation of crude oil from exposure to light and oxygen into new by-products over time. Tar, a by-product of this weathering process, can remain in coastal areas for decades after a spill. Despite the significant consequences of this weathering pathway, photooxidation was not taken into account in oil spill models or the oil budget calculations during the Deepwater Horizon spill.

由迈阿密大学（UM）Rosenstiel School of Marine和大气科学学院的科学家领导的一项新的研究表明，在DWH油溢出在几小时内的持久化合物中的逼真环境条件下在海洋中漂移，而不是长时间在2010深水地平线漏油期间思考的时间。这是支持从实验室研究中出现的新的光氧化范式的第一个模型结果。在油后，海面上的油滴可以通过称为光氧化的耐候过程来改变，这导致原油从暴露于轻微和氧气流入新的副产物的原油。 Tar是这种风化过程的副产品，泄漏后几十年可以留在沿海地区。尽管这种耐候途径的显着后果，但在深水地平线泄漏期间，在漏油模型或石油预算计算中没有考虑光氧化。

The UM Rosenstiel School research team developed the first oil-spill model algorithm that tracks the dose of solar radiation oil droplets receive as they rise from the deep sea and are transported at the ocean surface. The authors found that the weathering of oil droplets by solar light occurred within hours to days, and that roughly 75 percent of the photooxidation during the Deepwater Horizon oil spill occurred on the same areas where chemical dispersants were sprayed from aircraft. Photooxidized oil is known to reduce the effectiveness of aerial dispersants.

UM Rosenstiel学校研究团队开发了第一款轨道，追踪太阳辐射油滴的剂量，因为它们从深海上升并在海洋表面运输。作者发现，太阳灯的油滴风化在几小时内发生，大约75％的深水地平线油泄漏期间的光氧化发生在与飞机上喷射的相同区域。已知光氧化油可降低空中分散剂的有效性。

"Understanding the timing and location of this weathering process is highly consequential. said Claire Paris, a UM Rosenstiel School faculty and senior author of the study. "It helps directing efforts and resources on fresh oil while avoiding stressing the environment with chemical dispersants on oil that cannot be dispersed."
