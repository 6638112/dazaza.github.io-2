Count FEFE is a free-to-play game for developing accounting and finance literacy. You take the role of Count or Countess FEFE having been left money by your late Uncle (Dr Acula) to set up and manage a start-up retail business selling upcycled musical coffins. You run your business over a series of simulated ‘game months’, responding to events, making business decisions, interpreting financial information and completing timed multiple choice quizzes. As you prove your accounting and finance literacy and your business successfully meets its financial objectives, you progress through to new levels, which unlock new business features, new financial data and new challenges. Eventually, if you successfully complete the challenge, your business will become listed on the Transylvania stock market. The FEFE game is designed to support students on introductory financial accounting courses in vocational and higher education contexts, but will also be relevant to anyone who wishes to improve their accounting and finance skills for business. The terminology and accounting rules within the game are consistent with those used in International Financial Reporting Standards (‘IFRS’). The FEFE Game is the main output of the ‘Financial Education for Future Entrepreneurs’ (‘FEFE’) project, led by Aston Business School and funded by the Erasmus+ programme of the European Union. For further information about the FEFE project and the ‘Top 10 Leader Boards’ for the FEFE game visit: http://fefeproject.eu. Languages supported: - English - German - Greek - Spanish
