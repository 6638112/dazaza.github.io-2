播放日期2020年10月更新

2020-10-31 05:22:37

Quick summary: a tough year, but our Season One games are locked, with more games than planned, the hardware is locked, the OS is almost there, and mass production for batch one starts next month.

快速总结：这是艰难的一年，但我们的第一季比赛已经锁定，比计划的要多，硬件锁定，操作系统几乎就绪，下个月开始批量生产第一批游戏。

Also, when big things happen, we’ll e-mail people on the Playdate Mailing List first, in signup order. So, if we may:

此外，当重大事件发生时，我们会按照注册顺序，首先给PlayDate邮件列表上的人发电子邮件。所以，如果我们可以的话：

Despite 2020 trying very very hard to defeat us — from our factory shutting down for many months, to all of us learning to work together from home — we’ve never stopped work on Playdate. And, at last, we’re seeing a light at the end of the bright yellow tunnel.

尽管2020年我们非常非常努力地试图击败我们-从我们的工厂关闭了几个月，到我们所有人都在家里学习一起工作-我们从未停止过在PlayDate上的工作。最后，我们在明亮的黄色隧道的尽头看到了一丝曙光。

And folks, it’s an amazing lineup. There’s bonus great news too: we used our extra time to commission even more Season One games from more developers. Soon, in a special video update, we’ll reveal the names of each of the Season One games! Please look forward to it.

各位，这是一个令人惊叹的阵容。还有一个额外的好消息：我们利用额外的时间从更多的开发商那里委托制作了更多的第一季游戏。很快，在一个特别的视频更新中，我们将揭晓第一季每一场比赛的名字！请期待。

As a reminder, Season One is included with your Playdate. There’s no extra charge.

提醒一下，第一季包含在您的游戏日期中。没有额外的费用。

We’ve fine-tuned the crank. We’ve tweaked the boards. We’ve addressed some potential quirks found in early Developer Preview units. Playdate is locked and ready for production. (Unless we change it again.)

我们已经对曲柄进行了微调。我们已经调整了棋盘。我们已经解决了在早期开发人员预览单元中发现的一些潜在的怪癖。播放日期已锁定，可以生产了。(除非我们再次更改。)。

Also, after three months of paperwork, many days of tenuous travel, and a two week mandatory quarantine, our USA-based manufacturing specialist Steven has just made it to Malaysia. Next week he’ll be at our factory to supervise mass production and make sure everything is perfect.

此外，经过三个月的文书工作，多天的疲惫旅行，以及两周的强制隔离，我们在美国的制造专家史蒂文刚刚抵达马来西亚。下周他将到我们工厂监督大批量生产，确保一切都完美无缺。

With both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth, Playdate had to be thoroughly tested to make sure it operates within radio limits. We’re relieved to note that as of July, Playdate is fully FCC certified (ID 2AR29-PDU01 for the curious) and is MIC certified for Japan and CE certified for Europe.

有了Wi-Fi和蓝牙，PlayDate必须经过彻底的测试，以确保它在无线电限制内运行。令我们欣慰的是，截至7月，PlayDate完全通过了FCC认证(对于好奇的人来说，ID为2AR29-PDU01)，并为日本获得了MIC认证，为欧洲获得了CE认证。

I don’t know if anyone particularly cares, but our Playdate packaging is done and printed. We’re really living it up, using both deboss and spot gloss on the cover!) Here it is:

我不知道是否有人特别在意，但是我们的PlayDate包装已经做好并打印好了。我们真的很享受，在封面上同时使用了DeBoss和Spot光泽！)。这里是：

For developers, we’ve been working non-stop on our SDK. This includes adding better collision detection, a web-based font making tool, faster Lua performance with Lua 5.4, and greater parity between our Lua and C APIs.

对于开发人员，我们一直在马不停蹄地开发我们的SDK。这包括添加更好的冲突检测、基于Web的字体制作工具、Lua 5.4更快的Lua性能，以及我们的Lua和CAPI之间更好的奇偶校验。

But, the wildest SDK news? We’ve made Panic’s first Windows app! In addition to the Mac, our Playdate Simulator is now up and running on both Windows and Linux.

但是，SDK最疯狂的消息是什么呢？我们制作了Panic的第一款Windows应用程序！除了Mac之外，我们的PlayDate模拟器现在可以在Windows和Linux上运行。

We still plan to release the SDK to the public, for free, in the future. Make sure you’re on our developer mailing list if interested.

我们仍然计划在未来免费向公众发布SDK。如果感兴趣，请确保您在我们的开发人员邮件列表中。

We delivered hundreds of Playdate units to developers of all skill levels all around the globe, from industry veterans to pixel artists writing their first lines of code. We also set up a Developer Forum for support and guidance, and a very fun developer Discord.

我们向全球所有技能水平的开发人员交付了数百个Playdate单元，从业界资深人士到编写第一行代码的像素艺术家。我们还设立了开发者论坛进行支持和指导，与开发者产生了非常有趣的不和。

Here’s a few lovely highlights from Developer Preview folks who were ready to share:

以下是准备分享的Developer Preview爱好者的几个可爱的亮点：

And remember, every Playdate is a devkit. You won’t need any special hardware if you want to make your own games! And third-party games can be directly installed on a Playdate. They won’t need to go through us.

记住，每一个玩伴都是一个开发工具包。如果你想制作自己的游戏，你不需要任何特殊的硬件！第三方游戏可以直接安装在PlayDate上。他们不需要通过我们。

Ok, that all sounds great, but we know you have just one question: when can you order?

好的，听起来不错，但是我们知道您只有一个问题：什么时候可以订货？

We don’t want to take anyone’s money until Playdate is 100% done and ready to go.

我们不想拿任何人的钱，直到Playdate 100%完成并准备好走。

But at the same time, we know many of you want your Playdate “locked in” and reserved, and you really don’t enjoy being in limbo. And that makes total sense.

但与此同时，我们知道你们中的许多人想要“锁定”和保留你们的PlayDate，而你们真的不喜欢处于不确定的状态。这完全说得通。

That’s not the plan. We don’t want Playdate to ever be “sold out”. The idea is to make Playdates in batches and do an open order — everybody who wants one can order one.

这不是我们的计划。我们不希望PlayDate永远“卖光”。这个想法是分批安排玩耍日期，并进行开放订单-每个想要一个的人都可以订购一个。

Then, your Playdate will arrive based on when you ordered, and what batch you’re in.

然后，您的Playdate将根据您订购的时间和您所处的批次到达。

If you change your mind, you'll be able to cancel your order at any time before it ships.

如果您改变主意，您可以在订单发货前随时取消订单。

Well, we’ve officially ordered 20,000 Playdates for batch one, which we think should be enough to meet initial demand. We hope. (We also pray we didn’t overshoot it wildly, because that would be pretty embarrassing. Anyway.)

嗯，我们已经正式订购了20,000个第一批的玩伴，我们认为这应该足以满足最初的需求。我们希望如此。(我们还祈祷我们没有过分夸张，因为那会相当尴尬。不管怎么说。)。

Beyond that first batch, our factory will be making Playdates every weekday. But they can only make so many each day.

除了第一批外，我们工厂每个工作日都会安排玩耍。但他们每天只能做这么多。

When you order, we’ll do our best to tell you what batch you’re in, and when your Playdate should arrive.

当您点餐时，我们会尽力告诉您您的批次，以及您的玩耍日期应该在什么时候到达。

We really mean it. Thanks for your patience and interest in Playdate. We’re so glad you’re still excited about it, because we are too! We know it’s never fun to wait, but your waiting is almost over.

我们是认真的。感谢您对PlayDate的耐心和兴趣。我们很高兴你还对此感到兴奋，因为我们也是！我们知道等待从来都不是一件有趣的事，但是你的等待就要结束了。

To know exactly when that’s happening — and when orders are starting — sign up!

要确切知道这是何时发生的-以及订单何时开始-请注册！
