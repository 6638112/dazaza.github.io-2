First 3D-Printed House Goes On Sale, Foreshadowing Faster, Cheaper Homebuilding
首批3D打印房屋发售，预示着更快，更便宜的房屋建造

2021-02-09 20:56:15

(CNN)You've probably heard of 3D printed face masks and even 3D printed hands, as 3D printing technology has expanded over the past few decades.

（CNN）您可能听说过3D打印面罩，甚至3D打印手，因为在过去的几十年中3D打印技术不断发展。

Now a company says it has listed the first 3D printed house in the United States for sale. The Riverhead, New York, home is listed online through Zillow with an asking price of $299,999.

现在，一家公司表示已在美国上市了首批3D打印房屋。纽约Riverhead的房屋通过Zillow在网上列出，要价为299,999美元。

"This is the future, there is no doubt about it," says Kirk Andersen, the director of operations at SQ4D Inc.

＆＃34;这是未来，毫无疑问，＆＃34; SQ4D Inc.的运营总监Kirk Andersen说。

"What we want to do is print homes fast, and cheap and strong," Andersen said.

＆＃34;我们要做的是快速，廉价，坚固地打印房屋，＆＃34;安徒生说。

The company can set up its Autonomous Robotic Construction System at a build site in six to eight hours. It then lays concrete layer by layer, creating footing, the foundation of a house and the interior and exterior walls of the structure.

该公司可以在六到八个小时内在建筑工地上建立其自主机器人构造系统。然后，它逐层铺设混凝土，以创建基础，房屋的基础以及结构的内外墙。

3D printed houses could also end up having a radical effect on the wallets of potential buyers in America.

3D打印房屋也可能最终对美国潜在买家的钱包产生根本性影响。

"The cost of construction is 50% cheaper than the cost of comparable newly-constructed homes in Riverhead, New York, and 10 times faster," said Stephen King, the Zillow Premier agent who has the 3D house listing.

＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;价比纽约Riverhead的类似新建房屋便宜50％，快10倍，拥有3D房屋列表的Zillow Premier经纪人Stephen King说。

The 3D printed house will include 1,407 square feet of living space and will be built with concrete. The home has three bedrooms, two baths and a 2.5-car detached garage.

3D打印房屋将包括1,407平方英尺的生活空间，并用混凝土建造。该房屋有三间卧室，两个浴室和一个2.5车位的独立车库。

"I want people to not be afraid of automation...it is just a different tool and different method. But it's still the same product; we are still building a house at the end of the day," Andersen says.

＆＃34;我希望人们不要害怕自动化...这只是一种不同的工具和不同的方法。但是它仍然是相同的产品；我们还是要在一天结束时盖房子，＆＃34;安徒生说。

An open house, held for the 3D home on Saturday, featured the company's already built demo home.

周六为3D家举行的开放日活动中，公司已经建造了演示房。

Andersen and King say the Riverhead home is the first 3D printed home to receive a certificate of occupancy in the United States. That wasn't easy because of strict local building codes, they said.

安德森（Andersen）和金（King）说，Riverhead房屋是美国第一个获得居住证明的3D打印房屋。他们说，由于严格的当地建筑法规，这并非易事。

"We did it in one of the hardest places and there's a beauty in that because that means we can eventually do it anywhere," Andersen said.

我们在最艰苦的地方之一做到了，这是一种美，因为那意味着我们最终可以在任何地方做到这一点，安徒生说。

"We can make things more affordable and safer. We can use the technology to tackle homelessness, and aid in disaster relief in an eco-friendly way," Andersen said.

＆＃34;我们可以使事情变得更实惠，更安全。我们可以使用该技术来解决无家可归问题，并以生态友好的方式帮助救灾，＆＃34;安徒生说。
