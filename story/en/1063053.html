Making a phone that avoids Big-Tech spying is one thing (yep, we did that). Making a convergent operating system that is not Android nor iOS is another (yep, we did that too). But manufacturing that phone in the USA is a feat that few thought possible (yep, did that just now). Not only have we proved it’s possible… it’s shipping.

One of the many industry-changing goals Purism had set—and now accomplished—was to manufacture our Librem 5 phone in the USA, including doing all electronics manufacturing in our facility (note: we have already been doing this for a few years with the Librem Key—Made in the USA). The Librem 5 USA is a revolutionary phone with immense differentiators compared with the rest of the mobile phone industry. Making the most secure phone demands being able to have full verification of all steps from releasing schematics, using Made in USA electronics, releasing all source code, isolating hardware components, and running an Operating System that is under the full control of the customer—that is not cryptographically forced into Big-Tech’s oppressive and exploitative control—The Librem 5 USA is all of those things.

众多不断变化的目标纯粹纯粹的纯粹是 - 现在已经完成 - 是在美国制造我们的图书馆5电话，包括在我们的工厂中进行所有电子制造（注意：我们已经在这件事上完成了这一点建筑师在美国钥匙制造）。与移动电话行业的其他部分相比，LibrM 5美国是一种革命性的差异，差异。使最安全的电话要求能够充分验证释放原理图的所有步骤，在美国电子产品中使用，释放所有源代码，隔离硬件组件，并运行在客户的完全控制下的操作系统 - 即没有加密地被迫进入大型技术的压迫和剥削控制 - 这位Librm 5美国都是这些东西。

As an Electronics Engineer (EE) once told me “hardware is a compile once language”, while this is a slight oversimplification since you can repair hardware (as explained below), his point is not lost: once produced, hardware is painfully expensive to change. Preparing, procuring, planning, and triple-checking parts is essential to making sure you are able to produce hardware that works as designed.

作为电子工程师（EE）曾经告诉过我“硬件是一份语言编译”，虽然这是一种轻微的过度简化，因为您可以修复硬件（如下所述），他的观点不会丢失：一旦生产，硬件疼痛昂贵改变。准备，采购，规划和三重检查部分对于确保您能够生产设计的硬件至关重要。

It was already a challenge to produce a secure mobile phone that has a fairly complex board with over 140 unique and over 1,000 total parts—and that was before the largest parts shortage scare the industry has ever seen. Procuring the exact right parts is a line-by-line, part-by-part, package-by-package, reel-by-reel process that is saved only for the most patient and meticulous.

生产一个安全的手机已经是一项挑战，该手机具有相当复杂的董事会，拥有超过140个独特，超过1000个零件 - 这是在最大的零件缺乏行业所看到的缺点之前。采购确切的右部是一个逐行，逐个逐个包装，逐个卷轴进程，只能为最患者和细致的速度保存。

Fun side story, one brand of 32GB eMMC has test pins on the underside that confuses the optical scanner for SMT parts placement, while another brand does not have the test pins. Since there is no easy way to mask the optical scanner of those test pins, a black permanent marker is a quick fix to blot them out so as not to confuse the scanner.

有趣的侧面故事，一个品牌的32GB emmc在下面有测试销，使光学扫描仪混淆了SMT部件放置，而另一个品牌没有测试引脚。由于没有简单的方法来掩盖那些试验销的光学扫描仪，因此黑色永久标记是快速修复，以将它们呈现出来，以免混淆扫描仪。

Another part of the parts story is replacing the End of Life (EOL) 3GB RAM for (the more expensive) 4GB RAM (that still represents itself as the 3GB max available from the CPU), these parts alternatives are entirely due to the Ball and Supply Chain shortages. Procuring enough safety stock to manufacture for the next year is critically important with the current uncertainties in the supply chain.

零件故事的另一部分是替换生命结束（EOL）3GB RAM（更昂贵的）4GB RAM（仍然代表自CPU中可获得的3GB最大值），这些部件替代方案完全是由于球和供应链短缺。利用足够的安全股票为明年制造，对供应链中当前的不确定性来说至关重要。

Once you believe to have all the parts triple-checked against your Bill Of Materials (BOM), the process of kitting can begin.

一旦你相信将所有零件都与您的材料账单（BOM）进行三核，就可以开始。

This probably varies a bit from outsourced CM to in-house manufacturing, in our in-house case we scan and load parts into feeders or trays for the (GNU/Linux based) SMT machines to import easily into its magazines. This kitting process is yet-another test against the BOM where upon entering into the database the parts get matched to orientation (pin 1) for the SMT head to place and inspect properly across the entire top (and then bottom) of the Printed Circuit Board (PCB).

这可能在外包厘米到内部制造中的速度变化，我们的内部案例我们扫描并将零件扫描到馈线或托盘中（基于GNU / Linux的）SMT机器，以便轻松进入其杂志。该套件过程尚不为BOM进行另一个测试，其中进入数据库时​​，部件与SMT头部的方向（引脚1）匹配，以便在印刷电路板的整个顶部（然后底部）上正确地检查。 （PCB）。

According to every single line operator I have met there has never been a PCB they have liked. You can hear the groan from across the room when they discover one smaller-than-desired solder pad. Fixing that means adjusting the pad itself or adjusting solder quantity to make sure the part gets proper connection without floating off the pad and wreaking havoc on neighboring parts.

根据我遇到的每一条线操作员，他们从未有过他们喜欢的PCB。当他们发现一个小于所需的焊盘时，您可以从房间中听到呻吟声。固定意味着调节垫本身或调节焊料量以确保零件得到适当的连接，而不会漂浮在垫上并在相邻部件上造成损坏。

In our case we use rapid development SMT which utilizes solder paste (think inkjet printer) to lay the paste onto the board before inspection.

在我们的情况下，我们使用快速开发SMT，该SMT利用焊膏（思考喷墨打印机）将粘贴放在电路板上。

Initially inspection is a combination of machine inspection and manual inspection. As volume increases, inspection is less manual inspection and more fully automated. The first panels are inspected under a microscope pad-by-pad on top and bottom of the board, this helps ensure all subsequent processes are done with minimal error.

最初检查是机器检查和手动检查的组合。随着体积的增加，检查较少手动检查，更全面的自动化。第一面板在主板顶部和底部的显微镜垫上检查，这有助于确保使用最小误差完成所有后续过程。

SMT is basically giant parts printing and the wow-factor of what these machines can do is short-lived, because having expert line operators makes it all seem pretty routine. The process from solder paste, to pick-and-place, to vapor phase (this is the process we utilize) means that most all of the excitement waits until when the boards are actually brought up after thorough inspection.

SMT基本上是巨大的零件印刷，而这些机器可以做的魔法因素是短暂的，因为拥有专家的线路运营商使其看起来很漂亮。来自焊膏的过程，挑选到蒸气阶段（这是我们利用的过程）意味着大多数兴奋等待直到彻底检查后电路板实际上提升。

Inspecting the first few panels and board manually (which is done before setting up Automated Optical Inspection (AOI)) helps confirm parts are on pads and there are no “tombstones” or “billboards” where parts lift off a pad or rotate on a pad. When these issues arise, it is important to hand fix them, as well as see if it was a solder issue (too much on one pad or too little on another) that can allow these parts to float in solder soup.

手动检查前几个面板和电路板（在设置自动光学检测（AOI）之前进行）有助于确认部件在垫上，没有“墓碑”或“广告牌”，其中部件抬起垫或旋转垫上旋转。当出现这些问题时，手部修复它们是重要的，以及看它是焊接问题（在一个垫上过多或在另一个垫上），可以让这些部件漂浮在焊锡汤中。

A common misconception around SMT and PCBA production is that a “failed” board is tossed/recycled (like printer paper where when you make a mistake, just print another); this is the polar opposite of what actually occurs. While the desire is to iterate and improve to have no SMT errors, the earliest of boards off a line have a lot of hand inspection and correction. Another misconception is that a machine placed part is in some way superior to the same connected part placed by hand. It’s not. It’s faster and more efficient, but hand (re)placement is equally stable. Electronics off a line are hand repaired more often than people understand. This only increases cost (labor and time), it doesn’t reduce reliability. After you inspect boards and repair obvious issues, it’s time to bring up the board.

围绕SMT和PCBA生产的常见误解是“失败”板被抛出/再循环（如打印机纸，当您犯错时，只打印另一个）;这与实际发生的极性相反。虽然欲望是迭代和改善没有SMT错误，但最早的董事会偏离一条线有很多手检测和纠正。另一个误解是机器放置部分以某种方式优于手动放置的相同的连接部件。不是。它更快，更高效，但手（RE）放置同样稳定。从一条线上的电子产品比人们更频繁地修复。这只会增加成本（劳动力和时间），它不会降低可靠性。检查董事会并修复明显的问题后，是时候提出董事会了。

Bringing up a board for the first time is exciting and nerve-racking: first put a fresh-out-of-the-oven PCBA into a test jig, make sure the pogo pins align and connect, current limit the power supply, and then take a deep breath of clean air before it may fill with blue smoke. The common debug cycles apply to board bring-up, it is a development environment, so produce only a few boards, and systematically go through every part and trace to make sure every single component acts as desired.

首次推出一个板是令人兴奋和勇气的：首先将一个清新的烤箱PCBA放入测试夹具中，确保Pogo引脚对齐和连接，电流限制电源，然后在可能填充蓝烟之前，深呼吸清洁空气。常见的调试周期适用于Board Grass-Up，它是一个开发环境，因此只生产几个板，并系统地通过每个部分和跟踪，以确保每个单个组件都根据需要。

Power on, if current is good proceed; in the case of the NXP CPU used in the Librem 5 booting over Serial Download Protocol (SDP) supports automated scripting to probe (bring-up) each part across the schematics to see how far you get before a failure (also referred to as issues to make them sound less severe).

打开电源，如果当前是好的继续;在Librem 5通过串行下载协议（SDP）上引导的NXP CPU的情况下，支持自动脚本以探测（调出）在该原理图中的每个部分，以查看您在失败之前获得多远（也称为问题让他们听起来不那么严厉）。

Let’s say hypothetically that a Texas-based Instrument and parts maker has a part, let’s say that part is something like a TPS65892 (revision AB), and like all parts that Electronics Engineers (EEs) select, needs to be kitted exactly to part number (TPS65892) and package (NFBGA 96). Normally parts vary by part number (I am pretty sure it’s why they’re called part numbers), but in rare instances (I can think of only one) that part is a completely different part if it is appended by what you normally would read as a revision number: TPS65892BB. In this example these are pin-matching parts that do completely different things and where all things work fine with the exception of charging the battery and providing USB connectivity. After a number of hours tracing schematics to board read values, this hunting manifested itself into a data sheet comparison where we learned these are unrelated parts. <insert parts procurement scramble />. After getting the correct part, the incorrect part is desoldered and correct part is soldered on to test. In this case all things test positive and we now are proud owners of a giant quantity of (incorrect) TPS65892BB chips that we get to worry about later.

假设基于德克萨斯州的仪器和零件制造商有一部分，让我们说部分是TPS65892（修订版AB），也是电子工程师（EES）选择的所有部分，需要准确地塑造部件号（TPS65892）和包装（NFBGA 96）。通常，部分因部分数量而异（我很确​​定是为什么他们被称为部件号的原因），但在罕见的情况下（我只想到一个）那部分是一个完全不同的部分，如果它通常会读取什么作为修订号：TPS65892BB。在此示例中，这些都是针对完全不同的东西的匹配部件，并且除了对电池充电并提供USB连接，所有内容都能正常工作。经过多个小时跟踪原理图到板读取值，此狩猎将自己表现为数据表比较，我们学到的这些是不相关的部分。 ＆lt;插入零件采购争夺/＆gt ;.在获得正确的部件后，不正确的部分是焊接和正确的部分焊接测试。在这种情况下，所有的东西都会测试正面，我们现在是我们担心的巨大数量的巨大数量，我们稍后担心。

Upon the first panel (in our case we have qty 4 boards per panel) passing through all tests, it’s time to move onto a quick assembly test of the first full Librem 5 USA phone.

在第一个面板（我们的案例中，我们每个面板的数量4个电路板）通过所有测试，是时候进入第一个完整的Sibrem 5美国手机的快速装配测试了。

Giving the green-light for a mass production feels extremely satisfying (due entirely to the expert staff Purism has that puts the mind at ease that mass production will yield functioning product). Machines begin to whir in a repetitive sounding motion that is remarkably comforting, only interrupted upon a reel running out alarm that creates a fluid and natural flow for the line operators to load more parts on. Parts are placed, panels are separated, through hole parts are added, and now we enter per-PCBA testing.

为批量生产提供绿灯感觉非常令人满意（完全归功于专家员工，这使得心灵放松批量生产将收益运作产品）。机器在重复的声音运动中开始垂直，显着舒适，只能在卷轴耗尽警报时中断，该报警为线路运营商创造流体和自然流动，以加载更多的部件。零件放置，面板分离，通过孔部件添加，现在我们进入每个PCBA测试。

Using the same tools during board bring up, the process is streamlined: note serial number label, provide power, script test all components work, log the results, flag errors for rework (rework and repeat), rack successes for assembly.

使用相同的工具在Board启动期间，该过程简化：注意序列号标签，提供电源，脚本测试所有组件的工作，记录结果，返工的返工错误错误（返工和重复），机架成功。

Almost everywhere assembly is done at separate facilities from manufacturing (this is typically called Contract Manufacturing (CM)), but at Purism we do it all from within our same facility, so we don’t have to package and ship, we just wheel the cart to the assembly team. Assembly initially is done by a manager doing all the steps, timing them, and iterating and improving. We not only assign stations to the tasks, we also happen to rotate staff stations to have full redundancy of all stations. Assembly of the Librem 5 USA is a meticulous process that includes a very specific order and tight tolerances. After a successful assembly the Librem 5 USA enters testing.

几乎到处都是组装在不同的设施中完成（通常称为合同制造（CM）），但在Purism中我们从我们的设施内完成所有，所以我们不必包装和船，我们只是轮子推车到装配团队。最初的组装是由经理完成所有步骤，定时和迭代和改进的。我们不仅为任务分配了站点，我们也恰好旋转员工的所有车站的完全冗余。 Librm 5 USA的组装是一种细致的过程，包括一个非常特定的顺序和狭义的公差。在成功装配后，Libre 5 USA进入测试。

Testing begins by flashing the PureOS testing image, conducting an interactive phone test to confirm all peripherals work (or get reworked, sometimes via re-assembly, sometimes by going back to PCBA testing (and iterating to improve the tests there)), upon successful testing it enters final quality control.

通过闪烁pureos测试图像开始，进行交互式电话测试以确认所有外围设备工作（或者有时通过重新装配，有时通过返回PCBA测试（并迭代以改善测试）），成功测试它输入最终质量控制。

The process of physical inspection and physical interaction with the device to make sure all things work properly, volume rocker buttons, power button, WiFi, sound, headphones, speaker, hardware kill switches, modem, SIM card, PGP smart card reader, battery, battery charging, cables, microphones, cameras, proximity sensor, haptic (vibrator) motor, touch, multi-touch, display, flashlight (torch), etc. are all manually tested before reflashing the customer-facing PureOS image for shipping.

物理检测和物理互动与器件，以确保所有内容正常工作，体积摇臂按钮，电源按钮，WiFi，声音，耳机，扬声器，硬件杀死交换机，调制解调器，SIM卡，PGP智能读卡器，电池，电池充电，电缆，麦克风，摄像机，接近传感器，触发器（振动器）电机，触摸，多点触控，显示器，手电筒（割炬）等都在刷新客户面向客户的运输之前手动测试。
