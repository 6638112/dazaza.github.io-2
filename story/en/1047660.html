The Mac OS That Can't Be Tweaked
无法调整的Mac OS

2021-02-10 03:18:39

View Slideshow For years, one of the big attractions of the Mac was the ability to customize the operating system. Users could completely overhaul the machine's interface, sometimes to the point where it was entirely idiosyncratic. But all that has changed with OS X. Apple is trying to close the operating system to tweakers, a policy some critics think will ultimately do more harm than good.

查看幻灯片放映多年来，Mac的一大亮点是自定义操作系统的能力。用户可以彻底检查机器的界面，有时甚至可以完全改变机器的界面。但是，随着OS X的改变，一切都改变了。苹果试图将操作系统关闭，让一些批评家认为，这样做最终弊大于利。

Before OS X, the Mac operating system was relatively open to interface manipulators: There were many open and well-documented APIs (Application Program Interfaces), which allowed people to change or enhance many aspects of the system's behavior.

在OS X之前，Mac操作系统对接口操纵者相对开放：有许多开放且有据可查的API（应用程序接口），使人们可以更改或增强系统行为的许多方面。

It went beyond adding desktop pictures of kittens. Programs such as Kaleidoscope, Apple's Appearance Manager and several third-party utilities afforded complete makeovers of the Mac interface.

它不仅可以添加小猫的桌面图片。诸如Kaleidoscope，Apple的Appearance Manager和一些第三方实用程序之类的程序对Mac界面进行了完整的改编。

Users could customize icons, change the shape of windows, add funky buttons and alter pulldown menus and window scroll bars. The result was thousands of custom, futuristic interfaces, some of which were truly alien.

用户可以自定义图标，更改窗口的形状，添加时髦的按钮以及更改下拉菜单和窗口滚动条。结果是成千上万个自定义的，未来派的界面，其中一些确实是陌生的。

Compare Gavin Robb's moody desktop with Evan Sharp's " Rusty Apple" or Christopher Moore's colorful sci-fi scheme.

将Gavin Robb喜怒无常的桌面与Evan Sharp的进行比较生锈的苹果或克里斯托弗·摩尔（Christopher Moore）丰富多彩的科幻计划。

In Mac OS X, however, most of the APIs that affect the interface do not allow third parties to gain access to them. Independent software developers have figured out some of them and have created dozens of utilities for customizing the interface, such as Conundrum Software's Duality or Panic's CandyBar. The interface can also be tweaked through Mac OS X's Unix command line.

但是，在Mac OS X中，大多数影响接口的API均不允许第三方访问它们。独立软件开发人员已经找出其中的一些，并创建了数十种用于自定义界面的实用程序，例如Conundrum Software的Duality或Panic的CandyBar。该界面也可以通过Mac OS X的Unix命令行进行调整。

But every time Apple updates the operating system, as it did recently with the Jaguar upgrade, many utilities are broken and developers have to upgrade their software.

但是，每次苹果更新操作系统时，就像最近进行的Jaguar升级一样，许多实用程序都被破坏了，开发人员必须升级他们的软件。

"It's the end of an era," said Greg Landweber, co-developer of Kaleidoscope, one of the most popular Mac customization tools ever created. "Under the old Mac system, doing these little interface tweaks was really easy. You could change almost anything. Now, you can't change the way they work, only their appearance."

这是一个时代的终结， Kaleidoscope的共同开发者Greg Landweber说，Kaleidoscope是有史以来最受欢迎的Mac定制工具之一。 ＆＃34;在旧的Mac系统下，进行这些小的界面调整非常容易。您几乎可以更改任何内容。现在，您无法更改它们的工作方式，只能更改其外观。

According to Raul Gutierrez, an avid Mac interface tweaker, the open architecture of the Mac actively encouraged GUI tweaking. The result wasn't just cosmetic, he said. Interface experimentation led to a lot of innovation that is taken for granted today.

根据狂热的Mac界面调整器Raul Gutierrez的说法，Mac的开放式体系结构积极鼓励GUI调整。他说，结果不仅是美容。界面实验带来了很多创新，而这些创新如今已被视为理所当然。

"Whereas Apple pioneered the completely customizable system, they are now headed in the other direction, trying to close most APIs that deal with the interface," Gutierrez said. "Apple seems to be regressing when it comes to control over the look and function of OS X."

＆＃34;尽管Apple率先推出了完全可定制的系统，但现在他们正朝着另一个方向发展，试图关闭大多数处理该接口的API，＆＃34;古铁雷斯说。 ＆＃34;在控制OS X的外观和功能时，Apple似乎正在退步。

By the late 1980s, there were all sorts of Mac GUI enhancements that users could make through shareware and commercial programs: hierarchical menus, tear off menus, enhancements to open/save dialog boxes, sets of interchangeable background patterns and improvements to navigation aids like scroll bars.

到1980年代后期，用户可以通过共享软件和商业程序对Mac GUI进行各种增强：分层菜单，删除菜单，增强的打开/保存对话框，可互换的背景图案集以及对导航辅助工具（如滚动）的改进酒吧。

Apple took the best ideas and added them to the Mac OS. Slowly but surely, dialog boxes were improved, menus were enhanced and windows customized. The result was a mature, full-featured and flexible operating system that became the most influential in the industry.

苹果公司采纳了最佳创意，并将其添加到Mac OS中。可以肯定但缓慢地改进了对话框，增强了菜单并自定义了窗口。结果是一个成熟，功能齐全且灵活的操作系统，成为业界最具影响力的操作系统。

In 1998, Apple showed off its own software for customizing the windowing system called the Appearance Manager. The Appearance Manager even had a "scheme to theme" feature, which translated Kaleidoscope schemes into Mac "themes." Aqua, the much-lauded look and feel of OS X, is essentially an updated OS 9 theme.

1998年，Apple展示了自己的用于自定义窗口系统的软件，称为外观管理器。外观管理器甚至有一个主题主题计划。功能，将万花筒方案转换为Mac＆＃34;主题。 Aqua是OS X备受赞誉的外观，实际上是OS 9主题的更新。

But shortly after the Appearance Manager was introduced, Steve Jobs returned to Apple and killed it. Jobs was asked several times about reviving themes, but always responded, "Themes are dead."

但是在引入外观经理之后不久，乔布斯就回到了苹果公司并杀死了它。有人问乔布斯关于恢复主题，但总是回答说，主题已经死了。

That philosophy has persisted in OS X. The APIs in Mac OS X that control the placement, function and look of windows and menus are closed. Third-party software developers are busy reverse-engineering the system and have had some success, but the ability to fundamentally alter OS X's look and feel has yet to be cracked.

这种理念一直存在于OS X中。MacOS X中用于控制窗口和菜单的放置，功能以及外观的API已关闭。第三方软件开发人员正忙于对系统进行逆向工程并取得了一些成功，但是从根本上改变OS X外观的能力尚未被破解。

Oddly, Apple allows some parts of Mac OS X system to be themed. The look of the QuickTime Player, for example, can be altered, but only by media with a theme embedded in the QuickTime file. This allows corporations to create themes for the media player, but locks out the little guy.

奇怪的是，Apple允许以Mac OS X系统的某些部分为主题。例如，可以更改QuickTime Player的外观，但只能通过在QuickTime文件中嵌入主题的媒体来更改。这允许公司为媒体播放器创建主题，但将小家伙拒之门外。

To the chagrin of users, a lot of features that were standard in previous versions of the Mac OS, and on other platforms, are missing from OS X. Even basic things like changing the system font, or turning off on-screen font smoothing -- a resource hog on older machines -- can't be done.

令用户感到恼火的是，OS X缺少了Mac OS早期版本和其他平台上的许多标准功能。即使是诸如更改系统字体或关闭屏幕上的字体平滑之类的基本操作， -在较旧的机器上消耗资源-无法完成。

When Apple was publicly beta-testing OS X, many users found the transition to its radical new interface jarring. Users clamored for old interface features like collapsible windows and spring-loaded folders. The OS was, and still is, a work in progress.

当苹果公司公开测试OS X时，许多用户发现其向新的界面颠覆性过渡。用户大声疾呼使用旧的界面功能，例如可折叠的窗口和弹簧式文件夹。该操作系统过去仍在进行中。

But because third-party developers are tapping into non-public APIs, Apple has no obligation to maintain access. Every time Apple updates OS X, the software often ceases to function.

但是，由于第三方开发人员正在利用非公共API，因此Apple没有义务维护访问权限。每次Apple更新OS X时，该软件通常都会停止运行。

And because the APIs are closed, hackers have to go to great lengths to get their tweaks to work. Responding to the outcry over the removal of the Happy Mac icon from the Mac boot sequence, Fishback Research created a utility for putting it back in. But to do so, their programmers had to delve into Darwin, the open-source version of OS X, to figure out how to do it.

而且由于API是封闭的，因此黑客必须竭尽全力来进行调整。出于对从Mac启动序列中删除Happy Mac图标的强烈抗议，Fishback Research创建了一个实用程序来重新安装它。但是，为此，他们的程序员不得不研究OS X的开源版本Darwin。 ，找出解决方法。

Even users are legally prohibited from making changes to OS X: The license agreement basically says Apple owns the interface and users aren't allowed to mess with it.

法律上甚至禁止用户对OS X进行更改：许可协议基本上说，苹果拥有该界面，并且不允许用户使用该界面。

"Apple is uptight about (changes to the interface)," said Brian Wilson, business manager at Unsanity, which has created a number of OS X interface utilities. "But at the same time they haven't given us any grief. We've had neither help nor hassle."

＆＃34; Apple对（更改界面）保持严格态度，＆＃34; Unsanity业务经理Brian Wilson说，该公司创建了许多OS X接口实用程序。 ＆＃34;但与此同时，他们并没有给我们带来任何悲伤。我们既没有帮助也没有麻烦。

Apple's lawyers may turn the other cheek, but its engineers have taken a more active approach. To prevent interface changes in OS 10.2, known as Jaguar, the software prevents programs from taking up certain bits of screen real estate. For example, the API that allows for custom menus and icons on the right side of the top menu bar, next to the clock, prohibits all but Apple-approved menu items.

苹果的律师可能会另辟che径，但苹果的工程师采取了更加积极的态度。为了防止OS 10.2（称为Jaguar）中的界面更改，该软件阻止程序占用屏幕上的某些空间。例如，时钟旁边的顶部菜单栏右侧允许自定义菜单和图标的API禁止除Apple批准的菜单项之外的所有菜单项。

Before 10.2, the API had been reverse engineered and was being widely used by shareware developers. WeatherPop, for example, used it to show the current weather, while Homeland Alert shows the U.S. government's level of terrorist alert. These utilities were broken by the Jaguar update. Unsanity recently released a utility, Menu Extra Enabler, to restore them.

在10.2之前，API已经过反向工程，并且被共享软件开发人员广泛使用。例如，WeatherPop使用它来显示当前天气，而“国土警报”则显示美国政府的恐怖警报级别。这些实用程序被Jaguar更新所破坏。 Unsanity最近发布了实用程序Menu Extra Enabler，用于还原它们。

In its defense, Apple has good reasons for preventing interface hacks. One of the major selling points of OS X is its stability, and changes to the underlying system undermine that. Previous versions of the Mac OS could be enormously flaky thanks to extensions that altered basic operating system behavior.

在辩护中，Apple有充分的理由防止界面被黑客入侵。 OS X的主要卖点之一是它的稳定性，而对基础系统的更改破坏了它的稳定性。由于扩展程序改变了基本的操作系统行为，因此Mac OS的早期版本可能会非常脆弱。

The look and feel of Aqua is also a marketing tool, and Apple, somewhat justifiably, wants its distinctive "brand" to remain untarnished.

Aqua的外观和感觉也是一种营销工具，苹果在某种程度上有理由要求其独特的＆brand;品牌。保持光泽。

Support issues have surfaced as well -- technicians on the phone can't advise a user to open a window by clicking a button on the left if the button has been moved to the right.

支持问题也已浮出水面-手机上的技术人员不能建议用户通过单击左侧的按钮（如果按钮已移至右侧）来打开窗口。

Apple has responded to users' feedback. Slowly, the company is adding missing features, like spring-loaded folders, and controls for customizing Aqua's look and feel. An option to make Aqua monochrome was added following complaints from graphic designers that the bright, colorful interface interfered with their color perception.

苹果已经回应了用户反馈。慢慢地，该公司正在添加缺少的功能，例如弹簧加载的文件夹以及用于自定义Aqua外观的控件。平面设计师抱怨明亮多彩的界面干扰了他们的色彩感知之后，又增加了使Aqua单色的选项。

Lloyd Wood, an amateur interface historian who runs Kaleidoscope Way, a site devoted to Kaleidoscope culture, said Apple has the manpower to undertake big projects like OS X, but lacks the resources to really make it sparkle.

业余界面历史学家劳埃德·伍德（Lloyd Wood）经营着致力于万花筒文化的网站万花筒之路（Kaleidoscope Way），他说苹果有人力进行OS X等大型项目，但缺乏真正使之发光的资源。

"(Apple) can't add all the small details that make their operating systems compelling and comfortable," said Wood. "Only users can add and polish those small details, and small developers who are experienced users have more chance of getting the little touches right."

（Apple）无法添加所有使其操作系统引人注目和舒适的小细节，＆＃34;伍德说。 ＆＃34;只有用户可以添加和修饰这些小细节，而经验丰富的用户的小型开发人员则更有机会正确地进行小改动。

Wood said that even after three years of development, OS X is still an "empty shell."

伍德说，即使经过三年的开发，OS X仍然是一个空的外壳。

"It's going to take a few more years of Apple rolling in the best ideas users have expressed in add-ons to take the rough edges off and make OS X seem like home," he said. "Apple is shooting itself in the foot. They need all the developers they can get."

苹果要花几年的时间才能推出用户在附加组件中表达的最佳创意，以消除粗糙的边缘并使OS X像家一样。他说。 ＆＃34; Apple正在脚下射击。他们需要所有可以得到的开发人员。＆＃34;

Ryan Cabell of Fishback Research said several developers are perfectly capable of creating utilities for tweaking the interface without making the system unstable.

Fishback Research的Ryan Cabell说，几个开发人员完全有能力创建用于调整接口的实用程序，而不会导致系统不稳定。

"I think it's important for Apple to try to maintain their brand identity and stability claims, but people have always seen UI tweaks as part of making the Mac a truly personal computer and so these sorts of hacks will be around forever," he said.

我认为对于苹果公司来说，保持其品牌标识和稳定性要求很重要，但是人们一直将UI调整视为使Mac成为真正的个人计算机的一部分，因此，这类黑客攻击将成为永远围绕，＆＃34;他说。

Gutierrez noted that a good measure of Aqua's failings are the number of third-party programs that address certain issues, like the Dock. There are about half a dozen Dock replacements.

Gutierrez指出，Aqua失败的一个很好的衡量标准是解决某些问题（例如Dock）的第三方程序的数量。大约有六种Dock替代品。

"The Dock doesn't scale well for pro users," he said. "If enough people are annoyed by something, someone will figure out how to fix it, whether is sanctioned by Apple or not."

＆＃34; Dock对于专业用户而言扩展性不强，＆＃34; 他说。 ＆＃34;如果有足够多的人对某事感到厌烦，那么有人会想出如何解决该问题的方法，无论是否受到Apple的认可。
