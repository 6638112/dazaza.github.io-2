DJI推出了售价449美元的Mini 2，配备了升级的摄像头，可进行4K 30fps的录制；OcuSync 2.0，具有10公里的视频传输范围，声称飞行时间为31分钟

2020-11-05 20:34:00

“Don't let its small size fool you because the DJI Mini 2 flies confidently and features 4K video recording.”

“不要让它的小巧欺骗了你，因为DJI Mini 2飞行起来很自信，而且有4K的视频录制功能。”

I thought DJI was done with announcements for the year. After the launch of the DJI Mavic Air 2 and Pocket 2, it seemed unlikely that we would hear anything else from the drone maker for 2020, but it turns out that it has one last exciting product for consumers before the holidays. And yes, it’s another drone!

我以为大疆已经不再发布今年的公告了。在DJI Mavic Air 2和Pocket 2发布后，我们似乎不太可能在2020年听到这家无人机制造商的其他消息，但事实证明，在假期之前，它还有最后一款令消费者兴奋的产品。是的，这是另一架无人机！

Drones don’t typically receive yearly refreshes, which is exactly why the DJI Mini 2’s announcement comes as a surprise. The lightweight drone continues to fly under the Federal Aviation Administration’s (FAA) radar because of its 249-gram weight, making it ideal for first timers because it doesn’t require registration. More importantly, though, it squarely addresses some of the shortcomings of its predecessor.

无人机通常不会每年更新一次，这就是为什么大疆Mini 2的声明令人惊讶的原因。由于重量249克，这架轻型无人机继续在美国联邦航空管理局(FAA)的雷达下飞行，这使它成为初学者的理想之选，因为它不需要注册。不过，更重要的是，它直截了当地解决了前身的一些缺点。

In an age where 4K video recording is standard among smartphones and cameras, it was a bit of a letdown that last year’s Mavic Mini topped out at 2.7K resolution. Well, that’s the first item on the list addressed by the Mini 2, which boasts proper 4K video capture. While the frame rate at 4K tops out at 30 frames per second (FPS), DJI has bumped up the bit rate from 40 Mbps on the Mavic Mini to 100 Mbps for the Mini 2.

在4K视频录制在智能手机和相机中都是标配的时代，去年的Mavic Mini达到了2.7k的最高分辨率，这让人有点失望。嗯，这是Mini 2解决的第一个问题，它号称有合适的4K视频捕捉功能。虽然4K的帧速率最高可达每秒30帧，但大疆已经将Mavic Mini的比特率从40 Mbps提高到了Mini 2的100 Mbps。

What does it all mean? I’m particularly pleased with the extra resolution, since it offers some additional wiggle room in post for me — such as being able to crop in and out of a scene when I export it in 1080p. Meanwhile, for those who are watching videos and clips on 4K screens, the 4K resolution means that there’s no upscaling necessary this time around.

这一切意味着什么？我对额外的分辨率特别满意，因为它在POST中为我提供了一些额外的回旋余地--比如当我以1080p格式输出时，可以切换场景。与此同时，对于那些在4K屏幕上观看视频和剪辑的人来说，4K分辨率意味着这一次没有必要进行升级。

Details are expectedly plentiful and crisp, but the best results are found during the daytime and afternoon when lighting is abundant. Colors have an even, neutral tone to them — never too saturated to work with in post. However, its performance crumbles as soon as the sun goes down, often appearing murky, dark, and unusable unless there are strong sources of light in the scene. That’s partly because there’s no change with the sensor, a 12-megapixel 1/2.3-inch sensor perched on a 3-axis gimbal.

细节出人意料地丰富和清爽，但最好的效果出现在白天和下午光线充足的时候。颜色具有均匀、中性的色调-不会太饱和而不能在开机自检时使用。然而，太阳一落山，它的性能就会崩溃，除非场景中有很强的光源，否则通常会显得昏暗、黑暗和不可用。这在一定程度上是因为传感器没有变化，一个1200万像素的1/2.3英寸传感器安装在一个3轴万向节上。

Dynamic range performance isn’t close to the level I got with the Mavic Air 2, but some tweaking in post can draw out details that are otherwise lost in the shadows. Photographers will appreciate the Mini 2’s still-capture performance, but just like video, it struggles under low light and dynamic range.

动态范围的性能没有接近我在Mavic Air 2上得到的水平，但在POST中进行一些调整可以绘制出一些细节，否则这些细节就会消失在阴影中。摄影师会欣赏Mini 2的静态捕捉性能，但就像视频一样，它在微光和动态范围内也很挣扎。

If you’ve ever piloted the Mavic Mini, you know how video transmission can be susceptible to interference. It’s annoying for sure, but also scary if it’s prolonged. Luckily, the DJI Mini 2 eases concerns thanks to DJI’s OcuSync 2.0 transmission technology. The controller is an exact facsimile of the Mavic Air 2’s. It’s larger in size, but I like how my smartphone is gripped on top of the controller — as opposed to the bottom with the Mavic Mini’s controller.

如果你曾经驾驶过Mavic Mini，你就会知道视频传输是如何容易受到干扰的。这当然很烦人，但如果持续很长时间，也会让人害怕。幸运的是，由于大疆的OcuSync 2.0传输技术，大疆Mini 2缓解了人们的担忧。这款控制器和Mavic Air 2的一模一样。它的尺寸要大一些，但我喜欢我的智能手机紧贴在控制器上的方式--而不是Mavic Mini的控制器在底部。

With OcuSync 2.0, there’s more reliability with the connectivity between the drone and controller. Live video, in fact, rarely stuttered during my flights. And while I did manage to push its distance to over 1,000 feet from my location, DJI says that it’s capable of reaching a 10 kilometer range (6.2 miles). There’s nothing worse than latency issues whenever I’m piloting a drone, so it’s assuring that OcuSync 2.0 provides me with more responsive controls on the Mini 2.

有了OcuSync 2.0，无人机和控制器之间的连接更加可靠。事实上，在我的飞行过程中，直播视频几乎不会卡顿。虽然我确实设法将它的距离推到了离我所在位置1000英尺以上，但大疆表示，它的射程可以达到10公里(6.2英里)。每当我驾驶无人机时，没有什么比延迟问题更糟糕的了，所以OcuSync 2.0为我提供了更灵敏的Mini 2控制，这是有保证的。

One of my biggest fears about flying the Mavic Mini is pushing its range farther away because of the potential of interference with its video transmission. There’s none of that here with the Mini 2.

我对驾驶Mavic Mini最大的担忧之一是将它的航程推得更远，因为它的视频传输可能会受到干扰。而Mini 2则不具备这些功能。

Flying under windy conditions is always risky, but it’s more profound when you’re dealing with a lightweight drone like the Mavic Mini. I can’t tell you how many times I’ve aborted flying it because of windy conditions. There are also several videos online of owners reacting to it floating away from their location because of strong winds. Even though I’m still cautious, the DJI Mini 2 handles the wind a lot better.

在有风的条件下飞行总是有风险的，但当你面对像Mavic Mini这样的轻型无人机时，风险就更大了。我数不清有多少次因为刮风而取消飞行。网上也有几个视频显示，业主们对此做出了反应，因为大风使他们漂离了自己所在的位置。尽管我仍然很谨慎，但DJI Mini 2对风的驾驭能力要好得多。

On the first day of flying the Mini 2, the Jersey Shore was being blasted by 17 mph winds. Thankfully, DJI upgraded the motors in the Mini 2 to better withstand winds up to 24 mph. I’ve always aborted flights with the Mavic Mini in the past whenever winds reached 12 mph. In the air, it’s clear that the Mini 2 was working hard to maintain stability, and while it looked like it was rattling around, the video footage remained buttery smooth.

在驾驶Mini 2的第一天，泽西海岸就遭遇了时速17英里的大风。值得庆幸的是，大疆升级了Mini 2的发动机，以更好地抵御时速高达24英里的风。过去，每当风速达到每小时12英里时，我都会取消乘坐Mavic Mini的航班。在空中，很明显Mini 2正在努力保持稳定，虽然看起来它像是在嘎嘎作响，但视频片段仍然保持着黄油般的流畅。

In truly testing out its performance, I decided to see how it handles flying against the wind. Shockingly enough, it performed like a champ — even in normal mode. Switching over to sport mode increases the power, which allowed it to cut through the wind with ease. Its top speed still peaks at about 14 mph, but the acceleration from a hovering position is evident that the motors are stronger.

为了真正测试它的性能，我决定看看它是如何应对逆风飞行的。令人震惊的是，它的表现就像冠军一样--即使在正常模式下也是如此。切换到运动模式增加了功率，这使得它可以轻松地切断风力。它的最高时速仍然达到每小时14英里左右，但从悬停位置的加速度可以明显看出马达的动力更强了。

Are you worried that these upgraded motors will yield a shorter flight time? You don’t need to worry because its battery life has been increased to 31 minutes, which is a minute better than its predecessor. Unfortunately, the Mini 2 uses a different kind of battery, so you won’t be able to use existing packs you may have from the Mavic Mini.

你担心这些升级后的发动机会缩短飞行时间吗？你不必担心，因为它的电池续航时间增加到了31分钟，比之前的版本长了一分钟。不幸的是，Mini 2使用了不同类型的电池，所以你将无法使用Mavic Mini的现有电池包。

I have to confess that I’ve been spoiled by the Mavic Air 2, specifically its enhanced HDR capture, 4K 60 FPS capture, 8K hyperlapse, and asteroid quickshot mode. To be fair, the Mini 2 does offer some neat cinematic shots that autonomously pilots the drone for me — like dronie, boomerang, and circle quickshot modes. However, the single area that’s still greatly lacking with the Mini 2 is obstacle avoidance. The Mavic Air 2 features various sensors on its body to detect obstacles, so it can stop midflight and try to avoid them. You get none of that with the Mini 2, which means that I need to be cognizant of my surroundings at all times — plus that required line of sight distance.

我不得不承认，我被Mavic Air 2宠坏了，特别是它增强的HDR捕捉、4K 60FPS捕捉、8K Hyperapse和小行星快拍模式。公平地说，Mini 2确实提供了一些巧妙的电影镜头，可以为我自动驾驶无人机-比如无人机、回旋镖和圆形快拍模式。然而，Mini 2还有一个很大的不足之处，那就是避障。Mavic Air 2在其身上安装了各种传感器来探测障碍物，因此它可以在飞行中停止并试图避开它们。Mini 2不具备这一点，这意味着我需要时刻了解周围环境--外加所需的视线距离。

There’s also no automatic tracking mode that would allow the Mini 2 to follow me, so manual piloting with the controller is still the main way of capturing footage. It’s not a terrible thing, but when you’ve been spoiled by the Mavic Air 2’s Active Track 3.0 system, it’s something I wouldn’t mind having to follow me while biking around a lake, without that fear of it colliding with something.

此外，Mini 2也没有自动跟踪模式可以跟踪我，所以用控制器手动驾驶仍然是拍摄镜头的主要方式。这不是一件可怕的事情，但当你被Mavic Air 2的Active Track 3.0系统宠坏的时候，我不介意在湖边骑车时跟着我，而不用担心它会撞到什么东西。

Also, there were a couple of times when the DJI Fly app crashed on me while flying the drone. I suspect it’s because it’s not final software, but luckily I restarted it and got back to where I left off.

此外，在驾驶无人机时，DJI Fly应用程序也有几次在我身上崩溃。我怀疑这是因为它不是最终的软件，但幸运的是我重新启动了它，并回到了我停止的地方。

There are many reasons to pick up the DJI Mini 2, especially if you’re a first-time drone flyer or an enthusiast. Sporting a price tag of $449, it’s an easy recommendation over the $400 Mavic Mini. I’d recommend picking up the $599 Fly More Combo instead because it comes with a few goodies that make it a sound package.

选择DJI Mini 2的理由有很多，特别是如果你是第一次使用无人机或者是一名爱好者。它标价449美元，与400美元的Mavic Mini相比，这是一个简单的推荐。我建议选择售价599美元的Fly More Combo，因为它附带了一些好东西，使它成为一个完美的套餐。

Not only does it come with three batteries, a charging hub, and extra propellers, but I prefer it over the Fly More Combo that included last year’s Mavic Mini because it comes with a proper carrying case (shoulder strap included) and a propeller guard that wraps around the drone.

它不仅配备了三节电池、一个充电集线器和额外的螺旋桨，而且我更喜欢它而不是Fly More Combo，后者包括去年的Mavic Mini，因为它有一个合适的手提箱(包括肩带)和一个环绕无人机的螺旋桨护罩。

Is the Mini 2 perfect? Not quite, but it’s still an amazing, tiny drone that doesn’t require FAA registration because of its 249-gram weight. The addition of 4K recording makes it a competent player in the space, but what truly makes this offering substantially superior to its predecessor is the confidence instilled in flying it — thanks to OcuSync 2.0 and the upgraded motors. Flying with confidence is always a good thing, and the Mini 2 assures me of that with its responsiveness.

Mini 2完美吗？不完全是，但它仍然是一架令人惊叹的微型无人机，因为它的重量为249克，不需要联邦航空局注册。4K录音的加入使其成为该领域的一名称职玩家，但真正使这款产品大大优于其前身的是对驾驶它的信心-这要归功于OcuSync 2.0和升级的发动机。满怀信心地飞行总是一件好事，Mini 2的响应性让我确信了这一点。

The same all-plastic construction is still evident here, which certainly makes it feel a bit on the fragile side. I don’t expect it to handle crashes all that well, so flying cautiously is imperative. You’ll also want to be extra cautious packing it up for travel because of its fragile feel.

同样的全塑料结构在这里仍然很明显，这肯定让它感觉有点脆弱。我不指望它能很好地处理坠机事件，所以谨慎飞行势在必行。你还需要格外小心地打包旅行，因为它的手感很脆弱。

You can purchase a DJI Care Refresh with the Mini 2 at a cos. It provides up to two replacement units within one year due to water damage and collisions.

你可以以低价购买一台带有Mini 2的大疆护理更新。由于水损坏和碰撞，它在一年内提供最多两个更换装置。

At this price point? Not really. But, if you’re willing to spend more, the DJI Mavic Air 2 is the all-around drone for all levels thanks to its safety features, superior photo/video performance, and advanced tracking system. But you’re paying nearly double at $799 to pick it up.

在这个价位？不怎么有意思。但是，如果你愿意花更多的钱，DJI Mavic Air 2是所有级别的全能无人机，这要归功于它的安全功能、卓越的照片/视频性能和先进的跟踪系统。但你花了将近双倍的799美元才能拿到它。

Yes. It’s a downright bargain given how easy it is to pilot, as well as the 4K video recording, cinematic quickshot options, and compact size. Unlike before, I have more confidence flying it because of OcuSync 2.0 and its upgraded motors. It’s the ideal starter drone for those looking to step up their game beyond flying for fun.

是。考虑到它是多么容易驾驶，以及4K视频录制，电影快速拍摄选项，以及紧凑的尺寸，这是一个彻头彻尾的便宜货。与以前不同的是，由于OcuSync 2.0及其升级的发动机，我对驾驶它更有信心了。对于那些想要提升自己的游戏水平而不是为了好玩而飞行的人来说，这是一款理想的首发无人机。
