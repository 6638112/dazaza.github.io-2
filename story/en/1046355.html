Apple reports double-digit sales booms for every product category in Q1 2021
苹果公司报告2021年第一季度每种产品类别的销售均实现两位数增长

2021-01-28 23:09:44

The last 24 hours have been flooded with stock market news, from the normal to the nutty. But based on Apple's performance in recent quarters, the earnings the Cupertino company reported today for its first quarter of 2021 were very much on the normal side. And by that, we mean big numbers yet again.

从正常到疯狂，过去的24小时充斥着股市新闻。但根据苹果最近几个季度的表现，库比蒂诺公司今天报告的2021年第一季度收益与正常情况相当。到那时，我们又意味着大量数字。

According to the report, Apple crossed the threshold for $100 billion in revenue in a single quarter for the first time, and the company posted double-digit sales increases for every single one of its defined product categories. Overall, sales were up 21 percent year over year, despite many consumers' struggles in the pandemic-stricken economy. iPhone revenue was $65.6 billion, surpassing analysts' expected $59.8 billion and beating the same quarter last year by 17 percent. This coincides with the introduction of the iPhone 12 lineup (iPhone 12, iPhone 12 mini, iPhone 12 Pro, and iPhone 12 Pro Max), which was the most substantial redesign and upgrade to iPhones since the iPhone X three years earlier.

根据这份报告，苹果公司单个季度的收入首次超过了1000亿美元的门槛，并且该公司公布其定义的产品类别中的每一个产品销售额均实现两位数增长。总体而言，尽管有许多消费者，销售额还是同比增长了21％。在大流行的经济中挣扎。 iPhone收入为656亿美元，超过分析师预计将达到598亿美元，比去年同期增长17％。这与iPhone 12阵容（iPhone 12，iPhone 12 mini，iPhone 12 Pro和iPhone 12 Pro Max）的推出相吻合，这是自三年前iPhone X以来最重大的重新设计和升级到iPhone。

While the iPhone is Apple's big-ticket item, that product's growth was actually modest compared to a couple of others. The iPad's sales were up 41 percent year over year, reaching $8.44 billion. This was likely driven in part by the launch of the redesigned iPad Air.

尽管iPhone是苹果的头号产品，但与其他几款产品相比，该产品的增长实际上并不大。 iPad的销售额同比增长41％，达到84.4亿美元。这可能部分是由于重新设计的iPad Air的推出。

And services—that business segment that Apple executives and investors are always looking to as the obvious growing revenue source—were up 24 percent year over year. This quarter included the launch of Apple Fitness+, but otherwise, this growth appears to have been driven by existing services like Apple Music, Apple TV+, or Apple Arcade.

服务（苹果高管和投资者一直希望将其视为明显增长的收入来源）的业务部门同比增长了24％。本季度包括Apple Fitness +的发布，但除此之外，这一增长似乎是由现有服务（如Apple Music，Apple TV +或Apple Arcade）推动的。

Not all of them grew in equal measure, though: an Apple spokesperson said on the investor call that AppleCare has grown slower than other services because of its dependence on retail stores to provide its full value to Apple's customers.

不过，并不是所有这些产品的增长都相等：苹果公司的一位发言人在投资者电话会议上说，AppleCare的增长速度比其他服务要慢，因为它依赖零售店为苹果客户提供全部价值。

The Mac also rose—21 percent in this case, up to $8.68 billion. (This quarter included the launch of the M1 Macs.) And a catch-all for all other products, of which wearables like the Apple Watch and AirPods make up a significant part, grew 29 percent year over year to $12.97 billion.

Mac也上涨了21％，达到86.8亿美元。 （本季度包括M1 Mac的发布。）所有其他产品的通货膨胀，其中诸如Apple Watch和AirPods等可穿戴设备占很大比重，同比增长29％，达到129.7亿美元。

CEO Tim Cook told investors on the call that the total install base for iPhones has risen to 1 billion, up from the 900 million he stated last time that metric was reported. Total active install base for all Apple products was 1.65 billion—so yes, the iPhone is still Apple's dominant product and the one that forms the foundation for many of the services and wearables the company has introduced over the past few years. These positive numbers follow a two-year rally that itself followed some investor skepticism about declining iPhone sales.

首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）在电话会议上告诉投资者，iPhone的总安装量已从他上次报告该指标时的9亿增加到10亿。苹果所有产品的有效安装总数为16.5亿个-是的，iPhone仍然是苹果的主导产品，并且构成了该公司在过去几年中推出的许多服务和可穿戴设备的基础。这些积极的数字是在连续两年的上涨之后，其本身受到了一些投资者对iPhone销量下降的怀疑。

Apple again declined to provide guidance to investors for the next quarter. The company hasn't given guidance on upcoming quarters since the start of the COVID-19 pandemic just under a year ago.

苹果再次拒绝为下一季度的投资者提供指导。 自从一年前COVID-19大流行开始以来，该公司就没有为未来几个季度提供指导。
