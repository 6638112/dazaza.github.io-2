更快增长、更公平增长：迈向高速路、高绩效经济的政策

2020-11-02 09:48:10

Over the past several decades, the American Dream has been caught in a pincer movement. On the one hand, deep-seated social forces have combined to slow down growth and accelerate inequality. At the same time, sustained and dramatic changes in public policy have worked not to counteract those forces, but to exacerbate them.

在过去的几十年里，美国梦陷入了一场钳制运动。一方面，深层次的社会力量联合在一起，减缓了增长，加速了不平等。与此同时，公共政策的持续和戏剧性变化不仅没有抵消这些力量，反而加剧了它们。

America’s 21st-century economic malaise and deepening social divisions have spurred a desperate search for radical alternatives to the status quo. In Faster Growth, Fairer Growth, the Niskanen Center's Brink Lindsey and Samuel Hammond lay out an ambitious, full-spectrum agenda to undo the damage caused by regulatory capture, counter the forces pushing against inclusive prosperity, and put the United States on a high road, high performance path going forward.

美国21世纪的经济不景气和日益加深的社会分歧促使人们不顾一切地寻找取代现状的激进选择。在更快、更公平的增长方面，尼斯卡宁中心(Niskanen Center)的布林克·林赛(Brink Lindsey)和塞缪尔·哈蒙德(Samuel Hammond)制定了一个雄心勃勃的全方位议程，旨在消除监管俘虏造成的损害，对抗推动包容性繁荣的力量，并让美国走上一条高绩效的大道。
