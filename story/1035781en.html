Xfinity/Comcast to apply data caps nationally now starting 2021 instead of select states

2020-11-22 03:08:04

Our Xfinity Internet plans include 1.2 terabytes (TB) of data a month. Only about 5% of our customers use that much. Sign into My Account today to see a breakdown of your own data usage.

我们的Xfinity Internet计划每月包含1.2 TB的数据。只有大约5％的客户使用过那么多商品。立即登录“我的帐户”以查看您自己的数据使用情况的细分。

Customers who use more than 1.2TB of data in a month for the first time will not be billed for exceeding the limit.
