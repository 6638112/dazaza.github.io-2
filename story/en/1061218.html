Bill.com, a cloud-based payments service for SMBs, has agreed to acquire expense management software provider Divvy for $2.5B in cash and stock
Bill.com是SMBS的基于云的支付服务，已同意以2.5亿美元的现金和股票收购费用管理软件提供商Divvy

2021-05-07 09:19:50

The deal, announced Thursday, calls for Bill.com (ticker: BILL) to pay $625 million in cash plus $1.875 billion in stock, a statement said. The boards of both Divvy and Bill.com have approved the sale, which is expected to close by the end of Bill.com’s first fiscal quarter ending Sept. 30.

声明说，周四宣布，宣布，呼吁比尔（Ticker：Bill）支付6.25亿美元的现金支付$ 18.75亿美元。 Divvy和Bill.com的董事会已批准销售，预计将于9月30日结束的比尔新财政季度结束。

Founded in 2016, Divvy provides software that helps businesses combine expense management software and smart corporate cards onto a single platform. Divvy has raised $417.5 million in funding, Crunchbase said. This includes a $165 million Series D round in January that saw Hanaco, PayPal Ventures, Whale Rock Capital Management, and Schonfeld Strategic Advisors participate. The round valued Divvy at $1.6 billion. Divvy has about 440 employees, according to an investor deck about the deal.

Divvy成立于2016年，提供了帮助企业将费用管理软件和智能公司卡结合在一个平台上的软件。 Crunchbase说，Divvy筹集了417.5百万美元的资金。这包括1月份的1.65亿美元系列D型，看到汉卡，PayPal Ventures，Whale Rock Capital Management和Schonfeld战略顾问参加。圆形尊贵的Divvy为16亿美元。根据这笔交易的投资者甲板，Divvy有大约440名员工。

“We are excited to be joining forces with Bill.com to help SMBs grow and thrive by modernizing and transforming their financial operations,” Blake Murray, Divvy CEO and co-founder, said in a statement.

“我们很高兴能够通过比尔（Bill.com）加入武力来帮助SMB通过现代化和改造其金融业务来增长和壮大，”Divvy Ceo和联合创始人在一份声明中表示。

The much bigger Bill.com, which employs roughly 800 people and has a $10.7 billion market capitalization, offers cloud-based software that helps small and midsize businesses automate their back-office financial operations. By buying Divvy, Bill.com will be able to offer expense management and budgeting software, along with smart corporate cards, to its more than 115,000 customers and its 2.5 million network members. News of the deal caused Bill.com’s stock to gain nearly 14% in aftermarket trading to $148.50.

比较大的比尔网雇用大约800人，拥有107亿美元的市值，提供基于云的软件，有助于小型和中型企业自动化其后台金融业务。通过购买Divvy，Bill.com将能够提供费用管理和预算软件，以及智能公司卡，达到超过115,000名客户及其250万网络成员。这笔交易的新闻导致比尔网的股票在售后市场交易到148.50美元的售价近14％。

“Since founding Bill.com, I have been driven by the desire to build solutions that make a real difference for small and midsize businesses. Customers have been asking us to help them with their spend management, and I am excited that together with Divvy, we can deliver on that ask, furthering our vision to transform SMB financial operations. Our expanded platform will provide more automation and real-time information to SMBs, enabling them to make more informed decisions,” said René Lacerte, Bill.com CEO and founder, in the statement.

“自创建比尔（Bill.com）以来，我已经被建立了对小型和中型企业产生了实际差异的解决方案的推动。客户一直要求我们帮助他们与他们的支出管理，我很兴奋，与Divvy一起，我们可以在那个方面提供，进一步推动我们的愿景来改变SMB金融业务。我们的扩展平台将为SMB提供更多的自动化和实时信息，使他们能够做出更明智的决策，“比尔市首席执行官和创始人在声明中。
