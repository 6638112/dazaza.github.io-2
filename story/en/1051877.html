U.S. had its coldest February in more than 30 years
U.S.超过30年的2月最冷的

2021-03-12 05:26:56

Temperatures plunged to historic lows in some parts of the country last month, including nearly all of Texas, as an Arctic air mass gripped much of the nation.

上个月在该国的某些地区的历史低位中，包括几乎所有德克萨斯州的历史低位，因为北极空气群体抓住了大部分国家。

Despite the chilly February, winter wrapped up warmer than normal, according to scientists at NOAA’s National Centers for Environmental Information.

据Noaa的环境信息中心的科学家称，尽管2月寒冷的2月，冬季比正常抱鲜温暖。

The average temperature across the contiguous U.S. last month was 30.6 degrees F, 3.2 degrees below the 20th-century average, making it the 19th-coldest February in the 127-year record. It was also the coldest February since 1989.

上个月的平均温度是30.6度F，3.2度低于20世纪的平均水平，使其于127年的2月19日最冷于127年。自1989年以来，这也是最寒冷的2月。

Below-average temperatures dominated much of the nation from the Northwest to the Great Lakes and south to the Gulf of Mexico. For six states — Arkansas, Iowa, Kansas, Missouri, Nebraska and Oklahoma — February 2021 placed among the top 10-coldest Februaries on record. Texas had its 11th-coldest February.

低于平均气温的温度从西北部到伟大的湖泊和南部到墨西哥湾的大部分地区。对于六个州 - 阿肯色州，爱荷华州，堪萨斯州，密苏里州，内布拉斯加州和俄克拉荷马州 - 2月2021年2月在纪录中排名前10名最冷的恐怖娱乐中。德克萨斯州的11月11日。

February’s average precipitation was 1.99 inches (0.14 of an inch below average), which ranked in the middle-third of the historical record.

2月的平均降水量为1.99英寸（平均水平为0.14英寸），其中排名在历史记录的中间三分之一。

Above-average precipitation fell across much of the Northwest, as well as portions of the East Coast and Ohio Valley. North Carolina saw its eighth-wettest February on record for the month, while South Carolina had its 10th wettest.

在西北大部分地区的平均水平上面落下，以及东海岸和俄亥俄州山谷的一部分。北卡罗来纳州的2月份在本月的纪录中看到了它的第八次潮湿，而南卡罗来纳州的第10次潮湿。

Meteorological winter (December through February) was quite mild and dry across the contiguous U.S. The average temperature was 33.6 degrees F, 1.4 degrees above average, placing Winter 2021 in the warmest third of the winter record. Maine had its third-warmest winter; California had its 12th warmest.

气象冬季（12月至2月）在邻近的美国，平均温度为33.6度，平均水平为1.4度，将冬季2021放在冬季记录中最温暖的三分之上。缅因冬季最热门的冬季;加利福尼亚州的第12次最温暖。

Total precipitation this winter was 6.10 inches — 0.69 of an inch below average — which ranked among the driest one-third of winters in 126 years. North Dakota had its third-driest winter on record, while Virginia and Delaware had their ninth and 11th-wettest winters, respectively.

今年冬季的总降水为6.10英寸 - 低于平均水平的0.69英寸 - 这在126年内排名最干燥的三分之一的冬季。北达科他在纪录中的第三次最干燥的冬天，而弗吉尼亚和特拉华州分别有第九和第11次潮湿的冬天。

A brutally cold month for Texans: February 2021 brought the coldest air since December 1989 to much of the state. Several locations across central Texas — including Austin and Waco — broke records for the longest streak of below-freezing temperatures. Every county in Texas was under a Winter Storm Warning in mid-February and experienced wind chill values below zero as far south as the Rio Grande River and northeastern Mexico.

德克萨斯人的残酷寒冷的月份：2021年2月以自1989年12月至大部分国家以来将最冷的空气带来了最寒冷的空气。德克萨斯州中部的几个地点 - 包括奥斯汀和Waco - 破碎的记录，用于低于冻结温度的最长条纹。德克萨斯州的每个县都在2月中旬的冬季风暴警告中，并且在南部的零下的风冷价值观为Rio Grande River和墨西哥东北部。

Alaska was colder than usual: Alaska saw its coldest February in 22 years. In Anchorage, the temperature never rose above 30 degrees F for the entire month, the first month since December 1998 with all daily high temperatures remaining below freezing.

阿拉斯加比平常更冷：阿拉斯加22年的2月2月所看到的。在安克雷奇，整个月的温度从未超过30度以上，这是自1998年12月以来的第一个月，每天都在冻结后的所有高温。

Drought got a bit worse: According to the U.S. Drought Monitor report, 46.6% of the contiguous U.S. was in drought at the beginning of March, up slightly from 45.8% at the beginning of February. Drought expanded or intensified across portions of the northern and southern Plains, and Great Basin.

干旱有点差：根据美国干旱监测报告，46.6％的连续美国在3月初进行干旱，略高于2月初的45.8％。在北部和南部平原的部分和大盆地的一部分扩大或加剧了干旱。
