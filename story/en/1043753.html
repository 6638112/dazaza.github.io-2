Signal gaining popularity in India after WhatsApp policy changes
WhatsApp政策变更后，信号在印度越来越受欢迎

2021-01-09 17:45:10

Encrypted messaging app Signal said it saw a “giant spike” in India after WhatsApp announced it would be sharing more user data with parent Facebook under an updated privacy policy and new terms of service. The move has sparked concern among WhatsApp users. One of them was Tesla chief executive and world's richest man Elon Musk, who asked them to join Signal on Thursday. India is WhatsApp's biggest market with over 400 million users and some experts are doubtful whether privacy concerns will make much of a dent in its dominance. “We have seen a giant spike in India since the WhatsApp news and also with Elon Musk’s recent tweet,” Jun Harada, head of growth and communication at Signal, told ET. “We do hope to have much more growth in India in the near future. We saw a very large spike over summer in India and have seen it only continue to grow.” Signal saw 2,200 installs on India's app stores on Wednesday up 38% from 1,600 installs in the week ended December 30, according to mobile intelligence firm Sensor Tower. Signal saw about 51,000 installs in December, up 11% from 46,000 in November. Harada declined to provide numbers, citing company policy, saying the Sensor Tower data was “directionally correct, but generally the numbers are off by a few magnitudes.” Signal has also been endorsed by whistleblower and privacy advocate Edward Snowden as well as Twitter CEO Jack Dorsey. It was created by Moxie Marlinspike, cofounder of the Signal Foundation, which received initial funding from WhatsApp cofounder Brian Acton. Signal says it has “state-of-the-art” end-to-end encryption and is an independent nonprofit, not tied to any major tech company and cannot be acquired by one either. WhatsApp’s new privacy policy, announced on Monday, has raised concerns over user privacy and data sharing around the globe. It will enable the app to share much more commercial user data with Facebook. Users who don’t accept the updated privacy policy, which comes into force in February, won’t be able to access chats on the platform, as per alerts sent to some Indian users this week. In a blog post on January 4, WhatsApp said that sharing data with Facebook will help “personalisation” of content and the display of relevant advertisements across the group’s multiple social platforms. It also enables users to interlink services, such as using a Facebook Pay account to pay for things on the messaging app. In a tweet on Thursday, Signal said verification codes as a means of registering on the app were delayed across several providers because many new users were trying to join. “We are working with carriers to resolve this as quickly as possible,” the company tweeted and later thanked carriers who “flipped the right switches so that people could keep switching.” While WhatsApp’s update was Facebook’s most visible and widespread effort to gather data, the switch to other apps after the move will probably be led by more affluent users and most won’t be too perturbed, said Sanchit Vir Gogia, chief executive, Greyhound Research. “India is a mass market and a large segment of users do not value privacy as much as other markets,” he said. “WhatsApp is only going to go from strength to strength.”

加密消息应用程序Signal表示，在WhatsApp宣布将根据更新的隐私权政策和新的服务条款与母Facebook共享更多用户数据之后，印度出现了“巨额增长”。此举引发了WhatsApp用户的关注。特斯拉（Tesla）首席执行官和世界首富埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）就是其中之一，他于周四要求他们加入Signal。印度是WhatsApp的最大市场，拥有超过4亿用户，一些专家怀疑隐私问题是否会在其主导地位中起到很大作用。 Signal的增长和传播负责人Jun Harada告诉ET：“自WhatsApp新闻以来，印度就出现了大幅增长，而Elon Musk的最新推文也是如此。” “我们确实希望在不久的将来在印度有更多的增长。我们看到印度整个夏季都有非常大的高峰，并且只在持续增长。”根据移动智能公司Sensor Tower的数据，Signal在印度的应用商店上安装了2200个安装，比截至12月30日的一周的1600个安装增长了38％。 Signal在12月的安装量约为51,000，比11月的46,000增长了11％。 Harada援引公司政策拒绝提供数字，称传感器塔数据“方向正确，但通常数字相差不大。”举报人，隐私权倡导者爱德华·斯诺登（Edward Snowden）和Twitter首席执行官杰克·多尔西（Jack Dorsey）也认可Signal。它是由信号基金会的联合创始人Moxie Marlinspike创建的，该基金会从WhatsApp联合创始人Brian Acton那里获得了初始资金。 Signal表示，它具有“最先进的”端到端加密功能，是一个独立的非营利组织，与任何大型科技公司均无关联，也无法被任何一家公司收购。 WhatsApp周一宣布的新隐私政策引起了人们对全球用户隐私和数据共享的担忧。它将使该应用程序与Facebook共享更多的商业用户数据。根据本周发送给某些印度用户的警报，不接受于2月生效的更新的隐私权政策的用户将无法访问该平台上的聊天。 WhatsApp在1月4日的博客文章中表示，与Facebook共享数据将有助于“个性化”内容以及在该组织的多个社交平台上显示相关广告。它还使用户能够互连服务，例如使用Facebook Pay帐户在消息传递应用程序上付款。 Signal在周四的一条推文中说，由于许多新用户正试图加入，因此验证码作为在该应用程序上进行注册的一种方式在多个提供商之间被延迟。该公司在推特上写道：“我们正在与运营商合作，以尽快解决这一问题，后来对运营商表示感谢，他们“拨动了正确的开关，以便人们可以继续切换。” Greyhound Research首席执行官Sanchit Vir Gogia表示，虽然WhatsApp的更新是Facebook收集数据方面最可见和最广泛的努力，但此举可能会由更富裕的用户带动，而转向其他应用程序后，大多数人不会太担心。他说：“印度是一个大众市场，很大一部分用户对隐私的重视程度不如其他市场。” “ WhatsApp只会越来越强大。”
