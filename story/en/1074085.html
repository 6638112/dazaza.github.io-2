Snapchat is rolling out a new buddy system-like real-time location sharing feature
Snapchat推出了一个新的好友系统，比如实时位置共享功能

2022-02-19 18:23:33

Users can only share their real-time location with individual friends. Snapchat says that for safety reasons, there isn’t an option for users to send their real-time location details to all of their Snapchat friends. The company also notes that both parties have to accept each other as friends on Snapchat before they can share their location. When users decide the turn on the feature, they’ll be prompted with a reminder that the tool is meant to be used with close friends and family only.

用户只能与个人朋友共享他们的实时位置。Snapchat表示，出于安全考虑，用户无法向所有Snapchat好友发送实时位置详细信息。该公司还指出，双方必须在Snapchat上接受对方作为朋友，然后才能分享自己的位置。当用户决定打开该功能时，系统会提示他们，该工具只适用于亲密的朋友和家人。

Since 2017, the app has given users the option to share their location with their friends on the Snap Map when using Snapchat. Now, 250 million people use the Map to connect with their friends each month. The company says this new real-time feature was built on feedback from users about how they were using Snap Map with their friends. It notes that 78% of Snapchat users in the United States say they aren’t hesitant to share their location on the Snap Map and that they do so because they think it’s a safe way to stay connected with others.

自2017年以来，该应用程序为用户提供了在使用Snapchat时在Snap Map上与朋友共享位置的选项。现在，每个月有2.5亿人使用地图与朋友联系。该公司表示，这项新的实时功能是基于用户对他们如何与朋友使用Snap Map的反馈而建立的。报告指出，78%的美国Snapchat用户表示，他们毫不犹豫地在Snap地图上分享自己的位置，他们这样做是因为他们认为这是与他人保持联系的安全方式。

The company is releasing the new feature as part of a broader partnership with It’s On Us, a national nonprofit dedicated to combating campus sexual assault. In addition to the new feature, the app will also publish a new PSA focused on increasing bystander awareness.

该公司将发布这一新功能，作为其与致力于打击校园性侵犯的国家非营利组织It's On Us更广泛合作关系的一部分。除了新功能，该应用程序还将发布一个新的PSA，重点是提高旁观者的意识。

The launch of the new feature comes a day after Snapchat announced that users will be able to change their usernames starting February 23rd. The username change won’t impact aspects of users’ accounts, such as their friend lists, Snap codes, Snap scores, memories, etc. You won’t be able to select usernames that have already been used and you can only change your username once a year.

Snapchat宣布从2月23日开始，用户可以更改用户名，一天前，这项新功能发布。用户名更改不会影响用户帐户的各个方面，例如他们的好友列表、快照代码、快照分数、记忆等。您将无法选择已经使用过的用户名，并且您只能每年更改一次用户名。
