Lyft的两名高管将在加州第22号提案投票前夕离开公司，他们分别负责战略举措和设计

2020-11-04 06:16:00

Two senior executives at Lyft, in charge of strategic initiatives and design, are leaving the ride-hailing firm, The Information has learned. News of their departures, which follow the exits of three other senior executives in the past few months, came on the eve of the vote on Proposition 22, arguably the most important moment in the company’s history.

据The Information了解到，Lyft负责战略举措和设计的两名高管将离开这家叫车公司。他们离职的消息是在22号提案投票前夕传来的，可以说这是该公司历史上最重要的时刻。过去几个月，还有另外三名高管离职。

The California ballot initiative is aimed at overriding a state law that requires drivers for Lyft, Uber and other gig economy companies to be treated as employees rather than independent contractors. Defeat of the initiative could financially crush Lyft. While the departures, which were announced internally toward the end of last week, appear to be due to personal circumstances, their exits add to the uncertainty of this time for the company.

加州的投票倡议旨在推翻一项州法律，该法律要求Lyft、优步和其他零工经济公司的司机被视为员工，而不是独立的承包商。该倡议的失败可能会在财务上摧毁Lyft。虽然上周末内部宣布的离职似乎是由于个人情况，但他们的离职增加了公司这段时间的不确定性。
