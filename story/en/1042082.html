Reddit suspends blind people for using an accessible Reddit client
Reddit暂停使用可访问Reddit客户端的盲人

2020-12-28 03:47:37

My name was SLJ7 until a few days ago, when that account got suspended. For those who don't know, suspentions are Reddit's answer to the controversial shadowban. I'm still able to view Reddit, but can't interact in any way with my subs. I got a PM alerting me to this fact, and it very unhelpfully referenced the content policy, which I'm 99% certain I haven't violated. So, I went through the fairly easy appeal process, stating that I don't know what I did and would like to have my account back. I received what was certainly an automated response telling me my suspention would not be lifted, and I should familiarize myself with the content policy.

我的名字叫SLJ7，直到几天前那个帐户被暂停。对于那些不知道的人来说，悬疑是Reddit对有争议的影子禁令的答案。我仍然可以查看Reddit，但是无法与我的订阅者进行任何互动。我有一个PM提醒我这个事实，它非常无助地引用了内容政策，我99％确信我没有违反该政策。因此，我经历了相当简单的上诉程序，说我不知道​​自己做了什么，想收回我的帐户。我收到的肯定是自动答复，告诉我我的怀疑不会被解除，我应该使自己熟悉内容政策。

Long story short, I have a second account which also got suspended even though I didn't post from it at all. In that case, I was able to appeal the suspention and have it lifted.

长话短说，即使我根本没有发布过第二个帐户，该帐户也被暂停了。在那种情况下，我能够提出上诉并解除。

I was ready to say this was a fluke until I found out I wasn't alone. Around the same time, the reddit account of Fastfinge also got suspended. He also appealed, and also got denied.

我准备说这是a幸，直到我发现自己并不孤单。大约在同一时间，Fastfinge的reddit帐户也被暂停。他也提出上诉，也遭到拒绝。

I have several accounts attached to my dystopia, and the two that were banned are the only two that are signed into chat. My theory is that, despite the documentation claiming suspentions are all performed by actual reddit admins, something in Dystopia's code is causing us to get flagged. And don't get me wrong; this is entirely on the Reddit administration: How dare they give such a dehumanizing and canned response and make us play guessing games to begin with? I get better support from Facebook, and they should be ashamed of themselves for this experience.

我的反乌托邦有几个帐户，被禁止的两个帐户是唯一登录聊天的帐户。我的理论是，尽管文档声称所有怀疑都是由实际的reddit管理员执行的，但反乌托邦代码中的某些内容却导致我们被标记出来。不要误会我的意思；这完全取决于Reddit政府：他们如何敢于做出如此不人道化和固定的响应，并让我们从一开始就玩猜谜游戏？我从Facebook获得了更好的支持，因此他们应该为此感到羞耻。

That being said, I think Dystopia gets access to chat by essentially rendering the Reddit website locally, rather than using the API with a registered app ID as it does for everything else. So, is it possible something about the way Dystopia is scraping the website is causing the problem? Who can tell. But it's something to keep in mind, and I would rather put out the warning than stay quiet, because trying to figure out what I did wrong and navigate the Reddit support experience has been exhausting to say the least. I'm going to put in another appeal and suggest that maybe this is the problem, and log out of chat. I'll keep this post updated.

话虽这么说，我认为反乌托邦可以通过本质上在本地渲染Reddit网站来访问聊天，而不是像其他任何事情一样使用带有已注册应用ID的API。那么，反乌托邦抓取网站的方式是否可能引起问题？谁能告诉。但这是需要牢记的，我宁愿发出警告而不是保持沉默，因为至少要说出要弄清我做错了什么并浏览Reddit支持经验的努力。我要提出另一项上诉，建议也许这是问题所在，然后退出聊天。我会随时更新此帖子。
