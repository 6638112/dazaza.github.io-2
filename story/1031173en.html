史塔西使用的隐蔽监听设备

2020-10-26 08:22:09

Apart from placing covert listening devices (bugs)with their adversaries, the task of the Stasiwas also to check – or sweep – important objects,such as government offices, hotels and embassies, in order todiscover bugs and other espionage activities from their adversaries,both foreign and domestic.This assignment was known as Aufgabe X (assignment X) and the technology forit was commonly known as Kontertechnik (countermeasures),or Operatieve Kontroll- und Suchtechnik (operational check and searchtechnology), or more generally as: X-Technik (X-technology).

除了在敌人身上安放秘密监听装置(窃听器)外，斯塔西的任务还包括检查或清扫重要的物体，如政府办公室、酒店和大使馆，以发现来自国内外敌人的窃听和其他间谍活动。这项任务被称为Aufgabe X(任务X)，其技术通常被称为KonterTechnik(对策)，或Operatieve Kontroll-und Suchtech ik(操作检查和搜索技术)，或者更一般地称为：X-Technik(X-技术)。这项任务被称为Aufgabe X(任务X)，其技术通常被称为KonterTechnik(对策)或Operatieve Kontroll-und Suchtech ik(操作检查和搜索技术)，或者更一般地称为：X-Technik(X技术)。

Although some devices have a codename, all technology that was used bythe Stasi, was given a project number. This was also the case for equipmentthat was bought from security services in other countries, such as Bulgaria,although in some cases the project number is unknown. The diagram above shows how the project number is constructed.The first digit tells us which department was responsible for it. In thiscase it is department 33, which was Außenstelle Beucha (OutstationBeucha). Before 1977, the prefix '3' was omitted, or the prefix 'AB' was used.The next two digits define the theme and the group withinthe theme. The next two digits define the actual project (within the group).If a device is part of a kit, the number behind the dash specifies the itemnumber (within the kit). The last two digits are optional, andspecify the version or variant.

虽然有些设备有代号，但斯塔西使用的所有技术都有一个项目编号。从保加利亚等其他国家的安全部门购买的设备也是如此，尽管在某些情况下项目编号不详。上图显示了项目编号是如何构造的，第一个数字告诉我们哪个部门负责。在这种情况下，是第33部，也就是Auüenstelle Beucha(Beucha前哨)。1977年前，省略前缀或使用前缀AB，后两个数字定义主题和主题中的组。接下来的两个数字定义实际项目(在组内)。如果设备是套件的一部分，则破折号后面的数字指定(在套件内)项目编号。最后两位是可选的，指定版本或变体。

德意志民主共和国(DDR)--前德意志民主共和国国家安全局记录联邦专员(DDR)的正式地址：德国联邦驻斯塔塞舍尔斯共和国国家安全局记录专员(DDR)--前德意志民主共和国国家安全局记录联邦专员(以下简称“联邦专员”)：德国联邦民主共和国(DDR)--前德意志民主共和国(德意志民主共和国)国家安全局记录的联邦专员(以下简称“德意志联邦共和国”)(以下简称“德意志联邦共和国联邦委员”)，由前德意志民主共和国国家安全局记录委员会成员组成。➤网站。

Centrales Kontrollsystem Contral Control System — Electronic system, developed by the Stasi in the mid-1960s — for processing telephone taps and wired covert liestening devices (bugs). The system was succeeded by CEKO 2.

Centrales Kontrollsystem控制系统-电子系统，由Stasi在20世纪60年代中期开发，用于处理电话窃听和有线隐蔽窃听装置(Bug)。该系统由Ceko 2号接替。

Endstellengestell German word for terminating unit or end-of-line device. In this context used for the final (remote) listening post or monitoring station.

Endstellengestell德语单词，表示终端单元或行尾设备。在此上下文中用于最终(远程)监听站或监测站。

Hochfrequenz German word for High Frequency (HF), also known as Radio Frequency (RF). In this context used to identify radio bugs.

Hochrequenz德语单词的高频(HF)，也称为无线电频率(RF)。在此上下文中用于识别无线电窃听器。

Hauptverwaltung Aufklärung Main Directorate for Reconnaissance, a department of the Stasi, responsible for foreign and domestic espionage and for foreign covert operations.

Hauptverwaltung Aufklärung侦察总局是史塔西的一个部门，负责国内外间谍活动和外国秘密行动。

Inoffizielle Mitarbeiter Unofficial Employee — Stasi name for civil informants, inside and outside the DDR, often recruted by means of compromise and manipulation.

Inoffizielle Mitarbeiter非官方雇员-Stasi是复员方案内外的民间告密者的名字，通常是通过妥协和操纵的方式招募的。

Infrarot German word for Infra-Red (IR). Light rays at the red end of the spectrum, invisible to the human eye.

Infrarot德语中的红外线(IR)一词。光谱中红端的光线，肉眼看不见。

Ministerium für Staatssicherheit Ministery for State Security of the former DDR (East Germany); one of most effective and repressive intelligence and secret police agencies in the world. The agency is commonly known as Stasi, which is the abbreviation of Staats- Sicherheit, and had its headquarters in East-Berlin. The Stasi had strong ties with the Russian intelligence service KGB. ➤  Wikipedia  ➤  Wikipedia Germany

前东德(东德)国家安全部部长；世界上最有效和最专制的情报和秘密警察机构之一。该机构通常被称为Stasi，是Staats-Sinherheit的缩写，总部设在东柏林。斯塔西与俄罗斯情报机构克格勃关系密切。➤、维基百科、➤、维基百科德国。

Niederfrequenz German word for Audio Frequency (AF), also known as Low Frequency (LF). In this context used to identify audio bugs.

Niederfrequenz德语单词的音频(AF)，也称为低频(LF)。在此上下文中用于识别音频错误。

Passive Element Common expression for a covert listening device (bug) that does not need its own local power source. It is commonly powered by energy that is beamed at it from a nearby location.

不需要自己的本地电源的隐蔽监听设备(Bug)的被动元素通用表达式。它通常是由从附近位置发射到它身上的能量提供动力的。

Trägerfrequenz German word for Carrier Frequency (CF), commonly abbreviated to Carrier. In this context used to identify carrier bugs.

Trägerfrequenz德语中载波频率(CF)的单词，通常缩写为Carrier。在此上下文中用于识别载体错误。
