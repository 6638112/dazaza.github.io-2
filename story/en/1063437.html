How to opt-out of Amazon Sidewalk
如何选择退出亚马逊人行道

2021-06-04 10:15:19

Amazon Sidewalk is a wireless standard that has advantages over a Wi-Fi or Bluetooth connection. However, there are privacy concerns, as it enables your smart home devices (such as Echo and Alexa speakers) to communicate with others outside of your home. Here’s how to disable and turn off Sidewalk.

亚马逊人行道是一种无线标准，具有优于Wi-Fi或蓝牙连接的优势。但是，存在隐私问题，因为它使您的智能家居设备（如回声和Alexa扬声器）能够与您家外面的其他人沟通。这是如何禁用和关闭人行道。

First, to opt-out of Sidewalk on your Echo speakers and smart displays, open the “Amazon Alexa” app on your iPhone, iPad, or Android device, and make sure it’s been updated to the latest version. Next, tap the “More” tab at the bottom of your screen.

首先，要在回声扬声器和智能显示屏上选择侧面行人行道，请在iPhone，iPad或Android设备上打开“Amazon Alexa”应用程序，并确保它已更新到最新版本。接下来，点击屏幕底部的“更多”选项卡。

Locate and open the “Account Settings” menu, found near the top of the page.

找到并打开“帐户设置”菜单，在页面顶部附近找到。

Note: The Amazon Sidewalk menu will only be available if you have a compatible device linked to your account. If you don’t, the listing won’t be shown.

注意：如果您有与您的帐户相关的兼容设备，则亚马逊侧面行动菜单将仅适用。如果您没有，则不会显示列表。

Here you’ll find more information on the Amazon Sidewalk feature. If the switch on the Amazon Sidewalk toggle is to the left, you’ve opted out of Sidewalk. If it’s not, tap the toggle to disable the feature.

在这里，您可以找到有关Amazon Sidewalk功能的更多信息。如果Amazon Sidewalk切换到左侧，则您可以选择退出人行道。如果不是，请点击切换以禁用该功能。

In the future, if you’d like to opt back in, simply tap the toggle again.

将来，如果您想选择退回，只需再次点击切换即可。

On other devices—such as Ring Video Doorbells—the setting for opting-out of Amazon Sidewalk is found in the corresponding app.

在其他设备上 - 例如环视频门铃 - 在相应的应用程序中找到了选择亚马逊人行道的设置。

To opt out of Amazon Sidewalk on Ring smart home products, start by opening the Ring app on your iPhone, iPad, or Android phone or tablet. From the main screen, tap the three-line hamburger menu icon in the top-left corner.

要选择退出环形智能家居产品的亚马逊人行道，请首先在iPhone，iPad或Android手机或平板电脑上打开环形应用程序。从主屏幕中，点击左上角的三行汉堡菜单图标。

Before the feature can be turned off, Ring will confirm that you want to disable the feature. A text box will list out some of the benefits of Sidewalk, including the ability to continue to receive motion alerts even if the device loses its internet connection.

在关闭功能之前，Ring将确认您要禁用该功能。文本框将列出人行道的一些好处，包括即使设备丢失其互联网连接，也可以继续接收动态警报的能力。

Similar steps for opting out of Amazon Sidewalk should be available in the companion app for your Sidewalk-compatible smart devices.

在Companion App中为Sidewalk兼容的智能设备提供类似的步骤。
