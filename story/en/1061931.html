Copenhagen-based contract automation service Contractbook raises $30M Series B led by Tiger Global

Contractbook, a contract automation platform that claims users from companies such as Domino’s, KPMG, TED, and E.On, today announced it has raised $30 million in a series B round of funding.

合同簿，一个合同自动化平台，这些平台声称Domino，KPMG，TED和E.ON等公司，今天宣布它已宣布筹集了3000万美元的B系列资金。

Although Contractbook works with larger enterprise customers, the problem it’s trying to solve is perhaps more likely to impact smaller businesses that don’t already have a structured workflow for their contracts.

虽然合成书与更大的企业客户合作，但试图解决的问题可能更有可能影响较小的企业，这些企业尚未为其合同具有结构化工作流程。

“That leaves them vulnerable, prone to legal errors, inefficient, and with a lot of manual routine work that they don’t like to do and find very difficult,” Contractbook cofounder and CEO Niels Martin Brochner told VentureBeat.

“这让他们易受伤害，容易出现法律错误，效率低下，并且与他们不喜欢做的很多手工常规工作，并找到非常困难，”合成书Cofounder和Ceo Niels Martin Brochner告诉Vidturebeat。

Contractbook is all about automating as much of the manual administrative work as possible through integrations, with tools for drafting, editing, collaborating, signing, storing, organizing, and executing contracts. It’s an all-in-one platform that replaces any number of existing services, from Word to Adobe Acrobat, Box, Excel, and DocuSign.

合成簿全部是关于通过集成的尽可能多的手动管理工作，具有起草，编辑，协作，签名，存储，组织和执行合同的工具。它是一个替换任意数量的现有服务的一体化平台，从Word到Adobe Acrobat，Box，Excel和DocuSign。

“We don’t believe that a contract lifecycle ends with a signature,” Martin Brochner added. “It takes a few days to create a contract and minutes to sign it, but it stays in effect for years. Ninety-nine percent of a contract’s lifecycle lies post-closing, and 99% of all businesses have no workflow for it. That is why we are focused on building the world’s best post-closing contract workflow.”

“我们不相信合同生命周期以签名结束，”Martin Brochner补充道。 “创建合同和分钟签署它需要几天时间才能签署，但它保持效果多年。合同生命周期的百分之九十九十九十九十九十九十九十年后，所有企业的99％都没有工作流程。这就是为什么我们专注于建立世界上最好的结束后合同工作流程。“

Founded out of Copenhagen, Denmark in 2016, Contractbook had previously raised around $13 million. With its latest cash injection, which was spearheaded by Tiger Global, the company said it’s targeting expansion into the U.S. market. The company’s latest investment also comes just a few months after it announced its $9.3 million series A round.

在2016年丹麦公司建于丹麦，合约书以前筹集了1300万美元。该公司表示，其最新的现金注入是由Tiger Global Spearhead的，该公司表示将其瞄准扩张进入美国市场。该公司的最新投资也仅仅是几个月后，它宣布其每轮930万美元。

DocuSign is arguably one of the better-known incumbents in the digital contract management space, having spent the better part of two decades introducing tools to make paper-based contracts obsolete, spanning e-signatures, AI-powered automation, and more. DocuSign has thrived since its 2018 IPO, particularly due to the COVID-19 crisis, which has pushed the world toward cloud-based tools that enable remote work. The company’s market cap currently sits at $37 billion, well over double its pre-pandemic valuation.

DocuSign可以说是数字合同管理空间中的更好的现名单位之一，花了两十年的更好的一部分，介绍了将纸质合同过时，跨越电子签名，AI动力自动化等等。 Docusign自2018年首次公开募股自2017年以来蓬勃发展，特别是由于Covid-19危机，这已经将世界推向基于云的工具，使能远程工作。该公司的市场概率目前占地370亿美元，其次大流行前估值。

Other notable activity in the remote contract management space includes Ironclad, which recently secured $100 million, and Box, which acquired e-signature startup SignRequest for $55 million. Pactum, meanwhile, secured $11 million last month to expand its automated contract negotiation toolset.

远程合同管理空间中的其他值得注意的活动包括Ironclad，最近获得了1亿美元和盒子，该盒子获得了5500万美元的电子签名启动SignRequest。同时，上个月巩固了1100万美元以扩展其自动合同协商工具集。

According to Martin Brochner, Contractbook is best used for companies with a “high frequency of low complexity contracts,” such as employment contracts, NDAs, and sales agreements.

根据Martin Brochner的说法，合成簿最适合用于“高频率低复杂性合同”的公司，如就业合同，NDA和销售协议。

“We allow companies to automate all the mundane manual tasks related to those contracts — whether you want to auto-generate contracts based on data input, push data between systems, or set up automated tasks to make self-executing contracts,” he said. “We make the entire lifecycle of a contract easy, efficient, and error-free.”

“我们允许公司自动化与这些合同相关的所有Mundane手动任务 - 无论您是否要根据数据输入自动生成合同，推送系统之间的数据，或建立自动任务以使自动执行合同，”他说。 “我们使合同的整个生命周期容易，高效，无错误。”
