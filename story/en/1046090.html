MkLinux is a project begun by the OSFResearch Institute (now Silicomp RI) and Apple Computer to port Linux, a freely distributed UNIX-like operatingsystem, to a variety of Power Macintosh platforms running on top of OSFResearch Institute's implementation of the Mach microkernel.

MkLinux是由OSFResearch Institute（现为Silicomp RI）和Apple Computer发起的一个项目，目的是将Linux（一种自由分发的类UNIX操作系统）移植到在OSFResearch Institute对Mach的实现之上运行的各种Power Macintosh平台上。微内核。

Beginning in the summer of 1998, development work on MkLinux transitionedfrom Apple and OSF to a community-led effort. A more-complete-but-still-briefhistory can be found on the About MkLinux page.

从1998年夏季开始，MkLinux的开发工作从Apple和OSF过渡到社区主导的工作。可以在About MkLinux页面上找到更完整但仍然简短的历史记录。

Currently, developers are working on completing release 1 (R1). A previewrelease called Pre-R1 is available on the FTP site. A CD image is also available, and a fewusers have offered to burn andmail CDs to those people who do not have access to high speed Internetaccess for a small charge.

当前，开发人员正在努力完成版本1（R1）。 FTP站点上提供了一个预览版，称为Pre-R1。还提供CD映像，一些用户提供了刻录CD并将CD邮寄给那些无法访问高速Internet的人的费用，但需要支付少量费用。

Apple continues to host a number of mailing lists related to MkLinux. The mostactive of the lists is mklinux-users. If you are new to UN*X, or youneed help with MkLinux, please subscribe to this list! All of the Apple-hostedlists have digest versions available.

Apple继续托管许多与MkLinux相关的邮件列表。列表中最活跃的是mklinux-users。如果您不熟悉UN * X，或者需要MkLinux的帮助，请订阅此列表！所有Apple托管列表都有可用的摘要版本。
