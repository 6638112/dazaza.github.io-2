Ticket for space flight with Jeff Bezos is auctioned for $28 million
与Jeff Bezos的太空飞行票价为2800万美元拍卖

2021-06-14 03:37:32

A ticket to take a brief trip to space with Amazon founder Jeff Bezos next month has been sold at auction for $28 million.

下个月与亚马逊创始人Jeff Bezos介绍空间的票据售票已在拍卖中以2800万美元的价格出售。

The bidding process, which began in early May, drew offers from more than 7,000 participants from 159 countries, Blue Origin said. The price had stood at $4.8 million ahead of Saturday’s live auction, which was streamed online.

Blue Origy表示，5月初开始于5月初开始的招标进程，从来自159个国家的超过7,000名参与者提供。星期六的现场拍卖会在网上流动的价格为480万美元。

The identity of the winning bidder has not yet been made public but will be revealed in the coming weeks, Blue Origin said.

Blue Origin表示，获胜投标人的身份尚未公开，但将在未来几周内透露。

Whoever it is will be traveling with three other passengers, including Bezos and his younger brother, Mark. The “fourth and final” passenger will be announced soon, the company said.

无论是谁将与其他三名乘客一起旅行，包括Bezos和他的弟弟，标记。该公司表示，“第四和最终”乘客将很快公布。

At approximately 10 minutes from launch to landing, around three of which will be spent in zero gravity, the trip will cost the winning bidder more than $9 million per minute spent in space.

在从发射到着陆的大约10分钟，其中三个将在零重力下花费，这次旅行将花费超过900万美元的空间。

The winning bid amount will be donated to Club for the Future, Blue Origin’s foundation focused on STEM education programs.

获奖金额将捐赠给未来的俱乐部，蓝色原产地的基金会专注于Stem教育计划。

The launch is scheduled to take place in Texas on July 20, 15 days after Bezos is due to step aside as Amazon chief executive.

该发布计划于德克萨斯州于德克萨斯州于德克萨斯州举行，在贝奥斯之后15天是由于亚马逊首席执行官的一步。

A spokesperson for the company declined to comment on whether the timing of Bezos’ transition to executive chair was related to his desire to take the trip, which will be the first by the company to include human passengers.

该公司的发言人拒绝发表评论贝斯科队向行政主席的转型时间是否与他参加旅行的愿望有关，这将是公司第一个包括人类乘客。

“Ever since I was five years old, I’ve dreamed of traveling to space,” Bezos said in an Instagram post last week. “On July 20, I will take that journey with my brother. The greatest adventure, with my best friend.”

“自从我五岁以来，我曾梦想着到太空，”Bezos上周在一个Instagram帖子中说。 “7月20日，我将与兄弟一起旅行。最伟大的冒险，我最好的朋友。“

To date, 15 test flights of the New Shepard capsule have been undertaken. All have been successful with the exception of the first, in April 2015, when the rocket suffered a crash landing, though the capsule returned to Earth safely.

迄今为止，已经开展了15个测试飞行的新谢泼德胶囊。除了第一届，2015年4月，虽然胶囊遭遇了撞击着陆，但虽然胶囊安全地返回地球，但所有人都成功了。

If successful, the trip on New Shepard would be the first commercial space flight to take a civilian beyond the Kármán line, the internationally recognized edge of space, more than 60 miles (100 km) above Earth.

如果成功，新谢泼德的旅行将是第一个以克尔曼线，国际公认的空间边缘，超过60英里（100公里）以上的第一个商业空间飞行。

However, Blue Origin’s schedule has reportedly prompted a rush from Virgin Galactic, the competing space tourism company founded by Sir Richard Branson, to advance its plans.

然而，据报道，Blue Origin的时间表促使由Richard Branson爵士成立的竞争空间旅游公司急于从处女的航天局提升计划。

“Watch this space...” the businessman wrote in a recent tweet. The company held a successful test last month.

“看这个空间......”商人在最近的推文中写道。该公司上个月举行了成功的测试。

Blue Origin, which has so far been bankrolled entirely by funds raised by Bezos selling off his Amazon shares, has not announced any details on pricing for future trips—but said the “most competitive” bidders will be approached for interest in upcoming flights.

迄今为止，贝索斯销售亚马逊股票的资金的蓝色起源尚未宣布有关未来旅行的定价详情 - 但表示将在即将到来的航班上接近“最具竞争力”投标人。
