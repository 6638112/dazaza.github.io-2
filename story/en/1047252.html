Two new efforts could widen the pool of people investing directly in venture funds
两项新的努力可以扩大直接投资于风险基金的人数

2021-02-06 20:53:02

It’s a fairly small universe, in other words, but two new initiatives, both announced this week and both very different, are looking to change the equation — and could usher in similar efforts soon.

换句话说，这是一个相当小的领域，但是本周宣布的两项新举措都非常不同，它们正在寻求改变方程式，并且可能很快会采取类似的措施。

Arlan Hamilton came out with her news first. Hamilton is the founder of Backstage Capital, a venture firm focused on investing in startups founded by people of color, women, and members of the LGBTQ community. In short, diversity is at Backstage’s very core. But Hamilton, who is herself Black, isn’t interested in funding diverse founders alone; she is also interested in enabling more people from diverse backgrounds — including socioeconomically — to invest in venture capital as an asset class.

艾伦·汉密尔顿（Arlan Hamilton）首先发布了她的消息。汉密尔顿是Backstage Capital的创始人，这是一家致力于投资由有色人种，女性和LGBTQ社区成员创立的初创公司的风险投资公司。简而言之，多样性是Backstage的核心。但是汉密尔顿，本身就是黑人，对仅资助多元化的创始人并不感兴趣。她还想让更多来自不同背景（包括社会经济背景）的人投资于风险投资作为一种资产类别。

Toward that end, earlier this week, on the private investing platform Republic, she opened a new fund that anyone — including unaccredited investors — could back under a Securities and Exchange Commission rule called Reg CF, or Regulation Crowdfunding.

为此，本周早些时候，她在私人投资平台Republic上开设了一只新基金，任何人（包括未经认可的投资者）都可以根据证券交易委员会（Reg CF）或Regular Crowdfunding的规定归还。

Hamilton hit the upper boundary of what Reg CF allows an outfit to raise — $1,070,000 within a 12-month period — in what seemed like hours from 2,790 investors who were invited to invest as little as $100. But more could be coming. The reason why: that rule underwent a change in November under former SEC chair Jay Clayton, and will next month begin allowing outfits to crowdsource up to $5 million.

汉密尔顿达到了Reg CF允许机构筹集资金的上限-在12个月内筹集到1,070,000美元-在2790名被邀请投资至100美元的投资者看来，数小时之内。但是可能会有更多。原因：该规则在11月由前SEC主席杰伊·克莱顿（Jay Clayton）领导下进行了修改，并将于下个月开始允许公司向众筹资金500万美元。

The process could be slowed down by the incoming SEC chief. President Biden has appointed former regulator and former Goldman partner Gary Gensler, who must now receive Senate confirmation. The new administration is also reviewing many of the measures moved along late in the Trump administration. If it’s all systems go, however, it’s easy to imagine more unaccredited investors being invited to fund other, and larger, venture funds soon.

即将上任的SEC负责人可能会减慢该过程。拜登总统任命了前监管者和前高盛合伙人加里·根斯勒，他们现在必须获得参议院的确认。新政府还正在审查特朗普政府后期采取的许多措施。但是，如果所有系统都运转起来了，那么很难想象会有更多未经认可的投资者被邀请为其他更大的风险基金提供资金。

A second initiative this week has similar objectives to Hamilton — bringing more diverse investors into the ranks of LPs — though it has a different approach and it’s targeting accredited investors only, which basically means individuals who are earning $200,000 a year and/or have a net worth of $1 million or more.

本周的第二项举措的目标与汉密尔顿相似-将更多不同的投资者加入有限合伙人行列-尽管它采用不同的方法，并且仅针对认可的投资者，这基本上意味着那些年收入为20万美元和/或拥有净收入的个人价值超过100万美元。

Launched by Acrew Capital — a Palo Alto- and San Francisco-based early-stage venture spearheaded by veteran VC Theresia Gouw — the firm revealed yesterday that it’s currently raising a traditional growth-stage fund with a twist. In addition to giving its current LPs a crack at investing in the new fund, it is also opening the vehicle up to more women, people of color, and underrepresented individuals who may not have had a chance previously to invest in a later-stage private vehicle.

该公司由Acrew Capital发起，该公司是由资深风险投资人Theresia Gouw牵头的帕洛阿尔托市和旧金山市的早期风险投资公司，昨日透露，该公司目前正在筹集一笔传统的成长阶段基金。除了让现有的有限合伙人在投资新基金方面有所突破之外，它还向更多妇女，有色人种和代表性不足的个人开放了工具，他们以前可能没有机会投资于较晚的私人机构。车辆。

The key here is Acrew’s emphasis on growth-stage investing. While more women and people of color are breaking into the ranks of seed-stage investing, it takes a long time to make money with early-stage funding. Meanwhile, growth-stage funds are more exclusive because the companies they back are closer to an “exit” typically. That makes them very appealing to institutions — including mutual funds and hedge funds — and doesn’t leave much, if any, room for the kinds of individuals who Acrew now hopes to bring into the fold.

这里的关键是Acrew重视成长阶段的投资。虽然越来越多的妇女和有色人种进入了种子期投资的行列，但通过早期阶段的资金赚钱需要很长时间。同时，成长阶段的基金更具排他性，因为它们所支持的公司通常更接近“退出”。这使得它们对机构（包括共同基金和对冲基金）非常有吸引力，并且对于Acrew现在希望参与其中的各类个人没有太多的余地（如果有的话）。

Like Backstage, diversity is in the DNA of Acrew, which Gouw cofounded with Laura Kolodny, Vishal Lugani and Mark Kraynak, colleagues from their previous fund, Aspect Ventures.

就像Backstage一样，多样性来自Acrew的基因，Gouw与Laura Kolodny，Vishal Lugani和Mark Kraynak共同创立了Acrew，这是他们之前的基金Aspect Ventures的同事。

It’s little surprise that the firm — which says 88% of its overall team is female and 63% comes from underrepresented backgrounds —  would be the first to publicly focus on pulling more diverse angel investors, board members, and C-level execs into the world of later-stage deals.

该公司表示其整体团队中88％是女性，而63％的人代表不足，这将是第一个公开关注将更多多元化的天使投资人，董事会成员和C级高管推向世界的公司，这不足为奇后期交易。

Like Backstage, which gets fresh capital to invest through its new effort, the new effort of Acrew — which focuses largely on fintech and cybersecurity, and which has a stake in the highly valued challenger bank Chime, among many others others — is both inclusive and strategic.

与Backstage一样，通过新的努力获得了新的资本进行投资，Acrew的新努力既包容又包容。Acrew的主要投资对象是金融科技和网络安全，并且在估值很高的挑战者银行Chime等众多企业中拥有股份。战略。

As Kolodny explains it, a growing number of startups is focused on enhancing diversity in the board room, and having a diverse LP base of individuals who it can connect with interesting board roles is likely to work out well for everyone involved, from Acrew to the companies to its new LPs.

正如Kolodny所解释的那样，越来越多的初创公司专注于增强董事会会议室的多样性，拥有可以与有趣的董事会角色联系起来的多元化LP基地对于从Acrew到公司使用新的LP。

In fact, it’s an approach that Acrew’s partners hope won’t distinguish the firm for long, says Kolodny. “Our hope is that five years from now, a venture firm helping companies to add diverse independent board members and diverse executives won’t be a unique strategy.”

实际上，Acrew的合作伙伴希望这种方法不会在长期内使公司脱颖而出，Kolodny说。 “我们的希望是，从现在开始的五年后，一家帮助公司增加多元化的独立董事会成员和多元化高管的风险投资公司将不是一个独特的战略。”

The hope,” she adds, “is [this effort] gets people to embrace a new standard around what is what is expected of venture firms.”

她补充说，“希望是[这项努力]使人们围绕风险企业的期望接受新标准。”
