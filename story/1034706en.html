大胆的火球

2020-11-12 10:26:12

My basic theory since the announcement during this year’s WWDC keynote that Apple was — finally — moving the Mac platform from Intel’s x86 architecture to their own custom silicon, has been that they would not merely be using A-series chips for this. That Macs wouldn’t be sharing chips with iPhones and iPads. Apple could have gone this route, performance-wise, at least for the consumer grade devices, but the Apple Silicon segment of the WWDC keynote sounded to me like they were suggesting a far more ambitious endeavor. I was right. It’s the difference between using their amazing mobile chips in desktops, and using their chip expertise to design desktop-focused chips.

我的基本理论是，在今年的全球数据中心的主题演讲中宣布，苹果最终是 -  - ，将Mac平台从英特尔的x86架构转移到他们自己的定制硅片，而不只是使用A系列芯片来实现这一点。Mac电脑不会与iPhone和iPad共享芯片。在性能方面，苹果本可以走这条路，至少在消费级设备上是这样，但在我看来，WWDC主题演讲中的苹果硅片似乎是在暗示一项雄心勃勃得多的努力。我是对的。这就是在台式机上使用他们令人惊叹的移动芯片和利用他们的芯片专业知识设计台式机专用芯片之间的不同之处。

Apple billed yesterday’s event as “One More Thing”, and while they announced three new Macs, it really was about one new thing: the M1. The new M1-based MacBook Air, 13-inch MacBook Pro, and Mac Mini are best thought of not as three different computers, but rather three different manifestations of the same computer.

苹果公司称昨天的发布会是“又一件事”，虽然他们宣布了三款新的Mac电脑，但它实际上是关于一件新的东西：M1。基于M1的新MacBook Air、13英寸MacBook Pro和Mac Mini最好不要被认为是三台不同的电脑，而是同一台电脑的三种不同表现形式。

These are, by all accounts and measures, far faster machines than the Intel-based Macs they’re replacing. But the big win, and clear focus from Apple, isn’t speed but battery life. Apple’s quoted battery life times for the Intel-based MacBook Air from March of this year for “wireless web browsing” and “Apple TV app movie playback” were 11 and 12 hours, respectively. The new M1 MacBook Air pushes those times to 15 and 18 hours. The difference is even more striking with Apple’s specs for the new M1 MacBook Pro, which claim battery life of 17 and 20 hours for web browsing and movie playback. The current Intel-based 13-inch MacBook Pro gets just 10 hours on each of those tests. That’s very close to double the battery life. Double!

无论从哪个角度来看，这些电脑都比他们要取代的基于英特尔的Mac电脑快得多。但最大的胜利，也是苹果明确的关注点，不是速度，而是电池续航时间。苹果称，自今年3月起，搭载英特尔处理器的MacBook Air在“无线网页浏览”和“Apple TV应用程序电影播放”方面的电池续航时间分别为11小时和12小时。新款M1 MacBook Air将这两种时间分别延长至15小时和18小时。这一差异在苹果新款M1 MacBook Pro的规格上更加明显，它声称在网页浏览和电影播放方面的电池续航时间分别为17小时和20小时。目前基于英特尔的13英寸MacBook Pro每次测试只有10个小时。这几乎是电池寿命的两倍。双倍!。

This is the sellable bullet point for the mass market consumer who has no idea what “Apple Silicon” means: battery life is now truly all-day. Charge your MacBook Air or Pro overnight, and you can use it hard all day without ever once being near a power outlet. You’re clearly intended to be able to use these new MacBooks like iPads in that regard — where charging them and using them are entirely different actions. There’s no question in my mind that Apple could have made this transition to Apple Silicon a few years ago, but they clearly wanted to wait until the advantages were overwhelming and undeniable. And nowhere is that more evident than with these figures for battery life.

对于不知道“苹果硅”是什么意思的大众市场消费者来说，这是一个很好的卖点：电池续航时间现在真的是全天候。为你的MacBook Air或Pro充电一整晚，你就可以整天辛苦地使用它，而不需要靠近电源插座。在这一点上，你显然希望能够像iPad一样使用这些新的MacBook，在 - 上，充电和使用它们是完全不同的行为。在我看来，毫无疑问，苹果在几年前就可以实现向Apple Silicon的转变，但他们显然想等到优势压倒性和不可否认的时候再做。这一点在电池寿命数据中表现得最为明显。

The M1 really is an entire system on a chip. Everything is on the M1. The various processors, of course: the CPU cores, the GPU cores, the Neural Engine cores. But everything else is on the M1 too: the storage controller, the Secure Enclave, the memory controller, and, yes, the memory itself. The DRAM for M1-based Macs is on the package (“on the substrate”, I believe is the technical lingo).

M1实际上是一个芯片上的整个系统。所有东西都在M1上。当然是各种处理器：CPU核心、GPU核心、神经引擎核心。但其他一切也都在M1上：存储控制器、安全飞地、内存控制器，是的，还有内存本身。基于M1的Mac的DRAM在封装上(我相信“在基板上”是技术行话)。

The downside of this design is that DRAM is not something you can configure after the fact. But this has been true for Apple’s entire MacBook lineup, from consumer Airs to high-end 16-inch Pro models, for years. But with the M1, memory isn’t just soldered onto a board, it’s integrated into the SoC — just like it has been for A-series SoCs. Apple calls this “unified memory architecture”, or UMA:

这种设计的缺点是，DRAM不是您可以事后配置的东西。但多年来，从消费者机型到高端16英寸Pro机型，苹果的整个MacBook产品阵容都是如此。但有了M1，存储器不仅仅是焊接在电路板上，它还集成到了SoC - 中，就像它在A系列SoC中所做的那样。苹果称其为“统一内存架构”(UMA)：

M1 also features our unified memory architecture, or UMA. M1unifies its high‑bandwidth, low‑latency memory into a single poolwithin a custom package. As a result, all of the technologies inthe SoC can access the same data without copying it betweenmultiple pools of memory. This dramatically improves performanceand power efficiency. Video apps are snappier. Games are richerand more detailed. Image processing is lightning fast. And yourentire system is more responsive.

M1还采用了我们的统一内存架构(UMA)。M1将其高带宽、低延迟内存统一到一个定制包内的单个池中。因此，SoC中的所有技术都可以访问相同的数据，而无需在多个内存池之间进行复制。这极大地提高了性能和能效。视频应用程序更快。游戏内容更丰富、更详细。图像处理速度非常快。而且你的整个系统反应更灵敏。

There’s no separate “video memory” and “system memory” — just memory. “Shared memory” and “integrated graphics” have gotten a bad name historically, because they signified cheap low-performance compromises. Apple’s chip team is really proud of this UMA system and the integrated GPU on the M1. It’s a design that increases performance and power efficiency.

 - 没有单独的“视频内存”和“系统内存”，只有内存。“共享内存”和“集成显卡”在历史上声名狼藉，因为它们意味着廉价的低性能妥协。苹果的芯片团队真的为这款UMA系统和M1上的集成GPU感到自豪。这是一种提高性能和能效的设计。

These systems are, according to Apple’s stated numbers, fast. They’ve more than earned our trust on that. We know, for a fact, that the A14 chip in the iPhones 12 and new iPad Air is both faster and far more power efficient than all but the very highest end Intel and AMD x86 laptop chips. AnandTech published a detailed comparison yesterday showing just that. And we also know that Apple has promised that the M1’s high-performance cores are faster than the A14’s —  and the M1 has four high performance cores, while the A14 only has two. Each high-performance core is faster, and it has twice the number of them.

根据苹果公布的数据，这些系统运行速度很快。在这一点上，他们已经赢得了我们的信任。事实上，我们知道，iPhone 12和新款iPad Air中的A14芯片比除最高端的英特尔和AMD x86笔记本电脑芯片以外的所有芯片都要快得多，能效也要高得多。AnandTech昨日发布了一份详细的对比报告，显示了这一点。我们还知道，苹果承诺M1的高性能内核比A14的 - 更快，M1有四个高性能内核，而A14只有两个。每个高性能内核的速度都更快，而且其数量是高性能内核的两倍。

So if the A14 clearly outperforms all but the latest and greatest x86 laptop chips — and holds it own even against those — it would be rather shocking if Apple’s boasting that the M1 is the fastest CPU on the market is not true.

因此，如果A14的表现明显好于除最新、最棒的x86笔记本电脑芯片 - 之外的所有产品，并保住自己的地位，即使是与那些 - 相比，如果苹果吹嘘M1是市场上速度最快的CPU的说法是不正确的，那将是相当令人震惊的。

The mind-blowing part of all this is that the M1 is only being used in Apple’s less expensive consumer Macs.

所有这一切令人震惊的部分是，M1只用于苹果价格较低的消费者Mac电脑。

Wait, wait, wait, you might be saying, the MacBook Pro is pro. But as I’ve written numerous times, pro, in Apple’s product-naming parlance, doesn’t always stand for professional. Much of the time it just means better or nicer. The new M1 13-inch MacBook Pro is pro only in the way, say, AirPods Pro are. This has been true for Apple’s entry-level 13-inch MacBook Pros — the models with only two USB ports — ever since the famed MacBook “Escape” was suggested as a stand-in for the then-still-missing retina MacBook Air four years ago.

等等，等等，你可能会说，MacBook Pro是专业的。但正如我已经写过无数次的那样，在苹果的产品命名术语中，PRO并不总是代表专业的。很多时候，它只是意味着更好或更好。新的M1 13英寸MacBook Pro只是在某种程度上是专业的，比如说AirPods Pro。自从四年前著名的Macbook“Escape”被建议作为当时仍然失踪的视网膜MacBook Air的替代品以来，苹果的入门级13英寸MacBook Pro - 就一直是这样，这款机型只有两个USB接口的 - 。

The new M1 MacBook Pro is the same basic computer as the M1 MacBook Air, but adds:

新的M1 MacBook Pro是与M1 MacBook Air相同的基本电脑，但增加了：

The M1 13-inch MacBook Pro will outperform the MacBook Air in sustained performance, not because it has a better CPU or GPU, but because it has a fan that allows the high performance cores to run faster for longer stretches. (The M1 Mac Mini has even more thermal headroom, because it’s a bigger enclosure and isn’t battery-powered, and thus is the fastest machine of the three. But because it isn’t battery powered, it seemingly has the fewest advantages over its equivalent Intel predecessor.) Also noteworthy: the $999 entry-model MacBook Air only has 7 GPU cores, not 8. It’s logical to conclude that this is the result of chip binning, but Apple, unsurprisingly, has no comment on the matter.

M1 13英寸的MacBook Pro将在持续性能上超过MacBook Air，这不是因为它有更好的CPU或GPU，而是因为它有一个风扇，可以让高性能内核在更长的时间内运行得更快。(M1 Mac Mini的散热余地更大，因为它的外壳更大，不是由电池供电的，因此是三款机器中速度最快的。但由于它不是由电池驱动的，它似乎比同等的英特尔前身优势最小。)。同样值得注意的是，售价999美元的入门级MacBook Air只有7个GPU核心，而不是8个。可以得出这样的结论：这是芯片堆积的结果，但苹果对此不予置评，这一点也就不足为奇了。

But fundamentally these are the same two MacBooks — the Pro version just has a bit more oomf thanks to its cooling system. And the M1 Mac Mini is just the same computer in a desktop enclosure.

但从根本上说，这两款Macbook是一样的， - 专业版只是因为它的冷却系统而更有吸引力。而M1 Mac Mini则与台式机外壳中的电脑一模一样。

The fact that these machines all share the same limitations, as well — a maximum of 16 GB of RAM and 2 TB of SSD storage — is not in any way an indication that Apple is regressing an iota on professional memory and storage needs. Intel-based 13-inch MacBook Pros support up to 32 GB of RAM and 4 TB of storage. Intel Mac Minis support up to 64 GB of RAM. And then there’s the 16-inch MacBook Pro, which supports up to 64 GB of RAM and 8 TB of SSD storage.

这些机器都有相同的限制，以及 - 最高16 GB的内存和2TB的固态硬盘存储 - ，这一事实绝不表明苹果在专业内存和存储需求上有丝毫的倒退。基于英特尔技术的13英寸MacBook Pro支持高达32 GB的RAM和4TB的存储空间。英特尔Mac Mini支持高达64 GB的RAM。还有16英寸的MacBook Pro，它支持高达64 GB的RAM和8 TB的固态硬盘存储。

The only Intel-based Macs that have been completely replaced by M1 models are the MacBook Airs — the Intel models of which have never offered specs of any kind that aren’t met or exceeded by the new M1 models. Apple continues to sell Intel-based 13-inch MacBook Pros and Mac Minis. That doesn’t betray a lack of confidence in Apple Silicon; it’s simply the result of the M1 only being intended for “low-power systems or small size and power efficiency”, per Apple’s keynote. Apple hasn’t yet unveiled its professional-grade M-series chips. (I’m guessing “M1X” as the name for the first high-power Mac SoC. The “M2”, when you think about it, would be the name of the next-generation SoC for MacBook Airs and lower-end MacBook Pros and Mac Minis. And I’m thinking the Mac Pro and iMac Pro might get their own series of SoCs, say, the “X1”.)

唯一完全被M1机型取代的基于英特尔的Mac是Macbook Air - ，英特尔的机型从未提供过新的M1机型无法达到或超过的任何规格。苹果继续销售基于英特尔系统的13英寸MacBook Pro和Mac Minis。这并不是说人们对苹果的硅芯片缺乏信心，而是因为按照苹果公司的主旨，M1只适用于“低功耗系统或小尺寸、高能效”。苹果尚未推出专业级M系列芯片。(我猜“M1x”是第一代高功率Mac SoC的名字。想想看，M2应该是下一代用于MacBook Air、低端MacBook Pro和Mac Mini的SoC的名字。我认为Mac Pro和iMac Pro可能会有自己的SoC系列，比如“X1”。)。

I don’t know if the upcoming higher-end Apple Silicon MacBook Pros are going to include four USB/Thunderbolt ports — this is the company that sold a 12-inch semi-premium-priced MacBook with just one port for years — but I’m damn certain that they’ll support more than 16 GB of RAM and 2 TB of storage. My sincere and I think technically reasonable hope is that the 13-inch high-end MacBook Pros will reach spec-parity with the 16-inch models, supporting up to 64 GB of RAM and 8 TB of SSD storage, and perhaps offering equivalent graphics performance. But 32 GB of RAM and 4 TB of storage in future Apple Silicon-based 13-inch MacBook Pros should be considered a given.

我不知道即将推出的更高端的苹果Silicon MacBook Pro是否会配备四个USB/迅雷接口 - 这家公司多年来一直在销售只有一个接口的12英寸半高价位MacBook，但我非常肯定它们将支持超过16 GB的内存和2TB的存储空间。我真诚地希望，我认为技术上合理的是，13英寸的高端MacBook Pro将达到与16英寸机型相当的规格，支持高达64 GB的RAM和8TB的固态硬盘存储，或许还能提供同等的图形性能。但在未来的苹果硅基13英寸MacBook Pro中，32G的RAM和4TB的存储空间应该被认为是既定的。

What Apple announced yesterday was an Apple Silicon-based MacBook Pro, not the only Apple Silicon-based MacBook Pro. And I would bet that future high-end configurations will have four USB/Thunderbolt ports — two on each side — as well.

苹果昨天发布的是一款基于硅片的苹果MacBook Pro，而不是唯一一款基于硅片的苹果MacBook Pro。我敢打赌，未来的高端配置将有四个usb/迅雷接口 - ，两边各有两个 - 接口。

Initial coverage of Apple’s M1 announcement in the greater tech sphere — the cross-platform perspective, if you will — seems to want to place the Mac’s transition to Apple Silicon in the same context as ARM-based Windows laptops. I think that’s the wrong way to look at it — and it’s only going to lead to bad assumptions right out of the gate. Apple Silicon is in an entirely different class than the ARM chips from companies like Qualcomm, with a phone-focused background. Asking if M1 Macs are going to face the same problems as ARM-based Windows laptops is like asking if Teslas are going to hit the same limits as electric scooters. ARM-based Windows PCs necessitate significant trade-offs — you get high efficiency and small form factors, but you lose performance and suffer from severely limited software compatibility. The promise of Apple Silicon is that Mac users get higher performance, longer battery life, and lots of native software and high-performance compatibility with legacy software thanks to Rosetta. It’s like having your cake, eating it too, and getting to share your cake with your friends.

在更大的技术领域，苹果M1发布的最初报道 - 跨平台的视角，如果你愿意的话， - 似乎想把Mac向苹果硅的过渡放在与基于ARM的Windows笔记本电脑相同的背景下。我认为这样看待问题是错误的， - ，这只会直接导致糟糕的假设。与高通(Qualcomm)等公司的ARM芯片相比，苹果硅芯片属于完全不同的类别，它们的背景都是手机。问M1 Mac是否会面临与基于ARM的Windows笔记本电脑相同的问题，就像问特斯拉是否会达到电动滑板车的极限一样。基于ARM的Windows PC需要进行巨大的权衡 - 您可以获得高效率和小巧的外形尺寸，但您会损失性能，并且软件兼容性受到严重限制。Apple Silicon的承诺是，多亏了Rosetta，Mac用户获得了更高的性能、更长的电池续航时间、大量的本地软件以及与传统软件的高性能兼容性。这就像拥有你的蛋糕，也吃它，并与你的朋友分享你的蛋糕。

Apple remains more enamored with the Touch Bar than many Mac enthusiasts are. There are a lot of Mac users who despise the Touch Bar, but if you want an M1 MacBook with a fan, your only choice is the MacBook Pro. There is no M1 MacBook with a fan but without a Touch Bar — and a lot of people would buy one if there were.

苹果仍然比许多Mac发烧友更迷恋Touch Bar。有很多Mac用户鄙视Touch Bar，但如果你想要一台带风扇的M1 MacBook，你唯一的选择就是MacBook Pro。没有配备风扇但没有触摸栏 - 的M1 MacBook，如果有的话，很多人都会买。

The new M1 Mac Mini is only available in Silver. The current Intel Mac Mini that remains available for sale is only available in Space Gray.

新的M1 Mac Mini只有Silver版本。目前仍在销售的英特尔Mac Mini仅在Space Gray发售。

No touchscreen support in these initial Apple Silicon Macs. That’s what I both expected and hoped for. However, this is a disappointment and seemingly a surprise to others. There are certainly elements of Big Sur that look more iOS-y, no question, but I don’t know why so many people think they look designed for touch. They don’t and they aren’t — and, says me, they shouldn’t be.

这些最初的苹果硅谷Mac没有触摸屏支持。这正是我所期待和希望的。然而，这令人失望，似乎也让其他人大吃一惊。毫无疑问，Big Sur确实有一些元素看起来更像iOS-y，但我不知道为什么这么多人认为它们看起来是为触摸而设计的。他们不是，也不是 - ，我认为他们不应该是。

The bottom line is that for over a decade, iPhones and iPads have had Apple-designed chips the competition could not and still cannot match. Now the Mac does too.

归根结底，十多年来，iPhone和iPad的芯片都是苹果设计的，竞争对手无法匹敌，现在仍然无法匹敌。现在，Mac电脑也开始这么做了。
