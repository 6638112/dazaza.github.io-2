科维德在医院的病情即将恶化。：哥伦布

2020-11-12 20:59:46

In my hospital we have 4x the COVID patients we had 8 weeks ago, ~150 staff sick or quarantined. Our dedicated COVID beds are almost 100% occupied meaning they are going to cut into other units. ICU nurses are now tripling up. No announcements, but postponing/canceling elective procedures seems pre-ordained. We can manage for now, but the curve isn’t doing anything other than exponentially increasing and flu season hasn’t hit yet.

在我的医院，我们有4倍于8周前的COVID患者，大约150名工作人员生病或被隔离。我们的专属COVID病床几乎100%都有人了，这意味着他们将切入其他病房。重症监护室的护士人数现在增加了两倍。没有宣布，但推迟/取消选修程序似乎是命中注定的。我们目前可以应付，但曲线除了呈指数级增长外，没有其他作用，流感季节还没有到来。

I can’t help but be bitter at all the people that didn’t think THEY needed to wear masks, didn’t see WHY they shouldn’t go to that party or that bar or that restaurant and just went back to life as normal because they were “just tired of it”. The blame ultimately rests with our feckless leaders who sowed doubts about all of it and were cowed and dominated by the crazy fringe.

我忍不住怨恨那些认为自己不需要戴口罩的人，不明白为什么他们不应该去那个派对、那个酒吧或那个餐厅，只是因为他们“只是厌倦了”而恢复了正常的生活。归根结底，责任在于我们无能的领导人，他们对所有这些都提出了质疑，并被疯狂的边缘分子吓倒和控制。

The healthcare system and our society is going to bear the brunt of people’s selfishness. My coworkers and I risk getting sick and dying all because people couldn’t be bothered to wear a fucking mask.

医疗体系和我们的社会将首当其冲地承受人们的自私。我和我的同事冒着生病和死亡的风险，这一切都是因为人们不愿费心戴上该死的口罩。
