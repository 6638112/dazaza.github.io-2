推特取消了对“纽约邮报”推特账户的限制，更新了其“不追溯性地推翻先前的执行”的做法。

2020-10-31 07:11:25

Twitter TWTR -21.11% on Friday unlocked the New York Post’s Twitter account, ending a stalemate between the social-media company and the newspaper stemming from the latter’s publication of stories based on documents allegedly obtained from the laptop of Hunter Biden.

推特(Twitter)上周五解锁了“纽约邮报”(New York Post)的推特账户，结束了这家社交媒体公司与该报之间的僵局，原因是后者根据据称从亨特·拜登(Hunter Biden)笔记本电脑获得的文件发表了报道。

Twitter posted Friday afternoon that it was revising its policies in a way that would enable the Post to be reinstated.

Twitter周五下午发布消息称，它正在修改政策，以使“华盛顿邮报”得以恢复。

“We believe this is fair and appropriate,” the company said in its tweet announcing the rule change.

“我们相信这是公平和适当的，”该公司在宣布规则改变的推文中说。

The Post had been unable to tweet to its 1.9 million Twitter followers since Oct. 14, the date it began publishing a series of stories based on the alleged Hunter Biden material.

自10月14日开始发布一系列基于所谓的亨特·拜登(Hunter Biden)材料的报道以来，《华盛顿邮报》一直无法向其190万Twitter追随者发推文。

Twitter at the time determined that linking to the Post stories violated the social-media company’s policies against posting material that contains personal information and is obtained via hacking. As the story broke, Twitter began preventing users from tweeting the stories and it locked the Post’s account, saying it would be unlocked only after it deleted earlier tweets that linked to the stories.

Twitter当时认定，链接到“华盛顿邮报”的报道违反了这家社交媒体公司禁止发布包含个人信息的材料的政策，这些材料是通过黑客获得的。随着故事的曝光，推特开始阻止用户发布这些故事，并锁定了“华盛顿邮报”的账户，称只有在删除了之前链接到这些故事的推文后，才会解锁。

By Oct. 16, Twitter had reversed policy and was allowing the story to be linked in tweets, but the company continued to insist that the Post remove its earlier tweets, saying that they were in violation Twitter’s policy at the time they were posted.

到10月16日，Twitter已经改变了政策，允许在推文中链接这个故事，但该公司继续坚持要求“华盛顿邮报”删除之前的推文，称这些推文在发布时违反了Twitter的政策。

That left the Post unable to tweet until Friday, when Twitter revised its policies again. In a series of tweets, the company said it was modifying its policies so that a similar situation wouldn’t occur in the future and that Twitter would “no longer restrict their account under the terms of the previous policy and they can now tweet again.”

这使得邮报直到周五才能发布推文，当时Twitter再次修改了政策。在一系列推文中，该公司表示正在修改其政策，以便未来不会再发生类似的情况，而且推特将“不再根据之前的政策条款限制他们的账户，他们现在可以再次发推文了。”

A Post spokeswoman couldn’t immediately be reached for comment, but the newspaper posted a story simultaneous with Twitter’s announcement saying that the tech company had “backed down” in its battle over the tweets.

记者无法立即联系到“华盛顿邮报”的一位女发言人请其置评，但该报在Twitter宣布的同时发布了一篇报道，称这家科技公司在围绕这些推文的斗争中已经“让步”。

Twitter’s chief executive, Jack Dorsey, had faced harsh criticism over its handling of the Post story earlier this week during testimony before the Senate Commerce Committee.

本周早些时候，Twitter首席执行官杰克·多尔西(Jack Dorsey)在参议院商务委员会作证时，因其对“华盛顿邮报”报道的处理方式而受到严厉批评。

“Who the hell elected you and put you in charge of what the media are allowed to report?” said Sen. Ted Cruz (R., Tex.) during the hearing, Wednesday.

“该死的是谁选了你，让你负责允许媒体报道什么？”德克萨斯州共和党参议员特德·克鲁兹(Ted Cruz)说。在周三的听证会上。

News Corp NWS 0.46% the corporate parent of Wall Street Journal publisher Dow Jones & Co., also owns the New York Post.

新闻集团是“华尔街日报”出版商道琼斯公司(Dow Jones&；Co.)的母公司，也是“纽约邮报”(New York Post)的所有者。
