建筑物更换专有电池组

2020-10-22 21:40:04

Sony’s MiniDisc format has been a love affair since it first came out in the 90s. I purchased one as soon as I could afford it at a shop called Dixons close to where I lived in rural England. Since that first enthusiastic purchase, I’ve collected a few other player/recorders over the years and a healthy collection of discs.

索尼的MiniDisc格式自90年代首次问世以来，一直是一件令人喜爱的事情。我一买得起就在离我住的英格兰乡村很近的一家叫Dixons的商店买了一辆。自从第一次热情购买以来，多年来我已经收集了一些其他的播放器/录音机和一些健康的光盘收藏。

Back in the day, using Sony’s SonicStage software to manage my collection was a pain. It was slow at the get go, and became increasingly choppy as tracks grew into the thousands. Rights management restrictions, imposed to stop folks from pirating perfect digital copies, were a nuisance. Yet, I loved that first MiniDisc player and the amazing little discs that slotted into it.

回到过去，使用索尼的SonicStage软件来管理我的收藏是一件痛苦的事情。它一开始就很慢，随着轨道增加到数千条，它变得越来越起伏不定。为了阻止人们盗版完美的数字拷贝而实施的版权管理限制是一件令人讨厌的事情。不过，我还是很喜欢第一台迷你盘播放器和放在里面的令人惊叹的小碟片。

Two and a half decades later, I’ve added a few more MiniDisc devices and a lot more discs in my collection. My favorite is the Sony MZ-RH1, the last MiniDisc player/recorder Sony ever produced. It is sleek and sexy, sounds amazing, can record 34 hours of music onto a single disc, and is as much a work of art as a piece of electromechanical and electro-optical engineering can achieve.

25年后，我在收藏中又增加了几个MiniDisc设备和更多的光盘。我最喜欢的是索尼MZ-RH1，这是索尼生产的最后一款迷你盘播放器/录像机。它时尚性感，听起来令人惊叹，可以将34小时的音乐录制到一张光盘上，是一件机电和光电工程所能实现的艺术品。

Unfortunately, Sony doesn’t make the lithium ion batteries for this masterpiece anymore, and the one I have, which arrived with it packed in the box, no longer accepts a charge. A quick search on eBay brings up a few new “old stock” batteries to buy, but folks want $150+.

不幸的是，索尼已经不再为这款杰作生产锂离子电池了，我买的那个和包装盒一起送来的，也不再接受充电了。在eBay上快速搜索一下，会出现几个新的“旧货”电池可供购买，但人们想要150美元以上的价格。

I found an excellent article from someone who carefully took apart their old Sony LIP-4WM and replaced the worn out battery with the best approximation version of the original they could find. The hack looked a little too thick to easily slide into the MZ-RH1, and I believe retaining both the original and the new cell’s charge protection circuit is not optimal. Also, I would need an original LIP-4WM to take apart but I only have the one and I’m not onboard with cannibalizing it.

我发现有人写了一篇很棒的文章，他小心翼翼地拆开了他们的旧索尼LIP-4WM，用他们能找到的最接近原版的电池取代了破旧的电池。黑客看起来有点太厚，不容易滑入MZ-RH1，我认为保留原电池和新电池的电荷保护电路并不是最优的。另外，我需要一个原装的LIP-4WM才能拆开，但我只有一个，而且我不会让它自相残杀。

I decided to order the same bare naked Li-ion cell and set about designing a new battery module around it. My goals were:

我决定订购同样的裸露锂离子电池，并着手围绕它设计一个新的电池模块。我的目标是：

Design so anybody with basic tools and access to a 3D printer can make their own

设计使任何拥有基本工具和3D打印机访问权限的人都可以制作自己的3D打印机。

The new battery works great, it slides in and out of the device easily, and a single charge lasts for around 10 hours of play, which is just shy of the original Sony when it was new.

新的电池工作得很好，它很容易滑入滑出设备，一次充电可以持续大约10个小时的播放，这只是略低于最初的索尼在它是新的时候。

In addition to these essentials, you’ll also need some cutting pliers to cut the brass strip, needle nose pliers to bend the brass strip, a soldering iron, and glue to affix the brass contacts and hold the Li-ion cell in the 3D printed frame.

除了这些必需品外，你还需要一些切割钳来切割黄铜条，针嘴钳来弯曲黄铜条，一个烙铁和胶水来固定黄铜触点并将锂离子电池固定在3D打印框架中。

Print the SIM-4WM.stl file. I’m using an Ultimaker and it turns out great in PLA and ToughPLA at 0.15 mm layer height. I’m sure other layer heights would work just fine too.

打印SIM-4WM.stl文件。我用的是UltiMaker，它在0.15 mm层高的PLA和ToughPLA中很棒。我相信其他层的高度也会很好。

Hold the piece of brass in your needle nose pliers with 2.5mm sticking out, then press it against something hard to bend it at a right angle.

把黄铜夹在你的尖嘴钳里，伸出2.5毫米，然后把它压在硬的东西上，把它弯成直角。

Poke the wires from the Li-ion battery cell through the two holes in the printed part. Red wire corresponding to the little ‘+’ printed into the part and black going to the ‘-’ symbol.

将锂离子电池单元的导线穿过打印部件的两个孔。红线对应于印在零件中的小‘+’，黑色线对应于‘-’符号。

Cut the wires so there is 5 mm to 10 mm poking out the end of the printed part and strip a millimeter or two of insulation from the ends.

剪断电线，使其有5毫米到10毫米的长度，露出打印部分的末端，并从末端剥离一到两毫米的绝缘层。

Tin the ends of the wires with solder and the underside of the brass right angles, and then solder the ends of the wire to each of the brass pieces.

用焊料焊接导线的末端和黄铜的直角底面，然后将导线的末端焊接到每个黄铜片上。

Apply glue to the printed plastic where the brass contacts will sit, and press each of the contacts into place. I used a UV cure adhesive but epoxy or superglue should work too.

将胶水涂抹到黄铜触点所在的打印塑料上，然后将每个触点压入到位。我用的是紫外光固化粘合剂，但环氧树脂或强力胶也可以。

Apply glue to hold the wires in place inside the frame and all down the inside edges of the frame where the cell fits inside its walls. Again I used a UV cure adhesive, but epoxy or superglue should work well too.

使用胶水将电线固定在框架内，并沿着框架的内侧边缘将电池固定在墙内。我再一次使用了紫外光固化粘合剂，但环氧树脂或强力胶也应该可以很好地工作。

And there you have it! A working replacement battery and years more pleasure listening to this delightful MiniDisc player.

现在你就知道了！一个正常工作的更换电池和更多年的愉快收听这个令人愉快的迷你磁盘播放器。
