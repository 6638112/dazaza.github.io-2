Pine Labs has received fresh funding of around $75-100 million from Stephen Mandel's hedge fund Lone Pine Capital, and others, at a valuation of $2 billion. PineLabs counts Sequoia India, Temasek, Mastercard, Paypal and Actis LLP among its investors.
