ThoughtExchange主持大型小组对话，同时保护参与者的身份，以减少性别和种族偏见，该公司在B系列融资3400万美元

2020-11-16 07:26:17

Thoughtexchange, a Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada-based advanced platform for scaling conversations, received an investment from HarbourVest Partners.

Thoughtexchange是一家总部位于加拿大不列颠哥伦比亚省温哥华的高级对话扩展平台，获得了HarbourVest Partners的投资。

Led by Dave MacLeod, co-founder & CEO, Thoughtexchange Thoughtexchange provides a voice platform that enables leaders to scale mission critical conversations to include hundreds and even thousands of people. Participants are invited to share candid thoughts and then rate others’ thoughts without knowing who shared them, eliminating an enormous list of biases such as ethnicity, gender, rank etc. Patented data analysis and AI provides deep insights and surfaces actionable intelligence to help leaders make informed decisions.

在联合创始人兼首席执行官戴夫·麦克劳德(Dave MacLeod)的带领下，Thoughtexchange Thoughtexchange提供了一个语音平台，让领导者能够将关键任务对话扩大到数百人甚至数千人。参与者被邀请分享坦诚的想法，然后在不知道是谁分享了别人的想法的情况下对其进行评分，消除了种族、性别、排名等大量偏见。专利数据分析和人工智能提供了深刻的见解和可操作的情报，以帮助领导者做出明智的决策。

The company has recently passed 100 corporate customers which include many Fortune 500 brands like Allstate Insurance, McDonalds and American Airlines.

该公司最近已超过100家企业客户，其中包括许多财富500强品牌，如好事达保险(AllState Insurance)、麦当劳(McDonalds)和美国航空(American Airlines)。
