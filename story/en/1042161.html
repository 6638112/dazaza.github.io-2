Bitcoin Surges 50% in Just One Month. CNN Ponders 'Insane' Record Run
比特币在短短一个月内飙升了50％。 美国有线电视新闻网思考“疯狂”的记录运行

2020-12-28 16:49:20

New York (CNN Business)Bitcoin is crashing -- upward. It just passed $28,000 and shows no signs of stopping.

纽约（CNN商业）比特币崩溃-向上。它刚刚超过了28,000美元，并且没有停止的迹象。

The digital currency has a market value north of $500 billion. Think Bitcoin is just a fad? It's worth more than Visa ( V) or Mastercard ( MA). Or Walmart ( WMT).

数字货币的市值超过5,000亿美元。认为比特币只是一种时尚？它的价值超过Visa（V）或Mastercard（MA）。或沃尔玛（WMT）。

Bitcoin passed $20,000 for the first time just 11 days ago. Now it's knocking at $30,000's door.

比特币在11天前首次超过20,000美元。现在，它要价30,000美元。

Its rapid rise has been remarkable -- or insane, depending on your appetite for risk. But there's some logic to the run-up: Investors are pouring money into bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies during the Covid-19 pandemic as the Federal Reserve sent interest rates near zero (and expects to keep them there for several more years), severely weakening the US dollar.

它的迅速崛起令人瞩目-或发疯，这取决于您的风险偏好。但这有一定的逻辑：在美联储（Covid-19）大流行期间，由于美联储将利率降至接近零，投资者正在向比特币和其他加密货币投入资金（并希望将利率保持在这几年以上）。 ，严重削弱了美元。

That makes bitcoin, comparatively, an attractive currency. There's a set limit to the number of bitcoins on the planet, and investors believe that once the supply runs out, the digital coin's value can only increase.

相对而言，这使比特币成为一种有吸引力的货币。对地球上的比特币数量有一个设定的限制，投资者认为，一旦供应耗尽，数字硬币的价值只能增加。

Also aiding in bitcoin's soaring valuation: Big, name-brand investors are stockpiling it, and huge consumer companies are embracing it. That's adding a dose of validity and appeal to cryptocurrency for mainstream investors. For example, a top executive at BlackRock ( BLK) recently said the cryptocurrency can replace gold, and Square ( SQ) and PayPal ( PYPL) have both embraced bitcoin.

这也有助于提高比特币的估值：大型，知名品牌的投资者正在储备它，而大型的消费公司正在拥抱它。这为主流投资者增加了一定的有效性和吸引力。例如，贝莱德（BLK）的一位高管最近表示，这种加密货币可以代替黄金，而Square（SQ）和PayPal（PYPL）都采用了比特币。

As bitcoin surges to all-time highs, cryptocurrency brokerage Coinbase, the most prominent cryptocurrency exchange, has signaled its intent to go public.

随着比特币飙升至历史新高，最著名的加密货币交易所-加密货币经纪公司Coinbase已表明了其上市的意图。

Still, the recent cryptocurrency surge is showing signs of a melt-up -- over-enthusiasm fueled by the fear of missing out, not simply market fundamentals. Take Elon Musk's sarcastic tweet about bitcoin rival Dogecoin last week: The digital coin, which itself was constructed as a cryptocurrency parody, shot up 20% immediately after Musk threw his support behind it on Twitter.

尽管如此，最近的加密货币激增仍显示出崩溃的迹象-由于担心会错过市场而不仅仅是市场基本面加剧了过度热情。以埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）上周对比特币竞争对手狗狗币（Dogecoin）的讽刺推文为例：该数字硬币本身被构造为一种加密货币的模仿，在马斯克在Twitter上表示支持后立即飙升了20％。

Anthony Scaramucci, Skybridge Capital's founder, has a big stake in bitcoin, but even he says people need to watch out. He told CNN Business earlier this month that it could be a solid addition to the average investor's portfolio -- but you've got to have the stomach for it.

Skybridge Capital的创始人Anthony Scaramucci在比特币中拥有很大股份，但即使他说人们也需要提防。他在本月初向CNN Business表示，这可能是对普通投资者投资组合的坚实补充-但您必须对此有所准备。

On CNN Business' "Markets Now" live show earlier this month, Scaramucci said people have begun to accept bitcoin -- and since it appears in so few portfolios, it has plenty of room to grow. Still, bitcoin is a volatile asset and will be a risky holding if you invest in it.

"This thing has a tendency to crash up," he said. "It is due for a correction, and these corrections can be violent."

But he also highlighted bitcoin's staying power over the course of the past decade: If you took $1 and put 99 cents of it in cash and a penny in bitcoin, that investment strategy would have outperformed $1 invested in the S&P 500 ( SPX) over the last 10 years, he noted.

但他还强调了比特币在过去十年中的持久力：如果您花了1美元，将其99美分的现金和1便士的比特币投入，该投资策略的表现将超过在标准普尔投资的1美元。他指出，过去10年中有500（SPX）。

"Bitcoin's best days are ahead of it, but it's going to be volatile and I think people need to be prepared for it," Scaramucci told CNN Business.
