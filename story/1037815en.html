Stay-at-home order issued for San Francisco Bay Area, ahead of state mandate
在州政府授权之前发布了旧金山湾区的“不在家”订单

2020-12-05 06:03:16

San Francisco Bay Area health officials announced on Friday that they would implement California's new stay-at-home order early despite having more than 15% capacity in their intensive-care units.

旧金山湾区卫生官员周五宣布，尽管其重症监护病房的产能超过15％，他们仍将尽早实施加利福尼亚州的新在家保单。

"Today is a really tough day," San Francisco Mayor London Breed said during a press briefing. "Our hospitalization rates are rising locally, especially in our ICU right now. And just as importantly, hospitalizations are rising everywhere, so if we run out of beds, there won't be another county that can help us."

＆＃34;今天是非常艰难的一天，＆＃34;旧金山市长伦敦品种在新闻发布会上说。 ＆＃34;我们当地的住院率正在上升，尤其是在目前的ICU中。同样重要的是，各地的住院率都在上升，所以如果我们没有床铺，就不会有另一个县可以帮助我们。

On Thursday, Gov. Gavin Newsom said the state would be split into five regions — the Bay Area, Greater Sacramento, Northern California, San Joaquin Valley and Southern California. If the remaining ICU capacity in a region falls below 15%, it will trigger a three-week stay-at-home order, Newsom said.

州长加文·纽瑟姆（Gavin Newsom）周四表示，该州将分为五个地区-湾区，大萨克拉曼多，北加利福尼亚，圣华金谷和南加利福尼亚。 Newsom说，如果该地区剩余的ICU容量降至15％以下，则将触发三周的在家下单。

The order would require bars, wineries, personal services, hair salons and barbershops to temporarily close. Personal services are businesses like nail salons, tattoo parlors and body waxing, according to the state's  website.

该命令将要求酒吧，酒厂，个人服务，美发沙龙和理发店暂时关闭。根据该州的网站，个人服务是指美甲沙龙，纹身店和身体打蜡等业务。

Schools that meet the state's health requirements and critical infrastructure would be allowed to remain open, and retail stores could operate at 20% capacity and restaurants would be allowed to offer take-out and delivery, Newsom said

Newsom说，符合州卫生要求和关键基础设施的学校将被允许开放，零售商店的运力可达到20％，餐馆将被允许提供外卖和送货服务。

While none of the regions met the ICU threshold to trigger the stay-at-home order on Thursday, Newsom warned that every area was projected to drop below 15% ICU capacity at some point in December. The Bay Area was expected to be the last region to reach that mark, he said.

虽然没有一个地区达到周四触发居家定单的ICU门槛，但Newsom警告说，预计12月某个地区每个地区的ICU容量都将降至15％以下。他说，预计湾区将是达到这一目标的最后一个地区。

Health officers for the counties of Alameda, Contra Costa, Marin, San Francisco and Santa Clara as well as the city of Berkeley made the joint announcement on Friday, saying they would implement the order early.

阿拉米达，孔戴科斯塔，马林，旧金山和圣克拉拉以及伯克利市的县卫生官员在周五宣布了联合声明，称他们将尽早执行该命令。

However, the announcement did not include some counties in the Bay Area region, as defined by the state's order, including Santa Cruz, Monterey, Napa, San Mateo, Santa Cruz, Solano, Sonoma.

但是，该公告并未包括州令所定义的海湾地区的一些县，包括圣克鲁斯，蒙特雷，纳帕，圣马特奥，圣克鲁斯，索拉诺，索诺玛。

"It takes several weeks for new restrictions to slow rising hospitalizations and waiting until only 15 percent of a region's ICU beds are available is just too late," said Dr. Tomas Aragon, San Francisco health officer, in a statement. "Many heavily impacted parts of our region already have less than 15 percent of ICU beds available, and the time to act is now."

新的限制措施要花几周的时间才能减缓住院率的上升，并且等到该地区只有15％的ICU病床可用时，才来不及了，＆＃34;旧金山卫生官员托马斯·阿拉贡（Tomas Aragon）博士在一份声明中说。 ＆＃34;我们地区许多受灾最严重的地区已经拥有不到15％的ICU病床，现在该采取行动了。

Breed in a tweet after Newsom's announcement on Thursday said that while the order didn't apply to San Francisco yet, the county was "looking closely at local data and talking to our neighboring counties about next steps."

在Newsom星期四宣布之后，一条推文中说，虽然该命令还不适用于旧金山，但该县正在密切关注当地数据，并与我们邻近的县谈论下一步的措施。 。＆＃34;

"I don't want do any of this," Breed said. "I know this means people's jobs, their businesses, their livelihoods are at stake. This is going to be painful."
