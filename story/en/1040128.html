Sources: Facebook is building a tool within Marketplace that lets users find and book services like home repairs, competing with Fiverr, TaskRabbit, and others
资料来源：Facebook正在市场中构建一个工具，该工具可让用户查找和预订房屋维修等服务，与Fiverr，TaskRabbit等竞争

2020-12-17 01:23:22

Facebook is planning to let its users find and book services through its app, a move that could position the social media giant as a competitor to gig economy marketplaces such as TaskRabbit, Fiverr, and Angie’s List.

Facebook计划让用户通过其应用程序查找和预订服务，此举可能使这家社交媒体巨头成为TaskRabbit，Fiverr和Angie's List等演出市场的竞争对手。

The social network is building a team to focus on services, potentially including home repairs or freelance jobs, within its larger Marketplace classifieds group, according to two people familiar with the matter. The move signals Facebook’s growing ambition to be more of a force in online commerce, which it sees as a way to improve its ad targeting and diversify its business through payments.

两名知情人士表示，该社交网络正在组建一个团队，专注于其较大的Marketplace分类组内的服务，可能包括房屋维修或自由职业。此举表明，Facebook越来越渴望在在线商务中发挥更大的作用，它认为这是改善广告定位和通过付款实现业务多元化的一种方式。
