level 2 Oh I know that feeling of being overwhelmed by this project sooooo well. I spend months trying not to despair and only look at the one specific problem currently ahead. Here is an 'as short as I can make it'- writeup :)

2级噢，我知道被这个项目淹没的感觉很好。我花了几个月的时间试图不感到绝望，只看当前的一个具体问题。这是一个我能做到的简短内容：）

It just started with this image in my head: https://vimeo.com/101996957 and the thought that most PoV-Displays were kiind of uncool because they are often either just 2D or can only play some canned animations.

它只是从我脑海中的这张图片开始：https：//vimeo.com/101996957，并认为大多数PoV显示器都是不酷的，因为它们通常只是2D或只能播放某些罐装动画。

I spend the first 6 weeks (I kid you not) just trying to get a stable, still image onto the display with my ESP32. Most libraries are made for 30fps color, I needed 2000fps monochrome, so I couldn't use those.

我花了头6周的时间（我没告诉你），只是想用我的ESP32将稳定的静止图像显示在显示屏上。大多数库都是为30fps的颜色制作的，我需要2000fps的单色，所以我不能使用它们。

After 6 weeks I had one solid color without flickering. Then I spend a lot of time to get TCP/IP data transmission working between the ESP32 on the Display and my computer. I tried to send 30fps for another week. So much trial and error. Of-by-one-errors, logical problems, electrical finicyness.

6周后，我只有一种纯色而没有闪烁。然后，我花了很多时间来使人机界面上的ESP32与计算机之间的TCP / IP数据传输正常。我尝试将30fps发送另外一周。如此反复试验。一次错误，逻辑问题，电气性。

After that worked I tried to send every one of those 30 frames 30 times (my minimum target fps was 900) within one second. If the animation still looked smooth to me, it shouldve displayed 900 frames. (I didn't have a camera that fast to reliably check).

在工作之后，我尝试在一秒钟内将30帧中的每帧发送30次（我的最低目标fps为900）。如果动画对我来说仍然很平滑，则它应该显示900帧。 （我没有可快速可靠检查的相机）。

For the content-calculation I learned the basics of compute-shaders (which sounds waaaay more scary than it is. Its basically just a for-loop on steroids). The algorithm was about 3 days of coffee-and-whiteboard-and-coffee-and-whiteboard time and then it made click and took about 10 lines of code in the end :)

对于内容计算，我了解了计算着色器的基础（听起来比实际更可怕。它基本上只是类固醇的for循环）。该算法大约需要3天的咖啡，白板，咖啡和白板时间，然后单击鼠标，最后花费大约10行代码：)

So yeah: just methodical work from the ground up, just starting to solve some problems, then some more, then run away from some (try to do it elegantly!), solve some more and eventually... yeah, your deadline will arrive before there are no more problems. But you get the point!

所以，是的：只是从头开始有条不紊的工作，才刚开始解决一些问题，然后再解决一些问题，然后逃避一些问题（尝试做得很优雅！），再解决一些问题，最终……是的，您的最后期限将到在没有其他问题之前。但是你明白了！
