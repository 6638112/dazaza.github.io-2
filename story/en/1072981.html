An Ode to Chocolate Chip Ice Cream
巧克力冰激凌颂歌

2022-02-14 08:56:04

There is an epidemic sweeping ice cream shelves across America (or, well, maybe just Manhattan) and we need to talk about it.

有一种流行病席卷了美国（或者，嗯，也许只是曼哈顿）的冰淇淋货架，我们需要谈谈它。

I’ve scoured the cases at Whole Foods. I’ve dug through the backs of freezers at CVS and Duane Reade. I’ve journeyed to Gristedes. I’ve plumbed the depths of even Fresh Direct — all with no avail.

我在Whole Foods上查过了。我在CVS和Duane Reade的冰箱后面翻了翻。我去了格里斯泰德。即使是最新的Direct，我也深陷其中，但都无济于事。

I’m not going to lie, I’ve considered buying an ice cream maker, I’m that desperate.

我不会撒谎，我考虑过买一台冰淇淋机，我真是太绝望了。

Let’s be clear: Mint chocolate chip, chocolate chip cookie dough, and chocolate chocolate chip are not chocolate chip ice creams. We’re talking about a vanilla, or a sweet cream, with little chunks of chocolate in it. That’s it. Hell, my by-the-scoop ice cream joints around me don’t even have that.

让我们明确一点：薄荷巧克力片、巧克力片饼干面团和巧克力片不是巧克力片冰淇淋。我们说的是香草或甜奶油，里面有小块巧克力。就这样。见鬼，我身边的冰淇淋店连这个都没有。

What has happened to the world that we’ve lost the simple things, the little graces like scooping into plain ice cream to bite down on a deliciously chilly chunk of chocolate?

世界上发生了什么事，我们失去了简单的东西，失去了像舀进普通冰激凌咬下美味冰凉的巧克力块这样的小优雅？
