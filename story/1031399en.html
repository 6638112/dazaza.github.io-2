新美国宣言。在人类社会中时不时地

2020-10-27 11:14:48

From the People of the United States of America: From time to time in human societies, things get so bad with the governments that we set up that we have to take a step back, stop being citizens of that government and just be basic humans again, loyal only to the primary needs of humanity. This is one of those times and it’s only fair if we are going to take such a drastic step, that we first explain why. We owe everybody that.

来自美利坚合众国人民的话：在人类社会，情况不时变得如此糟糕，以至于我们成立的政府不得不后退一步，停止成为那个政府的公民，重新成为基本的人，只忠于人类的基本需求。这就是其中之一，如果我们要采取如此激烈的步骤，我们首先解释为什么，这才是公平的。这是我们欠每个人的。

First of all, we start by taking for granted that nobody’s life should matter more than anyone else’s. Everyone should have the right to live their life without people oppressing them. At minimum everyone should have the right to live. Nobody should be prevented from moving around freely or doing whatever they want as long as they are not causing harm to someone else. More than that, every human has the right to seek out things that bring them joy.

首先，我们从理所当然地认为，没有人的生命应该比其他任何人的生命更重要。每个人都应该有权利过自己的生活，而不受别人的压迫。至少每个人都应该有生存的权利。只要不对他人造成伤害，任何人都不应该被阻止自由活动或做他们想做的任何事情。更重要的是，每个人都有权利去寻找能给他们带来快乐的东西。

The whole reason we create governments is so that communities can come together and agree among themselves to set up structures that make sure that ALL the humans among them have access to these fundamental needs. If the structure that they set up stops functioning properly, if it becomes corrupted, if it stops serving the needs of the people, then it is critical that those humans be allowed to dismantle that structure in order to build one that does a better job. They need to be allowed to create new structures that are better at keeping them safe and happy.

我们创建政府的全部原因是，社区可以走到一起，在他们之间达成一致，建立结构，确保他们中的所有人都能获得这些基本需求。如果他们建立的结构不能正常运作，如果它变得腐败，如果它不再为人民的需求服务，那么关键是允许那些人拆除这个结构，以便建立一个工作更好的结构。他们需要被允许创建新的结构，以便更好地保持他们的安全和快乐。

Nobody is suggesting that we take the dismantling of a government lightly. Things must have gotten really bad for this to be the only option left. Humans by nature don’t like change and even when they’re being harmed, they’re more likely to just endure that harm than to break down a whole system that they’re comfortable with and used to. But if things get so bad that the government starts abusing and harming its citizens in the name of gaining more power, it’s every human’s moral duty to burn that government down and build one that protects them better from would-be dictators.

没有人建议我们对解散政府掉以轻心。事情一定变得非常糟糕，这是唯一的选择。人类天生不喜欢变化，即使当他们受到伤害时，他们也更有可能只是忍受这种伤害，而不是破坏他们熟悉和习惯的整个系统。但是，如果情况变得如此糟糕，以至于政府开始以获得更多权力的名义虐待和伤害公民，那么每个人都有道德责任烧毁这个政府，建立一个更好地保护他们免受潜在独裁者伤害的政府。

The American people are now facing down these exact same kinds of abuses. We have tried to be patient, but now we’re fed up and the whole thing needs to come down.

美国人民现在正面临着完全相同的虐待行为。我们试着耐心等待，但现在我们受够了，整件事需要平息下来。

The entire Trump administration has been a long list of human and civil rights abuses and every single thing he has done has been to gain absolute power over the government.

整个特朗普政府一直是一长串侵犯人权和公民权利的行为，他所做的每一件事都是为了获得对政府的绝对权力。

He has interfered with state Governors’ abilities to take care of their states in times of crisis, constantly breaking promises and being unreliable, just to wear them down so that they will do whatever he wants and then neglects them even when they do it.

他干预州长在危机时期照顾他们州的能力，不断违背承诺，变得不可靠，只是为了让他们做他想做的任何事情，然后即使他们做了也忽视了他们。

He has abused the powers of the Presidency, leveraging people’s rights for business purposes.

他滥用总统的权力，将人民的权利用于商业目的。

He turns public events into personal campaign stunts, wearing all the rational people down with his antics and erratic behavior

他把公众事件变成个人竞选噱头，用他的滑稽动作和反复无常的行为把所有理性的人都累垮了。

He has repeatedly fired the heads of critical agencies for refusing to support his illegal and abusive orders.

他多次解雇关键机构的负责人，因为他们拒绝支持他的非法和辱骂命令。

He routinely leaves these positions unfilled so that Congress doesn’t have a formal say in confirming nominations. This allows him to keep unconfirmed lackeys at the heads of agencies where Congress can’t touch them and they can sabotage and exploit the agencies without consequence, leaving the public without the safety those agencies provide. This causes chaos.

他通常会让这些职位空缺，这样国会在确认提名时就不会有正式的发言权。这使得他可以将未经证实的走狗留在国会无法接触的机构负责人，他们可以破坏和剥削这些机构，而不会造成后果，让公众失去这些机构提供的安全。这会造成混乱。

He has imposed harsh immigration laws and is directly attacking the economies of States and cities that allow people he doesn’t like into their borders.

他实施了严厉的移民法，并直接攻击允许他不喜欢的人进入边境的州和城市的经济。

He has rigged the Judicial system, conspiring with Congress to break rules to secure his judge appointments.

他操纵了司法系统，与国会合谋违反规则，以确保他的法官任命。

He has flooded our cities with militarized personnel against the wishes of local populations and their governments.

他违背当地居民和政府的意愿，在我们的城市里充斥着军事化的人员。

He glorifies violent law enforcement and elevates them above the rest of the humans in the country

他颂扬暴力执法，并将他们提升到这个国家的其他人类之上。

He has corrupted the Justice Department and Congress, using them to help him create a wholly unconstitutional set of policies

他腐化了司法部和国会，利用它们帮助他制定了一套完全违宪的政策

He pardons and covers for crimes committed by these henchmen and refuses to allow any accountability for their treason

他赦免并掩盖了这些追随者所犯下的罪行，并拒绝对他们的叛国行为追究任何责任。

He has leveraged the oversized jurisdiction of the Border Patrol to impose their power on areas and people they were never intended to.

他利用边境巡逻队超大的管辖权，将他们的权力强加给他们从未打算过的地区和人民。

He has trampled the rule of law, all protocol and policy. He shows no respect whatsoever for the institutions of government.

他践踏了法治，践踏了所有的礼仪和政策。他丝毫不尊重政府机构。

He has withdrawn us from crucial environmental treaties, deregulated industry, refused aid for wildfires, and has allowed over 200,000 people to die needlessly from a preventable pandemic

他让我们退出了重要的环境条约，放松了对工业的管制，拒绝了对野火的援助，并允许20多万人不必要地死于一场可预防的大流行。

He turns dissidents against each other, threatening them with high crimes if they don’t turn against their fellow protestors.

他让持不同政见者相互反对，威胁说，如果他们不反对其他抗议者，就会以重罪相威胁。

He starts riots and leverages those brutal, merciless folks embedded in our every community, the police, whose known rules of engagement are “anyone who’s not a cop can die”

他挑起骚乱，利用那些植根于我们每个社区的残暴、无情的人，警察，他们已知的交战规则是“任何不是警察的人都会死”

Over and over again we have pointed these things out. We have complained. We have petitioned. We protested in the streets. None of this has worked and instead of listening to us, Trump has merely responded with increased violence. To put it plainly, a President whose every act is the same as a dictator isn’t fit to run a country. And a government who lets him isn’t fit to govern.

我们一次又一次地指出了这些事情。我们已经投诉过了。我们已经请愿了。我们在街上抗议。所有这些都没有奏效，特朗普没有听取我们的意见，只是以增加暴力来回应。坦率地说，一个一举一动都像独裁者的总统不适合治理一个国家。一个允许他执政的政府是不适合执政的。

We tried to be empathetic to those who support Trump and his nationalism. We tried to give them the heads up that they had made a terrible choice. We tried to remind them that many of them were immigrants and that they should think twice about how we deny others entry. We tried to appeal to their sense of honor. To their sense of civics. To their sense of duty. We tried to appeal to the fact that we’re all in this together and that we are all one nation. We tried to explain that backing authoritarianism would be terrible for everyone. But they wouldn’t listen. We tried. We wanted to fight Trump together. But they mock justice and shun the idea of unity. So nothing personal, but they picked a side.

我们试图同情那些支持特朗普和他的民族主义的人。我们试图给他们提个醒，让他们知道他们做了一个糟糕的选择。我们试图提醒他们，他们中的许多人都是移民，在我们如何拒绝别人入境时，他们应该三思而后行。我们试图唤起他们的荣誉感。他们的公民意识。他们的责任感。我们试图呼吁这样一个事实，即我们都在这件事上，我们都是一个国家。我们试图解释说，支持威权主义对每个人都是可怕的。但他们不听。我们试过了。我们想一起对抗特朗普。但他们嘲笑正义，回避团结的想法。所以不是针对你，但他们选择了立场。

So we, the true Patriots of the United States of America, in the name of all that is good and holy, declare that we aren’t standing for it anymore. This country failed to live up to its promises and it’s time to build something new and better.

因此，我们，真正的美利坚合众国爱国者，以所有善良和神圣的名义，宣布我们不再支持它。这个国家没有兑现它的承诺，现在是时候建设一些新的、更好的东西了。

We intend to and we swear our lives, our money, and our sacred honor to each other.

我们打算，我们发誓，我们的生命、我们的金钱和我们神圣的荣誉都属于彼此。
