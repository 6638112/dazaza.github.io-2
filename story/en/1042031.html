The Site of Hawaii's First Tesla Supercharger? Probably Larry Ellison's 3,400-Person Island
夏威夷第一台特斯拉增压器的所在地？ 大概是拉里·埃里森（Larry Ellison）的3,400人岛

2020-12-27 22:47:59

Lanai Island, a former pineapple plantation that was almost entirely (98%) purchased by Oracle founder and Tesla board member Larry Ellison for $300 million in 2012, is the first in Hawaii to see a Supercharger permit filed by Tesla.

拉奈岛（Lanai Island）是一个前菠萝种植园，2012年几乎全部（98％）由甲骨文创始人和特斯拉董事会成员拉里·埃里森（Larry Ellison）以3亿美元的价格收购，是夏威夷第一个获得特斯拉申请超级充电器许可证的地方。

The permit, filed in the county named of its bigger neighboring Island of Maui, can be seen here and contains the following language:

可以在此处看到以较大邻国毛伊岛命名的许可证，其中包含以下语言：

ELECTRICAL WORK INCLUDES INSTALLATION OF UNDERGROUND HECO SERVICE (480/277V, 3PH) AND PAD MOUNT METERING SWITCHBOARD. INSTALL (2) TESLA SUPER CHARGER CABINETS FOR (4) TESLA WALL CONNECTORS. INSTALL UNDERGROUND HECO SERVICE (208/120V, 3PH) AND PAD MOUNT METERING SWTICHBOARD. INSTALL (36) TESLA WALL CONNECTORS. INSTALL SOLAR POWERED LIGHT POLES.

电气工作包括安装地下HECO服务（480 / 277V，3PH）和PAD MOUNT METERING SWITCHBOARD。为（4）TESLA壁式连接器安装（2）TESLA超级充电器柜。安装地下HECO服务（208 / 120V，3PH）和垫子安装测井仪。安装（36）TESLA墙壁连接器。安装太阳能灯杆。

Note: Partitions in handholes not required due to 600V insulation for all conductors. Conductors with similar voltages/electrical systems should be idetified and bundled. New Solar Powered Light Poles exempt from fees.

注意：由于所有导体均为600V绝缘，因此不需要在手孔中进行分区。具有相似电压/电气系统的导体应被识别和捆扎。新的太阳能灯杆免收费用。

Fee Summary: 1 Elec Service (480/277V, 3Ph) 800-1200A, 1 Elec Service (208/120V, 3Ph) >1200A, 1 Feeder 800-1200A, 1 Feeder >1200A, 38 Misc Branch Circuits

费用摘要：1条Elec服务（480 / 277V，3Ph）800-1200A，1条Elec服务（208 / 120V，3Ph）＆gt; 1200A，1条馈线800-1200A，1条馈线＆gt; 1200A，38个杂项分支电路

The 145-square-mile island doesn’t have any traffic lights, only 30ish miles of paved roads and the 3,400 person population lives almost exclusively in the small Lana’i City, pictured below, with the proposed Supercharger location (via Supercharge.info):

这座145平方英里的小岛没有任何交通信号灯，只有30英里的铺砌道路，而3400人的人口几乎完全生活在拉纳市小城（如下图所示），其中拟议的增压器位置（通过Supercharge.info） ）：

This would seem to indicate that the chargers would be of the Urban Supercharger variety and in likely service of Larry Ellison’s Four Seasons Hotels, which rely on Model X vehicles to shuttle guests to and from the airport and around the island’s luxury amenities.

这似乎表明这些充电器将属于Urban Supercharger（城市增压器）类型，并且可能会为Larry Ellison的“四季酒店”提供服务，该酒店依靠Model X车辆往返机场和岛上的豪华设施往返于宾客。

Ellison plans to convert the island’s power from diesel to solar/battery, and obviously Tesla’s expertise here is likely to be tapped.

埃里森计划将岛上的电力从柴油转换为太阳能/电池，显然特斯拉在这里的专业知识很可能会得到利用。
