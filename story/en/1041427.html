3D Modeling Finds Surprising Source of the Shots That Killed Iranian Scientist
3D建模发现杀死伊朗科学家的令人惊讶的镜头来源

2020-12-23 21:15:42

A 3D model of the scene of the assassination of Iran’s top nuclear scientist, Mohsen Fakhrizadeh, reveals a surprising result: all the bullets fired seem to have originated from the same place, apparently supporting the highly contested official explanation of the killing. But there may be more to the case than meets the 3D eye.

伊朗最高核科学家莫森·法赫里扎德（Mohsen Fakhrizadeh）被暗杀的场景的3D模型揭示了一个令人惊讶的结果：所有发射的子弹似乎都来自同一地方，显然支持了有关这场杀戮的备受争议的官方解释。但是，这种情况可能不止是遇到3D眼睛。

The Iranian authorities have insisted that the assassination was carried out with a remote-controlled machinegun in the back of a Nissan pickup truck parked by the side of the road, which then blew up. Other accounts suggest the attack was carried out by up to 12 gunmen who then fled the scene.

伊朗当局坚持说，暗杀是在停在路边的日产皮卡车后部用遥控机关枪进行的，然后将其炸毁。其他说法表明，袭击是由多达12名枪手进行的，他们随后逃离现场。

The modelling was carried by one of a growing army of online OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) analysts using freely-available data to investigate crimes, an approach made famous by the Bellingcat website. The anonymous analyst, who uses the handle Putin Is A Virus, and was supported by the OsintEditor community, is a video game designer and used the Unity 3D simulation software to recreate the scene of the killing.

这种建模是由不断增长的在线OSINT（开放源代码情报）分析师队伍之一使用免费提供的数据来调查犯罪的方法，这种方法在Bellingcat网站上广为人知。这位匿名分析员使用视频播放器Putin Is A Virus，并得到OsintEditor社区的支持，是一位视频游戏设计师，他使用Unity 3D模拟软件重新创建了杀戮现场。

His model, which is interactive and comes with explanatory notes, can be found here. (Keyboard and mouse required).

他的模型是交互式的，带有解释性注释，可以在这里找到。 （需要键盘和鼠标）。

“I approached the investigation believing I could disprove what I considered the absurd theory of an automated turret,” the analyst told me. “The first step was to precisely map everything, bullet holes included. Once I had everything in the most accurate place possible I assigned a number to entry and exit holes.”

这位分析师告诉我：“我参加了调查，认为我可以反驳我认为是自动炮塔的荒谬理论。” “第一步是精确绘制所有内容，包括弹孔。一旦将所有东西都放在最准确的位置，我就会为进出孔分配一个数字。”

This gave 120 possible combinations of entry and exit holes for the five bullets which left them.

这给出了留给五个子弹的120个进出孔的可能组合。

“For each bullet of each of the 120 combinations, I then created 10,000 possible trajectories assuming a max horizontal deviation of 2.5 degrees and max vertical deviation of 6 degrees, as bullets could have deviated once inside the vehicle.”

“然后针对120种组合中的每种子弹，我创建了10,000条可能的轨迹，假设最大水平偏差为2.5度，最大垂直偏差为6度，因为子弹一旦在车内就可能偏离。”

After removing those that were impossible, there were still over two million possible bullet trajectories. The analyst then divided the scene into a grid of spheres, known as volume cells or voxels, each 0.4 meters in diameter. If a sphere intersected with one or more possible trajectories for each of the five bullets analyzed, it would be considered a valid firing position.

除去那些不可能的子弹后，仍有超过200万种子弹弹道。然后，分析人员将场景划分为球形网格，称为体积单元或体素，每个直径为0.4米。如果一个球体与被分析的五个子弹的每一个相交有一个或多个可能的轨迹，则将其视为有效的射击位置。

“It appears that there is a single, possible, firing position responsible for all of the bullet holes, and that position is exactly in the pickup rear,” concludes the analyst. “I ended up with the opposite result to what I expected.”

分析师总结说：“似乎有一个可能的射击位置负责所有弹孔，而该位置恰好在拾音器后部。” “最终我得到了与预期相反的结果。”

The method was intended to see if a single firing position was possible, but cannot exclude the possibility, however improbable, of multiple firing positions.

该方法旨在查看单个点火位置是否可能，但不能排除多个点火位置的可能性（无论多么不可能）。

The Iranian authorities claim that the car stopped after it was hit by a bullet in the radiator — there are signs of damage there — and was then fired on. Bloodstains by the road suggest that Fakhrizadeh was carried out of the car and died on the road, or he may have been hit by a second burst.

伊朗当局声称，这辆汽车在散热器上被子弹击中后停了下来，那里有损坏的迹象，然后被开火。道路上的血迹表明，法赫扎迪（Fakhrizadeh）被带出汽车，在道路上死亡，或者他可能被第二次爆炸击中。

These results are impressive, according to Philip Boyce, a ballistics expert with decades of experience at U.K. company Matrix Legal & Forensic.

据英国Matrix Legal＆amp; Company公司数十年经验的弹道专家Philip Boyce称，这些结果令人印象深刻。法医。

“If you have an entry and an exit hole, then software can plot the trajectory with a high degree of confidence,” says Boyce.

“如果您有一个入口和一个出口孔，则软件可以高度自信地绘制轨迹，” Boyce说。

Boyce notes that lining up bullet holes is more straightforward for long-range attacks like this one, rather than situations where the shooter is a few meters away.

博伊斯指出，像这样的远程攻击要比在几米远的射手情况下更容易对准弹孔。

“With multiple bullet holes the process is more accurate the further away the shooter is,” says Boyce. “At 150 meters you should be able to pinpoint them. This is assuming that the target and the shooter are stationary.”

博伊斯说：“有多个弹孔，射手越远，过程越准确。” “在150米处，您应该能够找到它们。这是假设目标和射手是静止的。”

The weapon used may be something like the Smash Hopper portable remote firing station unveiled by SmartShooter earlier this year. This can be fitted with a machinegun or other weapon for precision attacks; the makers claim it is highly accurate and suitable for ambushes and remote offensive action. Improvised remote-controlled weapons aimed with robot servos have been used in Syria and elsewhere in recent conflicts.

使用的武器可能类似于SmartShooter今年早些时候推出的Smash Hopper便携式远程发射站。可以装配机枪或其他武器进行精确攻击；制造商声称它是高度准确的，适合伏击和远程进攻行动。在叙利亚和其他最近发生冲突的地方，已使用针对机器人伺服器的即兴遥控武器。

The use of this type of weapon is not wholly implausible, according to counterterrorism expert Robert Bunker of C/O Futures, LLC .

根据C / O Futures，LLC的反恐专家Robert Bunker的说法，使用这种武器并不是完全不可行的。

“The Iranian account is reminiscent of a Dr. Evil plot from an Austin Powers movie. While improbable, it is not as far-fetched as it may currently sound,” says Bunker. “In my own writings in the past I have discussed a threat scenario in which an IED was used to destroy a terrorist deployed remote-controlled sniper rifle after it was utilized.”

“伊朗人的叙述让人想起奥斯汀·鲍尔斯电影中的邪恶博士阴谋。尽管不太可能，但它并不像现在听起来那么牵强。”邦克说。 “过去我曾在自己的著作中讨论过一种威胁情景，即简易爆炸装置在使用恐怖分子部署的遥控狙击步枪后，便被用来摧毁它。”

However, Bunker notes that everything we know is controlled by the Iranian authorities who have only released the information they wish to share, none of which can be verified.

但是，邦克指出，我们所知道的一切都是由伊朗当局控制的，伊朗当局只发布了他们希望共享的信息，没有一个可以被核实。

“Part of the issue is what has been removed, added or staged at the incident scene. The best misinformation is based on some truth and creates cohesive narratives with the reader filling in the blanks. This way our brains work against themselves,” says Bunker.

“问题的一部分是在事件现场已删除，添加或上演的内容。最好的错误信息是基于某些事实，并在读者填补空白的情况下产生了凝聚力的叙述。这样，我们的大脑就会对自己不利。”邦克说。

Bunker points to numerous questions that surround the official account. In particular he is bothered by the carloads of bodyguards who supposedly accompanied Fakhrizadeh. Why is there no sign they played any part in the action? Why did the advance guards not spot the ambush vehicle? Why did Fakhrizadeh’s vehicle stop when fired on? And why do the Iranians claim that Fakhrizadeh’s vehicle was bulletproof when this is clearly not the case? (It's a fair guess that much of this may be covering up the incompetence of the security detail, which completely failed to protect a known target or apprehend the killers.)

邦克指出了有关官方帐户的众多问题。特别是，他被据说陪伴着法赫里扎德的保镖所困扰。为什么没有迹象表明他们在行动中发挥了作用？为什么预备警卫没有发现埋伏车？为什么Fakhrizadeh的车辆开火时会停下来？为什么伊朗人声称法赫扎扎德的车辆是防弹的，而事实并非如此？ （可以很合理地猜测，其中的大部分可能掩盖了安全细节上的不足，这些细节完全无法保护已知目标或逮捕凶手。）

We have no way of telling whether the crime scene that has been so carefully modeled is the actual site, or wholly staged by the authorities.

我们无法告诉我们经过精心设计的犯罪现场是实际现场还是完全由当局上演。

“The scene could be faked by setting the cars up and firing the shots, which would produce exactly the same results,” Boyce points out.

博伊斯指出：“通过设置汽车并开枪射击，可能会伪造场景，这将产生完全相同的结果。”

“New forensic analysis our consultancy is conducting now suggests a small kill team was involved,” says Bunker. “Iranian news releases are pure fabrication of the actual events that occurred.”

邦克说：“我们的顾问公司正在进行的新法证分析表明，其中涉及一个小型杀戮小组。” “伊朗新闻稿纯粹是对发生的实际事件的捏造。”

'Putin is a Virus' notes however that the images of the scene date from a few hours after the event, giving little chance to restage the shooting, whose location can be confirmed by videos taken soon afterwards. And the Iranians did not come up with the 'remote turret' explanation until two days afterwards.

＆amp; Putin是一种病毒＆＃39;但是请注意，场景的图像是从事件发生几小时后开始的，几乎没有机会重新拍摄，可以通过随后拍摄的视频来确认其位置。伊朗人没有提出“远程炮塔”直到两天后再进行解释。

The case is far from solved, and there is certainly misinformation in the mix. The method of the killing, let alone the identity of the killers, is far from certain. But the skill and ingenuity of the open-source intelligence community equipped with increasingly sophisticated software tools may crack it open and prove what happened one way or another.

此案远未解决，而且组合中肯定存在错误信息。杀人的方法，更不用说杀手的身份了，还远不能确定。但是，配备有越来越复杂的软件工具的开源情报社区的技能和独创性可能会使其打开并证明发生了什么方式。
