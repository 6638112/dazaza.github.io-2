The LackRack is a stackable modular product. Additional elements can be stacked on top easily. Brackets to secure the top element to the bottom one are not included but available from your local non-computer hardware store.

LackRack是可堆叠的模块化产品。其他元素可以轻松堆叠在顶部。不包括将顶部元件固定到底部元件的支架，但可以从当地的非计算机硬件商店购买。

If you put several LackRacks side by side, the higher levels can be staggered to form a pyramid. This feature is not present in any other of the commercially available rack products.

如果您将多个LackRacks并排放置，则较高的级别可以交错形成金字塔。此功能在任何其他市售机架产品中均不存在。

Optional but recommended - Put the table on its side (which one is your decision) or upside down

Mount the hardware between the left and right leg If you mount the first item, it is recommended to install it against the table top for good fit. This happens automatically if you have the LackRack upside down, except in zero gravity environments.

在左腿和右腿之间安装硬件如果要安装第一个项目，建议将其安装在桌面上，以保持良好的配合。如果您将LackRack倒置，则会自动发生，除非在零重力环境中。

Screw all the screws that fit in the rack mount in the left and right leg (for stability). With deep units, a Z-shaped bracket may be advisable to secure the rear of the unit against the underside of the tabletop.

拧紧左右腿上所有适合机架安装的螺钉（以确保稳定性）。对于较深的单元，建议使用Z形支架以将单元的后部固定在桌面的下侧。

However, if you have appliances in the LackRack, there is a chance that these appliances cannot handle the coffee (or any other liquid). You should take precautions in order to protect your appliances
