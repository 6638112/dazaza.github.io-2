Filing: Xerox PARC accuses Facebook and Twitter of patent infringement, including through tools both companies use to slow the spread of misinformation
归档：Xerox PARC指控Facebook和Twitter侵犯专利，包括通过两家公司用来减缓错误信息传播的工具

2020-11-26 10:51:13

A Xerox Corp. subsidiary has accused Twitter Inc. and Facebook Inc. of infringing several of its patents, including through tools the social media companies use to help stop the spread of false or misleading news.

施乐公司的一家子公司指控Twitter和Facebook Inc.侵犯了其多项专利，包括通过社交媒体公司用来阻止虚假或误导性新闻传播的工具。
