Stop Spotify Surveillance
停止Spotify监控

2021-04-08 22:03:33

Streaming giant Spotify has come under fire for drastically underpaying musicians, running blatant Payola schemes for major labels, and lacking transparency in its payment structures.

流巨头Spotify在大幅下行的音乐家中，已经遭到了火灾，为主要标签运行了公然的Payola计划，并缺乏支付结构的透明度。

But now the company has filed a terrifying patent to use artificial intelligence for emotional surveillance and manipulation: spying on our conversations and using the sound of our voices to target you with ads and music to keep you on the platform. Imagine telling a friend that you’re feeling depressed and having Spotify hear that and algorithmically recommend music that matches your mood to keep you depressed and listening to the music they want you to hear.

但现在公司提出了一个可怕的专利，以便使用人工智能的情感监测和操纵：在我们的谈话中进行间谍，并使用我们的声音的声音来针对您的广告和音乐来保持平台。想象一下，告诉一个朋友，你感到沮丧，发现spotify听到它与你的心情相匹配的音乐，让你沮丧和听听他们希望你听到的音乐。

Digital rights organization Access Now sent a letter to Spotify explaining that such a practice would be deeply invasive and could expose Spotify users to security threats from stalkers or government surveillance. It could also disproportionately harm trans people and be used to emotionally manipulate all of us. Emotion recognition software is largely seen as racist pseudoscience, it’s disgusting that Spotify is even considering using such a technology to extract data and profit from music listeners. And it’s horrifying to think about what kind of impact this could have on independent artists and creators, when music is promoted based on surveillance rather than artistry.

数字权利组织访问现在发送了一封信来发现，解释这种做法是深切侵入性的，可以将Spotify用户暴露于追踪者或政府监督的安全威胁。它也可能不成比例地伤害跨越人，并习惯于情绪化的人。情感识别软件在很大程度上被视为种族主义伪科学，它令人厌恶的是，Spotify甚至考虑使用这种技术来提取音乐听众的数据和利润。当考虑在独立艺术家和创造者上，当基于监视而不是艺术性的艺术促进音乐时，思考这可能会有什么样的影响。

Queer indie-punk musician and Fight for the Future director Evan Greer has released a new music video for the song “ Surveillance Capitalism,” ahead of the release of the album Spotify is Surveillance. The video will be used to promote this petition effort demanding that Spotify publicly abandon this patent and commit to not using invasive surveillance technology on listeners. 100% of the artists proceeds from the song will be donated to the Union of Musicians and Allied Workers, who are leading a Justice at Spotify campaign demanding more transparency and better pay for independent artists.

Queer Indie-Punk音乐家和对未来导演Evan Greer的斗争已经发布了一个新的音乐视频，用于歌曲“监控资本主义”，前面发布了专辑Spotify是监视。该视频将用于促进这一请愿措施，要求在公开放弃这项专利并不在听众上使用侵入性监视技术来探讨。 100％的艺术家从这首歌收到的艺术家将捐赠给音乐家和盟军工人的联盟，他在Spotify竞选方面领导司法要求要求更多的透明度和独立艺术家的薪酬更好。
