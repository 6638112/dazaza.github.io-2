Catacomb Games: Play the series that pioneered the first-person shooter genre
地下墓穴游戏：播放开创第一人称射击类游戏的系列

2020-11-21 12:03:22

Created in 1991 by John Carmack and John Romero of id Software, Catacomb 3D pioneered a fast technique for texturing a three-dimensional scene. It predates Doom (1993) and Wolfenstein 3D (1992) and is widely regarded as the first modern shooter.

Catacomb 3D由id Software的John Carmack和John Romero于1991年创建，Catacomb 3D开创了一种用于对三维场景进行纹理化的快速技术。它早于《毁灭战士》（1993年）和《德军总部3D》（1992年），被广泛认为是第一位现代射击者。

The publisher, Softdisk, developed three more games in the series. Released in May 1992, Catacomb Abyss was the first game in the Catacomb Adventure Trilogy. Development of the trilogy was led by Greg Malone, who went on to produce Duke Nukem 3D.

发行商Softdisk在该系列中又开发了三款游戏。 《地下墓穴深渊》于1992年5月发行，是《地下墓穴冒险三部曲》中的第一款游戏。三部曲的开发由格雷格·马龙（Greg Malone）领导，他后来制作了Duke Nukem 3D。

Released in October 1992, Catacomb Armageddon was the second game in the Softdisk-produced trilogy. This game dials up the camp, featuring enemies like demonic trees, killer rabbits, and giant eyeballs. This game was also released under the name Curse of the Catacombs.

地下墓穴世界末日于1992年10月发行，是软件光盘制作的三部曲中的第二款游戏。该游戏调动了营地，聚集了诸如魔鬼树，杀手兔和巨型眼球之类的敌人。该游戏也以地下墓穴的诅咒名称发布。

Released in November 1992, the final game in the series features a futuristic setting. Similar to Catacomb 3D, the player discovers a central hub with Time Gates leading to differently themed areas. This game was also released under the name Terror of the Catacombs.

该系列的最后一款游戏于1992年11月发行，具有未来派风格。与地下墓穴3D相似，玩家可以发现一个带有时间门的中央枢纽，通向不同的主题区域。该游戏也以地下墓穴的恐怖名称发行。
