Steady Hamster – Mouse Movement Smoothing Under X I noticed a lack of any software that deals with mouse movement smoothing for people suffering from Parkinson’s Disease, Essential Tremour, and similiar conditions that works under X. There already exists some software called SteadyMouse for Windows, and IBM make a hardware adapter.

steady仓 - 在xi下稳定的仓鼠平滑注意到缺乏任何对患有帕金森病，精神的疾病的人的运动平滑的软件，这些软件平滑为x下工作的人员。已经存在一些称为Windows的Steadymouse的软件，以及IBM制作硬件适配器。

So I decided to have a go at writing something. It’s only had a few hours work on it so far. It implements some simple low pass filtering to try and remove shakes and isn’t especially good at the moment (in fact it might even make things worse :P) but it’s a start. I probably won’t do much more on it until Easter when I’ll have a computer with an actual mouse instead of a trackpad to experiment with but the current code is available in my bazaar repository.

所以我决定去写一些东西。到目前为止，它只有几个小时的工作。它实现了一些简单的低通滤波，试图删除摇晃，目前并不是特别擅长（事实上它甚至可能会使事情变得更糟：P），但这是一个开始。在复活节时，我可能不会在其上做得更多，直到复活节有一个具有实际鼠标而不是触控板来试验，但我的Bazaar存储库中有当前代码。

*Very* well done and coming straight after that badly thought out newsforge title FOSS community, disabled users must learn to communicate it couldn’t have happened at a better time.

*非常*完成并直接来直接在这次思考新闻简单的遗传社区后，残疾人用户必须学会沟通，它不能发生更好的时间。
