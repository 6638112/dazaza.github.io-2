Learn about the importance of accessible product design at TechCrunch Sessions: Justice
在TechCrunch会议上了解无障碍产品设计的重要性：正义

2021-02-06 20:56:14

At TechCrunch Sessions: Justice on March 3, we will examine the importance of ensuring accessible product design from the beginning. We’ll ask how the social and medical models of disability influence technological evolution. Integrating the expertise of disabled technologists, makers, investors, scientists and software engineers into the DNA of your company from the very beginning is vital to the pursuit of a functioning and equitable society. And could mean you don’t leave money on the table.

在3月3日举行的TechCrunch会议：正义中，我们将从一开始就研究确保无障碍产品设计的重要性。我们将询问残疾的社会和医学模式如何影响技术发展。从一开始就将残疾技术人员，制造商，投资者，科学家和软件工程师的专业知识整合到您公司的DNA中，对于追求一个运转良好，公平的社会至关重要。可能意味着您不会把钱留在桌上。

Join us at TechCrunch Sessions: Justice for a wide-ranging discussion as we attempt to answer these questions and further explore inclusive design with Cynthia Bennett, Mara Mills and Srin Madipalli.

加入我们参加TechCrunch会议：正义，进行广泛的讨论，我们尝试回答这些问题，并与Cynthia Bennett，Mara Mills和Srin Madipalli进一步探讨包容性设计。

Cynthia Bennett is a post-doc at Carnegie Mellon University’s Human-Computer Interaction Institute, as well as a researcher at Apple. Her research focuses on human-computer interaction, accessibility and Disability Studies, and, she says on her website, spans “the critique and development of HCI theory and methods to designing emergent accessible interactions with technology.” Her research includes Biographical Prototypes: Reimagining Recognition and Disability in Design and The Promise of Empathy: Design, Disability, and Knowing the “Other.”

辛西娅·贝内特（Cynthia Bennett）是卡内基梅隆大学人机交互研究所的博士后，也是苹果公司的研究员。她的研究重点是人机交互，可访问性和残障研究，并且在她的网站上说，它涵盖了“ HCI理论和方法的批评和发展，以设计与技术的新兴可访问性交互。”她的研究包括传记原型：重新构想设计中的识别和残障，以及移情的承诺：设计，残障和了解“其他”。

Mara Mills is the Associate Professor of Media, Culture, and Communication at New York University and a co-founder and co-director of the NYU Center for Disability Studies. Mills’ research focuses on sound studies, disability studies and history. (You can hear her discuss the intersection of artificial intelligence and disability with Meredith Whittaker, co-founder of the AI Now Institute and Minderoo Research Professor at NYU, and Sara Hendren, professor at Olin College of Engineering and author of the recently published  What Can a Body Do: How We Meet the Built World, on the TechCrunch Mixtape podcast here.)

玛拉·米尔斯（Mara Mills）是纽约大学媒体，文化和传播学副教授，也是纽约大学残疾研究中心的联合创始人和联合主任。 Mills的研究重点是声音研究，残疾研究和历史。 （您可以听到她与AI Now学院和纽约大学Minderoo研究教授的联合创始人梅雷迪思·惠特克（Meredith Whittaker）以及奥林工程学院教授，​​最近出版的《什么可以身体做：我们如何遇见建筑世界，在此处的TechCrunch Mixtape播客上。）

Srin Madipalli is an investor and co-founder of Accomable, an online platform that helped users find accessible vacation properties, which he sold to Airbnb. His advocacy work focuses on disability inclusion. Be sure to snag your tickets for just $5 here.

Srin Madipalli是Accomable的投资者和联合创始人，Accomable是一个在线平台，可帮助用户找到无障碍度假物业，并将其出售给Airbnb。他的倡导工作侧重于包容残疾。请确保在这里以5美元的价格抢购门票。

Make sure you can join us for this conversation and more at TC Sessions: Justice on March 3. Secure your seat now!

请确保您可以加入我们进行此次对话，并参加3月3日举行的TC会议：正义大会。
