Cults begin or evolve with a charismatic leader who claims supreme knowledge. These leaders are able to influence their followers to believe their perspectives and obey their dictates. 1. Is your leader the ultimate authority of your group?

邪教起源于或发展于一位声称拥有至高无上知识的魅力领袖。这些领导人能够影响他们的追随者，让他们相信自己的观点，服从他们的命令。1.你的领导是你团队的最高权威吗？

Most cult leaders give themselves impressive made-up names or titles which elevate their status to their followers. They may call themselves the man of God, pastor, prophet, messiah, messenger, enlightened teacher, guru, apostle etc... 2: Does your leader have impressive, made-up names or titles?

大多数邪教头目给自己起了令人印象深刻的化名或头衔，从而提升了他们在追随者中的地位。他们可能称自己为神人、牧师、先知、弥赛亚、信使、开明的老师、古鲁、使徒等等。。。2：你的领导有令人印象深刻的、虚构的名字或头衔吗？

Cult leaders convince members to forfeit their critical thinking ability in return for a sense of belonging, authority, and purpose. To members, it doesn’t matter what the evidence or logic may suggest, the leader is always right, and their misdeeds are always justified. Criticism of the leader is forbidden. 3: Is your leader always right and therefore you are not allowed to criticise him/her, even if the criticism is true?

邪教领袖说服成员放弃批判性思维能力，以换取归属感、权威感和目的感。对成员来说，无论证据或逻辑表明什么，领导者总是正确的，他们的错误行为总是合理的。禁止批评领导人。3：你的领导是否总是正确的，因此你不允许批评他/她，即使批评是真的？

Cults generally only allow their members to study their organisation through approved sources. They view critical thinking as an infectious disease and every effort is made to suppress it. Doubting members are encouraged to isolate themselves from outside influences and focus solely on the doctrine of the cult. People who contradict the group are viewed as persecutors and are often given labels like “anti,” “apostate,” or “rival” Members are discouraged from consuming any material that is critical of the group. 4: Are members of your group discouraged from reading anything that is critical of the group?

邪教通常只允许其成员通过批准的来源研究其组织。他们将批判性思维视为一种传染病，并尽一切努力抑制它。我们鼓励持怀疑态度的成员将自己与外部影响隔离开来，只关注邪教教义。反对该团体的人被视为迫害者，通常被贴上“反”、“叛教”或“敌对”等标签，不鼓励成员使用任何批评该团体的材料。4：你的团队成员是否不愿意阅读任何批评团队的内容？

Because religious cults consider themselves the ultimate authority on truth, the members can't imagine anybody leaving the group with their integrity intact. Thus, they have to perpetuate a false narrative that former members were deceived, backslidden, demonic, bitter, evil, or fallen away etc. 5: As far as your group is concerned, would they say that there is no legitimate reason to leave them?

Does your group insist that the end of the world is near? Cults position themselves as the sole refuge from an evil outside world that is intent on their destruction. Cults thrive on conspiracy theories, catastrophic thinking, and persecution complexes. In an effort to draw in more paying members, cults are often very aggressive in their recruitment efforts which are usually justified as “saving” people from the evil world. Those who reject the cult’s message are condemned, unelected, damned, evil, goats, the great unwashed etc... 6: Is your group suspicious about the outside world?

你们的团队是否坚持世界末日即将到来？邪教把自己定位为躲避外部邪恶世界的唯一避难所，外部邪恶世界意图摧毁邪教。邪教以阴谋论、灾难性思维和迫害情结为生。为了吸引更多的付费成员，邪教在招募工作中往往非常激进，通常被认为是在“拯救”邪恶世界的人。那些拒绝邪教信息的人被谴责、未经选举、诅咒、邪恶、山羊、未经洗礼的大人物等等。。。6：你的团队对外部世界有怀疑吗？

Cults all believe they are ‘right.’ They are the ‘right’ group with the ‘right’ doctrines, the ‘right’ leader, the ‘right’ interpretation of their holy book and their leader is ‘right’ in dictating what laws and rules they are to obey. 7: Does your group believe that they are the ‘right’ religion/church/group/ones, or only ones, who have ‘right’ teaching/doctrine?

邪教都相信自己是“对的”他们是“正确”的团体，有“正确”的教义，“正确”的领袖，对他们的《圣经》有“正确”的解释，他们的领袖在规定他们应该遵守的法律和规则方面是“正确”的。7：你的团队是否认为他们是“正确的”宗教/教会/团体/人，还是只有他们有“正确的”教义/教义？

Cults exercise control over their followers. The leader determines the rules/laws and expects to be obeyed or else there are consequences. Rules/laws can include what you can’t wear, where you can’t live, who you can’t eat with, who you can’t marry, what job you can’t do, who you can’t keep company with, what education you can’t receive, what your stance is to those who have left the group, and many more. 8: Are there any of the above such rules/laws in your group?

邪教控制信徒。领导者决定规则/法律，并期望被遵守，否则会产生后果。规则/法律可以包括你不能穿什么，你不能住在哪里，你不能和谁一起吃饭，你不能和谁结婚，你不能做什么工作，你不能和谁交往，你不能接受什么教育，你对那些离开团体的人的立场，等等。8：您的团队中是否有上述规则/法律？

Emotional Separation. Cults are not happy when members leave, primarily, because they think there is never a legitimate reason to leave. Therefore, those who leave are treated negatively. Remaining members, even family members, distance themselves emotionally often shunning and ostracising ex-members. They now see them as unclean, evil, deceived, apostate, an opposer, etc. 9: If you were to leave your religious group, would remaining members, even family members separate themselves from you whereby there would be any emotional detachment?

情感分离。邪教成员离开时不高兴，主要是因为他们认为永远没有合法的理由离开。因此，那些离开的人会受到负面对待。剩下的成员，甚至是家庭成员，在情感上与自己保持距离，常常回避和排斥前成员。他们现在认为他们是不洁的、邪恶的、受骗的、叛徒的、反对者的等等。9.如果你要离开你的宗教团体，剩下的成员，甚至家庭成员是否会与你分离，从而产生情感上的分离？

Physical Separation. Cults separate families. This is how it works. A member of a family leaves. The cult now separates the remaining family members with laws/rules. These dictate they can now no longer have ‘normal family life’ with that member who has left. For example: They are no longer allowed to freely eat and drink with them. They are no longer allowed to stay or live with them. They are no longer allowed to go on holiday with them. They are no longer allowed to celebrate the birth of their children or go to their wedding. They are no longer allowed to fully join in any funeral arrangements. They are no longer allowed to celebrate birthdays or special occasions with them. They are no longer allowed to go shopping with them. They are no longer allowed to contact them. 10: If you were to leave your religious group, would remaining members separate themselves from you and cease to have ‘normal family life’ as in any of the above examples?

物理分离。邪教把家庭分开。这就是它的工作原理。家庭成员离开。邪教现在用法律/规则将剩下的家庭成员分开。这意味着他们现在不能再与离开的成员有“正常的家庭生活”。例如：他们不再被允许自由地与他们一起吃喝。他们不再被允许和他们住在一起。他们不再被允许和他们一起去度假。他们不再被允许庆祝孩子的出生或参加婚礼。他们不再被允许完全参与任何葬礼安排。他们不再被允许与他们一起庆祝生日或特殊场合。他们不再被允许和他们一起去购物。他们不再被允许联系他们。10：如果你要离开你的宗教团体，剩下的成员会与你分离，不再像上面任何一个例子那样拥有“正常的家庭生活”吗？
