Russian hacker group REvil claims attack on televangelist Kenneth Copeland
俄罗斯黑客组织REvil宣称对电信传播家肯尼斯·科普兰（Kenneth Copeland）的攻击

2020-11-22 03:26:08

Russian hacker group REvil claims massive attack on televangelist Kenneth Copeland - who responded to Biden victory with bizarre fake laugh - and threatens to release 1.2 terrabytes of 'sensitive' data if he refuses to pay their ransom

俄罗斯黑客组织REvil声称对电信传播家肯尼斯·科普兰（Kenneth Copeland）进行了大规模攻击-后者以怪异的假笑回应了拜登的胜利-并威胁说，如果他拒绝支付赎金，将释放1.2 TB的“敏感”数据

The Russian hacker group known as REvil has named televangelist Kenneth Copeland as its latest victim, threatening to release 1.2 terrabytes of sensitive data if he declines to pay their unspecified ransom demands.

被称为REvil的俄罗斯黑客组织已将电视传播家肯尼斯·科普兰（Kenneth Copeland）指定为其最新受害者，威胁说如果他拒绝支付未指定的赎金要求，将释放1.2兆字节的敏感数据。

REvil on Wednesday issued a statement saying it had taken over the servers of Kenneth Copeland Ministries, the Texas-based international church of the prominent 83-year-old pastor.

雷维尔周三发表声明说，它已经接管了肯尼思·科普兰各部的服务器，肯尼斯·科普兰各部是总部位于德克萨斯州的著名83岁牧师的国际教堂。

'Absolutely all servers and working computers of the company are hacked and encrypted,' the group said in the statement on the Dark Web, which was reviewed by DailyMail.com.

该组织在《 Dark Web》上的声明中说，该公司的所有服务器和正常工作的计算机绝对都是被黑客攻击和加密的。

REvil released images of a file library purporting to show the stolen data, and claiming the information included 'financial documents, contracts, bank documents, sales history, [and] emails.'

REvil发布了一个文件库的图像，目的是显示被盗的数据，并声称该信息包括“财务文件，合同，银行文件，销售历史和[和]电子邮件”。

Copeland, who founded the ministry in 1967 and sat on President Donald Trump's faith advisory board, has a reported net worth of between $300 million and $760 million.

Copeland于1967年成立了该部，并曾担任唐纳德·特朗普总统的信仰顾问委员会成员，据报道其净资产在3亿至7.6亿美元之间。

REvil did not specify their financial demand in the public statement, which was removed on Thursday, possibly indicating negotiations had been entered with the victim.

REvil在公开声明中没有具体说明其财务需求，该声明已于周四删除，这可能表明已与受害者进行了谈判。

REvil's signature tactic -- encrypting a company's servers and then threatening to release or auction off their data -- is an increasingly common scam among ransomware groups.

REvil的签名策略-加密公司的服务器，然后威胁要发布或拍卖其数据-是勒索软件组织中越来越普遍的骗局。

'Ransomware groups are indiscriminate in their choice of victims. They’ll attack any body they believe can afford to pay, whether it’s a private company, government, hospital, charity or religious organization,' Brett Callow, a threat analyst at cybersecurity firm Emsisoft, told DailyMail.com.

勒索软件组织在选择受害者方面是不分青红皂白的。网络安全公司Emsisoft的威胁分析师布雷特·卡洛（Brett Callow）告诉DailyMail.com。

'Ransomware is becoming ever more of a problem,' Callow added. 'The demands are increasing, the sophistication of attacks is increasing and tactics the groups deploy are becoming more extreme.'

卡洛补充说：“勒索软件正变得越来越成为一个问题。”需求在增加，攻击的复杂性也在增加，这些组织部署的策略也越来越极端。

Copeland made headlines last week, when he appeared in a bizarre video where he forced a fake laugh for almost a minute as he mocked the media for calling the 2020 presidential election in favor of Joe Biden.

谷轮上周登上头条新闻，当时他出现在一部离奇的录像带中，在那里他假笑了近一分钟，嘲笑媒体呼吁2020年总统大选胜过拜登。

Copeland has also drawn criticism for his statements about the coronavirus pandemic, which he repeatedly claimed had passed or would soon end.

谷轮还因其关于冠状病毒大流行的言论而受到批评，他一再声称已经过或即将结束。

In March, the pastor 'executed judgment' on the virus and declared it to be 'finished' while demanding 'a vaccination to come immediately.'

3月，牧师对该病毒“执行了判断”，并宣布其“完成”，同时要求“立即进行疫苗接种”。

The hacker group REvil previously targeted celebrity law firm Grubman, Shire, Meiselas & Sacks, which  refused to pay the millions in ransom that REvil demanded.

黑客组织REvil以前针对的是名人律师事务所Grubman，Shire，Meiselas＆Sacks，后者拒绝支付REvil要求的数百万赎金。

The 'damaging' details against Trump that REvil had promised from the law firm hack never materialized.

REvil从律师事务所的黑客中向特朗普承诺的“破坏性”细节从未实现。

In a recent interview with a Russian-language tech blog, a purported representative of REvil said that the group's most successful method of attack was through Remote Desktop Protocol, which allows for remote administration of a desktop over the internet.

在最近一次对俄语语言技术博客的采访中，据称是REvil的代表，该组织最成功的攻击方法是通过远程桌面协议，该协议允许通过Internet远程管理桌面。

Security experts recommend disabling RDP on company computers, and say that the best protection against hackers is quickly and frequently updating software with the latest updates.

安全专家建议在公司的计算机上禁用RDP，并说，针对黑客的最佳保护是快速，频繁地使用最新更新来更新软件。

Callow, the cybersecurity expert, estimated that almost $1.7 billion is paid annually in hacker ransoms, saying the total cost of attacks including downtime approaches $10 billion.

网络安全专家卡洛（Callow）估计，每年大约有17亿美元是以黑客赎金形式支付的，他说，包括停机时间在内的攻击总成本接近100亿美元。

'The only way to deal with the problem is to prohibit the payment of ransoms,' he said. 'If the flow of cash stops, the attacks will stop too. It’s that simple.'

他说，解决这个问题的唯一方法是禁止支付赎金。 '如果现金流停止，攻击也将停止。就这么简单。
