英特尔报告第三季度营收为183亿美元，同比下降4%，而预计为182.4亿美元，数据中心集团营收为59亿美元，同比下降7%；股价盘后下跌10%以上

2020-10-23 04:34:35

Intel shares fell as much as 10% in extended trading on Thursday after the company reported fiscal third-quarter earnings that were stronger than analysts had expected but showed new weakness in its data center business.

英特尔股价周四在盘后交易中下跌多达10%，此前该公司公布的第三财季获利强于分析师预期，但显示其数据中心业务出现新的疲软。

Earnings: $1.11 per share, adjusted, vs. $1.11 per share as expected by analysts, according to Refinitiv.

根据Refinitiv的数据，调整后每股收益为1.11美元，而分析师预期为每股1.11美元。

Overall, Intel's revenue in the quarter, which ended Sept. 26, declined 4% on an annualized basis, according to a statement.

总体而言，英特尔在截至9月1日的季度中的收入。26，根据一份声明，按年率计算下降了4%。

Intel's Data Center Group came up with $5.91 billion in revenue, down 7% and less than the FactSet consensus estimate of $6.21 billion. Intel said revenue from enterprises and governments went down 47% after two quarters of growth above 30% because of the coronavirus pandemic, but cloud revenue in the quarter grew 15%.

英特尔数据中心集团的营收为59.1亿美元，下降了7%，低于FactSet普遍预期的62.1亿美元。英特尔表示，由于冠状病毒大流行，企业和政府的收入在连续两个季度增长30%以上后下降了47%，但该季度云收入增长了15%。

Intel's largest business, the Client Computing Group that sells PC chips, produced $9.85 billion in revenue, up 1% year over year and above the $9.09 billion consensus among analysts polled by FactSet. Earlier this month technology research company Gartner estimated that PC shipments grew 3.6% in the third quarter, as people kept buying machines to work and study from home.

英特尔最大的业务是销售个人电脑芯片的客户计算集团(Client Computing Group)，创造了98.5亿美元的收入，同比增长1%，高于FactSet调查分析师普遍预期的90.9亿美元。本月早些时候，科技研究公司Gartner估计，第三季度个人电脑出货量增长3.6%，因为人们继续购买机器在家工作和学习。

Intel's Non-Volatile Memory Solutions Group had $1.15 billion in revenue, down about 11% and lower that the $1.50 billion consensus. On Tuesday South Korean memory maker SK hynix said it would buy part of that division, Intel's NAND memory and storage business, for $9 billion.

英特尔的非易失性存储器解决方案部门的收入为11.5亿美元，下降了约11%，低于市场普遍预期的15亿美元。周二，韩国内存制造商SK hynix表示，将以90亿美元收购该部门的一部分，即英特尔的NAND存储和存储业务。

Intel's operating margin narrowed to 27.6% from 33.6% in the year-ago quarter. Operating margin for the Data Center Group reached 32%, down from 49%.

英特尔的营业利润率从去年同期的33.6%收窄至27.6%。数据中心集团的运营利润率从49%下降到32%。

With respect to guidance, Intel said it expects $1.10 in adjusted earnings per share on $17.4 billion in revenue in the fiscal fourth quarter. The revenue forecast implies a decline of about 14%. Analysts polled by Refinitiv had been looking for $1.07 in adjusted earnings per share and $17.36 billion in revenue.

在指引方面，英特尔表示，预计第四财季调整后每股收益为1.10美元，营收为174亿美元。营收预估暗示下滑约14%。接受Refinitiv调查的分析师此前预计调整后每股收益为1.07美元，营收为173.6亿美元。

In the quarter Intel announced the departure of engineering leader Murthy Renduchintala and said McKinsey executive Safroadu Yeboah-Amankwah was joining as chief strategy officer.

在本季度，英特尔宣布工程主管Murthy Renduchintala离职，并表示麦肯锡高管Safroadu Yeboah-Amankwah将加盟，担任首席战略官。

During the quarter Intel did not announce progress on its goal of increasing the ranks of women and minorities in leadership positions. The company last issued statistics in December, saying that 72.6% of its global employees were male and that 61.2% of U.S. employees were White or Asian.

在本季度，英特尔没有宣布其增加女性和少数族裔担任领导职位的目标的进展情况。该公司上一次发布统计数据是在去年12月，称其全球员工中有72.6%是男性，61.2%的美国员工是白人或亚洲人。

Excluding the after-hours move, Intel share are down about 10% since the beginning of 2020, while the S&P 500 is up 7%.

剔除盘后变动，英特尔股价自2020年初以来下跌了约10%，而标准普尔500指数上涨了7%。

Executives will discuss the results with analysts on a conference call starting at 5 p.m. Eastern time.

高管们将在下午5点开始的电话会议上与分析师讨论结果。东部时间。
