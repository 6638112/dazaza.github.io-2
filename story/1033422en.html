Roku在第三季度的收入为4.517亿美元，同比增长73%，而预计收入为3.678亿美元，活跃账户同比增长43%，达到4600万，货币化视频广告印象同比增长近90%。

2020-11-06 07:30:55

Amid the ongoing pandemic, ’s third-quarter financial results topped Wall Street’s estimates Thursday afternoon as streaming hours spiked for another consecutive quarter, sending the stock up over 4% in after-hours trading, on top of a 4.4% gain during the regular trading session.

在疫情持续之际，雅虎周四下午公布的第三季度财报超出了华尔街的预期，流媒体播放时间连续第二个季度飙升，推动该股在盘后交易中上涨逾4%，此前该股在正常交易时段上涨了4.4%。

Active accounts popped by 43% to 46 million, according to the company, while streaming hours rose 54% to 14.8 billion.

根据该公司的数据，活跃账户增加了43%，达到4600万，流媒体时长增加了54%，达到148亿。

Roku reported $451.7 million in Q3 revenue, marking an 73% year-over-year increase during the quarter, better than the $367.8 million in revenue that analysts had expected for the September-ended quarter. Diluted earnings of 9 cents a share beat forecasts for a loss of 40 cents a share.

Roku公布第三季度营收为4.517亿美元，同比增长73%，好于分析师对截至9月底的季度3.678亿美元的预期。稀释后每股收益为9美分，超出每股亏损40美分的预期。

As the coronavirus crisis continues into its eighth month, viewers continue to turn to streaming programming for entertainment in lieu of going to movie theaters or opting for other group entertainment options. That has translated to increased ad revenue and player unit sales for Roku, the latter of which grew 57% from the prior-year quarter, the “strongest year-over-year player revenue growth in over seven years,” per the streaming-device company.

随着冠状病毒危机持续到第八个月，观众继续转向流媒体节目娱乐，而不是去电影院或选择其他集体娱乐选择。这意味着Roku的广告收入和播放器销量都有所增加，后者比上一季度增长了57%，这是这家流媒体设备公司称，这是这家流媒体设备公司七年多来最强劲的播放器收入同比增长。

“We’re not going back to the way it was — COVID triggered a lasting, durable change in how marketers… are thinking about their TV ad spend,” said Roku CFO Steve Louden on the call. Roku is seeing 100% retention in advertisers who spend more than $1 million, he said, and does not anticipate them moving back to linear platforms.

“我们不会回到过去的样子--Covid引发了营销者…方式的持久、持久的改变。”Roku首席财务官史蒂夫·劳登在电话会议上说。他说，Roku看到花费超过100万美元的广告商100%留住了广告商，预计他们不会回到线性平台。

During the quarter, Roku introduced several new products, incluing the 4K HDR streaming player Roku Ultra and he Roku Streambar, a streamer-soundbar hybrid device.

在本季度，Roku推出了几款新产品，包括4K HDR流媒体播放器Roku Ultra和拖缆-音棒混合设备He Roku Streambar。

“Monetized video ad impressions were up almost 90% year-over-year in Q3, up sharply vs roughly 50% year-over-year in Q2,” said Scott Rosenberg, senior vice president of Roku’s platform business in a statement. “Not only are existing brands growing spend but many new advertisers are shifting into streaming as more and more cord-cutting consumers become unreachable to brands on traditional linear television. First-time advertisers more than doubled year-over-year in Q3 and once brands become accustomed to the superior performance of streaming they tend to stay with Roku. Ninety-seven percent of brands that spent $1 million or more with Roku in Q3 2019 continued to invest in Q3 2020.”

Roku平台业务高级副总裁斯科特·罗森博格在一份声明中表示：“第三季度货币化视频广告播放量同比增长近90%，而第二季度同比增长约50%。”“不仅现有品牌的支出在增加，许多新的广告商也在转向流媒体，因为越来越多的剪线消费者变得无法接触到传统线性电视上的品牌。”第三季度，首次投放广告的客户同比增加了一倍多，一旦品牌习惯了流媒体的卓越表现，他们往往会继续使用Roku。在2019年第三季度在Roku上花费100万美元或更多的品牌中，有97%的品牌在2020年第三季度继续投资。

Roku estimates that Q4 revenue growth will be in line with previous holiday seasons, in the mid-40% range.

Roku估计，第四季度营收增长将与之前的假日季持平，在40%左右。

During the earnings call, Roku CEO Anthony Wood said the quarter reflected a confluence of positive trends for the company, but noted that the uncertainty in the macro environment — from global coronavirus resurgences to consumer spending levels — contributed to the lack of formal guidance for Q4 numbers. The company’s pre-pandemic full-year outlook, issued in February, guided for 42% year-over-year revenue growth for the year, approximately breakeven EBITDA and a loss of $160 million to $180 million.

在财报电话会议上，Roku首席执行官安东尼·伍德(Anthony Wood)表示，该季度反映了公司积极趋势的汇合，但他指出，宏观环境的不确定性-从全球冠状病毒卷土重来到消费者支出水平-导致第四季度数据缺乏正式指引。该公司2月份发布的疫情前全年展望显示，全年营收同比增长42%，EBITDA接近盈亏平衡，亏损1.6亿至1.8亿美元。

The Roku Channel grew its reach to 54 million people, which execs on the call said they were every pleased with. Louden said it “opens more possibilities” for Roku as a channel.

Roku Channel的覆盖范围扩大到5400万人，参加电话会议的高管表示，他们对此非常满意。劳登说，这为Roku作为一个频道打开了更多的可能性。

“As we continue to offer users more content and features in The Roku Channel, we believe our viewers will stream more content from within it and it will become an even more important source of economics to our content partners,” Wood and CFO Steve Louden noted in the company’s shareholder letter.

伍德和首席财务官史蒂夫·劳登在公司的股东信中指出：“随着我们继续在Roku Channel上为用户提供更多的内容和功能，我们相信我们的观众将从Roku Channel中获得更多内容，它将成为我们内容合作伙伴的一个更重要的经济来源，”伍德和首席财务官史蒂夫·劳登(Steve Louden)在公司的股东信中指出。

Addressing concerns about reports that Walmart and Comcast are in talks to sell smart TVs, Wood said that its platform is “purpose-built for television,” and allows manufacturers to build TVs that cost less. He also reiterated faith in the Roku brand and its competitive advantage in the market.

针对沃尔玛和康卡斯特正在洽谈销售智能电视的报道，伍德表示，其平台是专门为电视打造的，允许制造商生产成本更低的电视。他还重申了对Roku品牌及其在市场上的竞争优势的信心。

“Walmart is a very strong partner of Roku’s — we have a great relationship with them, they sell millions of Rokus a year,” he said, adding that the company puts a lot of work into making sure its relationship with Walmart remains strong.

他说：“沃尔玛是Roku的一个非常强大的合作伙伴，我们与他们的关系很好，他们每年销售数百万台Rokus。”他补充说，沃尔玛投入了大量工作来确保其与沃尔玛的关系保持牢固。
