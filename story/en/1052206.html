After 'Defiant' Reopening, Tesla Plant Had 450 Covid-19 Cases
经过“挑衅”重新开放后，特斯拉植物有450个Covid-19案例

2021-03-14 02:20:27

SAN FRANCISCO - Tesla's Bay Area production plant recorded hundreds of positive covid-19 cases following CEO Elon Musk's defiant reopening of the plant last May, according to county-level data obtained by a legal transparency website.

根据法律透明度网站获得的县级数据，旧金山 - 特斯拉和＃39; S湾区生产厂录得数百名历史悠久的Covid-19案例，历史兴奋剂＆＃39;常见的植物的重新开放。

The document, obtained by the website PlainSite following a court ruling this year, showed Tesla received around 10 reports of covid-19 in May when the plant reopened, and saw a steady rise in cases all the way up to 125 in December, as the disease caused by the novel coronavirus peaked around the country.

本年度法院统治后的网站Plainsite获得的文件显示，在植物重新开放的情况下，在5月19日的Covid-19左右收到了Tesla，并且在12月的125岁的情况下稳步上升，为125件由新型冠状病毒引起的疾病达到全国各地。

The revelation follows The Washington Post's reporting in June that there had been multiple positive covid-19 cases reported at Tesla's facilities in Fremont, Ca., after Musk decided to reopen despite a countywide shelter-in-place order, daring officials to arrest him.

6月份的举报伴随着华盛顿邮政，在弗里蒙特·弗里蒙特的设施中报告了多个积极的Covid-19案件。尽管在康斯郡庇护所在的麝香秩序，大胆的官员逮捕他。

The data, covering the months between May and December, showed there were around 450 total reported cases. Roughly 10,000 workers work at the plant.

涵盖5月至12月期间的数据显示，报告案件总数约为450个。大约10,000名工人在工厂工作。

For nearly a year, the Alameda County Public Health Department, which is where Fremont is located, argued it could not release data on the number of cases under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act, which grants privacy over health records.

对于近一年来，阿拉米达县公共卫生部门是弗里蒙特所在的，认为它无法在健康保险流动性和问责法下的案件数量释放数据，这授予了对健康记录的隐私。

As part of an agreement struck in mid-May allowing Tesla to reopen, Tesla was required to report positive cases to the Alameda County Public Health Department. Despite around 10 positive cases in May, according to the data, the health department told The Post in early June there were no known cases of workplace infections affecting county residents.

作为允许特斯拉在中旬达到的协议的一部分，特斯拉被要求向阿拉米达县公共卫生部门报告积极案件。尽管5月10日左右，但卫生署在6月初告诉该职位，没有已知的影响县居民的工作场所感染病例。

Tesla and the Alameda County Public Health Department and representatives did not immediately respond to a request for comment.

特斯拉和阿拉米达县公共卫生部门和代表没有立即回复评论请求。
