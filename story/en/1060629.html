LoanSnap, whose AI system analyzes users' finances and recommends a loan to improve their financial health, raises $30M Series B led by True Ventures
Loansnap，其AI系统分析用户的财务并建议贷款以提高其财务状况，提高了由真实企业领导的30万系列

2021-05-05 21:05:13

Serial entrepreneur Karl Jacob has tried to tackle a lot of industries in his career, from social networking to cybersecurity. So when a friend asked the repeat founder and former strategic advisor to Facebook if he could help him with his mortgage company, he said “sure.” He didn’t know what he was walking into. After spending time at the company’s call center, he realized how broken the system was. Jacob witnessed representatives repeatedly ask customers to fork over financial information that the reps could easily access on their own, only to then suggest loans that would generate the most profit, even if they weren’t the best choice for the consumer. Jacob quickly realized what his next company would be.

Serial Entrepreneur Karl Jacob试图在社交网络到网络安全的职业生涯中解决很多行业。因此，当朋友向Facebook要求重复创始人和前战略顾问时，如果他可以帮助他的抵押贷款公司，他说“当然。”他不知道他走的东西。在公司的呼叫中心度过时间后，他意识到系统的崩溃。雅各布目睹代表反复要求客户通过财务信息叉，即代表可以轻松地自己访问，然后建议将产生最多的利润的贷款，即使他们不是消费者的最佳选择。雅各布很快意识到他的下一家公司是什么。

“What if we try to modernize, not just the technology stack, but the mortgage process?” Jacob remembers thinking. “How do we know more about this person we are about to lend money to, so we can guide them as much as possible?” The resulting company is LoanSnap. Launched in 2018, the home mortgage platform aims to issue loans that improve the underlying consumer’s financial future. The startup, which has seen strong demand, just raised a fresh round of funding, as originally reported in the Midas Touch newsletter.

“如果我们试图现代化，而不是技术堆栈，而是抵押过程？”雅各布记得思考。 “我们如何了解我们即将借钱的人，所以我们可以尽可能地指导他们？”由此产生的公司是Loansnap。 2018年推出，家庭抵押贷款平台旨在发行改善潜在消费者财务未来的贷款。最初的初创公司，刚刚提高了新一轮的资金，最初在Midas触控时事通知。

True Ventures led LoanSnap’s $30 million Series B round, with participation from venture firms including Baseline Ventures, The Virgin Group and Mantis VC, among others, and individuals including former NFL quarterback Joe Montana, LinkedIn co-founder Reid Hoffman and Zynga founder Mark Pincus. This fresh influx, which raises the startup’s funding to a total $64 million, will go towards expanding its services in other areas. “There are tons of other financial products that we are taking a look at,” he adds. “Particularly those that tend to take advantage of consumers where they shouldn’t.”

真正的企业LED Loansnap的3000万美元B轮，与Venture公司的参与，包括基准企业，Virgin集团和Mantis VC等人和以前NFL四分卫Joe Montana，LinkedIn联合创始人Reidman和Zynga创始人Mark Pincus。这种新鲜的涌入，将创业公司的资金提高到总共6400万美元，将旨在扩大其在其他领域的服务。 “有很多其他金融产品，我们正在看看，”他补充道。 “特别是那些倾向于利用他们不应该的消费者的人。”

LoanSnap is not the only startup trying to tackle the mortgage industry. But Jacob says his company is different. LoanSnap isn’t just looking to move the process online or make it faster — although the software does look to close loans within 15 days — it hopes to leave the consumer in a better financial situation than where they started. Users provide LoanSnap with basic info, like the last four digits of their social security number, and its AI system scans their financial information and shows users where they are losing money, like student loan interest or credit card debt, then recommends a “smart” loan that packages all those payments together to give users a way to improve their financial health by paying their monthly mortgage payment.  “Our approach is focused on showing [consumers] very specifically how much they are losing every month, and then using an AI machine that has one goal, to optimize that person's financial system,” Jacob says.

LoanSnap并不是唯一试图解决抵押贷款行业的创业。但雅各布说他的公司是不同的。 LoanSnap不只是希望在线移动过程或使其更快 - 虽然该软件在15天内进行关闭 - 但希望将消费者留在更好的财务状况之外。用户提供的基本信息提供Loannap，如其社会安全号码的最后四位数字，其AI系统扫描其财务信息并显示他们正在损失金钱的用户，如学生贷款利息或信用卡债务，然后建议“聪明”贷款将所有这些款项汇集在一起​​，使用户通过支付每月抵押贷款支付来提高其财务健康的方法。 “我们的方法专注于显示[消费者]非常特别是他们每月丢失多少，然后使用一个有一个目标的AI机器，以优化该人和第39族的金融体系，”Jacob说。

The startup’s services are currently available in 21 states. LoanSnap originates the loans itself, as opposed to being a mediary or a marketplace for other underwriters. Jacob says that keeping the entire process within the company allows for a quicker approval timeline. The company currently issues loans for mortgages, refinances and home equity lines of credit. Jacob says that LoanSnap was able to save its users $35 million last year by transferring their payments to one smart loan, and $16 million so far in 2021. The company expects to be profitable by the end of this year.

Startup的服务目前有21个州。 LoanSnap原始贷款本身，而不是作为其他承销商的Mediary或市场。 Jacob说，将整个过程保留在公司内允许更快的批准时间表。该公司目前为抵押贷款，炼油和家庭股权信贷贷款发行贷款。雅各布表示，LoanSnap能够通过将其付款到一个智能贷款转移到一个智能贷款，并在2021年迄今为止将获得3亿美元的贷款。该公司预计将于今年年底盈利。

The startup is looking to tap into the housing market at a time when activity is booming. The National Association of Realtors show that house sales in March 2021 were up another 12% year-over-year, after months of steady growth, which creates a flurry of activity in the mortgage sector. Despite the recent growth spurt, the mortgage industry still remains fragmented. Quicken Loans holds the largest slice of the market with a mere 5% market share as of 2019, which leaves the door wide open for players like LoanSnap to take advantage of the dissolution.

启动时希望在活动蓬勃发展的时候挖掘住房市场。全国房地产经纪人协会表明，3021年3月的房屋销售额同比上涨12％，经过几个月的稳步增长，这在抵押贷款部门创造了一系列活动。尽管最近的增长刺激，但抵押贷款行业仍然是碎片化的。 Quicken Loans拥有最大的市场，仅为2019年的市场份额，截至2019年的市场份额，为贷款等球员带来了门宽，以利用溶解。
