Apple launches a display module replacement program for iPhone 11 devices manufactured between November 2019 and May 2020 experiencing touch issues

2020-12-05 08:06:41

Apple today launched a new display replacement program for iPhone 11 devices to address a problem that cases a "small percentage" of ‌iPhone 11‌ displays to stop responding to touch.

Apple今天启动了一项针对iPhone 11设备的新的显示器更换计划，以解决导致“百分比很小”的问题。 ‌iPhone 11‌的显示屏停止响应触摸。

Apple says that affected devices were manufactured between November 2019 and May 2020, and if there are touch issues, the ‌iPhone 11‌ models could have a problem with the display module.

苹果表示受影响的设备是在2019年11月至2020年5月之间制造的，如果出现触摸问题，则``iPhone 11''型号的显示模块可能会出现问题。

Those with ‌iPhone 11‌ models exhibiting this issue can have eligible devices serviced by Apple. ‌iPhone 11‌ owners can use the serial number checker available through Apple's support document to see if their devices are part of the replacement program. For devices that are eligible, Apple or an Authorized Apple Service Provider will provide service at no charge.

出现此问题的装有“ iPhone 11”型号的人可以使用Apple维修的合格设备。 “ iPhone 11”所有者可以使用Apple支持文档中提供的序列号检查器，以查看其设备是否是替换程序的一部分。对于合格的设备，Apple或Apple授权服务提供商将免费提供服务。

Customers with ‌iPhone 11‌ models in need of service can find an Apple Authorized Service Provider, make an appointment at an Apple retail location, or contact Apple Support to arrange a mail-in repair. The program covers affected ‌iPhone 11‌ devices for two years after the first retail sale of the unit, and those who paid to have their devices repaired can contact Apple about a refund.

拥有“ iPhone 11”型号需要维修的客户可以找到Apple授权服务提供商，在Apple零售店预约或联系Apple支持以安排邮寄维修。该程序涵盖了受影响的retailiPhone 11‌设备的首次零售后两年，并且那些付费维修设备的人可以与Apple联系以获得退款。
