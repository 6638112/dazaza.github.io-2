AppsFlyer, which helps businesses evaluate ad campaign performance, extends its Series D from $210M to $225M+ from Salesforce and others at a $2B valuation
AppsFlyer可帮助企业评估广告系列的效果，其D系列从Salesforce等获得的D轮融资从2.1亿美元扩展至2.25亿美元+

2020-11-25 00:08:42

Salesforce Ventures, the investing arm of the marketing software tech giant Salesforce.com is putting money into AppsFlyer Ltd., an analytics firm that helps marketers, app developers and others evaluate the performance of their advertising campaigns.

营销软件技术巨头Salesforce.com的投资部门Salesforce Ventures将资金投入AppsFlyer Ltd.，这是一家分析公司，可帮助营销人员，应用程序开发人员和其他人员评估其广告活动的效果。

The new investment is a late entry to the Series D funding that AppsFlyer announced in January, raising the size of the round to more than $225 million from $210 million, according to the company. The software firm is now valued at $2 billion, up from $1.6 billion in January, it said.

新的投资是AppsFlyer在一月份宣布的D轮融资的较晚进入，该轮融资的规模从2.1亿美元增加到2.25亿美元以上。微软表示，这家软件公司的市值目前已从1月份的16亿美元增至20亿美元。

Founded in Israel, AppsFlyer is best known for its mobile analytics tools, which provide information on how different types of ad campaigns or bits of content drive behavior, such as visiting a website, installing an app or buying something.

AppsFlyer成立于以色列，以其移动分析工具而闻名，该工具可提供有关不同类型的广告活动或部分内容如何驱动行为（例如访问网站，安装应用或购买商品）的信息。

The company said its tools evaluate $28 billion in ad spending a year. They also allow app developers to integrate ad and marketing technology from more than 8,000 other providers including Facebook Inc., Alphabet Google, Adobe and Salesforce.

该公司表示，其工具每年估算的广告支出为280亿美元。它们还允许应用程序开发人员集成来自8,000多家其他提供商的广告和营销技术，包括Facebook Inc.，Alphabet Google，Adobe和Salesforce。

AppsFlyer said its business has grown as marketers seek to understand how viewers go from seeing an ad to acting upon it—known as “attribution” in the industry. “Attribution is a must-have,” said Oren Kaniel, co-founder and chief executive of the company.

AppsFlyer表示，随着营销人员寻求了解观众从看广告到对广告采取行动的方式（在行业中称为“归因”），其业务已经发展壮大。该公司联合创始人兼首席执行官Oren Kaniel说：“归因是必不可少的。”

AppsFlyer has also developed tools for marketers to measure performance while navigating new privacy regulations and decisions by tech giants such as Apple to limit consumer tracking, he said.

他说，AppsFlyer还开发了一些工具，供营销人员在评估性能的同时浏览新的隐私法规和苹果等技术巨头的决定，以限制消费者跟踪。

“We have been looking at delivering software to allow for the kinds of insights that app developers and marketers can benefit from while protecting privacy for the end-user,” Mr. Kaniel said.

坎尼尔说：“我们一直在考虑提供软件，以使应用程序开发人员和营销人员可以从中受益，同时为最终用户保护隐私。”

AppsFlyer will use the latest funding in part to develop new products more quickly. It will also seek to increase its head count of 1,000 employees by around a third, Mr. Kaniel said.

AppsFlyer将使用部分最新资金来更快地开发新产品。 Kaniel先生说，它还将寻求将其1000名员工的人数增加大约三分之一。

AppsFlyer has raised more than $300 million since its founding in 2011 from investors including General Atlantic, Qumra Capital, Goldman Sachs Private Capital Investing, Deutsche Telekom Capital Partners and Magma Venture Partners.

自2011年成立以来，AppsFlyer已从General Atlantic，Qumra Capital，Goldman Sachs Private Capital Investing，Deutsche Telekom Capital Partners和Magma Venture Partners等投资者筹集了超过3亿美元。
