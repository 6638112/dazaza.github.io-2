Super Nintendo World apparently took scenery from a Mario fangame
超级任天堂世界显然是从马里奥粉丝游戏中获得风景的

2021-02-09 21:15:02

A piece of scenery at Japan's newly opened Super Nintendo World theme park area appears to draw direct inspiration from an original piece of art created for a fangame. Twitter user meatball123 was among the first to notice the uncanny similarity after seeing a cactus decoration in the background of a video for the park's Yoshi's Adventure ride. The specific three-pronged design of the cactus in that video doesn't look like anything seen in an official Mario game, but it does match almost precisely the background cacti found in World 2 of Newer Super Mario Bros. Wii, a fan-made mod released in 2013.

日本新开放的“超级任天堂世界”主题公园区的一处风景似乎从为粉丝游戏创作的原始艺术品中汲取了直接灵感。 Twitter用户Meatball123是第一个注意到这种奇异之处的人，此前在公园Yoshi冒险之旅的视频背景中看到了仙人掌装饰。该视频中仙人掌的特定三管齐下的设计看起来并不像在正式的《马里奥》游戏中看到的任何东西，但它确实与《超级超级马里奥兄弟》 Wii的世界2中发现的背景仙人掌完全匹配。制造的模组于2013年发布。

Meatball123 also notes that, as of last weekend, the three-pronged cactus sprite appeared prominently when a search for " New Super Mario Bros. Wii cactus" was performed on Google Images, which could explain how the mixup happened. Representatives for Nintendo and Universal Japan have yet to respond to a request for comment from Ars Technica.

Meatball123还指出，截至上周末，当搜索＆＃34;时，三叉仙人掌子画面显着出现。新的超级马里奥兄弟Wii仙人掌＆＃34;是在Google图片上执行的，这可以解释混淆的发生方式。任天堂和环球日本的代表尚未回应Ars Technica的置评请求。

Newer Super Mario Bros. Wii was the result of a years-long effort by the fan-developers at Newer Team, who've also created a similar mod for the DS version of New Super Mario Bros.. It features new levels, enemies, art, and an original soundtrack that builds on the 2009 Wii original. The mod officially requires a retail copy of the original New Super Mario Bros. Wii to play on a jailbroken Wii or Wii U console, but there are also pre-built ISOs floating around to allow for emulator-based play on a PC.

较新的超级马里奥兄弟Wii是较新团队的粉丝开发者多年努力的结果，他们还为新超级马里奥兄弟的DS版本创建了类似的mod。它具有新的关卡，敌人，艺术以及以2009 Wii原创为基础的原创配乐。该mod正式需要原始的《超级超级马里奥兄弟》 Wii的零售副本，以便在越狱的Wii或Wii U控制台上播放，但周围也有预建的ISO，可以在PC上进行基于模拟器的播放。

Super Nintendo World's apparent use of an original fangame asset takes on some added irony given Nintendo's strict stance on fangames in general. In recent years, the company has issued numerous DMCA takedown notices for fangames—and even some of the tools used to build them. The cactus discovery also comes a month after a fan found his personal 3D rendering of Mario being used on the Super Nintendo World website.

考虑到任天堂对粉丝游戏的严格立场，超级任天堂世界的明显使用原始的粉丝游戏资产具有某些讽刺意味。近年来，该公司发布了许多有关同人游戏的DMCA删除通知-甚至包括一些用于制作它们的工具。一个月前，一个粉丝在超级任天堂世界网站上发现了自己的Mario 3D渲染，这也是仙人掌发现的一个月。

In a somewhat similar bit of history, there's some evidence to suggest that the version of Super Mario Bros. available on the Wii Virtual Console was originally sourced from a pirated ROM dump found on the Internet.

在类似的历史记录中，有一些证据表明Wii虚拟控制台上可用的Super Mario Bros.版本最初是从Internet上的盗版ROM转储中获得的。

In the end, this is probably nothing more than a careless snafu caused by a less-than-scrupulous theme park designer. But it does highlight how the fangame community and more "official" efforts from the IP holders continually feed on each other, as can be seen more directly (and intentionally) in the Sonic fangame community.

最后，这可能仅是由不那么谨慎的主题公园设计师引起的粗心大意。但这确实凸显了同人游戏社区以及更多的“官方”机构。在Sonic粉丝游戏社区中，可以更加直接（有意地）看到，知识产权持有人的努力不断相互借鉴。

I took a Java ME version of Mega Man II, tilesets and assets from Sprints Inc, VGMaps to make sure the levels were correct, and google searches for music when it was re-done for iOS. We were told they had lost *all* assets, and that Keiji Inafune no longer had assets for anything. Hell, the version I was given as my starting point was missing bosses, enemies and most of the background assets and animations. We were owned by Capcom, and couldn't get assets. I can't imagine how much worst it could be as a third party.
