Maryland-based SparkPost, an email delivery, optimization, and analytics service, raises $180M from LLR Partners and others
总部位于马里兰州的SparkPost是一家电子邮件交付，优化和分析服务，从LLR Partners等公司筹集了1.8亿美元

2021-01-28 22:41:40

SparkPost, an email delivery, optimization, and analytics platform, has raised $180 million from LLR Partners, NewSpring Capital, and PNC Bank.

电子邮件传递，优化和分析平台SparkPost已从LLR Partners，NewSpring Capital和PNC Bank筹集了1.8亿美元。

Founded in 2008, Columbia, Maryland-based SparkPost gives enterprises deep insights into their email campaigns, including delivery rate and engagement, while serving up real-time data and analytics to monitor bounce rates, spam traps, and ISP responses to determine whether emails are arriving at their intended destination. Delays or delivery failures can lead to revenue loss or customer churn, particularly if transactional emails are going astray.

SparkPost成立于2008年，总部位于马里兰州哥伦比亚市，可为企业提供有关电子邮件活动的深刻见解，包括传递率和参与度，同时提供实时数据和分析功能，以监控跳出率，垃圾邮件陷阱和ISP响应，以确定电子邮件是否到达他们的预定目的地。延误或交付失败会导致收入损失或客户流失，尤其是在交易电子邮件误入歧途的情况下。

SparkPost had previously raised around $94 million, and with its fresh $180 million the company plans to invest in “the next era of growth” and build on its existing customer base, which includes Zillow, Adobe, Pinterest, Twitter, the New York Times, Booking.com, Rakuten, and Zynga.

SparkPost之前已经筹集了大约9400万美元，并计划以新的1.8亿美元投资“下一个增长时代”，并在现有客户群（包括Zillow，Adobe，Pinterest，Twitter，《纽约时报》， Booking.com，乐天和Zynga。

Despite the hypergrowth surrounding modern communication tools such as messaging apps, Microsoft Teams, and Slack (soon to be a $27.7 billion Salesforce subsidiary), it’s estimated that around 80% of businesses still use email as their primary communication tool, especially when it comes to external communications. That is why email technology continues to garner interest — just last month Exclaimer raised $133 million to help companies manage their email signatures.

尽管围绕现代通信工具（例如消息传递应用程序，Microsoft Teams和Slack（不久将成为277亿美元的Salesforce子公司））发展迅猛，但据估计，仍有大约80％的企业仍将电子邮件用作其主要通信工具，尤其是在外部通讯。这就是为什么电子邮件技术继续引起人们的兴趣的原因-就在上个月，Exclaimer筹集了1.33亿美元，以帮助公司管理其电子邮件签名。

Other notable players in the email management and analytics sphere include SendGrid, which Twilio snapped up in a $2 billion deal two years ago, and MailGun, which was bought by Thoma Bravo a year ago. And all-in-one digital market platform Sendinblue, which offers various email management and optimization tools, raised $160 million a few months back.

电子邮件管理和分析领域的其他知名企业包括：SendGrid（两年前Twilio以20亿美元的价格收购）和MailGun（一年前被Thoma Bravo收购）。而提供多种电子邮件管理和优化工具的多功能数字市场平台Sendinblue在几个月前筹集了1.6亿美元。
