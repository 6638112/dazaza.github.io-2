Charm delivers Stripe's carbon removal purchase ahead of schedule
魅力提前提供条纹的碳删除购买

2021-04-22 11:32:08

Stripe purchased 416 tons CO₂e in carbon removal from Charm in May 2020, as our very first customer. That carbon is now permanently sequestered in geological storage.

STRIPE在2020年5月从魅力中购买了416吨CO 2E，作为我们的第一款客户。现在，该碳在地质储存中永久地隔离。

When Stripe made their purchase, our proposed carbon removal method was only a few weeks old and untested. In the 11 months since then we’ve built, tested, patented, and confirmed our methodology with deep scientific panels not only at Stripe but at Shopify, Microsoft, CarbonPlan, CarbonDirect and others. Even better, we delivered the first 150 tons CO₂e of carbon removal in January 2021, and are announcing that as of March 26, 2021 we’ve completed delivery of the entirety of Stripe’s carbon removal contract and delivered the final documentation of completion to Stripe.

当条纹采购时，我们所提出的碳拆卸方法只有几周而且未经测试。从那时起，我们在11个月内，我们已经建立了，测试，获得了专利，并确认了我们的方法论，不仅在Shoripe，Microsoft，CarbonPlan，Carbondirect和其他人的ShoreIve。更好的是，我们在2021年1月交付了第一个150吨碳删除的碳删除，并且宣布截至3月26日，我们已经完成了整个条纹的碳拆卸合同，并交付了完成的最终文件。

We’re on track to permanently sequester carbon for all contracts ahead of schedule, and are continuously scaling up our sales and sequestration volume.

我们正在追踪以提前的所有合同永久隔离碳，并且不断扩大我们的销售和封存量。

Conscious consumers and companies are moving towards engineered carbon removals after carbon registries, verification bodies and trusted brands have sold fraudulent carbon offsets with abandon. Research from Oxford, Stanford and Berkeley has found that 85% of nature-based carbon offsets sold today are not “additional”, meaning nothing happens as a result of the carbon offset purchase. This has triggered a momentous shift towards permanent removals beginning in 2019 with Stripe’s landmark carbon removal RFP, followed by Shopify’s later in 2019 and then Microsoft’s in 2020.

有意识的消费者和公司正在碳登记机构后迈向工程碳，核查机构和可信品牌已卖出欺诈性碳抵消。牛津，斯坦福和伯克利的研究发现，今天销售的85％的自然碳偏移量不是“额外”，这意味着由于碳抵消购买而不会发生任何事情。这引发了2019年开始的永久删除的重大移除，其中有条纹的地标碳删除RFP，其次是2019年晚些时候的购物，然后在2020年。

对于公司和个人来说，寻求对碳除去和整体气候的异常催化作用，随着条纹已经，我们希望在魅力的碳拆除途径上分享更多信息。在此处了解更多并通过Sales@charmindustrial.com与我们联系。

我们非常感谢Stripe的“第一次搬家”购买，并对我们的持续快速扩大和与条纹合作兴奋。
