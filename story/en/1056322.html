Cullen Hoback: “I’m the guy who made that QAnon series on HBO Max. AMA!”
Cullen Hoback：“我是那个在HBO Max上制作了Qanon系列的人。 AMA！“

2021-04-06 03:05:25

Hi all! Been about 7 years since I did an AMA with my digital privacy expose Terms and Conditions May Apply. Thought I’d try my luck with another AMA about my new HBO series Q: Into the Storm. I suspect there will be SPOILERS AHEAD, so if you haven’t watched the series yet, you may want to come back in 6 hours (the whole show is now available on HBO Max)!

大家好！大约7年以来，我用我的数字隐私作出了AMA，暴露条款和条件可能适用。以为我会尝试我的运气与另一个关于我的新的HBO系列问答：进入风暴。我怀疑将会有剧透，所以如果你还没有看到该系列，你可能想在6个小时内回来（整个节目现在在HBO Max上提供）！

Reddit is actually the reason I made this series. Back in 2018, when QAnon was gaining steam, the site decided to ban the r/greatawakening subreddit. I was only peripherally aware of Q at the time, but having talked about digital rights for a decade, I wondered what it was about Q that deserved to be banned, and might that be a sign of where the internet was headed.

Reddit实际上是我制作了这个系列的原因。回来于2018年，当Qanon正在获得蒸汽时，该网站决定禁止R / GREEAWAKENING DUSHEDDIT。我当时只有周边意识到Q，但是已经谈到了十年的数字权利，我想知道它是关于被禁止的Q，并且可能是互联网领导的标志。

While I started with a question about digital free speech (and now we’re seeing a lot of talk about reforming or repealing Section 230), I was also attracted to the mystery of who was behind QAnon. I spent a few weeks researching theories before directing my attention to 8chan, the site where Q posts. I figured, if anyone knew who was behind the operation, it would be those who have the actual data related to Q. However, I continued to explore various theories in tandem.

虽然我开始了关于数字自由讲话的问题（现在我们看到了很多关于改革或废除第230节的谈话），但我也被谁落后于Qanon的谜团。我花了几周的几周研究理论，然后引起我的注意力，即Q帖子的网站。我想，如果有人知道谁在运作背后，那些有与Q相关的实际数据的人就是那些。然而，我继续探讨串联的各种理论。

At the time, 8chan was relatively unknown, as were the players behind it. I initially contacted Fred Brennan, the site’s creator, who was the most public facing figure on the 8chan team. According to Fred, he agreed to talk to me because I had trolled Mark Zuckerberg in my previous film. Fred was on the outs with the Watkinses, who owned and operated the site.

当时，8chan相对未知，就像它背后的球员一样。我最初联系了该网站的创造者的Fred Brennan，他是8茶队团队中最公开的人物。据弗雷德说，他同意和我谈谈，因为我在我以前的电影中遇到了马克扎克伯格。弗雷德与沃特金斯的外出，他们拥有和运营的网站。

A lot of people wonder why Ron and Jim Watkins agreed to talk with me. I think it’s a mixture of reasons. I had a background in digital rights, they wanted to counter Fred’s narrative, and I suspect they thought it would be fun to troll me. Over time, I believe their motivations for continuing with the project evolved, as is often the case with any documentary.

很多人想知道为什么罗恩和吉姆沃特金斯同意与我交谈。我认为这是一个原因的混合。我在数字版权中有一个背景，他们想要抵消弗雷德的叙述，我怀疑他们认为漫步我会很有趣。随着时间的推移，我相信他们继续进化项目的动机，正如任何纪录片的案例通常如此。

I aimed to investigate Q’s origins with a neutral eye and was upfront with everyone about my intentions, from the experts and anti-Q researchers to those bolstering Q. This meant moving between sides that hate each other.

我旨在调查Q的起源与中性的眼睛，并从专家和反Q研究人员那里与每个人都与每个人的意图提升到那些博尔斯特雷Q.这意味着在彼此讨厌的两侧之间移动。

QAnons (yes, QAnon is the correct term) are deeply distrustful of mainstream media. But as with every project I’ve made, this was produced independently until almost the end. Most of the time it was just me finding cheap flights and shooting solo. I think many of the sources in this story could identify with the DIY approach. I filmed over 1700 hours of footage for this project, collected more than 30k archival assets and filmed in 8 different countries trying to track down Q.

Qanons（是的，Qanon是正确的术语）深刻不信任主流媒体。但与我所做的每个项目一样，这是独立生产直到几乎结束。大部分时间都只是我找到特价航班和射击独奏。我认为这个故事中的许多消息来源可以识别DIY方法。我在这个项目中拍摄了超过1700小时的镜头，收集了30多个档案资产，并在8个不同的国家拍摄，试图追踪Q.
