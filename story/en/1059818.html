Supreme Court Cuts Federal Trade Commission Powers to Recover Ill-Gotten Gains
最高法院削减了联邦贸易委员会的权力，以恢复生病的收益

2021-04-23 04:25:04

WASHINGTON—The Supreme Court on Thursday curbed the Federal Trade Commission’s longtime practice of seeking to recover ill-gotten gains in court from companies and individuals who cheat consumers, upending a central enforcement tool the agency has relied upon for decades.

华盛顿周四最高法院遏制了联邦贸易委员会的长期实践，寻求在欺骗消费者的公司和个人中恢复法庭和个人的收益，上台原子能机构已经依赖于几十年。

The court, in an opinion by Justice Stephen Breyer, ruled unanimously that a 1973 law, which gives the FTC the right to seek court injunctions to stop fraudulent or deceptive commercial activity, doesn’t grant the commission the power to seek financial judgments as well.

在斯蒂芬雷耶的意见中，法院一致地统治了1973年的法律，使FTC禁止寻求欺诈或欺骗性商业活动的法院禁令的权利，并没有授予委员会也征收委员会寻求财务判断的权力。

The court said if the FTC wants to seek financial recoveries on behalf of defrauded consumers, it needs to rely on other, more-limited legal provisions that require it to go through detailed administrative proceedings aimed first at identifying and halting the fraudulent conduct.

法院表示，如果FTC代表欺诈的消费者寻求财务恢复，则需要依靠其他有限的法律规定，要求它通过详细的行政诉讼，以首先识别和停止欺诈行为。

The commission had argued such an approach would be slow and ineffective. Relying on those means alone, it said, would leave the commission only able to stop future fraudulent conduct while allowing defendants to keep the profits they already made at consumers’ expense.

委员会认为这种方法会缓慢而无效。它说，依靠这些手段，据说，将使委员会只能阻止未来的欺诈行为，同时让被告能够保持他们已经以消费者的费用所取得的利润。

“If the commission believes that authority too cumbersome or otherwise inadequate, it is, of course, free to ask Congress to grant it further remedial authority,” Justice Breyer wrote.

“如果委员会认为，当然，当然，当然，如果是自由要求国会将其授予其进一步补救权力，”雷雷德写道。
