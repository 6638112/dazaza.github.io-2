Users should be allowed to sue U.S. tech giants under EU rules, civil groups say
民间团体表示，根据欧盟规则，用户应该被允许起诉美国科技巨头

2022-02-22 18:22:06

BRUSSELS, Feb 22 (Reuters) - Individual users should be allowed to take U.S. tech giants to court for breaching landmark EU rules aimed at curbing their power, Privacy International, pan-European consumer group BEUC and a number of academics said on Tuesday.

布鲁塞尔，2月22日（路透社）-泛欧洲消费者组织BEUC和一些学者周二表示，个人用户应该被允许将美国科技巨头告上法庭，因为他们违反了欧盟旨在限制其权力的标志性规则。

The call by the coalition of 31 groups and academics comes as EU lawmakers and EU countries thrashed out the final points of the Digital Markets Act (DMA) proposed by EU antitrust chief Margrethe Vestager just over a year ago before it can become law.

由31个团体和学者组成的联盟发出这一呼吁之际，欧盟立法者和欧盟国家正在讨论欧盟反垄断主管玛格丽特·维斯塔格（Margrethe Vestager）一年多前提出的《数字市场法》（DMA）的最后几点，该法案才得以成为法律。

The draft DMA sets out a list of dos and don'ts for online gatekeepers, in effect targeting Apple (AAPL.O), Alphabet (GOOGL.O) unit Google, Facebook parent Meta (FB.O), Amazon and Microsoft (MSFT.O).

DMA草案列出了待办事项和待办事项的清单'；针对在线守门人的ts，实际上是针对苹果（Apple）、Alphabet（Google）、Facebook母公司Meta（FB.O）、亚马逊（Amazon）和微软（Microsoft）的。

"The DMA must enable users, both individually and collectively, to bring enforcement actions for violation of DMA rules before national courts," the group said in an open letter to EU institutions.

"DMA必须允许用户，无论是个人还是集体，向国家法院提起违反DMA规则的执法行动，"；该组织在致欧盟机构的公开信中表示。

While EU lawmakers are open to the idea, EU countries have so far ignored the demand.

尽管欧盟立法者对这一想法持开放态度，但欧盟国家迄今为止忽视了这一要求。

The coalition also urged lawmakers and EU countries to let consumer bodies and representatives of civil society take part in processes set out under the draft rules so that their needs would be reflected in the Commission's decisions on the tech giants.

该联盟还敦促立法者和欧盟国家让消费者团体和民间社会代表参与规则草案规定的程序，以便他们的需求在委员会中得到反映；这是科技巨头的决定。

Other signatories of the open letter include U.S.-based Center for Digital Democracy and the Consumer Federation of America, the Civil Liberties Union for Europe, European Digital Rights and academics from the University of Oxford, the Vienna University of Economics and Business and the University of Amsterdam.

公开信的其他签署者包括美国数字民主中心和美国消费者联合会、欧洲公民自由联盟、欧洲数字权利和来自牛津大学、维也纳经济和商业大学和阿姆斯特丹大学的学者。
