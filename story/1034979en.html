Onfido的Husayn Ksai后退一步，在计划中的IPO之前引入新的首席执行官

2020-11-14 08:32:05

Kassai has of this week handed over the reins to Mike Tuchen, the former CEO of Talend who successfully took the company to the Nasdaq in 2016.

卡赛本周将控制权移交给了塔伦德(Talend)前首席执行官迈克·图晨(Mike Tuchen)，他于2016年成功地将公司带到了纳斯达克(Nasdaq)。

Kassai says he will remain with Onfido, working “2-3 days a week” to assist Tuchen as he leads the company through the next period of growth across sectors and geographies, and toward that IPO.

卡塞表示，他将继续留在Onfido，每周工作2-3天，协助图晨度过下一个跨行业、跨地域的增长期，并实现首次公开募股(IPO)。

Prior to Talend, a leader in cloud data integration, Tuchen led Rapid7, a security software startup, and founded a marketing analytics startup in between senior management roles at Microsoft and Polycom.

在担任云数据集成领导者Talend之前，图晨领导了安全软件初创公司Rapid7，并在微软和Polycom担任高级管理职务期间创立了一家营销分析初创公司。

Kassai co-founded Onfido with Eamon Jubbawy and Ruhul Amin in 2012 to make digital identity verification simpler and more accurate, and it now employs more than 400 people globally.

2012年，卡赛与埃蒙·朱巴维(Eamon Jubbawy)和鲁胡尔·阿明(Ruhul Amin)共同创立了Onfido，以使数字身份验证变得更简单、更准确，目前该公司在全球拥有400多名员工。

Speaking exclusively to TechCrunch, Kassai said: “It’s kind’ve been non-stop for 10 years. I’ve taken us from zero to one. And in order to go from ‘one to 100’, specifically an IPO and listing in large part, that’s the stage where you have to meet with consulting firms, banks etc., which has a lot of fun in it, but it’s not what I’m interested in. So I’d rather step down, still work with the company and help an experienced CEO take this forward for the next stage.”

在接受TechCrunch的独家采访时，卡塞说：“这种感觉已经持续了10年。我把我们从零带到了一。为了从1到100，特别是在很大程度上是首次公开募股(IPO)和上市，这是你必须与咨询公司、银行等会面的阶段，这其中有很多乐趣，但这不是我感兴趣的。因此，我宁愿辞职，继续在公司工作，帮助一位经验丰富的首席执行官把这件事推进到下一阶段。

Asked if he was stepping down for any other reason Kassai told TechCrunch: “No this is predominantly about the IPO focus, plus I’ve been speaking internally for a year about taking a break after this 10-year period. My personal life has been on hold for this whole time. Exercise, eating well, family birthdays, everything else that goes with it. On hold. This move, in part, helps me get back some of my personal life, while the company will be in good hands.”

当被问及他是否出于其他原因辞职时，卡塞告诉TechCrunch：“不，这主要是因为IPO的重点，而且我已经在内部谈论了一年，想在这10年之后休息一下。”我的个人生活在这段时间里一直处于搁置状态。锻炼身体，吃得好，家人过生日，以及其他一切随之而来的事情。请稍等。这一举动在一定程度上帮助我找回了一些个人生活，同时公司也会得到很好的管理。

Asked if he would be taking the title of chairman or co-CEO or some other title, Kassai said that was undecided that this point.

当被问及他是否会担任董事长或联席首席执行官或其他头衔时，卡赛表示，目前还没有决定。

On the joint call between Tuchen and Kassai, Tuchen said: “My first company was a security company in Boston. It’s now public on the Nasdaq. We took Talends public in 2016. So, I’ve had a lot of experience working across Europe and the U.S.”

关于图晨和卡塞的联合通话，图晨说：“我的第一家公司是波士顿的一家安保公司。该公司现已在纳斯达克(Nasdaq)公开上市。我们在2016年将故事公之于众。所以，我在欧洲和美国工作过很多次。“

Tuchen said he would be based in California, now that the whole company has moved to remote working because of the pandemic: “I don’t expect to move a lot during COVID. We are a remote-first company right now and will be for the medium term at least, and still trying to work out what our longer-term plans are, as we get — post-vaccine — back in the office. I spent a lot of time on the road before, but we’ll be figuring out how to develop that same kind of relationship with people over Zoom, with all the stuff that, you know, we’re used to doing in the regular world. So we’re kind of experimenting and figuring that out. The entire process of me joining was done over Zoom. And now the whole onboarding and getting to know the company is all being done on top, so we’re all kind of breaking new ground here in this dynamic environment.”

图晨说，既然整个公司因为疫情已经转移到远程工作，他将常驻加利福尼亚州：“我预计在COVID期间不会有太多搬家。我们现在是一家远程优先的公司，至少在中期内会是这样，当我们在疫苗接种后回到办公室时，仍在努力弄清楚我们的长期计划是什么。我之前在旅途中花了很多时间，但我们会弄清楚如何通过Zoom与人们建立同样的关系，你知道，我们在常规世界中习惯做的所有事情。因此，我们正在进行实验，并弄清楚这一点。我加入的整个过程都是通过Zoom完成的。现在，整个入职和对公司的了解都是在顶层完成的，所以我们在这个充满活力的环境中都在某种程度上开辟了新的天地。“。

Tuchen said no decision had been made about where the IPO would be placed, but “most likely on one of the U.S. exchanges.”

图晨说，目前还没有就IPO的地点做出决定，但“很可能是在美国的一家交易所”。

An IPO decision may well have been precipitated by increasing competition in the identity verification space from Stripe, Berbix and Persona entering the market, alongside pressure from incumbents like Acuant and Mitek.

之所以做出IPO决定，很可能是因为Strip、Berbix和Persona进入市场的身份验证领域的竞争日益激烈，以及来自Acuant和Mitek等老牌公司的压力。
