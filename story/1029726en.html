不仅仅是电子邮件，还可以将您的(Twilio)Webhooks发送给Mailinator

2020-10-19 14:35:06

You can now direct JSON webhooks from anywhere to Mailinator.com. Mailinator has always been known for email but has actually supported SMS for several years.Mailinator now allows anyone to post their webhooks directly into the Public (i.e. FREE) Mailinator system.

现在，您可以将JSON webhooks从任何地方定向到mailinator.com。Mailinator一直以电子邮件闻名，但实际上支持SMS已经有几年了。Mailinator现在允许任何人直接将他们的网络挂钩发布到公共(即免费)Mailinator系统中。

Messages that arrive this way will arrive in the destination inbox just as an email would. Of course, incoming webhooks will retain their original formand are visible by clicking the link on the inbox page. It’s easy too! Here’s the URL:

Posting JSON data to that url will inject that data into the respective Mailnator inbox. Here’s a full curl command to try it out right now:

将JSON数据发送到该URL会将该数据注入相应的Mailnator收件箱。下面是一个完整的curl命令，现在就可以试用一下：

curl -v -d '{"from":"MyMailinatorTest", "subject":"testing message", "text" : "hello world", "to" : "jack" }'-H "Content-Type: application/json"-X POST "https://www.mailinator.com/api/v2/domains/public/webhook/bob/"

For Mailinator subscribers, the functionality goes deeper. Firstly, you can publish webhooks to your Private Domain as you’d expect. In addition, incoming webhooks enjoy allthe advantages of private messages with Mailinator including access via Web or API, and execution through the Mailinator Rule System.

对于Mailinator订阅者，该功能更加深入。首先，您可以像您预期的那样将WebHook发布到您的私有域。此外，传入的WebHook享受Mailinator私有消息的所有优势，包括通过Web或API访问，以及通过Mailinator规则系统执行。

By webhooking your SMS messages into your Private Domain, your automated tests now have a one-stop-shop (with just one API you’re already used to), to access the incoming messagesthat make up your Message Workflow.

通过将您的SMS消息通过网络连接到您的专用域中，您的自动化测试现在有了一站式服务(只有一个您已经习惯的API)，可以访问组成您的消息工作流的传入消息。
