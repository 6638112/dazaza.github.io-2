Alienware introduces its first AMD-based gaming laptop in 14 years
Alienware在14年内介绍了基于AMD的游戏笔记本电脑

2021-04-09 00:56:53

Recap: Alienware has announced its first AMD-powered gaming laptop since the Aurora mALX dropped way back in 2007. That behemoth shipped with an AMD Turion 64 ML-44 CPU, 2GB of DDR PC3200 RAM and a pair of Nvidia GeForce Go 7900 GTX graphics cards. My, how times have changed.

RECAP：Alienware已宣布其首批AMD动力的游戏笔记本电脑，自从AuroraMalx于2007年返回后。该庞然大巨头用AMD Turion 64 ML-44 CPU，2GB的DDR PC3200 RAM和一对NVIDIA GeForce Go 7900 GTX图形牌。我的，时间已经发生了变化。

The redesigned Alienware m15 Ryzen Edition R5 is a 15-inch gaming laptop that can be outfitted with your choice of FHD 360Hz, QHD 240Hz, or FHD 165Hz display options (the 360Hz and 240Hz panels are also compatible with Nvidia G-Sync and Advanced Optimus tech.

重新设计的Alienware M15 Ryzen Edition R5是一款15英寸的游戏笔记本电脑，可以选择您选择的FHD 360Hz，QHD 240Hz或FHD 165Hz显示选项（360Hz和240Hz面板也兼容NVIDIA G-SYNC和Advanced Optimus技术。

Systems can be configured with up to an AMD Ryzen 5000 H-Series processor and Nvidia GeForce RTX 30-series graphics. What’s more, there’s user-upgradeable 3200MHz DDR4 memory for the first time, and an optional low-profile mechanical keyboard that was co-developed with Cherry MX.

系统可以配置为AMD Ryzen 5000 H系列处理器和NVIDIA GeForce RTX 30系列图形。更重要的是，第一次拥有用户升级的3200MHz DDR4内存，以及与樱桃MX共同开发的可选的低调机械键盘。

Should buyers spring for the upgraded keyboard, they can expect a 15 million keystroke lifecycle, 1.8mm total key travel, N-key rollover technology and per-key RGB lighting.

应为升级的键盘买家春季，他们可以预期为1500万击键生命周期，1.8毫米的关键旅行，N-键翻转技术和每键RGB照明。

In terms of connectivity, you’ll get a Killer-branded AX1650 Wi-Fi 6 card, a 2.5Gbps Ethernet jack, HDMI 2.1, two USB 3.2 Gen 1 ports, a single USB 3.2 Gen 2 port and one USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C port.

在连接方面，您将获得一个杀手品牌的AX1650 Wi-Fi 6卡，一个2.5Gbps以太网插孔HDMI 2.1，两个USB 3.2 Gen 1端口，单个USB 3.2 Gen 2端口和一个USB 3.2 Gen 2类型-c端口。

The Alienware m15 Ryzen Edition R5 launches in the US on April 20 starting at $1,793.98.

Alienware M15 Ryzen Edition R5于4月20日在美国推出，起价为1,793.98美元。
