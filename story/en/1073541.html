Mozilla Foundation places two-page advocacy ad in The New York Times (2004)
Mozilla基金会在纽约时报刊登两页宣传广告（2004）

2022-02-15 22:16:05

MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif. – December 15th, 2004 – The Mozilla Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to preserving choice and promoting innovation on the Internet, today announced that it has placed a two-page ad in the December 16th edition of the New York Times. The ad, coordinated by Spread Firefox, features the names of the thousands of people worldwide who contributed to the Mozilla Foundation’s fundraising campaign to support last month’s highly successful launch of the open source Mozilla Firefox 1.0 web browser.

芒廷维尤，Calif. - 2004年12月15日- Mozilla基金会，一个致力于保存选择和促进互联网创新的非营利组织，今天宣布它已经在12月16日版的纽约时报上放置了一个两页的广告。这则广告由Firefox发布，它的特点是全球成千上万的人参与了Mozilla基金会的募捐活动，以支持上个月成功推出开源Mozilla Firefox 1网络浏览器。

Spread Firefox is the volunteer-run Mozilla advocacy site, with over 50,000 registered members, where community marketing activities are organized to raise awareness and to promote the adoption of Firefox. The Spread Firefox site is powered by the open source CivicSpace web site software. Community members may submit ideas and join volunteer teams by visiting http://www.spreadfirefox.com.

Spread Firefox是一个由志愿者运营的Mozilla倡导网站，拥有超过50000名注册会员，在这里组织社区营销活动，以提高人们对Firefox的认识并促进Firefox的采用。Spread Firefox网站由开源CivicSpace网站软件提供支持。社区成员可以通过访问提交想法并加入志愿者团队http://www.spreadfirefox.com.

The design and development of the New York Times ad was led by Christopher Messina, a San Francisco-based designer and a volunteer leader at Spread Firefox.

《纽约时报》广告的设计和开发由克里斯托弗·梅西纳（Christopher Messina）领导，他是旧金山的设计师，也是Spread Firefox的志愿者领导者。

获取Firefox Mozilla Firefox 1可以免费下载，也可以从Mozilla基金会的Firefox指南中购买CD-ROM版本。http://www.mozilla.org.

About the Mozilla Foundation Established in July 2003, the Mozilla Foundation is a non-profit organization hosting a collection of leading innovators whose mission is to preserve choice and innovation on the Internet. The Foundation provides organizational, legal, and financial support for the Mozilla open source software project. The Foundation is based in Mountain View, California and is the heir to the legacy and tradition of the Internet’s first widely used browser, Netscape. Learn more about the Mozilla Foundation at http://www.mozilla.org.

关于Mozilla基金会成立于2003年7月，Mozilla基金会是一个非营利性组织，托管一系列领先的创新者，其使命是保持互联网上的选择和创新。该基金会为Mozilla开源软件项目提供了组织、法律和财政支持。该基金会总部位于加利福尼亚芒廷维尤，是互联网第一个广泛使用的浏览器Netscape的遗产和传统的继承人。了解更多关于Mozilla基金会的信息http://www.mozilla.org.
