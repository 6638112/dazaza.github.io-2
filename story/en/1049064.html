Pixel 5a leak shows a headphone jack, flat screen, and a familiar design
Pixel 5a泄漏显示耳机插孔，纯平屏幕和熟悉的设计

2021-02-24 23:09:28

Steve Hemmerstoffer, aka OnLeaks, is back with our first look at a render of Google's next midrange phone, the Pixel 5a. Hemmerstoffer previously nailed the design of the Pixel 4a all the way back in January 2020, so it's smart to take his Pixel 5a info seriously.

史蒂夫·海默斯托弗（Steve Hemmerstoffer，又名OnLeaks）再次展示了Google的下一代中端手机Pixel 5a。 Hemmerstoffer早在2020年1月就一直对Pixel 4a进行设计，因此认真对待Pixel 5a信息很明智。

There really isn't much to see in the renders since the Pixel 5a looks identical to previous Pixel devices like the Pixel 4a 5G and the Pixel 5, and it isn't that much different from the Pixel 4a. In this case, Google isn't fixing what isn't broken. The design looks perfectly modern with slim bezels and a hole-punch camera, and there's really no need to demand change for change's sake.

由于Pixel 5a看起来与以前的Pixel设备（例如Pixel 4a 5G和Pixel 5）完全一样，因此在渲染中确实看不到太多东西，并且与Pixel 4a并没有太大区别。在这种情况下，Google不会修复未解决的问题。该设计采用超薄边框和打孔相机，看起来非常现代，因此确实不需要为进行更改而要求更改。

Like a few other midrange phones, the Pixel 5a offers some design decisions you might actually prefer to a flagship smartphone. There's a flat-screen without any curved sides, a headphone jack, and a rear capacitive fingerprint reader. Hemmerstoffer says the phone has a plastic back, stereo speakers, and a 6.2-inch display, which is a size increase over the 5.8-inch Pixel 4a. The back has an unknown extra camera, but if Google follows the lead of the Pixel 4a 5G and Pixel 5, the camera will have a wide-angle lens. After using the same camera sensor on the Pixel 2, 3, 4, and 5, will the 5a be the first time in four years Google upgrades its camera hardware? That might be too much to ask from a midrange phone.

与其他一些中端手机一样，Pixel 5a提供了一些您可能实际上更喜欢旗舰智能手机的设计决策。平板电脑没有任何弯曲的侧面，耳机插孔和后置电容式指纹读取器。 Hemmerstoffer说，这款手机具有塑料后盖，立体声扬声器和6.2英寸显示屏，比5.8英寸Pixel 4a尺寸要大。背面有一个未知的额外摄像头，但是如果Google遵循Pixel 4a 5G和Pixel 5的领导，则摄像头将具有广角镜。在Pixel 2、3、4和5上使用相同的相机传感器后，5a会不会是Google四年来第一次升级其相机硬件？从中端手机问这个问题可能太多了。

We're still missing the other important details, like the specs and a price. The Pixel 3a was $400 and the Pixel 4a was $350, making both of them hard to argue with when you were getting great performance for so little.

我们仍然缺少其他重要细节，例如规格和价格。 Pixel 3a的售价为400美元，Pixel 4a的售价为350美元，这使得当您获得如此出色的性能时，两者都难以与众不同。

It's anyone's guess as to when the Pixel 5a will come out. The Pixel 4a was first leaked eight months before it launched in August, but a lot of that time was probably due to delays from COVID-19. The Pixel 3a launched in the middle of the year, at Google I/O 2019, but there's a good chance there won't even be a Google I/O this year.

有人猜测Pixel 5a何时发布。 Pixel 4a于8月发布前八个月首次泄漏，但其中大部分时间可能是由于COVID-19的延迟。 Pixel 3a于年中在2019年Google I / O上发布，但今年很有可能甚至没有Google I / O。
