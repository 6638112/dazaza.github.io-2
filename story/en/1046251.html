Robinhood says due to “recent volatility” it is restricting trading in certain stocks, including GameStop, AMC, BlackBerry, and Nokia
罗宾汉表示，由于“近期波动”，它限制了某些股票的交易，包括GameStop，AMC，黑莓和诺基亚

2021-01-28 22:33:54

Amid the flurry of speculative activity in GameStop, brokers took steps to restrict the trading in GameStop stock and options and other related securities. In some cases, investors would only we able to sell their positions and not open new ones.

在GameStop中大量的投机活动中，经纪人采取措施限制GameStop股票，期权和其他相关证券的交易。在某些情况下，投资者只能出售他们的头寸而不能开设新头寸。

Free-stock trading pioneer Robinhood and Interactive Brokers both took steps to curb the wild trading activity in heavily shorted names on Thursday.

自由股票交易的先驱罗宾汉（Robinhood）和盈透证券（Interactive Brokers）都在周四采取了措施，以严重做空的名义遏制疯狂的交易活动。

Shares of GameStock reversed course and slid quickly into negative territory as word of the trading restrictions spread. The stock, which traded above $500 at one point in premarket trading, was below $290 per share shortly before the opening bell.

随着交易限制的传开，GameStock的股价逆转并迅速跌至负数。该股在盘前交易中一度交易价格超过500美元，在开盘前不久跌破了每股290美元。

Here is the statement by Robinhood: "We continuously monitor the markets and make changes where necessary. In light of recent volatility, we are restricting transactions for certain securities to position closing only, including $AMC, $BB, $BBBY, $EXPR, $GME, $KOSS, $NAKD and $NOK. We also raised margin requirements for certain securities."

这是罗宾汉（Robinhood）的声明：＆＃34;我们不断监视市场并在必要时进行更改。鉴于最近的波动，我们将某些证券的交易限制为仅平仓，包括$ AMC，$ BB，$ BBBY，$ EXPR，$ GME，$ KOSS，$ NAKD和$ NOK。我们还提高了某些证券的保证金要求。＆＃34;

Raising margin requirements increases how much money an investor using leverage and derivatives must have in their brokerage account after a stock purchase.

提高保证金要求增加了使用杠杆和衍生工具的投资者在购买股票后必须在经纪帐户中拥有多少资金。

Here is the statement to CNBC from Interactive Brokers: "As of midday yesterday, (1/27/2021) Interactive Brokers has put AMC, BB, EXPR, GME, and KOSS option trading into liquidation only due to the extraordinary volatility in the markets. In addition, long stock positions will require 100% margin and short stock positions will require 300% margin until further notice. We do not believe this situation will subside until the exchanges and regulators halt or put certain symbols into liquidation only. We will continue to monitor market conditions and may add or remove symbols as may be warranted."

这是盈透证券向CNBC的声明：＆＃34;截至昨天中午，（1/27/2021）盈透证券仅由于波动性极大而将AMC，BB，EXPR，GME和KOSS期权交易清算在市场上。此外，多头头寸将需要100％的保证金，而空头头寸将需要300％的保证金，直至另行通知。我们认为，直到交易所和监管机构停止或仅将某些符号清算之前，这种情况不会消退。我们将继续监视市场状况，并可能根据需要添加或删除符号。＆＃34;

Shares of GameStop have ballooned more than 400% this week and nearly 1,750% this year thanks to emboldened retail investors trying to stick it to Wall Street pros. The rookies are piling into names heavily shorted by hedge funds, squeezing the stocks higher as the institutions rush to cover their losses.

由于有勇气的散户投资者试图将GameStop坚持为华尔街专业人士，因此GameStop的股价本周飙升了400％以上，今年飙升了近1,750％。新秀们涌入大量被对冲基金做空的名字，随着机构争相弥补损失，股票被迫走高。

The steps taken Thursday were even more drastic than what brokers did earlier in the week. TD Ameritrade and Charles Schwab raised margin requirements on Wednesday.

周四采取的措施比经纪人在本周早些时候采取的措施更为严厉。 TD Ameritrade和Charles Schwab在周三提高了保证金要求。
