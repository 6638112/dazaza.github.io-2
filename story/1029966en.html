Pixel 5用户报告手机显示屏和边框之间存在差距

2020-10-20 13:50:09

As the Pixel 5 starts to make its way into customers' hands, we've received several reports of a gap between the screen's plastic bumper and the bioresin-coated aluminum frame of the phone. Reports vary, but the area around the front-facing camera appears to be a hotspot for the problem, and customers are concerned this gap could affect the phone's IP-rated water resistance.

随着Pixel5开始进入消费者手中，我们收到了几份报告，称屏幕的塑料保险杠和手机的生物树脂涂层铝框之间存在缺口。报道各不相同，但前置摄像头周围的区域似乎是问题的热点，客户担心这个缺口可能会影响手机的IP级防水性能。

The volume of reports isn't especially large by itself, but we have to assume the limited number of Pixel 5 units in the wild has something to do with that, and the number of new reports is increasing at a consistent rate. Complaints have been picking up over the last two days at venues like the Pixel Phone product forum and XDA Forum, with plenty of photos showing off not just the gap itself, but how much it can vary in location and prominence between units.

报告的数量本身并不是特别大，但我们必须假设Pixel5在野外的有限数量与此有关，而且新报告的数量正在以稳定的速度增长。在过去的两天里，Pixel手机产品论坛和XDA论坛等场所的投诉不断增加，大量的照片不仅展示了差距本身，还展示了不同单位之间的位置和显著性有多大的不同。

We can confirm that our own Pixel 5 review unit is also affected, though to a lesser degree than some of those pictured above. Most reports show it in the corner closest to the display's camera cutout, along the top of the phone, and above the SIM tray on the left side, but it may also appear in other areas as well. Though most images demonstrate the problem in the Just Black colorway (probably because it's most visible with the stronger contrast), it also affects the Sage Green color.

我们可以确认，我们自己的Pixel5审查单元也受到了影响，尽管程度比上图中的一些要小。大多数报告显示，它位于最靠近显示屏摄像头插口的角落，沿着手机顶部，在左侧的SIM卡托盘上方，但它也可能出现在其他区域。虽然大多数图像在纯黑色配色中显示了这个问题(可能是因为它在对比度较强的情况下最明显)，但它也会影响鼠尾草绿色。

We do not know if this gap affects the phone's rated water resistance, but we do know it accrues dust, and the irregularity doesn't look too nice on a premium device. Based on recent reports, these gaps are present from the start, so the screen doesn't appear to be separating over time.

我们不知道这个间隙是否会影响手机的额定防水性能，但我们知道它会积灰，而且这种不规则性在高端设备上看起来不太好看。根据最近的报告，这些差距从一开始就存在，因此屏幕似乎不会随着时间的推移而分离。

As reports continue to pick up, we've reached out to Google for more information regarding this issue, and we'll be sure to update if we hear anything back.

随着报道的不断增多，我们已经联系了谷歌，以获取关于这个问题的更多信息，如果我们得到任何回复，我们一定会更新的。
