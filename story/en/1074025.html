How advertisers are dealing with Facebook ad price increases prompted by Apple's ATT changes, with some shifting their ad budgets to Google, Amazon, and TikTok
广告商如何应对因苹果ATT变化而导致的Facebook广告价格上涨，一些广告商将广告预算转移到了谷歌、亚马逊和TikTok

2022-02-19 05:27:12

Tech Privacy features prompt e-commerce businesses to slash ad spending; ‘Apple has changed the game’

科技隐私功能促使电子商务企业大幅削减广告支出苹果改变了游戏规则

Martha Krueger, who runs a gift-basket business called Giften Market, used to spend her entire advertising budget on Meta Platforms FB -0.75% Facebook and Instagram. She picked up a new customer for every $14 she spent.

玛莎·克鲁格（Martha Krueger）经营着一家名为礼品篮市场（Giften Market）的企业，她曾将全部广告预算花在元平台FB上——Facebook和Instagram的0.75%。她花了每14美元就有一位新顾客。

When Apple AAPL -0.94% introduced a privacy feature for mobile devices last year that restricts user tracking, she said, her costs to acquire such customers rose 10-fold. In October, she shifted her whole ad budget to search ads on Alphabet GOOG -1.39% Google.

她说，去年，苹果AAPL（0.94%）推出了一项限制用户跟踪的移动设备隐私功能，她获得此类客户的成本增加了10倍。去年10月，她将全部广告预算转移到了字母谷歌搜索广告上——1.39%是谷歌。

Lots of other companies that depend on e-commerce sales, including makers of nutritional powders, eyebrow stencils and toilet sprays, are taking a look at their bottom lines and deciding the same thing. They are slashing their spending on Facebook and Instagram and sending their ad money to Google, Amazon.com Snap and other platforms, according to ad buyers and e-commerce companies.

许多依赖电子商务销售的其他公司，包括营养粉、眉毛模板和马桶喷雾剂的制造商，正在审视自己的底线，并做出同样的决定。他们正在大幅削减在Facebook和Instagram上的支出，并将广告费发送给谷歌、亚马逊。根据广告买家和电子商务公司的说法，com Snap和其他平台。

The privacy change is hitting the heart of Meta’s business: its ability to target ads at users with precision and prove to marketers that the ads generate sales. Earlier this month, Meta said it expects a roughly $10 billion hit to sales this year as the result of the Apple change, which requires apps to ask users for permission to track their activity and share it.

隐私权的变化正触及Meta业务的核心：它能够准确地将广告瞄准用户，并向营销人员证明广告产生了销售额。本月早些时候，Meta表示，由于苹果的变化，预计今年的销售额将受到约100亿美元的影响。苹果的变化要求应用程序向用户申请跟踪和分享其活动的许可。

“It kind of feels like the end of an era with Facebook’s targeting ability,” said Ms. Krueger.

克鲁格说：“这感觉就像是Facebook定位能力时代的终结。”。

Google this week unveiled its own proposal to curtail tracking of users across apps on Android devices, potentially exacerbating Meta’s challenges.

谷歌本周公布了自己的提案，旨在减少Android设备上应用程序对用户的跟踪，这可能会加剧Meta面临的挑战。

“Facebook has known for some time that they operated at the whim of the platforms on which consumers access their app,” said Ari Paparo, an advertising-technology executive who sold his company to Comcast “And now those platforms are changing the rules, and there’s little they can do about it.”

将公司出售给康卡斯特的广告技术高管阿里·帕帕罗（Ari Paparo）说：“Facebook一段时间以来就知道，他们是在消费者访问其应用程序的平台上随心所欲地操作的。现在，这些平台正在改变规则，他们对此无能为力。”

Meta has said that macroeconomic forces including inflation and supply-chain disruption also are putting pressure on ad spending. But the privacy push appears to be the biggest threat to the social-media giant’s once ironclad grip on ad spending by small online businesses and e-commerce companies.

梅塔表示，包括通货膨胀和供应链中断在内的宏观经济因素也给广告支出带来了压力。但是，对于这家社交媒体巨头曾经对小型在线企业和电子商务公司的广告支出实施的铁腕控制来说，隐私保护措施似乎是最大的威胁。

Investor fears about the fallout from Apple’s change are one of the reasons that Meta’s market value has dropped by more than $300 billion since the recent earnings report.

投资者对苹果变化的影响的担忧是自最近的盈利报告以来，Meta市值下跌逾3000亿美元的原因之一。

Facebook has been trying to blunt the effects of the Apple changes, and some analysts are optimistic that Meta will contain the damage and that its business could begin to rebound in the second half of 2022.

Facebook一直在试图削弱苹果变化的影响，一些分析师乐观地认为，Meta将控制损失，其业务可能在2022年下半年开始反弹。

Meta said in a written statement that it has more than 10 million advertisers. “Apple’s harmful policy is making it harder and more expensive for businesses of all sizes to reach their customers,” it said. “We believe Facebook and Instagram remain the best platforms for businesses to grow and connect with people, and we’ll always keep working to improve performance and measurement.”

Meta在一份书面声明中表示，它拥有1000多万广告商。“苹果的有害政策使得各种规模的企业更难和更昂贵地接触到他们的客户，”它说。“我们相信Facebook和Instagram仍然是企业发展和与人沟通的最佳平台，我们将始终努力提高绩效和衡量标准。”

Meta has been working to show advertisers that ads on Facebook and Instagram are driving more sales than its analytics show. And the company is developing new technology it hopes will give marketers nearly as much information about ad performance as they had before Apple’s changes.

Meta一直在努力向广告商展示，Facebook和Instagram上的广告推动的销售额超过其分析显示。该公司正在开发新技术，希望能为营销人员提供几乎与苹果变革前一样多的广告表现信息。

Meta also has appealed to users to opt into device tracking. But data from app analytics provider Flurry shows that only 18% of U.S. users are choosing to do so across all apps. Meta has rolled out more e-commerce features within Facebook and Instagram so that more transactions happen within its own ecosystem.

Meta还呼吁用户选择设备跟踪。但来自应用分析提供商Flurry的数据显示，只有18%的美国用户选择在所有应用中这么做。Meta在Facebook和Instagram中推出了更多的电子商务功能，以便在其自身的生态系统中进行更多交易。

Apple’s change could be the most disruptive innovation Meta ever faces, said analyst Daniel Newman of Futurum Research, which focuses on digital technology. “Facebook has built its empire on the ability to acquire intimate details about user behavior across the internet,” he said. “Apple has changed the game.”

专注于数字技术的Futurum Research的分析师丹尼尔·纽曼（Daniel Newman）表示，苹果的变化可能是有史以来最具颠覆性的创新。“Facebook的帝国建立在通过互联网获取用户行为隐私细节的能力之上，”他说。“苹果改变了游戏。”

Meta’s big investment in the metaverse is an attempt to create a future in which it is not beholden to other device-makers, some ad industry executives said.

一些广告业高管表示，Meta对metaverse的巨额投资是为了创造一个不受其他设备制造商约束的未来。

Meta has been critical of Apple’s move, saying it would hurt not only its revenue, but also small-business advertisers and app makers, and that Apple has created a false trade-off between personalized services and privacy.

Meta一直对苹果的举动持批评态度，称此举不仅会损害其收入，还会损害小企业广告商和应用程序制造商，而且苹果在个性化服务和隐私之间做出了错误的权衡。

Inside Meta, some employees saw Apple’s change, and Google’s move in the same direction, as efforts to damage Meta’s business, according to a former employee of Meta’s ad-product division familiar with internal discussions.

据熟悉内部讨论的Meta广告产品部门的一位前员工说，在Meta内部，一些员工认为苹果的变化和谷歌的行动是破坏Meta业务的努力。

On Meta’s Feb. 2 earnings call, Chief Financial Officer David Wehner criticized Apple and Google for contributing to its ad business’s struggles and suggested that Apple’s changes had benefited Google. He noted that Google pays billions of dollars to Apple annually to be the default search engine on iPhones. Meta has struck a more positive tone about Google’s proposal to limit tracking, which isn’t expected to go into effect for at least two years.

在Meta 2月2日的财报电话会议上，首席财务官David Wehner批评苹果和谷歌为其广告业务的挣扎做出贡献，并暗示苹果的改变已经使谷歌受益。他指出，谷歌每年向苹果支付数十亿美元，作为iPhone的默认搜索引擎。Meta对谷歌限制追踪的提议采取了更积极的态度，该提议预计至少两年内不会生效。

Brows by Bossy, an eyebrow stencil maker, used to spend up to $7,000 a day on Facebook ads. As Apple’s changes rolled out, ad prices “started increasing, tracking was clearly not accurate any longer and the performance decreased,” said Amanda Siebert, co-founder of the e-commerce company. She said the company slashed its Facebook spending by about 80% and shifted money elsewhere, including to Amazon search ads.

眉毛模板制造商Bossy的眉毛过去每天在Facebook上的广告花费高达7000美元。随着苹果的变化，广告价格“开始上涨，跟踪显然不再准确，性能下降，”这家电子商务公司的联合创始人阿曼达·西伯特（Amanda Siebert）说。她说，该公司将Facebook支出削减了约80%，并将资金转移到其他地方，包括亚马逊搜索广告。

Ath Sport, a company that makes and sells nutritional supplements for athletes, used to acquire a new customer for every $40 it spent on Facebook and Instagram ads. In summer 2021, that cost doubled, said Ath Sport owner Stuart Kam. Ath Sport cut its spending on Facebook ads and started spending 60% of its total marketing budget on Google search ads, which were more effective, he said. As companies like his spend more on Google, he said, the prices to advertise there are rising.

ATS体育公司，一家为运动员生产和销售营养补充剂的公司，用于在脸谱网和Instagram广告上花费每40美元的一个新客户。在夏季2021，这花费了一倍，ATH体育老板斯图亚特·卡姆说。他说，Ath Sport削减了在Facebook广告上的支出，并开始将其总营销预算的60%用于更有效的谷歌搜索广告。他说，随着像他这样的公司在谷歌上花费更多，在那里做广告的价格也在上涨。

Advertising industry executives said marketers began to think more about moving money away from Facebook back in the summer of 2020, before Apple’s change. In the aftermath of the George Floyd murder, some brands temporarily boycotted Facebook over concerns about hate speech and faulty information on the platform. The ad executives said that was an inflection point for many companies who felt they had become too reliant on Facebook.

广告业高管表示，早在2020年夏天，也就是苹果变革之前，营销人员就开始更多地考虑从Facebook转移资金。乔治·弗洛伊德（George Floyd）谋杀案发生后，一些品牌暂时抵制Facebook，原因是担心该平台上的仇恨言论和错误信息。广告高管们表示，这是许多公司的一个转折点，他们觉得自己太依赖Facebook了。

“It was a learning for everyone to understand that things could change. You have to have a backup plan and you have to diversify,” said Kate Black, vice president of social strategy at Horizon Next, an ad firm that works with e-commerce companies.

与电子商务公司合作的广告公司Horizon Next负责社会战略的副总裁凯特·布莱克（Kate Black）说：“对每个人来说，了解事情可能会发生变化是一种学习。你必须有一个后备计划，而且必须多样化。”。

The Apple change was a tipping point. Ms. Black said the majority of her firm’s clients have begun to shift 10% to 15% of their Facebook spending to other platforms such as TikTok.

苹果的变化是一个转折点。布莱克说，她的公司的大多数客户已经开始将Facebook支出的10%到15%转移到TikTok等其他平台。

Clients are saying “let’s test and learn and see what we can do on other social platforms,” she said. “In terms of reach and scale, Meta is still the biggest player in the space and will definitely continue to be for many years to come, but others are definitely growing.”

她说，客户们说，“让我们测试和学习，看看我们能在其他社交平台上做些什么。”。“就覆盖范围和规模而言，Meta仍然是该领域最大的玩家，而且在未来的许多年里肯定还会继续，但其他人肯定在增长。”

Toilet-spray maker -Pourri, which has relied heavily on Meta during the pandemic to generate e-commerce sales, said during the second half of 2021 that its return on its ad spending on Facebook began to decline as ad prices increased.

厕所喷洒器Purri，在大流行期间严重依赖于Meta来产生电子商务销售，在2021下半年说，随着广告价格的上涨，其在脸谱网上的广告支出开始下降。

While Facebook and Instagram still account for the majority of -Pourri’s ad spending, the Apple changes have caused the company to start “looking into new advertising channels,” said Suzy Batiz, -Pourri’s founder and chief executive. The company said it is seeking to shift about 30% of its ad dollars from Meta to diversify its spending. It already has begun to move some of its Facebook spending to TikTok, Amazon search ads and digital ad offerings from retailers such as Walmart -Pourri spends roughly $10 million a year on digital advertising.

虽然Facebook和Instagram仍占Pourri广告支出的大部分，但苹果的变化已导致该公司开始“寻找新的广告渠道，”Pourri的创始人兼首席执行官苏西·巴蒂斯（Suzy Batiz）说。该公司表示，它正在寻求将30%的广告资金从Meta转移到多样化的支出上。它已经开始将Facebook的部分支出转移到TikTok、亚马逊搜索广告和沃尔玛等零售商的数字广告产品上。Pourri每年在数字广告上的支出约为1000万美元。

Facebook’s disclosure after its recent quarter that its number of users had declined for the first time is worrisome for advertisers, said Diana DiGuido, chief client officer of Tinuiti, a digital-marketing agency that works with consumer-products and e-commerce companies.

Tinuti是一家与消费品和电子商务公司合作的数字营销机构，其首席客户官戴安娜·迪吉多（Diana DiGuido）说，Facebook在最近一个季度披露其用户数量首次下降，这让广告商感到担忧。

Ms. DiGuido said some of her clients want to test TikTok and Snap more robustly to see whether they can reduce their spending on Facebook without hurting their businesses.

迪吉多说，她的一些客户希望测试TikTok并更有力地抓拍，看看他们能否在不损害业务的情况下减少在Facebook上的支出。

The data squeeze from Apple’s privacy measure has affected all digital ad players to some degree, but other companies have been more insulated from the impact than Facebook. Snap has relied on less data from across the web to target ads, and therefore it doesn’t have as much work to do to compensate for the lost data, said Nii Ahene, Tinuiti’s chief strategy officer.

苹果隐私措施带来的数据压缩在一定程度上影响了所有的数字广告播放器，但其他公司比Facebook更不受影响。Tinuti的首席战略官尼伊·阿赫尼（Nii Ahene）说，Snap在目标广告上依赖的网络数据较少，因此它没有那么多工作要做来弥补丢失的数据。

Snap posted a net profit of $22.6 million in the most recent quarter, after sales rose 42% from the year-ago period. Snap said earlier this month that its ad business has begun to recover from the Apple changes faster than it anticipated.

Snap最近一个季度净利润为2260万美元，售后服务同比增长42%。Snap本月早些时候表示，其广告业务已开始以超出预期的速度从苹果的变化中恢复。

Google serves up search ads to a user based on the keywords they enter. That differs from Facebook’s effort to match ads to users based on its data and the targeting choices made by advertisers. Meta’s chief financial officer said on the recent earnings call that he believed Google was somewhat shielded from the Apple change because it does a lot of business based on searches in desktop browsers, which weren’t affected.

谷歌根据用户输入的关键词向用户提供搜索广告。这与Facebook根据数据和广告商的目标选择将广告与用户匹配的做法不同。Meta的首席财务官在最近的财报电话会议上说，他相信谷歌在某种程度上是因为苹果公司的改变而被屏蔽，因为它在桌面浏览器上搜索的业务不受影响。

That difference helped Google attract ad spending from e-commerce businesses, said digital-ad consultant Eric Seufert. Google’s search-ad revenue increased 36% in the fourth quarter from the year-earlier period, to $43.3 billion.

数字广告顾问埃里克·塞弗特（Eric Seufert）表示，这种差异帮助谷歌吸引了电子商务企业的广告支出。谷歌第四季度的搜索广告收入同比增长36%，达到433亿美元。

Rivals have been working to snatch ad dollars away from Facebook, ad buyers said. Snap, Pinterest and TikTok increased incentives they offer to companies that make a significant commitment, such as free credits to use toward future ad buys. TikTok and Snap have tried to help brands repurpose Facebook ads so they work better on their platforms, the ad buyers said.

广告买家表示，竞争对手一直在努力从Facebook抢夺广告收入。Snap、Pinterest和TikTok增加了对做出重大承诺的公司的激励，例如用于未来广告购买的免费信用。广告买家表示，TikTok和Snap已试图帮助品牌重新调整Facebook广告的用途，以便在其平台上更好地发挥作用。

TikTok has benefited from having lower ad pricing than Facebook, though the increased interest in the platform among advertisers might change that, said Mr. Ahene of Tinuiti.

Tinuti的阿赫尼说，TikTok从比Facebook更低的广告定价中获益，不过广告商对该平台的兴趣增加可能会改变这一点。

Just as Apple’s changes made it harder to measure the effectiveness of Facebook ads on iPhones, Google’s proposal could do the same when it comes to Facebook ads delivered to Android users.

正如苹果的改变让衡量iPhone上Facebook广告的有效性变得更加困难一样，谷歌的提议也可以在向Android用户发布Facebook广告时做到同样的效果。

Google said it would give the industry substantial notice before ending its current system. The long timeline will give the ad industry time to prepare.

谷歌表示，将在终止其现有系统之前向业界发出实质性通知。漫长的时间线将给广告业时间做准备。

Meta is navigating an unprecedented challenge to its advertising business, but not an unsolvable one, said Emad Hasan, former head of business marketing operations and analytics at Facebook and CEO of Retina AI, which helps brands target customers on social media. He said Meta can build new ad tools to find new ways to show marketers that their ads are performing well. “Facebook knows that its way of measurement will have to change,” he said.

梅塔正在应对其广告业务面临的前所未有的挑战，但不是无法解决的挑战，Facebook前商业营销运营和分析主管、视网膜人工智能首席执行官埃马德·哈桑说。视网膜人工智能帮助品牌在社交媒体上瞄准客户。他说，Meta可以建立新的广告工具，找到新的方式向营销人员展示他们的广告表现良好。“Facebook知道它的衡量方式必须改变，”他说。

Women’s handbag brand Hammitt worked with Measured Inc., a company that uses statistical modeling to estimate how well Facebook ads are performing. The official rates reported by Facebook are abysmal, said Hammitt founder Tony Drockton, but Measured’s reports showed Hammitt’s Facebook ads are nearly as effective as they were before the privacy changes took effect.

女性手袋品牌哈米特（Hammitt）与Measured Inc.合作，该公司使用统计建模来评估Facebook广告的表现。哈米特创始人托尼·德罗克顿（Tony Drockton）表示，Facebook报道的官方广告费率非常低，但Measured的报告显示，哈米特在Facebook上的广告几乎与隐私权变更生效前一样有效。

As a result, Hammitt reduced its Facebook ad spending only slightly, from 50% of its total ad budget to 43%. Hammitt’s total dollars spent on Facebook actually increased because the company boosted its overall ad budget.

因此，哈米特只略微减少了Facebook的广告支出，从广告总预算的50%降至43%。哈米特在Facebook上的花费总额实际上有所增加，因为该公司提高了整体广告预算。

Facebook has said it has been undercounting how effectively its ads have worked for advertisers, and that it has recently made progress in solving the problem.

Facebook表示，它一直在低估其广告对广告商的有效性，并且最近在解决这一问题方面取得了进展。

Facebook’s longer-term challenge is to shore up its ability to efficiently target ads at users. Rebuilding targeting systems with less data “will be a several year process,” said analyst Michael Nathanson. “Nevertheless, we are encouraged by the strides Facebook is making on measurement, and believe this will feed into their targeting efforts longer-term.”

Facebook的长期挑战是增强其有效地将广告瞄准用户的能力。分析师迈克尔·纳坦森（Michael Nathanson）说，用更少的数据重建目标系统“将是一个数年的过程”。“尽管如此，我们对Facebook在衡量方面取得的进步感到鼓舞，并相信这将有助于他们的长期目标定位工作。”
