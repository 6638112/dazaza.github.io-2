Artwork from Silicon and PCBs
硅和PCB的艺术品

2021-02-18 12:07:52

ChipScapes artworks use computer chips and silicon wafers. Wall art, jewelry, and ornaments are featured. Steve Emery is the artist. Chips such as the Intel 4004, AMD 2901, MOS 6502, IBM PowerPC are the subjects of many of the artworks. ChipScapes use micro-photography with Cyberpunk, Steampunk, Art Deco influences.
