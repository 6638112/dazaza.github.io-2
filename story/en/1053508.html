Its now 30 years since I finished making The Secret Life of Machines TV series with Rex Garrod. Since then Ive spent most of my time happily in my workshop, mainly making arcade machines for my two arcades, one in central London and the other on Southwold pier. Ive been very fortunate to have had such freedom to make exactly what I like for so long.

它现在30年后，我完成了用Rex Garrod的机器电视剧秘密生活。从那以后，我的工作室大部分时间都在愉快地度过了大部分时间，主要是为我的两个拱廊制作街机机器，一个在伦敦市中心，另一个在Southwold码头。我一直非常幸运能够做出这么长时间的自由。

For some Id been wanting to find a way of passing on something of what Ive learnt about making things. Covid 19 brought the idea into focus and it has been the perfect time to do it without distractions. The videos bring together two aspects of my career explaining things and making things.

对于一些人来说，想要找到一种方法来传递关于我了解的东西的东西。 Covid 19将这个想法带入了重点，它一直是在没有分心的情况下做到这一点的完美时间。视频带来了我职业生涯的两个方面，解释了事物和制造事物。

Its impossible to teach experience. I obviously have the experience to make complicated machines but I dont know exactly what it is that I know. So much of it is non verbal or tacit. I work with my hands and at times they just seem to take over.

它不可能教授经验。我显然有经验制作复杂的机器，但我不确定我知道的是什么。这么大的是非口头或默契。我用手和他们一起工作，有时他们似乎只是接管。

In the past, when people asked me how to learn practical skills, Ive told them they just have to make things badly to start with but to keep going and they will improve. I made things badly for the first half of my life. However, I now learn a lot from watching practical youtube videos and realise that they can teach the sort of informal tips that used to be part of traditional apprenticeships. So Im delighted to be contributing to this wonderful new learning resource! I hope my videos, each about 45 minutes long, are entertaining enough to be fun for beginners, but also detailed enough to be useful for pros.

在过去，当人们问我如何学习实践技能时，我告诉他们他们只是必须让事情变得糟糕地开始，但要继续前往，他们会改善。我生命中上半年的事情非常糟糕。但是，我现在学到了观看实用的YouTube视频并意识到他们可以教导曾经是传统学徒的一部分的非正式提示。所以我很高兴为这个美妙的新学习资源做出贡献！我希望我的视频，每长时间约45分钟，足以为初学者娱乐，但也有足够详细的优点对专业人士有用。

The subjects of the eight episodes are: Chain, Switches, LEDs, Springs, Connectors, Hinges and Glue.

八集的主题是：链，开关，LED，弹簧，连接器，铰链和胶水。

Working out how to film them was interesting. My wife has a Canon Legria Hg30 which is a great camera. It orginally cost about 1200 but the model is now 8 years old and on Ebay are now about 400 so I bought a couple. At first I thought my assistant, Bill Parks, could be the cameraman. But he only comes on Wednesdays and I gradually found the flexibility of filming whenever I wanted was essential. So I experimented with various camera setups to do the filming myself, making a rig to suspend the 'closeup' camera from the roof of my workshop. Then I have a monitor so I can see  the closeup camera image to check things are in frame. At first I mounted it behind my shoulder but then my head kept getting in shot (you can see this in the Bearings episode). I changed to having the closeup camera near to the other camera. This makes the picture in the monitor very confusing because my hands moved in the opposite direction to their actual movement. The breakthrough was turning the monitor upsidedown - surprisingly this makes the movements completely intuitive.

解决如何拍摄他们很有意思。我的妻子有一个佳能羊肉hg30，这是一个很棒的相机。它的成本艰难约1200，但模特现在已经8岁，eBay现在大约400所以我买了一对夫妇。起初我以为我的助手，比尔帕克斯，可能是摄影师。但他只在星期三出现，我逐渐发现，每当我想要的时候，我逐渐发现了拍摄的灵活性。所以我尝试了各种相机设置来做自己的拍摄，使钻机暂停＆＃39;特写效果＆＃39;从我的车间屋顶的相机。然后我有一个显示器，所以我可以看到特写相机映像来检查框架。起初我将它安装在我的肩膀后面，但然后我的头部一直进入射击（你可以在轴承中看到这个）。我改变为在另一个相机附近的特写相机。这使得监视器中的图片非常令人困惑，因为我的双手以相反的方向移动到他们的实际运动。突破在upsidedown转向监视器 - 令人惊讶的是，这使得运动完全直观。

Lighting was relatively simple. I really like the natural light in my workshop with the big skylights. To make 'natural 'light simple to work with, I added translucent blinds to the skylights to cut out direct sunlight. On dark days I found that a couple of 600mm square LED office lights (intended for suspended ceilings) mounted on tripods shining up at the white painted roof cast an even light which didn't look too unnatural.

照明相对简单。我真的很喜欢我的车间的自然光线。制作＆＃39;自然＆＃39;轻盈简单，我向天窗添加了半透明的百叶窗，以削减阳光直射。在黑暗的日子上，我发现，一间600毫米的方形LED办公用灯（用于悬挂天花板）安装在三脚架上，在白色绘制的屋顶闪闪发光，甚至闪光，甚至没有看起来太不自然。

The animations at the start of each episode were amazingly quick. The slowest part was arranging all my components to make the title. A still camera mounted above the table then started taking photos every few seconds.( I connected the remote control to a microcontroller) while I went round the table gradually breaking up the word and creating chaos. The actual filming never took more than 15 minutes. Obviously I then reversed the film in the edit. The films were edited in Premiere 6, the last version that could be bought outright before Adobe switched to monthly rentals.

每一集开始时的动画非常快速。最慢的部分是安排所有组件来制作标题。静止的摄像头安装在桌子上方，然后每隔几秒钟开始拍照。（我将遥控器连接到微控制器），同时逐渐逐渐打破这个词并创建混乱。实际拍摄从未超过15分钟。显然我然后在编辑中扭转了电影。这些电影在首映6中编辑，在Adobe转换为每月租金之前，可以彻底购买的最后一个版本。
