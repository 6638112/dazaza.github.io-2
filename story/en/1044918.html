Toyota wants to win Le Mans with its new GR010 hybrid prototype
丰田希望通过其新的GR010混合动力原型车赢得勒芒

2021-01-19 01:02:24

The future of the 24 Hours of Le Mans is resolving into shape. On Friday, Toyota Gazoo Racing unveiled its new GR010 Hybrid, an all-wheel drive prototype that will compete in the overall win at the annual French race later this year (pandemic willing). It's the first competitor to break cover from the new Le Mans Hypercar (LMH) class, which replaces the old LMP1h category that shone so brightly for a few short years in the middle of the last decade.

勒芒24小时耐力赛的未来正在逐步成形。周五，丰田汽车Gazoo Racing推出了其新的GR010 Hybrid，这是一款全轮驱动的原型车，将在今年晚些时候的年度法国大赛中大获全胜（大流行性）。它是第一个从新的勒芒超级跑车（LMH）类别中脱颖而出的竞争对手，该类别取代了在过去十年中短短几年内表现出色的旧LMP1h类别。

LMP1h gave rise to  some of the fastest and most technologically advanced racing cars the world has ever seen. But it wasn't cheap, and by 2018 Toyota was all that was left, its cars hobbled by ACO (the race organizers) to give the non-hybrid LMP1 privateer teams a fighting chance.

LMP1h催生了世界上一些最快，技术最先进的赛车。但这并不便宜，到2018年，丰田只剩下了剩下的一切，它的赛车被ACO（比赛组织者）bble陷，为非混合LMP1私人车队提供了战斗的机会。

Car manufacturer interest in racing series waxes and wanes, and it doesn't require much imagination to see how a combination of global economic uncertainty and an expensive ruleset could negatively impact participation. The ACO's answer was a new category called the hypercar, which would open the doors to racing versions of those seven-figure slices of unobtainium-on-wheels like the Adrian Newey-designed Aston Martin Valkyrie—albeit running at much lower power levels than the unrestricted road cars.

汽车制造商对赛车系列的兴趣起伏不定，并且不需要太多的想象力就能了解全球经济不确定性和昂贵的规则集如何对参与度产生负面影响。 ACO的答案是一个名为“超级跑车”的新类别，它将为诸如阿德里安·纽维（Adrian Newey）设计的阿斯顿·马丁·瓦尔基里（Aston Martin Valkyrie）之类的七位数的不带车轮的赛车版本敞开大门，尽管它的行驶速度要低得多功率水平比无限制的公路车要高。

The new LMH rules don't offer quite the same technical freedom as dearly departed LMP1h. But they do have a bit more leeway than another ruleset called LMDh (for Le Mans Daytona hybrid—more on this later), in that competitors are allowed to use the front wheels to capture and deploy up to 200kW (268hp) of kinetic energy from the hybrid motor-generator unit (MGU), with an internal combustion engine (ICE) powering the rear wheels. Overall power output is capped at 500kW (670hp) to keep lap times at Le Mans within a sane time range, and the MGU can only be activated above 74.5mph (120km/h) in the dry. (The activation threshold is higher if it's raining and the car is on wet-weather tires.)

新的LMH规则不提供与已淘汰的LMP1h完全相同的技术自由。但是它们确实比另一个称为LMDh的规则集（对于勒芒·代托纳混合动力车-稍后有更多介绍）有更多的余地，因为竞争对手被允许使用前轮来捕获和部署高达200kW（268hp）的动能。混合动力电动发电机组（MGU），由内燃发动机（ICE）为后轮提供动力。总功率输出上限为500kW（670hp），以使勒芒的单圈时间保持在合理的时间范围内，而MGU在干燥状态下只能在74.5mph（120km / h）以上被激活。 （如果下雨并且汽车在潮湿天气的轮胎上，则激活阈值会更高。）

As it turns out, interest among the road-going hypercar fraternity has been underwhelming; Aston Martin has shelved any plans to race the delayed Valkyrie, McLaren has said no thanks to LMH, and we doubt we'll see anyone try to enter a Gordon Murray Automotive T.50 or one of those Mercedes-AMG Project ONEs if and when that last car is ever ready for the public. Boutique brand Glickenhaus is readying an LMH entrant, as is the ByKolles team, which is respected more for its dogged perseverance than for a long list of racing success.

事实证明，公路超级跑车界的兴趣一直在浓厚。阿斯顿·马丁已经搁置了任何与延迟的女武神赛跑的计划，迈凯轮也对LMH表示感谢，我们怀疑我们是否会看到有人尝试进入Gordon Murray Automotive T.50或其中一辆梅赛德斯-AMG项目ONE。以及最后一辆汽车何时可供公众使用。精品品牌Glickenhaus和BYKOLLES车队正在为LMH参赛者做准备，这比BYKOLLES车队的顽强毅力更受尊敬，而不是一连串的赛车成功。

But two big OEMs are on board for LMH, even if it they're using the ruleset to build vehicles that are racing cars first and foremost. One of those is Peugeot—now part of Stellantis—which will enter the 2022 World Endurance Championship with an as-yet-unseen prototype powered by a recently revealed Hybrid4 powertrain that marries a 500kW (670hp) 2.6L twin-turbo V6 driving the rear wheels and a 200kW (268hp) MGU at the front axle. (Again, overall power output is capped at 500kW, so the car's brains won't ever let it use all 500kW from the ICE at the same time as all 200kW from the MGU.)

但是，LMH有两家大型OEM参与其中，即使他们正在使用规则集来制造首先是赛车的车辆。标致汽车之一就是标致汽车（现为Stellantis的一部分），它将带着尚未出现的原型车进入2022年世界耐力锦标赛，该原型机由最近展示的Hybrid4动力总成提供动力，该动力总成与500kW（670hp）2.6L双涡轮增压V6一起驱动后部车轮和前轴的200kW（268hp）MGU。 （同样，总功率输出上限为500kW，因此汽车的大脑永远不会让它同时使用ICE中的所有500kW和MGU中的所有200kW。）

Toyota Gazoo racing is a year ahead of Peugeot, and its GR010 is more than just a hybrid powertrain—it's a complete car. If you were hoping for something that looks like a high-end road-legal sports car, I hate to be the bearer of bad tidings, but the GR010 looks like an endurance prototype first and foremost, with a narrow bubble cockpit that does better things for frontal area than it does passenger room.

丰田Gazoo赛车比标致领先一年，它的GR010不仅仅是混合动力总成，而是一台完整的汽车。如果您希望获得看起来像高端公路法律跑车的产品，那么我不愿成为糟糕消息的承担者，但GR010首先看起来像是耐力原型，其狭窄的气泡座舱可以做得更好正面区域比客舱区域大。

The Toyota GR010 uses a 200kW MGU at the front axle, with a 500kW 3.5L twin-turbo V6 internal combustion engine driving the rear wheels. While the GR010 bears at least a passing resemblance to the outgoing TS050, it's 32 percent less powerful and 357lbs (162kg) heavier, which should equate to a 10-second slower lap time at Le Mans.

丰田GR010在前轴使用200kW MGU，并用500kW 3.5L双涡轮V6内燃机驱动后轮。 GR010与传出的TS050至少具有相似之处，但功率却降低了32％，重357磅（162千克），这相当于勒芒的单圈时间慢了10秒。

Endurance racing wouldn't really be endurance racing if there wasn't an overly complicated set of technical rules for fans to grapple with, and the coming seasons will be no exception, when LMH is joined in 2022 by LMDh.

如果没有一套过于复杂的技术规则供球迷使用，那么耐力赛车将不是真正的耐力赛车，而随着LMDh于2022年加入LMH，接下来的赛季也不例外。

As stated, the D in LMDh stands for Daytona, and it's a new rule set for North America's IMSA series (which includes the Rolex 24 at Daytona and the 10-hour Petit Le Mans races, among others), which will also allow entrants to compete at Le Mans each year. Manufacturers have wanted to race prototypes for overall wins in IMSA, but none has had the appetite to spend hundreds of millions of dollars to do so. So IMSA is evolving its successful DPi class to add a spec hybrid system and more freedom to style the cars according to an OEM's road car lineup.

如前所述，LMDh中的D代表代托纳，这是北美IMSA系列赛的新规则（包括代托纳的劳力士24和10小时的小勒芒比赛等） ，这也使参赛者每年都可以在勒芒参加比赛。制造商曾想为IMSA的整体胜利而争夺原型，但没有人愿意花数亿美元这样做。因此，IMSA正在发展其成功的DPi类，以增加规格混合动力系统，并根据OEM的公路用车产品线为汽车提供更多的设计风格。

LMDh and LMH are both capped at 500kW (670hp), but LMDh only allows for a spec 50kW (67hp) MGU, which has to recover and deploy energy to the rear wheels only. (Albeit at any speed, unlike LMH.) Additionally, OEMs are required to use one of four base chassis, as is currently the case in DPi and LMP2. This should make the category even cheaper to enter than LMH, although it's a category where arguments about racecar-to-road technology transfer will be more than a little tenuous.

LMDh和LMH的上限均为500kW（670hp），但LMDh仅允许规格为50kW（67hp）的MGU，该MGU只能将能量回收并分配给后轮。 （尽管与LMH不同，但速度是任意的。）此外，OEM必须使用四个基本机架之一，就像目前在DPi和LMP2中一样。这应该使该类别的输入比LMH更便宜，尽管该类别中有关赛车到公路技术转让的争论将有些微不足道。

I'm expecting to see quite a few OEMs sign on to LMDh, but so far the only two definites are Audi, which enters in 2022, and Porsche, which arrives in 2023. Which means the round-the-clock race in France that year should be quite the event. I'm willing to bet we'll even have gotten a grip on COVID-19 by then, which means some of us might be able to watch it in person.

我希望看到很多OEM都签约LMDh，但到目前为止，只有两个定义是奥迪和保时捷，这两个定冠名是2022年进入的奥迪和2023年进入。法国那年应该是相当大的事件。我敢打赌，那时我们甚至已经掌握了COVID-19，这意味着我们当中有些人可能可以亲自观看。
