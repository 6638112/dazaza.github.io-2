Ars online IT roundtable tomorrow: What’s the future of the data center?
明天是在线IT圆桌会议：数据中心的未来是什么？

2021-01-20 02:47:41

If you're in IT, you probably remember the first time you walked into a real data center—not just a server closet, but an actual raised-floor data center, where the door wooshes open in a blast of cold air and noise and you're confronted with rows and rows of racks, monolithic and gray, stuffed full of servers with cooling fans screaming and blinkenlights blinking like mad. The data center is where the cool stuff is—the pizza boxes, the blade servers, the NASes and the SANs. Some of its residents are more exotic—the Big Iron in all its massive forms, from Z-series to Superdome and all points in between.

如果您从事IT业，您可能还记得第一次走进真正的数据中心-不仅是服务器机房，还是真正的高架数据中心，那里的门在冷空气和热气中呼啸而过。嘈杂的声响，您将面对成排的机架，整体式和灰色机架，到处都是服务器，冷却风扇尖叫着，眨眼间就像疯了似的闪烁着。数据中心是最酷的地方-比萨饼盒，刀片服务器，NASes和SAN。它的一些居民更具异国情调-从Z系列到Superdome及其之间的所有点，各种形式的巨铁。

For decades, data centers have been the beating hearts of many businesses—the fortified secret rooms where huge amounts of capital sit, busily transforming electricity into revenue. And they're sometimes a place for IT to hide, too—it's kind of a standing joke that whenever a user you don't want to see is stalking around the IT floor, your best bet to avoid contact is just to badge into the data center and wait for them to go away. (But, uh, I never did that ever. I promise.) But the last few years have seen a massive shift in the relationship between companies and their data—and the places where that data lives. Sure, it's always convenient to own your own servers and storage, but why tie up all that capital when you don't have to? Why not just go to the cloud buffet and pay for what you want to eat and nothing more?

数十年来，数据中心一直是许多企业的跳动之心-拥有大量资本的坚固机密房间，忙于将电力转化为收入。而且它们有时也是IT隐藏的地方-这是一个常开的笑话，每当您不希望看到的用户在IT层中徘徊时，最好的选择就是避免联系只是为了进入数据中心并等待他们离开。 （但是，嗯，我从没做过。我保证。）但是最近几年，公司与数据之间的关系以及数据所处的位置发生了巨大的变化。当然，拥有自己的服务器和存储总是很方便，但是为什么在不必时占用所有资金呢？为什么不去云自助餐并支付您想吃的东西而已呢？

There will always be some reason for some companies to have data centers—the cloud, for all its attractiveness, can't quite do everything. (Not yet, at least.) But the list of objections to going off-premises for your computing needs is rapidly shrinking—and we're going to talk a bit about what comes next.

对于某些公司而言，总有一些理由拥有数据中心-云尽管具有吸引力，但不能完全做任何事情。 （至少还没有。）但是，针对您的计算需求而异地部署的异议列表正在迅速缩小-我们将谈论下一个问题。
