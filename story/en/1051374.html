Apple will randomize Mac serial numbers starting this year
Apple将在今年开始随机化Mac序列号

2021-03-10 23:51:54

According to MacRumors, Apple will completely change how the serial numbers for Macs are generated. The numbers will soon be fully randomized, meaning that users, IT managers, repair-shop technicians, and others will not be able to glean the same information from them that the current serial number system provides.

根据Macrumors，Apple将完全改变Macs的序列号的方式。数字将很快完全随机化，这意味着用户，IT经理，维修店技术人员和其他人将无法从它们提供当前序列号系统提供的相同信息。

Serial numbers on Macs shipping today reflect the time and place where the Mac was assembled, as well as some configuration details like storage capacity. Moving forward, serial numbers will be fully random, with no code or consistency that can be used to learn information about the product. The new numbers will be between eight and 14 characters long. MacRumors claims it learned of the change when it gained access to an internal AppleCare email. This change does not apply to Mac models that are shipping today, but it will go into effect for future Macs the company will introduce. That includes the rumored MacBook Air, MacBook Pro, and iMac refreshes expected later this year, as well as new Mac Pro models that appear to be planned for a later date.

MAC上运输的序列号反映了MAC组装的时间和地点，以及一些配置细节等存储容量。向前移动，序列号将是完全随机的，没有代码或一致性，可用于学习有关产品的信息。新数字长度为8到14个字符。 Macrumors声称它在获得内部AppleCare电子邮件时获取的变化。此更改不适用于当今运输的MAC模型，但它将对未来的MAC介绍，这将生效。其中包括谣传MacBook Air，MacBook Pro和IMAC刷新，预计今年晚些时候，以及似乎计划稍后计划的新型Mac Pro模型。

According to the email, the change was initially planned to go into effect in 2020, but it was pushed back. The email now says it will happen in "early 2021." Curiously, the most reliable rumors and leaks about upcoming Mac products have placed them in the second half of 2021, with only iPad refreshes initially expected in the coming weeks. But the date may refer to something besides the products' final ship date; we'll have to wait and see.

Serial numbers are used in online forums, repair shops, and company IT departments to expedite troubleshooting and other tasks by quickly learning more about the machine in question—for example, whether a computer that is exhibiting a problem is part of a certain set of models manufactured in a certain time period that all have that issue. Some of the information will still be accessible after either booting the machine up or taking it apart.

序列号用于在线论坛，维修店和公司IT部门，通过快速学习有关问题的机器的更多信息来加快故障排除和其他任务 - 例如，展览问题的计算机是一组模型的一部分在一定的时间内制造，所有这些都有这个问题。在启动机器时或将其移开后，一些信息仍将可访问。
