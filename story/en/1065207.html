CD Project Red does an about-face, says ransomware crooks are leaking data
CD Project Red是一个关于面部的，说兰非沃尔骗子骗局正在泄露数据

2021-06-12 04:54:22

CD Projekt Red, the maker of The Witcher series, Cyberpunk 2077, and other popular games, said on Friday that proprietary data taken in a ransomware attack disclosed four months ago is likely circulating online.

CD Projekt Red，Witcher系列的制造商，Cyber​​punk 2077和其他流行的游戏，周五表示，在四个月前透露的赎金软件攻击中拍摄的专有数据可能在线流通。

“Today, we have learned new information regarding the breach and now have reason to believe that internal data illegally obtained during the attack is currently being circulated on the Internet,” company officials said in a statement. “We are not yet able to confirm the exact contents of the data in question, though we believe it may include current/former employee and contractor details in addition to data related to our games.”

“今天，我们学会了关于违约的新信息，现在有理由相信在互联网上攻击期间非法获得的内部数据，”公司官员在一份声明中表示。 “我们尚未能够确认有关数据的确切内容，尽管我们认为它可能包括当前/前员工和承包商细节，除了与我们的游戏有关的数据。”

The update represents an about-face of sorts, as it warns that the information of current and former employees and contractors is now believed to be among the compromised data. When The Poland-based game maker disclosed the attack in February, it said it didn’t believe the stolen data included personal information for employees or customers. A week later, the company maintained that the probability of employee personal data being disclosed was “low.” It went on to say that “after our investigation, we have not found any evidence that any personal data was actually transferred outside the company network” and that “due to the attackers’ course of action, we may never be able to say for certain if they actually copied any personal data.”

该更新代表了一个关于面对的各种面对面，因为它警告说，当前和前雇员和承包商的信息现在被认为是受损数据之一。基于波兰的游戏制造商在2月披露了攻击时，它表示不相信被盗的数据包括员工或客户的个人信息。一周后，公司认为，透露员工个人数据的概率是“低位”。它继续说“我们调查后，我们还没有找到任何证据表明任何个人数据实际上都在公司网络之外转移”，由于攻击者的行动方案，我们可能永远无法对某些人说如果他们实际复制了任何个人数据。“

It’s not clear why it took CD Projekt Red four months to determine that employee data has likely been affected. Presumably, a forensic investigation could have made that determination before now. Attempts to reach CD Projekt Red representatives for comment didn’t immediately succeed.

目前尚不清楚为什么它花了CD Projekt Red四个月来确定员工数据可能受到影响。据推测，可以在现在之前进行法医调查。达到CD Projekt Red代表的尝试没有立即成功。

Shortly after CD Projekt Red’s initial disclosure, researchers said they uncovered data showing that source code for games including Cyberpunk 2077, Gwent, and The Witcher 3 had been put up for auction with a starting bid of $1 million. A separate team of researchers reported that the auction had been closed after a buyer outside of the auction forum had offered a price that was acceptable to the sellers. The price was never disclosed. There’s no proof a sale actually went through, though, and some researchers have speculated that when no buyer emerged, the sellers lied to save face.

CD Projekt Red的最初披露后不久，研究人员表示，他们发现了显示包括Cyber​​punk 2077，Gwent和Witcher 3在内的游戏的源代码的数据被拍卖，以100万美元的开始出价。一个单独的研究人员团队报告说，拍卖会在拍卖论坛以外的买方提供了一个可接受的卖家的价格。价格从未披露。尽管如此，没有证据销售实际经历过，一些研究人员推测，当没有买家出现时，卖家撒谎拯救面部。

Researchers say that the CD Projekt Red breach was carried out by HelloKitty, a little-known ransomware group that some researchers refer to as DeathRansom.

研究人员称，CD Projekt RedBreac区是由Hellokitty进行的，一些研究人员称为死亡人员的鲜为人知的赎金软件集团。

From the beginning, the game maker has steadfastly refused to pay or even negotiate with the ransomware operators. That stance is admirable, although it’s much easier to take when victims can quickly rebuild their networks using backups, as Projekt Red was. Even then, there are prices to pay, as the game maker is finding out first-hand.

从一开始，游戏制造商坚定地拒绝支付或甚至与赎金软件运营商谈判。当受害者可以快速重建他们的网络时，姿势是令人钦佩的，尽管当Projekt红色是时，受害者可以快速重建网络。即便如此，随着游戏制造商找到一手，有价格支付。
