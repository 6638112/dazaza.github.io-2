Players will be able to continue to purchase games on PS3 and PS Vita
玩家将能够在PS3和PS Vita上继续购买游戏

2021-04-20 03:04:58

Recently, we notified players that PlayStation Store for PS3 and PS Vita devices was planned to end this summer.

最近，我们通知了PlayStation商店PS3和PS Vita设备的玩家，计划今年夏天结束。

Upon further reflection, however, it’s clear that we made the wrong decision here. So today I’m happy to say that we will be keeping the PlayStation Store operational for PS3 and PS Vita devices. PSP commerce functionality will retire on July 2, 2021 as planned.

然而，在进一步反思后，很明显，我们在这里做出了错误的决定。所以今天我很高兴地说，我们将保留PlayStation商店为PS3和PS Vita设备运行。 PSP Commerce功能将于7月2日，2021年7月2日退休。

When we initially came to the decision to end purchasing support for PS3 and PS Vita, it was born out of a number of factors, including commerce support challenges for older devices and the ability for us to focus more of our resources on newer devices where a majority of our gamers are playing on. We see now that many of you are incredibly passionate about being able to continue purchasing classic games on PS3 and PS Vita for the foreseeable future, so I’m glad we were able to find a solution to continue operations.

当我们最初来决定结束购买PS3和PS Vita的购买支持时，它诞生了一些因素，包括商业支持旧设备的挑战以及我们将更多资源集中在较新设备上的能力我们的大部分游戏玩家正在玩。我们现在看到了很多人对能够在可预见的未来继续购买PS3和PS Vita上的经典游戏，所以我很高兴能找到一个继续运营的解决方案。

I’m glad that we can keep this piece of our history alive for gamers to enjoy, while we continue to create cutting-edge new game worlds for PS4, PS5, and the next generation of VR.

我很高兴我们可以让这篇历史活着，因为游戏玩家享受，而我们继续为PS4，PS5和下一代VR创造尖端的新游戏世界。

Thank you for sharing your feedback with us – we’re always listening and appreciate the support from our PlayStation community.

感谢您与我们分享您的反馈 - 我们总是倾听并欣赏我们PlayStation社区的支持。
