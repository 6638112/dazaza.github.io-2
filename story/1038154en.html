Is Microsoft's 'Find Your Joy' Holiday Ad Sad?
微软的“寻找快乐”假期广告难过吗？

2020-12-07 07:12:43

The 90-second spot, titled “Find Your Joy” and created by McCann New York, shows Rufus bored at home while his human family members busy themselves with Halo, Minecraft, Microsoft Teams and Flight Simulator.

纽约麦肯（McCann New York）创作的90秒广告名叫“找到你的喜悦”，显示鲁弗斯在家里感到无聊，而他的家人则在Halo，Minecraft，Microsoft Teams和Flight Simulator忙碌。

He misses his fellow dog buddy across the road, but can’t see him due to lockdown restrictions.

他想念马路对面的同伴，但由于封锁限制而看不到他。

But when Rufus falls asleep, the duo are reunited in an epic dream that sees them go on an exciting adventure into the world of Microsoft, playing fetch in Halo, flying planes, and hanging out in their own Minecraft world.

但是，当Rufus入睡时，二人在一个史诗般的梦中团聚，看到他们踏上激动人心的冒险之旅，进入Microsoft世界，玩Halo，玩飞机，并在自己的Minecraft世界中闲逛。

‘This year more the ever, we felt that it was important to give people a little lift, to remind them that while we are facing a lot of challenges, there are many ways we can connect, be productive and enjoy the time we have at home,” said Kathleen Hall, corporate vp of brand, advertising and research at Microsoft.

“今年比以往任何时候都更重要，我们觉得重要的是要给人们一些帮助，提醒他们在我们面临许多挑战的同时，我们可以通过多种方式进行联系，提高工作效率并享受自己拥有的时间微软的品牌，广告和研究公司副总裁凯瑟琳·霍尔（Kathleen Hall）说。

As part of the campaign, which will run on TV and social from Dec. 4, Microsoft is bringing elements of the campaign to life for consumers.

作为该活动的一部分，该活动将从12月4日开始在电视和社交网站上投放，微软为消费者提供了该活动的元素。

In Minecraft, Rufus’ dream “Dogtopia” complete with bacon rollercoasters and bone-shaped pools will be available for free in the Minecraft Marketplace here.

在Minecraft中，Rufus的梦想“ Dogtopia”（配有培根过山车和骨头形水池）将在此处的Minecraft市场免费提供。

There will also be custom backgrounds from the spot available on Microsoft Teams, while the vintage planes that Rufus and his friend fly in will be available on Microsoft Flight Simulator in late December.

Microsoft Teams上还将提供当场的自定义背景，而Rufus和他的朋友乘坐的老式飞机将于12月下旬在Microsoft Flight Simulator上提供。

The campaign follows Microsoft’s “ Holiday Magic: Lucy and the Reindeer” spot from last year where a child named Lucy, through the “magic” of Microsoft Teams and Microsoft Translator, finds she is able to communicate with reindeer.

该活动是微软去年的“假日魔术：露西和驯鹿”活动之后的活动，一个名叫露西的孩子通过Microsoft Teams和Microsoft Translator的“魔术”发现她能够与驯鹿进行交流。
