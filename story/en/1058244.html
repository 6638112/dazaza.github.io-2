Boba shortage: the U.S. may soon have no bubbles for tea
鲍巴短缺：美国可能很快就会喝茶

2021-04-14 08:51:09

Shipping containers from Asia are piling up at West Coast ports, delaying the arrival of much of the country’s supply of chewy tapioca balls. Boba wholesalers say their orders of boba just aren’t arriving, and they have no idea when they’ll get them. That means boba tea shops all over the Bay Area — and the rest of the country — are running out of their namesake boba.

来自亚洲的运输集装箱正在西海岸港口堆积起来，延迟了该国大部分地区的耐嚼木薯球的到来。鲍巴批发商表示他们的鲍巴的命令刚刚到来，他们不知道他们会得到它们的想法。这意味着鲍巴茶店遍布海湾地区 - 以及该国的其余部分 - 正在脱离他们的名称鲍巴。

“In the next week or so, tapioca will be a luxury because no one is going to have it,” said Tommy Huang, senior sales manager at Hayward’s Leadway International Inc., one of the biggest boba suppliers in Northern California.

“在下周左右，Tapioca将是一种奢侈，因为没有人会有它，”海沃德Leadway International Inc.的高级销售经理Tommy Huang表示，北加州最大的鲍巴供应商之一。

While most U.S. boba shops, which primarily sell tea and other beverages with a layer of black tapioca balls, use boba from Taiwan, even those that buy local boba from Hayward’s U.S. Boba Co. are likely to see their supply wane. That’s because the shipping containers from Thailand, the main producer of the world’s tapioca starch, are also stuck in the water. It’s impossible to make boba without tapioca.

虽然大多数美国鲍勃商店，主要销售茶叶和其他饮料与一层黑色的木薯丸，甚至那些从海沃德美国鲍巴公司购买当地鲍巴的鲍巴可能会看到他们的供应。这是因为来自泰国的主要生产商，世界塔皮萨淀粉的主要生产商，也陷入了水中。没有木薯的鲍巴是不可能的。

This is the first time Leadway International anticipates running out of boba, according to Huang. Earlier in the pandemic, the company maintained a large supply of tapioca balls because so many Bay Area boba shops were temporarily closed. Leadway’s sales plunged, but it was able to keep boba in stock even as international shipping grew more unpredictable.

据王某称，这是第一次获得铅钢铁队的国际预测。在大流行早期，该公司保持了大量的木薯丸供应，因为许多湾区鲍巴商店暂时关闭。 Leadway的销售令人困惑，但它能够保持鲍巴的股票，即使国际航运增长更加不可预测。

Even if Leadway gets its shipping container soon, the company may not be able to fully supply boba to all of its clients nationwide. That means local boba shops could be cleaned out of boba, restock briefly and then be out again. An employee at another major boba wholesaler said their supply is almost gone, too.

即使Leadway很快获得了运输集装箱，公司也可能无法全国各地的客户充分供应BOBA。这意味着当地的鲍巴商店可以用鲍巴清理鲍巴，简单地恢复，然后再出现。另一个主要的鲍巴批发商的员工表示，他们的供应差不多了。

“It’s going to take a long time to be able to say we will not have a shortage of tapioca,” Huang said, adding he expects the supply to catch up in no more than two months.

“能够说我们不会说我们不会有很短的木薯，”黄说，加入他预计供应不超过两个月。

Andrew Chau and Bin Chen of Boba Guys said they’ll likely no longer have boba at their cafes within the next week, according to a video they posted on Instagram. Chen urged customers not to get mad at their favorite boba shop for not having boba and instead try other, not-tapioca toppings like grass jelly.

根据他们在Instagram上发布的视频，Andrew Chau和Bin Chen of Boba Guys表示，他们可能在下周不再在下周在他们的咖啡馆里有鲍巴。陈敦敦促客户不要生气，因为没有鲍巴，而不是鲍巴，而是尝试其他，而不是木薯果冻。

Other boba shops may not know a shortage is looming, or they might work with a smaller supplier that still has boba in stock. Alex Ou, owner of Tea & Others in San Francisco, just secured another week’s worth of boba, which will be important for her estimated 70% of customers who prefer their boba drinks with some chew.

其他鲍巴商店可能不知道短缺是迫在眉睫的，或者他们可能与较小的供应商合作，仍有鲍巴库存。 Alex Ou，茶叶的所有者。旧金山的其他人刚刚获得了另一周的鲍巴，这对她估计70％的顾客对一些咀嚼的鲍巴饮酒很重要。

“Some people will not buy a drink if we’re out of boba,” she said. “They’re literally here for the boba.”

“如果我们离开鲍巴，有些人不会买饮料，”她说。 “他们在这里为鲍巴而言。”
