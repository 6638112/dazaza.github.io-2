Two days with Volkswagen’s electric ID.4
大众电子ID.4行驶两天

2021-02-28 12:41:28

The Volkswagen ID.4 is a big deal for its manufacturer. After getting busted six years ago for fibbing about diesel emissions, VW underwent a corporate transformation, throwing all its chips into electrification. As a big believer in modular architectures that it can use to build a wide range of vehicles from a common set of parts, it got to work on a new architecture just for battery electric vehicles, called MEB ( Modularer E-Antriebs-Baukasten or Modular Electrification Toolkit).

大众ID.4对制造商来说意义重大。六年前因涉嫌柴油排放而被淘汰后，大众进行了一次公司转型，将其所有芯片投入电气化。作为模块化架构的忠实拥护者，它可以使用一组通用零件来制造各种车辆，因此必须致力于一种仅用于电池电动车的新架构，即MEB（Modularer E-Antriebs-Baukasten或Modular）。电气化工具包）。

Since then, we've seen a dizzying array of MEB-based concepts, including that electric bus that everyone wants, and even a bright green buggy. But the ID.4 is no mere concept. It's the first production MEB vehicle to go on sale here in the US, designed with the crossover-crazy US market firmly in mind. Last September we got our first good look at the ID.4 in under studio lights in Brooklyn, and a month later, Ars got to spend 45 minutes on the road with a pre-production ID.4. But now we've had two full days in a model year 2021 ID.4 1st Edition, getting to know it on local turf.

从那时起，我们已经看到了令人眼花of乱的基于MEB的概念，包括每个人都想要的电动公交车，甚至还有一辆鲜绿色的越野车。但是ID.4不仅仅是一个概念。这是第一款在美国市场发售的量产MEB车辆，其设计充分考虑了跨界疯狂的美国市场。去年9月，我们在布鲁克林的演播室灯光下首次对ID.4进行了很好的展示，一个月后，Ars花费了45分钟的时间使用预生产的ID.4。但是现在，在2021年ID.4第一版模型年中，我们已经整整两天了，在本地草坪上逐渐了解了这一天。

Volumetrically, it's about the same size as a Toyota RAV4 or VW Tiguan: 181 inches (4,585mm) long, 73 inches wide (1,852mm), and 64 inches tall (1,637mm), with a 109-inch (2,766mm) wheelbase. Depending on the angle it can be quite a handsome shape. That's helped by the way the 1st Edition's aerodynamic 20-inch alloy wheels fill their arches, helping convince the brain that the car is smaller than it actually is, as well as the designer's trick of making bits disappear by cladding them in glossy black panels.

从体积上讲，它的尺寸与丰田RAV4或大众途观的尺寸相同：长181英寸（4,585毫米），宽73英寸（1,852毫米），高64英寸（1,637毫米），高109英寸（ 2,766mm）轴距。根据角度，它可能是一个很漂亮的形状。借助第一版的20英寸空气动力学铝合金轮毂充满了拱门，这有助于大脑使汽车相信它比实际尺寸要小，以及设计师的绝招。通过将它们包裹在光滑的黑色面板中而使它们消失的过程。

At a measurement of 0.28, the ID.4's drag coefficient would be considered very slippery just a few years ago. VW says it could have lowered that figure by making it a fastback (like the Audi e-tron Sportback or MEB-based e-tron Q Sportback) but that would have compromised rear headroom and cargo capacity.

以0.28的测量值，ID.4的阻力系数在几年前就被认为是非常滑的。大众表示，可以通过将其设为后备箱（例如奥迪e-tron Sportback或基于MEB的e-tron Q Sportback）来降低这一数字，但这将损害后部的净空高度和载重量。

For now, all ID.4s have the same powertrain configuration. A twin-motor, all-wheel drive option arrives in late summer, but the $43,995 1st edition, the $39,995 ID.4, and the $44,495 ID.4 Pro S are all rear-wheel drive, with a single 150kW (201hp), 310Nm (229lb-ft) permanent magnet synchronous motor located above the rear axle. (All prices are before applicable federal or local tax credits and incentives, and the 1st Edition is now sold out.)

目前，所有ID.4都具有相同的动力总成配置。双马达全轮驱动选项将在夏末上市，但第一版的价格为$ 43,995，ID.4的价格为$ 39,995和ID.4的价格为$ 44,495。Pro S均为后轮驱动，单功率150kW（201hp），后轴上方有310Nm（229lb-ft）的永磁同步电动机。 （所有价格均在适用的联邦或地方税收抵免和优惠政策之前，并且第一版现已售罄。）

American-made ID.4s will begin churning out of VW's Chattanooga factory next year, and some time after that we should see a ~$35,000 version with a smaller battery pack using locally made cells. Until then, there's just a single pack option with a useable capacity of 77kWH (82kWh total capacity), made of 288 pouch cells split across 12 modules.

美国制造的ID.4明年将开始在大众汽车的查塔努加工厂生产，之后一段时间，我们应该会看到售价35,000美元的版本，带有使用本地制造的电池的较小电池组。在那之前，只有一个单包选项，可用容量为77kWH（总容量为82kWh），由288个袋式电池组成，分为12个模块。

For the time being, range is the main thing people want from a battery EV. The official EPA rating for the ID.4 is 250 miles of range (402km) from the battery's useable 77kWh. I averaged 3.3 miles/kWh, using both the heating and air conditioning thanks to some late February weather that ranged from below freezing to warm enough for short sleeves in the space of day.

目前，续航能力是人们从电池电动汽车中获得的主要东西。 ID.4的官方EPA等级距离电池可用的77kWh距离为250英里（402公里）。由于平均2月下旬的天气，从零度以下到足够温暖的一天中的短袖，我使用暖气和空调的平均速度为3.3英里/千瓦时。

The vast majority of ID.4 owners will plug them in each night and with enough amps, the onboard AC charger can reach 11kW, recharging the battery in 7.5 hours. A DC fast charger takes just 38 minutes to charge the ID.4 from five percent to 80 percent, at a maximum of 125kW. (As we have found with most other BEVs and fast charging, if you arrive with a greater state of charge, it still takes roughly the same time to get to 80 percent, the battery just draws a lower amount of power.) VW is even including three years of unlimited DC charging with Electrify America, which is good news for potential buyers who don't have a garage or carport where they can charge at home.

绝大多数ID.4拥有者每晚都会插入它们，并且只要有足够的安培数，车载交流充电器就可以达到11kW，可在7.5小时内为电池充电。直流快速充电器只需38分钟即可为ID.4充电，从5％充电到80％，最大充电功率为125kW。 （正如我们在大多数其他BEV和快速充电中所发现的那样，如果到达更高的充电状态，则达到80％的电量仍需要大致相同的时间，电池消耗的电量只会更少。）VW甚至是包括三年与Electrify America进行的无限直流充电，对于没有车库或车棚可在家充电的潜在买家来说，这是个好消息。

The new ISO15118 Plug and Charge protocol hasn't made its way to the ID.4 yet, but VW tells us that should arrive with the first over-the-air software update later this year. Once that happens, you won't even need the Electrify America app on your phone, as the car will handle authentication and billing when it handshakes with the charger.

新的ISO15118即插即用协议尚未进入ID.4，但是大众汽车告诉我们，应该在今年晚些时候进行首次空中软件更新。一旦发生这种情况，您甚至不需要手机上的Electrify America应用程序，因为汽车在与充电器握手时将处理身份验证和计费。

By now I've sat through at least three briefings about the ID.4, and each time VW stressed that it wanted to make a crossover to appeal to normal VW customers, something that could tempt the electrocurious out of a Tiguan without scaring them. That's achieved in part by the interior, which is instantly recognizable as a VW through the design language but also the use of materials, like the pebble-textured soft plastic that it so often uses for dashboards and door cards.

到目前为止，我至少听了3次有关ID.4的简报，每次大众汽车强调要进行分频以吸引普通大众客户时，都可以在不引起惊慌的情况下将电子好奇心引诱到Tiguan上。他们。这部分是通过内部实现的，通过设计语言可以立即将其识别为大众，但也可以通过使用材料来实现，例如经常用于仪表板和门卡的卵石质软塑料。

Our 1st Edition stands out by having a white steering wheel, as well as whimsical Play and Pause symbols on the pedals, but as you'll see in the gallery above, it looks a lot more sober with a black steering wheel. Ahead of the driver is a 5.3-inch main instrument display, and to the right of it, the drive selector. Twist it away from you to engage D (or B, which increases regenerative braking), and towards you to engage reverse. To park, simply push the button on the end. Turn signals and windshield wipers are activated by conventional stalks, and there are the usual controls on the wheel for adaptive cruise control and media.

我们的第一版采用白色方向盘以及踏板上异想天开的Play和Pause符号，从而脱颖而出，但是正如您在上方的图库中看到的那样，黑色方向盘看上去更加清醒。驱动程序前面是5.3英寸的主仪表显示屏，右侧是驱动器选择器。将其扭离您以接合D（或B，这将增加再生制动），并朝您反向接合。要停放，只需按一下末端的按钮即可。转向信号灯和挡风玻璃刮水器由传统的茎杆激活，方向盘上有通常的控件，用于自适应巡航控制和媒体控制。

We'll get to the infotainment experience in a bit more depth shortly, but the 1st Edition and ID.4 Pro S both use a 12-inch touchscreen infotainment system, with a smaller 10-inch unit in the ID.4 Pro. Unlike some other new BEVs, there are still plenty of soft-touch buttons to push for common controls like the wing mirrors, climate, volume, lights, and so on.

我们将在不久的将来更深入地介绍信息娱乐体验，但是第一版和ID.4 Pro S均使用12英寸触摸屏信息娱乐系统，而ID.4 Pro中使用的是10英寸较小的设备。 。与其他一些新的BEV不同，仍然有大量的轻触按钮可推动常见的控制，例如后视镜，气候，音量，灯光等。

The passenger experience in the ID.4 is a spacious one, whether that's in the front or back seats. As you'll note in the pictures, there's plenty of leg room for the rear seats (37.6-inches/954mm) and there's a completely flat floor at the back. There is a decent array of storage bins and cubbies, and the cargo area grows from 30.3 cubic feet (858L) to 64.2 cubic feet (1,818L) with the rear seats folded down. We didn't test it as a tow vehicle, but it's rated for 2,000lb (907kg) loads in the US, with the forthcoming AWD version rated for 2,700lbs (1,225kg).

无论是在前排还是后排座椅中，ID.4的乘客体验都是宽敞的。正如您在图片中所指出的，后排座椅有足够的腿部空间（37.6英寸/ 954毫米），后排地板完全平坦。仓库和杂物箱种类繁多，货物区域从30.3立方英尺（858L）增加到64.2立方英尺（1,818L），后排座椅向下折叠。我们并未将其作为拖车进行测试，但在美国其额定负载为2,000磅（907千克），而即将推出的AWD版本则为2,700磅（1,225千克）。
