Belgian minister tweets EU's Covid vaccine price list to anger of manufacturers
比利时部长推特发布欧盟的Covid疫苗价格表，以激怒制造商

2020-12-20 02:51:54

Pharmaceutical companies complain of breach of confidentiality after amount EU has agreed to pay for leading vaccines goes public

欧盟同意为领先疫苗支付的费用公开后，制药公司抱怨违反保密规定

A Belgian minister has blown the lid off a sensitive and commercial secret – the price that the EU has agreed to pay for the leading Covid vaccines.

一位比利时部长已经揭开了一个敏感的商业秘密的秘密，这是欧盟同意为领先的Covid疫苗支付的价格。

Belgium’s budget state secretary, Eva De Bleeker, posted the price list on Twitter, with the amounts of each vaccine that her country intends to buy from the EU. The tweet was quickly deleted, but not soon enough to prevent interested parties taking screenshots, which have now made it public knowledge.

比利时预算国务秘书伊娃·德·布雷克（Eva De Bleeker）在推特上发布了价格表，并附有该国打算从欧盟购买的每种疫苗的价格。该推文被迅速删除，但还不足以阻止有兴趣的各方截取屏幕快照，该消息现已公开。

While campaigners for access to medicines were delighted at the transparency, pharmaceutical companies were not. Pfizer complained of a breach of confidentiality. “These prices are covered by a confidentiality clause in the contract with the European commission,” said Elisabeth Schraepen, the US drugmaker’s spokeswoman for the Benelux region to the Belgian daily Le Soir.

尽管争取药品的活动家对透明度感到高兴，但制药公司却不满意。辉瑞公司抱怨违反保密规定。 “这是与欧洲委员会签订的合同中的保密条款所涵盖的价格，”比荷卢经济联盟（Benelux）地区的美国制药商发言人伊丽莎白·施拉彭（Elisabeth Schraepen）说。

The price list revealed that the Oxford/AstraZeneca vaccine is the cheapest and Moderna is the most expensive – as was already known. But the details allow countries that may be negotiating with the vaccine manufacturers to take a harder line.

价格表显示，牛津/阿斯利康的疫苗最便宜，而Moderna的疫苗最昂贵-众所周知。但细节允许可能正在与疫苗生产商进行谈判的国家采取强硬路线。

Every country in the world has an interest in mass vaccination against Covid and there is a big effort to put together a programme to ensure all countries can access enough to vaccinate the vulnerable. But drug and vaccine prices have always been very closely guarded commercial secrets. “We can’t say anything about this case, everything about vaccines and prices are covered by confidentiality clauses, in the interests of society and also in the interests of negotiations ongoing,” said a spokesman for the European commission at a news briefing.

世界上每个国家都对针对Covid的大规模疫苗接种感兴趣，并且正在大力努力制定一项计划，以确保所有国家都能为脆弱人群接种疫苗。但是药品和疫苗的价格一直是非常严密的商业秘密。欧盟委员会发言人在新闻发布会上说：“我们对此事无话可说，有关疫苗和价格的一切都属于保密条款，既符合社会利益，也符合正在进行的谈判的利益。”

The UK, which is not part of the EU scheme, has secured 357m doses of seven different vaccines, including 40m doses of the Pfizer/BioNTech, which was authorised for use in the UK before the rest of the world. It also has 100m doses of the AstraZeneca vaccine.

英国不是欧盟计划的一部分，已经获得了3.57亿剂七种不同的疫苗，其中包括4000万剂辉瑞/ BioNTech，该药已在世界其他地区获准在英国使用。它还有100m剂量的阿斯利康疫苗。

A spokesperson for the British government said: “The cost of each vaccine secured by the government was negotiated on a case-by-case basis and was assessed to ensure value for money, while giving the UK the best chance of rapidly obtaining and deploying a safe and effective vaccine.”

英国政府发言人说：“由政府担保的每种疫苗的成本均根据具体情况进行谈判，并经过评估以确保物有所值，同时为英国提供了快速获得和部署疫苗的最佳机会。安全有效的疫苗。”

The UK, like the EU, has paid a considerable amount of money up front to help in the development of a number of vaccines that may or may not work, including AstraZeneca’s, so the final prices for those will be lower. However, it did not support Pfizer/BioNTech nor Moderna, so will be left with a high price tag for those vaccines.

英国与欧盟一样，已经预先支付了大量资金，以帮助开发可能有效或无效的多种疫苗，包括阿斯利康的，因此最终价格会更低。但是，它不支持辉瑞/ BioNTech或Moderna，因此这些疫苗的价格很高。

The United States has paid higher prices than Europe. Bernstein Research, an analysis and investment firm, calculated that the EU has a 24% discount on the Pfizer vaccine compared with the United States. Part of the reason may be that Europe helped fund the original research by BioNTech.

美国付出的代价高于欧洲。分析和投资公司伯恩斯坦研究公司（Bernstein Research）计算得出，与美国相比，欧盟对辉瑞疫苗的折扣为24％。部分原因可能是欧洲帮助BioNTech资助了最初的研究。

The US will pay $4 a dose for the Oxford/AstraZeneca vaccine under development, compared with the EU price of €1.78, which is 45% cheaper, according to Bernstein.

据伯恩斯坦称，美国将为正在开发的牛津/阿斯利康疫苗支付每剂4美元的费用，而欧盟的价格为1.78欧元，便宜了45％。

At the other end of the scale, Moderna’s vaccine, developed in Cambridge, Massachusetts, with $2.5bn of funding and orders from Operation Warp Speed, will cost 20% more on the European market – $18 a dose compared with $15 in the US.

另一方面，在马萨诸塞州剑桥市开发的Moderna的疫苗将获得25亿美元的资金，并从Warp Warp Speed获得订单，在欧洲市场的价格将上涨20％，每剂18美元，而美国为15美元。

Drugs and vaccines have traditionally been more expensive in the US because there is no cost-effectiveness mechanism – as there is in the UK and some other countries – to decide whether they are value for money. The free market operates and drugs are paid for by health insurance schemes.

传统上，美国的药品和疫苗价格昂贵，因为没有成本效益机制（如英国和其他一些国家那样）来决定它们是否物有所值。自由市场运作，药品由健康保险计划支付。
