Microsoft Office 2021 is on its way
Microsoft Office 2021即将面世

2021-02-19 05:50:30

If Microsoft had its way, Office 2021 probably wouldn't be news at all—the Redmond giant would almost certainly prefer that everyone simply subscribe to Microsoft 365, pay a small monthly or annual fee, and get new features and fixes as they're rolled out. For many if not most Office users, the subscription-based service is the most convenient way to get Office, even when they want to use it as locally installed software rather than doing their work in-browser and on the cloud.

如果采用微软的方式，Office 2021可能根本就不是新闻-这家雷德蒙德（Redmond）巨人几乎肯定会希望每个人都简单地订阅Microsoft 365，支付少量的月费或年费，并在获得新功能和修复时＃39;重新推出。对于许多（即使不是大多数）Office用户来说，基于订阅的服务是获得Office的最便捷方法，即使他们希望将其用作本地安装的软件，而不是在浏览器中和在云端进行工作。

For the rest of us—and for those environments which Microsoft 365 fat clients inexplicably refuse to support, such as Remote Desktop Servers—there's Office 2019 now, and there will be Office 2021 later this year. There will also be a new Office LTSC (Long Term Service Channel), which trades a 10 percent price hike for a guarantee of longer support periods... longer than the consumer version of Office 2021, that is.

对于我们其余的人以及Microsoft 365胖客户端莫名其妙地拒绝支持的环境（例如远程桌面服务器），现在有了Office 2019，并将在今年晚些时候推出Office 2021。还将有一个新的Office LTSC（长期服务渠道），其价格上涨了10％，以保证更长的支持期限……比Office 2021的消费者版本更长。

In reality, the "Long Term Service Channel" version of Office 2021 will still have a shorter support life cycle than that enjoyed by previous versions of Office. Office 2019 had a seven-year support window—Office 2021 LTSC will only offer five. There's no official word yet on the support life cycle of the presumably shorter-lived consumer version of Office 2021.

实际上，“长期服务频道” Office 2021版本将比以前版本的Office具有更短的支持生命周期。 Office 2019具有七年支持窗口-Office 2021 LTSC仅提供五个。关于Office 2021消费者版本寿命较短的支持生命周期，尚无官方消息。

So far, Office 2021 looks like it will mostly focus on visual updates—its apps include a new Dark Mode for those who prefer light text on a dark background, and unspecified accessibility improvements are on the way as well. Excel is scheduled to get a few meatier improvements ported in from Office 365, including dynamic arrays and xlookup. The few Office 2021 screenshots we've seen don't otherwise appear dramatically different from earlier versions.

到目前为止，Office 2021似乎将主要集中在视觉更新上，它的应用程序包括一个新的“暗模式”，适合那些喜欢在深色背景上使用浅色文本的人，并且未指定的可访问性改进也在进行中。 Excel计划从Office 365引入一些更重要的改进，包括动态数组和xlookup。我们看到的一些Office 2021屏幕截图在其他方面与以前的版本没有显着不同。

Both Office 2021 and Office 2021 LTSC will include OneNote and will be available in both 32-bit and 64-bit versions. Office 2021 is expected to be available on both Windows and Mac in the second half of 2021, with a Windows preview of the LTSC version expected in April.

Office 2021和Office 2021 LTSC都将包含OneNote，并且将提供32位和64位版本。 Office 2021预计将在2021年下半年在Windows和Mac上同时提供，并且LTSC版本的Windows预览版将于4月发布。
