MIT with Restrictions
麻省理工学院

2021-03-01 14:20:08

As of yesterday and continuing into today, I will be adding the following riderto the majority of my open-sourced projects:

从昨天开始一直延续到今天，我将在大多数开放源代码项目中添加以下附加条件：

The following terms additionally apply and override any above terms forapplicable parties:You may not use, copy, modify, merge, publish, distribute, sublicense,and/or sell copies of the Software in a military or law enforcement context,defined as follows:1. A military context is a professional context where the intended applicationof the Software is integration or use with or by military software, tools(software or hardware), or personnel. This includes contractors andsubcontractors as well as research affiliates of any military organization.2. A law enforcement context is a professional context where the intendedapplication of the Software is integration or use with or by law enforcementsoftware, tools (software or hardware), or personnel. This includescontractors and subcontractors as well as research affiliates of any lawenforcement organization.Entities that sell or license to military or law enforcement organizationsmay use the Software under the original terms, but only in contexts that donot assist or supplement the sold or licensed product.Students and academics who are affiliated with research institutions may usethe Software under the original terms, but only in contexts that do not assistor supplement collaboration or affiliation with any military or lawenforcement organization.

以下条款另外适用于适用方并覆盖了以上所有条款：您不得在军事或执法环境中使用，复制，修改，合并，发布，分发，再许可和/或出售本软件副本，定义如下： 1。军事环境是一种专业环境，其中软件的预期应用是与军事软件，工具（软件或硬件）或人员集成或与之结合使用。这包括任何军事组织的承包商和分包商以及研究附属机构。2。执法环境是一种专业环境，其中软件的预期应用程序是与执法软件，工具（软件或硬件）或人员集成在一起或由其使用或由其使用的。这包括承包商，分包商以及任何执法组织的研究分支机构。向军事或执法组织出售或许可的实体可以按原始条款使用软件，但仅在不辅助或补充所出售或许可的产品的情况下使用。隶属于研究机构的学者可以按原始条款使用软件，但前提是在不协助或补充与任何军事或执法组织的合作或从属关系的情况下。

In plain English: if you work for the military ( any military) or law enforcement( any law enforcement), you will not be allowed to use my projects in a professionalsetting. You may continue to use them in a personal setting with no restrictions.

用简单的英语来说：如果您为军队（任何军队）或执法部门（任何执法部门）工作，将不允许您在专业环境中使用我的项目。您可以不受限制地继续在个人设置中使用它们。

If you work for a company that sells or licenses to a military or law enforcementorganization, you may continue to use my projects in professional settings thatare not connected to the settings in which you sell or license to those organizations.Just as above, you may continue to use them under their original terms in a personal settingwith no restrictions.

如果您在向军事或执法组织销售或许可的公司工作，则可以继续在与您向这些组织出售或许可的环境无关的专业设置中使用我的项目。可以不受限制地在原始设置下按原始条款使用它们。

If you are a student or academic at a research institution, you may continue to use my projectsin professional settings that are not connected to the work of your institution onmilitary or law enforcement contracts or programs. Just as above, you may continue to use themunder their original terms in a personal setting with no restrictions.

如果您是研究机构的学生或学者，则可以继续在与军事机构或执法合同或计划的机构工作无关的专业环境中使用我的项目。就像上面一样，您可以在个人环境中不受限制地继续使用它们以其原始条款使用。

I’m not a lawyer, and I didn’t consult with one. I have no idea how enforceable this is.You’re welcome to use it as well, provided that you understand that it could be legal nonsense.

我不是律师，也没有咨询任何律师。我不知道它的可执行性。只要您了解它可能是法律上的废话，也欢迎您使用它。

Like all definitions, “open source” is a convention. We get to choose what we make of it 1.

像所有定义一样，“开源”是一个约定。我们可以选择要使用的材料1。

I’m okay with calling these terms “permissive” rather than “open,” but I’ll leave thatup to others to settle.

我可以将这些术语称为“宽松”而不是“公开”，但我会让其他人解决。

I am painfully aware of the fact that millions of people are employed by the defense and lawenforcement industries, either directly, indirectly, or by proxy. I count myself as one of them.

我很痛苦地意识到，国防和执法行业直接或间接雇用了数以百万计的人这一事实。我认为自己就是其中之一。

I am aware that defense and law enforcement are functionally middle-class job programs andare populated primarily by disaffected and myopic, not malicious, individuals. I realizethat these industries are frequently highly specialized and that individuals in these industriesmay feel like they have no other recourse for their livelihoods.

我知道，国防和执法部门实际上是中产阶级的工作计划，主要由不满和近视而不是恶意的人组成。我意识到这些行业通常是高度专业化的，这些行业中的个人可能会觉得自己没有其他谋生手段。

I am also aware that I am a small fry. My (personal) projects are insignificant within thegreater pantheon of open source. I will continue to contribute to other projects undertheir terms, advocating for modification of those terms when I believe it to be appropriate.

我也知道我是个小混混。我的（个人）项目在开源的万神殿中微不足道。我将继续根据其条款为其他项目做出贡献，并在我认为适当的时候主张对这些条款进行修改。

I plan to add a badge like this to projectsthat have been relicensed. My intent is to not sandbag users with new terms.

我计划将这样的徽章添加到已获得许可的项目中。我的意图是不给用户新术语打沙漏。

I act in spite of all of these things. I believe that I have a moral obligation to do so.I believe that it is the least I can do, in the context of my hobby and passion. I will do more.

尽管有这些事情，我还是要行动。我相信我有这样做的道义义务。在我的爱好和热情的背景下，我认为这是我至少可以做的。我会做的更多。

I’m happy to discuss it with you, reader. My contact information is on my site.

读者，我很高兴与您讨论。我的联系信息在我的网站上。

Without wading too deep into the shitfest that is the “open source” vs. “source available” / “commons clause” / SSPL debate: I consider this a more justified restriction on conventionally “open source” terms than restrictions that stem from a desire to profit on a project.  ↩

不必太过沉迷于“开放源代码”与“可用资源” /“公共条款” / SSPL辩论中最糟糕的事情：我认为，这对传统的“开放源代码”术语的限制要比出于欲望而产生的限制更为合理 从一个项目中获利。 ↩
