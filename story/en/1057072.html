New York-based Bitcoin trading and custody services provider NYDIG raises $100M, just one month after raising $200M, bringing its total raised to $405M
基于纽约比特币交易和监护服务提供商Nydig筹集了100万美元，仅在筹集200万美元后，只需一个月，将其总量升至405亿美元

2021-04-09 00:13:21

Nydig, a NYC-based provider of technology and investment solutions for Bitcoin, raised $100m in funding.

NYDIG是一家基于纽约的纽约市技术和投资解决方案提供商的比特币，筹集资金100亿美元。

Backers included Starr Insurance, Liberty Mutual Insurance, and other P&C insurers, which joined existing life insurance and annuity-focused strategic partners New York Life and MassMutual.

支持者包括STARR保险，自由互保和其他P＆amp; C保险公司，加入了现有的人寿保险和以年金的战略合作伙伴纽约生命和按摩量。

Led by Robert Gutmann, co-founder and CEO, and Ross Stevens, Executive Chairman, Nydig provides Bitcoin investment and technology solutions to insurers, banks, corporations, institutions, and HNW individuals.

NYDIG由Robert Gutmann，联合创始人兼首席执行官，联合创始人兼首席执行官和罗斯史蒂文斯罗斯史蒂文斯为保险公司，银行，公司，机构和HNW个人提供比特币投资和技术解决方案。

The company also announced that Mike Sapnar, CEO of TransRe, one of the world’s largest reinsurers, would be joining as Global Head of Insurance Solutions. While overseeing all of NYDIG’s insurance activities, Sapnar will focus on accelerating bitcoin-driven innovation in the global property and casualty (P&C) industry. He is joined by Matt Carey, U.S. Head of Insurance Solutions, who will be focused on bitcoin-powered solutions for U.S.-based life insurance and annuity providers. Prior to joining Nydig, Carey was co-founder and CEO of Blueprint Income, the largest online annuity marketplace in the U.S., recently acquired by MassMutual from Stone Ridge.

该公司还宣布，Transre的首席执行官Mike Sapnar是世界上最大的再保险公司之一，将加入全球保险解决方案负责人。虽然监督所有NYDIG的保险业务，但SAPNAR将专注于加速全球财产和伤亡（P＆amp; C）行业的比特币驱动创新。他加入了Umper Solutions的Matt Carey，将专注于基于U.S.的寿险保险和年金提供者的比特币供电解决方案。在加入NYDIG之前，Carey是蓝图收入的联合创始人兼首席执行官，是美国最大的在线年金市场，最近由来自石山脊的按摩量获得。
