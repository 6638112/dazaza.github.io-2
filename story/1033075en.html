密歇根州短信传播选举恶作剧

2020-11-04 07:24:50

A text message campaign is targeting people in Michigan with misinformation about “ballot sensor issues.” The messages claim a “typographical error” is causing people who voted for Joe Biden to have their votes switched to President Trump, and people who voted for Trump to have their votes switched to Biden, according to The Washington Post. The hoax message claims to be coming from the FBI.

密歇根州正在进行一场短信宣传活动，目标是提供有关“选票传感器问题”的错误信息。根据“华盛顿邮报”的报道，这些信息声称是一个“印刷错误”，导致投票给乔·拜登的人把票换成了特朗普总统，而投票给特朗普的人又把票换成了拜登。这条恶作剧信息声称来自联邦调查局。

Michigan Attorney General Dana Nessel warned voters the text messages are fake. “Dearborn voters, text messages are reportedly being sent to trick you into thinking there are ballot sensor issues,” she tweeted. “Do not fall for it, it’s a trick!”

密歇根州总检察长达娜·内塞尔(Dana Nessel)警告选民，这些短信是假的。她在推特上写道：“迪尔伯恩的选民们，据报道，发送短信是为了欺骗你，让你以为选票传感器有问题。”“别上当，这是个骗局！”

In Flint, robocalls have been trying to trick people into voting tomorrow (which is not allowed) due to supposedly long lines at polling stations. Nessel debunked this claim too, tweeting: “Getting reports of multiple robocalls going to Flint residents that, due to long lines, they should vote tomorrow. Obviously this is FALSE and an effort to suppress the vote. No long lines and today is the last day to vote. Don’t believe the lies! Have your voice heard! RT PLS.”

在弗林特，由于投票站应该排起了长队，机器人电话一直试图欺骗人们明天投票(这是不允许的)。内塞尔也驳斥了这一说法，他在推特上写道：“有报道称，弗林特的居民接到了多个机器人电话，由于排队很长，他们应该明天投票。”显然，这是错误的，是为了压制投票。没有长队，今天是投票的最后一天。别相信那些谎言！让我们听到你的声音！RT PLS。“。

These campaigns are just two of the many efforts to spread doubt about the outcome of the 2020 presidential election. In a separate campaign, robocallers have been warning people to “stay safe and stay home,” according to The Washington Post . The calls, which began over the summer, have increased leading up to the election — targeting nearly every city in the US. While they do not mention the 2020 race, one source told the Post: “I think they mean stay home and don’t vote.”

这些竞选活动只是散布人们对2020年总统选举结果的疑虑的众多努力中的两项。据“华盛顿邮报”报道，在另一场活动中，机器人呼叫者一直在警告人们“保持安全，呆在家里”。这些电话始于今年夏天，在大选前有所增加，目标几乎是美国每一个城市。虽然他们没有提到2020年的竞选，但一位消息人士告诉“华盛顿邮报”：“我认为他们的意思是呆在家里，不要投票。”

Voters in swing states have received the most misinformation about voting by mail leading up to the 2020 election, according to The New York Times. Between September 1st and October 29th, Pennsylvania saw 227,907 mail-in voting rumors, according to media intelligence company Zignal Labs.

据《纽约时报》报道，摇摆州选民收到的有关2020年大选前邮寄投票的错误信息最多。根据媒体情报公司Zignal Labs的数据，9月1日至10月29日期间，宾夕法尼亚州收到了227,907起邮寄投票的谣言。

Some of the rumors started after a contractor in Luzerne County discarded a small number of military ballots in error. President Trump latched onto the story, falsely claiming it was evidence of voter fraud. “RINO Governor Charlie Baker of Massachusetts is unsuccessfully trying to defend Mail In Ballots, when there is fraud being found all over the place,” he tweeted. “Just look at some of the recent races, or the Trump Ballots in Pennsylvania that were thrown into the garbage.”

一些谣言是在卢泽恩县的一名承包商错误地丢弃了少量军事选票后开始的。特朗普总统抓住这件事不放，谎称这是选民欺诈的证据。他在推特上写道：“马萨诸塞州RINO州长查理·贝克(Charlie Baker)试图在选票中捍卫邮件，但没有成功，因为到处都发现了欺诈行为。”“看看最近的一些竞选，或者宾夕法尼亚州被扔进垃圾桶的特朗普选票就知道了。”
