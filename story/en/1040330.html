Googling for ‘Baby Yoda’ will bring him into your living room via augmented reality
搜寻“ Baby Yoda”将通过增强现实将他带入您的客厅

2020-12-18 15:04:36

Now they’ve got another one. Rolled out just in time for tomorrow’s Season 2 finale of The Mandalorian, this one brings The Child (or, as the world has taken to calling him, “Baby Yoda”) into your living room.

现在他们又有了一个。该影片正好赶在明天的曼达洛人第2季大结局中推出，将“孩子”（或世界上常称呼他为“婴儿尤达”）带入您的客厅。

Want to check it out yourself? You just need to search for the right thing. This is the way:

想自己检查一下吗？您只需要搜索正确的内容。这是这样的：

Search for “The Child,” “Baby Yoda” or [The Child’s actual name here which I’ll omit because spoilers].

搜索“孩子”，“婴儿尤达”或[这里的孩子的真实姓名，因为剧透而被我省略了]。

Scroll down until the “View in 3D” button appears. Tap it, then the “View in your space” button. (Depending on your device, you might need the Google search app installed. It worked by default on a Pixel.)

向下滚动，直到出现“ 3D视图”按钮。点击它，然后点击“在您的空间中查看”按钮。 （取决于您的设备，您可能需要安装Google搜索应用。默认情况下，它在Pixel上可以运行。）

Wait until the camera view pops up, then wave your camera around a bit. Once the camera figures out where the floor is, he should appear. Be sure to bump your volume up.

等待相机视图弹出，然后稍微晃动一下相机。一旦摄像机弄清楚地板在哪里，他就应该出现。确保提高音量。

I just tested it myself and it worked well, albeit better on some devices than others. It was fast and flawless on a Pixel, but the lighting was bugging out hard on my iPhone.

我自己进行了测试，尽管它在某些设备上比其他设备要好，但是它运行良好。 Pixel上的速度很快且完美无瑕，但iPhone上的灯光却很难正常工作。

There’s a camera button that’ll let you snap photos and videos, and you can drag/pinch to move him around the room or rotate him in place for better shots. Have fun!

有一个相机按钮，可让您拍摄照片和视频，并且您可以拖动/捏住他以在房间中四处移动或将他旋转到适当的位置以获得更好的拍摄效果。玩得开心！
