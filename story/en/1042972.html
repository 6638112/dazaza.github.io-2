See Boston Dynamics' Robots Bust Freakishly Good Moves On the Dance Floor
欣赏波士顿动力公司的机器人在舞池上的疯狂摔倒

2021-01-03 05:54:02

Boston Dynamics has long impressed us with its robotic creations, from the adorable Spot robot dog to the  humanoid-like Atlas. Good ol' Spot is now a commercial product, and is available for anyone with enough coin ($74,500 to be exact) to purchase one; heck, it will even  fetch you a beer. Atlas, on the other hand, has impressed us with its gymnastic skills and its calm demeanor even when  abused by its human handlers. Now Boston Dynamics' family of robots is having some fun to celebrate the close of 2020. Spot and Atlas were joined by their oddball sibling Handle to shake their booties on the dance floor to "Do You Love Me" by The Contours. The video starts off impressive enough with just a single Atlas showing its incredible dexterity while busting out some sweet dance moves that would have made the late Patrick Swayze envious.

波士顿动力（Boston Dynamics）的机器人创造力给我们留下了深刻的印象，从可爱的Spot机器狗到类人动物般的Atlas。好的Spot现在是一种商业产品，任何拥有足够硬币（准确地说是74,500美元）的人都可以购买；哎呀，它甚至会给你拿啤酒。另一方面，即使被人类操作员滥用，阿特拉斯（Atlas）的体操技巧和沉稳的举止也给我们留下了深刻的印象。现在，波士顿动力公司机器人大家庭在庆祝2020年末时玩得很开心。Spot和Atlas参加了他们的异族兄弟姐妹Handle，他们在舞池里摇动赃物，以表达对“ Do Love Love Me”的钟爱。由等高线。该视频以令人印象深刻的视频开始，只有一个Atlas展现了其令人难以置信的灵活性，同时还破坏了一些甜美的舞步，这使已故的Patrick Swayze羡慕不已。

However, as the routine progresses, the camera pulls back to show that another twin Atlas is dancing along with the first one as they show off their synchronized and fresh rug-cutting ways. And as this robotic soul train continues to roll, Spot saunters in to join in on the fun with the distinct flare that only rover can bring.

但是，随着例行程序的进行，相机向后拉来显示另外一对双胞胎Atlas正在与第一个一起跳舞，因为他们展示了他们同步且新颖的地毯切割方式。随着这种机器人灵魂火车的不断滚动，Spot漫步者将加入只有漫游者才能带来的独特耀斑，一起享受乐趣。

Finally, Handle rolls into view while shaking its rump in the process --  literally. This is probably the closest thing we'll see to a robot twerking, despite how disturbing that might sound. With that being said, the video is good for nearly 3 minutes of ear-to-ear grin-fest, so be prepared. It's all fun and games in the headline above of course, but you have to admit the skills on display here are just a little funky-freaky.

最后，在处理过程中摇晃臀部的同时，Handle进入了视野-从字面上看。尽管这听起来很令人不安，但这可能是我们最接近机器人扭动的一件事。话虽如此，该视频在耳朵对耳朵咧嘴笑的近3分钟内效果很好，因此请做好准备。当然，上面的标题中包含了所有的乐趣和游戏，但是您必须承认这里展示的技能有些古怪。

In recent weeks, Boston Dynamics was acquired by an unlikely company: Hyundai Motor Group. Hyundai Motor Group is better known for its Hyundai/Kia vehicles here in the United States, but it decided to spend  $1.1 billion to purchase Boston Dynamics, which was first founded in 1992. Since that time, the company has changed hands a few times, with its most recent [previous] owners being  Google and SoftBank.

最近几周，波士顿动力被一家不太可能的公司收购：现代汽车集团。现代汽车集团以在美国的现代/起亚汽车而闻名，但它决定斥资11亿美元收购波士顿动力公司，该公司成立于1992年。从那时起，该公司几次易手，其最近的[先前]所有者是Google和SoftBank。
