An early look at VLC 4.0 – Hello darkness, my old friend

I first tried VLC around 2003 or so. It wasn't a good experience. The player's interface showed me a garbled view of the video file I was trying to play. Then, in 2006 or so, I tried it again. Since, it's become my staple media player on every single platform and operating system, including the mobile. The reasons are many: the king of codecs, tons of features, a simple no-frills interface.

我最初在2003年左右尝试过VLC。这不是一个很好的经验。播放器界面向我显示了一个 我尝试播放的视频文件出现乱码。然后，在2006年左右，我再次尝试了。从那以后 成为我在包括移动设备在内的每个平台和操作系统上的主要媒体播放器。这 原因有很多：编解码器之王，大量功能，简单的简洁界面。

Recently, the VLC team has started working on a visual revamp of the UI, which should come live in version 4.0. This marks a significant departure from the established look & feel of the player, which really hasn't seen any big visual updates throughout its history. So I thought, let's have a look at the early work and see what the future has in store for us. Early impressions, don't get too excited, things may rapidly evolve and change and whatnot. Follow me.

最近，VLC团队已开始着手对UI进行视觉改造，该改造应在 版本4.0。这标志着与既定外观和外观的重大偏离。玩家的感觉， 在它的整个历史中，确实没有看到任何重大的视觉更新。所以我想，让我们看看 在早期的工作中，了解我们的未来。早期印象，不要太过分 激动不已，事情可能会迅速发展变化，什么都不会发生。跟着我。

As soon as I launched the new player I was dismayed. Right away, I saw tons of problems, none of which are present in the old - VLC 3.X interface. The application launches with the Video "tab" open by default. Previously, or currently, VLC launches into a neutral screen that does not show or list any existing files or clips.

一推出新播放器，我就感到沮丧。马上，我看到了很多问题，没有一个 在旧的-VLC 3.X界面中存在。该应用程序随Video＆＃34; tab＆＃34;一起启动。打开者 默认。以前或当前，VLC会启动进入一个不显示或列出任何 现有文件或剪辑。

So I thought, OK, let's browse for a video file and play it. Except, you can't browse the local filesystem. VLC 4.0 shows you a bunch of location handlers, but it does not show you your disk! You need the file menu for that, which is hidden behind a three-dot icon in the right corner of the interface.

所以我想，好的，让我们浏览并播放视频文件。除非您无法浏览本地 文件系统。 VLC 4.0向您展示了一堆位置处理程序，但没有向您展示您的磁盘！你 为此需要文件菜单，该菜单隐藏在 界面。

Touch nonsense right there. The old player has the menu shown permanently, so you need fewer clicks to get to the desired action. As always, ALWAYS, when you introduce touch stuff to the desktop, you lose efficiency.

触摸废话就在那儿。旧播放器的菜单会永久显示，因此您只需点击几下 采取所需的行动。与往常一样，当您将触控内容引入桌面时， 失去效率。

The new UI also uses pale gray-on-gray colors - bad ergonomics. You can zoom in and out inside the main interface with Ctrl + mouse scroll - like you're in some browser. This rearranges the UI elements constantly, including changing the actual tab bar. So you don't have a simple and predictable layout you can rely on.

新的UI还使用了浅灰色/灰色-不良的人体工程学设计。您可以在内部放大和缩小 Ctrl +鼠标滚动的主界面-就像您在某些浏览器中一样。这将重新排列UI元素 不断更改，包括更改实际的标签栏。所以您没有简单可预测的布局 您可以依靠。

The new interface is not only less navigable and legible, it's also slower. The whole thing responded more sluggishly to my actions than 3.X. This is probably not important at this stage in the version's development cycle, but it is indicative nonetheless that whatever new implementation is used, it's less efficient than the old one.

新界面不仅导航性和可读性较差，而且速度也较慢。整个东西 比3.X对我的动作反应迟钝。在现阶段，这可能并不重要 版本的开发周期，但这仍然表明使用了新的实现， 它的效率不如旧的。

My music clips have no art preview. The old VLC, as I've already shown you, can do this quite elegantly. The whole thing reminds me of what VLC looks like on the phone. Except ... that's a phone! This is a desktop! Not only that, on the phone, the interface is actually more coherent, better integrated, and the font clarity and contrast are sharper. Why do I need this touch nonsense on my PC?

我的音乐剪辑没有艺术预览。旧的VLC，作为 我已经向您展示了，可以很优雅地做到这一点。整体 事情让我想起了VLC在手机上的样子。除了...那是电话！这是 台式机！不仅如此，在电话上，界面实际上更连贯，更好 集成，字体清晰度和对比度更清晰。为什么我的我需要这种废话 个人电脑？

So music, no thumbnails, ok. But video, now that's a hot potato. The video clips actually open in SEPARATE window! You get a borderless frame that plays the actual content, with no controls whatsoever. Mouse clicks don't pause the video, you need the space bar, or to switch context to the main interface. Why? How is this a good thing? Even if we put aside the massive loss of efficiency due to the increased amount of clicks and actions, if someone is watching a video, why would they need to Alt-Tab into a different window to control the first one? That's like saying you use Firefox, but then need to switch to Thunderbird to open a new tab.

音乐，没有缩略图，确定。但是视频，现在这是一个烫手山芋。视频片段实际上在其中打开 单独的窗口！您将获得一个无边界的帧，可以播放实际内容，没有任何控件。 鼠标单击不会暂停视频，您需要空格键或将上下文切换到主界面。 为什么？这怎么一件好事？即使我们撇开了由于效率提高而造成的大量效率损失， 大量的点击和操作（如果有人正在观看视频），为什么他们需要将Alt-Tab组合成一个 不同的窗口来控制第一个？就像说您使用Firefox，但随后需要进行切换 到Thunderbird打开一个新标签页。

Bad contrast, misaligned elements, extra mouse clicks, inefficient workflow. A far, far, far cry from what VLC is - a Swiss Army knife of media that does things extremely well. VLC is probably the most installed software in my arsenal. While I do install this or that application on this or that computer, VLC is the one program that gets installed everywhere, every time.

对比度差，元素未对齐，鼠标单击额外，工作流程效率低下。遥远的哭泣 从VLC是什么-瑞士军刀做得很好。 VLC可能是 我军火库中安装最多的软件。虽然我确实在这个或那个上安装这个或那个应用程序 在计算机中，VLC是每次都安装在各处的一个程序。

Not just that, I really enjoyed my journey with VLC, discovering its many powers and uses. Over the years, I've written dozens of articles on VLC, showing you how to accomplish tons of useful, practical and fairly advanced tasks - like embedding subtitles into videos, for instance. I'll stay with 3.X builds for as long as possible, maybe even compile it myself, and if things stop working after that, so be it. I have no use for touch nonsense on my desktop.
