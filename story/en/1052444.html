Facebook announces tools to boost COVID-19 vaccines: helping US users find slots, partnering with health authorities on WhatsApp, and labeling all vaccine posts

Facebook said Monday it's releasing new tools to make it easier for people to get vaccinated against COVID-19, including a feature to find a time and place to receive a shot.

Facebook周一表示，释放新工具，让人们更容易地接种Covid-19疫苗，包括找出接收时间和地点的特点。

The new tools show how tech companies are playing a role in public health messaging around the vaccine. Social networks have also had to battle misinformation about the vaccine such as debunked claims that the vaccine is toxic and causes autism. Last week, US President Joe Biden said states must make the vaccine open to all eligible adults by May 1.

新工具展示了科技公司如何在疫苗周围的公共卫生消息中发挥作用。社交网络也必须对疫苗进行误导，例如疫苗的疫苗，即疫苗有毒并导致自闭症。上周，美国总统Joe Biden表示，各国必须将疫苗开放至5月1日的所有合格成年人。

"The data shows the vaccines are safe and they work. They're our best hope for getting past this virus and getting back to normal life," said Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg in a statement, noting he's looking forward to getting the vaccine himself.

During the pandemic, Facebook launched an online hub for coronavirus information, which the company is expanding globally to its photo-service Instagram. Facebook users in the US who visit this online information center will also have access to a new feature available in 17 languages that displays a map of nearby places that offer the COVID-19 vaccine when they search for a city. The locations are provided by VaccineFinder and contains the place's contact information, hours of operations and a link to book an appointment. Facebook teamed up with Boston Children's Hospital to build the new feature.

在大流行期间，Facebook推出了Coronavirus信息的在线集线器，该公司在全球范围内扩展到其照片服务Instagram。访问此在线信息中心的Facebook用户还可以访问17种语言中提供的新功能，这些功能显示在搜索城市时提供Covid-19疫苗的附近地图。该位置由疫苗进展提供，并包含该地方＆＃39; S的联系信息，运营时间和预约的链接。 Facebook与波士顿儿童＆＃39;医院合作，建立新功能。

Facebook said it's also partnering with health authorities and governments to get people registered for vaccinations through notifications on its messaging service WhatsApp. The social network said governments, nonprofits and international organizations have sent 3 billion messages about COVID-19 to people through WhatsApp chatbots.

Facebook表示，它还与卫生当局和政府合作，通过对其消息服务的通知来获得疫苗接种的人。社交网络表示，政府，非营利组织和国际组织通过WhatsApp Chatbots向人们发送了3亿条关于Covid-19的信息。

Facebook said in the coming weeks it's also adding labels on all posts about the COVID-19 vaccines that direct them to the COVID-19 Information Center and plans to do more labeling. The company in February said it would remove more false claims about the COVID-19 vaccine that could lead to physical harm. Users will also see another screen when they try to share a post on Facebook and Instagram with a COVID-19 label. The social network is also launching new tools so journalists, publishers and others can track COVID-19 information in real time and public health officials can analyze data about why some people don't want to get vaccinated.

Facebook在未来几周内表示，它还在Covid-19疫苗的所有帖子上添加了标签，将它们引导到Covid-19信息中心并计划做更多的标签。该公司于2月份表示，它将取消对可能导致身体伤害的Covid-19疫苗的更多虚假索赔。当他们尝试在Facebook和Instagram上与Covid-19标签分享帖子时，用户也会看到另一个屏幕。社交网络也在推出新工具，所以记者，出版商和其他人可以实时跟踪Covid-19信息，公共卫生官员可以分析有关为什么有些人不想接种疫苗的数据。
