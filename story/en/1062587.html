SpaceX Partners With Google Cloud On Starlink, Placing Ground Stations At Data Centers
Spaptx与Google Cloud上的STARLINK合作伙伴，将地站放在数据中心

2021-05-14 07:09:04

Elon Musk-founded SpaceX is in the process of rolling out Starlink as a satellite internet provider around the world. As part of a new partnership, Google Cloud data centers will be home to key Starlink infrastructure in order to let enterprise users better access key services.

Elon Musk创立的SpaceX是在世界各地的卫星互联网提供商推出Starlink的过程中。作为新伙伴关系的一部分，Google Cloud数据中心将成为关键Starlink基础架构的所在地，以便让企业用户更好地访问关键服务。

This partnership starts with SpaceX building Starlink ground stations inside Google data centers for “secure, low-latency, and reliable delivery of data” from existing fiber networks to space and back to end users. There are currently over 1,500 Starlink satellites in orbit, with more launching on a regular basis aboard Falcon 9 rockets.

此伙伴关系始于Google数据中心内的Spacex Building Starlink地站，从现有的光纤网络到空间和返回最终用户的“安全，低延迟和可靠地传送数据”。目前轨道上有超过1,500颗Starlink卫星，在弗尔康9枚火箭中定期发射更多。

The end goal is to make cloud services, data, and applications available to businesses in rural or remote areas.

最终目标是在农村或偏远地区的企业中制作云服务，数据和应用程序。

Connectivity from Starlink’s constellation of low-Earth-orbit satellites provides a path for these organizations to deliver data and applications to teams distributed across countries and continents, quickly and securely.

STARLINK的低地球轨道卫星星座的连接为这些组织提供了一条路径，为这些组织提供快速和安全地向跨国和大陆分发的团队提供数据和应用程序。

The first Google Cloud and Starlink customers will be able to benefit from this partnership in the second half of 2021. Meanwhile, Microsoft has a similar arrangement ( Azure Space) with Starlink.

第一个谷歌云和Starlink客户将能够在2021年下半年从这个合作伙伴处受益。同时，Microsoft拥有类似的安排（Azure Space），具有Starlink。

“Applications and services running in the cloud can be transformative for organizations, whether they’re operating in a highly networked or remote environment,” said Urs Hölzle, Senior Vice President, Infrastructure at Google Cloud. “We are delighted to partner with SpaceX to ensure that organizations with distributed footprints have seamless, secure, and fast access to the critical applications and services they need to keep their teams up and running.”

“云中运行的应用程序和服务可以转型化组织，无论是在高度网络化还是远程环境中运营，”Google Cloud的高级副总裁UrsHölzle说，“UrsHölzle表示。 “我们很高兴与Spacex合作，以确保具有分布式脚印的组织具有无缝，安全，快速地访问他们需要保持其团队和运行的关键应用和服务。”

“Combining Starlink’s high-speed, low-latency broadband with Google’s infrastructure and capabilities provides global organizations with the secure and fast connection that modern organizations expect,” said SpaceX President and Chief Operating Officer Gwynne Shotwell. “We are proud to work with Google to deliver this access to businesses, public sector organizations, and many other groups operating around the world.”

“结合Starlink的高速，低延迟宽带与谷歌的基础设施和能力提供全球组织，拥有现代组织期望的安全快捷的连接，”Spacex总裁兼首席运营官Gwynne Shotwell说。 “我们很自豪能够与谷歌合作，为业务，公共部门组织和许多其他在世界各地运营的其他团体提供此机会。”
