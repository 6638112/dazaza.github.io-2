Carcinization
致癌作用

2020-11-25 05:19:05

Jump to navigation Jump to search Carcinisation (or carcinization) is an example of convergent evolution in which a crustacean evolves into a crab-like form from a non-crab-like form. The term was introduced into evolutionary biology by L. A. Borradaile, who described it as "one of the many attempts of Nature to evolve a crab". [1]

跳转至导航致癌化（或致癌化）是聚合进化的一个示例，其中甲壳类动物从非螃蟹状演变为螃蟹状。这个术语是由L. A. Borradaile引入进化生物学中的，他将其描述为“自然界进化蟹的许多尝试之一”。 [1]

Carcinisation is believed to have occurred independently in at least five groups of decapod crustaceans, [2] most notably king crabs, [3] [4] which most scientists believe evolved from hermit crab ancestors. The other examples are the family Porcellanidae or porcelain crabs (which are closely related to squat lobsters), [5] the hairy stone crab Lomis hirta, [6] the coconut crab Birgus latro, and true crabs ( Brachyura). [7] A prehistoric example is the extinct crustacean order Cyclida, which evolved their crab-like morphology before the existence of true crabs. [8]

据信致癌作用至少在五类十足足甲壳类动物中独立发生，[2]最著名的是帝王蟹，[3] [4]，大多数科学家认为这是隐居蟹祖先演变而来的。其他例子是波氏蟹科或瓷蟹（与龙虾有密切关系），[5]多毛石蟹Lomis hirta，[6]椰子蟹Birgus latro和真蟹（Brachyura）。 [7]史前的一个例子是灭绝的甲壳纲的Cyclida，它在真正的螃蟹存在之前就进化出了类似螃蟹的形态。 [8]

The example of king crabs (family Lithodidae) evolving from hermit crabs has been particularly well studied, and evidence in their biology supports this theory. For example, most hermit crabs are asymmetrical, so that they fit well into spiral snail shells; the abdomens of king crabs, even though they do not use snail shells for shelter, are also asymmetrical. [9] [10] [11] [12]

从寄居蟹进化而来的帝王蟹（Lithodidae家族）的例子已经得到了很好的研究，其生物学证据支持了这一理论。例如，大多数寄居蟹是不对称的，因此它们非常适合螺旋蜗牛壳；即使不使用蜗牛壳遮盖，帝王蟹的腹部也是不对称的。 [9] [10] [11] [12]

An exceptional form of carcinisation, termed "hypercarcinisation", is seen in the porcelain crab Allopetrolisthes spinifrons. [13] In addition to the shortened body form, A. spinifrons also shows similar sexual dimorphism to that seen in true crabs, where males have a shorter pleon than females. [13]

在瓷蟹Allopetrolisthes spinifrons中可以看到一种特殊的癌化形式，称为“超癌化”。 [13]除了体型缩短之外，棘棘龙还显示出与真螃蟹相似的性二态性，其中雄性比雌性短。 [13]
