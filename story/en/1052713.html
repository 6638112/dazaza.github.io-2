GeForce RTX 3060 Ethereum Mining Restrictions Have Been Broken
GeForce RTX 3060 Etereum挖掘限制已被破坏

2021-03-16 20:49:41

Update: Two german publications have now confirmed Ethereum mining limiter can be removed simply by using a special driver.

更新：现在两种德国出版物现在确认了以Etereum挖掘限制器只需使用特殊驱动程序即可删除。

The editorial team from PC Watch confirms that a hack developed by miners does indeed work.

来自PC手表的编辑团队确认矿工开发的黑客确实有效。

There have been reports last week that Ethereum mining restrictions on the Geforce RTX 3060 have somehow been broken, despite NVIDIA claiming that they are unhackable. Initial reports were wrong, the screenshots that have started to flood the tech forums have in fact been showing a different alternative cryptocurrency, such as Ravecoin. Those alt-coins use different algorithms and their hash rate has never been limited by NVIDIA.

上周已经有报道，尽管NVIDIA声称它们是不满意的，但是在GeForce RTX 3060对GeForce RTX 3060的日常采矿限制已经被破坏。初始报告错误，已经开始泛滥技术论坛的截图已经显示出不同的替代加密货币，例如RaveCoin。那些Alt-Coins使用不同的算法，他们的哈希速率从未受到NVIDIA限制。

Fast forward to this week and we have a least three different sources now claiming that the mining restrictions have indeed been broken, but users should not expect doubled hash rate just yet.

快进到本周，我们拥有至少三种不同的来源，现在声称采矿限制确实被破坏了，但用户不应该期待一倍哈希汇率。

PC Watch has today confirmed that the ETH mining restrictions have been broken. The editorial team has confirmed after contacting related parties. The site reports that no modifications to the driver or BIOS are required, which indicates that the restrictions might have been broken on a mining software level.

PC手表今天已经证实了Eth采矿限制已被破坏。编辑团队联系相关方后确认。该站点报告不需要对驱动程序或BIOS的修改，这表明限制可能已在挖掘软件级别中断。

[…] The editorial department was able to confirm the fact that restrictions could be circumvented by interacting with related parties. The specific method is not introduced here, but it does not require modification of the driver or BIOS, and anyone can easily do it with a little effort.

[...]该编辑部能够确认通过与关联方互动来规避限制的事实。这里没有介绍具体方法，但它不需要修改驾驶员或BIOS，并且任何人都可以轻松地用一点努力来实现。

The mining restrictions hack does not appear to be limited to one mining software. We have also been tipped about this message on Github, coming from Phoenix Miner developers. With the ETH mining lock removed, RTX 3060 may begin to attract miners. This is clearly not good news to gamers and NVIDIA. The company is also working on RTX 3080 Ti and RTX 3070 Ti, both expected to feature the same anti-mining technology. If miners manage to break those limiters as well, then one would ask what is the point of such a limitation in the first place.

随着僵化的锁定锁，RTX 3060可以开始吸引矿工。这显然不是游戏玩家和nvidia的好消息。该公司还在RTX 3080 TI和RTX 3070 TI工作，预计将采用相同的防矿业技术。如果矿工也设法打破这些限制者，那么人们会问一下这一限制的目的是什么。
