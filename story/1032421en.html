Where will Microsoft try to drag you today? Do you really want to go there?

微软今天会把你拖到哪里去？你真的想去那里吗？

In the last week of October 1998, a confidential Microsoft memorandumon Redmond's strategy against Linux and Open Source software wasleaked to me by a source who shall remain nameless. I annotated thismemorandum with explanation and commentary over Halloween Weekend andreleased it to the national press. Microsoft was forced toacknowledge its authenticity. The press rightly treated it as a majorstory and covered it (with varying degrees of cluefulness).

1998年10月的最后一周，一份关于雷蒙德针对Linux和开源软件的战略的微软机密备忘录被一位不愿透露姓名的消息人士泄露给了我。我在万圣节周末用解释和评论注释了这份备忘录，并将其发布给了全国媒体。微软被迫承认其真实性。媒体正确地将其视为主要新闻，并对其进行了报道(具有不同程度的线索)。

The now-infamous "Halloween Document" contained references to asecond memorandum specifically on Linux. Within days, copies of thesecond memo had been forwarded to me from two separate sources. Irenamed the first annotated version "Halloween I" and set aboutannotating the second. While not as dramatic or sinister in itsimplications as its predecessor, Halloween II includes a lot ofmaterial at variance with Microsoft's public party line on Linux.

现在臭名昭著的万圣节文档中提到了第二个专门针对Linux的备忘录。几天之内，第二份备忘录的副本就从两个不同的来源寄给了我。我将第一个注释版本重新命名为万圣节，并着手注释第二个版本。虽然不像它的前身那样戏剧性或险恶，但万圣节II包含了许多与微软在Linux上的公开聚会路线不同的材料。

This page originally continued with an anti-Microsoft jeremiad. On reflection, however, I think I'dprefer to finish by thanking the principal authors, Vinod Valloppillil

这一页最初是一则反微软的杰里米广告。然而，经过深思熟虑，我想我更愿意以感谢主要作者维诺德·瓦洛皮里尔(Vinod Valloppillil)来结束
