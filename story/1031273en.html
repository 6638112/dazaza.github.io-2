深受执法部门欢迎的电话窃听工具GrayKey的制造商GrayShift筹集了4700万美元，由PeakEquity Partners牵头

2020-10-27 00:32:35

Grayshift, the startup behind one of the U.S. government’s favourite iPhone hacking technologies, has raised $47 million in funding. It’s a huge round for a phone forensics company, especially one that’s focused almost exclusively on breaking the security of Apple’s famous device.

美国政府最喜欢的iPhone黑客技术之一背后的初创公司GrayShift已经筹集了4700万美元的资金。对于一家手机取证公司来说，这是一个巨大的回合，特别是一家几乎完全专注于破解苹果著名设备安全的公司。

Grayshift’s Graykey, the existence of which was first revealed by Forbes in 2018, is used by a wide range of federal agencies, from Immigration and Customs Enforcement and Customs and Border Protection to the FBI and the president’s office. As revealed in recent research from nonprofit Upturn, it’s also used by a massive number of local police agencies across America.

2018年，福布斯首次披露了GrayShift的Graykey的存在，从移民和海关执法局、海关和边境保护局到联邦调查局和总统办公室，广泛的联邦机构都在使用GrayShift的Graykey。根据非营利性组织UpTurn最近的研究显示，美国各地的大量地方警察机构也在使用它。

The round, led by PeakEquity Partners, shows how investors are happy to go big on what some see as a powerful but potentially controversial technology. As Upturn said in its report, the widespread use of forensics tools like the GrayKey “represents a dangerous expansion in law enforcement’s investigatory powers.” “Given how routine these searches are today, together with racist policing policies and practices, it’s more than likely that these technologies disparately affect and are used against communities of color,” Upturn wrote.

由PeakEquity Partners牵头的这轮融资表明，投资者多么乐意在一些人看来强大但可能引发争议的技术上大举投资。正如UpTurn在其报告中所说，像GrayKey这样的取证工具的广泛使用“代表着执法部门调查权力的危险扩张。”UpTurn写道：“考虑到今天这些搜查是多么例行公事，再加上种族主义的警察政策和做法，这些技术很可能以不同的方式影响并被用来对付有色人种社区。”

David Miles, founder and chief executive officer of Grayshift, boasted that in the last year his company has doubled revenues, customer adoption and employee growth. “This investment validates Grayshift's innovation and how we are transforming this industry," said David Miles, co-founder and CEO of Grayshift.

GrayShift创始人兼首席执行官大卫·迈尔斯(David Miles)吹嘘说，去年他的公司收入、客户采用率和员工增长都翻了一番。“这项投资验证了GrayShift的创新，以及我们正在如何改变这个行业，”GrayShift的联合创始人兼首席执行官大卫·迈尔斯(David Miles)说。

But not all are convinced by Grayshift’s long-term capabilities, given Apple’s consistent improvement of the iPhone’s security. The GrayKey is believed to be capable of hacking iPhones up to the iPhone 11, though it’s unclear how effective the tool is against the iPhone 12. “It’s most likely they can’t do much, if anything at all, with the iPhone 12 and iOS 14,” said Vladimir Katalov, CEO of another forensics company, Elcomsoft. “Perhaps they just want to cash out.”

但考虑到苹果对iPhone安全性的持续改进，并非所有人都对GrayShift的长期能力深信不疑。据信，GrayKey能够破解iPhone11之前的iPhone，尽管目前还不清楚该工具对iPhone12的攻击效果如何。另一家取证公司Elcomsoft的首席执行官弗拉基米尔·卡塔洛夫(Vladimir Katalov)表示：“他们很可能对iPhone 12和iOS 14无能为力。”“或许他们只是想套现。”

The company has been keen to avoid press as it focuses on expanding its reach across government. According to government contract records, in the last two months, Grayshift secured a $900,000 deal with ICE and multiple contracts with the FBI and the DEA.

该公司一直热衷于避免媒体，因为它专注于扩大其在政府部门的影响力。根据政府合同记录，在过去的两个月里，GrayShift与ICE达成了90万美元的交易，并与FBI和DEA签订了多份合同。

Previously, Forbes revealed Grayshft was planning to move into hacking Android devices for its government customers. A product has yet to make it to market, however.

此前，福布斯透露，Grayshft正计划为其政府客户侵入Android设备。然而，目前还没有一款产品投放市场。
