Cart.com, which provides end-to-end software and services to scale e-commerce businesses, raises $25M Series A led by Mercury Fund and Arsenal Growth
Cart.com提供以缩放电子商务业务的端到端软件和服务，培养了Mercury Fund和Arsenal增长的2500万美元的系列

2021-04-16 10:17:41

Cart.com, a Houston, TX-based end-to-end e-commerce services provider delivering a fully integrated and owned suite of software, services and infrastructure to scale businesses online, closed a $25m Series A round of financing.

Cart.com，休斯顿，TX的端到端电子商务服务提供商提供全面的综合和拥有的软件，服务和基础设施套件，以在线扩展企业，已关闭2500万美元的融资。

The round, which has raised over $45M in total funding to date, was led by Mercury Fund and Arsenal Growth, with participation from Moonshots Capital and Scarlet Venture Fund.

迄今为止迄今为止筹集超过4500万美元，由Mercury Fund和Arsenal Grower领导，参加Moonshots Capital和Scarlet Venture Fund领导。

The company intends to use the funds to increase customer demand, make key hires, and accelerate growth as a -commerce-as-a-Service (ECaaS) provider in the ecommerce services space.

本公司打算利用资金来提高客户需求，使重点雇用，并加速增长作为电子商务服务空间中的集团商品提供商。

Co-founded in September 2020 by CEO Omair Tariq, former executive at Home Depot and COO of Blinds.com, and Executive Chairman Jim Jacobsen, co-founder and former CEO of RTIC Outdoors, Cart.com is an ecommerce solutions provider delivering a fully integrated and owned suite of software, expert services and infrastructure to scale businesses online. The company offers business solutions such as online store software, digital marketing services, fulfillment services, financial services, and customer service capabilities so brands of any size are able to work with a single partner to attain the same capabilities as some of the world’s largest companies.

Cart.com是由Home Depot And Copot And Mode of Blinds.com上的Emair Tariq，前高级执行董事会和前任首席执行官Jim Jacobsen，户外行政主席和前首席执行官的Emair Tariq，以及Eccomic Solutions董事会提供商集成和拥有的软件套件，专家服务和基础设施到在线扩展业务。该公司提供的商业解决方案，如在线商店软件，数字营销服务，履行服务，金融服务和客户服务能力，因此任何规模的品牌都能够与单个合作伙伴合作，以获得与世界上最大的一些公司相同的功能。

The company has also made five acquisitions to date including online storefront software platform AmeriCommerce, a storage supplies business with nation-wide fulfillment capabilities, and two full service digital marketing agencies.

该公司还迄今为止提供了五项收购，包括在线店面软件平台AmericeMerce，该存储提供了全国范围内的履行能力和两个完整的服务数字营销机构的业务。
