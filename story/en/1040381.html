Toxicological analysis of George’s marvellous medicine: literature review
乔治奇妙医学的毒理学分析：文献综述

2020-12-18 15:43:21

1Emergency Department, University Hospitals of Derby and Burton NHS Foundation Trust, Royal Derby Hospital, Derby DE22 3NE, UK

1英国皇家德比医院德比大学医院急诊室和伯顿NHS基金会信托基金，英国德比DE22 3NE

Objective To analyse the therapeutic effects and toxicity of the eponymous concoction described in Roald Dahl’s book George’s Marvellous Medicine.

目的分析罗尔德·达尔（Roald Dahl）的《乔治奇妙医学》（George's Marvelous Medicine）一书中描述的同名药草的治疗效果和毒性。

Main outcome measures Clinical and toxic effects of the individual ingredients checked against those listed in ToxBase, the National Poisons Information Service’s poisons database.

主要结果指标根据国家毒物信息服务的毒物数据库ToxBase中列出的成分检查了各个成分的临床和毒性影响。

Results The medicine contained 34 ingredients. The most common toxic effect identified on ToxBase was nausea and vomiting (16 ingredients, 47%). Potentially life threatening effects were associated with 13 (38%) ingredients, including depression of the central nervous system, kidney injury, convulsions, cardiac toxicity, and mucosal erosion. The effects described in the book were accurate initially but then diverted from the most likely clinical outcome (death).

结果该药含有34种成分。在ToxBase上发现的最常见的毒性作用是恶心和呕吐（16种成分，占47％）。潜在的威胁生命的影响与13种成分（38％）相关，包括中枢神经系统抑制，肾脏损伤，抽搐，心脏毒性和粘膜侵蚀。书中描述的效果最初是准确的，但后来偏离了最可能的临床结果（死亡）。

Conclusions Although Dahl ought to be applauded for his initial accuracy about the toxicology of the ingredients in George’s marvellous medicine, the overall effect would be fatal catastrophic physiological collapse. Scientific exploration and experimentation should be encouraged in children, although any medicinal ingredients need to be checked for potential toxicity before being administered—to grandmas or anyone else.

结论尽管达尔因他在乔治奇妙的药物中成分的毒理学上的最初准确性而受到赞扬，但总体效果将是致命的灾难性生理崩溃。应该鼓励儿童进行科学探索和实验，尽管在对祖母或其他任何人服用之前，必须检查任何药用成分的潜在毒性。

Roald Dahl is one of the most popular children’s authors worldwide, with more than 200 million books published in 59 languages. 1 Dahl’s book George’s Marvellous Medicine has been enjoyed by generations of young readers since it was first published in 1981. 2 In the 1998 edition, the verso of the contents page warns “For Doctors Everywhere,” 3 but by the 2010 edition 4 the declaration had been replaced by “WARNING TO READERS: Do not try to make George’s Marvellous Medicine yourselves at home. It could be dangerous.” The medical import of this book was reasserted in the 2016 edition, 5 which published both statements. Is the eponymous marvellous medicine medically useful, dangerous, or both? The reason for the prefaced warning is not clear, but it is possible that the disclaimer was printed in response to inadvertent harm caused by children experimenting on their grandmas.

罗尔德·达尔（Roald Dahl）是全球最受欢迎的儿童作家之一，以59种语言出版了超过2亿本书。 1 Dahl的书《 George's Marvelous Medicine》自1981年首次出版以来，已经受到了几代年轻读者的喜爱。2在1998年版中，内容页面的背面警告“对于任何地方的医生”，但在2010年版中则警告4改为“警告读者：请勿试图在家中使乔治的奇妙药物成为自己。可能很危险。”本书的医学意义在2016年版中被重申，5均发表了这两种说法。同名的奇妙药物在医学上有用，危险还是同时兼有？前言警告的原因尚不清楚，但可能是由于儿童在祖母上进行试验而造成的无意伤害而印制了免责声明。

In both the United Kingdom and the European Union, unintentional poisoning is a leading cause of accidental death in children, 6 7 with more than 28 000 children receiving treatment for poisoning in the UK each year 8 and 3000 children dying across the EU annually. 6 The most common place for such poisonings is the home, 7 typically with smaller children exploring and putting things in their mouths, although an important proportion of unintentional and intentional poisonings are in older children, often including alcohol ingestion. During the first wave of the coronavirus disease 2019 (covid-19) pandemic, children were home schooled and relied on their parents or internet streaming services for their education, and this scenario produced the ideal conditions for children to be placed at increased risk of unintentional poisoning. 9 Following lockdowns in the UK, the number of people presenting with other unintentional injuries, such as burns, has increased. 10 11 12 Data for poisoning, however, are not yet available.

在英国和欧盟，无意中毒是导致儿童意外死亡的主要原因，英国每年有6 7 7的中毒儿童超过28 000，其中有8 000名儿童在欧盟范围内死亡。 6此类中毒最常见的地方是家庭，7通常是较小的孩子在探索并放入口中，尽管较大比例的无意和故意中毒发生在年龄较大的儿童中，通常包括饮酒。在2019年第一轮冠状病毒病（covid-19）大流行期间，孩子们在家上学并依靠父母或互联网流媒体服务进行教育，这种情况为孩子处于无意之中的危险增加提供了理想条件中毒。 9在英国封锁之后，遭受其他非故意伤害（例如烧伤）的人数有所增加。 10 11 12但是，尚无中毒数据。

As some of the ingredients in George’s marvellous medicine can be easily found in houses, the study’s home educators decided that it would be interesting to concoct a similar mixture to George’s but without using unsuspecting recipients such as cranky grandmas. Before embarking on our experiment, we deemed it prudent to examine the potential toxicity of the ingredients in George’s marvellous medicine. We then quantified the toxicological and therapeutic potential of the medicine and compared the known effects of the ingredients with the outcomes claimed in the book.

由于可以轻松地在房屋中找到乔治奇妙的药物中的某些成分，因此这项研究的家庭教育工作者认为，将类似的混合物炮制成乔治的奇妙混合物是很有趣的，但不要使用毫不客气的接受者，例如胡思乱想的祖母。在开始我们的实验之前，我们认为最好检查一下乔治奇妙的药物中这些成分的潜在毒性。然后，我们对药物的毒理学和治疗潜力进行了量化，并将成分的已知作用与书中声称的结果进行了比较。

Between March and August 2020, two researchers and their five young helpers read George’s Marvellous Medicine, contemporaneously recording the ingredients and amounts in the original eponymous concoction. Reading took place in three independent teams of young readers with no communication between the groups. The ingredients were originally documented on paper using a combination of pen and pencil (one researcher does not yet have his school pen licence) and then collated in a spreadsheet (Microsoft Excel 2010, Microsoft, WA).

在2020年3月至2020年8月之间，两名研究人员及其五名年轻助手阅读了《乔治奇妙医学》，并同时记录了原始同名药材的成分和含量。阅读由三个独立的年轻读者小组进行，小组之间没有任何交流。这些成分最初是使用钢笔和铅笔的组合记录在纸上的（一位研究人员尚未获得学校的笔执照），然后在电子表格中进行整理（Microsoft Excel 2010，Microsoft，WA）。

We then cross referenced the ingredients with the relevant entries in ToxBase, the National Poisons Information Service’s poisons database. 13 When we could not find exact matches, we chose the nearest approximation (for example, purple pills to treat hoarse horses in the book were matched to the pale purple chewable tablets of phenylbutazone, a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug used to treat “the strangles,” an equine disease 14). We graded the severity of any potential effects. As poisoning shows a dose-response, we presumed that a moderate dose had been administered to grandma.

然后，我们将成分与国家毒物信息服务的毒物数据库ToxBase中的相​​关条目进行交叉引用。 13当我们找不到精确匹配项时，我们选择了最接近的近似值（例如，书中用于治疗嘶哑马匹的紫色药丸与苯基丁a的淡紫色可咀嚼片相匹配，后者是一种非甾体抗炎药，用于治疗“勒死”，马病14）。我们对任何潜在影响的严重程度进行了分级。由于中毒显示出剂量反应，我们推测已对奶奶进行了中等剂量的治疗。

According to the National Health Service Health Research Authority, 15 our study would be defined as research (in that findings are generalisable to any child who wishes to recreate the marvellous medicine) but does not require ethical approval. We only present descriptive statistics.

根据美国国家卫生服务健康研究局（National Health Service Health Research Authority）的研究，15我们的研究将被定义为研究（因为研究结果可推广至任何希望重制奇妙药物的儿童），但无需获得伦理学批准。我们仅提供描述性统计数据。

Owing to the varied ages and reading skills of the reading teams, we wrote three versions of the paper (see supplementary files 1 and 2 for the other two). This main version is appropriate for undergraduate reading age level, whereas the other versions are appropriate for reading ages 9-10 years (grade 4.8) and 14-15 years (grade 9.6), based on the Flesch-Kincaid method. 16

由于阅读小组的年龄和阅读技巧各异，我们编写了三种版本的论文（另两个版本请参见补充文件1和2）。根据Flesch-Kincaid方法，该主要版本适用于本科生的阅读年龄水平，而其他版本则适用于9-10岁（4.8级）和14-15岁（9.6级）的阅读年龄。 16

Representatives of the parents and children of St Chad’s Primary School were consulted about the study. The child PPI members agreed that attempts at reproducing the medicine, or concocting their own with inspiration from the original, was an attractive proposition. Several parent PPI members reported experience of finding such mixtures being created or having to take measures to prevent them from being created in their own homes.

咨询了圣乍得小学家长和孩子的代表。儿童PPI成员一致认为，尝试复制该药物或从原始药物中汲取灵感来配制自己的药物是一个有吸引力的提议。几位家长PPI成员报告了发现这种混合物的经验，或者必须采取措施防止在自己的房屋中产生这种混合物。

All 95 pages of the story were read. Thirty four different ingredients were identified—10 from the bathroom, four from the bedroom, five from the laundry room, six from the kitchen, five from the shed (and a further one after heating, bringing the total to six), and three from the garage. No discrepancies were found between the three independent groups of readers, giving a κ statistic of 1.0.

故事的所有95页都被阅读了。确定了34种不同的成分-浴室中有10种，卧室中有4种，洗衣间有5种，厨房有6种，棚子有5种（加热后有5种，总共有6种），其余3种车库。在三组独立的读者之间未发现差异，κ统计量为1.0。

The most common potential symptom was nausea and vomiting, with this listed as a common effect for 16 (47%) ingredients, closely followed by depression of the central nervous system for 13 (38%) ingredients (five (15%) caused by the effects of ethanol). Eleven (32%) ingredients were linked to diarrhoea and six (18%) to cardiac involvement (myocarditis or arrhythmias). Four (12%) caused foaming or hypersalivation, or both, and four (12%) caused gastrointestinal tract ulceration and haematemesis. Seven (21%) caused lung injury and four (12%) caused seizures ( fig 1).

最常见的潜在症状是恶心和呕吐，被列为16种（47％）成分的共同作用，紧接着是13种（38％）成分（五种（15％））引起的中枢神经系统抑制。乙醇的影响）。十一（32％）成分与腹泻有关，六（18％）成分与心脏受累（心肌炎或心律不齐）有关。四（12％）人引起泡沫或唾液分泌过多，或两者兼而有之，四（12％）人引起胃肠道溃疡和呕血。七（21％）人引起肺损伤，四（12％）人引起癫痫发作（图1）。

Five (15%) of the ingredients were foodstuffs found in the kitchen which, although particularly spicy, are not considered too toxic. Although these ingredients might have contributed to vomiting in high doses, only the “extra hot” chilli sauce is listed in Toxbase.

五种（15％）成分是在厨房中发现的食品，尽管特别辛辣，但认为没有太大毒性。尽管这些成分可能导致高剂量呕吐，但Toxbase中仅列出了“特辣”辣椒酱。

Treatments for this multivariate poisoning were complex and would require immediate high level care. Treatments included (but were not limited to) airway protection, management of seizures and arrhythmias, antidotes, lipid infusions, and haemofiltration.

这种多变量中毒的治疗很复杂，需要立即进行高级护理。治疗包括（但不限于）气道保护，癫痫发作和心律不齐，解毒剂，脂质输注和血液滤过的管理。

Our findings suggest that far from being marvellous, George’s medicine is in fact incredibly toxic. Fortunately, the restrictions imposed by the UK lockdown ensured the researchers’ parents were safe from sample concoctions. It is known that mimicry plays a role in poisoning in children, 17 and both increased time at home and the ongoing search for a treatment for covid-19 might inspire budding scientists to search for a cure themselves. George’s Marvellous Medicine could be used as a starting point for any future pharmaceutical engineers.

我们的发现表明，乔治的药物远非奇妙，实际上具有令人难以置信的毒性。幸运的是，英国封锁所施加的限制确保研究人员的父母可以安全地免受样本混合物的伤害。众所周知，模仿在儿童中毒中起着作用17，并且这两种情况都增加了他们在家里的时间，并且持续寻找covid-19的治疗方法可能会激发萌芽的科学家自己寻求治疗方法。乔治的奇妙医学可以作为任何未来制药工程师的起点。

The account given of the likely effects of ingesting the medicine was extremely accurate (allowing for some poetic licence). Grandma initially “shot up whoosh into the air” and when she landed she shouted suddenly “My stomach’s on fire!” This immediate effect was likely due to the high level of capsaicin—however, ingredients such as sheep dip (organophosphate), dark tan shoe polish (white spirit, heavy naphtha, trimethylbenzene), and floor polish (heavy naptha) cause mucosal erosion and are likely to have contributed to severe gastric dyspepsia. George’s treatment using half a jug of water is unlikely to have been helpful and might have increased the risk of later aspiration as well as of later cardiogenic shock due to excess preload if grandma had developed myocarditis.

摄入药物可能产生的影响的描述非常准确（允许有诗意的许可）。奶奶最初“向空中飞快地飞奔”，当她着陆时，突然大喊“我的肚子着火了！”立即产生效果的原因可能是辣椒素含量高-但是，羊皮蘸酱（有机磷酸酯），深色棕褐色鞋油（白油，重石脑油，三甲基苯）和地板上光剂（重石脑油）等成分会引起粘膜侵蚀，并且可能导致严重的胃消化不良。如果奶奶患上心肌炎，乔治可能会使用半罐水来治疗，但效果不佳，并且可能会增加因过度预紧而导致后来发生误吸以及后来发生心源性休克的风险。

Subsequently grandma begins to swell, before developing a puncture and deflating. With four ingredients causing foaming, gastrointestinal bloating is to be expected. Puncture, however, was not a recommended treatment in ToxBase.

随后，奶奶开始肿胀，然后刺穿并放气。由于有四种成分会导致起泡，因此预计胃肠道会出现腹胀。但是，在ToxBase中建议不要使用穿刺。

Thereafter “Grandma’s body gave a sudden sharp twist and a sudden sharp jerk and she flipped herself clear out of the chair.” Four of the ingredients (sheep dip, engine oil, antifreeze, and grease) cause convulsions or myoclonic jerks, or both, so again Dahl’s research was accurate. Although it is highly unlikely that grandma would have “landed neatly on her two feet on the carpet,” as described.

此后，“奶奶的身体突然急转弯，突然猛跳，她从椅子上跳了起来。”四种成分（绵羊油，发动机油，防冻剂和油脂）会引起抽搐或肌阵挛性抽搐，或同时引起两者，因此，达尔的研究再次是准确的。如前所述，尽管奶奶极不可能“整齐地落在地毯上的两只脚上”。

From this point the account of the effects of the medicine diverges from reality. Not only does grandma no longer feel any ill effects, but she grows to the size of a house, breaking through the roof. None of the 34 ingredients are documented as having any individual effect on growth. 18

从这一点上，对药物作用的描述与现实不同。奶奶不仅不再感到不适，而且还长到了房子的大小，破了屋顶。没有记录这34种成分对生长有任何影响。 18岁

A more realistic version of the clinical course is considerably more sombre and would need some time to develop. The best case scenario is that immediate, intractable, and vigorous vomiting would occur in time to prevent systemic absorption of a fatal dose of toxic ingredients. However, the highly caustic nature of the medicine would almost certainly have resulted in immediate and severe oesophageal burns, potentially leading to a catastrophic oesophageal-aortic erosion and a lifetime of dysphagic complications.

临床过程的更现实的版本更加阴暗，需要一些时间来开发。最好的情况是，及时，顽固和剧烈的呕吐会及时发生，以防止全身吸收致命剂量的有毒成分。但是，该药物的高腐蚀性本质几乎可以肯定会导致立即和严重的食道灼伤，并可能导致灾难性的食道-主动脉糜烂和终生吞咽困难。

A pessimistic, but more realistic, scenario would be the high likelihood of rapid drowsiness and coma from the excessive amounts of alcohol consumed together with the other central nervous system depressants. The combination of vomiting and reduced conscious level would likely lead to aspiration pneumonitis, if not complete airway obstruction and suffocation.

悲观但更现实的情况是，由于过量饮酒和其他中枢神经系统抑制剂共同导致快速嗜睡和昏迷的可能性很高。呕吐和意识水平降低的结合，如果不完全阻塞气道和窒息，很可能导致吸入性肺炎。

Should grandma have survived this physiological insult, then seizures and respiratory depression would add to the risk of death from hypoxia. Myocarditis and arrhythmias would probably follow, along with gastrointestinal erosions, upper gastrointestinal haemorrhage, and gastrointestinal perforation. If these did not prove fatal, disseminated intravascular coagulopathies and irreversible hepatorenal failure would result. A suggested treatment algorithm has been provided, should a grandma (or any other unfortunate person) be administered George’s marvellous medicine ( fig 2).

如果奶奶幸免于这种生理伤害，则癫痫发作和呼吸抑制将增加缺氧导致死亡的风险。可能会继发心肌炎和心律不齐，以及胃肠道糜烂，上消化道出血和胃肠道穿孔。如果这些没有证明是致命的，将导致弥散性血管内凝血病和不可逆的肝肾衰竭。如果给奶奶（或任何其他不幸的人）服用乔治的奇妙药物，则提供了建议的治疗算法（图2）。

For an inquisitive child such as George, the Kranky’s dusty old shed and garage provided a wealth of ingredients to choose from, as these were where his father, a farmer, stored the most dangerous substances. The shed had the odd assortment of “thick yellowish liquid,” for cows (ivermectin and milbemycin) and sheep dip (organophosphate), and the garage provided engine oil and antifreeze (ethylene glycol), as well as windscreen washer fluid, which typically contains methanol, a toxic alcohol (and occasionally small amounts of ethylene glycol).

对于像乔治这样充满好奇心的孩子来说，Kranky尘土飞扬的旧棚屋和车库提供了丰富的食材供您选择，因为这些都是他父亲（一个农夫）储存最危险物质的地方。棚子里有各种各样的“浓黄色液体”，用于奶牛（伊维菌素和米尔贝霉素）和羊肉（有机磷酸盐），车库提供机油和防冻剂（乙二醇）以及挡风玻璃清洗液，通常含有甲醇，有毒酒精（偶尔还有少量的乙二醇）。

Most of the ingredients found in a typical home cause relatively mild effects (chiefly gastrointestinal). The most dangerous household items found at George’s were “superwhite” washing powder, “waxwell” floor polish, and the “brown shoe polish,” all of which are available in many modern households. Liquid laundry capsules are common sources of childhood toxicity 19; they can be dangerous even without ingestion as bursting a capsule can result in forceful injection into the eye, with major injury to the cornea.

在典型家庭中发现的大多数成分都会产生相对轻微的影响（主要是胃肠道）。乔治（George）发现的最危险的家用物品是“超白”洗衣粉，“蜡井”地板上光剂和“棕色鞋油”，所有现代家庭都可以买到。液体洗衣胶囊是儿童期毒性的常见来源19。即使不食入，它们也可能很危险，因为破裂胶囊会导致强行注入眼内，严重损害角膜。

Because of the extra time spent by children at home during lockdown, and their innate desire to experiment and explore, it is vital that parents are well informed to ensure their children’s safety. Much progress has been made in safety around the home since the publication of George’s Marvellous Medicine in 1981, with child proof packaging, clearer labelling, and bans on the most dangerous products. The Royal Society for the Prevention of Accidents produces a helpful factsheet 20 about the most potentially dangerous items in the home, and, crucially, a checklist 21 for parents to serve as an aide memoire. Importantly, this checklist contains a warning to “NEVER mix cleaning products.” Children have always been fascinated by potions with magical properties, which is consistently represented in children’s literature. 22 The current lockdown generation of children might well be as influenced by the potions of Harry Potter as by George’s marvellous medicine. Some ingredients used in magical potions are readily available in British gardens (eg, foxglove, wormwood, mistletoe, elder) and parents should be extra cautious about their budding pharmacists.

由于孩子们在锁定期间会在家里花费额外的时间，并且天生渴望进行实验和探索，因此，让父母充分了解确保孩子的安全至关重要。自1981年《乔治奇妙医学》出版以来，在儿童安全包装，清晰标签以及禁止使用最危险产品等方面，家庭安全已取得了很大进展。皇家预防事故协会提供了有关家庭中最潜在危险物品的有用的情况介绍20，以及至关重要的是，清单21供父母用作备忘录。重要的是，此清单包含警告：“切勿混用清洁产品”。一直以来，孩子们都对具有神奇特性的魔药着迷，这在孩子们的文学作品中始终如一。 22目前锁定的儿童一代可能受到哈利·波特药水的影响，也受到乔治奇妙药物的影响。魔法药水中使用的某些成分在英国花园中很容易买到（例如毛地黄，艾草，槲寄生，长者），并且父母应特别注意萌芽的药剂师。

Our study has limitations. Firstly, we did not combine the ingredients as described in the book so are unable to comment on potential chemical interactions. Secondly, although the quantities of each ingredient used are in most cases documented, the precise dose taken by grandma is not, leading to some assumptions about the effects. Thirdly, our study is an in vitro extrapolation; we did, however, judge it unlikely that an in vivo study would pass ethical review. Fourthly, we did not assess the effects of heat on the medicine. Although this process is usually used to catalyse chemical reactions, it most likely would have only made the mixture more concentrated. Fifthly, the effects of chanting and dancing (“And suddenly, George found himself dancing around the steaming pot, chanting strange words”), despite notable effects in rainmaking, 23 are unlikely to have materially affected the physical properties and physiological effects of the medicine. Finally, we did not analyse the contents of medicines numbers two, three, and four because the contents are not as explicitly detailed, but they are broadly similar to those of medicine number one, the eponymous concoction.

我们的研究有局限性。首先，我们没有按书中所述结合成分，因此无法评论潜在的化学相互作用。其次，尽管在大多数情况下都记录了每种成分的使用量，但是奶奶所服用的精确剂量却没有，导致人们对该效果有一些假设。第三，我们的研究是体外推断。但是，我们确实判断体内研究不太可能通过伦理审查。第四，我们没有评估热量对药物的影响。尽管此过程通常用于催化化学反应，但很可能只会使混合物更加浓缩。第五，念诵和跳舞的效果（“突然之间，乔治发现自己在蒸锅周围跳舞，念出奇怪的话”），尽管在造雨中有显着效果，但23不太可能对药物的物理特性和生理效果产生实质性影响。最后，我们没有分析二号，三号和四号药物的含量，因为其内容没有明确说明，但与一号药同名混合剂大致相似。

When George attempted to reassure grandma that the medicine will do her good, she prophetically exclaims “Doing me good? It’s killing me!” Experienced healthcare professionals know to take such proclamations seriously.

当乔治试图向奶奶保证该药对她有益时，她预言道：“对我有益吗？这太痛苦了！”经验丰富的医疗保健专业人员知道认真对待此类声明。

Although we applaud Dahl’s initial accuracy about the toxicological effects of the ingredients in George’s marvellous medicine, the overall outcome for grandma would be fatal catastrophic physiological collapse. It is unlikely that children will recreate each step in the making of a marvellous-type medicine, but it is worth being cautious as some of the household ingredients used by George are considerably dangerous and commonly cause severe morbidity in children. Although parents might encourage scientific exploration and experimentation in their children, it would be wise to check any medicinal ingredients for potential toxicity before use.

尽管我们赞扬达尔（Dahl）对于乔治奇妙的药物中的成分的毒理学作用的最初准确性，但祖母的总体结果将是致命的灾难性生理崩溃。儿童不太可能会重造奇妙类型药物的每一步，但值得谨慎，因为乔治使用的某些家庭成分相当危险，通常会导致儿童严重发病。尽管父母可能会鼓励孩子进行科学探索和实验，但明智的做法是在使用前检查任何药用成分是否具有潜在的毒性。

In George’s Marvellous Medicine, a hugely popular children’s book, grandma experiences sudden and major growth and invigoration after being administered the eponymous medicine

在乔治的奇妙医学（一本非常受欢迎的儿童读物）中，祖母服用同名药物后经历了突然的重大成长和振奋

Most unintentional poisonings occur at home, and coupled with children’s desire for experimentation and mimicry, the risk of attempted reproduction of George’s marvellous medicine has increased

大多数无意中毒发生在家里，再加上孩子对实验和模仿的渴望，试图复制乔治奇妙药物的风险增加了

Far from experiencing major growth and invigoration, grandmas and other equally unfortunate recipients of the medicine would be at high risk of death

奶奶和其他不幸的接受者远远没有经历重大的增长和振奋，他们将有很高的死亡风险。

It would be wise for parents of budding pharmacists to remain vigilant, particularly during lockdown

对于萌芽的药剂师的父母保持警惕是明智的，尤其是在锁定期间
