History of Programming Languages
编程语言的历史

2021-05-06 01:13:49

In this seminar course, we will study themes in the programminglanguage research area from a historical perspective.

在本次研讨会课程中，我们将从历史角度研究编程语言研究区的主题。

This idea of "several steps" is distinct from the modern idea ofpublishing every single thought that occurred to someone over breakfast so thatthe dean can get a high count for her yearly report.

这个＆＃34的想法;几个步骤＆＃34;与早餐过度发生的每一个想法的现代观念不同，所以院长可以为她的年度报告获得高点。

The primary goal is to understand some of the major themes in thediscipline as it exists today and how these themes evolved in severalsteps. Initially, the seminar will focus on two themes close to theinstructor’s heart: how semantics emerged and how languages evolved that do not adhere to the common idea of a fixed syntax, scope, andsemantics. The choice of other themes will entirely depend on students’interests and preferences. All programming language themes arewelcome.

主要目标是了解TheDicline中的一些主要主题，因为它存在于今天存在，以及这些主题如何在几个部门演变。最初，研讨会将专注于靠近TheInstructor的心脏的两个主题：语义如何出现以及语言如何发展，不遵守固定语法，范围，和中文的共同思想。其他主题的选择将完全取决于学生的兴趣和偏好。所有编程语言主题都是令人讨厌的。

The secondary goal is to develop basic skills for understanding anddescribing research themes. Every student will learn to approach a theme byreading a series of papers, preparing an annotated bibliography, andpresenting the key steps in the evolution of the theme.

二级目标是制定理解和识别研究主题的基本技能。每个学生都会学会接近一个主题，否则一系列论文，准备注释的参考书目，并提出了主题演变中的关键步骤。

The intended audience consists of PhD students who will write adissertation in the area and MS/undergraduate students who wish to deepentheir understanding of the programming language area. PhD students at allstages of research will benefit. Someone close to the writing stage will havea chance to develop the bibliography for the chapter on background work;students at an early stage can use this opportunity to find, refine, orreject an idea for dissertation research.

目标受众由博士学位学生组成，博士学位将在该地区和女士/本科学生编写崇拜的学生，他们希望深入了解编程语言地区。研究的博士学位学生将受益。靠近写作阶段的人将有机会开发关于背景工作章节的参考书目;早期的学生可以利用这个机会找到，优化，或者对论文研究的想法。

Prerequisites Ideally, a PhD student should have taken IntensivePrinciples of Programming Languages. An undergraduate or an MS student shouldhave taken Principles of Programming Languages and studied some additionaltopics of interest outside of courses.

先决条件理想情况下，博士生应该已经采取了重症编程语言。本科或女士学生应该讨论编程语言的原则，并研究了课程之外的一些辅助障碍。
