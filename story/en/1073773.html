The ZAPCon 2022 schedule is now live
ZAPCon 2022的时间表现在已经上线

2022-02-17 09:20:09

I am excited to share that we’ve just released the speaker lineup and schedule for the ZAPCon 2022!ZAPCon takes place on March 8-9, with one day of talks and one day of incredible workshops.

我很高兴与大家分享，我们刚刚发布了ZAPCon 2022的演讲者阵容和时间表！ZAPCon将于3月8日至9日举行，为期一天的讲座和一天令人难以置信的研讨会。

On March 8, ZAPCon will bring you a full day of talks from security and ZAP industry leaders including my friend Jim Manico, the CEO and Application Security Educator at Manicode Security. The schedule is packed with topics from “Out-of-band Application Security Testing with ZAP” to “Drive-By Pentesting with ZAP Scripts” from 9am until 5pm ET.

3月8日，ZAPCon将为您带来一整天的安全和ZAP行业领袖的演讲，其中包括我的朋友Jim Manico，Manicode security的首席执行官和应用程序安全教育家。从东部时间上午9点到下午5点，日程安排中包含了从“使用ZAP进行带外应用程序安全测试”到“使用ZAP脚本进行驾车测试”等主题。

On March 9, stay tuned for exclusive workshops that you can only find at ZAPCon. Hear about the latest in API Security Testing from industry experts, get a crash course in “Automated Security Testing with GitHub Actions” from a Senior DevOps Engineer at StackHawk, and watch my own presentation on “How to Contribute to ZAP”.

3月9日，请继续关注您只能在ZAPCon找到的独家研讨会。从行业专家那里了解最新的API安全测试，从StackHawk的一位高级DevOps工程师那里获得“使用GitHub操作的自动安全测试”速成课程，并观看我自己关于“如何为ZAP做出贡献”的演示。

In a different timezone? No problem – we won’t let you miss out on all of these special presentations. All of the ZAPCon talks and workshops will be recorded, so you can watch them after the fact at your own pace as long as you’re registered for ZAPCon.

在不同的时区？没问题——我们不会让你错过所有这些特别演示。所有的ZAPCon讲座和研讨会都会被录制下来，所以只要你注册了ZAPCon，你就可以在事后以自己的速度观看它们。

To tee-up ZAPCon, the OWASP Foundation will be hosting a variety of training workshops on March 7.Stop by to warm up your AppSec skills before the main event begins.

为了举办ZAPCON，OWASP基金会将于3月7日举办各种培训研讨会。在主要活动开始前，顺便来热身一下你的AppSec技能。
