Buying a Tesla car in Bitcoins cancels 4 times the CO2 savings for its lifetime
用比特币购买特斯拉汽车可在其一生中抵消4倍的二氧化碳减排量

2021-02-17 19:41:56

This is how insanely bad Bitcoin is for the environment : If you buy a Tesla car (around 40 000 $) in Bitcoins, the impact of the mining of those bitcoins is around 80 tons of CO2. This is 4 times more than the saving of CO2 you can expect from this car (compared to a gasoline one) on its entire lifetime (around -20 tons).

这就是比特币对环境的疯狂坏处：如果您购买一辆比特币的特斯拉汽车（约4万美元），这些比特币的开采会产生约80吨的二氧化碳。这是该车在整个使用寿命（约-20吨）中所能节省的二氧化碳的四倍（与汽油相比）。

Of course, the Bitcoins you use her e will eventually be used for other transactions. But still, it gives a good point of comparison : Suppose you want to buy this car with gold. It would take 3 to 5 less energy to mine this gold than to mine the same value in Bitcoins.

当然，您使用其e的比特币最终将用于其他交易。但是，它仍然提供了一个很好的比较点：假设您想用黄金购买这辆车。与开采相同价值的比特币相比，开采这种黄金所需的能源要少3至5倍。

Also, by investing it Bitcoins and making it’s value increase by 10%, Elon Musk / Tesla rise the electric consumption of the system. The increase of carbon impact corresponds to 800 000 gasoline cars added in the world … as long as this increase stands.

另外，通过投资比特币并使其价值增加10％，埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）/特斯拉（Tesla）会增加系统的电力消耗。碳排放量的增加相当于在世界范围内增加了80万辆汽油汽车……只要这种增加得以维持。

This shows that Tesla / Elon Musk couldn’t care less about ecology. It’s all about business.

这表明特斯拉/马斯克（Elon Musk）不太关心生态。一切都与业务有关。

We need stronger states capable of imposing strong environmental and social rules to limit the megalomaniac ravings of these companies and their leaders. Bitcoin is going exactly in the opposite direction, accelerating the liberal dream of a stateless society and total commodification of the world.

我们需要更强大的国家，这些国家能够施加强大的环境和社会规则，以限制这些公司及其领导人的狂妄言行。比特币正朝相反的方向发展，加速了无国籍社会的自由梦想和世界的全面商品化。

In 2020, the carbon impact for mining bitcoins is 36 millions of tons of CO2, for 18 billions $ of revenues for miners. Hence, 40 000 $ value of bitcoins represents 80 tons of CO2.

2020年，采矿比特币的碳影响量为3600万吨二氧化碳，为采矿者创造了180亿美元的收入。因此，价值4万美元的比特币代表80吨二氧化碳。
