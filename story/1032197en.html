WhatsApp现在每天大约发送1000亿条消息

2020-10-30 08:22:53

For some perspective, users exchanged 100 billion messages on WhatsApp on last New Year’s Eve. That is the day when WhatsApp tops its engagement figures, and as many of you may remember, also the time when the service suffered glitches in the past years. (No outage on last New Year’s Eve!)

从某种角度来看，去年除夕，用户在WhatsApp上交换了1000亿条消息。这一天，WhatsApp的订阅量达到了顶峰，正如你们中的许多人可能还记得的那样，这也是该服务在过去几年遭遇故障的时候。(去年除夕没有停电！)。

At this point, WhatsApp is just competing with itself. Facebook Messenger was being used to exchange 8 billion messages a day as of May 2018. Apple chief executive Tim Cook said in May that iMessage and FaceTime were seeing record usage, but did not share specific figures. The last time Apple did share the figure, it was far behind WhatsApp’s then usage (podcast).

在这一点上，WhatsApp只是在与自己竞争。截至2018年5月，Facebook Messenger每天交换80亿条消息。苹果首席执行官蒂姆·库克(Tim Cook)在5月份表示，iMessage和FaceTime的使用量创下纪录，但没有透露具体数字。苹果上一次分享这一数据时，远远落后于WhatsApp当时的使用量(播客)。

In early 2014, WhatsApp was being used to exchange about 50 billion texts a day, its then chief executive Jan Koum revealed at an event.

WhatsApp时任首席执行官简·库姆(Jan Koum)在一次活动上透露，2014年初，WhatsApp每天被用来交换约500亿条短信。

At the time, WhatsApp had fewer than 500 million users. WhatsApp now has more than 2 billion users and at least in India, its largest market by users, its popularity surpasses those of every other smartphone app including the big blue app.

当时，WhatsApp的用户不到5亿。WhatsApp现在拥有超过20亿用户，至少在其最大的用户市场印度，它的受欢迎程度超过了包括这款大型蓝色应用在内的所有其他智能手机应用。

“This year we’ve all relied on messaging more than ever to keep up with our loved ones and get business done,” tweeted Will Cathcart, head of WhatsApp.

WhatsApp负责人威尔·卡斯卡特(Will Cathcart)在推特上写道：“今年，我们比以往任何时候都更依赖短信与亲人保持联系，完成业务。”

No word today on when WhatsApp plans to resume its payments service in Brazil, and when it plans to launch its payments service in India, where it began testing the service in 2018.

WhatsApp计划何时在巴西恢复支付服务，以及计划何时在印度推出支付服务，今天没有消息。WhatsApp于2018年开始在印度测试这项服务。

“We are proud that WhatsApp is able to deliver roughly 100B messages every day and we’re excited about the road ahead,” said Cathcart.

卡斯卡特说：“WhatsApp每天能够发送大约1000亿条消息，我们为此感到自豪，我们对未来的道路感到兴奋。”
