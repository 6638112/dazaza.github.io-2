1Kosmos, which provides biometrics-based digital identity proofing and passwordless authentication through a blockchain, exits stealth and raises $15M Series A
1Kosmos通过区块链提供基于生物识别的数字身份证明和无密码身份验证，退出隐形并筹集了1500万美元的A轮融资

2021-02-21 08:05:45

1Kosmos, a Somerset, N.J.-based cybersecurity solution to provide digital identity proofing and passwordless authentication, raised $15m in Series A funding.

1Kosmos是一家位于新泽西州萨默塞特的网络安全解决方案，可提供数字身份证明和无密码身份验证，已获得1500万美元的A轮融资。

Led by CEO Hemen Vimadalal, CTO Rohan Pinto and CSO Mike Engle, 1Kosmos comes out of stealth mode to offer a security platform that brings high identity and authentication assurance levels through advanced biometrics and a private blockchain ecosystem. Its family of products include BlockID Verify, BlockID Workforce and BlockID Customer.

在首席执行官Hemen Vimadalal，CTO Rohan Pinto和CSO Mike Engle的带领下，1Kosmos脱离了隐身模式，提供了一个安全平台，该平台通过高级生物识别技术和私有区块链生态系统带来高身份和认证保证级别。其产品系列包括BlockID验证，BlockID劳动力和BlockID客户。
