As of December 2, Disney had 137.1M direct-to-consumers subscribers, with Disney+ having 86.8M subscribers, Hulu 38.8M, and 11.5M for ESPN+
截至12月2日，迪士尼的直接用户为1.371亿，迪士尼+的用户为8680万，Hulu为3880万，ESPN +为1150万

2020-12-11 07:01:20

Plus reached a new milestone, just over a year after its debut, hitting 86.8 million paid subscribers worldwide as of Dec. 2. That’s within shouting distance of the high end of the 90 million customers Disney originally expected to land — in year four of the streaming service’s life.

Plus首次亮相仅一年多，就达到了一个新的里程碑，截至12月2日，全球付费用户达到了8680万。与迪士尼最初预期的9000万客户的高端市场相距不远-在第四年流服务的生活。

The new sub count is up 13.1 million from the last reported number of 73.7 million paid subscribers globally for as of Oct. 3. The service has become an even more critical pillar for Disney, especially as the COVID crisis has depressed box office and theme park revenue.

截至10月3日，新订阅人数比上次报告的全球7370万付费用户增加了1310万。该服务已成为迪士尼的更为重要的支柱，尤其是在COVID危机使票房和主题公园不景气的情况下收入。

Disney CEO Bob Chapek made the announcement at the outset of its 2020 investor day event Thursday, saying the explosion of Disney Plus has exceeded the company’s “wildest expectations.” During the presentation, execs also announced that a huge swath of new originals will be coming to Disney Plus under the Star Wars, Marvel, Disney Animation and Pixar brands over the next few years. Going forward, the goal is to have new originals premiere on Disney Plus every week.

迪士尼首席执行​​官鲍勃·查佩克（Bob Chapek）在周四举行的2020年投资者日活动开始时宣布了这一消息，称迪士尼Plus的爆炸超出了公司的“最疯狂的期望”。在演讲中，高管们还宣布，在未来几年内，将以星球大战，漫威，迪士尼动画和皮克斯等品牌为迪士尼Plus带来大量新原件。展望未来，我们的目标是每周在Disney Plus上首映新作品。

As of Dec. 2, Disney overall had 137.1 million direct-to-consumer subscribers, which includes 38.8 million for Hulu (up from 36.6 million two months ago) and 11.5 million for ESPN Plus (vs. 10.3 million as of Oct. 3).

截止到12月2日，迪士尼的直接用户总数为1.371亿，其中Hulu的用户为3880万（两个月前为3660万），ESPN Plus的用户为1150万（截至10月3日为1030万）。 。

Of the 86.8 million Disney Plus subs, about 30% (or 24 million) are via Disney Plus Hotstar in India, according to Rebecca Campbell, chairman of Disney’s International Operations and Direct-to-Consumer segment. Last month Disney Plus launched in Latin America, and in 2021 the company expects to expand in 2021 into Eastern Europe, Korea and other countries.

迪士尼国际运营和直接消费业务部门主席丽贝卡·坎贝尔（Rebecca Campbell）表示，在8680万个Disney Plus订阅中，约有30％（即2400万个）是通过印度的Disney Plus Hotstar进行的。迪士尼Plus上个月在拉丁美洲推出，该公司预计到2021年将在2021年扩展到东欧，韩国和其他国家。

In the first quarter of 2021, Disney Plus and ESPN Plus will be available to Comcast’s Xfinity X1 and Flex more than 20 million subscribers directly on their set-tops, joining the operator’s existing distribution deal for Hulu, Disney Streaming Services president Michael Paull announced.

迪士尼流媒体服务总裁Michael Paull宣布，到2021年第一季度，Comcast的Xfinity X1和Flex可以直接在其机顶盒上使用Disney Plus和ESPN Plus，超过2000万订户，加入运营商现有的Hulu分销协议。

Separately, Disney is bowing a new bundle with Disney Plus, ESPN Plus and the ad-free version of Hulu for $18.99/month (vs. $12.99/month for a bundle with Hulu’s ad-supported tier). In addition, ESPN Plus content and subscriptions will be available directly through the Hulu app next year.

另外，迪士尼将与迪士尼Plus，ESPN Plus和Hulu的无广告版本捆绑销售，价格为每月18.99美元（相比之下，与Hulu的广告支持级别捆绑销售价格为每月12.99美元）。此外，明年将通过Hulu应用直接提供ESPN Plus内容和订阅。

Flash back to Disney’s investor day in April 2019: The Mouse House had projected 60 million-90 million subscribers for Disney Plus by the end of its fiscal year 2024 (which ends around Sept. 30). Having already neared the high end of that previous guidance, Disney execs are expected to provide new forecasts for Disney Plus at the investor event.

回顾2019年4月迪斯尼的投资者日：Mouse House预计到2024财年末（9月30日左右），迪士尼Plus的订户将达到6000万至9000万。迪斯尼高管们已经接近先前指导的高端，因此有望在投资者盛会上为迪斯尼Plus提供新的预测。

The media conglomerate first launched Disney Plus in November 2019. The service quickly gained traction, signing up 10 million by the day after launch and zooming to 50 million subs in its first five months. The service offers a rich library of Disney, Pixar, Marvel and Star Wars movies as well as breakout original series “The Mandalorian” and other exclusive TV shows and movies including “Hamilton” and the live-action “Mulan” (which it sold in a 90-day early-access window for $30 a pop).

媒体集团于2019年11月首次推出了迪士尼Plus。该服务迅速受到关注，在发布后的第二天就签约了1000万，并在头五个月扩大到5000万订阅用户。该服务提供了丰富的迪士尼，皮克斯，漫威和星球大战电影资料库，以及突破性的原创剧集《曼达洛人》以及其他独家电视节目和电影，包括《汉密尔顿》和真人版《花木兰》（在90天的抢先体验窗口，每次弹出$ 30）。

Besides coming to market with one of the world’s most powerful entertainment brands, a key part of Disney Plus’ appeal is its relatively low price point of $6.99/month (or $69.99/year), which is less than competing services like Netflix or HBO Max. That’s led analysts to speculate that the company will eventually seek to hike the price of the SVOD package.

除了以全球最强大的娱乐品牌之一进入市场之外，迪士尼Plus的吸引力的一个关键部分是其相对较低的价格点，即每月6.99美元（或每年69.99美元），这比Netflix或HBO Max等竞争性服务要低。 。这导致分析师推测该公司最终将寻求提高SVOD软件包的价格。

Prior to Disney’s investor day, some Wall Street analysts had upped their forecasts for Disney Plus. This week Morgan Stanley revised estimates for Disney Plus to end FY 2024 with 135 million customers and FY ’25 with 145 million — generating revenue of nearly $11 billion in FY25.

在迪士尼投资者日之前，一些华尔街分析师提高了对迪士尼Plus的预测。摩根士丹利（Morgan Stanley）上周修改了对迪士尼Plus的估计，到2024财年将有1.35亿客户，到25财年将有1.45亿客户，25财年的收入接近110亿美元。
