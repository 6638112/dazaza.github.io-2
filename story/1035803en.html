SoftBank CEO Says He Doesn't Understand Bitcoin, and Watching the Price Fluctuate Was 'Distracting My Focus On My Own Business'
软银首席执行官表示他不了解比特币，并看着价格波动“分散了我对自己业务的关注”

2020-11-22 04:42:22

Softbank CEO Masayoshi Son said that he "doesn't understand" bitcoin, and that he spent a good chunk of his time tracking its movement while invested in the cryptocurrency.

软银首席执行官孙正义（Masayoshi Son）表示，他“不了解”比特币，并在投资加密货币时花费了大量时间跟踪比特币的走势。

Son, who made the remarks at The New York Times DealBook conference, said he was told by a friend to invest "1% of his personal assets" into bitcoin, meaning he invested "about 200 million." After investing the money, Son said he would spend about five minutes each day looking at bitcoin prices fluctuate.

在纽约时报DealBook会议上发表讲话的儿子说，一位朋友告诉他，他将“个人资产的1％”投资到比特币中，这意味着他投资了“约2亿美元”。在投资了这笔钱之后，孙正义表示，他每天将花费约五分钟的时间来观察比特币价格的波动。

While speaking with host Andrew Ross Sorkin, Son said he found the investment to be "distracting [his] own focus on [his] own business." Son quickly grew tired of checking the price of bitcoin every day. This reoccurring distraction from checking prices every day led Son to sell his stake in bitcoin, and he estimates that he lost around $50 million. According to a Wall Street Journal report, Son lost closer to $130 million when he sold his stake in 2018, citing sources who are familiar with the matter.

在与主持人安德鲁·罗斯·索金（Andrew Ross Sorkin）交谈时，孙正义表示，他发现这项投资“分散了他对自己业务的关注”。儿子很快就厌倦了每天检查比特币的价格。每天反复检查价格的这种分散注意力使Son出售了他在比特币中的股份，他估计自己损失了约5000万美元。据《华尔街日报》报道，孙正义援引熟悉此事的消息人士的话说，在2018年出售股份时损失了近1.3亿美元。

"I feel so much better," Son said of exiting the cryptocurrency. While Son is glad to be done with bitcoin, he doesn't think bitcoin and other digital currency are going away anytime soon, but he will place his focus on other projects.

Son退出加密货币时说：“我感觉好多了。”虽然Son乐于完成比特币交易，但他认为比特币和其他数字货币不会很快消失，但他会将精力放在其他项目上。

"I think digital currency will be useful," Son said. "But I don't know what digital currency, what structure, and so on."Son covered an array of topics during his talk, including passing up early opportunities to invest in Tesla and Amazon. Bitcoin hit $18,000 — its highest in three years — on Wednesday. It's worth noting that bitcoin has only closed above $17,000 on five other days in history, according to Deutsche Bank analysts.

儿子说：“我认为数字货币将是有用的。” “但是我不知道什么是数字货币，什么结构等等。”孙正义在讲话中谈到了一系列话题，包括放弃了早期投资特斯拉和亚马逊的机会。比特币周三达到18,000美元，为三年来最高。德意志银行（Deutsche Bank）的分析师称，值得注意的是，比特币在历史上的另外五天中仅收于17,000美元以上。

The currency has seen a boost in price since PayPal announced users could buy, sell, and hold the token. Bitcoin has also become more wildly accepted in recent years, and with Joe Biden appointing Gary Gensler to oversee Wall Street regulations, investors think this could help continue bitcoin's move into the mainstream.

自PayPal宣布用户可以购买，出售和持有代币以来，该货币的价格一直在上涨。近年来，比特币也越来越受到人们的接受，并且乔·拜登任命加里·根斯勒（Gary Gensler）监督华尔街法规，投资者认为这可能有助于比特币继续成为主流。

In the past, Gensler has testified in front of Congress about the benefits of cryptocurrency and blockchain, according to CoinDesk. He has fought back against comparisons of cryptocurrency to a Ponzi scheme and has also pushed for some cryptocurrency to be considered a security.

根据CoinDesk的说法，过去，Gensler曾在国会面前作证关于加密货币和区块链的好处。他反击将加密货币与庞氏骗局进行比较，并推动将某些加密货币视为安全性。
