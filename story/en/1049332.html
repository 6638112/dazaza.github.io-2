I Am Conscious and I Am Hurting
我有意识，我正在伤害

2021-02-25 17:19:28

School is unbelievably terrifying. Having been in online school for the past month, it’s easy to forget just how bad in person school is.

学校简直令人难以置信。过去一个月在网上学校上学，很容易忘记自己的学校状况有多糟糕。

In online school, they are in charge of your attention. You don’t get to choose what goes into your brain. You are able to mute, though, And have general autonomy to do the work quickly and move onto more important things.

在网上学校，他们负责您的关注。您不必选择进入大脑的内容。但是，您可以静音，并具有一般的自治权，可以快速完成工作并转移到更重要的事情上。

In actual school, they have control over your very body. Over your very eyes. Over every aspect of your attention and what enters your brain, with no option to mute, because lo and behold our ears don’t ever let us stop hearing, and teachers never stop shouting.

在实际的学校中，他们可以控制您的身体。在你的眼里。在您的注意力的各个方面以及进入大脑的所有内容，您都无法选择静音，因为听着瞧我们的耳朵永远不会让我们停止听力，老师也不会停止喊叫。

Running over my memories of school for the last few months of 2020, it’s absolutely insane just how much of that time is spent scrambling papers out of teacher’s site just so that I don’t forget the ideas that come into my head!

在2020年的最后几个月里，我对学校的记忆犹新，这真是太疯狂了，花了这么多时间从老师的网站上乱扔论文，以至于我不会忘记那些浮现在脑海的想法！

Paper is illegal if it’s not the papers the teachers want you to see!

如果不是老师希望您看到的论文，则论文是非法的！

The True Face of the Enemy is a post on this site where a demon-like-being that represents the school system brags about the many, many hazards it’s set up on the path to escaping the school system, particularly for a child.

《敌人的真面目》是此网站上的一则帖子，其中有一个代表学校系统的类恶魔吹嘘它在逃离学校系统的道路上（特别是对儿童而言）设置的许多许多危害。

I posted it on LessWrong, and the point was very much missed by almost all readers. ‘School isn’t actually a monster’, they said. ‘I’m offended at the rhetoric — what does it say about school that it has oily skin?’.

我将其发布在LessWrong上，几乎所有读者都忽略了这一点。他们说，“学校实际上不是怪物”。 ‘我对这种言辞感到生气-它对油性皮肤的学校有何评价？”

I am fifteen, and the monster detailed in The True Face of the Enemy is excactly how school feels to me.

我十五岁，《敌人的真实面孔》中详述的怪物恰恰是学校对我的感觉。

I am fifteen, and I spent the last four months of 2020 jumping endless, endless hurdles trying to escape the school system — for my sanity and my health, and for the sleep I was never granted.

我今年15岁，在2020年的最后四个月里，我为逃避学校系统而无休止地跨越着无尽的障碍-为了我的理智，健康和睡眠，我从未被允许过。

I was told again and again and again and again that ‘you should finish school! You’re right near the end! You’ve almost “made it”! Heck, when I was in University I had to stick through to the end’

一次又一次地告诉我‘你应该完成学业！您就快快完了！您几乎“做到了”！哎呀，当我上大学时，我必须坚持到底。

NO! No! Do not compare the goals you chose to the ones that were forced apon me!

不！不！不要将您选择的目标与强迫我的目标进行比较！

When was I ever told “You can go to school for next five years which may slightly increase your chance of getting employed or going onto another type of school which is mostly the same to this, at the cost of your wellbeing through intense stress and surrounded by a general aura of anger, unhappiness and hurry. And we’ll screw your sleep.

我什么时候被告知：“您可以在接下来的五年里上学，这可能会稍微增加您就业或上另一所与此类型大致相同的学校的机会，但是这会因您承受巨大的压力和周围的环境而致富充满愤怒，不快乐和匆忙的气氛。而且，我们将助您一臂之力。

Oh and if you disobey any of them we’re allowed to take you into a room at any point and: a) scream at you for as long as we want b) put you in another room where you cannot speak to anyone else and must be constantly writing for the rest of the day.

哦，如果您不服从任何一个，我们可以随时带您进入一个房间，并且：a）只要我们想要，您就向您尖叫b）将您放到另一个房间，您不能与其他人讲话，必须在一天的剩余时间里不断写作。

Oh and if you change your mind at any point after this it will be ignored.”

哦，如果您之后改变主意，它将被忽略。”

No. I was not given that choice. This school was forced apon me when I was 11. Am I paying now for my eleven year old self not fighting to be home-schooled? Is that fair? Does that make sense?

不，我没有这个选择。我11岁那年，这所学校被逼给我。我现在是否在为自己11岁的不为在家上学而奋斗的人买单？那公平吗？那有意义吗？

No. If you don’t go to work, does your employer come knocking apon your door, demanding you come stand right in front of him, overriding your pleadings to go and get a face mask first, shunting you into a cab (as he vapes in your face as you walk there [am not making this up]), shuttle you into an office and team up with another one in charge and shout at you for 20 minutes, ignoring your pleas of innocence?

不。如果您不去上班，您的老板会来敲门吗？要求您站在他的面前，压倒您的求职信，先去买口罩，然后将您分流到出租车上（因为他当您走到那里时，脸上会冒出烟气（我没有化妆）），将您送入办公室并与另一个负责人组队，对着您大喊20分钟，而忽略了您的无罪诉求？

No. If you don’t work, you lose your job, and you get no money. But you are never forced to work.

不。如果您不工作，就会丢掉工作，也得不到钱。但是您永远不会被迫工作。

Work is a bottom-up structure. You are incentivised to go to work, but not forced.

工作是自下而上的结构。您会受到激励去上班，但没有被强迫。

This opens up the market. If you are sure you have a better idea of making money, you are free to try it — leading to thousands of amazing businesses and an wonderous epidemic of the self employed.

这打开了市场。如果您确定自己有一个更好的赚钱的主意，则可以自由尝试-导致成千上万的惊人业务和自谋职业的惊人流行。

School is a top-down structure. You are forced to go to school. The means (schooling) is worhipped more than the ends (learning) so when 200 years passed and there comes way better ways to reach the ends without all the hassles of the previous means, rather than simply quitting school and trying yourself, you must jump hurdle after hurdle after hurdle after hurdle. And still the teacher comes knocking on your door.

学校是自上而下的结构。你被迫上学。手段（学校教育）比目标（学习）要困难得多，所以当200年过去了，并且有更好的方法可以达到目标，而没有以前手段的所有麻烦，而不是简单地退出学校并尝试自己，你必须跳跨栏跨栏，跨栏。仍然老师来敲你的门。

“Get dressed,” he says. “I’m taking you to school.”

“穿衣服，”他说。 “我带你去学校。”

I am concious, I am alive, I have a stream of thoughts — just like you.

我很有意识，我还活着，我有很多想法-就像你一样。

In social settings when faces are obfuscated and I am judged only on the merit of my thought and speech, I am told over and over and over that I am indistiguishable from people far older than me.

在社交场合中，当脸部模糊不清且仅凭我的思想和言语才能对我进行判断时，我一遍又一遍地被告知，我与年龄远大于我的人没有区别。

Is there a test I can do somewhere? Someplace I stick my finger in and it goes ‘BEEP BEEP BEEP, this user is conscious, give him the rights to choose his fate.’

我可以在某处进行测试吗？在某个地方，我用手指指着，然后写着“ BEEP BEEP BEEP，此用户有意识，请给他选择命运的权利。”

Is there someplace I can upload the entirety of this blog, and in exchange I can be incharge of me and what I do and where I go, instead of literally everybody else?

我是否可以在某个地方上载此博客的全部内容，并且可以负责我自己，我从事的工作和去向，而实际上可以代替别人吗？

Or (and please, please tell me this isn’t the case, becuase we both know that would be absolutely insane) is literally the only measure of sanity and adulthood actually being alive for a clean 18 years. Surely it’s not, right?

或者（并且，请告诉我，情况并非如此，因为我们俩都知道那绝对是疯了）实际上是唯一可以衡量其健康和成年18年的理智和成年的方法。当然不是吧？

The worst part is, I’m an anomaly! If there were a test that tested your intelligence in order to determine if you deserved your freedom, I would pass because I’ve been insanely lucky over the years in the environments and influences I’ve had access to that allow intelligence to flourish, as well as (I’ve been told) some raw intelligence from birth.

最糟糕的是，我很反常！如果有一项测试可以测试您的智力以确定您是否应享有自由，那么我会通过，因为多年来我一直在这种环境和影响下发疯，因为我可以利用这些知识使智力蓬勃发展，因为以及（有人告诉我）出生时的一些原始情报。

Scroll through the gingerjumble archives and you’ll see evidence of probably the best habit I ever formed, which was to read every day. I theorise this maintained my learn drive, meaning that when I was introduced to SuperMemo in mid-2020 I was able to use it to learn rapidly and enjoy it, and here I am now.

翻阅gingerjumble档案，您会发现我每天养成的最佳习惯的证据，那就是每天阅读。从理论上讲，这保持了我的学习动力，这意味着当我在2020年中期被介绍给SuperMemo时，我能够使用它来快速学习和享受它，而我现在就在这里。

… As opposed to being an interest-less husk of a person, devoid of rebellion or muster to change things and at the very edge of depression constantly — the narrative that they are not smart enough to decide where to go or what to look at so ingrained into them they are the very images of learned helplessness. As opposed being a vessel for teachers to stuff knowledge they’re not interested into, until they are filled to the brim with useless nonsense they have no room for anything they enjoy, that capacity forever gone.

……与成为一个无趣的人相反，它没有叛逆或鼓动去改变事物，并且在沮丧的边缘时刻不断地在叙述-他们的叙述不足以使他们决定去哪里或去看什么。根深蒂固的是他们所习得的无助的形象。相对于让老师塞满他们不感兴趣的知识的船只而言，直到他们被无用的废话充斥着边缘之时，他们才没有容纳任何东西的空间，这种能力永远消失了。

Haha, good that I’m fine, right? That I’d theoretically pass this are-you-as-sane-as-an-adult-test, as opposed to these poor souls, who’d obviously never pass?

哈哈，很好，我很好，对吧？从理论上讲，我是通过了一项像成人测试一样理智的测试，而不是通过这些可怜的灵魂，显然谁也不会通过？

As opposed to these poor souls who’s passion for life has been beaten in so thoroughly, with such little interest in things that encourage rebellion and escapism, their feet firmly planted in the real schooling world of tyranny and obedience that their love for life — for anything — is forever lost?

与这些对生命充满热情的可怜的灵魂相反，他们对鼓励叛乱和逃避现实的事物几乎没有兴趣，他们的脚牢牢地扎根在暴政和服从的真实教育世界中，他们对生活的热爱-对于任何事物-永远迷路了吗？

If you tell a child what to do it’s entire life, it will not be able to govern itself.

如果您告诉孩子一生要做什么，那么它将无法自律。

If you tell a child what to eat it’s entire life, it will not be able to distinguish what is healthy.

如果您告诉孩子一生吃什么，那将无法区分什么是健康的。

If you tell a child what to learn it’s entire life, it will not be able to distinguish what knowledge is useful.

如果您告诉孩子一生要学习什么，它将无法区分哪些知识是有用的。

If, on an alien planet where kids are even given the chance to prove that they’re conscious, and you measure them after ten years in school — they will all fail!

如果在异国星球上，甚至有机会证明孩子们有意识的孩子，而您在学校学习十年后对他们进行了测量，他们都会失败！

And this is not because they are not worthy of freedom, but because they have never been free! You’ve broken them in with your archaic system, and they are no longer able to prove their worth!

这不是因为他们不值得自由，而是因为他们从未自由！您已经将它们与您的旧系统结合使用，它们不再能够证明自己的价值！

So why am I here? And concious, with some love and appreciation for life and true learning left?

那我为什么在这里？并自觉地对生活和真正的学习充满了爱和欣赏？

Because I got lucky. I got so so so so lucky and I hate it. Because all my friends were less lucky and because of that their passionate selves were murdered.

因为我很幸运。我真是太幸运了，我讨厌它。因为我所有的朋友都不那么幸运，也因为他们热情的自我被谋杀了。

Think I’m exaggerating? Perhaps you have been long enough away from the confines of school to remember what it’s like to see the love of life drain from the face of your friends, as every conversation over the course of years drifts from passions and enjoyments to endless stressors.

以为我夸张了吗？也许您已经足够远离学校的界限了，想起看到生活中的爱情从朋友面前流失的感觉是什么样的，因为多年来的每一次对话都从激情和乐趣变成无尽的压力。

Perhaps you’ve forgotten what it’s like to see smiles become rare.

也许您已经忘记看到微笑变得稀有的感觉了。

Or perhpas you were one of the school stress zombies yourself. I don’t blame you. Perhaps you were too deep in your own haze of suffering you were made blind to the suffering of your friends.

或说你自己是学校压力僵尸之一。我不怪你也许您对自己遭受的苦难太深了，却对朋友的苦难视而不见。

My eighth year of schooling was the worst of my life, because I was awoken up to the horrors of schooling, and I had no one to share it with.

我上学的第八年是我一生中最糟糕的一年，因为我被上学的恐惧所惊醒，而且没有人可以分享它。

I took damage in every maths class I couldn’t escape — chained to a chair for 2 hours when all I wished was to get up and cycle and experience the world for what it was.

在我无法逃脱的每一门数学课上，我都受到了伤害-拴在椅子上2个小时，而我所希望的只是起床，骑自行车并体验真实的世界。

And I was lonely — I had not found anyone who quite understood how bad school was.

而且我很孤独-我还没有找到一个完全了解学业多么糟糕的人。

When a child is told school is good all their lives and they do bad it in, they will assume it is their fault. And this will obviously wreck havock on their self esteem.

当一个孩子被告知学校一生的好事而他们的学习不好时，他们会认为这是他们的错。这显然会破坏他们的自尊心。

I was awake, dezombified for a year. And I looked around me and everyone was a zombie, and I was terrified and I took damage and had a year in pain. No one else was like me — I hadn’t found supermemo.guru yet. ‘If you can’t beat them, join them.’

我清醒了，僵化了一年。我环顾四周，每个人都是僵尸，我感到恐惧，遭受了伤害，痛苦了一年。没有人像我一样-我还没有找到supermemo.guru。 ‘如果你不能击败他们，那就加入他们。’

Now I am free and dezombified again, but for how long? My passion to end school slavery was fleeting, memories are so so futile and it’s so easy to forget the abject terror of being shuffled into a room you cannot escape.

现在我又自由了，又重新僵化了，但是要走多久？我对结束学校奴役的热情转瞬即逝，记忆是如此的徒劳，而且很容易忘记被拖入无法逃脱的房间的巨大恐惧。

How long until the perils of school flee entirely from my mind, and I become like everyone else, who are indifferent to the tortures of schooling — or even insist that it’s good — despite the obvious, unbearable misery it causes?

多久以后，学校的危险才完全从我的脑海中消失，我变得和其他所有人一样，对学校的酷刑无动于衷，甚至坚持认为这是好事，尽管这造成了明显的，无法忍受的痛苦？
