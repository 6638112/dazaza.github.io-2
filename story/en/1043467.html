Pro-Trump mob storms the US Capitol, touting ‘Stop the Steal’ conspiracy
亲特朗普的暴民席卷美国国会大厦，吹捧“停止窃取”的阴谋

2021-01-08 07:39:36

The Trump supporters flooded into the nation’s capital to attend a rally held earlier by President Trump outside the White House. The rally was timed to protest lawmakers gathering Wednesday to certify President-elect Joe Biden’s electoral win.

特朗普的支持者涌入美国首都，参加特朗普总统在白宫外举行的一次集会。这次集会的时间定抗议国会议员周三聚会，证明总统当选人拜登的选举胜利。

At his own event, Trump encouraged his supporters to continue demonstrating against Congress, claiming incorrectly that Vice President Mike Pence holds the power to overturn the election results. While the situation is still unfolding, protesters penetrated the Capitol building and injuries have been confirmed, including at least one gunshot victim.

在自己的活动中，特朗普鼓励他的支持者继续抗议国会，错误地声称副总统迈克·彭斯拥有推翻选举结果的权力。尽管局势仍在继续，但示威者侵入了国会大厦，并确认伤亡，包括至少一名枪击受害者。

There’s pounding on the chamber door. Guns are drawn by police officers. Those of us left are laying low on the floor of the gallery.

房间门上有重击。枪支是由警察拉的。我们剩下的那些人躺在画廊的地板上。

Capitol Police are leading evacuation efforts following a shelter in place order for lawmakers and reporters pic.twitter.com/oLTf85Ga3Y

照片：抗议者在特朗普进行游行后违反了参议院的议事日程。游行示威，以回应国会今天选举选举团投票赞成乔·拜登的投票。在为国会议员和记者提供庇护所之后，国会警察正在领导疏散工作pic.twitter.com/oLTf85Ga3Y

As Trump supporters flooded up the Capitol steps with “Make America Great Again” hats and “Stop the Steal” banners, the president encouraged his supporters with more conspiratorial talk. “Mike Pence didn’t have the courage to do what should have been done to protect our Country and our Constitution, giving States a chance to certify a corrected set of facts, not the fraudulent or inaccurate ones which they were asked to previously certify,” Trump wrote in a tweet. “USA demands the truth!”

当特朗普的支持者戴着“使美国再次伟大”的帽子和“停止窃取”标语充斥着国会大厦的台阶时，总统鼓励他的支持者进行更多的阴谋论。 “迈克·彭斯（Mike Pence）没有勇气去做应做的事情来保护我们的国家和宪法，给各国提供了机会来证明一整套更正的事实，而不是要求他们先前证明的欺诈或不实事实，特朗普在一条推文中写道。 “美国要求真理！”

Twitter appended a warning label calling Trump’s election fraud claims “disputed” to the tweet. After his supporters already made their way into the Capitol building, the president seemed to walk back his calls to action, calling for supporters to remain peaceful.

Twitter附加了警告标签，称特朗普的选举欺诈指控在推特上“存在争议”。在他的支持者已经进入国会大厦之后，总统似乎退后了行动号召，呼吁支持者保持和平。

Update: Twitter has limited retweets and other forms of engagement on Trump’s tweet, but is not hiding it behind a warning screen at this time. The company says it is “exploring other escalated enforcement actions” though declined to elaborate on what those steps were.

更新：Twitter对特朗普的推文进行了有限的转发和其他形式的参与，但目前并未将其隐藏在警告屏幕后面。该公司表示，尽管拒绝详细说明这些步骤是什么，但它正在“探索其他逐步升级的执法行动”。

We are also exploring other escalated enforcement actions and will keep the public updated with any significant developments.

我们还将探索其他逐步升级的执法行动，并将随时向公众通报任何重大进展。

The Stop the Steal movement grew out of online conspiracies boosting Trump’s unfounded claims that Democrats had in some way rigged the presidential election. In reality, U.S. electoral results were decisively in favor of Biden, though votes trickled in over an extended period of time, as expected, due to a massive expansion of pandemic-related mail-in voting.

停止窃取运动源于网络阴谋，助长了特朗普毫无根据的说法，即民主党以某种方式操纵了总统选举。实际上，由于大流行病相关邮寄投票的大规模扩大，美国选举结果果断地支持拜登，尽管投票延误了预期的时间。

Facebook made efforts to rein in Stop the Spread groups soon after the election, blocking the hashtag for violating its rules around election misinformation. “The group was organized around the delegitimization of the election process, and we saw worrying calls for violence from some members of the group,” Facebook spokesperson Andy Stone said at the time.

Facebook努力在选举后不久遏制Stop Spread小组，阻止该标签用于违反有关选举错误信息的规则。 Facebook发言人安迪·斯通（Andy Stone）当时说：“该组织是围绕选举过程的合法化而组织的，我们看到该组织某些成员对暴力的呼吁令人担忧。”

Stop the Steal supporters also found a foothold on many other platforms, including Reddit, Twitter and alternative social networks like Gab and Parler, which have attracted far-right users with policies much friendlier to extremist content. The crowd at the capitol also shares considerable overlap with QAnon, a constellation of conspiracy theories that exploded on Facebook, YouTube and other online platforms over the last few years.

Stop Steal的支持者还在许多其他平台上立足，包括Reddit，Twitter和Gab和Parler等备用社交网络，这些平台已经吸引了极右翼用户，其政策比极端主义内容更为友好。国会大厦的人群还与QAnon有着相当大的重叠，QAnon是在过去几年中在Facebook，YouTube和其他在线平台上爆炸的一系列阴谋论。

In a live address Wednesday, President-elect Joe Biden said the events unfolding on Capitol Hill “bordered on sedition.”

在直播地址周三，总统当选人拜登说展开国会山事件“接壤煽动。”

“At their best, the words of a president can inspire,” Biden said. “At their worst, they can incite.”

拜登说：“在最好的情况下，总统的话可以激发灵感。” “在最坏的情况下，他们会煽动。”
