What Are You Paying For in a $300 Chess Set? Mostly the Knights
300美元的国际象棋套装需要支付什么？ 主要是骑士

2020-12-26 00:15:36

If you bought a wooden chess set after watching “The Queen’s Gambit,” the price you paid was most likely dictated by just four pieces.

如果您是在看完《女王的恶魔》之后买了一套木制国际象棋，那么您支付的价格很可能仅由四件决定。

The knights alone can account for as much as 50 percent of the cost of a nice wooden set. While the rest of the pieces can be machine-made, the knights are carved by hand to resemble the head of a horse, a tedious process to make sure all four are exactly the same.

单单骑士就可以占一套精美木套装成本的50％。虽然其余的零件可以机械制造，但骑士都是手工雕刻的，像马的头，这是一个冗长的过程，以确保所有四个零件完全相同。

The knights in the set used in World Chess Championship matches ($310 for the pieces and $220 for the board) were inspired by a horse carving from the Parthenon in Athens, said Ilya Merenzon, the chief executive of World Chess, the company that licenses the rights to the matches. The process of creating the set when it was redesigned in 2013 required extensive back-and-forth communication with carvers in India to discuss minutiae like the horse’s smile.

World Chess的执照公司Ilya Merenzon说，世界象棋锦标赛比赛中的骑士（棋子310美元，棋盘220美元）的灵感来自雅典帕台农神庙的马雕。比赛的权利。在2013年进行重新设计时，其制作过程需要与印度雕刻师进行广泛的来回交流，以讨论细节，例如马的笑容。

About 10 people specialize in carving knights for the World Chess sets, Mr. Merenzon said. It takes about two weeks to produce 100 sets, with a set of knights requiring about six hours to carve, he said.

梅伦宗说，大约有10个人专门为世界象棋雕刻骑士。他说，生产100套装置大约需要两个星期，而一组骑士需要大约六个小时的雕刻时间。

Chess sales spiked 125 percent after the October premiere of “The Queen’s Gambit,” a Netflix show about an orphaned chess prodigy, Beth Harmon, who crushes the male-dominated game. Many sets sold out before Christmas.

Netflix在10月的首映式“女王的甘比特”（Queen’s Gambit）上放出了125％的票房。圣诞节前很多套都卖光了。

The House of Staunton in Alabama, one of the world’s largest chess retailers, offers wooden sets with relatively simple knights, such as the $129 tournament-style set in boxwood and rosewood, as well as sets with more detail. They go all the way up to a luxurious $5,995 set in “antiqued” boxwood and ebony and featuring intricate horses.

世界上最大的国际象棋零售商之一，阿拉巴马州的斯汤顿故居提供带有相对简单骑士的木器套装，例如以黄杨木和紫檀木制成的$ 129锦标赛式套装，以及更多细节的套装。他们一路攀升至豪华的5,995美元，镶嵌在“仿古”黄杨木和乌木中，并配有错综复杂的马匹。

In the higher-end sets, “you can literally see the teeth carved into the horse’s mouth,” said Noelle Kendrick, the House of Staunton’s business development director. “They are extremely detailed. You can see the mane, the rivets of the mane, if it has a flowing mane.”

斯坦顿公司业务发展总监诺埃尔·肯德里克（Noelle Kendrick）说，在高端套装中，“您可以从字面上看到牙齿刻在马的嘴里。” “它们非常详细。如果鬃毛流动，您可以看到鬃毛，铆钉的铆钉。”

The ornamentation isn’t strictly decorative. In tournament play, milliseconds matter, and so does how a piece fits into your hand, Mr. Merenzon said. This is particularly true in the especially fast-paced games that can be used to break ties at tournaments: “blitz”-style games, which generally last less than 10 minutes, and “rapid” games, in which players have 25 minutes to make all their moves. The players tend to move their pieces and press their clock in one swift motion.

装饰不是严格的装饰。 Merenzon先生说，在锦标赛比赛中，毫秒很重要，一件手握的大小也很重要。在特别快节奏的游戏中尤其如此，这种游戏可以用来打破锦标赛的并列关系：“闪电战”式游戏通常持续不到10分钟，而“快速”游戏则需要25分钟才能完成比赛他们的所有举动。玩家倾向于移动他们的棋子并以一种快速的动作按下他们的时钟。

If you drop a piece and press the clock before standing it back up — effectively cutting into your opponent’s time — you could lose the game under some tournament rules. At a 2016 tournament put on by India’s league for professional players, the grandmaster Abhijeet Gupta lost the chance to advance after dropping his queen in a tiebreaking blitz game.

如果您放下一块棋子并在站起来之前按一下时钟（实际上会缩短对手的时间），那么您可能会在某些比赛规则下输掉比赛。在印度职业球员联盟举办的2016年比赛中，大师级Abhijeet Gupta在突破决胜的闪电战中丢掉女王后失去晋升的机会。

“That’s why expensive, hand-carved pieces are used in the championship,” Mr. Merenzon said. “The pieces are also weighted. They feel significant and kind of special.”

梅伦宗说：“这就是在锦标赛中使用昂贵的手工雕刻作品的原因。” “作品也被加权。他们感到重要而特殊。”

If you don’t want to spend $150 or more, there are more basic wooden sets, like those from big-box retailers. The knights are produced by machines and are more in the style of a Picasso than a da Vinci.

如果您不想花费150美元或更多，则可以使用更多基本的木制玩具，例如大型零售商的木制玩具。骑士是用机器生产的，比起达芬奇，更像是毕加索风格。

“When you shop for chess sets,” Ms. Kendrick said, “you find yourself almost solely focused on what the knight looks like. It’s the only piece that has a real significant difference.”

肯德里克女士说：“当您购买国际象棋时，几乎发现自己只专注于骑士的面貌。这是唯一具有真正重大差异的作品。”
