America’s Three Richest People Are Now Positioned To Pay Zero State Income Tax
美国三大富豪现在已准备缴纳零州所得税

2020-12-11 08:13:24

Tesl TSLA a CEO Elon Musk has moved his primary residence from California to Texas, according to reports on Tuesday, which means the three richest people in American—Musk, Amazon AMZN CEO Jeff Bezos and Microsoft MSFT cofounder Bill Gates—now all reside in states that don’t collect income tax.

根据周二的报道，Tesl TSLA的首席执行官埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）已将他的主要住所从加利福尼亚搬到了德克萨斯州，这意味着美国人中最富有的三个人-马斯克，亚马逊AMZN首席执行官杰夫·贝佐斯和微软MSFT联合创始人比尔·盖茨现在都居住在美国各州。不收所得税。

Musk’s move was anticipated, following reported rumors from his friends and associates that the Lone Star State would become his next home. This summer, he relocated his private foundation to the city of Austin. Musk, who is worth just shy of $140  billion, previously clashed with California officials over the state’s coronavirus restrictions, going so far as to sue Alameda County in May and threaten to move his company out of the state after its factory wasn’t allowed to reopen.

在他的朋友和同事传言说孤星州将成为他的下一个家园之后，马斯克的举动是可以预料的。今年夏天，他将自己的私人基金会搬到了奥斯丁市。身家不到1400亿美元的马斯克，此前曾因该州的冠状病毒限制与加州官员发生冲突，甚至在5月起诉阿拉米达县，并扬言在不允许其工厂运营后将其公司迁出该州。重新打开。

Bezos (worth an estimated $183.3 billion) and Gates (worth an estimated $118.7 billion) both reside in Washington State, the birthplace of their companies. Washington and Texas are two of nine U.S. states that don’t collect income tax. The rest are: Alaska, Florida, Nevada, New Hampshire, South Dakota, Tennessee and Wyoming. The Tax Foundation, a leading nonprofit, notes that Tennessee and New Hampshire still tax interest and dividends, but the latter is scheduled to phase out this tax in 2025.

贝索斯（身价约1,833亿美元）和盖茨（身价约1,187亿美元）都居住在华盛顿州，这是他们公司的发源地。华盛顿和德克萨斯州是美国九个不征收所得税的州中的两个。其余的是：阿拉斯加，佛罗里达州，内华达州，新罕布什尔州，南达科他州，田纳西州和怀俄明州。领先的非营利组织税务基金会（Tax Foundation）指出，田纳西州和新罕布什尔州仍对利息和股息征税，但后者计划于2025年逐步淘汰该税。

Musk is far from the only billionaire to decamp for income tax havens, however. Donald Trump switched his official residence to Florida in October 2019, after decades of living in his namesake Manhattan tower. Wall Street titans Carl Icahn and Paul Singer moved their hedge funds to the Sunshine State in 2019 and 2020, respectively. Using public records, Forbes confirmed Icahn resides on the private Indian Creek Island near Miami—where Jared Kushner and Ivanka Trump reportedly recently bought a $30 million plot of land—therefore making him exempt from state income tax. Tom Golisano, the billionaire founder of Paychex PAYX , says he saves “$13,800 a day” in taxes just because he relocated from New York to Florida in 2009.

但是，马斯克并不是唯一一个逃避所得税天堂的亿万富翁。唐纳德·特朗普（Donald Trump）在他同名的曼哈顿塔楼居住了数十年后，于2019年10月将其官方住所转移到佛罗里达州。华尔街巨头卡尔·伊坎（Paul Icahn）和保罗·辛格（Paul Singer）分别于2019年和2020年将对冲基金移至阳光州。根据公开记录，福布斯证实伊坎居住在迈阿密附近的私人印第安克里克岛上（据报道，贾里德·库什纳和伊万卡·特朗普最近在那儿购买了3000万美元的土地），因此使他免收州所得税。 Paychex PAYX的亿万富翁创始人汤姆·戈利萨诺（Tom Golisano）表示，仅因为他于2009年从纽约搬到佛罗里达州，他就每天节省了“ 13,800美元”的税款。

These moves can dent state coffers, given the immense income some billionaires generate. When hedge fund manager David Tepper moved from New Jersey to Florida in 2016, the decision looks to have cost the Garden State hundreds of millions of dollars in revenue and upended its income tax forecasts, raising concerns for state officials. American hedge fund billionaire John Arnold pointed out another concern in a Wednesday morning tweet, noting that California’s 13.3% capital gains tax, the country’s highest, effectively falls to zero the moment high earners like Musk simply decide to leave the state for lower-tax locales.

鉴于某些亿万富翁产生的巨额收入，这些举动会使国家的财政收入缩水。当对冲基金经理戴维·泰珀（David Tepper）在2016年从新泽西搬到佛罗里达州时，该决定似乎使花园州立大学损失了数亿美元的收入，并上调了其所得税预测，这引起了州官员的担忧。美国对冲基金亿万富翁约翰·阿诺德（John Arnold）在周三早上的一条推文中指出了另一点担忧，指出当马斯克（Musk）这样的高收入者决定离开该州进入低税率地区时，加利福尼亚州最高的13.3％资本增值税实际上降为零。 。

“It's quite possible [California] is on the wrong side of the Laffer Curve,” Arnold wrote on Twitter, referencing the economic theory that supposes tax revenues will fall if governments set rates too high and can increase if rates are lowered. Arnold lives in Texas, where he pays—you guessed it—no state income tax.

阿诺德（Arnold）在推特上写道：“很有可能[加利福尼亚州]处于拉弗曲线的另一侧，”他指的是一种经济理论，即如果政府将税率定得过高，税收将减少，而如果税率降低，则税收收入将增加。 。阿诺德（Arnold）居住在得克萨斯州（Texas），您在那儿缴纳-您猜不到-没有州所得税。
