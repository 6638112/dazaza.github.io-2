WhatsApp can now read your recent messages when you report a user
现在，当您报告用户时，WhatsApp可以阅读您最近的消息

2020-11-26 01:53:03

WhatsApp has just submitted a new update through the Google Play Beta Program, bringing the version up to 2.20.206.3. What’s new in this update? Discover all details below!

WhatsApp刚刚通过Google Play Beta计划提交了新更新，使该版本的最新版本为2.200.36.3。此更新有哪些新功能？在下面发现所有详细信息！

I’ve the same version but I don’t see this news, why?

我使用的是同一版本，但没有看到此消息，为什么？

It’s not possible. WhatsApp has introduced this feature for beta testers having the 2.20.206.3 update.

这是不可能的。 WhatsApp已为具有2.20.206.3更新的Beta测试人员引入了此功能。

WhatsApp is introducing a new feature for beta testers, already available once updating to the latest 2.20.206.3 update. Actually, when you report a contact to WhatsApp, WhatsApp elaborates your report considering several factors, like the number of reports received for that phone number from different users, if the account of the reported user is new and it has already received some reports, etc… With this update, WhatsApp is introducing a new way to verify if a report is reliable: receiving a copy of recent messages from that chat. WhatsApp never receives your messages without your permissions. If you decide to report a contact, you agree to forward a copy to WhatsApp of the recent messages from that chat.

WhatsApp为Beta测试人员引入了一项新功能，一旦更新到最新的2.20.206.3更新，该功能便已可用。实际上，当您向WhatsApp报告联系人时，WhatsApp会考虑多个因素来详细说明您的报告，例如从不同用户那里收到的该电话号码的报告数量，所报告用户的帐户是新用户并且已经收到了一些报告等。 …在此更新中，WhatsApp引入了一种验证报告是否可靠的新方法：从该聊天中接收最近消息的副本。未经您的许可，WhatsApp绝不会收到您的消息。如果您决定举报联系人，则表示您同意将来自该聊天的最新消息的副本转发给WhatsApp。

This feature is available TODAY for Android beta testers and it will be following soon for iOS beta testers in the next few weeks.

该功能今天可用于Android Beta测试人员，并且很快将在接下来的几周内为iOS Beta测试人员使用。
