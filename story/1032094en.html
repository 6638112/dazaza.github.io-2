谷歌表示“致力于”Nest安全，但不会发布任何新功能

2020-10-30 02:23:07

No one is quite sure what to make of Google's home security plans lately. The company recently discontinued the Nest Secure, its $500 home security system, so, on one hand, it is out of the home security market. On the other hand, Google also recently signed a $450 million deal with home monitoring firm ADT, which will see it "combine Nest's award-winning hardware and services, powered by Google's machine-learning technology, with ADT's installation, service and professional monitoring." With the Nest Secure being discontinued, does this mean Google is rebooting its security hardware into something that goes hand in hand with ADT? Is the Nest Secure product line dead? How much longer will the existing Nest Secure be supported?

没有人确切地知道谷歌最近的家庭安全计划是怎么回事。该公司最近停止了其价值500美元的家庭安全系统Nest Secure，因此，一方面，它退出了家庭安全市场。另一方面，谷歌最近还与家庭监控公司ADT签署了一项价值4.5亿美元的协议，将Nest屡获殊荣的硬件和服务(采用谷歌的机器学习技术)与ADT的安装、服务和专业监控相结合。随着Nest Secure的停产，这是否意味着谷歌将重新启动其安全硬件，进入与ADT齐头并进的领域？Nest Secure产品线死了吗？现有的Nest Secure还能支持多久？

About a week after news broke that the Nest Secure was discontinued, Google started to communicate to Nest Secure owners in a forum post,  a help page, and an email about what is going on. Regardless, it still hasn't provided a very clear picture of the future.

在Nest Secure停产的消息传出大约一周后，谷歌开始在论坛帖子、帮助页面和电子邮件中与Nest Secure所有者沟通正在发生的事情。无论如何，它仍然没有提供一个非常清晰的未来图景。

The most concrete message out of all that communication is that existing Nest Secure users will have at least one more chance to stock up on hardware soon. The Nest Secure works by having a hub/keypad (the Nest Guard) monitor your house via sensors on the doors and windows (the Nest Detect sensors), and users can authorize themselves either with the code or by tapping an NFC tag (the Nest Tag) against the hub. The sudden stoppage of hardware sales was a real bummer for anyone who was already invested in the system and maybe wanted to monitor one more door or window in the future, or someone who was worried about a sensor breaking.

在所有这些交流中，最具体的信息是，现有的Nest安全用户很快将至少再有一次机会囤积硬件。Nest Secure的工作原理是让集线器/键盘(Nest Guard)通过门窗上的传感器(Nest Detect传感器)监控您的房子，用户可以使用代码或在集线器上点击NFC标签(Nest标签)来授权自己。硬件销售的突然停止对于任何已经对该系统进行了投资，并可能希望在未来再监控一扇门或窗的人来说，或者是担心传感器损坏的人来说，都是一个真正令人沮丧的问题。

Google heard these calls for more hardware. "We understand some of you would like to purchase additional Nest Secure products, and our team is working diligently to have more Nest Detect sensors available for sale in mid-December," the company wrote. The main "Nest Guard" hub is still dead, though, so there will be no new Nest Secure customers. For existing users who want to ride out the Nest Secure rollercoaster, this might be your last chance to pick up more hardware.

谷歌听到了这些要求增加硬件的呼声。我们知道你们中的一些人想购买更多的Nest安全产品，我们的团队正在努力工作，以便让更多的Nest检测传感器在12月中旬上市。该公司写道。不过，主要的Nest Guard集线器仍然是死的，所以不会有新的Nest安全客户。对于想要安然度过Nest安全过山车的现有用户来说，这可能是您获得更多硬件的最后机会。

It's still not actually clear if investing more in the Nest Secure ecosystem is a good idea. Here's Google's statement on the future of Nest Secure support, from the help page:

目前还不清楚在Nest安全生态系统上投资更多是否是一个好主意。以下是谷歌关于Nest Secure Support未来的声明，请访问帮助页面：

We are committed to bringing our users the same feature and software support they’ve always had with Nest Secure, including existing cross-product integrations within the Nest ecosystem. We will also continue to deliver critical security updates and software fixes. Nest Secure owners shouldn’t notice any difference with their service.

我们致力于为我们的用户提供他们在Nest Secure中一直拥有的相同功能和软件支持，包括Nest生态系统内现有的跨产品集成。我们还将继续提供重要的安全更新和软件修复。Nest安全所有者应该不会注意到他们的服务有什么不同。

Google is "committed" to Nest Secure, but notice it never says how long it will be "committed" for. How much longer the Nest Secure will be supported is still anyone's guess. Other Google products like Pixel phones and Chromebooks have public end-of-life dates, and while these timelines can be extended, having a firm minimum date makes investing in a product a lot easier.

谷歌致力于Nest Secure，但请注意，它从未说过将承诺多久。Nest Secure还能支持多久还是个未知数。其他谷歌产品，如Pixel Phones和Chromebook，都有公开的停产日期，虽然这些时间线可以延长，但有一个固定的最低日期会让投资产品变得容易得多。

It certainly sounds like the Nest Secure's software support is winding down, and that probably means there will be no direct successor to the product. Notice that Google will "continue to deliver critical security updates and software fixes" but not "new features." A product being discontinued is not a huge deal if there is a direct sequel coming, but the lack of new features suggests that the Nest Secure line is truly dead. Google pretty much always backports software features from a new product to the old product, since keeping everything on the same codebase is an easy way to minimize software support costs. The lack of new features for the Nest Secure suggests there's no "Nest Secure 2" in the works that will maintain compatibility with the old system.

这听起来肯定像是Nest Secure的软件支持正在逐渐减少，这可能意味着该产品将不会有直接的继任者。请注意，谷歌将继续提供关键的安全更新和软件修复，但不会提供新功能。如果直接推出续集，停产的产品并不是什么大不了的事情，但缺乏新功能表明Nest Secure Line真的死了。Google几乎总是将软件功能从新产品移植到旧产品，因为保持所有功能都在相同的代码库上是最小化软件支持成本的简单方法。Nest Secure缺乏新的功能，这表明Nest Secure 2正在开发中，它将保持与旧系统的兼容性。

No matter what vague statements Google makes, with no hardware product on the market and therefore no new customers, the support future for the Nest Secure can only ever be a countdown clock. A minimum public date for support (even one that gets extended) seems like the least Google could do for customers who have invested hundreds of dollars in its hardware. We still don't know how much longer the Nest Secure will work.

无论谷歌发表什么模糊的声明，由于市场上没有硬件产品，因此也没有新的客户，Nest Secure的支持未来只能是一个倒计时时钟。对于那些在其硬件上投资了数百美元的客户来说，最短的公开支持日期(即使是延长的日期)似乎是谷歌所能做的最起码的事情。我们仍然不知道Nest Secure还能工作多久。
