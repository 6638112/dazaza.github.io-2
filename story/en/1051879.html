Apple sues its former materials lead Simon Lancaster for allegedly stealing trade secrets and leaking them to the media
Apple起诉其前材料牵引Simon Lancaster，据称窃取商业秘密并将其泄漏到媒体上

2021-03-12 05:27:51

Apple is taking legal action against its former materials lead, Simon Lancaster, over allegedly leaking trade secrets to the media. The lawsuit accuses Lancaster of abusing his “position and trust within the company to systematically disseminate Apple’s sensitive trade secret information in an effort to obtain personal benefits.”

Apple正在针对其前材料铅，Simon Lancaster的法律行动，据称泄露给媒体的商业秘密。诉讼指责兰开斯特滥用他的“公司内部的职位和信任，以便在系统地传播苹果的敏感性秘密信息，以获得个人利益。”

As first reported by AppleInsider, the court document detailing the lawsuit was made public on Thursday. Lancaster allegedly used his senior role at Apple to “gain access to internal meetings and documents outside the scope” of his job as materials lead at Apple. In fact, on his last day at Apple, November 1, 2019, he allegedly downloaded a “substantial number” of confidential Apple documents.

正如AppleInsider第一次报告的那样，法院文件在周四公开上市。兰卡斯特据称，苹果公司的高级角色将其在苹果公司领先的材料领先“获得内部会议和范围内的内部会议和文件”。事实上，在2019年11月1日的Apple的最后一天，他据称下载了“大量的机密苹果文件”。

Lancaster departed Apple after a decade to join the materials research and development company Arris, which is actually an Apple vendor. The trade secrets he stole from Apple continued to benefit him in his new position at Arris, the report explains. Lancaster described the role at Arris as scratching a “startup itch.”

兰卡斯特在十年后离开了苹果，加入了物资研发公司阿拉斯，实际上是一个苹果供应商。该报告解释说，他从Apple偷走的商业秘密继续在他的新立场中受益。兰卡斯特将Arris的角色描述为抓起“启动痒”。

But things get even more interesting when the media aspect of this story comes into play. The lawsuit explains that the reporter first contacted Lancaster in November 2018, a full year before he departed Apple. Apple trade secrets were allegedly exchanged throughout 2018 and 2019.

但是当这个故事的媒体方面发挥作用时，事情会变得更加有趣。该诉讼解释说，记者首先在2018年11月联系了兰卡斯特，在他离开苹果之前的全年。据称，苹果商业秘密在2018年和2019年兑换。

In Spring 2019, Lancaster expressed to the Correspondent in profane terms his displeasure with Apple. His displeasure, on information and belief, was based on a story published that day that reported a rumor that Apple would produce a new hardware product. Shortly thereafter, Lancaster asked the Correspondent to investigate the substance of that story because “it could mean trouble for my [Lancaster’s] startup.” The Correspondent replied “I’ll see what I can find out.”

2019年春季，兰开斯特对亵渎术语的记者表示，他与苹果不悦。他的令人满意的是，关于信息和信仰，是基于当天发布的故事，报告了苹果将产生新的硬件产品的谣言。此后不久，兰卡斯特向记者询问了该故事的实质，因为“这可能意味着我的[兰开斯特]的启动。”记者回答“我会看到我能找到的东西。”

Less than two weeks later, Lancaster communicated to a third party that the Correspondent had committed to publishing an article about Lancaster’s startup if it obtained $1 million in funding. On information and belief, the Correspondent agreed to publish that article in exchange for Lancaster’s ongoing misappropriation of Apple trade secrets.

不到两周后，兰开斯特向第三方传达了这位名人致力于发布关于兰开斯特的初创公司的文章，如果它获得100万美元的资金。关于信息和信仰，该代理商同意发布该文章以换取兰开斯特持续挪用苹果商业秘密。

The anonymous media who used the information in multiple articles and attributed the information to a “source” at Apple. Details about the topics are unclear, but Lancaster attended multiple meetings about “Project X” at Apple and shared all of that information with the media correspondent.

使用多个文章中的信息的匿名媒体并将信息归因于Apple的“源”。有关主题的详细信息尚不清楚，但兰开斯特参加了关于Apple的“项目X”的多次会议，并与媒体记者共享所有信息。

The trade secrets Lancaster stole and sent to the Correspondent for publication included details of unreleased Apple hardware products, unannounced feature changes to existing hardware products, and future product announcements, all of which Apple guards closely.

商业秘密兰卡斯特偷走并派往通讯员出版物包括未发布的Apple硬件产品的详细信息，未经发布的功能更改现有硬件产品，以及未来的产品通知，所有苹果卫兵都密切相关。

Going forward, Apple warns in the lawsuit that Lancaster remains an “ongoing threat of misappropriation” given his continued work at Arris.

展望不，苹果警告在兰开斯特仍然是他在阿里斯继续工作的“持续挪用盗用威胁”的诉讼中。

Through the lawsuit, Apple wants Lancaster to be charged with violations of the Defense of Trade Secret Act and California Uniform Trade Secret Act, as well as breaching a written contract. Apple seeks a trial by jury, restitution, injunctive relief, and punitive damages.

通过诉讼，苹果希望兰开斯特违反违反贸易委员会和加州统一贸易委员会的辩护，以及违反书面合同。 Apple试图通过陪审团，恢复，禁令缓解和惩罚性损害审判。
