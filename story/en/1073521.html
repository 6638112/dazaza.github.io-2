A new silicon battery design beat Tesla's energy density and it's shipping
一种新的硅电池设计超过了特斯拉的能量密度，并且已经上市

2022-02-15 21:00:10

After the first batch of commercially available lithium-ion battery cells were shipped out last week, Amprius Technologies is being called the "leading provider of the highest energy density cells available."

上周，首批商用锂离子电池出货后，安普瑞斯科技公司被称为"；领先的高能量密度电池供应商"

The  450 Wh/kg, 1150 Wh/L lithium-ion battery cells — the first of their kind to be deployed commercially, per Amprius, — were shipped to an  industry leader of a new generation of High-Altitude Pseudo Satellites (HAPS), but no exact name was provided.

450 Wh/kg、1150 Wh/L的锂离子电池组——这是第一个按Amprius标准商业化部署的锂离子电池组——被运送给了新一代高空伪卫星（HAPS）的行业领导者，但没有提供确切名称。

"This advancement from the 405 Wh/kg product, [...], highlights the acceleration of our roadmap towards delivering products with unrivaled performance," said  Jon Bornstein , COO of Amprius Technologies.

"这是从405 Wh/kg产品[…]的进步，重点介绍了我们的路线图的加速，以提供无与伦比的性能和#34；Amprius Technologies首席运营官乔恩·伯恩斯坦（Jon Bornstein）说。

"Our proprietary Si-Nanowire TM  platform and the comprehensive solutions we have developed enable unparalleled performance and continue to sustain our product leadership," he continued.

"我们专有的Si纳米线TM平台和我们开发的综合解决方案实现了无与伦比的性能，并继续保持我们的产品领先地位；他接着说。

As the journal Nature points out, "Silicon (Si) has been recognized as one of the most promising anode materials for Li-ion batteries due to its high gravimetric theoretical lithium storage capacity (3,579 mAh g −1) 1, compared to conventionally used graphite anodes (372 mAh g −1) 2, while also having a relatively low discharge voltage."

正如《自然》杂志指出的那样，"；硅（Si）因其高重量理论储锂容量（3579 mAh g）而被认为是最有前途的锂离子电池负极材料之一−1） 1，与常规使用的石墨阳极（372 mAh g）相比−1） 2，同时具有相对较低的放电电压"

Bearing that in mind, Amprius' Silicon nanowire technology uses 100 percent silicon nanowire anodes and directly replaces graphite anodes. The other components of the battery are made up of the already-established global supply chain for lithium-ion batteries, explains Amprius.

记住这一点，安普里乌斯'；硅纳米线技术使用100%的硅纳米线阳极，并直接取代石墨阳极。Amprius解释说，电池的其他组件由已经建立的锂离子电池全球供应链组成。

By using only silicon, Amprius is able to provide lithium-ion batteries which have what the company claims are the highest energy density available. On top of that, silicon nanowires enable the batteries to have the highest ratio of energy to power, because they're connected directly to the substrate without any binders. And finally, the life cycle is longer.

通过仅使用硅，Amprius能够提供锂离子电池，该公司称其具有最高的能量密度。最重要的是，硅纳米线使电池具有最高的能量与功率比，因为它们'；不使用任何粘合剂直接重新连接到基板上。最后，生命周期更长。

Flight time increases nearly three times more than regular graphite anodes and electric vehicle range nearly doubles.

飞行时间比普通石墨阳极增加了近三倍，电动汽车的续航里程几乎翻了一番。

In numbers, Enpower points out that Tesla's current Model 3 cells (the best comparison to date) hold around 260 Wh/kg and 730 Wh/l, whereas Amprius' new cells hold  450 Wh/kg and 1,150 Wh/l — a significant jump up.

Enpower指出，在数字上，特斯拉'；s目前的模型3电池（迄今为止最好的比较）的容量约为260 Wh/kg和730 Wh/l，而Amprius和#39；新电池的容量分别为450 Wh/kg和1150 Wh/l，这是一个显著的飞跃。
