How to turn your Xbox Series X/S into an emulation powerhouse
如何将您的Xbox Series X / S变成仿真引擎

2020-11-25 03:29:05

After a new console is released, it usually takes hackers months or years to find a hole in the console's security that lets them install homebrew software like emulators. So it may come as a surprise that you can already load RetroArch—and its vast array of emulation cores for dozens of classic systems—on the newly released Xbox Series X/S consoles. The installation vector here comes not through an unforeseen security hole, but through Microsoft's policy of allowing any retail Xbox One console to become a full-fledged dev kit. After promising that functionality in 2013, there were signs that Microsoft was thinking of abandoning those plans in 2014. By 2016, though, Microsoft officially opened up the Xbox One, allowing registered Universal Windows Platform (UWP) developers to load and test content directly onto a stock retail console. Enter Libretro, which decided in late 2018 that it would commit to creating an Xbox One-compatible UWP build of its popular emulator package. That version launched in Alpha in 2019 and has been updated sporadically since. Ars has confirmed that a new build works on the Xbox Series X as well, allowing your new console to pretend to be anything from an Atari 2600 to a Wii, with a whole lot of consoles in between.

发布新控制台后，黑客通常需要数月或数年的时间才能发现控制台安全漏洞，使他们可以安装模拟器等自制软件。因此，令人惊讶的是，您已经可以在新发布的Xbox Series X / S控制台上加载RetroArch及其用于许多经典系统的大量仿真核心。这里的安装过程不是通过无法预见的安全漏洞而来的，而是通过微软的政策来允许任何零售Xbox One控制台成为成熟的开发工具包。在2013年承诺该功能后，有迹象表明微软正在考虑在2014年放弃这些计划。不过，到2016年，微软正式开放Xbox One，允许注册的通用Windows平台（UWP）开发人员直接在其上加载和测试内容库存零售控制台。输入Libretro，该公司在2018年末决定将致力于为其流行的模拟器程序包创建与Xbox One兼容的UWP版本。该版本于2019年在Alpha中推出，此后不定期进行更新。 Ars已经确认，新版本也可以在Xbox Series X上运行，从而使您的新游戏机可以装作从Atari 2600到Wii的任何东西，并且介于两者之间。

Getting RetroArch on your brand-new Xbox isn't as simple as just inserting a USB drive and puttering away. First, you have to sign up for a Microsoft Developer Account through the Windows Dev Center portal. There's a one-time $19 fee associated with registering an individual account, so you'll have to decide early what the possibility of running emulators on the Xbox is worth to you.

在全新的Xbox上安装RetroArch并不只是简单地插入USB驱动器然后轻推即可。首先，您必须通过Windows Dev Center门户注册Microsoft开发者帐户。注册个人帐户需要一次性支付$ 19的费用，因此您必须尽早确定在Xbox上运行模拟器的可能性对您来说是值得的。

Once you're registered, go to your console and search for the "Dev Mode Activation" app in the Store. The system will guide you through a few steps to link the console to your new Developer account, and you may have to download some updates before restarting in Developer Mode (if the update doesn't take for some reason, this trick may work to force the system into Developer Mode).

注册后，转到控制台并在商店中搜索“开发模式激活”应用程序。系统将指导您完成一些步骤，以将控制台链接到新的Developer帐户，并且您可能必须先下载一些更新，然后才能以Developer Mode重新启动（如果由于某些原因而没有进行更新，此技巧可能会强制执行系统进入开发人员模式）。

Be aware that an Xbox console in Development Mode won't be able to play any retail Xbox games, either on disc or download. It's relatively simple to switch back and forth to/from retail mode using the on-screen menu, though, as long as you're willing to wait for the system to reboot.

请注意，处于开发模式的Xbox控制台将无法播放任何零售的Xbox游戏，无论是光盘上的还是下载的。不过，只要您愿意等待系统重启，使用零售菜单来回切换零售模式相对简单。

With your console in Developer Mode (and connected to the Internet), the screen should display an IP address for local network access to the system. Type that address in a Web browser on your computer to open up the Xbox Device Portal. From there, simply download the Xbox One RetroArch files and dependencies (labeled as "UWP runtime package") from the RetroArch website, then upload them to your console using the green "Add" button on the Device Portal page.

在控制台处于“开发人员模式”（并且已连接到Internet）的情况下，屏幕应显示用于本地网络访问系统的IP地址。在计算机上的Web浏览器中键入该地址，以打开Xbox设备门户。从那里，只需从RetroArch网站下载Xbox One RetroArch文件和依赖项（标记为“ UWP运行时程序包”），然后使用“设备门户”页面上绿色的“添加”按钮将它们上传到控制台。

When you go back to your console, RetroArch should appear as a launchable project whenever you're in Developer Mode. From inside RetroArch, you should be able to use the on-screen menus to directly download updates to the front-end interface and backend cores directly on the system itself.

当您回到控制台时，无论何时处于开发人员模式，RetroArch都应显示为可启动项目。从RetroArch内部，您应该能够使用屏幕上的菜单直接将更新直接下载到前端界面和后端核心上，直接在系统本身上。

Note that some of the emulation cores included in the RetroArch package require a BIOS file pulled from actual hardware to work; you'll have to source and upload those yourself (from your own legitimate hardware, of course). And while RetroArch has a number of homebrew, shareware, and open source ROMs available for download directly through its system menus, you'll have to find and upload any additional ROMs (such as backups ripped from your own game collection) on your own.

请注意，RetroArch软件包中包含的某些仿真核心需要从实际硬件中拉出的BIOS文件才能正常工作。您必须自己（当然是从您自己的合法硬件中）获取和上传这些文件。尽管RetroArch有许多自制软件，共享软件和开源ROM可直接通过其系统菜单下载，但您必须自行查找并上传任何其他ROM（例如从您自己的游戏收藏中提取的备份）。

RetroArch can already run on everything from the original GameCube to the Switch to a cheap Raspberry Pi, so another console full of emulators might not seem that exciting. But the folks at Modern Vintage Gamer put the $299 Xbox Series S through its emulation paces, and they found "some of the very best emulation that I've seen on a console." This is especially true when it comes to recreating relatively recent and/or difficult-to-emulate 3D hardware like the Gamecube/Wii, Saturn, or PSP. For these consoles, the extra hardware power on the new Xbox consoles helps emulation run more smoothly than you might expect from cheaper devices. As long as you don't expect completely perfect authenticity or compatibility, it seems that Xbox Series X/S hardware can stand in pretty well for older systems.

RetroArch已经可以在从原始GameCube到Switch到便宜的Raspberry Pi的所有设备上运行，因此另一个充满模拟器的游戏机似乎并不那么令人兴奋。但是Modern Vintage Gamer的人们通过仿真步伐将299美元的Xbox Series S推向市场，他们发现“我在游戏机上看到的一些最好的仿真器”。在重新创建相对较新和/或难以仿真的3D硬件（例如Gamecube / Wii，Saturn或PSP）时，尤其如此。对于这些控制台，新Xbox控制台上的额外硬件功能可帮助您比廉价设备预期的仿真运行更为平稳。只要您不期望完全完美的真实性或兼容性，Xbox Series X / S硬件似乎就可以很好地适用于较旧的系统。

The developers at Libretro will continue to update RetroArch and its underlying emulation cores as time goes on, too, so new advancements in emulation technology should make their way to the Xbox UWP build in due time. Right now, the team seems close to getting PlayStation 2 emulation core PCSX2 into workable shape in RetroArch, which would be a bit ironic considering that PS2 games are not natively compatible with the PlayStation 5.

随着时间的流逝，Libretro的开发人员还将继续更新RetroArch及其基础的仿真核心，因此，仿真技术的新进步应在适当的时候应用于Xbox UWP的构建。目前，该团队似乎已接近在RetroArch中将PlayStation 2仿真核心PCSX2变成可行的形状，考虑到PS2游戏本身与PlayStation 5不兼容，这有点讽刺。
