Are you buying domains for fun? you can setup email easily
您是在购买域名吗？ 您可以轻松设置电子邮件

2020-12-07 02:43:10

Mailway will accept any emails sent to your domain and let you decide what to do.

Mailway将接受发送到您的域的任何电子邮件，并让您决定要做什么。

Incoming emails will be matched against rules you configured. A rule allows you to forward the email to your personal email address or multiple addresses, or by default simply ignore it.

收到的电子邮件将与您配置的规则匹配。规则允许您将电子邮件转发到您的个人电子邮件地址或多个地址，或者默认情况下，将其忽略即可。

Forwarded emails are not changed by Mailway which allows your email client to apply labels and other filters (like spam filtering).

Mailway不会更改转发的电子邮件，这使您的电子邮件客户端可以应用标签和其他过滤器（例如垃圾邮件过滤）。

Catch-all allows to receive all mail sent to your domain, even if the email doesn't match any of your rules.

全部接收允许接收发送到您的域的所有邮件，即使该电子邮件不符合您的任何规则。

To fully utilize the routing and blocking capability of Mailway, or your local mail client, we recommend that you create dedicated email addresses for every website. Emails from a bad website could then be easily blocked.

为了充分利用Mailway或本地邮件客户端的路由和阻止功能，我们建议您为每个网站创建专用的电子邮件地址。这样一来，来自不良网站的电子邮件就很容易被阻止。

Your email is none of our business and we won't keep any trace of them by default. Make sure you backup your incoming emails in the event of a failure.

您的电子邮件与我们无关，默认情况下，我们不会保留任何电子邮件。确保在发生故障时备份传入的电子邮件。

Account limits: 50 domains, receive 1000 emails per day. Domain limits: 10 rules. Forwarding limits: 20 recipients per rule.

帐户限制：50个域，每天接收1000封电子邮件。域限制：10条规则。转发限制：每个规则20个收件人。
