Upcoming Changes for Some US Businesses
即将到来的一些美国企业的变化

2021-06-19 22:38:30

We are making changes to our published rates in the United States to better align our pricing with the value that our products and services provide. Our goal is to be transparent and upfront with our customers about the changes. These rates will apply to a portion of our merchant customers in the U.S. beginning August 2, 2021. We began notifying some customers today, and you can find a notice on our Policy Updates page.

我们正在改变美国的发布利率，以更好地对准我们的产品和服务提供的价值。我们的目标是与客户有关更改的透明和前期。这些利率将适用于美国2002年8月2日开始的一部分商家客户。我们开始通知一些客户，您可以在我们的策略更新页面上找到通知。

PayPal Digital Payments: For PayPal payment products (such as PayPal Checkout, Pay with Venmo, PayPal Credit, Pay in 4, PayPal Pay with Rewards, Checkout with crypto), which include Seller Protection on eligible transactions, the rate for online transactions will be 3.49% + $0.49 1 per transaction.

Paypal Digital付款：对于PayPal付款产品（如PayPal结账，用Venmo，PayPal Credit，支付4，PayPal支付奖励，结帐与加密），其中包括卖方对符合条件的交易的保护，在线交易的速度将是每笔交易3.49％+ 0.49美元。

In-person Payments: For PayPal and Venmo QR code transactions over $10, the rate will be 1.90% + $0.10, and for such transactions $10 and under, the rate will be 2.40% + $0.05. For certain in-person debit and credit transactions the rate will be 2.29% + $0.09.

个人支付：对于PayPal和Venmo QR QR码交易超过10美元，率将为1.90％+ 0.10美元，而且此类交易10美元及以下，利率为2.40％+ 0.05美元。对于某些内部借记和信用交易，费率将为2.29％+ 0.09美元。

Credit and Debit Card payments:  Online credit and debit card transactions will be 2.59% + $0.49 1 per transaction without Chargeback Protection, or 2.99% + $0.49 with Chargeback Protection.

信用卡和借记卡付款：在线信用卡和借记卡交易将每笔交易2.59％+ 0.49 1美元，无需退款保护，或2.99％+ 0.49美元，退款保护。

Charity Transactions: Fees for charity transactions will be 1.99% + $0.49 for confirmed charities (subject to application and pre-approval).

慈善交易：确认慈善机构的慈善交易费用为1.99％+ 0.49美元（申请和预批准）。

Non-standard Pricing: For U.S. merchants who have custom, non-standard pricing, rates will remain unchanged for those services as agreed.

非标准定价：对于拥有习俗，非标准定价的美国商家，可根据商定的这些服务保持不变。
