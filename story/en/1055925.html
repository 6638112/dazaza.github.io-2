The map was created by Kiln based on data from the UCL Energy Institute (UCL EI)

该地图是根据UCL Energy Institute（UCL EI）的数据为基础创建的

UCL EI took data showing location and speed of ships and cross-checked it with another database to get the vessel characteristics, such as engine type and hull measurements. With this information they were able to compute the CO 2 emissions for each observed hour, following the approach laid out in the Third IMO Greenhouse Gas Study 2014. Kiln took the resulting dataset and visualized it with WebGL on top of a specially created base map, which shows bathymetry (ocean depth), based on the GEBCO_2014 Grid (version 20150318), as well as continents and major rivers from Natural Earth.

UCL EI采取了显示船舶的位置和速度的数据，并用另一个数据库交叉检查，以获得船舶特性，例如发动机类型和船体测量。通过这些信息，他们能够计算每次观察到的每小时的二氧化碳排放量，按照第三IMO温室气体研究2014年的方法。窑在特殊创建的基地地图上用WebGL拍摄了生成的数据集，并用WebGL可视化它，显示基于Gebco_2014 Grid（20150318版）以及来自自然地球的大陆和主要河流的浴约集（海洋深度）。

Our data sources for shipping positions are exactEarth for AIS data (location/speed) and Clarksons Research UK World Fleet Register (static vessel information). We are very grateful to our funders, the European Climate Foundation.

我们的运输职位的数据来源是AIS数据（位置/速度）和Clarksons Research UK World Fleet Register（静态船舶信息）的Excelearth。我们非常感谢我们的资助者，欧洲气候基础。
