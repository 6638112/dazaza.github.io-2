Nearby, which lets users buy from multiple local businesses and checkout through one cart, raises $21M Series A led by Redpoint Ventures
附近，让用户从多个本地企业购买并通过一辆购物车进行结账，通过Redpoint Ventures提高2100万系列A.

2021-05-05 22:36:50

Last March, as the physical world went offline, April Underwood watched her neighborhood businesses struggle to survive as foot traffic evaporated and ordering everything from Amazon or Walmart became the national norm. Mom-and-Pop shops took phone orders or attempted the online pivot as Twitter activists supported Main Street by organizing Google spreadsheets of independent shops and restaurants open for businesses. Underwood, who led product development at Slack and Twitter, knew there was a better way.

去年3月，随着物理世界离线，4月御伍德观察了她的邻里企业在脚踏实地蒸发和排序从亚马逊或沃尔玛的一切成为国家常态。 MOM-AND POP商店拿到电话订单或尝试在线枢纽作为Twitter Activists通过组织独立商店和餐馆为企业开放的谷歌电子表格而支持主要街道。伍德伍德，在松懈和推特上领导了产品开发，了解有更好的方法。

A self-described “software person,” Underwood saw these early online attempts to answer “what’s open in my town?” as a huge flashing light. “You can’t just find this stuff on Google Maps or Yelp,” Underwood tells Forbes referring to the small entities. Plus the available tools to help standalone businesses pivot to selling online were lacking or costly.

一个自我描述的“软件人士”，乌克德伍德看到这些早期的在线试图回答“我的镇上的开放？”作为一个巨大的闪光灯。 “你不能只在谷歌地图或yelp上找到这些东西，”德满德伍德告诉Forbes指的是小实体。再加上有助于帮助独立业务销售在线的独立业务的工具缺乏或昂贵。

So in September 2020, she launched Nearby, a platform of hyperlocal marketplaces where users can buy from multiple local businesses online and checkout through one cart. Nearby started small with just 17 merchants in Oakland, California. The startup has since doubled the number of sellers on the platform which has seen more than $100,000 in sales to date. The startup just closed a $21 million Series A round which will help launch the startup into other markets.

因此，在2020年9月，她推出了附近的，一个高级市场平台，用户可以在线购买多个本地企业并通过一辆购物车结账。附近开始于加利福尼亚州奥克兰的仅有17名商家。启动自平台上的卖家数量增加了一倍以上，这些销售额超过100,000美元的销售日期。初创公司刚刚关闭了2100万美元的系列，这将有助于启动启动进入其他市场。

“One thing I really appreciate about April and the team is that they are really focused on the problem as opposed to precisely what the solution has to be,” says Annie Kadavy, managing director of Redpoint Ventures, which led the round. Kadavy, who has known Underwood since her time at Twitter, says she was eager for an opportunity to work together. Nearby also happens to be solving an issue that was top of mind for Kadavy. “It’s pretty easy to see the expansion opportunities,” she says. GV, Obvious Ventures, Moxxie Ventures, Sound Ventures, Cowboy Ventures, and Tina Sharkey, the former founder of Brandless, also participated in the round.

“我真正欣赏的一件事是4月和团队的是，他们真的专注于这个问题，而不是恰恰是解决方案必须是什么，”Redpoint Ventures董事总经理安妮卡达夫说，这是一圈。在Twitter时，在她的时间以来，卡达沃有底层伍德，说她渴望共同努力。附近也恰好解决了卡达亚的头脑之外的问题。 “很容易看到扩张机会，”她说。 GV，明显的企业，Moxxie Ventures，Sound Ventures，牛仔冒险和Tina Sharkey，Friarl Inderless的创始人，也参加了这一轮。

Nearby offers free sign-ups to merchants and covers shipping and logistics. The company takes a fee for each transaction, which Underwood notes is “on the floor” compared to other online marketplaces where businesses can lose up to 30% of their margins because of fees and shipping costs. Austin and Charleston, South Caroline are set to be the next Nearby marketplaces, which the company chose based on the number of nominations made via the platform’s website. Each new location will include a team hired locally as well as its own website.  Currently, 120 locations in the U.S. and beyond have been nominated, including Olney, Maryland which applied through the coordinated effort of 50 people.

附近提供免费注册商家，涵盖运输和物流。该公司对每个交易的费用，伍德笔记是“在地板上”相比，与其他在线市场相比，由于费用和运费，企业可能会失去高达30％的利润率。奥斯汀和查尔斯顿，南卡罗琳被设定为下一个附近的市场，公司根据通过平台网站所取得的提名人数选择。每个新的位置都将包括一支团队，聘用本地以及自己的网站。目前，美国及以后的120个地点被提名，包括通过50人的协调努力应用的马里兰州Olney。

Customers clamoring for Nearby in their neighborhood is no surprise to Underwood. Sure, the pandemic accelerated online spending, but the pivot to the Internet was inevitable. These same shoppers want more options than Amazon, preferably those that support local businesses. A survey conducted by Sitecore found that 40% of consumers want to reduce their spending on Amazon, with more than half of Generation Z looking to shop less on the site overall. Nearby also emerged at a time when small business creation is also on the rise. More than 4.3 million new businesses applied to the Internal Revenue Service for Employee Identification Numbers in 2020, up 18% from the 3.5 million in 2019, with the trend continuing into 2021, more than 440,000 new businesses applied in March alone.

在邻居附近的顾客克切的顾客毫不奇怪。当然，大流行加速在线支出，但互联网的枢轴是不可避免的。这些相同的购物者想要比亚马逊更多的选项，最好是支持当地企业的选项。 Sitecore进行的一项调查发现，40％的消费者希望减少他们在亚马逊的支出，超过一半的发电Z期望在整体场地上少购物。在小型企业创作的时候也在附近出现。超过430万业务适用于2020年的员工识别号码的内部收入服务，从2019年的350万增长18％，趋势持续到2021年，超过440,000名新企业仅在3月份申请。

“We want to make it a no brainer for merchants,” Underwood says about getting businesses — both existing and new — online. For current businesses, it’s about building their customer base. “We are really growing the pie, and not just bringing on their existing customers,” she says. We are also really excited to welcome all those new businesses which will be building their audience for the first time.”

“我们希望让它成为商人的脑子，”德满伍德说，让企业 - 既有现有和新的 - 在线。对于当前的企业，它是关于构建客户群。 “我们真的在成长馅饼，而不仅仅是带领他们现有的客户，”她说。我们也非常兴奋地欢迎所有那些将首次建立观众的新业务。“
