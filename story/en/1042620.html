Apple Co-Founder Wozniak Has a New Startup That Melds Blockchain and Green Tech

Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak has a new company that’s promising energy industry innovation through a virtual currency called “WOZX.”

苹果公司联合创始人史蒂夫·沃兹尼亚克（Steve Wozniak）拥有一家新公司，该公司通过称为“ WOZX”的虚拟货币对能源行业进行创新。

We know, it sounds weird. But Wozniak is hoping the new startup, Efforce, will help stop climate change by promoting “green” energy-efficient technologies.

我们知道，这听起来很奇怪。但是沃兹尼亚克希望新的创业公司Efforce通过推广“绿色”节能技术来帮助阻止气候变化。

“We can save the environment simply by making more energy improvements," Wozniak said in today’s announcement. "We created Efforce to be the first decentralized platform that allows everyone to participate and benefit financially from worldwide energy efficiency projects, and create meaningful environmental change."

“我们可以简单地通过改善能源消耗来节省环境，＆＃34;沃兹尼亚克在今天的公告中说。 ＆＃34;我们创建了Efforce，使其成为第一个去中心化的平台，该平台使每个人都可以参与全球节能项目并从中受益，并创造有意义的环境变化。

So how will this actually work? Well, Efforce is supposed to act as a digital marketplace that companies can use to raise funding for energy-efficiency projects. This might include switching to low-energy LED lighting, or adopting a better manufacturing process to save on electricity.

那么，这实际上如何工作？好吧，Efforce应该充当数字市场，供公司用来为能效项目筹集资金。这可能包括改用低能耗的LED照明，或采用更好的制造工艺以节省电力。

“The companies will then have more available cash to use for other critical projects such as infrastructure or hiring,” said Jacopo Visetti, another co-founder of Efforce.

Efforce的另一位联合创始人Jacopo Visetti说：“然后，这些公司将有更多的可用现金用于其他关键项目，例如基础设施或招聘。”

Specifically, Efforce plans to work with energy service companies ( ESCOs), which specialize in arranging financing for energy-saving projects. Wozniak’s startup will evaluate the investment needed for the projects, calculate the anticipated savings, and then come up with a contract that’ll detail the returns for both the company and investors.

具体来说，Efforce计划与能源服务公司（ESCO）合作，后者专门为节能项目安排融资。沃兹尼亚克（Wozniak）的初创公司将评估项目所需的投资，计算预期的节省额，然后拿出一份合同，详细说明公司和投资者的回报。

“The platform then lists the project for crowd contribution. The participants may buy into the project using fractional or whole WOZX tokens,” Efforce added. The startup will then use special meters to measure the energy savings from the project and log them on the WOZX blockchain. Any savings made will be distributed into the investor's profile as energy credits.

然后，平台将列出该项目以供人群参与。参与者可以使用部分或全部WOZX代币购买该项目。” Efforce补充道。然后，初创公司将使用特殊的仪表来测量项目的节能量并将其记录在WOZX区块链上。所节省的任何款项都将作为能源信用分配到投资者的个人资料中。

"The energy savings generated become a tangible asset in the hands of token holders, who may decide to sell them or use them to reduce their electricity bills," the company's whitepaper says. "Through the wallet, the energy savings obtained over time can be kept and spent to reduce their bill whenever the price of electricity is such as to be convenient for offsetting."

＆＃34;产生的能源节约成为代币持有者手中的有形资产，代币持有者可以决定出售它们或使用它们来减少电费，＆＃34;该公司的白皮书说。 ＆＃34;通过钱包，随着时间的流逝而获得的能源节省可以被保留下来，并用于减少电费，只要电价便于抵销。

Although Wozniak is one of six co-founders of the company, it’s unclear what role he’ll have in leading Efforce. For now, the startup’s website merely lists him as an engineer. However, his name and presence will certainly drive publicity for a company that claims to be already valued at around $1 billion after listing the WOZX token on the cryptocurrency exchange HBTC.

尽管沃兹尼亚克是该公司的六位联合创始人之一，但尚不清楚他在领导Efforce中将扮演什么角色。目前，这家初创公司的网站仅将他列为工程师。然而，他的名字和存在肯定会推动一家公司的宣传，该公司声称在WOZX代币在加密货币交易所HBTC上市后已经估值约10亿美元。

Editor's Note: This story previously mentioned Efforce was the second company Wozniak co-founded since creating Apple in 1976. Wozniak has actually founded several other ventures since then including Woz U, a technical education company.

编者注：前面提到的这个故事是Efforce是Wozniak自1976年创建Apple以来第二家共同创立的公司。Wozniak从那以后实际上还成立了其他几家公司，包括技术教育公司WozU。
