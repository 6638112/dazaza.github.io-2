搜索收入：谷歌增长14%，微软必应下降10%

2020-10-31 13:53:34

Microsoft (Bing) reported earnings on October 27th and Alphabet (Google) reported earnings on on October 29th. Microsoft said that its earnings on Microsoft Advertising, i.e. ads on Bing and other properties, saw a 10% decrease year-over-year. Alphabet (Google) said its revenue was up 14% year-over-year.

微软(Bing)10月27日公布财报，字母表(Google)10月29日公布财报。微软表示，其在微软广告(即必应和其他网站的广告)上的收入同比下降了10%。字母表(Google)称其营收同比增长14%。

From the Microsoft earnings "search advertising revenue excluding traffic acquisition costs decreased 10% (down 11% in constant currency)"

从微软的收益来看，不包括流量获取成本的搜索广告收入下降了10%(按不变货币计算下降了11%)。

Google overall had a 14% increase in earnings but if you look at just Google Search it was an increase of 6.5% but if you add in partners where others use Google Search a 9.7% increase.

谷歌的整体收益增长了14%，但如果你单看谷歌搜索，就会发现增长了6.5%，但如果加上其他使用谷歌搜索的合作伙伴，则增长了9.7%。

It is interesting to see Google's revenues increase in search when Microsoft Advertising and Bing has not. Keep in mind, I am not a financial analyst of any sorts.

有趣的是，在微软广告和必应没有增长的情况下，谷歌在搜索领域的收入却在增长。请记住，我不是任何形式的金融分析师。
