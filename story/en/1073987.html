The Big Here Quiz
这里的大测验

2022-02-19 02:03:52

You live in the big here. Wherever you live, your tiny spot is deeply intertwined within a larger place, imbedded fractal-like into a whole system called a watershed, which is itself integrated with other watersheds into a tightly interdependent biome. At the ultimate level, your home is a cell in an organism called a planet. All these levels interconnect. What do you know about the dynamics of this larger system around you? Most of us are ignorant of this matrix. But it is the biggest interactive game there is. Hacking it is both fun and vital.

你住在这里的大房子里。无论你住在哪里，你的小地方都深深地交织在一个更大的地方，像分形一样嵌入到一个称为分水岭的整体系统中，这个系统本身与其他分水岭整合成一个紧密相互依赖的生物群落。在终极层面上，你的家是一个叫做行星的有机体中的细胞。所有这些层次相互关联。你对你周围这个大系统的动力学了解多少？我们大多数人都不知道这个矩阵。但这是最大的互动游戏。破解它既有趣又重要。

The following exercise in watershed awareness was hatched 50 years ago by Peter Warshall, naturalist extraordinaire. Variations of this list have appeared over the years with additions by Jim Dodge, Peter Berg, and Stephanie Mills among others. In 2003 I added new questions from Warshall and myself, and I edited or altered most of the rest of the questions to make them clearer or more relatable. I posted the revised quiz on my website in 2003, but it was hard to find. I also published the list on page 228 in my book Cool Tools: A Catalog of Possibilities. Rob Walker of Art of Noticing recently reminded me of it, so I am reposting it here. It’s still a work in progress. If you have a universal question you think fits, submit it to me.

50年前，非凡的博物学家彼得·沃沙尔（Peter Warshall）策划了以下关于流域意识的活动。这些年来，该榜单出现了一些变化，其中包括吉姆·道奇、彼得·伯格和斯蒂芬妮·米尔斯。2003年，我添加了来自沃沙尔和我自己的新问题，并编辑或修改了其余大部分问题，使其更清晰或更具相关性。2003年，我在自己的网站上发布了修订后的测验，但很难找到。我还在我的书《酷工具：可能性目录》第228页上公布了这个列表。《注意的艺术》的罗伯·沃克最近让我想起了它，所以我把它转发到这里。这项工作仍在进行中。如果你有一个普遍的问题，你认为合适，提交给我。

I am extremely interested in hearing from anyone who scores a 26 or better on the quiz on their first unassisted-by-Google try.

我非常感兴趣的是，如果有人第一次在没有谷歌帮助的情况下，在测验中取得26分或更好的成绩，我会告诉他们。

30 questions to elevate your awareness (and literacy) of the greater place in which you live:

30个问题来提高你对自己居住的地方的认识（和识字能力）：

1) Point north. 2) What time is sunset today? 3) Trace the water you drink from rainfall to your tap. 4) When you flush, where do the solids go? What happens to the waste water? 5) How many feet (meters) above sea level are you? 6) What spring wildflower is consistently among the first to bloom here? 7) How far do you have to travel before you reach a different watershed? Can you draw the boundaries of yours? 8) Is the soil under your feet, more clay, sand, rock or silt? 9) Before your tribe lived here, what did the previous inhabitants eat and how did they sustain themselves? 10) Name five native edible plants in your neighborhood and the season(s) they are available. 11) From what direction do storms generally come? 12) Where does your garbage go? 13) How many people live in your watershed? 14) Who uses the paper/plastic you recycle from your neighborhood? 15) Point to where the sun sets on the equinox. How about sunrise on the summer solstice? 16) Where is the nearest earthquake fault? When did it last move? 17) Right here, where you are, how deep do you have to drill before you reach water? 18) Which (if any) geological features in your watershed are, or were, especially respected by your community, or considered sacred, now or in the past? 19) How many days is the growing season here (from frost to frost)? 20) Name five birds that live here. Which are migratory and which stay put? 21) What was the total rainfall here last year? 22) Where does the pollution in your air come from? 23) If you live near the ocean, when is high tide today? 24) What primary geological processes or events shaped the land here? 25) Name three wild species that were not found here 500 years ago. Name one exotic species that has appeared in the last 5 years. 26) What minerals are found in the ground here that are (or were) economically valuable? 27) Where does your electric power come from and how is it generated? 28) After the rain runs off your roof, where does it go? 29) Where is the nearest wilderness? When was the last time a fire burned through it? 30) How many days till the moon is full?

1） 指向北方。2） 今天日落几点？3） 追踪你从降雨中喝到水龙头的水。4） 当你冲洗的时候，固体会去哪里？废水会怎么样？5） 你在海平面以上多少英尺（米）？6） 哪一种春天的野花一直是最先在这里开花的？7） 你需要走多远才能到达另一个分水岭？你能划出你的界限吗？8） 你脚下的土壤是粘土、沙子、岩石还是淤泥？9） 在你的部落生活在这里之前，以前的居民吃什么，他们是如何养活自己的？10） 说出你所在社区的五种本地食用植物，以及它们的供应季节。11） 风暴通常来自哪个方向？12） 你的垃圾去哪了？13） 有多少人住在你的分水岭里？14） 谁在使用你从社区回收的纸张/塑料？15） 指向太阳在春分点落下的地方。夏至日出怎么样？16） 最近的地震断层在哪里？它最后一次搬家是什么时候？17） 就在这里，你在哪里，你必须钻多深才能到达水面？18） 你所在流域的哪些（如果有的话）地质特征现在或过去特别受到你所在社区的尊重，或被认为是神圣的？19） 这里的生长季节是多少天（从霜冻到霜冻）？20） 说出五只生活在这里的鸟。哪些是迁徙的，哪些留在原地？21）去年这里的总降雨量是多少？22）你的空气污染来自哪里？23）如果你住在海边，今天什么时候涨潮？24）什么主要的地质作用或事件塑造了这里的土地？25）说出500年前在这里没有发现的三种野生物种。说出一个在过去5年中出现的外来物种。26）在这里的地下发现了哪些（或曾经）具有经济价值的矿物？27）你的电力来自哪里，是如何产生的？28）雨水从屋顶上落下后，雨水会流到哪里？29）最近的荒野在哪里？上次是什么时候发生火灾的？30）离满月还有几天？

The Bigger Here Bonus Questions: 31) What species once found here are known to have gone extinct? 32) What other cities or landscape features on the planet share your latitude? 33) What was the dominant land cover plant here 10,000 years ago? 34) Name two places on different continents that have similar sunshine/rainfall/wind and temperature patterns to here.

这里更大的额外问题是：31）曾经在这里发现的哪些物种已经灭绝？32）地球上还有哪些城市或景观与你的纬度相同？33）一万年前这里主要的土地覆盖植物是什么？34）说出两个不同大陆上与这里有相似日照/降雨/风和温度模式的地方。
