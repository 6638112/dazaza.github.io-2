The I2C host drivers do, in fact, have a de-facto maintainer; it's WolframSang, who also maintains the core I2C subsystem. These include the MAINTAINERSfile itself, naturally. Of the rest, the vast majority are header filesunder include/, most of which probably do have maintainers and shouldbe added to the appropriate entries. A harder case to find is that of files that are covered by a MAINTAINERS entry, but which are not actually maintained by thenamed person; this will happen often with entries that cover entiredirectory trees.
