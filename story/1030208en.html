Jeffrey Katzenberg may be nearing the end of the road with his mobile video streaming service Quibi. He recently tried to sell its catalog of programming to companies such as NBCUniversal and Facebook. Both passed, according to several people familiar with the matter.

杰弗里·卡森伯格(Jeffrey Katzenberg)的移动视频流媒体服务Quibi可能即将走到尽头。他最近试图将其节目目录出售给NBC环球(NBC Universal)和Facebook等公司。据几位知情人士透露，这两件事都通过了。

Meanwhile, Katzenberg has told people in the industry that he may have to shut down the company, according to one person who spoke to him. Internally at Quibi, employees have said important strategy meetings have been cancelled, according to two people familiar with the matter. Some employees have informally been scheduling goodbye drinks, these people said. A Quibi spokeswoman said any talk of Quibi shuttering was “speculative.” Katzenberg referred questions to the spokeswoman.
