Kids from ages 3+ can start playing with intelino just like with a regular toy train. Made compatible with most wooden train sets from BRIO, IKEA, Thomas & Friends and many others, intelino offers new ways to experience play with the classic favorites (universal track adapters sold separately).

3岁以上的孩子可以像玩普通玩具火车一样开始玩intelino。与BRIO，宜家，托马斯和安培的大多数木制火车套件兼容朋友和许多其他人，intelino提供了使用经典收藏夹（通用轨道适配器单独出售）体验游戏的新方法。

The intelino train can read the colors it sees on its path. To control the train's actions, put chains of small colored tiles called Action Snaps along the tracks. Use this play mode to speed the train up or slow it down, get it to steer left or right at track junctions, make it change its movement direction or stop, drop off the attached wagon and more. It's engaging and fun for kids of all ages, and it teaches them basics of coding.

intelino火车可以读取其在道路上看到的颜色。要控制火车的动作，请沿着轨道放置称为“动作捕捉”的彩色小块瓷砖链。使用此播放模式可加快火车的速度或使其减速，使其在路口处向左或向右转向，改变其运动方向或停止，放下连接的货车等等。它为各个年龄段的孩子带来了乐趣和乐趣，并且教了他们编码的基础知识。

The interactive fun extends even further with the free intelino mobile app (available on iOS and Android). Kids get to master their driving skills in Autopilot and Manual drive modes and race trains around the tracks at top speeds of up to 3.3 ft/sec ( 100 cm/sec). The app is loaded with features like displaying the realtime speed of the train and the distance driven, controlling the train's lights and sounds, and more.

免费的intelino移动应用程序（适用于iOS和Android）进一步增强了互动乐趣。孩子们可以在自动驾驶和手动驾驶模式下掌握他们的驾驶技巧，并以最高3.3英尺/秒（100厘米/秒）的最高速度绕着赛道比赛。该应用程序具有显示火车的实时速度和行驶距离，控制火车的灯光和声音等功能。

There are 17 action snap commands that work right out of the box. Want to have even more control of the train? Use the app-based Snap Editor to create custom commands and store them instantly on the train with a press of a button. Custom commands are simple to create and they add another dimension of playing and learning possibilities.

开箱即用有17个动作捕捉命令。想要对火车进行更多控制吗？使用基于应用程序的快照编辑器创建自定义命令，并通过按一下按钮立即将其存储在火车上。自定义命令很容易创建，它们增加了游戏和学习可能性的另一个维度。

As children play with the smart train, they progressively develop confidence and interest in coding and STEM. This naturally prepares them to take another step forward - programming the intelino train with Scratch. Our free intelino extension for Scratch 3.0 helps kids advance their coding skills, from basic to advanced, while playing with the smart train. It is easy, captivating and fun!
