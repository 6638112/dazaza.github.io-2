Google acquired Neverware, makers of CloudReady ChromeOS variant
Google收购了CloudReady ChromeOS变体的制造商Neverware

2020-12-17 07:34:29

This week, Google acquired OS vendor Neverware, makers of the CloudReady distribution of ChromiumOS. ChromiumOS is the fully open source, upstream version of ChromeOS—the sharply limited, cloud-focused operating system which Chromebooks and Chromeboxes run on. The acquisition sounds great on paper—Google certainly has more resources than Neverware, including but not limited to the developer base for ChromeOS itself. According to Neverware's FAQ on the transition, big G will honor all existing license agreements and has no current plans to curtail availability of the free Home edition of CloudReady.

本周，谷歌收购了ChromiumOS的CloudReady发行商的操作系统供应商Neverware。 ChromiumOS是ChromeOS的完全开源的上游版本，它是运行Chromebook和Chromebox的功能非常有限的，以云为中心的操作系统。这项收购听起来很不错-Google当然比Neverware拥有更多的资源，包括但不限于ChromeOS本身的开发人员基础。根据Neverware关于过渡的常见问题解答，big G将遵守所有现有的许可协议，并且目前没有计划减少CloudReady的免费家庭版的可用性。

All of this good news comes with "at this time" caveats on every bullet point, unfortunately—which strikes us as a bit unnerving, coming as it does immediately after Red Hat announced that it was  killing off CentOS Linux and replacing it with CentOS Stream. While there is no actual connection between Google and Red Hat, the relationship between CloudReady and Google's own ChromeOS was more or less an exact replica of that between RHEL and CentOS. The flagship distribution in both cases is open source, and the source code is available—but compiling it into a working product is a significant undertaking, and that final, working product is not available freely from the vendor.

目前，所有这些好消息都带有＆＃34;不幸的是，每个要点都有警告-这让我们有些不安，就像Red Hat宣布杀死CentOS Linux并将其替换为CentOS Stream之后立即采取的行动一样。尽管Google与Red Hat之间没有实际的联系，但CloudReady与Google自己的ChromeOS之间的关系或多或少地完全是RHEL与CentOS之间的关系的真实副本。在这两种情况下，旗舰发行版都是开源的，并且源代码可用，但是将其编译成可用的产品是一项艰巨的任务，并且最终的可用产品不能从供应商处免费获得。

CloudReady, like CentOS before it, takes the freely available source of its parent distribution and turns it into a freely available and immediately functional clone.

与之前的CentOS一样，CloudReady可以获取其父发行版的免费资源，并将其转变为免费可用且立即可用的克隆。

The analogy between CentOS and CloudReady does break down a bit when the question of hardware comes into play. RHEL and CentOS both installed on the same generic x86 hardware—but Google ChromeOS is only distributed on Google-sanctioned Chromebooks and Chromeboxes, with sharply limited life cycles and support windows. CloudReady is intended to spread the ChromeOS experience beyond those hardware bounds and short lifeycles—it can be installed on any arbitrary PC hardware, and it doesn't automatically "expire" when that hardware reaches a certain age. For schools with aging fleets of Windows 7-based PCs that wouldn't do well with Windows 10, CloudReady offers a way to modernize the operating system without refreshing the hardware itself.

当硬件问题开始起作用时，CentOS和CloudReady之间的类比确实破裂了。 RHEL和CentOS都安装在相同的通用x86硬件上-但是Google Chrome操作系统仅在受Google认可的Chromebook和Chromebox上分发，生命周期和支持窗口非常有限。 CloudReady旨在将ChromeOS的体验扩展到这些硬件范围和短生命周期之外-它可以安装在任何任意PC硬件上，并且不会自动过期。当硬件达到一定年龄时。对于拥有老化的，基于Windows 7的PC机群无法与Windows 10完美结合的学校，CloudReady提供了一种无需更新硬件本身即可现代化操作系统的方法。

While the primary income stream for Neverware comes from educational and enterprise customers who pay per-device license fees, the company also offers a freely available Home version. The Home edition of CloudReady allows anyone to turn an old PC (or a new, very cheap PC) into a Chromebook-like system.

虽然Neverware的主要收入来自教育和企业客户，他们需要按设备许可费付费，但该公司还提供了免费的家庭版。借助CloudReady的家庭版，任何人都可以将旧PC（或新的非常便宜的PC）变成类似Chromebook的系统。

The free Home version cannot be managed with the Google Admin console, like Chromebooks in education or enterprise environments would be, but is otherwise a functional ChromeOS substitute, including regular, automatic security upgrades. Education and Enterprise CloudReady variants take this a step further, being fully manageable with Chrome Management and Google Admin console.

免费的家庭版无法像教育或企业环境中的Chromebook那样使用Google管理控制台进行管理，但可以替代功能的ChromeOS，包括常规的自动安全升级。 Education和Enterprise CloudReady变体更进一步，可通过Chrome管理和Google管理控制台进行全面管理。
