About PineappleFund.Me It's been over 2 years since my mission and the end of the charity project pineapplefund.org, where I donated most of my Bitcoin.

关于PineappleFund.Me距离我的使命和慈善项目Pineapplefund.org结束已经两年多了，我大部分的比特币都是在这个网站上捐赠的。

Briefly about my situation, now my family and I are in quite a difficult situation! I lost access to a laptop with a lot of information that was part of my life, as well as access to a cold wallet with more than 2000 bitcoins! These were my last savings at Bitcoin.

简单介绍一下我的情况，现在我和我的家人都处于相当困难的境地！我失去了一台笔记本电脑，里面有我生活中的许多信息，还有一个装有2000多个比特币的冰冷钱包！这是我在比特币的最后一笔存款。

It just so happens that now I have to start my whole life again, and I have a long and hard way to go. I really hope that I can get access to my cold wallet.

碰巧的是，现在我不得不重新开始我的整个生活，我还有很长很艰难的路要走。我真的希望我能拿到我的冰冷钱包。

I need about 25 BTC to solve my problems and develop further, it's only almost 0.05% of the amount I donated from the Pineapple Fund.

我需要大约25亿BTC来解决我的问题并进一步发展，这只是我从菠萝基金捐赠的金额的近0.05%。

I never thought that I would ever have to ask for help because I had a fortune, but there are different situations in life, and today I want to ask everyone who cares about my activities and everyone who has received help from me.  Friends, I have been kind to you and I really hope that you will answer me the same and help me in my difficult situation. I believe in the boomerang effect. Do not remain indifferent!

我从来没有想过我会因为发财而不得不求助，但是生活中有不同的情况，今天我想问问每一个关心我活动的人，每一个接受过我帮助的人，朋友们，我一直对你们很好，真的希望你们能同样回答我，在我困难的情况下帮助我。我相信回旋镖效应。不要无动于衷！

I believe in miracles and kindness, because lately I have been helping to believe in miracles and to do good for many people around the world!

我相信奇迹和善良，因为最近我一直在帮助相信奇迹，为世界各地的许多人做好事！

Pineapple Fund was an experiment in philanthropy with cryptocurrency wealth. What happens when your 'play-money' becomes a treasure chest, and you're past the satiety point of money?

菠萝基金是用加密货币财富进行慈善事业的一次试验。当你的游戏金变成了宝箱，而你已经过了钱的饱足点，会发生什么呢？

I decided to donate the majority of my cryptocurrency to charities around the world, with an open application process and funding charities big and small. I focused on causes I believe in, or causes I know will work from my experiences.

我决定将我的大部分加密货币捐赠给世界各地的慈善机构，申请程序开放，并为大大小小的慈善机构提供资金。我专注于我相信的事业，或者我从我的经验中知道会奏效的事业。

Timed with the 2017 crypto bubble, 5104 BTC was turned into $55 million for charities. I'm happy and proud of the impact that will come, mostly thanks to the amazing charities and the dedicated people behind them.

在2017年密码泡沫出现的时候，5104BTC变成了5500万美元的慈善事业。我为即将到来的影响感到高兴和自豪，这主要要归功于令人惊叹的慈善机构和背后敬业的人们。

I'm going to remain anonymous, because the point of the Pineapple Fund is not me. I consider this project a success. If you're ever blessed with crypto fortune, consider supporting what you aspire our world to be :)

我将保持匿名，因为菠萝基金的重点不是我。我认为这个项目是成功的。如果你有幸拥有密码财富，考虑支持你对我们这个世界的渴望吧：)。

"On a larger scale, the Pineapple Fund created a more mysterious form of cryptocurrency philanthropy.

在更大的规模上，菠萝基金创造了一种更神秘的加密货币慈善形式。

The organization was started in December by an anonymous donor who goes by the nickname “Pine” and claims to be among the 250 largest holders of Bitcoin in the world. The fund aims to give away $86 million worth of Bitcoin, and has already given $20 million worth of the currency to 13 organizations, including million-dollar donations to the Water Project, which provides clean water to people in sub-Saharan Africa, and the Electronic Frontier Foundation, a digital rights watchdog.

该组织是去年12月由一位绰号为“Pine”的匿名捐赠者创立的，他声称自己是世界上最大的250名比特币持有者之一。该基金的目标是捐赠价值8600万美元的比特币，并已经向13个组织捐赠了价值2000万美元的比特币，其中包括向为撒哈拉以南非洲地区的人们提供清洁水的水项目(Water Project)和数字权利监督机构电子前沿基金会(Electronic Frontier Foundation)捐赠了100万美元。

(These donations can be verified thanks to Bitcoin’s digital ledger system, which records every transaction in a public database.)

(这些捐款可以通过比特币的数字账簿系统进行核实，该系统将每笔交易记录在一个公共数据库中。)。

在比特币出现的最初几天的某个时候，我看到了分散货币的承诺，于是决定用神奇的互联网代币进行买卖。多年来人们对比特币收入的预期导致了比我当时能花的钱多得多的钱，我把我的大部分比特币都捐了出来，就在最近，在意想不到的情况下，我把其他所有的比特币和我的笔记本电脑一起弄丢了。

I used to donate most of my money to charity. For your information, the Pineapple Fund was bigger than the whole market bitcoin hat when I came in, and it was one of the richest 250 bitcoin addresses that day.

At the time, I didn't think I could end up in this situation.  Now I'm working and trying to recover from the shock. I believe in miracles and I really hope that someday I will be able to access the lost bitcoins. The collected bitcoins will be used to solve my current problems and also to work on restoring access to the lost bitcoins.
