SSC以每小时316英里的新量产车速度纪录夺得布加迪的桂冠

2020-10-19 23:05:50

If you want a car that can go really, really, really fast, forget about ordering that Bugatti and give the people at SSC North America a call. On October 10, racing driver Oliver Webb got behind the wheel of one of SSC's new Tuatara hypercars and, on a closed stretch of Nevada State Route 160, reached a top speed of 331.15mph (532.93km/h). When averaged with his 301.07mph (484.53km/h) run in the opposite direction, SSC North America set a new world speed record for production vehicles at 316.11mph (508.73km/h).

如果你想要一辆跑得真的，真的，真的很快的车，忘了订那辆布加迪吧，给SSC北美公司的人打个电话吧。10月10日，赛车手奥利弗·韦伯(Oliver Webb)驾驶着一辆SSC'；的新型Tuatara超级跑车，在内华达州160号公路的一段封闭路段上，最高时速达到331.15英里/小时(532.93公里/小时)。当他的301.07英里/小时(484.53公里/小时)的平均速度以相反的方向运行时，SSC北美公司创造了生产车辆的新的世界速度纪录，为316.11英里/小时(508.73公里/小时)。

Until now, the record for the world's fastest production car belonged to Bugatti, which claimed it in 2019. Andy Wallace was behind the wheel for that attempt, driving a 1,578hp (1,177kW) Bugatti Chiron Super Sport to top speed of 304.77mph (490.48km/h) at Volkswagen's massive test track in Ehra-Lessien, Germany. The SSC Tuatara packs even more power than the Chiron: 1,750hp (1,305kW) of power on E85, and it all gets sent to just the rear wheels, too. The Tuatara also has a more slippery shape, with a smaller frontal area than the Chiron (1.672m 2 vs 2.072m 2) and a lower drag coefficient (0.279 vs 0.319).

到目前为止，世界上生产速度最快的汽车的记录属于布加迪，布加迪在2019年声称它是世界上生产速度最快的汽车。安迪·华莱士坐在方向盘后面，驾驶一辆1,578马力(1,177千瓦)的布加迪奇龙超级跑车，在德国埃拉-莱辛的大众大型测试赛道上达到了304.77英里/小时(490.48公里/小时)的最高时速。SSC Tuatara的功率比CHIRON更大：E85的功率为1750马力(1305千瓦)，而且所有的功率都只送到后轮上。图阿塔拉的形状也更滑，其锋面面积(1.672m2比2.072m2)和较低的阻力系数(0.279比0.319)要小得多。

Those were conscious decisions during the Tuatara's design—SSC's founder Jerod Shelby has had his sights on the production speed record for some years now. "My goal was always to beat this record by such a substantial amount that maybe it's going to stand in for a little while. I felt like that's what McLaren did back in the late nineties, and they held that record a long time because they just smashed the record. That was my dream in a perfect world," Shelby said.

这些都是图阿塔拉在设计过程中的有意识的决定--SSC的创始人杰罗德·谢尔比多年来一直将目光投向生产速度纪录。我的目标一直是以巨大的优势打破这一纪录，也许它会在一段时间内顶住这一纪录。我觉得这就是迈凯轮在90年代末所做的，他们保持了很长一段时间的纪录，因为他们刚刚打破了纪录。谢尔比说，那是我在一个完美世界里的梦想。
