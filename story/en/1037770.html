Samsung finally starts its Android 11 rollout, three months after release
三星发布3个月后终于开始推出Android 11

2020-12-05 03:23:36

Android 11 came out about three months ago, and that means it's finally time for Samsung to get its act together and start shipping the OS out. Samsung announced on its company blog that the Galaxy S20, S20+, and S20 Ultra would all start getting Android 11 right now, provided you're in "Korea, the US, and most markets in Europe."

Android 11大约在三个月前问世，这意味着三星终于有时间共同行动起来，开始发售该操作系统。三星在其公司博客上宣布，只要您位于韩国，美国和欧洲大多数市场，Galaxy S20，S20 +和S20 Ultra都将立即开始使用Android 11。 ;

A three-month delay on the latest Android release is on par with what Samsung did last year for Android 10, which, at the time, was a record pace for the company. Of course, Samsung's lineup is more than just the S20, and the company's official timeline has the Note20, Z Fold 2, and Z Flip waiting until January. Samsung's best-selling devices are all from the midrange "Galaxy A" line, and those are getting updated anywhere from March to June, so anywhere from six to nine months after Android 11's release.

最新发布的Android版本推迟了三个月，与三星去年在Android 10上所做的一样，当时这是该公司创纪录的速度。当然，三星的产品线不只是S20，而且该公司的官方时间表中还有Note20，Z Fold 2和Z Flip等到1月。三星最畅销的设备全部来自中端的GalaxyA。线，并且这些更新会在3月到6月的任何时间进行更新，因此会在Android 11发布后的6到9个月的任何时间更新。

After the absolutely massive Android 10 release and a year plagued with COVID issues, Google didn't pack a huge amount of changes into Android 11. The notification panel has a new persistent media player, a history section, and support for floating bubble notifications. There are new one-time permission approvals for the microphone, camera, and location, new emojis, more work toward modularity, and easier autofill. Of course, on a Samsung phone, this is all getting paved over with a custom UI—Samsung's "One UI"—and that skin is getting updated along with Android. Android Police has a great hands-on of the beta, showing that most of the Android 11 features are intact, along with things like a new volume slider UI and a few other light touches. As always, rollouts can take weeks to hit your device, so even if you have a Galaxy S20, don't expect the update right away. It's rolling out now to a small population, which will slowly increase while Samsung waits to hear about any problems. If you don't have the update notification yet, keep waiting.

在绝对庞大的Android 10版本发布以及一年困扰COVID问题的一年之后，Google并未在Android 11中进行大量更改。通知面板具有一个新的持久性媒体播放器，一个历史记录部分，并支持浮动气泡通知。麦克风，摄像头和位置获得了新的一次性许可批准，新的表情符号，在模块化方面的工作更多，自动填充更轻松。当然，在三星手机上，所有这些都通过自定义UI（三星的一个UI）铺平了道路，并且皮肤也随着Android一起更新。 Android Police具有出色的Beta版动手功能，表明大多数Android 11功能均完好无损，并且具有新的音量滑块UI和其他一些轻巧的功能。与往常一样，首次展示可能需要数周时间才能影响您的设备，因此即使您拥有Galaxy S20，也不要指望立即进行更新。现在它正在向一小部分人推广，而在三星等待听到任何问题时，它会慢慢增加。如果您还没有更新通知，请继续等待。
