The FreeBSD CI team maintains the continuous integration systemof the FreeBSD project. The CI system firstly checks the committedchanges can be successfully built, then performs various tests andanalysis over the newly built results. The artifacts from thosebuilds are archived in the artifact server for further testing anddebugging needs. The CI team members examine the failing builds andunstable tests and work with the experts in that area to fix thecode or adjust test infrastructure. The details of these effortsare available in the weeklyCI reports.
