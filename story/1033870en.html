把软盘交给星际飞船(2013)

2020-11-08 23:54:05

I had this old floppy disk left over and on a website I saw how to make a Starship out of it. So I wanted to give it a shot. All you need is the floppy disk and a pair of scissors. Start by taking the floppy disk apart. Do not bend the metal cover. Cut the cover like this. Now fold the wings outwards. You can also fold the wingtips up. Remove the "floppy" part to keep the metal disk. The metal disk will become the "saucer". Hold it next to the rest to see where to cut away bits of the "neck". The cut "neck". Put the saucer on... ... and fold the metal strips around it to hold it in place. Voila, there's your starship! :-)

我把这张旧软盘留了下来，在一个网站上我看到了如何用它制作一艘星际飞船。所以我想试一试。你所需要的只是一张软盘和一把剪刀。从拆开软盘开始。不要弯曲金属盖。像这样剪封面。现在把翅膀向外折叠。你也可以把翼尖折叠起来。取出软盘部件以保留金属磁盘。金属碟子将成为碟子。把它放在其他的旁边，看看哪里可以剪掉脖子上的一小块。割伤的脖子。把茶碟放上来……。..。然后用金属条把它折叠起来，把它固定在适当的位置。瞧，你的星际飞船来了！：-)
