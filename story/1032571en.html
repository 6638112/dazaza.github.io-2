Odaseva是一家总部位于法国的数据保护服务提供商，面向大规模Salesforce客户，由Eight Roads Ventures牵头筹集2500万美元的B系列资金

2020-11-01 10:26:27

French SaaS company Odaseva has raised $25 million in Series B funding to continue growing its data governance platform for enterprise. The round was led by Eight Roads Ventures with new investor F-Prime Capital and included previous backers Partech, Salesforce Ventures and Serena.

法国SaaS公司Odaseva在B系列融资中筹集了2500万美元，以继续发展其面向企业的数据治理平台。这轮融资由八路风险投资公司(Eight Roads Ventures)牵头，新投资者为F-Prime Capital，其中包括之前的支持者Partech、Salesforce Ventures和Serena。

“The Odaseva team started their journey focused on a major problem for large-scale Salesforce customers, helping them through their move to the cloud,” explains Reza Malekzadeh of Partech.

Partech的Reza Malekzadeh解释说：“Odaseva团队开始了他们的旅程，专注于大规模Salesforce客户的一个主要问题，帮助他们完成向云计算的迁移。”

Since the company launched in 2012, Odaseva has broadened the scope of data protection for large-scale Salesforce customers, ‘building their trust in the cloud’. Customers use Odaseva’s technology for everything from backing up and archiving data to ensuring data privacy.

自2012年公司成立以来，Odaseva为大规模Salesforce客户拓宽了数据保护范围，“建立了他们对云的信任”。客户将Odaseva的技术用于从备份和归档数据到确保数据隐私的一切。

The company claims three consecutive years of 100 percent revenue growth, driven by its expansion in North America and other international markets. During that time, the company doubled its headcount and added enterprises such as Dropbox, Boston Scientific and Manulife to a customer list that includes Heineken, Schneider Electric, Robert Half and Toyota.

该公司声称，在北美和其他国际市场扩张的推动下，该公司连续三年实现了100%的收入增长。在此期间，该公司的员工人数翻了一番，并将Dropbox、波士顿科学公司和宏利等企业添加到包括喜力、施耐德电气、Robert Half和丰田在内的客户名单中。

“With the market potential, growth profile and unit economics, it is no wonder that Odaseva is one of the very few companies who managed to attract a US investor this year,” commented Marie Brayer, partner at Serena. The Series B brings the French company’s total funding to $40 million. With the fresh funding, the team will pursue a few growth goals: further product development; hiring in San Francisco, Sydney and Paris; and more partnerships.

Serena合伙人玛丽·布雷耶(Marie Brayer)评论说：“凭借市场潜力、增长状况和单位经济效益，奥达塞娃成为今年成功吸引美国投资者的极少数公司之一也就不足为奇了。”B系列使这家法国公司的总融资达到4000万美元。有了新的资金，该团队将追求几个增长目标：进一步的产品开发；在旧金山、悉尼和巴黎招聘；以及更多的合作伙伴关系。
