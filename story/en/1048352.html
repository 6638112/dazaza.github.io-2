Let them fight! Mortal Kombat red-band trailer gives fans what they want
让他们打架！ 真人快打红乐队预告片为歌迷提供了他们想要的东西

2021-02-19 04:01:14

Warner Bros. has released the red-band trailer for its forthcoming Mortal Kombat reboot, which had long languished in development hell. A Russian-dubbed version of the trailer initially leaked on reddit, according to Eurogamer, before being pulled. It's basically a nonstop, blood-soaked fighting fest, featuring all the fan favorites from the popular video game franchise.

华纳兄弟公司（Warner Bros.）为即将到来的《真人快打》（Mortal Kombat）重新启动发布了红带预告片，该预告片长期以来一直困扰着开发领域。根据Eurogamer的说法，俄罗斯配音版的拖车最初在reddit上泄漏，然后才被拉出。它基本上是不间断的，鲜血淋淋的格斗巨星，汇集了热门视频游戏系列中所有粉丝的最爱。

Midway Games released the first Mortal Kombat game in 1992, which proved hugely influential for  many a teenager who frequented video arcades during that era. There have been 11 main games and multiple spinoffs in the franchise thus far, all set in a fictional universe of eight different realms. The greatest warriors from the various realms must compete in a series of Mortal Kombat tournaments to conquer other realms.

Midway Games于1992年发布了第一款真人快打游戏，事实证明，该游戏对那个时代经常访问视频游戏厅的许多青少年具有巨大的影响力。迄今为止，该系列游戏共有11个主要游戏和多个衍生产品，所有游戏都设置在一个由八个不同领域组成的虚构世界中。来自各个领域的最伟大的战士必须参加一系列的真人快打比赛来征服其他领域。

The popularity of the game soon brought Hollywood knocking for a film adaptation. The original plan was for a three-picture franchise, beginning with 1995's Mortal Kombat, followed in 1997 by Mortal Kombat: Annihilation. But the sequel bombed badly, both critically and at the box office, so the planned third film was indefinitely shelved. There were a couple of animated films released direct-to-video— Mortal Kombat: The Journey Begins (1995) and Mortal Kombat Legends: Scorpion's Revenge (2020)—as well as a spinoff comic book series and even a card game.

游戏的受欢迎程度很快就使好莱坞为改编电影而大吃一惊。最初的计划是制作三张图片的专营权，从1995年的《真人快打》开始，然后在1997年推出《真人快打：歼灭》。但是续集在票房和票房上都遭到严重炸弹袭击，因此计划中的第三部电影被无限期搁置。有几部直接播放视频的动画电影，如《真人快打：旅程开始》（1995年）和《真人快打传奇：蝎子的复仇》（2020年），还有一部衍生漫画集，甚至还有一张卡片游戏。

The shelved third film underwent multiple script rewrites and cast changes over the years, a bankrupt Midway Games found itself embroiled in a lawsuit, and in the end, Warner Bros. bought up most of Midway's remaining assets, including the rights to Mortal Kombat. Director Kevin Tancharoen made a pitch to the studio to make a full-length feature by creating a short film in 2010 ( Mortal Kombat: Rebirth), which Warner Bros. approved. But production was delayed due to budget constraints, and Tancharoen eventually left the project.

多年来，被搁置的第三部电影进行了多次剧本重写并进行了更改，破产的Midway Games发现自己卷入了诉讼，最后，华纳兄弟公司收购了Midway剩余资产的大部分，包括真人快打。导演凯文·坦查罗恩（Kevin Tancharoen）向制片厂投稿，准备在2010年制作一部短片（真人快打：重生）来制作完整片，这部影片得到了华纳兄弟公司的批准。但是由于预算限制，生产被推迟了，Tancharoen最终离开了该项目。

It wasn't until James Wan ( Insidious, Aquaman) came on board as producer in 2015 that the reboot movie finally got off the ground, with a planned R rating, the better to showcase the game's famous fatalities on the big screen. He tapped Simon McQuoid to direct the film. More shooting delays followed, and then the COVID-19 pandemic hit, but at long last, Mortal Kombat is almost here.

直到2015年James Wan（Insidious，Aquaman）出任制片人时，重启电影才终于正式上线，并计划R等级，以更好地展示游戏中的著名死亡消息。大屏幕。他拍了西蒙·麦奎德（Simon McQuoid）导演这部电影。随后出现了更多的拍摄延迟，然后大流行COVID-19，但最终，真人快打几乎快到了。

MMA fighter Cole Young, accustomed to taking a beating for money, is unaware of his heritage—or why Outworld's Emperor Shang Tsung has sent his best warrior, Sub-Zero, an otherworldly Cryomancer, to hunt Cole down. Fearing for his family's safety, Cole goes in search of Sonya Blade at the direction of Jax, a Special Forces Major who bears the same strange dragon marking Cole was born with. Soon, he finds himself at the temple of Lord Raiden, an Elder God and the protector of Earthrealm, who grants sanctuary to those who bear the mark. Here, Cole trains with experienced warriors Liu Kang, Kung Lao and rogue mercenary Kano, as he prepares to stand with Earth’s greatest champions against the enemies of Outworld in a high stakes battle for the universe. But will Cole be pushed hard enough to unlock his arcana—the immense power from within his soul—in time to save not only his family, but to stop Outworld once and for all?

习惯于为钱而战的MMA战斗机年轻人科尔（Cole Young）不知道自己的传承，也不知道为何外族的尚宗皇帝派出他最好的武士Sub-Zero（超凡脱俗的冰冻巫师）追捕科尔。由于担心家人的安全，科尔在特种部队少校杰克斯（Jax）的方向上寻找索妮娅·布拉德（Sonya Blade），他生有与科尔相同的奇怪龙标记。不久，他发现自己来到了雷登勋爵的殿堂，雷登勋爵是一位上古之神，也是大地之王的保护者，他为那些拥有印记的人提供了庇护所。在这里，科尔准备与经验丰富的勇士刘康，功劳和流氓雇佣兵卡诺一起训练，他准备与地球上最伟大的冠军一较高下，与外星人的敌人站在一起，为宇宙进行高额赌注。但是，科尔是否会被推到足够大的力量来释放他的奥秘-来自他内心的巨大力量-及时挽救他的家人，并一劳永逸地制止Outworld？

Ars' own Aurich Lawson gave the most recent MK video game (released in 2019)  a mixed review, declaring it "a visual tour de force that can be a little overly gross and grindy feeling," although it was also surprisingly story-rich. This trailer, however, is light on story, heavy on combat and gore, including what looks like Sub-Zero's signature spine rip finish. The iceman gets rather a lot of screen time, in fact, even freezing an opponent's gushing blood into a dagger to finish him off. Liu Kang transforms into a fire dragon, and Scorpion gets to utter his signature line ("Get over here!").
