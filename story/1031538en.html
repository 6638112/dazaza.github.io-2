Netflix正在开发一部真人版的“刺客信条”(Assassin‘s Creed)节目

2020-10-28 00:50:39

The franchise jumps around in history, telling the story of a secret society of assassins with “genetic memory” and their centuries-long battle the knights templar. It has sold 155 million games worldwide and was also turned into a nearly incomprehensible 2016 film starring Michael Fassbender and Marion Cotillard, which underperformed at the box office.

这部系列在历史中跳跃，讲述了一个拥有“基因记忆”的秘密刺客社团以及他们与圣殿骑士团长达数百年的战斗。它在全球售出了1.55亿款游戏，还被拍成了一部几乎令人费解的2016年电影，由迈克尔·法斯宾德(Michael Fassbender)和马里恩·歌迪亚(Marion Cotillard)主演，票房表现不佳。

The companies say that they’re currently looking for a showrunner. Jason Altman and Danielle Kreinik of Ubisoft’s film and television division will serve as executive producers. (In addition to working on adaptations of Ubisoft’s intellectual property, the publisher is also involved in the Apple TV+ industry comedy “Mythic Quest.”)

这两家公司表示，他们目前正在寻找一名节目制作人。育碧影视部门的杰森·奥特曼(Jason Altman)和丹妮尔·克莱尼克(Danielle Kreinik)将担任执行制片人。(除了致力于对育碧知识产权的改编，该出版商还参与了Apple TV+行业喜剧《神话探索》。)。

“We’re excited to partner with Ubisoft and bring to life the rich, multilayered storytelling that Assassin’s Creed is beloved for,” said Netflix’s vice president of original series Peter Friedlander in a statement. “From its breathtaking historical worlds and massive global appeal as one of the best selling video game franchises of all time, we are committed to carefully crafting epic and thrilling entertainment based on this distinct IP and provide a deeper dive for fans and our members around the world to enjoy.”

Netflix原创系列剧副总裁彼得·弗里德兰德(Peter Friedlander)在一份声明中表示：“我们很高兴能与育碧合作，将”刺客信条“(Assassin‘s Creed)广受喜爱的丰富、多层次的故事讲述带入生活。”从其令人叹为观止的历史世界和作为有史以来最畅销的视频游戏特许经营之一的巨大全球吸引力来看，我们致力于基于这一独特的IP精心制作史诗般的和惊险的娱乐节目，并为世界各地的粉丝和我们的会员提供更深层次的享受。“。

It sounds like there could be follow-up shows as well, with the announcement saying that Netflix and Ubisoft will “tap into the iconic video game’s trove of dynamic stories with global mass appeal for adaptations of live action, animated, and anime series.”

听起来可能还会有后续节目，声明称Netflix和育碧将“利用这款标志性的视频游戏的动态故事宝库，这些故事在全球范围内具有大众吸引力，可以改编成真人版、动画片和动画片。”

Netflix recently placed an eight-episode order for “Resident Evil,” another video game franchise that was previously adapted for the big screen. And it also had a big hit with its adaptation of “The Witcher,” which is based on a fantasy book series that was popularized via video games.

Netflix最近订购了另一个视频游戏系列“生化危机”(Resident Evil)，共八集，此前曾被改编为大屏幕游戏。它还凭借改编自奇幻系列小说的“巫师”大获成功，该系列小说通过视频游戏广为流传。
