Strange object recaptured by Earth is 1966 booster rocket from Surveyor 2
被地球重新捕获的奇怪物体是来自Surveyor 2的1966年助推火箭

2020-12-09 05:05:20

Fifty-four years ago, NASA launched the Surveyor 2, an uncrewed mission to explore the surface of the Moon. Alas, the spacecraft went into a tumble en route after a failed course-correction burn, and it slammed into the lunar surface at 2.7 kilometers per second. But the rocket booster used during its launch followed a different trajectory into space and has now begun orbiting the Earth. That's the conclusion of astronomers who have been studying 2020 SO, an unusual object first spotted this past August.

五十四年前，NASA发射了Surveyor 2，这是一项无任务的探索月球表面的任务。 course，航天器在经过一次不正确的航向校正燃烧后进入了一次跌落路线，并以每秒2.7公里的速度撞入月球表面。但是，在发射过程中使用的火箭助推器沿着不同的轨迹进入太空，现在已经开始绕地球轨道飞行。这就是一直在研究2020 SO的天文学家的结论，今年8月首次发现了一个不寻常的物体。

NASA's Surveyor program was designed to demonstrate the feasibility of launching, communicating with, controlling, and landing an uncrewed spacecraft on the Moon, thereby paving the way for later crewed missions. Surveyor 1, launched on May 30, 1966, was a resounding success, easily meeting its primary objectives, while also returning many images of the lunar surface and critical engineering data. So NASA's hopes were high for building on that success with the second mission. The intent was to land Surveyor 2 on the Moon just east of its predecessor to demonstrate the feasibility of an oblique approach and landing.

NASA的Surveyor程序旨在演示在月球上发射，与之通信，控制其以及将其降落的可行性，从而为以后的载人飞行铺平道路。 1966年5月30日发射的Surveyor 1获得了巨大成功，轻松实现了其主要目标，同时还返回了许多月球表面图像和关键工程数据。因此，NASA希望在第二次飞行任务中取得成功，就寄予厚望。目的是在其前身以东的月球上测量2号测量师，以证明倾斜进近和着陆的可行性。

After a series of minor delays, Surveyor 2 launched successfully on September 20, 1966, at 7:31:59.8 EST. The launch vehicle was an Atlas-Centaur rocket using liquid hydrogen and liquid oxygen as propellants. Once the Centaur's engines were shut down and the spacecraft had coasted for 66 seconds, Surveyor 2 departed from spent Centaur. The rocket expelled its residual propellants to set a safe distance between itself and the Surveyor 2, giving it a trajectory that would miss the Moon by a good 5,675 kilometers and send the rocket into a solar orbit. That Centaur rocket is what astronomers have confirmed to be 2020 SO.

经过一系列小小的延误后，Surveyor 2于1966年9月20日美国东部标准时间7：31：59.8成功发射。运载火箭是使用液态氢和液态氧作为推进剂的Atlas-Centaur火箭。一旦半人马座的引擎关闭，航天器滑行了66秒钟，验船师2离开了用过的半人马座。火箭将其剩余的推进剂驱逐出去，以设定其自身与Surveyor 2的安全距离，从而使其轨迹偏离月球约5675公里，并将其送入太阳轨道。天文学家已经确认那颗半人马火箭是2020年。

Everything seemed to be going smoothly for Surveyor 2. Its trajectory toward the Moon was almost perfectly on target, with just a light burn needed for a course correction 16.5 hours after launch. That's when things went horribly wrong. The craft's Vernier Engine 3 failed to ignite along with the others, sending Surveyor 2 tumbling through space. NASA scientists tried to get the engine started 39 times before accepting defeat. That's when they tried to glean what useful data they could from a series of engineering tests before Surveyor 2 crashed into the lunar surface on September 23, southeast of the crater Copernicus.

对于Surveyor 2来说，一切似乎都进行得很顺利。它朝月球的轨迹几乎完全对准了目标，发射后16.5小时只需稍加燃烧即可进行航向校正。那是当事情变得非常糟糕的时候。飞船的游标引擎3未能与其他引擎一起点燃，从而使Surveyor 2在太空中翻滚。在接受失败之前，美国宇航局的科学家试图使引擎启动39次。就是这样，当他们试图在9月23日“勘测员2”坠入月球表面（哥白尼山的东南部）之前，通过一系列工程测试可以收集到哪些有用数据。

NASA never officially determined the cause of the engine's failure to ignite, although an issue with the flow of MON-10 oxidizer seems to be the culprit. There were five more Surveyor missions, four of them successful, before NASA switched its focus to crewed exploration of the Moon.

尽管MON-10氧化剂的流量问题似乎是罪魁祸首，但NASA从未正式确定发动机未能点火的原因。在NASA将重点转移到乘员探索月球之前，又进行了五次Surveyor任务，其中四次成功。

Meanwhile, the spent Centaur rocket continued on its merry way through space and was pretty much forgotten—at least until August of this year, when astronomers using the Panoramic Survey Telescope and Rapid Response System (Pan-STARRS) in Hawaii spotted a mysterious object in a solar orbit that was due to pass close by the Earth. The Pan-STARRS observatory is a key instrument in detecting fast-moving objects like new asteroids. But this new object, dubbed 2020 SO, was moving too slowly to be an asteroid, and it also had a rather strange orbit.

同时，用完的半人马座火箭继续沿太空飞驰，几乎被人们遗忘了-至少直到今年8月，当时夏威夷的天文学家使用全景观测望远镜和快速反应系统（Pan-STARRS）在太空中发现了一个神秘物体。即将通过地球的太阳轨道。 Pan-STARRS天文台是探测快速移动物体（例如新小行星）的关键工具。但是这个被称为2020 SO的新天体的移动速度太慢而无法成为小行星，而且它的轨道也很奇怪。

Paul Chodas, who manages JPL's Center for Near Earth Object Studies, was among the many scientists eager to learn more about 2020 SO. He ran computer simulations of its nearly circular path, noting that its orbit was barely tilted relative to Earth. Intrigued, Chodas ran the simulation in reverse and found that the object passed close enough to Earth in September 1966 to have originated from there. In other words, 2020 SO could be the spent Centaur rocket that launched the doomed Surveyor 2, particularly given its slightly anomalous orbit. That is evidence that the object is being pushed around by the photons emitted by the Sun, so 2020 SO would have to be relatively large and have low mass, just like an empty rocket booster. (Rocky asteroids, in comparison, are relatively small and massive.)

管理着JPL近地物体研究中心的保罗·乔达斯（Paul Chodas）是许多渴望了解2020 SO的科学家之一。他对它的近乎圆形的路径进行了计算机模拟，并指出它的轨道相对于地球几乎没有倾斜。对此，乔达斯很感兴趣，他以相反的方式进行了模拟，发现该物体在1966年9月足够接近地球，以至于起源于地球。换句话说，2020 SO可能是发射了注定的Surveyor 2的废旧Centaur火箭，特别是考虑到其轨道略微异常。这证明了该物体被太阳发出的光子推动，因此2020 SO必须相对较大且质量较低，就像一个空的火箭助推器一样。 （相比之下，岩石小行星相对较小且较大。）

Chodas sent out an email alert to his fellow astronomers around the world, so everyone could help track 2020 SO's progress over the next few months as it moved closer and closer to Earth. Follow-up spectroscopy observations yielded insight into the object's composition, and the spectrum data was consistent with the stainless steel used to manufacture Centaur rocket boosters in the 1960s. But the hypothesis wasn't yet a slam dunk, although the small discrepancies in the data could be explained by the weathering of the steel after 54 years of exposure to harsh space weather.

"We knew that if we wanted to compare apples to apples, we'd need to try to get spectral data from another Centaur rocket booster that had been in Earth orbit for many years to then see if it better matched 2020 SO's spectrum," said Vishnu Reddy of the University of Arizona, who headed the team that performed the critical follow-up observations. "Because of the extreme speed at which Earth-orbiting Centaur boosters travel across the sky, we knew it would be extremely difficult to lock on with the IRTF [NASA Infrared Telescope Facility] long enough to get a solid and reliable data set."

2020 SO was captured by Earth's gravity in November and came within 27,400 miles from Earth on December 1. That's when Reddy and his colleagues at the IRTF were able to capture the infrared spectrum of another Centaur D rocket booster—this time from a 1971 launch of a communication satellite. When they compared that spectrum to the data gathered about 2020 SO, the spectra matched. 2020 SO is also a Centaur rocket booster, most likely the one used for the Surveyor 2 mission.

2020年11月被地球引力捕获，并于12月1日距地球27,400英里。那时，雷迪和他在IRTF的同事能够捕获另一架半人马D火箭助推器的红外光谱-这次是从1971年发射的通信卫星开始的。当他们将该光谱与大约2020 SO收集的数据进行比较时，光谱匹配。 2020 SO也是半人马座火箭的助推器，很可能是用于Surveyor 2任务的助推器。

2020 SO is now moving away from the Earth and should escape Earth's gravity within a few months, at which point it will follow a new solar orbit. But astronomers expect it to return to Earth in 2036, and they will be ready to learn even more when it does.

2020 SO现在正在远离地球，应该在几个月内逃脱地球引力，届时它将遵循新的太阳轨道。但是天文学家们希望它能在2036年重返地球，而当它发生时，他们将准备学习更多。
