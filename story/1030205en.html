特斯拉今天将向早期访问计划(EAP)成员发布FSD Beta

2020-10-21 08:03:06

Today Tesla will release a fully functional version of its Full Self Driving package, which is the result of a "fundamental rewrite," but only for private beta testers. Last week, Tesla CEO Elon Musk announced via Twitter that a limited beta will be released on Tuesday, October 20, in line with his earlier promises. He emphasized that a really small number of people who are experts and careful drivers will have access to it.

今天，特斯拉将发布其全自动驾驶软件包的全功能版本，这是对其进行了根本性重写的结果，但仅限私人测试者使用。上周，特斯拉首席执行官埃隆·马斯克(Elon Musk)通过Twitter宣布，将于10月20日(周二)发布有限的测试版，这与他早先的承诺一致。他强调，只有非常少数的专家和谨慎的司机才能接触到它。

Today, a limited number of drivers will receive an update that should significantly change FSD. Tesla has been working diligently on a transition to a more complex AI framework that would improve the capabilities of Autopilot. If beta testing is successful, then Tesla owners who have purchased the FSD package will soon be able to use the full range of its advanced capabilities.

今天，有限数量的驱动程序将收到更新，这应该会显著改变FSD。特斯拉一直在努力过渡到更复杂的人工智能框架，以提高Autopilot的能力。如果测试版测试成功，那么购买了FSD软件包的特斯拉车主很快就可以使用其全部高级功能了。

Deep learning systems used for applications such as autonomous driving are developed by training a machine learning model. Typically, the performance of the deep learning system is limited at least in part by the quality of the training set used to train the model. Now Autopilot will receive simultaneous surround video from eight cameras. All frames will be stitched into one frame, and then video segments will be created for marking and training. Musk previously explained that Tesla is currently operating at 2.5D, but 2.5D is not well-correlated in time. We think about the world in three dimensions, but there is also a fourth—time. The improved FSD 4D will operate on all four dimensions, making it "a quantum leap." "It's capable of things that if you just look - looking at things as individual pictures as opposed to video - basically, like you could go from like individual pictures to surround video. So, it's fundamental," said Musk. "So, that architectural change, which has been underway for some time but has not really been rolled out to anyone in the production fleet, is what really matters for full self-driving," he continued. Operating in 4D will be mind-blowingly advanced. Compared to your average automaker's driver-assist tech, 4D Autopilot isn't just a game-changer: It'll change the entire sport.

用于自动驾驶等应用的深度学习系统是通过训练机器学习模型来开发的。通常，深度学习系统的性能至少部分地受到用于训练模型的训练集的质量的限制。现在，Autopilot将同时接收来自八个摄像机的环绕视频。将所有帧拼接成一帧，然后创建视频片段进行标记和训练。马斯克此前解释称，特斯拉目前以2.5D运行，但2.5D在时间上没有很好的相关性。我们从三维角度思考世界，但也有第四次。改进后的FSD 4D将在所有四个维度上运行，这将是一个巨大的飞跃。如果你只是将事物作为单独的图片来看待，而不是视频，基本上，就像你可以从单独的图片看到环绕视频一样，它能够做到这一点。所以，这是最基本的，马斯克说。因此，这种架构上的改变已经进行了一段时间，但还没有真正推广到生产车队中的任何人，这才是全自动驾驶真正重要的因素，他继续说。在4D模式下运行将是令人惊叹的先进技术。与普通汽车制造商的驾驶员辅助技术相比，4D Autopilot不仅仅是游戏规则的改变者：它将改变整个运动。
