Pornhub Is Facing Legal Action from the Louvre over Classical Content
Pornhub 正面临卢浮宫对经典内容的法律诉讼

2021-07-23 02:15:04

Pornhub, one of the most highly visited adult websites in the United States, is reportedly facing legal action from the Louvre museum in Paris and the Uffizi in Florence after debuting a new interactive website and app, “Show Me the Nudes,” that features porn actors re-envisioning classic works of art as jumping off points for sexually explicit content. Pornography is famously difficult to describe, but it could certainly be argued that a clear continuum exists between unclothed Titian muses and 21st century graphic imagery. However, the aforementioned museums are initiating legal action against Pornhub for rights infringements and pushing for the website to take down the content. “No one has granted authorizations for the operation or use of the art” a spokesperson for the Uffizi told The Daily Beast. “In Italy, the cultural heritage code provides that in order to use images of a museum, compressed works for commercial purposes, it is necessary to have the permission, which regulates the methods and sets the relative fee to be paid. All this obviously if the museum grants the authorization which, for example, would hardly have been issued in this case.” Some of the works featured on the Pornhub website from the Uffizi Gallery include Bacchus by Caravaggio, Spring by Botticelli and Angelica hides from Ruggiero by Giovanni Bilivert. “Pornhub has heard from our lawyers,” a Louvre spokesperson added to The Daily Beast. “We expect the works to be removed at once.” Works from the National Gallery in London, the Prado in Madrid, and Musee d’Orsay in Paris are also used as reference points on the website. Whether or not Pornhub’s experiment in highbrow smut is to your taste, it must be conceded that the platform’s new venture spotlights some truly creative copywriting. Take, for instance, this colorful explanation of Botticelli’s The Birth of Venus: “Just like you and I, she came into the world completely naked. But our girl Venus was a bit different in that she was spewed forth, fully formed, from a giant clamshell. And if you thought that shell was a symbol for some WAP, well then you were absolutely right.” Or, elsewhere, consider this description of Caravaggio: “He was a bisexual bar brawler who hung out with sex workers, feuded with other artists, and once killed a man over a particularly heated tennis match.”

据报道，Pornhub 是美国访问量最大的成人网站之一，在推出新的互动网站和应用程序“Show Me the Nudes”后，面临来自巴黎卢浮宫博物馆和佛罗伦萨乌菲兹美术馆的法律诉讼，其中包含色情内容演员们将经典艺术作品重新设想为色情内容的起点。色情是出了名的难以描述，但可以肯定的是，在未穿衣服的提香缪斯和 21 世纪的图像之间存在明显的连续体。但是，上述博物馆正在对 Pornhub 提起侵权诉讼，并要求该网站删除内容。乌菲兹美术馆的一位发言人告诉《每日野兽》杂志，“没有人授予操作或使用艺术作品的授权”。 “在意大利，文化遗产法规定，为了商业目的使用博物馆的图像、压缩作品，必须获得许可，该许可规定了方法并规定了要支付的相关费用。很明显，如果博物馆授予授权，例如，在这种情况下几乎不会发出授权。”乌菲兹美术馆在 Pornhub 网站上展出的一些作品包括卡拉瓦乔的巴克斯、波提切利的春天和乔瓦尼·比利维特的鲁杰罗的安吉丽卡皮革。 “Pornhub 收到了我们律师的来信，”卢浮宫发言人向 The Daily Beast 补充道。 “我们希望这些作品能立即被移除。”伦敦国家美术馆、马德里普拉多美术馆和巴黎奥赛博物馆的作品也被用作网站上的参考点。无论 Pornhub 在高雅黑穗病方面的实验是否符合您的口味，必须承认该平台的新冒险突出了一些真正有创意的文案。以波提切利的《维纳斯的诞生》为例，这个丰富多彩的解释：“就像你和我一样，她完全赤身裸体来到这个世界。但是我们的女孩维纳斯有点不同，她是从一个巨大的蛤壳中喷出的，完全成形的。如果你认为 shell 是某种 WAP 的象征，那么你是绝对正确的。”或者，在别处，考虑一下对卡拉瓦乔的这种描述：“他是一个双性恋酒吧斗殴者，与性工作者一起出去玩，与其他艺术家不和，曾经在一场特别激烈的网球比赛中杀死一个男人。”
