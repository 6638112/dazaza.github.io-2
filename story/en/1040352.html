Jeremy Bulloch, the original Boba Fett, has passed away
最初的Boba Fett杰里米·布洛赫（Jeremy Bulloch）去世了

2020-12-18 15:13:01

Jeremy Bulloch, the original actor behind the role of famed Star Wars bounty hunter Boba Fett, has passed away at the age of 75, his family confirmed via a Facebook post. Though the post is currently labeled "private," the information was publicly confirmed on Thursday by Daniel Logan, arguably best known as the child actor who portrayed Boba in the Star Wars prequel films.

家人通过Facebook帖子证实，杰里米·布洛赫（Jeremy Bulloch）是著名的《星球大战》赏金猎人波巴·菲特（Boba Fett）背后的原始演员，享年75岁。尽管该帖子目前被标记为“私人”，这些信息已由丹尼尔·洛根（Daniel Logan）公开证实，他可以说是最著名的儿童演员，他在《星球大战》前传电影中饰演波巴。

The Facebook post, as reviewed by Deadline, includes statements from his family pointing to "health complications, including several years of living with Parkinson's disease." It also stated that Bulloch died close to his longtime home in South London. Logan's Instagram post on the matter said he would "never forget all you've taught me" and "may the force be with you always."

经过Deadline审查的Facebook帖子包含他家人的声明，指出健康并发症，包括患有帕金森氏病数年。报告还说，布洛赫（Bulloch）死于他在伦敦南部的老家附近。洛根（Logan）在此事上的Instagram帖子说，他永远不会忘记你教给我的一切。并且＆＃34;力量永远伴随着您。＆＃34;

Bulloch's acting history, mostly in the UK, established a certain nerd cred well before becoming Star Wars' popular, mysterious semi-villain. He had recurring roles in the James Bond series (as the Q Branch staffer Smithers in Octopussy and For Your Eyes Only) and as multiple characters through Doctor Who's many, many seasons on BBC1.

Bulloch的表演历史（主要在英国）在成为《星球大战》之前就建立了一定的书呆子形象。流行，神秘的半恶棍。他曾在詹姆斯·邦德（James Bond）系列中担任过经常性角色（担任《八达通》中的Q分部史密瑟斯乐队和《只为您的眼睛》），并在BBC1的《神秘博士》中扮演多个角色。

While Boba Fett would debut in the Star Wars film series in 1980's Empire Strikes Back, Bulloch introduced him to the world two years earlier as a cameo in the infamous Star Wars Holiday Special. Bulloch never voiced Fett in any Star Wars films or other media (those duties fell to the late Jason Wingreen), though he did eventually return to the series, unmasked, as Capt. Jeremoch Colton in Episode III: Revenge of the Sith. Boba Fett's legacy in Star Wars has been born anew in the past year thanks to the character's original order, the Mandalorians, figuring prominently in the Disney+ series The Mandalorian. Saying more would be a spoiler for anyone not caught up on that series' second season, but it's arguably not surprising to figure that the Fett family might be intertwined in such a fan-service series.

当波巴·费特（Boba Fett）在1980年的《帝国反击战》（Empire Strike Back）中出演星球大战电影系列时，布卢克（Bulloch）在两年前以臭名昭著的《星球大战》假期特别节目中的客串身份将他介绍给世界。布洛赫（Bulloch）从未在任何《星球大战》电影或其他媒体中表达过费特（这些职责落在已故的杰森·温格林（Jason Wingreen）身上），尽管他最终确实重返了该剧集，但未作掩饰，如第三集：西斯的复仇中的杰罗莫奇·科尔顿上尉。鲍勃·费特（Boba Fett）在《星球大战》中的遗产在过去的一年里有了新的诞生，这要归功于角色的原始订单-曼达洛人（Mandalorians），在迪斯尼+系列曼达洛人中占有重要地位。对于不熟悉该系列的任何人来说，多说些都是破坏人的事情。第二季，但可以肯定的是，费特家族可能会在这样的球迷服务系列中纠缠在一起。

The news of Bulloch's passing follows that of David Prowse, the silent actor behind Darth Vader in the original Star Wars trilogy, who passed away last month at the age of 85.

Bulloch逝世的消息是在原星球大战三部曲中达斯·维达（Darth Vader）背后的沉默演员大卫·普劳斯（David Prowse）逝世后于上个月去世的，享年85岁。
