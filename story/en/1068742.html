Beets: the music geek’s media organizer
甜菜：音乐怪人的媒体组织者

2021-06-30 05:54:00

The purpose of beets is to get your music collection right once and for all. Itcatalogs your collection, automatically improving its metadata as it goes usingthe MusicBrainz database. Then it provides a bouquet of tools formanipulating and accessing your music.

甜菜的目的是让您的音乐收藏一次和所有人。 ITCatalogs您的集合，自动改进其使用MusicBrainz数据库的元数据。然后它提供了一束Formanigal和访问您的音乐的工具。

Because beets is designed as a library, it can do almost anything you canimagine for your music collection. Via plugins, beets becomes a panacea:

因为甜菜被设计为图书馆，所以它可以为您的音乐集合做几乎您的任何东西。通过插件，甜菜变成灵丹妙药：

Fetch or calculate all the metadata you could possibly need: album art, lyrics, genres, tempos, ReplayGain levels, or acousticfingerprints.

获取或计算您可能需要的所有元数据：专辑艺术，歌词，流派，节奏，重新开始级别或Acousticfingersts。

Get metadata from MusicBrainz, Discogs, or Beatport. Or guessmetadata using songs’ filenames or their acoustic fingerprints.

获取MusicBrainz，Discogs或Beatport的元数据。或使用歌曲的文件名或其声学指纹的猜测。

Browse your music library graphically through a Web browser and play it inany browser that supports HTML5 Audio.

通过Web浏览器浏览您的音乐库，并在支持HTML5音频的浏览器中播放它。

If beets doesn’t do what you want yet, writing your own plugin isshockingly simple if you know a little Python.

如果甜菜不做你想要的东西，如果你知道一个小python，写出自己的插件很简单。

Install beets by typing pip install beets.You might then want to read the GettingStarted guide. Then follow @b33tson Twitter for updates.

通过键入pip安装甜菜来安装甜菜。然后，您可能希望读取getStarted指南。然后按照@ B33TSON Twitter进行更新。
