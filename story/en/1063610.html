Kidnapped as a child, Gannibal was taken to Russia and presented as a gift to Peter the Great, where he was freed, adopted and raised in the Emperor's court household as his godson. [2]

Gannibal eventually rose to become a prominent member of the imperial court in the reign of Peter's daughter Elizabeth. He had 11 children, most of whom became members of the Russian nobility; he was a great-grandfather of the author and poet Alexander Pushkin. [3]

The main reliable accounts of Gannibal's life come from The Moor of Peter the Great, Pushkin's unfinished biography of his great-grandfather, published after Pushkin's death in 1837. Scholars argue that Pushkin's account may be inaccurate due to the author’s desire to elevate the status of his ancestors and family. There are a number of contradictions between the biographies of Pushkin and the German novel The Blackamoor of Peter the Great, based on his great-grandfather. [4] An historical biography by Gannibal's son-in-law, Rotkirkh, was largely responsible for the myth, propagated by some historians, that Gannibal was born in Ethiopia. [5] However, more recent research by the scholars Dieudonné Gnammankou and Hugh Barnes has established that the general was instead likely born in Central Africa, in an area bordering Lake Chad in present-day Cameroon. [5] [4]

Richard Pankhurst, the former professor at the Institute of Ethiopian Studies at the University of Addis Ababa in Ethiopia, believed the young Abram, Ibrahim or Abraham, as he named him, was born around 1698. He was the son of a minor "prince" or chief whose capital was Logon (now part of present-day Cameroon). His father was relatively affluent, owning many slaves both inherited and taken in battle. He had several wives and 19 children. However, after his father died in battle trying to defend his territory from the Ottoman Turks, Abram was captured and taken to Constantinople by ship (see Slavery in the Ottoman Empire). His sister, Lagan, is said to have drowned in the sea in a desperate attempt to save her brother.

Abram stayed in the Ottoman Empire for about a year in the service of Sultan Ahmed III's household. At the time, the Russian ambassador Sava Vladislavich-Raguzinsky, representing Peter the Great, was looking for "a few clever little African slaves" for the Tsar's palace in Moscow, as was the custom in those days at the great courts in Europe. On orders of Vladislavich's superiors (one of whom was Pyotr Andreyevich Tolstoy, great-grandfather of the celebrated writer Leo Tolstoy), Abram was selected for this purpose and soon ransomed from the Sultan's viziers with a bribe. In 1704, the ambassador immediately dispatched him by land to Moscow in order to be presented to Tsar Peter the Great.

亚伯拉姆住在奥斯曼帝国苏丹·艾哈迈德III＆＃39;家中约一年。当时，俄罗斯大使Sava Vladislavich-Raguzinsky代表彼得伟大，正在寻找＆＃34;一些聪明的小非洲奴隶＆＃34;在莫斯科的沙皇＆＃39;在欧洲伟大的法院的那些日子里的习俗。关于vladislavich＆＃39; s的上级订单（其中一个是Pyotr andreyevich tolstoy，庆祝作家Leo tolstoy的曾祖父），为此目的被选中，很快就与苏丹苏丹＆＃39;有贿赂的viziers ransomed。 1704年，大使立即将他派遣了莫斯科，以便赠送到彼得的沙皇。

The Tsar is noted to have taken a liking to the boy’s intelligence and potential for military service, and brought the child into his household. [4]Abram was baptized in 1705, in St. Paraskeva Church in Vilnius, with Peter as his godfather. [2] The date of Gannibal’s baptism held personal significance; he used that date as his birthday because he did not know his actual date of birth. [4]

Tsar被认为是喜欢这个男孩的智慧和军事服务潜力，并将孩子带入他的家庭。 [4]亚伯兰在1705年受洗，在维尔纽斯的圣帕斯基娃教堂，彼得作为他的教父。 [2] Gannibal洗礼的日期持有个人意义;他用他的日期作为他的生日，因为他不知道他的实际出生日期。 [4]

Abram valued his relationship with his godfather, as well as that of Peter's daughter ( Elizabeth), and was loyal to them as if they were family. [4]Starting at a young age, the boy Abram would travel alongside the emperor during his military campaigns, and at these military journeys he served as his godfather’s valet. [6]

亚伯兰与他的教父和彼得＆＃39;女儿（伊丽莎白）有重视他的关系，并且忠于他们，好像他们是家人一样。 [4]从年轻时开始，男孩艾滋病队会在军事竞选期间和皇帝一起旅行，并在这些军事旅程中担任他的教父的代客。 [6]

In 1717, Abram was sent to Metz to receive an education in the arts, sciences and warfare from the highest institutes available. By then he was fluent in several languages and excelled in mathematics and geometry. In 1718 Abram joined the French Army with hopes of pleasing his godfather by expanding his learning in military engineering. [6] He enrolled in the royal artillery academy at La Fère in 1720.

1717年，艾布拉姆被派往Metz，从最高的机构接受艺术，科学和战争的教育。然后，他精通了几种语言，并在数学和几何中表现出色。 1718年，亚伯兰加入了法国军队，希望通过扩大军事工程学习来赏心愉快的教父。 [6]他于1720年在LaFère的皇家炮兵学院注册。

During Abram's studies, conflict broke out between France and Spain, and he fought in the War of the Quadruple Alliance, rising to the rank of captain. While fighting in the French war against Spain, Abram received a head injury and was captured by the Spanish army. He was released in 1722 and continued his studies in Metz. [6]

在Abram＆＃39期间的研究期间，法国和西班牙之间的冲突爆发了，他在四轮联盟的战争中丧生，崛起为队长的等级。在法国对阵西班牙战争的同时，艾布拉姆受到了头部受伤，并被西班牙军队捕获。他于1722年发布，并在梅斯继续研究。 [6]

It was during his time in France that Abram adopted the surname "Gannibal" in honor of the Carthaginian general Hannibal ( Gannibal being the traditional transliteration of the name in Russian). [7] In Paris he met and befriended such Enlightenment figures as the Baron de Montesquieu and Voltaire (this claim by his biographer Hugh Barnes is disputed by reviewer Andrew Kahn [8]). Voltaire called Gannibal the "dark star of the Enlightenment". [9]

这是在法国的时间，亚伯兰通过姓氏＆＃34;甘尼比亚＆＃34;为了纪念迦太基·汉尼拔（吉尼尔是俄罗斯名称的传统音译）。 [7]在巴黎，他遇到并与Baron de Montesquieu和Voltaire（他的传记家Hugh Barnes的这一索赔是由审稿人争议的[8]）。伏尔泰叫Gannibal The＆＃34;启蒙＆＃34的黑暗之星。 [9]

Gannibal returned to Russia the following year, and his advanced training enabled him to apply for and successfully acquire posts first as an engineer and then as a mathematics tutor for one of the Tsar's private guard units. [6]

第二年盖内纳返回俄罗斯，他的高级培训使他能够以首先作为工程师申请并成功收购帖子，然后作为一个TSAR＆＃39;私人保护单位的数学导师。 [6]

Gannibal's education was completed by 1723, and he was due to return to Russia. After the death of Peter in 1725, Prince Menshikov gained power in Russia due to his good standing with Peter. However, Menshikov was not fond of Gannibal and was suspicious of his foreign origins and superior education. [6] Gannibal was exiled to Siberia in 1727, some 4,000 miles to the east of Saint Petersburg. He first traveled to Kazan, then to Tobolsk and Irkutsk, and then to Selenginsk near the Mongolian border. He was pardoned in 1730 due to his technical skills, and completed his service in Siberia in 1733. During this time he built a fortress and led several construction projects, where he became a master engineer.

Gannibal＆＃39的教育在1723年完成，他归还返回俄罗斯。在1725年彼得去世后，Menshikov王子由于他与彼得的良好站立而获得俄罗斯的权力。然而，Menshikov不喜欢Gannibal，并且怀疑他的外国起源和卓越的教育。 [6]在1727年，Gannibal于1727年被排放到西伯利亚，到圣彼得堡以东约有4,000英里。他第一次前往喀山，然后到托博和伊尔库茨克，然后到蒙古边境附近的Selenginsk。由于他的技术技能，他在1730年被赦免，并于1733年在西伯利亚完成了他的服务。在此期间，他建造了一个堡垒并领导了几个建筑项目，他成为了一位大师工程师。

Elizabeth of Russia became the new monarch in 1741. Gannibal became a prominent member of her court, rose to the rank of major-general, and became superintendent of Reval (now Tallinn, Estonia), a position he held from 1742 to 1752. A letter signed on 22 March 1744 by "A. Ganibal" is held at the Tallinn City Archives. In 1742, the Empress Elizabeth gave him the Mikhailovskoye estate in Pskov Oblast with hundreds of serfs. [9] [11] He retired to this estate in 1762.

俄罗斯伊丽莎白成为1741年的新君主。盖内尔成立了她的法院杰出的成员，升级为主要一般的级别，并成为雷瓦尔的主管（现在塔林，爱沙尼亚），他从1742年举行的一个职位，他是1752年的职位。a在1744年3月22日签署的信＆＃34; a。 Ganibal＆＃34;在塔林市档案馆举行。 1742年，Empress elizabeth在PSKOV Oblast中举办了普华氏牧场庄园，拥有数百名SERF。 [9] [11]他在1762年退休到这个庄园。

In an official document that Gannibal submitted in 1742 to Empress Elizabeth, while petitioning for the rank of nobility and a coat of arms, he asked for the right to use a family crest emblazoned with an elephant and the mysterious word "FVMMO", which may mean "homeland" in the Kotoko language. In his book, Gannibal: The Moor of Petersburg, Hugh Barnes writes of meeting with the sultan of Logone-Birni, who gave him the same translation of the word. [3] However, Frances Somers-Cocks, author of The Moor of St Petersburg: In the Footsteps of a Black Russian, met the same sultan and received a different translation for FVMMO. She also suggested that FVMMO stands for the Latin expression Fortuna Vitam Meam Mutavit Omnino which means "Fortune has changed my life entirely." [3]

在官方文件中，在1742年提交给皇后伊丽莎白的官方文件中，同时向贵族和一层武器的级别提出，他要求使用家庭徽章与大象和神秘的词＆＃34; fvmmo＆＃ 34;，可能意味着＆＃34;祖国＆＃34;在kotoko语言中。在他的书中，Gannibal：Petersburg的停泊，Hugh Barnes与Logone-Birni的苏丹会面写道，他们给了他同样的这个词的翻译。 [3]但是，弗朗斯Somers-Cocks，圣彼得堡Moor的作者：在黑色俄罗斯的脚步，遇到了同样的苏丹，并为FVMMO接受了不同的翻译。她还建议FVMMO代表拉丁表达Fortuna Vitam Meam Mutavit Omnino，这意味着＆＃34;财富完全改变了我的生活。＆＃34; [3]

Gannibal married twice. His first wife was Evdokia Dioper, a Greek woman. The couple married in 1731. Dioper despised her husband, whom she was forced to marry. The marriage between Dioper and Gannibal was very volatile, and he suspected her of infidelity early in their marriage. [12] Gannibal’s suspicions were confirmed when Dioper gave birth to a white daughter. [4] When Gannibal found out that she had been unfaithful to him, he had her arrested and thrown into prison, where she spent eleven years.

甘地尔结婚了两次。他的第一任妻子是evdokia迪普勒，希腊女人。这对夫妇于1731年结婚。迪奥勒鄙视她的丈夫，她被迫结婚。屈服者和甘尼亚之间的婚姻是非常挥发的，并且他在婚姻早期怀疑她的不忠。 [12]当屈光度生下一个白人女儿时，甘尼比亚的怀疑被证实。 [4]当纳尼布尔发现她对他不忠时，他让她被捕并扔进监狱，在那里她花了十一岁。

Gannibal began living with another woman, Christina Regina Siöberg (1705–1781), daughter of Mattias Johan Siöberg and wife Christina Elisabeth d'Albedyll, and married her bigamously in Reval, in 1736, a year after the birth of their first child and while he was still lawfully married to his first wife. His divorce from Dioper did not become final until 1753, upon which a fine and a penance were imposed on Gannibal, and Dioper was sent to a convent for the rest of her life. Gannibal's second marriage was nevertheless deemed lawful after his divorce. Gannibal’s second marriage to Christina was much happier, and he appreciated her fidelity and affection towards him. [4]

On her paternal side, Gannibal’s second wife was descended from noble families in Scandinavia and Germany: Siöberg ( Sweden), Galtung ( Norway), and Grabow ( Denmark) / von Grabow ( Brandenburg). [1] [13] Her paternal grandfather was Gustaf Siöberg, Rittmester til Estrup, who died in 1694, whose wife Clara Maria Lauritzdatter Galtung (ca. 1651–1698) was the daughter of Lauritz Lauritzson Galtung (ca. 1615–1661) and of Barbara Grabow til Pederstrup (1631–1696).

在她的父亲的一边，盖内班的第二任妻子被斯堪的纳维亚和德国的贵族家庭下降：Siöberg（瑞典），加州（挪威）和Grabow（丹麦）/ von grabow（勃兰登堡）。 [1] [13]她的祖父是GustafSiöberg，Rittmester Til Estrup，他在1694年去世，他的妻子Clara Maria Lauritzdatter Galtung（CA.1651-1698）是Lauritz Lauritzson Galtung（CA.1615-1661）的女儿芭芭拉Grabow Til Pederstrup（1631-1696）。

Abram Gannibal and Christine Regina Siöberg had ten children, including a son, Osip. Osip in turn would have a daughter, Nadezhda, the mother of Alexander Pushkin. Gannibal's oldest son, Ivan, became an accomplished naval officer who helped found the city of Kherson in southern Ukraine in 1779 and attained the rank of general-in-chief, the second-highest military rank in imperial Russia.

Abram Gannibal和Christine ReginaSiöberg有十个儿童，包括儿子互惠考。逆转互惠生将有一个女儿，Nadezhda，亚历山大·普希金的母亲。 Gannibal＆＃39;最古老的儿子Ivan成为一名成熟的海军军官，他们在1779年帮助找到了乌克兰南部的卡森市，并达到了董事会普通州，第二大军衔。

Some British aristocrats descend from Gannibal, including Natalia Grosvenor, Duchess of Westminster and her sister, Alexandra Hamilton, Duchess of Abercorn. George Mountbatten, 4th Marquess of Milford Haven, a cousin of Queen Elizabeth II, is also a direct descendant, as the grandson of Nadejda Mountbatten, Marchioness of Milford Haven.

一些英国贵族从纳尼布尔下降，包括纳米尼亚格罗夫纳，威斯敏斯特公爵夫人和她的妹妹，亚历山德拉·汉密尔顿，阿伯恩爵士。乔治·哈登·哈登·哈登·哈芬岛（Milford Haven），伊丽莎白二世王后，也是一个直接的后裔，作为Nadejda Mountbatten的孙子，Milford Haven Marchioness。

Gannibal's actual place of birth continues to be uncertain, and is subject to speculation by modern historians. Until recent scholarly field work, it was generally assumed that he originated in Ethiopia. [3] Chiefly Russian scholars for many years believed that he was from the vicinity of Medri Bahri, an ancient kingdom in present-day Eritrea. [14] In a letter he wrote to Empress Elizabeth, Peter the Great's daughter, Gannibal stated that he was from the town of "Logon" [7] or "Lagone". [3] Anthropologist Dmitry Anuchin wrote an essay about Alexander Pushkin in which he theorized that "Lagone" referred to Logo-chewa in Eritrea. [3] In 1999, the Russian Institute in Addis Ababa, the capital of Ethiopia, also campaigned for a commemorative stamp to honor Pushkin's bicentennial. [3]

Gannibal＆＃39;实际的出生地仍然不确定，并且受到现代历史学家的猜测。直到最近的学术领域工作，一般认为他起源于埃塞俄比亚。 [3]主要俄罗斯学者多年来，他认为他是一家古代厄立特里亚古老的王国中梅里巴哈里的附近。 [14]在一封信中，他写到了皇后伊丽莎白，彼得伟大的＆＃39; S的女儿，Gannibal表示，他来自镇上的＆＃34;登录＆＃34; [7]或＆＃34; LAGONE＆＃34; [3]人类学家Dmitry Anuchin写了一篇关于亚历山大·普希金的一篇文章，其中他理论为那个＆＃34; alagone＆＃34;提到厄立特里亚的徽标。 [3] 1999年，俄罗斯在埃塞俄比亚首都亚的斯亚贝巴研究所，也竞选纪念邮票，以荣誉普希金＆＃39;诗句。 [3]

The governments of Ethiopia and Eritrea each claim that Gannibal was born in their respective territories. Under this belief, the Ethiopian government named a street in Addis Ababa after Alexander Pushkin, Gannibal's great-grandson. It also placed a bust of him near the African Union headquarters in 2002, and arranged for a statue of Pushkin to be transferred from Moscow to Addis Ababa in 2014. [15] However, the notion that Gannibal may have been born in Ethiopia holds little currency with the general Ethiopian population. [3] The Eritrean government asserts that Gannibal was instead born in Loggo Sarda, [15] an area in modern Eritrea. [16] It also erected its own statue of Pushkin and named a street for him in 2009. [15]

埃塞俄比亚和厄立特里亚的政府每个索赔吉尼尔出生于各自的领土。在这一信念下，埃塞俄比亚政府在亚历山大·普希金，吉尼尔·普利金＆＃39之后在亚的斯亚贝巴命名一条街道。它在2002年的非洲联盟总部附近坐了一下他的萧条，并于2014年安排了普希金雕像，以将于2014年从莫斯科转移到亚亚亚亚贝巴。然而，吉尼尔可能出生在埃塞俄比亚的观念很少货币与埃塞俄比亚一般人口。 [3]厄立特里亚政府断言，吉尼尔是在Roggo Sarda，[15]现代厄立特里亚的一个地区。 [16]它还竖立了自己的普希金雕像，并于2009年为他命名一条街道。[15]

Vladimir Nabokov cast doubt on Gannibal's ancestry, based on research findings during his work translating Pushkin's novel Eugene Onegin. Nabokov disagreed with Anuchin's theory, stating that it was just as likely that Gannibal was referring to "the Lagona region of equatorial Africa, south of Lake Chad." [3] Support for Anuchin's theory of Ethiopian birth declined after it was exposed as racially based, implying that " hamitic" Ethiopian origins better explained Gannibal's success than " negroid" origins. [3]

Vladimir Nabokov基于在他的工作翻译中的研究结果转换普希肯＆＃39;新的eugene onegin时，纳博冈州突尼斯＆＃39;祖先举行了怀疑。 Nabokov不同意Anuchin＆＃39; S的理论，说明甘尼比亚的可能是指＆＃34;赤道非洲的Lagena地区，乍得南部的南部。＆＃34; [3]支持ANUCHIN＆＃39;埃塞俄比亚的诞生理论在其作为种族为基础之后下降，意味着它暗示它; Hamitic＆＃34;埃塞俄比亚起源更好地解释了吉尼尔＆＃39;成功而不是＃34;黑人＆＃34;起源。 [3]

The Beninese historian Dieudonné Gnammankou, an expert on Russia, [3] studied Russian, French and African sources and argued that Gannibal was indeed from Logone-Birni and was most likely the son of a chief in the ancient sultanate. In 1995, Gnammankou asserted that the "Logon" Gannibal wrote about was actually Logone, capital of the old Kotoko kingdom of Logone-Birni, now located in northern Cameroon. [7] He believed that the pattern of slave trade around Lake Chad made that region a more plausible likelihood for Gannibal's birthplace than Gondar, Ethiopia. [3] Gnammankou's biography of Gannibal was translated into Russian, and was voted the best book on Pushkin at the 1999 Moscow Book Fair. [7]

俄罗斯专家的Beninese历史学家DieudonnéGnammankou，[3]研究了俄罗斯，法国和非洲来源，并认为Gannibal确实来自Logone-Birni，很可能是古代苏丹古代酋长的主席的儿子。 1995年，Gnammankou断言和＃34;登录＆＃34; Gannibal写了关于Logone-Birni的旧科托王国的首都Logoge，现在位于喀麦隆北部。 [7]他认为，乍得湖周围的奴隶贸易模式使得该地区为埃塞俄比亚戈尼河的出生地比Gonnibal＆＃39;出生地更具合理的可能性。 [3] Gnammankou＆＃39; Gannibal的传记转化为俄罗斯，并在1999年莫斯科书展上投了最佳的普希金书。 [7]

Gnammankou's findings were in turn buttressed by the field work of Hugh Barnes. After consulting with the Sultan of Logone-Birni, Barnes found that an inscription on Gannibal's crest, which was hitherto undecipherable, corresponded with the term for "homeland" in the local Kotoko language of central Africa. [3]

Gnammankou＆＃39; S的调查结果又由Hugh Barnes的野外工作而逆转。在与Logone-Birni的苏丹咨询后，巴恩斯发现吉尼巴尔＆＃39; S冠军的铭文，迄今为止尚不掩饰，与＆＃34的术语相对应; Homeland＆＃34;在中非的当地科罗托语言中。 [3]

In November, 2010, representatives from Russia and Estonia, the ambassador of Cameroon, and the sultan of Logone-Birni went to La Fère, France to unveil a commemorative plaque honoring Abram Petrovich Gannibal as a graduate of La Fère's royal artillery academy. The academy, which closed in the 1990s, had been started by King Louis XV shortly before Gannibal's enrollment there in 1720. The plaque declares that he was a graduate of the royal artillery academy of La Fère, and later became chief military engineer and general-in-chief of the Imperial Russian Army. It also notes that Gannibal is the great-grandfather of Alexander Pushkin, Russia's greatest poet. Dieudonné Gnammankou, whose research into Gannibal's background was largely responsible for the ceremony at La Fère taking place, also served as the main speaker at a symposium following the event. [7]

Alexander Pushkin used his great-grandfather Abram Gannibal as the model for Ibrahim, the lead character in his unfinished novel The Moor of Peter the Great. After leaving school in 1817, Pushkin met Abram's last surviving son, Peter. He met Peter again in 1825, after writing in his diary about wanting to "get from him some memoirs about my great-grandfather." [17] He seemed to use his own experiences, along with Gannibal's to create the plot for The Moor of Peter the Great. [17] A stage version of the work was written by Carlyle Brown and premiered at the Alabama Shakespeare Festival's 13th Southern Writers' Project in March 2001. [18]

亚历山大·普希金用他的曾祖父亚伯兰·纳尼布尔作为伊布拉希姆的模型，他未完成的小说中的杰克·彼得的伟大。在1817年离开学校后，普希金遇到了伯克姆·彼得·彼得。他在1825年再次遇到彼得，写在他的日记上关于想要＆＃34;从他那里得到一些关于我曾祖父的回忆录。＆＃34; [17]他似乎可以使用自己的经验，以及Gannibal＆＃39; s为彼得的沼地创造剧情。 [17]这项工作的舞台版本是由Carlyle Brown写的，在阿拉巴马州莎士比亚节日和第13岁的第13届南部作家＆＃39; 2001年3月的项目。[18]

Abram Gannibal is a protagonist of the Soviet comedy movie How Czar Peter the Great Married Off His Moor, although the film's plot has almost nothing to do with Gannibal's real biography. The film is partly based on Pushkin's Moor of Peter the Great. [19]

Abram Gannibal是苏联喜剧电影的主角，彼得彼得的伟大嫁给了他的沼地，虽然这部电影＆＃39;剧情几乎与Gannibal＆＃39;真正的传记无关。这部电影部分基于普希金＆＃39;彼得的伟大的停泊。 [19]

There are several portraits thought to depict Gannibal, which include a painting of the Battle of Lesnaya by Pierre-Denis Martin the Younger. The young boy present in Martin’s painting is argued to be Gannibal, because of the young boy’s role as valet to Peter during military campaigns and Gannibal’s possible connection to the artist while in France. [4] A portrait by Adriaan Schoonebeeck is also believed to portray Gannibal during his time with Peter the Great. In Schoonebeeck’s portrait of Peter the Great, the young servant boy directly behind Peter is thought to be Gannibal. [6] Although there are a variety of portraits that claim to contain Gannibal, there is little evidence to suggest the claims are accurate. [20]In the Lesnaya painting, the young boy is dressed in traditional slave attire, which Gannibal did not wear due to his status under Peter the Great. [20]
