Schoonschip – Unique residential area: floating, sustainable, circular
Schoonschip - 独特的住宅区：浮动，可持续，圆形

2021-04-06 13:57:03

Schoonschip Amsterdam is a unique residential area: floating, sustainable, circular and initiated by a group of enthusiasts with a shared dream.

Schoonschip Amsterdam是一个独特的住宅区：浮动，可持续，通函，由一群与共同梦想的爱好者发起。

The initiators of Schoonschip commissioned Space&Matter to develop a smart urban plan. Together with a team of multi-disciplinary consultants and the inhabitants Space&Matter designed the urban plan, the plot passports and a smart jetty, which connects each house with the neighbourhood, and with the necessary technical infrastructures they need. Each resident designed their own house together with an architect of their choice, therefore the neighbourhood has a unique appearance with great diversity of materials, styles and building types.

Schoonschip委托空间＆amp的发起者;事物发展智能城市计划。与一支多学科顾问和居民空间＆amp的团队一起设计了城市计划，剧情护照和智能码头，它与附近的每个房子连接，以及他们所需的必要技术基础设施。每个居民都与他们选择的架构师一起设计了自己的房子，因此附近具有独特的外观，具有巨大的材料，款式和建筑类型。

Schoonschip consists of a total of 30 water plots. Half of the boats are shared by 2 families, creating 46 unique water dwellings for more than 100 residents. Space&Matter designed two of the floating houses, the last of which will be completed in 2021.

Schoonschip共有30个水块。一半的船只由2个家庭共享，创造了超过100名居民的独特水住宅。空间＆amp;物质设计了两个浮动房屋，最后一个将在2021年完成。

“‘Seventy percent of the world is covered in water, and the good thing is that we can easily live on it! Since urban areas struggle with high density, we should make better use of the space on the water. With Schoonschip we want to set the example, and show how living on water can be a great and better alternative for people and our planet.’ ”

“”百分之七十的世界被水覆盖，善之上我们可以轻松地生活！由于城市地区具有高密度的斗争，因此我们应该更好地利用水面上的空间。随着Schoonschip我们想要设置这个例子，并展示水上的生活如何对人和我们的星球来说是一个伟大而更好的替代品。“

Schoonschip is built on a circular community model, which rethinks how we organise our cities, both socially and spatially, to tackle the systemic roots of climate change and create a connected community with strong social values. With decentralised and renewable solutions to water, energy and waste systems, Schoonschip includes solar panels connected to a smart-grid where residents can trade energy, submersed heat exchangers for heating and cooling, and water treatment technologies used to (re)collect energy and nutrients from wastewater.

Schoonschip建立在循环社区模式，重新思考我们如何在社会和空间上组织城市，以解决气候变化的系统根源，并创造一个具有强大社会价值的关联社区。 Schoonschip通过分散和可再生能力的水，能源和废物系统解决方案，Schoonschip包括连接到智能电网的太阳能电池板，其中居民可以交易能量，用于加热和冷却的浸没式热交换器，以及用于（重新）的水处理技术收集能量和营养来自废水。

Schoonschip is not only sustainable in an ecological sense, but also socially: the residents work closely together to realise and improve their residential area and coordinate their plans. They have agreed to renounce their personal cars and instead share electric cars together. The ‘smart jetty’ that connects all houses serves as a pavement and meeting place for the neighbours. Schoonschip demonstrates how circular neighbourhoods can be created by residents, taking responsibility of their resource consumption and waste, and working together to achieve local loop closure.

Schoonschip不仅可以在生态学中可持续，而且在社会方面是社会的：居民在一起努力，实现他们的住宅区并协调他们的计划。他们同意放弃他们的个人车，而是将电动汽车分享在一起。连接所有房屋的“智能码头”是邻居的人行道和会议场所。 Schoonschip演示了居民如何创建循环社区，负责其资源消耗和浪费，并共同努力实现当地环路关闭。

Living on water offers a great solution for places where climate change and a rise in sea levels rise are a looming hazard. It not only protects people against nature, it also protects nature itself. Housing on water can be completely footloose, reversible and has very little impact on nature, especially when combined with smart energy and water-sanitation systems like those deployed by Schoonschip. Building on water has other advantages too: houses can be manufactured elsewhere and towed towards their destination, enabling an overnight neighbourhood without any building nuisance for its surrounding area.

生活在水面上为气候变化和海平面上升的地方提供了一个很好的解决方案是令人留下的危险。它不仅可以保护人们免受自然，它也可以保护大自然本身。水上的外壳可以完全脚踏，可逆，对自然影响很小，特别是当与Schoonschip部署的智能能量和水卫生系统相结合时。在水上建造有其他优点：房屋可以在其他地方制造并拖向目的地，使一夜之间邻居在其周边地区没有任何建筑物滋扰。

On a small scale, Schoonschip explores innovative solutions to some of the global challenges that come with the changing climate, including water scarcity and drought, extreme rainfall and flooding, and rising sea levels. It applies simple yet effective innovations which offer not only environmental but social and economic benefits. These innovations offer points of learning and can be translated in different environmental and geographical contexts. Indeed, it has been hailed internationally as a prototype for floating urban development, and innovative resilient city design. It rethinks how are cities are designed, where and by who.

在小规模上，Schoonschip探讨了伴随着变化气候的全球挑战的创新解决方案，包括水资源稀缺和干旱，极端降雨量和洪水，海平面上升。它适用于简单但有效的创新，不仅提供环境而是社会和经济效益。这些创新提供了学习点，可以在不同的环境和地理环境中翻译。实际上，它作为浮动城市发展的原型被称为原型，创新的弹性城市设计。它重新考虑了城市的设计，谁以及谁。

Schoonschip is open source. VvE Schoonschip aspires to a role as frontrunner and pioneer: a breeding ground for the latest sustainable techniques and solutions. To achieve this, the association cooperates with innovative companies and shares its experiences. "We find it enjoyable and necessary to share the sustainable and social mindset with others and to advance knowledge and skills. We like to learn from others and everyone is welcome to learn from us. By jointly formulating ambitions and frameworks and offering freedom to residents and architects within those frameworks, a very diverse, creative residential district is created. We have compiled our experiences on this website.”

Schoonschip是开源的。 Vve Schoonschip渴望成为Frontrunner和Pioneer的角色：最新的可持续技术和解决方案的繁殖场。为实现这一目标，协会与创新公司合作并分享其经验。 ＆＃34;我们发现与他人共享可持续和社会心态并推进知识和技能的令人愉快和必要。我们喜欢从别人那里学习，欢迎各界人士从我们这里学习。通过共同制定野心和框架，并为这些框架内提供居民和建筑师的自由，创建了一个非常多样化的创意住宅区。我们已经在本网站上编制了我们的经验。“
