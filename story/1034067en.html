消息人士称，在2019年初软银(SoftBank)投资9.4亿美元后，以T.Rowe Price为首的自动驾驶送货公司NURO以50亿美元的估值筹集了5亿美元

2020-11-10 00:17:32

(Reuters) - Self-driving delivery company Nuro, whose R2 unit has no pedals or steering wheel and only room for packages, said Monday it has raised $500 million as e-commerce gets a huge boost from the pandemic.

(路透社)-无人驾驶快递公司NURO周一表示，随着电子商务从疫情中获得巨大提振，该公司已经筹集了5亿美元。NURO的R2单元没有踏板或方向盘，只有容纳包裹的空间。

Nuro joins other self-driving firms that raised big money this year. Earlier this year Waymo, the self-driving unit of Alphabet Inc raised $3 billion, helping to boost the total raised in 2020 by autonomous car companies to $7.1 billion as of November 4, according to data firm PitchBook. While that slightly exceeds 2019’s total, it falls short of the record $9.4 billion raised in 2018. Also far fewer startups are getting funded.

诺诺加入了其他自动驾驶公司的行列，这些公司今年筹集了大量资金。根据数据公司PitchBook的数据，今年早些时候，Alphabet Inc.的自动驾驶部门Waymo筹集了30亿美元，帮助推动自动驾驶汽车公司在2020年筹集的资金总额在11月4日达到71亿美元。虽然这一数字略高于2019年的总额，但低于2018年创纪录的94亿美元。此外，获得融资的初创公司也少得多。

Early last year Nuro raised $940 million from SoftBank and a source close to the company said the valuation doubled from that last funding round to $5 billion.

去年初，NURO从软银(SoftBank)筹集了9.4亿美元，一位接近该公司的消息人士称，该公司的估值比上一轮融资翻了一番，达到50亿美元。

This round was led by T. Rowe Price Associates and includes new investors Fidelity Management & Research Company, LLC. and Baillie Gifford. Existing investors SoftBank and Greylock also participated.

本轮融资由T.Rowe Price Associates牵头，新投资者包括富达投资管理与研究公司(Fidelity Management&Amp；Research Company，LLC)。还有贝莉·吉福德。现有投资者软银(SoftBank)和格雷洛克(Greylock)也参与其中。

The funding will help Nuro expand its team but also scale up manufacturing, said Nuro co-founder Dave Ferguson. Its R2 vehicle is currently manufactured in Detroit with Roush Enterprises, but he declined to say how many were produced and on the road today.

纽诺联合创始人戴夫·弗格森表示，这笔资金将帮助纽诺扩大其团队，同时也将扩大制造规模。该公司的R2汽车目前是与Roush Enterprise在底特律生产的，但他拒绝透露今天生产了多少辆并上路了。

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration in February gave Nuro approval to deploy up to 5,000 R2 vehicles on public roads every year, said Ferguson. They currently drive around in Houston, Texas, Scottsdale, Arizona, and nine cities in California.

弗格森说，美国国家公路交通安全管理局(National Highway Traffic Safety Administration)在2月份批准NORO每年在公共道路上部署多达5000辆R2车辆。他们目前在德克萨斯州的休斯顿、亚利桑那州的斯科茨代尔和加利福尼亚州的9个城市开车。

Nuro is currently using Prius cars outfitted with its self-driving technology to deliver groceries and prescriptions for Kroger and CVS in Houston. Ferguson said in the “next couple of quarters” the R2 would be doing the deliveries to “ramp up a full unmanned delivery service”.

诺诺目前正在使用普锐斯(Prius)汽车，配备其自动驾驶技术，为克罗格(Kroger)和CVS在休斯顿运送食品杂货和处方。弗格森说，在“接下来的几个季度”，R2将进行送货，以“提升全面的无人送货服务”。
