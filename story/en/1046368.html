Sony adds HDMI to last year’s Android phone, charges $2,500
索尼在去年的Android手机中增加了HDMI，收费2500美元

2021-01-28 23:12:46

We've seen smartphones with stratospheric price tags before, but usually, something is special about them. Phones like the Samsung Galaxy Fold and the Huawei Mate X are anywhere from $2,000 to $2,600, but those were first-gen foldable smartphones with brand-new display technology. Sony's latest entry into the smartphone market, the Sony Xperia Pro, is a boring, old slab phone that seems utterly forgettable until you look at the price: an astounding $2,500, or the price of three brand-new, $800 Samsung Galaxy S21s. Sony has really outdone itself.

我们以前曾见过带有平流层价格标签的智能手机，但通常情况下，它们有一些特殊之处。三星Galaxy Fold和华为Mate X等手机的价格在2,000美元至2,600美元之间，但这些都是具有全新显示技术的第一代可折叠智能手机。索尼最新进军智能手机市场的是索尼Xperia Pro，它是一款无聊的老式平板手机，在您看价格之前，它似乎完全可以被遗忘：惊人的2500美元，或三个全新的三星800美元的价格Galaxy S21s。索尼真的超越了自己。

Mostly the Xperia Pro seems a lot like the  Xperia 1 II, Sony's already-overpriced $1,300 flagship smartphone from 2020. Both have 6.5-inch, almost-4K, 3840×1644, 60Hz OLED displays, the Snapdragon 865 SoC, three rear cameras, an IP65 rating, and a 4000mAh battery. This year's Xperia Pro gets a tier bump to 12GB of RAM and 512GB of storage, but that's only usually worth $100 extra. There's also mmWave 5G this year, two "app shortcut" buttons on the side, and a shutter button. Fancy.

大多情况下，Xperia Pro看起来很像索尼的Xperia 1 II，从2020年起已经定价1300美元。旗舰产品都配备6.5英寸，近乎4K，3840×1644、60Hz OLED显示器，Snapdragon 865 SoC，三个后置摄像头，IP65防护等级和4000mAh电池。今年的Xperia Pro获得了12GB的RAM和512GB的存储空间，但是通常仅值100美元。今年还有mmWave 5G，其中两个是应用程序快捷方式。按钮和快门按钮。花式的。

None of that explains why this phone is at least $1,000 more than it should be. Sony's justification for the outrageous price is (drumroll please) an HDMI port. Yes, in addition to the USB-C port on the bottom, there is also a Micro HDMI port that can be used as a video input. Sony suggests either hooking the phone up to a Sony Alpha camera and using it as a live video monitor or pushing an external video source out to the Internet for livestreaming. Just this one feature and the camera-adjacentness is worth $1,000 extra by Sony's logic.

这些都无法解释为什么这款手机至少比它的价格高出1,000美元。索尼提出高价的理由是HDMI端口。是的，除了底部的USB-C端口外，还有一个Micro HDMI端口可以用作视频输入。索尼建议将手机连接到索尼Alpha相机上并用作实时视频监视器，或者将外部视频源推送到Internet进行实时流传输。按照索尼的逻辑，仅此一项功能和与相机相邻的功能就额外价值1,000美元。

There have been other Android phones with HDMI before. The Motorola Atrix comes to mind, a $480 phone released in 2011. That was micro HDMI output, though, and it could hook up to a TV or a special laptop. Sony is calling this "the world's first smartphone with HDMI input," letting the phone act as a mini TV. If the idea of an Android phone attached on top of your camera is appealing to you, you can create basically the same setup as Sony's example at home, using a $20 dongle. Android phones come standard with USB support, and today's tiny HDMI capture dongles mean you use an Android phone as a video monitor or livestreamer relatively easily.

之前还有其他带HDMI的Android手机。想到了摩托罗拉Atrix，这是2011年发布的价格为480美元的手机。不过，这是微型HDMI输出，可以连接电视或特殊的笔记本电脑。索尼称这是全球第一款具有HDMI输入的智能手机，让手机充当迷你电视。如果将Android手机安装在相机顶部的想法很吸引您，您可以使用20美元的加密狗在家里创建与Sony的示例基本相同的设置。 Android手机标配USB支持，并且今天很小的HDMI捕获软件狗意味着您相对容易地将Android手机用作视频监视器或直播器。

An upcycling-type setup with an adapter seems more appropriate for an idea like this rather than running out and buying a multi-thousand-dollar bespoke Android phone. If you're really willing to spend thousands of dollars on a camera monitor, you probably want one with a removable battery, since the permanent battery in a smartphone will be dead in a few years. A purpose-built monitor will also be cheaper, have more inputs, have a bigger screen, and have a sun visor. If you really need a phone, you probably don't want your actual phone attached to your camera all the time.

带有适配器的升级型设置似乎更适合于这种想法，而不是用完并购买价值数千美元的定制Android手机。如果您真的愿意在相机监视器上花费数千美元，那么您可能想要一个带有可拆卸电池的电池，因为智能手机中的永久电池将在几年后耗尽。专用显示器也将更便宜，输入更多，屏幕更大，还有遮阳板。如果您确实需要电话，则可能不希望一直将实际的电话连接到相机。

Plus, as an Android phone, the Xperia Pro doesn't seem very good. It has last year's SoC, the Snapdragon 865, instead of the Snapdragon 888, an unforgivable problem when you're charging $2,500. Last year's Xperia 1 II has a side-mounted fingerprint reader, but this does not, and there are no biometrics of any kind listed on the website. The phone's camera-adjacentness has also apparently led Sony to give it an ugly design, with a thicker body and thicker bezels over the Xperia 1 II. But the battery isn't any bigger, and the phone doesn't seem to be any more rugged.

另外，作为Android手机，Xperia Pro看起来不太好。它具有去年的SoC，即Snapdragon 865，而不是Snapdragon 888，当您收取2500美元的费用时，这是一个不可原谅的问题。去年的Xperia 1 II具有侧面安装的指纹读取器，但没有，并且网站上没有列出任何种类的生物识别信息。这款手机与相机的相邻性也显然使索尼给它提供了一个丑陋的设计，在Xperia 1 II上机身更厚，边框也更厚。但是电池并没有更大，手机似乎也更坚固。
