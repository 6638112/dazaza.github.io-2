Palestinian Officials: Iran Is Giving Us the Rockets We Use to Attack Israel
巴勒斯坦官员：伊朗给我们我们用来攻击以色列的火箭

2021-05-13 13:03:08

Multiple officials with the Palestinian Islamic Jihad (PIJ), a designated terrorist organization, have said in recent days that the rockets and weapons that they use to attack Israel come from Iran.

与巴勒斯坦伊斯兰教圣战（PIJ）是一个指定的恐怖主义组织的多个官员，最近几天表示，他们用来攻击以色列的火箭和武器来自伊朗。

The remarks were revealed by various watchdogs who monitor foreign media for the purpose of tracking extremists, and come as Palestinian terrorists have fired well over a thousands rockets at Israel this week.

这些言论被监督外国媒体的各种看门狗透露，以便跟踪极端主义者，随着巴勒斯坦恐怖主义者在本周在以色列的成千上万的火箭队中发射。

“Yesterday and today, the IDF attacked hundreds of Hamas & Islamic Jihad terrorist targets in the Gaza Strip. We have eliminated dozens of terrorists, including senior commanders,” Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu said on Tuesday. “Hamas and Islamic Jihad have paid — I tell you here — will pay a very heavy price for their aggression. I say here this evening — their blood in on their heads.”

“昨天和今天，IDF袭击了数百名哈马斯＆amp;伊斯兰圣战恐怖主义目标在加沙地带。我们已经消除了几十个恐怖主义者，包括高级指挥官，“以色列总理本杰明内塔尼亚胡周二说。 “哈马斯和伊斯兰圣战已经付了 - 我告诉你这里 - 将为他们的侵略支付非常沉重的价格。我今晚在这里说 - 他们的血液。“

David A. Daoud, a research analyst at the United Against Nuclear Iran (UANI), highlighted one such clip in a tweet yesterday.

曼联伊朗（UANI）的研究分析师大卫A. Daoud昨天在推文中突出了一个这样的剪辑。

“Palestinian Islamic Jihad Quds Force Brigade spox thanks ‘Resistance Axis, headed by Islamic Republic of Iran,’ for the addition of Badr-3 missiles to PIJ arsenal,” Daoud wrote.

“巴勒斯坦伊斯兰教圣战可以强迫旅，斯诺克斯感谢”阻力轴，被伊朗伊斯兰共和国为首“，为Pij Arsenal添加了Badr-3导弹，”Daous写道。

Another clip came from the Middle East Media Research Institute (MEMRI), which highlighted remarks made last week from Palestinian Islamic Jihad Official Ramez Al-Halabi, who said that Iran is the one that “support us with weapons, money, and food.”

另一个剪辑来自中东媒体研究所（MEMIRI），突出了上周从巴勒斯坦伊斯兰圣战官员Ramez al-Halabi发表的备注，他说伊朗是“支持我们武器，金钱和食物”的言论。

The mujahideen in Gaza and in Lebanon use Iranian weapons to strike the Zionists. We buy our weapons with Iranian money. An important part of our activity is under the supervision of Iranian experts. The contours of the victories in Palestine as of late were outlined with the blood of Qasem Soleimani, Iranian blood.

加沙和黎巴嫩的Mujahideen使用伊朗武器来袭击犹太岛。我们用伊朗金钱购买我们的武器。我们活动的重要组成部分是在伊朗专家的监督下。截至近期巴勒斯坦的胜利的轮廓与伊朗血液Qasem Soleimani的血液概述。

Today, the patronage of the axis of resistance has begun to prevail in the region, thanks to Allah and to the blood of the martyrs, and it has begun to make an impact, and what an impact!

今天，抗抵抗轴的赞助已经开始在该地区占上风，归功于真主和烈士的血液，并且已经开始产生影响，并且有什么影响！

The axis between Jerusalem and Beirut, the axis between Jerusalem and Baghdad, the axis between Jerusalem and Damascus, the axis between Jerusalem and Sanaa, and first and foremost, the axis between Jerusalem and Tehran – it is a victorious axis.

耶路撒冷和贝鲁特之间的轴，耶路撒冷和巴格达之间的轴，耶路撒冷和大马士革之间的轴，耶路撒冷和萨纳之间的轴，以及耶路撒冷和德黑兰之间的轴 - 这是一个胜利的轴。

I am not sorry, I am proud to say that the rockets that are used to pound Tel Aviv have an Iranian signature on them, the signature of Qasem Soleimani. The Kornet missiles that we use to blow up the Israeli tanks – the pride of the Israeli and global defense industry – are the Kornet missiles of Qasem Soleimani and Iran. The guns that we use to shoot at the Israeli enemy… Those who use these guns were trained by our brothers in the IRGC. We say this loud and clear.

我不是抱歉，我很自豪地说，习惯于努力捣平特拉维夫的火箭在他们身上有一个伊朗签名，Qasem Soleimani的签名。我们用来炸毁以色列坦克的Kornet导弹 - 以色列和全球国防工业的骄傲 - 是Qasem Soleimani和伊朗的Kornet导弹。我们用来在以色列敌人拍摄的枪支...那些使用这些枪支的人受到IRGC的兄弟们的训练。我们响亮而清晰地说。

My dear brother, most homes in the Gaza strip today… I would like to say just one last thing, if I may. Almost every home in Gaza has a picture of Qasem Soleimani, a picture of the Islamic Republic, as well as a picture of the Imam Khomeini Relief Foundation, so that those who fast [in Ramadan] can have their iftar meals. The Shafi’i Sunni Gazans in the Gaza Strip, from Rafah to Beit Hanoun receive plates of food to break their fast with the seals of Qasem Soleimani and the Islamic Republic of Iran.

亲爱的兄弟，今天加沙地带的大多数家都在......我想说最后一件事，如果我五月。 Gaza的几乎每个房子都有Qasem Soleimani的照片，伊斯兰共和国的一张照片，以及伊玛内·霍梅尼救济基金会的照片，使那些快速[在斋月]的人可以享用欧姆特。来自加沙地带的Shafi'i Sunni Gazans，从拉法到Beit Hanoun接受食物的盘子，以Qasem Soleimani和伊朗伊斯兰共和国的密封迅速打破它们。

I ask Allah to reward Iran, in my name, and the Palestinian people’s name. They are the ones who support us with weapons, money and food.

我要求安拉以我的名义和巴勒斯坦人民的名义奖励伊朗。他们是支持我们武器，金钱和食物的人。
