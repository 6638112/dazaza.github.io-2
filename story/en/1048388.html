Perseverance rover has landed safely on Mars
恒心漫游者已安全降落在火星上

2021-02-19 05:28:33

NASA's Perseverance rover has successfully landed on the surface of Mars, transmitting telemetry information and the first images of its landing site. A low-resolution driving-camera image shows a field of dust-covered rocks, with the unmistakable shadow of the rover hardware. The early images are so fresh that you can still see the dust kicked up by the landing settling.

NASA的恒心漫游车已成功降落在火星表面，传输遥测信息和其降落地点的第一张图像。低分辨率的驾驶摄像机图像显示了一块布满灰尘的岩石，以及流动站硬件的明显阴影。早期的图像是如此新鲜，以至于您仍然可以看到降落过程中扬起的灰尘。

The landing came at the end of a cruise through interplanetary space and a dive through the Martian atmosphere, as the rover and its rocket-supported crane shed parachutes, a heat shield, and a lot of speed. The voyage culminated in the skycrane gently lowering the rover to the surface before rocketing off to land at a safe distance.

降落是在穿越星际空间的巡航和在火星大气层的俯冲之后的，这是因为流动站及其运载火箭的起重机掉落了降落伞，防热罩并提高了速度。航程最终到达了天顶，然后将漫游车轻轻降到水面，然后迅速升空降落到安全距离。

NASA refers to the landing protocol as "seven minutes of terror," due to its complicated, multistage nature, all of which is run under automated guidance. Adding to the tension, all of the outcomes already happened over 10 minutes ago by the time any indication of their success reaches Earth.

NASA将着陆协议称为“恐怖的七分钟”，由于其复杂，多阶段的性质，所有这些操作均在自动化指导下运行。更令人紧张的是，所有成功的迹象都已经在十多分钟前发生了，直到它们成功的迹象到达地球。

Since this isn't the first time NASA has talked about seven minutes of terror, it's tempting to be complacent about the chances for success with the Perseverance landing. But Perseverance is only the second time this complicated landing procedure has been attempted, so past performance wasn't necessarily going to be an indication of future success. But everything about today's landing appears to have worked just as well as its predecessor.

由于这并不是NASA第一次谈论七分钟的恐怖，因此对于毅力登陆成功的机会很自满。但是毅力只是第二次尝试这种复杂的着陆程序，因此过往的表现并不一定表示未来的成功。但是，今天登陆的一切似乎都和它的前任一样运作良好。

With the rover now successfully on the ground, there will be several days of deploying hardware from the compact configuration it was held in for landing while ensuring that everything on board the rover is operating as expected. We should, however, expect to see images from the landing site in the very near future. Photos of the immediate environment will also be used to start planning Perseverance's first activities on the red planet.

随着流动站现已成功在地面上运行，将需要几天的时间将其保持在紧凑的配置中，以用于降落的方式部署硬件，同时确保流动站上的所有设备均按预期运行。但是，我们应该期望在不久的将来会看到来自着陆点的图像。周围环境的照片也将用于开始在红色星球上计划恒心的首次活动。

During this time, the rover will likely transmit images and video taken during the landing protocol. Although we know the landing worked, we'll have to wait for that to have any sense of how it worked, at least from the landing hardware's perspective.

在这段时间内，流动站将可能传输在着陆协议期间拍摄的图像和视频。尽管我们知道着陆是可行的，但至少从着陆硬件的角度来看，我们必须等待它对它的工作方式有任何了解。

In the past, NASA has also arranged for images of the landing area using the superb telescope on the Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter, which can often pick up discarded hardware like the parachutes and heat shield. But the timing of those images will depend on the orbit of the MRO.

过去，NASA还使用火星侦察轨道飞行器上的超卓望远镜为着陆区安排了图像，该望远镜通常可以捡起诸如降落伞和隔热罩之类的废弃硬件。但是这些图像的定时将取决于MRO的轨道。

Perseverance is a bold mission, carrying a small flying drone, a chemistry experiment to test the generation of oxygen from the Martian atmosphere, and hardware to stash samples for a future return to Earth. Expect plenty of updates as the mission proceeds.

坚持不懈是一个大胆的使命，携带小型飞行无人机，化学实验来测试来自火星氛围的氧气的产生，以及硬件填补样品，以便将来返回地球。 期待在任务所需的大量更新。
