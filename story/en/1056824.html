Google announces a virtual I/O from May 18 to May 20, with registration opening soon; the date was hidden behind a puzzle
Google于5月18日至5月20日公布了一个虚拟I / O，即将注册开放; 日期隐藏在难题后面

2021-04-08 01:24:57

After canceling entirely last year, Google today announced that I/O 2021 will be virtual and free to attend. The conference is historically held in May and where Google makes announcements about its biggest developer platforms.

在去年完全取消之后，谷歌今天宣布I / O 2021将是虚拟的，可以免费参加。会议在历史上举行于5月，谷歌对其最大的开发人员平台进行公告。

Update: Our Kyle Bradshaw and Ben Schoon solved the puzzle to reveal that I/O 2021 will take place from May 18-20 and that registration opens soon.

更新：我们的Kyle Bradshaw和Ben Suoon解决了拼图，揭示了从2010年5月18日至2010年5月举行的I / O 2021，即将开放。

This includes Android — with the developer preview switching over to beta, Chrome, and Google Assistant. In 2019, the conference was home to a mid-year Made by Google hardware event that saw the Pixel 3a/XL and Nest Hub Max announced, as well as the unified “Google Nest” brand.

这包括Android - 使用Developer预览切换到Beta，Chrome和Google Assistant。 2019年，会议是由Google Hardaly事件的中期举办的，看到像素3A / XL和Nest Hub Max宣布的，以及统一的“Google Nest”品牌。

In going virtual, I/O is following Apple’s World Wide Developer Conference (WWDC, June 7-11) and Microsoft Build (May 25-27). It’s usually held at the Shoreline Amphitheater in Mountain View right across the Googleplex headquarters. The concert venue’s main stage is used for the keynote and other big talks, while the parking lot is covered in tents for sessions.

在走向虚拟中，I / O在苹果世界宽阔的开发人员大会上（WWDC，6月7日至11日）和Microsoft Build（5月25日至27日）。它通常在山景旁边的海岸线圆形剧场，在山景中横跨广泛的总部。音乐会场地的主舞台用于主题演讲和其他大会谈，而停车场被停车场覆盖。

Google last year took the unprecedented step of not hosting an event whatsoever in light of COVID-19 and surrounding restrictions. The Android team did hold a multi-week event later on in the summer, and other teams hosted smaller events.

谷歌去年采取了前所未有的步骤，而不是在Covid-19和周围的限制范围内举办一个事件。 Android团队在夏季稍后举行了一个多周的活动，其他团队托管了较小的活动。

In announcing the existence of I/O 2021, Google — like in past years — is shielding the actual dates behind a puzzle. This year’s “punch card” is available here, and the solution is below:

在宣布存在I / O 2021的存在时，谷歌 - 就像在过去几年一样 - 屏蔽拼图后面的实际日期。今年的“打卡”可在此处提供，解决方案如下：

Tap “sort” button, rearrange functions from 1-6, use arrow keys to navigate to column 79, click “sort” again, repeat until column 70, and then “submit”

点按“排序”按钮，重新排列函数从1-6，使用箭头键导航到第79栏，再次单击“排序”，重复直到第70栏，然后“提交”
