NASA和诺基亚将在月球表面安装4G

2020-10-20 23:49:18

With competition among Earth’s telecoms providers as fierce as ever, equipment maker Nokia has announced its expansion into a new market, winning a deal to install the first cellular network on the moon.

随着地球电信运营商之间的竞争一如既往地激烈，设备制造商诺基亚宣布向一个新市场扩张，赢得了在月球上安装第一个蜂窝网络的协议。

The Finnish equipment manufacturer said it was selected by Nasa to deploy an “ultra-compact, low-power, space-hardened” wireless 4G network on the lunar surface, as part of the US space agency’s plan to establish a long-term human presence on the moon by 2030.

这家芬兰设备制造商表示，它被美国国家航空航天局(NASA)选中，在月球表面部署“超紧凑、低功耗、太空加固”的无线4G网络，这是美国航天局在2030年之前在月球上建立长期人类存在计划的一部分。

The $14.1m contract, awarded to Nokia’s US subsidiary, is part of Nasa’s Artemis programme which aims to send the first woman, and next man, to the moon by 2024.

这份价值1410万美元的合同授予了诺基亚的美国子公司，是美国宇航局Artemis计划的一部分，该计划的目标是在2024年之前将第一名女性，也就是下一名男性送上月球。

The astronauts will begin carrying out detailed experiments and explorations which the agency hopes will help it develop its first human mission to Mars.

宇航员们将开始进行详细的实验和探索，该机构希望这将有助于它开展第一次人类登上火星的任务。

Nokia’s network equipment will be installed remotely on the moon’s surface using a lunar hopper built by Intuitive Machines in late 2022, Nokia said.

诺基亚表示，2022年末，诺基亚的网络设备将使用直观机器建造的月球漏斗远程安装在月球表面。

“The network will self-configure upon deployment,” the firm said in a statement, adding that the wireless technology will allow for “vital command and control functions, remote control of lunar rovers, real-time navigation and streaming of high definition video.”

“网络将在部署后自我配置，”该公司在一份声明中说，并补充说，无线技术将允许“重要的指挥和控制功能，远程控制月球车，实时导航和高清视频流媒体”。

The 4G equipment can be updated to a super-fast 5G network in the future, Nokia said.

诺基亚表示，未来4G设备可以升级到超高速的5G网络。

In all, Nasa announced last week it would distribute $370m to 14 companies to supply “Tipping Point” technologies for its mission, which include robotics and new methods of harvesting the resources required for living on the moon, such as oxygen and energy sources.

总体而言，美国国家航空航天局(NASA)上周宣布，将向14家公司拨款3.7亿美元，为其任务提供“引爆点”技术，其中包括机器人技术，以及获取月球上生活所需资源(如氧气和能源)的新方法。

The bulk of the funding went to companies researching cryogenic propellants, freezing liquids used to fuel spacecraft.

大部分资金流向了研究低温推进剂的公司，低温推进剂是用于为航天器提供燃料的冷冻液体。

Among them, Elon Musk’s SpaceX received $53.2m for a demonstration of the transferring of ten metric tons of liquid oxygen between tanks on a starship vehicle, Nasa said.

其中，美国宇航局表示，埃隆·马斯克的SpaceX获得5320万美元，用于演示在星际飞船上的坦克之间转移10吨液氧。
