Samsung is will soon ship Micro LED TVs, but MiniLED still leads the lineup
三星即将推出Micro LED电视，但MiniLED仍然领先

2021-03-03 05:36:12

It's that time of year when many TV manufacturers begin announcing prices for and shipping their annual product refreshes. We took a look at Sony's OLED lineup yesterday, but today we're turning our attention to Samsung, which just announced imminent availability (most models will start shipping this month) for its high-end Micro LED and MiniLED TV lineup.

每年的那个时候，许多电视制造商开始宣布其年度产品更新的价格并发布。昨天我们看了索尼的OLED产品阵容，但今天我们将注意力转向三星，三星刚刚宣布其高端Micro LED和MiniLED即将上市（大多数型号将在本月开始发货）。电视节目单。

We'll get to Micro LED in a minute, but let's start with the mainstream high-end, which are the MiniLED TVs. Samsung is giving these a proprietary "Neo QLED" label.

我们将在一分钟内介绍Micro LED，但让我们从主流高端产品开始，即MiniLED电视。三星为这些产品提供了专有的“ Neo QLED”。标签。

The top-end QN900A is the most tricked-out 8K option, with 65-inch ($5,000), 75-inch ($7,000), and 85-inch options ($9,000). One step down while keeping the 8K banner flying is the QN800A, offered in the same sizes but at $3,500, $4,700, and $6,500, respectively.

高端QN900A是配置最丰富的8K选件，具有65英寸（5,000美元），75英寸（7,000美元）和85英寸（9,000美元）选项。 QN800A在保持8K横幅飘扬的同时又下降了一步，它的尺寸相同，但价格分别为3500美元，4700美元和6500美元。

Since there's hardly any 8K content out there to enjoy, most people who aren't just looking for bragging rights will want to opt for the 4K models. The flagship there is the QN90A, at 55 inches ($1,800), 65 inches ($2,600), 75 inches ($3,500), and 85 inches ($5,000).

由于几乎没有8K内容可以享受，因此大多数不只是在吹牛的人都会选择4K模型。旗舰产品是QN90A，分别为55英寸（1,800美元），65英寸（2,600美元），75英寸（3,500美元）和85英寸（5,000美元）。

One step down gets you the QN85A, which comes in the same sizes as the QN90A at $1,600, $2,200, $3,000, and $4,500.

降低一倍即可获得QN85A，其尺寸与QN90A相同，分别为1,600美元，2,200美元，3,000美元和4,500美元。

Across the new TVs offered, we're looking at the usual suspects for high-end TVs in 2021, regardless of manufacturer, like HDMI 2.1 with all the features you expect to come with it, including VRR, 4K120, ALLM, and eARC. There's also Filmmaker Mode, and a one-stop pop-up menu for accessing HDMI 2.1 and gaming-related features like VRR. (LG introduced something similar in its 2021 TVs.)

在提供的所有新电视中，我们正在研究2021年高端电视的常见嫌疑人，不论制造商如何，例如HDMI 2.1，它具有您希望随附的所有功能，包括VRR，4K120，ALLM和eARC。还有电影制片人模式，以及一站式弹出菜单，用于访问HDMI 2.1和与游戏相关的功能，例如VRR。 （LG在其2021年电视中推出了类似的产品。）

And as usual, Samsung isn't playing ball with Dolby, so there's no support for Dolby Vision HDR (or Atmos, for that matter). Rather, you'll have to lean on either the just-as-good-in-most-cases-but-inferior-in-some-situations HDR-10 standard, as well as HDR-10+, which remains a little light on content.

而且与往常一样，三星不与杜比竞争，因此不支持杜比视界HDR（就此而言为Atmos）。相反，您将不得不依靠在大多数情况下都很好但在某些情况下不如HDR-10标准以及HDR-10 +仍然是内容很少。

And of course, like every other big 2021 TV, the new MiniLED sets will feature an improved AI processor that does video and audio processing to maximize the wow factor.

当然，与其他所有大型2021电视一样，新的MiniLED电视机将配备经过改进的AI处理器，该处理器可以进行视频和音频处理，以最大程度地提高哇音系数。

The other big news with the 2021 TVs is that Samsung is (sort of) walking back a widely criticized move it made to its lineup in 2020. That was when the company actually downgraded the number of dimming zones and some other features in its 4K TVs relative to their 2019 predecessors, in favor of pushing the envelope in its 8K portfolio instead. The 8K TVs still have more dimming zones, but we're not looking at a huge year-over-year drop like last time. That's because Samsung's new 4K models will also feature MiniLED tech just like the 8K TVs—which wasn't a forgone conclusion, given what happened last year.

2021年电视的另一个大新闻是，三星正在（某种程度上）退回它在2020年对其阵容进行的广泛批评。这是该公司实际上降低了其4K电视的调光区数量和其他一些功能的时候相对于其2019年的前代产品，赞成在其8K产品组合中突破极限。 8K电视仍具有更多的调光区域，但我们并没有像上次那样看到较大的同比下降。那是因为三星的新型4K型号还将采用MiniLED技术，就像8K电视一样，考虑到去年的情况，这并不是一个定局。

Samsung says its less expensive TVs will launch later in the year, and it didn't provide any information about them that we didn't already see at the Consumer Electronics Show in January.

三星表示，其价格较低的电视将在今年晚些时候推出，并且没有提供我们在一月份的消费电子展上尚未看到的有关电视的任何信息。

While much of the hype in the world of TVs is currently focused on OLED, Samsung's LCD TVs remain the best-selling TVs in many regions, and in-depth technical reviewers like Rtings pretty consistently name Samsung's sets as the best non-OLED ones available in terms of picture quality, albeit not always in bang-for-buck. Samsung doesn't even make OLED TVs, though it produces OLED panels for other products. And to battle OLED potentially in the long term, Samsung is relying on Micro LED technology, which has individually emissive pixels just like OLED does. That means Micro LED matches OLED's chief advantage, which is that pixels of maximum brightness appear right next to pixels that are completely black. But Samsung claims the burn-in risk associated with OLED is not a factor in the same way with Micro LED.

尽管目前电视界的大肆炒作都集中在OLED上，但三星的LCD电视仍然是许多地区最畅销的电视，而像Rtings这样的深入技术评论家也始终将三星的电视机命名为“电视机”。就图片质量而言，它是最好的非OLED显示器，尽管并非总是物有所值。三星甚至不生产OLED电视，尽管它为其他产品生产OLED面板。为了长期与OLED对抗，三星依靠Micro LED技术，该技术与OLED一样具有单独的发射像素。这意味着Micro LED符合OLED的主要优势，即最大亮度的像素出现在全黑像素的旁边。但是三星声称与OLED相关的老化风险不是Micro LED带来的老化因素。

Plus, OLED TVs have been knocked for not matching the HDR peak brightness of the best traditional LED TVs. Micro LED is said to combine the best of both worlds: perfect blacks with very high peak brightness, and all the granularity you expect in-between.

另外，OLED电视因无法与最佳传统LED电视的HDR峰值亮度匹配而被淘汰。据说Micro LED结合了两个方面的优点：完美的黑色和极高的峰值亮度，以及您期望的所有粒度。

Micro LED TVs have been talked up as the future TV tech for years, and they've been commercially available in very limited contexts before, but this year marks Samsung's first quasi-mainstream attempt to sell a bunch of them.

微型LED电视多年来一直被认为是未来的电视技术，并且以前在非常有限的环境中已经可以商业使用，但是今年标志着三星首次准主流销售此类产品的尝试。 。

They still won't be for everyone though. They're sure to be colossally expensive, for one thing, but they'll also only come in 110- and 99-inch sizes to start. Later we'll get 88- and 76-inch sizes, but even those are bigger than most people's living rooms can accommodate.

他们仍然不会适合所有人。一方面，它们肯定会非常昂贵，但它们也只有110英寸和99英寸两种尺寸。稍后我们将获得88英寸和76英寸的尺寸，但即使那些尺寸也超出了大多数人的客厅所能容纳的尺寸。

So for its more mainstream flagship TVs, Samsung is leaning on MiniLED, which is not the same as similarly named Micro LED. MiniLED TVs are still fundamentally the same technology as any other LCD TV the company has sold for years, but with a new approach that allows much more granular backlighting to reduce blooming around bright objects and other problems associated with LCD TVs while still delivering strong peak brightness.

因此，对于更主流的旗舰电视而言，三星正采用MiniLED，这与类似的Micro LED不同。 MiniLED电视在本质上仍与该公司多年来销售的任何其他液晶电视相同，但采用了一种新方法，该技术可以提供更细粒度的背光，以减少明亮物体周围的光晕和与液晶电视相关的其他问题，同时仍可提供强大的峰值亮度。

Expect to see the term MiniLED popping up a lot in the very near future, while Micro LED will probably stay out of the mainstream for a while yet. Other companies, like Apple, are bullish on MiniLED and are poised to roll it out to all sorts of products like laptops and tablets in the coming months.

希望在不久的将来会看到“ MiniLED”一词大量出现，而Micro LED可能会暂时退出主流。苹果等其他公司则看好MiniLED，并准备在未来几个月内将其推广到笔记本电脑和平板电脑等各种产品。

As if the stew of terms like "OLED," "LED," "LCD," "MiniLED," and "Micro LED" wasn't confusing enough, Samsung has opted to brand its MiniLED sets "Neo QLED" TVs, which essentially means nothing other than that Samsung can claim it's the only one making "Neo QLED" TVs.

It called its previous high-end LED TVs "QLED" TVs because of an optimization dubbed quantum dot technology, which was the previous big push to make LCD TVs more competitive with OLED before MiniLED came along. "QLED" will remain the label for the company's mid-range LCD sets.
