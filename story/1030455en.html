美国宇航局的奥西里斯-雷克斯成功接触到小行星本努

2020-10-22 10:06:21

10/21/2020 3:37AM      For the first time, a NASA spacecraft successfully brushed the surface of an ancient asteroid to collect samples and return them to Earth. WSJ’s Robert Lee Hotz explains how Osiris-REx’s mission to Bennu could shed light on the origins of life on Earth. Photo: Handout/AFP/Getty Images
