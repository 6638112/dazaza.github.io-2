Photographer Sues Kat Von D over Miles Davis Tattoo
摄影师在Miles Davis纹身上起诉Kat Von D

2021-02-17 18:28:34

A photographer has filed a lawsuit against celebrity tattoo artist Kat Von D, accusing her of infringing on his copyright by using his photo of iconic jazz musician Miles Davis as a tattoo for a client. Los Angeles-based photographer Jeff Sedlik created the iconic portrait of Davis in 1989, and the photo was subsequently published in a cover story in JAZZIZ magazine in August of that year. Since then, it has been widely published in magazines around the world, and it was featured by LIFE magazine in a “Pictures of the Year” issue.

一位摄影师对名人纹身艺术家Kat Von D提起诉讼，指责她使用其标志性爵士音乐家Miles Davis的照片作为客户纹身来侵犯其版权。洛杉矶摄影师杰夫·塞德里克（Jeff Sedlik）于1989年创作了标志性的戴维斯肖像，随后这张照片随后于当年8月发表在《爵士》杂志的封面故事中。从那以后，它在世界各地的杂志上广泛发行，并被《生活》杂志在“年度图片”杂志上刊登。

Kat Von D is the owner of High Voltage Tattoo in Hollywood, and both she and her tattoo business were made famous by the ongoing TLC reality TV series LA Ink. Kat Von D’s Instagram account boasts over 7.4 million followers.

凯特·冯·D（Kat Von D）是好莱坞“高压纹身”的所有者，她和她的纹身业务都因正在进行的TLC真人秀电视连续剧《洛杉矶墨水》而闻名。 Kat Von D的Instagram帐户拥有超过740万关注者。

In March 2017, Kat Von D used Sedlik’s photo to create a tattoo that she drew on a client’s arm. She publicized this tattoo several times on social media, including in these two Instagram posts.

2017年3月，Kat Von D使用Sedlik的照片制作了她画在客户手臂上的纹身。她在社交媒体上多次宣传了这种纹身，包括在这两个Instagram帖子中。

The problem is, Kat Von D never requested or received a license or permission from Sedlik to reproduce the photo, which has been sold with a non-exclusive license for reproduction, distribution, and display ever since its creation back in 1989.

问题是，Kat Von D从未要求或未获得Sedlik的许可或复制照片，自1989年创建以来，该照片就以非专有许可出售，用于复制，分发和展示。
