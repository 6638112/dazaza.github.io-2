After teasing a new social media platform, Trump debuts a new section on his website that's effectively a blog, ahead of the Oversight Board's decision on Wed.
在取笑新的社交媒体平台后，特朗普在他的网站上首次亮相，这是一个有效的博客，领先于监督委员会关于周三的决定。

2021-05-05 21:01:47

After months of promising his own social media network for banned posters, former President Donald Trump on Tuesday launched a new section of his website — essentially a standard-issue blog.

经过几个月的对禁止海报的社交媒体网络，前总统唐纳德特朗普星期二推出了他网站的新部分 - 基本上是一个标准问题博客。

The “platform” is styled like of Twitter but hosted as a running blog of Twitter-length commentary from Trump. People can sign up for post alerts on the platform through their email and phone numbers and are allegedly able to like them, although that function doesn’t appear to work as of publication. Users are also allowed to share Trump’s posts on Facebook and Twitter. The Twitter sharing option doesn’t currently work, but Facebook’s does allow people to share Trump’s posts.

“平台”是如Twitter的样式，但托管为特朗普的Twitter-Lenge评论的运行博客。人们可以通过电子邮件和电话号码注册平台上的警报，并被据称能够喜欢它们，虽然该功能似乎没有发布。用户也可以在Facebook和Twitter上分享特朗普的帖子。 Twitter共享选项目前不起作用，但Facebook的确实允许人们分享特朗普的帖子。

The new “platform” is effectively a blog styled like a generic version of Twitter

新的“平台”有效地是一个像推特的通用版本一样风格的博客

“Generally, sharing content from the website reference is permitted as long as the material does not otherwise the Twitter Rules,” a Twitter spokesperson told The Verge on Tuesday. Facebook did not immediately respond to a request for comment.

“一般来说，只要材料没有否则推特规则，就允许从网站参考分享内容，”Twitter发言人周二告诉边缘。 Facebook没有立即回复评论请求。

Even though the platform formally launched on Tuesday, there are posts dating back to March 24th. Trump’s latest post is a video advertising his new platform, calling it “a place to speak freely and safely, straight from the desk of Donald J. Trump.” According to Fox News, Trump will “eventually” be able to communicate with his supporters directly, although it’s not clear how that will happen. Trump’s press office did not immediately respond to a request for comment

尽管该平台在星期二正式启动，但有员额可追溯到3月24日。特朗普的最新帖子是一个视频广告他的新平台，称之为“从唐纳德J.特朗普桌面自由而安全地说话的地方。”根据Fox新闻，特朗普将“最终”能够直接与他的支持者沟通，虽然目前尚不清楚如何发生这种情况。特朗普的新闻处没有立即回复评论请求

The launch comes just a day before Facebook’s Oversight Board is set to announce whether Trump will be allowed back on Facebook and other Facebook-owned social media platforms like Instagram. Trump was banned from Facebook and his favorite posting platform Twitter in January after a right-wing mob stormed the US Capitol, leaving five people dead. Facebook’s quasi-judicial moderation panel plans to announce whether the former president can return on Wednesday morning.

在Facebook的监督委员会宣布Facebook和其他Facebook拥有的社交媒体平台上，这次发布会仅在Facebook的监督委员会宣布宣布。从Facebook禁止特朗普和他最喜欢的发布平台Twitter在一个右翼暴徒撞上美国国会大厦后，让五个人死了。 Facebook的准司法审核小组计划宣布前总统是否可以在周三早上返回。

The platform appears to have been built by Campaign Nucleus, a digital services company founded by Trump’s former campaign manager Brad Parscale.

该平台似乎是由Campaign Nucleus建造的数字服务公司，该公司由特朗普前竞选经理Brad Parscale成立。

在5月5日更新7:53 ET：本文的早期版本使用WordPress作为现成的博客软件的示例，导致一些读者假设特朗普的网站在WordPress上运行。 它不是，我们已经删除了参考以避免混淆。
