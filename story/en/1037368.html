Coinbase says that, starting in early 2021, it will begin supporting Ethereum 2.0, letting users convert ETH into ETH2 and earn staking rewards
Coinbase表示，从2021年初开始，它将开始支持以太坊2.0，让用户将ETH转换为ETH2并获得赌注

2020-12-01 03:14:09

On December 1, the Ethereum Beacon Chain will launch, beginning a multi-year upgrade to Ethereum 2.0 (ETH2).

12月1日，以太坊信标链将启动，开始对以太坊2.0（ETH2）的多年升级。

Coinbase intends to support ETH2 through staking and trading. Coinbase customers will be able to convert ETH in their Coinbase accounts to ETH2 and earn staking rewards.

Coinbase打算通过抵押和交易来支持ETH2。 Coinbase客户将能够将其Coinbase帐户中的ETH转换为ETH2，并获得赌注奖励。

While staked ETH2 tokens remain locked on the beacon chain, Coinbase will also enable trading between ETH2, ETH, and all other supported currencies providing liquidity for our customers.

当抵押的ETH2代币仍保持在信标链上时，Coinbase也将允许ETH2，ETH和所有其他受支持货币之间的交易，从而为我们的客户提供流动性。

We’ll be launching the above features to customers in eligible jurisdictions starting in early 2021. We’ll communicate more details closer to the launch of each feature.

从2021年初开始，我们将向符合条件的辖区的客户推出上述功能。

Since Ethereum launched in 2015, a vibrant developer community has developed building various applications such as decentralized lending protocols, decentralized exchanges, games, and many others.

自2015年以太坊启动以来，一个充满活力的开发者社区已开发出构建各种应用程序的应用程序，例如去中心化的借贷协议，去中心化的交易所，游戏等。

ETH2 intends to solve two of the biggest challenges within blockchain development: limited scalability and high gas prices. Over the course of two years, the Ethereum community plans to increase scale and migrate the current Ethereum chain to the ETH2 chain.

ETH2旨在解决区块链开发中的两个最大挑战：有限的可扩展性和高油价。在两年的过程中，以太坊社区计划扩大规模，并将当前的以太坊链迁移到ETH2链。

We’re excited for this major milestone for ETH2, and the potential that a final rollout of ETH2 holds for the entire cryptocurrency community.

我们为ETH2的这一重要里程碑感到高兴，并为整个加密货币社区拥有最终推出ETH2的潜力而感到兴奋。
