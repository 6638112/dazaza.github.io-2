India proposes tougher e-commerce rules to address ‘widespread cheating’ complaints
印度提出了更强硬的电子商务规则来解决“广泛欺骗”投诉

2021-06-22 01:47:10

The proposal, unveiled by India’s Ministry of Consumer Affairs on Monday evening, comes at a time when brick-and-mortar retailers in India have ramped up their complaints to raise concerns about the what they allege as unfair practices employed by Amazon and Flipkart as they expand their operations in the country.

印度傍晚的消费者事务部推出的提案是在印度的砂浆零售商升起投诉时提出对亚马逊和弗里帕特雇用的不公平做法的担忧来提高担忧扩大他们在该国的业务。

In its proposal, India’s Ministry of Consumer Affairs said that e-commerce firms should not be allowed to hold flash sales in India. These flash sales, akin to Black Friday and Cyber Monday sales in the U.S., are very popular during festive season in the country. During flash sales e-commerce firms have traditionally observed the biggest spikes in customer orders as brands offer heavy discounts on their products.

在其提案中，印度的消费者事务部表示，在印度不应允许电子商务公司举行闪存销量。这些闪光销售，类似于美国的黑色星期五和网络周一销量在该国节日期间非常受欢迎。在Flash销售期间，电子商务公司传统上传统地观察了客户订单中最大的尖峰，因为品牌为其产品提供重大折扣。

“Certain e-commerce entities are engaging in limiting consumer choice by indulging in ‘back to back’ or ‘flash’ sales wherein one seller selling on platform does not carry any inventory or order fulfilment capability but merely places a ‘flash or back to back’ order with another seller controlled by platform. This prevents a level playing field and ultimately limits customer choice and increases prices,” the ministry said in a statement.

“某些电子商务实体正在通过沉迷于”背靠背“或”闪存“销售来限制消费者选择，其中一个在平台上销售的卖方不携带任何库存或订单履行能力，而只是将”闪光或返回给予返回'与另一个卖方的订单由平台控制。这可以防止水平竞争场，最终限制客户选择并提高价格，“该部在一份声明中表示。

As it has done with its recent IT rules, India is also proposing that e-commerce firms appoint a chief compliance officer, a nodal contact person for 24×7 coordination with law enforcement agencies, and officers to ensure compliance to their orders and as well as a resident grievance officer for redressing of the grievances of the consumers on the e-commerce platform.

由于它曾遵守其最近的IT规则，印度还提出电子商务公司委任一名首席合规人员，一个节点联系人，一个节点联系人24×7与执法机构协调，以及官员，以确保遵守其订单以及其订单作为驻地申诉官员，用于纠正电子商务平台上消费者的申诉。

“This would ensure effective compliance with the provisions of the Act and Rules and also strengthen the grievance redressal mechanism on e-commerce entities,” the ministry said.

“这将确保有效遵守法案和规则的规定，并加强了对电子商务实体的申诉建立机制，”该部说。

Amazon, which has invested over $6.5 billion in its India business, said it was reviewing the proposed policies while Flipkart, whose majority stake Walmart bought for $16 billion in 2018, had no immediate comment.

在印度业务中投入超过65亿美元的亚马逊表示正在审查拟议的政策，而Flipkart于2018年以每年为160亿美元购买的大多数股权沃尔玛，没有立即发表评论。

In a court hearing on Monday, a Flipkart lawyer said the company sees nothing wrong in offering to cut charges for sellers on its platform if they lower product prices.

在星期一的法院听证会上，一家佛里帕特律师表示，如果他们降低产品价格，该公司在其平台上削减了卖方的收费没有错。

The ministry said it is making the proposal, for which it plans to seek industry feedback over the next 15 days, after receiving “several complaints against widespread cheating and unfair trade practices being observed in e-commerce ecosystem.”

该部表示正在提出该提案，它计划在收到“在电子商务生态系统中观察到的普遍作弊和不公平贸易实践”的几个投诉后，在未来15天内寻求行业反馈。

Additionally, the new proposal asks e-commerce firms to introduce a mechanism to identify goods on their platforms based on their country of origin and suggest alternatives to “ensure fair opportunity to domestic goods.”

此外，新的提案要求电子商务公司介绍一项机制，以根据其原产国识别其平台上的货物，并建议“确保为国内商品提供公平机会”的替代方案。

The announcement comes at a time when Flipkart is in talks to raise as much as $3 billion and explore the public markets. Both Amazon and Flipkart are also the subject of an ongoing antitrust probe in India.

宣布是在Flipkart在谈判中筹集30亿美元并探索公共市场的时刻。 Amazon和Flipkart都也是印度正在进行的反垄断探针的主题。

This is the second major amendment the Indian government has proposed in recent years. Long-standing laws in India have constrained Amazon and other e-commerce firms to not hold inventory or sell items directly to consumers. To bypass this, firms have operated through a maze of joint ventures with local companies that operate as inventory-holding firms.

这是印度政府近年来提出的第二大修正案。印度的长期法律约束了亚马逊和其他电子商务公司不会将物品直接存放给消费者。为了绕过这一点，公司已经通过迷宫经营着合资企业，当地公司作为库存控股公司运营。

After complaints from offline retailers, India got around to fixing this loophole in late 2018 in a move that was widely seen as the biggest blowback to the American firm in the country at the time. Amazon and Walmart-owned Flipkart scrambled to delist hundreds of thousands of items from their stores and made their investments in affiliated firms way more indirect.

在从下线零售商投诉后，印度在2018年底在2018年底举行了这一漏洞，这一举措将被广泛被广泛被视为当时美国公司最大的倒置。亚马逊和沃尔玛所有的Flipkart争先恐后地从他们的商店争夺了数十万物品，并使他们对附属公司的投资更加间接。
