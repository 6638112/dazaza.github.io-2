Microsoft releases new all-in-one Office app for iPads
微软发布适用于iPad的全新多合一Office应用程序

2021-02-17 19:17:26

Microsoft yesterday released an all-in-one Office app for iPads, bringing a new approach to Office on mobile previously seen on iOS and Android to iPadOS. The app combines Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and a number of mobile device-specific features, including PDF management tools and a file browser.

微软昨天发布了一款适用于iPad的多合一Office应用程序，为以前在iOS和Android上到iPadOS的移动办公带来了一种新的方法。该应用程序结合了Word，Excel，PowerPoint和许多特定于移动设备的功能，包括PDF管理工具和文件浏览器。

A year ago almost to the day, Microsoft released this update to iPhones and Android phones, and the update has appeared on some Android tablets as well. As an iPhone app, it previously ran on iPads but only in a windowed mode of sorts. This new version has a full-screen tablet interface and supports various iPad multitasking features. Users can still download individual Word, Excel, or PowerPoint apps in the iPad's App Store; the standalone apps and this all-in-one option seem likely to coexist at least for a while, as the standalone alternatives haven't gone away on the iPhone.

一年前，直到今天，Microsoft才向iPhone和Android手机发布了此更新，并且该更新也出现在某些Android平板电脑上。作为iPhone应用程序，它以前可以在iPad上运行，但只能以某种窗口模式运行。此新版本具有全屏平板电脑界面，并支持各种iPad多任务处理功能。用户仍可以在iPad的App Store中下载单个Word，Excel或PowerPoint应用程序；独立应用程序和这种多合一选项似乎至少可以并存一段时间，因为独立替代品尚未在iPhone上消失。

Apart from making working between the three productivity apps a little more seamless, the app's main value proposition is the "Actions" panel. In it, users can access quick-action icons to transfer files between devices, convert images to text or tables, sign or scan PDFs, convert PDFs to word, scan QR codes, and do a few other things. The full list is pictured in the gallery above.

除了使这三个生产力应用程序之间的工作更为无缝之外，该应用程序的主要价值主张是“动作”。控制板。在其中，用户可以访问快速图标来在设备之间传输文件，将图像转换为文本或表格，对PDF进行签名或扫描，将PDF转换为单词，扫描QR码以及执行其他一些操作。完整列表显示在上方的画廊中。

Users can access files stored locally on the device, in the cloud via OneDrive, or in iCloud.

用户可以访问本地存储在设备上，通过OneDrive或iCloud在云中的文件。

The app is available for download today in the App Store for free, but it requires a Microsoft 365 subscription to access all features.

该应用程序今天可以在App Store中免费下载，但需要Microsoft 365订阅才能访问所有功能。

iPad manufacturer Apple has been making significant software and hardware changes to make the iPad a fully viable desktop or laptop replacement, including branching iOS off into a new, iPad-specific version named iPadOS. In our reviews of iPadOS, we have found the operating system functional and sometimes even exceptional, but many frustrations still remain.

iPad制造商Apple一直在进行重大的软件和硬件更改，以使iPad成为完全可行的台式机或笔记本电脑替代品，包括将iOS分支到名为iPadOS的新的iPad专用版本中。在我们对iPadOS的评论中，我们发现该操作系统可以运行，有时甚至是出色的，但仍然存在许多挫败感。

Apple is expected to debut a new iPad Pro in the next few months, and the company may add more productivity features with that hardware update.

预计苹果将在未来几个月内推出新的iPad Pro，并且该硬件更新可能会增加更多的生产力功能。
