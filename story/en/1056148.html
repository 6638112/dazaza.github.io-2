谷歌允许所有应用程序一直在最终查看所有安装的应用程序（怀孕，跟踪，保险，银行，家庭设备）

2021-04-05 03:17:51

Google has announced an update to its Developer Program Policy that will help to prevent applications from viewing which other apps are installed on an Android device. The company states that they consider installed apps to be private user information and therefore, aim to protect Android users by keeping this data secure. That is to say, Google will limit which apps can request the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission, presently mandatory for application targeting API level 30 (Android 11) and above that wish to query the list of application a user has installed for an Android 11 or later .

Google已宣布更新其开发人员程序策略，有助于防止应用程序查看Android设备上安装了哪些其他应用程序。该公司表示，他们认为已安装应用程序是私人用户信息，因此，旨在通过保持此数据安全来保护Android用户。也就是说，谷歌将限制哪些应用程序可以请求query_all_packages权限，目前强制性地用于目标API级别30（Android 11）及以上希望查询用户已安装用于Android 11或更高版本的应用程序列表。

From now on, the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission will only be available when the core functionality of an app in question must query any of the device's installed applications. Therefore, in order to dispute this , developers will have to provide reasonable evidence for how querying the API of an Android devices installed applications is absolutely necessary in order for that device to properly function.

从现在开始，query_all_packages权限只会在问题的核心功能必须查询任何设备和＃39; s已安装的应用程序时可用。因此，为了解决这个问题，开发人员必须提供合理的证据，了解如何查询Android设备的API是绝对必要的，以便该设备正确运行。

Google describes the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission as allowing any apps that require access to the installed apps list in order to remain aware an interoperable during use. Such a requirement would be relevant for antivirus apps, browsers, device search and file managers.

谷歌介绍了Query_All_Packages权限，允许任何需要访问已安装的应用程序列表的应用程序，以便在使用期间保持互操作。这种要求对于防病毒应用程序，浏览器，设备搜索和文件管理器是相关的。

If an app doesn't meet the aforementioned eligibility points, the Play Policy dictates that the must remove the application. In fact, even when an app does meat the packages requirement, the developer must still sign a declaration form in the Play Console. Failing to sign this form could result in the app being removed from the Google Play Store.

如果APP不符合上述资格点，则播放策略决定了必须删除应用程序。实际上，即使应用程序确实肉类要求，开发人员仍必须在播放控制台中签署声明表单。未能签署此表单可能导致应用程序从Google Play商店删除。

This update will go into effect on May 5 of 2021, and as of November of 2021, all apps submitted to the Google Play Store must target Android 11 or higher. The policy ultimately seeks to safeguard users against any malicious advertising or other such activity that may happen when an external entity can view apps installed on a device. In fact, a similar and longer-standing developer policy had a similar goal with enabling the user to opt out of applications such as Facebook interacting with apps installed on their mobile device.

此更新将于2021年5月5日生效，截至2021年11月，提交给Google Play商店的所有应用程序必须针对Android 11或更高版本。该政策最终要求保护用户免受任何恶意广告或其他可能发生的此类活动，当外部实体可以查看设备上安装的应用程序时可能会发生。事实上，类似和更长的开发人员政策具有类似的目标，使用户能够选择退出诸如Facebook的应用程序与在其移动设备上安装的应用程序进行交互。
