Unfortunately, proving who YOU are with any sort of ID or ID verification does NOT prove you own any specific account. Any solution like you propose would require such proof of ID when the account was created so there would be something to compare with. VERY few people would agree to that for the creation of an e-mail account.

不幸的是，通过任何种类的ID或ID验证来证明您的身份并不能证明您拥有任何特定帐户。像您建议的任何解决方案在创建帐户时都需要提供此类ID证明，以便进行比较。很少有人会同意创建电子邮件帐户。

A MUCH more useful procedure would be for users to add a recover phone and recovery e-mail to their account so they have a way to verify ownership if there is a problem. Connected to that is keeping those options up-to-date over the years so when they are needed, they actually work.

对于用户来说，更有用的过程是将备用电话和备用电子邮件添加到他们的帐户，以便他们可以在出现问题时验证所有权。与此相关的是，这些年来这些选项一直保持最新状态，因此在需要它们时，它们便会真正起作用。
