PS5拆箱：索尼的大个子，曲线优美的男孩在任何房间里都很突出

2020-10-27 20:07:46

As you may have heard by now, we have received the PlayStation 5 at Ars' orbiting headquarters, ahead of its official launch on Nov. 12. We're still limited in just what we can tell you about the system itself until that launch gets a little closer. One thing we can talk about, though, is the console's design—that is, the physical shell that houses all those electronics.

你可能已经听说了，在11月12日正式发布之前，我们已经在Ars轨道总部收到了PlayStation5。在发布日期变得更近之前，我们对该系统本身所能提供的信息仍然有限。不过，有一件事我们可以谈论，那就是游戏机的设计--也就是容纳所有这些电子产品的物理外壳。

The most striking thing about the PS5 case (especially the disc-drive-sporting version we received) remains just how big it is. We've known for a while that the PS5 was set to be the biggest console in decades, of course. But it's one thing to know that intellectually. It's another to see it in your home, dwarfing most any other piece of consumer electronics you've ever owned (as you can see in some of these photos).

PS5最引人注目的地方(尤其是我们收到的光驱运动版)仍然是它有多大。当然，我们早就知道PS5将成为几十年来最大的游戏机。但在智力上了解这一点是一回事。在你家里看到它是另一回事，几乎让你曾经拥有过的任何其他消费电子产品都相形见绌(正如你在一些照片中看到的那样)。

When you lift the PS5, though, it feels a little lighter than you might expect, given its physical size. At 9.92 pounds, it's slightly less heavy than the (smaller) launch-era PlayStation 3, for instance. And while the PS5 is heavier than the 7.3 pound PS4 Pro comes, it feels much less dense than that earlier system when lifted.

不过，当你拿起PS5时，考虑到它的物理尺寸，它感觉比你想象的要轻一点。例如，它重9.92磅，比(更小的)发射时代的PlayStation3稍微轻一些。虽然PS5比7.3磅的PS4 Pro更重，但抬起时感觉比之前的系统密度要小得多。

That might be because a lot of the PS5's apparent size is taken up by empty space. There's a lot of room in between the PS5's undulating white exterior panels that's taken up by nothing but air, especially around the rounded corners of the core black casing (and we've seen just how that space plays out when the system is taken apart by Sony engineers). The stark contrast between the smooth white paneling and the shiny black interior casing can trick the eye, too, making the system seem a bit sleeker than a simple hard-cornered box with similar dimensions.

这可能是因为PS5的外观尺寸有很大一部分被空白处占据了。PS5起伏的白色外板之间有很大的空间，只有空气占据了这些空间，特别是在核心黑色外壳的圆角周围(我们已经看到，当系统被索尼的工程师拆卸时，这个空间是如何发挥的)。光滑的白色镶板和闪亮的黑色内壳之间的鲜明对比也可以欺骗眼睛，使系统看起来比类似尺寸的简单硬角盒子更时髦一些。

Visually, this lets the PS5 cut a striking figure that changes heavily depending on the angle of visual approach. The system is generally symmetrical, but not along the left-right axis that you'd expect when the system is sitting horizontally. It's the kind of norm-breaking that adds visual impact and interest to the system as a focal point of the room.

在视觉上，这让PS5切割了一个引人注目的数字，根据视觉接近的角度变化很大。系统通常是对称的，但不是沿着系统水平坐着时所期望的左右轴。这是一种打破常规的方式，作为房间的焦点，它为系统增加了视觉冲击力和趣味性。

For what it's worth, you can balance the PS5 vertically on its side just fine without installing the included plastic stand. With the disc drive near the floor, the system can even take a bit of jostling without tipping over in this configuration.

对于它的价值，你可以平衡PS5垂直的侧面刚刚好，而不是安装包括塑料支架。由于光盘驱动器靠近地面，在这种配置下，系统甚至可以进行一点推挤而不会倾倒。

Still, the stand adds a good deal of stability without adding too much to the system's physical footprint. The vertical stand is also relatively easy to install in a few seconds with a single screw—a fingernail or a coin will do to secure this in place even if you can't find a screwdriver.

尽管如此，展台在不增加太多系统物理足迹的情况下增加了很好的稳定性。垂直支架也相对容易，只需一颗螺丝就可以在几秒钟内安装好--即使你找不到螺丝刀，用指甲或硬币也能将其固定到位。

When sat horizontally, though, the PS5's wavy white shell means the system leans lazily to the side without a stabilizing stand. To fix this, you hook the same vertical stand into place onto a pre-marked location on the edge of the bottom outer panel, providing a nice, flat base for the system to balance on (while letting one side float teasingly above the ground, like the system is defying gravity).

然而，当水平放置时，PS5波浪形的白色外壳意味着系统懒洋洋地向一边倾斜，没有稳定的支架。要解决这个问题，您可以将相同的垂直支架挂钩到底部外面板边缘的预先标记位置上，为系统提供一个良好的平坦底座以供其保持平衡(同时让一侧戏谑地漂浮在地面上，就像系统正在对抗重力一样)。

This works well enough, but it feels a little flimsy compared to the vertical orientation. The stand hooks to the outer shell with something of a weak grip, lacking the secure "snap" you might expect for such an important attachment. Without a screw to hold it securely in place, the stand can come loose relatively easily while shifting or rotating the system.

这个效果很好，但与垂直方向相比感觉有点脆弱。支架与外壳挂钩，抓地力较弱，缺乏你可能会期待的如此重要的附件所需的牢靠的卡扣。如果没有螺钉将其牢牢固定到位，在移动或旋转系统时支架可能相对容易松动。

This probably won't be an issue if, like most people, you tend to put the PS5 down in your entertainment center and leave it in the same place for years at a time. But if you plan on moving the system around or changing its orientation a lot, the stand could quickly become an annoyance. (Ars Tech Culture Editor Sam Machkovech learned this the hard way, as he has already lost the screw that inadvertently popped out of its storage slot in the stand while he shuffled it around to take photos for this very article. Hopefully Sony has a replacement plan for poor Sam.)

如果你像大多数人一样，倾向于把PS5放在你的娱乐中心，然后把它放在同一个地方好几年，那么这可能不是问题。但是，如果你计划移动系统或大量改变它的方向，展台可能很快就会成为一个烦人的地方。(Ars Tech Culture编辑Sam Machkovech痛苦地认识到了这一点，因为他已经弄丢了螺丝钉，这颗螺丝钉在他拖着螺丝钉为这篇文章拍照时，不经意间从展台的储物槽里弹了出来。希望索尼能为可怜的山姆制定一个替代计划。)。

We talk about the feeling of using the PS5's DualSense controller in a separate piece. From a visual standpoint, though, the controller is a dramatic departure from the solid grey and black color schemes of PlayStation's past. The two-tone design matches well with the system itself, cutting a striking figure that seems slim, even though the controller is a tad bigger than previous PlayStation controllers.

我们将单独讨论使用PS5的DualSense控制器的感觉。然而，从视觉的角度来看，控制器与PlayStation过去纯灰和黑色的配色方案有戏剧性的不同。双色调设计与系统本身非常匹配，尽管控制器比以前的PlayStation控制器稍大，但它的外形看起来很苗条。

The visual effect is heightened by the face buttons, now labeled with a dull grey under transparent casing, lacking the four-color coding Sony has used for PlayStation buttons since 1994. It's a change that lets the controller's face form a smoother impression from the front, though it's also a change that may make it harder for newcomers to learn the positions of the buttons without the aid of color.

脸部按钮增强了视觉效果，现在透明外壳下贴上了暗灰色的标签，缺少索尼自1994年以来一直用于PlayStation按钮的四色编码。这一改变可以让控制器的脸从正面形成更平滑的印象，尽管这也可能会让新手在没有颜色帮助的情况下更难记住按钮的位置。(这是一种改变，可以让控制器的脸从正面形成更平滑的印象，尽管这也可能会让新手在没有颜色的帮助下更难记住按钮的位置)。

The PS5 HD camera, Pulse 3D Audio headset, and controller charging station all lean heavily into that same aesthetic of black and white plastic with undulating curves. Together, it forms a strong visual branding for the PS5 as a stark departure from what's come before. It's a futuristic look that could come to be associated with the 2020s the same way wood-paneled electronics have come to define the 1970s.

PS5高清摄像头、Pulse 3D音频耳机和控制器充电站都在很大程度上偏向于曲线起伏的黑白塑料的相同美学。总而言之，它形成了PS5强大的视觉品牌，与之前的截然不同。这是一种未来主义的外观，可能会与本世纪20年代联系在一起，就像木质镶板电子产品已经成为20世纪70年代的定义一样。

We'll have more to say about how the PlayStation 5 as a game-playing device in the days to come. For now, though, the system serves as a striking piece of visual design that stands out in any entertainment center that it can fit in.

在未来的日子里，我们将更多地谈论PlayStation5如何作为一种游戏设备。不过，就目前而言，该系统是一款引人注目的视觉设计，在任何可以容纳它的娱乐中心都能脱颖而出。
