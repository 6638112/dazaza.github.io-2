Netherlands offshore wind farm with ability to power a million homes operational
荷兰海上风电场有能力为100万户家庭供电

2020-12-01 05:53:15

A major offshore wind farm in the Netherlands is now fully operational, with its owners, Danish energy firm Orsted, claiming it provides enough green electricity to power one million households. Situated 23 kilometers (around 14.3 miles) off the coast of Zeeland, in the southwest of the Netherlands, the 752 megawatt (MW) Borssele 1 & 2 offshore wind farm spans an area of 112 square kilometers. It uses 94 wind turbines from Siemens Gamesa. In an announcement Friday, Orsted described the facility as the second-largest operating offshore wind farm in the world. The largest, Hornsea One, has a capacity of 1.2 gigawatts (GW) and was also developed by Orsted.

荷兰的一个大型海上风电场现已全面投入运营，其所有者丹麦能源公司Orsted声称其提供了足够的绿色电力来为100万个家庭供电。 752兆瓦（MW）的Borssele 1和2海上风电场位于荷兰西南部，距Zeeland海岸23公里（约14.3英里），面积112平方公里。它使用了来自Siemens Gamesa的94台风力发电机。在周五的公告中，Orsted将其描述为全球第二大海上风电场。最大的Hornsea One容量为1.2吉瓦（GW），也是由Orsted开发的。

News of Borssele 1 & 2's commissioning is the latest example of European countries embracing offshore wind and comes after the European Union said it wanted to increase its offshore wind capacity from 12 to 300 GW by 2050.

《 Borssele 1＆2》投产的消息是欧洲国家拥抱海上风电的最新例证，此前欧盟表示希望在2050年之前将海上风电容量从12 GW增加到300 GW。

The "Offshore Renewable Energy Strategy" from the European Commission, the EU's executive arm, also aims for 40 GW of ocean energy such as tidal and wave power within the same time frame. A number of major offshore wind projects located in European waters are now in the pipeline. These include the Dogger Bank Wind Farm in Britain, which left the EU in January 2020.

欧盟执行机构欧盟委员会的“离岸可再生能源战略”还旨在在同一时间内开发40吉瓦的海洋能，例如潮汐能和波浪能。现在，许多在欧洲水域的大型海上风电项目正在筹备中。其中包括英国的Dogger Bank风电场，该风电场于2020年1月离开欧盟。

A 50:50 joint venture between SSE Renewables and Equinor, the Dogger Bank facility will have a total capacity of 3.6 GW once completed, making it the largest in the world.

Dogger Bank设施是SSE Renewables和Equinor之间的50:50合资企业，建成后的总容量将为3.6 GW，使其成为世界上最大的设施。

At the end of last week, it was announced that a deal to fund the first two phases of the project had been completed. According to SSE, investment for Dogger Bank A and B will amount to approximately £6 billion (around $8 billion).

上周末，宣布完成了为该项目的前两个阶段提供资金的交易。根据SSE的数据，对Dogerg银行A和B的投资将约为60亿英镑（约合80亿美元）。

While Europe is now home to a mature offshore wind sector, the one in the U.S. is still relatively new.

尽管欧洲现在拥有成熟的海上风电行业，但美国的风电行业仍相对较新。

The country's first offshore wind farm – the 30 MW, five-turbine Block Island Wind Farm, which is also operated by Orsted – only started commercial operations at the end of 2016. The next few years could see the sector develop, however, with companies starting to invest large amounts of money in schemes located off the East Coast.

该国第一个海上风电场-30兆瓦，五台涡轮机的布洛克岛风电场（也由Orsted经营）-于2016年底才开始商业运营。接下来的几年中，该领域可能会随着公司的发展而发展开始在东海岸以外的计划中投入大量资金。

Back in September, for instance, oil and gas giant BP took 50% stakes in Equinor's Empire Wind and Beacon Wind projects, which are located off the coasts of New York State and Massachusetts respectively.

例如，早在9月份，石油和天然气巨头BP就收购了Equinor的Empire Wind和Beacon Wind项目50％的股份，这些项目分别位于纽约州和马萨诸塞州的沿海地区。
