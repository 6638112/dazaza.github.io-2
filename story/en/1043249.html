Haven, the Amazon-Berkshire-JPMorgan venture to disrupt healthcare, is disbanding after three years
亚马逊-伯克希尔-摩根大通破坏医疗保健业务的避风港，在三年后解散

2021-01-05 02:12:39

Haven began informing employees Monday that it will shut down by the end of next month, according to people with direct knowledge of the matter.

据知情人士透露，避风港周一开始通知员工，它将在下个月底关闭。

Many of the Boston-based firm's 57 workers are expected to be placed at Amazon, Berkshire Hathaway or JPMorgan Chase as the firms each individually push forward in their efforts, the people said.

知情人士说，随着这两家公司各自的努力，预计这家位于波士顿的公司的57名工人中有许多将被安置在亚马逊，伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司或摩根大通公司。

One key issue facing Haven was that each of the three founding companies executed their own projects separately with their own employees, obviating the need for the joint venture to begin with, according to the people, who declined to be identified speaking about the matter.

知情人士说，避风港面临的一个关键问题是，三家创始公司中的每一个都与自己的员工分别执行自己的项目，从而消除了合资企业成立的需要。

Haven, the joint venture formed by three of America's most powerful companies to lower costs and improve outcomes in health care, is disbanding after three years, CNBC has learned exclusively.

CNBC独家了解到，避风港是由美国三家最有实力的公司组成的合资公司，旨在降低成本并改善医疗保健的效果。三年后，该公司将解散。

The company began informing employees Monday that it will shut down by the end of next month, according to people with direct knowledge of the matter.

据知情人士透露，该公司周一开始通知员工，它将在下个月底关闭。

Many of the Boston-based firm's 57 workers are expected to be placed at Amazon, Berkshire Hathaway or JPMorgan Chase as the firms each individually push forward in their efforts, and the three companies are still expected to collaborate informally on healthcare projects, the people said.

预计这家位于波士顿的公司的57名工人中有许多人将被安置在亚马逊，伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司或摩根大通公司，因为两家公司各自都在各自努力，而这三家公司仍有望在医疗项目上进行非正式合作，人们说。

The announcement three years ago that the CEOs of Amazon, Berkshire Hathaway and JPMorgan Chase had teamed up to tackle one of the biggest problems facing corporate America – high and rising costs for employee health care  – sent shock waves throughout the world of medicine. Shares of healthcare companies tumbled on fears about how the combined might of leaders in technology and finance could wring costs out of the system.

三年前，亚马逊，伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司（Berkshire Hathaway）和摩根大通（JPMorgan Chase）的首席执行官联手解决了美国公司面临的最大问题之一-员工医疗保健的高昂和不断上涨的公告-在全世界医学界掀起了一股冲击波。由于担心技术和财务领域的领导者的综合实力如何使系统成本降低，医疗保健公司的股票大跌。

The move to shutter Haven may be a sign of how difficult it is to radically improve American health care, a complicated and entrenched system of doctors, insurers, drug makers and middle men that costs the country $3.5 trillion every year. Last year, Berkshire CEO Warren Buffett seemed to indicate as much, saying that were was no guarantee that Haven would succeed in improving health care.

关闭黑文的举动可能表明，从根本上改善美国的医疗保健有多么困难。美国是一个复杂而根深蒂固的医生，保险公司，制药商和中间人体系，每年给国家造成3.5万亿美元的损失。去年，伯克希尔（Berkshire）首席执行官沃伦·巴菲特（Warren Buffett）似乎表示了同样的看法，并表示这并不能保证Haven能够成功改善医疗保健。

One key issue facing Haven was that while the firm came up with ideas, each of the three founding companies executed their own projects separately with their own employees, obviating the need for the joint venture to begin with, according to the people, who declined to be identified speaking about the matter.

知情人士说，避风港面临的一个关键问题是，尽管公司想出了主意，但三家创始公司中的每家公司都与自己的员工分别执行了自己的项目，从而消除了合资企业成立的需要。被发现谈论此事。

Coming just three years after the initial rush of fanfare about the possibilities for what Haven could accomplish, its closure is a disappointment to some. But insiders claim that it will allow the founding companies to implement ideas from the project on their own, tailoring it the specific needs of their employees, who are mostly concentrated in different cities.

在人们开始大张旗鼓地宣传避风港可能实现的成就仅三年之后，其关闭令某些人感到失望。但是内部人士声称，这将允许创始公司自行实施该项目的构想，并根据其员工的具体需求量身定制，这些员工大多集中在不同的城市。

The move comes after Haven's CEO Dr. Atul Gawande stepped down from day-to-day management of the non-profit in May, a change that sparked a search for his successor.

在此之前，Haven的首席执行官Atul Gawande博士于5月退出了非营利组织的日常管理工作，这一变化引发了对他的继任者的搜寻。

Haven的发言人布鲁克·瑟斯顿（Brooke Thurston）确认了该公司的关闭计划，并发表了以下声明：

"The Haven team made good progress exploring a wide range of healthcare solutions, as well as piloting new ways to make primary care easier to access, insurance benefits simpler to understand and easier to use, and prescription drugs more affordable," Thurston said in an email.

"Moving forward, Amazon, Berkshire Hathaway, and JPMorgan Chase & Co. will leverage these insights and continue to collaborate informally to design programs tailored to address the specific needs of our individual employee populations and locations," she said.
