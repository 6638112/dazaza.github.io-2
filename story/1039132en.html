There is a lot of data to look at in many different ways. For now, I performed a simple question-by-question analysis using a Jupyter Notebook.

有许多数据可以通过许多不同的方式查看。现在，我使用Jupyter Notebook进行了简单的逐个问题分析。

Also, since free text was available for most questions, it can be hard to categorize some of the results. For multiple choice questions, I did a best effort attempt to bundle responses with low cardinality into an "other" section, which can get quite big in some cases! I also did not attempt to graph anything for pure free text questions. I encourage anyone who is curious to inspect the full responses, either in the notebook or looking at the data directly. The omitted free text questions are: If there is another survey in 2021, would you be opposed to it containing optional & general demographics questions?

另外，由于大多数问题都可以使用自由文本，因此很难对某些结果进行分类。对于多项选择题，我尽了最大的努力将低基数的响应捆绑到“其他”中。部分，在某些情况下可能会变得很大！对于纯自由文本问题，我也没有尝试绘制任何图形。我鼓励任何有兴趣检查完整响应的人，无论是在笔记本中还是直接查看数据。省略的自由文本问题是：如果在2021年进行另一项调查，您是否会反对包含可选的＆amp;一般人口统计问题？
