Dell Announces Long-Awaited Spinoff of VMware
戴尔宣布期待已久的VMware旋转

2021-04-16 00:54:31

Dell Technologies Inc. today announced the long-expected spinoff of VMware Inc., the computing virtualization company it has majority-owned since it bought then-owner EMC Corp. in 2016.

Dell Technologies Inc.今天宣布了VMware Inc.的长期预期的旋转，计算虚拟化公司拥有多数资产，因为它于2016年购买了当时所有人。

The computing giant said it will spin off its 81% equity ownership in VMware, creating two standalone companies when the move is completed in the fourth quarter of this year. That timing depends on conditions such as a favorable Internal Revenue Service opinion that the transaction qualifies for tax-free status for Dell shareholders.

计算巨头表示，它将在VMware中旋转其81％的股权所有权，在今年第四季度完成时创建两个独立公司。该时间取决于诸如有利的内部收入服务意见的条件，即交易符合戴尔股东的免税地位。

The idea is to simplify the companies’ capital structures, since arguably investors have valued both companies’ stocks lower than they might have because of the uncertainties related to the complex capital structures. Dell’s shares rose about 9% in after-hours trading, while VMware’s shares rose about 1.6% in late trading.

这一想法是简化公司的资本结构，因为可以使用的投资者的股票估值低于它们的股票，因为与复杂的资本结构相关的不确定性。戴尔的股票在余下的交易中上升了约9％，而VMware的股票在延迟交易中上涨约1.6％。

Under the spinoff, which Dell had signaled last year, VMware will distribute a cash dividend of about $11.5 billion to $12 billion to shareholders, which of course include publicly held Dell itself. Chairman and Chief Executive Michael Dell, along with financial partner Silver Lake Partners, own 60% of Dell shares.

在戴尔去年发出信号的疏浚下，VMware将向股东分配约115亿美元至120亿美元的现金股息，当然是公开持有戴尔本身。主席兼首席执行官Michael Dell以及金融合作伙伴银湖合作伙伴，拥有60％的戴尔股份。

Dell will get $9.3 billion to $9.7 billion of that dividend, which the company said will help it get more investment-grade ratings and enable it to pay down debt it has gradually been reducing since buying EMC. Dell shareholders will get about 0.44 shares of VMware for each of their Dell shares.

戴尔将获得93亿美元的股息，这家公司所说的股息将有助于它获得更多的投资级别评级，并使它能够偿还债务，因为自购买EMC以来逐渐减少。戴尔股东将为每个戴尔股份获得大约0.44份VMware。

“This is about paying off debt, restructuring Dell’s balance sheet and unlocking value for core Dell, without messing up the ‘special relationship’ they have with VMware,” said Dave Vellante, chief analyst at SiliconANGLE sister market research firm Wikibon. “It’s a classic cake-and-eat-it-too strategy — quite genius, actually.”

“这是关于偿还债务，重组戴尔的资产负债表和核心戴尔的解锁价值，而不会弄乱他们拥有VMware的”特殊关系“，”Siliconangle姐妹市场研究公司Wikibon首席分析师Dave Vellante说。 “这是一个经典的蛋糕和吃它太策略 - 实际上是天才相当的。”

Dell is also converting all its Class B VMware shares to Class A shares, so there will be only a single class of shares, a simpler and more understandable proposition for investors. On a conference call, Dell Chief Financial Officer Tom Sweet said the move will make the companies e ligible for key indices such as the S&P 500 and provide a better float for the shares.

戴尔还在将其所有类B VMware股票转换为课程，因此只有单一类别的股票，投资者的一个更简单和更可理解的主张。在电话会议上，戴尔首席财务官汤姆甜蜜表示，该举动将使公司能够呼夺诸如S＆amp; P 500等的关键指数，并为股票提供更好的浮动。

Sweet also said Dell w ill repay a $4 billion loan before the transaction closes, and VMware’s $8 billion in debt will no longer be part of Dell’s debt.

甜蜜还说戴尔在交易结束前偿还了40亿美元的贷款，而VMware的80亿美元的债务将不再是戴尔债务的一部分。

“In the end, this move is more about maximizing the Dell Technologies and VMware stock price and doesn’t impact customers, ISVs or the channel,” Moor Insights & Strategy President Patrick Moorhead told SiliconANGLE. “Wall Street wasn’t valuing the two companies together nearly as much as I believe it will as separate entities.”

“最终，这一举措更加强大地提高了戴尔技术和VMware股票价格，不会影响客户，ISV或频道，”Moor Insights＆amp;战略总统帕特里克·莫霍夫告诉硅瓜。 “华尔街几乎没有像我认为它一样单独的实体的公司将两家公司重视。”

Moorhead noted that Dell brought in a lot of VMware’s recent growth –some 35% of VMware’s revenue came through Dell in fiscal 2021 ended Jan. 29, according to Sweet — and Dell benefited as VMWare’s “first” partner on new initiatives. “Given the five-year strategic agreement, it doesn’t look like much will change operationally,” he said. “The optics might allow Dell Tech to partner more closely with VMware competitors and vice-versa, but the two companies were already doing this.”

Moorhead指出，戴尔提出了大量的VMware最近的增长 - 35％的VMware收入通过戴尔在2012年1月29日止了29号，根据甜蜜，戴尔作为VMware的“第一”合作伙伴在新举措中受益。 “鉴于五年战略协议，它看起来不太可能会在运作上，”他说。 “光学器件可能允许戴尔技术与VMware竞争对手更密切地合作，反之亦然，但这两个公司已经在这样做。”

VMware has thrived in recent years as it reoriented its virtualization business to the cloud, in particular with successful integrations with cloud computing providers such as Amazon Web Services Inc. Its chief executive, Pat Gelsinger, however, recently left to become CEO of Intel Corp.

近年来，VMware繁殖了它将其虚拟化业务重新定向到云端，特别是与云计算提供商的成功集成，如亚马逊Web服务公司的首席执行官，帕特盖林格最近留下了英特尔公司首席执行官

“By spinning off VMware, we expect to drive additional growth opportunities for Dell Technologies as well as VMware, and unlock significant value for stakeholders,” Dell Chairman and CEO Michael Dell (pictured) said on a conference call. He added on the call that “we’re going to retain the benefits of the VMware relationship” while eliminating the stock discount from the existing capital structures.

“通过旋转VMware，我们希望为Dell Technologies以及VMware推动额外的增长机会，并为利益相关者解锁重大价值，”戴尔董事长兼首席执行官Michael Dell（图）在电话会议上说道。他在呼叫中添加了“我们将保留VMware关系的好处”，同时消除现有资本结构的股票折扣。

At the same time, Dell Technologies will continue to modernize its core infrastructure and PC businesses and embrace new opportunities through an open ecosystem to grow in hybrid and private cloud, edge and telecom.”

与此同时，戴尔技术将继续使其核心基础设施和PC业务现代化，并通过开放的生态系统来拥抱新的机会，以在混合动力和私有云，边缘和电信中增长。“

Dell said the two companies will enter into a commercial agreement that will “preserve the companies’ unique and differentiated approaches to the co-development of critical solutions and alignment on sales and marketing activities.” In particular, VMware will continue to use Dell Financial Services for customers to finance their “digital transformations.”

戴尔表示，这两家公司将进入商业协议，将“保护公司独特和差异化的方法”，以协调关键解决方案和对销售和营销活动的一致性的共同发展。“特别是，VMware将继续使用Dell Financial Services来为客户提供资金“数字转换”。

Once the deal is completed, Dell will remain chairman of VMware and current interim VMware CEO Zane Rowe will remain in that job. VMware’s board also will remain unchanged.

一旦交易完成，戴尔将仍将留在VMware和当前的临时VMware CEO Zane Rowe董事会留在那份工作中。 VMware的电路板也将保持不变。

One analyst questioned Dell’s selling off the stake in the faster-growing software company. Michael Dell responded that “t he market does not appear to appreciate a hardware-software combination. Shareholders’ feedback is overwhelmingly positive on the spin.”

一位分析师质疑戴尔在增长速度的软件公司销售股份。 Michael Dell回复了“T HE Market似乎没有欣赏硬件软件组合。股东的反馈在旋转时压倒性地是积极的。“

Vellante also said some unanswered questions remain, such as how much new value this will actually create for Dell, how taking another big chunk of cash out of VMware will affect that company and how the continuing commercial agreement between the two companies will change.

Vellante还表示一些未答复的问题仍然存在，例如这将为戴尔创造多少新价值，如何从VMware拍摄另一大块的现金会影响该公司以及两家公司之间的持续商业协议如何改变。
