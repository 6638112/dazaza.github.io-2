New Videogame Bug Turns Spider-Man Into a Trash Can
新的电子游戏错误将蜘蛛侠变成垃圾桶

2020-11-29 08:38:04

The PS5 and Xbox Series X / Series S might be a generational leap ahead in terms of tech and performance, but these beautiful new games have bugs, too. Spider-Man: Miles Morales has offered up a particularly amusing example that turns Miles into various inanimate objects, including bricks, cardboard boxes, and even a trash can.

PS5和Xbox Series X / Series S在技术和性能方面可能是一代人的飞跃，但是这些漂亮的新游戏也有bug。蜘蛛侠：迈尔斯·莫拉莱斯提供了一个特别有趣的例子，将迈尔斯变成了各种无生命的物体，包括砖块，纸板箱，甚至是垃圾桶。

It's not immediately clear what causes the bug, but several of the posts seem to reference falling through walls or experiencing general collision issues just prior to the transformation. Despite Miles' changed appearance, he can still perform many of his heroic antics, including web-swinging and beating up bad guys. It's an important lesson to all of us in these trying times: You might look like trash, but you can still do your job.

目前尚不清楚是什么原因导致了该错误，但是在转换之前，有几篇文章似乎提到从墙上掉下来或遇到一般的碰撞问题。尽管Miles的外表发生了变化，他仍然可以进行许多英勇的滑稽动作，包括网络摇摆和殴打坏人。在这些艰难时期，这对我们所有人都是重要的一课：您可能看起来像垃圾，但仍然可以完成工作。

This strange bug isn't the only technical issue that next-gen console owners are experiencing. Over the past few days, PS5 owners--including several GameSpot staffers-- have reported serious issues with the system's Rest mode, from unexpected restarts to outright wiping saves. In terms of small details that the game's creators intended for you to find, Miles Morales also features a touching tribute to the late Chadwick Boseman in the game's credits, as well as a memorial dedicated to the Black Lives Matter movement in Harlem.

这个奇怪的错误并不是下一代控制台所有者遇到的唯一技术问题。在过去的几天里，PS5所有者（包括数名GameSpot员工）报告了系统的“休息”模式出现了严重问题，从意外重启到彻底擦除保存。在游戏创作者希望您找到的小细节方面，迈尔斯·莫拉莱斯（Miles Morales）还以已故的游戏致谢致敬已故的查德威克·玻色曼（Chadwick Boseman），以及为哈林的“黑住事”运动致敬的纪念馆。
