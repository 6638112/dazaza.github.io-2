iOS, web versions of Dark Sky weather app will shut down in 2022
iOS，Web版本的Dark Sky天气应用程序将在2022年关闭

2021-06-11 03:35:11

A new blog post from the developers of Apple-owned, hyperlocal weather app Dark Sky has announced that the iOS and web versions of the app, as well as the Dark Sky API, will sunset at the end of 2022.

来自Apple所有的开发人员的新博客文章，高级天气应用程序黑暗的天空宣布了应用程序的IOS和Web版本以及黑暗的天空API将在2022年底日落。

Support for the Dark Sky API service for existing customers will continue until the end of 2022. The iOS app and Dark Sky website will also be available until the end of 2022.

支持对现有客户的Dark Sky API服务将持续到2022年底。IOS应用程序和Dark Sky网站也将可提供，直到2022年底。

Dark Sky's developers initially said the API would shut down at the end of 2021, but this new end-of-2022 target obviously moves things back a bit. This is the first time we've heard about an end date for the iOS app, though.

When news first broke that Apple acquired Dark Sky early last year, a blog post from the Dark Sky team stated that the iOS app would not see any changes "at this time," while also warning that the Android version would be shuttered.

当新闻首先打破了苹果在去年年初获得了黑暗的天空时，黑暗天空团队的博客文章表示iOS应用程序不会看到任何变化＆＃34;此时，＆＃34;虽然还会警告，Android版本将被快门。

Apple went on shut down the Android version of the app last August, but the iOS and web experiences (as well as the API) continued apace.

Apple去年8月继续关闭Android版本，但IOS和Web体验（以及API）继续Apace。

Apple's iOS 14 added some Dark Sky-like features last year, and even more are coming in iOS 15 later this year. New Weather features planned for iOS 15 include hour-by-hour precipitation notifications, an overhaul of the animated backgrounds, and various other design changes.

Apple＆＃39; iOS 14去年增加了一些黑暗的天空功能，甚至更多地在今年晚些时候推出。为iOS 15计划的新天气特征包括单小时降水通知，动画背景的大修，以及各种其他设计变化。

Before its acquisition by Apple for an undisclosed sum, Dark Sky was a widely beloved third-party weather app for both iOS and Android. It offered more granular and local data than the baked-in solutions on those two platforms.

在Apple获取未公开的金额之前，Dark Sky是IOS和Android的广泛心爱的第三方天气应用程序。它提供比这两个平台上的烘焙解决方案更多的粒度和本地数据。
