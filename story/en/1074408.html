Resuming maintenance of libxml2 and libxslt (2022-01)
恢复对libxml2和libxslt的维护（2022-01）

2022-02-21 09:05:22

Thanks to a donation from Google, I'm able to resume maintenance of libxml2 (and libxslt) for the remainder of 2022. My immediate plans are:- Make a bug fix release fixing many regressions.- Establish a new release schedule, possibly with multiple branches being maintained.- Move releases from the old FTP server to GNOME's Gitlab infrastructure.- Move documentation to GNOME infrastructure.- Set up an official way to sponsor libxml2 maintainers.
