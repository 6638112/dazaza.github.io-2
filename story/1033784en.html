前宇航员赢得共和党人约翰·麦凯恩曾经占据的美国参议院席位

2020-11-08 09:57:40

Democrat Mark Kelly will soon be the fourth NASA astronaut to serve in Congress, after flipping an Arizona Senate seat held by Republican Martha McSally.

民主党人马克·凯利(Mark Kelly)很快将成为美国国家航空航天局(NASA)第四位在国会任职的宇航员，此前他推翻了共和党人玛莎·麦克萨利(Martha McSally)在亚利桑那州的参议院席位。

According to the Arizona Secretary of State's website, Kelly, 54, won with more than 52 percent of the vote. In a tweet posted Wednesday, he said he was "deeply honored" to have been elected and to serve in the seat once held by the late Sen. John McCain.

根据亚利桑那州国务卿的网站，54岁的凯利赢得了超过52%的选票。在周三发布的一条推文中，他说，他对当选并担任已故参议员约翰·麦凯恩(John McCain)曾经担任的职位深感荣幸。

I am deeply honored that Arizonans have trusted me to be their next United States Senator and to serve in this seat once held by Senator John McCain. pic.twitter.com/scNwzYpmoJ

我深感荣幸的是，亚利桑那州人相信我会成为他们的下一任美国参议员，并担任参议员约翰·麦凯恩(John McCain)曾经担任过的这个席位。Pic.twitter.com/scNwzYpmoJ。

A retired U.S. Navy captain and astronaut, Kelly has flown in four space missions, including the final mission of  Space Shuttle  Endeavour in 2011. He is married to former Arizona Congresswoman Gabby Giffords, was shot and nearly killed in 2011.

凯利是一名退役的美国海军上尉和宇航员，他已经执行了四次太空任务，其中包括2011年奋进号航天飞机的最后一次任务。他与前亚利桑那州国会议员加比·吉福兹(Gabby Giffords)结婚，2011年被枪杀，差点被杀。

His identical twin Scott Kelly is also a retired astronaut. The two participated in NASA's landmark " twins study," in which Scott spent a year aboard the space station while scientists collected Mark's physiological data back home for comparison.

他的同卵双胞胎斯科特·凯利也是一名退休宇航员。两人参与了美国国家航空航天局具有里程碑意义的双胞胎研究，斯科特在空间站呆了一年，科学家们将马克的生理数据收集回国进行比较。

In an appearance Tuesday evening in Tucson, Kelly made allusions to his time in space, referring to his campaign as a "mission" as votes continued to be tallied.

周二晚上在图森露面时，凯利提到了他在太空的时光，称他的竞选活动是一项使命，因为计票工作仍在继续。

"When we launched this campaign from this very spot, right here, 619 days ago, I could have never anticipated just how many Arizonians would be part of this mission," said Kelly. "This mission does not end when the last vote is counted. It is only the beginning."

凯利说：619天前，当我们在此地发起这场运动时，我从未预料到会有多少亚利桑那州人参与到这项使命中来。这项任务并没有在最后一次投票计票时结束。这只是个开始。

The Associated Press officially called the race for Kelly just before 1 a.m. on Wednesday morning.

就在凌晨1点之前，美联社(Associated Press)正式宣布了凯利的竞选。星期三早上。

Kelly launched his campaign in Feb. 2019, running on a platform that he said would emphasize affordable healthcare, improving wages, increasing employment, bolstering the economy and addressing climate change. He is also a gun safety advocate, and has been particularly vocal about that stance since his wife was shot.

凯利于2019年2月发起了他的竞选活动，他表示，他的竞选平台将强调负担得起的医疗保健、提高工资、增加就业、提振经济和应对气候变化。他也是一名枪支安全倡导者，自从他的妻子被枪击后，他一直特别直言不讳地表达了这一立场。

His opponent, McSally, lost a race for the Arizona Senate in 2018, but found herself with a seat anyway after the state's governor appointed her to the seat vacated by Jon Kyl, who temporarily filled the seat after the Aug. 2018 death of McCain.

他的对手麦克萨利在2018年亚利桑那州参议员竞选中落败，但在该州州长任命她填补乔恩·凯尔(Jon Kyl)空出的席位后，她还是找到了一个席位。乔恩·凯尔在2018年8月麦凯恩去世后暂时填补了这个空缺。

Kelly's win came during an election in which Democrat Joe Biden is projected by the Associated Press to win Arizona, flipping a state that voted for Donald Trump in the 2016 election. Bill Clinton, who won Arizona in 1996, had been the last Democrat to win the state.

凯利的胜利发生在民主党人乔·拜登(Joe Biden)被美联社(Associated Press)预测将赢得亚利桑那州的选举期间，这个在2016年大选中投票给唐纳德·特朗普(Donald Trump)的州发生了翻天覆地的变化。1996年赢得亚利桑那州的比尔·克林顿(Bill Clinton)是最后一位赢得该州的民主党人。

Analysts speculated that the flip had at least something to do with Trump's mocking of former Sen. McCain.

分析人士猜测，这一转变至少与特朗普嘲弄前参议员麦凯恩有关。

When he is sworn in, Kelly will be the only active member of Congress to have flown in space. He is preceded by three former NASA astronauts: former Sens. John Glenn and Jack Schmitt and Rep. Jack Swigert. Two other former members of Congress — Sen. Jake Garn and Rep. Bill Nelson — have flown in space as payload specialists.

当凯利宣誓就职时，他将是唯一一位飞上太空的现役国会议员。在他之前，有三名前NASA宇航员：前参议员约翰·格伦(John Glenn)、杰克·施密特(Jack Schmitt)和众议员杰克·斯威格特(Jack Swigert)。另外两名前国会议员-参议员杰克·加恩(Jack Garn)和众议员比尔·纳尔逊(Bill Nelson)-已经作为有效载荷专家飞入太空。

Apollo 13 astronaut John "Jack" Swigert was elected to the House of Representatives in 1982, but died of cancer before he could take office.

阿波罗13号宇航员约翰·斯威格特于1982年再次当选为众议院议员，但在上任前死于癌症。
