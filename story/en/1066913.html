Steam on ChromeOS: Not a Rumor Anymore
Chromeos上的蒸汽：不再是谣言了

2021-06-20 00:53:15

If you follow us or other sources like Chrome Unboxed you are by now aware that there’s ample rumors about Google/Valve working on bringing Steam on ChromeOS. We know the technology pieces are there, as recently discussed with Luke Short in our recent podcast. However, we are still waiting for an official announcement that would turn the expected rumors into reality.

如果您遵循美国或其他码头，如Chrome Unboxed，您现在就意识到谷歌/阀门在Chromeos上播放蒸汽有充足的谣言。我们知道技术碎片在那里，最近在我们最近的播客中与Luke Short讨论过。但是，我们仍在等待官方宣布，将预期的谣言变为现实。

While not an official confirmation yet, I have received the following information through a source that will remain anonymous:

虽然不是官方确认，但我通过源将保持下列信息，这将保持下列信息：

There are now some QA testers being hired to work on the “ ChromeOS Steam Launch Team” to triage games, find defects and test performance in specific configurations.

现在有一些QA测试人员正在聘请用于分类游戏的“Chromeos Steam Mail发布团队”，在特定配置中找到缺陷和测试性能。

As I don’t have first hand confirmation, take this with a grain of salt, but I feel confident enough that this is very likely true to publish this.

正如我没有第一手确认，那就用一粒盐来，但我觉得足够有信心，这很可能是为了发布这一点。

That’s probably as close as a confirmation as we are going to get before Google/ChromeOS or Valve actually announces anything on this matter.

这可能与确认一样紧密，因为我们将在Google / Chromeos或Valve实际上在此问题上宣布任何内容之前。

This is absolutely excellent news, as this would now bring Google as an additional company supporting Linux Gaming front and center – and not just Valve. We all know that Google is a Linux shop at heart, but we have yet to see how this all fits with their Stadia platform.

这是绝对的好消息，因为这现在会把谷歌作为一个支持Linux游戏前沿和中心的额外公司 - 而不仅仅是阀门。我们都知道谷歌是一个在心脏的Linux商店，但我们还没有看到这一切都与他们的体育平台合作。

Who knows, maybe in return Valve will help bring titles to Stadia via the Steam catalogue? That would not be a completely crazy idea. What do you think?

谁知道，也许在回流阀将有助于通过Steam目录带来Stadia？这不是一个完全疯狂的想法。你怎么看？
