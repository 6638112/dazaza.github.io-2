Xbox Series X Controller Support Coming To Apple Devices
Xbox系列X控制器支持即将出现在Apple设备上

2020-11-22 07:58:27

Apple and Microsoft are working on adding support for the Xbox Series X controller to Apple devices, according to an Apple Support page spotted by a Reddit user.

根据Reddit用户发现的Apple支持页面，Apple和Microsoft正在努力为Xbox设备增加对Xbox Series X控制器的支持。

The support page states that Apple devices only support the Xbox Wireless Controller with Bluetooth, Xbox Elite Wireless Controller Series 2, Xbox Adaptive Controller, PlayStation DualShock 4 Wireless Controller, and various other MFi Bluetooth controllers. However, small print on the page states:

支持页面指出，Apple设备仅支持带蓝牙的Xbox无线控制器，Xbox Elite无线控制器系列2，Xbox自适应控制器，PlayStation DualShock 4无线控制器以及其他各种MFi蓝牙控制器。但是，页面上的小字指出：

Microsoft and Apple are working together to bring compatibility for the Xbox Series X controller to customers in a future update.

Microsoft和Apple正在共同努力，以在将来的更新中为客户带来Xbox Series X控制器的兼容性。

Support for gaming controllers on Apple devices allows for better control in supported games from Apple Arcade or the App Store, navigation on the Apple TV, and more.

通过支持Apple设备上的游戏控制器，可以更好地控制Apple Arcade或App Store中支持的游戏，Apple TV上的导航等。

There is no mention of the Sony PlayStation 5 DualSense Controller or the Amazon Luna Controller on the Apple Support page, but MacRumors has spotted code mentioning the controllers in the iOS and iPadOS 14.3 betas.

Apple支持页面上没有提及Sony PlayStation 5 DualSense控制器或Amazon Luna控制器，但是MacRumors在iOS和iPadOS 14.3 beta中发现了提及该控制器的代码。

I don't understand why a bluetooth adapter is needed to get the controller to work with a Windows or Mac PC. What's special about that adapter that makes it required beyond any regular bluetooth device?

我不明白为什么需要蓝牙适配器才能使控制器与Windows或Mac PC一起使用。该适配器有什么特别之处，它使它不需要任何常规的蓝牙设备？

At least for the Xbox one controllers under Windows you can use them in 3 ways: plugged in with USB, using the wireless USB adapter or over regular Bluetooth (some of the early Xbox one controllers don’t support this method). According to Microsoft the difference between using the wireless adapter and Bluetooth is latency (the wireless adapter should provide lower latency). In reality, I’ve tried both ways and honestly can’t find any discernible difference. Would only make sense that the new Xbox controllers and the Dual Sense controllers would support similar methods. On the iPad I’ve tried it over Bluetooth and using Steam Link to play Persona 4. Worked flawlessly and no lag whatsoever!

至少对于Windows下的Xbox One控制器，您可以通过3种方式使用它们：通过USB插入，使用无线USB适配器或通过常规蓝牙插入（某些早期的Xbox One控制器不支持此方法）。根据Microsoft的说法，使用无线适配器和蓝牙之间的区别在于延迟（无线适配器应提供较低的延迟）。实际上，我尝试了两种方式，老实说找不到任何明显的区别。新的Xbox控制器和Dual Sense控制器将支持类似方法，这才有意义。在iPad上，我已经通过蓝牙进行了尝试，并使用Steam Link播放了Persona4。它工作正常，没有任何滞后！
