Apple Silicon M1 MacBook Pro Review: This Changes Everything
Apple Silicon M1 MacBook Pro评论：这改变了一切

2020-12-22 21:42:09

On January 15th, 2008, Steve Jobs pulled a MacBook Air out of a manila envelope and changed laptop computers forever. Blink and you might have missed it: the instant when an entire industry shifted to accommodate a new breed of device that made more sense to more people. And last month, Apple had the audacity to do it again. It took 12 years and 10 months, but Apple finally has a new “game-changer.” Not an incremental update. Not a computer for fanboys. But a true game-changer that will force the industry to re-configure yet again.

2008年1月15日，史蒂夫·乔布斯（Steve Jobs）从一个马尼拉信封中取出MacBook Air，并永远更换了笔记本电脑。眨眼，您可能会错过它：整个行业转移以容纳对更多人更有意义的新型设备的那一刻。上个月，苹果敢于再做一次。花了12年零10个月，但苹果终于有了一个新的“游戏规则改变者”。不是增量更新。不是供粉丝使用的计算机。但是，真正的游戏规则改变者将迫使业界再次进行重新配置。

The Apple Silicon Macs are a new breed of device. Because while ARM-based computers are not new; highly functional, blazing-fast, ultra-efficient ARM-based computers were a pipe dream until about a month ago. If ARM-based Windows machines represent the first slow, plodding steps of a mutant fish on land, Apple has created a full-blown land mammal.

Apple Silicon Macs是一种新型设备。因为基于ARM的计算机不是新事物；直到一个月前，高性能，超快速，超高效的基于ARM的计算机还是一个空想。如果基于ARM的Windows机器代表陆地上一条变异鱼类的缓慢而缓慢的第一步，那么苹果公司已经创造出了成熟的陆地哺乳动物。

Apple released three Apple Silicon Macs last month, all of them featuring the Apple M1 SOC at their core. There’s the M1 MacBook Air that is now completely fanless, the M1 MacBook Pro which boasts a reported 20 hours of battery life in Apple’s metrics, and the M1 Mac Mini, the one computer out of this trio that doesn’t have to concern itself with battery life.

苹果上个月发布了三台Apple Silicon Macs，它们均以Apple M1 SOC为核心。现在有M1 MacBook Air完全无风扇，M1 MacBook Pro拥有据报道的20个小时的苹果电池续航时间，还有M1 Mac Mini，这三人中的一台不必担心自己的计算机电池寿命。

That extra 1TB of hard drive space adds a lot to the price. If I were recommending a configuration for photographers, I’d tell you to get this same computer with only 1TB of built-in storage instead of 2, which brings the total price down to $1,900.

额外的1TB硬盘空间增加了价格。如果我为摄影师推荐一种配置，我会告诉您让这台计算机只有1TB的内置存储空间，而不是2台，这样总价格会降到1,900美元。

On the outside, there is almost nothing to distinguish an M1 MacBook Pro from the 13-inch Intel-based machine we reviewed earlier this year. Same chassis, same keyboard, same trackpad, same fingerprint sensor, same webcam (sigh), same Touch Bar (meh), no SD card slot (ugh).

在外观上，M1 MacBook Pro与我们今年早些时候评测的13英寸基于Intel的机器几乎没有区别。相同的机箱，相同的键盘，相同的触控板，相同的指纹传感器，相同的网络摄像头（叹气），相同的触控栏（meh），没有SD卡插槽（ugh）。

The only thing setting these two computers apart on the outside is the Thunderbolt 3 ports: the top-end Intel Macs still come with 4 ports, while the new Apple Silicon Macs feature only two 1. This might be a deal-breaker for some, but if it is, all you have to do is wait. More ports are almost certainly coming to future iterations of the M(x) processor as Apple Silicon is developed further.

这两款计算机在外面唯一的区别是Thunderbolt 3端口：高端Intel Macs仍带有4个端口，而新的Apple Silicon Macs仅具有2个端口。对于某些人来说，这可能是一个大问题，但是，如果是这样，您只需等待。随着Apple Silicon的进一步发展，M（x）处理器的未来迭代中肯定会有更多的端口。

Beyond the lack of ports, the computer is identical to its predecessor. The new “Magic” keyboard is among the best on the market, the touchpad is still the most responsive you’ll find on a laptop, and the display is bright and color accurate. Based on our measurements using a DataColor Spyder X Elite, you can expect 100% coverage of the Display P3 gamut and 89% coverage of AdobeRGB.

除了缺少端口外，该计算机与其前身相同。新的“魔术”键盘是市场上最好的键盘之一，触摸板仍然是笔记本电脑上响应速度最快的键盘，并且显示屏明亮，色彩准确。根据我们使用DataColor Spyder X Elite进行的测量，您可以预期Display P3色域的100％覆盖率和AdobeRGB的89％覆盖率。

In summary: on the outside, it’s business as usual. Nothing much has changed, and while I would have loved to see Apple use this opportunity to reveal the mythical 14-inch MacBook Pro with smaller screen bezels and an even bigger battery, the current design and build quality is still “premium” by any definition, even if it’s starting to look a bit dated.

总结：在外面，一切照旧。没什么大变化，虽然我很希望看到苹果利用这次机会来展示具有较小屏幕边框和更大电池的神话般的14英寸MacBook Pro，但无论从什么意义上讲，当前的设计和制造质量仍然是“溢价” ，即使它看起来有点过时了。

Everything that makes this computer so special is tucked away inside. The Apple Silicon M1 System on a Chip (SOC), combined with some brilliant software from Apple, allows the new MacBook Pro to do things that no Intel-based laptop I’ve ever owned could do.

使这台计算机如此特别的所有东西都藏在里面。 Apple Silicon M1片上系统（SOC）与Apple的一些出色软件相结合，使新款MacBook Pro能够完成我所拥有的基于Intel的笔记本电脑无法做到的事情。

The fans almost never turn on, the chassis stays cool to the touch during the most extreme benchmarks, and every app we used—weather it was optimized for Apple Silicon or being emulated in real-time by Apple’s Rosetta 2 software—performed better than we expected. This was particularly true of Lightroom Classic, which didn’t seem to struggle at all.

风扇几乎永远不会打开，机箱在最极端的基准测试中保持凉爽的触感，而且我们使用的每个应用程序（针对Apple Silicon进行了优化或由Apple的Rosetta 2软件进行了实时仿真）的性能均优于我们预期。 Lightroom Classic尤其如此，它似乎一点也不挣扎。

To quote a fellow tech editor who purchased the new M1 Mac Mini for himself:

引用一位为自己购买了新M1 Mac Mini的技术编辑的话：

I’m honestly running out of ways to test its limits. I’ve transcoded 10-bit ProRes 4K video from my EOS R (recorded over HDMI to Ninja V), imported thousands of images into Lightroom Classic (which is obviously still run through Rosetta 2), and run every benchmarking tool I can think of. This thing doesn’t bat an eye. Unless I can find a way to get some 8K Raw video from Red, I’m not sure I’ll be able to see the limitations.

老实说，我已经没有办法测试其极限。我已经将EOS R中的10位ProRes 4K视频（从HDMI录制到Ninja V）进行了转码，将成千上万的图像导入Lightroom Classic（显然仍然通过Rosetta 2运行），并运行我能想到的所有基准测试工具。这东西不引人注目。除非我找到从Red获取一些8K Raw视频的方法，否则我不确定能否看到这些限制。

Our own set of Lightroom Classic and Photoshop benchmarks 2 help quantify this performance. While Adobe’s apps are more than capable of walloping any computer within walloping distance, the Apple Silicon Mac handled our tests like a champ. The charts below show the results of our standard Lightroom Import, Lightroom Export, and PugetBench benchmarks for both the Intel-based 2020 13-inch MacBook Pro (2.3GhZ Quad-Core i7, 32GB of RAM) and the M1 MacBook Pro, with the new Dell XPS 17 as a high-end reference point.

我们自己的Lightroom Classic和Photoshop基准测试2有助于量化这种性能。尽管Adobe的应用程序具有足够的能力对任何距离内的计算机进行破坏，但Apple Silicon Mac却像冠军一样处理了我们的测试。下图显示了针对基于Intel的2020年13英寸MacBook Pro（2.3GhZ四核i7、32GB RAM）和M1 MacBook Pro的标准Lightroom Import，Lightroom Export和PugetBench基准测试结果，其中新的Dell XPS 17作为高端参考点。

Keep in mind that the M1 MacBook was using Rosetta 2 to emulate Lightroom Classic and Photoshop 2021 3 for all of these tests.

请记住，在所有这些测试中，M1 MacBook均使用Rosetta 2来模拟Lightroom Classic和Photoshop 2021 3。

As you can see, M1 Mac beats Intel Mac in every Lightroom benchmark across the board; and while it still lost out in Pugetbench, the margin isn’t exactly huge. All this against an Intel MacBook Pro that costs $700 more and has 2x the RAM 4. If this is how well the M1 chip runs Lightroom Classic and Photoshop via emulation, fully optimized versions are absolutely going to fly while using a fraction of the power.

如您所见，在所有Lightroom基准测试中，M1 Mac都击败了Intel Mac。尽管它在Pugetbench中仍然输了，但利润并不是很大。所有这一切都与售价700美元以上且具有2倍RAM 4的英特尔MacBook Pro相比。如果这是M1芯片通过仿真运行Lightroom Classic和Photoshop的能力，那么经过完全优化的版本绝对可以在使用一小部分电源的情况下运行。

Even the Dell didn’t outperform the M1 MBP by much in Lightroom, although the discreet graphics card and extra cores made a big difference in PugetBench.

即使谨慎的图形卡和额外的内核在PugetBench中也有很大的不同，即使在Dell中，戴尔在Lightroom中也没有表现出比M1 MBP更好的性能。

Which, finally, brings us to battery life. To see how the M1 MacBook Pro compared to the Intel-based version, I ran a simple test. I opened Google Chrome and queued up a very very long 4K video on YouTube, with 5 random tabs (mostly Serious Eats recipes) open in another window in the background. I then ran that video full screen for as long as it took to kill the battery.

最后，这将我们带到了电池续航时间。为了查看M1 MacBook Pro与基于Intel的版本相比，我进行了一个简单的测试。我打开了Google Chrome浏览器，并在YouTube上排队了一个非常长的4K视频，在后台的另一个窗口中打开了5个随机标签（主要是“严重饮食食谱”）。然后，我将视频全屏播放，直到耗尽电池为止。

The Intel Mac drained fully in 5 hours and 27 minutes, which should give you an idea of how much of a battery hog Chrome is. The M1 Mac? It lasted 15 hours and 50 minutes.

Intel Mac在5小时27分钟内完全耗尽了电量，这应该让您了解Chrome的耗电量。 M1 Mac？历时15小时50分钟。

Keep in mind that these machines are otherwise identical. In fact, the M1 MacBook Pro was set up using a Time Machine backup of the Intel-based version. Both are running the same software, the same background processes, and both were fully charged and unplugged only moments before hitting “play” on the YouTube video.

请记住，这些机器在其他方面是相同的。实际上，M1 MacBook Pro是使用基于Intel版本的Time Machine备份设置的。两者都运行相同的软件，相同的后台进程，并且在点击YouTube视频上的“播放”之前，都已充满电并拔下电源。

15 hours and 50 seconds… IN CHROME. And under normal use, I was frequently able to get two full days of work done without ever having to plug up. I have never been able to say that about any other computer, no matter how lightly I tried to use it. Judging the computer on efficiency alone, it would be a game-changer; pair that with the unprecedented emulation and the performance metrics above, and you’ve got something that practically demands your attention.

15小时50秒…在CHROME中。在正常使用下，我经常能够完成两整天的工作，而无需插电。无论我多么轻易地使用它，我都无法对其他任何计算机这么说。仅从效率来看，这将改变游戏规则；再加上上述空前的仿真和性能指标，您实际上需要引起注意。

Apple is much further ahead than anybody expected them to be with these 1st generation Apple Silicon devices. Rosetta 2 emulation is so good it’s practically invisible, performance exceeds price point by a lot (at least by Apple standards), and efficiency is absolutely mind-blowing. I hoped for “good” with the ARM-based apps, and “usable” with non-optimized apps; I did not expect improved performance at 1/4 the power draw and 3/4 the price.

苹果公司比任何人都预料到的这些第一代Apple Silicon设备要领先得多。 Rosetta 2仿真非常好，几乎看不见，性能大大超过了价格点（至少按照Apple标准），效率绝对令人赞叹。我希望对基于ARM的应用程序“好”，对未优化的应用程序“可用”。我没想到在1/4的功耗和3/4的价格下性能会有所改善。

It’s always difficult to recommend a first-generation product. The first few iterations of a nascent technology typically bring the most aggressive improvements, and if the M1 is already this good I can’t wait to see what the M1X or the M2 bring to the table. What’s surprising is that Apple has successfully eliminated every other reason to wait around.

推荐第一代产品总是很困难。新生技术的最初几次迭代通常会带来最积极的改进，如果M1已经很好，我就迫不及待想看看M1X或M2带来了什么。令人惊讶的是，苹果公司成功消除了其他一切待命原因。

The M1 Macs are not “early adopter” technology. These products are already ready for prime time. Full support for Apple Silicon is already being prioritized by every major developer of creative software, Rosetta 2 is more than capable of taking care of you in the meantime, and the battery life gains alone make the M1 laptops an easy choice over the Intel alternatives.

M1 Macs不是“早期采用者”技术。这些产品已经准备好黄金时间。每个主要的创意软件开发人员都已经优先考虑对Apple Silicon的全面支持，Rosetta 2在此期间可以照顾您，并且电池寿命的增加使M1笔记本电脑比Intel替代产品更容易选择。

But whether you need a new Mac right away or you’re willing to wait and see what Apple sticks inside the iMac or next-gen 16-inch MacBook Pro, there’s one thing I can say without any hesitation: this changes everything.

但是，无论您是立即需要一台新Mac还是愿意等待看看Apple在iMac或下一代16英寸MacBook Pro中保留了什么，我可以毫不犹豫地说一件事：这改变了一切。
