自定义域现在子堆栈上可用

2020-10-23 02:56:39

Custom domains are now available on Substack. Many writers have told us they’d like to use custom domains to build a home for their newsletters, so we’re excited to finally bring this feature to everyone.

自定义域现在在子栈上可用。许多作家告诉我们，他们想要使用自定义域名为他们的时事通讯建立一个主页，所以我们很兴奋终于能把这个功能带给每个人。

To use custom domains, you’ll be charged a one-time $50 fee per publication. After you’ve unlocked this feature, you can add and remove custom domains at no additional cost.

要使用自定义域，您将被收取每份出版物50美元的一次性费用。解锁此功能后，您可以免费添加和删除自定义域。
