DisCO is an organisational model for cooperative groups
DisCO是合作社团体的组织模式

2021-01-05 15:24:53

A DisCO (Distributed Cooperative Organisation) is an organisational model for cooperative groups that combines ideas and practices from cooperativism, the commons, P2P and feminist economics. ‍ It aims to prototype new and radical forms of ownership, governance, entrepreneurship, and value accounting meant to counteract pervasive economic inequality, and offers an alternative to the aims and outcomes of DAOs (Decentralised Autonomous Organisations).

DisCO（分布式合作社组织）是合作社组织的组织模型，它结合了合作主义，公地，P2P和女权经济学的思想和实践。它旨在原型化所有权，治理，企业家精神和价值会计等新的激进形式，以抵消普遍存在的经济不平等，并为DAO（分散式自治组织）的目标和成果提供替代方案。
