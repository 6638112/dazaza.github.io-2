Germany-based Staffbase, which provides a suite of employee engagement tools for companies, raises $145M led by General Atlantic

Chemnitz-based employee engagement firm Staffbase has received $145 million in a financing round led by growth equity firm General Atlantic.

Chemnitz的员工订婚公司员工员工已收到145,000,000美元，由增长股权公司大西洋领导的融资。

Existing investors Insight Partners and e.ventures also participated with KIZOO and Capnamic Ventures remaining invested. As part of the deal Achim Berg, Operating Partner at General Atlantic and President of Bitkom will join the Staffbase advisory board.

现有的投资者洞察力合作伙伴和E.Ventures还参加了剩余的Kizoo和Capnycic Ventures。作为交易Achim Berg的一部分，Artantic Attrantic和Bitkom总裁的操作伙伴将加入员工咨询委员会。

The partnership is expected to strengthen Staffbase’s position in the employee engagement space through international growth.

预计伙伴关系将通过国际增长加强员工参与空间的员工在员工参与空间的立场。

Founded in 2014, Staffbase provides employers with a suite of communications tools including an employee app and internal newsletter and intranet platforms. Staffbase is currently in use by over 8 million people worldwide, and counts organisations such as Adidas, Audi, BHP, Ikea, Johns Hopkins University, and Volvo amongst its client base.

员工公司成立于2014年，为雇主提供一套通信工具，包括员工应用程序和内部通讯和内联网平台。员工队伍在全球范围内有超过800万人使用，并计数阿迪达斯，奥迪，BHP，宜家，约翰霍普金斯大学和沃尔沃之间的组织。

The firm currently has a workforce of 450 employees in 11 locations across the globe including its headquarters in Chemnitz, London, New York, Vancouver, Amsterdam, and Berlin.

该公司目前在全球11个地点拥有450名员工的员工，包括其在Chemnitz，伦敦，纽约，温哥华，阿姆斯特丹和柏林的总部。

This strategic partnership comes just on the heels of Staffbase’s merger with Canada’s leading internal comms provider Bananatag.

这种战略合作伙伴关系恰好是员工与加拿大领先的内部通讯提供商香蕉架的合并的高跟鞋。

“Our vision is to unite all of a company’s employees through strong internal communications and a shared mission. To bring this about, we provide managers and communications specialists at enterprise companies with the leading digital platform for successful employee communications – a platform that we are expanding very rapidly,” comments Staffbase co-founder and CEO Dr. Martin Böhringer.

“我们的愿景是通过强大的内部通信和共享任务团结一家公司的员工。要带来这件事，我们为企业公司提供了管理人员公司，为企业公司提供了成功的员工通信的领先数字平台 - 这是一个我们正在迅速扩张的平台，“员工班班业联合创始人兼首席执行官MartinBöhringer博士。
