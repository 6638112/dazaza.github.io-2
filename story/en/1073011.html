Futurama Revival Ordered at Hulu with Multiple Original Cast Members Returning
在Hulu订购Futurama复兴，多名原演员返回

2022-02-14 10:36:10

Variety has learned that the streaming service has ordered 20 new episodes of the adult animated sci-fi comedy series. The revival hails from David X. Cohen and . Cohen developed the original series with Groening, the series creator.

《综艺》获悉，该流媒体服务已订购了20集成人动画科幻喜剧新剧集。这次复兴来自大卫·X·科恩和。科恩与该系列的创作者格罗宁共同开发了该系列的原创作品。

Original series cast members Billy West, Katey Sagal, Tress MacNeille, Maurice LaMarche, Lauren Tom, Phil LaMarr and David Herman will all return. John DiMaggio, who voiced Bender and several minor characters, is not currently attached. According to an individual with knowledge of the project, the producers are hopeful DiMaggio will return. Should that not happen, Bender will be recast.

原创剧集演员比利·韦斯特、凯蒂·萨加尔、特雷斯·麦克尼尔、莫里斯·拉马尔奇、劳伦·汤姆、菲尔·拉马尔和大卫·赫尔曼都将回归。约翰·迪马吉奥（John DiMaggio）为本德（Bender）和几个小角色配音，目前尚未加入。据一位了解该项目的人士透露，制片人希望迪马吉奥能回来。如果不发生这种情况，本德将被重铸。

Though DiMaggio did not initially make a statement about the revival, he retweeted several posts in which others expressed their desire for him to return. “@TheJohnDiMaggio needs to come back as Bender or this isn’t good news, everyone,” one retweet read, while another simply said: “Come on, pay the man!”

虽然迪马吉奥最初没有就复兴发表声明，但他转发了几篇其他人希望他回来的帖子。“@TheJohnDiMaggio需要以本德的身份回来，否则这不是好消息，大家，”一个转发者写道，而另一个只是简单地说：“来吧，付钱给那个人！”

Later Wednesday afternoon, DiMaggio did provide an update through his Twitter, thanking fans for their concerns and promising to keep people posted on his potential involvement with the project.

周三下午晚些时候，迪马吉奥确实通过他的推特提供了最新消息，感谢粉丝们的关注，并承诺随时向人们通报他可能参与该项目的情况。

Thanks for the concern and the props, everyone. I really appreciate it. Don’t worry, I’ll keep you posted, but until then… CHEESE IT! #bendergate

谢谢大家的关心和支持。我真的很感激。别担心，我会随时通知你的，但在那之前……加油#本德盖特

“I’m thrilled to have another chance to think about the future… or really anything other than the present,” said Cohen.

科恩说：“我很高兴有机会再考虑一下未来……或者除了现在以外的任何事情。”。

“It’s a true honor to announce the triumphant return of ‘Futurama’ one more time before we get canceled abruptly again,” added Groening.

格罗宁补充道：“在我们再次被突然取消之前，再次宣布《Futurama》的胜利回归，真是一种荣幸。”。

“Futurama” focuses on the life of Philip Fry (West), a 25-year-old pizza delivery boy who accidentally freezes himself on December 31, 1999 and wakes up 1,000 years later with a fresh start at life and a new group of friends including Leela (Sagal), a tough but lovely one-eyed mutant, and Bender, a robot who is fond of drinking excessively, smoking cigars, and stealing anything not nailed down.

《未来》聚焦于菲利普·弗莱（西）的生活。菲利普·弗莱（西）是一名25岁的披萨送货员，1999年12月31日意外冻僵，1000年后醒来，重新开始了自己的生活，还有一群新朋友，包括坚强但可爱的独眼变种人莉拉（萨加尔）和喜欢酗酒的机器人本德（Bender），抽雪茄，偷任何不确定的东西。

“When presented with the opportunity to bring fans and viewers new episodes of ‘Futurama,’ we couldn’t wait to dive in,” said Craig Erwich, president of Hulu Originals and ABC Entertainment. “This iconic series helped blaze the trail for the success of adult animation since its initial launch and we look forward to Matt & David continuing to pave the way and further establishing Hulu as the premiere destination for fans of the genre.”

Hulu Originals和ABC Entertainment的总裁克雷格·埃尔维奇（Craig Erwich）说：“当我们有机会为粉丝和观众带来《Futurama》的新剧集时，我们迫不及待地想投入其中。”。“这一标志性系列帮助开创了成人动画自首次推出以来的成功之路，我们期待马特和大卫继续为这一类型的粉丝铺平道路，并进一步将Hulu打造为首映目的地。”

20th Television Animation is producing “Futurama” with Rough Draft Studios, Inc. contributing the animation.

第20电视动画公司正在制作《Futurama》，由草稿工作室公司出品。

This marks the second time the show has been brought back since it originally launched in 1999. After its initial four-season run on Fox, reruns of the show found new life as part of the Adult Swim lineup on Cartoon Network and on DVD. Four direct-to-video films were then produced, which were later re-edited into a fifth season that aired on Comedy Central starting in 2008. Comedy Central would go on to air two more seasons consisting of 26 episodes each between 2010 and 2013.

这是该剧自1999年首播以来第二次回归。在福克斯电视台首次播出四季后，该剧在卡通网络和DVD上重播，成为成人游泳阵容的一部分，获得了新的生命。随后制作了四部直接视频电影，后来被重新剪辑成第五季，从2008年开始在喜剧中心播出。喜剧中心将在2010年至2013年间再播出两季，每季26集。

The show developed a strong cult following over the years and was also met with critical success. It was nominated for 14 Emmy Awards in total, winning six, including best animated program twice as well as winning twice for best individual achievement in animation. LaMarche won twice for best voiceover performance.

多年来，该剧受到了强烈的追捧，也获得了巨大的成功。该片共获得14项艾美奖提名，获得6项，包括两次最佳动画节目奖，以及两次最佳个人动画成就奖。拉马尔奇两次赢得最佳配音表演奖。

“What I love about animation is that it’s possible for a successful show to take a pause and then resume years later, even on a different platform, and pick up right where it left off. ‘Futurama’ is one of those shows,” said Marci Proietto, head of 20th Television Animation. “The excitement from Hulu about returning Matt and David’s genius creation for all-new episodes has been off the charts. I’m thrilled that this incredible team will get to tell more stories, and that our Planet Express crew will have more adventures together. It’s a win for the fans who have loved the show since the beginning, and for the ones who will now discover it for the very first time.”

“我喜欢动画的地方在于，一部成功的电视剧有可能在几年后暂停，然后再继续，即使是在不同的平台上，然后从它停止的地方重新开始。《未来》就是其中之一，”第20部电视动画的负责人Marci Proietto说。“Hulu回归Matt和David天才创作的全新剧集让我兴奋不已。我很高兴这个不可思议的团队将讲述更多的故事，我们的星球快车团队将有更多的冒险在一起。这对从一开始就热爱这部剧的粉丝们来说是一场胜利，对其他人来说也是如此。”他们现在将第一次发现它。"

Note: An earlier version of this story incorrectly identified Leela as an alien. She is in fact a sewer mutant. The error has been corrected.

注：这个故事的早期版本错误地将莉拉识别为外星人。她实际上是一个阴沟变种人。错误已被纠正。
