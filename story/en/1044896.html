If you watched Clive’s video, you’d learn that driving the LEDs less hard also leads to less degradation of them over time and they stay their intended color for longer. (He cites the example of his porch light) And yes I am aware that the plural of anecdote is not data but Arrhenius does tell us that aging (or the speed of (chemical) processes) slows by about half per 10 °C less.

如果您观看了Clive的视频，就会了解到，用较少的力来驱动LED也会随着时间的推移减少其退化，并且它们保持预期颜色的时间更长。 （他以门廊灯为例），是的，我知道不是轶事的复数形式，但阿伦尼乌斯确实告诉我们，老化（或（化学）过程的速度）每降低10°C就会降低大约一半。
