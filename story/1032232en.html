Microsoft PowerToys-拾色器

2020-10-30 12:29:12

Color Picker will let you easily and quickly pick any color from your screen and copy it into the clipboard.

拾色器可以让您轻松、快速地从屏幕上选取任何颜色，并将其复制到剪贴板中。

To start using Color Picker, you need to enable the utility in PowerToys settings (Color Picker section) first.Once you have enabled it, you can launch Color Picker by pressing the activation shortcut Win+ Shift+ C (note that this shortcut can be changed in the settings dialog)

要开始使用拾色器，您需要先在PowerToys设置(拾色器部分)中启用该实用程序。启用后，您可以通过按激活快捷键Win+Shift+C来启动拾色器(请注意，此快捷方式可以在设置对话框中更改)。

When you hover you mouse cursor over the color you would like to copy, press the left mouse button. If you want to see the area around your cursor in a more detail, scroll up to zoom in.Copied color will be stored in your clipboard in the format that is configured in the settings (HEX by default)

将鼠标光标悬停在要复制的颜色上时，按鼠标左键。如果要更详细地查看光标周围的区域，请向上滚动以放大。复制的颜色将以设置中配置的格式(默认为十六进制)存储在剪贴板中
