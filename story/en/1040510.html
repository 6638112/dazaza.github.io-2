The Ampere Altra Review: 2x 80 Cores Arm Server Performance Monster
Ampere Altra评论：2个80核Arm Server Performance Monster

2020-12-18 20:29:40

As we’re wrapping up 2020, one last large review item for the year is Ampere’s long promised new Altra Arm server processor. This year has indeed been the year where Arm servers have had a breakthrough; Arm’s new Neoverse-N1 CPU core had been the IP designer’s first true dedicated server core, promising focused performance and efficiency for the datacentre. Earlier in the year we had the chance to test out the first Neoverse-N1 silicon in the form of Amazon’s Graviton2 inside of AWS EC2 cloud compute offering. The Graviton2 seemed like a very impressive design, but was rather conservative in its goals, and it’s also a piece of hardware that the general public cannot access outside of Amazon’s own cloud services.

在我们完成2020年的同时，Ampere长期以来承诺的新型Altra Arm服务器处理器将是今年的最后一个大型评测项目。今年确实是Arm服务器取得突破的一年。 Arm的新Neoverse-N1 CPU内核是IP设计人员的第一个真正的专用服务器内核，为数据中心带来了集中的性能和效率。在今年早些时候，我们有机会在AWS EC2云计算产品中以Amazon Graviton2的形式测试了第一个Neoverse-N1芯片。 Graviton2看起来是一个非常令人印象深刻的设计，但在目标上却比较保守，它也是一种普通大众无法在Amazon自己的云服务之外访问的硬件。

Ampere Computing, founded in 2017 by former Intel president Renée James, built upon initial IP and design talent of AppliedMicro’s X-Gene CPUs, and with Arm Holdings becoming an investor in 2019, is at this moment in time the sole “true” merchant silicon vendor designing and offering up Neoverse-N1 server designs.

Ampere Computing由前英特尔总裁勒内·詹姆斯（RenéeJames）于2017年创立，以AppliedMicro的X-Gene CPU的最初IP和设计才华为基础，并在Arm Holdings于2019年成为投资者时，目前是唯一的“真正”商人芯片供应商设计并提供Neoverse-N1服务器设计。

To date, the company has had a few products out in the form of the eMAG chips, but with rather disappointing performance figures - understandable given that those were essentially legacy products based on the old X-Gene microarchitecture.

迄今为止，该公司已经推出了几种以eMAG芯片形式出现的产品，但性能数据却令人失望-可以理解，因为这些产品实际上是基于旧X-Gene微体系结构的传统产品。

Ampere’s new Altra product line, on the other hand is the culmination of several years of work and close collaboration with Arm – and the company first “true” product which can be viewed as Ampere pedigree.

另一方面，Ampere的新Altra产品线是经过数年工作和与Arm紧密合作的结晶，也是公司第一个“真正的”产品，可以被视为Ampere的血统书。

Today, with hardware in hand, we’re finally taking a look at the very first publicly available high-performance Neoverse based Arm server hardware, designed for nothing less than maximum achievable performance, aiming to battle the best designs from Intel and AMD.

如今，有了可用的硬件，我们终于可以看到第一个公开可用的高性能基于Neoverse的Arm服务器硬件，其设计目标不亚于可实现的最大性能，旨在与Intel和AMD的最佳设计抗衡。

Ampere has supplied us with the company’s server reference design, dubbed “Mount Jade”, a 2-socket 2U rack unit sever. The server came supplied with two Altra Q80-33 processors, Ampere’s top-of-the-line SKU with each featuring 80 cores running at up to 3.3GHz, with TDP reaching up to 250W per socket.

Ampere为我们提供了该公司的服务器参考设计，被称为“ Mount Jade”，这是一个2插槽2U机架式服务器。该服务器配备了两个Altra Q80-33处理器，这是Ampere的顶级SKU，每个处理器具有80个内核，运行频率高达3.3GHz，每个插槽的TDP高达250W。

We suspect the server was designed with close collaboration with GIGABYTE, as previously hinted by the two company’s announcements of leading hyperscale deployments of the Altra platforms. The Ampere-branded Mount Jade DVT reference motherboard comes in a typical GIGABYTE-blue colour scheme and features 2 sockets with up to 16 DIMM slots per socket, reaching up to 4TB DRAM capacity per socket, although our review unit came equipped with 256GB per socket across 8 DIMMs to fully populate the chip’s 8-channel memory controllers.

我们怀疑该服务器是与技嘉紧密合作设计的，正如两家公司此前宣布的领先的超大规模Altra平台部署所暗示的那样。 Ampere品牌的Mount Jade DVT参考主板采用典型的GIGABYTE蓝色配色方案，并具有2个插槽，每个插槽最多具有16个DIMM插槽，每个插槽最高可以达到4TB DRAM容量，尽管我们的评测单元配备了每个插槽256GB的容量。跨越8个DIMM，以完全填充芯片的8通道内存控制器。

This is also our first look at Ampere’s first-generation socket design. The company doesn’t really market any particular name to the socket, but it’s a massive LGA4926 socket with a pin-count in excess of any other commercial server socket from AMD or Intel. The holding mechanism is somewhat similar to that of AMD’s SP3 system, with a holding mechanism tensioned by a 5-point screw system.

这也是我们对Ampere第一代插座设计的首次了解。该公司并没有在市场上特别使用该插座的名称，但它是一款大型LGA4926插座，其针脚数量超过了AMD或Intel的其他任何商用服务器插座。该固定机构与AMD SP3系统的固定机构有些类似，其固定机构由5点螺钉系统张紧。

The chip itself is absolutely humongous and amongst the current publicly available processors is the biggest in the industry, out-sizing AMD’s SP3 form-factor packaging, coming in at around 77 x 66.8mm – about the same length but considerably wider than AMD’s counterparts.

该芯片本身绝对是庞然大物，在当前可公开获得的处理器中是业内最大的，它的尺寸超过了AMD SP3外形尺寸的包装，尺寸约为77 x 66.8mm-长度相同，但比AMD同类产品要宽得多。

Although it’s a massive chip with a huge IHS, the Mount Jade server surprised me with its cooling solution as the included 250W type cooler only made contact with about 1/4 th the surface area of the heat spreader.

尽管它是具有IHS的大型芯片，但Mount Jade服务器的冷却解决方案使我感到惊讶，因为随附的250W型冷却器仅与散热器表面积的1/4接触。

Ampere here doesn’t have a recessed “lip” around the IHS for the mounting bracket to hold onto the chip like on AMD or Intel systems, so the actual IHS surface is actually recessed in relation to the bracket which means you cannot have a flat surface cooler design across the whole of the chip surface.

这里的Ampere在IHS周围没有凹进的“唇”，可以像AMD或Intel系统那样将安装支架固定在芯片上，因此实际的IHS表面实际上相对于支架凹进了，这意味着您不能平坦表面冷却器设计贯穿整个芯片表面。

Instead, the included 250W design cooler uses a huge vapour chamber design with a “pedestal” to make contact with the chip. Ampere explains that they’ve experimented with different designs and found that a smaller area pedestal actually worked better for heat dissipation – siphoning heat off from the actual chip die which is notably smaller than the IHS and chip package.

取而代之的是，随附的250W设计冷却器采用巨大的蒸气室设计，并带有“基座”以使其与芯片接触。安培（Ampere）解释说，他们已经尝试了不同的设计，并发现较小面积的基座实际上对散热更有效-吸走实际芯片裸片上的热量，该芯片明显小于IHS和芯片封装。

The cooler design is quite complex, with vertical fin stacks dissipating heat directly off the vapour chamber, with additional large horizontal fins dissipating heat from 6 U-shaped heat pipes that draw heat from the vapour chamber. It’s definitely a more complex and high-end design than what we’re used to in server coolers.

冷却器的设计非常复杂，垂直散热片堆栈直接从蒸气室散发热量，另外较大的水平散热片从6个U形热管散发热量，这些U型热管从蒸气室吸收热量。绝对比我们在服务器散热器中使用的设计更为复杂和高端。

Although the Mount Jade server is definitely a very interesting piece of hardware, our focus today lies around the actual new Altra processors themselves, so let’s dive into the new Q80-33 80-core chip next.

尽管Mount Jade服务器绝对是一件非常有趣的硬件，但我们今天的重点是实际的新型Altra处理器本身，因此接下来让我们深入研究新的Q80-33 80核芯片。
