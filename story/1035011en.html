新冠肺炎限制疫苗接种，麻疹疫情激增

2020-11-14 09:01:10

LONDON (Reuters) - Measles surged to infect almost 870,000 people across the world in 2019, the worst figures in almost a quarter of a century as vaccination levels fell below critical levels, a report said on Thursday.

伦敦(路透社)-周四的一份报告称，2019年麻疹感染人数激增，全球近87万人感染，这是近25年来最糟糕的数字，因为疫苗接种水平降至临界水平以下。

Millions of children are at risk of the disease again this year as restrictions imposed to contain the COVID-19 pandemic further disrupt immunisation programmes, the report co-led by the World Health Organization (WHO) said.

这份由世界卫生组织联合领导的报告称，随着为遏制新冠肺炎疫情而实施的限制措施进一步扰乱了免疫接种计划，今年数以百万计的儿童再次面临感染这种疾病的风险。

Measles is one of the most contagious known diseases - more so than COVID-19, Ebola, tuberculosis or flu.

麻疹是已知的传染性最强的疾病之一，比新冠肺炎、埃博拉、结核病或流感更具传染性。

More than 207,000 people died of it last year alone, the report found. With immunisation coverage below the critical 95% needed for community protection, infections rose in all WHO regions last year to the worst levels since 1996, it said.

报告发现，仅去年一年就有超过20.7万人死于这种疾病。报告说，由于免疫覆盖率低于社区保护所需的关键95%，世卫组织所有地区的感染率去年都上升到了1996年以来的最低水平。

“These data send a clear message that we are failing to protect children from measles in every region of the world,” the WHO’s Director-General, Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus, said in a statement.

世界卫生组织总干事Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus在一份声明中说：“这些数据发出了一个明确的信息，那就是我们未能保护世界每个地区的儿童免受麻疹的侵袭。”

The surge in fatal cases means global measles deaths have risen nearly 50% since 2016.

死亡病例的激增意味着自2016年以来，全球麻疹死亡人数上升了近50%。

The report, co-led by the United States Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), cited a collective failure to fully immunise children on time with two doses of measles vaccine as the main driver of the deadly increases.

这份由美国疾病控制和预防中心(CDC)联合领导的报告指出，集体未能按时为儿童接种两剂麻疹疫苗是导致死亡人数增加的主要原因。

Looking ahead to 2020, the report warned that disruptions to vaccination due to the COVID-19 pandemic have crippled efforts to curb measles outbreaks.

展望2020年，报告警告说，新冠肺炎疫情导致的疫苗接种中断已经削弱了遏制麻疹爆发的努力。

As of this month, more than 94 million people were at risk of missing measles vaccinations due to paused immunisation campaigns in 26 countries, it said.

报告称，截至本月，由于26个国家暂停了疫苗接种活动，超过9400万人面临错过麻疹疫苗接种的风险。

“COVID-19 has resulted in dangerous declines in immunization coverage,” Seth Berkley, chief executive of the GAVI global vaccine alliance, said.

全球疫苗联盟的首席执行官塞思·伯克利说：“新冠肺炎已经导致免疫覆盖率的危险下降。”

He described the “alarming” measles report was “a warning that, with the COVID-19 pandemic occupying health systems across the world, we cannot afford to take our eye off the ball.”

他说，这份“令人震惊”的麻疹报告是“一个警告，随着新冠肺炎疫情占据了世界各地的卫生系统，我们不能把目光从球上移开。”

After steady downward progress from 2010 to 2016, measles cases began rising again from 2017. The report said there were a total of 869,770 measles cases, with 207,500 deaths, in 2019.

在2010年至2016年稳步下降之后，麻疹病例从2017年开始再次上升。报告称，2019年共有869,770例麻疹病例，其中207,500人死亡。

WHO and the UN children’s fund UNICEF urged governments last week to act now to prevent epidemics of measles, polio and other infectious diseases.

世卫组织和联合国儿童基金会上周敦促各国政府立即采取行动，预防麻疹、脊髓灰质炎和其他传染病的流行。
