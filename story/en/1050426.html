Volvo’s next EV is this C40 Recharge fastback crossover
沃尔沃的下一款电动车是这款C40 Recharge fastback crossover

2021-03-02 23:51:14

Calling climate change "the greatest safety test" it needs to pass, Volvo is vowing that half the cars it sells in 2025 will be battery electric vehicles, with a model range that's only BEVs by 2030. And by 2040, the company plans to be entirely carbon neutral, working with its partners to reduce emissions across the entire supply chain. (For a little more explanation, we talked to Volvo about sustainability and battery supply chains last year.) The statements were made at an online event on Tuesday morning to launch the company's latest BEV, the C40 Recharge.

称气候变化为“最大的安全测试”沃尔沃誓言要在2025年之前售出的汽车中有一半将是电动汽车，到2030年其车型范围将是唯一的BEV。到2040年，该公司计划完全实现碳中和，与合作伙伴一起减少整个供应链的排放。 （为获得更多解释，我们去年与沃尔沃就可持续发展和电池供应链进行了交谈。）这些声明是在周二上午举行的在线活动上发表的，以发布公司最新的BEV，即C40 Recharge。

Previously, Volvo mostly used the C-prefix for compact hatchbacks. Now, the prefix denotes a fastback version of the XC40 Recharge crossover, probably because everyone wants crossovers and no one wants hatchbacks anymore. The family resemblance is immediately obvious, but Volvo says that everything from the A pillar backward is new. This becomes evident when you look at it in profile—there's a more steeply canted windshield at the front, and at the rear a spoiler integrated into the roof helps maintain rear headroom.

以前，沃尔沃大多将C前缀用于紧凑型掀背车。现在，前缀表示XC40 Recharge跨界车的后备版本，这可能是因为每个人都想要跨界车，再也没有人想要掀背车了。家庭的相似之处立即显而易见，但沃尔沃表示，从A支柱向后的所有事物都是新事物。当您从侧面看时，这一点变得很明显-前部的挡风玻璃倾斜度更大，而后部的车顶扰流板则有助于保持后部净空。

The fastback crossover might be even more distinctive once the sun goes down. New "Thor's Hammer" headlights have pixel technology for the first time and also have elements that move with the steering to better illuminate the road. And at the rear, there is a new twilight signature that looks particularly cool in the dark (and which was influenced by childhood memories of Battlestar Galactica, according to one of Volvo's designers.

太阳落山后，快速后退跨界车可能会变得更加独特。新《雷神之锤》大灯首次采用像素技术，并且还具有随转向而移动的元素，以更好地照亮道路。沃尔沃的一位设计师表示，在后部，有一个新的暮光招牌，在黑暗中显得特别酷（并且受到了太空堡垒卡拉狄加的童年记忆的影响）。

The C40 Recharge also comes with the same Android Automotive infotainment system that impressed me so much in the XC40 Recharge and Polestar 2. And mechanically, the C40 Recharge is identical to the XC40 Recharge that we drove briefly last November. So that means an electric motor for each axle with a combined power output of 300 kW (408 hp), powered by a 78 kWh lithium-ion battery pack made in-house. It will have the same usable capacity of 75 kWh, but the more aerodynamic range should equate to a slight increase in range over the XC40 Recharge's official EPA estimate of 208 miles (335 km).

C40 Recharge还配备了相同的Android Automotive信息娱乐系统，这在XC40 Recharge和Polestar 2中给我留下了深刻的印象。从机械上讲，C40 Recharge与我们去年11月短暂驾驶的XC40 Recharge相同。因此，这意味着每个车轴的电动机总输出功率为300 kW（408 hp），由内部制造的78 kWh锂离子电池组提供动力。它将具有75 kWh的可用容量，但更高的空气动力学范围应等同于XC40 Recharge的官方EPA估计的208英里（335公里）略有增加。

During our online briefing this morning, Volvo was asked whether this was a disappointingly low range, but the company said the C40 Recharge (and the XC40 Recharge) will mostly be bought as second cars and that waking up every morning with more than 200 miles of range is sufficient for day-to-day activities. Like its less-sleek sibling, the C40 Recharge will DC fast charge at up to 150 kW, which will take the battery from 0 to 80 percent in 40 minutes.

在今天早上我们的网上发布会上，沃尔沃被问及这是否是令人失望的低射程，但该公司表示C40 Recharge（和XC40 Recharge）将主要作为第二辆汽车购买，并且每天早晨醒来时会行驶200英里以上。范围足以进行日常活动。像其不太光滑的兄弟姐妹一样，C40 Recharge将以高达150 kW的速度进行直流快速充电，这将在40分钟内将电池的电量从0％提高到80％。

During the event this morning, Volvo also announced that it is transitioning to an entirely online sales process. State laws that protect the dealership model mean Volvo still needs to work out how to implement this move in the US, but the company says it's working together with its dealership partners to make it happen. And its "Care by Volvo" experiment evidently worked well, because it's expanding the concept to all its future electric Volvos. This is a subscription service where the monthly fee includes the car but also bundles maintenance and insurance (with no obligation to keep the car at the end of the subscription).

在今天早上的活动中，沃尔沃还宣布将过渡到完全在线销售流程。保护经销商模式的州法律意味着沃尔沃仍需要研究如何在美国实施这一举措，但该公司表示，沃尔沃正在与经销商合作伙伴一起努力实现这一目标。沃尔沃（Volvo）的＆＃34;实验显然效果很好，因为它将概念扩展到了未来的所有沃尔沃电动汽车。这是一项订阅服务，其中的月租费包括汽车，还捆绑了维护和保险（没有义务在订购结束时保留汽车）。

Production of the C40 Recharge begins later this year in Sweden, but Volvo isn't ready to commit to US deliveries earlier than "later next year." Pricing information should follow closer to that date.

C40 Recharge的生产将于今年晚些时候在瑞典开始生产，但沃尔沃尚未准备好于明年晚些时候承诺向美国交付。定价信息应在该日期之前。
