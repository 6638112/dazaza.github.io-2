Fukushima radiation did not damage health of local people, UN says
联合国说，福岛辐射没有损害当地人的健康

2021-03-11 00:51:50

‘No adverse health effects’ detected despite three nuclear reactors being destroyed by an earthquake and tsunami in 2011

尽管2011年地震和海啸销毁了三个核反应堆，但检测到“没有不良健康影响”

Radiation caused by the nuclear meltdown in Fukushima a decade ago has not damaged the health of local people, according to a UN report.

根据联合国报告，十年前福岛核崩溃引起的核崩溃引起的辐射没有损害当地人的健康状况。

Gillian Hirth, chairwoman of the UN’s scientific committee on the effects of atomic radiation (Unscear), said that “no adverse health effects among Fukushima residents have been documented that could be directly attributed to radiation exposure from the accident” in March 2011.

联合国原子辐射影响（未准备）的科学委员会主席吉利亚袭人表示，“福岛居民的不利健康影响已被记录，这可以直接归因于事故的辐射暴露”2011年3月。

Unscear said the latest findings supported a 2013 report on the health impact of radiation released after three reactors suffered meltdown at Fukushima Daiichi nuclear power plant.

未经准备的说明最新调查结果支持2013年关于在福岛核电站崩溃后释放的辐射的健康影响的报告。

The latest report was released as Japan prepared to mark 10 years since a powerful earthquake triggered a tsunami that killed more than 18,000 people and triggered the worst nuclear crisis since Chernobyl a quarter of a century earlier.

最近的报告被释放为日本准备10年以来，由于强大的地震引发了一个杀死了超过18,000人的海啸，并引发了自25年前的切尔诺贝利以来最严重的核危机。

The incident forced the evacuation of about 160,000 people, many of whom have not returned to their homes 10 years later.

事件强迫疏散约160,000人，其中许多人10年后没有回到家园。

Concern over the potential health effects of the accident rose after reports of a high incidence of thyroid cancer in children living in Fukushima prefecture at the time of the disaster.

在灾难发生在福岛县福岛县的儿童的高发病率后，对事故的潜在健康效应的关注。

Unscear and other experts have attributed the higher rates to the use of highly sensitive ultrasound equipment and the large number of children who have been examined.

未经检查和其他专家归因于使用高度敏感的超声设备和已被检查的大量儿童的使用率。

The first round of tests, conducted between 2011 and 2015, identified 116 cases of actual or suspected thyroid cancer among more than 300,000 people aged 18.

在2011年和2015年之间进行的第一轮测试，确定了116岁以上的18岁以上300,000人的实际或疑似甲状腺癌的116例。

“On the balance of available evidence, the large increase ... in the number of thyroid cancers detected among exposed children is not the result of radiation exposure,” Unscear said.

“关于现有证据的平衡，大幅增加......在暴露的儿童中检测到的甲状腺癌的数量不是辐射曝光的结果，”未准备说。

“Rather, they are the result of ultrasensitive screening procedures that have revealed the prevalence of thyroid abnormalities in the population not previously detected.”

“相反，它们是超细筛查程序的结果，揭示了先前未检测到的人口中的甲状腺异常的患病率。”

Gerry Thomas, director of the Chernobyl Tissue Bank and chair of molecular pathology at Imperial College London, said she was not surprised that Unscear and other bodies had ruled out a link between the thyroid cancer cases and radiation from Fukushima.

伦敦帝国学院切尔诺贝利组织库和分子病理椅总监Gerry Thomas表示，她并不感到惊讶，没有考虑甲状腺癌病例与来自福岛的辐射之间的联系。

“The thyroid radiation doses post-Fukushima were about 100 times lower than after Chernobyl due to a number of factors”, Thomas said, adding that “all the evidence we have on levels of exposure and the data from the health screening programme in Fukushima suggests that it is very unlikely that we will see any increase in thyroid cancer in these children, who are now adolescents and young adults”.

“福岛的甲状腺辐射剂量比切尔诺贝利由于多种因素为低约100倍”，托马斯表示，在福岛的所有证据和福岛的健康筛查计划中的所有证据以及来自福岛的健康筛查计划的所有证据表明我们非常不太可能在这些儿童中看到甲状腺癌的任何增加，现在是青少年和年轻的成年人“。

But in a report released to coincide with the 10th anniversary of the triple disaster, Greenpeace Japan warned that large areas near the plant where evacuation orders have been lifted in recent years had still not been properly decontaminated, leaving returning residents exposed to potentially harmful levels of radiation for decades.

但在一份报告中，据警告近年来疏散订单已解除疏散订单附近的大面积仍未得到妥善净化，仍未适当净化，但仍未适当净化，仍未得到妥善净化，留下潜在有害的居民差异的大区域辐射数十年。

“Successive governments during the last 10 years … have attempted to perpetrate a myth about the nuclear disaster,” said Shaun Burnie, senior nuclear specialist at Greenpeace East Asia. “They have sought to deceive the Japanese people by misrepresenting the effectiveness of the decontamination programme and ignoring radiological risks.”

“过去10年的连续政府......试图犯下关于核灾难的神话，”GreenPeace East Asia的高级核专家Shaun Burnie说。 “他们试图通过歪曲去污计划的有效性和忽视放射性风险的效果来欺骗日本人。”
