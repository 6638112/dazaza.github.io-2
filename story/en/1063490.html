Currently, 43% of youth in South Africa are unemployed, and not in education
目前，南非43％的青年失业，而不是教育

2021-06-04 20:31:07

Every quarter in South Africa, Statistics South Africa releases the unemployment data. For over a decade, this data has been getting worse and worse.

南非每季度，统计南非宣布失业数据。十多年来，这种数据变得越来越糟。

During the same period, every president South Africa has had, Cyril Ramaphosa included, has been talking about creating jobs. Yet, more and more people are being reported to be unemployed with no solution in sight or the trend reversing.

在同一时期，南非总统所拥有的每个主席拉斐萨都在谈论创造就业机会。然而，越来越多的人被据报道，在没有解决方案或趋势逆转的情况下被失业。

However, worse than all of this, is the alarmingly growing number of youth that is unemployed in South Africa.

然而，比所有这一切差，是南非失业的惊人越来越多的青年。

Let me be more specific: 43,0% of young people aged between 15 - 34 years old are not only unemployed according to Statistics South Africa, but they are also not in “education or training.” That’s right, they are not employed as well as not being educated or trained in any skills they can use to be employed in the future.

让我更具体地：43,0％的年轻人在15至34岁之间不仅根据南非统计数据而失业，而且它们也不在“教育或培训”中。这是对的，他们没有受雇，也没有受过教育或培训，他们将来可以使用任何技能。

Despite these very unsettling statistics, you will hear the South African President, Cyril Ramaphosa, talk about how the government is creating jobs and improving the country. In fact, just this week, the President could be heard across different media platforms talking about how the government has been steering the country through a difficult period and making good progress with economic recovery and job creation.

尽管有这些非常令人令人市的统计数据，您将听到南非总统Cyril Ramaphosa，谈谈政府如何创造就业机会并改善国家。事实上，就在本周，总统可以在不同的媒体平台上听到谈论政府如何通过困难时期转向该国，并通过经济复苏和创造创造良好进展。

South Africa has a serious youth unemployment problem. Beyond being a problem, it is a ticking time bomb as young able people grow more frustrated with each passing day as they are unable to make ends meet. The data is also quite clear, a lot of the youth do not even complete high school, making them not only unemployed but unemployable.

南非有一个严重的青年失业问题。除了出现问题之外，它是一个滴答时间炸弹，因为年轻人能够与每次通过的日子变得更加沮丧，因为他们无法做出结束。数据也很清楚，很多年轻人甚至都不熟悉高中，不仅使他们失业但失业。

Thus, as much as South Africa has a youth unemployment problem, the solution is relatively available - get youth to first complete high school and then be equipped with skills they can use to earn a living.

因此，尽管南非有青少年失业问题，解决方案相对可用 - 让青少年首先完成高中，然后配备他们可以用来谋生的技能。

With that in mind, you have to ask: why isn’t the South African government putting all their energy into this?

考虑到这一点，你必须问：为什么南非政府不是将他们所有能量置于此事？
