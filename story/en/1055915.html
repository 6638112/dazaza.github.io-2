I had the same problem suddenly occur, and it was fixed by changing the useragent override for twitter. In about: config find general.useragent.override.twitter.com I changed it to this value: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 10.0; Win64; x64; rv:78.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/78.0 which works for me. It was a string i had listed for another site, I have no idea if it's a good one or if there are better ways of solving the problem. But it worked for me.
