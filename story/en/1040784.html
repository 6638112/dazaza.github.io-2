Don't Buy the Fucking Domain
不要买他妈的域名

2020-12-20 03:33:41

You rush to you favourite domain registrar, credit card burning in your hand. Oh, thank God I got there in time, no one else can touch me now. You buy it. You applaud. You do nothing. Your domain name sits sadly in your account gathering dust, only for you to renew it year-on-year and your idea flutters away like a feather in the wind.

您急于向您最喜欢的域名注册商求助，信用卡烧在您的手中。哦，感谢上帝，我及时到达了那里，现在没有人可以碰我。你买你鼓掌。你什么都不做。可悲的是，您的域名位于您的帐户中，尘埃落定，只是您要逐年续约，而您的想法却像羽毛一样飞扬。

Christ, I'm guilt of this, but the one thing I've learnt over the years, is that the best way to ship something, is not to start with the domain. Domain buying is a folly, a siren singing you to shipwreck. The very action, for me at least only tricks my Lizard brain into thinking I've done something about it. The reality is, you've only done "fake work" as opposed to "real work".

基督，我对此感到内，但是我多年来学到的一件事是，运送某物的最佳方法不是从领域开始。域名购买是愚蠢的，警报器使您沉船。对于我来说，这种动作至少只会使我的蜥蜴大脑误以为我已经做了一些事情。现实情况是，您只完成了伪造工作。与“真实作品”相对。

"Fake work" are the little things that make you feel as if you've moved the needle on your project, but really you've just been procrastinating. Buying domains, reading e-books on marketing, reading this blog post, refactoring code that you aren't happy with - all fake work. Feels good to do it, but leaves your business and projects no closer to moving forwards. Real work on the other hand, actually creates something. A domain name is the sprinkles on the doughnut, not the goo that holds the pieces together. Go build your landing page, then worry about your domain.

＆＃34;伪造作品＆＃34;是让您感觉好像在项目上动了针头的小事情，但实际上您只是在拖延。购买域名，阅读有关营销的电子书，阅读此博客文章，重构您不满意的代码-所有虚假的工作。做到这一点感觉很好，但是您的业务和项目就更接近前进了。另一方面，实际的工作实际上创造了一些东西。域名是撒在甜甜圈上的，而不是将两块在一起的粘稠物。去建立您的登陆页面，然后担心您的域。

Guess what, domains are just like ideas. Worthless unless you execute on them. Even if someone does decide to buy it, (which is unlikely anyway if it is available) - the domain name doesn't fucking matter.

猜猜，领域就像想法。除非您对它们执行，否则毫无价值。即使有人确实决定购买它（无论如何也不太可能购买）-域名无关紧要。

Case in point - who in their right mind would have bought the domain Google.com before Google existed? You are creative after all, I'm sure you've thousands of ideas - and no shortage of domain creativity to go along with it. What is it going to take once your project is ready to sit down and worry about the domain at that stage?

恰当的例子-在Google成立之前，谁能正确地选择Google.com域名？毕竟，您是有创造力的，我确信您有成千上万的想法-并且不乏领域创造力。一旦您的项目准备好坐下来并在那个阶段担心领域，该怎么办？
