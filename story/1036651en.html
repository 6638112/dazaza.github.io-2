GM To Leverage Customer Data In New Insurance Business
通用汽车在新保险业务中利用客户数据

2020-11-26 09:02:48

General Motors has launched its new insurance agency, OnStar Insurance Services, which will be the exclusive agent for its own OnStar Insurance company.

通用汽车公司已经启动了新的保险公司OnStar Insurance Services，它将成为其自己的OnStar Insurance公司的独家代理。

OnStar Insurance Services has been created to achieve a better understanding of the vehicles GM produces, in order to offer a personalised digital insurance experience for drivers.

创建OnStar保险服务旨在更好地了解GM生产的车辆，以便为驾驶员提供个性化的数字保险体验。

Andrew Rose, president of OnStar Insurance Services commented: “OnStar Insurance will promote safety, security and peace of mind. We aim to be an industry leader, offering insurance in an innovative way.

OnStar Insurance Services总裁Andrew Rose评论：“ OnStar Insurance将促进安全，保障和安心。我们旨在成为行业领导者，以创新的方式提供保险。

“GM customers who have subscribed to OnStar and connected services will be eligible to receive discounts, while also receiving fully-integrated services from OnStar Insurance Services.”

“订购了OnStar和相关服务的通用汽车客户将有资格获得折扣，同时还可以从OnStar Insurance Services获得完全集成的服务。”

The service has been developed to improve the experience for policyholders who have an OnStar Safety & Security plan, as Automatic Crash Response has been designed to notify an OnStar Emergency-certified Advisor who can send for help.

该服务的开发旨在改善具有OnStar安全与安保计划的投保人的体验，因为“自动崩溃响应”旨在通知可通过OnStar紧急认证的顾问发送帮助。

The service is currently working with its insurance carrier partners to remove biased insurance plans by focusing on factors within the customer’s control, which includes individual vehicle usage and rewarding smart driving habits that benefit road safety.

该服务目前正在与保险公司的合作伙伴合作，通过关注客户控制范围内的因素来消除有偏见的保险计划，其中包括个人使用车辆和奖励有益于道路安全的智能驾驶习惯。

OnStar Insurance Services plans to provide customers with personalised vehicle care and promote safer driving habits, along with a data-backed analysis of driving behaviour.

OnStar Insurance Services计划为客户提供个性化的车辆保养并提倡更安全的驾驶习惯，以及对驾驶行为进行数据支持的分析。

The service plans to build on the learnings of the OnStar Smart Driver feature to provide each policyholder with recommendations for smarter driving habits so customers can drive more safely and potentially earn discounts.

该服务计划基于对OnStar Smart Driver功能的学习，为每个保单持有人提供有关更智能驾驶习惯的建议，以便客户可以更安全地驾驶并有可能获得折扣。

The service will start in Arizoa and initially offer OnStar Insurance to GM employees in Q4 2020, slowly expanding to additional customers, including the general public, in early 2021.

该服务将从亚利桑那州开始，最初将在2020年第四季度为通用汽车员工提供OnStar Insurance，然后在2021年初逐步扩展到包括公众在内的更多客户。
