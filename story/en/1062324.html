Stripe announces pricing for invoicing
条纹宣布发票的定价

2021-05-13 06:40:47

Back in 2018, Stripe Billing made it easy to include an invoice with a subscription. As we spoke with more users, it became clear that businesses required sophisticated invoicing functionality.

回到2018年，条纹计费使得包含订阅的发票容易。正如我们与更多用户交谈所以，它就明确表示企业需要复杂的发票功能。

Most people underestimate how complex invoicing can be. Isn’t an invoice just a glorified PDF? Not quite. It turns out there are a lot of little details to get right beyond just emailing an invoice.

大多数人低估了发票的复杂程度。发票不是一个荣耀的pdf吗？不完全的。事实证明，只需通过电子邮件发送发票就有很多细节即可。

For example, you may want to accept card payments for customer convenience but switch to bank transfers on large invoices to lower your costs. Reconciling these bank payments can be tedious and error-prone without tools to help. You’ll want to automate sending payment reminders as your business grows. Once you start sending invoices globally, you have a whole host of things to think about—from which payment methods customers in each country prefer, to the complex regulations that govern invoicing in different regions.

例如，您可能希望接受客户便利的卡支付，但在大型发票上切换到银行转移，以降低您的成本。重新调整这些银行付款可能是乏味的，并且没有工具有助于帮助。随着业务增长，您将希望自动发送付款提醒。一旦您开始在全球发送发票，您就有一系列的事情来思考 - 从每个国家的客户倾向于到哪些支付方式，以管理在不同地区的发票的复杂条例。

As a result, we’re investing more in Invoicing to help businesses operate more effectively and scale faster. We want to build the best Invoicing solution for internet businesses of every size—from solo entrepreneurs to public companies.

因此，我们正在投资更多的发票，以帮助企业更有效地运营，更快地规模。我们希望为每一个尺寸的互联网业务开票解决方案 - 从独奏企业家到公共公司。

More than 50,000 businesses including Slack, Retool, Figma, and Divvy Homes use Stripe Invoicing to create and send custom invoices. Invoicing is integrated into the Stripe payments stack, so our customers can automatically collect invoice payments with smart retries and email reminders, reconcile ACH or wire payments, and invoice globally from day one.

超过50,000名业务，包括松弛，重新停止，图形和Divvy Homes使用条纹发票来创建和发送自定义发票。发票集成到条带支付堆栈中，因此我们的客户可以自动收集具有智能重试和电子邮件提醒，协调ACH或WILE支付的发票支付，以及从第一天全球发票。

We’re also offering two pricing plans for new Stripe Invoicing users going forward, with an option to get started with Invoicing at no additional cost. Existing Stripe Invoicing users can use Invoicing at their current pricing until January 2024.

我们还为新的条纹发票用户提供了两项定价计划，以便以不额外的成本开始使用发票开始。现有的条纹发票用户可以在目前的定价中使用发票到20124年1月。

Today, we’re sharing a number of improvements that we’ve been working on over the past few months.

今天，我们在过去几个月里分享了许多我们一直在运作的改进。

You can use Stripe Invoicing without writing any code. We’re rolling out a new Invoice editor, so you can preview invoices in real-time as you customize them. Try it out in the Dashboard.

您可以使用条带发票而不编写任何代码。我们正在推出一个新的发票编辑器，因此您可以在自定义时将发票实时预览。在仪表板上尝试一下。

We want to make sending an invoice incredibly simple, so we’ve added the ability to send an invoice from the Stripe iOS app. We’ve also set up “ invoice.new”—just type that into any browser URL bar and jump straight to our invoice editor.

我们希望令人难以置信的简单发送发票，因此我们已经增加了从Stripe IOS应用程序发送发票的能力。我们还在任何浏览器URL栏中设置了“发票.NEW”-JUST类型，并直接跳转到我们的发票编辑器。

We recently redesigned the hosted invoice page. It now looks nicer and is mobile-friendly, which makes things easier for your customers. Notably, our hosted pages can now automatically surface the most relevant payment methods for your customer based on their location to improve conversion. You can always turn payment methods on or off in the Dashboard.

我们最近重新设计了托管的发票页面。它现在看起来更好，是移动友好的，这使您的客户更容易。值得注意的是，我们的托管页现在可以根据他们的位置自动为客户提供最相关的付款方式，以改善转换。您可以随时在仪表板上打开或关闭付款方式。

Last year, we added the ability to send online invoices from your own email domain, which helps with delivery and SPF issues. Stripe invoice emails now also come with a PDF of the invoice attached to assist your customers with record keeping or easier connection with their accounting systems.

去年，我们增加了从您自己的电子邮件域中发送在线发票的能力，这有助于交付和SPF问题。 STRIPE发票电子邮件现在还附带了附加发票的PDF，以帮助您的客户与其会计系统进行记录或更容易连接。

Finally, we’re rolling out a new feature that lets you send invoices in multiple currencies to a single customer— sign up for the beta to request access. This is helpful for charging customers that have multiple offices globally and might pay you from more than one bank account.

最后，我们正在推出一个新功能，让您在多种货币中发送发票到单个客户签署测试版以请求访问权限。这有助于为全球多个办事处充电，并可能从一个以上的银行账户支付您。

We’re excited to invest in more invoicing features, so you can spend less time on accounts receivable and get paid even faster. In the next few months, look for better ACH debit support, partial payments, and more. You can also explore our invoicing partners, like Freshbooks, Invoice2Go, Invoiced, Sage, and Xero, that integrate with Stripe Payments.

我们很高兴能够投资更多的发票功能，因此您可以花费更少的时间在应收账款中，并获得更快的报酬。在未来几个月内，寻找更好的ACH借记支持，部分付款等。您还可以探索我们的发票合作伙伴，如新鲜簿，Invoice2Go，发票，Sage和Xero，与条纹付款集成。

To learn more about Stripe Invoicing, check out stripe.com/invoicing or create an invoice in the Dashboard.

要了解有关条纹发票的更多信息，请查看stripe.com/invocing或在仪表板中创建发票。

As always, please let us know if you have any questions or feedback—we’d love to hear from you.

一如既往，如果您有任何疑问或反馈，请告诉我们 - 我们很乐意收到您的来信。
