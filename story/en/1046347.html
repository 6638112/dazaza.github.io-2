GameStop, AMC surge after Reddit users lead chaotic revolt against big Wall Street funds
Reddit用户引发针对华尔街大型基金的混乱反抗后，AMC激增

2021-01-28 23:07:03

Shares of video game retailer GameStop and movie theater giant AMC Entertainment soared to astronomical levels Wednesday as an apparent swarm of ordinary investors, spurred on by a Reddit message board, took on big Wall Street funds that had bet the stock prices would fall.

视频游戏零售商GameStop和电影院巨头AMC娱乐公司的股票周三飙升至天文数字，因为在Reddit留言板的刺激下，明显的普通投资者涌入了华尔街的大型基金，他们押注股价会下跌。

GameStop shares more than doubled, to $347.51, and have soared more than 400 percent this week. AMC, beleaguered by the coronavirus pandemic and stay-at-home measures, quadrupled to $19.90 a share.

GameStop的股价翻了一倍多，达到347.51美元，本周飙升了400％以上。受冠状病毒大流行和居家措施困扰，AMC的股价翻了两番，至每股19.90美元。

The GameStop and AMC price surges were so dramatic that trading for both companies was temporarily halted by the stock exchanges to temper volatility. But once trading resumed, prices took off again. TD Ameritrade, a leading trading platform, took the unusual step of imposing restrictions on both stocks, citing “an abundance of caution amid unprecedented market conditions.”

GameStop和AMC的价格上涨如此剧烈，以至于两家公司的交易都被证券交易所暂时停止，以缓解波动性。但是一旦恢复交易，价格便再次上涨。领先的交易平台TD Ameritrade采取了非同寻常的步骤，即对这两只股票实施限制，理由是“在前所未有的市场条件下，人们要谨慎行事。”

The extraordinary trading, which many analysts linked to a pandemic-related surge in day trading by average investors, seemed disconnected from the companies’ financial prospects. GameStop, which sells video game software via a network of bricks-and-mortar retail outlets, has lost money in five of the past six quarters. Revenue in the three-month period that ended in October was down 30 percent from the same period one year earlier.

许多分析师将这种异常交易与普通投资者大流行相关的日间交易激增联系起来，似乎与公司的财务前景脱节了。通过实体零售网络销售视频游戏软件的GameStop在过去六个季度中有五个季度亏损。截止到十月的三个月中，收入比去年同期下降了30％。

As officials at the White House and Federal Reserve avoided detailed comment, there were signs that market watchdogs at the Securities and Exchange Commission will scrutinize the unusual trading.

由于白宫和美联储的官员回避了详细评论，有迹象表明，证券交易委员会的市场监管机构将对这种异常交易进行审查。

Federal securities law prohibits market participants from misrepresenting a company’s prospects to artificially affect its share price. With Reddit users cloaked in anonymity, there is no way of knowing whether messages touting GameStop come from average Joes — or scam artists executing a “pump-and-dump” stock scheme.

联邦证券法禁止市场参与者虚假陈述公司的人为影响其股价的前景。由于Reddit用户被匿名掩盖，因此无法得知向GameStop宣传的消息是否来自普通的Joes或执行“ pump-and-dump”股票计划的骗子艺术家。

In a statement after the markets closed, Allison Herren Lee, the SEC’s acting chair, and two subordinates said the commission was “actively monitoring the on-going market volatility in the options and equities markets” and was working with other regulators “to assess the situation and review the activities” of those involved.

美国证券交易委员会代理主席艾里森·赫伦·李（Allison Herren Lee）和两名下属在市场关闭后的一份声明中表示，该委员会“正在积极监测期权和股票市场的持续波动性”，并正在与其他监管机构合作“评估情况并审查相关人员的活动。

“Folks at the SEC are going to want to take a very close look at this,” said Harvey Pitt, who chaired the commission from 2001-2003. “When you have extreme volatility of this nature, the system is not necessarily working correctly. … You’re starting to see things that would look to a regulator as if they were manipulation.”

“ SEC的工作人员将要对此进行非常仔细的研究，” 2001年至2003年担任委员会主席的哈维·皮特（Harvey Pitt）说。 “当您具有这种性质的极大波动性时，该系统不一定能正常工作。 ……您开始看到一些看起来像是操纵者的监管者。”

The soaring stock prices for companies that many professional investors scorn illuminated the striking influence of social media, or what Peter Atwater, an economics lecturer at William & Mary, calls a “flash mob with money.”

许多专业投资者嘲笑的公司股价飞涨，说明了社交媒体的惊人影响力，或者威廉＆amp; amp;威廉姆斯大学经济学讲师彼得·阿特沃特（Peter Atwater）的影响。玛丽，称之为“有钱的暴民”。

In their open challenge to the deep-pocketed hedge funds that bet on individual stock prices falling, Reddit’s online mob brandishes an us-against-them rhetoric that echoes the populism of former president Donald Trump’s supporters.

Reddit的网上暴民面对押注个别股票价格下跌，资金雄厚的对冲基金的公开挑战，挥舞着反对美国的言论，呼应前总统唐纳德·特朗普的支持者的民粹主义。

Posters savaged CNBC, the financial news channel, for allegedly siding with Wall Street firms over the ordinary investor. One post touting GameStop was tagged “bankrupting institutional investors for Dummies.”

招贴画激怒了金融新闻频道CNBC，据称其与华尔街公司在普通投资者之上站在一起。一则吹捧GameStop的帖子被标记为“为傻瓜破产的机构投资者”。

On Twitter, Anthony Scaramucci, a hedge fund manager who was briefly a Trump adviser, wrote: “We are witnessing the French Revolution of finance.”

在Twitter上，曾短暂担任特朗普顾问的对冲基金经理Anthony Scaramucci写道：“我们正在见证法国金融大革命。”

Still, to many analysts, the frenzied buying that has driven the shares of companies like GameStop into a vertical climb was uncomfortably reminiscent of the dot-com-era financial bubble, which ended with technology stocks losing more than half their value. Some described the Dow Jones industrial average’s 63 percent gain from its March lows as evidence that the Federal Reserve’s near-zero interest rates were fueling a similar craze.

尽管如此，对许多分析师而言，疯狂购买已将GameStop等公司的股票推向了垂直攀升，这令人不安地让人联想到互联网时代的泡沫，而泡沫最终导致科技股的市值缩水了一半以上。一些人形容道琼斯工业平均指数较3月低点上涨了63％，这表明美联储接近零的利率正在助长类似的狂潮。

“Activity like this has been going on since the beginning of markets, but there is no question that the Fed’s easy money policy has been the liquor that has made many market participants drunk,” said Peter Boockvar, chief investment officer for Bleakley Advisory Group.

Bleakley Advisory Group首席投资官Peter Boockvar表示：“自市场开始以来，这种活动就一直在进行，但毫无疑问，美联储的宽松货币政策一直使许多市场参与者喝醉了。”

Wednesday’s trading frenzy played out amid a decline in the broader market. The Dow fell 633.87 points, or 2.1 percent, as investors sorted through a mixed bag of corporate earnings. The broader S&P 500 dropped 98.85 points, or 2.6 percent, while the tech-heavy Nasdaq shed 355.46 points, or 2.6 percent.

在大盘下跌​​中，周三的交易狂潮逐渐消退。道琼斯工业平均指数下跌633.87点，跌幅2.1％，这是因为投资者对公司业绩的喜忧参半。标准普尔500指数下跌98.85点，跌幅为2.6％，而技术含量高的纳斯达克指数下跌355.46点，跌幅为2.6％。

Treasury Secretary Janet Yellen and the rest of the Biden administration’s economic team are monitoring the matter, White House press secretary Jen Psaki told reporters at a midday briefing, adding that “the stock market is not the only measure of the health of our economy.”

白宫新闻秘书詹·普萨基（Jen Psaki）在午间发布会上对记者说，财政部长珍妮特·耶伦（Janet Yellen）和拜登政府其他经济部门正在监视此事，并补充说：“股市并不是衡量我们经济健康的唯一指标。”

Federal Reserve Chair Jerome H. Powell, speaking at a scheduled news conference, said he didn’t want to comment “on a particular company or day’s market activity.”

美联储主席杰罗姆·鲍威尔（Jerome H. Powell）在预定的新闻发布会上说，他不想对“某家公司或某天的市场活动发表评论”。

The stunning rise in AMC and GameStop shares has become one of Wall Street’s most-watched dramas, pitting an apparent army of retail investors against wealthy institutions. Some of the Reddit participants, who had organized in the message board r/wallstreetbets, claimed they were punishing Wall Street and “democratizing” trading.

AMC和GameStop股价的惊人涨幅已成为华尔街最受关注的戏剧之一，这使一支明显的散户投资者与富裕机构抗衡。在留言板r / wallstreetbets中组织的一些Reddit参与者声称，他们正在惩罚华尔街并“民主化”交易。

In a Twitter thread Wednesday, Reddit co-founder Alexis Ohanian said the mood on the message boards was one of retribution against big institutions.

Reddit联合创始人亚历克西斯·奥哈尼安（Alexis Ohanian）在周三的Twitter帖子中表示，留言板上的情绪是对大型机构的报应之一。

“This is an echo of what we’ve seen social media enable the public to challenge institutions for the last decade,” he said, adding, “And also — please — don’t invest money you can’t afford to lose, ESPECIALLY, in risky investments.”

他说：“这是过去十年我们看到社交媒体使公众向机构发起挑战的一种回响，而且-尤其是-不要投资无法承受的金钱，尤其是，进行风险投资。”

For weeks, users posting on wallstreetbets have rallied support for GameStop, which has been targeted by hedge fund short sellers as it struggles to adapt its bricks-and-mortar business for the age of e-commerce.

数周以来，张贴在华尔街上的用户都对GameStop进行了支持，GameStop一直是对冲基金卖空者的目标，因为它努力适应电子商务时代的实体业务。

The message board known as “wsb” describes itself as “like 4chan found a Bloomberg terminal,” in a reference to the anything-goes discussion site and the sophisticated software package used by financial professionals.

被称为“ wsb”的留言板将自己描述为“就像4chan在彭博终端上找到的一样”，指的是万事通讨论站点和金融专业人员使用的复杂软件包。

In daily posts, wsb’s 3.3 million members trade irreverent gibes, make liberal use of the f-word and cheer one another’s winning investments.

在日常工作中，wsb的330万会员交易不礼貌的礼节，自由使用f词，并为彼此的成功投资欢呼。

The Reddit posters began betting last year that GameStop’s stock price would rise, challenging Wall Street short sellers wagering the opposite. As the shares rose, the Wall Street traders have been forced to take massive losses.

Reddit海报从去年开始押注GameStop的股价将上涨，挑战华尔街做空者做出相反的选择。随着股价上涨，华尔街交易员被迫承受巨额亏损。

Among those rooting on the Reddit crowd this week was Elon Musk, the chief executive of Tesla, who tweeted “Gamestonk!!” with a link to the message board. (Musk has waged his own battles against short sellers, saying in 2018 that “what they do should be illegal.”)

特斯拉首席执行官埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）是本周在Reddit人群中扎根的人，他在推特上写道“ Gamestonk！”。带有指向留言板的链接。 （马斯克已与卖空者进行了自己的斗争，在2018年表示“他们的行为应该是非法的。”）

“GameStop has become a pyramid scheme,” said Michael Pachter, an analyst with Wedbush Securities. Investors buying the stock at $200 are convinced someone else will buy it from them at $250, he said. But that won’t last forever, he said.

Wedbush Securities的分析师Michael Pachter说：“ GameStop已经成为一个金字塔计划。”他说，投资者以200美元的价格购买股票时，确信其他人会以250美元的价格从他们手中购买股票。他说，但这不会永远持续下去。

“Pyramid schemes work as long as new investors believe there will be new investors behind them. When it’s clear nobody else is going to come in, they are less likely to participate.”

“金字塔计划有效，只要新投资者相信背后会有新的投资者。很明显，没有其他人会进来，他们参加的可能性较小。”

Over the past week, the Reddit board has started putting its support behind AMC, which recently announced it had raised nearly $1 billion to avoid bankruptcy. Its stock price also has jumped.

在过去的一周中，Reddit董事会已开始为AMC提供支持，AMC最近宣布已筹集了近10亿美元以避免破产。其股价也上涨了。

Other stocks that had fallen out of favor with institutional investors, such as BlackBerry and Bed Bath & Beyond, also are enjoying a second act.

其他受到机构投资者青睐的股票，例如黑莓（BlackBerry）和Bed Bath＆amp;除此之外，还享受着第二幕。

On the Reddit forum, which a company spokeswoman said was set to private by volunteer moderators later Wednesday, participants who claimed to be ordinary investors celebrated their collective efforts to take down big hedge funds. This week, Melvin Capital, a large hedge fund, was forced to seek a financial rescue from two other funds.

在公司女发言人说的Reddit论坛上，志愿者主持人将其定为私有，星期三晚些时候，自称普通投资者的与会者庆祝了他们为减少大型对冲基金而做出的集体努力。本周，大型对冲基金梅尔文资本（Melvin Capital）被迫从另外两家基金中寻求财务援助。

Citadel and Point72 Asset Management injected a combined $2.75 billion into the wounded short seller in return for a stake in future revenue. Melvin lost big after betting that GameStop’s stock would fall.

Citadel和Point72 Asset Management向受伤的卖空者注入了总计27.5亿美元，以换取未来收益的股份。梅尔文（Melvin）在押注GameStop的股票会下跌之后损失惨重。

John Stark, former director of the SEC’s office of Internet enforcement, said the commission almost certainly has taken the first steps to investigate the trading activity.

SEC美国互联网执法办公室前主任约翰·斯塔克（John Stark）说，委员会几乎可以肯定已经采取了调查贸易活动的第一步。

“I can’t imagine there isn’t an open investigation and probably a formal order to find out who’s on these message boards,” he said.

他说：“我无法想象没有公开的调查，也可能没有正式的命令来确定谁在这些留言板上。”

Some Reddit users seem to have the same idea. “Dear SEC. I may be guilty for a lot but if you give me a deal ill gladly rat out a bunch of other people,” user w8w8dont posted on Wednesday.

一些Reddit用户似乎有相同的想法。 “尊敬的SEC。用户w8w8dont在星期三说：“我可能很内gui，但是如果您给我一份协议，很不幸地招募了很多其他人，”

“It’s going to be hard for the SEC to find blatant manipulation, but they owe it to look,” he said. “The normal locker-room chatter you see in these chat rooms is probably not enough to get convictions. But are there bigger players here? We’ll see.”

他说：“对美国证券交易委员会来说，要找到公然的操纵将非常困难，但他们值得一看。” “您在这些聊天室中看到的普通更衣室聊天可能不足以使人信服。但是这里有更大的参与者吗？走着瞧。”

To establish manipulation that runs afoul of securities laws, Angel said regulators would need to prove traders engaged in “an intentional act to push a price away from its fundamental value to seek a profit.” In market parlance, this is typically known as a pump-and-dump scheme: Participants buy a stock; artificially inflate its price, say, by circulating rumors about the company; then sell for a profit.

为了建立违反证券法的操纵手段，安吉尔表示，监管机构将需要证明交易者从事了“故意使价格偏离其基本价值以谋取利润的行为”。用市场术语来说，这通常被称为“抽水甩卖”计划：参与者购买股票；例如，通过散布有关公司的谣言人为地抬高价格；然后卖出利润。

There is no evidence yet of such a strategy behind the surge in GameStop and other stocks, Angel notes. The SEC is likely to examine “who’s really moving the market. Is it a bunch of Robinhooders trading 10 shares each, or are there larger players out there engaging in a bigger pump-and-dump?” he said.

Angel指出，目前还没有证据表明GameStop和其他股票大涨的背后是这种策略。 SEC可能会检查“谁在真正推动市场发展”。是一群Robinhooders各自交易10股，还是那里有更大的参与者从事更大的抽水和抛售？”他说。

Customers of several retail brokerage firms — including TD Ameritrade, Robinhood Crypto, E-Trade, Charles Schwab, Fidelity Investments, Vanguard and Interactive Brokers — experienced service disruptions Wednesday morning, which many attributed to higher volume. The website Downdetector reported log-in and website issues for some of the firms, as well as Reddit, starting after 9 a.m. Eastern, with most issues resolved within an hour.

几家零售经纪公司（包括TD Ameritrade，Robinhood Crypto，E-Trade，Charles Schwab，Fidelity Investments，Vanguard和Interactive Brokers）的客户在周三上午经历了服务中断，其中许多原因是服务量增加。网站Downdetector报告了东部时间上午9点后开始的一些公司以及Reddit的登录和网站问题，大多数问题在一小时内得到解决。
