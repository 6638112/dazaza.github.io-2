Listenlater.fm – A Read-It-Later service for your ears
Listenlater.fm –耳后即读服务

2020-12-08 02:01:06

ListenLater.fm generates a personalized podcast feed for you to listen to. You let it know about articles you'd like to read later. When you do, it adds a spoken version of the article to your feed for you to listen to whenever your ears are free. I recommend you try listening to this page with the audio player below to see how you like it.

ListenLater.fm生成个性化的播客供稿，供您收听。您让它知道您以后想阅读的文章。当您这样做时，它将在您的提要中添加文章的语音版本，以便您随时随地收听。我建议您尝试使用下面的音频播放器收听此页面，以了解您的喜好。

I use ListenLater.fm every day, and want to share it with more people. I used to skim the headlines of a lot of essays online. I wanted to read them, but I always had an excuse not to. When I'd use other read-it-later services, articles would pile up because I didn't make time to sit down and read.

我每天都使用ListenLater.fm，并希望与更多人分享。我以前经常在网上浏览很多文章的标题。我想读它们，但我总是有一个借口不读。当我使用其他稍后阅读服务时，文章会堆积如山，因为我没有时间坐下来阅读。

ListenLater.fm is different. With my previous attempts at using read-it-later services, I'd never make time to read through my queue. I've already built up the habit of checking my podcast app for a new podcast, so I never forget about it.

ListenLater.fm是不同的。在我先前尝试使用后读服务的过程中，我从未花时间阅读我的队列。我已经养成了检查我的播客应用程序是否有新播客的习惯，因此我永远不会忘记它。

Podcasts are a big part of my life — they let me listen to thoughtful, interesting content during my commute and while I'm doing chores. ListenLater.fm doesn't replace podcasts, but it augments the podcast experience, adding another set of things to listen to in your podcast player.

播客是我一生的重要组成部分-在我上下班途中和做家务的过程中，播客让我听了周到，有趣的内容。 ListenLater.fm不会取代播客，但会增加播客体验，并在播客播放器中添加另一组要收听的内容。

There are so many amazing long form articles I never would have read if it weren't for ListenLater.

如果没有ListenLater，那么我将永远不会读到太多令人惊奇的长篇文章。

Adding new things to listen to is simple. When you're on a computer, the easiest way to add new articles is through our browser extensions. When you're on your phone and want to add an article, it's simple to share an article via email. You can also use our bookmarklet to add new articles.

添加新的要听的东西很简单。当您使用计算机时，添加新文章的最简单方法是通过我们的浏览器扩展程序。当您在手机上想要添加文章时，通过电子邮件共享文章很简单。您也可以使用我们的书签添加新文章。

Listening's a breeze. You get your own custom podcast feed, that you can subscribe to in any podcast app. Anything you send to ListenLater.fm will be kept there for you to listen to on your own time.

轻轻松松听。您将获得自己的自定义播客Feed，可以在任何播客应用中进行订阅。您发送到ListenLater.fm的所有内容都将保留在那里，供您自己听。

I made ListenLater.fm to scratch my own itch, and found it was extremely useful. I hope you do too.

我制作了ListenLater.fm来抓痒，发现它非常有用。 我希望你也这样做。
