Apple's Fitness Video Service That Competes With Peloton Is Cheaper and Just As Good
与Peloton竞争的Apple健身视频服务更便宜，更出色

2020-12-15 07:31:06

Apple's subscription fitness app, Fitness+, launches Monday. I've been using it for the past several days and I think it offers a nice variety of workouts that people will like. You need an Apple Watch to take the prerecorded exercise classes, which are available on iPhones, iPads and the Apple TV.

苹果的订阅健身应用Fitness +将于周一启动。我过去几天一直在使用它，我认为它提供了人们喜欢的多种锻炼方法。您需要使用Apple Watch来进行预先录制的锻炼课程，该课程可在iPhone，iPad和Apple TV上使用。

It's a smart way for Apple to make the Apple Watch even stickier. If people get really into the fitness classes, like I have, it will be yet another way Apple keeps people locked in to its ecosystem of products. Why buy another phone, tablet or watch if you really like Fitness+? It also comes at a great time, when people aren't in gyms and are at home looking for ways to exercise.

对于苹果公司来说，这是使Apple Watch更具粘性的明智之举。如果人们像我一样真正参加健身课程，那将是苹果让人们专注于其产品生态系统的另一种方式。如果您真的喜欢Fitness +，为什么还要购买另一部手机，平板电脑或手表？当人们不去体育馆而在家中寻找锻炼方法时，这也是一个绝佳的时机。

Like other fitness apps, including Peloton's, which starts at $12.99 a month for classes that don't need the company's connected spin bike, you don't need anything to use it. But, you'll get more out of it if you have any indoor cycle, treadmill, rowing machine or free weights, since some of the classes require equipment. But you don't need anything special. I've been riding a hand-me-down exercise bike, for example.

像其他健身应用程序一样，包括Peloton健身车，起价为每月12.99美元，不需要使用公司的旋转自行车的班级，您不需要任何使用。但是，如果您有任何室内自行车，跑步机，划船机或举重器械，您会从中得到更多的收益，因为某些课程需要装备。但是您不需要任何特别的东西。例如，我一直在骑着手动健身车。

Fitness+ costs $9.99 a month or $79.99 a year. It's also part of the Premier Apple One plan, which costs $29.95 per month, and includes other Apple products like Apple Music, Apple TV+ and extra iCloud storage bundled together at a discount.

Fitness +每月收费9.99美元，或每年收费79.99美元。这也是Premier Apple One计划的一部分，该计划每月收费29.95美元，其中包括其他Apple产品，例如Apple Music，Apple TV +和额外的iCloud存储（打折后）。

I started taking my health a lot more seriously a few months ago and have cut a bunch of weight. A great deal of that was through diet, but I've also been addicted to Peloton's iPad app, which I use on a regular indoor bike and not a real Peloton and that, like Fitness+, has a variety of noncycling workouts.

几个月前，我开始更加认真地对待自己的健康，并减轻了体重。其中很大一部分是通过饮食，但我也沉迷于Peloton的iPad应用程序，我在普通的室内自行车上使用它，而不是真正的Peloton，它像Fitness +一样具有多种功能非循环锻炼。

The Peloton instructors are really encouraging. (Shout out to Alex Toussaint, Emma Lovewell and Kendall Toole.) So, I was worried that I wouldn't find that kind of coaching in Fitness+. If the coaches weren't good, I'd go back to Peloton.

Peloton的讲师真的令人鼓舞。 （向Alex Toussaint，Emma Lovewell和Kendall Toole大喊大叫。）因此，我担心自己在Fitness +中找不到这种教练。如果教练不好，我就回佩洛顿。

But, after I took about eight cycling classes on Fitness+, I started to get used to and enjoy the different Apple Fitness+ instructors. My favorites so far are the cycling instructors Emily Fayette, Bakari Williams and Tyrell Desean, but I haven't taken a class with all the 21 instructors for a variety of workouts yet. There are a lot to choose from with classes like high-intensity interval training (HIIT), spin, yoga, treadmill, rowing, strength and dance.

但是，当我在Fitness +上参加了大约八次自行车课程后，我开始习惯并享受不同的Apple Fitness +教练。到目前为止，我最喜欢的是自行车教练Emily Fayette，Bakari Williams和Tyrell Desean，但是我还没有和21位教练一起上过各种锻炼班。高强度间歇训练（HIIT），旋转，瑜伽，跑步机，划船，力量和舞蹈等课程有很多选择。

I love that Fitness+ syncs seamlessly with the Apple Watch. You just pick the workout you want and then hit start. Your heart rate, the time elapsed (or time left if you want) and your Apple Watch Fitness rings (red for calories, green for exercise minutes and blue for standing minutes) appear on the screen and close as you continue to work out. Any Apple Watch owner knows how addictive it can be to try to close all of your rings every day, and I like that the app puts this right on the screen to encourage me to close mine.

我喜欢Fitness +与Apple Watch无缝同步。您只需选择所需的锻炼，然后单击开始即可。屏幕上会显示您的心律，经过的时间（或您想剩余的时间）和Apple Watch Fitness铃声（红色代表卡路里，绿色代表运动时间，蓝色代表站立时间），并在您继续锻炼时关闭。任何Apple Watch拥有者都知道每天尝试关闭所有戒指有多令人上瘾，我喜欢该应用程序将此功能显示在屏幕上，以鼓励我关闭我的手表。

You can stream workouts to your iPad, iPhone or Apple TV, or download them for when you don't have an internet connection. I preferred cycling with an iPad, but liked that I could come upstairs and do a cooldown or a quick yoga class while watching on the Apple TV. Also, as long as you own an Apple Watch and subscribe to Fitness+, you can workout on any Apple TV with Fitness+ installed. So, if you're staying in a hotel that has one, or more likely an Airbnb, it'll see your watch nearby and let you take classes even if the owner of the Apple TV doesn't pay for Fitness+. This will be fun when we can all travel again.

您可以将锻炼内容流式传输到iPad，iPhone或Apple TV，或者在没有互联网连接时下载锻炼内容。我更喜欢使用iPad骑自行车，但喜欢在苹果电视上看电视时可以上楼去做冷静或快速瑜伽课。另外，只要您拥有Apple Watch并订阅Fitness +，就可以在安装了Fitness +的任何Apple TV上锻炼。因此，如果您住在一家拥有Airbnb或更可能是Airbnb的酒店，即使Apple TV的所有者不付款，它也会在附近看到您的手表并让您上课适用于Fitness +。当我们都可以再次旅行时，这将很有趣。

It's also easy to find classes on the Apple TV, iPad or iPhone. The app recommends classes based on your previous workouts (including in any app that saves data to Apple Health, like Peloton). You can filter by time (10 minutes up to 45 minutes), by instructor, by exercise or by type of music. And, I like that you can automatically save music or play it back inside the Apple Music app. You don't need to be a subscriber to Apple Music to hear songs in Fitness+, however.

在Apple TV，iPad或iPhone上也很容易找到课程。该应用程序会根据您以前的锻炼来推荐课程（包括在任何将数据保存到Apple Health的应用程序中，例如Peloton）。您可以按时间（10分钟到45分钟），教练，运动或音乐类型进行过滤。而且，我喜欢您可以自动保存音乐或在Apple Music应用程序中播放音乐。但是，您无需成为Apple Music的订阅者即可收听Fitness +中的歌曲。

The workouts are great. The 45-minute bike rides definitely left me sweaty and showed that I burned about 450 to 500 calories, about what I get in the same time from the Peloton app. I'm not a huge yoga guy, but I liked the 10-minute intro class and plan to try to take some of the longer ones. I really need to work on my flexibility. A 10-minute strength class I snuck in after a bike ride really worked out my thighs. There's plenty to choose from already and more classes will be added each Monday. You'll find enough to do.

锻炼很棒。 45分钟的自行车旅行肯定让我满头大汗，显示我燃烧了大约450至500卡路里的热量，这与我同时从Peloton应用程序获得的热量差不多。我不是一个瑜伽大佬，但我喜欢10分钟的入门班，并计划尝试一些较长的入门班。我确实需要提高灵活性。骑完自行车后，我偷偷参加了10分钟的力量训练，确实锻炼了我的大腿。已经有很多可供选择的内容，每个星期一将增加更多的课程。您会发现有足够的事情要做。

Finally, and maybe the most important part, is Apple Fitness does a great job of encouraging all fitness levels. There are additional instructors during each workout showing recommended tweaks or changes you might make if you're not in perfect shape like the trainers. In the cycling workouts, I was encouraged to push myself but not to set a specific level of resistance or cadence, which I liked better than on Peloton where I first felt like I wasn't working out as hard if I couldn't maintain the harder resistance levels. And speaking of inclusion, the classes all start and end with a bit of sign language for people who are hard of hearing. Nice touch.

最后，也许是最重要的部分是Apple Fitness在鼓励所有健身水平方面做得很好。在每次锻炼期间，还会有其他教练显示建议的调整或更改，如果您不像教练那样处于完美身材，则可能会进行调整。在骑自行车锻炼中，我受到鼓舞，但不要设置特定的阻力或节奏水平，这比在Peloton上更喜欢。在Peloton上，我最初觉得自己如果不做就不那么努力；保持较硬的阻力水平。说到包容性，所有课程的开头和结尾都是针对听不清的人的手语。很好

There are a couple of things I still prefer from the Peloton app. Apple's Fitness+ instructors are great, but I want to learn more about them and their individual personalities.

我仍然更喜欢Peloton应用中的几件事。 Apple的Fitness +教练很棒，但我想了解更多有关他们及其个性的信息。

I like that Peloton instructor Toussaint serves as a sort of drill sergeant-style instructor. No excuses for not doing your best. This extends even into the music. You'll hear explicit language in Peloton. (Even the instructors curse occasionally.) You don't get that in Fitness+. Maybe it's weird, but I just don't get as amped working to a hip-hop playlist with the curse words missing.

我喜欢佩洛顿（Peloton）教练图桑（Toussaint）担任演习中士风格的教练。没有任何理由不尽全力。这甚至延伸到音乐中。您会在Peloton中听到露骨的语言。 （即使是偶尔的咒骂，也是如此。）您不能在Fitness +中得到它。也许很奇怪，但我只是不喜欢那些缺少诅咒字眼的嘻哈音乐播放列表。

And I like that Peloton instructors chat more about their lives and experiences. Peloton's Lovewell talks about being raised on Martha's Vineyard. Massachusetts. Her dad was a musician. She talks about her favorite new tunes with custom workout playlists. About how she's restoring a new home. I don't yet know a whole lot about Fitness+ instructors, aside from small profiles posted online, but I'm told that should start to come through more and I'm looking forward to more of that.

我喜欢佩洛顿（Peloton）讲师更多地谈论他们的生活和经历。佩洛顿（Peloton）的洛夫威尔（Lovewell）谈到要在玛莎（Martha）的葡萄园里长大。马萨诸塞州。她的父亲是一位音乐家。她通过自定义锻炼播放列表谈论自己喜欢的新曲调。关于她如何恢复新房。除了在线发布的个人资料外，我对健身+教练还不了解很多，但我告诉我应该开始更多，我也期待更多。

Peloton also offers live classes while Fitness+ does not. And if you don't take the live class, Peloton still shows you who's taking the prerecorded class with you at the same time, which I like. Fitness+ lets you compete with everyone else who's taken a class through "burn bar" that appears on the screen and tells you where you stand in the pack. It's fun in cycling classes, but I wish there were more social features that showed me who else was riding in real time, or how my friends did in a specific class. It doesn't show any user names (that's by design as a privacy feature) and instructors don't shout out encouragements for hitting milestones like a 100th ride or workout. I just love the community feel in Peloton and themed classes like the holiday and Thanksgiving rides where instructors might even dress up or play holiday music. I hope that comes to Fitness+ too.

佩洛顿还提供现场课程，而Fitness +则不提供。而且，如果您不参加现场授课，Peloton仍会向您显示谁同时与您一起进行了预录课。 Fitness +可让您与其他通过“燃烧吧”学习课程的人竞争。出现在屏幕上，并告诉您站在背包中的位置。骑自行车上课很有趣，但我希望有更多的社交功能可以实时显示谁在骑车，或者我的朋友在特定课程中的表现。它不会显示任何用户名（在设计上是作为隐私保护功能设计的），而讲师则不会大声疾呼达到第100次骑行或锻炼等里程碑。我只是喜欢在Peloton和主题课程（如假期和感恩节游乐设施）中的社区感觉，在这些课程中讲师甚至可能会打扮或播放假期音乐。我也希望这也适用于Fitness +。

Also, while the Apple Watch has a bunch of digital badges and awards you can win for workout streaks, I wish there were some dedicated to Fitness+. So, maybe I'd get badges for working out with Fitness+ in a week. Or a badge for doing specific holiday workouts if and when those ever appear.

此外，尽管Apple Watch拥有大量的数字徽章和奖章，您可以通过锻炼条纹赢得大奖，但我希望能有一些专门用于Fitness +。因此，也许我会在一周内获得使用Fitness +锻炼的徽章。或用于进行特定假期锻炼的徽章（如果有的话，何时出现）。

Finally, it's obvious, but you need Apple devices to use Fitness+. It doesn't run on Android or TV boxes like Roku, and you have to have an Apple Watch. So, Peloton is the way to go if you want an app that runs on different kinds of TV boxes, tablets, phones and fitness trackers.

最后，这很明显，但是您需要Apple设备才能使用Fitness +。它无法在Android或Roku等电视盒上运行，并且您必须拥有Apple Watch。因此，如果您想要在不同类型的电视盒，平板电脑，手机和健身追踪器上运行的应用程序，Peloton是必经之路。

This is the part of a review is where I'd normally say if you should buy something or not, but both Fitness+ and the Peloton app come with free one-month trials. And anyone who bought a new Apple Watch this year gets a three-month trial. So, really, you should try both and see which one you like better. I'm pretty torn right now.

这是评论的一部分，我通常会说您是否应该购买某些东西，但是Fitness +和Peloton应用程序都提供免费的1个月试用版。今年购买新Apple Watch的任何人都将获得为期三个月的试用期。因此，实际上，您应该同时尝试两者，看看您更喜欢哪一个。我现在快被撕裂了。

I love how well Fitness+ syncs and works across devices, how well it works with the Apple Watch and that it's really inviting for all fitness levels. You'll end Peloton and Fitness+ classes feeling sweaty and happy.

我喜欢Fitness +在各种设备上同步和工作的良好程度，以及与Apple Watch配合使用的良好程度，并真的吸引所有健身水平的人。您将结束Peloton和Fitness +班的出汗和快乐。

Considering I used to pay more than $150 a month for a gym before the coronavirus pandemic, I'm probably just going to keep using both apps. Apple Fitness+ is included in the Premier family plan I already pay for, and I like being able to switch up instructors and workout styles between the apps.

考虑到我以前在冠状病毒大流行之前每月要花150多美元去健身房，所以我可能会继续使用这两个应用程序。我已经付费的Premier Family计划中包括Apple Fitness +，我喜欢能够在应用程序之间切换教练和锻炼方式。

The way I see it, I'm just getting even more classes if I subscribe to both. Plus, this way I'm not quitting on the Peloton instructors I love so much, or missing the fun special classes and workout streaks, and I can continue getting in shape while continuing to explore Fitness+.

我的看法是，如果我同时订阅这两个课程，我将获得更多的课程。 此外，这样一来，我就不会放弃我非常喜欢的Peloton教练，也不会错过有趣的特殊课程和锻炼条纹，在继续探索Fitness +的同时，我可以继续保持健康。
