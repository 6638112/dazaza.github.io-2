Laptop Review: ThinkPad X1 Extreme (Gen 2)
笔记本评论：ThinkPad X1 Extreme（Gen 2）

2021-06-06 06:45:20

For many of my school and university years, I used and liked my ThinkPad X200ultraportable laptop. But now that these years are long gone, I realized myuse-case for laptops had changed: instead of carrying my laptop with me everyday, I am now only bringing it on occasion, for example when I travel toconferences, visit friends, or do volunteer work.

对于许多我的学校和大学年来，我使用并喜欢我的ThinkPad X200ultraportable笔记本电脑。但是现在这些年来，我意识到笔记本电脑的案例已经改变了工作。

With each of these devices, I have felt limited by the lack of connectors andslim compute power that comes with the Ultrabook brand, even after years oftechnical progress.

通过这些设备中的每一个，我感到缺乏连接器缺乏连接器的限制，即使在超级技术进展之后也带来了Ultrabook品牌的Compute Power。

More compute power is nice to be able to work on projects with larger data sets,for example debiman (scanning and converting all manpages in Debian), or distri(building Linux packages).

更加计算权力很好能够在具有较大数据集的项目上工作，例如Debiman（扫描和转换Debian中的所有编码）或Distri（构建Linux包）。

More peripheral options such as USB ports are nice when connecting a keyboard,trackball, USB-to-serial adapter, etc., to work on a micro controller orRaspberry Pi project, for example.

例如，在连接键盘，轨迹球，USB到串行适配器等时，诸如USB端口的更多外设选项可以在微控制器上工作。

So, I was ready to switch from the heaviest Ultrabooks to the lightest of the“mobile workstation” category, when I stumbled upon Lenovo’s ThinkPad X1 Extreme(Gen 2), and it piqued my curiosity.

所以，当我偶然发现联想的ThinkPad X1 Extreme（Gen 2）时，我已准备好从最沉重的超薄到最轻的“移动工作站”类别的最轻，并激起了我的好奇心。

Let me start by going into the key peripherals of a laptop: keyboard, touchpadand screen. I will talk about these independently from the remaining hardwarebecause they define the experience of using the computer.

让我开始进入笔记本电脑的关键外设：键盘，触摸板屏幕。我将独立地从剩余的硬件中讨论这些问题，因为它们定义了使用计算机的体验。

After having used the Dell XPS 9360 for a few years, I can confidently say thatthe keyboard of the ThinkPads is definitely much better, and in a noticeableway.

在几年中使用了戴尔XPS 9360后，我可以自信地说Thinkpad的键盘绝对更好，并且在一个明显的道路中。

It’s not that the Dell keyboards are bad. But comparing the Dell and ThinkPadside-by-side makes it really clear that the ThinkPad keyboards are the bestnotebook keyboards.

这不是戴尔键盘很糟糕。但是，比较戴尔和ThinkPadde-By-antive使ThinkPad键盘是BestnoteBook键盘的真实明确。

On the ThinkPad keyboard, every key press lands exactly as I imagine. Never do Ineed to hit a key twice because I didn’t press it just-right, and never do Inotice additional ghost key presses.

在ThinkPad键盘上，每个关键按下都像我想象的那样。永远不要做两次击中钥匙，因为我没有按下它，而且永远不会做额外的幽灵钥匙压力机。

Even though I connect my external Kinesis Advantage keyboard when doing longerstretches of work, the quality of the built-in keyboard matters: a good keyboardenables using the laptop on the couch.

尽管我在执行工作时连接我的外部Kinesis Advantage键盘时，内置键盘的质量很重要：使用沙发上的笔记本电脑的良好键盘。

Unfortunately, while the keyboard is great, I can’t say the same about thetouchpad. I mean, it’s not terrible, but it’s also not good by any stretch.

不幸的是，键盘很棒时，我不能对TheTouchPad说同样的话。我的意思是，它是不可思议的，但任何伸展也不好。

This seems to be the status quo with PC touchpads for decades. It really blowsmy mind that Apple’s touchpads are consistently so much better!

这似乎是几十年来PC触摸板的现状。它真的令人沮丧地思考苹果的触摸板始终如一！

My only hope is that Bill Harding(GitClear), who is working on improvingthe Linux touchpad experience, will eventually find a magic software tweak orsomething…

我唯一的希望是，比尔·哈丁（略微），正在努力改善Linux触摸板经验，最终会找到一个魔术软件调整orsomething ...

I have high demands regarding displays: since 2013, every device of mine has ahi-dpi display.

我对显示器有很高的要求：自2013年以来，我的每个设备都有AHI-DPI显示。

The industry hasn’t improved displays across the board as fast as I’d like, sonon-hi-dpi displays are still quite common. The silver lining is that it makeslaptop selection a little easier for me: anything without a decent display I candiscard right away.

当我想的情况下，该行业并没有改进整个板上的显示器，Sonon-Hi-DPI显示器仍然很常见。银色衬里是它对我来说，它可以更轻松地选择：没有体面展示我的坎迪德。

I’m glad to report that the 4K display in the ThinkPad X1 Extreme with its3840x2160 pixels is sharp, bright, and generally has good viewing angles.

我很高兴地报告认为ThinkPad X1 Extreme中的4K显示器与其3840x2160像素锐利，明亮，并且通常具有良好的观察角度。

It’s also a touchscreen, which I don’t strictly need, but it’s nice to use itfrom time to time.

它也是一个触摸屏，我不严格需要，但是很高兴使用它时间。

I use the display in 200% scaling mode, i.e. I set Xft.dpi: 192. See also HiDPI in ArchWiki.

我在200％缩放模式下使用显示器，即我设置Xft.dpi：192。另请参见Archwiki的HIDPI。

Tip: In case your brightness control keys don’t work, check if the requiredpatches have not beenapplied in your environment yet.

提示：如果您的亮度控制键不起作用，请检查RegressPatches是否尚未在环境中脱离。

Another big plus of the ThinkPad series over other laptop series is theavailability of the official Hardware Maintenance Manual: you can put “ThinkPadX1 Extreme Gen 2 Hardware Maintenance Manual” into Google and will find p1_gen2_x1extreme_hmm_v1.pdfas the first hit. This manual describes in detail how to repair or upgrade yourdevice if you want to (or have to) do it yourself.

ThinkPad系列在其他笔记本电脑系列中的另一个大量大量的是官方硬件维护手册：您可以将“ThinkPadx1 Extele Gen 2硬件维护手册”放入Google中，并会发现P1_Gen2_X1Extreme_hmm_v1.pdfas首次命中。本手册详细介绍了如何修复或升级您的Device，如果您愿意（或必须）自己做。

The built-in Intel AX200 WiFi interface works fine, provided you have anew-enough linux-firmware package and kernel version installed.

提供内置的Intel AX200 WiFi接口工作正常，只要您拥有足够重新的Linux-Firmware包和内核版本。

I had trouble with Linux 5.6.0, and Linux 5.6.5 fixed it. Luckily, at the timeof writing, Linux 5.11 is the most recent release, so most distributions shouldbe recent enough for things to just work.

我对Linux 5.6.0遇到了麻烦，Linux 5.6.5修复了它。幸运的是，在写作时，Linux 5.11是最近的发布，因此大多数分布应该足够努力工作。

The WiFi card reaches almost the same download speed as the most modern WiFidevice I can test: a MacBook Air M1. Both are connected to my UniFiUAP-AC-HD access point.

WiFi卡几乎达到与最现代的下载速度相同的下载速度，我可以测试：MacBook Air M1。两者都连接到我的unifiuap-ac-hd接入点。

I had hoped that after many years of laptops containing Intel/nVidia hybridgraphics, this setup would largely work, but was disappointed.

我希望经过多年的含有英特尔/ NVIDIA杂交学的笔记本电脑，这个设置将在很大程度上工作，但很失望。

Both the proprietary nVidia driver and the nouveau driver would not workreliably for me. I ran into kernel error messages and hard-freezes, with evenSSH sessions to the machine breaking.

专有的NVIDIA司机和Nouveau驱动程序都不会为我工作。我遇到了内核错误消息和硬冻结，带有Evenssh Sessions到机器打破。

Obviously this isn’t a great setup — I would prefer to be able to actually usethe GPU. If you have any tips or a better experience, please let me know.

显然这不是一个很好的设置 - 我宁愿能够实际上雇用GPU。如果您有任何提示或更好的体验，请告诉我。

Also note that the HDMI port will be unusable if you go this route, as the HDMIport is connected to the nVidia GPU only.

另请注意，如果您走这条路线，HDMI端口将无法使用，因为HDMIPORT仅连接到NVIDIA GPU。

The 80 Wh battery lasts between 5 to 6 hours for me, without any extra powersaving tuning beyond what the Linux distribution Fedora 33 comes with bydefault.

80 WH电池持续5到6个小时，没有任何额外的PowerAving调整，超出了Linux发行版Fedora 33附带的ByDefault。

This is good enough for using the laptop away from a power socket from time totime, which matches my expectation for this kind of mobile workstation.

这足以使笔记本电脑远离时间串的电源插座，这与我对这种移动工作站的期望相匹配。

Linux support is generally good on this machine! Yes, I provide a few pointersin this article regarding problems, patches and old software versions. But, ifyou use a newer Linux distribution, all of these fixes are included and thingsjust work out of the box. I tested with Fedora 33.

Linux支持通常很好！是的，我提供了一些关于问题，修补程序和旧软件版本的文章。但是，如果您使用较新的Linux发行版，所有这些修复程序都包含在内，并在框中开发件事。我用Fedora 33测试了。

For a few months, I was using this laptop exclusively with my research Linuxdistribution distri, so even if you just track upstreamsoftware closely, the machine works well.

几个月后，我正在使用这款笔记本电脑，专门使用我的研究LinuxDistribution Distri，因此即使您只是仔细追踪上游的软件，机器也很好。

Lenovo partnered with the Linux Vendor Firmware Service Project(LVFS), which means that through fwupd, ThinkPad laptopssuch as this X1 Extreme can easily receive firmware updates!

联想与Linux供应商固件服务项目（LVFS）合作，这意味着通过FWUPD，ThinkPad LopyopsSuch，因为这个X1 Extreme可以很容易地接收固件更新！

This is a huge improvement in comparison to earlier ThinkPad models, where youhad to jump through hoops with Windows-only software, or CD images that youneeded to boot just right.

与早期的ThinkPad模型相比，这是一个巨大的改进，其中您使用Windows的软件跳过跳跃，或者您必须正确启动的CD图像。

If your laptop has a very old firmware version (before 1.30), you might beaffected by the skippingkeystrokesissues. You can check using the always-handy lshw(1)tool.

如果您的笔记本电脑有一个非常古老的固件版本（1.30之前），您可能会被SkippingKeyBrokeSiss Befffected Befffect。您可以使用始终如一的LSHW（1）工具来检查。

You can google for CPU benchmarks and comparisons yourself, and those likely aremore scientific and carefully done than I have time for.

您可以谷歌为CPU基准测试和比较，以及这些可能的人科学和仔细完成，而不是我的时间。

A warm apt-cacher-ng cache was present, i.e. network download was not part of the test.

存在温暖的APT-Cacher-NG缓存，即网络下载不是测试的一部分。

This reaffirms my impression that even high-end laptop hardware just cannot beata workstation setup (which has more space and better thermals), but it comesclose enough to be useful.

这重申了我的印象，即使高端的笔记本电脑硬件也不能击败工作站设置（具有更多空间和更好的热量），但它既足够有用。

The ergonomics of the device really are great. It is a pleasure to type on afirst-class, full-size ThinkPad keyboard. The screen has good quality and ahigh resolution.

设备的人体工程学真的很棒。很高兴地键入AFIRST-Class，全尺寸ThinkPad键盘。屏幕具有良好的质量和高分辨率。

Notably, all of these could be improved by better power saving, so perhaps it’sjust a matter of time until Linux kernel developers land some improvements…? :)

值得注意的是，所有这些都可以通过更好的省电来提高，因此它可能会这样做，直到Linux内核开发人员降落了一些改进......？ :)
