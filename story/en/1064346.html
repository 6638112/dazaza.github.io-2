NHS plan to share GP patient data in England delayed to September
NHS计划在英格兰分享GP患者数据推迟到9月

2021-06-08 22:07:26

Plans to allow an NHS system to extract patient data from doctors’ surgeries in England have been delayed amid concerns around privacy.

计划允许NHS系统从英格兰医生的手术中提取患者数据已被推迟在隐私问题中。

The new GP data sharing system was due to start in July but has been pushed back to 1 September, a health minister told MPs.

新的GP数据共享系统是由于7月份开始，但已被推回9月1日，卫生部长告诉国会议。

Jo Churchill, the public health minister, said the data programme would still go ahead this year. The coming weeks would see the plan “strengthened” and work undertaken to “ensure that data is accessed securely”, she said.

公共卫生部长Jo丘吉尔表示，数据计划仍将今年迈进。未来几周将看到“加强”计划和工作的计划“确保数据被安全地访问”。

The UK information commissioner welcomed the delay to the launch. Elizabeth Denham said there remained “considerable confusion regarding the scope and nature” of the programme.

英国信息专员欢迎延迟发布。伊丽莎白登德姆表示，该计划的范围和性质的相当困惑。

The scheme will collect information on people’s treatments, referrals and appointments over the past 10 years, alongside other data from medical records held on GPs’ systems. The collected data is coded by NHS Digital to protect patient identities.

该计划将在过去10年中收集有关人民治疗，转介和约会的信息，以及GPS系统上持有的医疗记录的其他数据。收集的数据由NHS数字进行编码以保护患者身份。

Churchill told the House of Commons: “Data saves lives, it’s as simple as that. We’ve seen that in the pandemic, it’s one of the lessons of the vaccine rollout.

丘吉尔告诉公众的房子：“数据拯救生命，这就像那样简单。我们在大流行中看到过，它是疫苗推出的教训之一。

“The GP data programme will strengthen this system and save lives and patient data is, of course, owned by the patient.

“GP数据计划将加强该系统，并将生命和患者数据当然由患者拥有。

“And we are absolutely determined to take people with us on this journey. We have therefore decided we will proceed with the important programme but we will take some extra time.

“我们绝对决心在这段旅程中与我们带走。因此，我们决定继续进行重要计划，但我们将花费一些额外的时间。

“As we have conversed with stakeholders over the past couple of days, the implementation date will now be on September 1 and we will use this time to talk to patients, doctors, health charities and others to strengthen the plan, build a trusted research environment and ensure that data is accessed securely.

“随着过去几天的利益攸关方交谈，实施日期现在将于9月1日，我们将使用这次与患者，医生，卫生慈善机构和其他人交谈，以加强该计划，建立一个值得信赖的研究环境并确保安全访问数据。

“This agenda is so important, because we all know in here, data saves lives.”

“这个议程是如此重要，因为我们都知道在这里，数据拯救了生命。”

Simon Bolton, the interim chief executive of NHS Digital, said: “The vaccine rollout could not have been delivered without effective use of data to ensure it reached the whole population. We are absolutely determined to take people with us on this mission. We take our responsibility to safeguard the data we hold incredibly seriously.

Insim首席执行官Simon Bolton Digital表示：“无需有效地使用数据，无法提供疫苗卷展览，以确保其达到整个人口。我们绝对决心在这项任务中与我们联系。我们承担责任保护我们令人难以置信的数据。

“We intend to use the next two months to speak with patients, doctors, health charities and others to strengthen the plan even further.”

“我们打算使用未来两个月与患者，医生，健康慈善机构和其他人交谈，以进一步加强计划。”

The programme, General Practice Data for Planning and Research (GPDPR), would replace a 10-year-old system that “needs to be replaced”, NHS Digital said.

NHS Digital表示，该计划是规划和研究（GPDPR）的一般实践数据（GPDPR）将取代一个10岁的系统“需要更换”。

The system would not collect entire GP records and all the data would be pseudonymised before it left the GP surgery to ensure patients could not be directly identified.

该系统不会收集整个GP记录，并且在离开GP手术之前，所有数据都将是假期的，以确保患者无法直接识别。

Information would be accessed by organisations “which will legitimately use the data for healthcare planning and research purposes”, and all requests would be subject to independent oversight and scrutiny, NHS Digital said. Patients would be able to opt out.

NHS Digital表示，组织“将合法地利用医疗保健规划和研究目的的数据来访问”这将获得“这将是”这将合法地使用该数据的资料“，所有要求将受到独立监督和审查。患者能够选择退出。

Denham said: “I welcome the decision to delay the launch of the General Practice Data for Planning and Research data collection scheme.

丹恩说：“我欢迎决定推迟推出规划和研究数据收集方案的一般实践数据。

“The appropriate use of health data is an important part of health and care research and planning in England, and better sharing of health data could offer substantial benefits.

“适当使用健康数据是英格兰健康和护理研究和规划的重要组成部分，更好地分享健康数据可以提供大量利益。

“However, it is clear that there remains considerable confusion regarding the scope and nature of the GPDPR, among both healthcare practitioners and the general public. This includes how data protection rights can be exercised in practice.

“但是，很明显，医疗保健从业者和公众都仍然有关GPDPR的范围和性质仍然相当困惑。这包括如何在实践中行使数据保护权。

“It is sensible for NHS Digital to take more time to engage with its stakeholders, and consider the feedback it is receiving about its plans.”

“数字NHS数字是富有更多时间才能与其利益相关者进行融合，并考虑其接受其计划的反馈。”

She continued: “The success of any project will rely on people trusting and having confidence in how their personal data will be used.”

她继续说：“任何项目的成功都将依靠人们信任并对其个人数据的使用方式有信心。”
