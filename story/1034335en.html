苹果发布第一款苹果Mac硅SoC M1，采用5纳米制程、16B晶体管、8核CPU、最高8核GPU和16核神经引擎

2020-11-11 02:31:47

Apple has introduced the new M1 chip that will power its new generation of Arm-based Macs. It’s a 5nm processor, just like the A14 Bionic powering its latest iPhones.

苹果公司推出了新的M1芯片，这将为其基于ARM的新一代Mac电脑提供动力。这是一个5纳米的处理器，就像为最新iPhone提供动力的A14仿生处理器一样。

Apple says the new processor will focus on power efficiency. It has an eight-core CPU, which Apple says offers the world’s best performance per watt of an CPU. Apple says it delivers the same peak performance as a typical laptop CPU at a quarter of the power draw. It says this has four of the world’s fastest CPUs cores, paired with four high-efficiency cores.

苹果公司表示，新处理器将专注于能效。它有一个8核的CPU，苹果公司说这是世界上每瓦特CPU性能最好的。苹果公司表示，它的峰值性能相当于普通笔记本电脑CPU的四分之一功耗。该公司表示，这款机型拥有世界上速度最快的四个CPU核心，再配上四个高效率的核心。

It pairs this with up to an eight-core GPU, which Apple claims offers the world’s fastest integrated graphics, and a 16-core Neural Engine. In addition, the M1 processor has a universal memory architecture, a USB 4 controller, media encode and decode engines, and a host of security features. These include hardware-verified secure boot, encryption, and run-time protections.

它与高达8核的GPU和16核的神经引擎配对，苹果声称GPU提供了世界上最快的集成显卡。此外，M1处理器具有通用内存架构、USB 4控制器、媒体编码和解码引擎以及大量安全功能。其中包括硬件验证的安全引导、加密和运行时保护。

Apple claims the chip allows the Mac to instantly wake from sleep mode, and offer good performance even when running 3D programs or editing RAW photos.

苹果公司声称，该芯片可以让Mac立即从睡眠模式中唤醒，甚至在运行3D程序或编辑原始照片时也能提供良好的性能。

Apple says it’s optimized all of its apps for the new processors. Final Cut Pro is up to six times faster, Apple says, and Logic Pro can handle up to three times the amount of audio tracks. Apps from third party providers like Adobe are also coming, Apple says. Lightroom will launch later this year, with Photoshop following next year.

苹果公司表示，它已经针对新处理器优化了所有应用程序。苹果公司表示，Final Cut Pro的速度最高可达6倍，Logic Pro处理的音频曲目量最高可达3倍。苹果公司表示，来自Adobe等第三方供应商的应用程序也将陆续推出。Lightroom将于今年晚些时候推出，Photoshop将于明年推出。

Apple is offering Rosetta 2 translation software for apps not optimized for the new processors, and existing iOS and iPadOS apps will also run natively on the new processors.

苹果正在为没有针对新处理器优化的应用程序提供Rosetta 2翻译软件，现有的iOS和iPadOS应用程序也将在新处理器上本地运行。
