TikTok Bans Misogyny, Misgendering in Attempt to Clean Up Platform
TikTok禁止厌恶女性和性别歧视，试图清理平台

2022-02-14 18:26:05

TikTok says it is taking additional steps to help promote what it calls “safety, security, and well-being” on its video-sharing platform.

TikTok表示，它正在采取更多措施，帮助在其视频共享平台上推广所谓的“安全、安保和福祉”。

TikTok says it is updating its community guidelines to explicitly ban misogyny, deadnaming and misgendering on its social-media app. The move is meant to clarify the types of hateful ideologies that it prohibits.

TikTok表示，它正在更新其社区指南，以明确禁止其社交媒体应用程序上的厌女、死名和性别歧视。此举旨在澄清它所禁止的仇恨意识形态的类型。
