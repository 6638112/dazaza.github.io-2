Aegis Authenticator – open-source 2FA for Android
Aegis Authenticator –适用于Android的开源2FA

2021-01-17 02:57:54

Aegis Authenticator is a free, secure and open source app for Android to manage your 2-step verification tokens for your online services.

Aegis Authenticator是一款适用于Android的免费，安全和开放源代码应用程序，用于管理在线服务的两步验证令牌。

All of your one-time passwords are stored in a vault. If you choose to set a password (highly recommended), the vault will be encrypted using strong cryptography. If someone with malicious intent gets a hold of the vault file, it’s impossible for them to retrieve the contents without knowing the password. Entering your password each time you need access to a one-time password can be cumbersome. Fortunately, you can also enable biometric unlock if your device has a biometrics sensor (i.e. fingerprint or face unlock).

您所有的一次性密码都存储在Vault中。如果您选择设置密码（强烈建议），则将使用强密码术对保管库进行加密。如果有恶意的人保留了Vault文件，则他们将无法在不知道密码的情况下检索内容。每次需要访问一次性密码时都输入密码会很麻烦。幸运的是，如果您的设备具有生物特征传感器（例如指纹或面部解锁），您还可以启用生物特征解锁。

Over time, you'll likely accumulate tens of entries in your vault. Aegis Authenticator has lots of organization options to make finding the one you need at a particular moment easier. Set a custom icon for an entry to make it easier to find. Search by account name or service name. Have a lot of one-time passwords? Add them to custom groups for easier access. Personal, Work and Social can each get their own group.

随着时间的流逝，您可能会在保管库中积累数十个条目。 “宙斯盾身份验证器”具有许多组织选项，可以使您在特定时刻轻松找到所需的选项。为条目设置自定义图标，使其更易于查找。按帐户名称或服务名称搜索。有很多一次性密码吗？将它们添加到自定义组以便于访问。个人，工作和社交都可以拥有自己的小组。

To make sure you will never lose access to your online accounts, Aegis Authenticator can create automatic backups of the vault to a location of your choosing. If your cloud provider supports the Storage Access Framework of Android (like Nextcloud does), it can even create automatic backups to the cloud. Creating manual exports of the vault is also supported.

为确保您永远不会失去对在线帐户的访问权限，Aegis Authenticator可以将保管库自动备份到您选择的位置。如果您的云提供商支持Android的Storage Access Framework（就像Nextcloud一样），它甚至可以创建到云的自动备份。还支持创建库的手动导出。

Compared to other 2FA apps, we think Aegis stands out in terms of its simplicity and security. Most popular apps like Google Authenticator and FreeOTP don't bother with additional security measures. They allow access to your tokens right after opening the app. Aegis, on the other hand, encrypts all of your tokens at rest and requires a password or the touch of a finger to decrypt them.

与其他2FA应用程序相比，我们认为Aegis在其简单性和安全性方面脱颖而出。诸如Google Authenticator和FreeOTP之类的最流行的应用程序都不必担心其他安全措施。他们允许在打开应用程序后立即访问您的令牌。另一方面，宙斯盾会在静止状态下对所有令牌进行加密，并且需要密码或手指触摸才能对其进行解密。

Another important feature is the ability to export your tokens and import them into another device. Google Authenticator doesn't have this, which has not only annoyed users for years, but has also resulted in loss of access to lots accounts.

另一个重要功能是能够导出令牌并将其导入另一台设备的功能。 Google身份验证器没有此功能，这不仅使用户烦恼了很多年，而且还导致无法访问很多帐户。
