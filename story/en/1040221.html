Roku and WarnerMedia have reached an agreement to distribute HBO Max on the Roku platform, starting Thursday
Roku和WarnerMedia已达成协议，从周四开始在Roku平台上分发HBO Max

2020-12-17 06:09:16

and have reached an agreement for the distribution of the on the Roku platform — nearly seven months after the streaming service launched.

并已在流媒体服务启动近七个月后就Roku平台上的分发达成协议。

HBO Max will be live on Roku effective tomorrow, Thursday, Dec. 17, the companies announced. The deal now gives the streaming service coverage on all major over-the-top platforms. Its absence on Roku was a big hole: Roku counted 46 million active user accounts as of the end of September.

两家公司宣布，HBO Max将于12月17日星期四在Roku上直播。该交易现在使流媒体服务覆盖所有主要的顶级平台。它在Roku上的缺席是一个大漏洞：截至9月底，Roku拥有4600万活跃用户帐户。

Terms of the deal were not disclosed, but both sides claimed they were pleased with finally resolving their differences.

交易的条款没有透露，但双方都称他们很高兴最终解决分歧。

WarnerMedia was looking to nail down an HBO Max pact with Roku as the service’s last major distribution platform ahead of the day-and-date premiere of “Wonder Woman 1984” starring Gal Gadot on HBO Max and in theaters on Dec. 25. Meanwhile, as previously announced, all films in Warner Bros.’s 2021 slate will debut on HBO Max in the U.S. concurrently with its theatrical release and will be available to stream exclusively for one month.

WarnerMedia希望与Roku达成HBO Max协议，该协议是该服务的最后一个主要发行平台，此前由Gal Gadot主演的Gad于12月25日在影院上映的首映式“ Wonder Woman 1984”之前。正如先前宣布的那样，华纳兄弟公司2021年提名中的所有电影都将在美国HBO Max上映，并同时上映戏剧，并将独家播放一个月。

Under the deal, Roku users who have already subscribed to HBO through Roku will have the existing HBO app on their devices automatically updated to become the HBO Max app, and they will be able to log in using their existing HBO credentials. It appears that going forward, Roku will no longer be able to sell HBO as a channel subscription in the Roku Channel store, a concession that both Amazon and Apple made in their agreements for HBO Max.

根据协议，已经通过Roku订阅HBO的Roku用户将自动更新其设备上现有的HBO应用程序，使其成为HBO Max应用程序，并且他们将能够使用其现有的HBO凭据登录。看来，未来，Roku将不再能够在Roku Channel商店中以频道订阅的形式出售HBO，这是亚马逊和苹果在HBO Max协议中做出的让步。

Shares of Roku were up nearly 4% in after-hours trading after the HBO Max deal was announced.

在HBO Max交易宣布后，盘后交易中Roku的股价上涨了近4％。

“We believe that all entertainment will be streamed and we are thrilled to partner with HBO Max to bring their incredible library of iconic entertainment brands and blockbuster slate of direct to streaming theatrical releases to the Roku households with more than 100 million people that have made Roku the No. 1 TV streaming platform in America,” Scott Rosenberg, SVP of Roku’s platform business, said in a statement.

“我们相信所有娱乐活动都将得到流传，我们很高兴与HBO Max合作，将他们令人难以置信的标志性娱乐品牌库和重磅炸弹直接带到Roku家庭中，这些流放的戏剧作品流到Roku家庭中，有超过1亿人制作了Roku Roku平台业务高级副总裁Scott Rosenberg在一份声明中说。

Rosenberg added, “Reaching mutually beneficial agreements where Roku grows together with our partners is how we deliver an exceptional user experience at an incredible value for consumers and we are excited by the opportunity to deepen our longstanding relationship with the team at WarnerMedia.”

Rosenberg补充说：“ Roku与我们的合作伙伴共同成长，达成互惠互利的协议，这就是我们如何为消费者提供令人难以置信的价值的卓越用户体验，并且我们有机会加深与WarnerMedia团队的长期合作关系，我们为此感到兴奋。”

Tony Goncalves, chief revenue officer at WarnerMedia, said in a statement, “We’re breaking new ground in the months ahead, and we can’t wait to work with our longtime partners at Roku to build on our past successes and bring HBO Max’s best-in-class quality entertainment to Roku’s large and highly engaged audience.”

WarnerMedia首席营收官Tony Goncalves在一份声明中说：“我们在接下来的几个月中正在开拓新的领域，我们迫不及待地想与Roku的长期合作伙伴合作，以巩固我们过去的成功并将HBO Max的成功向Roku庞大且高度参与的观众提供一流的优质娱乐。”

News of the deal between WarnerMedia and Roku comes a day after Comcast announced the rollout of the streaming app on Xfinity X1 and Xfinity Flex while HBO Max launched on PlayStation 5 earlier today. Amazon clinched a deal in November to carry HBO Max on Fire TV and Fire Tablet devices, after holding out for several months and ultimately agreeing that it will stop selling HBO in Amazon Prime Video Channels.

昨天，Comcast宣布在Xfinity X1和Xfinity Flex上推出流媒体应用程序，而HBO Max今天早些时候在PlayStation 5上发布了这一消息，这是WarnerMedia与Roku之间达成交易的消息。亚马逊在坚持了几个月并最终同意将停止在亚马逊Prime Video Channels中出售HBO之后，在11月达成了在Fire TV和Fire Tablet设备上携带HBO Max的协议。

Even before HBO Max launched in late May, Roku and WarnerMedia had been haggling over terms of the distribution deal. Roku wanted to continue to have the ability to sell HBO through the Roku Channel store, while WarnerMedia was insisting on distributing HBO Max as a separate app. The two parties also were trying to come to an agreement over what ad inventory would be split with Roku for the ad-supported, reduced-price version of HBO Max slated to come out in late 2021.

甚至在5月底推出HBO Max之前，Roku和WarnerMedia一直在就发行协议的条款进行讨价还价。 Roku希望继续具有通过Roku Channel商店出售HBO的能力，而WarnerMedia则坚持要求将HBO Max作为单独的应用程序进行分发。两家公司还试图就将与Roku拆分哪些广告资源达成协议，以争取在2021年下半年投放广告支持的HBO Max降价版本。

HBO Max, which carries a regular subscription price of $14.99 per month, touts 10,000 hours of curated premium content, including TV shows and movies from HBO, Warner Bros., DC, Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, and Turner Classic Movies.

HBO Max的定期订阅价格为每月14.99美元，兜售10,000小时的精选优质内容，包括HBO，华纳兄弟，DC，卡通网络，成人游泳和特纳经典电影的电视节目和电影。

In addition to the release of “Wonder Woman 1984,” HBO Max this month will feature exclusive holiday special “My Gift: A Christmas Special” from Carrie Underwood, a new “Euphoria” special episode starring Zendaya, Steven Soderbergh’s comedy film “Let Them All Talk,” and four-part docuseries “Heaven’s Gate: The Cult of Cults.”

除了发行“ Wonder Woman 1984”外，HBO Max本月还将推出Carrie Underwood的独家假日特别节目“ My Gift：A Christmas Special”，由史蒂芬·索德伯格的喜剧电影Zendaya主演的新的“ Euphoria”特别节目“ Let Them” “所有谈话”和四部分的纪录片系列“天堂之门：对邪教的崇拜”。

In January, HBO Max programming highlights include the second “Euphoria” special episode; “Search Party” Season 4; a new season of “Selena + Chef,” the entire “Gossip Girl” library, Season 1 of “Snowpiercer,” “The Alienist: Angel of Darkness” and “King of Staten Island.”

1月，HBO Max节目的重头戏包括第二个“ Euphoria”特别节目； 「搜寻派对」第4季；新赛季的“塞莱娜+厨师”，整个“绯闻女孩”图书馆，“飞雪者”，“外星人：黑暗天使”和“史泰登岛之王”的第一季。
