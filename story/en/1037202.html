Say I managed to save $500 from my day job. I find 5 people who begrudgingly agree to give me $100 because I told them I can turn it into $200 in a month. I give them $200 each at the end of the month. Now, I am out of savings but I have 5 people who doubled their money in a month with no hard work at all.

假设我设法从日常工作中节省了$ 500。我发现有5个人勉强同意给我100美元，因为我告诉他们我可以在一个月内将其变成200美元。月底，我给他们每人200美元。现在，我没钱了，但是我有5个人，一个月内就把自己的钱翻了一番，一点也不努力。

I use their story as an example to show people that my idea works. Now it’s easier to find people who pay me. I take $100 from 10 more people, wait for a month, and use the proceeds ($1,000) to give $200 each to 5 of those people.

我以他们的故事为例，向人们展示我的想法有效。现在，更容易找到付钱给我的人。我从另外10个人中收取$ 100，等待一个月，然后使用收益（$ 1,000）向其中5个人提供​​$ 200。

Now I have a proven business model, and a good number of people who will vouch for my method. I use the proceeds from each successive group to pay the previous cohort of people.

现在，我有了一个行之有效的商业模型，并且有很多人会支持我的方法。我使用每个连续小组的收益来支付先前的人员。
