PiDP-11 – Recreating PDP-11/70 with Raspberry Pi
PIDP-11 - 用覆盆子PI重建PDP-11/70

2021-04-20 01:15:17

The PiDP-11 is a modern replica of Digital Equipment Corporation’s influential PDP-11 minicomputer. Before we had a computer on every desk and in every home–and long before they were in every pocket–computers were large, intimidating, and locked in special rooms at universities and corporations. The PDP series paved the way for making computers more accessible. PDP stood for Programmed Data Processor, and this distinguished line of computers was produced from 1957 to 1990. Each PDP model was numbered sequentially from the one to sixteen (skipping PDP-2 and unlucky 13). The PDP-1, PDP-8, and PDP-11 are best remembered today. The PDP-11 was DEC’s first 16-bit computer, and cost $20,000 when released in 1970. Reportedly, 600,000 units were sold over its long life, and it was the tool of choice for countless computing innovations, including the creation of UNIX and the C programming language. The 32-bit VAX minicomputer eventually replaced the venerable PDP-11.

PIDP-11是数字设备公司有影响力的PDP-11小型计算机的现代化复制品。在我们在每张桌子上和每个家庭的电脑和长期以来一直在每个桌子上进行电脑之前，在每个口袋计算机上都很大，令人恐惧，并在大学和公司的特殊房间里锁定。 PDP系列铺平了使电脑更易进入的方式。 PDP支持编程数据处理器，并且这种杰出的计算机线路从1957年到1990年生产。每个PDP模型按一到十六次编号（跳过PDP-2和不置位13）。 PDP-1，PDP-8和PDP-11今天最好记住。 PDP-11是DEC的第一个16位电脑，并在1970年发布时花费了20,000美元。据报道，60万个单位在其长时间寿命上销售，这是无数计算创新的选择工具，包括创建UNIX和unix的创建C编程语言。 32位VAX小型机最终取代了尊贵的PDP-11。

Oscar Vermeulen is the creator of this innovative PiDP-11 kit. There is no store; you add your name to a waiting list for future shipments. I didn’t have to wait long before he emailed, saying another run was on the way. After paying and waiting for the package to arrive from Switzerland, I was ready to assemble!

Oscar vermeulen是这款创新PIDP-11套件的创造者。没有商店;您将您的名字添加到等候名单以供未来的出货量。在电子邮件之后，我不必等等，说另一次跑步就在路上。支付并等待包裹抵达瑞士后，我已准备好组装！

I was excited by this kit for several reasons: first, I wanted a good-looking PDP-11 replica; second, I wanted to bask in the glow of blinkenlights; third, I was eager for my first Raspberry Pi project; and finally, I needed to hone my soldering skills. I was able to achieve each of these in reverse order. Following Oscar’s instructions and after watching a series of walk-through videos, I was ready to start with the passive components. After placing and taping the diodes and resistors in place, I flipped the board and started soldering. Up to this point, I had handled both through-hole and surface-mount projects, but always in small numbers. This project allowed me to spend quality time with my iron. For some reason, my kit was missing the 330-ohm resistors. I had resistors close to that value in my stash, but I wasn’t sure how important it was to match the designed resistance. After looking at the board, it was clear the resistors were connected to the LED lights. Thanks to help online, I learned the level of resistance could vary within a reasonable range, though it might affect the brightness of the lights. I decided to take the opportunity to expand my in-house supply and ordered a variety kit with different sizes, including the 330s I needed.

我被这个套件兴奋了几个原因：首先，我想要一个漂亮的PDP-11复制品;其次，我想在Blinkenlights的辉光中晒太阳;第三，我渴望我的第一个覆盆子PI项目;最后，我需要磨练我的焊接技巧。我能够以相反的顺序实现这些中的每一个。在奥斯卡的说明之后和观看一系列步行视频后，我已准备好开始使用被动组件。放置和胶带二极管和电阻后，我翻转了电路板并开始焊接。到目前为止，我处理了两个通孔和表面贴装项目，但总是少量。这个项目让我用铁来花很好的时间。出于某种原因，我的套件缺少330欧姆电阻。我的藏匿处有电阻器靠近该价值，但我不确定匹配设计阻力有多重要。在看电路板后，很清楚电​​阻连接到LED灯。由于在线帮助，我了解到阻力水平可能在合理的范围内变化，但它可能会影响灯光的亮度。我决定借此机会扩展我的内部供应，并订购了不同尺寸的品种套件，包括我需要的330s。

Next, I followed the instructions and placed the fiddly spacers on the LED lights and aligned them on the PCB using the provided guide. Next, I installed the pair of rotary encoders, the chip socket, and the Pi connector (on the back).

接下来，我遵循了指令，并将LED灯的细线间隔放置在PCB上使用提供的指南将它们与放置在PCB上。接下来，我安装了一对旋转编码器，芯片插座和PI连接器（在背面）。

I was now ready to test my work. This involved setting up the Raspberry Pi. There is one electronics shop remaining in my area, so I stopped by to see what Pi-related gear they offered. Due to what I assumed was a pricing error (which I brought to their attention), I was able to pick up a Pi 3 Model B for less money than a stock Pi 3. Thankful for my good luck, I also purchased a suitable power supply and a 16GB memory card. The Raspberry Pi website makes it very easy to install the Raspbian OS on the memory card and get the Pi up and running. Next, I downloaded the files needed to emulate the PDP-11 from the link provided by Oscar. After going through the testing process listed in the documentation, I felt good about my progress.

我现在准备好测试了我的工作。这涉及设置覆盆子pi。我所在的地区有一个电子商店，所以我停下来看看他们提供的相关档。由于我认为是一个定价错误（我引起了他们的注意），我能够拿起PI 3型号B，而不是股票PI 3.感谢我的好运，我也购买了合适的力量供应和16GB存储卡。 Raspberry PI网站使得在存储卡上安装Raspbian OS并将PI启动并运行非常容易。接下来，我下载了从奥斯卡提供的链接模拟PDP-11所需的文件。经过文档中列出的测试过程后，我对我的进度感到愉快。

Next, I needed to wrangle switches. The instructions make it evident that some struggle to align the switches correctly. I did not have too much trouble, but I was prepared for difficulty. There is both a lower and upper guide. The lower guide helps you put the right switch in the right place. The upper guide helps align the spacing of the switches, so they aren’t cockeyed once soldered into place. Zip ties are used to squeeze the guides together to fit everything into place.

接下来，我需要拍打开关。说明使其显而易见的是，有些斗争正确地对齐开关。我没有太多麻烦，但我准备困难。有一个下导向和上部引导件。下指南可帮助您将右侧的开关放在正确的位置。上部指南有助于对准开关的间距，因此它们不会被焊接到位。拉链扎带用于将导轨挤在一起，以将所有东西适合于适当位置。

Finally, I turned my attention to the case. The case and front panel are well-made and provide a great fit and finish to the kit. I struggled to get the board and faceplate properly screwed into the case. The combination of spacers, nuts, and bolts was more troublesome than I expected. But once everything was aligned, it looked great.

最后，我对此案的注意力。案例和前面板是精心制造的，提供良好的合适和完成套件。我努力让董事会和面板正确拧入箱子里。间隔物，螺母和螺栓的组合比我预期的更麻烦。但一旦一切都对齐，它看起来很棒。

By default, the front key switch is not connected, but the board is configured so the key can either turn off the 5V power or issue a software command to shutdown the Raspberry Pi. Not wanting to make a permanent decision, I soldered two wires to the key switch and installed screw terminals at both locations on the board. I then decided to place a barrel jack on the side of the case and install another screw terminal for the 5V power, bypassing the Raspberry Pi’s micro USB power connection.

默认情况下，前键开关未连接，但电路板配置为键可以关闭5V电源或发出软件命令以关闭覆盆子PI。不想制作永久的决定，我将两根电线焊接到钥匙开关上并在电路板上的两个位置安装螺钉端子。然后，我决定在壳体的一侧放置一个桶千斤顶，并为5V电源安装另一个螺钉端子，绕过覆盆子PI的Micro USB电源连接。

First, I played around with the software shutdown option. It took some trial and error to figure out which way the key needed to turn to operate correctly, but once you flip down the Halt switch, you turn the key to issue the proper “shutdown -h now” command before unplugging the Raspberry Pi. However, I preferred to use the key to cut power to the board-mounted barrel jack. This way, I could use the PiDP-11’s “secret” shutdown switch enabled by pressing the rotary encoder, and then cut power to the Pi by turning the key. Also, it is satisfying to use the key to power on the unit. Since the Pi automatically boots into the blinkenlight display, it is easy to operate without an attached keyboard and screen.

首先，我在软件关闭选项上播放。它需要一些试验和错误来弄清楚将要正确运行的关键方式正确操作，但一旦翻转停止开关，就会在拔掉覆盆子PI之前打开键以发出正确的“shutdown -h”命令。但是，我更愿意使用钥匙将电力切割到板安装的桶形千斤顶。这样，我可以通过按下旋转编码器来使用PIDP-11的“秘密”关闭开关，然后通过转动键将电源切割到PI。此外，使用键对设备电源令人满意。由于PI自动靴子进入Blinkenlight显示屏，因此在没有附加的键盘和屏幕的情况下易于操作。

I did attempt to install panel mount connectors to the back cover extending the Raspberry Pi’s power, USB, HDMI, and Ethernet ports, but there was not enough clearance for the cover to close properly.

我确实尝试将面板安装连接器安装到后盖，延伸覆盆子PI的电源，USB，HDMI和以太网端口，但盖子没有足够的间隙恰当地关闭。

I’m not done yet. I may enable a serial terminal connection for the PDP-11 emulator, and I will certainly continue to explore both the PDP and Raspberry Pi software. The point of any hobbyist kit is to tinker and learn. I can do both with this well-made piece of ’70s computing nostalgia.

我还没搞定。我可以为PDP-11仿真器启用串行端子连接，我肯定会继续探索PDP和Raspberry PI软件。任何Hobbyist套件的点就是修补并学习。我可以用这款精心制作的70年代计算怀旧的。
