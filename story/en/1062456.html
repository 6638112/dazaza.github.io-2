Amazon Echo Buds (2nd gen) review: affordable, better sound, smaller, True ANC, optional wireless charging, but middling battery life and mediocre call quality
亚马逊回声芽（第2 Gen）评论：经济实惠，声音更好，更小，真正的ANC，可选的无线充电，但中间电池寿命和平庸的呼叫质量

2021-05-13 22:12:34

Compared to its closest rivals, Amazon was late to the true wireless earbud game. By the time the company introduced its Echo Buds in 2019, Apple was already on version two and the AirPods Pro came shortly after. Google’s wired Pixel Buds were two years old, and the company would go on to launch an improved version in 2020. As is typically the case when a company enters a new product category, the first Echo Buds had some flaws despite offering hands-free access to Alexa at an affordable price. Now Amazon has returned with the “all-new” Echo Buds ( $100 and $120) which feature true active noise cancellation (ANC), improved audio quality and a redesign. And Amazon managed to do all of that while keeping the price low.

与最近的竞争对手相比，亚马逊迟到了真正的无线耳塞游戏。当公司在2019年介绍了其回声芽的时候，苹果已经在版本二，Airpods Pro很快就到了。谷歌的有线像素芽是两岁的，公司将于2020年推出一个改进的版本。通常情况下，当公司进入一个新产品类别时，尽管提供了无人驾驶措施，但第一个回声芽仍有一些缺陷以实惠的价格到alexa。现在，亚马逊已返回“全新”回声芽（100美元和120美元），其具有真正的主动噪声消除（ANC），改进了音频质量和重新设计。亚马逊设法竭尽所能，同时保持价格低。

Amazon’s redesign of the second-gen Echo Buds starts on the outside. The earbuds themselves are 20 percent smaller, and they’re about two grams lighter. The company shortened the nozzle and paired it with new vents, which relieves some of the pressure of having something crammed in your ear for long periods of time. Four sets of tips and two sizes of wings give you several options to find the best fit. Plus, Amazon put an ear tip fit test inside the Alexa app so you don’t have to wonder if you’ve made the right choice.

亚马逊的重新设计了第二岁的回声芽开始于外面。耳塞本身比较小的20％，它们大约两克打火机。该公司缩短了喷嘴并用新的通风口配对，这减轻了一些长时间在耳朵里挤满了一些东西的压力。四组尖端和两种翅膀为您提供了几种选择最适合的选项。此外，亚马逊在Alexa应用程序内放置耳机贴身测试，因此您不必怀疑您是否已做出正确的选择。

The touch panel on the first Echo Buds was a raised glossy circle, but Amazon nixed that and made the outside a seamless matte black. The company added its iconic smile/arrow branding, but it’s tone-on-tone, so it doesn’t stand out. (The promo images make it look more prominent than it actually is.) Amazon opted for external microphones at the top and bottom of each bud and reduced the charging contact points from five to three. Like the previous version, the new Echo Buds are IPX4 rated against water splashes. This isn’t robust protection, but it should be enough to withstand sweat during a workout.

第一个回声芽上的触摸面板是一个凸起的光泽圆圈，但亚马逊尼克斯，并制作了一个无缝哑光黑色的外部。该公司增加了其标志性的笑容/箭头品牌，但它是色调的语气，所以它并没有脱颖而出。 （促销图像使它看起来比实际更突出。）亚马逊在每个芽的顶部和底部的外部麦克风选择外部麦克风，并将充电接触点从五到三个减少。像以前的版本一样，新的回声芽是IPX4额定水溅。这并不稳健，但它应该足以承受锻炼过程中的汗水。

The included charging case was also redesigned. It’s 40-percent smaller and more vertical than flat, like much of the competition offers these days. Additional battery status LEDs and wireless charging are also among the upgrades, as is the switch to USB-C for the times you need to plug in.

包含的充电案例也被重新设计。这比平坦更小，垂直越来越多，就像这些日子一样。额外的电池状态LED和无线充电也是升级之一，因为您需要插入的时间是USB-C的开关。

While the initial setup isn’t quite as quick as AirPods and iOS, Amazon did make things simple. As was the case with the first Echo Buds, everything is run through the Alexa app on your phone. All you need to do is flip open the Echo Buds case and wait for the blue light to indicate you’re in pairing mode. From there, a pop-up will guide you through the initial setup.

虽然初始设置并不像Airpods和iOS一样快，但亚马逊确实让事情变得简单。与第一个回声芽一样，一切都通过手机上的Alexa应用程序进行。所有您需要做的就是翻转回声芽盒并等待蓝光指示您处于配对模式。从那里，弹出窗口将指导您完成初始设置。

Inside the Alexa app Amazon gives you a lot of options to customize the Echo Buds. First, the company put quick access to battery levels, ANC mode, microphone mute and workout info right on the home screen. A tap on the gear icon to the right opens up the detailed settings for the earbuds. Here, you can switch on Power Save to extend battery life, enable hands-free Alexa and adjust Sidetone (the amount of your voice you hear during calls). There are also manual EQ sliders with options for bass, mid and treble alongside an ear tip fit test, workout history and a Find My tool to help you locate a misplaced earbud.

Alexa App Amazon内部为您提供了许多自定义回声芽的选项。首先，该公司在主屏幕上快速访问电池电量，ANC模式，麦克风静音和锻炼信息。右侧的齿轮图标上的点击打开了耳塞的详细设置。在这里，您可以打开省电以延长电池寿命，使免提Alexa能够调整旁观通（在呼叫期间听到的声音数量）。还有手动EQ滑块，带有贝司的选项，中间和高音搭配耳机拟合测试，锻炼历史记录和一个找到我的工具，以帮助您找到错误的耳塞。

You can also view info on the tap controls, but only the long press is customizable. By default, it’s set to toggle between ANC and ambient sound (Passthrough), but the alternate options include volume, your native assistant, Alexa tap to talk, mic mute or you can disable the function entirely. The app allows you to set different things for the press-and-hold on each earbud, instead of the same one being mirrored on both sides like the other gestures. This means you can put volume up on one bud and volume down on the other. Lastly, you can also disable any of the other tap controls in the app individually.

您还可以在点击控件上查看信息，但只有长按可自定义。默认情况下，它设置为在ANC和环境声音之间切换（PASSthrough），但备用选项包括卷，您的本机助手，Alexa Tap to Talk，MIC MUTE或您可以完全禁用该功能。该应用程序允许您为每个耳机上的按钮设置不同的东西，而不是与其他手势的两侧镜像相同的东西。这意味着您可以在另一个芽和音量上放大音量。最后，您还可以单独禁用应用程序中的任何其他点击控件。

Alexa has learned some new tricks. In addition to asking the assistant to control music and podcasts playback, it can now find specific shows you follow on Amazon Music rather than making a general recommendation. Amazon also recently added a command that allows you to set a specific listening time: “Alexa, play jazz for 15 minutes” for example. There’s also the ability to help with non-audio tasks like reminders, calendar, shopping lists and even transit info in some US cities.

Alexa已经了解了一些新的技巧。除了要求助手控制音乐和播客播放外，它现在可以找到特定的节目您在亚马逊音乐上遵循，而不是制定一般性推荐。亚马逊还添加了一个命令，允许您设置特定的聆听时间：“Alexa，播放15分钟的爵士乐”。还有能力帮助在一些美国城市的提醒，日历，购物清单和甚至过境信息等非音频任务。

Audio quality is the biggest area Amazon needed to improve on the first-gen Echo Buds. Even with the optional EQ in the Alexa app, which returns on this new version, the sound was inconsistent. I could only find one volume where I would say they sounded “good.” The company says the new 5.7mm "high-performance driver” in each of the new Echo Buds offers "increased fidelity in bass and treble,” and I’ve found that to be accurate. Not only does the new model sound better, but the audio improvements are more evenly applied at low, medium and high volumes. Still, this new version sounds its absolute best at about 75-80 percent volume.

音频质量是亚马逊最大的领域，需要改进第一局的回声芽。即使Alexa应用程序中的可选eq，它在这个新版本上返回，声音是不一致的。我只能找到一个卷，我会说他们听起来“好”。该公司表示，新的5.7mm＆＃34;在每个新的回声芽提供的高性能司机和＃34;增加了低音和高音的保真度，“我发现它是准确的。新模型不仅可以听起来更好，但音频改进更均匀地应用于低，中高卷。尽管如此，这个新版本仍然是它的绝对最佳尺寸约为75-80％。

Overall, the sound is less compressed on the second-gen Echo Buds. Across genres, there’s more room for each element to stand on its own, especially when you’re listening to acoustic-driven bluegrass like Punch Brothers and metal bands like Mastodon and Gojira. The latter two tend to get treble-heavy and chaotic at full volume, which was an issue that plagued the previous model as well. The kick drum doesn’t get completely drowned out, but there’s noticeably more high end, so things like cymbals and guitars can be grating. When you move to hip-hop, the discrepancy isn’t as pronounced as there’s plenty of bassy thump to counter the cutting high at full volume.

总的来说，在第二局回声芽上的声音较小。在流派中，每个元素都有更多的空间来独立，特别是当您在听声兄弟和金属乐队等声音驱动的蓝草时，如Mastodon和Gojira等。后两者往往会在全卷上进行高音重和混乱，这是一个困扰以前的模型的问题。踢鼓没有完全淹死，但是很高的结束，所以钹和吉他等东西可以光栅。当你搬到嘻哈时，差异不是很明显，因为有很多巴西砰的砰砰声可以在全体积上抵抗切割高。

The EQ sliders in the Alexa app do offer relief. By increasing bass and mids, I was able to counter the painfully loud cymbal blasts on Mastodon’s “Tread Lightly” to make things more enjoyable. When you make these adjustments, you lose some clarity, and things start to sound a bit muddy. Amazon’s improvements to sound quality are apparent, but there’s still work to be don

Alexa App中的EQ滑块确实提供了救济。通过增加低音和中间，我能够在乳房的“轻微”的“胎面”上抗衡痛苦的钹爆炸，以使事情更加愉快。当您进行这些调整时，您会失去一些清晰度，事情开始听起来有点浑浊。亚马逊对音质的改进是显而易见的，但仍有工作仍然是唐

One area Amazon massively improved is noise cancellation. By switching from active noise reduction (ANR) to true ANC, the company gave the Echo Buds the ability to actually block out sounds around you. I was impressed by how much better this new version can drown out a nearby conversation or the consistent roar of a whitte noise machine. Amazon’s Passthrough or ambient sound feature also works well, and it sounds more natural than a lot of the earbuds I’ve tested.

亚马逊一个区域巨大改善是噪声消除。通过从活跃的降噪（ANR）切换到真正的ANC，该公司给出了回声芽的能力实际阻挡你周围的声音。这个新版本可以淹没在附近的谈话或一致的噪音机器的一致咆哮中，我印象深刻。亚马逊的Passthough或环境声音功能也很好，它听起来比我测试的很多耳塞更自然。

With two beamforming microphones on the outside of each earbud, Amazon clearly planned for these to be used for calls. As was the case with the previous model, the company offers adjustable Sidetone. This allows you to dial in how much of your voice you hear through the earbuds during calls, which helps you be a bit less shouty when you have both buds in. And trust me, it makes a difference. At a time when my entire family is home for part of the day, I can no longer get by with one earbud in on calls. Being able to use both but also hear myself without raising my voice is a handy feature.

在每个耳塞的外部有两个波束成形麦克风，亚马逊清楚地计划用于这些呼叫。与以前的模型一样，公司提供可调节的Sidetone。这允许您拨打您在呼叫期间通过耳塞听到的大部分声音，这有助于您在您有两个芽中的时候有点呼喊。并相信我，它会有所不同。在我的整个家庭的一天中的家中，我可以在呼叫中使用一只耳机获得。能够使用两者，也可以在不提升我的声音的情况下听到自己的声音是一个方便的功能。

In terms of audio quality, the second-gen Echo Buds make your voice come through clear. Unlike most true wireless earbuds, it doesn’t immediately sound like you’re on speakerphone. There is a bit of an echo (sorry), but it isn’t nearly as pronounced as if you were speaking with your phone away from your face. The new Echo Buds don’t do a great job with background noise though, so I’d avoid sitting near your dog’s food bowl or the TV when you take an important call.

在音频质量方面，第二岁的回声芽使你的声音通过清晰。与最真实的无线耳塞不同，它并没有立即听起来像你在扬声器上。有一点回声（抱歉），但它并不像你从你的手上谈论你的脸上。新的回声芽与背景噪音做出了一件很棒的工作，所以我避免在你坐在你的狗的食物碗或电视上坐在附近。

Just like the original Echo Buds, Amazon promises five hours of battery life on the earbuds themselves. That’s with both ANC and hands-free Alexa active. The company offers two additional charges via the case and it says you can add an hour and a half if you disable those two key features. At five hours, that figure is on-par with much of the competition, but some similarly priced earbuds offer 10 or more. In a pinch, a 15-minute charge will give you two hours of listening time. During my tests, I consistently hit five hours almost right on the dot.

就像原来的回声芽一样，亚马逊承诺在耳塞本身上有五个小时的电池寿命。这是ANC和免提ALEXA活跃。该公司通过此类提供两项额外费用，如果禁用这两个关键功能，您可以添加一个小时半。在五个小时的时间里，这个数字是与大部分竞争对手的，但一些类似的耳塞提供了10个或更多。在捏，15分钟的收费将为您提供两小时的聆听时间。在我的测试期间，我始终达到圆点的五个小时。

The retooled Echo Buds case has LEDs for each earbud in addition to one on the outside. These lights give you a battery estimate based on color (green, yellow and red). The new case is equipped with wireless charging (if you pay $20 more) and Anker made a pad just for it. That accessory has separate sets of lights that show you the status of both the earbuds and the case individually. There are four for each, so you have a better idea of where things stand than with the LEDs on the case. The pad will also charge your phone, so it’s not exclusively for use with the Echo Buds. Amazon sells the entire set as a bundle for $137.98.

除了一个在外面之外，重滑回声芽盒还为每个耳机带来了LED。这些灯根据颜色（绿色，黄色和红色）为您提供电池估算。新案例配备无线充电（如果您支付20美元），Anker为它制作了垫子。该配件有单独的灯，可以单独向您展示耳塞和案例的状态。每个人都有四个，所以你更好地了解事情的位置，而不是在案件上的LED。垫也将对您的手机充电，因此它不仅仅是与回声芽一起使用。亚马逊以137.98美元的价格销售整个集合作为捆绑包。

Amazon is currently offering the new Echo Buds for $100 without the wireless charging case, but that will go up to $120 at some point. Even if you pay the extra $20 for the case ( currently $120, but will be $140), the rates are hard to beat right now. At their eventual full prices, both of these options are quite attractive for a set of noise-cancelling earbuds with hands-free Alexa, decent sound and some degree of customization. Wireless charging is usually reserved for more expensive devices, and it’s rare to see on earbuds under $150.

亚马逊目前正在为100美元提供新的Echo Buds，没有无线充电案例，但这将在某些时候高达120美元。即使您为案件支付额外的20美元（目前为120美元，但为140美元），现在仍然很难击败。以最终的全部价格，这两个选项对于一套充满免噪声Alexa，不错的声音和一定程度的定制，这两个选择都非常有吸引力。无线充电通常是为更昂贵的设备保留的，并且很少见在150美元的耳塞上。

Since the Echo Buds are the prime choice if Alexa is your voice assistant, the direct comparisons are AirPods and Pixel Buds. Both of these give you hands-free access to Siri and Google Assistant respectively, along with deep integration with iOS and Android. Neither offer the customization that Amazon does, but they do support wireless charging. They’re also both more expensive and new models from Apple and Google are reportedly imminent. We could see the rumored Pixel Buds A-Series as soon as next week at Google I/O.

由于alexa是您的语音助手，因此回声芽是主要的选择，直接比较是Airpods和像素芽。这两者都可以分别向Siri和Google助手提供免提访问，以及与iOS和Android的深度集成。亚马逊的定制既不是亚马逊，而且他们确实支持无线充电。据报道，他们也既昂贵的苹果和谷歌的新型号也迫在眉睫。我们可以在谷歌I / O下一周内看到传闻狂热的像素芽A系列。

Amazon’s second-gen Echo Buds are improved in nearly every way. A new design makes the smaller buds more comfortable to wear for long periods of time and the sound quality is noticeably better. However, you’ll likely still need to make some EQ adjustments across genres and battery life hasn’t improved at five hours. Conveniences like optional wireless charging, Sidetone for calls and new Alexa skills make these earbuds an attractive option for anyone who has bought into Amazon’s ecosystem. Plus, the company managed to make a better product without raising the price, which is always a nice surprise.

亚马逊的第二届Echo Buds几乎各种方式得到改善。新设计使较小的芽长时间佩戴更舒适，并且音质明显更好。但是，您仍可能需要在流派中进行一些EQ调整，并且电池寿命在五个小时内没有提高。便利设施如可选的无线充电，Sidetone用于呼叫和新的Alexa技能使这些耳罩成为任何已经购买亚马逊生态系统的人的迷人选择。此外，公司设法在不提高价格的情况下进行更好的产品，这总是一个惊喜。
