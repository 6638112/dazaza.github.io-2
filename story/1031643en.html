随着微软努力利用远程工作趋势，微软团队在第一季度突破了1.15亿DAU，比4月份增长了53%

2020-10-28 06:56:31

Microsoft Teams is continuing to ride the remote work and learning wave kicked off by the coronavirus pandemic. During Microsoft’s Q1 2021 earnings call, CEO Satya Nadella shared that Microsoft Teams has passed 115 million daily active users. That’s up 53% from the 75 million daily active users in April. Some 15 months ago, Microsoft Teams had just 13 million daily active users.

微软团队正在继续利用冠状病毒大流行引发的远程工作和学习浪潮。在微软2021年第一季度财报电话会议上，首席执行官萨蒂亚·纳德拉(Satya Nadella)分享了微软团队的日活跃用户已超过1.15亿。这比4月份7500万的日活跃用户增长了53%。大约15个月前，微软团队的日活跃用户只有1300万。

“Teams now has more than 115 million daily active users,” Nadella said. “We are seeing increased usage and intensity as people communicate, collaborate, and coauthor content across work, life, and learning. Microsoft 365 users generated more than 30 billion collaboration minutes in a single day this quarter.”

纳德拉说：“球队现在每天有超过1.15亿的活跃用户。”随着人们在工作、生活和学习中交流、协作和共同创作内容，我们看到使用率和强度都在增加。本季度，微软365用户在一天内创造了超过300亿分钟的协作时间。

Microsoft Teams is the company’s Office 365 chat-based collaboration tool that competes with Slack, Facebook’s Workplace, Google Chat/Meet, and even Zoom. Indeed, Microsoft is in a virtual meeting war with Google and Zoom. Google Meet has seen 100 million meeting participants in a single day, Teams has seen more than 200 million meeting participants, and Zoom has seen 300 million daily meeting participants. (Unlike daily active users, “meeting participants” can count the same user more than once.)

微软团队是该公司基于Office 365聊天的协作工具，可与Slake、Facebook的Workplace、Google Chat/Meet，甚至Zoom竞争。事实上，微软正与谷歌和Zoom展开虚拟会议大战。Google Meet在一天内有1亿人参加会议，团队有超过2亿人参加会议，Zoom每天有3亿人参加会议。(与日常活跃用户不同，“会议参与者”可以多次计算同一用户。)。

Since 2018, Microsoft Teams has been the company’s fastest-growing business app ever, long before lockdowns started juicing up remote work and learning numbers. In May, Microsoft’s Jeff Teper told VentureBeat Teams “ will be even bigger than Windows.”

自2018年以来，微软团队一直是该公司有史以来增长最快的商业应用，早在封锁开始提振远程工作和学习数字之前很久。五月份，微软的Jeff Teper告诉VentureBeat团队“将比Windows更强大”。

Microsoft has been moving quickly to capitalize on the new remote work normal. At its Build 2020 developers conference in May, the company gave business developers new tools to build Teams apps. And its Inspire 2020 partners conference in July, Microsoft launched Dataflex, a relational database that lets business developers create, deploy, and manage Power Platform apps and chatbots without leaving Teams. More recently, at its Ignite 2020 IT Pros conference in September, the company talked about mental health and promised features like virtual commutes and Headspace meditation.

微软一直在迅速采取行动，利用新的远程工作常态。在5月份的Build 2020开发者大会上，该公司为业务开发者提供了构建团队应用程序的新工具。在7月份的Inspire 2020合作伙伴大会上，微软推出了Dataflex，这是一个关系数据库，允许业务开发人员在不离开团队的情况下创建、部署和管理Power平台应用程序和聊天机器人。最近，在9月份的Ignite 2020 IT Pros大会上，该公司谈到了心理健康，并承诺提供虚拟通勤和头顶空间冥想等功能。

On the call, Nadella highlighted how quickly features are being added to Teams. “We are accelerating our innovation for both first-line as well as knowledge workers, with over 100 new capabilities in the last six months,” he said. “Including breakout rooms, meeting recaps, shift scheduling, and large scale digital events, up to 20,000 participants. To help people transcend both time and distance, employee health and wellbeing is a top concern for every CEO. We are innovating with new experiences to help people prioritize wellbeing in the flow of work. New insights in Teams provides personalized recommended actions, making it easier for employees to create healthy work habits and for leaders to build high performing teams.”

在电话会议上，纳德拉强调了团队添加功能的速度有多快。他说：“我们正在加快一线和知识型员工的创新，在过去的六个月里有超过100个新的能力。”包括分组会议室、会议重述、排班和大型数字活动在内，最多可参加20000人。为了帮助人们超越时间和距离，员工的健康和福祉是每个CEO最关心的问题。我们正在用新的体验进行创新，帮助人们在工作流程中优先考虑福祉。团队中的新洞察力提供了个性化的推荐行动，让员工更容易养成健康的工作习惯，让领导者更容易建立高绩效的团队。

Microsoft was already adding features to Teams at a heavy clip, but the user growth in 2020 seems to shifted development into an even higher gear. Many have observed that Microsoft has gone from chasing Slack to paving the way with new capabilities. (In July, Slack filed an EU antitrust complaint against Microsoft for bundling Teams with Office.)

微软已经在大力增加团队的功能，但2020年的用户增长似乎将开发转移到了更高的档次。许多人观察到，微软已经从追逐Slake到用新功能铺平了道路。(7月份，Slake向欧盟提起反垄断诉讼，指控微软将团队与Office捆绑在一起。)。

Microsoft defines DAUs for Teams as “the maximum daily users performing an intentional action in a 24-hour period across the desktop client, mobile client, and web client. Intentional actions include sending or replying to a chat, joining a meeting, or opening a file in Teams. Passive actions like auto boot, minimizing a screen, or closing the app are not included.”

Microsoft将团队DAU定义为“在24小时内跨桌面客户端、移动客户端和Web客户端执行有意操作的每日最大用户数”。有意的操作包括发送或回复聊天、加入会议或在团队中打开文件。不包括自动启动、最小化屏幕或关闭应用程序等被动操作。“

In Microsoft’s Q3 2020, Nadella reported 258 million paid Office 365 seats. It’s difficult to gauge how many of these are also Teams users given the difference between daily and monthly users. Furthermore, Microsoft has a free version of Teams and Teams for consumers in the works. Still, given that Teams is primarily a business tool, it’s clear there’s still plenty of room for the app to grow.

在微软2020年第三季度，纳德拉报告了2.58亿个Office365付费席位。考虑到每日用户和每月用户之间的差异，很难衡量这些用户中有多少也是团队用户。此外，微软正在为消费者提供免费版本的团队和团队。尽管如此，考虑到团队主要是一种商业工具，很明显，这款应用程序仍有很大的增长空间。
