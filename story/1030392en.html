GMC悍马电动汽车VS特斯拉CyberTruck，Bollinger和Rivian

2020-10-22 09:11:32

For a while after  Tesla  launched its Cybertruck it looked like Elon Musk and Co. would be the only electric truck on the market. Those days are long past, and now the Big T's impossibly angular truck-thing looks like it will have some pretty stiff competition, both from legacy brands and new ones.

在特斯拉推出CyberTruck后的一段时间里，看起来埃隆·马斯克公司(Elon Musk And Co.)将是市场上唯一的电动卡车。那些日子早已一去不复返了，现在大T&39；的卡车产品看起来将面临一些相当激烈的竞争，既有来自传统品牌的，也有来自新品牌的。

In addition to trucks from startups like Bollinger and Rivian, the Cybertruck will have to battle the newly revealed GMC Hummer EV. Let's take a look at how all these EV trucks compare, not just to each other, but to a conventional, gas-powered truck like the Ford F-150, as well.

除了来自Bollinger和Rivian等初创公司的卡车外，CyberTruck还将不得不与新推出的GMC悍马电动汽车(GMC Hummer EV)展开竞争。让我们来看看所有这些电动汽车卡车之间的比较，不仅是彼此之间的比较，还有与福特F-150这样的传统汽油动力卡车的比较。

We'll start with horsepower and torque. The figures here are definitely stacked in favor of the electric vehicles, with the F-150's internal combustion engine options struggling to keep up. The Cybertruck's top-trim packs three motors, and while Mr. Musk and Co. didn't mention specific power figures, we feel confident that it will eclipse the Model S Performance. By how much? It's anyone's guess.

我们将从马力和扭矩开始。这里的数字肯定是有利于电动汽车的，F-150的内燃机选择难以跟上。CyberTruck的顶饰装有三个发动机，虽然马斯克和公司没有提到具体的功率数字，但我们相信它将使Model S的性能黯然失色。差多少钱？这是任何人的猜测。

GMC says the Hummer EV will pack a whopping 1,000 horsepower and 11,500 pound-feet of torque. Of course, that impressive torque number is more than likely axle torque, which is a significantly higher number than the SAE-certified spec the auto industry commonly uses. ( Let Engineering Explained's Jason Fenske tell you what we mean.) The actual SAE number will be available closer to the Hummer's launch in fall 2021 and GMC says it'll be enough to propel the EV to 60 mph in 3 seconds flat.

GMC表示，悍马EV将提供惊人的1000马力和11500磅英尺的扭矩。当然，这个令人印象深刻的扭矩数字超过了可能的轴扭矩，这是一个明显高于汽车行业通常使用的SAE认证规格的数字。(让工程部解释一下，杰森·芬斯克告诉你我们的意思。)。实际的SAE数字将在接近悍马2021年秋季推出时公布，通用汽车表示，这将足以推动电动汽车在3秒内达到每小时60英里。

The Bollinger B2 is a very different take on the electric truck formula. It's focused on having incredible off-road talents and is less concerned with outright power and torque. That isn't to say that it's not packing some real heat under its boxy body though. Bollinger claims a respectable 614 hp and 668 lb-ft of torque from its dual-motor setup.

布林格B2是对电动卡车配方的一种非常不同的看法。它专注于拥有令人难以置信的越野人才，而不是关注直接的动力和扭矩。不过，这并不是说它方方正正的身体下没有一些真正的热量。Bollinger声称其双电机设置提供了可观的614马力和668磅英尺的扭矩。

The Rivian R1T is probably a more fair comparison to Tesla's truck, seeing as it's much more on-road focused than the Bollinger. While the company will -- like Tesla -- offer several motor/battery combinations, the top-spec truck will make 750 hp and 829 lb-ft.

里维安R1T可能是一个更公平的比较特斯拉的卡车，因为它是更多的道路上的重点比布林格。虽然该公司将像特斯拉一样提供几种发动机/电池组合，但顶级卡车将生产750马力和829磅英尺。

Finally, we have the best-selling vehicle in America, the truck that seems to best suit the needs of both private customers and fleet buyers -- the Ford F-150. The most potent F-150 you can buy from the Blue Oval in terms of power is the Raptor, and its high-output turbocharged V6 makes a respectable 450 hp and 510 lb-ft of torque, but in this crowd, that's small potatoes.

最后，我们有美国最畅销的汽车，似乎最适合私人客户和车队买家需求的卡车--福特F-150。就动力而言，你可以从蓝色椭圆形购买到的最强大的F-150是猛禽，其高输出涡轮增压V6发动机的扭矩相当高，有450马力和510磅英尺的扭矩，但在这群人中，这只是小巫见大巫。

When it comes to people's perception of an electric vehicle, there is perhaps no figure more critical to a potential customer than range, even if in their daily life, most people won't go more than a couple dozen miles.

当谈到人们对电动汽车的看法时，对于潜在客户来说，或许没有比续航里程更重要的数字了，即使在日常生活中，大多数人的行驶里程不会超过几十英里。

Here, the Tesla Cybertruck, with its claimed 500 miles of range on the top-tier Tri-Motor version, looks to be king. Now, 500 miles is, frankly, an insane figure, and we'd have to imagine that carrying around a battery pack big enough to make that possible would adversely affect other aspects of the vehicle's performance, namely handling and braking.

在这里，特斯拉赛车(Tesla CyberTruck)看起来是王者，它声称自己的顶级三轮车续航里程为500英里。现在，坦率地说，500英里是一个疯狂的数字，我们不得不想象，随身携带一个足够大的电池组来实现这一点，会对车辆的其他性能产生不利影响，即操控和刹车。

Right now, GMC says the 2022 Hummer EV will have an estimated range of around 350 miles with its three-motor setup. GMC also says that the Hummer EV is compatible with 350-kW DC fast-charging, which can put about 100 miles of range into the battery in just 10 minutes.

目前，通用汽车表示，2022年悍马电动汽车的三马达设置估计续航里程将在350英里左右。通用汽车还表示，悍马电动汽车与350千瓦直流快速充电兼容，可以在短短10分钟内为电池充电约100英里。

After the Cybertruck comes Rivian's R1T. We thought that the brand's claims of a 400-mile range on its highest-spec model were the stuff of science fiction when it was announced a little over a year ago, but in the months since, the company has shown off its pack design and we've been impressed. Still, as with the Cybertruck, that's a lot of weight to be slinging around all the time.

赛博卡车之后是里维安的R1T。当该品牌在一年多一点前宣布时，我们认为该品牌声称其最高规格车型的续航里程为400英里是科幻小说，但在那之后的几个月里，该公司展示了其背包设计，给我们留下了深刻印象。尽管如此，就像CyberTruck一样，一直被抛来抛去的重量是很大的。

The Bollinger B2 is likely to be the most capable of our competitors off-road, but it's by far the least capable when it comes to cruising range. Despite having a reasonably sizable battery pack rated at 120 kWh, Bollinger is only estimating that it'll do 200 miles on a charge. Realistic? Yeah. Impressive? Nope.

布林格B2很可能是我们的竞争对手中越野能力最强的，但到目前为止，它在巡航范围内的能力是最差的。尽管有一个相当大的电池组，额定功率为120千瓦时，但布林格估计一次充电只能行驶200英里。现实吗？嗯。印象深刻吗？不是的。

Finally, the F-150 has a big gas tank and its engines are getting more efficient than ever. Range isn't really an issue, but just for posterity's sake, the new six-cylinder Power Stroke diesel in the F-150 manages a cruising range of around 850 miles on a single 26-gallon tank of diesel.

最后，F-150有一个很大的油箱，它的发动机比以往任何时候都更有效率。续航里程其实不是问题，但为了后代着想，F-150的新款六缸Power Stroke柴油在一箱26加仑的柴油上可以行驶约850英里。

Truck buyers are rightfully concerned about their prospective vehicle's ability to haul whatever and tow anything. That's why truck commercials on TV tend to be montages of stuff getting dumped into beds and ungainly-looking objects getting dragged behind vehicles. So, when it comes to hauling and towing, how do our competitors do?

卡车购买者理所当然地担心他们未来的车辆是否有能力拖曳任何东西。这就是为什么电视上的卡车广告往往是物品被扔到床上，难看的物体被拖到车辆后面的蒙太奇画面。那么，当谈到拖车和拖车时，我们的竞争对手做得怎么样呢？

Well, in the towing column, we have the Tesla claiming to be able to lug 14,000 pounds in Tri-Motor trim. That handily beats out the Ford F-150, which, in its most capable format -- two-wheel drive, SuperCrew, 3.5-liter EcoBoost -- can haul 13,200 pounds. Next would be the Rivian, which claims 11,000 pounds and the Bollinger, once again in last place, with the ability to tow just 7,500 pounds.

嗯，在拖曳柱中，我们有一辆特斯拉声称能够拖曳14,000磅的三轮马达装饰物。这轻而易举地击败了福特F-150，后者最强大的形式-两轮驱动，SuperCrew，3.5升EcoBoost-可以运载13,200磅。接下来是里维安号，它声称重达11000磅，而波林格号，同样排在最后，只能拖拽7500磅。

Payload is a little trickier to talk about because only Ford and Bollinger list their numbers, but that doesn't mean we can't speculate. So, Ford's most capable configuration can take 3,270 pounds in its bed. The Bollinger dwarfs that figure with a claimed capacity of 5,000 pounds.

有效载荷是一个有点棘手的讨论，因为只有福特和波林格列出了他们的数字，但这并不意味着我们不能推测。因此，福特最强大的配置可以在床上负重3270磅。布林格号称载重量为5000磅，令这一数字相形见绌。

The Ford is able to gain ground back by being the only truck available with an eight-foot bed. The rest of the competitors can only muster 6.5-feet of bed length at best, though the Bollinger can cheat a little and expand its bed space by intruding into the cab, and that nets it 8 feet, 2 inches of bed length.

福特是唯一一辆配备8英尺床的卡车，因此能够夺回市场份额。其他参赛者最多只能聚集6.5英尺的床长，尽管布林格可以稍微作弊，通过闯入驾驶室来扩大床位空间，这会让它的床长达到8英尺2英寸。

Tesla claims a bed (or "vault") length of 6.5-feet and a total storage capacity of 100 cubic feet, but offers no weight capacity and doesn't elaborate on whether that 100 cubic feet of storage includes the front trunk. To its credit, Tesla's bed is capable of being fully sealed and locked, thanks to a sliding cover.

特斯拉声称床(或保险库)长度为6.5英尺，总存储容量为100立方英尺，但没有提供重量容量，也没有详细说明这100立方英尺的存储空间是否包括前后备箱。值得称赞的是，由于有滑盖，特斯拉的床能够完全密封和锁定。

Rivian's R1T doesn't offer a bed length figure nor a bed volume. It does tell us that its unique gear pass-through tunnel and front-trunk together have a volume of 23.5 cubic feet If we had to hazard a guess, we'd say that Rivian's bed is smaller than that of a Honda Ridgeline, so don't plan on carrying full sheets of plywood if you opt for the Rivian. Though, similar to Tesla, it has a powered, locking tonneau cover for its bed.

里维安的R1T既不提供床长数字，也不提供床容。它确实告诉我们，里维安独一无二的齿轮直通隧道和前后备箱总共有23.5立方英尺的体积。如果我们必须大胆猜测的话，我们会说里维安的床比本田Ridgeline的床小，所以如果你选择里维安，就不要打算搬运整张胶合板。不过，与特斯拉相似的是，它的床上有一个动力十足的锁定托尼奥式床罩。

GMC has not yet released any towing or payload estimates for the Hummer EV. That said, the three marker lights on the top of the vehicle shown in the original teaser lead us to believe it'll be a big boy, so expect competitive numbers.

通用汽车尚未发布任何悍马电动汽车的拖曳或有效载荷估计。也就是说，在最初的预告片中，车辆顶部的三个标志灯让我们相信它会是一个大男孩，所以期待有竞争力的数字。

When it comes to price, it should come as no surprise that the Ford F-150 is king. It's available in an almost dizzying number of configurations, so odds are good that you'll be able to find an F-150 that does what you want within your budget. The cheapest ones start at just $28,495, with the most expensive Limited trim coming in at $67,485 to start. That's a massive delta, but choice is a good thing.

谈到价格，福特F-150是王者也就不足为奇了。它有几乎令人眼花缭乱的配置可供选择，所以很有可能你会找到一架F-150，它可以在你的预算内做你想要的事情。最便宜的起价仅为28,495美元，最昂贵的有限装饰起价为67,485美元。这是一个巨大的三角洲，但选择是一件好事。

Next up would be Tesla's Cybertruck, but we do have to add a caveat. See, the Big T is saying that the base model, rear-wheel drive Cybertruck will start at "under $40,000," but we've seen this kind of talk from Tesla before. Remember that $30,000 Model 3? It took years to arrive and only stuck around for what seemed like weeks. Tesla doesn't offer pricing for its more expensive Cybertruck trims, but we wouldn't be surprised to see them scrape six digits.

下一个是特斯拉的CyberTruck，但我们必须补充一个警告。你看，大T公司说，基本款后轮驱动的CyberTruck起价在4万美元以下，但我们以前也见过特斯拉的这种说法。还记得3万美元的Model3吗？它花了几年时间才到达，而且只停留了几个星期。特斯拉没有提供其更昂贵的赛博卡车内饰的定价，但我们不会对他们的售价达到6位数感到惊讶。

When it launches, the GMC Hummer EV will cost $112,595 including destination, but that's for the fancy Edition 1. Less powerful, lower-range, less expensive versions will follow in the coming years.

当GMC悍马电动汽车上市时，包括Destination在内的售价为112,595美元，但这只是华丽的Edition 1的价格。未来几年，动力较弱、续航能力较低、价格较低的版本将陆续推出。

Rivian's R1T will start at $69,000 and will go up from there. The base Rivian will likely be reasonably equipped but don't expect that 180 kWh battery pack, and its promised 400 miles of range at that entry price. Again, Rivian hasn't broken out pricing for its more expensive models, but we'd also expect to see them touch the low-$100,000 range.

Rivian&39；的R1T起价为6.9万美元，以后会更高。基地里维安可能会装备合理，但别指望180千瓦时的电池组，以及它承诺的400英里的射程在这个入门价格。里维安还没有公布其价格较高的机型的价格，但我们也预计它们会触及10万美元的低端。

And finally, in the last place once again is the Bollinger B2 with a starting sticker price of $125,000. That number ruined several keyboards around the office when we saw it, thanks to coffee spit takes, and it's no less shocking now. As we've stated before, you're getting a lot of off-road capability and rugged design for your money, but not a lot else.

最后，最后一款是起步价为125,000美元的Bollinger B2。当我们看到这个数字时，它毁掉了办公室里的几个键盘，这要归功于咖啡吐痰，现在它同样令人震惊。正如我们以前说过的，你将获得大量的越野能力和坚固耐用的设计，但不会有太多其他的东西。

One thing we opted not to discuss, or weigh against each truck was styling. Clearly, each of the vehicles we've talked about here has a very different idea of what a truck should look like, with Tesla's being the most, shall we say, dystopian. Will that turn traditional truck buyers away? That, like all our other questions, will likely be answered in time.

我们选择不讨论或权衡每辆卡车的一件事是款式。显然，我们在这里谈到的每一辆车对卡车应该是什么样子都有非常不同的想法，可以说，特斯拉是最反乌托邦的。这会让传统的卡车买家望而却步吗？这一点，就像我们所有的其他问题一样，很可能会及时得到回答。
