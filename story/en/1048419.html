New Version of Microsoft Office Won't Require You To Pay For a Subscription
新版本的Microsoft Office不需要您为订阅付费

2021-02-19 07:07:42

In a company blog post Thursday, Microsoft released more details about the new, flat-price version of its Office productivity software coming later this year. The company emphasized that while its main focus remains in its subscription offering,  Microsoft 365, it will release the one-time purchase Office 2021 for those who aren't ready to move to the cloud.

在周四的公司博客中，微软发布了有关其Office生产力软件的新的，统一价格版本的更多详细信息，该版本将于今年晚些时候发布。该公司强调，尽管其主要重点仍在其订阅产品Microsoft 365中，但它将为尚未准备好迁移到云中的用户发布一次性购买的Office 2021。

Office 2021 will arrive in two versions: one for commercial users, called Office LTSC (which stands for Long Term Servicing Channel), and one for personal use. Office LTSC will include enhanced accessibility features, performance improvements across Word, Excel and PowerPoint, and visual improvements, like dark mode support across apps. It's meant for specialty situations, as opposed to for an entire organization, such as process control devices on the manufacturing floor that are not connected to the internet.

Office 2021将提供两种版本：一种用于商业用户，称为Office LTSC（代表长期服务频道），另一种用于个人使用。 Office LTSC将包括增强的辅助功能，Word，Excel和PowerPoint的性能改进以及视觉上的改进，例如跨应用程序的暗模式支持。这是针对特殊情况的，而不是针对整个组织的情况，例如未连接到互联网的生产车间的过程控制设备。

More details about pricing and new features for the commercial version and the personal version will be announced when Office 2021 is closer to general availability. Both will have both Windows and Mac versions, and will ship with the OneNote app. They will also ship both 32- and 64-bit versions, according to the post. Microsoft will support the software for five years, and said it does not plan to change the price at the time of release.

当Office 2021即将面市时，将宣布有关商业版和个人版的定价和新功能的更多详细信息。两者都将同时具有Windows和Mac版本，并将随OneNote应用一起提供。他们还发布了32位和64位版本。微软将对该软件提供五年支持，并表示不打算在发布时更改价格。

In April, Microsoft transitioned Office 365 into Microsoft 365, a subscription service that added more features to the suite of software tools, but required a monthly payment of $7 for an individual plan or $10 for a family of up to six people. The company said that its main focus will continue to be on the cloud, but it understands that not everyone is ready to take that step. While you can use Microsoft 365 apps like Word, PowerPoint and Excel online for free with limited functionality, you'll need either a perpetual version or a subscription to take full advantage of their capabilities.

4月份，Microsoft将Office 365转换为Microsoft 365，这是一项订阅服务，为该软件工具套件添加了更多功能，但个人计划每月需要支付7美元，最多六个人的家庭则需要每月支付10美元。该公司表示，其主要重点将继续放在云上，但它了解并非所有人都愿意采取这一步骤。虽然您可以免费在线使用功能有限的Microsoft 365应用程序（如Word，PowerPoint和Excel），但您需要永久版本或订阅才能充分利用其功能。

Some wondered if Office 2019 would be the last perpetual version of the software, since Microsoft 365 came along. But then Microsoft announced its plan for a perpetual release of Office in a September  blog post.

一些人想知道，自Microsoft 365出现以来，Office 2019是否将是该软件的最后永久版本。但随后微软在9月的博客文章中宣布了永久发布Office的计划。
