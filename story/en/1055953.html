The ancient civilizations-namely Egyptian era created art with pigments from minerals, and colors like blue became more prominent in their art. Over the years, the color evolution continued while notable artists contributed heavily to art. Even the great Leonardo da Vinci explored color principles that are found in some of his works.

Since then, the history of color has evolved through exploration and scientific experiments. In the 18 th century, the color theory began to take shape. Finally, in 1704 Sir Isaac Newton did a breakthrough experiment with the prism, which stated that light is formed by combining different colors. Although some deemed it controversial because pure light was considered colorless, the breakthrough proved to be the foundation of the color theory that you have today. Experiments showed that red, yellow and blue are considered primary colors from which other colors can be derived. He also created an early version of the color wheel that changed the course of history and colors. Simultaneously, other concepts began to materialize that defined that the mixing of primary colors can lead to colors like violet, orange, and green. The transformation continued, thereby giving shape to the color wheel.

从那时起，颜色的历史通过探索和科学实验演变。在18世纪，颜色理论开始形成。最后，在1704年，艾萨克·纽尔顿爵士进行了突破性的棱镜，这使得光线通过组合不同的颜色而形成。虽然有些人认为它是有争议的，因为纯光被认为是无色的，所以突破被证明是你今天的颜色理论的基础。实验表明，红色，黄色和蓝色被认为是可以导出其他颜色的原色。他还创建了早期版本的色轮，改变了历史和颜色的过程。同时，其他概念开始实现定义原色的混合可以导致紫罗兰色，橙色和绿色的颜色。变换继续，从而为色轮提供形状。

The addition of tertiary colors like yellow-orange, red-orange, red-purple, blue-purple, blue-green, and yellow-green complete the color wheel- thanks to the Munsell Hue Circle. A.H Munsell was another notable personality that took the works of Sir Isaac Newton into a different dimension. His works defined that the two-dimensional hue circle was appropriate for describing the single dimension of color. Munsell was the first to represent color in uniform dimensions in three attributes of color-hue, value, and Chroma in the three-dimensional color space. Today, the color wheel assist designers in creating appealing designs with the use of color palettes that are formed with the underlying color theory.

加入三级颜色，如黄橙色，红橙色，红色，蓝紫色，蓝绿色和黄绿色，依靠Munsell Hue Circle填充色轮。 A.H Munsell是另一个非常值得注意的个性，使艾萨克牛顿爵士的作品成为一个不同的维度。他的作品定义了二维色调圈适合描述颜色的单尺寸。 Munsell是第一个代表三维颜色空间中的三个属性的均匀尺寸的颜色。如今，色轮辅助设计师在使用与底层颜色理论形成的颜色调色板使用的彩色调色板创造吸引人的设计。

To understand color and the theory, let’s refresh what color categories exist in the color wheel.

要了解颜色和理论，让我们刷新颜色轮中存在的颜色类别。

Primary Colors are created by combining two or more colors. Typically, the primary colors are red, blue, and yellow.

通过组合两种或更多种颜色来创建原色。通常，原色是红色，蓝色和黄色。

Secondary Colors include orange, purple and green. As it is evident from the historical explanations of color theory, secondary colors are created by combining two or more primary colors.

二次颜色包括橙色，紫色和绿色。由于从色彩理论的历史解释中可以看出，通过组合两个或更多原色来创建二次颜色。

Tertiary Colors are created by the mix of primary colors as well as secondary colors. Some of these colors are magenta, vermillion, violet, teal, amber, and chartreuse.

三级颜色是由原色的混合以及二次颜色创建的。其中一些颜色是洋红色，朱米，紫罗兰色，小野野，琥珀和沙粒。

As you explore the color wheel, you will discover that it represents the primary, secondary and tertiary colors and the hues, tints, and the various shades and tones of these colors. On the visualization of each color, you can relate different colors closely associated on the color scale. The color wheel acts as the guide to the color theory that enables the designers to derive color palettes that are unique and appealing.

当您探索色轮时，您将发现它代表了主要，次级和三级颜色以及这些颜色的各种色调和色调。在每种颜色的可视化上，您可以将不同颜色与颜色刻度密切相关。色轮作为颜色理论的指南，使设计人员能够推导出独特和吸引力的颜色调色板。

Hue is a basic color term that specifies the color pigment and can be rightfully termed as the original color.

色调是一种基本颜色术语，指定彩色颜料，可将其定制称为原始颜色。

Chroma denotes color purity. When a hue comprises high Chroma, it signifies no black, white, or grey in it. On the other hand, if there is any existence of black or grey, it merely reduces the Chroma. Designers need to understand that you should avoid using hues that have similar Chroma. Likewise, it would be best to opt for hues with Chroma similar to a few shades away.

色度表示颜色纯度。当一个色调包括高色度时，它在其中表示没有黑色，白色或灰色。另一方面，如果存在黑色或灰色的存在，则它仅减少了色度。设计师需要了解您应该避免使用具有相似色度的色调。同样，最好选择用与几个色调类似的色调选择色调。

Saturation defines the appearance of hue in different lighting conditions. Therefore, it is highly recommended to use saturation levels similar in nature for achieving color consistency in your designs.

饱和度在不同的照明条件下定义了色调的外观。因此，强烈建议使用本质上类似的饱和度水平，以实现设计中的颜色一致性。

Tones are the resultant color output that is created when grey is added to a hue. Tones have a software appeal and look dull. Therefore it is not suitable for all designs. However, when a project requires a vintage effect to be added in its designs, the addition of tones could make the designs more elegant.

音调是将灰色添加到色调时产生的颜色输出。音调有一个软件吸引力，看起来沉闷。因此，它不适合所有设计。但是，当项目需要在其设计中添加复古效果时，添加音调可以使设计更优雅。

Shade results in the darkening of color as it refers to the amount of black present in the hue.

阴影导致颜色的变暗，因为它是指色调中存在的黑色量。

A tint is the opposite of shade as it refers to the amount of white added to a color. Typical uses of tints are seen for feminine designs and common in websites associated with parenting or toddlers.

色调与阴影相反，因为它是指添加到颜色的白色的量。对于女性设计和与育儿或幼儿相关联的网站，看出色调的典型用途。

Analogous color palettes exist next to each other on the color wheel. Such color schemes are used when you want to create a design without any contrasting colors. For website designs, using analogous colors means that the web page’s background has a similar color scheme to maintain color harmony.

在色轮上彼此相邻的类似颜色调色板。当您想要创建设计而没有任何对比颜色的设计时，使用此类颜色方案。对于网站设计，使用类似颜色意味着网页的背景具有类似的颜色方案来维持色彩和谐。

Complementary color palettes comprise colors that are situated opposite of each other on the color wheel. It is used for designs to provide a contrast effect.

互补的颜色调色板包括在色轮上彼此相对的颜色。它用于设计以提供对比效果。

The split-complementary color is based on a color on one side and two other colors on the opposite side of the color wheel.
