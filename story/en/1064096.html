Facebook says it will show creators who are using its new tools how much Apple, Google, and others take as a cut of their earnings, through a payout interface
Facebook表示，它将展示正在使用其新工具的创造者，通过支付界面，使用其新工具的苹果公司，谷歌和其他人作为削减其收益。

2021-06-08 01:12:16

Less than two hours before Apple’s big Worldwide Developers Conference, Facebook CEO Mark Zuckerberg announced that the company would be launching a new interface for creators that shows how different fees affect their earnings on the platform. The announcement comes as Apple is under intense scrutiny for its App Store fees.

Facebook首席执行官Mark Zuckerberg在苹果大的全球开发人员大会前不到两个小时，该公司将为展示不同费用对平台的收益的创造者推出新界面。该公告来源于Apple对其应用商店费用强烈审查。

Zuckerberg wasn’t clear as to when the new interface would be launching beyond saying that there’d be “more to come soon.”

Zuckerberg不清楚，当新界面将推动以外，以便“更多来即将到来”。

Zuckerberg also says the company will keep paid online events, fan subscriptions, badges, and “our upcoming independent news products” free for creators until 2023. This is an extension of a policy announced in August. When Facebook introduced the events feature last year, it had promised that it wouldn’t collect fees until “at least” 2021.

扎克伯格还表示，该公司将在2023年之前为创造者提供付费的在线活动，粉丝订阅，徽章和“我们即将到来的独立新闻产品”。这是8月宣布的政策的延伸。去年Facebook推出了活动功能时，它承诺，直到“至少”2021，它不会收取费用。

The company eventually plans to introduce a revenue share, Zuckerberg says — but when it does, it will be “less than the 30% that Apple and others take.”

该公司最终计划引入收入份额，Zuckerberg说 - 但是当它确实时，它将是“少于苹果和其他人的30％”。

Facebook and Apple are also embroiled in a public spat over Apple’s App Tracking Transparency feature introduced with iOS 14.5. Facebook has come out strongly against the change, taking out full-page newspaper ads criticizing the changes ahead of their eventual release, and more recently, it asked users for permission to track them to help keep Facebook and Instagram “free of charge.”

Facebook和Apple也嵌造在Apple的App的App Tracking透明度特征上的公共蜘蛛中嵌入了IOS 14.5。 Facebook已经强烈反对变革，取出全页报纸广告批评他们最终发布的变化，最近，它要求用户允许跟踪它们以帮助保持Facebook和Instagram“免费的”免费“。
