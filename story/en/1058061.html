Microsoft makes the Surface Laptop 4 official, offers choice of AMD or Intel
微软使表面笔记本电脑4官方，提供AMD或英特尔的选择

2021-04-13 21:11:03

After recent leaks suggested an imminent arrival, Microsoft has officially launched the latest entry in its line of Surface PCs: the Surface Laptop 4.

近期泄漏建议迫在眉睫的抵达后，微软已正式在其表面PC系列中开展了最新进入：表面笔记本电脑4。

The new laptop starts at $999 and will be available to purchase in the US, Canada, and Japan as of today, with shipping beginning on April 15. Microsoft says more markets will follow in the “coming weeks.” In the US, those who buy the Surface Laptop 4 through Microsoft or Best Buy before April 15 will get a pair of Surface Earbuds headphones bundled in at no extra cost.

新款笔记本电脑以999美元的价格开头，并可在美国，加拿大和日本购买，截至今日，从4月15日开始发货。微软表示更多市场将在“未来几周”中追随。在美国，在4月15日之前通过微软或百思买购买表面笔记本电脑4的人将获得一对表面耳塞耳机，无需额外费用。

As for the device itself, the Surface Laptop 4 is more of a refinement than a reinvention of the previous Surface Laptop 3, which launched in late 2019. That laptop’s build quality was generally well-regarded, and the design here is more or less identical to before. There are still two models, one with a 13.5-inch touch display and one with a 15-inch touch display. Both have a pixel density of 201 pixels per inch and a taller-than-usual 3:2 aspect ratio that’s well suited for reading webpages and documents. The weight (about 2.8 lbs for the 13.5-inch model, about 3.4 lbs for the 15-inch model) and dimensions (12.1x8.8x0.57 inches for the 13.5-inch model, 13.4x9.6x0.58 inches for the 15-inch model) are unchanged.

至于器件本身，表面笔记本电脑4更像是在2019年底推出的前表面笔记本电脑3的重生。该笔记本电脑的构建质量普遍认为，这里的设计或多或少相同到之前。还有两种型号，一个带13.5英寸的触摸显示器，一个带15英寸触摸显示屏。两者都具有每英寸的201个像素的像素密度和更高于3：2的宽高比，非常适合读取网页和文档。重量（约2.8磅为13.5英寸型号，为15英寸型号为3.4磅）和尺寸（12.1x8.8x0.57英寸，为13.5英寸型号，15.4x9.6x0.58英寸为15 -inch模型）不变。

Each SKU is made from aluminum, save for a version of the 13.5-inch model that coats the keyboard deck in Alcantara fabric. There are a variety of color options available, including a new “Ice Blue” finish. There’s still a glass trackpad and a backlit keyboard with 1.3 mm of key travel, which should remain comfortable.

每个SKU都是由铝制成的，另存为13.5英寸型号的版本，可在Alcantara面料中涂上键盘甲板。有各种颜色选项可用，包括新的“冰蓝色”完成。仍然是一个玻璃轨道板和一个带有1.3毫米的钥匙旅行的背光键盘，这应该保持舒适。

In terms of connectivity, the Surface Laptop 4 doesn’t change Microsoft’s long-held aversion to Thunderbolt 3 or 4 ports, so there’s still no option for connecting an external GPU. Instead, the selection is similar to that of the Surface Laptop 3: one USB-C 3.1 Gen 2 port, a USB-A port, a 3.5 mm headphone jack, and Microsoft’s proprietary Surface Connect port.

在连接方面，表面笔记本电脑4不会将Microsoft的长期厌恶更改为Thunderbolt 3或4端口，因此仍然没有连接外部GPU的选项。相反，选择类似于表面笔记本电脑3：一个USB-C 3.1 Gen 2端口，USB-A端口，3.5毫米耳机插孔和Microsoft专有表面连接端口。

Similarly, the front-facing webcam remains limited to a 720p resolution. There’s still no fingerprint reader, but the front-facing camera still supports Windows Hello sign-ins through facial recognition. Dual far-field microphones remain built-in, and there’s technically support for Dolby Atmos audio now, though we wouldn’t expect especially powerful surround sound from most laptop speakers. Both the 13.5- and 15-inch models support Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.

类似地，前面的网络摄像头仍然限于720p分辨率。仍然没有指纹读卡器，但前面的相机仍然通过面部识别支持Windows Hello标志。双场麦克风仍然内置，现在我们对杜比Atmos音频进行了技术支持，尽管我们不会期待大多数笔记本电脑扬声器的尤其是强大的环绕声。 13.5和15英寸型号都支持Wi-Fi 6和蓝牙5。

The Surface Laptop 4’s biggest updates are internal. The Surface Laptop 3 introduced AMD chips to the Surface lineup in the form of lightly customized “Microsoft Surface Edition” versions of Ryzen 3000 series mobile processors. These were only available in the 15-inch model, however. The smaller and less expensive 13.5-inch variant only featured Intel processors, which have generally lagged behind AMD in terms of price-to-performance ratio in recent years. With the Surface Laptop 4, Microsoft is offering AMD and Intel chips in both 13.5- and 15-inch models. The new catch is that the AMD-based models have Microsoft Surface Edition variants of last-gen Ryzen 4000 APUs based on the Zen 2 architecture, not the latest and greatest Ryzen 5000 series mobile processors.

表面笔记本电脑4的最大更新是内部的。表面笔记本电脑3以明确定制的“Microsoft Surface Edition”版本的Ryzen 3000系列移动处理器的形式引入了AMD芯片。然而，这些仅适用于15英寸模型。较小且较低昂贵的13.5英寸变体仅特色的英特尔处理器，通常在近年来的价格与性价比方面一般落后于AMD。使用表面笔记本电脑4，微软在13.5和15英寸和15英寸型号中提供AMD和Intel芯片。新捕获是基于AMD的模型基于ZEN 2架构的Last-Gen Ryzen 4000 Apus的Microsoft Seight Edition Valiants，而不是最新和最伟大的Ryzen 5000系列移动处理器。

Specifically, Microsoft says the 13.5-inch model uses a 6-core/12-thread Ryzen 5 4680U with Radeon RX Graphics, while the 15-inch model uses an 8-core/16-thread Ryzen 7 4980U with Radeon Graphics. The company will also sell a 13.5-inch model equipped with the latter APU, but only in commercial channels. Microsoft did not specify exactly how it has updated the chips but said its tweaks primarily focus on optimizing battery life for this form factor.

具体而言，微软表示，13.5英寸模型使用带Radeon RX图形的6核/ 12线Ryzen 5 4680U，而15英寸型号使用带有Radeon Graphics的8核/ 16线Ryzen 7 4980U。该公司还将出售13.5英寸型号，配备后者APU，但仅限于商业渠道。微软没有准确地指定它如何更新筹码，但表示其调整主要专注于优化这种形式因素的电池寿命。

The options are a bit more up to date in the Intel-based models, which come with 11th-gen Tiger Lake mobile chips. The 13.5-inch Surface Laptop 4 is available with a 4-core/8-thread Core i5-1135G7 or 4-core/8-thread Core i7-1185G7, while the 15-inch model comes equipped with the latter. All of these use Intel’s Iris Xe integrated graphics. (A laptop with this form factor is not designed for gaming, to be clear.) Another 13.5-inch model with a quad-core Core i5-1145G7 will be available for commercial users. In general, Microsoft claims the chip upgrades will result in “up to 70 percent faster” performance than that of the Surface Laptop 3, with particularly improved multitasking capabilities. That will likely vary by model, though; we'll have to put the device through its paces and compare it to competing devices before declaring how much that's worth.

在基于英特尔的模型中，选项有点达到了更新的型号，其中包含11个虎湖移动筹码。 13.5英寸表面笔记本电脑4提供4芯/ 8螺纹芯I5-1135G7或4芯/ 8线核心I7-1185G7，而15英寸型号配备了后者。所有这些都使用英特尔的Iris XE集成图形。 （具有此表格因子的笔记本电脑不设计用于游戏，清晰。）商业用户将提供另外13.5英寸型号，具有四核核心I5-1145G7。通常，Microsoft声称芯片升级将导致“高达70％的更快”性能，而不是表面笔记本电脑3，特别提高了多任务处理功能。然而，这可能因模式而有所不同;我们＆＃39; LL必须通过它的步骤将设备放在竞争设备上，并在宣布有多少＆＃39;值得。

It’s worth noting that Microsoft will offer Surface Laptop 4 configurations with up to 32GB of RAM and up to 1TB of SSD storage, too, both of which are twice as high as the Surface Laptop 3's maximum. The SSD is now removable, as it is on the Surface Pro 7+. However, only the Intel models will reach that ceiling; the AMD-based models will instead top out at 16GB of RAM and a 512GB SSD. Microsoft says a 13.5-inch Ryzen 5 configuration with 16GB of RAM and a 256GB SSD won’t be available until roughly two months after the notebook’s launch, either. As noted above, the other core upgrade Microsoft is touting is improved battery life. The 13.5-inch Ryzen 5 models are advertised to reach up to 19 hours of runtime with average use, while the other SKUs are said to get between 16.5 and 17.5 hours of life. Again, it’s best to take these claims with a grain of salt until the device is out in the wild. But for reference, the company promised up to 11.5 hours of battery life with the Surface Laptop 3, so there should be at least some improvement. Otherwise, the new laptop will utilize the same 65W power supply as before. Aside from the Surface Laptop 4, Microsoft is also rolling out a handful of new productivity-focused accessories, including a variant of its noise-canceling Surface Headphones 2 optimized for Microsoft Teams; wireless and USB chat headsets; a small USB-C speaker aimed at conference calls; and a 1080p webcam. The headphones will be available to business and education channels today, while the other accessories will begin shipping in June.
