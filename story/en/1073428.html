New Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness trailer is a psychedelic treat
《疯狂的多元宇宙》预告片中的新怪人医生是一种迷幻疗法

2022-02-15 13:13:26

Marvel Studios dropped a spectacularly psychedelic new trailer for the upcoming Doctor Strange in the Multiverse of Madness just as the Super Bowl was getting underway.

就在超级碗开赛之际，漫威电影公司为即将上映的《疯狂的多元宇宙中的奇怪博士》推出了一部惊艳迷幻的新预告片。

As I've written previously, we know that the new Dr. Strange film is set after the events of Spider-Man:  No Way Home,  WandaVision, and the first season of Loki. The latter introduced us to the Time Variant Authority (TVA), with our mischievous protagonist encountering multiple versions of himself from different timelines, including a female incarnation named Sylvie (Sophia di Martino). The finale featured a confrontation with a variant of Kang the Conqueror, who had been controlling the Sacred Timeline—until an impulsive act by Sylvie unleashed more timelines than the TVA could prune, destabilizing the multiverse.

正如我'；我之前写过，我们知道新的Dr.Strange电影是在《蜘蛛侠：回家无路》、《万达视》和《洛基》第一季之后拍摄的。后者向我们介绍了时变权威（TVA），我们淘气的主人公遇到了来自不同时间线的多个版本，包括一个名为西尔维（Sophia di Martino）的女性化身。最后一集是与征服者康的一个变体的对峙，他一直控制着神圣的时间线，直到西尔维的冲动行为释放出比TVA所能删减的时间线更多的时间线，从而破坏了多元宇宙的稳定。

What If...? introduced us to Strange Supreme, an evil alternate version of Steven Strange who gained enormous power by absorbing other mystical beings, thereby tearing reality apart. The Watcher eventually recruits Strange Supreme to be one of the Guardians of the Multiverse who collectively defeat Ultron. As for the events of No Way Home, Strange's multiverse meddling in that film is going to have unforeseen repercussions that will likely play out in Multiverse of Madness.

如果…怎么办。。。？介绍给我们奇怪的至高无上，一个邪恶的替代版本史蒂文奇怪谁获得了巨大的权力，通过吸收其他神秘的存在，从而撕裂现实。《守望者》最终招募了奇异至尊作为多元宇宙的守护者之一，共同击败了奥创。至于没有回家的路，奇怪'；在这部电影中的多重宇宙干预将产生不可预见的影响，可能会在《疯狂的多重宇宙》中上演。

From the official synopsis: "After the events of Avengers: Endgame, Dr. Stephen Strange continues his research on the Time Stone. But an old friend-turned-enemy puts an end to his plans and causes Strange to unleash unspeakable evil. Journey into the unknown with Doctor Strange, who, with the help of mystical allies both old and new, traverses the mind-bending and dangerous alternate realities of the Multiverse to confront a mysterious new adversary."

从官方大纲来看："；在《复仇者：终局》之后，斯蒂芬·斯特兰奇博士继续他对时间之石的研究。但是一个老朋友变成了敌人，他终止了自己的计划，并导致奇怪的人释放出难以形容的邪恶。与斯特兰奇博士一起进入未知世界，他在新旧神秘盟友的帮助下，穿越多元宇宙中令人费解和危险的交替现实，面对一个神秘的新对手"

We saw the "old friend" in question when the first trailer dropped in December: Karl Mondo (Chiwetel Ejiofor), Strange's fellow Master of the Mystic Arts, who grew disillusioned with the order and who now hunts down other mages to take away their power. Strange seeks out Wanda Maximoff/The Scarlet Witch (Elizabeth Olsen) as an ally as reality begins to unravel. Benedict Wong is back as Wong, now the Sorcerer Supreme (because Strange disappeared for five years in the wake of the Snappening), and we catch a glimpse of Rachel McAdams' Dr. Christine Palmer in a wedding dress.

我们看到了"；老朋友"；12月第一部预告片落下的时间有疑问：卡尔·蒙多（Chiwetel Ejiofor），奇怪'；他是一位神秘艺术大师，对秩序的幻想破灭了，现在他追捕其他法师以夺走他们的力量。当现实开始瓦解时，Strange寻找万达·马克西莫夫/猩红女巫（伊丽莎白·奥尔森饰）作为盟友。本尼迪克特·王（Benedict Wong）以王（Wong）的身份回归，现在是最高巫师（因为奇怪的人在拍摄照片后消失了五年），我们瞥见了瑞秋·麦克亚当斯（Rachel McAdams）'；克里斯汀·帕尔默医生穿着婚纱。

This new trailer has a good chunk of the same footage, but it amps up the action as creatures from other dimensions begin to appear—ruining Christine's wedding in the process, it seems. We see Mondo telling Strange that he must be held accountable for meddling with the multiverse, followed by scenes of Strange in chains being brought before some kind of tribunal.

这部新的预告片有很多相同的片段，但它放大了动作，因为来自其他维度的生物开始出现在克里斯汀的废墟上'；看来婚礼正在进行中。我们看到Mondo告诉Strange，他必须为干涉多元宇宙负责，然后是Strange被带到某种法庭的场景。

We get a better look at Strange Supreme ("Things got out of hand..."), and it looks like Wanda, too, might encounter another version of herself, which could cause her to question her loyalty to Strange. "You break the rules, you become a hero," Wanda says. "I do it, I become the enemy. That doesn't seem fair." Plus, we get the briefest glimpse of America Chavez (Xochitl Gomez), a teenager who can travel between dimensions. That's a handy power to have when reality is unraveling.

我们可以更好地了解奇怪的Supreme（"；事情失控了…"；），看起来万达也可能会遇到另一个版本的自己，这可能会让她质疑自己对陌生人的忠诚"你违反规则，你就成了英雄，"；万达说"我做到了，我就成了敌人。这并不意味着'；这似乎不公平" 此外，我们还可以简单地看到美国查韦斯（Xochitl Gomez），一个可以在不同维度之间旅行的青少年。那'；当现实正在瓦解时，它是一种方便的力量。

As for that "mysterious adversary"—who knows? But the cast also includes Michael Stuhlbarg as Nicodemus West, a rival surgeon acquaintance of Strange. He appeared briefly in the first trailer, standing just behind Strange in a tuxedo. Fans of the comics will recognize him as the surgeon who operated on Strange after his accident but who was unable to repair the nerves, putting Strange on the road to becoming Sorcerer Supreme. In the comics, West also trains under the Ancient One, and ultimately the two clash when West steals a special elixir—an encounter that proved fatal for West. It's pure speculation, but the new trailer has a closeup of what appears to be mismatched eyes, and West was born with heterochromia (a very groovy mutation, as the young Professor X would say).
