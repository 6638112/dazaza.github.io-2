MacOS大压力

2020-11-13 15:13:32

macOS Big Sur, the latest version of the world’s most advanced desktop operating system, is now available to Mac users as a free software update. Big Sur introduces a beautiful redesign and is packed with new enhancements for key apps including Safari, Messages, and Maps, as well as new privacy features. And Big Sur has been engineered, down to its core, to take full advantage of all the power of the M1 chip to make the macOS experience even better for the new 13-inch MacBook Pro, MacBook Air, and Mac mini. The combination of Big Sur and M1 truly takes the Mac to a whole new level with incredible capabilities, efficiency, and more apps than ever before, while maintaining everything users love about macOS.

MacOS Big Sur是世界上最先进的桌面操作系统的最新版本，现在Mac用户可以免费获得软件更新。Big Sur引入了漂亮的重新设计，并为Safari、Messages和Maps等关键应用程序提供了新的增强功能，以及新的隐私功能。Big Sur经过精心设计，其核心是充分利用M1芯片的所有功能，为新的13英寸MacBook Pro、MacBook Air和Mac mini带来更好的MacOS体验。Big Sur和M1的结合确实将Mac带到了一个全新的水平，拥有令人难以置信的功能、效率和比以往任何时候都多的应用程序，同时保持了用户喜欢的MacOS的一切。

Big Sur delivers a spacious new design that makes navigation easier, while putting more controls at users’ fingertips. From the curvature of window corners to the palette of colors and materials, the thoughtful design will benefit everyone from first-time Mac owners to the most demanding pros. Dock icons retain their Mac personality while also appearing more consistent across the Apple ecosystem for a more cohesive look and feel. And a new design for apps makes it easier for users to stay focused on their content and interact with apps. Buttons and controls appear when needed and recede when they are not — reducing visual complexity and bringing the most relevant content to the forefront.

Big Sur提供了一个宽敞的新设计，让导航变得更容易，同时让用户可以在指尖上获得更多的控制。从窗角的弧度到颜色和材料的调色板，考虑周到的设计将使每个人受益，从第一次拥有Mac电脑的人到要求最苛刻的专业人士。Dock图标保留了Mac的个性，同时在整个苹果生态系统中看起来更一致，外观和感觉更有凝聚力。应用程序的新设计使用户更容易专注于他们的内容并与应用程序互动。按钮和控件在需要的时候会出现，在不需要的时候就会消失--降低了视觉复杂性，并将最相关的内容放在了最重要的位置。

The all-new Control Center gives users quick access to controls for Do Not Disturb, displays, keyboard brightness, and more, right from the menu bar. Users can see more relevant information at a glance in the updated Notification Center, including interactive notifications and redesigned widgets.

全新的控制中心让用户可以直接从菜单栏快速访问免打扰、显示、键盘亮度等控制功能。用户可以在更新的通知中心一目了然地看到更多相关信息，包括互动通知和重新设计的小部件。

The biggest update to Safari since its original launch in 2003 makes the world’s fastest browser even faster while maintaining industry-leading battery life. Safari’s JavaScript engine helps it outperform other browsers on Mac and PC, and loads frequently visited sites an average of 50 percent faster than Chrome. And on new Macs with M1, Safari is now up to 1.5x speedier at running JavaScript and nearly 2x more responsive. Streaming on popular platforms is more vivid and clear with the addition of both YouTube and Netflix in 4K HDR. Redesigned tabs make navigating Safari faster and more efficient by showing more tabs onscreen, revealing page previews when hovering over tabs and displaying favicons by default for easy identification. 1

Safari自2003年首次发布以来的最大更新，使这款世界上最快的浏览器在保持行业领先电池续航能力的同时，速度更快。Safari的JavaScript引擎帮助它超越了Mac和PC上的其他浏览器，加载经常访问的网站的速度平均比Chrome快50%。在安装了M1的新Mac电脑上，Safari现在运行JavaScript的速度提高了1.5倍，响应速度提高了近2倍。随着YouTube和Netflix在4K HDR中的加入，流行平台上的流媒体更加生动和清晰。重新设计的选项卡在屏幕上显示更多的选项卡，当鼠标悬停在选项卡上时显示页面预览，并在默认情况下显示便于识别的收藏图标，从而使Safari导航更快、更有效率。1个。

Safari offers an even more personal experience while browsing the web, including a new customizable start page where users can add a personal background image, Reading List, iCloud Tabs, and more. With built-in translation, Safari can detect and translate entire web pages from seven languages, and new Safari extensions are easily discoverable for download through the Mac App Store with categories including editorial spotlights and top charts.

Safari在浏览网页时提供了更个人化的体验，包括一个新的可定制的开始页面，用户可以在其中添加个人背景图片、阅读列表、iCloud标签等等。有了内置翻译，Safari可以检测并翻译七种语言的整个网页，新的Safari扩展很容易通过Mac App Store下载，类别包括编辑聚光灯和排行榜。

Building on the browser’s pioneering user privacy features, Safari comes with an innovative new Privacy Report that gives users an instant snapshot of trackers Safari prevents from profiling them on the web. New tools like data breach password monitoring keep an eye out for saved passwords that may have been compromised — all without revealing a user’s account information, not even to Apple. And to prevent extensions from having access to the websites users visit, the tabs they have open, and, in some cases, even what they type, Safari prompts users to choose when and which websites can work with a Safari extension.

Safari基于该浏览器开创性的用户隐私功能，提供了一个创新的新隐私报告，为用户提供了Safari禁止在网络上分析他们的追踪器的即时快照。像数据泄露密码监控这样的新工具会密切关注可能已经被泄露的保存的密码--所有这些都不会泄露用户的账户信息，甚至不会泄露给苹果。为了防止扩展程序访问用户访问的网站、打开的标签，甚至在某些情况下，甚至是他们输入的内容，Safari会提示用户选择何时以及哪些网站可以使用Safari扩展程序。

With an incredible boost in performance, amazing efficiency, new customization options, and enhanced privacy features, Safari delivers the best browsing experience on the Mac.

凭借令人难以置信的性能提升、惊人的效率、新的定制选项和增强的隐私功能，Safari在Mac上提供了最佳的浏览体验。

Messages on the Mac offers new tools to help users manage their favorite group conversations and fun, engaging ways to express themselves. Users can pin conversations to the top of their Messages list for fast access, and search has been completely redesigned to help users quickly find links, photos, and matching terms. Messages for Mac also works seamlessly across iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch, so conversations stay in sync.

Mac上的Messages提供了新的工具，帮助用户管理他们最喜欢的群聊和有趣的、引人入胜的表达方式。用户可以将对话固定在消息列表的顶部，以便快速访问，搜索已经完全重新设计，以帮助用户快速查找链接、照片和匹配词。Mac上的消息也可以在iPhone、iPad和Apple Watch上无缝工作，因此对话保持同步。

Fun effects enable Mac users to add personality to their messages with balloons, confetti, and more. Users can even create and customize Memoji right on the Mac, and express themselves with automatically generated Memoji stickers that match their mood and personality. And with a new photo picker and #images, users can easily share images, GIFs, and videos.

有趣的效果让Mac用户可以用气球、五彩纸屑等为他们的信息增添个性。用户甚至可以在Mac上创建和定制Memoji，并使用自动生成的Memoji贴纸来表达自己的情绪和个性。有了新的照片选择器和#图片，用户可以轻松地分享图片、GIF和视频。

New group messaging features streamline interactions with family, friends, and colleagues. Inline replies give users the ability to respond directly to a message in a thread. Users can also send a message to an individual in a group conversation by simply typing their name, and choose to receive notifications only when their name is mentioned. And users can assign a photo or emoji to a conversation to further personalize their group messages.

新的群发消息功能简化了与家人、朋友和同事的互动。内联回复使用户能够直接回复线程中的邮件。用户还可以在群组对话中向个人发送消息，只需输入他们的名字，并选择只有在提到他们的名字时才会收到通知。用户还可以将照片或表情符号分配给对话，以进一步个性化他们的群发消息。

Maps has been entirely redesigned and brings exciting new features for exploring the world on the Mac. Users can discover places to visit and things to do with Guides from brands they trust, as well as create custom guides of favorite restaurants, parks, and vacation ideas to share with friends and family. Look Around provides an immersive 360-degree view of a destination, and new detailed indoor maps help with navigating major airports and shopping centers. And for the first time, cycling and electric vehicle trips can be routed on a Mac and sent directly to iPhone to have when on the go.

地图已经完全重新设计，并为在Mac上探索世界带来了令人兴奋的新功能。用户可以从他们信任的品牌中找到要去的地方和与Guides一起做的事情，还可以创建他们最喜欢的餐厅、公园和度假想法的定制指南，与朋友和家人分享。环顾四周提供了一个身临其境的360度的目的地视图，新的详细的室内地图有助于导航主要的机场和购物中心。这也是第一次，骑自行车和电动汽车出行可以在Mac电脑上发送，并在旅途中直接发送到iPhone上。

Privacy is at the core of the Mac experience, and Big Sur expands on its industry-leading privacy to bring new levels of transparency and give users more control over their data. Coming later this year, the Mac App Store will include a new section on each product page showing a developer-reported summary of the privacy practices of an app. Inspired by the convenience and readability of food nutrition labels, the information offered includes the types of data the apps might collect — such as contact information, or location — and whether they are shared with third parties for tracking.

隐私是汉堡体验的核心，Big Sur扩展了其行业领先的隐私，将透明度提升到新的水平，并让用户更好地控制自己的数据。Mac App Store将在今年晚些时候在每个产品页面上增加一个新的部分，显示开发者报告的应用程序隐私实践的摘要。受食品营养标签的便利性和可读性的启发，提供的信息包括应用程序可能收集的数据类型-如联系信息或位置-以及这些数据是否被分享给第三方进行跟踪。

With Big Sur, Apple’s community of more than 28 million developers have access to the tools they need to create amazing experiences for iOS, iPadOS, macOS, watchOS, and tvOS. Developing apps is now even easier, as macOS provides a range of incredible technologies to simplify the transition to Apple silicon. Xcode 12 makes it easy for developers to update their existing Mac apps to Universal 2 application binaries, adding native support for both M1- and Intel-based Macs within a single app. Universal apps are quite simply the fastest, most powerful Mac apps available, with access to the full power of the M1 chip. And with the Neural Engine now coming to the Mac, developers have new opportunities for amazing machine learning apps.

有了Big Sur，苹果超过2800万的开发者社区就可以使用他们需要的工具，为iOS、iPadOS、MacOS、WatchOS和TVOS创造令人惊叹的体验。现在开发应用程序更容易了，因为MacOS提供了一系列令人难以置信的技术来简化向苹果硅片的过渡。Xcode12使开发人员可以轻松地将现有的Mac应用程序更新为Universal 2应用程序二进制文件，在单个应用程序中添加对基于M1和基于Intel的Mac的本地支持。通用应用程序是目前运行速度最快、功能最强大的Mac应用程序，可以使用M1芯片的全部功能。随着神经引擎在Mac上的出现，开发者有了新的机会来开发令人惊叹的机器学习应用程序。

Rosetta 2 is the new technology in Big Sur that enables existing Intel apps not yet upgraded to Universal to run seamlessly on Macs with Apple silicon. With the power of Metal and M1, developers will see some of their most graphically demanding apps perform even better under Rosetta 2 than they did running natively on previous Macs with integrated graphics. Developers can also choose to make their iPhone and iPad apps available to Mac users without doing any extra work, so users get access to a greater range of apps than ever before. And the foundations of Big Sur are optimized to unlock the power of M1, including developer technologies from Metal for graphics to Core ML for machine learning.

Rosetta 2是大苏尔的一项新技术，它使尚未升级到Universal的现有英特尔应用程序可以在装有苹果硅片的Mac上无缝运行。有了Metal和M1的强大功能，开发人员将看到他们的一些对图形要求最高的应用程序在Rosetta 2下的表现甚至比他们在之前集成了显卡的Mac电脑上运行的更好。开发者还可以选择向Mac用户提供他们的iPhone和iPad应用程序，而不需要做任何额外的工作，这样用户就可以访问比以往任何时候都更广泛的应用程序。Big Sur的基础经过优化以释放M1的能力，包括从用于图形的Metal到用于机器学习的Core ML的开发技术。

When Mac Catalyst debuted with macOS Catalina last year, developers were able to easily bring their iPad apps to the Mac. Now, Mac Catalyst apps automatically inherit the new design of Big Sur, and developers get access to powerful new APIs for greater control over the look and behavior of their apps. SwiftUI also gets a major upgrade, enabling developers to write entire apps and widgets with shared code across all Apple platforms and easily add custom Mac features.

当Mac Catalyst去年与MacOS Catalina一起首次亮相时，开发者能够轻松地将他们的iPad应用程序带到Mac上。现在，Mac Catalyst应用程序自动继承Big Sur的新设计，开发人员可以访问强大的新API，以便更好地控制应用程序的外观和行为。SwiftUI还进行了重大升级，开发者可以在所有苹果平台上使用共享代码编写完整的应用程序和小工具，并轻松添加自定义Mac功能。

Apple’s more than 100,000 employees are dedicated to making the best products on earth, and to leaving the world better than we found it.
