Kik和SEC提议罚款500万美元，以解决他们围绕2017年象征性出售的纠纷，该交易筹集了约1亿美元；和解协议仍需得到法官的批准

2020-10-21 02:44:57

Kik and the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) have proposed settling their dispute over a 2017 token sale with a $5 million fine.

Kik和美国证券交易委员会(SEC)提议以500万美元的罚款解决2017年象征性出售的纠纷。

The proposed joint settlement, which also enjoins the Canadian firm against future U.S. securities law violations, still needs approval from presiding federal district Judge Alvin K. Hellerstein, according to public court documents. If approved, the settlement would cap off a year-long legal fight between the two.

公开法庭文件显示，拟议中的联合和解协议还禁止这家加拿大公司未来违反美国证券法，仍需得到主审联邦地区法官阿尔文·K·海勒斯坦(Alvin K.Hellerstein)的批准。如果获得批准，这项和解协议将为两家公司长达一年的法律战画上句号。

Kik must give the SEC 45 days’ notice on any transactions related to its Kin token treasury under the proposal. That notice order expires three years after the judgement takes effect. CEO Ted Livingston declined to comment.

根据该提案，Kik必须就与其Kin象征性金库相关的任何交易向SEC发出45天的通知。该通知令在判决生效三年后失效。首席执行官泰德·利文斯顿(Ted Livingston)拒绝置评。

A similar lawsuit filed by the securities regulator against messaging platform Telegram saw that firm’s blockchain project, the Telegram Open Network, abandoned before it could launch.

证券监管机构对即时通讯平台Telegram提起的类似诉讼中，该公司的区块链项目Telegram Open Network在推出之前就被放弃了。

But Kik’s proposed settlement has a critical difference from Telegram’s ill-fated TON outcome: it would not destroy the defendant’s tokenized dreams. Mandating that Kik notify the SEC of any kin sales within the next three years is the settlement’s only effect.

但是，Kik提出的和解方案与Telegram不幸的Ton结局有一个关键的不同之处：它不会摧毁被告象征性的梦想。要求Kik在未来三年内将任何亲属出售通知SEC是和解协议的唯一效果。

The settlement would tie up a legal bout that only 20 days ago appeared to be heading toward a resounding loss for Kik.

这项和解协议将结束一场法律比赛，而就在20天前，这场比赛似乎还在走向Kik的巨大损失。

Kik originally announced it hoped to fight the SEC in court, potentially creating a precedent for how token sales might be treated under U.S. securities law. However, it backed away from a jury trial request in March, and lost a motion for summary judgement last month.

Kik最初宣布，希望在法庭上与SEC抗争，这可能会开创美国证券法下如何对待象征性销售的先例。然而，它在3月份放弃了陪审团的审判请求，并在上个月输掉了一项即决判决的动议。

At the time, a judge ruled that Kik’s issuance of Kin was an investment of funds in a joint enterprise that sought to boost the token’s price, satisfying the prongs of the Howey Test, a Supreme Court case used as a precedent for evaluating whether assets are securities.

当时，一名法官裁定，Kik发行Kin是对一家合资企业的资金投资，该企业试图提高代币的价格，满足了豪威测试(Howey Test)的要求。豪威测试是最高法院用来评估资产是否为证券的先例。

Kik General Counsel Eileen Lyon said the SEC should create clear rules for the crypto industry, rather than publish “conflicting statements” and other non-binding forms of guidance in September.

Kik总法律顾问Eileen Lyon表示，SEC应该为密码行业制定明确的规则，而不是在9月份发布“相互矛盾的声明”和其他不具约束力的指导形式。
