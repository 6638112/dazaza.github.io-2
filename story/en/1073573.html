GTA Metaverse? Rockstar Confirms 'GTA 6' Is In Active Production
GTA元宇宙？Rockstar确认《GTA 6》正在积极制作中

2022-02-16 20:17:39

The success of Grand Theft Auto 5 has been both a blessing and a curse. A blessing for Rockstar, making zillions from the success of GTA Online, and for those deeply invested in that world which has continually gotten new expansions and additions. But a curse for those waiting for GTA 6, as Rockstar is now about to release GTA 5 across three different generations, cementing its massive lead as the best-selling game in history, endlessly delaying a true sequel.

《侠盗猎车手5》的成功既是福也是祸。这是对Rockstar的祝福，GTA Online的成功为Rockstar带来了数不清的财富，也是对那些在这个不断得到新的扩张和补充的世界中进行了深度投资的人的祝福。但对于那些等待GTA 6的人来说，这是一个诅咒，因为Rockstar现在即将分三代发布GTA 5，巩固其作为历史上最畅销游戏的巨大领先地位，无休止地推迟真正的续集。

Now, however, Rockstar has finally stated the obvious, they are working on the next GTA game, which we’re all calling GTA 6, but Rockstar stops short of saying number that outright.

然而现在，Rockstar终于明确表示，他们正在进行下一场GTA比赛，我们都称之为GTA 6，但Rockstar没有直接说出这个数字。

“With the unprecedented longevity of GTAV, we know many of you have been asking us about a new entry in the Grand Theft Auto series. With every new project we embark on, our goal is always to significantly move beyond what we have previously delivered — and we are pleased to confirm that active development for the next entry in the Grand Theft Auto series is well underway. We look forward to sharing more as soon as we are ready, so please stay tuned to the Rockstar Newswire for official details.”

“随着GTAV前所未有的使用寿命，我们知道你们中的许多人一直在问我们关于侠盗猎车手系列的新产品。随着我们开始的每一个新项目，我们的目标始终是显著超越我们之前交付的产品——我们很高兴地确认，侠盗猎车手系列下一个产品的积极开发《乌托邦》系列正在进行中。我们期待着在准备好后尽快分享更多信息，所以请继续关注Rockstar新闻专线了解官方细节。"

This is the “catch” I’m talking about here, because I don’t know if we can say for sure if this is going to be GTA 6 specifically, either the name or the concept. While you would think the safest play might be to just exactly replicate what they did with GTA 5, do a big single player campaign and a separate online mode, the success of GTA Online itself may end up fundamentally change the way Rockstar makes this series going forward.

这就是我在这里所说的“陷阱”，因为我不知道我们是否可以确定这是否会是GTA 6，具体来说，无论是名称还是概念。虽然你会认为最安全的做法可能是完全复制他们在GTA 5上所做的，进行一场大型单人活动和一种单独的在线模式，但GTA在线的成功本身可能会从根本上改变Rockstar使本系列向前发展的方式。

As such, I can imagine a “new entry in the Grand Theft Auto series” being a massive game that is perhaps online from the start, including for its base campaign. And not to use the “m” word, but there are less compelling metaverses than what GTA Online has become, and Rockstar may want to lean into that even further to try to get ahead of competition trying to make their own virtual worlds. As such, I could see a future that blends a traditional numbered GTA sequel and GTA Online, and who knows what that would be called (“GTA World?” “GTA Infinite?”). Just saying, it may not be “GTA 6,” exactly.

因此，我可以想象“侠盗猎车手系列中的新条目”是一款可能从一开始就在线的大型游戏，包括它的基本活动。不要使用“m”这个词，但GTA Online已经成为了一个不那么引人注目的元宇宙，Rockstar可能想进一步深入这个领域，试图在试图打造自己的虚拟世界的竞争中领先。因此，我可以看到一个未来，融合了传统编号的GTA续集和GTA在线，谁知道这将被称为什么（“GTA世界？”“GTA无限？”）。只是说，它可能不是“GTA 6”，确切地说。

Rockstar gave no indication about when this game is actually coming out. Jason Schreier predicts a “fake” release date of holiday 2023 maybe being announced, but then that slips to 2024, as all huge games end up delayed at least a few times in this day and age. Regardless, GTA 5 will live on as long as it keeps selling, and Rockstar is gearing up for more additions and things like standalone GTA Online as well.

Rockstar没有透露这场比赛什么时候开始。Jason Schreier预测2023年假日的“假”发布日期可能会被宣布，但随后就滑到了2024年，因为所有大型游戏最终都会在这个时代推迟至少几次。不管怎样，只要GTA 5继续销售，它就会继续存在，而Rockstar正在准备更多的附加功能，以及类似独立GTA在线的东西。

I’m not exactly sure what comes next, but I’d be a little surprised if it was just plain old GTA 6, structured exactly like 5. After all this time and this much online revenue, I think things may be shifting into new directions.

我不确定接下来会发生什么，但如果它只是普通的GTA 6，结构与GTA 5一模一样，我会有点惊讶。经过这么长时间和这么多的在线收入，我认为事情可能正在向新的方向转变。
