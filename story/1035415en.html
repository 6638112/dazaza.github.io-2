备案：Snap UK 2019年的收入为6.53亿美元，高于上年的4.037亿美元，但亏损增至4.35亿美元，并将在未来五年从Snap借款至多5亿美元

2020-11-16 18:33:19

Social media company Snapchat has warned that the pandemic could “seriously harm” its business as operating losses at its UK arm widened to $435m (£325m), last year.

社交媒体公司Snapchat警告称，疫情可能“严重损害”其业务，因为其英国分公司去年的运营亏损扩大至4.35亿美元(3.25亿英镑)。

Snap Group Limited has agreed a deal with its US parent to borrow up to $500m over the next five years after securing a promissory note to do so, according to accounts filed with Companies House this week.

根据本周提交给Companies House的账目，Snap Group Limited已与其美国母公司达成协议，在获得一张本票后，将在未来5年借入至多5亿美元。

It said advertisers may halt “temporarily or permanently” spending on its app and said that a decline in ad sales could “seriously harm” its business.

该公司表示，广告商可能会暂时或永久停止在其应用程序上的支出，并表示广告销售额的下降可能会对其业务造成“严重损害”。

The company warned that the pandemic had already “adversely impacted” its results and that the uncertainty caused by the coronavirus meant it could find it difficult to forecast future revenue.

该公司警告称，疫情已经对其业绩产生了“负面影响”，冠状病毒带来的不确定性意味着，它可能很难预测未来的营收。

Sales jumped to $653m in 2019, up from $403.7m in the previous year, while operating expenses increased by 75pc to $598.9m. The company said the increase in operating losses was due to growth in its research and development spend, which required additional capital from its US parent.

2019年销售额跃升至6.53亿美元，高于前一年的4.037亿美元，运营费用增长75%，至5.989亿美元。该公司表示，运营亏损的增加是由于研发支出的增长，这需要其美国母公司提供额外资本。

The London-based subsidiary also warned of a potential loss in productivity caused by the advent of remote working. “It could also adversely affect results due to inability to meet in person with potential advertisers,” Snap said.

这家总部位于伦敦的子公司还警告称，远程工作的出现可能会导致生产率下降。Snap说：“由于无法与潜在广告商面对面，这也可能对结果产生不利影响。”

Snapchat’s US parent, Snap Inc, is yet to make a profit more than two years on from its initial public offering.

Snapchat的美国母公司Snap Inc.在首次公开募股(IPO)两年多后仍未实现盈利。

The company created a UK entity in 2017 and insisted that it would route all of its non-US revenues through Britain. At the time, Claire Valoti, general manager of Snap Group Limited, said that the UK was “where our advertising clients are”.

该公司在2017年创建了一家英国实体，并坚称其所有非美国收入都将通过英国转账。当时，Snap Group Limited总经理克莱尔·瓦洛蒂(Claire Valoti)表示，英国是“我们广告客户的所在地”。

Despite the warnings associated with the coronavirus, Snap’s share price has continued to rise throughout 2020, rising 24pc. The company reported a considerable jump in valuation after advertisers began to boycott Facebook in October as part of a campaign against its hate speech policies.

尽管有与冠状病毒相关的警告，Snap的股价在整个2020年里持续上涨，上涨了24%。该公司报告称，在广告商10月份开始抵制Facebook(作为反对其仇恨言论政策的运动的一部分)后，该公司的估值大幅跃升。

In addition, Snap posted strong third quarter earnings this year with daily active users up 18pc and revenues up 55pc for the period.

此外，Snap今年第三季度盈利强劲，日活跃用户增加了18%，同期收入增加了55%。
