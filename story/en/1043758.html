Twitter has banned the accounts of several Trump advisers, including Sidney Powell and Michael Flynn, for violating its ban on “Coordinated Harmful Activity”
Twitter已禁止包括Sidney Powell和Michael Flynn在内的几位特朗普顾问的帐户，因为其违反了其对“协调有害活动”的禁令

2021-01-09 17:47:03

Twitter on Friday removed the accounts of Michael Flynn, Sidney Powell and other high-profile supporters of President Donald Trump who promoted the QAnon conspiracy theory.

推特上周五取消了迈克尔·弗林（Michael Flynn），西德尼·鲍威尔（Sidney Powell）以及唐纳德·特朗普总统提倡QAnon阴谋论的其他知名支持者的言论。

The permanent bans are among the highest profile that the company has instituted as part of its efforts to crack down on misinformation and calls for violence.

永久禁令是该公司制定的最引人注目的禁令，这是该公司打击虚假信息和呼吁采取暴力行动的一部分。

Flynn and Powell both met with Trump at the White House in recent weeks as part of efforts to overturn the presidential election results. They are also high-profile figures in the QAnon community, and Flynn even took an “oath” to the conspiracy theory last year.

作为推翻总统选举结果的努力的一部分，弗林和鲍威尔最近几周在白宫与特朗普会晤。他们也是QAnon社区中备受瞩目的人物，弗林（Flynn）甚至在去年对阴谋论表示了“誓言”。

“The accounts have been suspended in line with our policy on Coordinated Harmful Activity," a Twitter spokesperson told NBC News. "We’ve been clear that we will take strong enforcement action on behavior that has the potential to lead to offline harm, and given the renewed potential for violence surrounding this type of behavior in the coming days, we will permanently suspend accounts that are solely dedicated to sharing QAnon content."

“帐户已根据我们关于“协调有害活动”的政策而被暂停，＆＃34; Twitter发言人告诉NBC新闻。 ＆＃34;我们很清楚，我们将对可能导致线下伤害的行为采取强有力的执法行动，并且鉴于未来几天内围绕此类行为再次出现暴力的可能性，我们将永久停用帐户专门用于共享QAnon内容的信息。＆＃34;

Twitter also removed the account of Ron Watkins, the administrator of the website 8kun, which was formerly named 8chan and hosts posts from Q, the false digital “prophet” at the heart of the QAnon conspiracy theory.

Twitter还删除了网站8kun的管理员罗恩·沃特金斯（Ron Watkins）的帐户，该网站以前的名称为8chan，并托管来自QAnon阴谋论核心的虚假数字“先知” Q的帖子。

8chan changed its name last summer after white supremacists used the site to post manifestos before committing mass murder, including a terror attack at a mall in El Paso, Texas, that killed 17 in 2019.

在白人至上主义者在大规模屠杀之前使用8chan的网站发布宣言后，包括在德克萨斯州埃尔帕索的一家购物中心发生的恐怖袭击在2019年杀死了17人，去年8chan改名。

Watkins, who lives in Japan, and Powell have spent recent weeks pushing false claims about Dominion Voting Systems and the company's role in the U.S. elections, as well as targeting private citizens with false claims of election fraud. Powell was sued by Dominion Voting Systems on Friday for $1.3 billion.

居住在日本的沃特金斯（Watkins）和鲍威尔（Powell）最近几周花了很多时间来推销有关Dominion Voting Systems及其公司在美国大选中的角色的虚假声明，并以虚假的选举欺诈指控为目标锁定私人公民。鲍威尔周五被Dominion Voting Systems起诉，要求赔偿13亿美元。

The bans come two days after Twitter locked down Trump's account and warned that he risks a permanent ban if he continues to violate the company's policies. Trump posted a video in response to the mob that stormed the Capitol in which he continued to spread false claims about the election. His account is no longer locked.

禁令是在Twitter锁定特朗普的帐户并警告说，如果他继续违反公司政策的话，他将面临永久禁令的威胁，两天后。特朗普发布了一段视频，以回应暴民席卷国会大厦的事件，他继续在其中散布有关选举的虚假主张。他的帐户不再被锁定。

Twitter, which for years resisted calls to moderate its platform, has recently taken a stronger stance against conspiracy theories that have been tied to violence, most notably the QAnon movement. In July, Twitter removed 7,000 accounts and limited many others for dabbling in QAnon. The company also designated the QAnon movement as coordinated harmful activity, meaning it was then limited from the platform's search functions and "Trending Topics" feature.

多年来一直拒绝调整平台的呼吁的Twitter，最近对与暴力相关的阴谋论采取了更强硬的立场，最著名的是QAnon运动。 7月，Twitter删除了7,000个帐户，并限制了其他许多人涉嫌QAnon。该公司还将QAnon运动指定为协调的有害活动，这意味着该活动仅限于平台的搜索功能和“趋势主题”。特征。

The bans add to complaints from conservatives who have claimed that Twitter's policies and enforcement unfairly target them. Some users say they have left the platform for similar alternatives, such as Parler, which surged during the election when Twitter was aggressive in limiting misinformation.

禁令增加了保守派的抱怨，保守派声称Twitter的政策和执法不公正地针对了他们。一些用户表示，他们已经离开了平台，选择了类似的替代方案，例如Parler。在选举期间，Twitter积极限制错误信息，Parler激增了。

But they also come as many people in the technology industry who have been reticent to permanently ban many accounts have changed their positions following the violence at the Capitol.

但是，他们也来了，因为在国会大厦发生暴力事件之后，许多不愿永久禁止许多帐户的技术行业人士改变了立场。
