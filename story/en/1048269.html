Nvidia announces mining GPUs, cripples hash rate of RTX-3060
NVIDIA宣布挖掘GPU，RTX-3060的跛子哈希率

2021-02-18 23:10:02

NVIDIA has finally conceded to the overwhelming mining demand facing the GPU market right now and launched a lineup of dedicated cryptocurrency mining GPUs (can we even call them that considering they have no display port?) called CMP. This is short for Cryptocurrency Mining Processor While AIBs have previously rolled out mining variants of GPUs without display ports, this is the first time we are seeing an official product launch straight from NVIDIA itself. It is accompanied by an action that will likely be very controversial: software limiting the hash rate of NVIDIA's RTX 3060 GPU (which will be launching on the 25th) to just 50% of its actual rate.

NVIDIA最终承认了当前GPU市场面临的巨大采矿需求，并推出了专用于CMP的专用加密货币采矿GPU阵容（我们甚至可以称其为没有显示端口吗？）。这是Cryptocurrency Mining Processor的缩写，虽然AIB以前已经推出了不带显示端口的GPU的挖掘变体，但这是我们第一次看到NVIDIA本身直接发布正式产品。随之而来的动作可能会引起很大争议：该软件将NVIDIA RTX 3060 GPU（将于25日推出）的哈希率限制为实际速率的50％。

Before we go any further, NVIDIA is announcing a total of 4 CMP (GPUs?) out of which two, the NVIDIA CMP 30HX and 40HX will launch in Q1 and CMP 50HX and 90HX will launch in Q2. At this moment it is unclear which HX correlates to which GeForce GPU although you can make some decent guesses using the memory size (which correlates to bus width and can give away the rough model number) we can also use the hash rate and TDP to arrive at similar conclusions. With hash rates of up to 86 MH/s (and these are likely unoptimized considering NVIDIA always underpromises and over-delivers), it is clear that the company has plans to ensure a full lineup.

在我们采取进一步措施之前，NVIDIA宣布总共发布4个CMP（GPU？），其中两个将在第一季度发布NVIDIA CMP 30HX和40HX，在第二季度发布CMP 50HX和90HX。目前尚不清楚哪个HX与哪个GeForce GPU相关联，尽管您可以使用内存大小（与总线宽度相关联并且可以放弃粗糙的型号）做出一些不错的猜测，我们还可以使用哈希率和TDP得出在类似的结论。哈希速率高达86 MH / s（考虑到NVIDIA始终承诺不足和超额交付，这些速率可能未优化），很明显，该公司已计划确保完整的阵容。

Here is the thing though, the RTX 3070 for example has a hash rate between 50-60 MH/s. None of the CMPs in the table match this rate. I have a suspicion [caution: educated speculation] that what we are looking at also contains lower binned dies of the GeForce models with slightly lower core counts. This would allow NVIDIA to retarget some of those wasted dies more efficiently.

事情就是这样，例如RTX 3070的哈希率在50-60 MH / s之间。表中的CMP均不符合此速率。我怀疑[警告：有根据的推测]我们正在寻找的还包含GeForce模型的较低装仓模具，其核心数量略少。这将使NVIDIA能够更有效地重新定位其中一些浪费的模具。

Designed for professional mining operations NVIDIA CMP allows a fully open, airflow-optimized bracket and is configured to allow a greater number of GPUs to be controlled by one CPU.

专为专业采矿作业而设计的NVIDIA CMP允许完全开放的气流优化支架，并配置为允许一个CPU控制更多数量的GPU。

Optimized for best mining performance NVIDIA GPU architecture allows you to mine more efficiently and recoup your mining investment faster.

针对最佳挖掘性能进行了优化NVIDIA GPU架构可让您更高效地挖掘并更快地收回您的挖掘投资。

Available from authorized partners Available from authorized partners, including ASUS, Colorful, EVGA, Gigabyte, MSI, Palit, and PC Partner.

从授权合作伙伴处可获得从授权合作伙伴处可获得，包括ASUS，Colorful，EVGA，技嘉，MSI，Palit和PC合作伙伴。

It is also unclear at this point how their decision to halve RTX 3060 mining rate will be received. Some would argue that if you are buying the hardware, you reserve the right to use it to its full potential. Another obvious problem could be the fact that software blocks are usually overcome quite easily when the wider internet puts its mind to it.

目前还不清楚他们将如何将RTX 3060挖矿速率减半的决定。有人会争辩说，如果您要购买硬件，则保留充分利用其潜力的权利。另一个明显的问题可能是这样的事实，即当更广泛的互联网开始考虑时，通常很容易克服软件模块。

The only person this software block actually stops from mining is the retail miner. Gamers that want to put their GPU to mine part-time won't be able to. While mining operations continue to enjoy full use. Distribution of mining resources just got more unequal. 😀

该软件块实际停止开采的唯一人员是零售矿工。希望将GPU用作兼职的游戏玩家将无法做到。采矿作业继续得到充分利用。采矿资源的分配变得更加不平等。 😀

We have even seen AMD GPUs get bios flashed to their higher-end siblings so this would be child's play for an enthusiastic coder unless NVIDIA took hardware-based steps to mitigate this. Since a full GeForce GPU offers resale value - which is part of the ROI - a multi-million USD mining operation would likely have enough resources to bypass this block. In other words, this restriction would likely only impact the retail miner and disallow actual gamers from recouping some costs by putting their GPU to mine part-time.

我们甚至已经看到AMD GPU将bios刷新到其高端同级产品，因此，除非NVIDIA采取基于硬件的步骤来缓解这种情况，否则这对于狂热的编码人员来说是小孩子的游戏。由于完整的GeForce GPU可提供转售价值（这是ROI的一部分），数百万美元的采矿业务可能会拥有足够的资源来绕开此区块。换句话说，这种限制可能只会影响零售矿工，并禁止实际游戏玩家通过兼职使用GPU来收回部分成本。

We are gamers, through and through. Yet NVIDIA GPUs are programmable. And users are constantly discovering new applications for them, from weather simulation and gene sequencing to deep learning and robotics. Mining cryptocurrency is one of them.

我们一直都是游戏玩家。但是NVIDIA GPU是可编程的。用户不断为他们发现新应用，从天气模拟和基因测序到深度学习和机器人技术。挖矿加密货币就是其中之一。

With the launch of GeForce RTX 3060 on Feb. 25, we’re taking an important step to help ensure GeForce GPUs end up in the hands of gamers.

随着2月25日发布GeForce RTX 3060，我们迈出了重要的一步，以确保GeForce GPU最终落在游戏玩家手中。

RTX 3060 software drivers are designed to detect specific attributes of the Ethereum cryptocurrency mining algorithm, and limit the hash rate, or cryptocurrency mining efficiency, by around 50 percent.

RTX 3060软件驱动程序旨在检测以太坊加密货币挖掘算法的特定属性，并将哈希率或加密货币挖掘效率限制在50％左右。

To address the specific needs of Ethereum mining, we’re announcing the NVIDIA CMP, or, Cryptocurrency Mining Processor, product line for professional mining. CMP products — which don’t do graphics — are sold through authorized partners and optimized for the best mining performance and efficiency. CMP lacks display outputs, enabling improved airflow while mining so they can be more densely packed. CMPs also have a lower peak core voltage and frequency, which improves mining power efficiency.

为满足以太坊挖矿的特定需求，我们宣布推出NVIDIA CMP或Cryptocurrency Mining Processor专业挖矿产品系列。 CMP产品-不做图形-通过授权合作伙伴出售，并针对最佳采矿性能和效率进行了优化。 CMP缺乏显示输出，因此在采矿时改善了气流，因此可以更密集地包装。 CMP还具有较低的峰值核心电压和频率，从而提高了采矿的效率。
