UTP was originally published by Hayden Books (which was acquired byHoward W. Sams before the book was actually published).In the early 21^st^ century, the authors made the book available atthe Open Books Projectunder the Creative Commons Attribution License.Scanned pages are available inPBM (Portable BitMap) and PDF formats.The original troff markup is lost.

UTP最初由Hayden Books出版（在该书实际出版之前被Howard W.Sams收购）。在21世纪初，作者通过知识共享署名许可证在开放图书项目上提供了这本书。扫描的页面有inPBM（便携式位图）和PDF格式。原始的troff标记丢失。

In 2002,some members of the GNU Troff( groff) mailing listbegan transcribing the book and recreating the source code.It took a little over a year of manic activity,punctuated by long naps,and we released "UTP Revival" in 2004.We still hope to modernize the book ("UTP Revisited").Whether you want to use GNU Troff,or Plan 9 or one of its derivatives (Heirloom Troff and Neatroff),UTP is still an excellent guide to the nuts and bolts of print publishing.

2002年，GNU特罗夫（groff）邮件列表的一些成员开始转录这本书并重新编写源代码。我们花了一年多的时间进行狂躁的活动，时不时地小睡片刻，然后发布了"；UTP复兴"；2004年。我们仍然希望使这本书现代化（"；UTP重访"；）。无论你是想使用GNU特罗夫，还是Plan 9或它的一个衍生产品（传家宝特罗夫和尼特罗夫），UTP仍然是印刷出版的重要指南。

The current (main) branch is UTP 1.1,which provides a clickable index, TOC, and cross-references within the PDF(thanks to Deri James).You will need groff 1.22 or newer to format it,as it uses the -Tpdf driver.If you have an older groff, switch to the 1.0 branch.

目前（主要）的分支是UTP1.1，它在PDF中提供了可点击的索引、TOC和交叉引用（多亏了Deri James）。您需要groff 1.22或更新版本来格式化它，因为它使用了-Tpdf驱动程序。如果您有一个较旧的groff，请切换到1.0分支。

The UTP Revival Release isdistributed according to the terms of theCreative Commons Attribution License.A copy of the license is available at http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/1.0.

UTP复兴版本是根据Creative Commons Attribution License的条款发布的。许可证副本可在以下网址获得：http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/1.0.

Note: The transcription project predated Plan 9 and its derivatives.Thus, the recreated source and macros were written for groff,and we made no attempt to be compatible with the original troff.Patches for Heirloom or Neatroff (if needed) are welcome!

注：转录项目早于计划9及其衍生产品。因此，重新创建的源代码和宏是为groff编写的，我们没有尝试与原始troff兼容。欢迎使用传家宝或Neatroff（如果需要）补丁！

First, a big thanks to the original authorsfor making the book available in the first place.Nothing else could have happened without that first step.

首先，首先要感谢原著作者让这本书面世。没有这第一步，其他一切都不可能发生。
