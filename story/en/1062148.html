Volvo and Daimler bet on hydrogen truck boom this decade
沃尔沃和戴姆勒在这十年的氢气车上打赌

2021-05-12 18:56:32

Hydrogen-powered heavy trucks capable of driving long distances are likely to reach a tipping point toward the end of the decade, according to the heads of the world’s two biggest truck makers.

根据世界两个最大的卡车制造商的头部，能够驾驶长距离的氢气动力重型卡车可能达到十年的末端。

Martin Daum, chair of industry leader Daimler Truck, told the Financial Times that, while diesel trucks would dominate sales for the next three to four years, hydrogen would take off as fuel between 2027 and 2030 before going “steeply up.”

行业领导者戴姆勒卡车椅子马丁·戴姆告诉金融时报，柴油卡车将在未来三到四年中占据销售，氢气将在2027和2030之间燃料起飞前“陡峭”。

Martin Lundstedt, chief executive of Volvo Group, which has just bought into a hydrogen joint venture with Daimler, said that, after fuel-cell production started in 2025, there would be a “much steeper ramp-up” toward the end of the decade.

沃尔沃集团首席执行官Martin Lundstedt，刚刚用戴姆勒才能进入氢合资企业，表示，在2025年开始燃料电池生产后，将有一个“非常陡峭的斜坡”到十年结束时。

The Swedish truck maker is aiming for half its European sales in 2030 to be trucks powered by batteries or hydrogen fuel cells, while both groups want to be fully zero emissions by 2040.

瑞典卡车制造商旨在欧洲销售的一半销售于2030年，成为由电池或氢燃料电池供电的卡车，而两个组织希望通过2040年完全零排放。

The German and Swedish groups’ joint venture, Cellcentric, will start fuel cell production in 2025. Both truck makers will use electric batteries predominantly for smaller trucks as well as heavier vehicles based in one place that can recharge overnight.

德国和瑞典群体的合资企业，蜂窝节，将在2025年开始燃料电池生产。两辆卡车制造商都将主要用于较小的卡车以及基于一夜之间充电的一个地方的较重车辆使用电池。

But hydrogen is viewed as essential for the long-distance, heavy trucks that criss-cross Europe, the US, and other parts of the world delivering goods to multiple destinations and where refueling stops need to be as short as possible.

但是，氢气被视为强烈的重型卡车，克里斯欧洲，美国和世界其他地方将货物交给多个目的地的长途重型卡车，以及加油停止需要尽可能短暂。

Daum, who predicted the split between hydrogen and battery sales would end up being about 50-50, said that to move “40 tonnes up a hill you need an enormous amount of energy” and that, after diesel, the most efficient fuel for such tasks, hydrogen was the best option.

预测氢气和电池销售之间的分裂的Daum将最终为50-50，表示将“40吨山上需要大量的能量”，而且在柴油之后，最有效的燃料任务，氢是最好的选择。

Both men urged governments not just to ensure that the necessary fuel infrastructure would be in place for hydrogen but also to provide sufficient incentives for transport companies to shift to greener trucks.

两名男子敦促各国政府不仅可以确保必要的燃料基础设施将用于氢气，也可以为运输公司转向更环保的卡车提供足够的激励措施。

About 300 high-performance hydrogen refueling points would be needed in Europe by 2025 and 1,000 by 2030, the companies said.

该公司表示，在欧洲将在2025年和1,000到2030年在欧洲需要约300种高性能的氢气加油点。

Of the need to build the infrastructure at the same time as the trucks, Lundstedt said: “It can be seen as a chicken and egg. But we have said we will go for it. We will deliver the chicken. Someone else can deliver the egg.”

Lundstedt说：“它可以看作是鸡肉和鸡蛋的同时建立基础设施。但我们已经说过我们会去。我们将送鸡肉。别人可以提供鸡蛋。“

Conceding that hydrogen and battery-powered trucks would remain more expensive than those powered by diesel “at least for the next 15 years,” Daum noted that customers typically spent three to four times more on fuel over a truck’s lifespan than on the vehicle itself.

承认氢和电池动力卡车比柴油的动力仍然昂贵“至少在未来15年”中，Daum指出，客户通常在卡车的寿命上花费三到四倍，而不是车辆本身。

He added that early adopters—who would otherwise have to “pay a penalty” with high prices—could be helped by government support through the EU’s Green Deal or other incentives. But he said that, by the tipping point in 2027, a proper price for CO2 would be better as there would be too many trucks for subsidies.

他补充说，早期的采用者 - 否则将以高价格“支付刑罚” - 通过欧盟的绿色交易或其他激励措施，政府支持可以帮助。但是，他说，通过2027年的提示点，二氧化碳的适当价格会更好，因为有太多的卡车用于补贴。

Lundstedt stressed the truckmakers’ commitment was also important in terms of helping develop green hydrogen, made using renewable energy rather than natural gas as is common now, as other heavy industries such as shipping and steel consider the fuel. “This joint venture is a clear stick in the ground” from the truckmakers, he said.

Lundstedt强调了卡车制造商的承诺在帮助开发绿色氢气方面也很重要，使用可再生能源而不是天然气，现在是运输和钢等其他重工业的燃料。他说：“这位合资企业是在地上的透明棍子”来自卡车制造商。
