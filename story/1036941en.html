TLDR: Extreme Summarization of Scientific Documents
TLDR：科学文献的极端总结

2020-11-28 06:54:10

Download PDF Abstract: We introduce TLDR generation, a new form of extreme summarization, forscientific papers. TLDR generation involves high source compression andrequires expert background knowledge and understanding of complexdomain-specific language. To facilitate study on this task, we introduceSciTLDR, a new multi-target dataset of 5.4K TLDRs over 3.2K papers. SciTLDRcontains both author-written and expert-derived TLDRs, where the latter arecollected using a novel annotation protocol that produces high-qualitysummaries while minimizing annotation burden. We propose CATTS, a simple yeteffective learning strategy for generating TLDRs that exploits titles as anauxiliary training signal. CATTS improves upon strong baselines under bothautomated metrics and human evaluations. Data and code are publicly availableat this https URL.
