A Bulgarian company sells a device that unlocks almost any car
一家保加利亚公司出售一种几乎可以解锁任何汽车的装置

2022-02-14 10:30:34

The Bulgarian company SOS Auto Keys sells a modified Game Boy electronic game with which any car can be opened. The modified device sells for $ 25,000 and manages to break the car’s protection in minutes.

保加利亚公司SOS Auto Keys销售一款经过改进的Game Boy电子游戏，任何汽车都可以用它打开。这种改进后的装置售价2.5万美元，几分钟内就打破了汽车的保护。

The Game Boy series of electronic games was produced by the Japanese company Nintendo between 1989 and 2005. Today, however, it is no longer the harmless toy it was in the past, but a tool used by car thieves, he writes. Thesun.co.uk.

Game Boy系列电子游戏由日本任天堂公司于1989年至2005年生产。然而，他写道，如今，它已不再是过去那种无害的玩具，而是偷车贼使用的工具。太阳。co.uk。

British police warn modern car owners that thieves who use such a device in the parking lot can easily steal a car. Apparently, the device is very similar to the Nintendo Game Boy – the electronic game that was popular among children in the ’90s.

英国警方警告现代车主，在停车场使用这种装置的小偷很容易偷车。显然，这款设备与90年代在儿童中流行的任天堂Game Boy非常相似。

There was even a video on YouTube with instructions for using this device to unlock a KIA, Mitsubishi and Hyundai car, but at the time of publishing this article it was deleted. With the help of the device, the car was stolen in just 60 seconds.

YouTube上甚至有一段视频，上面有使用该设备解锁起亚、三菱和现代汽车的说明，但在发表本文时，该视频已被删除。在该设备的帮助下，汽车在60秒内被盗。

The thief just has to press the button from the door handle, and the device recorded all the data transmitted by the car, and after only a few seconds the vehicle could be operated as if the key were inside.

小偷只需按下车门把手上的按钮，设备就会记录下汽车传输的所有数据，几秒钟后，汽车就可以像钥匙在车内一样操作。

These devices cost about $ 25,000 and accordingly Thesun.co.uk, is technologically based on the Nintendo Game Boy game. The exact components of the game are not known, but apparently the software has been replaced.

这些设备的价格约为25000美元，因此太阳能电池的价格也相应上涨。在技术上基于任天堂Game Boy游戏。该游戏的确切组件尚不清楚，但显然该软件已被替换。

Bulgarian company SOS Auto Keys sells these devices, explicitly recommending that they not be used for illegal purposes, but British police say such a “toy” is unlikely to be bought by law-abiding people.

保加利亚公司SOS Auto Keys销售这些设备，明确建议不要将其用于非法目的，但英国警方表示，守法的人不太可能购买这种“玩具”。
