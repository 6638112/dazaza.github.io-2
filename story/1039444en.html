UK supermarkets report a surge in vegan food sales while expanding ranges
英国超级市场报告纯素食食品销售激增，范围不断扩大

2020-12-13 20:22:05

Waitrose, Tesco and M&S report surges in vegan food sales, as grocery chains expand plant-based ranges

Waitrose，Tesco和M＆amp; S报告称，随着杂货连锁店扩大了基于植物的产品范围，纯食品的销售量激增

Forget nut roast, or boring old vegetable wellington. From so-called “veganettone” to sticky toffee pudding and even meat-free whole “no turkey” crowns, retailers and food manufacturers are pulling out all the stops to offer a dizzying range of plant-based options for the traditional Christmas spread.

忘记坚果烘烤，或无聊的老菜惠灵顿。从所谓的“ veganettone”到粘稠的太妃糖布丁，甚至是无肉的整个“ no turkey”王冠，零售商和食品制造商都竭尽全力为传统的圣诞节蔓延提供令人眼花plant乱的基于植物的选择。

As research reveals that more than one in four Britons plan to eat more vegan food at Christmas this year, supermarkets are reporting strong sales of alternatives to festive staples, with some “limited edition” alternatives to the turkey centrepiece already selling out.

研究表明，今年圣诞节圣诞节中，不止四分之一的英国人计划吃更多的纯素食，超市报告称，节日​​主食的替代品销量强劲，其中一些“限量版”替代火鸡的核心产品已经售罄。

Tesco is tempting taste buds with a record 10 plant-based “centrepieces” – up from six last year – including its new Wicked Kitchen “no turkey roast crown”, which has already sold out online.

乐购（Tesco）吸引了创纪录的10种基于植物的“中心”（去年是6种），其中包括新的Wicked Kitchen“无火鸡烤冠”，已经在网上售罄，从而诱使味蕾。

Derek Sarno, a US chef and self-proclaimed “plant pusher” hired by the supermarket as its “director of plant-based innovation” to overhaul and bolster the entire range, said: “It looks like turkey, carves like turkey and even tastes like it. It has taken us two years to bring this to market and it’s the first of its kind, and what I believe will be a game-changer for the industry. Made of wheat protein and soy, we also worked with a specialist flavour house to create a natural plant-base turkey flavouring that gives the full experience of the real thing.”

Derek Sarno是美国厨师，自称为“植物推手”，被超市聘为“植物创新总监”，以大修和加强整个产品范围。他说：“它看起来像火鸡，像火鸡一样雕刻，甚至有味道喜欢它。我们花了两年的时间将它推向市场，这是同类产品中的第一个，我相信它将改变整个行业。我们用小麦蛋白和大豆制成，我们还与一家专业的调味屋合作，创造了一种天然植物基火鸡调味料，为您带来了真实的完整体验。”

According to research carried out for the Vegan Society, 26% of Britonsplan to eat more plant-based foods this Christmas, continuing the trend of eating less meat during lockdown. That reflects people paying closer attention to what they eat on health and environmental grounds, with increasing numbers adopting “ flexitarian” diets – cutting down on meat and dairy while eating more plant-based foods.

根据针对素食主义者协会的研究，今年圣诞节期间，英国人计划中有26％的人计划吃更多的植物性食品，这延续了禁食期间少吃肉的趋势。这反映出人们越来越关注基于健康和环境的饮食，越来越多的人采用“自由主义”饮食-减少肉类和奶制品，而食用更多的植物性食品。

Waitrose says pre-orders of vegan Christmas foods are up by 700% comparedwith last year while searches on Waitrose.com for “vegan cheese” increased by 76% on the same period in 2019. Its Vegan Festive Filo Swirls are proving a popular main dish,. It is also is offering the first supermarket dairy-free vegan fondue.

Waitrose说，素食圣诞节食品的预订量比去年增加了700％，而Waitrose.com上的“素食奶酪”搜索量相比2019年增加了76％。其素食节日Filo漩涡证明是一种受欢迎的主菜，。它还提供了第一个超级市场的​​无乳纯素食火锅。

Charlotte McCarthy, vegan buyer at Waitrose said: “We anticipate this Christmas will be the biggest ever for vegan food. We’ve worked really hard to ensure that there is an exciting range available for our customers that is delicious, whether they follow a vegan diet or not.”

怀特罗斯的素食主义者买主夏洛特·麦卡锡（Charlotte McCarthy）说：“我们预计今年圣诞节将是有史以来最大的素食食品。无论我们是否遵循纯素食，我们一直在努力确保为客户提供令人兴奋的美味佳肴。”

The grocery chain Planet Organic has recently expanded its online business to go fully nationwide, with a new website allowing customers to shop by ‘‘lifestyle” – gluten-free, dairy-free, keto, vegetarian and vegan – with the last category enjoying highest online traffic.

杂货连锁店Planet Organic最近将其在线业务扩展到了全国范围，新的网站允许客户按照“生活方式”进行购物-无麸质，无乳制品，酮，素食和纯素食-最后一类的享受最高在线流量。

Shoppers have a choice of nine different Christmas hampers, three of which are specifically branded as vegan (vegan snack attack, vegan chocolate lover and ultimate vegan) and together account for more than half of all hamper sales. Within its general seasonal range the overall best-seller is veganettone; vegan panettones made using traditional sourdough starters and leavening techniques, but without butter and egg yolks. Montezuma’s dairy-free “Like no udder” chocolate bars are also flying off the shelves.

购物者可以选择9种不同的圣诞节礼物篮，其中3种专门打上纯素食主义者的名字（纯素食小吃攻击，纯素食巧克力爱好者和终极纯素食者），加起来占所有阻碍销售的一半以上。在整个季节范围内，总体畅销书是venetetone；使用传统的发酵母发酵剂和膨松技术制成的纯素潘妮托妮，但不含黄油和蛋黄。蒙特祖玛（Montezuma）的不含乳制品的“无乳”巧克力棒也已经下架。

Al Overton, Planet Organic’s buying director, said: “Countless Christmas vegan items are selling well, including dairy-free chocolate and cheese platters, Christmas cakes and wine; traditional items made to vegan recipes so nobody has to miss out. The seitan roast – an alternative to turkey – has recently sold out, as has the truffle and mushroom faux gras pate, but there will be more available at least a week before Christmas.”

Planet Organic的采购总监Al Overton表示：“无数的圣诞节纯素食产品销售良好，其中包括不含乳制品的巧克力和奶酪拼盘，圣诞节蛋糕和葡萄酒。纯素食谱制作的传统食品，因此没有人可以错过。 seitan烤（火鸡的替代品），松露和蘑菇仿鹅肝酱最近都卖光了，但至少在圣诞节前一个星期会有更多。

Lakeland said it had sold 65% more vegan hampers in October than last year as customers shopped earlier, and while now sold out online they are still available in store.

莱克兰说，由于顾客购物较早，十月份的纯素食礼篮的销量比去年增加了65％，而现在网上销售一空，它们仍然可以在商店中购买。

Marks & Spencer is rolling out more than 30 new vegan foods for Christmas under its Plant Kitchen brand in its biggest festive collection to date. Its Plant Kitchen vegan festive wreath – named BBC Good Food’s best vegan main course at this year’s Christmas Taste awards – goes on sale in stores next week; a pastry main course stuffed with roasted mushrooms and chestnuts and topped with a cranberry and orange glaze. M&S said its Plant Kitchen frozen sticky toffee pudding – launched last month – was now selling three times as much as the standard dessert. Its plant-based frozen party food range now accounts for 30% of its frozen party food sales, with the top-selling line its mini “no chicken” kievs.

商标斯宾塞（Spencer）将以植物厨房（Plant Kitchen）品牌为圣诞节推出30多种新的纯素食食品，这是迄今为止最大的节日收藏。它的Plant Kitchen素食节日花环-在今年的圣诞节口味大奖中被评为BBC Good Food最佳素食主菜-下周将在商店发售；糕点主菜，里面塞满了烤蘑菇和栗子，上面放有蔓越莓和橙子釉。 M＆amp; S表示，其上个月推出的Plant Kitchen冷冻粘太妃糖布丁现在的销量是标准甜点的三倍。目前，其以植物为原料的冷冻方食品种类占其冷冻方食品销售额的30％，其中最畅销的产品是迷你“无鸡肉”基辅。
