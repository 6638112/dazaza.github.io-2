Amazon shutters its Prime Pantry delivery service, and folds thousands of products under that banner into its main retail site
亚马逊关闭了其Prime Pantry送货服务，并将成千上万种商品打入其主要零售网站

2021-01-09 08:17:43

The Amazon.com Inc. shopping cart icon is displayed on a wall at the company's office campus in Hyderabad, India, on Friday, Sept. 6, 2019. , Bloomberg

2019年9月6日，星期五，在印度海得拉巴公司办公园区的墙上，显示了Amazon.com Inc.购物车图标。

Amazon.com Inc. has shuttered Prime Pantry, a grocery and household essentials delivery service that was one of the retailer’s early forays into selling food online.

Amazon.com Inc.已经关闭了Prime Pantry，这是一家杂货店和家庭必需品送货服务，这是零售商在网上销售食品的早期尝试之一。

The program closed on Wednesday, an Amazon spokeswoman said, and thousands of products previously available under the Prime Pantry banner were folded into the company’s main retail site.

亚马逊的一位女发言人说，该计划于周三关闭，以前在Prime Pantry旗帜下出售的数千种产品被折叠到该公司的主要零售网站中。

Launched in 2014, Prime Pantry featured a selection of shelf-stable food and snacks, as well as cleaning products, and was designed to get shoppers to stock up on the bulky, often expensive-to-ship products in orders that could fit into a single large box.

Prime Pantry于2014年推出，精选了一系列货架稳定的食品和小吃以及清洁产品，旨在使购物者可以购买大批，通常是昂贵的船运产品，以适合他们的订单。单个大盒子。

Initially the service was offered only to members of the Prime free shipping program, but Amazon added a US$5 a month subscription option in 2018. Those who were still paying the monthly fee were notified of the shutdown in December and received refunds, the spokeswoman said.

最初，这项服务仅向Prime免费送货计划的会员提供，但亚马逊在2018年增加了每月5美元的订购选项。女发言人说，那些仍在按月支付费用的人在12月被通知关闭并获得退款。 。

“As part of our commitment to delivering the best possible customer experience, we have decided to transfer Amazon Pantry selection to the main Amazon.com store so customers can get everyday household products faster, without an extra subscription or purchase requirement,” the spokeswoman said in an email.

发言人说：“作为我们致力于提供最佳客户体验的承诺的一部分，我们决定将Amazon Pantry的选择转移到主要的Amazon.com商店，以便客户可以更快地获得日常家用产品，而无需额外的订阅或购买要求。”在电子邮件中。

In addition to shelf-stable food offered on Amazon’s main retail site, members of the $119-a-year Prime program in many cities can also order fresh food from Amazon Fresh and the company’s Whole Foods Market chain.

除了在亚马逊主要零售网站上提供的货架稳定食品外，在许多城市中，每年119美元的Prime计划的会员还可以从Amazon Fresh及其公司的Whole Foods Market Chain中订购新鲜食品。
