Top U.S. pipeline operator shuts major fuel line after cyber attack
顶级美国管道运营商在网络攻击后关闭主要燃油管线

2021-05-08 23:37:39

Top U.S. fuel pipeline operator Colonial Pipeline has shut its entire network after a cyber attack that industry sources said was caused by ransomware software.

顶级美国燃料管道操作员殖民地管道在网络攻击后关闭了整个网络，即行业来源所说的是由赎金软件引起的。

Colonial's network supplies fuel from U.S refiners on the Gulf Coast to the populous eastern and southern United States. The company transports 2.5 million barrels per day of gasoline, diesel, jet fuel and other refined products through 5,500 miles (8,850 km) of pipelines, and transports 45% of East Coast fuel supply.

殖民地＆＃39;网络提供从美国博物馆的美国炼油厂的燃料到墨西哥湾沿岸到有人口的东部和美国南部。该公司通过5,500英里（8,850公里）的管道运输每天250万桶汽油，柴油，喷气机和其他精炼产品，并运输45％的东海岸燃料供应。

The malicious software used in the attack was ransomware, two cybersecurity industry sources familiar with the matter said. Ransomware is a type of malware that is designed to lock down systems by encrypting data and demanding payment to regain access. The malware has grown in popularity over the last five years and is most often deployed by cybercriminal groups.

攻击中使用的恶意软件是赎金软件，两个网络安全行业来源熟悉此事。 Ransomware是一种恶意软件，旨在通过加密数据和要求恢复访问来锁定系统来锁定系统。在过去的五年里，恶意软件在普及中变得普及，最常由网络犯罪分组部署。

The company shut down systems to contain the threat after learning of the attack on Friday, Colonial said in the statement. That action has temporarily halted operations and affected some of its IT systems, the company said.

该公司在周五学习袭击后，殖民地在声明中表示，该公司在学习攻击后遏制威胁。该公司表示，该行动暂时停止了运营并影响其一些IT系统。

Colonial has engaged a third-party cybersecurity firm to launch an investigation and contacted law enforcement and other federal agencies, it said.

它说，殖民地已聘请第三方网络安全公司进行调查和联系执法和其他联邦机构。

Colonial did not give further details or say for how long its pipelines would be shut.

殖民地没有提供进一步的细节或说出其管道将被关闭多长时间。

"Cybersecurity vulnerabilities have become a systemic issue," said Algirde Pipikaite, cyber strategy lead at the World Economic Forum's Centre for Cybersecurity.

＆＃34;网络安全漏洞已成为一个系统性问题，＆＃34;抗血管潮普鸟，网络战略领先世界经济论坛＆＃39; SCENTION安全中心。

"Unless cybersecurity measures are embedded in a technology's development phase, we are likely to see more frequent attacks on industrial systems like oil and gas pipelines or water treatment plants," Pipikaite added.

＆＃34;除非网络安全措施嵌入技术＆＃39;发展阶段，否则我们可能会看到更频繁地对石油和天然气管道或水处理厂等工业系统攻击＆＃34; pipikaite补充道。

Reuters reported earlier on Friday that Colonial had shut its main gasoline and distillate lines.

路透社周五早些时候报道，殖民地闭合了主要的汽油和馏分线。

During the trading session on Friday, Gulf Coast cash prices for gasoline and diesel edged lower.

周五交易期间，海湾海岸汽油和柴油的现金价格下跌。

Both gasoline and diesel futures on the New York Mercantile Exchange rose more than crude prices during the day. Gasoline futures gained 0.6% to settle at $2.1269 a gallon, while diesel futures rose 1.1% to settle at $2.0106 a gallon.

纽约商品交易所的汽油和柴油期货均在白天的原油价格上涨。汽油期货上涨0.6％，以1,2269美元的加仑定居，而柴油期货价格上涨1.1％，以0.0106美元。

"The fact that this attack compromised systems that control pipeline infrastructure indicates that either the attack was extremely sophisticated or the systems were not well secured," said Mike Chapple, a professor at the University of Notre Dame's Mendoza College of Business and a former computer scientist with the U.S. National Security Agency.

＆＃34;这种攻击受到妥协的事实，控制管道基础设施的系统表明攻击非常复杂或系统并不充分保护，＆＃34;迈克查5，圣母院大学教授＆＃39; Mendoza商务学院和美国国家安全局的前电脑科学家。

Longer-term price effects will depend on the amount of time that the lines are shut. If barrels are not able to make it onto the lines, Gulf Coast prices could weaken further, while benchmark prices in New York Harbor could rise, one market participant said. Rising benchmark prices are typically followed by price hikes at the pump.

长期价格效果取决于线路关闭的时间量。如果桶无法进入线路，海湾海岸价格可能会削弱，而纽约港的基准价格可能会上升，一位市场参与者表示。基准价格上涨通常是泵上的价格徒步旅行。

Colonial shut down its gasoline and distillate lines during Hurricane Harvey, which hit the Gulf Coast in 2017. That contributed to tight supplies and gasoline price rises in the United States after the hurricane forced many Gulf refineries to shut down.

殖民地在哈维飓风袭击其汽油和馏分线，这在2017年袭击了墨西哥湾沿岸。在飓风迫使许多海湾炼油厂关闭后，在美国造成严格的用品和汽油价格。
