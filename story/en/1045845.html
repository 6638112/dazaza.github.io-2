James Bond Film No Time To Die Delayed Again Over Covid
詹姆斯·邦德电影《死不了》再次推迟

2021-01-23 02:17:26

Daniel Craig’s final outing as 007 hit by coronavirus disruption, along with Ghostbusters sequel and Cinderella

丹尼尔·克雷格（Daniel Craig）的最后一次郊游，是被冠状病毒破坏，《捉鬼敢死队》的续集和《灰姑娘》击中的007

James Bond film No Time To Die has been delayed again as Hollywood grapples with the continued disruption caused by the pandemic.

詹姆斯·邦德（James Bond）的电影《没有时间死》（No Time To Die）再次被推迟，因为好莱坞正努力应对这种流行病造成的持续破坏。

Daniel Craig’s final outing as 007 will now arrive on 8 October, the official Bond Twitter account announced. It had been set to be released in April following multiple pandemic-enforced delays.

邦德推特官方帐户宣布，丹尼尔·克雷格（Daniel Craig）的最后一次郊游（007）现在将于10月8日到达。由于多次大流行导致的延误，原定于四月发布。

No Time To Die is the latest major release to be pushed back as Hollywood studios scramble to protect their films from certain box office doom, with cinemas remaining closed in markets around the world.

随着好莱坞制片厂争相保护自己的电影免遭某些票房的厄运，世界各地的电影院都关门大吉，No Time To Die是推迟发行的最新主要发行片。

Despite once saying he would rather take his own life than reprise his role as 007, Daniel Craig confirms he will return as James Bond for the franchise's 25th movie.

尽管曾经说过他宁愿自己活着而不是重新扮演007角色，但丹尼尔·克雷格（Daniel Craig）确认他将以詹姆斯·邦德（James Bond）的身分加盟这部特许经营的第25部电影。

Danny Boyle confirms he is working on a script for the next Bond film alongside his Trainspotting collaborator John Hodge.

丹尼·博伊尔（Danny Boyle）证实，他正在与Trainspotting合作者约翰·霍奇（John Hodge）合作制作下一部邦德电影的剧本。

EON Productions’ Michael G Wilson and Barbara Broccoli, the custodians of the Bond movies, confirm production will begin at the start of December 2018, with the film released in the UK on 25 October 2019 and in the US a fortnight later.

Bond电影的保管人EON Productions的Michael G Wilson和Barbara Broccoli确认制作将于2018年12月开始，并将于2019年10月25日在英国上映，两周后在美国上映。

Boyle quits the movie over 'creative differences'. Sources close to the production confirm that the next 007 movie will miss its scheduled release date, after disputes between the director, producers and star Craig

博伊尔因“创意差异”而退出电影。接近制作人的消息来源证实，在导演，制片人和明星克雷格之间发生纠纷后，下一部007电影将错过原定的发行日期

Writers Neal Purvis and Robert Wade return to James Bond for a seventh time, following Boyle’s late-hour departure from the project. True Detective's Cary Fukunaga is named as the new director.

博伊尔（Boyle）晚点离开项目后，作家尼尔·珀维斯（Neal Purvis）和罗伯特·韦德（Robert Wade）第七次回到詹姆斯·邦德。真侦探卡里·福永被任命为新导演。

Another new writer arrives as Bourne Ultimatum writer Scott Z Burns is hired to rewrite the screenplay. The working title of the film - Shatterhand - is released to widespread derision.

聘请《伯恩最后通writer》作家斯科特·Z·伯恩斯（Scott Z Burns）改写剧本后，另一位新作家登场。这部电影的作品名称-Shatterhand-发行时被广泛嘲笑。

At the risk of being labelled 'too PC' about the climate emergency, it emerges that Bond will be behind the wheel of an electric Aston Martin Rapide E in the new film.

可能会被标记为“ PC过多”关于气候紧急情况，邦德将在新电影中成为电动阿斯顿·马丁·拉皮德E的推动者。

Riding high on the back of the success of Fleabag, Phoebe Waller-Bridge is reported to have been brought in to liven up new Bond script. The production enjoys  a glitzy launch in Jamaica, officially adding Rami Malek to the cast, but failing to reveal the movie's new name.

据报道，在Fleabag的成功背后，Phoebe Waller-Bridge被带入了新的邦德剧本。制作在牙买加大放异彩，正式将Rami Malek加入了演员阵容，但未能透露这部电影的新名字。

Daniel Craig requires surgery on an ankle injured during the filming, putting him out of action for at least two weeks. The official James Bond social media account announced the news, saying production would continue while Craig 'rehabilitates'.

丹尼尔·克雷格（Daniel Craig）需要在拍摄过程中对脚踝受伤进行手术，这使他至少有两个星期不能行动。詹姆斯·邦德（James Bond）官方社交媒体帐户宣布了这一消息，称在克雷格（Craig）康复期间，生产将继续进行。

An explosion on the set injures a crew member and damages the outside of the famous 007 Stage at Pinewood Studios, Buckinghamshire.

爆炸发生后，机组人员受伤，并损坏了白金汉郡派恩伍德制片厂著名的007舞台的外部。

The first release date for the movie passes as Covid begins to impact world cinema.

随着Covid开始影响世界电影，该电影的首个发行日期已经过去。

Yet another delay as release is pushed back again until 8 October 2021, 14 months later than planned.

由于发布又一次推迟，再次推迟到2021年10月8日，比原计划晚了14个月。

Earlier this month Warner Bros announced it was delaying the release of Sopranos prequel The Many Saints Of Newark.

本月早些时候，华纳兄弟宣布将推迟女高音的前传《纽瓦克的许多圣徒》的发行。

And after MGM released the Bond news, Sony said Peter Rabbit 2: The Runaway was moving from April to June, Ghostbusters: Afterlife was pushed from June to November and Cinderella, which stars singer Camila Cabello, will now arrive in July.

在米高梅公司发布《邦德新闻》之后，索尼说《彼得兔2：逃亡》从4月移到6月，《捉鬼敢死队：来世》从6月移到11月，由歌手卡米拉·卡贝洛（Camila Cabello）主演的灰姑娘现在将在7月到来。

Video game adaptation Uncharted has been shifted from 2021 and moved to February 2022, Sony said, while Jared Leto superhero flick Morbius, which did have the 8 October slot now occupied by No Time To Die, is now slated for January next year.

索尼表示，视频游戏改编《未知海域》已从2021年转移到2022年2月，而贾里德·莱托的超级英雄电影《莫比乌斯》现在定于明年1月，该电影的确在10月8日被《无时无刻》占据。

No Time To Die was the first major film to fall victim to the pandemic when its April 2020 release was pushed to November as the virus swept around the world. In October it was delayed to April when it became clear Covid-19 was still rampant and cinemas would not be widely reopening.

No Time To Die是该大流行的第一部主要电影，该病毒于2020年4月上映至11月，当时该病毒席卷全球。在10月，它推迟到4月，直到很明显，Covid-19仍然很猖and，电影院也不会广泛开放。

As well as the film industry, the wider entertainment world has been battered by the pandemic. On Thursday it was announced the Glastonbury music festival had been cancelled for a second successive year.

除电影业外，更广泛的娱乐世界也被大流行所打击。周四宣布，格拉斯顿伯里音乐节已连续第二年取消。
