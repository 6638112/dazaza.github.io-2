Apple announces $549 over-ear headphones, the AirPods Max
Apple宣布推出549美元的入耳式耳机AirPods Max

2020-12-08 23:31:05

The wireless headphones feature active noise cancellation and cost $549. With this product, Apple competes directly with Sony’s and Bose’s wireless headphones — the Sony WH-1000XM4 and Bose 700. Pre-orders start today and they’ll ship on December 15.

无线耳机具有主动降噪功能，售价549美元。借助此产品，Apple可以直接与Sony和Bose的无线耳机竞争-Sony WH-1000XM4和Bose700。预购从今天开始，将于12月15日发货。

This isn’t the company’s first over-ear headphones as Apple acquired Beats back in 2014. Apple has released new Beats headphones over the past few years. For instance, last year, Apple released the Beats Solo Pro, wireless headphones that feature Apple’s H1 chip and cost $300. They also have active noise cancellation.

这并不是苹果公司在2014年收购Beats以来的第一款入耳式耳机。过去几年，Apple发布了新的Beats耳机。例如，去年苹果发布了Beats Solo Pro，这是一款具有苹果H1芯片的无线耳机，售价300美元。它们还具有主动降噪功能。

The AirPods Max come in multiple colors — silver, space gray, sky blue, pink and green. They are foldable and can be stored in a case — or, as Apple calls it, a Smart Case. When you put your headphones in the case, the device enters an ultra-low power state — but that’s about it. Apple promises 20 hours of battery life.

AirPods Max有多种颜色-银色，深空灰色，天蓝色，粉红色和绿色。它们是可折叠的，可以存储在手提箱中，或者如Apple所说，可以存储在Smart Case中。将耳机放入外壳后，设备将进入超低功耗状态，仅此而已。苹果承诺20小时的电池寿命。

Powered by Apple’s H1 chip with 10 audio cores, they bring many of the features that you can find in the AirPods Pro — active noise cancellation, transparency mode, spatial audio and adaptive EQ. The headband is made of stainless steel, which probably explains the pricing strategy. The top band is a flexible mesh. The ear cushions try to create a seal thanks to memory foam.

它们由Apple的H1芯片和10个音频核心提供支持，带来了AirPods Pro中可以找到的许多功能-主动降噪，透明模式，空间音频和自适应均衡器。头带由不锈钢制成，这可能解释了定价策略。顶部带是柔性网格。借助记忆泡沫，耳垫试图形成密封。

In addition to a noise control button, there’s an Apple Watch digital crown, which lets you adjust the volume, skip tracks, etc. Inside the device, you’ll find 40-mm dynamic drivers. Combined with computational audio and a dual neodymium ring magnet motor, Apple promises very little distorsion and high quality sound.

除了噪音控制按钮外，还有一个Apple Watch数字表冠，可让您调节音量，跳过音轨等。在设备内部，您会发现40毫米动态驱动器。结合计算音频和双钕环形磁铁电机，Apple承诺失真很小，声音质量很高。

If you’re not familiar with Adaptive EQ, the feature was originally introduced with the AirPods Pro. The device uses microphones to adjust the sound based on the fit and seal of the headphones or earbuds.

如果您不熟悉Adaptive EQ，则此功能最初是在AirPods Pro中引入的。该设备使用麦克风根据耳机或耳塞的贴合度和密封性来调整声音。

When it comes to active noise cancellation, the AirPods Max use three outward-facing microphones on each ear cup. When you pair the AirPods Max with an iPhone or iPad, Apple uses the gyroscope and accelerometer in both devices to compare motion data and deliver sound in 5.1, 7.1 and Dolby Atmos. Music also automatically stops when you remove the AirPods Max.

关于主动降噪，AirPods Max在每个耳罩上使用三个向外的麦克风。当您将AirPods Max与iPhone或iPad配对时，Apple会在两个设备中都使用陀螺仪和加速度计来比较运动数据并以5.1、7.1和Dolby Atmos格式传送声音。卸下AirPods Max时，音乐也会自动停止。
