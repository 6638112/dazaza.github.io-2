Pay-per-mile auto insurance firm Metromile, which has a consumer app and licenses tech to large insurers, says it's going public via a SPAC at a $1.3B valuation
每英里收费汽车保险公司Metromile，该公司拥有一个消费者应用程序并将技术许可给大型保险公司，它表示已通过SPAC上市，估值为1.3B美元

2020-11-25 05:26:38

Insurtech Metromile announced Tuesday that it is the latest fintech to go public via a SPAC (special purpose acquisition company) at a valuation of $1.3 billion.

Insurtech Metromile周二宣布，这是最新的金融科技，通过SPAC（特殊目的收购公司）以13亿美元的估值上市。

Specifically, it will go public via INSU Acquisition Corp. II, a publicly traded SPAC sponsored by Cohen & Company.

具体而言，它将通过由科恩公司（Cohen＆Company）赞助的公开交易的SPAC INSU Acquisition Corp. II公开上市。

Upon closing of the transaction, the combined company will be named Metromile Inc. and is expected to remain listed on NASDAQ under the new ticker symbol “MLE.

交易完成后，合并后的公司将更名为Metromile Inc.，并有望以新的股票代码“ MLE”在纳斯达克上市。

Founded in 2011, Metromile launched its personalized pay-per-mile auto insurance a year later. The company has raised $293 million in funding from the likes of NEA and Index Ventures.

Metromile成立于2011年，一年后推出了个性化的每英里收费汽车保险。该公司已经从NEA和Index Ventures等公司筹集了2.93亿美元的资金。

The premise behind Metromile is that if you don’t drive much, you shouldn’t have to pay as much for auto insurance as those who do drive a lot. Customers pay a base rate, and then pennies per mile, according to the company.

Metromile背后的前提是，如果您不开车太多，那么您不必像那些经常开车的人那样花那么多钱购买汽车保险。该公司表示，客户需要支付基本费率，然后每英里支付几便士。

I spoke with Rick Chen, the company’s communications director, who said the company is slated to go public in the first quarter of 2021.

我与该公司传播总监Rick Chen进行了交谈，他说该公司定于2021年第一季度上市。

He said that besides being an insurance provider, Metromile is a true technology play that also licenses out its underlying technology to large incumbents – a big revenue-generator for the company. It also has built car sensors and mobile APIs that it uses for phones and integrate directly with car manufacturers for connected vehicles.

他说，除了成为保险提供商之外，Metromile还是一个真正的技术公司，它也将其基础技术授权给了大型运营商-这是该公司的一大创收来源。它还构建了用于手机的汽车传感器和移动API，并与汽车制造商直接集成以连接汽车。

“All this telematics data helps us understand risk moment-by-moment,” Chen told FinLedger. “Typical insurance companies rely on decades of data to figure out th erisk of each driver and policy. How they price is from taking a look back. For us, we see it more in real-time. We’re an insurance company built for the future.”

Chen告诉FinLedger：“所有这些远程信息处理数据都有助于我们瞬间了解风险。” “典型的保险公司依靠数十年的数据来确定每个驾驶员和保单的风险。他们如何定价是通过回顾。对于我们来说，我们可以更实时地看到它。我们是为未来而建立的保险公司。”

The company asserts that its AI claims system, called AVA, is the first of its kind system designed to accelerate the process of verifying and paying out insurance claims.

该公司声称其AI索赔系统称为AVA，是旨在加速核实和支付保险索赔过程的同类系统中的第一个。

Metromile said the data it is able to extract gives it the ability to do things such as reconstruct the scene of an accident to instantly determine if claim details are accurate. The company also says it can automatically approve payments once a claim is verified.

Metromile表示，它能够提取的数据使其能够执行诸如重建事故现场之类的事情，从而立即确定索赔细节是否准确。该公司还表示，一旦索赔得到核实，它可以自动批准付款。

The transaction includes $160 million PIPE led by Social Capital and joined by Miller Value, Clearbridge, Hudson Structured, Mark Cuban and New Enterprise Associates.

该交易包括由Social Capital牵头的1.6亿美元的PIPE，并由Miller Value，Clearbridge，Hudson Structured，Mark Cuban和New Enterprise Associates加入。

Metromile has two lines of business. First, it offers the straight to consumer pay per mile car insurance. And that unit’s run rate for 2020 is expected to be $111 million, according to Chen. Its second line of business revolves around its data science and cloud-based software-as-a-service (SaaS) business segment that it launched in 2019.

Metromile有两条业务线。首先，它向消费者提供每英里汽车保险的直接付款。根据Chen的说法，该部门2020年的运转率预计将达到1.11亿美元。它的第二条业务线围绕其于2019年推出的数据科学和基于云的软件即服务（SaaS）业务部门展开。

Looking ahead, Metromile is projecting $1 billion of premium run-rate by year-end 2024. Its SaaS business segment lets it profit from large incumbent insurers’ profit improvements. This has led to growing and recurring high-margin revenue, which is estimated to reach $48 million in 2024, according to the company.

展望未来，Metromile预计到2024年年底将实现10亿美元的保费收入率。其SaaS业务部门可使其从大型老牌保险公司的利润改善中获利。据该公司称，这导致高利润的收入不断增长和经常性收入，到2024年估计将达到4800万美元。

Specifically, Metromile said that by licensing key components of its technology platform – including claims automation and fraud detection tools –it is able to accelerate P&C carriers’ digital roadmaps and “meaningfully participates in the profit improvements they realize, generating growing and recurring high-margin revenue.”

Metromile特别指出，通过许可其技术平台的关键组件（包括理赔自动化和欺诈检测工具），它可以加速财产险承运人的数字路线图，并“有意义地参与他们实现的利润改善，产生不断增长的经常性利润收入。”

Metromile also says that it has a 59% loss ratio through Sept. 30, 2020, meaning that it has paid out 59 cents in claims on every $1.00 it has earned in premiums.

Metromile还表示，到2020年9月30日，损失率达到59％，这意味着它每赚取1.00美元的保费，就已经支付了59美分的理赔。

“What we’ve been doing for the last 18-24 months is really focusing on the right unit economics to scale a business, focus on profitability and build something really durable and sustaining,” Chen told FinLedger.

Chen告诉FinLedger：“过去18-24个月，我们一直在专注于正确的单位经济学以扩展业务，关注盈利能力并构建真正持久和可持续的业务。”

The company claims its customers save 47% on average compared to what they were paying their previous auto insurer.

该公司声称，与以前向汽车保险公司支付的费用相比，其客户平均节省了47％的费用。

In related news, Billtrust – a B2B provider of cloud-based software and integrated payment processing solutions – announced in October it would go public through a merger via SPAC  South Mountain Merger Corp. in a deal also valued at $1.3 billion, including debt.

在相关新闻中，Billtrust是一家基于云的软件和集成支付处理解决方案的B2B提供商，该公司于10月宣布将通过SPAC South Mountain Merger Corp.的合并进行公开发售，该交易的价值也包括债务在内，为13亿美元。

As FinLedger has previously  reported, SPACs (special purpose acquisition companies) are becoming an increasingly popular method to go public. In recent months, we’ve covered the launch of a number of fintech-focused SPACs, including  Cascade and  Lefteris.

正如FinLedger先前报道的那样，SPAC（特殊目的收购公司）正在成为一种越来越流行的上市方式。在最近几个月中，我们涵盖了一系列针对金融科技的SPAC的推出，包括Cascade和Lefteris。
