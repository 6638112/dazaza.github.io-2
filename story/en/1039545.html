Review of Apple's Fitness+ subscription service: a relatively good deal compared to rivals, with plenty of workouts, but requiring a Watch might put people off
回顾Apple的Fitness +订阅服务：与竞争对手相比，这是一笔不小的交易，需要进行大量的锻炼，但是需要配备Watch才能让人们失望

2020-12-14 22:34:36

One revelation I’ve had during the coronavirus crisis is that there is no way I’m paying for a gym again. Working out from home is more affordable and convenient. Depending on the app, it can be pretty dang fun, too. Goodbye forever, grimy locker-room showers. It’s been nice knowing you.

在冠状病毒危机期间，我得到的一个启示是，我再也无法为健身房付费。在家锻炼更实惠，更方便。取决于应用程序，它也可能非常有趣。永远再见，肮脏的更衣室淋浴。认识您真高兴。

Apple AAPL 0.40% Fitness+ is another digital workout subscription after your gym money. While the new program, launching Monday, arrives late to a well-established, highly competitive app category, it is right on time for people like me, who are still experimenting with different online training solutions. But the app’s chief requirement—that you own an Apple Watch—will likely turn off people who would otherwise give it a try.

Apple AAPL 0.40％Fitness +是继健身金后的另一项数字锻炼订阅。虽然新程序将于周一启动，但已进入成熟，竞争激烈的应用类别的后期，对于像我这样仍在尝试各种在线培训解决方案的人来说，这是正确的时机。但是，该应用程序的主要要求（即您拥有Apple Watch）可能会关闭那些会尝试的人。

What are your favorite at-home workouts? How have your workout habits changed during the pandemic? Join the conversation below.

您最喜欢的家庭锻炼是什么？大流行期间您的锻炼习惯有何变化？加入下面的对话。

Fitness+ will appear as a new tab in Apple’s existing Fitness app. A subscription comes with new weekly video workouts across 10 disciplines, including cycling, treadmill running, yoga and strength. An attractive cast of high-energy trainers draped head to toe in Nike —which has a partnership with Apple—hosts the sessions. Equally energetic music playlists serve as the soundtrack. (For the most part, that is: You can opt for the “Chill Vibes” playlist if that’s your thing.)

Fitness +将在Apple现有的Fitness应用中显示为新标签。订阅提供了涵盖10个学科的全新每周视频锻炼，包括骑自行车，跑步机，瑜伽和力量。与苹果有合作关系的耐克公司吸引了一批高能量的教练参加会议。同样充满活力的音乐播放列表将用作配乐。 （在大多数情况下，那就是：如果您愿意的话，您可以选择“冷颤”播放列表。）

Compared with similar fitness apps, it is a relatively good deal. Fitness+ costs $9.99 a month, and you can save by paying the $79.99 annual rate. Everyone in your Apple “family”—up to six people—gets access to the subscription. And there are potentially more savings on the $29.95-a-month Apple One Premier plan if you already subscribe to Apple Music and two terabytes of iCloud storage. The most analogous app, Peloton Digital, costs $12.99 a month for one user.

与类似的健身应用相比，这是一个相对不错的交易。 Fitness +每月收费9.99美元，您可以通过支付79.99美元的年费来节省费用。苹果“家庭”中的每个人（最多六个人）都可以访问订阅。如果您已经订阅了Apple Music和两个TB的iCloud存储，则每月29.95美元的Apple One Premier计划可能会节省更多费用。最类似的应用程序Peloton Digital，一个用户每月的费用为12.99美元。

There’s a catch with a not-so-small price tag: A Fitness+ subscription requires an Apple Watch, Series 3 ($199) or newer. While the app’s core workout experience is video, streamed on an iPhone, iPad or Apple TV, you can’t access the app without the smartwatch. If you forget to charge your watch, or left it at home while you are traveling, you can still access the videos and do workouts, but you need to physically own an Apple Watch to sign up for Fitness+.

有一个价格不菲的诱人之处：Fitness +订阅需要Apple Watch Series 3（199美元）或更高版本。该应用程序的核心锻炼体验是在iPhone，iPad或Apple TV上流传输的视频，但如果没有智能手表，您将无法访问该应用程序。如果您忘记为手表充电或在旅途中将手表留在家中，仍然可以观看视频并进行锻炼，但是您需要亲自拥有Apple Watch才能注册Fitness +。

Wherever you fire up the app, it syncs immediately with your watch—even if you use an Apple TV in somebody else’s house—and your heart rate and calorie data appear on screen. Any song featured in a workout can be added to your Apple Music library, if you are a subscriber. The watch serves as a remote control, able to play and pause classes on other Apple devices.

无论您在哪里启动该应用程序，它都将立即与您的手表同步（即使您在别人家中使用Apple TV），您的心率和卡路里数据也会显示在屏幕上。如果您是订户，则可以将锻炼中精选的任何歌曲添加到您的Apple Music库中。手表用作遥控器，可以在其他Apple设备上播放和暂停课程。

Apple also leverages any workout data you have shared to your iPhone’s Health app, including from third-party fitness apps. So if you’ve done yoga with Glo or cycling with Peloton, Fitness+ will use that history to recommend similar workouts.

苹果还利用您共享给iPhone的“健康”应用程序的任何锻炼数据，包括来自第三方健身应用程序的数据。因此，如果您使用Glo做瑜伽或使用Peloton骑自行车，Fitness +将使用该历史记录来推荐类似的锻炼。

The workouts I tried are comparable to the pricey in-person versions offered at boutique fitness studios—the cycling ones, in particular. One trainer, Ty, is a former SoulCycle instructor. Of course, this workout requires a stationary bike setup. The rides range from longer 45-minute sessions to ultra-quick 10-minute sprees. The workout itself includes a period of fast sprints, slower high-resistance climbs and recovery. You can match your pedal to the beat or meet the target rotations per minute called out by the trainer. A “burn bar” turns up the competition by comparing your calories-burned effort with others who have completed the workout.

我尝试的锻炼方式与精品健身工作室提供的昂贵的面对面锻炼相当，尤其是骑自行车的锻炼。一位培训师Ty是前SoulCycle讲师。当然，此锻炼需要固定的自行车设置。游乐设施从更长的45分钟课程到超快速的10分钟演出。锻炼本身包括快速冲刺，较慢的高阻力爬升和恢复的一段时间。您可以将踏板与节拍相匹配，也可以达到教练指定的每分钟目标旋转数。 “燃烧棒”通过将您消耗卡路里的精力与已完成锻炼的其他人进行比较，来增强竞争能力。

There is chill yoga, and there is energizing yoga that will work up a sweat. The high-intensity interval training, strength and core workouts are short—30 minutes or less—and I often played a couple in a row.

有凉爽的瑜伽，还有充满活力的瑜伽，将使您流汗。高强度的间歇训练，力量和核心锻炼很短（30分钟或更短），而且我经常连续玩几次。

Dance and rowing are two disciplines here that aren’t common in other fitness apps. While I didn’t have access to a rower, I could at least dance. Or try to, anyway. The workout—a 20-minute cardio-infused hip-hop class—was both terrible (as in, terribly hard) and stupidly fun. And according to the numbers on screen, I successfully arm-pumped my way to some burned calories.

舞蹈和划船是这里的两个学科，在其他健身应用中并不常见。虽然我没有划船的机会，但我至少可以跳舞。或者尝试尝试。这项为时20分钟的有氧运动嘻哈课的锻炼既可怕（非常艰难）又很愚蠢。根据屏幕上的数字，我成功地将自己的手臂抽到燃烧的卡路里中。

What differentiates Apple’s app is its approach to workout newbies. There is a starter collection of videos designed for anyone who is “brand new to exercise,” as a sort of introductory course to each of the app’s disciplines. And even in the harder sessions, one of the trainers in the background offers a modified version for less-advanced practitioners.

苹果应用程序的与众不同之处在于其锻炼新手的方法。有一个入门视频集，专为“初学者”而设计，它是该应用程序各个学科的入门课程。即使是在较艰苦的课程中，后台的一位培训师也为较不熟练的从业人员提供了改进版本。

Overall, Fitness+ feels like a Peloton Digital Lite (which doesn’t otherwise exist). Apple’s app and Peloton Digital have high production value, motivating music playlists and charismatic trainers. But Fitness+ doesn’t have as many workout types, and the library of workouts isn’t as large. It also lacks Peloton’s live and social features. The Apple app does have one edge over Peloton’s: You can download classes and play them offline. While you can preload Peloton content for more stable playback, the workouts require an active internet connection to play.

总体而言，Fitness +感觉就像是Peloton Digital Lite（原本就不存在）。苹果的应用程序和Peloton Digital具有很高的生产价值，可以激发音乐播放列表和超凡魅力的教练。但是Fitness +的锻炼类型并不多，并且锻炼库也不大。它还缺乏佩洛顿的生活和社交功能。 Apple应用程序确实具有Peloton的优势：您可以下载课程并离线播放。虽然您可以预加载Peloton内容以实现更稳定的播放，但这些锻炼需要有效的Internet连接才能播放。

There is plenty of room for improvement. Some high-intensity, strength and core workouts require dumbbells, while others don’t. I would like to be able to filter results, by “no equipment” or “weights,” for instance.

有很大的改进空间。一些高强度，力量和核心锻炼需要哑铃，而其他则不需要。我希望能够通过例如“没有设备”或“重量”来过滤结果。

Fitness+’s focus on metrics can be confusing, especially for the beginners at which the app is aimed. Fitness+ doesn’t provide any context around heart rate. Is the number shown good or bad? Is it higher or lower than a minute ago? Why care about heart rate at all? Actionable insights would be helpful here.

Fitness +对指标的关注可能会造成混淆，尤其是对于该应用针对的初学者而言。 Fitness +不提供有关心率的任何背景信息。显示的数字是好是坏？是高于还是低于一分钟前？为什么要完全关心心率？可行的见解在这里会有所帮助。

I like Zova’s approach. The iOS-only $60-a-year fitness and nutrition app similarly uses the Apple Watch to measure heart rate, and shows the data alongside the streamed exercise. The app’s workouts are based on heart-rate “zone” training, which is an assessment of the intensity of your workout as a percentage of your maximum heart rate. In other words, Zova shows you what to do with your numbers—something Apple could learn from.

我喜欢Zova的方法。每年仅售60美元的iOS健身和营养应用程序类似地使用Apple Watch来测量心率，并在流式运动旁边显示数据。该应用程序的锻炼基于心率“区域”训练，它是对锻炼强度与最大心率百分比的评估。换句话说，Zova向您展示了如何处理数字-这是Apple可以从中学到的东西。

Still, Fitness+ is a great value—at least for Apple Watch owners. But don’t pay up just yet. The one-month free trial should tide you over until you read my comprehensive guide, comparing the top fitness apps, sometime in January.

尽管如此，Fitness +还是很有价值的-至少对于Apple Watch所有者而言。但是暂时还不付款。这个为期一个月的免费试用应该可以帮助您，直到您阅读我的综合指南，比较一月份的顶级健身应用程序。
