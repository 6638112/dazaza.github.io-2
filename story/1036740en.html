AMD Ryzen 7 5800U Zen 3 Mobile Cezanne CPU Benchmark Leak Confirms Big IPC Gains
AMD锐龙7 5800U Zen 3移动塞尚CPU基准测试泄漏证明IPC取得了重大增长

2020-11-27 02:07:28

Eventually AMD will get around to porting its Zen 3 architecture over to its mobile CPUs. Exactly when remains to be seen, but perhaps sooner than later, if the handful of leaks we have covered are any indication. Zen 3 is still brand new, but what are purported to be AMD's unannounced Ryzen 5000 series of mobile CPUs have shown up in different places. Most recently, a supposed Ryzen 7 5800U has found its way to Geekbench.

最终，AMD会设法将其Zen 3架构移植到其移动CPU上。确切地说，还有待观察，但如果我们发现的少量泄漏有任何迹象，那么可能早于以后。 Zen 3仍然是全新的，但据称是AMD尚未宣布的Ryzen 5000系列移动CPU在不同的地方出现。最近，一个假定的Ryzen 7 5800U已找到通向Geekbench的路。

According to past leaks, the Ryzen 7 5800U is one of half a dozen upcoming Ryzen 5000 series mobile CPUs on tap from AMD. Interestingly, rumor has it that not all of them will be based on Zen 3—the lineup is said to be a mix of Zen 2 and Zen 3. That makes things needlessly confusing as far as I'm concerned, but if that is the case, I suppose AMD has its reasons.

根据过去的泄漏，Ryzen 7 5800U是AMD即将推出的六款即将推出的Ryzen 5000系列移动CPU之一。有趣的是，有传言说并非所有人都基于Zen3。据说该阵容是Zen 2和Zen 3的混合体。就我而言，这让事情变得不必要地令人困惑，但是如果那是如此，我想AMD有其原因。

The ones that are based on Zen 3 are codenamed Cezanne, while the Zen 2 parts carry the codename Lucienne. Here is the leaked lineup...

基于Zen 3的零件代号为塞尚，而Zen 2零件的代号为Lucienne。这是泄漏的阵容...

Ryzen 7 5800U (Zen 3): 8 cores/16 threads, 2GHz to 4.4GHz, 16MB L3, 8 Vega CUs at 2GHz

Ryzen 7 5800U（Zen 3）：8核/ 16线程，2GHz至4.4GHz，16MB L3，8 GHz Vega CU在2GHz

Ryzen 7 5700U (Zen 2): 8 cores/16 threads, 1.8GHz to 4.3GHz, 8MB L3, 8 Vega CUs at 1.9GHz

Ryzen 7 5700U（Zen 2）：8核/ 16线程，1.8GHz至4.3GHz，8MB L3，8个Vega CU在1.9GHz

Ryzen 5 5600U (Zen 3): 6 cores/12 threads, 2.3GHz to 4.2GHz, 12MB L3, 7 CUs at 1.8GHz

Ryzen 5 5600U（Zen 3）：6核/ 12线程，2.3GHz至4.2GHz，12MB L3，1.8GHz时有7个CU

Ryzen 5 5500U (Zen 2): 6 cores/12 threads, 2.1GHz to 4GHz, 8MB L3, 7 CUs at 1.8GHz

Ryzen 5 5500U（Zen 2）：6核/ 12线程，2.1GHz至4GHz，8MB L3，1.8GHz时有7个CU

Ryzen 3 5400U (Zen 3): 4 cores/8 threads, 2.6GHz to 4GHz, 8MB L3, 6 CUs at 1.6GHz

Ryzen 3 5400U（Zen 3）：4核/ 8线程，2.6GHz至4GHz，8MB L3，6个CU在1.6GHz

Ryzen 3 5300U (Zen 2): 4 cores/8 threads, 2.6GHz to 3.85GHz, 4MB L3, 6 CUs at 1.5GHz

Ryzen 3 5300U（Zen 2）：4核/ 8线程，2.6GHz至3.85GHz，4MB L3，6个CU在1.5GHz

None of this is confirmed, but if it turns out to be accurate, the Ryzen 7 5800U will be the top chip, at least initially. How will it perform? Let's have a look at its alleged Geekbench cameo...

这些都没有得到证实，但是如果事实证明是准确的，至少在最初，Ryzen 7 5800U将是顶级芯片。效果如何？让我们看看它所谓的Geekbench客串...

The specifications mostly match up with previous leaks, in that Geekbench recognizes the chip as having 8 cores and 16 threads, albeit with a 1.9GHz base clock (instead of 2GHz). Everything else is the same, though, including the 4.4GHz boost clock and 16MB of L3 cache. It is possible that Geekbench is not identifying the base clock correctly, but it is also possible that the specifications are simply different on what is an early sample, or that previous leaks had it wrong.

规格主要与先前的泄漏相匹配，因为Geekbench认为该芯片具有8个内核和16个线程，尽管具有1.9GHz基本时钟（而不是2GHz）。但是，其他所有内容都相同，包括4.4GHz增强时钟和16MB的L3缓存。 Geekbench可能无法正确识别基本时钟，但也有可能是早期样品的规格有所不同，或者先前的泄漏有误。

Regardless, we have some performance numbers to work with here. Specifically, it scored 1,421 in the single-core test, and 6,450 in the multi-core test. How does that compare?

无论如何，我们在这里需要一些性能指标。具体来说，它在单核测试中得分1,421，在多核测试中得分6,450。比较一下呢？

Well, if looking to compare IPC performance, perhaps the best approach is to look at the leaked Ryzen 7 5700U results. It's basically a Zen 2 variant of the Ryzen 7 5800U, but with slower clocks. Ideally the clocks would be the same, but we can still get a rough idea of what we're looking at here.

好吧，如果要比较IPC性能，也许最好的方法是查看泄漏的Ryzen 7 5700U结果。它基本上是Ryzen 7 5800U的Zen 2变体，但时钟速度较慢。理想情况下，时钟应该是相同的，但是我们仍然可以大致了解此处的内容。

Sifting through the scores, the best single-core result for a Ryzen 7 5700U is 1,192. The Ryzen 7 5800U scored 19.2 percent higher, with a base clock that is 5.5 percent higher if it is being read correctly, and a boost clock that is 2.3 percent faster. Meanwhile, the best multi-threaded score for a Ryzen 7 5700U is 6,595, which flips the script and puts it a little ahead of the Ryzen 7 5800U (most of the scores come out a couple hundred points below, though, as we would expect to be the case).

筛选分数，Ryzen 7 5700U的最佳单核成绩为1,192。 Ryzen 7 5800U得分提高了19.2％，如果正确读取，则基本时钟提高了5.5％，增强时钟提高了2.3％。同时，Ryzen 7 5700U的最佳多线程得分是6,595，这会翻转脚本并将其放在Ryzen 7 5800U的前面（尽管大多数得分低于我们预期的几百分）确实如此）。

Based on the available data, we are potentially looking an IPC gain of 15 percent or more. But of course we will need to wait for these parts to actually be announced and shipped out to actually make that determination. In the meantime, we're sure there will be more leaks to evaluate as time goes on.

根据现有数据，我们潜在的IPC增益有望达到15％或更高。但是，当然，我们将需要等待这些零件被实际宣布并发货后才能真正做出决定。同时，我们相信随着时间的流逝，还会有更多的泄漏需要评估。
