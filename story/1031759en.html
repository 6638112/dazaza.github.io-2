手机游戏发行商Scopely融资3.4亿美元，消息人士称，融资后估值为33亿美元

2020-10-28 21:28:24

Scopely, the publisher of games like Star Trek: Fleet Command and Marvel Strike Force, has raised $340 million to continue its mobile gaming growth plans as players embrace games during the pandemic.

星际迷航：舰队司令部和漫威打击部队等游戏的发行商Scopely筹集了3.4亿美元，以继续其手机游戏增长计划，因为玩家在大流行期间接受了游戏。

The deal values Scopely at $3.3 billion (according to a source close to the matter), post-money, and it gives the company a war chest so that it can continue to make big acquisitions like the purchase of FoxNext Games last January from Disney. It also shows that some big, experienced investors are willing to double down on games as they become a giant industry in the midst of COVID-19.

这笔交易对Scopely的估值为33亿美元(据知情人士透露)，这笔交易为该公司提供了一笔资金，这样它就可以继续进行大规模收购，比如去年1月从迪士尼手中收购FoxNext Games。这也表明，一些经验丰富的大投资者愿意在游戏上加倍下注，因为游戏在新冠肺炎中间成为了一个巨大的产业。

The fifth round of funding comes from Wellington Management, NewView Capital, TSG Consumer Partners, CPP Investments, funds managed by BlackRock, D1, Battery Ventures, Eldridge, Declaration Partners, and Moore Strategic Ventures. Additional investors in the round include Greycroft, Baillie Gifford, Sands Capital, Revolution Growth, and Highland Capital Partners.

第五轮资金来自惠灵顿管理公司(Wellington Management)、NewView Capital、TSG Consumer Partners、CPP Investments、贝莱德(BlackRock)管理的基金、D1、Battery Ventures、Eldridge、宣言伙伴公司和摩尔战略风险投资公司(Moore Strategic Ventures)。这轮融资的其他投资者包括Greycroft、贝利·吉福德(Baillie Gifford)、金沙资本(Sands Capital)、Revine Growth和Highland Capital Partners。

The amount of money is impressive, and it shows that game companies are becoming more valuable and expensive to buy. So Scopely needs more money to do its deals. Scopely has a diversified portfolio of top-grossing games, and it will use the funds to accelerate its ambition to become the top mobile games company in the West. The capital will continue to support Scopely’s acquisition strategy, as well as its expansion into new product categories.

金额令人印象深刻，这表明游戏公司正在变得更有价值和更昂贵的收购。因此，Scopely需要更多资金来完成交易。Scopely拥有收入最高的游戏的多元化投资组合，它将利用这笔资金加快成为西方顶级手机游戏公司的雄心。首都将继续支持Scopely的收购战略，以及向新产品类别的扩张。

“We had a really successful year, and we will close the year with more than $900 million in revenue,” said Javier Ferreira, Scopely co-CEO, in an interview with GamesBeat.

Scopely联席首席执行官哈维尔·费雷拉(Javier Ferreira)在接受GamesBeat采访时表示：“我们度过了非常成功的一年，我们将以超过9亿美元的收入结束这一年。”

Ferreira said that the company is profitable and has been for some time. Star Trek: Fleet Command has completed its eighth consecutive quarter of revenue growth. Yahtzee with Friends, a five-year-old game, continues to grow. And the company had a successful launch of Scrabble Go in March.

费雷拉说，该公司是盈利的，而且已经有一段时间了。《星际迷航：舰队司令部》已完成连续第八个季度营收增长。一款已有五年历史的游戏“与朋友在一起”(Yahtzee With Friends)继续增长。该公司在3月份成功推出了拼字游戏Go。

“On the organic growth side of things, things have been going really well. And then, on the M&A side, we made a large acquisition, back in February with the acquisition of FoxNext Games,” Ferreira said. “It was a big bet for us. We have also been able to grow Marvel Strike Force by integrating it into our technology platform, and it is growing at more than 50% in its monthly run rate.”

“在有机增长方面，事情进行得非常顺利。然后，在并购方面，我们进行了一次大规模收购，早在2月份就收购了FoxNext Games，“费雷拉说。“对我们来说，这是一个很大的赌注。我们还通过将Marvel Strike Force整合到我们的技术平台来壮大它，而且它每月的运行速度都在以50%以上的速度增长。“。

Los Angeles-based Scopely has grown to more than 950 employees, many who are game developers in offices spread around the world. It also works with second-party studios, which makes games for Scopely under contract. Scopely occasionally buys those studios, as it did in April when it acquired PierPlay, the developer of its successful Scrabble Go game. Scopely also has a centralized tech platform and user acquisition teams that help its games get traction.

总部位于洛杉矶的Scopely已经发展到950多名员工，其中许多人是世界各地办公室的游戏开发人员。它还与第二方工作室合作，后者根据合同为Scopely制作游戏。Scopely偶尔会收购这些工作室，就像它在4月份收购了PierPlay时所做的那样，PierPlay是其成功的拼字游戏Go的开发商。Scopely还拥有一个集中的技术平台和用户获取团队，帮助其游戏获得吸引力。

“Scopely has built a highly differentiated platform that allows them to consistently develop and launch hit games across multiple genres and garner best-in-class user engagement,” said Jenny Baxter Moser, managing director at TSG Consumer Partners, in an email. “We are thrilled to have the opportunity to partner with the Scopely team as they continue their rapid expansion in the gaming industry.”

TSG Consumer Partners董事总经理珍妮·巴克斯特·莫泽(Jenny Baxter Moser)在一封电子邮件中表示：“Scopely建立了一个高度差异化的平台，使他们能够持续开发和推出多种流派的热门游戏，并获得一流的用户参与度。”我们很高兴有机会与Scopely团队合作，因为他们继续在游戏行业快速扩张。“。

In September, Scopely deepened its partnership with ViacomCBS to bring the entire Star Trek film and TV universe to its Star Trek: Fleet Command game. The title has an engaged audience,  with players spending an average of four hours playing each day. Marvel Strike Force has also seen dramatic growth since joining Scopely’s ecosystem following the company’s acquisition of FoxNext Games LA from Disney earlier this year.

今年9月，Scopely加深了与维亚康姆CBS的合作伙伴关系，将整个星际迷航影视世界带入其“星际迷航：舰队司令部”游戏中。这个游戏拥有一批敬业的观众，玩家平均每天花在游戏上的时间为4个小时。今年早些时候，Scopely从迪士尼手中收购了FoxNext Games LA，自加入Scopely的生态系统以来，Marvel Strike Force也出现了戏剧性的增长。

Scopely was founded in 2011, and it has offices in Los Angeles, Barcelona, Boulder, Dublin, London, and Tokyo. But like many places, the employees are working from home in many locations.

Scopely成立于2011年，在洛杉矶、巴塞罗那、博尔德、都柏林、伦敦和东京设有办事处。但像许多地方一样，员工们在许多地方在家工作。

Ferreira said there is some uncertainty related to the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), as Apple is retiring it in the name of privacy and possibly to the detriment of targeted advertising. But he said while it may affect the marketing ecosystem, he said it is not causing Scopely to hesitate, and he said he does not believe it will change the fundamentals of player demand for games.

费雷拉说，广告商标识符(IDFA)存在一些不确定性，因为苹果以隐私的名义将其停用，可能会损害定向广告。但他表示，虽然这可能会影响营销生态系统，但他表示，这并没有让Scopely犹豫不决，他说，他不相信这会改变玩家对游戏需求的基本面。

“We want to continue to make big bets in the interactive entertainment space,” Ferreira said.

费雷拉说：“我们希望继续在互动娱乐领域下大赌注。”

Asked if the company considered going public through a special purpose acquisition company or an initial public offering, Ferreira said the company is focused on its core strategy of acquiring companies and growing its own games as a private company. But he said Scopely will have options in the future as it continues to grow.

当被问及该公司是否考虑通过特殊目的收购公司或首次公开募股(IPO)上市时，费雷拉表示，该公司专注于收购公司的核心战略，并作为一家私人公司发展自己的游戏。但他表示，随着Scopely的继续增长，它在未来将有选择。

Scopely isn’t launching any major games this year, but it will likely have big launches in 2021 and 2022, Ferreira said.

费雷拉说，Scopely今年不会推出任何大型游戏，但它可能会在2021年和2022年推出大型游戏。

“We hope that between our organic growth of products and our M&A, we will see continued acceleration in 2021 and beyond,” Ferreira said. “We have big hopes and grand ambitions for the company we are trying to build. Our roadmap has never been more exciting than it is today.”

费雷拉说：“我们希望，在我们产品的有机增长和我们的并购之间，我们将在2021年及以后看到持续的加速。”“我们对我们正在努力打造的公司抱有很大的希望和宏伟的抱负。我们的路线图从未像今天这样激动人心。“
