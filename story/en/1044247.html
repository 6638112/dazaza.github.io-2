US grid will see 80 percent of its new capacity go emission-free
美国电网将看到其新装机容量的80％实现无排放

2021-01-15 20:58:21

Earlier this week, the US Energy Information Agency (EIA) released figures on the new generating capacity that's expected to start operating over the course of 2021. While plans can obviously change, the hope is that, with its new additions, the grid will look radically different than it did just five years ago. This includes the details, where a new nuclear plant may be started up, although it will be dwarfed by the capacity of new batteries. But the big picture is that, even ignoring the batteries, about 80 percent of the planned capacity additions will be emission-free.

本周早些时候，美国能源信息署（EIA）发布了有关新发电量的数据，预计该发电量将在2021年开始运营。尽管计划可以明显改变，但希望是，随着新发电量的增加，网格看起来将与五年前完全不同。这包括细节，可能会在哪里启动新的核电站，尽管这将与新电池的容量相形见war。但是，大的情况是，即使不考虑电池，计划增加的容量中约有80％将是无排放的。

The EIA's accounting shows that just under 40 Gigawatts of capacity will be placed on the grid during 2021, but there are a number of caveats to this. First and foremost is the inclusion of batteries, which account for over 10 percent of that figure (4.3GW). While batteries may look like short-term generating capacity from the perspective of "can this put power on the grid?", they're obviously not actually a net source of power. Typically, they're used to smooth over short-term fluctuations in supply or demand rather than a steady source of power.

EIA的核算显示，到2021年，将并网发电量将近40吉瓦，但是对此有很多警告。首先是电池，占电池总数（4.3GW）的10％以上。从“这可以将电力投入电网吗？”的角度来看，电池看起来像是短期发电能力，但它们显然实际上并不是净电力来源。通常，它们用于缓解供应或需求的短期波动，而不是稳定的动力源。

Still, given the rarity of grid-scale batteries even a few years ago, 4.3GW of them is striking.

尽管如此，考虑到甚至几年前电网规模电池的稀缺性，其中的4.3GW还是惊人的。

Another oddity is a matter of timing. Last year, Virginia's Dominion Energy started up two offshore wind turbines as part of a pilot project that will lead the way toward the much larger Coastal Virginia Offshore Wind, which will top out at 2.6GW when it's completed in 2024. But these turbines won't formally enter commercial service until this year, so their 1.2MW count towards the 2021 figure.

另一个奇怪之处是时间问题。去年，作为一项试点项目的一部分，弗吉尼亚州的Dominion能源公司启动了两台海上风力涡轮机，这将带动更大的弗吉尼亚沿海离岸风能发电，建成后将达到2.6GW的最高水平。这些涡轮机将于2024年投入运营。但直到今年，这些涡轮机才能正式投入商业运营，因此它们的1.2MW功率将计入2021年的数字。

Another case where the figures may end up not matching reality is the addition of 1.1GW of nuclear power in the form of a new reactor at the Vogtle site in Georgia. Construction of this plant and its twin has run consistently late and over-budget. If it's actually completed, it will represent the first new nuclear capacity added to the US grid in several years, and will partly offset the closure of some smaller plants in recent years.

数字可能最终与现实不符的另一种情况是在佐治亚州Vogtle基地以新反应堆的形式增加了1.1GW的核电。该工厂及其孪生工厂的建设一直持续到后期且预算过高。如果它真正完成，它将代表几年来美国电网增加的第一个新核能，并将部分抵消近年来一些较小工厂的关闭。

But the big story in 2021 will be the shift away from fossil fuels. Despite the Trump administration's attempts to tilt the playing field in favor of coal, there will be no new coal capacity added in 2021. Given the recent closure of several large plants, this is a guarantee that the rapid plunge in US coal use is set to continue.

但是2021年的大故事将是摆脱化石燃料的转变。尽管特朗普政府试图倾斜竞争环境以支持煤炭，但2021年将不会增加新的煤炭产能。考虑到最近关闭了几家大型工厂，这保证了美国煤炭迅速下跌。使用将继续。

Up until very recently, coal was largely being displaced by natural gas. But in recent years, wind power prices dropped to the point where a well-sited wind farm in the US could produce power at a price that was lower than the cost of buying fuel for an existing natural gas plant. And grid-scale photovoltaics had actually started to undercut wind power. But the market didn't respond to this new reality instantly, leaving natural gas dominating the capacity added to the US grid in recent years.

直到最近，煤炭很大程度上还是被天然气替代。但是近年来，风电价格下降到了这样一个地步，即美国位置良好的风电场可以以低于购买现有天然气厂燃料成本的价格发电。网格规模的光伏发电实际上已经开始削弱风力发电。但是市场并没有立即对这一新现实做出反应，这使得天然气成为近年来美国电网新增产能的主导。

The market is now clearly responding. Under 20 percent of the capacity added in 2021 will be natural gas (6.6GW), even if you disregard the battery capacity being added. That's down from 34 percent just two years ago, and over 60 percent three years ago. new additions are heavily concentrated in areas where a lot of natural gas is produced: southern Texas and near the Ohio/Pennsylvania/West Virginia borders.

市场现在显然正在做出反应。即使不考虑增加的电池容量，到2021年增加的容量中不到20％将是天然气（6.6GW）。与两年前的34％和三年前的60％相比有所下降。新产品主要集中在生产大量天然气的地区：德克萨斯州南部和俄亥俄州/宾夕法尼亚州/西弗吉尼亚州边界附近。

Instead, wind and solar dominated, with 12.2GW of new wind capacity, and 15.4GW of solar power. This is especially striking given that tax credits for renewable energy were set to phase out in 2020, leading many projects to be pushed through to completion late in the year. (Those credits ended up being extended in a recent budget bill.) Normally, the EIA predicts that these sorts of expirations cause the pipeline of new projects to empty out, leading to a temporary drop. There is absolutely no indication that this took place in 2021.

取而代之的是，风能和太阳能占主导地位，新增风电容量为12.2GW，太阳能发电量为15.4GW。鉴于可再生能源税收抵免计划在2020年逐步淘汰，这导致许多项目在今年下半年完成，这尤其令人震惊。 （这些信用最终在最近的预算法案中得到了延长。）通常，EIA预测，这些到期会导致新项目的管道用完，从而导致暂时的下降。绝对没有迹象表明这发生在2021年。

Notably, these values don't include residential solar, which is expected to add another 3GW to 4GW of capacity in 2021.

值得注意的是，这些价值不包括住宅太阳能，预计到2021年将再增加3GW至4GW的容量。

As in the past, Texas is one of the major sites of new wind construction, joined by its northern neighbor Oklahoma. Planned additions include a windfarm that's just a tiny bit short of a GigaWatt. Texas is also dominating solar additions, with over a quarter of the expected new capacity. Nevada and California will also be installing about 10 percent each, but they're closely followed by North Carolina, part of a growing trend of solar installations in the US Southeast.

与过去一样，得克萨斯州是新风电建设的主要地点之一，北部邻国俄克拉荷马州也加入了这一行列。计划中的新增项目包括一个风力发电场，仅比GigaWatt短一点。得克萨斯州也以新增产能的四分之一为主导，增加了太阳能。内华达州和加利福尼亚州也将分别安装约10％的太阳能，但紧随其后的是北卡罗莱纳州，这是美国东南部太阳能安装增长趋势的一部分。

The Biden administration expects to promote additional renewable energy projects as part of its pandemic recovery plan. While they're unlikely to arrive quickly enough to alter the 2021 numbers, they could boost wind and solar even further in 2022. There are also a number of very large (GW-plus) offshore wind projects being developed along the US East Coast, some of which may start entering service over the next few years.

拜登政府希望将更多的可再生能源项目作为其大流行恢复计划的一部分。尽管它们不太可能很快到达以改变2021年的数字，但它们可能会在2022年进一步推动风能和太阳能的发展。在美国东部沿岸也有许多大型（GW +）海上风电项目正在开发中海岸，其中一些可能会在未来几年开始服役。
