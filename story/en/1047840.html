Amazon partners with Foxconn to establish its first device manufacturing line in India, operational later this year, to make Fire TV sticks locally
亚马逊与富士康合作，在印度建立了第一条设备生产线，并将于今年晚些时候投入运营，以在当地生产消防电视棒。

2021-02-17 18:44:20

Amazon is setting up its first device manufacturing line in India to make Fire TV sticks locally and has partnered with Cloud Network Technology, a subsidiary of Foxconn, to begin producing the device at its Chennai plant later this year. The e-commerce giant claimed that the production line will have an annual capacity of “hundreds of thousands” of units, and will also continuously evaluate manufacturing capacity based on domestic demand for its devices. “We are delighted to announce Amazon’s first manufacturing line in India to produce hundreds of thousands of Fire TV Stick devices every year, catering to the demands of Indian customers,” said Amit Agarwal, global senior vice president and country leader at Amazon India. “This further reiterates our commitment to the Government of India’s ‘Make in India’ initiative.” The move, the company said, is in line with its plan to partner with the government’s vision of a self-reliant India, or Atmanirbhar Bharat. It reiterated its commitment to invest $1 billion into the country to digitise 10 million small and medium businesses by 2025 and create 1 million additional jobs by then. “India is an attractive investment destination and is poised to become a major player in the global supply chain in the electronics and IT products industry. We welcome Amazon’s decision to set up a manufacturing line in Chennai, as it will enhance domestic production capacities, and create jobs as well,” said Ravi Shankar Prasad, Minister for Communications, Electronics & Information Technology and Law & Justice. Prasad also mentioned that it was only the beginning of Amazon's efforts to manufacture electronics products in India for the domestic market and for exports. People privy of the discussions told ET that the Union Minister told Amazon that while doing business in India they must also provide international exposure to Indian artisans and their products. Madhubani paintings from Bihar, Varanasi’s wooden toys, Benarasi Sarees are examples of some products, which must receive attention, he added. “Value addition to a whole range of Indian products must be a thrust area for Amazon. Another important area, which must receive focus, is skilling of Indian artisans. In line with the PM’s vision, promotion of Eastern and North Eastern India is critical for overall development of India and special attention should be paid to these geographies. Next, the hinterland of India especially of eastern India including Bihar should also see the fruits of manufacturing and today’s announcement should just be the beginning of manufacturing in a big way,” Prasad is understood to have told Amazon according to sources. Amazon’s move to begin locally manufacturing some of its devices in India comes at a time when the company is facing increased heat over its e-commerce marketplace operations. It is also engaged in a battle to stop rival Reliance Industries from acquiring assets of Future Group in which it had invested.

亚马逊正在印度建立其第一条设备生产线，以在当地生产消防电视棒，并已与富士康的子公司Cloud Network Technology合作，于今年晚些时候开始在其钦奈工厂生产该设备。这家电子商务巨头声称，该生产线的年产能将达到“数十万”，并且还将根据其设备的国内需求不断评估其制造能力。 “我们很高兴宣布亚马逊在印度的第一条生产线，以满足印度客户的需求，每年生产数十万台消防电视棒设备。”亚马逊印度全球高级副总裁兼国家领导人阿米特•阿加瓦尔（Amit Agarwal）说。 “这进一步重申了我们对印度政府“印度制造”倡议的承诺。”该公司表示，此举符合其计划与政府关于自力更生的印度或Atmanirbhar Bharat的愿景合作的计划。它重申了向该国投资10亿美元的承诺，到2025年使1000万家中小企业数字化，并在那时之前创造100万个额外就业机会。 “印度是一个有吸引力的投资目的地，并有望成为电子和IT产品行业全球供应链的主要参与者。我们欢迎亚马逊决定在金奈建立生产线，因为这将提高国内生产能力，并创造就业机会。”电子，通讯与通信部长Ravi Shankar Prasad说。信息技术与法律正义。普拉萨德还提到，这仅仅是亚马逊在印度为国内市场和出口生产电子产品的努力的开始。缺乏讨论的人士告诉ET，联邦部长告诉亚马逊，在印度开展业务时，他们还必须向印度的工匠及其产品提供国际服务。他补充说，比哈尔邦的Madhubani画，瓦拉纳西的木制玩具，贝纳拉西·萨雷斯（Benarasi Sarees）是一些产品的例子，必须引起注意。 “整个印度产品的增值必须成为亚马逊的重点领域。另一个必须引起关注的重要领域是印度工匠的技能。与总理的愿景一致，促进印度东部和东北部地区的发展对于印度的总体发展至关重要，应特别注意这些地区。接下来，印度的腹地，尤其是包括比哈尔邦在内的印度东部，也应该看到制造业的成果，而今天的宣布应该仅仅是制造业的开始。”据消息人士称，普拉萨德告诉亚马逊。亚马逊开始在印度本地生产某些设备的举动是在该公司面对其电子商务市场运营日益激烈的时候。它还参与了阻止竞争对手Reliance Industries收购其投资的Future Group资产的斗争。
