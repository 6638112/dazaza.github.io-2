-2000 Lines of Code
-2000代码行

2021-03-11 00:37:57

In early 1982, the Lisa software team was trying to buckle down for the big push to ship the software within the next six months. Some of the managers decided that it would be a good idea to track the progress of each individual engineer in terms of the amount of code that they wrote from week to week. They devised a form that each engineer was required to submit every Friday, which included a field for the number of lines of code that were written that week.

1982年初，丽莎软件团队正试图为大推动在未来六个月内发货的大推动。一些管理人员决定在他们从一周写入的代码中跟踪每个人工工程的进度是一个好主意。它们设计了一种表格，每个工程师都需要提交每个星期五，其中包括那周写入的代码行数的领域。

Bill Atkinson, the author of Quickdraw and the main user interface designer, who was by far the most important Lisa implementor, thought that lines of code was a silly measure of software productivity. He thought his goal was to write as small and fast a program as possible, and that the lines of code metric only encouraged writing sloppy, bloated, broken code. He recently was working on optimizing Quickdraw's region calculation machinery, and had completely rewritten the region engine using a simpler, more general algorithm which, after some tweaking, made region operations almost six times faster. As a by-product, the rewrite also saved around 2,000 lines of code. He was just putting the finishing touches on the optimization when it was time to fill out the management form for the first time. When he got to the lines of code part, he thought about it for a second, and then wrote in the number: -2000. I'm not sure how the managers reacted to that, but I do know that after a couple more weeks, they stopped asking Bill to fill out the form, and he gladly complied.

Bill Atkinson是QuickDraw和主要用户界面设计师的作者，迄今为止最重要的LISA实现者，认为代码线是软件生产力的愚蠢衡量标准。他认为他的目标是尽可能小而快速地写作，并且代码公制的行只鼓励写邋，臃肿，破碎的代码。他最近正在努力优化QuickDraw＆＃39; S区域计算机械，并使用更简单，更常规的算法完全重写该区域发动机，这在一些调整后，使区域操作速度速度几乎六倍。作为副产品，重写还保存了大约2,000行代码。他刚刚在优化时才触及完成，这是时候填写了第一次填写管理表。当他到达代码部分时，他考虑了一秒钟，然后写在数字：-2000。我不确定经理如何对此作出反应，但我知道，在几周后，他们停止要求票据填写表格，他很乐意遵守。
