问Slashdot：你升级到MacOS Big Sur了吗？

2020-11-14 07:37:39

Several users have complained about Apple services such as iMessage, or even Apple Pay, not working for them. For those wanting to download and install the company’s major update for Macs titled macOS Big Sur, they’re experiencing problems with that too. Apple has apparently acknowledged the issue, with its employees working around the clock to ensure timely restoration of all services.

一些用户抱怨iMessage，甚至Apple Pay等苹果服务不能为他们服务。对于那些想要下载和安装苹果公司针对Mac的名为MacOS Big Sur的重大更新的人来说，他们也遇到了问题。苹果显然已经承认了这个问题，其员工夜以继日地工作，以确保所有服务的及时恢复。

If you visit Apple’s System Status page to see which services are available for a wide range of products, you’ll be able to monitor why exactly your device or Mac aren’t functioning properly. One of those problems happens to be with the macOS Software Update tool. Considering that Mac owners all around the world wanted to experience what macOS Big Sur was all about today, this isn’t great timing for Apple at all.

如果你访问苹果的系统状态页面，看看各种产品都有哪些服务可用，你就能监控到你的设备或Mac电脑无法正常工作的确切原因。其中一个问题恰好出现在MacOS软件更新工具上。考虑到世界各地的Mac用户都想体验MacOS Big Sur今天的一切，现在对苹果来说根本不是一个好时机。

At the time of writing, Apple’s System Status page shows that its services have resumed normal activity. However, when we kept on refreshing Twitter, we noticed a significant number of Tweets talked about the same issue. In addition to the macOS Software Update tool, Apple was experiencing issues with Apple Card, with owners not managing their credit card via the website. Apple Pay was also experiencing issues, along with Maps Traffic and Maps Routing & Navigation.

在撰写本文时，苹果的系统状态页面显示其服务已恢复正常活动。然而，当我们不断更新推特时，我们注意到有相当多的推文谈论同样的问题。除了MacOS软件更新工具，苹果还遇到了Apple Card的问题，用户无法通过网站管理他们的信用卡。Apple Pay也遇到了问题，还有地图流量和地图路线导航。

Thankfully, for those who are still experiencing issues, there might be a fix for this, though it’s not from Apple, meaning it may or may not work for you.

值得庆幸的是，对于那些仍在经历问题的人来说，可能会有解决这个问题的方法，尽管它不是来自苹果，这意味着它可能会对你起作用，也可能不会。

According to Jeff Thompson, who goes by the Twitter handle @lapcatsoftware, a temporary fix involving a paid app called Little Snitch appears to help fix the problem. Thompson has highlighted the steps to follow below, though we also recommend our readers to go through the entire thread if they continue running into hiccups.

杰夫·汤普森(Jeff Thompson)在推特上的账号是@rapcatsoft，他表示，一个涉及一款名为Little Snitch的付费应用的临时修复程序似乎有助于解决这个问题。汤普森强调了下面要遵循的步骤，但我们也建议我们的读者，如果他们继续遇到问题，请仔细阅读整个帖子。

If you're now experiencing hangs launching apps on the Mac, I figured out the problem using Little Snitch.

如果你现在遇到在Mac上启动应用程序挂起的问题，我用小飞贼解决了这个问题。
