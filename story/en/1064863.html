Self-driving Waymo trucks to haul loads between Houston and Fort Worth
自行车的Waymo卡车在休斯顿和沃斯堡之间的载重量

2021-06-10 22:35:39

On Thursday morning, Waymo announced that it is working with trucking company JB Hunt to autonomously haul cargo loads in Texas. Class 8 JB Hunt trucks equipped with the autonomous driving software and hardware system called Waymo Driver will operate on I-45 in Texas, taking cargo between Houston and Fort Worth.

星期四早上，Waymo宣布它正在使用Tracking Company JB Hunt，在德克萨斯州自动运输货物负荷。 8级JB狩猎卡车配备自动驾驶软件和名为Waymo驱动程序的硬件系统将在德克萨斯州的I-45上运营，在休斯顿和沃斯堡之间乘运货物。

However, the trucks will still carry humans—a trained truck driver and Waymo technicians—to supervise and take over if necessary.

然而，卡车仍将携带人类 - 一个培训的卡车司机和Waymo技术人员 - 在必要时监督和接管。

Although Waymo is better known for the autonomous taxi service it operates in a suburb of Phoenix, the company started experimenting with adding its autonomous technology to freight haulers several years ago. And in 2018, it began testing those trucks in the Atlanta area. What makes today's news more notable is the partnership with a major truck operator.

虽然Waymo对于它在凤凰城郊运营的自主出租车服务中更好地了解，但该公司开始尝试在几年前增加其自主技术来运费运输。在2018年，它开始在亚特兰大地区测试这些卡车。是什么让今天的新闻更加值得注意是与主要卡车运营商的合作关系。

"This will be one of the first opportunities for JB Hunt to receive data and feedback on customer freight moved with a Class 8 tractor operating at this level of autonomy. While we believe there will be a need for highly skilled, professional drivers for many years to come, it is important for JB Hunt as an industry leader to be involved early in the development of advanced autonomous technologies and driving systems to ensure that their implementation will improve efficiency while enhancing safety," said Craig Harper, chief sustainability officer at JB Hunt.
