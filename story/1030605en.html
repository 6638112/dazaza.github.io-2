Render为其DevOps平台筹集了450万美元

2020-10-23 00:45:39

The round was led by General Catalyst, with participation from previous investors South Park Commons Fund and a group of angels that includes Lee Fixel, Elad Gil and GitHub CTO (and former VP of Engineering at Heroku) Jason Warner.

这轮融资由General Catalyst牵头，之前的投资者South Park Commons Fund和一群天使参与其中，其中包括Lee Fixel、Elad Gil和GitHub CTO(以及Heroku的前工程副总裁)Jason Warner。

The company, which describes itself as a ‘Zero DevOps alternative to AWS, Azure and Google Cloud,’ originally raised a $2.25 million seed round in April 2019, but it got a lot of inbound interest after winning the Disrupt Battlefield. In the end, though, the team decided to simply raise more money from its existing investors.

该公司自称是AWS、Azure和谷歌云(Google Cloud)的“Zero DevOps替代品”，最初在2019年4月筹集了225万美元的种子轮资金，但在赢得“扰乱战场”(Disrupt Battlefield)后获得了大量入场兴趣。但最终，该团队决定简单地从现有投资者那里筹集更多资金。

“We spoke to a bunch of people after Disrupt, including Ashton Kutcher’s firm, because he was one of the judges,” Render co-founder and CEO Anurag Goel explained. “In the end, we decided that we would just raise more money from our existing investors because we like them and it helped us get a better deal from our existing investors. And they were all super interested in continuing to invest.”

Render的联合创始人兼首席执行官阿努拉格·戈尔解释说：“我们在Disrupt之后采访了很多人，包括阿什顿·库彻的公司，因为他是评委之一。”“最终，我们决定从现有投资者那里筹集更多资金，因为我们喜欢他们，这帮助我们从现有投资者那里获得了更好的交易。他们都对继续投资非常感兴趣。“。

What makes Render stand out is that it fulfills many of the promises of Heroku and maybe Google Cloud’s App Engine. You simply tell it what kind of service you are going to deploy and it handles the deployment and manages the infrastructure for you.

让Render脱颖而出的是它实现了Heroku的许多承诺，也许还有Google Cloud的App engine。您只需告诉它您要部署哪种服务，它就会为您处理部署和管理基础设施。

“Our customers are all people who are writing code. And they just want to deploy this code really easily without having to worry about servers, or maintenance, or depending on DevOps teams — or, in many cases, hiring DevOps teams,” Goel said. “DevOps engineers are extremely expensive to hire and extremely hard to find, especially good ones. Our goal is to eliminate all of that work that DevOps people do at every company, because it’s very similar at every company.”

“我们的客户都是编写代码的人。他们只是想真正轻松地部署这些代码，而不必担心服务器、维护或依赖于DevOps团队--或者在许多情况下，雇佣DevOps团队。“Goel说。“DevOps的工程师非常昂贵，而且很难找到，特别是优秀的。我们的目标是消除DevOps人员在每家公司所做的所有工作，因为每家公司的情况都非常相似。“。

One new feature the company is launching today is preview environments. You can think of them as disposable staging or development environments that developers can spin up to test their code — and Render promises that the testing environment will look the same as your production environment (or you can specify changes, too). Developers can then test their updates collaborative with QA or their product and sales teams in this environment.

该公司今天推出的一项新功能是预览环境。您可以将它们视为一次性试运行或开发环境，开发人员可以启动它们来测试他们的代码，并承诺测试环境将与您的生产环境看起来相同(或者您也可以指定更改)。然后，开发人员可以在此环境中与QA或其产品和销售团队协作测试他们的更新。

Development teams on Render specify their infrastructure environments in a YAML file and turning on these new preview environments is as easy as setting a flag in that file.

渲染时开发团队在一个YAML文件中指定他们的基础设施环境，打开这些新的预览环境就像在该文件中设置标志一样简单。

“Once they do that, then for every pull request – because we’re integrated with GitHub and GitLab — we automatically spin up a copy of that environment. That include that can include anything you have in production, or things like a Redis instance, or managed Postgres database, or Elasticsearch instance, or obviously API’s and web services and static sites,” Goel said. Every time you push a change to that branch or pull request, the environment is automatically updated, too. Once the pull request is closed or merger, Render destroys the environment automatically.

“一旦他们这样做了，那么对于每个拉取请求-因为我们与GitHub和GitLab集成-我们会自动启动该环境的副本。这包括生产中的任何东西，或者像Redis实例、托管Postgres数据库、Elasticsearch实例，或者显然是API、Web服务和静态站点。“Goel说。每次您将更改推送到该分支或拉入请求时，环境也会自动更新。拉取请求关闭或合并后，Render会自动销毁环境。

The company will use the new funding to grow its team and build out its service. The plan, Goel tells me, is to raise a larger Series A round next year.

该公司将利用新的资金来壮大其团队，并扩大其服务。戈埃尔告诉我，计划是明年筹集更大规模的首轮融资。
