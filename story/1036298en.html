5 top investors in Dutch startups discuss trends, hopes and 2020 opportunities
荷兰初创公司的5位顶级投资者讨论了趋势，希望和2020年机会

2020-11-24 21:36:15

Plenty of good funding rounds, a highly skilled workforce and a strong entrepreneurial culture have given Amsterdam a booming startup ecosystem. And Brexit is helping: The Dutch are highly proficient in English and Dutch law is similar to English law, which means U.K.-based tech founders are welcomed with open arms.

大量的良好融资，高技能的员工队伍和强大的企业家文化为阿姆斯特丹提供了一个蓬勃发展的创业生态系统。英国脱欧正在提供帮助：荷兰人精通英语，荷兰法律与英国法律相似，这意味着英国的科技创始人会受到热烈欢迎。

In 2020, the venture industry continued to invest in startups, despite the COVID-19 crisis. According to a study by KPMG and and NL Times, startups raised $591.2 million in the third quarter, more than double the $252.4 million raised in the quarter before.

到2020年，尽管发生了COVID-19危机，风险投资行业仍继续向初创企业投资。根据毕马威和《 NL Times》的研究，初创公司在第三季度筹集了5.912亿美元，是上一季度筹集的2.524亿美元的两倍多。

For obvious reasons, this year has seen more cash go into companies that were able to adapt to the pandemic. KPMG found that while the total amount of investment increased in the past six months, the number of overall investments decreased. New startups pulled in fewer investments, KPMG sees this trend continuing and likely leading to consolidation amongst startups in similar sectors.

由于明显的原因，今年已经有更多的现金流入能够适应大流行的公司。毕马威会计师事务所发现，尽管过去六个月投资总额有所增加，但总投资额却有所减少。毕马威（KPMG）认为，新成立的初创公司的投资减少了，这种趋势仍在继续，并可能导致类似行业的初创公司之间的合并。

According to a report by Dealroom.co and StartupAmsterdam, there are 1,661 tech companies in Amsterdam, while the city ranked fifteenth in Startup Genome’s 2019 report “Global Startup Ecosystem Report,” moving up four places since 2017. The median seed round is $500,000 (above the global average of $494,000) and a median Series A round for a startup is $2.4 million. The average salary for a software engineer is around €54,000.

根据Dealroom.co和StartupAmsterdam的报告，阿姆斯特丹有1,661家科技公司，而该市在Startup Genome的2019年报告“全球初创生态系统报告”中排名第15位，自2017年以来上升了四位。种子轮融资中位数为500,000美元（高于全球平均水平的494,000美元），而初创公司的A轮融资中位数为240万美元。软件工程师的平均工资约为54,000欧元。

Amsterdam has tech industry “schools” such as Growth Tribe, The Talent Institute and THNK for educational courses, as well as accelerators like Rockstart, Startupbootcamp and Fashion for Good. Co-working is well-catered for with TQ, Startup Village and B.Amsterdam, and workers can cycle everywhere in minutes.

阿姆斯特丹拥有高科技行业的“学校”，例如成长部落，人才学院和THNK等教育课程，以及诸如Rockstart，Startupbootcamp和Fashion for Good的加速器。与TQ，Startup Village和B.Amsterdam的合作非常合适，工人可以在几分钟内到处骑自行车。

While taxes are high, entrepreneurs won’t find the staggering income inequality so often seen in cities like San Francisco and New York. In Amsterdam, rich people take public transport, not private buses.

尽管税收很高，但企业家们不会发现在旧金山和纽约这样的城市中经常出现的惊人的收入不平等。在阿姆斯特丹，有钱人乘坐公共交通工具，而不是私人巴士。

During COVID-19, the Dutch government has also announced support packages such as tax deferrals, temporary employment bridging schemes and other initiatives. It also launched a national program, TechLeap.NL, to boost the ecosystem with more international investor visibility. StartupDelta, a Dutch startup lobby group, keeps the pressure on the politicians.

在COVID-19期间，荷兰政府还宣布了支持计划，例如延税，临时就业衔接计划和其他措施。它还启动了一项国家计划TechLeap.NL，以提高国际投资者的知名度来促进生态系统的发展。荷兰创业游说团体StartupDelta一直在给政客施加压力。

The Netherlands’ most famous unicorns include Booking.com, Adyen, Virtuagym, MessageBird, Swapfiets, Backbase, Picnic and Takeaway, among several others.

荷兰最著名的独角兽包括Booking.com，Adyen，Virtuagym，MessageBird，Swapfiets，Backbase，Picnic和Takeaway等。

Adyen launched in 2006, and in June 2018, it was listed as one of Europe’s largest tech IPOs with a value of €7 billion. Booking.com started in 1996 and was later acquired by Priceline Group (now called Booking Holdings) in 2005. Elastic, the provider of subscription-based data search software used by Dell, Netflix, The New York Times and others, was another gangbuster IPO in 2018.

Adyen于2006年成立，并于2018年6月被列为欧洲最大的科技公司之一，价值70亿欧元。 Booking.com始于1996年，后来于Priceline Group（现称为Booking Holdings）于2005年收购。Elastic是戴尔公司的又一IPO，Elastic是戴尔，Netflix，纽约时报等公司使用的基于订阅的数据搜索软件的提供商在2018年。

Are there startups that you wish you would see in the industry but don’t? What are some overlooked opportunities right now? More overlooked founders than opportunities.

是否有您希望在行业中看到但没有的初创公司？现在有哪些被忽视的机会？被忽视的创始人多于机会。

Which areas are either oversaturated or would be too hard to compete in at this point for a new startup? What other types of products/services are you wary or concerned about? Delivery, taxis, scooters.

哪些领域要么饱和，要么在这一点上很难竞争新的创业公司？您还警惕或关注其他哪些类型的产品/服务？送货，出租车，踏板车。

How much are you focused on investing in your local ecosystem versus other startup hubs (or everywhere) in general? More than 50%? Less? Less.

一般而言，相对于其他启动中心（或其他地方），您有多少专注于投资本地生态系统？超过50％？减？减。

Which industries in your city and region seem well-positioned to thrive, or not, long term? What are companies you are excited about (your portfolio or not), which founders? NL seems well-positioned for fintech, deep tech. I am really excited about Tracy Chou and Diane Janknegt, two incredible founders.

您所在城市和地区的哪些行业似乎很适合长期发展？您对哪些公司感到兴奋（无论您是否投资于公司），哪些创始人？ NL在金融科技，深度科技领域似乎处于有利位置。我对两位令人难以置信的创始人Tracy Chou和Diane Janknegt感到非常兴奋。

How should investors in other cities think about the overall investment climate and opportunities in your city? Very positive. Lots of innovation, great infrastructure, good talent. Do you expect to see a surge in more founders coming from geographies outside major cities in the years to come, with startup hubs losing people due to the pandemic and lingering concerns, plus the attraction of remote work? I think startups have always been there, investors just don’t tend to look at them. I think the opportunity is more that they now will.

其他城市的投资者应如何考虑您城市的总体投资环境和机会？非常积极。大量的创新，完善的基础设施，优秀的人才。您是否希望在未来几年内看到更多来自主要城市以外地区的创始人，由于流行病和持续的担忧以及偏远地区工作的吸引力，新兴的集线器使人员流失？我认为初创公司一直存在，投资者只是不倾向于看他们。我认为他们现在将获得更多的机会。

Which industry segments that you invest in look weaker or more exposed to potential shifts in consumer and business behavior because of COVID-19? What are the opportunities startups may be able to tap into during these unprecedented times? None. We look at digital health, education and SaaS and they all thrive in this climate. Of course an economic crisis will have an impact on spending in general.

由于COVID-19，您所投资的哪个细分市场显得更弱或更受消费者和商业行为的潜在变化的影响？在这些空前的时期，初创企业可能会利用哪些机会？没有。我们关注数字健康，教育和SaaS，它们都在这种气候下蓬勃发展。当然，经济危机将总体上影响支出。

How has COVID-19 impacted your investment strategy? What are the biggest worries of the founders in your portfolio? What is your advice to startups in your portfolio right now? It has confirmed our approach. We have a data-driven approach to teams, which is great when people cannot meet.

COVID-19如何影响您的投资策略？您的投资组合中创始人最大的担忧是什么？您现在对投资组合中的初创企业有何建议？它证实了我们的方法。我们对团队采用数据驱动的方法，这在人们无法见面时非常有用。

Are you seeing “green shoots” regarding revenue growth, retention or other momentum in your portfolio as they adapt to the pandemic? We invest so early that companies are growing regardless.

当您适应大流行时，您是否看到有关收入增长，保留或其他动量的“绿芽”？我们投资得如此之早，以至于无论公司如何发展。

What is a moment that has given you hope in the last month or so? This can be professional, personal or a mix of the two. When I explained to my little boy what racism is and he answered: Mummy that is just really weird. That gives me hope that the generations after us might do better.

在过去一个月左右的时间里，给您带来希望的时刻是什么？这可以是专业的，个人的或两者的结合。当我向小男孩解释什么是种族主义时，他回答：木乃伊真的很奇怪。这给了我希望，我们后代可能会做得更好。
