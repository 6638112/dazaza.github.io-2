The Sweden Solar System
瑞典太阳系

2021-03-11 23:18:34

The Sweden Solar System (SSS) is the world’s largest model of our planetary system. The Sun is represented by the Globe in Stockholm, the largest spherical building in the world, and the planets are lined up in direction north from here.

瑞典太阳能系统（SSS）是世界上最大的行星系统模式。太阳由世界上最大的球形建筑斯德哥尔摩的地球代表，行星在北方方向上排队。

Distances and sizes are scaled according to 1:20 million, and the inner planets are all in the Stockholm area. The outer planets follow in the same direction with for instance Neptune in Söderhamn and the dwarf planet Pluto in Delsbo, 300 km from the Globe. A number of minor planets and comets also populate SSS, which now extends from the very south to the very north of Sweden. There is a host institution for each model. SSS is a pedagogical instrument and conveys a direct feeling of the enormous distances in space, and how small the planets are compared to the Sun. Art, mythology and science merge in this project, and SSS connects to different places and activities in Sweden.

距离和尺寸按照1:20万升，内部行星全部位于斯德哥尔摩区。外面的行星在同一个方向上，例如在Söderhamn和德沃尔波·德沃夫行星冥王星的海王星，距离地球有300公里。许多小行星和彗星也填充了SSS，它现在从南部到瑞典北部的南部延伸。每个模型都有一个主机机构。 SSS是一种教学仪器，并传达在太空中的巨大距离的直接感，以及行星与太阳相比的小程度。该项目中的艺术，神话和科学合并，SSS连接到瑞典的不同地方和活动。

The Globe Arena in Stockholm is the largest spherical building in the world, and it represents the Sun in the SSS. This picture shows the Globe lit by an animation, which included a sequence showing flares on the solar surface, during the inauguration of the International Year of Astronomy 2009.

斯德哥尔摩的地球竞技场是世界上最大的球形建筑，它代表了SSS的太阳。这张照片显示了由动画点亮的地球，其中包括在2009年国际天文年的就职典礼期间在太阳能表面上显示耀斑的序列。

A new model of Venus was inaugurated on May 9, 2016 in the park outside the House of Science (Vetenskapens Hus), AlbaNova University Centre, Stockholm. The model is sculpted by Peter Varhelyi, and on five plates surrounding the model are schematic descriptions of: Venus in the sky as morningstar, Venus in its orbit, Venus’ phases, the greenhouse effect on Venus, and names of the goddess of love from different cultures.

2016年5月9日在科学院（Vetenskapens Hus），阿尔巴诺瓦大学中心，斯德哥尔摩的公园内开设了一个新的金星模型。该模型由Peter Varhelyi雕塑，并且在围绕模型的五个板上是：金星在天空中作为晨星，金星，金星的阶段，维纳斯的温室效果以及爱情女神的名字，以及爱情女神的名字不同的文化。

The Observatory museum in Stockholm is closed, and its Venus model has been moved to the upper floor in Vetenskapens Hus.

斯德哥尔摩的天文台博物馆已关闭，其维纳斯模型已被迁移到Vetenskapens Hus的上层。

Cosmonova, Naturhistoriska riksmuseet, Stockholm Diameter: 65 cm / 18 cm (20 m apart) Distance from Globen: 7,6 km

宇航员，Naturhistoriska riksmuseet，斯德哥尔摩直径：65厘米/ 18厘米（距离20米）距离Globen：7,6公里

The models of our planet Earth and the Moon are located outside the entrance of the space theatre Cosmonova at the Natural History Museum (in the ticket hall). The model of the Moon is made by Peter Varheyi and is situated on a pillar 20 m away from the model of the Earth.

我们的地球和月球的型号位于自然历史博物馆（在售票大厅）的太空剧院宇航的入口处。月亮模型由Peter Varheyi制造，位于距离地球型号的支柱上。

Mars is connected to a plate in steel symbolizing the Earth. Chemical symbols for common compounds on Mars can be found on the sphere.

火星连接到象征地球的钢板上。在球体上可以找到MARS上常见化合物的化学符号。

Jupiter was for many years represented by an arrangement of flowers outside Sky City at Arlanda Airport. In may 2019 a new model was inaugurated in Hotel Clarion connected to Sky City. The model is in the form of a shining ring over the entrance to a conference centre. In addition, the four large Galilean moons are featured along the corridor leading to the hotel reception. In this new design there is plentiful of information about the planet and its environment. Sponsor: Storebrand S/P. Design: Strategisk Arkitektur and collaborators.

木星是由阿兰达机场的天空城外的花朵的安排代表了多年。 2019年5月，在酒店Clarion落成了一个新的模型，连接到天空城市。该模型采用在会议中心入口处的闪亮环的形式。此外，这四个大型伽利略棉门沿着走廊展出，通往酒店接待处。在这种新设计中，有关于地球及其环境的信息丰富。赞助商：Storebrand S / p。设计：战略arkitektur和合作者。

The plans to create a nice model of Saturn are advancing. In 2010 an artistic model of the moon Titan was inaugurated in central Uppsala at the Celsius House, where the 18th century astronomer Anders Celsius worked. In addition, several Saturn moons have been given form by pupils in school classes in the area.

创造一个很好的土星模型的计划正在推进。 2010年，一个泰坦的艺术模式在Celsius House的乌普萨拉中部落成，在那里，18世纪天文学家和科尔斯利斯队的工作。此外，在该地区的学校课程中，几个土星卫生组织被给予了表现形式。

A symbolic and playful outdoor model of Uranus was inaugurated in Oct 2012 (see picture) in Lövstabruk south of Gävle. The model was built in steel by Forsmark Mechanical Workshop at the Forsmark nuclear plant located close to Lövstabruk, and where the element Uranium plays an important role. The unusual tilt of Uranus’ axis is marked.

2012年10月（参见图片）在戈尔南部的Lövstabruk举行的象征性和俏皮的户外模型。该型号由Forsmark Mechanichorpop在靠近Lövstabruk的Forsmark核电站中建造的，其中元素铀发挥着重要作用。天王星轴的不寻常倾斜标记。

Neptune, symbolizing the god of the water and the sea, is located in the central park of Söderhamn, a coast town with long traditions of fishing and sailing. The model is lit from inside by blue light in the night.

海王星，象征着水和海的上帝，位于Söderhamn的中央公园，这是一个海岸小镇，钓鱼和航行传统。夜晚的蓝光从内部点燃了模型。

Pluto and Charon are supported by two tomb-like pillars reminding of the mythological meaning of Pluto (Hades), the god of the underworld. One can also be reminded of the meteorite impact that took place here 90 million years ago, when all life became extinct over a large area, and from which the Dellen lake system formed.

Pluto和Charon由两个古墓的柱子支持，提醒冥王星（HAVES），内海神的神话意义。还可以提醒一下9000万年前在这里发生的陨石的影响，当所有生活都灭绝在一个大面积上，而德琳湖系统则形成。

Eros, the god of love, was inaugurated on a Valentine’s day during a close encounter by a NASA space probe. The model, only 1.5 mm in size, was designed and exhibited as a school project.

爱情之神，爱情之神在NASA空间探针紧密遭遇期间在情人节落成。该模型仅为1.5毫米，设计并作为学校项目展出。

A sculpture of an asteroid discovered by A. Brandeker in 2000 with a telescope at the Stockholm Observatory, now ”Kunskapsskolan”, was unveiled in 2010. The object was named “Saltis”, after the nickname of Saltsjöbaden.

2000年，在斯德哥尔摩天文台的望远镜上发现了一个由斯德哥尔摩天文台的望远镜发现的小行星的雕塑，在2010年，该目的被命名为“Saltis”，在萨尔茨奇登的绰号之后。

Picture from the inauguration of on Sept. 6, 2017, when the initiator Åke Dahlöf (teacher at Åva senior high school) put candles into the sculpture (Vesta was the goddess of the hearth in Roman religion). To the right Peter Varhelyi, who sculpted Vesta.

从2017年9月6日的就职典礼，当Åkedahlöf（Åva高中的老师）将蜡烛放入雕塑（Vesta是罗马宗教的壁炉之女神）。到右边的彼得·瓦尔利，谁雕刻了Vesta。

Below a sketch of the panels forming the base showing antique symbols for Vesta and the Vesta family, a collection of smaller blocks that were ejected from Vesta after a major impact at the southern pole. The model is oblate, about 3 cm across and with a crater landscape on its surface.

下面，形成了底座的面板的草图，显示了Vesta和Vesta系列的古色古香的符号，在南极的重大影响后，一系列较小的块被从Vesta弹出。该模型是扁平的，大约3厘米，表面上的火山口横向。

The asteroid Palomar-Leiden 5025 is a small dot on a relief describing SSS and placed in a park in Knivsta with “cosmic” sculptures by Eric Ståhl standing in front of the relief.

小行星Palomar-leiden 5025是一个小点，在浮雕描述SSS并放置在Knivsta的公园，并通过EricStåhl站在浮雕前面的“宇宙”雕塑。

Haumea is featured as a shining oval in a deep blue background, where also the the two moons are depicted (Left image taken by Anna-Lena Rågfälts).

Haumea在深蓝色背景中被称为闪亮的椭圆形，其中两个Moons被描绘（Anna-LenaRågfälts拍摄）。

The dwarf planet Haumea was discovered in 2003 and got its name from a hawaiian goddess. It is different from many other similar bodies in that it rotates very fast, one turm in 4 hours, which may have caused it elongated form (ellipsoid). Two moons, Hi’laka och Namaka, circles around the planet and the three bodies, featured by Heléne Ecström, are found in 2047 Science Centre, Borlänge.

矮星哈鲁姆于2003年发现，并从夏威夷女神获得了名字。它与许多其他类似的体不同，因为它在4小时内旋转非常快，一个菌疹，这可能导致它细长形式（椭圆体）。两个卫星，Hi'laka Och Namaka，星球周围的圈子和HeléneCERStröm特色的三具尸体，位于2047科学中心，Borlänge。

The dwarf planet Quaoar was discovered in 2002 by the american astronomers Chad Trujillo and Michael Brown. Quaoar was the creator in the minds of the Tongva people, who lived in the area around  Los Angeles (USA). The model of Quaoar, created by Poul Jörgensen Kastlösa Glasbruk, Öland, was inaugurated on November 18, 2017, in Gislaved’s library (right image). The project was iniiated  by Gislaved’s Astronomical Society Orion.

Dwarf Planet SeaoAR被美国天文学家乍得特鲁希略和迈克尔·棕色发现于2002年。由于在洛杉矶（美国）周围的地区生活在桐瓦人民的思想中的创造者。由PoulJörgensenKastlösaGlasbruk（Öland）创建的Seaoar模型于2017年11月18日在Gislaved的图书馆（正确的形象）开幕。该项目由Gislaved的天文学会orion侵略。

Ixion is one of the largest minor planets discovered by a team including Swedish scientists. The model is shown in the science centre Technicus in Härnösand.

Ixion是一支团队发现的最大小型行星之一，包括瑞典科学家。该模型显示在Härnösand的科学中心Technicus。

Makemake is named after the God of creation on Easter Island, and is the third largest dwarf planet in our solar system. It is located in Slottsskogen in Gothenburg, not far from the famous  Easter Island tree in the Botanic Garden. The model is designed by Katja Lindblom to the right on the right image In connection with the inauguration on September 23, 2017 by  J ohn Conway (left), Director of Onsala Space Observatory.

Makemake以复活节岛上的创作之神命名，是我们太阳系的第三大矮星球。它位于哥德兰堡的Slottsskogen，不远离着名的复活节岛树在植物园。该模型由Katja Lindblom设计在右侧图像的右侧，即2017年9月23日由JOHN Conway（左），Onsala Space天文台主任John Conway（左）。

To the left a photo (by Halvard Nilsson) showing ‘Oumuamua as it looks through the telescope seen on the right image where Charlotte Helin, chairman of the Astronomical Society of Halland, demonstates during the inauguration in 2018.

到左边一张照片（由Halvard Nilsson）显示'Oumuamua，因为它看起来穿过望远镜在右侧图像上看到的望远镜，夏洛特·赫隆于2018年就职典礼期间的扑克斯特队。

’Oumuamua is the Hawaiian name of a stony, elongated interstellar object once formed around a distant star from where it was ejected. On its journey through the Milky Way it happened to cross our solar system. It paid a visit to our neighbourhood in 2017 and is now on its way out again.

'Oumuamua是夏威夷的石头的名称，延长了围绕遥远的星星的伸长的星际物体，从它被喷射的地方形成。在它的旅程中，通过银河系碰巧穿越我们的太阳系。它在2017年访问了我们的社区，现在再次出发。

A presentation of this unique celestial body was inaugurated in August 2018 in Plönninge, Halland with a model created with the support from the Astronomical Society of Halland.

在2018年8月，在Hlanninge，Halland的展示中揭示了这一独特的天体的展示，其中包括从Halland天文学学会的支持。

Gonggong is one of the largest dwarf planets and located in the Kuiper Belt about 80 times further out from the Sun than our Earth. The diameter is about 1500 km and the colour is red. The orbit is strongly eccentric and it takes 500 years to circle the Sun. The model of the dwarf planet and its moon was created by the artist Stefan Agnani and the inauguration took place on Septemeber 23, 2017, when also the discoverer Megan Schwamb participated via skype from Hawaii.

贡贡是最大的矮人行星之一，位于Kuiper皮带中，从阳光下进一步超过我们的地球。直径约为1500公里，颜色为红色。轨道强烈偏心，需要500年才能圈出太阳。矮球星球和月亮的模型是由艺术家Stefan Agnani创建的，并且在2017年Septemeber 23,2017上发生的就职典礼，当时发现梅根·施维姆来自夏威夷的Skype。

The model of Eris, the goddess of conflict, reflects an ancient story when Eris initiated a big quarrel starting with a controversial inscription on a golden apple.

冲突女神的eris模型反映了一个古老的故事，当时埃里斯开始以金苹果上的争议题字开始的大争吵。

The model of the dwarf planet Sedna, created by Antero Koskitalo, was inaugurated in 2005 at the science centre ”Teknikens Hus” located in Luleå in northern Sweden, not far from the arctic cirle. In arctic mythology, Sedna represented the ”goddess of the frozen sees” from where she supplied the eskimos with seals and whales. Sedna is comparable to Pluto in size, and belongs to a distant swarm of minor planets called the Kuiper Belt Up

由Antero Koskitalo创建的Dwarf Planet Sedna的模型于2005年在瑞典北部的Luleå的“Teknikens HUS”科学中心，不远离北极圆圈。在北极神话中，Sedna代表了从她用密封和鲸鱼提供爱斯基摩人的“冻结的女神”。 SEDNA与冥王星的大小相当，属于一个遥远的小星球，称为Kuiper Belt

Comet Halley was inaugurated at the science centre Balthazar in Skövde on December 2009. Halley is prepresented by three outdoor models inspired by drawings made by school children, and in addition one indoor model formed by laser in a block of glass. At this place the comet is at its largest distance from the Sun.

2009年12月，奥尔·哈利在巴罗扎尔科学中心落成了Skövde的科学中心。哈利被学校儿童制造的图纸的吸引力，并且另外由激光形成的一个室内模型在一块玻璃块中。在这个地方，彗星距离太阳最大的距离。

Swifft-Tuttle has an elongated orbit, and in SSS the comet passes at its largest distance from the Sun (aphelium) at this location in the science centre Kreativum on the Swedish southcoast.

Swifft-Tuttle拥有一个细长的轨道，在SSS，彗星在瑞典南岸科学中心Kreetivum科学中心的阳光下距离太阳（佛罗里大岛）最大的距离。

The distant interface between the solar wind and the surrounding galactic gas, the terminal shock, has been manifested in Kiruna. A future sculpture is envisioned to express this phenomenon reminiscent of aurorae frequently seen in northern Sweden.

太阳风与周围银河之间的远端接口，端子冲击，已经在基鲁纳表现出来。设想未来的雕塑，以表达这种现象让人想起瑞典北部经常看到的奥罗拉。
