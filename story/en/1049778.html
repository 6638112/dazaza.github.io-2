Think you don’t need a VPN? Here are five times ordinary people might need a VPN

As more of daily life takes place across internet connections, privacy and security issues become even more important. A VPN — Virtual Private Network — can help anyone create a secure, private connection to the internet. Encrypting your traffic between you and your VPN provider so that no one on your local network can decipher or modify it.

对您和VPN提供商之间的流量进行加密，以便您本地网络上的任何人都无法解密或修改它。

Having a VPN at your fingertips when you need it is a good idea. Here are a few times having a trustworthy VPN will come in handy that you might not have considered.

在需要时让您的VPN触手可及是一个好主意。在某些情况下，您可能没有考虑过使用值得信赖的VPN时会派上用场。

It’s a beautiful Saturday morning, and you’ve settled down on a park bench to sip a coffee and catch up on your favorite news sites, watch a few videos and do some quick online shopping while getting sun and fresh air. Rather than draining your allotted mobile data, you hop onto your city’s public WiFi service. It’s free, and run by the city, so it must be ok, right? The fact is, you can’t be certain the municipal network is secure or even real (see the next point) and set up to protect you from malicious people or entities spying on you while you’re connected. Turning on a VPN can help protect your device from anyone who tries to snoop on the sites you’re visiting or hijack your sensitive login information while you’re kicked back on that park bench any day of the week.

这是一个美丽的星期六早晨，您已经坐在公园的长椅上喝咖啡，赶上自己喜欢的新闻网站，观看一些视频，并进行一些快速的在线购物，同时获得阳光和新鲜空气。您可以使用城市的公共WiFi服务，而不用浪费分配的移动数据。它是免费的，由城市运行，所以一定可以，对吧？事实是，您不能确定市政网络是安全的甚至是真实的（请参阅下一点），并且要进行设置以保护您免受连接时监视您的恶意人员或实体的攻击。开启VPN可以帮助保护您的设备免受任何试图窥探您正在访问的网站或劫持您敏感的登录信息的人的侵害，而这又是您在一周中的任何一天都被踢到公园长椅上的时候。

Have you ever connected to a hotspot called something like C0MCAST-WiFi-77th-St or Verizon3-Hotspot-Baltimore? Looks legit, right? Not so fast. In reality, anyone can set up a phony public WiFi with a legitimate sounding name to lure people to use it. Connecting to any unknown WiFi makes you an easy target for creeps and criminals who want to access your device to steal private information, install malware or worse. Mozilla VPN can boost your security any time you’re connected to a public WiFi by blocking unknown entities from seeing private data that travels from your phone or laptop. This goes for connecting to WiFi networks at coffee shops, stores, doctor’s offices and so on.

您是否曾经连接到诸如C0MCAST-WiFi-77th-St或Verizon3-Hotspot-Baltimore之类的热点？看起来合法，对不对？没那么快。实际上，任何人都可以使用合法的冠名建立一个伪造的公共WiFi，以吸引人们使用它。连接到任何未知的WiFi，使您成为想要访问您的设备以窃取私人信息，安装恶意软件或更糟的蠕虫和犯罪分子的轻松目标。当您连接到公共WiFi时，Mozilla VPN可以阻止未知实体查看从您的手机或笔记本电脑传来的私人数据，从而提高您的安全性。这用于连接咖啡店，商店，医生办公室等的WiFi网络。

Open WiFi abounds when you’re on the go, from networked buses to hotspots in train stations to the free airport internet. And no matter how secure they seem to be, treat these WiFi hotspots with care and suspicion. Many of us will use these free connections at times for convenience, yet all the same risks outlined in this article apply to any open, unverified WiFi network. Using a VPN can help protect your identity, file transfers and online activities from bad actors attempting to spy on you through the same WiFi network.

旅途中，到处都是开放的WiFi，从联网的巴士到火车站的热点，再到免费的机场互联网，一应俱全。而且，无论它们看起来有多安全，都应小心谨慎地对待这些WiFi热点。为了方便起见，我们许多人有时会使用这些免费连接，但是本文中概述的所有相同风险也适用于任何开放的，未经验证的WiFi网络。使用VPN可以帮助保护您的身份，文件传输和在线活动，防止恶意行为者企图通过同一WiFi网络监视您。

If you are renting a temporary place for remote work or planning a future Airbnb getaway, chances are it will come with free WiFi. Even if the WiFi is protected behind a password, that doesn’t make it automatically safe and private. For starters, the host could be accessing the network to track what you do online. Also, if they haven’t changed the password between guests, any prior guest could reconnect alongside you. Plus, an earlier guest could have accessed the rental’s router and installed software that allows them to snoop on it remotely. Much to consider! Short of skipping the WiFi entirely, the best course of action is to use a trusted VPN whenever you’re connecting your computer, phone or tablet to a rental’s internet. This goes for hotels and hostels, too.

如果您租用临时工作地点进行远程工作或计划将来的Airbnb逍遥游，则可能会提供免费WiFi。即使WiFi受到密码保护，也无法自动保护其安全性和私密性。对于初学者来说，主机可能正在访问网络以跟踪您在网上所做的事情。另外，如果他们没有更改访客之间的密码，则任何以前的访客都可以与您重新连接。另外，更早的访客可能已经访问了租用的路由器并安装了允许他们远程监听的软件。有很多考虑！除了完全跳过WiFi，最好的做法是在将计算机，手机或平板电脑连接到租借互联网时使用可信的VPN。这也适用于酒店和旅馆。

For the most part, the scenarios above don’t apply to your home network, provided it’s been set up properly with a strong password and security measures. But that doesn’t mean you’re free from all prying eyes.

在大多数情况下，上述情况不适用于您的家庭网络，但前提是它已使用强大的密码和安全措施进行了正确设置。但这并不意味着您就可以摆脱所有窥探的眼睛。

Because your internet traffic at home moves to and from your devices through your internet service provider — ISP for short — they can see where you go online. An ISP can see what sites you visit, how long you’re on them, your location and information about your devices. An ISP may not know the specifics of what you did on those sites (like what you bought, searched for or read) thanks to encryption, but they could make inferences about you based on the sites that you visited. That personal data can be used by ISPs to create detailed profiles about you. Browsing in private mode doesn’t prevent ISPs from seeing where you go online. But sending your web traffic through a VPN can make it much harder.

由于您在家中的互联网流量通过互联网服务提供商（简称ISP）往返于设备，因此他们可以看到您上网的地方。 ISP可以查看您访问了哪些网站，您在这些网站上停留了多长时间，您的位置以及有关设备的信息。由于进行了加密，ISP可能不知道您在这些站点上所做的操作的详细信息（例如，您购买，搜索或阅读的内容），但是ISP可以根据您访问的站点对您进行推断。 ISP可以使用该个人数据来创建有关您的详细个人资料。以专用模式浏览不会阻止ISP看到您的上网位置。但是，通过VPN发送网络流量可能会变得更加困难。

A VPN provides an important security link by protecting your private data before it travels over the internet, and it also conceals your IP address, helping to protect your identity and location. It won’t, however, prevent you from things like clicking on suspicious links, downloading malware or being victimized by email fraud. You still need to practice good habits to stay safe online.

VPN通过保护您的私有数据在通过Internet传输之前提供了重要的安全链接，并且还隐藏了您的IP地址，从而有助于保护您的身份和位置。但是，它不会阻止您执行诸如单击可疑链接，下载恶意软件或遭受电子邮件欺诈的攻击之类的事情。您仍然需要养成良好的习惯，以确保上网安全。

The most important thing to consider when picking a VPN is choosing a trustworthy company. Be sure you understand the terms you’re agreeing to. Many claim to be great and focused on privacy, but a large number of them fall short on their promise. Not all proxy and VPN services are secure and private. Some will log your online activities so they can sell your data and information to marketing firms themselves. Other services will try to convince you to install malware on your devices.

选择VPN时要考虑的最重要的事情是选择一家值得信赖的公司。确保您了解所同意的条款。许多人声称自己很棒并且专注于隐私，但是其中很多人都没有兑现他们的承诺。并非所有的代理和VPN服务都是安全的和私有的。有些人会记录您的在线活动，以便他们可以将您的数据和信息出售给行销公司。其他服务将试图说服您在设备上安装恶意软件。
