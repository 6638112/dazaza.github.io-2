Apple’s MagSafe Duo charger finally shows up in online stores
苹果的MagSafe Duo充电器终于出现在在线商店中

2020-12-04 22:29:17

Today, Apple finally began selling and shipping the MagSafe Duo charger, an accessory that was announced alongside the new iPhone 12 lineup on October 13.

今天，苹果终于开始销售和销售MagSafe Duo充电器，该附件于10月13日与新的iPhone 12阵容一同发布。

The MagSafe Duo is a charging pad that uses Qi wireless charging tech to charge two devices at once. Apple says it supports any Qi-enabled device but, of course, it specifically notes that the Duo can charge any two-device combination of an iPhone, an Apple Watch, or AirPods cases.

MagSafe Duo是一款充电垫，使用Qi无线充电技术可以同时为两个设备充电。苹果表示，它支持任何支持Qi的设备，但是，当然，它特别指出Duo可以为iPhone，Apple Watch或AirPods外壳的任何两种设备组合充电。

The MagSafe Duo charger is just one of a number of MagSafe accessories Apple announced in October. New iPhones have a magnet built into the back that is designed to allow chargers and cases to latch on, and MagSafe iPhones also have built-in sensors to detect what kind of accessory is being connected and communicate with it via NFC. Apple also announced at that same iPhone event that new iPhones wouldn't come with charging bricks in their boxes, and that also applies to this charger. Apple is assuming here that most anyone who buys this already has a charging brick on hand, but if you don't, Apple's store page says the following about what you should make sure to get to use the MagSafe Duo:

MagSafe Duo充电器只是Apple十月份宣布的多种MagSafe配件之一。新iPhone的后部内置有一块磁铁，旨在允许充电器和手机壳扣紧，MagSafe iPhone还具有内置传感器以检测正在连接的附件类型并通过NFC与之通信。苹果公司还在同一场iPhone活动上宣布，新iPhone的包装盒中不会带有充电砖，这也适用于这款充电器。苹果在这里假设大多数购买它的人都已经准备好了充电砖，但是如果您不这样做，苹果的商店页面会说以下内容，说明您应该确保使用MagSafe Duo ：

– 20W USB-C Power Adapter (sold separately) for faster wireless charging up to 11W – 27W or higher USB-C Power Adapter (sold separately) for faster wireless charging up to 14W

– 20W USB-C电源适配器（另售），可实现高达11W的更快的无线充电– 27W或更高版本的USB-C电源适配器（另售），可实现高达14W的更快的无线充电

Obviously, wired charging is going to be much faster. But if you like the convenience of wireless charging, this is Apple's intended multidevice solution for you—though it doesn't support three devices at once like the long-ago announced-then-seemingly-canceled AirPower mat.

显然，有线充电将会更快。但是，如果您喜欢无线充电的便利性，这是Apple想要的多设备解决方案-尽管它不像早就宣布但后来被取消的AirPower垫一次支持三台设备。

The MagSafe Duo is priced at $129 and ships right away, with the first deliveries expected to arrive at buyers' doorsteps later this week.

MagSafe Duo的价格为129美元，可即刻发货，预计首批交付将到达买家手中。本周晚些时候的家门口。
