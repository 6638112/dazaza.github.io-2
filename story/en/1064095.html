Apple’s 2021 Worldwide Developers Conference is upon us again, and we will be tracking all of the new software announcements from the keynote. Apple has a lot of operating system plates spinning and has done a decent job keeping a lot of things under wraps. But there are some obvious questions that could be answered in the online-only event.

Apple的2021年全球开发人员会议再次在我们身上，我们将跟踪来自主题演讲的所有新软件公告。苹果有很多操作系统板旋转，并做了一个体面的工作，保持了很多东西的包装。但是有一些明显的问题可以在唯一的活动中回答。

Apple’s Arm-based M1 Macs have exceeded most people’s expectations for performance and battery life — but the future of macOS still seems fuzzy with so many different kinds of supported apps. All of that could be forgiven if Apple makes one of its rare WWDC hardware announcements with new MacBook Pro models.

iOS, iPadOS, and tvOS are also all likely to get some attention — and we may even see tvOS get renamed to HomeOS in a nod to the HomePod and Apple’s smart home ambitions. The iPad is expected to get a revamped home screen and maybe another revision to its windowing system.

iOS，iPados和TVOS也都有可能得到一些关注 - 我们甚至可以看到TVOS在点头上将TVOS更名为HomePod和Apple的智能家居野心。 iPad预计将获得一个改进的主屏幕，也可能对其窗口系统进行另一个版本。

All that, some surprises, and what’s sure to be a lot of swooping drone transitions about Apple Park are in store. But the biggest question of all is whether Apple will directly address the growing developer discontent around its App Store policies. It’s a long shot, but that’s why we live blog: tune in to watch along with us!
