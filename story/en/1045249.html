The English Language Wikipedia Just Had Its Billionth Edit
英语维基百科刚刚达到十亿编辑

2021-01-20 07:48:30

Just after 1 A.M. on January 12, a prolific Wikiepdian edited the entry for the album Death Breathing. The small edit was the addition of a hyperlink and it was the billionth edit done to the English language Wikipedia.

凌晨1点之后1月12日，一位多产的Wikiepdian编辑了专辑Death Breathing的条目。小修改是添加了超链接，这是对英语Wikipedia进行的第十亿次编辑。

“The article on the album Death Breathing was amended by Wikipedian Ser Amantio di Nicolao, one of over 3.9 million edits done by the Wikipedian with the highest edit count other than bots,” said a note in Wikimedia-l, a listserv that documents various Wikimedia matters.

“维基百科Ser Amantio di Nicolao修改了专辑《 Death Breathing》中的文章，这是Wikipedian进行的超过390万次编辑操作之一，其中除机器人以外，编辑数量最高。”维基媒体很重要。

Wikipedia relies on volunteers who constantly assess, edit, and argue over the specifics of the information in its vast online encyclopedia. Every edit is catalogued, tagged, and assigned a unique URL when it’s pushed through. The Death Breathing edit secured the billionth.

维基百科依靠志愿者不断评估，编辑和争论其庞大的在线百科全书中信息的细节。每次编辑后，所有编辑都会进行分类，标记和分配唯一的URL。死亡呼吸编辑获得了十亿分之一。

Death Breathing is a noise album released by Alec Empire in 1998. Empire is a Berliner and a founding member of Atari Teenage Riot, an anti-fascist band that opposed German neo-Nazis in the 1990s.

《死亡呼吸》是Alec帝国于1998年发行的一张噪音专辑。Empire是一名柏林人，也是1990年代与德国新纳粹分子对抗的反法西斯乐队Atari Teen Riot的创始成员。

The announcement on Wikimedia’s listserv noted that this is only the billionth edit if you don’t count the early years of Wikipedia when it had yet to migrate to its own servers and software.

Wikimedia的listserv上的公告指出，如果您不算Wikipedia的早期年份（尚未迁移到其自己的服务器和软件），那么这仅仅是十亿分之一的编辑。

“Pedants may be aware that this is only the thousand million since the move to MediaWiki software and not all of the hundreds of thousands of previous edits have since been reloaded,” the notice said. “So if we could work out the true counts since edit one it probably came one, maybe two days earlier.” “I don't have the exact numbers but there were definitely many edits made that aren't recorded in the current system,” Wikiepdian “The Cunctator” told Motherboard in an email. “Many of the UseModWiki edits were reintegrated with the history but there is a lacuna that covers my peak of editing in about August 2001 to February 2002 (I was the primary editor of September 11 related content). I don't know if the edit count reflects deleted edits or edits on deleted pages. One point that isn't made enough when discussing Wikipedia is how much of Google's wealth is built on its abuse of Wikipedia copyleft.”

通知说：“人们可能意识到这只是从迁移到MediaWiki软件以来的十亿美元，而且自那以后并不是所有数十万次以前的编辑都已经重新加载。” “因此，如果我们能算出自编辑一次以来的真实数字，它大概会提前一两天，”维基百科“ The Cunctator”在一封电子邮件中告诉母亲说：“我没有确切的数字，但是肯定有很多修改没有记录在当前系统中。” “许多UseModWiki的编辑已与历史重新融合在一起，但是存在一个空白，涵盖了我在2001年8月至2002年2月左右的编辑高峰（我是9月11日相关内容的主要编辑）。我不知道编辑计数是否反映了已删除的编辑或已删除页面上的编辑。在讨论Wikipedia时，还有一个不足之处是Google滥用Wikipedia copyleft创造了多少财富。”

“But Death Breathing got the edit with the thousand million counter,” the Wikimedia-l notice said.

Wikimedia-l通知说：“但是Death Breathing得到了十亿个计数器的编辑。”
