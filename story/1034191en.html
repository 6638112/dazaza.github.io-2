索尼给你的PS4第二次生命：把PS5挂到你房子的另一个房间

2020-11-10 10:24:29

SAN FRANCISCO (Reuters) - Apple Inc AAPL.O on Tuesday is expected to unveil new Mac computers using its own in-house processor chips, a move that could reignite a race to control the market for desktop and laptop chips and benefit players such as Qualcomm Inc QCOM.O .

路透旧金山8月23日电-苹果(AAPL.O：行情)周二预计将发布采用其内部处理器芯片的新款Mac电脑，此举可能重新点燃控制台式机和笔记本电脑芯片市场的竞争，并使高通(Qualcomm Inc)QCOM.O等厂商受益.。

The market has been dominated by Intel Corp INTC.O and Advanced Micro Devices Inc AMD.O since 2006, when Apple joined most other major computer makers in using chips based on Intel's 'x86' computing architecture.

自2006年以来，英特尔(Intel Corp INTC.O)和先进微设备公司(Advanced Micro Devices Inc AMD.O)一直主导着这一市场，当时苹果加入了大多数其他主要电脑制造商的行列，使用基于英特尔计算架构的芯片。

On Tuesday Apple is expected to start a two-year process of ending its nearly 15-year relationship with Intel by introducing Mac computers with Apple-designed chips based on computing architecture technology from Arm Ltd, which Nvidia Corp NVDA.O has agreed to buy from SoftBank Group Corp 9984.T in a $40 billion deal.

周二，苹果预计将启动为期两年的进程，结束与英特尔近15年的合作关系，推出配备苹果设计的芯片的Mac电脑，芯片基于ARM有限公司的计算架构技术。NVIDIA Corp NVDA.O已同意以400亿美元从软银集团(SoftBank Group Corp)9984.T手中收购ARM。

Apple will design the chips using Arm technology and have them manufactured by a partner, most likely Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Co 2330.TW , which makes processors for Apple's iPhone. UK-based Arm's technology also powers most Android phones.

苹果将使用ARM技术设计芯片，并由合作伙伴制造，最有可能的是为苹果iPhone生产处理器的台积电制造有限公司(TSMC)2330.TW。总部位于英国的ARM技术也为大多数安卓手机提供动力。

Apple's forthcoming machines already have competition from Qualcomm, which since 2016 has worked with Microsoft Corp MSFT.O to adapt the Windows operating system to Qualcomm's Arm-based processors.

苹果即将推出的机型已经面临来自高通(Qualcomm)的竞争，高通自2016年以来一直与微软(Microsoft Corp MSFT.O)合作，让Windows操作系统适应高通基于ARM的处理器。

Qualcomm and Microsoft have worked with PC makers such as Lenovo Group Ltd 0992.HK and Asustek Computer 2357.TW to sell laptops using the new chips, and Microsoft's own Surface Pro X released last year uses a Qualcomm processor.

高通(Qualcomm)和微软(Microsoft)已与联想集团(Lenovo Group Ltd.)和华硕电脑(Asustek Computer 2357.TW)等个人电脑制造商合作，销售使用新芯片的笔记本电脑，微软去年发布的Surface Pro X使用的是高通处理器。

Those devices are niche sellers today, but Apple’s entry into the market is likely to grab consumer attention around the emerging technology shift, especially if Apple begins to develop chips that rival Intel’s performance.

这些设备如今是小众卖家，但苹果进入市场可能会吸引消费者对新兴技术转变的关注，特别是如果苹果开始开发与英特尔性能相匹敌的芯片的话。

“Apple diving headstrong into Arm will speed this up,” said Patrick Moorhead, founder of Moor Insights & Strategy.

Moor Insights&Amp；Strategy创始人帕特里克·穆尔黑德(Patrick Moorhead)表示：“苹果一头扎进ARM将加速这一进程。”

Arm-based PCs have key differences from Intel-based machines. Because the chips are derived from smartphones where power consumption is a key concern, they tend to claim better battery life than conventional machines. Like smartphones, they also turn on quickly and can remain constantly connected to cellular data networks.

基于ARM的个人电脑与基于英特尔的计算机有很大的不同。由于这些芯片来自智能手机，而智能手机的功耗是一个关键问题，因此它们往往声称比传统机器的电池续航时间更长。与智能手机一样，它们也能快速开机，并能保持与蜂窝数据网络的持续连接。

“Where the connectivity has shined is the work from home situation,” said Miguel Nunes, senior director for product management at Qualcomm. “We see a lot of people realizing that their WiFi at home can’t keep up with everything.”

高通(Qualcomm)产品管理高级总监米格尔·努内斯(Miguel Nunes)表示：“网络连接的亮点在于在家工作。”“我们看到很多人意识到，家里的WiFi无法跟上一切。”

But hurdles remain for Arm-based PCs. Most software written in the past 20 years was for Intel machines, and until it is rewritten, it may have to rely on ‘emulation’ that could slow down apps.

但对于基于ARM的个人电脑来说，障碍依然存在。过去20年里编写的大多数软件都是为英特尔机器编写的，在重新编写之前，它可能不得不依赖于可能会降低应用程序速度的“仿真”。

Intel’s chip lineup “enables people to use their favorite Windows applications without experiencing the potential performance penalties associated with running non-native apps on non-x86 architecture via Windows, or worry if their favorite applications will run on their platform,” Intel said in a statement.

英特尔在一份声明中说，英特尔的芯片阵容“使人们能够使用他们最喜欢的Windows应用程序，而不会感受到通过Windows在非x86架构上运行非本地应用程序的潜在性能损失，也不会担心他们最喜欢的应用程序是否会在他们的平台上运行。”

Ben Bajarin, principal analyst for consumer market intelligence at Creative Strategies, said the critical test for Arm-based computers will be whether developers rewrite software used by big businesses, which are still the largest purchasers of machines. Apple’s entry to the market does not guarantee that will happen.

Creative Strategy消费者市场情报首席分析师本·巴加林(Ben Bajarin)表示，基于ARM的电脑的关键考验将是开发者是否会重写大企业使用的软件，大企业仍是机器的最大买家。苹果进入市场并不能保证这种情况会发生。

“Most of Apple’s hardcore developer base is going to be using Apple’s proprietary developer tools,” he said. But with Microsoft also providing development tools for Windows on Arm, a broader shift “is not outside the realm of possibility.”

他说：“大多数苹果的核心开发者将使用苹果专有的开发者工具。”但随着微软也为ARM上的Windows提供开发工具，更广泛的转变“并不是不可能的”。
