mrcal is a toolkit (originating at NASA/JPL) for working with lens models,camera geometry, images, projections, and the various related operations such ascamera calibration. Any task that produces or consumes camera models can utilizethis toolkit. It was originally built to generate the high-accuracy calibrationsdemanded by long-range stereo, so it provides facilities to analyze calibrationaccuracy and to propagate and report uncertainties.

mrcal是一个工具包（起源于NASA / JPL），用于处理镜头模型，相机几何形状，图像，投影以及各种相关操作（例如相机校准）。任何产生或使用相机模型的任务都可以利用此工具包。它最初是为生成远程立体声所要求的高精度校准而构建的，因此它提供了分析校准精度以及传播和报告不确定性的工具。

OpenCV is a library often used to solve similar problems. It can't producecalibrations that are as good as mrcal's, and it can't report on how good itsresults really are.

OpenCV是经常用于解决类似问题的库。它无法产生与mrcal一样好的校准，也无法报告其结果的真实程度。
