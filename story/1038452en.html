Apple has today announced that its fitness subcription service, Apple Fitness+, is set to launch on Monday, December 14.

苹果今天宣布，其健身订阅服务Apple Fitness +将于12月14日星期一启动。

Apple Fitness+ syncs information from your Apple Watch with the workout video on an ‌ Apple TV‌, ‌ iPad‌, or iPhone. This allows users to see their heart rate, calories burned, and activity rings as they follow the moves from the instructors on screen.

Apple Fitness +可将Apple Watch中的信息与video Apple TV‌，‌ iPad Apple或iPhone上的健身视频同步。当用户跟随​​屏幕上的指导员移动时，这使用户可以看到他们的心律，消耗的卡路里和活动响起。

Fitness+ includes workouts for yoga, cycling, dance, strength, core, HIIT, rowing, and more. Apple noted that many of the workouts do not need any equipment, and at most just require a set of dumbbells.

Fitness +包括瑜伽，骑自行车，跳舞，力量，核心，HIIT，划船等运动。苹果公司指出，许多锻炼不需要任何设备，至多只需要一组哑铃即可。

The service will be refreshed with new workouts every week. Users will also be able to choose custom music while exercising, with options that sync with Apple Music.

每周都会通过新的锻炼来刷新该服务。用户还可以在锻炼时选择与Apple Music同步的自定义音乐。

Fitness+ will be located in the Fitness app on iOS, and there will be a new app for the ‌‌Apple TV‌‌. The service costs $9.99 per month or $79.99 per year. Apple is offering three free months of Apple Fitness+ with new Apple Watch purchases. It is also part of the Apple One Premier bundle for $29.99.

Fitness +将位于iOS上的Fitness应用程序中，并且将有适用于“ Apple TV”的新应用程序。这项服务每月收费9.99美元，或每年79.99美元。购买新的Apple Watch后，Apple将免费提供三个月的Apple Fitness +。它也是Apple One Premier套装的一部分，售价29.99美元。

Apple Fitness+ will launch in the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, Ireland, and New Zealand on Monday, December 14.

Apple Fitness +将于12月14日星期一在美国，英国，澳大利亚，加拿大，爱尔兰和新西兰推出。
