FTC's Khan appoints Erie Meyer as chief technologist, Holly Vedova as acting head of Bureau of Competition, Sam Levine as head of Bureau of Consumer Protection

WASHINGTON, June 24 (Reuters) - Tech critic and the new chair of the Federal Trade Commission, Lina Khan, has named three top staffers in a signal that the agency could become more aggressive in how it enforces antitrust law and laws against deceptive advertising.

华盛顿州，6月24日（路透社） - 技术评论家和联邦贸易委员会的新主席Lina Khan指定了三位顶级员工，以至于该代理机构如何变得更加积极地在其执行反垄断法和抗欺骗广告的法律方面。

Khan, who was sworn in on June 15, named Holly Vedova to be the acting head of the Bureau of Competition, Sam Levine to be the acting head of the Bureau of Consumer Protection and Erie Meyer to be the agency's chief technologist, an FTC spokesman said on Thursday.

汗，曾在6月15日宣誓，名叫霍莉·韦多瓦成为竞争局的代理主管，成为消费者保护局的代理负责人，伊利梅尔是代理商＆＃39;首席技术师一名FTC发言人周四说。

The three currently work at the FTC as advisors to Democratic Commissioner Rohit Chopra, according to their profiles on Linkedin. Chopra is a progressive who has been nominated to head the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau and is awaiting confirmation.

根据他们在LinkedIn的概况的说法，这三个目前在FTC担任民主委员会rohitChopra的顾问。 Chopra是一项被提名掌握消费者金融保护局的进步，正在等待确认。

Observers broadly expect the FTC to become more aggressive under Khan although in the instances that the agency cannot reach an agreement on matters their lawyers must argue their cases before a judge.

观察员广泛期望FTC在Khan下变得更加激进，虽然在原子能机构无法达成协议的情况下，其律师必须在法官之前争论其案件。

The FTC, which works with the Justice Department to enforce antitrust law, sued Facebook (FB.O) last year for violating antitrust law and has an investigation open into Amazon.com (AMZN.O).

去年与司法部合作的FTC与司法部一起执行反托拉斯法，为违反反托拉斯法律而起诉Facebook（FB.O），并向Amazon.com（Amzn.o）开放调查。
