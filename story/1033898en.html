阿联酋宣布放宽伊斯兰法律以保障个人自由

2020-11-09 02:38:21

DUBAI, United Arab Emirates (AP) — The United Arab Emirates announced on Saturday a major overhaul of the country’s Islamic personal laws, allowing unmarried couples to cohabitate, loosening alcohol restrictions and criminalizing so-called “honor killings.”

阿拉伯联合酋长国迪拜(美联社)-阿联酋周六宣布对该国的伊斯兰属人法进行重大改革，允许未婚夫妇同居，放松酒精限制，并将所谓的“荣誉杀戮”定为刑事犯罪。

The broadening of personal freedoms reflects the changing profile of a country that has sought to bill itself as a Westernized destination for tourists, fortune-seekers and businesses despite its Islamic legal code that has previously triggered court cases against foreigners and outrage in their home countries.

个人自由的扩大反映出这个国家的形象正在发生变化，尽管该国的伊斯兰法典此前曾在本国引发针对外国人的法庭诉讼和愤怒，但该国一直试图将自己标榜为游客、寻求财富的人和企业的西化目的地。

The reforms aim to boost the country’s economic and social standing and “consolidate the UAE’s principles of tolerance,” said state-run WAM news agency, which offered only minimal details in the surprise weekend announcement. The government decrees behind the changes were outlined extensively in state-linked newspaper The National, which did not cite its source.

国营的WAM通讯社表示，这些改革旨在提升阿联酋的经济和社会地位，并“巩固阿联酋的宽容原则”。该通讯社在周末出人意料的声明中只提供了最低限度的细节。与官方有关联的报纸《国家报》(The National)广泛概述了这些变化背后的政府法令，但没有透露消息来源。

The move follows a historic U.S.-brokered deal to normalize relations between the UAE and Israel, which is expected to bring an influx of Israeli tourists and investment. It also comes as skyscraper-studded Dubai gets ready to host the World Expo. The high-stakes event, expected to bring a flurry of commercial activity and some 25 million visitors to the country, was set for October but pushed back a year because of the coronavirus pandemic.

在此之前，美国斡旋达成了阿联酋和以色列关系正常化的历史性协议，预计这将带来以色列游客和投资的涌入。与此同时，摩天大楼林立的迪拜正准备举办世博会。这项事关重大的活动预计将带来一系列商业活动和约2500万游客，原定于10月份举行，但由于冠状病毒大流行而推迟了一年。

The changes, which The National said would take immediate effect, also reflect the efforts of the Emirates’ rulers to keep pace with a rapidly changing society at home.

国家表示，这些变化将立即生效，这也反映了阿联酋统治者努力跟上国内快速变化的社会的步伐。

“I could not be happier for these new laws that are progressive and proactive,” said Emirati filmmaker Abdallah Al Kaabi, whose art has tackled taboo topics like homosexual love and gender identity.

阿联酋电影制作人阿卜杜拉·阿尔·卡比(Abdallah Al Kaabi)说：“我为这些进步和积极主动的新法律感到非常高兴。”他的艺术作品解决了同性恋爱情和性别认同等禁忌话题。

“2020 has been a tough and transformative year for the UAE,” he added.

他补充道：“2020年对阿联酋来说是艰难而变革性的一年。”

Changes include scrapping penalties for alcohol consumption, sales and possession for those 21 and over. Although liquor and beer is widely available in bars and clubs in the UAE’s luxuriant coastal cities, individuals needed a government-issued license to purchase, transport or have alcohol in their homes. The new rule would allow Muslims who have been barred from obtaining licenses to drink alcoholic beverages freely.

变化包括取消对21岁及以上人群的饮酒、销售和持有酒的处罚。虽然烈酒和啤酒在阿联酋繁华的沿海城市的酒吧和俱乐部里随处可见，但个人需要政府颁发的许可证才能购买、运输或在家中饮酒。新规定将允许被禁止获得许可证的穆斯林自由饮用酒精饮料。

Another amendment allows for “cohabitation of unmarried couples,” which has long been a crime in the UAE. Authorities, especially in the more freewheeling financial hub of Dubai, often looked the other way when it came to foreigners, but the threat of punishment still lingered. Attempted suicide, forbidden in Islamic law, would also be decriminalized, The National reported.

另一项修正案允许“未婚同居”，这在阿联酋一直是一种犯罪。当涉及到外国人时，当局，特别是在更自由的金融中心迪拜，经常对外国人视而不见，但惩罚的威胁仍然挥之不去。据《国家报》报道，伊斯兰法律禁止的自杀未遂也将被定为非刑事犯罪。

In a move to better “protect women’s rights,” the government said it would get rid of laws defending “honor crimes,” a widely criticized tribal custom in which a male relative may evade prosecution for assaulting a woman seen as dishonoring a family. The punishment for a crime committed to eradicate a woman’s “shame,” for promiscuity or disobeying religious and cultural strictures, will now be the same for any other kind of assault.

为了更好地“保护女性的权利”，政府表示将废除维护“荣誉犯罪”的法律。“荣誉犯罪”是一种广受批评的部落习俗，在这种习俗中，男性亲属可能会因攻击被视为有损家庭名誉的女性而逃避起诉。对旨在消除女性“耻辱”、滥交或不遵守宗教和文化约束的罪行的惩罚，现在对任何其他类型的攻击都是一样的。

In a country where expatriates outnumber citizens nearly nine to one, the amendments will permit foreigners to avoid Islamic Shariah courts on issues like marriage, divorce and inheritance.

在一个外籍人士数量几乎是公民数量的九倍的国家，修正案将允许外国人在婚姻、离婚和继承等问题上避开伊斯兰教法法院。

The announcement said nothing of other behavior deemed insulting to local customs that has landed foreigners in jail in the past, such as acts of homosexuality, cross-dressing and public displays of affection.

声明没有提到其他被认为是对当地习俗的侮辱行为，这些行为过去曾让外国人入狱，比如同性恋行为、变装行为和公开示爱。

Traditional Islamic values remain strong in the federation of seven desert sheikhdoms. Even so, Annelle Sheline, a Middle East research fellow at the Quincy Institute for Responsible Statecraft, wrote on Twitter that the drastic changes “can happen without too much popular resistance because the population of citizens, especially in the main cities of Dubai and Abu Dhabi, is so small.”

在由七个沙漠酋长国组成的联盟中，传统的伊斯兰价值观依然牢固。即便如此，昆西负责任治国研究所(Quincy Institute For Responsible Statcraft)的中东研究员安妮尔·谢林(Annelle Sheline)在Twitter上写道，巨大的变化“可以在没有太多民众抵制的情况下发生，因为公民人口太少了，尤其是在迪拜和阿布扎比这两个主要城市。”

The roughly 1 million Emiratis in the UAE, a hereditarily ruled country long criticized for its suppression of dissent, closely toe the government line. Political parties and labor unions remain illegal.

阿联酋是一个世袭统治的国家，长期以来一直因镇压异见而受到批评。阿联酋大约100万阿联酋人紧跟政府的路线。政党和工会仍然是非法的。
