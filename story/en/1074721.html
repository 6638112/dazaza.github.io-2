Who Hacked the DAO on Ethereum? Here’s How We Jumped Past One Critical Step
是谁在以太坊上砍了刀？以下是我们如何跳过一个关键步骤

2022-02-23 21:19:22

Ethereum, the second-largest crypto network, has achieved success well beyond what its creator, Vitalik Buterin, could have imagined when he initially thought up the smart contract platform as a teenager in the fall of 2013.

第二大加密网络以太坊（Ethereum）所取得的成功远远超出了其创始人维塔利克·巴特林（Vitalik Buterin）在2013年秋天十几岁时最初构思智能合约平台时的想象。

It has a market cap of over $300 billion, it’s become popular with the likes of Mark Cuban, and it’s the leading force in many of the new trends in crypto, such as initial coin offerings back in the first big crypto mania in 2017 and non-fungible tokens (NFTs) today.

它的市值超过3000亿美元，受到马克·库班（Mark Cuban）等人的欢迎，是许多加密新趋势的主导力量，比如2017年第一次大规模加密热潮中的首次硬币发行和今天的不可替代代币（NFT）。
