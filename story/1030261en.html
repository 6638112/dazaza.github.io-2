研究人员提出新的意识理论

2020-10-21 15:12:12

Electromagnetic energy in the brain enables brain matter to create our consciousness and our ability to be aware and think, according to a new theory developed by Professor Johnjoe McFadden from the University of Surrey. Publishing his theory in the journal Neuroscience of Consciousness, Professor McFadden posits that consciousness is in fact the brain's energy field. This theory could pave the way toward the development of conscious AI, with robots that are aware and have the ability to think becoming a reality.

根据萨里大学约翰乔·麦克法登教授开发的一项新理论，大脑中的电磁能使大脑物质能够创造我们的意识以及我们意识和思考的能力。麦克法登教授在“意识的神经科学”杂志上发表了他的理论，他提出意识实际上是大脑的能量场。这一理论可能为有意识的人工智能的发展铺平道路，有意识并有能力思考的机器人将成为现实。

Early theories on what our consciousness is and how it has been created tended toward the supernatural, suggesting that humans and probably other animals possess an immaterial soul that confers consciousness, thought and free will—capabilities that inanimate objects lack. Most scientists today have discarded this view, known as dualism, to embrace a 'monistic' view of a consciousness generated by the brain itself and its network of billions of nerves. By contrast, McFadden proposes a scientific form of dualism based on the difference between matter and energy, rather than matter and soul.

早期关于我们的意识是什么以及它是如何被创造的理论倾向于超自然，认为人类，可能还有其他动物都拥有一个非物质的灵魂，赋予意识、思想和自由意志-这些能力是无生命的物体所缺乏的。今天，大多数科学家已经抛弃了这种被称为二元论的观点，转而接受大脑本身及其由数十亿神经组成的网络产生的意识的一元论观点。相比之下，麦克法登提出了一种科学的二元论形式，其基础是物质和能量之间的差异，而不是物质和灵魂之间的差异。

The theory is based on scientific fact: when neurons in the brain and nervous system fire, they not only send the familiar electrical signal down the wire-like nerve fibres, but they also send a pulse of into the surrounding tissue. Such energy is usually disregarded, yet it carries the same information as firings, but as an immaterial wave of energy, rather than a flow of atoms in and out of the nerves.

这一理论基于科学事实：当大脑和神经系统中的神经元燃烧时，它们不仅沿着线状神经纤维发送熟悉的电信号，而且还向周围组织发送脉冲。这种能量通常被忽视，但它携带的信息与发射相同，但它是一种无形的能量波，而不是进出神经的原子流。

This field is well-known and is routinely detected by brain-scanning techniques such as electroencephalogram (EEG) and magnetoencephalography (MEG) but has previously been dismissed as irrelevant to brain function. Instead, McFadden proposes that the brain's information-rich electromagnetic field is in fact itself the seat of consciousness, driving 'free will' and voluntary actions. This new also accounts for why, despite their immense complexity and ultra-fast operation, today's computers have not exhibited the slightest spark of consciousness; however, with the right technical development, robots that are aware and can think for themselves could become a reality.

这一领域是众所周知的，通常通过脑电图(EEG)和脑磁图(MEG)等脑扫描技术检测到，但之前曾被认为与大脑功能无关而不屑一顾。相反，麦克法登提出，大脑信息丰富的电磁场实际上本身就是意识的所在地，驱动着自由意志和自愿行动。这一新发现也解释了为什么今天的计算机尽管极其复杂，运行速度极快，却没有表现出丝毫的意识火花；然而，只要技术发展得当，能够感知并能独立思考的机器人就能成为现实。

Johnjoe McFadden, Professor of Molecular Genetics and Director of the Quantum Biology Doctoral Training Centre at the University of Surrey, said: "How becomes aware and manages to think is a mystery that has been pondered by philosophers, theologians, mystics and ordinary people for millennia. I believe this mystery has now been solved, and that is the experience of nerves plugging into the 's self-generated to drive what we call 'free will' and our voluntary actions." More information: Johnjoe McFadden. Integrating information in the brain's EM field: the cemi field theory of consciousness, Neuroscience of Consciousness (2020). DOI: 10.1093/nc/niaa016

分子遗传学教授、萨里大学量子生物学博士生培训中心主任约翰乔·麦克法登(Johnjoe McFadden)说：“几千年来，哲学家、神学家、神秘主义者和普通人一直在思考如何感知并设法思考。”我相信这个谜团现在已经解开了，那就是神经连接到自我生成的体验，以驱动我们所说的自由意志和我们的自愿行动。更多信息，约翰乔·麦克法登(Johnjoe McFadden)报道。整合大脑EM领域的信息：意识的CEMI领域理论，意识的神经科学(2020)。DOI：10.1093/NC/NIAA016。
