This is What Happens When You Fly a Drone Into a Volcano
这是将无人机飞到火山时会发生的事情

2021-03-29 01:28:59

Photographer Garðar Ólafs — and founder of the Iceland-based stock agency Airstock — recently took his DJI drone to the skies above the erupting Geldingadalir volcano and may have gotten a little bit too close.

摄影师GaržaróLafs - 基于冰岛的股票代理Airstock的创始人 - 最近将他的Dji无人机带到了喷发Geldingadalir火山上方的天空中，可能已经太近了。

The Geldingadalir volcano which is currently erupting about 15 minutes from Ólafs’s home town of Reykjanes.

Geldingadalir火山目前爆发了大约15分钟的距离ólafs的雷克林镇。

“It’s a special feeling having an erupting volcano 15 minutes from my home,” Ólafs writes on Instagram. “Reykjanes has always been an underestimated part of Iceland, but today it’s probably the most known part, funny how things can change in one night.”

“这是一个特殊的感觉，距离我的家15分钟后爆发了火山，”ólafs在Instagram上写道。 “雷克杰斯一直是冰岛的低估部分，但今天它可能是最着名的部分，有趣的是一晚的事情会如何变化。”

Ólafs and his brother Oli Haukur have been shooting a lot of drone content over the last five years in Iceland and thought it would be a good idea to list the footage instead of leaving it on hard drives to languish unappreciated. Instead of working with established stock agencies, they decided to start their own agency and have since sold some footage to Netflix, Apple, BBC, and others. Some of the footage, including the shots of the Geldingadalir volcano below, are available nowhere else.

óLAFS和他的兄弟Oli Haukur在过去五年中在冰岛上射出了很多无人机内容，并认为列出了镜头是一个好主意，而不是将其留在硬盘上未被签名。他们决定启动自己的代理机构，而不是与已建立的股票机构合作，以来已向Netflix，Apple，BBC等销售了一些镜头。一些镜头，包括下面的Geldingadalir火山的镜头，无处可用。

Ólafs tells PetaPixel that he was flying his drone around the eruption, capturing photos and videos, when he decided to try a shot looking straight down into the mouth of the fissure.

óLAFS告诉Petapixel，他在爆发，捕获照片和视频周围飞行他的无人机，当时他决定尝试一下射门直接朝向裂缝的嘴。

“I was flying my drone around the eruption and decided it would be cool to see it from straight above,” he says. “I slowly lowered the drone until all I could see was erupting lava, and when I looked up, I didn’t see the drone anymore. Basically, I was inside the crater of the volcano.”

“我在爆发中飞过我的无人机，并决定从上面直接看到它会很酷，”他说。 “我慢慢地降低了无人机，直到我所能看到的只是爆发熔岩，当我抬起头时，我没有看到无人机了。基本上，我在火山的火山口里面。“

He says that when he realized how close to the lava he had gotten, he was surprised and hurried to get his drone out of danger.

他说，当他意识到他所说的熔岩接近时，他很惊讶并匆匆赶上他的无人机出于危险。

“I was really surprised that the drone was still in the air since it was basically inside the crater,” he says.

“我真的很惊讶，无人机仍然在空中，因为它基本上在火山口里面，”他说。

When Ólafs flew his drone back to his takeoff location, he noticed that it did not emerge unscathed. The light under the drone was melted, and sensors were damaged from the extreme heat. He reports that the drone still works, but the vision sensor underneath no longer functions and gives constant errors during flights. It is likely that if he remained in that position for much longer or chose to fly closer, his drone would not have made it out.

当ólafs飞过他的无人机回到他的起飞位置时，他注意到它没有出现毫发损害。无人机下的光熔化，传感器从极热的热量损坏。他报道了无人机仍然有效，但视觉传感器下面不再是函数，并且在航班期间给出恒定的错误。如果他仍然在那个位置留下更长时间或选择越来越近，他的无人机就不会制定出来。

“In the end, I would say that the shot was worth it,” Ólafs says, referencing the footage below that he was able to capture.

“到底，我会说镜头是值得的，”ólafs说，参考下面的镜头可以捕获。

Below are a set of other images that Ólafs has captured of the volcano. They are no less beautiful than the other footage that has emerged from Iceland’s photography community this past week.

下面是ólafs已捕获火山的一组其他图像。在过去的一周，他们不如冰岛摄影社区出现的那种镜头不那么漂亮。

Volcanoes and drones have a history of not getting along. In 2015, multiple drones were lost capturing footage of an active volcano in the Pacific, and in 2017 a photographer melted his drone flying over a volcano in Hawaii. One common theme is that despite all the damage, these photographers all seem to think the results made the damage worth it.

火山和无人机的历史不相处。 2015年，多种无人机丢失了太平洋的活跃火山的捕获镜头，2017年，摄影师在夏威夷的火山上融化了他的无人机飞行。一个普遍的主题是，尽管所有伤害所造成的，但这些摄影师似乎认为结果是值得的。
