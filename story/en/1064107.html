Jeff Bezos Plans to Travel to Space on Blue Origin Flight Next Month
杰夫·贝佐斯计划在下个月前往蓝色的航班上的太空

2021-06-08 03:06:30

(Bloomberg) -- Jeff Bezos will go to space next month when his company, Blue Origin, launches its first passenger-carrying mission.

（布鲁姆伯格） - 杰夫贝斯在下个月将在其公司，蓝色起源推出其第一批携带任务时的空间。

The 57-year-old, who plans to travel alongside his brother, Mark, made the announcement in an Instagram post Monday. The scheduled launch next month will be about two weeks after the billionaire plans to step down as chief executive officer of Amazon.com Inc.

57岁的人计划与他的兄弟标记一起旅行，在星期一的Instagram帖子中宣布。下个月的预定发布将在亿万富翁计划落下亚马逊公司的首席执行官之后大约两周

“Ever since I was five years old, I’ve dreamed of traveling to space,” Bezos said in the post. “On July 20th, I will take that journey with my brother. The greatest adventure, with my best friend.”

“自从我五岁以来，我曾梦想着到太空，”Bezos在帖子中说。 “7月20日，我会和哥哥一起旅行。最伟大的冒险，我最好的朋友。“

Blue Origin is one of several high-profile space-tourism companies backed by a wealthy entrepreneur, alongside Elon Musk’s Space Exploration Technologies Corp. and Richard Branson-backed Virgin Galactic Holdings Inc. Both of those companies are making plans to carry paying customers.

Blue Origin是由富裕的企业家支持的几家高调的空间旅游公司之一，与Elon Musk的太空探索技术公司和理查德布兰森支持的处女Holding Inc.均制定载有支付客户的计划。

Blue Origin is auctioning off a seat on its New Shepard rocket for the July 20 flight, an 11-minute trip to suborbital space that will reach an altitude of about 100 kilometers (62 miles). The spot will be the only one available for purchase on the flight, and the proceeds will go to a Blue Origin foundation that promotes math and science education.

Blue Origin是7月20日航班的新谢泼德火箭队的座位上拍卖，距离副岩体空间11分钟，海拔约100公里（62英里）。目的将是唯一可用于航班购买的人，收益将进入一个促进数学和科学教育的Blue Origin基础。

The high bid stood at $2.8 million Monday morning, according to the company’s website. Bidding will end June 12 with a live online auction. It will be Blue Origin’s 16th flight.

据该公司的网站称，高价竞标于周一早上280万美元。竞标将在6月12日结束，直播在线拍卖。它将是Blue Origin的第16个航班。

The company has said that customers will have a four-day experience, including three days of preflight training at the company’s launch site near Van Horn, Texas. Blue Origin has built accommodations, including a cafeteria. The space capsule will be a first-class experience, with reclining leather seats designed to cushion the force of zipping into space. The capsule also has “the largest windows in spacecraft history,” according to Blue Origin’s website, so passengers can take in the view.

该公司表示，客户将拥有为期四天的经验，包括在德克萨斯州梵号附近的公司发射地点预检培训。蓝色起源建造了住宿，包括自助餐厅。太空舱将是一流的经验，倾斜的皮革座椅，设计用于缓冲拉链的力量。胶囊也有“航天器历史上最大的窗户”，根据Blue Origin的网站，所以乘客可以接受观点。

Branson, 70 years old, plans to fly on a Virgin Galactic flight to suborbital space, perhaps as early as this summer. The timing of that launch had been in doubt after several test failures and got back on track last month with a successful flight to space carrying two pilots.

Branson，70岁，计划在亚眠空间飞往处女飞行飞行，也许早在今年夏天。在几次测试失败之后，该发布的时间已经有所怀疑，并在上个月回到轨道上，成功飞往携带两名飞行员的空间。

John Glenn was the oldest person to fly in space, at 77, when he hitched a ride aboard a space shuttle as a U.S. senator in 1998. Glenn had been the first American to orbit the earth, as a National Aeronautics and Space Administration astronaut in 1962.

约翰格伦是最古老的人，在77岁时，当他在1998年作为美国参议员乘坐航天班车时，他坐在77岁的时候。格伦是世界上第一个轨道的美国人，是美国国家航空航天局担任宇航员1962年。

Bezos has been slowly selling off his stake in Amazon to invest in Blue Origin. In 2017 he said he spends about $1 billion a year on the sideline. He has compared space tourism to stunt-flying barnstormers, who helped fuel public fascination with aviation and made it a common mode of transportation. Bezos has said he envisions millions of people living and working in space, and lowering the cost of space travel with rockets that can be reused like airplanes is the first step.

Bezos一直在慢慢销售他在亚马逊的股份，以投资蓝色起源。 2017年，他表示，他每年花费约10亿美元的副业。他将空间旅游与飞行飞行的Barnstormers相比，帮助燃料与航空公众迷恋，使其成为共同的交通方式。 Bezos表示，他设想了数百万人的生活和工作在太空中，并降低了可以重用就像飞机一样重复使用的火箭的太空旅行的成本是第一步。

The billionaire plans to hand the reins as Amazon’s CEO to Andy Jassy on July 5. Amazon shares were little changed at 10:49 a.m eastern.

亿万富翁计划将缰绳作为亚马逊的首席执行官在7月5日举行。亚马逊股价在东方10:49几乎没有变化。
