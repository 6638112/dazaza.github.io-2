2020-11-08 12:57:45

Run Windows apps such as Microsoft Office in Linux (Ubuntu) and GNOME as if they were a part of the native OS, including Nautilus integration for right clicking on files of specific mime types to open them.

运行Windows应用程序，如Microsoft Office in Linux(Ubuntu)和GNOME，就像它们是本地操作系统的一部分一样，包括Nautilus集成，用于右键单击特定MIME类型的文件以打开它们。

Back in April, Hayden Barnes tweeted what appeared to be native Windows apps in a container or VM inside Ubuntu. However, no details have emerged on how this was accomplished, though it is likely a similar method to this but with an insider build Windows Container.

早在4月份，海登·巴恩斯就在推特上发布了Ubuntu内部容器或虚拟机中似乎是本地Windows应用的东西。然而，目前还没有关于这是如何实现的细节，尽管它很可能是一种类似的方法，但使用的是内部构建的Windows Container。
