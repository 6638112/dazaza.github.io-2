Zavvy wants to modernize employee enablement
Zavvy希望实现员工支持的现代化

2022-02-16 22:25:16

Zavvy, a Munich, Germany-based startup, today launched its take on modern employee enablement and announced a $4 million funding round led by La Famiglia VC. Other investors in this round include Emerge Education, Picus Capital and a number of angel investors, including Eventbrite co-founder Renaud Visage, Personio co-founder and CEO Hanno Renner and Forto co-founder and CEO Michael Wax.

总部位于德国慕尼黑的初创公司Zavvy今天启动了对现代员工支持的计划，并宣布了由La Famiglia VC牵头的一轮400万美元的融资。这一轮的其他投资者包括Emerge Education、Picus Capital和一些天使投资者，包括Eventbrite联合创始人雷诺·维萨奇、Personio联合创始人兼首席执行官汉诺·雷纳和Forto联合创始人兼首席执行官迈克尔·韦克斯。

The company’s co-founders Joshua Cornelius and Mehmet Yilmaz, who first met as students in Munich, previously also co-founded Freeletics, one of the most successful health and fitness apps in Germany, which they sold to FitLab in 2018. Going from a consumer fitness startup to employee enablement isn’t the most obvious route, but Cornelius argues that there is a lot of overlap.

该公司的联合创始人约书亚·科内利厄斯（Joshua Cornelius）和迈赫迈特·伊尔马兹（Mehmet Yilmaz）最初是在慕尼黑求学时认识的，此前他们还共同创建了Freeletics，这是德国最成功的健康和健身应用程序之一，并于2018年将其出售给FitLab。从一家消费者健身初创公司到员工培训并不是最明显的途径，但科内利厄斯认为两者有很多重叠之处。

“We really enjoy helping people to unfold their talents — to make the best of what they have been given,” he said. First, it was in the context of physical development and then, after selling the company, we wondered, what is it that we want to spend our time on? What had such an impact on our lives that we want to spend all of our time on that?” First, the two started exploring the education space but ultimately settled on professional development. “People they have to switch jobs all the time — and even if they don’t, they have to develop all the time, but there is no structured way to do that,” said Cornelius and added that in Europe, because of the protections that workers get, it’s very hard to fire somebody just because they don’t have the right skills.

他说：“我们真的很喜欢帮助人们发挥他们的才能——充分利用他们所得到的。”。首先，这是在物理开发的背景下，然后，在出售公司后，我们想知道，我们想花时间在什么上？是什么对我们的生活产生了如此大的影响，以至于我们想把所有的时间都花在这上面？“首先，两人开始探索教育空间，但最终决定从事专业发展。”科内利厄斯说：“人们必须随时更换工作——即使他们不这样做，他们也必须不断发展，但没有结构化的方法来做到这一点。”他补充说，在欧洲，由于工人得到的保护，很难仅仅因为他们没有合适的技能就解雇他们。

Currently, Zavvy focuses on a few core areas: onboarding, training, employee development and connection programs to encourage more casual conversations between colleagues. Businesses can pick and choose which of these different areas they want to enable for their users (and the pricing scales accordingly).

目前，Zavvy专注于几个核心领域：入职、培训、员工发展和联系计划，以鼓励同事之间进行更随意的对话。企业可以从这些不同的领域中选择他们想要为用户提供的服务（以及相应的定价比例）。

The company currently targets mid-sized companies and has already signed up quite a few customers. These include the likes of Freeletics (no surprise there), as well as fellow Munich-based startups like real estate finance platform Alasco and campervan rental service roadsurfer.

该公司目前的目标是中型公司，并已与不少客户签约。这些公司包括Freeletics（这并不奇怪），以及房地产金融平台Alasco和campervan租赁服务roadsurfer等位于慕尼黑的初创公司。

“The first days in a company are overwhelming for any new employee. It’s important to give new team members a warm and supportive onboarding experience,” said Barbara Imm, Head of HR at roadsurfer. “At the same time, we’re growing rapidly and need a reliable and scalable onboarding solution for both remote and in-office across 50 locations in twelve countries. Zavvy gives us exactly that. Our manual work is drastically reduced and new team members highly rate their onboarding experience. We’re looking forward to rolling out company-wide development plans with Zavvy later this year. In combination with onboarding it will allow us to offer our team continuous growth from day one.”

roadsurfer人力资源总监芭芭拉·伊姆（Barbara Imm）说：“在公司的第一天对任何新员工来说都是压倒性的。给新的团队成员一个热情和支持的入职体验非常重要。”。“与此同时，我们正在快速发展，需要一个可靠且可扩展的入职解决方案，用于12个国家/地区的50个地点的远程和办公室。Zavvy正好为我们提供了这一点。我们的手动工作大大减少，新团队成员高度评价他们的入职经验。我们期待着在公司范围内推广今年晚些时候与Zavvy的发展计划。结合入职培训，这将使我们的团队从第一天开始持续增长。"

At the core of the Zavvy experience is a no-code workflow builder that allows companies to quickly build their own training materials and other forms of content to the service. To speed this up, the service also offers templates for virtually all of the standard use cases (and for companies that are still using spreadsheets, Zavvy also offers a set of free playbooks).

Zavvy体验的核心是一个无代码的工作流生成器，允许公司快速为服务构建自己的培训材料和其他形式的内容。为了加快速度，该服务还提供了几乎所有标准用例的模板（对于仍在使用电子表格的公司，Zavvy还提供了一套免费的剧本）。

The service also integrates with many existing HR systems, as well as Slack and Microsoft Teams. As Cornelius noted, some of the new funding will go to building out these integrations, especially as Zavvy aims to go upmarket and bring on larger customers.

该服务还集成了许多现有的人力资源系统，以及Slack和Microsoft团队。正如科内利厄斯所指出的，一些新的资金将用于构建这些集成，尤其是在Zavvy致力于进入高端市场并带来更大客户的情况下。

For 2022, the team plans to build more listening and training solutions into its platform.

2022年，该团队计划在其平台中构建更多听力和培训解决方案。

“As companies increasingly become a commodity in the eyes of strong talent, we believe they need to rethink their entire employee experience to stay competitive,” said Judith Dada, General Partner at La Famiglia VC. “ Zavvy helps businesses understand and manage the growth path of their employees – starting from onboarding all the way to development cycles.”

La Famiglia VC的普通合伙人朱迪思·达达（Judith Dada）表示：“随着公司越来越成为优秀人才眼中的商品，我们认为他们需要重新思考自己的整个员工体验，以保持竞争力。”。“Zavvy帮助企业了解和管理员工的成长路径——从入职到发展周期。”
