Square buys Credit Karma Tax unit from Intuit for $50M and will integrate its free tax filing service into Cash App
Square以5000万美元从Intuit手中收购了Credit Karma Tax部门，并将其免费的报税服务整合到Cash App中

2020-11-26 02:22:46

Square  ( SQ) - Get Report shares rose Wednesday after the payments company said it’s buying Credit Karma’s tax unit for $50 million cash.

Square（SQ）-Get Report股票周三上涨，此前这家支付公司表示将以5000万美元现金收购Credit Karma的税务部门。

Credit Karma Tax, which provides a free do-it-yourself tax-filing service, will go into Square’s Cash App unit.

提供免费的自己动手报税服务的Credit Karma Tax将纳入Square的Cash App部门。

Cash App enables customers to transfer money, pay for goods and services, invest in stocks, and buy and sell bitcoin. The division has grown sharply in recent quarters.

Cash App使客户能够转移资金，购买商品和服务，投资股票以及买卖比特币。该部门在最近几个季度急剧增长。

“The acquisition provides an opportunity to further digitize and simplify the tax-filing process in the U.S., expanding access to the one in three households which are unbanked or underbanked,” Square said in a statement.

Square在一份声明中说：“此次收购提供了进一步数字化和简化美国税收申报流程的机会，从而扩大了三分之二没有银行账户或银行账户不足的家庭的使用范围，” Square在一份声明中说。

The deal came after the Justice Department required Intuit  ( INTU) - Get Report to sell Credit Karma’s tax business if it wants clearance for its $7.1 billion purchase of Credit Karma, a free credit and financial management platform.

这笔交易是在司法部要求Intuit（INTU）-Get Report出售Credit Karma的税务业务之后，如果它希望以71亿美元的价格收购Credit Karma，这是一个免费的信贷和财务管理平台，则要出售该业务。

Shares of Square, San Francisco, recently traded at $212.81, up 4.9%. They have well more than tripled year to date.

旧金山Square的股价最近上涨了4.9％，至212.81美元。到目前为止，他们的人数增长了两倍多。
