勇敢浏览器月度活跃用户突破2000万，日活跃用户突破700万

2020-11-03 04:33:52

Brave now has nearly 1 million Verified Creators, and delivered over 2,000 ad campaigns in nearly 200 countries

Brave现在拥有近100万名经过认证的创作者，并在近200个国家和地区投放了2000多个广告活动。

November marks one year since we launched Brave 1.0 , the most private, safest, and fastest (3-6x) browser. Over the past year, we’ve seen amazing growth on multiple fronts and we’re happy to celebrate Brave 1.0’s first anniversary by announcing that we officially passed 20 million monthly active users (20.5), up from 8.7 million this time last year. This represents a 2.3x increase in one year.

11月是我们推出Brave1.0的一周年纪念日，它是最私密、最安全、速度最快(3-6倍)的浏览器。在过去的一年里，我们在多个方面看到了惊人的增长，我们很高兴地庆祝Brave1.0的一周年，宣布我们的月度活跃用户(2050万)正式突破2000万，高于去年同期的870万。这意味着一年内增长了2.3倍。

Brave also passed 7 million daily active users, up from 3 million this time last year, showing a 2.3x increase in DAU over the year. Over the past year, Brave received numerous positive reviews and is now the #1 rated browser in Google Play. Since our iOS browser became a default option on Apple devices with iOS 14 this past September, our iOS daily active users have grown by 34%.

Brave还突破了700万日活跃用户，高于去年同期的300万，显示DAU比去年同期增长了2.3倍。在过去的一年里，Brave收到了无数的好评，现在是Google Play中排名第一的浏览器。自从我们的iOS浏览器在去年9月成为iOS14苹果设备的默认选项以来，我们的iOS日活跃用户增长了34%。

“Users are fed up with surveillance capitalism, and 20 million people have switched to Brave for an entirely new Web ecosystem with an opt-in ad economy that puts them back in control of their browsing experience,” said Brendan Eich, CEO and co-founder of Brave. “The global privacy movement is gaining traction, and this milestone is just one more step in our journey to make privacy-by-default a standard for all Web users.”

Brave首席执行官兼联合创始人布兰登·艾奇(Brendan Eich)表示：“用户受够了监视资本主义，2000万人转向了Brave，建立了一个全新的网络生态系统，拥有可选的广告经济，让他们重新控制自己的浏览体验。”全球隐私运动正在获得牵引力，这一里程碑只是我们让默认隐私成为所有网络用户的标准之旅的又一步。“。

Verified creators : There are now nearly one million Verified creators and publishers ( 972,000 as of Nov. 2nd), up from 300,000 last year, showing a 3.2x increase in 12 months.

经过认证的创作者：目前有近100万经过认证的创作者和出版商(截至11月2日为97.2万人)，高于去年的30万人，12个月内增长了3.2倍。

Advertisers and advertising campaigns: Brave Ads campaigns are now supported in nearly 200 countries with over 2 billion ad confirmation events to date. There have been 2,215 campaigns from over 460 advertisers , from brands such as Verizon, The Home Depot, The New York Times, Progressive Insurance, Newegg, Chipotle, Chess.com, Privacy.com, PayPal/Honey, Ledger, Amazon, Harry’s Razors, CBS, KIND snacks, Logitech, Lenovo, Grubhub, Belkin, Quickbooks, and Evernote. The average CTR (click-through rate) for a Brave Ads campaign is 9% , well above the industry average of just 2%, but many brands are seeing even higher engagement .

广告商和广告活动：勇敢的广告活动目前在近200个国家和地区得到支持，迄今已有超过20亿次广告确认活动。已经有来自460多个广告商的2215个广告宣传活动，这些品牌来自Verizon、家得宝、纽约时报、进步保险、Newegg、Chipotle、Chess.com、Privacy.com、PayPal/Honey、Ledger、亚马逊、哈里的剃须刀、CBS、Kind零食、罗技、联想、GrubHub、Belkin、Quickbook和Evernote。Brave广告活动的平均点击率(点击率)为9%，远高于行业平均水平仅为2%，但许多品牌的参与度甚至更高。

Amount of BAT donated to publishers and creators: Brave users have rewarded their favorite publishers and creators by contributing 26 million BAT to them. Brave users can support websites, or creators on YouTube, Twitch, Twitter, GitHub and more, by activating Brave Rewards and its integrated Brave wallet on both desktop and mobile. This feature allows users to send BAT as tips for great content, either directly as they browse or by defaulting to recurring monthly payments. Brave users earn BAT by opting in to view privacy-preserving Brave Ads (70% of ad revenue share).

向出版商和创作者捐赠BAT的数量：勇敢的用户奖励了他们最喜欢的出版商和创作者，向他们捐赠了2600万BAT。勇敢的用户可以通过在台式机和移动设备上激活Brave Rewards及其集成的Brave钱包来支持YouTube、Twitch、Twitter、GitHub等网站或创建者。这一功能允许用户发送BAT作为优秀内容的提示，要么直接在浏览时发送，要么默认按月付费。勇敢的用户通过选择观看保护隐私的勇敢广告(占广告收入份额的70%)来赚取BAT。

Partnerships : Over the past year, Brave has partnered with Binance, Gemini, Guardian VPN, bitFlyer, NYIAX, Splinterlands, TAP Network, Travala.com, and others to expand its product offerings and BAT utility. Brave also launched Global Privacy Control alongside The New York Times, The Washington Post, Financial Times, Automattic (WordPress.com & Tumblr), DuckDuckGo, Mozilla, Disconnect, Digital Content Next (DCN), Consumer Reports, the Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF), and others.

合作伙伴关系：在过去的一年里，Brave与Binance、Gemini、Guardian VPN、bitFlyer、NYIAX、Splinterland、TAP Network、Travala.com和其他公司合作，以扩大其产品供应和BAT实用程序。勇敢者还与“纽约时报”、“华盛顿邮报”、“金融时报”、Automattic(WordPress.com&；Tumblr)、DuckDuckGo、Mozilla、Disconnect、Digital Content Next(DCN)、“消费者报告”(Consumer Reports)、电子前沿基金会(EFF)等一起推出了“全球隐私控制”(Global Privacy Control)。

Community: Over the past year, the Brave community saw Reddit membership across r/BATProject and r/brave_browser nearly double, from 43,000 members to over 82,000. The community YouTube channel , which features the Weekly BAT Podcast, guest interviews, and other media content, grew to 6.3K subscribers—a 300% increase. Brave Community support forum went from 890,000 views per month to over 2,000,000, with over 40,000 new accounts created. Brave Help Center went from 250,000 monthly views to over 700,000.

社区：在过去的一年里，Brave社区看到r/BATProject和r/Brave_Browser的Reddit成员几乎翻了一番，从43,000名成员增加到82,000名以上。以每周蝙蝠播客、嘉宾采访和其他媒体内容为特色的社区YouTube频道，订阅量增长到6.3K-增长了300%。勇敢社区支持论坛的浏览量从每月89万次增加到200多万次，新创建的账户超过4万个。勇敢帮助中心的月浏览量从25万次增加到70万次。
