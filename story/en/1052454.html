Kuo: Apple AirPods 3 will not enter mass production until Q3 2021; AirPods shipments will decline from 90M in 2020 to 78M in 2021 due to increasing competition
Kuo：Apple Airpods 3将不会进入批量生产，直到Q3 2021; 由于竞争增加，Airpods出货量将在2020年的90米到78米下降

2021-03-15 19:28:36

Despite a flurry of leaks surrounding next-gen AirPods in the last couple of weeks, Ming-Chi Kuo this morning said that new AirPods will not enter mass production until the third quarter of 2021, which means they won’t be launching imminently.

尽管在过去几周内，Ming-Chi Kuo围绕下一个Gen Airpods围绕下一个泄漏，但明天古罗今天早上表示，新的Airpods不会进入大规模生产，直到2021年第三季度，这意味着它们不会立即发射。

Many leakers have suggested that Apple will hold a product event on March 23rd. However, the date is not yet confirmed by Apple and it remains unclear what product would feature. Today’s Kuo report indicates AirPods 3 are simply not ready yet.

许多泄漏都建议苹果将于3月23日举办产品活动。但是，苹果公司尚未确认日期，尚不清楚产品的功能。今天的Kuo报告表明Airpods 3根本尚未准备好。

The majority of Kuo’s report centers on AirPods shipment predictions. Kuo predicts that Apple will see a decline in Apple headphone shipments this year, as Apple faces increasing competition from rival manufacturers.

大多数Kuo在Airpods发货预测上的报告中心。 Kuo预测苹果将在今年看到苹果耳机出货量下降，因为Apple面临着竞争对手制造商的竞争增加。

Kuo says Apple shipped around 90 million AirPods in 2020 and expects that to fall to about 78 million for 2021.

Kuo表示，苹果在2020年发货了大约9000万Airpods，预计将跌至约7800万令吉为2021年。

The analyst says Apple may keep second-generation AirPods on the market when AirPods 3 are released. However, he is not sure on this matter yet.

分析师表示，当Airpods 3被释放时，Apple可能会在市场上保持第二代Airpods。但是，他还不确定此问题。

The latest addition to the AirPods family, the $549 over-ear AirPods Max headphones, are said to have limited impact on shipments. Kuo expects Apple will sell just 1 million units across the entire year.

Airpods家族的最新补充，549美元的超耳Airpods Max耳机，据说对货物的影响有限。 Kuo预计苹果将在整年内销售100万台。
