Classy, which builds fundraising software for non-profit institutions, raises $118M led by Norwest Venture Partners
优雅，为非营利机构建立筹款软件，由NORWEST VENTURE PARTNERS提高11800万美元

2021-04-21 09:05:59

Classy, a technology company which builds fundraising software for non-profit institutions, has raised $118 million in fresh capital from venture capital firm Norwest Venture Partners and other investors, it said on Tuesday.

它周二表示，为非营利机构建立筹款软件的技术公司，为非营利机构筹集了筹款软件，为新的风险投资伙伴和其他投资者筹集了1.18亿美元。

Classy also said co-founder Scot Chisholm would step down from his role as chief executive officer and become the executive chairman, and Chief Operating Officer Christopher Himes, a former senior Salesforce.com (CRM.N) executive, will assume the role of CEO.

优雅还表示，联合创始人苏格兰·Chisholm将从他作为首席执行官的角色下降，并成为行政主席，并将首席运营官克里斯托弗赫斯，前高级Salesforce.com（CRM.N）执行，将承担首席执行官的作用。

Existing investors of the San Diego, California-based company, including Salesforce Ventures and Hinge Capital, also participated in the funding round.

加利福尼亚州的圣地亚哥公司现有投资者，包括Salesforce Ventures和铰链资本，也参加了资金。

Classy intends to use the fresh capital to enter new segments, such as corporate donors, as part of its broader strategy to match more high-profile donors with non-profit companies, Scot Chisholm told Reuters.

优雅打算使用新的资本进入公司捐助者等新段，作为其更广泛的策略的一部分，以与非营利性公司的非营利性公司相匹配，苏格兰··奇洛姆告诉路透社。

The company, which started off as a local fundraising firm to help raise funds for the American Cancer Society in San Diego, relaunched as a technology venture in 2011. Classy then pivoted its focus to the online space which makes it easier for non-profit companies to connect with donors.

该公司开始作为当地筹款公司，帮助筹集圣地亚哥的美国癌症协会的资金，以2011年作为技术冒险。然后，然后将其重点归功于在线空间，这使得非营利性公司更容易与捐助者联系。

The start-up primarily focuses on two initiatives - firstly, to help non-profits improve their rates of converting more visitors to donors, and secondly, to retain more donors, Chisholm said.

初创公司主要关注两项举措 - 首先，为了帮助非营利利润提高他们将更多游客转换给捐助者的利率，其次是为了保留更多捐助者，Chisholm说。
