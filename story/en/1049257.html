Paramount+ will cost $4.99 per month with ads
派拉蒙+的广告费用为每月$ 4.99

2021-02-25 08:18:07

In addition to launching in the United States, executives said the service will be available across Latin America and Canada on March 4, with a Nordic launch a few weeks later and an Australian launch also planned for this year.

高管们表示，除了在美国推出该服务外，该服务还将于3月4日在整个拉丁美洲和加拿大推出，数周后将在北欧推出，今年也计划在澳大利亚推出。

And they said that Paramount+ will cost $4.99 per month with ads in the U.S. (less than the $5.99 charged for CBS All Access), or $9.99 without ads and with additional sports, news and live TV content. There are also plans to bundle this with the company’s premium subscriptions, such as Showtime.

他们说，派拉蒙+在美国的广告费用为每月4.99美元（低于CBS All Access收取的5.99美元），如果没有广告以及其他体育，新闻和电视直播内容，则为9.99美元。还计划将其与公司的高级订阅捆绑在一起，例如Showtime。

Yes, it’s yet another streaming service with a plus in its name. But the company’s streaming president and CEO Tom Ryan said research has shown that ViacomCBS brands — not just Paramount and CBS, but Comedy Central, MTV, Nickelodeon and more — are well-known to viewers, and they’ll all be front-and-center in the new service. Plus, it’s worth noting that ViacomCBS already produces a number of hit streaming shows on other services, such as “13 Reasons Why,” “Emily in Paris” and “Jack Ryan.”

是的，这是另一种流媒体服务，其名称带有加号。但是该公司的流媒体总裁兼首席执行官汤姆·瑞安（Tom Ryan）说，研究表明，维亚康姆（ViacomCBS）品牌-不仅是派拉蒙（Paramount）和哥伦比亚广播公司（CBS），而且还有喜剧中心（Comedy Central），音乐电视（MTV），尼克国际（Nickelodeon）等-都为观众所熟知，而且它们都将成为先锋集中在新服务中。另外，值得注意的是，ViacomCBS已经在其他服务上制作了许多热门的流媒体节目，例如“ 13个理由”，“巴黎艾米丽”和“杰克·瑞安”。

ViacomCBS executives also argued that Paramount+ will have a unique combination of live news, live sports and (to use a phrase that repeated throughout the event) “a mountain of entertainment.” And from a product perspective, the service will offer originals in 4K, HDR and Dolby Vision, with easy downloads.

ViacomCBS的高管们还认为，派拉蒙+将把现场新闻，现场体育节目和（“在整个活动中重复使用的短语”）“娱乐之山”的独特结合。从产品的角度来看，该服务将提供4K，HDR和杜比视界（Dolby Vision）的原件，并且易于下载。

On the entertainment side, the service is supposed to have more than 30,000 TV show episodes and 2,500 movies. And the library will expand with new shows like a new version of “Frasier” with Kelsey Grammer returning to the role, as well as a “Halo” TV show that will now debut on Paramount+ instead of Showtime in early 2022. The service is also rebooting a variety of Paramount properties like “Love Story,” “Fatal Attraction” and “Flashdance.”

在娱乐方面，该服务将拥有30,000多个电视节目和2500部电影。此外，图书馆还将扩展新节目，例如Kelsey Grammer重返角色的新版本“ Frasier”，以及将于2022年初在Paramount +而非Showtime上首次亮相的“ Halo”电视节目。重新启动各种最重要的属性，例如“爱情故事”，“致命吸引力”和“ Flashdance”。

And like CBS All Access before it, Paramount+ will be home to new Star Trek shows — not just the already-launched “Discovery,” “Picard” and “Lower Decks,” but also the upcoming “Strange New Worlds” and the kids animated series “Prodigy.”

像之前的CBS All Access一样，派拉蒙+将成为新星际迷航节目的所在地-不仅是已经推出的“发现”，“卡片”和“下层甲板”，还有即将推出的“新奇世界”和动画儿童系列“神童”。

On the movie side, Paramount CEO Jim Gianopulis said the company is still a big believer the theatrical model, but it will be bringing some 2021 releases — including “A Quiet Place Part 2,” the first “Paw Patrol” movie and “Mission Impossible 7” — to Paramount+ in an accelerated fashion, 30 to 45 days after they come to theaters (a much less aggressive strategy than HBO Max, which will stream all Warner Bros. movies this year simultaneously with their theatrical release). And there will be new straight-to-streaming movies as well, starting with reboots of “Paranormal Activity” and “Pet Sematary.”

在电影方面，派拉蒙首席执行官吉姆·吉亚诺普利斯（Jim Gianopulis）表示，该公司仍然是戏剧模型的忠实拥护者，但它将带来2021年的上映影片，包括“寂静的地方2”，首部“爪子巡逻”电影和“不可能的任务” 7英寸—到派拉蒙+影院上映后30到45天以加速的方式播放（比HBO Max更具侵略性的策略，HBO Max将于今年在影院上映的同时播放所有华纳兄弟电影）。从“超自然活动”和“宠物世俗”的重新启动开始，还将有新的直接流式电影。
