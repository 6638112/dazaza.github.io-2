Waymo CEO John Krafcik says he is leaving the company; COO Dmitri Dolgov and CTO Tekedra Mawakana will become Waymo's co-CEOs
Waymo Ceo John Krafcik说他正在离开公司; Coo Dmitri Dolgov和CTO Tekedra Mawakana将成为Waymo的合作中心

2021-04-03 01:22:12

Dear Waymonauts, After five and a half exhilarating years leading this team, I’ve decided to depart from my CEO role with Waymo and kick-off new adventures. To start, I’m looking forward to a refresh period, reconnecting with old friends and family, and discovering new parts of the world. And I’ll continue to serve as an advisor to Waymo, as you expand the world’s first autonomous public ride-hailing service, Waymo One, and ramp our autonomous delivery product with Waymo Via.

亲爱的WayMonauts，经过五年半的令人振奋的岁月，我决定与Waymo和新的冒险开关的首席执行官角色。要开始，我期待着刷新期，与老朋友和家人重新连接，并发现世界新的地区。当您将全球第一个自主公共乘车服务，Waymo Ane的首次自主公共乘车服务服务，Waymo Via拓展我们的自主交付产品，将继续作为Waymo的顾问。

Going forward, two extraordinary leaders, Dmitri Dolgov and Tekedra Mawakana will take the helm at Waymo as co-CEOs. Tekedra and Dmitri are an incredibly talented pair who are ideally suited for this moment. Waymo’s new co-CEOs bring complementary skill sets and experiences - most recently as COO and CTO respectively - and have already been working together in close partnership for years in top executive positions at Waymo. Dmitri and Tekedra have my full confidence and support, and of course, the full confidence of Waymo’s board and Alphabet leadership. My time leading Waymo has been the capstone of my career. Together, we’ve achieved remarkable firsts as we develop, deploy, and commercialize our fully autonomous Waymo Driver, and work to make our roads safer and mobility more accessible. The Waymo Driver has driven autonomously tens of millions of miles on public roads across 25 U.S. cities, and more than 20 billion miles in simulation; safely gets anyone in Phoenix with the Waymo One app to their destinations across thousands of miles each week; and is unlocking convenience and scale for our local delivery and freight partners through Waymo Via. We launched Waymo as an independent Alphabet subsidiary, partnered with an amazing group of OEM, supplier, and service companies, and raised our first external investment round of $3.25 billion. As co-CEOs, Tekedra and Dmitri will continue to drive Waymo’s technical and business leadership in the rapidly advancing autonomous industry. Waymonauts, you are the strongest, smartest, most experienced and most capable team in the space. Thank you for entrusting me with this phase of our journey, and for taking care of our next chapter. It has been the greatest privilege of my life to serve on this mission together with you. Keep pioneering. - JK John, Thanks to you, we have come an amazing distance together since you first joined the team now five and a half years ago. Huge thanks to you for your partnership along the way as we’ve advanced Waymo’s state-of-the-art autonomous driving tech, our autonomous ride-hailing and delivery businesses, and your guiding hand as we chart our ambitious future. We’re grateful you’ll be staying on as an advisor. Waymonauts, As we start this next chapter together, we could not be more excited and grateful to have the opportunity to lead this team and company as your co-CEOs. We’re committed to working alongside you to build, deploy, and commercialize the Waymo Driver and drive the success of our incredible team and this company. We’re energized by the road and opportunity ahead of us. Thank you for your passion, your world-class contributions, and your confidence in us as we lead Waymo forward together. - Dmitri & Tekedra

前进，两个非凡的领导人，Dmitri Dolgov和Tekedra Mawakana将在Waymo担任合作中心。 Tekedra和Dmitri是一个非常有才华的对，非常适合这一刻。 Waymo的新的Co-CeoS带来了互补的技能和经验 - 最近分别为COO和CTO - 并且已经在Waymo的顶级行政职位上进行了密切伙伴关系。 Dmitri和Tekedra拥有我充满信心和支持，当然，Waymo的董事会和字母领导的充分信心。我的领导Waymo一直是我职业生涯的帽子。在一起，我们在开发，部署和商业化我们的完全自主Waymo驱动程序时，我们已经取得了卓越的第一款，并努力使我们的道路更安全和移动更易于访问。 Waymo Driver在25美国城市的公共道路上行动了数千万英里，在模拟中超过20亿英里;安全地在凤凰城的任何人用Waymo一个应用程序到他们的目的地，每周数千英里;并为我们当地的交付和货运合作伙伴通过Waymo Via解锁方便和规模。我们推出Waymo作为一个独立的字母子公司，与一个惊人的OEM，供应商和服务公司合作，并提出了我们的第一个外部投资回合3.25亿美元。作为CO-CEO，Tekedra和Dmitri将继续在迅速推进的自治行业中继续推动Waymo的技术和业务领导。 WayMonauts，您是该空间中最强大，最聪明，最有经验，最有经验的团队。感谢您委托我的旅程委托，并根据下一章处理我们的旅程。我生命中的最大特权是与你一起服务的。保持开拓。 - JK John，感谢您，自从您首次加入该团队的五年前，我们已经过了一个惊人的距离。由于我们先进的Waymo最先进的自主驾驶技术，我们的自主乘车和送货业务，以及我们的引导手，因为我们越来越多，感谢您的伙伴关系。我们很感激你将被视为顾问。随着WayMonauts，随着我们在一起开始的下一章，我们无法更加兴奋和感激有机会领导这支球队和公司作为您的合作中心。我们致力于与您一起建立，部署和商业化Waymo驱动程序，并推动我们令人难以置信的团队和本公司的成功。我们在我们前方的道路和机会上充满活力。感谢您的激情，您的世界级贡献，以及我们在我们领导的Waymo一起对我们的信心。 - DMitri＆amp; Tekedra
