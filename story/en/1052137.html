How to Convert Your Old Laptop Screen into an External Monitor
如何将旧笔记本电脑屏幕转换为外部监视器

2021-03-13 10:16:49

Every good thing comes to an end, but that doesn’t have to be true with your old or damaged laptop. Yes, your laptop is dead, but you can still salvage some vital components, the LCD screen is a good place to start. Not all of us are good with tools, but it shouldn’t be too great a challenge to successfully take out your old laptop screen in a few steps. All you have to do is take out the front bezel by loosening the screws and removing snaps. What’s a bezel? That question probably crossed your mind. The bezel is the plastic frame work in front of the screen; We will try to keep this nice and simple by defining such strange terms. Once the bezel is off, you need to take carefully take off the laptop screen by unscrewing the bracket screws (screws beside the screen) and taking off the video connector cable and inverter.

每一件好事都结束了，但是你的旧笔记本电脑不一定是真的。是的，你的笔记本电脑已经死了，但你仍然可以挽救一些重要组成部分，液晶屏是一个很好的开始。并非我们所有人都对工具很好，但在几个步骤中成功取出旧笔记本电脑屏幕不应该过于巨大挑战。所有你所要做的就是通过松开螺钉并取出捕捉来取出前挡板。什么是挡板？这个问题可能越过你的思想。挡板是屏幕前方的塑料框架;我们将尝试通过定义这种奇怪的术语来保持这种良好和简单。挡板关闭后，您需要通过拧下支架螺钉（屏幕旁边的螺钉）仔细取下笔记本电脑屏幕，并取下视频连接器电缆和逆变器。

The inverter converts direct current coming from the motherboard into alternating current and then passes it onto the LCD screen. It is located at the base of the screen and has cables of its own, take them off one after the other, and then your LCD screen is free to be taken out. There is a bold “DO NOT TOUCH” warning on the seal protecting the screen’s circuit board, and you have to obey it STRICTLY.

逆变器将来自主板的直流转换为交流电，然后将其传递到LCD屏幕上。它位于屏幕的底部，并拥有自己的电缆，另一个接一个，然后您的LCD屏幕可以自由地取出。在保护屏幕的电路板上有一个大胆的“请勿触摸”警告，您必须严格遵守它。

The steps involved in taking out your laptop screens varies for different products, while most are as easy as described above, others might require you to take open up your laptop completely.If you are finding this difficult, I advise you DDIY (don’t do it yourself ) and get a technician to help take your screen out, so you don’t end up damaging the screen, motherboard or other components that could be useful for other projects.

取出笔记本电脑屏幕所涉及的步骤因不同的产品而变化，而大多数如上所述，其他可能需要您完全拆卸笔记本电脑。如果您发现这一困难，我建议您ddiy（不要自己这样做）并获得技术人员帮助拍摄屏幕，因此您不会最终损坏屏幕，主板或其他对其他项目有用的组件。

Now to the business end of this DIY project. I am assuming you now have your LCD screen. All you need now is an easy DIY guide to lead you through converting it to an external display unit for your computer, game consoles and other devices.

现在到这个DIY项目的业务结束。我假设你现在有你的液晶屏。您现在需要的只是一个简单的DIY指南，可以通过将其转换为计算机，游戏机和其他设备的外部显示单元。

The removed LCD cannot function as an external monitor alone. You have to get a matching controller/driver board. This device contains dials, switches, and meters and directs the signal from your computer or game console to the LCD screen. The controller must be compatible with your LCD screen so check your screen model number on the label behind it and search online for a matching controller board online. Before placing your order, ensure you contact the sellers, provide them your model number to help them get you the right controller board for your LCD screen. LCD controllers are all over Amazon, ebay and other online stores like aliexpress and DHgate. You can get one for as low as $16. Just in case it proves difficult to get a controller board for your LCD screen, I suggest you get a universal controller board though some of these come with just a VGA port with no room for HDMI cables.

删除的LCD不能单独使用作为外部监视器。您必须获得匹配的控制器/驱动板。该设备包含拨号，交换机和仪表，并将信号从计算机或游戏控制台指向LCD屏幕。控制器必须与液晶屏兼容，因此请在其背后的标签上查看屏幕型号，并在线搜索匹配的控制器板。在下订单之前，请确保您联系畅销书，为您提供型号，以帮助他们为液晶屏找到右侧控制器板。 LCD控制器遍布亚马逊，eBay和其他在线商店，如Aliexpress和Dhgate。您可以获得一个低至16美元。以防难以让控制板为液晶屏，我建议您获得一个通用控制板，但其中一些只有一个VGA端口，没有HDMI电缆的空间。

The package you will receive should contain the main controller board, a keyboard, and an inverter. The keyboard is a smaller board with five or more keys on it and serves as a remote control for your monitor. The keyboard enables you to adjust the monitor’s volume, brightness, contrast and a lot of other properties.

您将收到的包装应包含主控制器板，键盘和逆变器。键盘是一个较小的电路板，其上有五个或更多键，并用作显示器的遥控器。键盘使您可以调整显示器的卷，亮度，对比度和大量其他属性。

You will also get three cables, the LVDS cable which connects the controller board to the LCD screen, the keyboard cable and the inverter cable.

您还将获得三个电缆，将控制器板连接到LCD屏幕，键盘电缆和逆变器电缆的LVDS电缆。

This step should be brief and easy. I prefer to connect the smaller components before linking the whole unit to the LCD panel using the LVDS cable. As mentioned earlier, there are three cables in total, and it’s quite obvious where each one goes.

这一步骤应该是简短的简单。我更喜欢使用LVDS电缆将整个单元连接到LCD面板之前连接较小的组件。如前所述，总共有三根电缆，每一个都会相当明显。

First, connect the keyboard to the controller board using its cable. Next, connect the inverter to the controller panel and then connect the inverter to the screen’s backlit connector. Finally, link the controller board with the LCD panel using the LVDS cable. Remember NOT to touch the screen circuit while you connect this to the screen. Make sure the pins are correctly oriented; conventionally they should face down. The cable goes in easily and comes off easily as well. It is advisable to tape it in place so as not to strain the fragile screen circuit.

首先，使用其电缆将键盘连接到控制器板。接下来，将变频器连接到控制器面板，然后将变频器连接到屏幕的后照接器。最后，使用LVDS电缆将控制器板与LCD面板连接。请记住在将此连接到屏幕时不要触摸屏幕电路。确保引脚正确定向;传统上他们应该面朝下。电缆容易进入，也很容易出现。建议将其粘贴到位，以便不履行易碎的屏幕电路。

The appropriate adapter for this project is one with an output of 12 volts, 4 Amperes. This is very important. Most adapters would fit into the power port on your controller board so don’t be tempted to power on your monitor using just any adapter you lay your hands on; you would most probably end up ruining the controller board or LCD screen you are trying to salvage. The output voltage and current are always clearly indicated on the adapter’s label. Adapters do not cost much and are very easy to get. Some stores send free adapters with the controller panel.

该项目的适当适配器是输出12伏，4安培的适配器。这个非常重要。大多数适配器将适用于控制器板上的电源端口，因此不要仅使用您握住手的任何适配器对显示器电源;您最终可能会毁了您试图打捞的控制器板或液晶屏。随着适配器的标签始终清楚地显示输出电压和电流。适配器不会花费太多，很容易得到。有些商店用控制器面板发送免费适配器。

Don’t be in a hurry to put on your monitor double check your connections, once sure everything is in place, bring your monitor to life by switching on the power button.

不要急于放在您的监视器上仔细检查您的连接，一旦确定一切都已到位，请通过在电源按钮上切换您的显示器。

Monitors display signals coming from other components and your improvised monitor is no different. You will need different cables to connect your monitor to game consoles, PC, and other devices. These include HDMI, VGA, and DVI cables.

监视来自其他组件的显示信号，即可欣赏的显示器没有任何不同。您需要不同的电缆将显示器连接到游戏机，PC和其他设备。这些包括HDMI，VGA和DVI电缆。

There is also an audio out port on the controller board to connect your new monitor to audio output devices such as speakers and amplifiers.

控制器板上还有一个音频输出端口，将新显示器连接到音频输出设备，如扬声器和放大器。

Once your device is connected, the pictures should come up on your monitor if you have followed the preceding steps correctly and your LCD screen is without faults.

连接设备后，如果正确的步骤正确，图像应显示在显示器上，并且您的LCD屏幕没有错误。

This project is particularly awesome because not only does it drives you to tap into your creative side, it equally serves as a sort of recycling or repurposing of damaged laptops.

这个项目特别令人敬畏，因为它不仅驱使你挖掘你的创意方面，它同样用作一种回收或重新批准损坏的笔记本电脑。
