Microsoft Offers Cyberpunk 2077 Refunds For All Digital Sales, But It's Not Pulling the Game
微软为所有数字销售提供赛博朋克2077退款，但并没有成功

2020-12-19 06:54:17

Microsoft says it’s now offering Cyberpunk 2077 refunds to anyone who has purchased the game via its Microsoft Store. While the company has been offering refunds to some customers already, Microsoft is now expanding this to cover anyone who has purchased the game digitally.

微软表示，它现在向通过微软商店购买游戏的任何人提供“赛博朋克2077”退款。虽然该公司已经在向某些客户提供退款，但是Microsoft现在正在扩大退款范围，以涵盖以数字方式购买该游戏的任何人。

“We know the developers at CD Projekt Red have worked hard to ship Cyberpunk in extremely challenging circumstances,” explains a Microsoft spokesperson in a statement to The Verge. “However, we also realize that some players have been unhappy with the current experience on older consoles. To date, we have granted refunds to the vast majority of customers who have requested one. To ensure that every player is able to get the experience they expect on Xbox, we will be expanding our existing refund policy to offer full refunds to anyone who purchased Cyberpunk 2077 digitally from the Microsoft Store, until further notice.”

微软发言人在向The Verge发表的一份声明中解释说：“我们知道CD Projekt Red的开发人员在极具挑战性的情况下努力工作以运送Cyber​​punk。” “不过，我们还意识到，有些玩家对旧游戏机上的当前体验不满意。迄今为止，我们已向要求退款的绝大多数客户提供退款。为确保每个玩家都能在Xbox上获得他们期望的体验，我们将扩展现有的退款政策，向从Microsoft Store以数字方式购买Cyber​​punk 2077的任何人提供全额退款，直至另行通知。”

The refund policy expansion follows days of complaints around bugs, crashes, and the way the game runs on base PS4 and Xbox One consoles. Sony also moved to refund unhappy Cyberpunk 2077 players this week and even removed the game from the PlayStation Store. Microsoft isn’t going as far as Sony to fully remove the game, as the title is still listed and available on the Microsoft Store.

退款政策的扩大是在几天来关于错误，崩溃以及游戏在基本PS4和Xbox One控制台上运行方式的投诉之后。索尼本周还向不满意的《赛博朋克2077》玩家退款，甚至从PlayStation商店中删除了该游戏。由于标题仍在Microsoft Store上列出并且可以在Microsoft Store上找到，因此Microsoft不会像Sony那样将其完全删除。

Sony and Microsoft’s moves to refund players come just days after Cyberpunk 2077 developer CD Projekt Red said people unsatisfied with their purchase on the PS4 or Xbox One should request a refund. CD Projekt Red has also committed to releasing patches to improve the game, with one due in the coming days and more significant patches planned for January and February.

索尼和微软向玩家退款的举动是在《赛博朋克2077》开发商CD Projekt Red说几天后，人们对在PS4或Xbox One上购买游戏不满意的人应要求退款。 CD Projekt Red也致力于发布补丁来改进游戏，未来几天将发布一个补丁，而计划在一月和二月发布更多重要补丁。
