Penkesu Computer – A Homebrew Retro-style Laptop
Penkesu电脑——自制复古风格笔记本电脑

2022-02-15 04:42:05

Penkēsu (Japanese: ペンケース) is a retro-style laptop powered by a Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W, a 7.9 inch widescreen display (400 x 1280 resolution), and a 48-keys ortholinear mechanical keyboard.

Penkēsu（日语：ペンケース) 这是一款复古风格的笔记本电脑，由Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W、7.9英寸宽屏显示器（400 x 1280分辨率）和48键正交机械键盘供电。

The case of Penkesu Computer is designed around the display and keyboard to achieve a (relatively) compact physical dimensions.

Penkesu电脑的外壳围绕显示屏和键盘设计，以实现（相对）紧凑的物理尺寸。

Gameboy Advance SP hinges and Ribbon cable for HDMI are used to keep the hinge thin, yet holds the weight of the display so it won’t tip over.Electronics are kept to be minimal (3 internal components) and most of the parts are either 3D printable or off-the-shelf product.

Gameboy Advance SP铰链和HDMI带状电缆用于保持铰链薄，同时保持显示屏的重量，使其不会倾斜。电子产品保持最小（3个内部组件），大多数零件要么是3D打印的，要么是现成的产品。

Ever since the CutiePi tablet has been successfully funded and started shipping, I feel the need to work on a new project, something I don’t need to worry too much about commercial viability, and to remind myself why I started tinkering. A “rebound” project, so to speak.

自从CutiePi平板电脑获得成功资助并开始发货以来，我觉得有必要进行一个新项目，我不需要太担心商业可行性，也需要提醒自己为什么要开始修补。可以说是一个“反弹”项目。

And since there are no immediate plans on selling kits or making Penkesu Computer mass producible, I’d like to publish all the designs and plans so there’s enough information for anyone interested in making one.

由于目前还没有销售工具包或使Penkesu计算机可大规模生产的计划，我想公布所有的设计和计划，以便有足够的信息供任何有兴趣制作的人使用。

Display Adafruit DIY HDMI Cable Parts - Right Angle adapter, Mini HDMI adapter, and 20cm Ribbon Cable

显示Adafruit DIY HDMI电缆部件-直角适配器、迷你HDMI适配器和20厘米带状电缆

Glue two hinges to the chassis (my 3D printer is not accurate enough to print a fully functional hinge lock, so I simply glued them with 5 minute epoxy)

将两个铰链粘到机箱上（我的3D打印机不够精确，无法打印出功能齐全的铰链锁，所以我只是用5分钟的环氧树脂将它们粘在一起）

Add heat-set threaded inserts (M2x6) to the 4 corners of screen bezel, and 2 to the hinge cover

在屏幕挡板的4个角上添加热固螺纹嵌件（M2x6），在铰链盖上添加2个

Connect keyboard’s micro USB and display’s mini HDMI port to Pi Zero 2 W; inset micro SD card into Pi Zero 2 W

将键盘的micro USB和显示器的mini HDMI端口连接到Pi Zero 2 W；将micro SD卡插入Pi Zero 2 W
