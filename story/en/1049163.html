Francisco Partners is acquiring MyHeritage, sources say for $600M
Francisco Partners正在获取Myheritage，消息人士称以600亿美元

2021-02-25 02:55:02

A spokesperson for MyHeritage confirmed the deal to TechCrunch over the phone but not the price. Francisco Partners has not responded to our request for comment but we’ll update this post as we learn more.

MyHeritage的发言人通过电话向TechCrunch确认了交易，但没有确认价格。 Francisco Partners尚未回复我们的评论请求，但我们会在了解更多信息时更新此帖子。

From what we understand the deal will be announced officially very soon. Update: we now have received a press release, so I  guess that makes it “official.”

据我们了解，该交易将很快正式宣布。更新：我们现在收到了新闻稿，所以我认为这使其成为``官方的''。

The market for DNA tests and genealogy research is generally a very fragmented one, but within that MyHeritage has been an early player — it has been around since 2003 — and a prolific one at that.

DNA检测和家谱研究市场通常是一个非常零散的市场，但是MyHeritage一直是早期市场参与者（自2003年以来一直存在），并且市场上产值很高。

In addition to its 62 million active users of its site — which is available in 42 languages — it also has a database of some 13 billion historical records. Users have built and expanded on some 58 million family trees on its platform.

除了网站有6200万活跃用户（可用42种语言提供）外，它还拥有约130亿历史记录的数据库。用户已经在其平台上建立并扩展了约5800万棵家谱。

“When I founded the company from my home eighteen years ago, I had a clear vision that drove me, and continues to drive me today – to make family history discovery easier using technology and to unlock the fun in genealogy: the human pursuit that bonds people,” said Gilad Japhet, founder and CEO of MyHeritage, in a statement. “With the help of an excellent and dedicated team, years of hard work, and with constant technological innovation, we created new and exciting ways for people to learn about their origins. In Francisco Partners we see a true partner for our journey ahead, not only demonstrated by the trust they are placing in our company through this acquisition, but in their desire for us to remain true to our vision by continuing along our path and helping us do what we do best – putting our users first and giving them life-enriching, and sometimes life-changing, experiences. This move will enable us to reach new heights, invest more resources in creating greater value for our users and to reach a larger audience. We’re incredibly excited for this next chapter in our company’s evolution.”

“当我18年前在家中创立公司时，我有一个清晰的愿景驱使我，并一直驱动着我-使用技术使发现家族史变得更加容易，并释放家谱中的乐趣：与人联系的人类追求MyHeritage的创始人兼首席执行官Gilad Japhet在一份声明中说。 “在一支优秀而敬业的团队的帮助下，经过多年的努力，以及不断的技术创新，我们为人们创造了新颖而令人兴奋的方式来了解其起源。在Francisco Partners中，我们看到了一个真正的合作伙伴，这不仅体现在他们通过此次收购对公司的信任中，而且体现在他们希望我们通过继续前进并帮助我们做到这一点来忠于我们的愿景我们最擅长的事–将用户放在第一位，并为他们提供丰富的生活，有时甚至是改变生活的体验。此举将使我们能够达到新的高度，投资更多的资源来为用户创造更大的价值并吸引更多的受众。我们为公司发展的下一章感到异常兴奋。”

Indeed, while MyHeritage is already profitable, this deal represents a moment for the company (and its new owner) to double down on investing in what it does already and expanding its services to the next level. It has grown in part through some 11 acquisitions to date, so making more consolidating purchases might be part of that.

的确，尽管MyHeritage已实现盈利，但这项交易为公司（及其新所有者）提供了一个机会，将双倍的资金投入在已经做的事情上，并将服务扩展到一个新的水平。迄今为止，它已经通过大约11项收购获得了部分增长，因此进行更多合并购买可能是其中的一部分。

MyHeritage has had a colorful, not always positive, profile in the public eye. It emerged as a key player in solving one of the most elusive murder cases in decades, the hunt for the Golden State Killer.

MyHeritage在公众眼中的色彩并不总是正面的。它成为解决数十年来最难以捉摸的谋杀案之一的重要角色，即寻找“金州杀手”。

But is has also been the subject of a major data breach, compromising some 92 million accounts in 2018. Account details of some MyHeritage users were part of a huge trove of personal details — collated from a number of breaches of several sites — that were posted for sale online in 2019.

但是，这也一直是重大数据泄露的主题，在2018年使约9200万个帐户受损。一些MyHeritage用户的帐户详细信息是大量个人详细信息的一部分，这些个人详细信息是根据对多个站点的多次泄露整理而成的将于2019年在线销售。

To be completely clear on data, MyHeritage as a company is not in the business of selling or using data in any way. It said that it will soon be updating its privacy policy “to include the unequivocal prohibition for the company to license or sell genetic data to any third party. These updates will be highly unique amongst the larger genealogy and genetic DNA industry and are a testament to the commitment both MyHeritage and Francisco Partners share to privacy and consumers.”

要完全清楚地说明数据，MyHeritage作为一家公司，不以任何方式销售或使用数据。它说，它将很快更新其隐私政策“以明确禁止该公司向任何第三方许可或出售遗传数据。这些更新将在更大的族谱和基因DNA行业中非常独特，并证明MyHeritage和Francisco Partners对隐私和消费者的共同承诺。”

It also runs pro bono work including DNA Quest, for adoptees to find their biological families; and Tribal Quest, to help document the family histories and cultural heritage of remote tribes around the world. It has been running a Covid-19 testing lab in Israel as well to better track the spread of the coronavirus.

它还运行无偿工作，包括DNA Quest，供收养者找到他们的生物家族;和Tribal Quest，以帮助记录世界各地偏远部落的家庭历史和文化遗产。它也一直在以色列运行Covid-19测试实验室，以更好地跟踪冠状病毒的传播。

The deal is coming at a time when another major DNA player, 23andme , is going public way of a SPAC at a valuation of $3.5 billion.

这笔交易是在另一家主要的DNA公司23andme以35亿美元的估值公开向SPAC公开的时候进行的。

Although a significantly smaller sum, $600 million would be a really strong exit for MyHeritage, which says it has only raised $49 million in funding since being founded in 2003, with investors including Accel, Index and Bessemer.

尽管一笔很小的数目，但6亿美元对于MyHeritage来说确实是一个强大的退出机会，MyHeritage表示，自2003年成立以来，仅筹集了4,900万美元的资金，投资方包括Accel，Index和Bessemer。

Part of the deal will see some individual and institutional investors continuing to keep stakes in the company alongside Francisco Partners, MyHeritage said. They include HP Beteiligungs GmbH, Yuval Rakavy, Japhet, and Gigi Levy.

MyHeritage表示，部分交易将使一些个人和机构投资者继续与Francisco Partners保持股份。它们包括HP Beteiligungs GmbH，Yuval Rakavy，Japhet和Gigi Levy。

PE firm Francisco Partners has been a prolific acquirer and investor in the tech sector, and tapping a profitable company that has more obvious growth potential is a logical move for it.

私募股权公司Francisco Partners在科技领域一直是多产的收购者和投资者，因此，选择一家具有明显增长潜力的盈利公司是顺理成章的举动。

“By leveraging our operational expertise, market resources and strong industry networks, we believe Francisco Partners is uniquely positioned to help MyHeritage accelerate its vision for growth. We are deeply impressed by the incredible achievements and relentless determination of Gilad, a visionary leader in genealogy who has grown the company from a start-up to a profitable global market leader,” said Eran Gorev, Francisco Partners‘ President of Israel & Senior Operating Partner, in a statement. “We are looking forward to partnering with Gilad and the entire MyHeritage team to help drive market expansion for the company.” Gorev is joining the board along with Francisco’s Europe head Matt Spetzler with this deal.

“通过利用我们的业务专业知识，市场资源和强大的行业网络，我们认为Francisco Partners独特地定位，以帮助Myheritage加速其增长的愿景。 Francisco Partners的以色列＆amp总裁Eran Gorev表示，我们对吉拉德的令人难以置信的成就和无情的决心感到非常深刻高级经营合作伙伴，在一份声明中。 “我们期待着与Gilad和整个Myheritage团队合作，帮助推动公司的市场扩张。” Gorev正在加入董事会以及Francisco的欧洲头马特Spetzler。

“Francisco Partners shares MyHeritage’s vision for growth as well as its intense commitment to ensuring the privacy of its users. The users’ personal data is an extremely important priority and we will work together with MyHeritage to expand its already strong privacy framework going forward,” said Spetzler.

“Francisco Partners分享了Myheritage对增长的愿景，并致力于确保其用户隐私。用户的个人数据是一个极其重要的优先事项，我们将与Myheritage一起共同努力，扩大其已经强大的隐私框架，“Spetzler说。
