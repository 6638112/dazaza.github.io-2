Xbox Cloud Gaming was on track to launch for iPhones and iPads earlier, but Apple updated its App Store rules in September that impacted services like Xbox Gaming and Google Stadia. Apple's move forced the companies to use web browsers to redesign their services so that they could circumvent the App Store rules. Under the rules, Microsoft, Google and other companies with similar services would have had to offer each game as an individual download instead of offering a complete library the way Netflix does for movies.

Xbox Cloud Gaming早些时候正在追踪iPhone和iPad的轨道，但Apple于9月更新了其App Store规则，这些规则受影响Xbox游戏和Google Stadia等服务。 Apple＆＃39; s移动强迫公司使用Web浏览器重新设计他们的服务，以便他们可以规避App Store规则。根据规则，微软，谷歌和其他具有类似服务的公司将不得不将每个游戏提供作为个人下载，而不是Netflix为电影提供完整的库。

Xbox Cloud Gaming is sort of like Netflix for games. People who subscribe to Microsoft's $14.99/month Xbox Game Pass Ultimate plan can access more than 100 titles. The cloud gaming aspect lets you stream the games without having to download them, provided you have a fast enough internet connection. The streaming option is already available for Android phones.
