GameStop Jumps After Hours As Elon Musk Tweets Out Reddit Board That's Hyping Stock
几小时后GameStop跳下，因为Elon Musk在推特上发布了Reddit板

2021-01-28 22:45:52

Tesla CEO Elon Musk seemed to rally behind GameStop's epic surge, tweeting out a link to the Reddit board that's largely hyped the stock.

特斯拉（Tesla）首席执行官埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）似乎在GameStop史诗般的飙升背后反弹，在推特上发布了与Reddit董事会的链接，该链接在很大程度上炒作了该股。

Shares of GameStop were up more than 60% in after-hours trading following Musk's tweet, which linked to the "wallstreetbets" Reddit page that has more than 2 million subscribers.

在马斯克发布推文之后，盘后交易中，GameStop的股价上涨了60％以上，这条推文与“街头小巷”相关联。 Reddit页面拥有超过200万的订阅者。

在Musk发推文之后，GameStop的股价在周二的盘后交易中上涨了60％以上，这条推文与“ wallttreetbets”相关。 Reddit页面拥有超过200万的订阅者。特斯拉CEO发推文＆＃34; Gamestonk !!＆＃34;

The stock surged earlier in the day after Social Capital's Chamath Palihapitiya said in a tweet that he bought GameStop call options, betting the stock will go higher. The stock closed up 92.7% on Tuesday.

在Social Capital的Chamath Palihapitiya在一条推文中表示，他购买了GameStop看涨期权后，该股在当天早些时候飙升。该股周二收盘上涨92.7％。

Musk's call-outs to certain companies have influenced stock prices before. Earlier Tuesday, he tweeted about a hand-knit wool hat he bought for his dog on Etsy. That sent shares of the company up as much as 8% in premarket trading, though it ultimately closed down 2.1%.

马斯克对某些公司的要求以前曾影响过股价。周二早些时候，他在推特上发了一条推文，说他在埃特西（Etsy）为他的狗买了一个手工编织的羊毛帽子。这使得该公司股价在盘前交易中上涨了8％，尽管最终收盘下跌了2.1％。

The CEO has faced problems with the SEC for tweeting about Tesla's stock. In August 2018, he said he  wanted to take Tesla private at $420 per share and that he had secured the funding to do so. Musk and Tesla each had to pay the SEC a $20 million fine to settle the suit, and Musk has since  agreed to submit his public statements about Tesla's finances and other topics to vetting by its legal counsel. He infamously tweeted last year that Tesla's stock was "too high," sending shares down more than 10% immediately, though they more than rebounded within a week.

这位首席执行官因发布有关特斯拉股票的推文而面临美国证券交易委员会（SEC）的问题。 2018年8月，他说他想以每股420美元的价格将特斯拉私有化，并且他已经获得了这样做的资金。马斯克和特斯拉各要向美国证券交易委员会支付2000万美元的罚款才能和解该案，马斯克此后同意将其关于特斯拉财务状况和其他主题的公开声明提交法律顾问进行审查。他在去年臭名昭著的推特上说，特斯拉的股价太高了，尽管股价在一周内反弹超过10％，但股价立即下跌超过10％。
