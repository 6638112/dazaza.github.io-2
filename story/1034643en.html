SitRep：阿塞拜疆的无人机战争以类似死神的TB2扩大

2020-11-12 03:57:26

The renewed war between Azerbaijan and Armenia over the Nagoro-Karabakh region has captured the attention of military strategists worldwide, including the United States, because of the degree to which drones have changed the battlefield. While the wide-open, rugged terrain of the region has played a role, Turkish- and Israeli-built remotely piloted vehicles are dominating the battlefield, causing strategists to think a lot about land-battle tactics—and about the value of tanks in the 21st century.

由于无人机在很大程度上改变了战场，阿塞拜疆和亚美尼亚围绕纳戈罗-卡拉巴赫地区的新一轮战争吸引了包括美国在内的世界各地军事战略家的注意。虽然该地区广阔、崎岖的地形发挥了作用，但土耳其和以色列制造的遥控车辆在战场上占据主导地位，这让战略家们对陆战战术以及坦克在21世纪的价值进行了大量思考。

Azerbaijan has been using a number of weapons systems from both Turkey and Israel, including the Israeli Harop (seen in this Azerbaijani pop music video) and Orbiter-1k drones. Both are "loitering munitions"—i.e., drones that carry warheads and crash into their targets.

阿塞拜疆一直在使用土耳其和以色列的一些武器系统，包括以色列的Harop(在这段阿塞拜疆流行音乐视频中可以看到)和Orbiter-1k无人机。这两种武器都是游荡弹药--即携带弹头并撞向目标的无人机。

But the star of many of Azerbaijan's drone snuff films is Turkey's Bayraktar TB2 unmanned combat air vehicle, Turkey's counterpart to the General Dynamics Reaper used by the United States. The TB2 carries the UMTAS antitank missile—a Turkish equivalent to the Hellfire—as well as laser-guided missiles, rockets, and mini-smartbombs. And it can be used to designate targets for Turkish aircraft

但阿塞拜疆的许多无人机嗅探片中的明星是土耳其的Bayraktar TB2无人作战飞行器，土耳其与美国使用的通用动力收割机(General Dynamics Reaper)相提并论。TB2搭载了UMTAS反坦克导弹--土耳其版的地狱火导弹--以及激光制导导弹、火箭和迷你智能炸弹。它可以用来指定土耳其飞机的目标。

The TB2 has been deployed repeatedly in Libya and Syria, and it foiled Russian-backed groups' ambitions in both places. And Turkey has sold the TB2 to Qatar and Ukraine, as well as Azerbaijan. The drones have been the primary cause of heavy losses to the Armenian side, including nearly 70 tanks, 24 armored personnel carriers, 17 air defense systems, over 72 trucks, and a total of at least 50 artillery and multiple launch rocket systems, based on data tabulated by the threat intelligence consulting firm Treadstone 71.

TB2已多次部署在利比亚和叙利亚，挫败了俄罗斯支持的组织在这两个地方的野心。土耳其已经向卡塔尔、乌克兰以及阿塞拜疆出售了TB2。威胁情报咨询公司踏石71(Treadstone 71)列出的数据显示，无人机是亚美尼亚方面遭受重大损失的主要原因，包括近70辆坦克、24辆装甲运兵车、17个防空系统、超过72辆卡车，以及总共至少50门火炮和多管火箭发射系统。

The TB2 is the result of the US Congress banning sales of US combat drones to Turkey and of a bit of unanticipated technology transfer. Selçuk Bayraktar, founder and CTO of his namesake company, worked on drone communications technology while a grad student at Penn, MIT, and Georgia Tech, and he brought what he had learned back to Turkey to develop drones for the Turkish Security Forces. The TB2 also borrowed a recreational aircraft engine from Canadian aerospace firm Bombardier and lifted infrared and electro-optical sensors from Canada's L3 Wescam. It's that sensor that enables the TB2 to designate targets for both its own laser-guided weapons and bombs and rockets from other aircraft—including, possibly, the Turkish F-16 fighters that have been deployed to Azerbaijan since earlier this year.

TB2是美国国会禁止向土耳其出售美国战斗机的结果，也是一些意想不到的技术转让的结果。Selçuk Bayraktar是他的同名公司的创始人兼首席技术官，在宾夕法尼亚大学、麻省理工学院和佐治亚理工学院读研究生时，他从事无人机通信技术的研究，并将他所学的知识带回土耳其，为土耳其安全部队开发无人机。TB2还借用了加拿大航空航天公司庞巴迪(Bombardier)的休闲飞机发动机，并从加拿大的L3威斯康公司(L3 Wescam)取走了红外和光电传感器。正是这种传感器使TB2能够从其他飞机(可能包括自今年早些时候开始部署到阿塞拜疆的土耳其F-16战斗机)为自己的激光制导武器以及炸弹和火箭指定目标。

Canada has an arms embargo in place against Turkey but let the sale of the sensors go through—not knowing they would end up in Azerbaijan. After a TB2 was shot down by Armenian forces, it was discovered that these components had been passed along to Azerbaijan. Bombardier has stopped sales to Turkey, and the Canadian government has suspended permits for sale of Wescam sensors to Turkey as well.

加拿大对土耳其实施了武器禁运，但允许传感器的销售通过--加拿大并不知道这些传感器最终会运往阿塞拜疆。在一架TB2被亚美尼亚军队击落后，人们发现这些部件被转移到阿塞拜疆。庞巴迪已经停止了对土耳其的销售，加拿大政府也暂停了向土耳其销售Wescam传感器的许可。
