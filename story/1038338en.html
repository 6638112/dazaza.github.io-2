Uber exits self-driving car sector, sells business to Aurora
优步退出自动驾驶汽车领域，将业务出售给Aurora

2020-12-08 08:38:53

It's a fork in the road for Uber's self-driving car business. On Monday, startup Aurora announced it will acquire the ride-hailing company's Advanced Technologies Group, which has spent the past few years developing autonomous vehicle technology. The deal will bring Aurora's approximate valuation to $10 billion.

对于Uber的自动驾驶汽车业务来说，这是一个重要的分支。周一，初创公司Aurora宣布将收购这家乘车公司的先进技术集团，该集团过去几年一直在开发自动驾驶汽车技术。这笔交易将使Aurora的估值达到100亿美元左右。

"With the addition of ATG, Aurora will have an incredibly strong team and technology, a clear path to several markets, and the resources to deliver," Chris Urmson, Aurora CEO, said on the acquisition. "Simply put, Aurora will be the company best positioned to deliver the self-driving products necessary to make transportation and logistics safer, more accessible, and less expensive."

Aurora commended what ATG accomplished under Uber thus far and called out advances in hardware, software and safety. The company rolled out an in-depth safety case framework two years ago to embed safety in every mile a self-driving car prototype takes. The division wasn't without its controversies, however: An Uber self-driving car prototype struck and killed a pedestrian the same year.

Aurora赞扬了迄今为止ATG在Uber的领导下取得的成就，并呼吁在硬件，软件和安全性方面取得进步。该公司两年前推出了深入的安全案例框架，以将安全性嵌入自动驾驶汽车原型所需要的每一英里。但是，该部门并不是没有争议的：同年，Uber自动驾驶汽车原型车撞死了一名行人。

Although Uber exits the sector, it will still hold a minor link to its now-former division. The ride-hailing giant said it will invest $400 million in Aurora to partner with the company and bring autonomous technologies to life. Ideally, Aurora gains funding and a lot of brilliant minds, while Uber gets to benefit from the future technologies when they're ready without direct oversight. Aurora still plans to tackle commercial trucking with its technology first, but the Uber partnership may have the company primed to capitalize on ride hailing when the time is right.

尽管Uber退出了该部门，但它与现在的部门之间仍然只有很小的联系。这家网约车巨头表示将向Aurora投资4亿美元，与Aurora合作，将自动驾驶技术付诸实践。理想情况下，Aurora会获得资金和很多聪明的头脑，而Uber在没有直接监督的情况下就可以从未来的技术中受益。 Aurora仍计划首先利用其技术来解决商业卡车运输问题，但与Uber的合作可能会促使该公司在适当时机利用乘车欢呼。

Uber CEO Dara Khosrowshahi said he looks forward to working alongside Aurora in the new partnership. Khosrowshahi will join the self-driving company's board of directors.

优步首席执行官Dara Khosrowshahi表示，他期待与Aurora合作建立新的合作伙伴关系。霍斯罗萨西（Khosrowshahi）将加入自动驾驶公司董事会。
