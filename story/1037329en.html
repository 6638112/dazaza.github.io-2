FCC Chairman Ajit Pai Will Step Down on January 20
FCC主席Ajit Pai将于1月20日卸任

2020-12-01 00:44:31

FCC Chairman Ajit Pai has announced plans to depart the commission when President-elect Joe Biden is inaugurated on January 20th. Pai has served on the FCC since 2012.

FCC主席阿吉特排已经宣布计划离开的佣金时，总统当选人拜登是在1月20日宣誓就职。 Pai自2012年以来一直在FCC任职。

“It has been the honor of a lifetime to serve at the Federal Communications Commission, including as Chairman of the FCC over the past four years,” Pai said in a statement. “To be the first Asian-American to chair the FCC has been a particular privilege. As I often say: only in America.”

派在一份声明中说：“能够在联邦通信委员会任职，包括在过去四年中担任FCC主席，是一生以来的荣幸。” “成为第一位担任FCC主席的亚裔美国人是一项特殊特权。我经常说：仅在美国。”

Pai was appointed chairman in 2017 and served for the duration of the Trump administration, overseeing an unusually active period in federal telecom policy. He began his term with the controversial decision to roll back Title II classification, undoing the net neutrality rules put in place under President Obama.

Pai于2017年被任命为董事长，在特朗普政府任职期间，负责监督联邦电信政策异常活跃的时期。他的任期开始于一项颇有争议的决定，即回撤第二标题的分类，从而取消了奥巴马总统领导的净中立规则。

More recently, Pai oversaw the merger of T-Mobile and Sprint, which he called “a unique opportunity to speed up the deployment of 5G throughout the United States.” Pai also implemented new measures to fight robocalls and established a national suicide prevention hotline number. Pai’s tenure coincided with a significant shift in Republican telecom policy, with Republican commissioners like Brendan Carr advocating for a more aggressive FCC role to regulating social media platforms.

最近，Pai监督了T-Mobile和Sprint的合并，他称这是“在美国加快5G部署速度的独特机会。” Pai还采取了新的措施来对抗机器人电话，并建立了全国预防自杀热线电话。 Pai的任期恰好与共和党电信政策发生重大变化，Brendan Carr等共和党委员主张在监管社交媒体平台方面发挥更大的FCC作用。

In his statement, Pai placed particular emphasis on the procedural transparency measures he put in place. “For the first time ever, we’ve made public drafts of the proposals and orders slated for a vote three weeks before the agency’s monthly meetings,” Pai said, “making this the most transparent FCC in history.”

Pai在发言中特别强调了他采取的程序透明性措施。派恩说：“有史以来第一次，我们公开了提案和命令的初稿，该提案和命令定于该机构每月例会的三周前进行投票。这使它成为历史上最透明的FCC。”
