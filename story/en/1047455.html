Reddit raises $250M Series E led by Vy Capital at a $6B valuation, doubling in value since its last funding round in February 2019
Reddit筹集了由Vy Capital牵头的2.5亿美元E轮融资，估值为6B美元，自2019年2月上一轮融资以来价值翻了一番

2021-02-09 20:42:07

Reddit Inc. doubled its valuation to $6 billion in a new round of funding that comes as the social-media company has added users through the Covid-19 pandemic and more recently has gained attention for its role in the recent Wall Street trading frenzy.

Reddit Inc.在新一轮融资中将其估值翻了一番，达到60亿美元，原因是这家社交媒体公司通过Covid-19大流行增加了用户，最近又因其在最近华尔街交易狂潮中的作用而受到关注。

Reddit on Monday said it raised $250 million in a late-stage funding round led by venture-capital firm Vy Capital. Previously it was valued at $3 billion after its last funding round in February 2019, according to PitchBook, a provider of private-market data. Current investors in Reddit also include venture-capital firm Andreessen Horowitz and internet conglomerate Tencent Holdings

Reddit周一表示，在由风险投资公司Vy Capital领导的后期融资中筹集了2.5亿美元。根据私人市场数据提供商PitchBook的数据，在2019年2月的最后一轮融资之后，它的估值为30亿美元。 Reddit的当前投资者还包括风险投资公司Andreessen Horowitz和互联网巨头腾讯控股

“It’s a good market to fundraise,” Reddit Chief Executive Steve Huffman said in an interview. “Valuations are very high right now. It never hurts to raise money when there’s an opportunity to do so and Reddit had a strong year.” For example, advertising revenue for the company shot up 90% in the December-ended quarter from a year earlier, he said.

Reddit首席执行官史蒂夫·霍夫曼（Steve Huffman）在接受采访时说：“这是筹款的好市场。” “目前的估值很高。只要有机会，Reddit表现不错，就可以筹集资金。他说，例如，该公司的广告收入在去年12月底的一个季度猛增了90％。

The funding for Reddit comes just weeks after upstart audio-chat social network Clubhouse raised fresh capital. Clubhouse was valued at $1 billion last month after securing $100 million in a funding round led by Andreessen, according to PitchBook.

Reddit的资金来自新贵的音频聊天社交网络Clubhouse筹集了新资金的几周后。根据PitchBook的数据，Clubhouse在由安德森（Andreessen）牵头的一轮融资中获得了1亿美元后，上个月的估值为10亿美元。

San Francisco-based Reddit, founded in 2005, is known for its message boards on a multitude of topics, as well as its “ask me anything” digital town halls with celebrities, politicians and subject-matter experts. The company, which isn’t profitable, was sold to Condé Nast in 2006, and the magazine publisher’s parent, Advance Publications Inc., spun Reddit off in 2011 and remains a shareholder. The company has raised roughly $800 million since 2011.

总部位于旧金山的Reddit成立于2005年，以其涉及多个主题的留言板以及与名人，政客和主题专家组成的“问我什么”数字市政厅而闻名。该公司没有盈利，于2006年被卖给了CondéNast，该杂志出版商的母公司Advance Publications Inc.于2011年从Reddit分离出来，仍然是股东。自2011年以来，该公司已筹集了大约8亿美元。

Mr. Huffman said Reddit plans to use the new funding to invest in areas such as video, advertising and consumer products, as well as to expand into international markets. Late last year Reddit bought video-sharing app Dubsmash to expand its presence in user-created video, one of the hottest corners of the internet, for an undisclosed sum. “Our strategy isn’t materially changing,” Mr. Huffman said.

霍夫曼先生说，Reddit计划使用这笔新资金来投资视频，广告和消费产品等领域，并扩展到国际市场。去年下半年，Reddit购买了视频共享应用程序Dubsmash，以未公开的价格扩大其在用户创建的视频（互联网最热门的角落之一）中的存在。霍夫曼说：“我们的战略并未发生重大变化。”

Reddit is also planning to double its employee head count this year to around 1,400, Mr. Huffman said, noting that one reason the company raised new funding was to help it attract talent. Reddit recently hired a handful of executives who will start in the coming months, he added, declining to specify their titles or name them.

霍夫曼说，Reddit还计划今年将其员工总数增加一倍，达到1400名左右，并指出该公司筹集新资金的原因之一是帮助其吸引人才。他补充说，Reddit最近雇用了几位高管，他们将在未来几个月内开始任职，但拒绝指定他们的头衔或命名。

The financing deal comes after Reddit’s WallStreetBets community gained attention as some members successfully egged on people to buy shares of heavily shorted stocks such as GameStop U.S. regulators are now probing whether the ensuing market upheaval resulted in securities-law violations.

这笔融资交易是在Reddit的WallStreetBets社区获得关注之后，一些成员成功地怂恿人们购买严重做空的股票，例如GameStop美国监管机构现在正在调查随之而来的市场动荡是否导致违反证券法。

The incident has helped bring Reddit millions of new users as well as new advertisers, which provide the company’s main source of revenue, Mr. Huffman recently told The Wall Street Journal.

霍夫曼最近告诉《华尔街日报》，此事件帮助Reddit吸引了数百万新用户和新广告商，这些用户和新广告商是公司的主要收入来源。

Reddit lets users rate all posts and comments on the platform and relies on tens of thousands of its own users to voluntarily to police speech. By contrast, larger social-media platforms such as Facebook and Twitter rely mainly on algorithms and paid help to do that kind of work.

Reddit允许用户对平台上的所有帖子和评论进行评分，并依靠数以万计的自己的用户自愿进行警察讲话。相比之下，较大的社交媒体平台（例如Facebook和Twitter）主要依靠算法和有偿帮助来进行此类工作。

The most recent user numbers from Reddit put the daily average at 52 million as of October, which was up 44% from the same month a year earlier.

截至10月，Reddit的最新用户数为5200万，比去年同期增长了44％。

Facebook said it had roughly 1.85 billion daily users in the December quarter, while Twitter most recently said it averaged 187 million daily users in the three months ended in September. Snap the parent of ephemeral-messaging platform Snapchat, ended the December quarter with 265 million daily active users.

Facebook表示，截至12月季度，其日均用户约为18.5亿，而Twitter最新称，截至9月的三个月中，日均用户为1.87亿。 Snap是短暂消息平台Snapchat的母公司，截至12月季度，每日活跃用户为2.65亿。
