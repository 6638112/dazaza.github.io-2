Apple hardware chief Dan Riccio stepped down to focus on AR/VR
苹果硬件主管丹·里乔（Dan Riccio）辞职，专注于AR / VR

2021-02-09 21:12:16

A couple of weeks ago, Apple announced that longtime hardware engineering chief Dan Riccio will step down from his role to focus entirely on a "new project" within the company. According to yet another report at Bloomberg based on sources with knowledge of Apple's plans today, the project Riccio has focused his energies on is Apple's upcoming augmented reality, virtual reality, or mixed reality headset.

几周前，苹果公司宣布，长期的硬件工程负责人丹·里乔（Dan Riccio）将卸任，将精力完全集中在一个“新项目”上。在公司内部。根据彭博社（Bloomberg）的另一份报告，该消息基于对苹果计划的了解，今天Riccio致力于开发的项目是苹果公司即将推出的增强现实，虚拟现实或混合现实耳机。

Development of an AR headset at Apple seems to have hit a snag or two under current project lead Mike Rockwell, though the report does not outline exactly which obstacles have emerged. While Rockwell will remain in charge of day-to-day work on the project, Riccio will have "ultimate oversight" over the company's AR/VR efforts, which are said to involve "well over a thousand engineers."

在目前的项目负责人Mike Rockwell的领导下，苹果公司开发AR头显似乎遇到了一两个障碍，尽管该报告并未准确概述出现了哪些障碍。罗克韦尔（Rockwell）将继续负责该项目的日常工作，而里乔（Riccio）将接受“最终监督”。涉及公司的AR / VR工作，据说涉及一千多名工程师。

Riccio had already handed top-level management of most current consumer products like the iPhone to an executive named John Ternus. Apple announced Ternus will replace Riccio as the head of hardware engineering overall. The latest news indicates that he has also handed development of new camera and display technology to Johny Srouji, the executive who spearheaded the design and engineering of Apple Silicon. Apple is reportedly working on doing the same thing with displays that it did with processors: developing its own high-quality components that would replace those that Apple purchases from other suppliers like LG or Samsung.

里乔（Riccio）已经将大多数当前消费产品（例如iPhone）的高层管理交给了约翰·特努斯（John Ternus）主管。苹果宣布Ternus将取代Riccio担任整体硬件工程负责人。最新消息表明，他还将新相机和显示技术的开发交给了负责领导Apple Silicon设计和工程的高管Johny Srouji。据报道，苹果正在致力于与处理器的显示器做同样的事情：开发自己的高质量组件，以取代苹果从LG或三星等其他供应商那里购买的组件。

But despite these other moving parts, AR is a top priority in terms of new product development. Apple CEO Tim Cook has previously said he believes AR has the potential to be a watershed moment akin to the iPhone for Apple, and an expected rivalry with Facebook in that space has been an undercurrent of the recent feud over privacy between the two tech giants.

但是，尽管有这些其他活动部分，但在新产品开发方面，AR仍然是重中之重。苹果首席执行官蒂姆·库克（Tim Cook）此前曾表示，他认为AR可能会成为类似于苹果iPhone的分水岭，而在这一领域与Facebook的预期竞争一直是两家科技巨头之间最近争执隐私的暗流。

Last week, The Information cited several sources close to Apple to claim that Apple is working on an ultrahigh-end, 8K VR headset that could be $3,000. To date, most of Apple's publicly visible work on XR has been on AR, not VR. Cupertino has been steadily building out ARKit, a developer API that enables creation of AR experiences using the cameras and sensors on iPhones and iPads.

上周，《信息报》援引了几位接近苹果的消息人士称，苹果正在开发一款可能售价3,000美元的超高端8K VR头盔。迄今为止，Apple在XR上公开可见的大多数作品都是在AR上，而不是VR。 Cupertino一直在稳步开发ARKit，这是一个开发人员API，可使用iPhone和iPad上的摄像头和传感器创建AR体验。

Apple has occasionally talked about VR as well. While Cook says he believes AR is the more transformative technology, there have been developer talks and documents about supporting VR in the company's Metal graphics API, and the company worked with Valve to add support for SteamVR headsets to Macs around the launch of the iMac Pro.

苹果偶尔也会谈论VR。尽管Cook表示他相信AR是更具变革性的技术，但在公司的Metal graphics API中已有关于支持VR的开发人员讨论和文件，并且该公司与Valve合作，在发布时为Mac添加了对SteamVR头戴式耳机的支持。 iMac Pro的

It's too early to say what the first headset from Apple could be like. While The Information called it a VR headset, the headset also supports high-quality recording of the surrounding world and showing it to the user, so AR is likely still a part of the plan. It's possible that Apple will lean on some evolution of its SteamVR support as it builds out tools for developers to make VR experiences as well, and it's also possible that this high-priced headset is intended primarily for use by developers and professionals. In any case, Riccio is now in charge of getting future XR projects out of the lab and into users' homes. The report on the VR headset said we might see that first model sometime in 2022.

现在说苹果的第一款耳机会是什么样子还为时过早。尽管The Information称其为VR头戴式耳机，但该头戴式耳机还支持对周围世界进行高质量录制并将其展示给用户，因此AR可能仍是计划的一部分。苹果有可能会依靠对SteamVR支持的一些改进，因为它会为开发人员构建工具以提供虚拟现实体验，而且这款高价位头盔也有可能主要用于使用由开发人员和专业人士提供。无论如何，Riccio现在都负责将未来的XR项目从实验室移交给用户。家园。关于VR头戴设备的报告称，我们可能会在2022年的某个时候看到第一个模型。
