App privacy labels now live on the App Store
App隐私标签现已发布在App Store中

2020-12-15 22:07:07

The App Store now helps users better understand an app’s privacy practices before they download the app on any Apple platform. On each app’s product page, users can learn about some of the data types an app may collect, and whether that data is linked to them or used to track them. As a reminder, your app’s privacy information is required to submit new apps and app updates to the App Store, and some data is optional to disclose if it meets specific criteria, such as certain data from health research apps and regulated financial services. You may update your answers at any time without resubmitting your app or going through App Review.Learn more about the details you’ll need to provideLearn how to provide app privacy information in App Store Connect

App Store现在可帮助用户在任何Apple平台上下载应用之前更好地了解应用的隐私惯例。 在每个应用程序的产品页面上，用户可以了解应用程序可能收集的某些数据类型，以及该数据是链接到它们还是用于跟踪它们。 提醒您，您的应用程序的隐私信息是向App Store提交新应用程序和应用程序更新所必需的，如果满足特定条件，则某些数据是可选的以进行披露，例如来自健康研究应用程序和受监管金融服务的某些数据。 您可以随时更新答案，而无需重新提交您的应用程序或不通过应用程序审阅。了解有关您需要提供的详细信息的更多信息了解如何在App Store的Connect中提供应用程序隐私信息
