Spotify第三季度总收入为19.7亿欧元，同比增长14%，ARPU为4.19欧元，同比下降10%，MAU为3.2亿欧元，同比增长29%，付费用户1.44亿，同比增长27%

2020-10-29 19:29:33

Spotify Technology SPOT -3.93% moved past a slump that hit early in the pandemic, as customers collectively spent more time listening to the service than before Covid-19 shutdowns.

Spotify科技Spot-3.93%走出了疫情爆发之初的低迷时期，因为消费者花在聆听这项服务上的时间比新冠肺炎关闭之前更多。

The music-streaming giant added more users than expected in the most recent quarter and said consumption habits have normalized, with in-car listening hours—which had fallen with time spent commuting—exceeding their pre-pandemic peak. Listening on home devices, which exploded during lockdowns, also remained high.

这家音乐流媒体巨头在最近一个季度增加了比预期更多的用户，并表示消费习惯已经正常化，车内收听时间-随着通勤时间的减少-超过了大流行前的峰值。在封锁期间爆炸的家庭设备监听率也保持在较高水平。

At the close of the quarter ended Sept. 30, Spotify had 320 million monthly active users, higher than its guidance. Paying subscribers, its most lucrative type of customer, grew to 144 million, at the high end of the company’s forecast.

在截至9月1日的季度末。30，Spotify的月度活跃用户为3.2亿，高于其指引。付费用户是其最有利可图的客户类型，增长到1.44亿，处于该公司预测的高端。

During the quarter, average revenue per user for the subscription business slipped 10% to 4.19 euros, equivalent to $4.92, as Spotify brought in new subscribers via discounted plans and charged lower prices in new markets such as India and Russia. The company said it had raised the price of its family plan in seven markets this month.

在本季度，订阅业务的每位用户平均收入下滑10%，至4.19欧元，相当于4.92美元，因为Spotify通过折扣计划引入了新的订户，并在印度和俄罗斯等新市场收取了更低的价格。该公司表示，本月已在七个市场上调了家庭计划的价格。

Revenue from subscriptions was up 15% from a year earlier in the quarter, to €1.79 billion, equivalent to $2.1 billion. Advertising revenue returned to growth after sliding in the first half of the year, rising 9% to €185 million. Though advertising accounts for 10% or less of Spotify’s overall revenue, it has become a growth area—on a double-digit rise before the pandemic—as the company has expanded its podcast business, which led the rebound in the recent quarter.

该季度订阅收入较上年同期增长15%，至17.9亿欧元，相当于21亿美元。广告收入在上半年下滑后恢复增长，增长9%，至1.85亿欧元。虽然广告占Spotify总收入的10%或更少，但随着该公司扩大其播客业务，这一领域已经成为一个增长领域-在疫情爆发前，该领域的增幅达到了两位数-该业务在最近一个季度引领了Spotify的反弹。

The company said it now has 1.9 million podcasts available on its service, and during the period 22% of its monthly active users listened to one, up from 21% in the previous quarter. “The Michelle Obama Podcast,” a Spotify exclusive launched in July, was the most-listened to podcast through August. “The Joe Rogan Experience,” which arrived on Spotify in September, is now the No. 1 show in all of the service’s English-language markets, the company said.

该公司表示，其服务目前有190万个播客可用，在此期间，22%的月度活跃用户收听了播客，高于上一季度的21%。“米歇尔·奥巴马播客”(The Michelle Obama Podcast)是Spotify在7月份推出的独家节目，是整个8月份收视率最高的播客。Spotify表示，9月份在Spotify上线的“乔·罗根体验”(The Joe Rogan Experience)现在是该服务所有英语市场的头号节目。

Spotify swung to a loss of €101 million, or 58 European cents a share, in the third quarter, from a profit of €241 million, or 36 European cents a share, a year earlier. While the company has periodically reported a quarterly profit, executives have said it will continue to give priority to growth—attracting new subscribers and investing in podcasts.

Spotify第三季度亏损1.01亿欧元，合每股亏损58欧分，而去年同期该公司实现利润2.41亿欧元，合每股亏损36欧分。虽然该公司定期公布季度利润，但高管们表示，它将继续优先考虑增长-吸引新订户和投资播客。

Free cash flow, a measure of the cash a company generates from operations—viewed by many investors as a proxy for performance—was €103 million, more than double the €48 million a year earlier.

自由现金流是衡量公司运营产生的现金的指标-被许多投资者视为业绩的代表-为1.03亿欧元，是一年前4800万欧元的两倍多。

For the fourth quarter, the company forecast growth in monthly active users to between 340 million and 345 million, and in premium subscribers to between 150 million and 154 million. It guided for revenue of €2 billion to €2.2 billion.

该公司预计，第四季度月度活跃用户将增长至3.4亿至3.45亿，高端用户将增长至1.5亿至1.54亿。该公司的营收指引为20亿至22亿欧元。
