Toshiba To Double Power Chip Production With New Plant in Japan
东芝将在日本设立新工厂，将功率芯片产量提高一倍

2022-02-15 05:56:28

Toshiba's new fabrication facility will be built on the grounds of a chipmaking subsidiary in Ishikawa Prefecture. (Image courtesy of Toshiba Electronic Devices & Storage) TOKYO -- Toshiba will spend roughly 100 billion yen ($873 million) to build a power semiconductor fabrication facility in Japan, with production expected to begin by March 2025, Nikkei has learned.

东芝'；公司的新制造工厂将建在石川县一家芯片制造子公司的土地上。（图片由东芝电子设备与存储公司提供）东京——据日经新闻报道，东芝将在日本投资约1000亿日元（8.73亿美元）建造一座电力半导体制造厂，预计将于2025年3月开始生产。
