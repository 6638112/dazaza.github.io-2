Carvana acquires Adesa US auction business for $2.2B to jump-start used car sales
Carvana以22亿美元收购Adesa美国拍卖业务，以启动二手车销售

2022-02-25 12:26:20

The acquisition announcement, which was made alongside a fourth-quarter earnings report, marks a transition for the pure online business into a more traditional physical car dealer.

此次收购公告是在发布第四季度财报的同时发布的，标志着纯在线业务向更传统的实体汽车经销商的转变。

Today, Carvana customers can use the company’s mobile or web app to shop, buy and finance their vehicle purchase. Those vehicles can either be picked up at one of its 30 multi-story car vending machines or delivered directly to a customer’s home. Carvana also operates 15 inspection and reconditioning centers where vehicles are evaluated and spruced up before sale. Customers can also sell their vehicles to Carvana.

如今，Carvana的客户可以使用该公司的移动或网络应用来购物、购买和融资购买汽车。这些车辆可以在其30台多层自动售货机中的一台取货，也可以直接送到客户家中。Carvana还运营着15个检查和翻新中心，在销售前对车辆进行评估和美化。客户还可以将车辆出售给Carvana。

The additional revenue and physical footprint that Adesa U.S. offers appear to be far too appetizing and too big of an opportunity for Carvana to ignore. And it comes at an opportune time.

Adesa U.S.提供的额外收入和实体足迹似乎太诱人，对Carvana来说是一个不容忽视的巨大机会。它来得正是时候。

Carvana sold 113,016 vehicles and generated $3.75 billion in revenue in the fourth quarter, a 57% year-over-year sales growth. But that YoY figure masks a tapering of growth towards the end of 2021. In the third quarter, Carvana sold 111,949 retail units on $3.5 billion of revenue.

Carvana第四季度销售了113016辆汽车，创造了37.5亿美元的收入，同比增长57%。但这个数字掩盖了2021年底的增长。第三季度，Carvana以35亿美元的收入售出了111949个零售单元。

Carvana has yet to reach GAAP profitability. Its losses actually widened year-over-year  $182 million in the fourth quarter from from $154 million in the same period last year. However, its total losses for the year narrowed considerably. The company reported net losses of $287 million in 2021, an improvement from $462 million the previous year.

Carvana尚未达到公认会计原则的盈利能力。该公司第四季度的亏损实际上从去年同期的1.54亿美元扩大到了去年同期的1.82亿美元。然而，该公司今年的总亏损大幅收窄。该公司2021年度净亏损2亿8700万美元，较上年同期的4亿6200万美元有所改善。

Adesa has 56 physical sites, which Carvana will also be able to use to inspect and recondition the vehicles its sells online. Carvana will continue to operate Adesa U.S.’s physical auctions while simultaneously developing the sites to include Carvana’s standard retail inspection, reconditioning and logistics capabilities, the company said in its letter to shareholders.

Adesa拥有56个实体网站，Carvana还可以使用这些网站检查和修复其在线销售的车辆。Carvana在致股东的信中表示，Carvana将继续运营Adesa U.S.的实体拍卖，同时开发这些网站，包括Carvana的标准零售检查、翻新和物流能力。

Carvana said Adesa U.S. reconditioning operations could help expand its production capacity from 2 million units to over 3 million units annually.

Carvana表示，Adesa美国的修复业务有助于将其年产能从200万台扩大到300多万台。

The network of 56 sites coupled with Carvana’s existing infrastructure will put 78% of the U.S. population within 100 miles of inspection and reconditioning centers.

由56个地点组成的网络，加上Carvana现有的基础设施，将使78%的美国人口在距离检查和修复中心100英里的范围内。

Carvana also sees an opportunity to increase its auction capabilities and kickstart or deepen its “relationships with many large and important players in the automotive industry,” the company said in its shareholder letter.

Carvana在其股东信中表示，该公司还认为有机会提高其拍卖能力，启动或深化其“与汽车行业许多大型重要参与者的关系”。

Then there’s the revenue possibilities, an important factor for a company that saw sky-high used car sales spurred by the pandemic come back down to earth. Adesa U.S.’s business facilitated more than one million transactions through those sites, bringing in more than $800 million in revenue in 2021.

然后是收入的可能性，这是一家公司的重要因素，该公司看到了由疫情刺激的天价二手车销售回归现实。ADSSA公司的业务促进了超过一百万个交易通过这些网站，带来超过8亿美元的收入在2021。

Of course, this potential reward comes with the risk that Carvana will see its operational expenses grow past its profit potential.

当然，这种潜在的回报伴随着Carvana将看到其运营费用增长超过其利润潜力的风险。

Carvana is using a portion of the $3.275 billion in financing it received from JPMorgan Chase Bank N.A. and Citi to fund the purchase. It will use the remaining $1 billion for improvements across Adesa U.S.’s 56 sites through a committed debt financing.

Carvana从摩根大通银行（JPMorgan Chase Bank N.a.）和花旗银行（Citi）获得的32.75亿美元融资中，有一部分用于收购。它将通过承诺的债务融资，将剩余的10亿美元用于改善Adesa U.S.的56个场地。

Adesa U.S.’s wholesale auction business will continue to operate under its existing brand name. John Hammer, president of Adesa U.S., along with other senior-level executives, will move over to Carvana once the deal closes.

Adesa U.S.的批发拍卖业务将继续以其现有品牌运营。一旦交易完成，阿德萨美国公司总裁约翰·哈默（John Hammer）和其他高级管理人员将前往卡瓦纳。
