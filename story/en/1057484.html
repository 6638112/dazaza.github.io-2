I asked her about her Internet options, and she said since her husband likes gaming, and she is on a lot of online video calls through the day (she works remotely for Reputation), costly satellite plans like HughesNet were not an option, due to their very high latency (since the satellites were in a very high geostationary orbit).

我问她关于她的互联网选项，她说，自丈夫喜欢游戏以来，她在一天中的大量在线视频电话（她远程出于声誉，以声誉为声誉而工作），因为Hughesnet等昂贵的卫星计划不是一种选择，因此他们非常高的延迟（因为卫星处于一个非常高的地球静止轨道）。

SpaceX aims to take away the stigmas associated with satellite Internet by launching thousands of satellites into a much-closer (and thus lower-latency) Low Earth Orbit. There are some challenges and potential pitfalls associated with the building of an entire constellation of satellites so close to earth, and SpaceX is supposedly working with the space and astonomy communities to try to find solutions that prevent risk (e.g. Kessler Syndrome) and keep the skies clear for astonomy... and we'll see where that ends up in a few years' time.

Spacex旨在通过将数千颗卫星发射到更近的（并且因此下延迟）低地球轨道上，从卫星互联网上带走与卫星互联网相关联的耻辱。与整个卫星星座的建筑有一些挑战和潜在的陷阱，如此接近地球，并且SpaceX据说是与空间和术语社区合作，试图找到防止风险（例如Kessler综合症）的解决方案并保持天空清晰的术语......和我们＆＃39; ll看到几年后最终的位置＆＃39;时间。

At least the cables and PoE injector/switch provided feel rugged and better put-together. And I should mention the router runs OpenWRT, though it exposes precious few options to end-users.

After you plug in the gear, Dishy points straight up (turning into some sort of retro-futuristic side table... just don't put your drink on it!), then after finding a satellite, it aligns itself to a slight Northern inclination, so it can get the best signal. Inside Dishy is a flat PCB with an array of beam-forming antennae and a network SoC that controls everything about the connection. It's powered through PoE++ (using around 100W of power continuously), and has two motors inside to control tilt+rotation.

插入齿轮后，碟形直接向上（转变为某种复古未来派桌面......只是不要把你的饮料放在它上面！），然后在找到卫星之后，它自身对齐轻微的北方倾向，所以它可以获得最好的信号。内部碟形是一个扁平的PCB，带有一系列光束形成天线和网络SOC，控制有关连接的一切。它通过PoE ++（连续使用大约100W的电源）和＃39;＆＃39;持续大约100W），并有两个电动机内部控制倾斜+旋转。
