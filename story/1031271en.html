煤炭大亨罗伯特·默里去世了

2020-10-27 00:27:08

(Reuters) - U.S. coal baron Robert Murray, an ally of President Donald Trump and a leading industry voice who called global warming a hoax, died on Sunday at age 80, U.S. media reported.

(路透社)-据美国媒体报道，美国煤炭大亨罗伯特·默里(Robert Murray)周日去世，享年80岁。默里是唐纳德·特朗普(Donald Trump)总统的盟友，也是称全球变暖为骗局的行业主要声音。

Murray retired on Oct. 19 after 63 years in the coal business, shortly after applying for federal benefits to treat his black lung disease, a respiratory illness caused by prolonged exposure to coal dust, according to a report by West Virginia public radio.

据西弗吉尼亚州公共广播电台报道，穆雷在煤炭行业工作了63年后，于10月19日退休，此前不久，他申请了联邦福利，以治疗他的黑肺病，这是一种因长期暴露在煤尘中而引起的呼吸系统疾病。

A colorful and outspoken leader of the Ohio company he founded, Murray Energy, Murray blamed environmental regulation for the demise of the coal industry and opposed more stringent coal dust regulations for years.

默里是他创建的俄亥俄州公司Murray Energy的一位五颜六色、直言不讳的领导人，他将煤炭行业的灭亡归咎于环境监管，并反对多年来更严格的煤尘监管。

He saw an ally in Trump, and pressed his administration to roll back a slew of former President Barack Obama-era rules.

他在特朗普身上看到了盟友，并向他的政府施压，要求其撤销一系列前总统巴拉克·奥巴马(Barack Obama)时代的规定。

While Trump’s administration acted on many rollbacks on Murray’s wishlist, Murray’s company, once the largest privately-held coal company in the country, went bankrupt last year as demand for coal from power utilities slumped.

虽然特朗普政府对默里的愿望清单采取了多次倒退行动，但穆雷的公司，曾经是美国最大的私营煤炭公司，去年由于电力公用事业对煤炭的需求暴跌而破产。

Murray, who had worked underground for 16 years early in his career, wrote in his filing for black lung benefits that he was near death and heavily dependent on an oxygen tank.

默里在职业生涯早期已经在地下工作了16年，他在申请黑肺福利的文件中写道，他濒临死亡，严重依赖氧气罐。

He died at his home St. Clairsville, Ohio, on Sunday while surrounded by family, WTOV television reported, citing a family spokesman.

WTOV电视台援引一位家庭发言人的话报道，他周日在俄亥俄州圣克莱斯维尔的家中去世，当时正被家人包围。

Rich Nolan, president of the National Mining Association, which represents the coal industry, said of Murray’s passing: “There was no one more passionate about the importance and value of coal, and the absence of his voice will be felt by many.”

代表煤炭行业的全国矿业协会主席里奇·诺兰(Rich Nolan)在谈到穆雷的逝世时表示：“没有人比他更热衷于煤炭的重要性和价值，他的声音的缺席将被许多人感受到。”

In a May 2017 interview with Reuters, Murray said he had drafted an “action plan of priorities” for the Trump administration that would help the coal industry.

穆雷在2017年5月接受路透专访时表示，他已经为特朗普政府起草了一份将帮助煤炭行业的“优先事项行动计划”。

The list started with rolling back the Stream Protection Rule that restricted the dumping of coal waste in waterways, and included scrapping the Clean Power Plan that aimed to slash carbon emissions from power plants.

这份清单首先是取消限制在水道倾倒煤矸石的溪流保护规则，并包括取消旨在大幅削减发电厂碳排放的清洁电力计划(Clean Power Plan)。

“We have taken that list of those priorities and prioritized them and he [Trump] has gone right down that list to stop the war on coal,” he told Reuters in the interview.

他在接受路透社采访时表示：“我们已经拿到了这些优先事项的清单，并对它们进行了优先排序，他(特朗普)已经直接沿着这份清单走下去，以停止对煤炭的战争，”他在接受路透社采访时表示。

Despite the rollbacks, the coal industry has remained under pressure due to competition from cheap natural gas and rising use of solar and wind power.

尽管出现了回落，但由于来自廉价天然气的竞争以及太阳能和风能的使用不断增加，煤炭行业仍面临压力。

Murray Energy filed for bankruptcy protection last year and re-emerged as American Consolidated Natural Resources, which bills itself as the largest underground coal mining company in the United States.

Murray Energy去年申请破产保护，并重新出现为美国联合自然资源公司(American Consolidation Natural Resources)，该公司自称是美国最大的地下煤矿公司。

Murray remained an unrelenting booster for the coal industry, even after experiencing its dangers firsthand.

即使在亲身经历了煤炭行业的危险之后，默里仍然是煤炭行业坚持不懈的推动者。

His father was paralyzed from the neck down in a mining accident when Murray was nine years old, and Murray broke his neck twice in mining accidents.

他的父亲在穆雷九岁时的一次矿难中颈部以下瘫痪，穆雷在矿难中两次摔断了脖子。

“I’ve got a birdcage of titanium and vanadium between (vertebrae) C2 and C8,” Murray told Reuters in a 2013 interview, pulling back his collar to show a scar running down the back of his neck, the trace of a past surgery.

穆雷在2013年的一次采访中告诉路透社：“我在(椎骨)C2和C8之间有一个钛和钒的鸟笼。”他拉开衣领，看到脖子后面有一道伤疤，这是他过去手术的痕迹。

While Murray applied for black lung benefits, bankruptcy enabled his company to avoid obligations to employees diagnosed with black lung, shifting its $74.4 million liability to the federal government’s Black Lung Disability Trust Fund, according to Labor Department estimates provided to Congress.

据劳工部提供给国会的估计，在穆雷申请黑肺福利的同时，破产使他的公司得以避免对被诊断为黑肺的员工承担义务，将其7,440万美元的负债转移到联邦政府的黑肺残疾信托基金。
