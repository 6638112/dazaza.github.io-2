Universal Music Group unveils a global agreement with TikTok, days after pulling its music catalog from TikTok-rival Triller
在从TikTok竞争对手Triller提取其音乐目录数天后，环球音乐集团与TikTok宣布了一项全球协议

2021-02-09 20:44:01

Just days after Triller and Universal Music Group essentially declared war on each other, UMG has unveiled a global agreement with “that delivers equitable compensation for recording artists and songwriters and significantly expands and enhances the companies’ existing relationship, promoting the development of new innovative experiences,” the announcement reads.

在Triller和环球音乐集团之间基本宣战之后的几天，UMG宣布了一项全球协议，内容是“为唱片艺术家和词曲作者提供公平的报酬，并显着扩大和加强两家公司的现有关系，促进新的创新体验的发展，”该公告显示。

Via the agreement, which covers recorded music from artists at UMG’s labels and songwriters with Universal Music Publishing Group, “TikTok users will be more empowered than ever to express themselves through music, soundtrack their video creations with songs about which they are passionate and build communities around artists and music-centered culture.” The companies have additionally pledged to experiment with new features: TikTok users will now be able to incorporate clips from UMG’s full catalog of music, spanning the company’s iconic labels, songwriters and global territories.

通过该协议，该协议涵盖了UMG唱片公司的歌手和环球音乐出版集团的歌曲作者的录制音乐，“ TikTok用户将比以往任何时候都更有能力通过音乐表达自己，用他们热爱的歌曲配乐其视频创作并建立社区围绕艺术家和以音乐为中心的文化。”两家公司还承诺将尝试新功能：TikTok用户现在将能够合并UMG完整音乐目录中的片段，涵盖公司的标志性唱片公司，词曲作者和全球地区。

“We are excited to enter this new era with UMG and UMPG to continue supporting artists and songwriters, by working together to help reach music fans on TikTok. Our platform has been a driver in creating chart hits and licensing the world’s biggest catalog of tracks will continue to inspire our community. In turn, we are proud to partner with UMG and UMPG to be a source to help new talent emerge and to re-introduce legacy acts to a new audience,” said Ole Obermann, Global Head of Music for TikTok.

“我们很高兴与UMG和UMPG一起进入这个新时代，通过共同帮助在TikTok上吸引音乐迷，继续支持艺术家和词曲作者。我们的平台一直是创造流行歌曲排行榜的推动力，并且许可全球最大的曲目目录将继续激励我们的社区。反过来，我们很荣幸与UMG和UMPG合作，成为帮助新人才涌现并向新听众重新介绍传统行为的资源，” TikTok全球音乐总监Ole Obermann说道。

“UMG and TikTok will now work more closely than ever to promote ambitious experimentation, innovation and collaboration — with the shared objective of developing new music experiences and features. Driving new and deeper connections with fans, this agreement delivers equitable compensation to our recording artists and songwriters, as well as a commitment to develop industry-leading tools, A&R insights and models necessary to advance their careers,” said Michael Nash, Executive Vice President of Digital Strategy at UMG.

“ UMG和TikTok现在将比以往任何时候都更加紧密地合作，以促进雄心勃勃的实验，创新和合作，其共同目标是开发新的音乐体验和功能。通过与粉丝建立新的更深层次的联系，这项协议为我们的唱片艺术家和词曲作者提供了公平的报酬，并承诺开发行业领先的工具，A＆amp; R见解和促进职业发展的模型。” UMG数字战略副总裁。

“This alliance sets an industrywide example of social media companies acknowledging, respecting and compensating the music creators whose songs are instrumental to their platforms. We appreciate Tik Tok’s partnership and look forward to working together to provide support and opportunities to our songwriters,” added Marc Cimino, Chief Operating Officer of UMPG.

“这个联盟为社交媒体公司树立了行业榜样，他们承认，尊重和补偿音乐创作者，他们的歌曲对他们的平台至关重要。 UMPG首席运营官Marc Cimino补充说：“我们感谢Tik Tok的合作伙伴关系，并期待与我们合作，为我们的歌曲作者提供支持和机会。
