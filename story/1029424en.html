新的基准显示iPhone的节流如此之猛，以至于失去了对Android的优势

2020-10-17 18:01:06

Yesterday, we brought you news of the new  3DMark WildLife graphics benchmark, which is the latest cross-platform test from the folks at UL Benchmarks. UL explains that WildLife is primarily tasked with measuring GPU performance across platforms, and two distinct tests are available.  The standard WildLife test is designed to give feedback on how a game performs over a short period of time. With mobile games, people typically play in brief spurts when they find some free time; be it on the bus, on the subway, or a quick battle royale session on your lunch break, but not always.

昨天，我们为您带来了全新的3DMark野生动物图形基准测试的消息，这是UL基准测试人员的最新跨平台测试。UL解释说，野生动物的主要任务是测量跨平台的GPU性能，有两种不同的测试可用。标准的野生动物测试旨在给出游戏在短时间内表现如何的反馈。对于手机游戏，人们通常会在找到空闲时间后短暂地玩一玩；无论是在公交车上、地铁上，还是在午休时进行一次快速的皇家战斗，但也不总是如此。

The 3DMark WildLife Stress Test, on the other hand, shows how a device performs over a longer stretch of time, and takes note of performance degradation that can crop up due to increased heat levels and throttling (which we'll get to a bit later in this article). As UL explains, "Instead of producing a single score, the main result from the stress test is a chart that shows you how the device’s performance changed during the test."

另一方面，3DMark野生动物压力测试显示了设备在较长一段时间内的性能，并注意到由于热量增加和节流(我们将在本文稍后介绍到这一点)可能会突然出现的性能下降。正如UL解释的那样，压力测试的主要结果不是产生一个单一的分数，而是一个图表，它向您显示设备在测试期间的性能是如何变化的。

When running on Android devices or Windows PCs, WildLife uses the Vulkan API. On iOS devices (like the iPhone and iPad), WildLife uses Apple's Metal API.

当在Android设备或Windows PC上运行时，野生动物使用Vulkan API。在iOS设备(如iPhone和iPad)上，野生动物使用苹果的Metal API。

Given that we make heavy use of 3DMark benchmarks for our smartphone reviews, we decided to take a look at how some smartphones that we have in our lab (both new and old) stack up against each other. The results were somewhat surprising, but we caution you to reserve your opinion and final analysis until you've digested the next segment of our testing efforts here as well. Alas, without further ado...

考虑到我们在智能手机评论中大量使用3DMark基准，我们决定看看我们实验室里的一些智能手机(包括新的和旧的)是如何相互对比的。结果有些令人惊讶，但我们提醒您，在您消化我们测试工作的下一阶段之前，请保留您的观点和最终分析。唉，没有更多的麻烦了.。

We decided to run WildLife Unlimited, which allows each device to run as fast as it can independent of the given refresh rate thanks to off-screen rendering; the standard WildLife test is capped by the device's refresh rate. What we can clearly see here is that the GPU in Apple's line of iPhones is an absolute performance monster, as it's easily able to dispatch its Android rivals in this short-burst benchmark.

我们决定运行野生动物无限版，它允许每个设备以尽可能快的速度运行，而不受给定刷新率的影响，这要归功于屏幕外渲染；标准的野生动物测试受到设备刷新率的限制。我们在这里可以清楚地看到，苹果iPhone系列中的GPU是一个绝对的性能怪物，因为它在这个短时间内的基准测试中轻松击败了Android竞争对手。

What's remarkable is just how far the divide is between the iPhones and flagship Android devices. To put things in perspective, the iPhone XR, which was at the bottom rung of Apple's 2018 flagship smartphone lineup, put up a score of 5424, which is 42 percent faster than the OnePlus 8/8T. The iPhone XR’s performance was also 27 percent faster than the mighty ASUS ROG Phone 3 in X-Mode.

值得注意的是，iPhone和Android旗舰设备之间的差距有多大。相比之下，iPhone XR在苹果2018年旗舰智能手机阵容中排名垫底，得分为5424分，比OnePlus 8/8T快42%。IPhone XR的性能也比功能强大的华硕ROG Phone 3在X模式下快了27%。

The iPhone 11 Pro, Apple's 2019 flagship device, really threw down the gauntlet with a score of 7800, more than doubling the performance that the OnePlus 8/OnePlus 8T could muster, while outpacing the ROG Phone 3 (X-Mode) by 87 percent. The spread could grow even wider with the iPhone 12 family, as recent leaked benchmarks show the A14 Bionic outpacing flagship Android phones  by as much as 60 percent in single-threaded synthetic CPU tests. And Apple is claiming up to a 50 percent boost over its Android rivals in GPU benchmarks. We've reached out to UL Benchmarks with a few questions to better understand the company's new benchmark, how it measures and weights performance, as well as how it utilizes these 3D graphics APIs.  But there's a LOT more to the picture here than just these performance runs...

作为苹果2019年旗舰手机的iPhone 11 Pro以7800分的成绩真正挑战了对手，它的性能是OnePlus 8/OnePlus 8T的两倍多，比ROG Phone 3(X模式)高出87%。随着iPhone 12系列的推出，这种差距可能会进一步扩大，因为最近泄露的基准显示，在单线程合成CPU测试中，A14仿生手机的表现比旗舰Android手机高出60%。苹果声称在GPU基准方面比其Android竞争对手高出50%。我们向UL基准提出了几个问题，以更好地了解该公司的新基准，它如何衡量和衡量性能，以及它如何利用这些3D图形API。但是，这里的情况远远不止这些性能运行的情况。

Where things really get interesting is with the WildLife Stress Test Unlimited, which we alluded to earlier. Since this test take a lot longer to run (20 minutes versus roughly a minute for WildLife Unlimited), we were paying close attention to any performance drop-offs, and this is where the iPhones showed their Achilles heel.

事情真正变得有趣的是我们之前提到的野生动物压力测试无限。由于这项测试的运行时间要长得多(野生动物无限版需要20分钟，而野生动物无限版大约需要一分钟)，我们密切关注任何性能下降，这就是iPhone表现出致命弱点的地方。

During its WildLife Stress Test Unlimited run, the iPhone 11 Pro put in a best loop score of 7945, which was of course recorded on its first run. However, once it completed that first run, performance dropped off considerably over the next 19 minutes until 20 runs were completed. By the end, exhausted from thermal throttling, the iPhone 11 Pro put in a final run score of 4106. That puts its benchmark stability at 51.7 percent.

在其野生动物压力测试无限跑期间，iPhone11Pro的最好循环得分为7945，这当然是在第一次运行时就记录下来的。然而，一旦它完成了第一次运行，在接下来的19分钟内，性能会大幅下降，直到完成了20次运行。到最后，由于热量节流而筋疲力尽，iPhone11Pro的最终运行分数为4106分。这使得其基准稳定性为51.7%。

Taking into account the steep drop-off in performance, the iPhone 11 Pro’s lowest benchmark run score was still higher than most of its Android rivals. However, the overachieving ASUS ROG Phone 3 in X-Mode was able to topple the iPhone 11 Pro's worst run and it should be noted that iPhone's performance levels off and stays down at the 4K mark after about the 13 minute mark. Speaking of the Android competition, the OnePlus 8/8T barely encountered any thermal throttling during the 20-minute exercise, posting a benchmark stability rating of over 99 percent. Regardless, the performance picture is completely different in the stress test, and our OnePlus 8 score on its fastest loop is much more on par with the iPhone 11 Pro after just a few runs. Note that the sharp drop off continues, regardless of iPhone model. We will also note that we stress tested Samsung's Galaxy S20 Ultra (Snapdragon 865+) as well, with similar results to our OnePlus 8 results (top right). Almost no performance bleed off occurs with these Android phones and a 99+% stability rating is rendered by the WildLife Stress Test benchmark for the Snapdragon 865 models we tested.

考虑到性能的急剧下降，iPhone11 Pro的最低基准运行分数仍然高于大多数Android竞争对手。然而，在X模式下表现出色的华硕ROG Phone 3击败了iPhone11Pro的最差表现，应该指出的是，iPhone的性能水平下降，并在大约13分钟后保持在4K大关。说到Android的竞争，OnePlus 8/8T在20分钟的演习中几乎没有遇到任何热量节流，基准稳定性评级超过99%。无论如何，在压力测试中的性能图景是完全不同的，我们的OnePlus8在其最快循环上的得分在短短几次运行后就与iPhone11Pro平起平坐。值得注意的是，每次跑步的时长只有一分钟左右，显然，人们通常一次只玩一分钟，但很可能至少超过几分钟。-更新时间为10/16/20，晚上9：16：iPhone SE(2020-A13仿生)和iPhone XS Max(A12仿生)压力测试增加。请注意，无论iPhone型号如何，急剧下降的趋势仍在继续。我们还会注意到，我们也对三星Galaxy S20 Ultra(骁龙865+)进行了重点测试，结果与我们的OnePlus8结果(右上角)相似。这些Android手机几乎没有出现性能下降，我们测试的骁龙865机型的野生动物压力测试基准给出了99+%的稳定性评级。

It appears that Apple is targeting “peak” over “sustained” performance when it comes to the GPU in the A13 or A12 Bionic, while the Adreno 650 GPU in the Snapdragon 865 is a cool as a cucumber no matter the situation in the WildLife Stress Test. Whatever the case, this has been a rather eye-opening first look at 3DMark WildLife, and we’ll be interested in seeing how things pan out once we get an A14 Bionic-equipped Apple device in house, to see how far the lead for iPhones extends and or shrinks with continuous load. The rumored  Qualcomm Snapdragon 875, which could be unveiled as early as December 1 st, will have a lot of ground to makeup if it wants to vie for supremacy in this space, at least when it comes to the top benchmark scores, but perhaps not with respect to performance over time and thermal saturation.

看来，苹果在A13或A12仿生机型的GPU上，瞄准的是性能上的“峰值”而非“持续性”，而骁龙865上的Adreno 650 GPU，无论在野生动物压力测试中的情况如何，都是酷毙了的黄瓜了。在A13或A12仿生的GPU上，无论情况如何，骁龙865中的Adreno 650 GPU都是酷炫的。无论如何，这是一个相当令人大开眼界的3DMark野生动物第一眼，我们很有兴趣看看一旦我们有了一台配备A14仿生技术的苹果设备，情况会如何，看看iPhone的领先优势在持续加载的情况下扩大和/或缩小了多少。传言中的高通骁龙875最早可能在12月1日亮相，如果它想要在这一领域争夺霸主地位，至少在最高基准得分方面将有很大的补足余地，但可能不会涉及到随着时间的推移和热饱和度的表现.QUALCOMM骁龙875最早可能在12月1日发布，如果想要争夺这一领域的霸主地位，将有很大的补位空间.。
