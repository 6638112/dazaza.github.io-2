Sony is removing Cyberpunk 2077 from PlayStation Store until further notice, and offering full refunds to people who bought it, amid reports of it being buggy
索尼将从PlayStation Store移除Cyberpunk 2077，直至另行通知，并在报道有缺陷的情况下向购买它的人提供全额退款

2020-12-18 16:21:27

Sony is pulling Cyberpunk 2077 from the PlayStation Store and offering full refunds for anyone who bought the game from the digital storefront, the company said on Thursday. If you want to start the refund process now, Sony says to visit this site and sign into your PlayStation account to submit a request.

索尼周四表示，索尼将从PlayStation商店撤出Cyber​​punk 2077，并向从数字店面购买游戏的任何人提供全额退款。如果您想立即开始退款程序，Sony说访问该站点并登录您的PlayStation帐户以提交请求。

However, later on Thursday evening, Sony said it is “aware some users are experiencing issues” accessing the refund form. If you’re seeing issues, you may want to try again later.

但是，索尼在星期四晚上晚些时候说，“知道某些用户遇到了问题”访问退款表格。如果您发现问题，则可能需要稍后再试。

Cyberpunk 2077 has already been removed from the PlayStation Store for a few Verge staffers on their PS5s, and the game doesn’t come up in a search for “ Cyberpunk 2077” on the web version of the store. Following older, online listing links to the game will show its store page, but with the option to wish list it only.

赛博朋克2077已从PS5上的一些Verge工作人员中从PlayStation商店中删除，并且该游戏并未在商店的网络版本中搜索“赛博朋克2077”时出现。较旧的在线游戏的在线列表链接将显示其商店页面，但可以选择仅列出它。

SIE strives to ensure a high level of customer satisfaction, and we will begin to offer a full refund for all gamers who have purchased Cyberpunk 2077 via PlayStation Store and want a refund. Please visit the following link to initiate the refund: https://t.co/DEZlC0LmUG.

SIE努力确保高水平的客户满意度，我们将开始为通过PlayStation Store购买了Cyber​​punk 2077并想要退款的所有游戏玩家提供全额退款。请访问以下链接以开始退款：https：//t.co/DEZlC0LmUG。

-Ask PlayStation（@AskPlayStation）2020年12月18日

Players have found that Cyberpunk 2077, which has only been out for a little more than one week, has been riddled with bugs. And while game looks and performs well in backwards compatibility mode on the PS5, it is prone to routine crashes and a number of distracting visual glitches. On PS4, however, the game fares a lot worse — Eurogamer reported poor performance, low framerate, and texture pop-in.

玩家已经发现，只有不到一个多星期才出现的《赛博朋克2077》充满了漏洞。而且，尽管在PS5上向后兼容模式下游戏外观和性能都不错，但它容易出现常规崩溃和许多分散注意力的视觉故障。但是，在PS4上，游戏的性能要差很多-Eurogamer报告性能差，帧率低和弹出纹理。

Sony’s move comes just days after Cyberpunk 2077 developer CD Projekt Red said people unsatisfied with their purchase on the PS4 or Xbox One should request a refund. Yet due to Sony’s stringent refund policy, many who bought digital versions of the game from the PlayStation Store were unable to get refunds. (Players who bought the Xbox One version of the game have reported better luck with Microsoft’s more generous refund policy.)

几天前，Cyber​​punk 2077开发人员CD Projekt Red表示，对在PS4或Xbox One上购买的产品不满意的人应该要求退款，这是索尼采取这一行动的几天。然而，由于索尼严格的退款政策，许多从PlayStation商店购买了数字版本游戏的人无法获得退款。 （购买了Xbox One版本游戏的玩家报告说，由于微软实行了更为慷慨的退款政策，因此运气更好。）

CD Projekt Red posted a note to its investor website disclosing Sony’s decision, saying ( PDF):

CD Projekt Red在其投资者网站上发布了说明索尼决定的便条，并表示（PDF）：

The decision [to disclose Sony’s decision] was undertaken following our discussion with SIE [Sony Interactive Entertainment] regarding a full refund for all gamers who had purchased Cyberpunk 2077 via PlayStation Store and want a refund at this time. All copies of the game previously purchased digitally on PlayStation Store remain available for use by their respective buyers. Gamers can still buy physical versions of the game in retail and mail order stores. All copies, whether digital or physical, will continue to receive support and updates from the Company.

决定[披露索尼的决定]是在我们与SIE [Sony Interactive Entertainment]进行讨论之后做出的，该决定涉及所有通过PlayStation Store购买了Cyber​​punk 2077并希望现在退款的游戏玩家的全额退款。先前在PlayStation商店中以数字方式购买的所有游戏副本仍可供其各自的购买者使用。玩家仍然可以在零售和邮购商店中购买游戏的实体版本。所有副本，无论是数字副本还是物理副本，都将继续获得公司的支持和更新。

It’s unclear when Sony plans to return Cyberpunk 2077 to the PlayStation Store; the company’s note says the game will be unavailable in its digital format “until further notice.” It’s also not clear if Microsoft plans to similarly yank the game from its digital storefront or offer full refunds. Microsoft didn’t immediately reply to a request for comment.

目前尚不清楚索尼何时计划将《赛博朋克2077》送回PlayStation商店；该公司的说明说，该游戏将无法以其数字格式“直到另行通知”才能提供。还不清楚微软是否计划从其数字店面中撤出该游戏或提供全额退款。微软没有立即回复置评请求。

CD Projekt Red has committed to releasing patches to improve Cyberpunk 2077. The company has already released one post-launch update, and in a December 13th statement, the studio said another update is “coming within the next 7 days.” More significant patches are planned for January and February. “Together these should fix the most prominent problems gamers are facing on last-gen consoles,” the company said.

CD Projekt Red致力于发布补丁以改善Cyber​​punk2077。该公司已经发布了一个发布后的更新，并且在12月13日的声明中，该工作室表示，另一个更新“将在未来7天内推出”。计划在1月和2月发布更多重要补丁。该公司表示：“这些将共同解决游戏玩家在最新一代游戏机上面临的最突出的问题。”
