Preview in macOS Big Sur is irreversibly destroying PDFs – again
在macOS Big Sur中进行预览将不可逆转地破坏PDF –再次

2020-12-17 04:44:53

This image has three components: On the left is an OCR’ed PDF from my ScanSnap iX500. I have selected most of the text, and on the right side you can see two copy&paste results. In the upper half is the result directly after scanning, right after the bundled ABBYY FineReader that comes with the iX500 did its magic. In the lower half is the result after modifying (removed a blank page) and saving that same PDF in Preview.

该图像包括三个部分：左侧是我的ScanSnap iX500的OCR格式的PDF。我选择了大多数文本，在右侧可以看到两个复制粘贴结果。上半部分是直接在扫描后的结果，紧随iX500附带的捆绑ABBYY FineReader发挥了神奇作用。下半部分是修改（删除空白页）并将相同的PDF保存在预览中后的结果。

Hard to believe, but that’s not the first time Apple messed this up. Sure, even Apple can’t account for all use cases when changing complex stuff like internal PDF handling. But:

难以置信，但这不是Apple第一次搞砸了。当然，在更改内部PDF处理等复杂内容时，即使Apple也无法说明所有用例。但：

I don’t know any OCR software that is more popular than ABBYY FineReader

我不知道任何比ABBYY FineReader更流行的OCR软件

macOS used to be the absolute best in class OS for dealing with PDFs by a long shot

从长远来看，macOS曾经是同类操作系统中绝对最佳的，可以处理PDF

I wish Apple was still charging for OS updates, so I could at least refund it. 1 This is such a nasty bug – if you don’t already know to expect it, you will only find out months or possibly years later. I almost missed it this time, because even after modifying and saving the file it’s still not happening. You have to completely close the file and reopen it, only then will you realize that it has been destroyed.

我希望苹果仍在为操作系统更新收费，所以我至少可以退款。 1这是一个令人讨厌的错误-如果您不知道会发生什么，那么您只会发现数月甚至数年之后。这次我几乎错过了它，因为即使在修改和保存文件后，它仍然没有发生。您必须完全关闭文件并重新打开它，然后您才能意识到它已被破坏。

Yes, I blame only Apple for this. I’ll repeat what I told Philipp (noted Apple apologist!) when we argued about this last week after I discovered the problem: ABBYY says they don’t support Big Sur yet, that’s fine. But Apple didn’t tell me that I can’t upgrade to Big Sur when I use ABBYY. I’d be a lot less angry if there was a changelog or release notes from Apple where it says there is a known problem with OCR’ed PDFs in Preview. Their software is broken, they need to tell me. I don’t care if it only worked because they had workarounds for super shitty PDFs that ABBYY possibly produces, I just need my OS to keep working for me. This bug could hit me without even owning a scanner at all – someone sending me a PDF that I then unknowingly break before archiving it. That’s the part I’m mad about. ↩︎

是的，我为此只怪苹果。在我发现问题后的上周我们就此事进行辩论时，我将重复我告诉菲利普（Philipp）的事情：ABBYY说他们还不支持Big Sur，那很好。但是Apple并没有告诉我使用ABBYY时无法升级到Big Sur。如果有Apple的变更​​日志或发行说明中说，OCR的PDF预览版存在已知问题，我会生气得多。他们的软件坏了，他们需要告诉我。我不在乎是否仅因为它们具有ABBYY可能会产生的超级劣质PDF的变通办法而已，我只需要我的OS即可继续为我工作。这个错误可能使我什至根本没有拥有扫描仪就打了我-有人给我发送了一份PDF，然后我在不知不觉中破坏了它，然后将其存档。那就是我生气的部分。 ↩︎
