Berkshire Hathaway Inc.每比例超过421,000美元，市场乐观。这是一个问题。纳斯达克公司Ontues Day，纳斯达克Inc.暂时暂停广播价格在几个流行的数据饲料上消除伯克希尔的股票。这种在许多在线和金融网站提供实时价格更新。

Nasdaq’s computers can only count so high because of the compactdigital format they use for communicating prices. The biggestnumber they can handle is $429,496.7295. Nasdaq is rushing tofinish an upgrade later this month that would fix the problem.

纳斯达克的计算机只能根据他们用于沟通价格的紧凑型格式计算得如此之高。他们可以处理的BiggestNumber是429,496.7295美元。纳斯达克在本月晚些时候急于升级，这将解决问题。

That number will look familiar to the programmers among you: it’s the limit of an unsigned 32-bit integer. Using 32-bit integers for share prices, with four digits reserved for decimals, isn’t that crazy, though, given that no other stock in the U.S. has a share price that’s even close to the limit:

该数字看起来将熟悉您的程序员：这是无符号32位整数的限制。使用32位整数进行股票价格，为小数保留了四位数字，但鉴于美国没有其他股票，甚至没有截止到极限的股价：

The U.S. stock with the second-highest share price, home builderNVR Inc., is trading just above $5,100 a share. Using compactformats that take up less memory can make software more efficient,a high priority in the world of electronic stock trading.

U.S.股票具有第二高的股价，家庭建筑物，股票价格高于5,100美元的交易。使用占用更少内存的CommanceFormats可以使软件更高效，在电子股票交易中的高度优先。

At the root of the problem is Mr. Buffett’s decadeslong refusal toexecute a stock split of Berkshire’s Class A shares. The90-year-old billionaire has signed birthday cards to friends withthe message, “May you live until Berkshire splits,” according toFortune magazine.

问题的根源是巴菲特的十几岁的拒绝拒绝伯克希尔班股份的股票分裂。这位90岁的亿万富翁向朋友签了生日贺卡，“愿你才居住，直到伯克希尔分裂，”根据Tofortune杂志。

Update: Worth noting that they’re not using integers to store fractional values — what they’re doing is using 10,000ths of a dollar as their integral unit. The decimal gets shifted left by four digits simply to display prices as dollar values, but the math is all done in 10,000ths of a dollar units.

更新：值得注意的是，它们不使用整数来存储分数值 - 他们正在做的是使用10,000个美元作为其整体单元。十进制将左侧移动四位数字，只需将价格显示为美元值，但数学就在10,000个美元单位中完成。

Not sure what their fix is going to be, but going to 64-bit integers would let them handle per-share prices up to $1,844,674,407,370,955.1615 — over $1.8 quadrillion — which should buy them some time, even if Berkshire continues to grow yet refuses to split.

不确定他们的修复将是什么，但要去64位整数将让他们处理每股价格高达1,844,674,407,844,674,407,370,955.1615 - 超过1.8英镑 - 这应该在一段时间内购买，即使伯克希尔继续增长但拒绝分裂。
