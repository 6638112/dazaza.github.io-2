CrowdStrike to acquire log data analysis startup Humio for approximately $400M
CrowdStrike以约4亿美元的价格收购日志数据分析初创公司Humio

2021-02-19 00:09:31

CrowdStrike, a cloud-native cybersecurity company focused on endpoint protection and threat intelligence for enterprises, has announced plans to acquire U.K.-based log analysis and observability startup Humio in a mostly cash deal worth approximately $400 million.

CrowdStrike是一家云原生网络安全公司，专注于为企业提供端点保护和威胁情报，该公司宣布了计划以约4亿美元的现金交易收购总部位于英国的日志分析和可观察性初创公司Humio。

Humio emerged as a notable player in the cloud log-management and observability sphere after being founded out of London in 2016 with the native ability to ingest and analyze both unstructured and semi-structured data. The startup had secured more than $30 million in funding from backers like Accel and Dell, with customers including Microsoft and Bloomberg.

Humio于2016年在伦敦成立后，凭借其固有的能力来提取和分析非结构化和半结构化数据，在云日志管理和可观察性领域中成为显着的参与者。这家初创公司已经从Accel和Dell等支持者那里获得了超过3000万美元的资金，客户包括Microsoft和Bloomberg。

Founded in 2011, CrowdStrike helps companies spot signs of an impending attack through entry points such as employee laptops or mobile devices and thwart it before a breach occurs. The Sunnyvale, California-based company claims a number of big-name customers, including Goldman Sachs and Rackspace.

成立于2011年的CrowdStrike帮助公司通过员工笔记本电脑或移动设备之类的入口点发现即将发生的攻击的迹象，并在发生违规行为之前予以阻止。这家总部位于加利福尼亚州桑尼维尔的公司宣称拥有许多知名客户，包括高盛和Rackspace。

As with just about every cloud-focused company, CrowdStrike has benefited significantly from global events over the past year, with its share price growing sixfold and giving the company a valuation north of $50 billion. With Humio under its wing, CrowdStrike said it’s well-positioned to expand its detection and response capabilities by “ingesting and correlating data from any log, application, or feed to deliver actionable insights and real-time protection,” according to a press release.

与几乎所有专注于云计算的公司一样，CrowdStrike在过去一年中从全球事件中受益匪浅，其股价增长了六倍，使公司的估值超过500亿美元。新闻稿说，有了Humio，CrowdStrike处于有利位置，可以通过“吸收和关联任何日志，应用程序或提要中的数据以提供可行的见解和实时保护”来扩展其检测和响应功能。

The acquisition also comes amid a flurry of consolidation across the cybersecurity landscape. Just last week, CrowdStrike rival Datadog bolstered its app security and observability capabilities with the acquisition of Sqreen and Timber, which came shortly after New Relic announced it was acquiring Pixie Labs, an observability-focused product for Kubernetes developers.

此次收购还发生在整个网络安全领域的一连串整合中。就在上周，CrowdStrike的竞争对手Datadog通过收购Sqreen和Timber来增强其应用程序安全性和可观察性功能，这是在New Relic宣布收购Pixie Labs之后不久的，该产品是针对Kubernetes开发人员的可观察性产品。
