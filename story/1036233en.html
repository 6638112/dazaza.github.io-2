Tech CEOs Are Saying Goodbye to San Francisco
科技CEO向旧金山告别

2020-11-24 09:55:00

Photo: Brex co-CEO Henrique Dubugras, Dropbox CEO Drew Houston and Splunk CEO Doug Merritt. Photos: Bloomberg

照片：Brex联合首席执行官Henrique Dubugras，Dropbox首席执行官Drew Houston和Splunk首席执行官Doug Merritt。图片：彭博社

Splunk, Dropbox and Brex followed similar formulas as they grew from small startups to established companies. To attract employees and customers, each set up elaborate San Francisco offices and plastered billboards for its services around the city. Dropbox three years ago leased a giant new office building on the city’s eastern edge. Fintech firm Brex even opened its own cafe in a neighborhood popular with venture capitalists.

当Splunk，Dropbox和Brex从小型初创公司成长为成熟公司时，它们遵循类似的公式。为了吸引员工和客户，他们各自在旧金山设立了精致的办事处，并在其遍布城市的服务上贴了广告牌。三年前，Dropbox在该市东部边缘租用了一座巨型新办公楼。金融科技公司Brex甚至在受风险投资家欢迎的社区开设了自己的咖啡馆。

Now, all three companies embody a new trend: Their chief executives are leaving the Bay Area. Dropbox CEO Drew Houston and Splunk CEO Douglas Merritt both recently bought homes in Austin, with plans to make the Texas capital their permanent residences, they have told people at their firms. Brex’s 20-something co-founders, Henrique Dubugras and Pedro Franceschi, have decamped to Los Angeles and don’t plan to renew the company’s San Francisco office lease next year, they told The Information. None of the moves has been previously reported.

现在，这三家公司都体现了一种新的趋势：他们的首席执行官离开了湾区。 Dropbox首席执行官德鲁·休斯顿（Drew Houston）和Splunk首席执行官道格拉斯·梅里特（Douglas Merritt）最近都在奥斯汀购买了房屋，并计划将得克萨斯州的首都作为永久居民。他们告诉《信息报》，Brex的20多岁的联合创始人Henrique Dubugras和Pedro Franceschi已经来到洛杉矶，不打算明年续签该公司在旧金山的办公室。以前没有任何举动的报道。

The stock market is acting like the Covid vaccines have already been administered. Meanwhile, in the real world, what could be a long-term consequence of the pandemic is unfolding: there are signs the San Francisco Bay Area is losing its iron grip on the tech industry. We ran a story today on high-profile tech CEOs such as Dropbox’s Drew Houston and Splunk’s Douglas Merritt...

股市就像已经接种了Covid疫苗一样。同时，在现实世界中，大流行的长期后果正在逐步显现：有迹象表明，旧金山湾区正在失去对科技行业的控制力。今天，我们为Dropbox的Drew Houston和Splunk的Douglas Merritt等知名的技术首席执行官讲了一个故事。
