Foursquare首席执行官大卫·辛(David Shim)将于1月1日卸任，将由公司董事会成员加里·利特尔(Gary Little)接替

2020-11-07 14:20:30

Foursquare Labs Inc. Chief Executive David Shim is stepping down Jan. 1, the company said, and will be succeeded by Gary Little, a member of the company’s board.

Foursquare Labs Inc.。该公司表示，首席执行长沈大伟(David Shim)将于1月1日卸任，继任者将是公司董事会成员加里·利特尔(Gary Little)。

In an interview, Mr. Shim said he is leaving to pursue new opportunities after a decade running location-based data businesses.

Shim在接受采访时说，在经营基于位置的数据业务十年后，他将离开去寻找新的机会。

Foursquare rose to prominence as an app that let users log visits to their favorite restaurants and other establishments and share their location with friends, but pivoted in recent years toward providing location data and software to businesses including marketers and ad agencies. The company’s tools, for example, help brands see how well their ads steered people into visiting stores.

Foursquare作为一款应用程序而声名鹊起，它可以让用户记录对自己喜欢的餐厅和其他机构的访问，并与朋友分享他们的位置。但近年来，Foursquare转向向包括营销人员和广告公司在内的企业提供位置数据和软件。例如，该公司的工具可以帮助各大品牌了解他们的广告如何引导人们光顾商店。

Mr. Shim joined Foursquare in 2019 when the company purchased Placed, a location-data provider he founded, from Snap which itself acquired Placed for $135 million in 2017.

希姆于2019年加入Foursquare，当时该公司从Snap手中收购了他创立的位置数据提供商Placed。Snap于2017年以1.35亿美元收购了Placed。

Foursquare’s acquisition of Placed made Mr. Shim president of the company. He became CEO at the end of 2019.

Foursquare收购Placed后，辛姆成为该公司的总裁。他于2019年底成为首席执行官。

The Placed acquisition coincided with a $150 million investment by the Raine Group, where Mr. Little is managing director and head of technology. He was instrumental in Foursquare’s evolution into a provider of location-data software and ad services, the company said.

此次收购恰逢雷恩集团(Raine Group)投资1.5亿美元，利特尔是雷恩集团的董事总经理兼技术主管。该公司表示，他在Foursquare演变为位置数据软件和广告服务提供商的过程中发挥了重要作用。

“The potential of location technology and data is massive, which led to my role as lead investor of Foursquare’s $150 million fundraise round last year and today as an active Foursquare board member,” Mr. Little said in a post on the company’s website.

利特尔在公司网站上的一篇帖子中说，“定位技术和数据的潜力是巨大的，这使得我成为Foursquare去年1.5亿美元融资的主要投资者，今天又成为Foursquare的活跃董事会成员。”

Earlier this year, Foursquare merged with another location-focused startup, Factual Inc., in an all-stock deal. At the time, the company said Mr. Shim would remain as CEO.

今年早些时候，Foursquare与另一家专注于地理位置的初创公司FACTIVE Inc.以全股票交易的形式合并。当时，该公司表示，申宗均将继续担任首席执行长。

While the Placed deal improved Foursquare’s ability to gauge the effectiveness of ads by measuring foot traffic, the merger with Factual was designed to build on its ad-targeting capabilities, executives said at the time.

高管们当时表示，虽然这笔交易提高了Foursquare通过测量客流量来衡量广告效果的能力，但与FACTIVE的合并旨在增强其广告定向能力。

Mr. Shim said he made the decision to step down in the early fall after shepherding the integration of Placed and Factual into Foursquare.

辛说，他是在带领Placed and Foursquare整合了Placed and Foursquare之后，在初秋决定辞职的。

The company’s revenue is rising after its business took an initial hit during the outset of the coronavirus pandemic, which broadly hurt the advertising business and related services. Foursquare’s services focused on audience-targeting, measurement and attribution were affected, Mr. Shim said, while the impact on its enterprise business that provides location data on venues and other places was negligible.

该公司的收入正在上升，此前其业务在冠状病毒大流行开始时受到初步打击，这场疫情广泛损害了广告业务和相关服务。希姆说，Foursquare专注于受众定位、测量和归因的服务受到了影响，而对其提供场馆和其他地点位置数据的企业业务的影响微乎其微。

Revenue bounced back as businesses such as fast-food restaurants and retail outlets began to open up, ad spending resumed and marketers focused on getting more information on how their ads were driving results, Mr. Shim added.

希姆补充说，随着快餐店和零售店等业务开始开放，广告支出恢复，营销人员专注于获得更多关于广告如何推动结果的信息，营收出现反弹。

Foursquare was profitable in the second and third quarters, he said, and is on a path to be profitable for the 12 months following the Factual merger in April. He declined to provide specific revenue figures.

他表示，Foursquare在第二季度和第三季度实现了盈利，并在4月份事实合并后的12个月内走上了盈利之路。他拒绝提供具体的营收数字。

Foursquare and Factual together generated more than $150 million in revenue in 2019, executives previously said. Foursquare had its first profitable quarter in the final period of last year on the basis of earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation and amortization, a spokeswoman for the company told The Wall Street Journal in April.

高管们此前表示，Foursquare和FACTIVE在2019年总共创造了超过1.5亿美元的收入。Foursquare的一位女发言人在4月份告诉《华尔街日报》(The Wall Street Journal)，根据利息、税项、折旧和摊销前的收益(EBITDA)，Foursquare在去年最后一个季度实现了第一个盈利季度。

Mr. Shim said he feels Foursquare is well-positioned for its next stage of growth, but overseeing that would be a long-term endeavor.

辛说，他觉得Foursquare为下一阶段的发展做好了充分的准备，但监督这一阶段将是一项长期的努力。

“There is optionality and opportunity to take that next step,” Mr. Shim said. “While we are not announcing anything, that requires a certain level of commitment [from a CEO].”

“这是可以选择的，也是采取下一步行动的机会，”辛说。“虽然我们不会宣布任何事情，但这需要(首席执行官)做出一定程度的承诺。”
