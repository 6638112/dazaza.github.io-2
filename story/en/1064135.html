Apple debuts iCloud+ as part of iCloud plans, featuring Hide My Email, Private Relay for encrypting device traffic and bouncing it through relays, and more
Apple首次亮相iCloud +作为iCloud计划的一部分，具有隐藏我的电子邮件，私人继电器，用于加密设备流量并通过继电器弹跳它，更多

2021-06-08 03:14:21

During its WWDC keynote today, Apple unveiled a new iCloud+ service with new features alongside iCloud storage. iCloud+ includes three new features, and Apple is not raising the prices of iCloud.

在今天的WWDC主题演讲期间，Apple推出了一个新的ICLoud +服务，并与iCloud存储一起使用新功能。 iCloud +包括三个新功能，苹果不是提高iCloud的价格。

Private Relay is the headlining new feature of iCloud+. Through Private Relay, all traffic leaving your device is encrypted and sent through two separate relays. Apple says that no one, not even Apple, can access or view this data. This feature sounds quite a bit like an Apple VPN.

私有继电器是iCloud +的标题新功能。通过私人继电器，所有带有设备的流量都被加密并通过两个单独的继电器发送。 Apple说，没有人，甚至不是Apple，可以访问或查看此数据。此功能听起来很像Apple VPN。

Hide My Email is a new feature built into Mail, Safari, and other services and it allows you to randomized, unique emails that forward to your main account. This means you can hide your email from services for increased privacy.

隐藏我的电子邮件是邮件，Safari和其他服务中内置的新功能，它允许您随机化，唯一的电子邮件转发到您的主帐户。这意味着您可以将您的电子邮件从服务中隐藏，以增加隐私。

iCloud+ also includes unlimited HomeKit Secure Video cameras. Currently, iCloud users are limited to five cameras per account, but this limit has been removed for iCloud+.

iCloud +还包括无限的主页安全摄像机。目前，iCloud用户仅限于每个帐户的五个摄像机，但此限制已为iCloud +删除。
