Roku has acquired global rights for Quibi content, sources say for less than $100M, and will make it available free to stream in 2021 on the Roku Channel
消息人士称，Roku以不到1亿美元的价格获得了Quibi内容的全球版权，并将在2021年免费在Roku频道上进行流媒体播放

2021-01-09 17:47:48

Roku ROKU 5.23% agreed to acquire the rights to content from Quibi, a deal that will make the defunct short-form streaming service’s shows available on the biggest streaming-media player in the U.S.

Roku ROKU 5.23％同意从Quibi处获得内容权，该交易将使已解散的短格式流媒体服务的节目在美国最大的流媒体播放器上播放。

Roku paid less than $100 million to acquire the shows, according to people familiar with the matter. One person said it was “significantly less” than $100 million.

知情人士说，Roku以不到1亿美元的价格收购了这些节目。一位人士说，这比“ 1亿美元”少得多。

Roku will display Quibi’s content in the Roku Channel, an app that is filled with more than 40,000 movies and TV shows. Roku sells advertising on the channel and has kept it stocked with fresh content by negotiating with programmers who want their content to be carried on Roku’s media player.

Roku将在Roku频道中显示Quibi的内容，Roku Channel是一个应用程序，其中包含40,000多个电影和电视节目。 Roku在该频道上出售广告，并与希望将其内容在Roku的媒体播放器上播放的编程人员进行谈判，从而使该频道储备了新鲜的内容。

Roku will have the exclusive global distribution rights to Quibi’s content—more than 75 shows and documentaries—beginning this year, the companies said Friday. The Wall Street Journal earlier this week reported the two sides were in advanced talks.

两家公司周五表示，从今年开始，Roku将拥有Quibi内容的全球独家发行权（超过75场演出和纪录片）。 《华尔街日报》本周早些时候报道说，双方正在进行高级谈判。

The shows will be exclusive to Roku for the time that is left in Quibi’s exclusive two-year window with content creators. After that exclusivity window closes, Roku will retain the right to show the content until 2027, a spokesman said.

在Quibi与内容创作者的独家两年窗口中剩下的时间内，这些节目将是Roku专有的。一位发言人说，在排他性窗口关闭后，Roku将保留在2027年之前显示内容的权利。

Quibi’s deal with Roku is the latest deal to put exclusive content on its service, which is largely a compendium of movies and TV shows that can also be viewed elsewhere. Adding Quibi’s shows to the Roku Channel could give the streaming player more leverage in negotiations with advertisers, who may pay a premium to show their ads alongside shows viewers can’t watch elsewhere.

Quibi与Roku的交易是在其服务上投放独家内容的最新交易，这主要是电影和电视节目的摘要，也可以在其他地方观看。将Quibi的节目添加到Roku频道中可以使流媒体播放器在与广告商的谈判中发挥更大的作用，广告商可能会为与观众无法在其他地方观看的节目一起展示广告而付出额外的费用。

The catalog of Quibi, a service that was designed for people to consume entertainment in short increments on their smartphones, includes shows like “Murder House Flip,” a grisly mashup of home-improvement programming and true-crime; “Punk’d,” a revival of the MTV hidden-camera prank series; and “Dummy,” a series starring Anna Kendrick about a sex doll come to life.

Quibi的目录是一项服务，旨在使人们能够在其智能手机上短暂消费娱乐内容，其中包括诸如“ Murder House Flip”之类的节目，这些节目是对家庭装潢节目和真实犯罪的严格混搭； “朋克”，这是MTV隐藏式摄像机恶作剧系列的复兴；和“假人”，由安娜·肯德里克（Anna Kendrick）主演的有关性玩偶的系列节目应运而生

The deal is a coda for the tempestuous saga of Quibi, a streaming service that raised $1.75 billion and ultimately shut down barely six months after launching its app. The streaming service, founded by movie mogul Jeffrey Katzenberg and led by former Hewlett Packard Chief Executive Meg Whitman, was one of the most spectacular busts to come out of Hollywood in a generation.

这笔交易是Quibi的传奇故事，Quibi是一项流媒体服务，筹集了17.5亿美元，并在启动其应用程序后仅短短六个月就关闭了。该流媒体服务由电影大亨杰弗里·卡森伯格（Jeffrey Katzenberg）创建，并由前惠普首席执行官梅格·惠特曼（Meg Whitman）领导，是一代人以来好莱坞最壮观的破产事件之一。

Roku will face certain restrictions with regards to how it presents the Quibi content, people familiar with the matter said. The content must be presented in short-form as it was on Quibi: Roku can’t string together the 10-minute episodes of a series to create a movie or one long show.

知情人士说，Roku在如何呈现Quibi内容方面将面临某些限制。内容必须像在Quibi上一样以简短的形式呈现：Roku无法将一系列10分钟的剧集组合在一起来制作电影或一场长剧。

Some programmers are already working on new lives for their Quibi content. ViacomCBS VIAC -0.15% CBS, which made “Most Dangerous Game,” a thriller about human-hunting for Quibi, is in talks with Amazon.com AMZN 0.65% Prime Video service about making a version of the program there, a person with knowledge of the talks said.

一些程序员已经在为Quibi内容从事新生活。 ViacomCBS VIAC -0.15％的CBS制作了“最危险的游戏”，这是一部关于人类寻找Quibi的惊悚片，正在与Amazon.com AMZN 0.65％的Prime Video服务商谈在那里制作该程序的版本，这是一个有知识的人的谈判说。

The Roku deal may not go well with some of the shows’ producers. “We agreed to make a show to run on the Quibi platform, not Roku,” said one producer of shows for Quibi.

与某些节目的制作人达成的与Roku的交易可能并不顺利。 “我们同意制作一个在Quibi平台而不是Roku上运行的节目，” Quibi节目的制作人说。

Rob Holmes, Roku’s vice president of programming, said Roku is an ideal home for Quibi’s content because it can be viewed free by millions of people on the company’s services.

Roku程序副总裁Rob Holmes表示，Roku是Quibi内容的理想之选，因为该公司服务的数百万人可以免费观看。

“We’re in early days on this conversation, but I was talking to somebody yesterday, and they’re just excited to have their content available,” Mr. Holmes said. He said the deal includes some shows that haven’t made their debuts on Quibi yet.

Quibi, which has pledged to return $350 million to its early shareholders, is winding its operations down. The company is fighting a legal battle with video-technology firm Eko over a key feature of its streaming service, Turnstyle. In its lawsuit, Eko alleged patent infringement and theft of trade secrets. Quibi has denied the allegations, and the case is pending.

Quibi承诺将向早年股东返还3.5亿美元，目前该公司的业务正在逐步缩减。该公司正在就其流媒体服务Turnstyle的一项关键功能与视频技术公司Eko进行法律斗争。 Eko在诉讼中声称侵犯专利和盗窃商业秘密。 Quibi否认了这些指控，此案仍在审理中。
