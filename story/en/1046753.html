LONDON — British share trading app Freetrade told clients Friday that it had disabled purchases of U.S. stocks.

伦敦-英国股票交易应用程序Freetrade周五告诉客户，该公司已禁止购买美国股票。

Freetrade blamed the halt to U.S. stock purchases on a move by its currency exchange provider and their bank to limit the platform's trading volume. Freetrade uses U.K. fintech firm Currencycloud for foreign exchange transactions.

Freetrade将其股票交易提供商及其银行的举动归咎于美国股票购买的停止，以限制平台的交易量。 Freetrade使用英国金融科技公司Currencycloud进行外汇交易。

Barclays made an unexpected decision to significantly limit the volume of foreign exchange transactions it could process for Freetrade, a person familiar with the matter told CNBC. The person preferred to remain anonymous as they were not authorized to speak publicly.

一位知情人士告诉CNBC，巴克莱做出了意想不到的决定，即大幅限制可以为自由贸易进行的外汇交易量。该人希望保持匿名，因为他们无权公开发言。

The move affects buy orders that were placed ahead of the U.S. market open, Freetrade said, while sell orders will also be restricted for a short period of time at the market open.

Freetrade表示，此举将影响在美国市场开盘之前下达的买单，而在市场开盘时，卖单也将在短时间内受到限制。

"We received no warning of what we consider an extremely poor decision," Freetrade's co-founder and CEO Adam Dodds said in a statement Friday. "We are deeply unhappy with this decision and we are doing everything possible to rectify the situation."

"Due to high volumes, we will not be able to respond to individual queries about this at this time."

Freetrade, which has more than 300,000 users, says it's experienced a surge in activity this week as a result of wild volatility in equity markets.

拥有30万用户的Freetrade表示，由于股票市场的剧烈波动，本周活动活跃。

A number of heavily-shorted stocks, including GameStop and AMC, have skyrocketed this week as a gush of amateur investors inspired by the Reddit community WallStreetBets piled into such stocks, resulting in massive losses for some hedge funds.

受Reddit社区WallStreetBets大量涌入的业余投资者的涌入，包括GameStop和AMC在内的许多严重做空的股票在本周飙升，导致一些对冲基金蒙受巨大损失。

U.S. investing app Robinhood on Thursday was criticized by users after restricting trading in thirteen stocks, including GameStop and AMC. The firm subsequently said it would resume trades in those securities.

在限制GameStop和AMC等13只股票的交易后，周四美国投资应用程序Robinhood受到用户批评。该公司随后表示将恢复这些证券的交易。

Robinhood said its decision to restrict trading was necessary in order to comply with capital requirements mandated by the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission for broker dealers.

Robinhood also restricted trading in cryptocurrencies on Friday, halting instant deposits for crypto purchases "due to extraordinary market conditions." The move came as bitcoin and meme-inspired token dogecoin saw huge rallies.
