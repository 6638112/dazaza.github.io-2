通过以太网传输无线电信号

2020-11-08 22:41:55

This works by switching between 10Mbps and 100Mbps, which resultsin a change of the electromagnetic radiation that leaks from the devices.Switching to 100Mbps produces a signal at 125MHz, which is used totransmit morse code.

它的工作原理是在10 Mbps和100 Mbps之间切换，这会导致设备泄漏的电磁辐射发生变化。切换到100 Mbps会产生125 MHz的信号，用于传输莫尔斯电码。

./etherify1.sh /tmp/secret.txt #to leak out the contents of /tmp/secret.txt./etherify1.sh # or just to send the standard text

./etherify1.sh/tmp/Secret.txt#泄漏/tmp/Secret.txt的内容。/etherify1.sh#或仅发送标准文本。

Tested on 2 raspberry pi 4 running Raspbian 10connected together via 2m ethernet cableThis probably works by loading the supply voltage when the packetsare generated. A change of voltage probably changes the frequencyof some clock slightly, thus generating FSK (F1A to be exact).

在运行Raspbian 10的2个覆盆子pi 4上进行测试，通过2M以太网电缆连接在一起这可能是通过在产生数据包时加载电源电压来工作的。电压的变化可能会略微改变某些时钟的频率，从而产生FSK(准确地说是F1a)。

./etherify2.sh /tmp/secret.txt #to leak out the contents of /tmp/secret.txt./etherify2.sh # or just to send the standard text

./etherify2.sh/tmp/Secret.txt#泄漏/tmp/Secret.txt的内容。/etherify2.sh#或仅发送标准文本。

Both were tested on 2 raspberry pi 4 connected together via a 2methernet cable included in the raspberry pi starter kit. The choice ofhardware was done so that it would be simple to reproduce it anywhere.The tests were also done with other hardware, etherify1.sh works with mosthardware, etherify2.sh works only with some.

这两种测试都是在2个覆盆子pi 4上进行的，这些覆盆子pi 4通过覆盆子pi初学者工具包中包含的2Methernet电缆连接在一起。硬件的选择是为了便于在任何地方复制它。测试也是使用其他硬件进行的，etherify1.sh可以与一些硬件一起使用，而etherify2.sh只能与一些硬件一起使用。

Please tune the receiver to around 125MHz in CW mode with avery narrow filter. Sometimes AM mode can also be used. The testswere performed with a Yaesu FT-817 receiver with a 500Hz CW filter(cw decoded by ear), and with and SDR receiver using an rtl-sdr dvb-tdongle, with gqrx as the receiver and fldigi as the morse decoder (ordecoded by ear).

请将接收器调到125 MHz左右，在CW模式下使用非常窄的滤波器。有时也可以使用AM模式。测试用YAEU FT-817接收器和SDR接收器进行，前者带有500 Hz连续波滤波器(CW由EAR解码)，后者使用RTL-SDR DVB-TDONGLE，GQRX作为接收器，fldigi作为Morse解码器(由EAR编码)，而SDR接收器使用RTL-SDR DVB-TDONGLE，GQRX作为接收器，fldigi作为Morse解码器(由EAR编码)。

During tests etherify1.sh could be received at a distance of 100m,and etherify2.sh could be received at a distance of 30m.

在测试中，在100m处可以接收到etherify1.sh，在30m处可以接收到etherify2.sh。

software decoders are very bad at decoding morse code in the presenceof interference, and with imperfect timing. If you want to assess if thesignal is decodable, then get someone who can receive by ear(such as an experienced amateur radio operator).Humans are way better at this.

软件解码器在有干扰的情况下解码莫尔斯电码的能力很差，而且定时也不准确。如果你想评估信号是否可解码，那就找一个能靠耳朵接收信号的人(比如经验丰富的业余无线电操作员)。人类在这方面做得更好。
