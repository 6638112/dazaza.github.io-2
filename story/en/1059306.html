Project Veritas founder James O'Keefe, whose account Twitter blocked last week, sues Twitter in NY accusing the company of “false and defamatory” statements
Project Veritas创始人James O'keefe上周被阻止的账户被阻止，在纽约州的Twitter指责“虚假和诽谤”陈述

2021-04-20 08:45:10

James O’Keefe, the right-wing founder of Project Veritas, filed a defamation lawsuit against Twitter Monday afternoon, claiming the social media giant falsely accused him of operating fake accounts when it permanently banned him from the platform last week.

詹姆斯奥基特（James O'Keefe）项目Veritas的右翼创始人提起了诽谤诉讼，周一下午对Twitter提起诽谤诉讼，声称社交媒体巨头在上周永久地禁止他的平台时，社会媒体巨头被错误地指责他经营着假账户。

O’Keefe’s lawsuit comes just four days after Twitter suspended O’Keefe’s account and claimed he violated its platform manipulation rules, which bar users from setting up false accounts or using multiple accounts to “artificially amplify or disrupt conversations.”

Twitter暂停O'Keefe的账户后，奥基Eee的诉讼仅次于四天，并声称他违反了其平台操作规则，其中律师用户从建立虚假帐户或使用多个帐户以“人工放大或破坏对话”。

In a legal complaint Project Veritas says was filed Monday, O’Keefe said he didn’t operate fake accounts, insisting Twitter’s statements on his ban are “extremely damaging to his reputation” and undermine his investigative work.

在法律投诉项目中，Veritas说，周一提交，奥基德说，他没有经营假账户，坚持推特对他的禁令的陈述是“对他的声誉极其损害”，破坏了他的调查工作。

The suit also speculated that Twitter banned O’Keefe “in order to distract and detract from” embarrassing hidden-camera videos Project Veritas had posted of a CNN staffer (the legal complaint didn’t point to any hard evidence).

诉讼还推测，Twitter被禁止奥基菲“，以分散和贬低”尴尬的隐藏式相机视频项目Veritas发布了CNN工作人员（法律投诉没有指出任何硬证据）。

“The false accusation that Mr. O’Keefe operated ‘fake accounts’ is particularly damaging for Mr. O’Keefe because Mr. O’Keefe is a journalist,” the complaint read. “As such, his reputation for transparency and accurate reporting is fundamental to his profession.”

“奥基埃德先生的错误指责是对奥基特先生的责任尤为损害，因为奥基特先生是一名记者，”投诉读。 “因此，他对透明度和准确报告的声誉是他职业的基础。”

Defamation suits are generally tough to win in the United States. To prove defamation, public figures need to show not only that a defendant published false and damaging information, but that they also acted with “actual malice,” or reckless disregard for the truth.

诽谤诉讼通常很难在美国赢得胜利。为了证明诽谤，公众人物不仅需要展示被告出版了虚假和破坏性信息，而是他们也采取了“实际恶意”或鲁莽地无视真理。

Project Veritas casts itself as an investigative journalism outfit, and it’s best known for its hidden-camera sting operations against reporters and left-wing entities. The organization is often accused of making out-of-context claims or using deception to embarrass subjects, charges the group vehemently denies. CNN became the group’s most recent target this month, after Project Veritas posted videos appearing to show a CNN technical director suggesting the network tried to hurt former President Donald Trump’s reelection campaign. CNN did not respond to requests for comment.

Project Veritas将自己作为一种调查新闻服装，并为其隐藏的摄像机刺痛操作而闻名于记者和左翼实体。该组织往往被指控制定上下文的索赔或使用欺骗来令人难堪的主体，为集团强烈否认。 CNN成为本月的集团最近的目标，在Project Veritas发布视频出现展示CNN技术主任，建议网络试图伤害前总统唐纳德特朗普的裁量活动。 CNN没有回复评论请求。

Project Veritas is also suing the New York Times for characterizing its work as deceptive, and O’Keefe vowed to sue CNN last week.

Project Veritas也在起诉纽约时报，以表征其作为欺骗性的工作，并且奥基特向上周发誓来苏CNN。
