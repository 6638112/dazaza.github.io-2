自2015年以来，有人从一个休眠的比特币钱包中提取了约10亿美元

2020-11-05 19:39:39

Nearly $1 billion in bitcoin has been transferred out of a mysterious wallet that has been quiet since 2015.

自2015年以来，近10亿美元的比特币被从一个神秘的钱包中转移出来。

The haul of slightly more than 69,369 BTC— worth about $975 million at the time this post went live—was moved out in the past 24 hours, the bitcoin ledger shows. Alon Gal, co-founder and CTO of security firm Hudson Rock, was among the first—if not the first—to report the transaction.

比特币账簿显示，这笔略高于69369比特币的比特币--在这篇帖子上线时价值约9.75亿美元--是在过去24小时内被移走的。安全公司哈德逊岩(Hudson Rock)的联合创始人兼首席技术官阿隆·加尔(Alon Gal)是第一批(如果不是第一批)报道这笔交易的人之一。

“UNBELIEVABLE,” he wrote. “Someone was able to crack the password of the bitcoin wallet I reported on only a short time ago and spend the $1,000,000,000 that was inside it!” Gal went on to say that it wasn’t clear if the person responsible was the original wallet owner or someone who pulled off the unlikely feat of cracking the password.

“难以置信，”他写道。“有人破解了我不久前报道的比特币钱包的密码，花掉了里面的10亿美元！”盖尔接着说，目前还不清楚肇事者是最初的钱包所有者，还是完成了破解密码这一不太可能的壮举的人。

It was either the person who cracked the password or the original owner who may have noticed the recent articles about his wallet being circulated among hackers. The wallet was considered "dormant" since 2013. Either way this is pretty interesting.

要么是破解密码的人，要么是最初的所有者，他可能注意到了最近关于他的钱包在黑客之间流传的文章。自2013年以来，这个钱包一直被认为是休眠的。不管怎样，这都很有趣。

Tom Robinson, co-founder and chief scientist of blockchain analysis firm Elliptic, said on Twitter that the bitcoin wallet is the world’s fourth largest. He said he believed the funds came from sales on Silk Road, the underground market that peddled drugs, murder-for-hire, and other illicit goods and services before being brought down in 2013. Elliptic published this blog post earlier on Wednesday.

区块链分析公司Elliptic的联合创始人兼首席科学家汤姆·罗宾逊(Tom Robinson)在推特上表示，比特币钱包是世界第四大钱包。他说，他认为这些资金来自丝绸之路上的销售。丝绸之路是一个地下市场，在2013年被拆除之前，这个地下市场兜售毒品、雇佣谋杀和其他非法商品和服务。Elliptic在周三早些时候发布了这篇博文。

When the BTC moved out of Silk Road in 2013, Robinson said, they were worth roughly $350,000. In the intervening years, the exploding price of bitcoin saw their value soar to more than $955 million and beyond. The account has remained dormant since 2015, when someone transferred 101 BTC into BTC-e, a bitcoin exchange whose founder was arrested in 2017 on allegations that he committed $4 billion worth of bitcoin laundering.

罗宾逊说，2013年比特币公司搬出丝绸之路时，它们的价值约为35万美元。在其间的几年里，比特币价格暴涨，其价值飙升至9.55亿美元以上。该账户自2015年以来一直处于休眠状态，当时有人将101个比特币转入比特币交易所BTC-e，后者的创始人于2017年因被控从事价值40亿美元的比特币洗钱而被捕。

The person or party who transferred the windfall remains a mystery. It’s possible that it was either someone connected to Silk Road founder Ross Ulbricht or one of the many sellers who used the online crime bazaar. But there’s another possibility: an article published two months ago by Vice said that hackers had been trading the encrypted wallet on forums and underground marketplaces in hopes of recovering the cryptocurrency, which at the time was worth about $690 million.

转移这笔意外之财的人或当事人仍然是一个谜。有可能是与丝绸之路创始人罗斯·乌布利赫特有关联的人，或者是使用在线犯罪集市的众多卖家之一。但还有另一种可能性：《新闻周刊》两个月前发表的一篇文章称，黑客一直在论坛和地下市场交易加密钱包，希望能追回当时价值约6.9亿美元的加密货币。

The chances of successfully cracking the password that unlocked the wallet was widely viewed as a long shot. Passwords are generally long, and the encryption involved—a combination of AES-256-CBC and SHA-512—is extremely slow to process. What’s more, it was never certain that the wallet.dat file that was passed around was the real bitcoin wallet or a forgery.

人们普遍认为，成功破解解锁钱包的密码的可能性很小。密码通常很长，涉及的加密--AES-256-CBC和SHA-512的组合--处理起来极其缓慢。更重要的是，人们从来不能确定流传的wallet.dat文件是真的比特币钱包还是赝品。

For the moment, the quasi-anonymous nature of bitcoin transactions is concealing the identity of the person or party who transferred almost $1 billion worth of digital currency. Given the interest of law enforcement and the advances made in cryptocurrency forensics, you can bet people are working hard to solve this mystery.

目前，比特币交易的准匿名性掩盖了转移了价值近10亿美元数字货币的个人或交易方的身份。考虑到执法部门的兴趣和加密货币取证技术的进步，你可以打赌人们正在努力解开这个谜团。
