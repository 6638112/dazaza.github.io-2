Owncast: A self hosted Twitch alternative
Owncast：自托管的Twitch替代方案

2020-12-06 19:13:35

Owncast is a self-hosted live video and web chat server for use with existing popular broadcasting software. Point your live stream at a server you personally control and regain ownership over your content.

Owncast是可与现有流行广播软件一起使用的自托管实时视频和网络聊天服务器。将实时流指向您亲自控制的服务器，并重新获得内容的所有权。

In 2020 the world changed when everyone become stuck in their homes, looking for creative outlets to share their art, skills and themselves from inside their bedroom.

2020年，当每个人都陷入家中时，世界发生了变化，他们正在寻找创意渠道，从卧室内分享他们的艺术，技能和自己。

This created an explosion of live streaming on Facebook Live, YouTube Live, Instagram, and Twitch. These services provided everything they needed, an easy way to live stream to the world, and a chat for users to be a part of their community.

这使Facebook Live，YouTube Live，Instagram和Twitch上的实时流媒体激增。这些服务提供了他们所需的一切，提供了一种向全世界直播的简便方法，以及使用户成为其社区成员的聊天功能。

There deserves to be a better option for people. Something you could run yourself and get all the functionality of these services, where you could live stream to an audience and and allow them to take part in the chat, just like they’ve been used to on all the other services. There should be a independent, standalone Twitch in a Box.

人们应该有更好的选择。您可以自己运行并获得这些服务的所有功能，并在其中向观众直播并允许他们参与聊天，就像它们已经在所有其他服务上一样。 Box中应该有一个独立的独立Twitch。

The goal is to have a single service that you can run that works out of the box. Visit the Quickstart to get up and running.

我们的目标是使您可以运行一个单一的服务，即开即用。访问快速入门以启动并运行。
