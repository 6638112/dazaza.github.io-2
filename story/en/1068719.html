Lens – The Kubernetes IDE – Announces its biggest release, LENS 5
镜头 - Kubernetes IDE - 宣布其最大的释放镜头5

2021-06-30 04:15:28

Today, after months of development, we are proud to release Lens 5! This is our most significant release since we open sourced the project in March 2020. Lens 5 comes with multiple enhancements that will increase developer and operator efficiency when working with Kubernetes and other cloud native technologies.

今天，经过几个月的发展，我们很自豪地释放镜头5！这是我们在2020年3月开放的项目以来，这是我们最重要的发布。镜头5随着使用Kubernetes和其他云本机技术而增加开发人员和操作员效率的多种增强功能。

Lens 5 looks to improve collaboration and teamwork for anyone working with Kubernetes. We are lowering the barrier of entry for new users and improving productivity for experienced users. We are excited about this new release, and we hope you’ll enjoy it! The highlights of this new release are Catalog, Hotbars and a cherry on top: Spaces. Keep on reading to learn more about these cool new features.

镜头5希望改善与Kubernetes一起使用的人的合作和团队合作。我们正在降低新用户的进入障碍，提高经验丰富的用户的生产力。我们对这个新的发布感到兴奋，我们希望你能享受它！这个新版本的亮点是目录，热杆和顶部的樱桃：空格。继续阅读以了解更多关于这些很酷的新功能。

Organizing, accessing and finding clusters and related resources becomes challenging when systems and your cloud native footprint grows bigger. With Lens 5, we are introducing a unified catalog to bring all your clusters, services, workloads, tools, automations, and related resources together for easy access. You can use search, filters, categories, and labels to access the resources you need to work with easier than ever before.

当系统和云本机足迹变大时，组织，访问和查找集群和相关资源变得具有挑战性。使用镜头5，我们正在介绍一个统一的目录，将所有群集，服务，工作负载，工具，自动化和相关资源一起带到一起，以便轻松访问。您可以使用搜索，过滤器，类别和标签来访问您需要更容易工作所需的资源。

We had big plans for the 5 release, but couldn’t land all the features we wanted given the time constraints. We didn’t want to delay the Lens 5 release anymore so expect to see improvements to the catalog in the coming weeks and months!

我们有5个版本的重大计划，但不能降落我们所需的所有功能，因为时间限制。我们不想再延迟镜头5释放，以便在未来几周和月份看到对目录的改进！

We are all about getting things done, fast. Not long ago, we introduced a command palette that allows users to perform specific keyboard shortcuts, improving accessibility and efficiency. With Lens 5, we took another major step to make things faster. Say hi to Hotbar! If you are a gamer, you know what I’m talking about 😊.

我们都是关于让事情做的事情快。不久前，我们介绍了一个命令调色板，允许用户执行特定的键盘快捷键，提高可访问性和效率。凭借镜头5，我们采取了另一个重大步骤来更快地制造。打个招呼到Hotbar！如果你是游戏玩家，你知道我在说什么♥。

Hotbar is now the main navigation, allowing users to pick and choose the most important and commonly used functions (such as open cluster) from the catalog and assign those to the hotbar. Users have access to multiple hotbars, switch quickly between them and customize to their liking for easy recall.

HotBar现在是主导航，允许用户从目录中选择最重要且常用的函数（例如打开群集），并将那些分配给HotBar。用户可以访问多个Hotbars，在它们之间快速切换并自定义为其喜欢轻松调用。

We are always looking for ways to improve the quality of life and experience for people working with Kubernetes. With Lens 5, you can take the amazing Lens experience and share it with your whole team. Join a Space and get instant access to all their Kubernetes clusters and cloud-native resources. Need some help? Invite a friend to help troubleshoot your local cluster. Seamlessly manage access to clusters across local, on-prem, or public clouds all from within Lens.

我们一直在寻找提高与Kubernetes的人们生活质量和体验的方法。使用镜头5，您可以拍摄惊人的镜头体验并与整个团队共享。加入空间并立即访问所有Kubernetes集群和云原始资源。需要一些帮助？邀请朋友帮助解决本地群集。在镜头内无缝地管理对本地，在线或公共云的群集的访问。

In order to do this, we are introducing a centralized cloud based service called Spaces. Sign up via Lens desktop application to get started for free. Oh, and this is just the beginning; expect to see more features available via Spaces in the near future!

为此，我们正在引入一个称为空格的基于云的服务。通过镜头桌面应用程序注册以免费入门。哦，这只是一开始;期望在不久的将来可以通过空格查看更多功能！

Sharing access to the Kubernetes cluster is not always easy. To be honest, it’s one of the most difficult processes you’ll encounter when working with Kubernetes. You might have to work with IAMs, have a bunch of dedicated tooling installed, get access to kubectl files, get those files to work with your kubectl and finally ensure you are on the same network with the target cluster API. It’s so painful, users might even bypass some of the security best practices! We want to take this pain away and make it super easy for Lens users to access and share access to clusters without compromising security.

共享对Kubernetes集群的访问并不总是容易的。要诚实，它是使用Kubernetes时遇到的最困难的过程之一。您可能必须使用IAMS，安装了一堆专用工具，获取访问kubectl文件，获取这些文件以使用您的kubect，并最终确保您与目标群集API的同一网络。这是如此痛苦，用户甚至可能绕过一些安全最佳实践！我们希望将这种痛苦远离，使其超级镜头用户可以访问并共享对群集的访问而不会损害安全性。

When you use Spaces, you’ll get the option to share access to your cluster. You can also easily accept invitations from others to access their clusters. To make this possible, we created an entirely new technology: Cluster Connect. It allows Lens users to connect any of their clusters to Spaces without requiring an inbound port to be enabled on the firewall. It utilizes end-to-end encryption to secure connections between users and clusters, eliminating the need for a VPN. This means there is no need to expose the Kubernetes API via the Internet. Developers and operators can easily access and work with their Kubernetes clusters from anywhere.

使用空格时，您将获得选项以共享对群集的访问权限。您还可以轻松接受其他人访问其集群的邀请。为了使这一点成为可能，我们创建了一个完全新的技术：群集连接。它允许镜头用户将任何群集连接到空格，而无需在防火墙上启用入站端口。它利用端到端加密来确保用户和集群之间的连接，从而消除了对VPN的需求。这意味着无需通过Internet公开Kubernetes API。开发人员和运营商可以轻松访问和与他们的Kubernetes集群从任何地方一起使用。

As always, we are all about open source. If you are interested in seeing how we implemented Cluster Connect, please see the project on GitHub! It’s fully open source, so anybody can verify how we implemented the security features.

一如既往，我们都是关于开放来源的。如果您有兴趣了解我们如何实现群集连接，请参阅GitHub上的项目！它是完全开放的来源，所以任何人都可以验证我们如何实现安全功能。

代表整个镜头团队，我要感谢我们精彩的社区，以满足他们的所有支持和反馈。我们社区提供的支持是无价的，我们感到非常幸运！
