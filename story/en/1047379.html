BTC transaction uses the same energy as driving 2,500 miles in Tesla S Model
BTC交易消耗的能量与在Tesla S模型中行驶2500英里所需的能量相同

2021-02-09 20:18:40

I'm starting to get increasingly annoyed by this discussion, so let's make a deal: I will state my argument as thoroughly as I can, and you shall do the same. Discussing with one-liners gives me a feeling we are reiterating the same propositions, both believing they counter each other.

我开始对此讨论感到越来越烦恼，所以让我们达成协议：我将尽我最大的努力陈述自己的论点，您也将照做。与单线讨论使我感到我们正在重申相同的主张，都认为它们是相互对立的。

Bitcoin is wasteful by design, that is correct, and mass adoption would lead to even more waste. What I wanted to point out, that the comparison is not correct due to the following reasons:

比特币在设计上是浪费，这是正确的，而大规模采用将导致更多浪费。我想指出的是，由于以下原因，这种比较是不正确的：

1) Energy can not be easily transported, so you can not use power plant to provide electricity to the town like ~2000 miles away. It is not possible.

1）能源不易运输，因此无法使用发电厂为约2000英里之外的城镇供电。这不可能。

2) Spare energy, so, is a thing that sometimes occures: for example, a power plant which uses water energy and provides electricity to the nearby city will have a lot of spare energy at night. There are a lot more situations where energy is there and no one can actually use it.

2）备用能源因此有时会发生：例如，使用水能为附近城市供电的发电厂在晚上会有大量的备用能源。在很多情况下，能量在那里存在，没有人可以实际使用它。

3) Saying it is "wasteful" to use this energy, is, so, incorrect - these systems are wasteful by design and we either do not know how to switch to better systems, or just didn't manage to do so. Mining bitcoin, hence, is just some way of actually utilizing this energy - not a good one, in my opinion, but "better than nothing".

3）说“很浪费”因此，使用这种能量是不正确的-这些系统在设计上很浪费，我们要么不知道如何切换到更好的系统，要么就没有做到这一点。因此，开采比特币只是实际利用这种能量的一种方式-在我看来，这不是一种好能源，而是“总比没有好”。

What is actually worse than nothing is the fact that when we actually start to optimize these systems, and, in the same time, bitcoin adoption will increase, the incentives will come to conflict - which will need to be solved by either adopting less energy-demanding medium of exchange (like Nano) or pushing some L2 solution (like centralized parties issuing bitcoin-pegged asset, thus having centralized and censorable mean of exchange and decentralized store of value; nightmare scenario in my opinion but likely to happen).

真正比没有糟的是，当我们真正开始优化这些系统时，与此同时，比特币的采用将增加，激励机制将发生冲突-这需要通过减少能耗来解决-要求交换媒介（例如Nano）或推动某种L2解决方案（例如集中交易方发行与比特币挂钩的资产，从而具有集中且可审查的交易方式和分散的价值存储；我认为噩梦场景很可能会发生）。
