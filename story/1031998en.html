CodeSandbox是一家为协作应用程序开发构建基于网络的工具的公司，在EQT Ventures的带领下筹集了1270万美元，使其融资总额达到1500万美元

2020-10-29 21:25:42

Amsterdam-based CodeSandbox has raised $12.7 million to help teams collaborate on the development of web-based applications. With teams becoming more distributed and development cycles accelerating, the company sees an opportunity to facilitate the creative process by making it easy for non-developers to participate.

总部位于阿姆斯特丹的CodeSandbox已经筹集了1270万美元，用于帮助团队合作开发基于网络的应用程序。随着团队变得更加分散，开发周期加快，该公司看到了通过让非开发人员更容易参与来促进创意过程的机会。

EQT Ventures led the round, with participation from previous investors, including Kleiner Perkins, Arches Capital, and angel investor Guillermo Rauch. The company, which has now raised a total of $15 million, plans to use the money to launch Pro Workspaces and monetize its surprising growth.

EQT Ventures领导了这轮融资，之前的投资者参与其中，包括凯鹏华盈(Kleiner Perkins)、Arches Capital和天使投资者吉列尔莫·劳赫(Guillermo Rauch)。该公司现在已经总共筹集了1500万美元，计划用这笔钱推出Pro Workspace，并将其令人惊讶的增长货币化。

“We want to expand our use cases for CodeSandbox,” CEO and cofounder Ives van Hoorne said. “We want to make it possible for anyone on the product team to contribute to the final results.”

“我们希望扩大CodeSandbox的使用案例，”首席执行官兼联合创始人艾夫斯·范·霍恩(Ives Van Hoorne)说。“我们希望让产品团队中的任何人都能为最终结果做出贡献.”

CodeSandbox is part of a broader move to simplify coding and make it more accessible — commonly referred to as “low code” or “no code.” It’s a big trend, which means CodeSandbox will also face some tough competitors, like Airtable, which recently raised $185 million at a $2.585 billion valuatio n. On the collaboration side, GitHub’s Atom text editor lets coders work together.

CodeSandbox是一个更广泛的举措的一部分，目的是简化编码并使其更容易访问-通常被称为“低码”或“无码”。这是一个大趋势，这意味着CodeSandbox也将面临一些强有力的竞争对手，比如Airtable，它最近以25.85亿美元的估值筹集了1.85亿美元。在协作方面，GitHub的Atom文本编辑器可以让程序员协同工作。

Founded by van Horne and Bas Buursma in 2017, CodeSandbox bridges the collaboration and low-code elements by creating a browser-based service. The concept occurred to van Horne a few years ago while he was on vacation. Team members kept asking him questions, but he only had a phone with him and couldn’t access the development environment.

CodeSandbox由Van Horne和Bas Buursma于2017年创立，通过创建基于浏览器的服务在协作和低代码元素之间架起了桥梁。这个概念是范霍恩几年前度假时想到的。团队成员不停地问他问题，但他只带了一部手机，无法访问开发环境。

While there are web-based tools for individual coders, CodeSandbox is expanding this idea by making projects available to teams.

虽然有针对单个程序员的基于Web的工具，但CodeSandbox正在通过向团队提供项目来扩展这一想法。

In addition, coding projects are presented in a way that allows product designers and managers, who may not have coding skills, to directly make or accept changes. This avoids the cumbersome process of passing suggestions to developers, waiting for changes, and reviewing them before moving to the next step.

此外，编码项目的呈现方式允许可能没有编码技能的产品设计人员和经理直接进行或接受更改。这避免了将建议传递给开发人员、等待更改并在进入下一步之前对其进行审查的繁琐过程。

The cofounders started the service as a side project. It was only after usage began to surge two years ago that they decided to turn it into an official company. CodeSandbox’s more than 2.5 million monthly users have together created over 10 million projects on the platform.

联合创始人开始了这项服务，作为一个附带项目。直到两年前使用量开始激增后，他们才决定将其转变为一家官方公司。CodeSandbox的每月用户超过250万，在该平台上总共创建了超过1000万个项目。

While CodeSandbox has so far focused on collaboration, the upcoming Pro Workspaces product will introduce features that make projects accessible to non-developers.

虽然CodeSandbox到目前为止一直专注于协作，但即将推出的Pro Workspace产品将引入一些功能，让非开发人员可以访问项目。

Meanwhile, EQT’s investment was the eighth deal prompted by its AI-driven Motherbrain tool. The data being crunched highlighted CodeSandbox’s early seed investment, as well as factors like its rapidly growing userbase, prompting partners to reach out to the company when it wasn’t looking to raise money.

与此同时，EQT的投资是其人工智能驱动的MotherBrain工具推动的第八笔交易。正在处理的数据突显了CodeSandbox的早期种子投资，以及其快速增长的用户群等因素，促使合作伙伴在该公司不打算融资的时候与其接触。

In talking with EQT, van Hoorne said he was surprised how much the firm’s partners seemed to know about CodeSandbox’s business. When the company finally did decide to raise another round, EQT was at the top of the list.

在接受EQT采访时，范·霍恩表示，他很惊讶该公司的合伙人似乎对CodeSandbox的业务了解如此之多。当该公司最终决定再次融资时，EQT位居榜首。

“We were happy to see the fruits from our data-driven proactive approach with CodeSandbox,” EQT analytics partner Henrik Landgren said. “We saw this company early on, researched the space, tracked them, and then decided to reach out. Motherbrain also helped us to find and compare the various other related companies, which made us even more excited about CodeSandbox.”

EQT分析合伙人Henrik Landgren说：“我们很高兴看到我们的CodeSandbox数据驱动的主动方法取得了成果。”“我们很早就看到了这家公司，研究了这个空间，跟踪了他们，然后决定联系他们。MotherBrain还帮助我们找到并比较了其他各种相关公司，这让我们对CodeSandbox更加兴奋。“
