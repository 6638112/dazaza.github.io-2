Harpoon V
鱼叉V

2021-01-20 01:13:40

In July 1976 a young naval officer made the short walk from his warship to a destroyer tender docked nearby. Lieutenant (JG) Larry Bond returned to the USS McKean with a precious copy of the NAVTAG wargame. And because it was a Secret document, he promptly signed it in to his ship’s classified material locker. NAVTAG (Naval Tactical Game) was an official war game used to train U.S. Navy officers how to fight with their ships. It was a great training aid, but its classified status created a bureaucratic barrier to playing it, so it rarely came out of the safe. What Bond thought was needed was a non-classified version which could be played more easily. It was the beginning of the now famous Harpoon wargame lineage.

1976年7月，一位年轻的海军军官从他的战舰步行到附近停靠的一艘驱逐舰招标地点。陆军中尉（JG）拉里·邦德（Larry Bond）带着一本珍贵的NAVTAG战争游戏返回了麦肯号航空母舰。由于这是一个秘密文件，因此他迅速将其登录到了该船的机密材料储物柜中。 NAVTAG（海军战术游戏）是一种官方的战争游戏，用于训练美国海军军官如何与船只作战。这是一个很好的训练辅助工具，但是它的机密地位给玩它带来了官僚主义的障碍，因此它很少脱离保险柜。邦德认为需要的是一个非机密版本，可以更轻松地播放。这是现在著名的鱼叉战争游戏血统的开始。

The 5th iteration, Harpoon V, has just been released by Larry Bond and Chris Carlson. The Harpoon series has been a favorite of naval academies, other professional militaries, and amateur players alike since its inception.

Larry Bond和Chris Carlson刚刚发布了第五个版本HarpoonV。自成立以来，“鱼叉”系列一直是海军院校，其他专业军队和业余玩家的最爱。

Harpoon has stuck around because of its realism, and constant evolution. Many games come and go but Harpoon has stuck. And Harpoon V promises to be every bit as relevant as the past versions, even 40 years after the first. So where did it come from and what sets it apart?

鱼叉因其真实性和不断发展而坚持不懈。很多游戏来来去去，但是鱼叉卡住了。而且，鱼叉V承诺与过去的版本息息相关，甚至比第一个版本晚40年。那么它是从哪里来的呢？

When Bond released the first version in April 1980 it was an instant success, even winning the H.G. Wells award in 1981. Bond knew all about wargames, being an associate of Dave Arneson of Dungeons & Dragons fame. Arneson’s company even publish the first two editions. While it was popular with the civilian audience, it was also a hit with professional war fighters. It was easier to play than NAVTAG, and free from classified material, but retained the realism needed in a navy setting.

当邦德在1980年4月发布第一个版本时，取得了立竿见影的成功，甚至在1981年赢得了H.G.威尔斯奖。龙成名。 Arneson的公司甚至出版了前两个版本。尽管它在平民观众中很受欢迎，但也受到专业战争战士的欢迎。它比NAVTAG更容易玩，而且没有机密材料，但保留了海军环境中所需的真实感。

Arneson was not the only famous person associated with the game. Upcoming author Tom Clancy bought a copy of Harpoon and began corresponding with Larry Bond. Clancy used the game during his research for his first novel, The Hunt for Red October. His second book, Red Storm Rising, was based on scenarios tested out playing Harpoon. The bona fide wargaming gave the book a level of realism and credibility which sets it apart from many other Techno Thrillers. Bond was also Clancy’s co-author on the book.

Arneson不是唯一与游戏相关的名人。即将到来的作家汤姆·克兰西（Tom Clancy）购买了鱼叉的副本，并开始与拉里·邦德（Larry Bond）通讯。克兰西在研究第一部小说《红色十月的狩猎》时就使用了这款游戏。他的第二本书《红色风暴的升起》是根据在玩鱼叉游戏中经过测试的场景编写的。真正的战争游戏使该书具有一定的真实性和可信度，使其与许多其他《 Techno Thrillers》区分开来。邦德还是这本书的克兰西的合著者。

Red Storm Rising was essentially a Soviet Invasion of Europe war game written as a story. It was a scenario familiar to naval planners. So if you have ever wondered why Russia’s Tu-22 Backfire bombers featured so prominently, it was a real-world concern of NATO navies. Armed with powerful supersonic missiles, these could overwhelm all but the latest warships. It was the threat that AEGIS and the F-14 Tomcat were primarily intended to counter.

Red Storm Rising本质上是作为故事写成的苏联入侵欧洲战争游戏。这是海军计划人员熟悉的情况。因此，如果您曾经想知道为什么俄罗斯的Tu-22 Backfire轰炸机如此突出，那是北约海军的现实世界关注点。配备有强大的超音速导弹，除了最新的军舰外，它们可能不堪重负。这是AEGIS和F-14雄猫的主要威胁。

But Red Storm Rising could not be a literal narration of the wargames. To start with, the Soviets had to be winning in the first part of the book, so Clancy had to draw on his strategic genius to think up plot twists to keep the story on track.

但是《红色风暴崛起》并不是战争游戏的字面叙述。首先，苏维埃必须在本书的第一部分中获胜，因此克兰西（Clancy）必须利用他的战略天才来思考情节上的曲折，以使故事步入正轨。

With the success of Red Storm Rising, computer game companies queued up to create a digital version. In the end Sid Meier, the creator of Civilization, made the first Harpoon-based computer game. It was released on the Commodore 64 in 1988. It was a great game, but computer hardware of the time meant that it had to be limited to submarine warfare. Bond was already working on a more complete version however, which was released the next year simply as Harpoon.

随着Red Storm Rising的成功，计算机游戏公司开始排队创建数字版本。最后，文明的创造者西德·迈耶（Sid Meier）制作了第一个基于鱼叉的电脑游戏。它于1988年在Commodore 64上发布。这是一款出色的游戏，但是当时的计算机硬件意味着它必须仅限于海战。邦德已经在开发一个更完整的版本，但是第二年以鱼叉的形式发布。

This was at a time when even the latest home computers, 286s, were struggling to run the complex models involved in a simulation like Harpoon. A regiment of Backfires all launching their missiles was still enough to strain the 1990s computer hardware though. Smoke and mirrors could be used to simplify the mechanics, but each missile needed to be modeled separately to meet the Harpoon standards of realism. Consequently accuracy was, to the extent possible, maintained.

当时，即使是最新的家用计算机286都在努力运行涉及鱼叉之类的模拟中的复杂模型。虽然全部发射导弹的一支“逆火”部队仍然足以使1990年代的计算机硬件紧张。烟雾和镜子可以用来简化机械，但是每种导弹都需要分别建模以符合鱼叉的现实主义标准。因此，尽可能地保持了准确性。

Harpoon was a classic computer game of the 1990s. When I told people that I was writing an article more than one seasoned sailor told me that playing Harpoon was what got them into the Navy. But although multiple navies have used the computer game, it has not proven as popular as the underlying miniatures game, such as the latest Harpoon V.

鱼叉是1990年代的经典电脑游戏。当我告诉人们我正在写一篇文章时，不止一个经验丰富的水手告诉我，扮演鱼叉是使他们进入海军的原因。但是，尽管有多个海军使用了该计算机游戏，但尚未证明它像底层的微型游戏一样受欢迎，例如最新的鱼叉V。

I asked Larry Bond why this might be. The computer version was real time, as a single player might expect, but the table-top versions are turn-based. This gives the players time to think, which it turns out is important to navies. Bond told me that the ‘black box’ of computer game engines dulled the training experience. In a computer game if you miss a target, you move on. In the military setting, you want the players to understand why they missed and how they can increase the chances of success when they fire again. They need to understand how it works under the hood.

我问拉里·邦德为什么会这样。正如单个玩家可能期望的那样，计算机版本是实时的，但桌面版本是回合制的。这使玩家有时间思考，事实证明这对海军很重要。邦德告诉我，计算机游戏引擎的“黑匣子”使培训体验变钝了。在计算机游戏中，如果您错过了目标，您将继续前进。在军事环境中，您希望玩家理解他们为什么会错过，以及如何在再次射击时增加成功的机会。他们需要了解它是如何工作的。

This translates across all of Bond’s wargames, not just naval scenarios. Persian Incursion, released in 2010, simulates an Israeli air strike on the Natanz nuclear site in Iran. It was played by various government departments. Another factor which Persian Incursion amply demonstrates is that Harpoon is not ‘balanced’ in the way that most real-time-strategy (RTS) games are. The two sides may not be equivalent. Instead it offers realistic outcomes. It is a simulation which is also a game, rather than a game which pretends to be simulation.

这不仅涉及海军场景，还涉及邦德所有的战争游戏。 2010年发布的《波斯入侵》模拟了以色列对伊朗纳坦兹核基地的空袭。它由各个政府部门演奏。波斯入侵充分证明了另一个因素，就是鱼叉没有像大多数实时战略（RTS）游戏那样“平衡”。双方可能并不相同。相反，它提供了现实的结果。它是一种模拟，也是一种游戏，而不是伪装成模拟的游戏。

Harpoon V promises to be a faithful continuation of the series, with many refinements over the earlier versions. It is still a tabletop game which is what naval training institutions will like about it. I asked Bond whether there were plans for a new computer game based on the series. It’s an idea that the creators are open to and have plenty of ideas. Bond and Carlson tell me that with advances in AI the computer players could have more personality and act less predictably. Unfortunately there is no current project in that space. Let us see where the future takes this classic wargame series.

鱼叉V承诺将是该系列的忠实延续，并对早期版本进行了许多改进。它仍然是桌上游戏，海军训练机构会喜欢它。我问邦德是否有计划根据该系列制作新的电脑游戏。这是一个想法，即创作者愿意接受并有很多想法。邦德和卡尔森告诉我，随着AI的进步，计算机玩家可能会拥有更多个性，并且行为方式难以预测。不幸的是，该领域目前没有任何项目。让我们看看这个经典的战争游戏系列的未来。
