Google is Testing a Dark Mode for Desktop Search
Google正在测试桌面搜索的暗模式

2021-02-21 08:07:02

Google is testing a dark mode for desktop search, Google confirmed to The Verge. If the test has rolled out to you and your system is set to dark mode, the background of Google’s search pages will be a very dark gray.

谷歌向The Verge证实，谷歌正在为桌面搜索测试一种暗模式。如果您已经进行了测试，并且系统设置为暗模式，则Google搜索页面的背景将为深灰色。

It’s unclear how widely the test has been rolled out. A couple of Verge staffers had their search pages automatically switch to dark mode, and 9to5Google and some people on Twitter have seen the new dark mode, too. But Google’s search pages are still white for me, even though I have dark mode turned on right now on macOS.

目前尚不清楚该测试的推出范围。一些Verge员工将其搜索页面自动切换为暗模式，而9to5Google和Twitter上的一些人也看到了新的暗模式。但是，即使我现在在macOS上启用了暗模式，Google的搜索页面对我来说仍然是白色的。

It’s also unclear if or when dark mode for desktop search might be officially rolled out more broadly. “We’re always testing new ways to improve our experience for our users, but don’t have anything specific to announce right now,” Google said in a statement to The Verge.

还不清楚是否或何时正式将台式机搜索的暗模式正式或更广泛地推出。 Google在给The Verge的一份声明中说：“我们一直在测试改善用户体验的新方法，但目前尚无具体消息宣布。”

This isn’t the first time a desktop search dark mode has appeared: 9to5Google reported in January that some people were seeing dark mode then.

这不是第一次出现桌面搜索暗模式：9to5Google在1月份报道称当时有人看到了暗模式。

If you’re a dark mode fan, it’s been an eventful few days. Changes to Twitter’s dark mode caught some people unawares last week when their dark mode unexpectedly got even darker. And Microsoft is now testing changes to Word to make entire documents dark when you’re using dark mode.

如果您是黑暗模式迷，那就过了几天。上周，对Twitter暗模式的更改使一些人意识不到，当时他们的暗模式竟然变得更加暗。 Microsoft现在正在测试对Word的更改，以在使用暗模式时使整个文档变暗。

Update February 10th, 5:18PM ET : Google confirmed to The Verge that this rollout was a test, so we’ve updated the headline and article to reflect that.

美国东部时间2月10日下午5:18：Google向The Verge确认了此次发布是一次测试，因此我们更新了标题和文章以反映这一点。
