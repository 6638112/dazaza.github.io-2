Warner Bros. Believes that Theaters Will Still Exist in 2023
华纳兄弟认为剧院将在2023年仍然存在

2020-12-25 05:04:52

Warner Bros. ruffled some feathers when it announced it would release all of its new 2021 movies simultaneously on HBO Max, but the company seems to be betting that theaters won’t become an apocalyptic wasteland. Variety reports that the company plans to release Furiosa, the prequel to Max Max: Fury Road, and The Color Purple first in theaters in 2023.

华纳兄弟公司宣布将同时在HBO Max上放映其所有2021年新电影时，有些some之以鼻，但该公司似乎押注剧院不会成为世界末日的荒原。据《综艺》报道，该公司计划在2023年首次在影院上映《马克斯·麦克斯：狂暴之路》和《紫色》。

Warner’s plan to launch next year’s films online is a great thing for consumers who would rather stay at home than risk contracting a deadly virus to see, say, Godzilla vs. Kong. But the plan immediately made enemies of some Hollywood veterans. Director Christopher Nolan called HBO Max “the worst streaming service” and accused Warner Bros. of not telling anyone about its plan until just 90 minutes before it was announced.

对于那些宁愿呆在家里而不愿冒险感染致命病毒以观看《哥斯拉与金刚》的消费者而言，华纳计划在明年在线发行电影的计划是一件好事。但是该计划立即使一些好莱坞老兵成为敌人。导演克里斯托弗·诺兰（Christopher Nolan）将HBO Max称为“最糟糕的流媒体服务”，并指责华纳兄弟公司在计划宣布前仅90分钟就没有将计划告知任何人。

Despite Warner’s rosy outlook on the existence of theaters, the signs on their marquees might not look the same by 2023. AMC has warned investors that it will run out of cash in the middle of January if it can’t find $750 million.

尽管华纳对剧院的存在抱有乐观的态度，但到2023年，剧院上的标志可能看起来并不一样。AMC警告投资者，如果找不到7.5亿美元，它将在1月中旬用光现金。
