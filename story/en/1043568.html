Epic acquires RAD Game Tools
Epic收购RAD游戏工具

2021-01-09 07:41:41

We are excited to share that Epic Games has acquired the technology and business of RAD Game Tools, a video game software development company whose products and technology appear in close to 25,000 games and are leveraged by the world’s leading gaming companies, including Epic. Together, Epic and RAD plan to integrate RAD’s powerful technology into Unreal Engine to benefit the developer community and gamers alike.

我们很高兴分享Epic Games已收购了视频游戏软件开发公司RAD Game Tools的技术和业务，该公司的产品和技术出现在将近25,000种游戏中，并受到包括Epic在内的全球领先游戏公司的利用。 Epic和RAD计划一起将RAD的强大技术集成到虚幻引擎中，以使开发人员社区和游戏玩家受益。

We know first-hand how impressive RAD’s compression technology is, having used it to improve the load time and quality of our most popular games – including Fortnite. The RAD team includes some of the world’s leading compression, video and game dev tooling experts, and we are thrilled to welcome them to the Epic family.

我们直接了解RAD的压缩技术令人印象深刻，并使用它来改善我们最受欢迎的游戏（包括Fortnite）的加载时间和质量。 RAD团队包括一些世界领先的压缩，视频和游戏开发工具专家，我们很高兴欢迎他们加入Epic系列。

As graphics in game development and beyond become more photorealistic and powerful, developers need best-in-class compression software that can manage increased data requirements without compromising quality. Members of the RAD team will partner closely with Epic’s rendering, animation, insights, and audio teams, integrating key tech and improvements across Unreal Engine and beyond. RAD and Epic combining forces will allow even more developers access to tools that make their games load and download faster, and offer their players a better, higher quality video and gaming experience.  RAD will continue supporting their game industry, film, and television partners, with their sales and business development team maintaining and selling licenses for their products to companies across industries - including those that do not utilize Unreal Engine.

随着游戏开发及其他方面的图形变得更加逼真和强大，开发人员需要一流的压缩软件，该软件可以管理不断增长的数据需求，同时又不影响质量。 RAD团队的成员将与Epic的渲染，动画，见解和音频团队紧密合作，在Unreal Engine以及其他领域集成关键技术和改进。 RAD和Epic的联合力量将使更多开发人员可以使用工具，使其游戏加载和下载速度更快，并为他们的播放器提供更好，更高质量的视频和游戏体验。 RAD将继续通过其销售和业务开发团队为他们的游戏行业，电影和电视合作伙伴提供支持，以维护其产品的许可证并将其销售给各行各业的公司，包括那些不使用虚幻引擎的公司。

Our work with Epic goes back decades, and joining forces is a natural next step given our alignment on products, mission, and culture. We both believe that solid technologies enable developers to build beautiful, performant, and reliable experiences. We're excited and humbled to join the amazing Epic team.

我们与Epic的合作可以追溯到几十年前，鉴于我们在产品，使命和文化上的一致性，联手自然是下一步。我们俩都相信可靠的技术使开发人员能够构建美好，高性能和可靠的体验。我们很高兴和谦虚地加入了令人赞叹的Epic团队。
