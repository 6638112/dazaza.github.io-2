Live Streams Go Down Across Cox Radio and TV Stations in Apparent Ransomware Attack
Live Streams跨Cox收音机和电视电台在明显的赎金软件攻击

2021-06-04 05:45:43

Live streams for radio and TV stations owned by the Cox Media Group, one of the largest media conglomerates in the US, have gone down earlier today in what multiple sources have described as a ransomware attack.

Cox Media Group拥有的广播电视电台的直播溪流在美国最大的媒体集团之一，今天早些时候在多种来源被描述为赎金软件攻击时已经下降。

The incident took place earlier this morning and impacted live streaming capabilities for the Cox radio and TV stations. Official websites, telephone lines, and other IT systems remained running.

这一事件今天早些时候发生了，并影响了COX广播电视台的实时流动能力。官方网站，电话线和其他IT系统仍然运行。

While live streams for most of the impacted TV stations have now returned online, most of the Cox radio streams are still offline at the time of writing.

虽然大多数受影响的电视台的直播流现在在线返回，但大多数Cox无线电流在写作时仍处于离线状态。

In the aftermath of the incident, some radio stations had to cancel programming, according to tweets shared by some Cox radio program hosts.

在事件的后果中，根据某些Cox无线电程序主机共享的推文，一些无线电台必须取消编程。

Companies like Hulu, which pick up the company’s streams, have also confirmed issues with the Cox live broadcasts earlier in the day.

Hulu这样的公司拿起公司的溪流，也在当天早些时候就Cox Live广播的问题确认了问题。

Today’s ransomware attack has also been the main talking point of several private online communities dedicated to TV and radio reporters, who noticed that some of their colleagues have not gone on air earlier today.

今天的赎金软件攻击也是致力于电视和收音机记者的若干私人在线社区的主要谈话点，他注意到他们的一些同事们今天早些时候在空中尚未播出。

While  The Record has not been able to compile an exact list of impacted Cox radio and TV stations, we have been able to confirm issues with streams from News9, WSOC, WSB, WPXI, KOKI, and almost all Cox radio stations.

虽然记录无法编制受影响的Cox无线电和电视台的确切列表，但我们已经能够从新闻9，WSOC，WSB，WPXI，Koki和几乎所有Cox无线电台的信息中确认有关的问题。

The Cox Media Group owns 57 radio and TV stations across 20 US markets. A spokesperson did not return a request for comment sent earlier today via email.

Cox Media Group拥有超过20个美国市场的57个广播电视台。发言人没有返回今天早先通过电子邮件发送的评论请求。

Sources have told  The Record that the company’s autonomous system, AS397123, has also disappeared from the internet DFZ ( default-free zone) in what appears to be the company’s attempts to deal with the attack.

消息人士讲述了该公司的自治系统，AS397123的纪录也从互联网DFZ（默认区域）消失在似乎公司试图处理攻击的情况下。

Today’s incident marks the second time a ransomware group has hit a major media conglomerate in the US. In September 2019, a ransomware gang hit  CBS-owned Entercom, the second-largest radio broadcasting network in the States, taking some radio stations offline.

第二次赎金软件集团在美国袭击了一家主要媒体集团的事件标志。 2019年9月，一家Ransomware Gang击中了CBS所拥有的Entercom，在各州的第二大无线电广播网络中占据了各种电台。

Ransomware attacks have also temporarily took down some big TV channels like  France’s M6 and US-based  The Weather Channel in isolated attacks in 2019.

赎金软件攻击还暂时占据了法国M6等大型电视频道，2019年在孤立攻击中的天气渠道。
