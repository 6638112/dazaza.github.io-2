T-Mobile推出“TVision”超薄付费电视捆绑包，每月10美元起，适用于剪线机的新玩法

2020-10-28 02:46:05

is taking an aggressive new swing at the U.S. pay-TV sector, threatening to further roil the market’s dynamics: The carrier announced a new suite of TVision internet packages, available nationwide, that start at just $10 per month.

正在美国付费电视领域发起新一轮攻势，有可能进一步扰乱市场动态：该运营商宣布推出一套新的TVision互联网套餐，在全国范围内提供，起价仅为每月10美元。

With the new over-the-top TV play, T-Mobile wants to attract cord-cutters — and also poach existing cable and satellite TV customers who are fed up with traditional pay TV’s high prices and restrictive channel packaging. The TVision services are available in-home and also on wireless apps anywhere in the U.S.

通过这部新的夸张电视剧，T-Mobile希望吸引剪线公司-同时挖走受够了传统付费电视高昂价格和限制性频道包装的现有有线和卫星电视客户。TVision服务可以在家中使用，也可以在美国任何地方的无线应用程序上使用。

T-Mobile also is introducing TVision Hub, a $50 Android TV-based adapter (with a remote) that plugs into the back of an HDTV to provide access to the TVision OTT services and 8,000-plus apps including Netflix and YouTube.

T-Mobile还推出了TVision Hub，这是一款售价50美元的基于Android电视的适配器(带遥控器)，可以插入高清电视的背面，提供对TVision OTT服务和包括Netflix和YouTube在内的8000多个应用程序的访问。

“People sure love TV — but they sure don’t love their TV provider,” T-Mobile CEO Mike Sievert said in livestream announcing the TVision lineup. He said incumbent cable and satellite TV providers are holding customers “hostage,” because they bundle in “live news and sports with hundreds of other channels you don’t want.”

T-Mobile首席执行官迈克·西弗特(Mike Sievert)在宣布TVision阵容的直播中表示：“人们当然喜欢电视，但他们肯定不喜欢他们的电视提供商。”他说，现有的有线和卫星电视提供商将客户扣为“人质”，因为他们“将直播新闻和体育与数百个你不想要的其他频道捆绑在一起”。

The new TVision services are available on platforms including iOS, Android (and Android TV), Amazon Fire TV and Apple TV. The OTT packages and the TVision Hub are scheduled to be available starting Nov. 1, starting first with T-Mobile postpaid wireless subscribers. Later in November, TVision will be available to legacy Sprint customers. In 2021, T-Mobile plans to offer the OTT services to prepaid wireless customers and those who don’t have T-Mobile as well.

新的TVision服务可以在iOS、Android(和Android TV)、亚马逊Fire TV和Apple TV等平台上使用。OTT套餐和TVision Hub计划从11月1日开始上市，首先是T-Mobile后付费无线用户。11月晚些时候，TVision将向传统的Sprint客户推出。2021年，T-Mobile计划向预付费无线客户和没有T-Mobile的客户提供OTT服务。

The entry-level Vibe ($10/mo.) features 30 non-sports/non-news channels like AMC, BET, Comedy Central, Discovery, Food Network, HGTV, MTV and TLC, with two concurrent streams. The three “live” TV packages, which include 100 hours of DVR recording and up to three streams, are: TVision Live TV ($40/mo.) with 30-plus sports, news and local TV stations including from Disney, ESPN, ABC, Fox, NBC, CNN/Turner; Live TV Plus ($50/mo.), which adds NFL Network and other sports nets; and Live Zone ($60/mo.), which adds NFL RedZone and a handful of others.

入门级Vibe(10美元/月)。提供30个非体育/非新闻频道，如AMC、BET、喜剧中心、探索、美食网络、HGTV、MTV和TLC，并有两个并发流。三个“直播”电视套餐，包括100个小时的DVR录制和最多三个流，分别是：TVision Live TV(40美元/月)。有30多家体育、新闻和地方电视台，包括迪士尼、ESPN、ABC、福克斯、NBC、CNN/Turner；Live TV Plus(50美元/月)，增加了NFL网络和其他体育网络；Live Zone(60美元/月)，增加了NFL RedZone和其他几个网站。

T-Mobile also is offering a selection of TVision Channels — with options for Showtime ($10.99/mo.), Starz ($8.99/mo.) and Epix ($5.99/mo.), available as standalone packages or with other the TV bundles.

T-Mobile还提供了一系列TVision频道，包括Showtime(10.99美元/月)、Starz(8.99美元/月)。和Epix(5.99美元/月)，可以单独购买，也可以与其他电视捆绑包一起购买。

“It’s maximizing choice where we think we can offer it,” said Robert Gary, senior VP of T-Mobile Entertainment.

T-Mobile娱乐公司高级副总裁罗伯特·加里(Robert Gary)表示：“这是我们认为可以提供的最大限度的选择。”

Through Dec. 31, T-Mobile will give subs who take one of the two priciest TVision plans (Live TV Plus or Live Zone) free access to Apple TV Plus for 12 months and a discounted Apple TV 4K set-top ($99 after rebate; it’s normally $179).

到12月31日，T-Mobile将向参加两个最昂贵的TVision计划(Live TV Plus或Live Zone)之一的用户免费使用Apple TV Plus 12个月，并提供Apple TV 4K机顶盒折扣(扣除折扣后为99美元，通常为179美元)。

Overall, the most radical change with T-Mobile’s new TVision services is that it splits entertainment and lifestyle channels into the separate Vibe package, with news, sports and local TV in the “live” tiers. But that also means that if you want a complete cable TV lineup, you’re still going to pay rates comparable to traditional pay-TV providers or internet OTT TV services like YouTube TV or Hulu With Live TV.

总体而言，T-Mobile新的TVision服务最根本的变化是，它将娱乐和生活方式频道拆分成单独的Vibe套餐，新闻、体育和地方电视在“直播”层。但这也意味着，如果你想要一个完整的有线电视阵容，你仍然需要支付与传统付费电视提供商或互联网OTT电视服务(如YouTube TV或Hulu with Live TV)相当的价格。

Notably, the TVision Live lineups are missing CBS broadcast networks, and T-Mobile does not offer HBO or HBO Max — part of AT&T’s WarnerMedia — as an a-la-carte option. The carrier’s OTT services also are not available on Roku devices at this point.

值得注意的是，TVision Live阵容没有哥伦比亚广播公司(CBS)的广播网络，T-Mobile也没有提供HBO或HBO Max(AT&；T‘s WarnerMedia的一部分)作为点菜选项。该运营商的OTT服务目前也不能在Roku设备上使用。

Regarding the absence of CBS local stations in the product, Gary said customers are able to subscribe to CBS All Access (which will be rebranded Paramount Plus in 2021) separately from TVision. “Those who want that full CBS experience can get it for $5.99 per month, and so they have that choice,” he said. T-Mobile is looking to add Roku support for TVision in the future, Gary added, and may consider exploring bringing HBO Max into the lineup.

关于产品中没有CBS本地电视台，Gary表示，客户可以独立于TVision订阅CBS All Access(2021年将更名为派拉蒙Plus)。他说：“那些想要CBS的完整体验的人可以每月5.99美元获得，所以他们有这个选择。”Gary补充说，美国电话电报公司希望在未来增加对TVision的Roku支持，并可能考虑探索将HBO Max纳入阵容。

The company touted the TVision relaunch as its latest “Un-carrier” move — T-Mobile’s snarky marketing tagline that it uses to diss its bigger rivals, AT&T and Verizon.

该公司将TVision的重新推出吹捧为其最新的“非运营商”举措-T-Mobile的尖刻营销口号，用来抨击其规模更大的竞争对手AT&；T和Verizon。

Indeed, T-Mobile’s low-priced TVision offerings are aimed most directly at AT&T — whose pay-TV rolls have crumbled dramatically over the last two years. For the third quarter, AT&T’s “premium TV” subscribers (DirecTV and AT&T TV broadband service) totaled 17.1 million, a loss of 3.3 million over the past 12 months. AT&T TV Now, the telco’s OTT service that was supposed to offset such losses, shrank to 683,000 (down 40% year-over-year).

事实上，T-Mobile的低价TVision服务最直接针对的是美国电话电报公司(AT&；T)--该公司的付费电视业务在过去两年里大幅缩水。第三季度，AT&；T的“付费电视”用户(DirecTV和AT&；T TV宽带服务)总计1710万，过去12个月亏损330万。AT&；T TV Now，该电信公司的OTT服务，本应抵消这些损失，但现在缩减到68.3万人(同比下降40%)。

The new TVision services are based on technology developed by startup Layer3, which T-Mobile bought in 2017. The carrier promoted the reskinned TVision Home service as “an alternative to cable TV” — but with a starting price of $100 per month, it wasn’t appealing to cord-cutters. In addition, Layer3’s model was to deliver pay TV through deals with broadband providers who could guarantee throughput; the existing TVision Home has had limited availability, in metro areas including Chicago, Dallas-Fort Worth, Los Angeles, New York City, Philadelphia, San Francisco, Washington, D.C., and Longmont, Colo. By contrast, the new TVision OTT television services work over any broadband service.

新的TVision服务基于T-Mobile于2017年收购的初创公司Layer3开发的技术。该运营商将重新换皮的TVision Home服务宣传为“有线电视的另一种选择”-但由于起步价为每月100美元，对剪线者来说并不具吸引力。此外，Layer3的模式是通过与可以保证吞吐量的宽带提供商达成协议来提供付费电视；现有的TVision Home在芝加哥、达拉斯-沃斯堡、洛杉矶、纽约市、费城、旧金山、华盛顿特区和科罗拉多州朗蒙特等大都市地区的供应有限。相比之下，新的TVision OTT电视服务可以在任何宽带服务上工作。

T-Mobile has previously catered to streaming-video users with its Binge On program, offering wireless customers unlimited access to partner services. In 2017, in what Sievert noted has been one of T-Mobile’s most popular customer perks, the company added “Netflix On Us,” providing Netflix for no extra charge to customers on T-Mobile unlimited family plans.

T-Mobile此前曾通过其狂欢计划迎合流媒体视频用户，为无线客户提供无限制的合作伙伴服务。2017年，西弗特指出，这一直是T-Mobile最受欢迎的客户福利之一，该公司增加了“Netflix on us”，向T-Mobile无限家庭计划的客户提供Netflix，不收取额外费用。
