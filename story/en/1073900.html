After Just 24 Hours 'Lost Ark' Becomes the Second Most Played Game in Steam History
仅仅24小时后，《迷失方舟》就成为蒸汽历史上第二大热门游戏

2022-02-17 18:18:56

Lost Ark has comfortably passed 1 million concurrent players after just 24 hours, becoming the second most played game in Steam history by concurrent counts. The Diablo-like MMO launched yesterday in the West, after Amazon Games collaborated with Smilegate RPG to localize and translate Lost Ark and make it available in English. It has now passed concurrent records for both Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and Dota 2, which regularly dominate the top of Steam’s most-played games.

仅24小时，《迷失方舟》就轻松地超过了100万名并发玩家，成为Steam历史上并发次数第二多的游戏。亚马逊游戏公司（Amazon Games）与Smilegate RPG合作，对《迷失方舟》（Lost Ark）进行本地化和翻译，并将其翻译成英文，之后，类似暗黑破坏神的MMO于昨日在西方推出。现在，它已经同时通过了《反击：全球进攻》和《Dota 2》两项记录，这两项记录经常占据Steam最热门游戏的榜首。

Lost Ark is so popular right now that it has experienced server issues and there’s a queue just to start playing. SteamDB lists concurrent players of Lost Ark at 1,311,842, passing CS:GO’s record of 1,308,963 players and Dota 2’s of 1,295,114. It’s not clear exactly how many of those players are actually actively playing the game and not sitting in a server queue, though.

Lost Ark现在非常受欢迎，它遇到了服务器问题，而且有一个队列要开始播放。SteamDB列出了1311842名《迷失方舟》的并发玩家，超过了CS:GO的1308963名玩家和1295114名Dota 2的记录。不过，目前尚不清楚这些玩家中到底有多少人在积极地玩游戏，而不是坐在服务器队列中。

Either way, it’s now second place on the top concurrent list behind only PUBG. It still has a long way to go to beat PUBG’s all-time peak of 3,257,248 players. Lost Ark is a second huge hit for Amazon Games, after New World set a concurrent record of 913,634 players four months ago.

不管怎样，它现在是仅次于PUBG的第二大并发列表。要打败普格队历史上3257248名球员的巅峰状态，还有很长的路要走。《迷失方舟》是亚马逊游戏的第二大热门，四个月前，新世界创下了913634名玩家的同期记录。

Lost Ark was originally released in 2019 in South Korea, and has millions of active players in South Korea, Russia, and Japan. It’s free-to-play and has also quickly risen to the top of Twitch, overtaking the popular Just Chatting category.

《迷失方舟》最初于2019年在韩国发行，在韩国、俄罗斯和日本拥有数百万活跃玩家。它可以免费玩，而且也很快上升到Twitch的榜首，超过了流行的聊天类。
