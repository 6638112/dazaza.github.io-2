寻找哲学家的键盘

2020-11-06 04:23:10

What if your keyboard had access to your memories? What if it could completeyour thoughts, not based on what you'd previously typed or learning genericresponsesor dumb rules, but based on a personaldatabase of places, books, web pages, and notes of all kinds?

如果你的键盘可以访问你的记忆呢？如果它可以完成你的想法，而不是基于你之前输入的东西，或者学习一般性的回复或愚蠢的规则，而是基于一个包含各种地点、书籍、网页和笔记的个人数据库，那会怎么样？

To give an idea of what this would look like, when you type in Japanese, whatyou type on the keyboard isn't exactly what shows up on the screen. Instead youcan type a romanized version of the text you want a few words at a time. Aftereach span you lock it in, and if you need conversion you choose one of a fewalternatives from a drop-down list. In some input systems, short definitionsmay be shown next to the current selection.

当你用日语打字时，让我们了解一下这会是什么样子，你在键盘上输入的内容与屏幕上显示的内容并不完全相同。取而代之的是，你可以输入你想要的文字的罗马化版本，一次几个字。在每个跨度之后，您可以将其锁定，如果您需要转换，您可以从下拉列表中选择一个替换选项。在某些输入系统中，当前选项的旁边可能会显示简短的定义。

Autocomplete in Japanese is based on a dictionary supplied with your operatingsystem, plus a user dictionary. If you frequently have to type something not inthe dictionary you can register it in a personal dictionary to have it come outjust like any other word. You can also get creative and register things thataren't normal words, like converting "home" to "123 Mt. Pleasant Drive".

日语自动补全功能基于操作系统附带的词典和用户词典。如果你经常要输入字典里没有的东西，你可以把它注册到个人字典里，这样它就会像其他单词一样出现。你也可以获得创造性，注册一些不是普通单词的东西，比如把家换成123毫升。愉快的驾车路线。

This mediated input opened up potential for cleverness in software keyboards,acronymically known as Input Method Engines (IMEs). Introducing emojiwas just a matter of adding them to a font and the dictionary - you didn't haveto tell people how to use them, as they'd just be another completion optionwhen you typed "beer" 🍻 or "love" 😍 or what have you.

这种中介输入开启了软件键盘(缩写为输入法引擎(IMES))的智慧潜能。引入表情符号只需将它们添加到字体和词典中--你不必告诉人们如何使用它们，因为当你输入啤酒🍻或爱你的😍或其他东西时，它们只是另一个补全选项。

Now we all have smartphones, and we all have mediated input, and emoji areeverywhere. Hooray. 🎊

现在我们都有智能手机，我们都有媒介输入，表情符号无处不在。万岁。🎊。

What if you could type "Here's that gyoza place I went to last week" andautocomplete a map link?

如果你能在这里输入我上周去过的那个杂货店，然后自动完成一个地图链接，会怎么样？

"Here's that book I mentioned at lunch." "This is that game with indirectcontrols." "This is the page for that meetup you should go to." "This is whereyou can get tickets for that museum show."

这就是我在午餐时提到的那本书。这是一款带有间接控制的游戏。这是你应该去的那个聚会的页面。这是你可以买到博物馆展览门票的地方。

Of course you can already share this information thanks to the universal powerof links, but what if it didn't need a context switch? What if taking notes onthings made it easier for you to share them with people? (This is, of course,part of the dream of the Memex.)

当然，多亏了万能的链接，你已经可以分享这些信息了，但如果它不需要上下文切换呢？如果做笔记能让你更容易与人分享，那会怎样呢？(当然，这是Memex梦想的一部分。)

Six years ago when I started work on Deltos for personal notetaking, one of my goals was to use the notes in Deltos as fodder for aphilosopher's keyboard, but that ended up falling by the wayside. It turns outworking with IMEs is difficult - every operating system has a completelydifferent way of handling them, and they often aren't well documented.

六年前，当我开始在Deltos上做个人笔记时，我的目标之一是把Deltos中的音符用作口吃师键盘的素材，但这最终被搁置了。事实证明，使用IME是很困难的--每个操作系统都有完全不同的处理方式，而且它们往往没有很好的文档记录。

In particular, IMEs often work on the principle that for any given input, thepotential outputs are clearly defined. In a dictionary file these are justshort bits of text. But if you can search through all your notes, then inputsand outputs aren't well defined, and just updating the dictionary isn't enough -you need search functionality.

具体地说，IME的工作原则是，对于任何给定的输入，潜在的输出都是明确定义的。在字典文件中，这些只是很短的文本片段。但是，如果你可以搜索你所有的笔记，那么输入和输出就不是很好的定义，仅仅更新词典是不够的--你需要搜索功能。

One idea is to avoid using the IME interface directly. A small application,just a search box where a selection is copied to the clipboard, would be enoughto realize the main goal of the philosopher's keyboard, while still minimizingcontext switching. It would also make it easier to create a cross-platform app.Maybe that's good enough.

一种想法是避免直接使用IME接口。一个小的应用程序，只是一个搜索框，其中的选择被复制到剪贴板上，就足以实现哲学家键盘的主要目标，同时仍然最大限度地减少上下文切换。这也将使创建跨平台应用程序变得更容易。也许这就足够好了。

The other problem is that most people don't have an explicit personal database,just a bunch of personal data locked up in silos. Fixing that is another andhonestly bigger step, but it can be handled one person at a time.

另一个问题是，大多数人没有明确的个人数据库，只是被锁在孤岛上的一堆个人数据。解决这个问题是另一个也是更大的一步，但它可以一次只有一个人来处理。
