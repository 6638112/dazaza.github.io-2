France Orders Tech Giants To Pay Digital Tax
法国命令科技巨头支付数字税

2020-11-26 05:38:29

The French Finance Ministry has sent out notices to big tech companies liable for its digital service tax to pay the levy as planned in December, the ministry said on Wednesday.

法国财政部周三表示，法国财政部已经向应对其数字服务税负责的大型科技公司发出通知，要求其按计划于12月支付该税。

France suspended collection of the tax, which will hit companies like Facebook and Amazon, early this year while negotiations were underway at the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development on an overhaul of international tax rules.

法国在今年年初暂停了税收的征收，这将打击像Facebook和Amazon这样的公司，而经济合作与发展组织（OECD）正在就全面改革国际税收规则进行谈判。

The Finance Ministry has long said it would collect the tax in December as planned if the talks proved unfruitful by then, which is what happened when the nearly 140 countries involved agreed last month to keep negotiating until mid 2021.

财政部长期以来一直表示，如果到那时谈判仍未取得成果，它将按计划在12月征税，这是当涉及的近140个国家上个月同意继续谈判至2021年中期时发生的情况。

“Companies subject to the tax have received their notice to pay the 2020 installment,” a Finance Ministry official said.

财政部官员说：“要缴税的公司已经收到了通知，要求他们支付2020年的分期付款。”

France last year applied a 3 percent levy on revenue from digital services earned in France by companies with revenues of more than 25 million euros here and 750 million euros worldwide.

法国去年对法国收入超过2500万欧元，全球收入7.5亿欧元的公司在法国获得的数字服务收入征收了3％的税。

The ministry had hoped to raise about 500 million euros this year from the tax, but the 2021 budget bill puts the figure at 400 million.

该部曾希望今年从税收中筹集约5亿欧元，但2021年的预算法案将这一数字定为4亿欧元。

Facebook’s stance is “is to ensure compliance with all tax laws in the jurisdictions where we operate.” Other tech companies have made similar statements.

Facebook的立场是“确保遵守我们运营所在辖区的所有税法。”其他科技公司也发表了类似的声明。

Paris has said it will withdraw the tax as soon as an OECD deal is reached to update the rules on cross-border taxation for the age of online commerce, where big internet companies can book profits in low-tax countries regardless of where their customers are.

巴黎已经表示将在达成经合组织协议后立即撤销税收，以更新在线商务时代的跨境税收规则，大型互联网公司可以在低税率国家预订利润，而不论其客户在哪里。

The talks stalled as the Trump administration became reluctant to sign on to a multilateral agreement ahead of the U.S. presidential election while the global pandemic added to practical difficulties in the negotiations, officials have said.

官员们表示，由于特朗普政府变得不愿意在美国总统大选之前签署多边协议，而全球大流行病给谈判增加了实际困难，因此谈判陷入僵局。

“We will levy this digital taxation mid December as we always explained to the U.S. administration,” French Finance Minister Bruno Le Maire told a Bloomberg event on Monday.

法国财政部长布鲁诺·勒·梅尔（Bruno Le Maire）周一在彭博社的活动中说：“我们将一如既往地向美国政府解释，我们将在12月中旬征收这种数字税。”

“Our goal remains to have an OECD agreement by the first months of 2021 because we remain deeply convinced that ... the best way of dealing with this key question of digital taxation is to get a multilateral agreement within the framework of the OECD,” he added.

“我们的目标仍然是到2021年头几个月达成OECD协议，因为我们仍然深信...解决数字税收这一关键问题的最佳方法是在OECD框架内达成多边协议。”他加了。
