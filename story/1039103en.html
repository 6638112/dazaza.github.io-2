The M1 MacBook Air: Perfection
M1 MacBook Air：完美

2020-12-11 23:28:36

How much do I love the new M1-powered MacBook Air? So much so that I bought it twice. How’s that for fanboy exuberance?

我对新的M1驱动的MacBook Air满意多少？如此之多，以至于我买了两次。那对于狂热的粉丝来说怎么样？

But I’m serious. I really did buy it twice. Not two MacBook Airs (Macbooks Ai r?), mind you. But when I heard about the first batch of M1 Macs I was so excited that I wanted to order whatever one would get to me the fastest. That means the standard-order new MacBook Air with 8GB of RAM. While I’m normally the kind of guy to max out RAM when I can, I was okay with this because I also thought I would actually end up buying (and keeping) the new M1-powered MacBook Pro a few weeks later.

但是我很认真我确实确实买了两次。请注意，不是两个MacBook Air（Macbooks Ai r？）。但是，当我听到第一批M1 Mac的消息时，我感到非常兴奋，以至于我想订购任何最快得到我的产品。这意味着带有8GB RAM的标准订购新款MacBook Air。虽然通常我会尽可能地扩大RAM，但我对此还可以，因为我还认为我实际上会在几周后最终购买（并保留）采用M1的新款MacBook Pro。

But then I got this new MacBook Air and it completely blew away the already insanely high expectations I had. It is so goddamn fast, with such insane battery life, that I’ve been living in a hyperbolic chamber the past few weeks.¹ There aren’t enough superlatives. I love this machine.

但是后来我得到了这款新的MacBook Air，它完全吹走了我已经疯狂的期望。真是太快了，电池寿命如此疯狂，以至于我过去几周一直住在一个双曲线的房间里。¹没有足够的最高级的东西。我喜欢这台机器。

So… instead of getting an M1 MacBook Pro,² I ordered another M1 MacBook Air. This time with my standard 16GB of RAM, and I’ll return the 8GB model. But I’m even a little torn about that for a reason I wasn’t expecting: the 8GB model amazingly has seemed like more than enough RAM for everything I do. Mind you, I’m not doing video editing or anything really intensive on it. But even Chrome — resource hogging, memory devouring Chrome — seems to run silky smooth on this machine.³ So I just consider 16GB future proofing.

因此，我没有购买M1 MacBook Pro，而是订购了另一台M1 MacBook Air。这次使用标准的16GB RAM，我将返回8GB模型。但是由于某种意料之外的原因，我对此感到有些不知所措：令人惊讶的是8GB型号的内存似乎足以应付我的所有工作。请注意，我没有在做视频编辑或其他任何真正需要做的事情。但是，即使Chrome（占用大量资源，吞噬Chrome的内存）在这台计算机上运行起来也丝般顺滑。³因此，我只考虑16GB的未来打样。

Have I mentioned that I love this machine? I really believe it’s my favorite new Apple product since the AirPods. I know some people may not consider this machine to be new as it’s essentially just new internals in the tried and true old shell — here’s where I’ll note that this is also my first Mac with the new/old keyboard, oh Sweet Jesus it’s good to have this thing back. But this is one of those things you have to try for it to be believed.

我是否提到过我喜欢这台机器？我真的相信这是自AirPods以来我最喜欢的新Apple产品。我知道有些人可能不会认为这台机器是新机器，因为它本质上只是经过尝试和真正使用的旧外壳中的新内部组件-在这里我会注意到这也是我的第一台配备新旧键盘的Mac，哦，天哪，这是很高兴把这个东西还回来。但这是您必须尝试使其相信的事情之一。

I thought we were beyond “traditional” computers making leaps in speed improvements. It’s not like my two year old MacBook Air felt slow — hell, even my five-plus year old iMac still feels fast (even if it can’t run macOS Big Sur). But those old machines do feel slow relative to this machine. There are just a million little things this M1 machine does faster, and they add up to real time savings. Even just browsing the web feels significantly faster.

我认为我们已经超越了“传统”计算机，从而在速度改进方面取得了飞跃。这并不是说我有两年历史的MacBook Air感觉很慢–地狱，甚至我五岁以上的iMac仍然感觉很快（即使它无法运行macOS Big Sur）。但是那些旧机器确实比这台机器感觉慢。这台M1机器做的事只有一百万个小事情，它们共同节省了实时时间。即使只是浏览网页，感觉也明显更快。

My other MacBook Air, the two year old one, is a work machine so I still have to use it. And I now dread it. Each and every day. It’s so much more cumbersome to use from the moment you open the lid and you have to wait for the screen to pop on. Again, in a vacuum these seconds and micro-seconds saved sound silly. In the real world, they matter!

我的另一台MacBook Air已有两岁了，它是一台工作机，因此我仍然必须使用它。现在我害怕了。每一天。从打开盖子的那一刻起，使用起来非常麻烦，而且必须等待屏幕弹出。同样，在真空中，这些几秒钟和几微秒的声音保存起来很傻。在现实世界中，它们至关重要！

We haven’t even talked about the fact that this new MacBook Air doesn’t have a fan. While my beloved (RIP) 12-inch MacBook also didn’t have a fan, that thing would get toasty at the bottom, and it was often maxed out with its wimpy Intel chip. This thing never gets toasty. It never gets tested. And it never gets loud, like my work MacBook Air does.

我们甚至没有谈论过这款新的MacBook Air没有风扇的事实。尽管我心爱的（RIP）12英寸MacBook也没有风扇，但该产品在底部会显得很烤面包，而且它的笨拙的Intel芯片经常使这东西变得满满的。这东西永远不会烤面包。它永远不会得到测试。它再也不会像我的MacBook Air一样响亮。

Another true test of this machine: opening Music (the artist formerly known as iTunes). On the “old” MacBook Air, this took quite some time for whatever reasons. New MacBook Air? Instantaneous.

这台机器的另一个真实测试：打开音乐（艺术家，以前称为iTunes）。在“旧” MacBook Air上，无论出于何种原因，这都花费了相当长的时间。新的MacBook Air？瞬间。

Some other smaller tidbits: the new function keys are great. Having a dictation key is interesting, like Apple wants us to use that feature more. And they should, it’s good! Ditto for Spotlight search. But the real star of the show is the Do Not Disturb key. Focused work FTW. We’ve talked about instant-on, but the machine also instant-sleeps too when you hit the Touch ID key.

其他一些小窍门：新功能键很棒。拥有听写键很有趣，例如Apple希望我们更多地使用该功能。他们应该，这很好！同上用于Spotlight搜索。但是节目的真正明星是“请勿打扰”键。重点工作FTW。我们讨论了即时启动，但是当您按下Touch ID键时，机器也会即时休眠。

One thing that struck me as a bit odd: there is absolutely no branding on the outside of the MacBook Air box to let you know it’s an M1 chip inside. I couldn’t even find any fine print. Apple has never touted such innards, but still, I found this a little surprising when the Intel Macs are still on the market — though maybe that’s part of the point!

一件事让我感到有些奇怪：MacBook Air盒子的外面绝对没有品牌可以让您知道它是M1芯片。我什至找不到任何精美的印刷品。苹果公司从来没有吹捧过这种内脏，但是，当英特尔Mac仍在市场上时，我发现这有点令人惊讶-尽管这也许是重点！

That also speaks to something I briefly hit on above: this feels almost like a new host in an old shell. That is, this MacBook Air was designed around what Apple could do with the Intel chips, without such constraints, it feels like this shell is going to change soon. While there is a rumored MacBook Pro body change, I’m curious what they do with the Air as well. Does it go smaller, and fill the void left behind by the 12-inch MacBook? I hope so. Without a fan, it’s really just a battery life choice at this point. Would I sacrifice an hour or two to have a machine that is half as light in a smaller frame?⁴ I think I would. Maybe we go back to a world where there’s an 11-inch Air alongside the 13-inch one? I miss that little guy. Though not the awful performance, battery life, or bezel. All of those things are now solved for!

这也说明了我在上面简要提到的内容：感觉就像是旧外壳中的新主机。也就是说，这款MacBook Air围绕Apple可以使用Intel芯片进行的操作而设计，没有这些限制，感觉这种外壳即将改变。尽管有传闻称MacBook Pro的机身有变化，但我很好奇他们也对Air做了什么。它会变小，并填补12英寸MacBook留下的空白吗？希望如此。没有风扇，这实际上只是电池寿命的选择。我会牺牲一两个小时来使一台机器在较小的框架中重量减轻一半吗？⁴我想我会的。也许我们回到了一个11英寸Air和13英寸Air的世界？我想念那个小家伙。尽管性能，电池寿命或边框都不尽人意。现在解决了所有这些问题！

Such a machine I think may be the perfect Mac. I know I’ve said this a lot over the years, but now that Apple has clearly solved performance and battery life with the M1, the extreme form factors are worth revisiting. For now, this is the most perfect Mac ever made.

我认为这样的机器可能是完美的Mac。我知道我已经说了很多年了，但是既然Apple显然已经用M1解决了性能和电池寿命问题，那么极端的外形尺寸是值得回顾的。目前，这是有史以来最完美的Mac。
