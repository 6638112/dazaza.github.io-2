Atom Finance, which lets users track investments and research holdings, raises $28M led by SoftBank's Latin America Fund, source says at a ~$150M valuation
Source估价率达到150亿美元，旨在追踪投资和研究控股的原子融资筹集投资和研究持有，筹集了2800万美元的估价

2021-06-23 22:05:37

SoftBank Group 9984 -0.18% is investing in startup investing platform Atom Finance, in a bet by the Japanese conglomerate that a retail trading boom is here to stay.

SoftBank Group 9984 -0.18％正在投资启动投资平台atom金融，在日本集团的赌注中，零售交易繁荣在这里留下来。

Atom Finance, founded in 2018, lets individuals track investments and research holdings. In addition to SoftBank, existing investors General Catalyst and Base Partners also took part in the latest $28 million funding round. The investment values Atom at around $150 million, said a person familiar with the matter.

Atom金融，成立于2018年，让个人跟踪投资和研究控股。除软银外，现有投资者通用催化剂和基础合作伙伴还参加了最近2800万美元的资金。熟悉此事的人表示，投资价值约为1.5亿美元。

Atom was founded by Eric Shoykhet, who covered financial services firms and other sectors from 2015 to 2018 at hedge fund Governors Lane. He believed falling trading costs would prompt more individual investors to trade. This meant that they would need new kinds of tools to guide them.

Atom由Eric Shoykhet成立，他在2015年至2018年涵盖了金融服务公司和其他行业，在对冲基金州长巷。他认为交易成本下降会促使更多的个人投资者进行交易。这意味着他们需要新的工具来引导它们。

In 2019, major brokerages launched no-commission stock trading to stave off the threat from digital upstart Robinhood Markets Inc. Now, all kinds of brokerages compete against each other for retail traders with free trading having become a booming business. It has been particularly transformative in the past year as more investors with free time during the pandemic have wielded  growing power over markets, sending meme stocks such as GameStop soaring.

2019年，主要经纪商推出了禁止佣金股票交易，以避免数字上台抢劫罗宾尼市场市场公司的威胁。现在，各种经纪人互相竞争，为零售交易员互相竞争，拥有自由贸易商成为蓬勃发展的业务。在过去一年中，在大流行期间有空闲时间的投资者在市场上挥舞着市场的权力，它一直在变革者在市场上挥舞着，将MEME股等飙升飙升。

Atom isn’t a trading platform but it has still been a beneficiary. Hundreds of thousands of users have signed up since its platform launched in 2019. Paying customers increased in the past year, though the firm declined to provide exact details about user numbers.

atom不是交易平台，但它仍然是受益者。自2019年的平台以来，成千上万的用户已注册。虽然公司拒绝提供有关用户号码的确切详情，但仍有数以万计的用户在2019年推出以来。

“We think a lot of that increase in investor participation in markets is here to stay,” said Mr. Shoykhet, 29 years old. “The narrative we never bought into was that active investing was dead.”

“我们认为很多投资者参与市场的增加在这里，谢谢29岁的Shoykhet先生说。 “我们从未买入的叙述是积极投资已经死了。”

Mr. Shoykhet hopes his firm will grow as individuals seek cheap tools to help them invest. A full Atom subscription goes for $9.99 a month, a fraction of how much a Bloomberg Terminal costs.

Shoykhet先生希望他的公司将成长，因为个人寻求便宜的工具来帮助他们投资。全部Atom订阅每月9.99美元，一小部分彭博终端成本。

SoftBank led the Series B funding round in Atom through a $5 billion fund it created to focus on Latin America. It reflects SoftBank’s wager that the rise of individuals in U.S. markets will take off in that region too.

SoftBank通过创建的50亿基金领导A原子中的B系列圆形，以专注于拉丁美洲。它反映了SoftBank的赌注，美国市场的个人崛起也将在该地区起飞。

This year, Atom pursued a deal on its own to provide products for Banco Inter, a digital bank in Brazil that SoftBank’s Latin America fund has also invested in. Shu Nyatta, managing partner for SoftBank’s Latin America fund, said SoftBank later introduced Atom to other portfolio companies in hopes of fueling similar deals.

今年，Atom一项协议为Banco Inter提供产品，这是一家位于巴西的数字银行，Softbank的拉丁美洲基金也投入了。Softbank的拉丁美洲基金的管理合作伙伴，Softbank的管理合伙人稍后向其他人推出原子投资组合公司希望加油类似的交易。

“The whole idea is to bring high-quality data to people who wouldn’t have access to them,” he said.

“整个想法是为无法访问他们的人带来高质量的数据，”他说。

Mr. Nyatta said he was drawn to Mr. Shoykhet’s opportunism and willingness to make Latin America a strategic focus. Mr. Shoykhet recently moved to Miami and is planning to open an office there, in part to make it easier to do business with Latin America.

Nyatta先生说，他被绍克赫特先生的机会主义和愿意使拉丁美洲成为战略重点。 Shoykhet先生最近搬到了迈阿密，并计划在那里开设办事处，部分是为了使与拉丁美洲开展业务。

But there is one thing Atom Finance isn’t planning to do for now: It doesn’t want to become a trading application.

但是现在有一件事是atom金融的计划现在不打算为：它不想成为交易申请。

Mr. Shoykhet is skeptical about many e-brokerages’ practice of routing trades to big trading firms in exchange for payments. The industry has defended this practice, known as payment for order flow, arguing that it lowers the cost of trading for individuals. Others argue it hurts investors as firms will encourage heavy trading by users to maximize profits—even if those investors take too much risk.

Shoykhet先生对许多电子经纪人对大型贸易公司进行换取付款的持态度持怀疑态度。该行业已经为这种做法辩护，称为订单流量的付款，争论它降低了个人交易成本。其他人认为这是伤害投资者，因为公司会鼓励用户繁重的交易来最大化利润 - 即使这些投资者承担过多的风险。

“We don’t think it has users’ best interests in mind,” he said.

“我们认为这并不认为这是用户对思想的最大兴趣，”他说。
