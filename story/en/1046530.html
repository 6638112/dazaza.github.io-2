Has the Squeeze Been Squoze Yet?
挤压被挤压了吗？

2021-01-29 05:30:29

Over the past year, hedge fund supervillains have made money by selling shares of Gamestop they don't actually own - they've just borrowed them. Short selling. If they sell enough they can drive the price down so far that when they eventually need to return the shares they borrowed, they can get them cheap. It's free money. They throw a couple hundred mil at this, chill in their offices watching live video feeds of homeless people being exsanguinated on the hoods of their vintage sports cars, write up an investor report, and call it a fiscal year.

在过去的一年中，对冲基金超级大佬通过出售他们并不真正拥有的Gametop股票来赚钱，他们只是借来的。卖空。如果他们卖出足够多的股票，就可以将价格压低到如此之高，以至于当他们最终需要归还所借入的股票时，便可以使它们变得便宜。它是免费的钱。他们为此付出了数亿美元的资金，他们在办公室里冷落地观看无家可归者的实时视频，这些视频无家可归，他们被困在老式跑车的引擎盖上，写了一份投资者报告，并将其称为会计年度。

They borrowed and sold a record amount - they sold more shares, in fact, than are actually traded, far more than Gamestop's float. This shouldn't have been allowed to happen and probably means they were selling shares they never even bothered to borrow - naked shorts. (Where were you on that one, SEC?) Essentially, they were simultaneously betting on Gamestop going bankrupt and doing their best to drive them into bankruptcy. It's a good tactic when you need to find a way to pay for your old wife's alimony and your new wife's poolboy.

他们借入并卖出了创纪录的数量-实际上，他们卖出的股票数量超过了实际交易的数量，远远超过了Gametop的流通量。这不应该被允许发生，并且可能意味着他们正在出售他们甚至不愿借钱的股票-裸短裤。 （SEC，您在那儿呢？）本质上，他们同时押注Gametop破产，并尽最大努力使他们破产。当您需要找到一种方式来支付老婆的a养费和新妻子的男服务员时，这是一个很好的策略。

But it presents an opportunity for the savvy degenerate gambler. Because these shares eventually need to be returned - for one thing, they can only be borrowed for a certain number of days, and for another, it costs these funds money to borrow a share. This means that at some point, they need to buy back those borrowed shares they sold.

但这为精明的堕落赌徒提供了机会。因为这些股票最终需要归还-一方面，它们只能被借用一定天数，而另一方面，要借入这些资金会花费这些资金。这意味着他们在某个时候需要回购所出售的借入股票。

So our visionary gambler, if they were to invest in Gamestop, would have a guaranteed buyer for their shares. And if millions of fellow degenerates were to ask their mother for an advance on their allowance so that they could buy Gamestop, too -

因此，如果我们有远见的赌徒投资于Gamestop，他们的股票将得到保证的购买者。如果数百万堕落的同胞要求他们的母亲预支津贴，以便他们也可以购买Gametop，

And then, if a famous e-commerce CEO were to buy a ton of Gamestop shares, join the board, and announce they're going to be a big company again by doing internet things and esports and radical new stuff -

然后，如果一位著名的电子商务首席执行官打算购买一吨Gametop股份，加入董事会，并宣布他们将通过做互联网，电子竞技和新奇事物再次成为大公司-

And then, if a truly insane amount of call option buying - don't worry about it if you don't know - were to force market makers to rapidly buy up a ton of shares to fulfill all those options they sold in a wild phenomenon that's basically the stock equivalent of an atmospheric microburst, called a gamma squeeze, suddenly spiking Gamestop's price to unheard-of levels -

然后，如果真正疯狂的看涨期权购买量-如果您不知道，则不必担心-迫使做市商迅速购买大量股票以履行他们出售的所有这些期权这是一种疯狂现象，基本上相当于大气微爆发的股票（称为伽马挤压），突然使Gametop的价格飙升到前所未有的水平-

Well then you'd have the perfect conditions for a short squeeze. The price is insanely high. There's a whole street of funds with deep pockets who absolutely must find a way to buy all those insanely expensive shares. And by buying them - 140% of the amount traded! - they're going to drive up the price even more. So one day, a large enough block of borrowed shares will expire that a fund will have to drive GME's price through the roof in buying them back. And as the price climbs, other short holders are going to be required to cover their borrowed shares by buying them. It's a runaway reaction where the more it happens, the more it happens. You know, one of those cute little phenomena like virus spread. Or nuclear bombs.

好了，您就拥有了短暂挤压的理想条件。价格高得惊人。那里有一整条资金雄厚的资金，他们绝对必须找到一种方法来购买所有这些疯狂的昂贵股票。通过购买-交易量的140％！ -他们将进一步提高价格。因此，有一天，足够多的借入股票将到期，基金将不得不通过回购价格来推动GME的价格上涨。随着价格的上涨，将要求其他空头持有人通过购买股票来偿还所借股票。这是一种失控的反应，发生的次数越多，发生的次数就越多。您知道，像病毒传播那样的可爱现象之一。还是核弹。

So who are they buying from? That's right. At what price are they buying? Well, that depends.

那么他们从谁那里买呢？没错。他们以什么价格购买？好吧，这取决于。

Hedge fund managers holding GME shorts would really, really like to convince GME stock holders to sell them some shares right now, before it climbs any higher, so that they can return the shares they borrowed and get out before they get steamrolled into bankrupcy. And they've got lots of tools at their disposal to do this: they can pump up other stocks to create FOMO, causing GME holders to sell their shares to go chase some shiny new meme. They can hire PR companies to astroturf these stocks on Elon Musk fan clubs and gambling forums. They can buy up shares and then, after trading hours are over, sell them in progressively cheaper tranches to drive down the stock price. They can wipe the hobo blood off their wattle and go cry on television about how they're being bullied. They have, in fact, tried all of these things. But it hasn't worked - GME's price is higher than ever. It's out of control, now - there are too many people involved. The meme has reached critical mass.

持有GME空头的对冲基金经理真的很想说服GME股票持有人立即卖出一些股票，然后再攀升，以便他们可以归还借来的股票并在破产之前破产。而且他们有很多工具可以用来做到这一点：他们可以吸引其他股票来创建FOMO，从而导致GME持有人出售其股票以追逐一些闪亮的新模因。他们可以雇用公关公司在埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）的粉丝俱乐部和赌博论坛上对这些股票进行投资。他们可以购买股票，然后在交易时间结束后，以逐渐便宜的价格出售股票，以压低股价。他们可以抹掉自己身上的流浪汉的鲜血，然后在电视上大哭大叫。实际上，他们已经尝试了所有这些方法。但是它没有用-GME的价格比以往更高。现在，它已经失控了-涉及的人太多。模因达到了临界质量。

Now it's a classic million-player prisoner's dilemma: every GME holder has visions of selling their shares for unlimited chicken tendies and cocaine dipping sauce. Maybe they think they alone can sell, while everyone else can continue to drive the price up by holding. But if every degenerate gambler thought this way, and sold their shares, very quickly the short squeeze wouldn't happen. Short holders would buy up all the shares being sold at a painful but manageable loss, they would cover their position, and the nuke would never be detonated.

现在，这是一个经典的百万玩家囚徒困境：每个GME持有者都有愿景，希望以无限的鸡肉趋势和可卡因蘸酱出售其股票。也许他们认为自己一个人就能卖出，而其他所有人都可以通过持有来继续抬高价格。但是，如果每个堕落的赌徒都这样想，并卖掉了自己的股票，很快就不会发生短暂的挤兑。空头持有人将以痛苦但可控制的损失买入所有出售的股票，他们将弥补头寸，而核武器永远不会被引爆。

What's a prisoner in this dilemma to do? At last, the point arrives. To avoid selling too early, the savvy degenerate gambler would wait until short interest - the amount of shares shorted out there - started to decline substantially. As long as nobody was defecting, nobody selling early, that decline in shares shorted would come with a spike in the price of the stock, as the few shares available are bought at astronomical prices. And this decline in shares shorted would distinguish this spike from gamma squeezes or regular old stock run-ups.

在这个困境中的囚犯该怎么办？最后，要点到了。为了避免过早卖出，精明的堕落赌徒会等到空头兴趣（那里卖空的股票数量）开始大幅下降。只要没有人投降，就没有人卖得早，卖空的股票下跌将伴随着股票价格的飙升，因为很少的可用股票是以天文价格购买的。卖空股票的减少将使这种上涨与伽玛紧缩或常规旧股暴涨区分开来。

Then and only then, as the nuke goes off, the stock price ascends past Alpha Centauri, and the short interest finally starts declining, the short squeeze has begun. And then it's every gambler for themself.

然后，直到那时候，随着核武器的消失，股价上升到半人马座阿尔法（Alpha Centauri），空头兴趣终于开始下降，空头挤压开始了。然后是每个赌徒自己。
