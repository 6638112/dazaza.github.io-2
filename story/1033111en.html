克莱恩视觉公司的AirCar，一款飞行汽车的原型，完成了处女航

2020-11-04 11:51:34

We may call them air taxis but let’s face it, flying cars are really just small airplanes at this point. That may be about to change, though, thanks to the Klein Vision AirCar, an aircraft that actually looks and functions like the vehicle you drive to work each day.

我们可以称它们为空中出租车，但让我们面对现实吧，在这一点上，飞行的汽车实际上只是一架小型飞机。不过，多亏了克莱因视觉空中汽车(Klein Vision AirCar)，这种情况可能即将改变，这款飞机的外观和功能实际上都与你每天驾驶的上班车辆相似。

Late last week, the Slovakian startup shared a video of the AirCar prototype successfully completing its maiden flight. The short clip doesn’t just show off the aircraft soaring through the sky; it also displays the vehicle’s driving capabilities.

上周晚些时候，这家斯洛伐克初创公司分享了一段AirCar原型成功完成首飞的视频。这段短片不仅展示了飞机在天空中翱翔，还展示了车辆的驾驶能力。

It looks as if the AirCar will be just as comfortable flying through the clouds as it is speeding across asphalt. This is likely due to the fact that the prototype is a road-ready car that can transform into a working plane with the touch of a few buttons.

看起来，AirCar在云层中飞行就像在沥青上飞驰一样舒适。这很可能是因为原型车是一辆随时可以上路的汽车，只需轻触几个按钮就可以转变为工作飞机。

At the beginning of Klein Vision’s video, the futuristic-looking AirCar can be seen driving onto the tarmac at Piestany airport. The rear section of the car then slides back as two wings pop out of the car and begin to unfold. Once the wings are fully expanded, the prototype speeds off down the runway and takes off into the sky. It then flies around the airfield before safely landing back on the ground. Once the flight is complete, the wings retract back into the car, and it drives off.

在Klein Vision的视频一开始，可以看到外观未来主义的AirCar开到皮埃斯塔尼机场的停机坪上。然后，当两个机翼弹出车外并开始展开时，车的后部向后滑动。一旦机翼完全展开，原型就会沿着跑道加速起飞，然后升空。然后它绕着机场飞行，然后安全降落在地面上。一旦飞行完成，机翼就会缩回车内，然后它就会飞走。

While the AirCar’s design leaves something to be desired—it looks like something from a straight-to-VOD sci-fi movie—its driving abilities look certain to set it apart from the rest of the current crop of flying cars, including the SkyDrive SD03 and the Volocopter VoloCity. The ultra-lightweight two-seater is powered by a 1.6-liter BMW engine that put out 140 horsepower, according to a press release from the company. The startup also claims that the craft will have a flying range of roughly 620 miles and be able to reach a top flying speed of 124 mph.

虽然AirCar的设计还不尽如人意-它看起来就像一部直接从VOD到VOD的科幻电影-但它的驾驶能力看起来肯定会让它有别于目前其他会飞的汽车，包括SkyDrive SD03和Volocopter VoloCity。根据该公司的一份新闻稿，这款超轻型双座轿车由1.6升宝马发动机提供动力，可提供140马力。这家初创公司还声称，该飞行器的飞行距离约为620英里，最高飞行速度为每小时124英里。

While those numbers are pretty impressive, the company seems especially proud of the fact that its can actually drive. “With AirCar you will arrive at your destination without the hassle of getting a ride to [an] airport and passing through commercial security, you can drive your AirCar to the golf course, the office, the mall or your hotel and park it in a normal parking space,” co-founder Anton Zajac said in a statement.

虽然这些数字令人印象深刻，但该公司似乎特别自豪的是，它实际上可以开车。联合创始人安东·扎亚克在一份声明中说：“有了AirCar，你就可以到达目的地，而不需要搭便车去机场和通过商业安检，你可以把你的AirCar开到高尔夫球场、办公室、商场或你的酒店，然后把它停在一个正常的停车位上，”联合创始人安东·扎亚克(Anton Zajac)在一份声明中说。

With its first successful flights notched only 18 months after development begin, Klein Vision is ready to continue moving forward with the AirCar. The company plans to put the vehicle into production within the next six months.

Klein Vision在开发开始仅18个月后就首次成功飞行，已经准备好继续推进AirCar的发展。该公司计划在未来六个月内将这款车投入生产。
