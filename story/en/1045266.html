Sources: Andreessen Horowitz to launch an opinion section on its website that will publish articles related to technology and business from outside contributors
资料来源：安德森·霍洛维茨（Andreessen Horowitz）将在其网站上开设一个意见版块，该版块将发表来自外部贡献者的与技术和业务相关的文章

2021-01-20 10:27:16

Silicon Valley investment firm Andreessen Horowitz, which once courted attention from the news media, is ramping up its own media efforts. The firm plans to expand its publication of content related to technology and business on its website through an opinion section that publishes articles from outside contributors, according to people briefed on the discussions.

曾经引起新闻媒体关注的硅谷投资公司安德森·霍洛维茨（Andreessen Horowitz）正在加大自身的媒体投入。知情人士说，该公司计划通过一个意见栏扩大其在网站上与技术和商业有关的内容的发布，该意见栏将发表外部贡献者的文章。

The firm currently has two editorial roles open to helm the new efforts. It is in search of an executive editor and an opinions editor. A recent job listing by the firm for the executive editor position said the firm wants to “dramatically scale our editorial operation—across coverage areas and across mediums (especially video)…to grow and lead a talented team of creatives, producers, talent, and marketers.”

该公司目前有两个编辑角色可以掌控新的工作。它正在寻找执行编辑和意见编辑。该公司最近发布的一份执行编辑职位清单表示，该公司希望“在覆盖区域和跨媒体（尤其是视频）的范围内，大幅扩展我们的编辑业务……以发展并领导一支由创意，制作人，才智和营销人员。”
