Over 650 tech workers at the NYT announce they have formed a union to be represented by the NewsGuild of New York and are seeking union recognition from the NYT
在NYT超过650名科技工作者宣布，他们已形成一个由纽约的新闻指南代表的工会，正在寻求与NYT的联盟认可

2021-04-14 04:34:02

Tech workers at The New York Times announced on Tuesday that they had formed a union and would ask the company to recognize it.

纽约时报的技术人员周二宣布，他们已经形成了一个工会，并会要求公司认识到它。

The group, a majority of which signed cards in support of the effort, of more than 650 employees includes software engineers, designers, data analysts and product managers. It will be represented by the NewsGuild of New York. NewsGuild membership already includes more than 1,300 newsroom workers and business staff members at The Times, as well as workers at other media outlets.

该集团，其中大多数签名卡支持努力，超过650名员工包括软件工程师，设计师，数据分析师和产品经理。它将由纽约的新闻指示。 Newsguild会员资格已包含超过1,300名新闻室工作室和商务人员成员，以及其他媒体网点的工人。

As part of the Times Tech Guild, the tech workers would be in a separate bargaining unit from other Times employees represented by the NewsGuild.

作为Tech Tech Guild的一部分，技术工人将在另一次员工所代表的其他时候讨价还价单位。

In recent years, The Times has ramped up its hiring of tech workers as part of its strategy to reach 10 million paid digital subscribers by 2025. In 2020, digital-only subscriptions neared seven million and became the company’s largest revenue stream.

近年来，次数升高了其招聘技术人员，作为其2025年达到1000万支付数字用户的一部分。2020年，二维数字订阅达到七百万，成为该公司最大的收入流。

Kathy Zhang, a senior analytics manager and a member of the organizing committee, said in an interview that The Times felt like “an emerging company” in some ways, although it is a 170-year-old institution.

Kathy Zhang是一位高级分析经理和组委会的成员，在接受采访时表示，虽然它是一个170岁的机构，时代的时代感受到“新兴公司”。

“There’s a lot of stuff we’re trying out,” she said. “There’s a lot of starting and stopping of different projects. It’s been really exciting, but it’s also been pretty exhausting.”

“我们正在尝试很多东西，”她说。 “不同的项目有很多开始和停止。这真的很令人兴奋，但它也非常疲惫。“

The tech workers were concerned about pay equity, health care costs, job security and career advancement, Ms. Zhang added. The union also hoped to improve diversity and inclusion in the department.

张女役，技术工人致力于薪酬股权，医疗保健成本，职业安全和职业发展。联盟还希望改善多样性并将其列入该部门。

The New York Times Company can decide to voluntarily recognize the union, as it did with a union at Wirecutter, a product recommendation site owned by the company, in 2019. If it chooses not to, the union can file with the National Labor Relations Board for an election, in which the union will need a simple majority of votes to win recognition.

纽约时报公司可以决定自愿认识到联盟，因为它在2019年拥有的产品推荐网站上拥有的产品推荐网站。如果选择不，联盟可以与国家劳动关系委员会提交选举，其中联盟需要一个简单的大多数投票来赢得认可。

A spokeswoman for The New York Times Company said in a statement that the company had received the request for voluntary recognition from the union on Tuesday morning, and that because voluntary recognition was a significant decision the company wanted to “make sure all voices are heard.”

纽约时报公司发言人在一份声明中表示，该公司于周二早上收到了联盟的自愿承认请求，而且由于自愿认可是公司想要的重要决定“确保听到所有声音。 “

“At The New York Times, we have a long history of positive and productive relationships with unions, and we respect the right of all employees to decide whether or not joining a union is right for them,” the spokeswoman said. “We will take time to review this request and discuss it soon with representatives of the NewsGuild.”

“在纽约时报，我们与工会有悠久的积极和生产关系历史，我们尊重所有雇员的权利，以决定加入工会是否适合他们，”发言人说。 “我们需要时间审查此请求，并尽快与新闻指南的代表讨论。”

The organizing of The Times’s tech workers came months after more than 400 Google engineers and other workers formed a union, a rarity in Silicon Valley. An organizing drive at an Amazon warehouse in Alabama was voted down last week.

在400多名谷歌工程师和其他工人形成工会后，泰时的技术人员的组织来到几个月后，硅谷稀有。阿拉巴马州亚马逊仓库的组织驾驶上周投了投票。

Media companies have had a surge in such efforts. Workers at publications like BuzzFeed News, Vice, The New Yorker, Slate and Vox Media have all formed unions in recent years.

媒体公司在这种努力中产生了激增。 Puxzfeed新闻，副，纽约人，石板和VOX媒体等工人近年来都有成立的工会。
