Microsoft Increases Xbox Live Gold Prices
微软提高Xbox Live Gold价格

2021-01-23 00:48:45

Microsoft continues sending not-so-subtle signals that it would really, really like you to drop Xbox Live Gold in favor of Game Pass. The company has raised prices for new Xbox Live Gold memberships across the board, with the changes becoming more noticeable the longer you’re ready to commit. The one- and three-month plans aren’t much pricier at $11 and $30 respectively (up $1 and $5), but six months now costs you $60 — well above the $40 you used to pay. And when there’s no longer a 12-month membership, you’ll be looking at $120 per year if you insist on Gold.

微软继续发出不太微妙的信号，表明它真的，非常希望您放弃Xbox Live Gold，转而使用Game Pass。该公司已全面提高了Xbox Live Gold新会员的价格，随着您准备提交的时间越长，这些更改就越明显。一个月和三个月的计划价格分别为11美元和30美元（分别增加了1美元和5美元），价格并不昂贵，但是现在六个月的费用为60美元，远高于您过去所支付的40美元。而且当不再有12个月的会员资格时，如果您坚持使用Gold，则每年需要120美元。

Existing six- and 12-month members will renew at the current price, Microsoft said. Those who convert their Gold memberships to Xbox Game Pass Ultimate will get remaining time converted at no extra cost for up to three years. If you still have several months of Gold, you won’t face another significant outlay for a while.

微软表示，现有的六个月和十二个月的会员将以当前价格续订。那些将其金牌会员资格转换为Xbox Game Pass Ultimate的人将获得最多三年的剩余时间转换，而无需任何额外费用。如果您还有几个月的黄金时间，那么一段时间内您将不会再面临其他重大支出。

The company justified the increase by noting that Gold pricing “has not changed for years” in many places, and in some cases hadn’t changed for over a decade — the Xbox 360 was still in stores back then. In theory, Microsoft is just acknowledging that costs have changed. PlayStation Plus still has a $60 option, although Sony doesn’t bundle its Now game streaming.

该公司通过指出许多地方的黄金价格“多年未变”来证明涨价的合理性，而且在某些情况下十年没有变过，那时Xbox 360仍在销售中。从理论上讲，微软只是承认成本已经改变。尽管索尼没有捆绑其Now游戏流媒体，但PlayStation Plus仍然有60美元的选择权。

It’s not too difficult to see Microsoft’s grander plans, though. Xbox Game Pass Ultimate is a relatively easy upsell. You’ll pay more at $15 per month, but you’ll get all of Gold’s features as well as access to a growing library of titles and cloud gaming. The tech giant not only gets more money each month, but is more likely to keep members who might otherwise let their Gold memberships lapse.

不过，看到微软的宏伟计划并不难。 Xbox Game Pass Ultimate是一个相对容易的追加销售。您需要支付每月15美元的费用，但您将获得Gold的所有功能，并可以使用不断增长的游戏库和云游戏。这家科技巨头不仅每月可获得更多的钱，而且更有可能保留那些原本会让其金牌会员资格失效的会员。
