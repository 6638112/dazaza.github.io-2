Facebook says it will assist Epic Games in its legal battle against Apple, including providing supporting documents
Facebook表示将协助Epic Games与苹果进行法律斗争，包括提供支持文件

2020-12-17 02:42:36

Facebook FB 0.21% said it would assist the company behind popular videogame “Fortnite” in its high-profile legal battle with Apple AAPL 0.02% as the social-media giant ramps up its own counterattack against what it says are the iPhone maker’s self-serving measures cloaked in the interest of privacy.

Facebook FB 0.21％表示将协助背后受欢迎的视频游戏“ Fortnite”的公司与苹果APL进行备受瞩目的法律斗争0.02％，因为社交媒体巨头加大了对自己所说的iPhone制造商自我服务的反击力度为了隐私而隐瞒的措施。

Facebook has been feuding with Apple for months on issues ranging from prices for paid apps to privacy rule changes.

从付费应用程序的价格到隐私规则的变更，Facebook已经与苹果争执了几个月。

As part of a pledge to assist challenges to what it called Apple’s anticompetitive behavior, Facebook plans to provide supporting materials and documents to Epic Games Inc. The “Fortnite” parent sued Apple earlier this year, claiming the tech giant’s App Store operates like a monopoly. Facebook said it isn’t joining the lawsuit but helping with discovery as the case heads to trial next year.

为了帮助应对所谓的苹果反竞争行为的挑战，Facebook计划向Epic Games Inc.提供支持材料和文件。“ Fortnite”母公司于今年早些时候起诉苹果，声称这家科技巨头的App Store经营像垄断一样。 。 Facebook表示不会加入诉讼，但会在案件于明年开庭前协助发现。

In a blog post Wednesday Facebook also sharpened its criticism of Apple’s plan to enable users to restrict certain apps from collecting personal data, saying those policies could imperil small businesses while potentially benefiting its own bottom line.

在周三的博客中，Facebook还对苹果公司允许用户限制某些应用程序收集个人数据的计划提出了批评，称这些政策可能危害小型企业，同时可能使自己的底线受益。

“This is not really about privacy for them,” said Dan Levy, Facebook’s head of ad products. “This is about an attack on personalized ads and the consequences it’s going to have on small-business owners.”

Facebook广告产品负责人丹·利维（Dan Levy）表示：“这并不是真正意义上的隐私权。” “这是对个性化广告的攻击及其对小型企业所有者的后果。”

Apple has said starting early next year its iOS 14 operating system will give iPhone and iPad users the option to no longer share personal information that many developers rely on to tailor ads. When users open an app, they will see a message asking permission to track what other apps and websites they visit, their location and other behaviors.

苹果表示，从明年年初开始，其iOS 14操作系统将使iPhone和iPad用户可以选择不再共享许多开发人员用来定制广告的个人信息。当用户打开一个应用程序时，他们将看到一条消息，询问是否允许跟踪他们访问的其他应用程序和网站，其位置和其他行为。

Apple’s plan has drawn criticism from a range of businesses and trade groups. They include the publisher of DMG Media, operator of the Daily Mail and MailOnline, and the Partnership for Responsible Addressable Media, a coalition of advertisers and ad-tech companies that was formed in July in an attempt to forestall Apple’s change. A group of trade associations filed a complaint with France’s competition authority in October, saying that Apple’s plan was anticompetitive.

苹果的计划引起了众多企业和贸易团体的批评。他们包括DMG Media的发行商，Daily Mail和MailOnline的运营商，以及负责任的可寻址媒体合作伙伴关系，该伙伴关系是由广告商和广告技术公司组成的联盟，该联盟于7月成立，旨在阻止Apple的变化。一组行业协会于10月向法国竞争管理机构提出投诉，称苹果的计划具有反竞争性。

Facebook itself is facing challenges to its business practices. The Federal Trade Commission and 46 states sued the company earlier this month, accusing it of buying upstart rivals to choke off competition. The FTC’s case aims to force Facebook to unwind acquisitions of WhatsApp and Instagram, two of its landmark deals. Facebook countered that the FTC had previously approved those acquisitions and said that people and small-businesses choose to use its free services and advertising because of the value that it provides them.

Facebook本身面临其业务实践的挑战。联邦贸易委员会和46个州在本月初起诉该公司，指责该公司购买新贵竞争对手以阻止竞争。 FTC案旨在迫使Facebook取消对WhatsApp和Instagram的收购，这是其两项具有里程碑意义的交易。 Facebook反驳说，联邦贸易委员会此前已经批准了这些收购，并表示，人们和小型企业选择使用其免费服务和广告，是因为它提供了这些服务的价值。

By aiding Epic, Facebook is wading further into a battle over Apple’s App Store commission that is being waged by several other companies, including Spotify Technology SA and Tinder owner Match Group

通过帮助Epic，Facebook正在进一步与Apple App Store佣金一争高下，该佣金由Spotify Technology SA和Tinder所有者Match Group等多家公司承担

Epic filed suit against Apple and Alphabet Google in August after the companies pulled “Fortnite” from their app stores because the developer added an unauthorized payment system to the survival game that skirted a 30% fee they both collect on in-app purchases. Apple countersued a month later, accusing Epic of duplicity and greed.

在两家公司从其应用商店中撤出“ Fortnite”之后，Epic于8月对苹果和Alphabet Google提起诉讼，因为该开发商在生存游戏中添加了未经授权的支付系统，从而使两家公司都从应用内购买中收取了30％的费用。苹果在一个月后提出反诉，指责史诗的双重欺诈和贪婪行为。

Apple and Google have said the commission they charge is in line with what most other app marketplaces charge and helps cover the cost of store services they provide, such as user privacy and security.

苹果和谷歌表示，他们收取的佣金与大多数其他应用市场收取的佣金相符，并有助于支付其提供的商店服务的成本，例如用户隐私和安全性。

In August Facebook said Apple’s commission was hurting small businesses that had started charging for special events and experiences through their mobile apps to make up for financial losses caused by the pandemic. Apple a month later said it would give businesses a reprieve from paying the fee on such in-app sales until the end of the year.

8月，Facebook表示，苹果委员会正在伤害那些已经开始通过其移动应用程序收取特殊事件和体验费用的小型企业，以弥补大流行造成的经济损失。苹果一个月后表示，它将使企业在年底前支付此类应用程序内销售的费用而缓和。

More recently Apple said it would halve the commission it charges smaller developers that sell software through its App Store. The iPhone maker said last month that starting next year it will collect 15%, rather than 30%, of App Store sales from companies that generate less than $1 million in revenue through the software platform, including in-app purchases.

苹果最近表示，将通过向其App Store出售软件的小型开发商收取的佣金减半。这家iPhone制造商上个月表示，从明年开始，它将从那些通过软件平台产生的收入不足100万美元的公司（包括应用内购买）中获得15％（而不是30％）的App Store销售额。
