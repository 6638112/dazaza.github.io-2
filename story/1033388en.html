我们要加入罗宾汉

2020-11-06 07:13:56

The Quantopian team is joining Robinhood to continue to pursue our passion for opening access to markets and democratizing the financial system. Quantopian has always stood for greater access and deeper education, so we are fundamentally aligned with Robinhood’s mission to democratize finance for all.

Quantopian团队将加入罗宾汉的行列，继续追求我们对开放市场准入和金融体系民主化的热情。Quantopian一直支持更多的机会和更深入的教育，因此我们从根本上与罗宾汉的使命保持一致，即让所有人的金融民主化。

Robinhood continues to break down barriers and create a more inclusive financial system. Our merry band of Quantopians should fit right in as we work together to further expand access to financial information and education, and inspire greater participation in the financial markets.

罗宾汉继续打破壁垒，创建一个更具包容性的金融体系。在我们共同努力进一步扩大金融信息和教育的渠道，并鼓励更多人参与金融市场的过程中，我们这群快乐的宽托邦人应该恰如其分地融入其中。

While the Quantopian community and related services will be retired on November 14th, our passion and dedication to demystifying investing and finance will live on as we bring everything we learned and built to our new roles at Robinhood.

虽然Quantopian社区和相关服务将于11月14日退休，但我们对揭开投资和金融神秘面纱的热情和奉献将继续存在，因为我们将把我们学到的和建立的一切带到罗宾汉的新角色中。

Thank you so much for your participation in our very special community. Even though it is ending, our time together had meaning for the industry, our team, and, I hope, for you too. Please keep pursuing your passion for finance and we hope to see you again soon -- in the Robinhood community!

非常感谢您加入我们这个非常特别的社区。尽管它即将结束，但我们在一起的时光对整个行业、我们的团队，以及我希望对你们也有意义。请继续追求你对金融的热情，我们希望很快就能在罗宾汉社区再次见到你！
