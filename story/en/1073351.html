Cisco Made $20 Billion-Plus Takeover Offer For Splunk
思科提出200多亿美元收购Splunk

2022-02-15 09:32:27

Feb 11 (Reuters) - Network gear maker Cisco Systems Inc (CSCO.O) has made a takeover offer worth more than $20 billion for software maker Splunk Inc (SPLK.O), the Wall Street Journal reported on Friday, citing people familiar with the matter.

路透2月11日电---据《华尔街日报》周五援引知情人士的话报道，网络设备制造商思科系统公司（Cisco Systems Inc.）已向软件制造商Splunk Inc.发出价值逾200亿美元的收购要约。

The offer was made recently and the companies are not in active talks, the newspaper said, citing some of the sources.

该报援引部分消息人士的话称，该提议是最近提出的，两家公司没有进行积极谈判。

Cisco did not immediately respond to a request for comment. Splunk said it does not comment on rumors or speculation.

思科没有立即回应置评请求。Splunk表示不会对谣言或猜测发表评论。

In November last year, San Francisco-based Splunk announced Doug Merritt has stepped down as its chief executive officer (CEO) and that the company's chair, Graham Smith, would be the interim CEO.

去年11月，总部位于旧金山的Splunk宣布Doug Merritt已辞去首席执行官（CEO）的职务，该公司'；董事会主席格雷厄姆·史密斯将担任临时首席执行官。

Founded in 2003, the software solutions provider has a market capitalisation of $18.2 billion, according to Refinitiv Eikon data.

根据Refinitiv Eikon数据，这家软件解决方案提供商成立于2003年，市值182亿美元。
