Twitter officially launches labels to identify the ‘good bots’
Twitter正式发布标签来识别“好机器人”

2022-02-17 07:11:22

As during tests, the Twitter accounts that label themselves as bots will display this information on their Twitter profile. Underneath the account’s name and @username, a small robot icon appears next to the words “Automated by” followed by the name of the account’s operator. The Twitter bio, meanwhile, will detail the bot’s purpose.

与测试期间一样，将自己标记为机器人的Twitter帐户将在其Twitter配置文件中显示此信息。在帐户名和@username下面，一个小机器人图标出现在“自动执行者”旁边，后面是帐户操作员的名字。与此同时，Twitter上的个人简介将详细介绍这个机器人的用途。

When the bots tweet, their automated status will also be visible in users’ timelines.

当机器人发推特时，它们的自动状态也会在用户的时间线上显示出来。

Twitter says this information will help users to decide which accounts to follow, engage with and trust.

Twitter表示，这些信息将帮助用户决定关注、参与和信任哪些账户。

The account labels had been first made available to some 500 Twitter developer accounts in September, which tested the feature and offered feedback, including bots like @earthquakesSF, @vax_progress, @last100bills, @AltTxtReminder, @met_drawings and @EmojiMashupBot. These accounts represented a range of bot use, from critical updates to interesting information to fun content.

这些账户标签于9月首次提供给大约500个Twitter开发者账户，这些账户测试了该功能并提供了反馈，包括@earthquakesSF、@vax_progress、@last100bills、@AltTxtReminder、@met_drawings和@Emojimashubot等机器人。这些账户代表了机器人的一系列用途，从关键更新到有趣的信息再到有趣的内容。

In addition to the “good bots,” many people automate their Twitter accounts in other ways — like using IFTTT integrations to tweet out links, for example. But Twitter has been less concerned about that sort of automated tweeting, it said — noting only that users should investigate any third-party application they use with their account and make sure to follow Twitter’s rules.

除了“好机器人”之外，许多人还通过其他方式实现了Twitter账户的自动化——例如，使用IFTTT集成发送链接。但Twitter对这种自动推文的关注程度较低，它表示，只是指出用户应该调查他们在账户中使用的任何第三方应用程序，并确保遵守Twitter的规则。

The bot labels, while useful, don’t necessarily address the larger issue with bots on Twitter’s service.

机器人标签虽然有用，但不一定能解决Twitter服务上机器人的更大问题。

The bad bots can range from the annoying — like bots that tweet spam (or promote cryptocurrency schemes!) — to the more worrisome, like bots that try to influence upcoming elections. As an opt-in system, bot owners trying to do good with their creations will be able to add labels and promote themselves as the person behind the account. But bad bots won’t participate.

坏的机器人可以是令人讨厌的，比如推特垃圾信息（或推广加密货币方案！）的机器人更令人担忧的是，比如试图影响即将到来的选举的机器人。作为一个选择加入系统，试图善用自己创造的机器人所有者将能够添加标签，并将自己宣传为账户背后的人。但坏机器人不会参与。

The feature is available today to automated accounts that use the Twitter API. But Twitter says there is no auditing of the system for now.

如今，使用Twitter API的自动账户可以使用该功能。但Twitter表示，目前还没有对该系统进行审计。

“All accounts on Twitter are subject to the Twitter Rules,” a Twitter spokesperson said. “As the label is opt-in only, we will not be auditing accounts that choose to adopt it at this time and will rely on our reporting process should an account violate the Rules.”

“Twitter上的所有账户都要遵守Twitter规则，”Twitter发言人说。“由于该标签仅适用于选择性加入，我们目前不会对选择采用该标签的账户进行审计，如果账户违反规定，我们将依赖我们的报告流程。”

Twitter will discuss the news further on a Twitter Space at 3 pm PT today hosted by the Twitter Safety team, which will be followed by a developer demo on TwitterDev’s Twitch channel on Thursday at 11 am PT.

Twitter将于今天下午3点在Twitter安全团队主持的Twitter空间进一步讨论这一消息，之后将于周四上午11点在Twitter开发者的Twitch频道上进行开发者演示。
