The fosshost project is a non-profit organisation that exists to serve the hosting needs of the global open source community.

Fosshost项目是一个非营利性组织，旨在满足全球开源社区的托管需求。

We're on a mission to empower and support every free and open source software project. To go further, together. Our work never stops.

我们的使命是授权和支持每一个自由和开源软件项目。一起走得更远。我们的工作从未停止过。

We operate several nodes which are either donated by our hosting sponsors, or self-managed, and manage the infrastructure donated to us, on behalf of the projects we help. You can read more about the projects we help here.

我们提供的其他服务包括网络、电子邮件、数据库、通信、域名、域名和存储托管。你可以通过申请表申请我们的所有服务，或者在这里阅读更多关于我们提供的服务的信息。
