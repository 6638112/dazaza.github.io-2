Twitter says it will discontinue Periscope as a separate mobile app by March 2021, saying it's brought most of the core capabilities of Periscope into Twitter
Twitter表示它将在2021年3月之前停止将Periscope作为单独的移动应用程序使用，并表示已将Periscope的大部分核心功能引入了Twitter

2020-12-16 04:37:14

Today, we’re sharing that we have made the difficult decision to discontinue Periscope as a separate mobile app by March 2021.

今天，我们分享我们已经做出了艰难的决定，要在2021年3月之前停止将Periscope作为单独的移动应用程序使用。

First off, we want to be transparent about why we’re making this decision, es pecially to those of you who are active and passionate users of Periscope today. The truth is that the Periscope app is in an unsustainable maintenance-mode state, and has been for a while. Over the past couple of years, we’ve seen declining usage and know that the cost to support the app will only continue to go up over time. Leaving it in its current state isn’t doing right by the current and former Periscope community or by Twitter. We still believe in the power of live video to solve impactful problems, which is why we’ve brought most of the core capabilities of Periscope into Twitter. We probably would have made this decision sooner if it weren’t for all of the projects we reprioritized due to the events of 2020. We’re sharing our decision with you now because we want to be transparent and honest about where we’re at and what’s next.

首先，我们要透明地说明为什么要做出此决定，尤其是对于今天活跃于Periscope的热情用户。事实是，Periscope应用程序处于不可持续的维护模式状态，并且已经存在了一段时间。在过去的几年中，我们看到使用率不断下降，并且知道支持该应用程序的成本只会随着时间的推移而持续上升。当前和以前的Periscope社区或Twitter都无法将其保持在当前状态。我们仍然相信实时视频解决重大问题的能力，这就是为什么我们将Periscope的大多数核心功能引入Twitter的原因。如果不是由于2020年的事件而使我们重新确定优先级的所有项目，我们可能会更快地做出此决定。我们现在与您分享我们的决定，因为我们希望对自己所处的位置保持透明和诚实接下来是什么。

We’re planning to remove Periscope from the app stores by March 2021, but no one will be able to create a new account in-app starting with the next release. Broadcasts that were shared to Twitter will live on as replays, and all broadcasters will be able to download an archive of their Periscope broadcasts and data before the app is removed in March 2021. You can find more info here about what will happen to your Super Hearts and broadcasts, how you can go live on Twitter going forward, and more.

我们计划在2021年3月之前从应用商店中删除Periscope，但没人能从下一个版本开始在应用内创建新帐户。共享给Twitter的广播将作为重播继续播放，并且所有广播公司都可以在2021年3月该应用被删除之前下载其Periscope广播和数据的存档。您可以在此处找到有关Super将会发生的情况的更多信息。心和广播，今后如何在Twitter上直播等等。

Although it’s time to say goodbye, the legacy of Periscope will live on far beyond the boundaries of the app itself. The capabilities and ethos of the Periscope team and infrastructure already permeate Twitter, and we’re confident that live video still has the potential of seeing an even wider audience within the Twitter product.

尽管该说再见了，但Periscope的遗产将延续到应用程序本身的界限之外。 Periscope团队和基础架构的功能和精神已经渗透到Twitter，我们相信实时视频仍然有潜力在Twitter产品中看到更广泛的受众。

Most importantly, we’re so grateful to all of you who invested time and energy into making Periscope the place to show what’s happening in your world and creating vibrant communities. We’ve learned so much, shared incredible experiences together, witnessed unforgettable moments in history, and seen pockets of the world we could’ve only imagined when we first launched Periscope. It was a genuine honor to pour our heart and soul into building something that other people loved enough to use and build their own communities around. Thank you so much.

最重要的是，我们非常感谢所有花费时间和精力来使Periscope成为展示您的世界正在发生的事情并创建充满活力的社区的地方的人。我们学到了很多东西，一起分享了令人难以置信的经历，见证了历史上刻骨铭心的时刻，并看到了只有我们首次推出Periscope时才能够想象的世界上的许多地方。倾注我们的心血和灵魂来建设别人喜欢的东西，以便在周围使用和建立自己的社区，这是一种真正的荣幸。非常感谢。
