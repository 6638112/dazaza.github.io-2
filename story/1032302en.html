Reliance Jio平台超过4亿订户，探索将服务扩展到印度以外

2020-10-30 23:43:12

The Facebook and Google-backed telecom operator said its finances has improved, too, despite the pandemic. Its EBIDTA run-rate crossed $1 billion in the aforementioned quarter, while net profit jumped to $409 million. The average revenue it clocks per user now stands at Rs 145 $1.94), up from $1.88 from the quarter before. In the past two quarters, the company has expanded its workforce by 30,000 people, it said.

这家Facebook和谷歌支持的电信运营商表示，尽管有大流行，但其财务状况也有所改善。其EBIDTA运行率在上述季度超过10亿美元，而净利润跃升至4.09亿美元。(它现在每个用户的平均收入为145卢比(合1.94美元)，高于上一季度的1.88美元。该公司表示，在过去两个季度，该公司增加了3万名员工。

Jio Platforms also operates a range of services including JioTV, JioMusic, and JioMoney. Kiran Thomas, President of Reliance Industries (parent firm of Jio Platforms), said in an earnings call Friday that the company has proven that its services can be feasibly built in India, and the company plans to expand them outside of the country. However, he did not share a timeline for this expansion.

Jio平台还运营一系列服务，包括JioTV、JioMusic和JioMoney。信实工业(Jio Platforms的母公司)总裁基兰·托马斯(Kiran Thomas)在周五的财报电话会议上表示，该公司已经证明，其服务可以在印度可行地建立，该公司计划将这些服务扩展到印度以外。然而，他没有分享这次扩张的时间表。

Reliance Industries, the most valuable firm in India, announced some more investments in its businesses, continuing its eye-catching funding spree at the height of a global pandemic.

印度最有价值的公司信实工业(Reliance Industries)宣布对其业务进行更多投资，在全球疫情最严重的时候继续其引人注目的融资狂欢。

The oil-to-retails giant announced that Abu Dhabi Investment Authority and Saudi Arabia’s Public Investment Fund will invest $1 billion in the company’s digital fibre business.

这家从石油到零售的巨头宣布，阿布扎比投资局和沙特阿拉伯的公共投资基金将向该公司的数字光纤业务投资10亿美元。

Abu Dhabi Authority and Saudi Arabia’s PIF are also an investor in Jio Platforms, which this year has raised about $20 billion. A Reliance Jio spokesperson told TechCrunch that the investment announced today are separate and not part of the two firms’ previous deals.

阿布扎比当局和沙特阿拉伯的PIF也是Jio Platforms的投资者，Jio Platforms今年已经筹集了约200亿美元。Reliance Jio的一位发言人告诉TechCrunch，今天宣布的投资是单独的，不是两家公司之前交易的一部分。
