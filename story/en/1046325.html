Tesla Model S Gets a Radically Redesigned Interior and 520-Mile Range
特斯拉Model S获得了彻底重新设计的内饰和520英里射程

2021-01-28 22:52:52

As part of its fourth-quarter earnings announcement on Wednesday, Tesla unveiled the long-rumored refresh for its Model S sedan. ( The Model X SUV received major updates as well.) On the outside, the Model S has a new front bumper with slightly different intakes, a tweaked rear diffuser and new 19- and 21-inch wheel designs. All of the exterior trim is now finished in black to match the Model Y, but the paint color palette remains the same, with white being the only no-cost option.

作为周三公布的第四季度收益的一部分，特斯拉宣布了传闻已久的Model S轿车更新。 （Model X SUV也进行了重大更新。）在外观上，Model S具有一个新的前保险杠，其进气口略有不同，调整了后扩散器，并采用了新的19英寸和21英寸轮毂设计。现在所有的外部装饰都以黑色完成，以与Model Y匹配，但是油漆的调色板保持不变，白色是唯一的免费选择。

The interior is the star of the show, though. It's been completely redesigned, marking the Model S' first major update since its debut in 2012. There's a large 17-inch central screen much like that of the Model 3 and Model Y, but the S retains a 12.3-inch digital gauge cluster in front of the driver, as well. Tesla says the new center screen is basically a gaming computer with 10 teraflops of processing power, and the released images show it running the popular fantasy RPG game Witcher 3.

内饰是本次展览的明星。它经过了彻底的重新设计，标志着Model S＆＃39;这是自2012年首次亮相以来的首次重大更新。它具有一个大型的17英寸中央屏幕，与Model 3和Model Y的屏幕非常相似，但S还在驾驶员前方保留了12.3英寸数字仪表盘组。 。特斯拉表示，新的中央屏幕基本上是具有10 teraflops处理能力的游戏计算机，发布的图像显示该屏幕运行流行的幻想RPG游戏Witcher 3。

Most radical is the new steering wheel. It isn't exactly a wheel anymore, instead looking like a yoke right out of Star Wars or Knight Rider. There are no stalks, either, meaning the turn signals, lights and other typical features are now controlled by touch buttons on the "wheel."

最激进的是新的方向盘。它不再是一个轮子，而是看起来像是《星球大战》或《骑士骑士》中的a子。也没有茎，这意味着转向信号灯，照明灯和其他典型功能现在都由转盘上的触摸按钮控制。

There's more carbon fiber or wood trim covering parts of the dashboard and door panels, and the door cards have been redesigned. The new center console has more storage space and looks a lot nicer, and it has wireless charging for multiple devices. The rear seats look more sculpted and have a new fold-down armrest with cupholders. Rear-seat passengers get an 8-inch screen that offers the same infotainment and gaming functions as the main screen, and it even works with wireless gaming controllers. The Model S has three-zone climate control, a 22-speaker audio system, heated seats all around (and ventilated front seats), ambient lighting and a glass roof as standard. White, black and beige remain the only interior color options.

仪表板和门面板的零件还覆盖了更多的碳纤维或木质装饰，并且门卡已经过重新设计。新的中央控制台具有更大的存储空间，并且外观要好得多，并且可以为多个设备进行无线充电。后排座椅看起来更加造型优美，并具有带杯架的新型折叠式扶手。后排座位的乘客可获得一个8英寸的屏幕，该屏幕具有与主屏幕相同的信息娱乐和游戏功能，甚至可以与无线游戏控制器一起使用。 Model S具有三区气候控制，22扬声器音频系统，四周加热的座椅（和通风的前排座椅），环境照明和标准的玻璃天窗。白色，黑色和米色仍然是唯一的内部颜色选择。

The Model S is now available in three different specs. The $79,990 Long Range version has two motors and all-wheel drive, with Tesla quoting a 3.1-second 0-to-60-mph time, a 155-mph top speed and a range of 412 miles. The $119,990 Plaid gains a third motor, "carbon-sleeved rotors" and torque vectoring. The Plaid has 1,020 horsepower boasts a 2.0-second 0-to-60 time (Tesla technically says 1.99 seconds), a 200-mph top speed and a 390-mile range.

Model S现在提供三种不同规格。这款售价79,990美元的远程版本具有两个电机和全轮驱动，特斯拉给出了3.1秒的0到60英里/小时的时间，155英里/小时的最高速度和412英里的行驶里程。这款售价119,990美元的格子呢获得了第三个电动机，碳袖子转子。和扭矩矢量。拥有1,020马力的格子，拥有2.0秒的0到60时间（特斯拉从技术上讲为1.99秒），最高时速200英里每小时和390英里范围。

Then there's the $139,990 Plaid Plus, which is the real big deal. Tesla says it has over 1,100 hp and will hit 60 mph in under 2 seconds. It also boats a sub-9-second quarter-mile time, a top speed of 200 mph and a range of over 520 miles. Tesla also says the Model S is now the most aerodynamic production car in the world, a claim likely achieved by the Plaid Plus model

然后是139,990美元的Plaid Plus，这是真正的大问题。特斯拉表示，它的功率超过1100马力，将在2秒内达到60 mph的时速。它还在不到9秒的四分之一英里时间内划船，最高时速为200英里/小时，航程超过520英里。特斯拉还表示，Model S现在是世界上空气动力学最强的量产车，Plaid Plus车型很可能实现了这一要求

The only option besides color, trim and wheels is Tesla's Full Self-Driving suite of driver-assist systems, which costs $10,000. (Tesla's basic Autopilot setup is standard.) The updated Model S is available to order now, with Tesla saying that estimated deliveries for the Long Range and Plaid models will begin in March. If you want the Plaid Plus model, you'll be waiting until late 2021.

除了颜色，装饰和车轮之外，唯一的选择是特斯拉的全套自动驾驶驾驶员辅助系统套件，价格为10,000美元。 （Tesla的基本自动驾驶仪设置是标准配置。）现在可以订购更新的Model S，特斯拉表示，长距离和格子型车型的预计交付时间将在3月开始。 如果要使用Plaid Plus型号，则要等到2021年下半年。
