In 1995, the NASA Systems Engineering Handbook (NASA/SP-6105) was initially published to bring the fundamental concepts and techniques of systems engineering to the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) personnel in a way that recognized the nature of NASA systems and the NASA environment.

1995年，美国国家航空航天局系统工程手册（NASA / SP-6105）首次发行，目的是将系统工程学的基本概念和技术带给美国国家航空航天局（NASA）的工作人员，以认可NASA系统的本质和 NASA环境。
