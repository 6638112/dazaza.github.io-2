Roblox begins trading under RBLX at $64.50, up 43% from its Jan. funding round, giving the company a $33.5B valuation; NYSE set Roblox's reference price at $45
Roblox以64.50美元的价格开始于RBLX交易，从其1月份的资金回合达到43％，为公司提供了33.5亿美元的估值; 纽约证券交易所设定了罗布洛的参考价45美元

2021-03-11 03:48:09

Roblox, the kids gaming app that surged in popularity during the pandemic, started trading Wednesday at $64.50 on the New York Stock Exchange, giving the company a market cap of $35.5 billion.

Roblox，在大流行期间飙升的儿童游戏应用程序，在纽约证券交易所上周三的价格开始于64.50美元，为公司提供355亿美元的市场上限。

Roblox went public through a direct listing, following the lead of other tech companies like Spotify, Slack and Palantir. Instead of raising fresh capital in exchange for new shares, Roblox allowed existing shareholders to sell immediately, without being subject to a lock-up period.

roblox通过直接上市，遵循像Spotify，Slack和Palantir的其他科技公司的领先地位。 Roblox允许现有股东立即出售，而不是筹集新股份，而不是提高新的资本。

The opening price on the NYSE represents a 43% increase from a private financing round in January, when the company sold shares for $45. The NYSE set a reference price on Tuesday of $45, though no stock changed hands at those levels. The reference price tends to reflect private market trading and does not indicate where a stock will open.

纽约证券交易所开盘价比1月份私人融资增加43％，当该公司以45美元销售股票。纽约证券交易所设定了45美元的第25美元的参考价格，但没有股票在那些水平中换了手。参考价格倾向于反映私人市场交易，并不表明股票将开放的地方。

Based on a fully diluted share count of 652 million, which includes restricted stock units that will convert to common stock, the company is worth $42.05 billion.

基于652百万的完全摊薄股价，包括将转换为普通股的限制股单位，该公司价值4250.5亿美元。

Roblox hits the market coming off an explosive year of growth, primarily from kids who were stuck at home because Covid-19 forced schools to close and sports leagues to shut down. Within Roblox, which is one of the top-grossing apps on Apple and Google devices, users create avatars that they can move between the millions of games on the service, navigating theme parks, adopting virtual pets and fighting enemies with their friends.

Roblox击中了市场的爆炸性增长，主要来自被困在家的孩子，因为Covid-19强迫学校关闭和体育联盟关闭。在Roblox中，这是Apple和Google设备上的顶级应用程序之一，用户可以创建头像，他们可以在数百万游戏之间移动，导航主题公园，采用虚拟宠物和与朋友斗争敌人。

Revenue last year jumped 82% to $923.9 million. Still, Roblox's net loss widened to $253.3 million from $71 million in 2019 as the company almost tripled the amount of money it paid to developers through its revenue sharing program. More than 1,250 developers made at least $10,000 last year through virtual sales in their Roblox games.

Roblox had planned to go public late last year through a traditional IPO. The company delayed its offering after Airbnb and DoorDash debuted and underpriced their shares, leading to a huge discount for new investors. Roblox opted to go the direct listing route and raised a smaller funding round in January to minimize dilution.

Roblox已计划通过传统的IPO去年后于上市。该公司在Airbnb和Doordash首次亮相和低估的股票后推迟了其发售，导致新投资者的巨大折扣。 Roblox选择了直接上市路线，并在1月份筹集了一个较小的融资，以尽量减少稀释。

The company then again delayed its debut last month and restated its financials after the SEC scrutinized the way it recognizes revenue from sales of Robux, its digital currency.

然后，该公司再次推迟其上个月首次亮相，并在SEC审查后重建了其财务状况，以其承认销售销售人员的销售额，其数字货币的收入。

Roblox is a particularly difficult company to value because there's no telling what will happen when schools reopen and kids return to playing with their real-life friends. The company has been adding content for older audiences, such as virtual concerts, meet-ups and educational experiences, but it's still primarily reliant on kids using their parents money.

Roblox是一家特别困难的公司，因为有价值，因为有没有说出学校重新开放和孩子们回到与他们的真实朋友一起玩的情况发生什么。该公司一直为较旧的受众添加内容，例如虚拟音乐会，举行会议和教育体验，但它仍然主要依赖于使用父母的钱。

"Roblox has been growing for 15 years, driven by our community, driven by the awesome content, driven by our creators, and driven by our ability for people to do things together. That's a long term growth path and we believe that continues forward, even after Covid," Roblox CEO David Baszucki told CNBC's " Squawk Box" early Wednesday.

Roblox is forecasting rapid growth in 2021, albeit at a much slower clip than last year. Revenue is expected to climb between 56% and 64% to $1.44 billion to $1.52 billion, the company said last week. That suggests higher revenue per user, because the company sees the number of daily active users only increasing 6% to 12%, while total engagement hours are expected to be roughly flat.

Roblox预测2021年的快速增长，尽管比去年更慢的夹子。该公司上周表示，预计收入预计将攀升56％至64％至14.4亿美元至15.2亿美元。这表明每个用户的收入更高，因为公司认为每日活跃用户的数量只增加6％至12％，而且预计总接触时间将大致平坦。

"We headed into 2020 with strong organic growth which was further bolstered by social distancing restrictions," Michael Guthrie, Roblox's finance chief, said in the guidance statement. "As those restrictions ease, we expect the rates of growth in 2021 will be well below the rates in 2020, however, we believe we will see absolute growth in most of our core metrics for the full year."

At its opening price on Wednesday, Roblox is valued at about 29 times revenue for 2021, based on the mid-point of its guidance. That's slightly above software developer  Unity, which is valued at about 27 times forward sales, and significantly higher than game companies like  Zynga,  Electronic Arts and  Playtika, which all have multiples in the single digits.
