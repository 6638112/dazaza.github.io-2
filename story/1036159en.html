Navigate to where you downloaded AltDeploy to, and extract the AltDeploy .zip. You should have the AltDeploy application in the same directory as the AltDeploy .zip.

导航到下载AltDeploy的位置，然后解压缩AltDeploy .zip。您应该将AltDeploy应用程序与AltDeploy .zip放在同一目录中。

Click on 'No file selected.', and on the dropdown menu that appears, click on 'Browse...'

单击“未选择文件。”，然后在出现的下拉菜单上，单击“浏览...”。

AltDeploy will prompt about a missing mail plugin, which is required for the app to function. Click on 'Install'.

AltDeploy将提示您缺少邮件插件，该插件是应用程序运行所必需的。点击“安装”。

If you get a prompt to do so, enter the password for your Mac to install the mail plugin.

如果提示您这样做，请输入Mac的密码以安装邮件插件。

At the bottom of the Mail Preferences window, click on 'Manage Plug-ins...'. Check AltPlugin.mailbundle if it is not already checked, then click 'Apply and Restart Mail'.

在“邮件首选项”窗口的底部，单击“管理插件...”。检查AltPlugin.mailbundle（如果尚未选中），然后单击“应用并重新启动邮件”。

Upon your first time running, AltDeploy will ask for your Apple ID username and password. This is required for the installation process. Enter your Apple ID username and password, then click 'Save'. From now on, when you sideload an appication to your iOS device with AltDeploy, it will only ask for your Mac password.

首次运行时，AltDeploy将询问您的Apple ID用户名和密码。这是安装过程所必需的。输入您的Apple ID用户名和密码，然后单击“保存”。从现在开始，当您使用AltDeploy将应用程序加载到iOS设备上时，它只会询问您的Mac密码。

AltDeploy will start sideloading Odyssey onto your device! If you get an error that your device is not trusted, make sure your device is unlocked and make sure you trust the computer on your device when the prompt shows on your screen.

AltDeploy将开始将Odyssey侧面加载到您的设备上！如果出现错误，表明设备不受信任，请确保设备已解锁，并在屏幕上显示提示时确保您信任设备上的计算机。

Navigate to where you downloaded nullximpactor to, and extract the nullximpactor .zip. You should have the nullxi›mpactor application in the same directory as the nullximpactor zip.

导航到将nullximpactor下载到的位置，然后解压缩nullximpactor .zip。您应该将nullxi› mpactor应用程序与nullximpactor zip放在同一目录中。

Open the Mail application. In your Mac's status bar on the left, click on 'Mail', then in the dropdown menu that pops up, click on Preferences. If you have already installed AltDeploy, AltStore, or nullximpactor, and have enabled the mail plugin, you can skip this step.

打开邮件应用程序。在左侧Mac的状态栏中，单击“邮件”，然后在弹出的下拉菜单中单击“首选项”。如果您已经安装了AltDeploy，AltStore或nullximpactor，并且已启用邮件插件，则可以跳过此步骤。

At the bottom of the Mail Preferences window, click on 'Manage Plug-ins...'. Check AltPlugin.mailbundle if it is not already checked, then click 'Apply and Restart Mail'. If you have already installed AltDeploy, AltStore, or nullximpactor, and have enabled the mail plugin, you can skip this step.

在“邮件首选项”窗口的底部，单击“管理插件...”。检查AltPlugin.mailbundle（如果尚未选中），然后单击“应用并重新启动邮件”。如果您已经安装了AltDeploy，AltStore或nullximpactor，并且已启用邮件插件，则可以跳过此步骤。

Go back to your nullximpactor window, and in the 'IPA URL' textbox, right click, and click 'Paste'.

返回您的nullximpactor窗口，并在“ IPA URL”文本框中，右键单击，然后单击“粘贴”。

nullximpactor will ask for your Apple ID username and password. This is required for the installation process. Enter your Apple ID username and password, then click 'Install'. Odyssey will now be sideloaded onto your device! If you get a 'Could not connect to AltServer' error, make sure your device is paired to your computer in Finder/iTunes, then try again.

nullximpactor将询问您的Apple ID用户名和密码。这是安装过程所必需的。输入您的Apple ID用户名和密码，然后单击“安装”。 Odyssey现在将被侧面加载到您的设备上！如果出现“无法连接到AltServer”错误，请确保您的设备已与Finder / iTunes中的计算机配对，然后重试。
