Melting ice patch in Norway reveals large collection of ancient arrows
挪威融化的冰块揭示出大量古代箭矢

2020-11-29 09:01:50

A team of researchers affiliated with a host of institutions in Norway and one in the U.K., has unveiled their findings after collecting and studying a very large number of ancient arrows they found near a melting ice patch in Norway's Jotunheimen Mountains. In their paper published in the journal The Holocene, the group describes how they kept their research secret to avoid the possibility of others contaminating the site and what they have learned about the arrows thus far. Back in 2006, archeologist Reidar Marstein found an ancient shoe lying near a melting ice patch (which subsequent recent has shown to have formed approximately around 5600BC) in the Jotunheimen Mountains. The shoe was initially believed to have been from the Viking era, but subsequent study showed it to be approximately 3,300 years old. That led the researchers to further investigate the site. They discovered that the area was littered with arrows used by hunters thousands of years ago. That set off a research project that involved detailing the location of each found and then the study of it that followed. To date, the team has found 68 arrows at the site, dating from the Stone Age up to the Middle Ages—the oldest has been dated to 6,000 years ago, which they note is approximately 800 years before Ötzi.

与挪威和英国的许多机构相关的一组研究人员在收集并研究了他们在挪威的尤通黑门山脉融化的冰块附近发现的大量古代箭头后，揭露了他们的发现。该小组在《全新世》杂志上发表的论文中描述了他们如何保持研究秘密，以避免其他人污染该地点的可能性，以及迄今为止他们对这些箭所学到的知识。早在2006年，考古学家Reidar Marstein在Jotunheimen山中发现了一块古老的鞋，它躺在融化的冰块附近（后来的冰块显示大约在5600BC左右形成）。该鞋最初被认为是维京时代的，但随后的研究表明它大约有3300年的历史。这导致研究人员进一步调查了该地点。他们发现该地区散布着数千年前猎人使用的箭头。这就引发了一个研究项目，其中涉及详细介绍每个发现者的位置，然后对其进行研究。迄今为止，该团队已在该地点发现了68支箭，其历史可追溯至石器时代至中世纪-最古老的石器可追溯至6000年前，他们指出这距Ötzi约800年。

The researchers note that the ice patch is very nearly a glacier and thus is quite large—but not nearly as large as it once was. Global warming has been melting the patch and as that has occurred material once frozen inside the patch has been exposed. The researchers have found most of the arrows on the ground next to the ice patch, which has led to degradation due to exposure to the elements. But they have also found several arrows laying on top of the ice, an indication that they have only recently been exposed. They note that while some of the arrows found on top of the ice were in good condition, many were not, suggesting they had been degraded due to ice movement inside the patch—and perhaps exposure due to prior melting of the .

研究人员指出，冰块几乎是冰川，因此很大，但不比以前大。全球变暖一直在使贴片融化，并且一旦贴片内部冻结，就会暴露出这种物质。研究人员在冰块旁边的地面上发现了大部分箭头，由于暴露于这些元素，导致了箭头的退化。但是他们还发现了冰面上有几支箭，这表明它们只是最近才被暴露出来。他们注意到，虽然在冰上发现的某些箭头处于良好状态，但许多箭头却处于不良状态，这表明它们已由于补丁内部的冰移动而退化，并且可能是由于先前融化而暴露了出来。

The researchers have also found reindeer antlers and bones and other biological matter but have been surprised by how few of them have been revealed as the ice melts.
