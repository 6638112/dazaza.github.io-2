The Future of the Free Software Foundation
自由软件基金会的未来

2020-11-26 01:56:39

Although only a team of four, the Free Software Foundation (FSF) techteam delivers a huge range of services for the free softwarecommunity, providing infrastructure for not only the FSF, but alsohosting and mailing lists for hundreds of free software projectsaround the world. They're a key part of our mission and commitment tocomputer users, and pave the way for others to do their computing infull freedom.

尽管只有四个人，但自由软件基金会（FSF）技术团队为自由软件社区提供了广泛的服务，不仅为FSF提供了基础设施，而且还为全球数百个自由软件项目提供了托管和邮件列表。它们是我们对计算机用户的使命和承诺的重要组成部分，为其他人完全自由地进行计算铺平了道路。

Can you pledge your support for the tech team's important workfor software freedom by becoming an associate member? Thework of the tech team depends directly on support from the widerfree software community, and the FSF can't fulfill its missionwithout you. This fall fundraiser, our goal is to reach 500new associate members. If you join today, you can select aspecial gift, in addition to being able to enjoy all of ourassociate membership benefits, which include the free "as infreedom" videoconferencing server mentioned in thisappeal. If you're unable to become a member yourself, you canmake a donation instead. Anything you can contribute willenable the tech team to support the free software community.

您是否可以成为准成员来保证对技术团队在软件自由方面的重要工作的支持？技术团队的工作直接取决于更广泛的自由软件社区的支持，如果没有您，FSF将无法完成其使命。今年秋天的筹款活动，我们的目标是达到500名新的准会员。如果您今天加入，您除了可以享受我们所有关联会员的特权外，还可以选择一份特殊礼物，其中包括本呼吁中提到的免费的“作为自由”视频会议服务器。如果您自己无法成为会员，则可以捐款。您可以做出的任何贡献都会使技术团队支持自由软件社区。

The FSF is well-known for spearheading the advocacy and support offree software, not just by recommending it in the face of pervasiveproprietary options, but also by condemning nonfree softwarealtogether. Following this recommendation is hard, even for us,because of the ever-increasing dependency on software and computernetworks that we are all subject to. To follow through with ourcommitment, our tech team maintains a large list of services that manyother offices our size would have long ago been wrongly pressured intotransferring to one of the handful of gigantic corporations thatmonopolize those services.

FSF以带头倡导和支持免费软件而闻名，它不仅针对普遍的专有选项推荐了它，而且还谴责了非自由软件。即使对于我们来说，遵循此建议也很困难，因为对我们所有人都越来越依赖软件和计算机网络。为了兑现我们的承诺，我们的技术团队维护着大量的服务，而我们这些规模的许多其他办事处早就被错误地迫于压力，转移给少数垄断这些服务的巨型公司之一。

Your work email account is most likely implemented through Gmail orOutlook; your office's software is likely to be served by Amazon WebServices, along with all the data backups; your company's customerservice is likely to be managed through Salesforce or SAP, and soon. Make no mistake, this is true for your local government and schoolnetworks, too!

您的工作电子邮件帐户很可能是通过Gmail或Outlook实施的；您的办公室软件很可能由Amazon WebServices以及所有数据备份提供；您公司的客户服务很可能会很快通过Salesforce或SAP进行管理。没错，这对于您当地的政府和学校网络也是正确的！

In contrast, at the FSF, we never jumped on the outsourcing wagon, andwe don't use any Service as a Software Substitute (SaaSS) in ouroperations. We run our own email servers, telephony and fax service,print shop, full server stack, backups, networking, systemsmonitoring, accounting, customer relationship management (CRM)software, and a long list of other tasks and software developmentprojects, with a team of just four extremely dedicatedtechnicians. And we implement this on hardware that has been carefullyevaluated to meet very high ethical standards, criteria that we pushfor vendors to achieve through our "Respects Your Freedom"certification program.

相比之下，在FSF，我们从未跳过外包之路，我们在运营中不使用任何服务作为软件替代（SaaSS）。我们运行自己的电子邮件服务器，电话和传真服务，印刷车间，完整的服务器堆栈，备份，网络，系统监视，记帐，客户关系管理（CRM）软件以及其他一系列任务和软件开发项目，其中包括只有四个非常敬业的技术人员。并且，我们在经过严格评估以符合非常高的道德标准的硬件上实施此标准，这些标准是我们要求供应商通过“尊重您的自由”认证计划实现的标准。

We are the first to understand how hard it can be for people toliberate themselves, even more so when other problems in our livestake away our attention and energy in times of hardship. But we mustbe vigilant to not allow those hardships to be used as excuses tofurther erode our freedom. Because of this, we try our best not tojust liberate our own organization, but to help the free softwarecommunity, and society at large, in this demanding process.

我们是第一个了解人们解放自己的艰辛的人，当生活中的其他问题在困难时期失去了我们的注意力和精力时，我们将更加了解。但是，我们必须保持警惕，不要以这些苦难为借口进一步削弱我们的自由。因此，我们尽最大努力不只是解放我们自己的组织，而是在这个苛刻的过程中帮助自由软件社区和整个社会。

With this in mind, this year we implemented two freevideoconferencing services. One aimed to facilitate our associatemembers' own private communications with their friends andfamilies through a Jitsi Meet instance. And to facilitate theorganization's exploration for running meetings and conferences withfully free software, we started a BigBlueButton instance that has beenused for research and testing with the Boston Public Schools network,the City of Cambridge, and the Massachusetts Institute of Technology(MIT). It has also been used to run several online free softwareconferences, including SeaGL and the FSF's thirty-fifthanniversary event FSF35, and it will be used to run LibrePlanet2021, on March 20 and 21, 2021.

考虑到这一点，今年我们实施了两项免费的视频会议服务。一个旨在通过Jitsi Meet实例促进准会员自己与朋友和家人的私人交流。为了促进组织对使用完全免费的软件运行会议的探索，我们启动了一个BigBlueButton实例，该实例已与波士顿公立学校网络，剑桥市和麻省理工学院（MIT）一起用于研究和测试。它也已用于运行多个在线免费软件会议，包括SeaGL和FSF的30周年纪念活动FSF35，并将在2021年3月20日至21日运行LibrePlanet2021。

To continue with this effort, these are some of the projects that wehave lined up for 2021:

为了继续这项工作，我们为2021年安排了以下一些项目：

Forge: We want to offer an alternative to GitHub and other repositories that are not committed to freedom and privacy. This service is needed by the free software developer community in particular, but also by the larger community of creators of freely licensed materials of all kinds. We will be hosting our own repository implemented exclusively with free software, and that will allow participants to export their projects and migrate them to their own instances at any time.

Forge：我们想为GitHub和其他不致力于自由和隐私的存储库提供替代方案。尤其是自由软件开发人员社区需要此服务，更广泛的各种免费许可材料的创建者社区都需要此服务。我们将托管自己的存储库，该存储库专门使用免费软件实现，这将使参与者可以随时导出其项目并将其迁移到自己的实例中。

New FSF Web site: Following the recent round of improvements to our front page, we want to continue modernizing our online presence by increasing user friendliness and focusing on responsiveness.

新的FSF网站：在对首页进行了最近一轮改进之后，我们希望通过提高用户友好性和关注响应能力来继续实现在线状态的现代化。

Videoconferencing resources: Our associate members now have the option to use our videoconferencing service to talk to anyone they would like in freedom (including non-members), and with more resources, we could potentially offer this to the public. We are committed to show how organizations of all sizes -- from a classroom to a city governance, online conference, or university network -- can take control of their online communication platforms by providing better documentation on how to run your own videoconferencing instance.

视频会议资源：现在，准会员可以选择使用我们的视频会议服务与他们想要的任何人自由交谈（包括非会员），并且有了更多的资源，我们可以向公众提供此服务。我们致力于展示各种规模的组织（从教室到城市治理，在线会议或大学网络）如何通过提供有关如何运行自己的视频会议实例的更好文档来控制其在线交流平台。

Community servers: Aside from being the home for Emacs and many other packages of the GNU Project, we also host community servers for other free software projects, like KDE, Sugar Labs, Replicant, GNewSense, and Trisquel. If your community abides by the principles of free software, the FSF wants to have the capacity to host your free software project through our infrastructure. This is a project that is high on our wish list, and we are working on obtaining the funds and resources to make this happen.

社区服务器：除了作为Emacs和GNU Project的许多其他软件包的宿主外，我们还为其他免费软件项目（例如KDE，Sugar Labs，Replicant，GNewSense和Trisquel）托管社区服务器。如果您的社区遵守免费软件的原则，那么FSF希望有能力通过我们的基础架构托管您的免费软件项目。这个项目在我们的心愿单上很重要，我们正在努力获得资金和资源来实现这一目标。

All of these efforts, including the often invisible ones we do to keepthe FSF's own high standards for internal operation, could not bepossible without your help and generous contributions. The FreeSoftware Foundation is primarily sustained by individual donations andmemberships, keeping us independent. We hope you'll continuesupporting our efforts so that we can continue to expand our servicesto an ever-growing audience, until the day that free software is therule, rather than the exception.

没有您的帮助和慷慨的贡献，所有这些努力，包括我们为保持FSF自身内部运作的高标准而经常做出的无形努力都是不可能的。自由软件基金会主要靠个人捐款和会员身份来维持，从而使我们保持独立。我们希望您继续支持我们的努力，以便我们能够继续向不断增长的受众扩展我们的服务，直到免费软件成为惯例而非例外的那一天为止。
