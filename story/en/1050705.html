End to End Encrypted, Private Search
端到端加密的私有搜索

2021-03-04 00:37:17

Every single search request from our website and extension is encrypted locally on the client and proxied through our service, stripping away your public IP address ensuring that only the search provider is able to decrypt and see your query without any knowledge of who you are. The results are returned to you through our proxy and are rendered locally on your machine only using javascript running on your machine.

来自我们网站和扩展的每一个搜索请求都在客户端上本地加密，并通过我们的服务提出，剥离您的公共IP地址，确保只有搜索提供商能够解密并查看您的查询，而不知道您的查询。结果通过我们的代理返回给您，并且只使用计算机上运行的JavaScript在您的计算机上本地呈现。

Rather than relying solely on closed technologies, we've made a genuine effort to publish our code and to use well known and thoroughly tested open solutions to provide a truly private search experience. Our website and extension is built with open source technologies and can easily be audited and improved through our open source program (coming soon!).

我们而不是依赖于封闭式技术，而不是依赖于封闭的技术，这是真正的努力来发布我们的代码并使用众所周知和彻底测试的开放解决方案来提供真正的私人搜索体验。我们的网站和扩展包括开源技术，通过我们的开源计划（即将推出！）可以轻松审核和改进。
