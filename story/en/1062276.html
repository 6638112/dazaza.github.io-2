Ethereum Founder Regifts Unsolicited DOGE Knockoffs, Donates a Billion Dollars Worth of SHIB To an Indian COVID Relief Fund
Ethereum创始人揭开未经请求的Doge淘汰赛，向印度Covid救济基金提供了十亿美元的施

2021-05-13 03:44:25

In a move that captivated the attention of Crypto Twitter on Wednesday, the Ethereum founder regifted tokens sent to his public wallet by the creators of Shiba Inu coin (SHIB), Dogelon (ELON), Akita Inu (AKITA), mwDOGE (mwDOGE) and OURSHIB (OSHIB), blockchain records show.

在一个举例中，在周三迷住了Crypto Twitter的注意，Ethereum创始人被Shiba Inu Coin（Shib），Dogelon（Elon），Akita Inu（Akita），MWDOGE（MWDOGe）的创造者发送给他的公共钱包OuShib（Oshib），BlockChain Records Show。

Notably, Buterin donated 50 trillion SHIB tokens (worth a nominal $1.2 billion at press time) to the India Covid Relief Fund kicked off by Polygon founder Sandeep Nailwal late last month. He also sent about $431 million of AKITA to Gitcoin, according to the records, a public Ethereum-based fundraising platform.

值得注意的是，Buterin捐赠了50万亿的Shib令牌（价值12亿美元的新闻时间）到印度Covid救济基金由Polygon创始人Sandeep Nailwal延迟启动了。据纪录，他还送到了大约4.31亿美元的秋田到Gitcoin，这是一个基于公共的基于Ethereum的筹款平台。

Memecoin creators have been sending large amounts of their tokens to the Ethereum figurehead in recent days. CryptoSlate reported Monday Vitalik was sent trillions of SHIB tokens worth over $8 billion at one point.

MemeCoin创作者最近几天一直向Ethereum傀儡发送大量代币。 Cryptoslate报告星期一Vitalik在一点中向价值超过80亿美元的股票送达了万亿令牌。

The memecoin mania has caused Ethereum gas fees to surge to staggering highs, perhaps animating the network’s founder to strike back.

Memecoin Mania导致了以太um煤气费用飙升到惊人的高位，也许有动画网络的创始人罢工。

SHIB is down roughly 38% since Buterin started unloading but still boasts a market cap of $9 billion, according to CoinMarketCap.

根据CoinmarketCap的说法，Shib率开始卸载大约38％，距离Buterin开始卸载，但仍然拥有90亿美元的市场上限。

The coin has had more than $7 billion in volume in the last 24 hours.

过去24小时内，硬币已超过70亿美元。
