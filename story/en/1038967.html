MasterCard and Visa will stop processing cards for Pornhub
万事达卡和维萨卡将停止处理Pornhub卡

2020-12-11 07:44:57

Mastercard Inc said on Thursday it was ending the use of its cards on Pornhub after its investigation confirmed the presence of unlawful content on the sex videos site.

万事达卡公司（Mastercard Inc）周四表示，在调查确认性爱视频网站上存在非法内容后，将停止在Pornhub上使用其卡。

Pornhub said on Tuesday it had banned video downloads and was allowing only certain partner accounts to upload content after a New York Times column reported that many videos posted on the adult website depicted sexual assault of children.

Pornhub周二表示，它已禁止视频下载，并且在《纽约时报》专栏报道成人网站上发布的许多视频都描绘了对儿童的性侵犯之后，该网站仅允许某些合作伙伴帐户上传内容。

The newspaper column had also described some videos on the site as recordings of assaults on unconscious women and girls.

报纸专栏还描述了该网站上的一些录像带，记录了对失去知觉的妇女和女孩的袭击。

Billionaire investor Bill Ackman asked Visa Inc to follow suit. Visa did not immediately respond to a request for comment. (Reporting by Munsif Vengattil and Noor Zainab Hussain; Editing by Anil D’Silva)

亿万富翁投资者比尔·阿克曼（Bill Ackman）要求Visa Inc跟进。 Visa没有立即回应置评请求。 （Munsif Vengattil和Noor Zainab Hussain的报道； Anil D’Silva的编辑）
