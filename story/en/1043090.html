URL shorteners set ad tracking cookies
网址缩短器设置广告跟踪Cookie

2021-01-04 16:35:32

This Christmas, a family member sent me a to a family Zoom call. However, they didn’t send me a direct link to Zoom. Instead, they sent me a tinyurl.com” link.

这个圣诞节，一个家庭成员给我发送了一个“家庭变焦”电话。但是，他们没有给我发送直接链接到Zoom的链接。相反，他们给我发了一个tinyurl.com”链接。

When I clicked on the link, my bar flashed an intermediate domain that was neither Zoom nor TinyURL. Later, I used cURL to see where this was really going.

当我单击链接时，我的栏闪烁了一个既不是Zoom也不是TinyURL的中间域。后来，我使用cURL来查看实际情况。

Sure enough, the redirect wasn’t clean at all. TinyURL was first sending me to VigLink 1. VigLink is an advertising (tracking) company that specializes in affiliate marketing.

当然，重定向根本不干净。 TinyURL首先将我发送到VigLink1。VigLink是一家广告（跟踪）公​​司，专门从事会员营销。

Following the redirect in cURL reveals another unsavory fact. VigLink sets cookies before they send me to the intended destination on Zoom.

在cURL中进行重定向后，发现了另一个不愉快的事实。 VigLink会先设置cookie，然后再将它们发送到Zoom上的预期目的地。

These cookies give them the ability 2 to track me across every other site that uses their advertising tech. Who knows what VigLink is doing with my data, but I personally wouldn’t trust an advertising company to keep my browsing history to themselves.

这些Cookie使他们能够2在使用其广告技术的所有其他网站上跟踪我。谁知道VigLink在处理我的数据，但是我个人不信任广告公司将自己的浏览历史记录保存下来。

Furthermore, they didn’t give me a chance to opt-out of this tracking. I’m currently based in Europe and I would expect to see at least an interstitial asking for consent to be tracked. TinyURL’s privacy policy, last updated in 2012, has no mention of either third party data-sharing nor the cookies they share from affiliates.

此外，他们没有给我机会退出此跟踪。我目前居住在欧洲，希望能看到至少一个插页式广告要求您跟踪同意。 TinyURL的隐私政策最近一次更新是在2012年，没有提及第三方数据共享或他们从关联企业共享的Cookie。

This isn’t a phenomenon limited to TinyURL. More common shorteners like t.co (Twitter) and bit.ly set cookies when you click on a link. While neither redirect you to an advertising company like TinyURL, Twitter’s primary business model is advertising, and bit.ly’s privacy policy says they share data with third parties to …provide advertising products and services…”

这不是仅限于TinyURL的现象。单击链接时，更常见的起酥油（例如t.co（Twitter）和bit.ly设置cookie）。虽然没有将您重定向到TinyURL之类的广告公司，但Twitter的主要商业模式是广告，并且bit.ly的隐私权政策规定，他们与第三方共享数据以“提供广告产品和服务”。

Don’t use shorteners. And if you click on a link from a shortener, I recommend using tools like the Temporary Containers Firefox extension to limit the scope of ad tracking. Personally, I took the time to send Sovrn (VigLink’s parent company) a request, and made sure to give them my tracking cookie. I’ll update this blog and my newsletter if I actually get anything substantive back.

不要使用起酥油。而且，如果您点击了缩短程序中的链接，我建议您使用Firefox临时容器扩展程序之类的工具来限制广告跟踪的范围。我个人花了一些时间向Sovrn（VigLink的母公司）发送请求，并确保将其跟踪Cookie交给他们。如果我确实得到了实质性的帮助，我将更新此博客和新闻通讯。

Their main website was initially blocked by my ad-blocking software. I figured I’d just link to Wikipedia here. ↩

他们的主要网站最初被我的广告拦截软件封锁。我以为我只是在这里链接到Wikipedia。 ↩

Browsers like Safari and Firefox are getting better at catching these drive-by attempts to set cookies. I applaud those efforts, but since this type of tracking works in many cases and is explicitly limited by privacy law, I think it’s still noteworthy. ↩

像Safari和Firefox这样的浏览器在捕获这些设置cookie的偷渡行为方面越来越好。我为这些努力表示赞赏，但是由于这种跟踪在很多情况下都有效，并且受到隐私法的明确限制，因此我认为仍然值得关注。 ↩
