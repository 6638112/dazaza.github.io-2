Flying Cars Airport of the Future To Land in England
未来的飞行汽车机场将登陆英国

2021-01-30 02:02:12

LONDON (Reuters) - An airport for flying cars will thrust the English city of Coventry into the future later this year, with a project aimed at demonstrating how air taxis will work in urban centres.

伦敦（路透社）-一个飞行汽车的机场将在今年晚些时候将英国考文垂市推向未来，该项目旨在展示空中出租车在城市中心的运作方式。

Urban-Air Port, a British-based start-up, has partnered with car giant Hyundai Motor to develop the infrastructure required for when flying cars take to the skies to ferry around people and goods.

英国初创企业Urban-Air Port已与汽车巨头现代汽车（Hyundai Motor）合作，开发了将汽车飞上天空运送人和货物所需的基础设施。

From November, visitors to Coventry will be able to see what a flying car airport looks like and see a passenger-carrying drone and an operational electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) vehicle on the landing pad.

从11月起，考文垂的游客将能够看到飞机场的样子，并在着陆板上看到载客无人机和可操作的垂直起降电动（eVTOL）车辆。

Urban-Air Port was selected by a government programme aimed at developing zero-emission flying and new air vehicles, winning a 1.2-million-pound ($1.65-million) grant to help fund the temporary installation of the airport in Coventry city centre.

一项旨在开发零排放飞行和新型航空器的政府计划选择了城市航空港，赢得了120万英镑（合165万美元）的赠款，用于资助在考文垂市中心临时安装机场。

“With UK government backing and Hyundai Motor Group’s backing we’ll be realising the first fully operational airport in the world,” Urban Air-Port founder and executive chairman Ricky Sandhu told Reuters on Friday.

Urban Air-Port创始人兼执行主席里奇·桑德（Ricky Sandhu）周五对路透社表示：“在英国政府的支持和现代汽车集团的支持下，我们将实现世界上第一个全面运营的机场。”

While there has been progress on developing flying cars, Sandhu said the infrastructure was the missing piece.

尽管开发飞行汽车取得了进展，但桑德胡说，基础设施是缺失的部分。

“You can’t get off the train or get on the train unless you get to the train station so that supporting ground infrastructure is absolutely key,” he said.

他说：“除非到达火车站，否则您不能下车或坐火车，因此，支撑地面基础设施绝对是关键。”
