Apple Watch leak offers rare glimpse inside Apple’s ‘Ultra security program
Apple Watch泄漏提供了苹果“ Ultra安全计划”的难得一见

2020-12-29 20:32:28

New images and video purporting to show an Apple Watch prototype, concealed by a security case that resembles a tiny iPod, offer a rare look behind the product-development curtain of the notoriously secretive company built by Steve Jobs. First teased a few months ago by Twitter user Apple Demo, this is our first look at the device turned on and running Apple’s own internal development apps on a pre-watchOS 1.0 software build.

新的图像和视频看起来像是一个Apple Watch原型，被一个类似于iPod的小巧的安全保护盒所掩盖，在由史蒂夫·乔布斯（Steve Jobs）创立的秘密公司的产品开发帘幕下，难得一见。这是几个月前由Twitter用户Apple Demo嘲笑的，这是我们首次看到该设备已开启并在watchOS 1.0之前的版本上运行Apple自己的内部开发应用程序。

The video starts by showing what appears to be the original cardboard packaging used to deliver the prototype hardware to testers. “This product is classified as Apple Confidential and is designated an ‘Ultra’ security program,” reads the label. “This prototype MUST be returned when recalled or when your,” says the labelling before being obscured to hide the origins of the device. A sticker on the back of the prototype device itself shows it’s a “PVTe” configuration, which presumably means Prototype Validation Testing (engineering) in line with language seen on previous Apple development hardware, like the leaked EVT board for the original iPhone.

该视频首先显示了用来将原型硬件交付给测试人员的原始纸板包装。标签上写道：“该产品被归类为Apple机密，并被指定为“超”安全程序。” “该原型必须在召回时或在您使用时归还。”标签在被遮盖以隐藏设备来源之前说道。原型设备本身背面的贴纸显示它是“ PVTe”配置，这可能意味着原型验证测试（工程）与以前的Apple开发硬件上看到的语言一致，例如泄漏的原始iPhone EVT板。

When the touchscreen device is turned on, we can see that it’s configured to show Apple’s own internal apps. One is the “Lisa tester,” identified with an icon of Lisa Simpson but likely a tribute to Jobs’ daughter and namesake of Apple’s Lisa computer — one of the first computers to feature a GUI interface. The app lets testers tweak the UI elements of the prototype Watch. The “Springboard zoom” app found inside the Lisa Tester is very similar to the original watchOS home screen that shipped on the first Apple Watch in 2015.

触摸屏设备打开后，我们可以看到它已配置为显示Apple自己的内部应用程序。其中一个就是“丽莎测试仪”，它以Lisa Simpson的图标标识，但可能是对乔布斯的女儿和苹果公司Lisa计算机的同名产品的致敬，后者是第一批具有GUI界面的计算机之一。该应用程序使测试人员可以调整Watch原型的UI元素。 Lisa Tester内的“ Springboard zoom”应用程序与2015年首款Apple Watch随附的原始watchOS主屏幕非常相似。

There’s no Digital Crown for navigation. Instead buttons along the right side of the case can be used for home and power. The Home button on the front and what appear to be volume up/down buttons on the left are never shown being used and might not be functional. When clicking into settings, the device reports that it hasn’t received FCC approval for sales, adding additional confirmation of its prototype status.

没有用于导航的Digital Crown。取而代之的是，沿着箱子右侧的按钮可用于家庭和电源。前面的“主页”按钮和左边的似乎是音量增大/减小按钮从未被使用过，并且可能无法正常工作。点击设置后，设备会报告尚未获得FCC的销售批准，从而进一步确认了原型状态。

Assuming the prototype is real — and it definitely seems that way — then it’s a remarkable example of a pre-production Apple device we were never meant to see.

假设原型是真实的-而且肯定是那样-那么这就是我们从未见过的预生产Apple设备的杰出示例。
