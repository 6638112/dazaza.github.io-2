“深海之火”(1993)的超文本注释版

2020-10-23 15:49:50

Those of you reading this group have read a lot of talk about Vernor Vinge's novel "A Fire Upon the Deep" which is nominated for the Hugo and the Nebula Award. This novel advances much of it's super-plot via a Galactic USENET which is a parody of our own. It also centers around AI, as many of Vinge's ground-breaking stories have. We're pleased to publish this novel in electronic form, along with all the other Hugo nominees in one anthology. (See my other posting or ftp.clarinet.com:sf/info for more details.) However, when Vernor Vinge wrote this book, he (like a true programmer) filled the manuscript with comments, doing his author's notes right in the text of the manuscript. We've converted that manuscript to a hypertext form. There are 70,000 words of notes that annotate the book's development at every step. On most of the paragraphs, there are links which take you to the author's and proofer's notes on the book. Some of it is quite mundane, but there's also tons of interesting material for those interesting in this book or the development of books in general. Right now the hypertext version is available in two forms. Under MS windows, you can run it from our CD-ROM, using MS multimedia viewer. In this case, symbols appear in the margins which you can click on. They open a notes window (or move in it) to show you the notes for the paragraphs. Special symbols indicate the type of note. Another version exists as an RTF file with footnotes. In MS-Word and some other word processors that can use footnotes, you can open the footnotes window, and as you scroll through the book, the footnotes scroll through the footnotes window. I also plan, in a few weeks, to make an HTML version, for use with WWW browsers such as XMosaic. Unfortunately, XMosaic does not support popups or dual windows yet, so if you jump to the notes, you lose the text while looking at the notes. I'm working on some designs to help with this. We can also offer the novel in an ASCII format with embedded notes, but this is not really suitable for first-time readers of the novel, as they are too distracting. Those of you fascinated with this book will definitely enjoy this. Please read the other posting or get our info file for details on how to buy and FTP it or get the CD-ROM. -- Brad Templeton, ClariNet Communications Corp. -- Sunnyvale, CA 408/296-0366

阅读这组文章的人都读过很多关于弗诺·文吉的小说“深水之火”的讨论，这部小说获得了雨果奖和星云奖的提名。这部小说通过一个戏仿我们自己的银河系Usenet推进了它的大部分超级情节。它也以人工智能为中心，就像文吉的许多开创性故事一样。我们很高兴将这本小说以电子形式出版，与所有其他雨果提名者一起出版在一本选集中。(有关详细信息，请参阅我的其他帖子或ftp.clarinet.com：sf/info。)。然而，当Vernor Vinger写这本书的时候，他(就像一个真正的程序员一样)在手稿中写满了评论，在手稿的正文中做了他作者的笔记。我们已将那份手稿转换成超文本形式。这本书的每一步都有7万字的注释注释着这本书的发展。在大多数段落上，都有链接可供您转到作者和校对者关于这本书的注释。其中一些是相当平凡的，但也有吨有趣的材料，那些感兴趣的人在这本书或图书的发展一般。目前，超文本版本有两种形式。在MS WINDOWS下，您可以使用MS多媒体查看器从我们的CD-ROM运行它。在这种情况下，符号会出现在页边空白处，您可以单击这些符号。它们会打开一个备注窗口(或在其中移动)，向您显示段落的备注。特殊符号表示注释的类型。另一个版本是带有脚注的RTF文件。在MS-Word和其他一些可以使用脚注的文字处理器中，您可以打开脚注窗口，当您在书中滚动时，脚注也会在脚注窗口中滚动。我还计划在几周内制作一个HTML版本，用于XMosaic等WWW浏览器。不幸的是，XMosaic还不支持弹出窗口或双窗口，所以如果你跳到笔记，在查看笔记时会丢失文本。我正在做一些设计来帮助解决这个问题。我们也可以提供ASCII格式的小说，并嵌入注释，但这并不真正适合小说的首次读者，因为他们太分心了。那些对这本书着迷的人一定会喜欢这本书的。请阅读其他帖子或获取我们的信息文件，以了解如何购买并通过FTP传输或获取CD-ROM的详细信息。--布拉德·邓普顿(Brad Templeton)，单簧管通信公司--加利福尼亚州桑尼维尔，邮编：408/296-0366
