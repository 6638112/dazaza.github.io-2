The first God game was developed for the Amiga
第一款神游戏是为Amiga开发的

2020-11-30 05:07:02

Populous is a video game developed by Bullfrog Productions and published by Electronic Arts, released originally for the Amiga in 1989, and is regarded by many as the first God game. [2] [3] [4] [5] With over four million copies sold, Populous is one of the best-selling PC games of all time.

Populous是由Bullfrog Productions开发并由Electronic Arts发行的视频游戏，该游戏最初于1989年为Amiga发行，并且被许多人视为第一个上帝游戏。 [2] [3] [4] [5] Populous已售出超过四百万册，是有史以来最畅销的PC游戏之一。

The player assumes the role of a deity, who must lead followers through direction, manipulation, and divine intervention, with the goal of eliminating the followers led by the opposite deity. Played from an isometric perspective, the game consists of more than 500 levels, with each level being a piece of land which contains the player's followers and the enemy's followers. The player is tasked with defeating the enemy followers and increasing their own followers' population using a series of divine powers before moving on to the next level. The game was designed by Peter Molyneux, and Bullfrog developed a gameplay prototype via a board game they invented using Lego. [6]

玩家扮演神灵的角色，他必须通过引导，操纵和神圣干预来引导跟随者，以消除由相反神灵领导的跟随者。从等轴测角度看，该游戏包含500多个关卡，每个关卡都是一块土地，其中包含玩家的追随者和敌人的追随者。玩家的任务是打败敌人的追随者，并使用一系列神圣的力量来增加自己的追随者人数，然后再继续前进。该游戏由Peter Molyneux设计，Bullfrog通过他们使用Lego发明的棋盘游戏开发了一个游戏原型。 [6]

The game received critical acclaim upon release, with critics praising the game's graphics, design, sounds and replay value. It was nominated for multiple year-end accolades, including Game of the Year from several gaming publications. The game was ported to many other computer systems and was later supported with multiple expansion packs. It is the first game in the Populous series, preceding Populous II: Trials of the Olympian Gods and Populous: The Beginning.

该游戏在发行时获得了好评，评论家称赞该游戏的图形，设计，声音和重播价值。它获得了多个年终奖的提名，其中包括来自多家游戏出版物的“年度最佳游戏”。该游戏已移植到许多其他计算机系统上，后来得到了多个扩展包的支持。这是《人口稠密》系列中的第一款游戏，先于《人口稠密II：奥林匹亚众神的审判和人口稠密：开始》。

The main action window in Populous is viewed from an isometric perspective, and it is set in a "tabletop" on which are set the command icons, the world map (depicted as an open book) and a slider bar that measures the level of the player's divine power or " mana".

从等轴测图的角度查看Populous中的主操作窗口，并将其设置在“桌面”中，在该桌面上设置命令图标，世界地图（描绘为一本打开的书）和一个滑动条，用于测量水平玩家的神力或“法力值”。

The game consists of 500 levels, and each level represents an area of land on which live the player's followers and the enemy followers. In order to progress to the next level the player must increase the number of their followers such that they can wipe out the enemy followers. This is done by using a series of divine powers. There are a number of different landscapes the world (depicted on the page in the book) can be, such as desert, rock and lava, snow and ice, etc. and the type of landscape is not merely aesthetic: it affects the development of the player's and enemy's followers.

游戏包含500个关卡，每个关卡代表一个区域，玩家的追随者和敌人的追随者都将在这片土地上生活。为了进步到新的水平，玩家必须增加跟随者的数量，以便消灭敌人的跟随者。这是通过使用一系列神力来完成的。世界上可以有许多不同的风景（在书中的页面上有描述），例如沙漠，岩石和熔岩，雪和冰等。风景的类型不仅是审美的，它还影响着世界的发展。玩家和敌人的追随者。

The most basic power is raising and lowering land. This is primarily done in order to provide flat land for the player's followers to build on (though it is also possible to remove land from around the enemy's followers). As the player's followers build more houses they create more followers, and this increases the player's mana level.

最基本的权力是增减土地。这样做主要是为了为玩家的追随者提供平坦的土地（尽管也可以从敌人的追随者周围移走土地）。随着玩家的追随者建造更多房屋，他们会创建更多的追随者，这增加了玩家的法力值。

Increasing the mana level unlocks additional divine powers that allow the player to interact further with the landscape and the population. The powers include the ability to cause earthquakes and floods, create swamps and volcanoes, and turn ordinary followers into more powerful knights. [7]

增加法力值会释放额外的神力，使玩家能够与景观和人口进一步互动。力量包括引发地震和洪水，制造沼泽和火山以及将普通追随者变成更强大的骑士的能力。 [7]

In this game the player adopts the role of a deity and assumes the responsibility of shepherding people by direction, manipulation, and divine intervention. The player has the ability to shape the landscape and grow their civilization – and their divine power – with the overall aim of having their followers conquer an enemy force, which is led by an opposing deity. [7]

在该游戏中，玩家扮演神ity的角色，并承担通过指导，操纵和神圣干预来牧养人的责任。玩家有能力塑造景观并发展他们的文明-以及他们的神圣力量-的总体目标是让他们的追随者征服敌对的力量，这是由对立的神灵领导的。 [7]

Peter Molyneux led development, inspired by Bullfrog's artist Glenn Corpes having drawn isometric blocks after playing David Braben's Virus. [8] [9]

彼得·莫里纽克斯（Peter Molyneux）领导了开发工作，灵感来自牛蛙的艺术家格伦·科普斯（Glenn Corpes），他在玩过大卫·布拉本的《病毒》后绘制了等距图块。 [8] [9]

Initially Molyneux developed an isometric landscape, then populated it with little people that he called "peeps", but there was no game; all that happened was that the peeps wandered around the landscape until they reached a barrier such as water. He developed the raise/lower terrain gameplay mechanic simply as a way of helping the peeps to move around. Then, as a way of reducing the number of peeps on the screen, he decided that if a peep encountered a piece of blank, flat land, it would build a house, and that a larger area of land would enable a peep to build a larger house. Thus the core mechanics – god-like intervention and the desire for peeps to expand – were created. [8] [9]

最初，Molyneux开发了等轴测图景观，然后在他称之为“偷窥”的小人物中居住，但是没有游戏。所发生的一切只是偷窥者在景观中徘徊，直到它们到达诸如水之类的障碍物为止。他开发了升高/降低地形的游戏机制，只是为了帮助窥视者四处走动。然后，作为减少屏幕上窥视次数的一种方法，他决定，如果窥视器遇到一片空白的平坦土地，则可以建造房屋，而较大的土地面积将使窥视器能够建造一个窥视器。大房子。因此，创建了核心机制-像神一样的干预和对窥视的渴望。 [8] [9]

The endgame – of creating a final battle to force the two sides to enter a final conflict – developed as a result of the developmental games going on for hours and having no firm end. [8] [9]

最终游戏-建立最终的战斗以迫使双方进入最终的冲突-是由于发展性游戏进行了数小时且没有坚定的结局而发展起来的。 [8] [9]

Bullfrog attempted to prototype the gameplay via a board game they invented using Lego, and Molyneux admits that whilst it didn't help the developers to balance the game at all, it provided a useful media angle to help publicise the game. [8] [10]

Bullfrog尝试通过他们使用Lego发明的棋盘游戏来原型化游戏玩法，而Molyneux承认，虽然它完全不能帮助开发人员平衡游戏，但它提供了有用的媒体视角来帮助宣传游戏。 [8] [10]

During the test phase the testers requested a cheat code to skip the end of the game, as there was insufficient time to play through all 500 levels, and it was only at this point that Bullfrog realised that they had not included any kind of ending to the game. The team quickly repurposed an interstitial page from between levels and used it as the final screen. [8]

在测试阶段，测试人员要求作弊代码以跳过游戏的结尾，因为没有足够的时间在所有500个关卡中进行游戏，而就在这一点上，Bullfrog才意识到他们并没有包括任何结局。游戏。团队迅速在各个级别之间重新调整了插页的用途，并将其用作最终屏幕。 [8]

After demoing the game to over a dozen publishers, Bullfrog eventually gained the interest of Electronic Arts, who had a gap in their spring release schedule and was willing to take a chance on the game. Bullfrog accepted their offer, although Molyneux later described the contract as "pretty atrocious:" 10% royalties on units sold, rising to 12% after one million units sold, with only a small up-front payment. [8]

在将游戏演示给十几家发行商之后，Bullfrog最终引起了Electronic Arts的兴趣，Electronic Arts的春季发布时间表有所不足，并愿意借此机会参与游戏。 Bullfrog接受了他们的报价，尽管Molyneux后来将合同描述为“相当残酷：”售出产品的特许权使用费为10％，售出100万件产品后，特许权使用费只有少量的前期付款，涨至12％。 [8]

Peter Molyneux presented a post-mortem of the games development and work in progress on a related personal project at Game Developers Conference in 2011. [11]

彼得·莫利纽克斯（Peter Molyneux）在2011年的游戏开发者大会上介绍了游戏开发和正在进行的相关个人项目的验尸报告。[11]

Bullfrog produced Populous World Editor, which gave users the ability to modify the appearance of characters, cities, and terrain. [12] An expansion pack called Populous: The Promised Lands added five new types of landscape (the geometric Silly Land, Wild West, Lego style Block Land, Revolution Française, and computer themed Bit Plains). In addition, another expansion disk called Populous: The Final Frontier added a single new landscape-type and was released as a cover disk for The One. [13]

Bullfrog制作了Populous World Editor，它使用户能够修改角色，城市和地形的外观。 [12]名为Populous：Promised Lands的扩展包增加了五种新的景观类型（几何的傻地，狂野的西部，乐高风格的块地，革命的法国人和以计算机为主题的Bit Plains）。此外，另一个名为Populous：The Final Frontier的扩展磁盘添加了一个新的横向类型，并作为The One的封面磁盘发布。 [13]

Populous was released in 1989 to almost universal critical acclaim. The game received a 5 out of 5 stars in 1989 in Dragon #150 by Hartley, Patricia, and Kirk Lesser in "The Role of Computers" column. [14] Biff Kritzen of Computer Gaming World gave the game a positive review, noting, "as heavy-handed as the premise sounds, it really is a rather light-hearted game." The simple design and layout were praised, as were the game's colourful graphics. [10] In a 1993 survey of pre 20th-century strategy games the magazine gave the game three stars out of five, calling it a "quasi-arcade game, but with sustained play value". [22] MegaTech magazine stated that the game has "super graphics and 500 levels. Populous is both highly original and amazingly addictive, with a constant challenge on offer". They gave the Mega Drive version of Populous an overall score of 91%. [19]

Populous于1989年发行，获得了几乎全世界的一致好评。这款游戏在1989年的Hartley，Patricia和Kirk Lesser的“＃150龙”中获得了5星中的5星。 [14] Computer Gaming World的Biff Kritzen对这款游戏给予了正面评价，并指出：“尽管前提听起来有些笨拙，但它的确是一款轻松的游戏。”简单的设计和布局以及游戏的彩色图形受到好评。 [10]在1993年对20世纪前战略游戏的调查中，该杂志将该游戏评为五颗星中的三颗，称其为“准街机游戏，但具有持续的游戏价值”。 [22] MegaTech杂志指出，该游戏具有“超级图形和500个等级。Populous具有很高的原创性和令人上瘾的上瘾性，并且会不断提供挑战”。他们给Mega Drive版本的Populous总体得分是91％。 [19]

The game was released in the same month that The Satanic Verses controversy gained publicity in the United States following the publication of The Satanic Verses in the United States. Shortly after release, Bullfrog was contacted by the Daily Mail and was warned that the "good vs evil" nature of the game could lead to them receiving similar fatwā, although this did not materialize. By October 1997, global sales of Populous had surpassed 3 million units, a commercial performance that PC Gamer US described as "an enormous hit". [30] In total, Populous has sold over four million copies, [31] making it one of the best-selling PC games of all time.

到1997年10月，Populous的全球销量已超过300万台，这是PC Gamer US称之为“巨大成功”的商业表现。 [30]总计，Populous已售出超过四百万本，[31]使其成为有史以来最畅销的PC游戏之一。

In 1990 Bullfrog used the Populous engine to develop Powermonger, a strategic combat-oriented game with similar mechanics to Populous, but with a 3-dimensional graphical interface. In 1991 they developed and released a true sequel, Populous II: Trials of the Olympian Gods, and in 1998 a further direct sequel, Populous: The Beginning.

1990年，Bullfrog使用Populous引擎开发了Powermonger，这是一款战略性的面向战斗的游戏，其机制与Populous类似，但具有3D图形界面。 1991年，他们开发并发行了真正的续集《人口二：奥林匹亚众神的审判》，并在1998年发行了又一部直接续集《人口：起点》。

Populous was also released on the SNES, developed by Imagineer as one of the original titles for the console in Japan, [9] and features the addition of a race based on the Three Little Pigs.

Populous也在SNES上发布，由Imagineer开发，是日本游戏机的原始名称之一，[9]并且增加了基于“三只小猪”的比赛。

Populous DS, a new version of the game (published by Xseed Games in America and Rising Star Games in Europe), was developed by Genki for the Nintendo DS and released 11 November 2008. The game allows the user to shape the in-game landscape using the DS's stylus. It also features a multiplayer mode allowing four players to play over a wireless connection. [37]

Genul为Nintendo DS开发了该游戏的新版本Populous DS（由美国Xseed Games和欧洲Rising Star Games发行），并于2008年11月11日发行。该游戏允许用户塑造游戏中的风景使用DS的手写笔。它还具有多人游戏模式，允许四个玩家通过无线连接进行游戏。 [37]
