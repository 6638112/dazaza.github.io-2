What to expect from an online-only CES 2021, which promises to be a relatively slow show: TVs with Mini-LED tech, new laptops from PC manufacturers, and more
仅在线观看的CES 2021将会带来相对缓慢的展示：采用Mini-LED技术的电视，PC制造商的新型笔记本电脑等等

2021-01-09 16:10:45

Despite a global pandemic, the annual Consumer Electronics Show must go on. Well, virtually anyway. CES 2021 will officially take place from January 11th to 14th, but it’ll look very different this year. Instead of filling a giant convention center with loud and colorful booths, participating companies will simply announce their latest products online with virtual showrooms, live streamed press events or just news releases.  In fact, a lot more companies are announcing news before CES this time around. There won’t be as many brands showing their wares, but that doesn’t mean there won’t be plenty to talk about.

尽管全球流行，但每年的消费电子展仍必须继续进行。好吧，实际上无论如何。 CES 2021将于1月11日至14日正式举行，但今年看起来将大不相同。与会的公司不会在大型会议中心内摆满五颜六色的摊位，而只是在虚拟陈列室，实时新闻发布会或新闻发布中在线宣布其最新产品。实际上，这次有更多的公司在CES之前宣布了新闻。展示商品的品牌不会很多，但这并不意味着没有太多可以谈论的东西。

But not to fret, as we’ll cover it all regardless. Engadget will still be bringing you our own CES 2021 experience (plus our annual Best of CES awards) with a virtual stage, which you can watch starting Monday at 7:30AM ET. In the meantime, here’s an idea of what we can expect from CES 2021.

但不要烦恼，因为无论如何我们都会介绍所有内容。 Engadget仍将通过虚拟舞台带给您我们自己的CES 2021体验（加上我们的年度最佳CES奖项），您可以在美国东部时间星期一7:30开始观看。同时，以下是我们对CES 2021的期望。

TVs are often the biggest thing at CES -- both figuratively and literally -- and it’s unfortunate that we’re unable to see them in person this year. That hasn’t stopped television manufacturers from announcing their latest devices however, which some companies like Samsung, LG and Sony have already done.

无论从图形上还是从字面上看，电视通常是CES上最大的事情-不幸的是，我们今年无法亲自看到它们。但这并没有阻止电视制造商发布其最新设备，而三星，LG和索尼等一些公司已经做到了。

The biggest trend this year appears to be the adoption of Mini-LED technology, which is used in Samsung’s latest 8K and 4K Neo QLED sets, plus LG’s QNED TVs. It’s the same sort of tech we saw in TCL’s 8 series last year and is now on the 6 series TVs as well. Mini-LEDs promise to improve backlight performance in LCDs, leading to better black levels. They can even rival OLEDs in terms of picture quality, but for a lot less money.

今年最大的趋势似乎是采用Mini-LED技术，该技术已用于三星最新的8K和4K Neo QLED电视机以及LG的QNED电视中。这是去年我们在TCL的8系列电视中看到的技术，现在也在6系列电视中使用。迷你LED有望改善LCD的背光性能，从而改善黑电平。他们甚至可以在图像质量上与OLED匹敌，但所花的钱要少得多。

Samsung and Sony also announced new MicroLED TVs, but at 80-inches and up, they’re meant for corporate customers (or those with very deep pockets). Sony, for example, showed that its modular Crystal LED display panels could be used as virtual background sets for TV shows like The Mandalorian.

三星和索尼还宣布了新的MicroLED电视，但它们是面向80英寸及以上的企业客户（或财力雄厚的客户）的产品。以索尼为例，该公司展示了其模块化的Crystal LED显示面板可以用作The Mandalorian等电视节目的虚拟背景。

We’ll likely see some continuation of trends from previous years too. There will still be an emphasis on 8K displays as well as the upscaling necessary to make lower-res content look good on them. Tech like transparent and foldable displays will also be shown off, though they might not be ready for consumers just yet.

我们很可能还会看到往年趋势的延续。仍然将重点放在8K显示器上，以及为了使低分辨率内容看起来更好而必须进行的升级。诸如透明和可折叠显示器之类的技术也将被展示出来，尽管它们可能还没有为消费者准备。

The other big category at this year’s CES is likely to be PCs, specifically laptops. All the usual players such as Dell, Lenovo, HP, ASUS and Acer are set to announce new devices in their 2021 lineup. We expect to see updates across both consumer and gaming lines, and many of them have already come to light.

今年CES上的另一大类别可能是PC，特别是笔记本电脑。戴尔，联想，惠普，华硕和宏cer等所有普通厂商都将在其2021系列产品中宣布新设备。我们希望看到消费者和游戏领域的更新，其中许多已经曝光。

Dell, for example, has announced its new Latitude 9420 and 9520 which feature what the company claims is the industry’s first automatic webcam shutter, as well as an upgrade to Intel’s 11th gen Core vPro processors. LG has also announced five new thin and light Gram laptops, all of which are Intel Evo certified. Lenovo released a couple of new high-end laptops at this year’s CES as well, with two new IdeaPad 5 Pros that can be configured with either Intel or AMD processors.

例如，戴尔已经宣布了其新的Latitude 9420和9520，该产品具有该公司声称的业内首款自动网络摄像头快门功能，以及对英特尔第11代Core vPro处理器的升级。 LG还宣布了五款新的轻薄Gram笔记本电脑，所有这些笔记本电脑均通过了Intel Evo认证。联想也在今年的CES上发布了几款新的高端笔记本电脑，其中包括两款可以配置Intel或AMD处理器的新IdeaPad 5 Pro。

The 5G drumbeat will likely carry on with this year’s allotment of laptops as well. Lenovo, for example, already announced the IdeaPad 5G, which uses Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8cx chipset and runs Windows on ARM. It will connect to 5G networks where available, dropping to 4G LTE when it can’t.

5G鼓声很可能会在今年的笔记本电脑配置中继续保持下去。例如，联想已经宣布推出IdeaPad 5G，该产品使用高通公司的Snapdragon 8cx芯片组，并在ARM上运行Windows。它将连接到可用的5G网络，并在无法连接时连接到4G LTE。

Aside from that, we’ve seen the occasional desktop PC such as Lenovo’s Yoga 7 AiO (it can swivel from portrait to landscape and vice versa), plus the latest high-end monitors from companies like Dell.

除此之外，我们还看到了偶尔的台式机，例如联想的Yoga 7 AiO（它可以从纵向旋转到横向旋转，反之亦然），以及戴尔等公司最新的高端显示器。

In a stark difference compared to previous CES events, the smart home scene appears to be much quieter. Much of that is attributed to the fact that both Google and Amazon don’t have an official presence at the show this year. Of course, we still expect Assistant and Alexa to be integrated in everything from televisions to speakers, so Google and Amazon will still make their presence felt, even if they might not unveil anything officially.

与以前的CES事件相比，智能家居场景显得更加安静。其中大部分归因于Google和Amazon都没有在今年的展会上正式露面。当然，我们仍然希望Assistant和Alexa可以集成到从电视到扬声器的所有内容中，因此Google和Amazon仍然可以感受到它们的存在，即使它们可能不会正式发布任何内容。

Similarly, it’s highly likely that we’ll hear about new updates in connected kitchens and bathrooms -- perhaps a new smart faucet? -- from companies like Kohler or Moen, and maybe some announcements regarding other homebound gadgets such as security cameras and smart lights. LG, for example, has already unveiled a robot vacuum cleaner which can automatically empty its dust bin into a separate bag.

同样，很有可能我们会听到有关连通厨房和浴室的新更新-也许是新的智能水龙头？ -来自科勒（Kohler）或摩恩（Moen）等公司，也许还发布了一些有关其他家用设备的公告，例如安全摄像机和智能灯。以LG为例，它已经推出了一种机器人吸尘器，该吸尘器可以自动将其垃圾箱倒入一个单独的袋子中。

As far as drones go, CES has never been the most exciting event. DJI, one of the biggest names in drones, almost never launches new machines there. However, lesser-known brands from Asia often make a showing at CES, and they might do so again at this year’s virtual event. We also expect to see the usual selfie drones, and ones with industrial or non-photography applications. Last CES, for example, we saw Sunflower’s home security drone that whips around the house.

就无人机而言，CES从来不是最令人兴奋的事件。 DJI是无人机界最知名的公司之一，几乎从未在该处推出新机器。但是，亚洲知名度较低的品牌通常会在CES上亮相，他们可能会在今年的虚拟活动中再次亮相。我们还希望看到常见的自拍无人机以及具有工业或非摄影应用程序的无人机。例如，在上次CES上，我们看到了向日葵的家庭安全无人机在房屋周围鞭打。

Robots-wise, we might see several innovations from a wide variety of industries, such as food services (examples include the Miso Robotics’ burger-flipping bot) or delivery bots like Amazon’s Scout. Big Ag is also a prominent investor in robotics and we won’t be surprised if John Deere shows off any automated equipment there. We’ll also likely see the usual so-called “companion bots” for children or the elderly as we’ve seen in previous years.

在机器人方面，我们可能会看到来自各行各业的多项创新，例如食品服务（例如Miso Robotics的汉堡翻转机器人）或送货机器人，例如亚马逊的Scout。 Big Ag还是机器人技术领域的杰出投资商，如果John Deere在这里展示任何自动化设备，我们也不会感到惊讶。我们也可能会像往年一样看到针对儿童或老人的通常所谓的“伴侣机器人”。

Transportation tech has taken off at the CES in recent years, but it promises to be a lot more muted this year as a majority of automakers had little to no plans to exhibit at CES 2021. Still, we do expect some transportation news, especially in regards to EVs and outlandish concept vehicles. Mercedes, for example, has already announced a car that has a giant 56-inch MBUX “Hyperscreen” that spans the entire cabin of its EQS luxury EV. Large displays seem to be a theme with cars this year, as even Cadillac has given a sneak peek at its own 33-inch wraparound LED display.

近年来，运输技术已在CES上大放异彩，但由于大多数汽车制造商几乎没有计划在2021年CES上展出的计划，因此它有望在今年变得更加安静。不过，我们确实希望一些运输新闻，尤其是在关于电动汽车和奇特的概念车。例如，梅赛德斯（Mercedes）已经宣布了一款拥有56英寸巨型MBUX“ Hyperscreen”的汽车，该产品横跨EQS豪华电动汽车的整个驾驶室。大型显示屏似乎是今年汽车的主题，甚至连凯迪拉克也偷偷窥视了自己的33英寸环绕式LED显示屏。

Additionally, even if there might be no new car announcements, we’ll likely hear more about in-car tech like infotainment systems, heads up displays and vehicle connectivity features.

此外，即使可能没有新车发布，我们也可能会听到更多有关车载技术的信息娱乐系统，抬头显示器和车辆连接功能。

Perhaps the most obvious expectation regarding next week’s CES is that it will probably be a whole lot quieter than in years past. After all, COVID-19 has disrupted not just our lives but also the gadget product cycle. Sure, we’ll see a few weird and wacky devices, and there will probably be devices specifically designed as a reaction to the global pandemic (perhaps a health-tracking wearable or a smart facemask). But, CES 2021 promises to be a relatively slow show. To add insult to injury, Samsung is holding its very own annual Unpacked event on the very last day of CES, where we expect to hear about the Galaxy S21. It could very well be that CES is just the opening act to Samsung’s blockbuster.

对于下周的CES而言，最明显的期望也许是与往年相比要安静得多。毕竟，COVID-19不仅破坏了我们的生活，还破坏了产品的生产周期。当然，我们会看到一些奇怪而古怪的设备，并且可能会有专门针对全球大流行而设计的设备（也许是健康追踪型可穿戴设备或智能口罩）。但是，CES 2021有望成为一个相对缓慢的展会。更糟的是，三星在CES的最后一天举行了自己的年度Unpacked活动，我们希望能听到有关Galaxy S21的消息。 CES很可能只是三星大片的开场白。
