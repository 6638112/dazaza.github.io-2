Google discontinues Home Max speaker, tells you to buy two Nest Audios instead
Google停产Home Max扬声器，告诉您改为购买两个Nest Audio

2020-12-16 14:01:45

The Home Max was released in 2017 as Google's top-tier speaker, combining the brains of a Google Home with the massive subwoofers and tweeters more commonly found in hi-fi setups. It has gone on sale for lower and lower prices over the past few months, and now we know why — the Home Max is no longer being manufactured.

Home Max于2017年发布，是Google的顶级扬声器，它将Google Home的大脑与高保真音响设置中常见的大型低音炮和高音扬声器结合在一起。在过去的几个月中，它以越来越低的价格开始销售，现在我们知道原因了-Home Max不再生产。

Google has provided the following statement to Android Police, saying the Home Max is discontinued and that the Nest Audio is the intended successor:

Google已向Android Police提供了以下声明，称Home Max已停产，并且Nest Audio是预定的后续产品：

We’ve sold out of Google Home Max and will no longer be manufacturing the device. With the launch of Nest Audio, we’re offering a range of great home audio solutions, particularly with two Nest Audios paired for stereo sound.

我们已经售完了Google Home Max，将不再生产该设备。随着Nest Audio的推出，我们将提供一系列出色的家庭音频解决方案，尤其是将两个Nest Audio配对用于立体声的解决方案。

Existing Google Home Max users shouldn’t worry as they won’t see any change in their service. We'll continue to offer software updates and security fixes to Google Home Max devices. We're committed to delivering great sound and whole home audio features across all of our Assistant-enabled products.

现有的Google Home Max用户无需担心，因为他们不会看到服务的任何变化。我们将继续为Google Home Max设备提供软件更新和安全修复程序。我们致力于在所有支持Assistant的产品中提供出色的声音和整个家庭音频功能。

Buying two Nest Audio speakers to run in stereo mode will cost you $200, which isn't exactly cheap, but that's still half the introductory price of the Google Home Max. However, the newer Nest speakers can't reach the same volume levels as the Home Max — the Home Max has a pair of 114mm subwoofers and 18mm tweeters, while a single Nest Audio has a 75mm woofer and a 19mm tweeter. The Nest Audio also doesn't have an AUX input, which was available on the Home Max.

购买两个Nest Audio扬声器以立体声模式运行将花费您200美元，这确实不算便宜，但是那仍然是Google Home Max入门价的一半。但是，较新的Nest扬声器无法达到与Home Max相同的音量水平-Home Max具有一对114毫米低音扬声器和18毫米高音扬声器，而单个Nest Audio具有75毫米低音扬声器和19毫米高音扬声器。 Nest Audio也没有AUX输入，这在Home Max上可用。

The Google Home Max is still in stock at select stores, including Verizon and BuyDig, so there's some time left to pick up Google's biggest and baddest smart speaker in new condition.

Google Home Max仍在包括Verizon和BuyDig在内的部分商店有售，因此还有一些时间可以在新条件下使用Google最大和最差的智能扬声器。
