Ronja: Reasonable Optical Near Joint Access
Ronja：合理的光纤近联合接入

2022-02-15 01:57:32

Ronja (Reasonable Optical Near Joint Access) is an User Controlled Technology (likeFree Software) project of optical point-to-point data link. The devicehas 1.4km range and has stable 10Mbps full duplex data rate. Ronja is anoptoelectronic device you can mount on your house and connect your PC,home or office network with other networks. Or you can use it as a generalpurpose wireless link for building any other networking project.

Ronja（合理的光学近联合接入）是一个用户控制的光学点到点数据链路技术（likeFree软件）项目。该设备具有1.4km的范围，并具有稳定的10Mbps全双工数据速率。Ronja是一种电子设备，你可以把它安装在家里，把你的电脑、家庭或办公室网络与其他网络连接起来。或者，你也可以将其用作构建任何其他网络项目的通用无线链路。

The design is released under the GNU Free Documentation License: you get allthe necessary documentation and construction guides free. The material costsare very low, about 100 USD. The operation is immune to interference and quitereliable - interrupted only by dense fog.

该设计是根据GNU免费文档许可证发布的：您可以免费获得所有必要的文档和构造指南。材料成本很低，大约100美元。该行动不受干扰，而且相当可靠——只会被浓雾打断。

Output of Ronja project is a design. Twibright Labs' intent is not tomanufacture and sell pieces of hardware, but to solely engage in thedevelopment. Manufacture and selling parts and kits is meant to be performed byother entities, without explicit licensing requirements. This is intended toguard the user against overpricing. Moreover, the freely available completesource code and building guide helps the user to have transparent controlover quality of the goods he is buying. If you are interested in the device, then visit the homepage, buy componentsand make the device yourself. The homepage is mostly a building guide exactlydescribing how to do it. If you run into troubles following the guides, thereis an end-user-oriented support provided in the form of mailing list.

Ronja项目的输出是一个设计。Twibright实验室'；目的不是制造和销售硬件，而是完全参与开发。零件和套件的制造和销售将由其他实体进行，无需明确的许可要求。这是为了防止用户定价过高。此外，免费提供的completesource代码和建筑指南帮助用户透明地控制所购买商品的质量。如果您对该设备感兴趣，请访问主页，购买组件并自行制作该设备。主页主要是一个建筑指南，准确地描述了如何做到这一点。如果您在遵循指南的过程中遇到问题，我们将以邮件列表的形式提供面向最终用户的支持。

One Ronja device is a single long-distance optical transceiver that iscapable of running against the same or compatible device on the other side ofthe link. The topology is point-to-point.

一个Ronja设备是一个单一的远程光收发器，可以与链路另一侧的相同或兼容设备运行。拓扑结构是点对点的。

Building a Ronja is rather lenghty job (this will hopefully change infuture) that however pays off in a reliable and performant device that iscapable of delivering steady connectivity with little maintenance and can berun freely without a need of authorities' approval. Also a possibility ofinterference and eavesdropping is negligible. Dropouts are infrequent and determinedsolely by weather and are thus foreseeable.

建造Ronja是一项相当漫长的工作（这将有望改变未来），然而，在一个可靠且性能良好的设备中，这项工作是值得的，该设备能够在几乎不需要维护的情况下提供稳定的连接，并且可以在不需要权威机构的情况下自由运行；批准此外，干扰和窃听的可能性也可以忽略不计。辍学率很低，完全由天气决定，因此可以预见。

想要享受将原始的零售零件驱动到完美的合作中，以提供未受阻碍的全双工连接体验的人，必须承受这些干扰：

Ronja is somewhat labour expensive. Things have been made much easier byputting the most complicated electronics on a PCB. The cost of parts isnegligible in comparison with the labour, for example the components for thewhole Ronja 10M Metropolis cost just 1500CZK. Further reduction in labourdemands is planned by putting RX and TX on PCB too.

Ronja的劳动力成本有点高。通过将最复杂的电子元件放在PCB上，事情变得简单多了。与劳动力相比，零件成本可以忽略不计，例如，整个Ronja 10M Metropolis的组件成本仅为1500捷克克朗。通过在PCB上安装RX和TX，计划进一步减少劳动力需求。

The user must possess certain basic manual skills as soldering, drilling,painting, and technical drawing/schematic reading. But people withoutany previous experience with soldering have built a piece that worked onthe first try!

用户必须具备一定的基本手工技能，如焊接、钻孔、喷漆和技术图纸/原理图阅读。但之前没有焊接经验的人已经制作出了一件第一次尝试就成功的产品！

The parts were chosen to be of the widest availability possible andequivalents are provided where applicable

选择的零件具有尽可能广泛的可用性，并在适用的情况下提供同等产品

Innovative approach is used to speed up the work and make it convenient.Sector codes are present to make the population easy. Drilling is simplifiedby drilling templates - just print and no measurement is necessary in theworkshop! The device is based on the KISS rule (Keep It Simple, Stupid) which makesthe device plug-and-play immediately after the building provided the userhasn't botched anything.

采用创新的方法加快工作速度，使其更方便。部门代码的存在是为了方便人们。钻孔模板简化了钻孔——只需打印，无需在车间进行测量！该设备基于KISS规则（保持简单、愚蠢），该规则使设备在用户提供的大楼建成后立即即插即用'；我什么都没做。

The design is rugged and overdimensioned to withstand variations in thecomponents. As a consequence, the resulting device is rock solid in steadyperformance and provides outstanding electromagnetic intereference immunity andelectromagnetic compatibility. Withstands -20°C as well as direct sunlightand heat with obvious margin.During a lightning storm, lightning strike in proximity usually doesn'tgenerate even a single lost bit.

该设计坚固耐用，尺寸过大，能够承受部件的变化。因此，该设备性能稳定，具有出色的电磁干扰抗扰性和电磁兼容性。能够承受-20°C的温度，以及直接的阳光和热量，并具有明显的裕度。在雷暴期间，附近的雷击通常不会'；t产生哪怕是一点点损失。

In case of device failure (direct lightning strike), the measuring pointscan be inspected and bad components replaced without a need to throw the wholedevice out.

如果设备出现故障（直击雷击），可以检查测量点并更换不良部件，而无需扔掉整个设备。
