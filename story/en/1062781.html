Pinebook Pro Review
Pinebook Pro评论

2021-05-15 05:55:18

I received the original Pinebook for free from the good folks at Pine64 a fewyears ago, when I visited Berlin to work with the KDE developers. Honestly, Iwas underwhelmed. The performance was abysmal and ARM is a nightmare to workwith. For these reasons, I was skeptical when I bought the Pinebook Pro. I havealso spoken of my disdain for modern laptops in general before: the stateof laptops in $CURRENTYEAR is abysmal. As such, I have been using a ThinkPadX200, an 11 year old laptop, as my sole laptop for several years now.

当我访问柏林与KDE开发人员一起使用时，我收到了原来的Pinebook。老实说，iwas不知所措。表演是Abysmal，Arm是一个噩梦。出于这些原因，当我购买Pinebook Pro时，我持怀疑态度。之前，我一般来说，我对现代笔记本电脑的蔑视我的蔑视：$ Currentyear中的笔记本电脑是Abysmal。因此，我一直在使用ThinkPadx200，这台11岁的笔记本电脑，现在是我唯一的笔记本电脑。

I am pleased to share that the Pinebook Pro is a pleasure to use, and is likelyto finally replace the old ThinkPad for most of my needs.

我很高兴分享Pinebook Pro很高兴使用，并且可能最终更换老ThinkPad以获得我的大部分需求。

Let me get the bad parts out of the way upfront: ARM is still a nightmare towork with. I really hate this architecture. Alpine Linux’s upstream aarch64doesn’t work with this laptop, so I have to use postmarketOS, an Alpinederivative, instead. I do like pmOS — on phones — but I woulddefinitely prefer to use Alpine upstream for a laptop use-case. That beingsaid, the Pine community has been doing a very good job of working on gettingsupport for their devices upstream, and the situation has been steadilyimproving. I expect that one of the next batches of PBPs will include an updatedu-Boot payload which will make UEFI booting possible, and Linux distros with thenecessary kernel patches upstreamed will be shipping in the foreseeable future.This will alleviate mostof my ARM-based grievances.

让我从前期前进的零件：ARM仍然是一个噩梦。我真的很讨厌这种建筑。 Alpine Linux的上游AARCH64DOES不适用于这款笔记本电脑，所以我必须使用Postmarketos，一个AlpineDerivative。我喜欢PMOS - 在手机上 - 但我遗嘱更喜欢使用高山上游进行笔记本电脑用例。这是一个，松树社区一直在努力在上游的设备上工作的非常好的工作，情况一直是稳定的推动。我希望下一批Pbps中的一个将包括Updatedu-Boot有效载荷，它将使UEFI启动可能，而Linux Distras上游将在可预见的未来运输。这将减轻我的ARM的申诉。

The built-in speakers are also pretty tinny and weak. It has a headphone portwhich works fine, though. Configuring ALSA is a chore; these SoCs tend to haverather complicated audio setups. I have not been able to get the webcam working(some kernel option is missing, my contact at pmOS is working on it), but Iunderstand that the quality is pretty poor. It can supposedly be configured towork with a USB-C dock for an external display, but I have never got it workingand I understand that there are some kernel bits missing for this as well. Thetouchpad is also pretty bad, but thankfully I use mainly keyboard-drivensoftware. The built-in eMMC storage is pretty small, though it can be upgradedand I understand that there is an option to install an NVMe — at theexpense of your battery life.

内置扬声器也很敏感。然而，它有一个耳机港口良好。配置Alsa是一个苦难;这些SOC往往往返复杂的音频设置。我没有能够获得网络摄像头工作（一些内核选项缺失，我的PMOS联系正在致力于它），但Iunderstand认为质量很差。它可以据说是用USB-C Dock进行外部显示器配置磁纤维，但我从未有过操作顺利，我也明白它也缺少一些内核位。 TheTouchPad也非常糟糕，但谢天谢地使用主要使用键盘 - Drivensoftware。内置的EMMC存储非常小，但它可以升级，我明白有一个选项可以在电池寿命的展开上安装nvme。

Cons aside, what do I like about it? Well, many things. It’s lightweight andthin (1.3kg), but has a nice 14" screen that feels like the right size for me.The screen looks really nice, too. The colors look good, it works well at anybrightness level, and in most lighting situations. It’s definitely better thanthe old X200 display. The keyboard is not as nice as the ThinkPad (a high bar tomeet), but it’s pretty comfortable for extended use. The two USB-3 ports and thesole USB-C port are also nice to have. It can charge via USB-C, or via anincluded DC wall wart and barrel plug. The battery lasts for 6-8 hours: waybetter than my old ThinkPad.

缺乏缺点，我喜欢什么？好吧，很多事情。这是轻量级的，andthin（1.3kg），但有一个很好的14＆＃34;屏幕感觉就像对我一样的尺寸。屏幕看起来非常好。颜色看起来很好，它适用于符合您的符合水平，在大多数照明情况下。它肯定是最好的Thanthe旧X200展示。键盘不像Thinkpad（高级条形图）那么漂亮，但对于延长使用非常舒适。两个USB-3端口和Thesole USB-C端口也很好。它可以通过USB-C来充电，或通过ANINCLUDED DC墙壁疣和桶塞。电池持续6-8小时：比我的旧ThinkPad的Waybetter。

It is an ARM machine, so the performance is not competitive with modern x86_64platforms. It is somewhat faster than my 11-year-old previous machine, though.It has six cores and any parallelizable job (like building code) worksacceptably fast, at least for the languages I primarily use (i.e. not Rust orC++). It can also play back 1080p video with a little bit of stuttering, and720p video flawlessly. Browsing the web is a bit of a chore, but it always was. Sourcehut works fine.

它是一款臂机，所以性能与现代x86_64platforms不竞争。它比我的11岁以前的机器速度速度快。它有六个核心和任何并行的作业（如建筑物代码），至少对于我主要使用的语言（即不是RUST ORC ++）。它也可以用一点点口吃，并且可以完美无缺地播放1080p视频。浏览网页有点苦差，但它一直是。 Sourcehut工作正常。

The device is user-servicable, which I appreciate very much. It’s very easy totake apart (a small Phillips head screwdriver is sufficient) and you can buyindividual parts from the Pine64 store to do replacements yourself.

该设备是用户可维修的，我非常欣赏。它非常容易分开（小菲利普斯头螺丝刀足够），您可以从Pine64商店购买零件，以便自己购买。

In short, it checks most of my boxes, which is something no other laptop haseven come remotely close to in the past ten years. It is the only laptop Ihave ever used which makes a substantial improvement on the circa-2010 state ofthe art. Because ARM is a nightmare, I’m still likely to use the old ThinkPadsfor some use-cases, namely for hobby OS development and running niche operatingsystems. But my Pinebook Pro is here to stay.

简而言之，它检查了大多数盒子，这是其他没有其他笔记本电脑Haseven在过去的十年中远程接近。 它是唯一用过的笔记本电脑，这对2010年大约2010年艺术州进行了大量改进。 因为ARM是一个噩梦，我仍然可能使用旧的ThinkPadsfor一些用例，即对于业余爱好OS开发和运行利基操作系统。 但我的Pinebook Pro在这里留下来。
