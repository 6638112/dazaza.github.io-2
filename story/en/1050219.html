Sports and esports betting service Rivalry raises $20M ahead of a potential IPO this year, to help it expand to the US
体育和电子竞技博彩服务商Rivalry在今年可能进行首次公开募股之前筹集了2000万美元，以帮助其扩展到美国

2021-03-02 02:58:00

Rivalry has raised $20 million for its business of sports and esports betting. The company has a mobile and PC platform that makes it easy to place bets on sports and esports matches.

竞争对手Rivalry已经为其体育和电子竞技博彩业务筹集了2000万美元。该公司拥有一个移动和PC平台，可以轻松在体育比赛和电竞比赛中下注。

Rivalry’s average customer is 25 years old, and the company is going after esports and sports fans who are under 30. At the moment, Rivalry has an offshore gambling license with the Isle of Man, which allows it to facilitate gambling in places where there’s no domestic licensing in place. Right now, that means it doesn’t operate in the U.S., but it is working on getting licenses to do so, CEO Steven Salz said in an interview with GamesBeat. Rivalry’s bigger markets include Latin America, Russia, Southeast Asia, and Eastern Europe.

Rivalry的平均客户年龄为25岁，并且该公司正在追随30岁以下的电子竞技和体育迷。目前，Rivalry在马恩岛拥有离岸赌博牌照，这使其可以在没有人的地方进行赌博国内许可到位。首席执行官史蒂文·萨尔兹（Steven Salz）在接受GamesBeat采访时说，目前，这意味着它不在美国运营，但它正在努力获得许可。竞争对手的较大市场包括拉丁美洲，俄罗斯，东南亚和东欧。

“We’re an internationally regulated sportsbook and business has growth at 15% per month since we started,” Salz said.  “We’re looking to build products for a younger demographic.”

萨尔兹说：“我们是一家国际监管的体育博彩，自成立以来，业务每月以15％的速度增长。” “我们正在寻找针对年轻人群的产品。”

The investors in the oversubscribed round include institutional investors from Canada, the United States, the European Union, and a major land-based casino operator. The Toronto-based company isn’t saying who all those investors are yet, and it sees this as one step toward going public later this year. Esports veterans Billy Levy and Zack Zeldin serve as both advisers and investors.

参与超额认购的投资者包括来自加拿大，美国，欧盟和主要的陆基赌场运营商的机构投资者。这家总部位于多伦多的公司没有透露所有这些投资者是谁，它认为这是今年晚些时候上市的第一步。电竞老兵Billy Levy和Zack Zeldin都是顾问和投资者。

With the new money, Salz said the company will be able to accelerate its expansion plans through new licenses and gray markets under its existing regulatory regime. It will also enable new product development and help the company provide what it calls the safest and most engaging sports betting experience in the world.

萨尔兹说，有了这笔新资金，该公司将能够在现有监管制度下通过新的许可和灰色市场来加快其扩张计划。它还将支持新产品开发，并帮助公司提供世界上最安全，最吸引人的体育博彩体验。

While Rivalry started by focusing on esports betting, an increasing percentage of its business is traditional sports, including an upcoming suite of original casino intellectual property.

尽管Rivalry从专注于电子竞技博彩开始，但其业务中越来越多的业务是传统体育，包括即将推出的一整套原始赌场知识产权。

And Salz said that while it’s hard to make money in the fledgling market of esports, betting monetizes much better. So that means that the company can grow profitably as the excitement around esports itself picks up.

萨尔兹（Salz）表示，虽然在新兴的电子竞技市场上很难赚钱，但博彩可以更好地获利。这样一来，随着围绕电子竞技本身的兴奋逐渐升温，公司就可以实现盈利增长。

“Esports has not been an area where a lot of people have been able to monetize really well and build businesses,” said Billy Levy, an investor in Rivalry and an adviser, in an interview with GamesBeat.

Rivalry的投资者兼顾问Billy Levy在接受GamesBeat采访时说：“电子竞技并不是一个真正能够很好地货币化并建立业务的领域。”

Esports companies raised $2 billion in 2020 over 200 transactions in 2020, up 116% in value from $957 million in 123 deals in 2019, according to mergers and acquisitions advisory firm  Quantum Tech Partners.

并购咨询公司Quantum Tech Partners的数据显示，2020年电竞公司在2020年的200笔交易中筹集了20亿美元，比2019年的123笔交易中的9.57亿美元增长了116％。

The challenge of running a gambling company is more regulation, and that’s why the company hopes to enter the U.S. market through a sublicensing partnership with a land-based casino company, Salz said. Many of those casino companies are eager to partner, as their physical casinos are suffering in the pandemic. More states and Canada are moving toward legalization as a way to get more tax revenues.

萨尔兹说，经营一家赌博公司的挑战在于更多的法规，这就是为什么该公司希望通过与一家陆基赌场公司的再许可合作伙伴关系进入美国市场的原因。这些赌场公司中的许多公司都渴望成为合作伙伴，因为他们的实体赌场正遭受大流行的折磨。越来越多的州和加拿大正朝着合法化的方向发展，以期获得更多的税收收入。

“This capital helps us enter the U.S. market,” Levy said. “And legalization is moving forward, very similar to cannabis legalization.”

利维说：“这笔资金有助于我们进入美国市场。” “合法化正在向前发展，与大麻合法化非常相似。”

Rivalry owns all of its intellectual property and utilizes no white labels, which is a rarity in the world of sports betting and casino. This approach offers total control of the user experience. Its plan is to become like the Robinhood of esports and sports betting, making it simpler and easier to place bets, just as Robinhood has done for stock trading.

Rivalry拥有其所有知识产权，并且不使用白色标签，这在体育博彩和赌场世界中很少见。这种方法提供了对用户体验的完全控制。它的计划是变得像电子竞技和体育博彩的罗宾汉一样，使其更容易下注，就像罗宾汉在股票交易中所做的那样。

Rivalry also has a media strategy. Its social and content properties generate more engagement than any other brand in esports betting, and the company is still only in the first leg of its media vertical roadmap, Salz said. The social media is key to reaching the target audience, Salz said. Rivalry has 18 different social media properties in different languages, as well as multiple YouTube properties that generate more than a million views a month.

竞争也有媒体策略。萨尔茨说，其社交和内容属性在电竞博彩方面比任何其他品牌产生更多的参与度，而且该公司仍仅处于其媒体垂直路线图中的第一站。萨尔茨说，社交媒体是吸引目标受众的关键。 Rivalry拥有18种不同语言的社交媒体资源，以及多个YouTube媒体资源，每月产生的观看次数超过一百万。

“The content is … a way to reach the audience, but also a way to create brand equity and loyalty,” Salz said. “We sponsor more than 120 different content creators, and often we are their only sponsors.”

萨尔茨说：“内容是……吸引受众的一种方式，也是建立品牌资产和忠诚度的一种方式。” “我们赞助了120多个不同的内容创作者，通常我们是他们唯一的赞助者。”

That content has gone viral, and it has driven the cost of acquiring new users down dramatically, so the company makes money on all of the new users that sign up, Salz said. By contrast, other gambling companies have to spend a lot on ads to reach the right users.

萨尔兹说，这种内容已经风靡一时，并大大降低了获取新用户的成本，因此该公司从所有注册的新用户中获利。相比之下，其他赌博公司必须在广告上花费大量资金才能吸引合适的用户。

Rivalry has built a betting platform designed to appeal to a new demographic that is now coming into the fold of sports betting. Creating a playful social and content experience that flows through that entire betting experience is key to achieving that. The company believes that betting is entertainment and content, as much as something transactional for a next-gen customer base, and keeps this at the forefront of its growth plans.

Rivalry建立了一个博彩平台，旨在吸引新的人群，而这种人群现在正进入体育博彩领域。创造贯穿整个博彩体验的有趣的社交和内容体验是实现这一目标的关键。该公司认为，博彩既是娱乐性又是内容，对于下一代客户群而言，具有交易性，并且始终将其置于增长计划的前列。

Salz said that Rivalry launched in August 2018 and it saw its business grow 10 times in 2020 over 2019. Growth in 2021 so far is tracking at over 400% above 2020 growth rates.

萨尔兹说，Rivalry于2018年8月推出，其业务在2020年比2019年增长了10倍。到目前为止，到2021年的增长将比2020年增长400％以上。

The company has 60 employees. Rivalry is a subsidiary of Canadian parent company PMML, whose founders include Salz, Kevin Wimer, Ryan White, and Steve Isenberg.

该公司有60名员工。 Rivalry是加拿大母公司PMML的子公司，其创始人包括Salz，Kevin Wimer，Ryan White和Steve Isenberg。
