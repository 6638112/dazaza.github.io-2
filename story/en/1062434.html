Animoca Brands has raised $88 million at a $1 billion valuation to capitalize on its plan to combine games and rare digital items via nonfungible tokens (NFTs).

Animoca品牌已经筹集了8800万美元，以10亿美元的估价将其计划通过非良性标记（NFTS）结合将游戏和稀有数码物品结合起来。

Hong Kong-based Animoca Brands has gathered a handful of game companies — The Sandbox, Quidd, Gamee, nWay, Pixowl, and Lympo — that have pioneered the use of NFTs, which can authenticate the uniqueness of a digital item in games. If you’re surprised that an NFT company has become the game industry’s newest unicorn, you probably haven’t been paying attention to NFTs.

总部位于香港的Animoca品牌已经聚集了一些游戏公司 - 沙盒，Quidd，Gamee，Nway，Pixoyl和Lympo - 已经开创了NFT的使用，这可以在游戏中验证数字物品的独特性。如果您感到惊讶的是，NFT公司已成为游戏业最新的独角兽，您可能没有关注NFTS。

NFTs have exploded in other applications such as art, sports collectibles, and music.  NBA Top Shot (a digital take on collectible basketball cards) is one example. Built by  Dapper Labs, NBA Top Shot has surpassed $500 million in sales, five months after going public. And  an NFT digital collage by the artist Beeple sold at Christie’s for $69.3 million. Animoca is one of many blockchain-focused game companies that have bet that NFTs will also change the game industry by giving players the ability to own the items they normally rent in games. NFTs use blockchain, the secure and transparent digital ledger to verify transactions and authenticate rare items. And Animoca Brands has been in the NFT business for about three years.

NFT在其他应用程序中爆炸，例如艺术，体育收藏品和音乐。 NBA顶击（可收集篮球卡上的数字采取）是一个例子。 NBA顶级拍摄的Dapper Labs建造，在公共场合之后五个月超过销售额超过5亿美元。并由艺术家BEEPLE在Christie售出的NFT数字拼贴，以6930万美元。 Animoca是众多集结区块链条的游戏公司之一，您敢打赌，NFTS也将通过给球员提供他们通常在游戏中租用的物品的能力来改变游戏行业。 NFTS使用BlockChain，安全和透明的数字分类帐来验证事务并验证稀有物品。 Animoca品牌一直在NFT业务中大约三年。

Investors in the round included Kingsway Capital, RIT Capital Partners (formerly Rothschild Investment Trust), HashKey Fintech Investment Fund, AppWorks Fund, LCV Fund, Huobi, Octava,  Ellerston Capital, Perennial, Axia Infinity Ventures, SNZ, Liberty City Ventures, Metapurse, and other investors.

投资者包括Kingsway Capital，Rit Capital Partners（原罗斯柴德尔投资信托），哈希基金融公司投资基金，Appworks基金，LCV基金，Huobi，Octava，Elryson资本，多年生，Axia Infinity Ventures，Snz，自由城府，Metapurse，和其他投资者。

The company said the strategic round positions Animoca Brands for aggressive growth as it continues to bring digital property rights to video gamers through the use of blockchain and NFTs, enabling powerful benefits such as play-to-earn capabilities and digital asset interoperability. Play-to-earn is where people reap rewards in games that they can exchange into real money, and it reminds me of my ideas around the Leisure Economy. This will either gives us the next version of the internet or blow up in a big bubble explosion. With NFTs, players can establish true ownership of their in-game items, and they can take them with them if they leave the game, or sell them at a profit. In that way, the in-game items are more like an investment than an expense.

该公司表示，战略圆形职位Animoca品牌用于积极的增长，因为它继续通过使用区块链和NFT来为视频游戏玩家带来数字性质，从而实现强大的优势，如戏剧性的能力和数字资产互操作性。 Play-otime是人们在游戏中获得奖励的地方，他们可以兑换成真钱，它让我想起了我周围休闲经济的想法。这将为我们提供下一个版本的互联网或在一个大泡沫爆炸中爆炸。与NFTS，玩家可以建立对游戏内物品的真正所有权，如果他们离开游戏，或者以利润销售它们，他们就可以与他们一起服用。通过这种方式，游戏内物品更像是投资而不是费用。

Yat Siu, CEO of Animoca Brands, said in an interview with GamesBeat that he will be looking for acquisitions, make strategic investments, hire new people to add to the company’s base of 400 employees, secure licenses for popular intellectual properties, and develop new products.

Animoca品牌首席执行官Yat Siu表示，在一次采访中，他将寻找收购，使战略投资，雇用新人加入公司的400名员工基地，为流行知识产权的安全许可证，开发新产品。

Asked what he thought about the last few months of NFT news, Siu said, “I think a rocket ship comes to mind. One of the reasons why the space is growing as quickly as this is pent-up demand in going from a rental to an ownership economy.”

曾询问他对过去几个月的NFT新闻的想法，Siu说：“我认为一艘火箭船来到脑海中。空间迅速发展的原因之一，因为这是从租赁到所有权经济的需求。“

He added, “More people are rolling into this idea of how I should own my digital life. I should own my time, I should be respected for how I’m spending my time online. It’s a revolution. It seems to come from a small base.”

他补充道，“更多人正在努力推进我应该拥有我的数字生活的想法。我应该拥有我的时间，我应该尊重我如何在线度过我的时间。这是一场革命。它似乎来自一个小基地。“

In fact, Metamask has around five million accounts and Coinbase has 56 million accounts, which is a good indicator of the size of the crypto economy. That’s a lot of people, but Siu believes we’re just scratching the surface in terms of getting mainstream consumers involved. Last week, Tokyo-based online gaming giant said it has invested $100 million in Bitcoin. But there’s no question it’s a volatile market, part of the bigger ups and downs about crypto. Just yesterday, Elon Musk’s Tesla said it would no longer accept Bitcoin payments because of the environmental impact of blockchain computing, which is secure but consumes an enormous amount of computing power.

“Once they go into digital ownership, they don’t get out,” Siu said. “For those who are actually making NFT’s and buying and trading and selling them and and providing services for them, they’re in it for the long term. That’s how you know there is something powerful there. Why are we turning into a unicorn? Why is Dapper Labs exploding? I think that digital ownership is a paradigm shift that the industry needed. The VC firms are all rushing in. It’s getting more mainstream in attention and awareness.”

“一旦他们进入数字所有权，他们就不会出去，”思乌说。 “对于那些实际制作NFT和购买和销售他们的人以及为他们提供服务的人，他们是长期的。这就是你知道那里有些功能。我们为什么转向独角兽？为什么皮下实验室爆炸？我认为数字所有权是行业所需的范式转变。 VC公司全都涌入。它越来越多地主流和认识。“

Manuel Stotz，Kingsway的创始人兼首席执行官在一份声明中表示，在一份声明中，数字产权的出现，无论是通过比特币还是NFT，都是在新兴市场的底层30亿左右的财务包容性的最大机会。
