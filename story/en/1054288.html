The Federal Communications Commission wants to hear from Internet users about their experiences trying to find good broadband service. "Collecting data from consumers who are directly affected by the lack of access to broadband will help inform the FCC's mapping efforts and future decisions about where service is needed." The FCC's broadband maps help the commission develop policy and decide which parts of the country should get the most broadband-deployment funding.
