Cars24, which operates a used-car marketplace in India, raises $200M Series E led by DST Global at a $1B+ valuation
Cars24在印度经营二手车市场，由DST Global领投的2亿美元E轮融资，估值超过10亿美元

2020-11-24 23:13:29

Indian marketplace for second-hand vehicles Cars24 has raised $200 million in a Series E round led by DST Global, which also saw participation from its existing investors Exor Seeds, Moore Strategic Ventures, and Unbound. With the latest funding round, Cars24 valuation soars past a billion dollars, making it the latest unicorn in the country. “Traditionally, car selling or buying has been a tiresome process, however, over the last five years, we have strived to transform the customer's journey- 'the CARS24 way' that is hassle-free, safe and transparent,” said Vikram Chopra, Co-founder and CEO, Cars24 in a statement. Last year, the startup had also raised investments from Indian cricketer MS Dhoni. Chopra added that with the latest investment, the startup will continue to invest in its product to improve the online experience for sellers and buyers. From their very first location in 2015, next to a petrol pump in Gurugram where they saw sellers come all the way from Noida, today Cars24 is spread across cities and states in India. The startup said that with annual transactions exceeding 2,00,000 units and a 4X increase in website engagement, it has already surpassed pre-COVID levels. CARS24 has pioneered a significantly differentiated experience for the customer and dealer by deeply leveraging data and technology at each step, resulting in world-class operational efficiency and market leadership in the segment,” said Rahul Mehta, managing partner at DST Global. In 2019, the startup had also acquired a NBFC licence to kickstart its financing business with quick and easy two-hour loan disbursals. During the lockdown, the startup had also forayed into the two-wheeler business and claimed to have sold over 3,000 two-wheelers in the last six months. 印度二手车市场Cars24在DST Global牵头的E轮融资中筹集了2亿美元，其现有投资者Exor Seeds，Moore Strategic Ventures和Unbound也参与其中。通过最新一轮融资，Cars24的估值飙升至超过10亿美元，使其成为美国最新的独角兽。 “从传统上讲，汽车买卖一直是一个繁琐的过程，但是，在过去五年中，我们一直在努力改变客户的旅程-无忧，安全和透明的'CARS24方式'，” Vikram Chopra说， Cars24联合创始人兼首席执行官。去年，这家初创公司还从印度板球运动员MS Dhoni那里筹集了投资。 Chopra补充说，通过最新的投资，这家初创公司将继续对其产品进行投资，以改善买卖双方的在线体验。从2015年的第一处位置开始，在古鲁格拉姆的汽油泵旁，他们看到卖家一直从诺伊达（Noida）出发，如今，Cars24遍布印度的各个城市和州。这家初创公司表示，每年的交易量超过200万个，网站的参与度提高了4倍，它已经超过了COVID之前的水平。通过深深地利用数据和技术的每一步，CARS24为客户和经销商开创了显着差异化的体验，从而在该细分市场中实现了世界一流的运营效率和市场领导地位。” DST Global的管理合伙人Rahul Mehta说。在2019年，这家初创公司还获得了NBFC许可证，以快速便捷的两小时贷款支付来启动其融资业务。在锁定期间，这家初创公司也涉足了两轮车业务，并声称在过去六个月中售出了3000多辆两轮车。还请参见：Zerodha的投资基金向企业资源规划平台ERPNext投入了1亿卢比，在COVID-19疫苗出台前，莫迪警告不要自满—要求各州开始在冷藏设施上开展工作
