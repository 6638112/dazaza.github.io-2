出售业余AMPRnet TCP/IP地址筹集1.08亿美元

2020-10-29 13:04:22

President of Amateur Radio Digital Communications (ARDC) has confirmed they received $108 million from Amazon for 4 million amateur radio TCP/IP addresses

业余无线电数字通信(ARDC)总裁证实，他们从亚马逊获得了1.08亿美元的400万个业余无线电TCP/IP地址。

Since its allocation to Amateur Radio in the mid-1980s, Internet network 44 (44.0.0.0/8), known as the AMPRNet™, has been used by amateur radio operators to conduct scientific research and to experiment with digital communications over the radio with a goal of advancing the state of the art of Amateur Radio networking, and to educate amateur radio operators in these techniques.

互联网网络44(44.0.0.0/8)名为AMPRnet™，自八十年代中期分配给业余无线电以来，业余无线电操作员一直使用该网络进行科学研究和试验无线电数字通信，目的是提高业余无线电联网的技术水平，并教育业余无线电操作员使用这些技术。

Amateur Radio Digital Communications (ARDC) is a non-profit California corporation formed to further these goals.

业余无线电数字通信(ARDC)是加州成立的一家非营利性公司，目的是为了实现这些目标。

In mid-2019 a block (44.192.0.0/10) of approximately four million AMPRNet™ IP addresses, out of the 16 million available, was sold to Amazon by ARDC but it is only now that the sale price has been released. Amazon paid $27 for each IPv4 address.

2019年年中，ARDC将1600万个可用的AMPRnet™IP地址块(44.192.0.0/10)出售给了亚马逊，但直到现在才公布了售价。亚马逊为每个IPv4地址支付了27美元。

“The NDA with Amazon that covered the sale of our surplus IP addresses required us to keep the exact dollar amounts private until we were legally required to disclose them in our annual tax filings, audit and financial reports. These have just been made public and are available online through the California Attorney General’s website (since ARDC is incorporated in California). You can also get some background information through our (independently written) Wikipedia article. The bottom line is that the sale netted us about US $108M, and we expect to grant at least US $5 million *per year* on an ongoing basis, depending on how our investments perform, to a wide variety of Internet and amateur radio digital communication projects. To date we have made about $2.5 million in grants, so we’re only getting started. ”

“与亚马逊签订的涵盖出售我们剩余IP地址的保密协议要求我们对确切的美元金额保密，直到我们被法律要求在我们的年度纳税申报、审计和财务报告中披露这些金额。这些资料刚刚公之于众，并可通过加州总检察长的网站在线获取(因为ARDC是在加州注册成立的)。您还可以通过我们(独立编写的)维基百科文章获取一些背景信息。底线是，这笔交易为我们带来了约1.08亿美元的净收入，我们预计将根据我们的投资表现，持续*每年*至少拨款500万美元，用于各种互联网和业余无线电数字通信项目。到目前为止，我们已经获得了大约250万美元的赠款，所以我们才刚刚开始。“
