E3 will take place as a free virtual conference on June 12-15 and organizers say an in-person event will happen in LA in 2022
E3将于6月12日至15日作为免费虚拟会议举行，组织者表示，2022年在La中发生一个人的活动

2021-04-06 21:38:21

After last year’s E3 was completely canceled because of the COVID-19 pandemic, the trade group that runs the video-game convention confirmed there will be one in 2021 — although it will be online-only.

在去年的E3被彻底取消之后由于Covid-19大流行，运行视频游戏公约的贸易团体确认将在2021年有一个 - 虽然它将在线。

The Entertainment Software Association ( ) announced plans for a “reimagined” all-virtual 2021, set to take place June 12-15, 2021, featuring news and game reveals. The ESA said it will team up with media partners globally “to help amplify and make this content available to everyone for free.”

娱乐软件协会（）宣布计划为“Reimaged”全部虚拟2021，设为6月12日至15日，2021年6月12日，以新闻和游戏揭示。 ESA表示将与全球媒体合作伙伴合作“帮助放大并为每个人提供放大并使这些内容。”

ESA said it has “early commitments” for participation from Nintendo, Microsoft/Xbox, Capcom, Konami, Ubisoft, Take-Two Interactive, Warner Bros. Games and Koch Media. Notably absent from the lineup is Sony, which had sat out E3 2019 (and was going to skip it last year).

ESA表示，它有“早期承诺”，来自任天堂，Microsoft / Xbox，Capcom，Konami，Ubisoft，Take-Bame，华纳兄弟游戏和Koch Media。特别是从阵容中缺席的是索尼，它已经Sat Out E3 2019（去年将跳过）。

The ESA plans to bring E3 back as an in-person event in 2022 in Los Angeles. Until last year, the confab had taken place annually at the Los Angeles Convention Center since 1995.

ESA计划在洛杉矶2022年将E3回归作为一个人的活动。直到去年，自1995年以来，该组织每年在洛杉矶会议中心举行。

“For more than two decades, E3 has been the premier venue to showcase the best that the video game industry has to offer, while uniting the world through games,” Stanley Pierre-Louis, president/CEO of the ESA, said in a statement. “We are evolving this year’s E3 into a more inclusive event, but will still look to excite the fans with major reveals and insider opportunities that make this event the indispensable center stage for video games.”

“超过二十年，E3一直是展示视频游戏行业提供的最佳场所，同时通过游戏联合世界，欧安全盟署总裁/欧安省首席执行官Stanley Pierre-Louis表示。 “我们正在将今年的E3发展成为一个更包容的活动，但仍然会希望激发粉丝的主要揭示和内在的机会，使这一活动成为视频游戏的不可或缺的中心阶段。”

In 2019, E3 drew 66,100 attendees to the Los Angeles Convention Center in downtown L.A., according to the ESA.

根据ESA的说法，2019年，E3将66,100名与会者向洛杉矶会议中心推向洛杉矶会议中心。

With E3 nixed last year, games publishers held their own events. Industry veteran Geoff Keighley, creator of the Game Awards, produced the Summer Game Fest series of online events in 2020, and he’s bringing it back this year ( summergamefest.com) as well.

通过去年的E3尼克思，游戏出版商举行了自己的活动。工业资深杰夫·佩夫·克利，游戏奖的创造者，制作了2020年的夏季游戏节目系列在线活动，他也在将其推回（SummergameFest.com）。
