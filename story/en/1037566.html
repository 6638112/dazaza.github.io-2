Google will let users with ARCore-compatible Android phones submit Street View imagery, starting in Toronto, NYC, Austin, Nigeria, Indonesia, and Costa Rica
Google将允许使用兼容ARCore的Android手机的用户提交街景图像，从多伦多，纽约，奥斯丁，尼日利亚，印度尼西亚和哥斯达黎加开始

2020-12-04 21:16:01

Google Maps is getting a new update that lets you create Street View photos using just a phone. Android users with ARCore-compatible devices can now capture imagery and publish it to Google Street View in certain areas. Google is allowing submissions initially in Toronto, New York, Austin, Nigeria, Indonesia, and Costa Rica. More regions will support this feature soon, and Google will use this user-generated content when it doesn’t have its own Street View imagery available.

Google地图正在获得新的更新，可以让您仅用手机创建街景照片。使用兼容ARCore的设备的Android用户现在可以捕获图像并将其发布到某些区域的Google街景视图。 Google最初允许在多伦多，纽约，奥斯丁，尼日利亚，印度尼西亚和哥斯达黎加提交论文。更多地区将很快支持此功能，并且Google将在没有可用的街景图像的情况下使用该用户生成的内容。

“While our own Street View trekkers and cars have collected more than 170 billion images from 10 million miles around the planet, there are still many unmapped parts of the world,” says Stafford Marquardt, product manager of Google Maps Street View. “Where people contribute connected photos, they will appear in the Street View layer on Google Maps as dotted blue lines.”

Google Maps Street View产品经理Stafford Marquardt说：“虽然我们自己的Street View徒步旅行者和汽车已经从地球1000万英里处收集了超过1700亿张图像，但世界上仍有许多未映射的部分。” “人们在提供关联照片的地方，它们将以虚线显示在Google地图的街景视图层中。”

The new beta feature has been in testing for months, and Google will automatically rotate and position the series of connected photos that are captured in its Street View app for Android. While users have been able to submit Street View imagery in the past, it required a special 360-degree camera. This new feature just makes use of a standard phone camera.

新的测试版功能已经进行了几个月的测试，Google会自动旋转并定位在其Android版“街景”应用中捕获的一系列关联照片。尽管过去用户可以提交街景图像，但它需要一个特殊的360度摄像头。这项新功能仅使用标准的手机摄像头。

It opens up the Street View feature to many more areas, including hard to reach places that even Google’s Street View trekkers and cars haven’t visited. Google will also use this imagery to update Google Maps with information like new businesses or publicly posted open hours, and the company will apply its standard Street View privacy features to blur out faces and license plates.

它将“街景视图”功能扩展到更多地区，包括难以到达的地方，甚至Google的街景徒步旅行者和汽车都没有去过。 Google还将使用此图像来更新Google Maps的信息，例如新业务或公开发布的营业时间，并且该公司将应用其标准Street View隐私权功能来模糊面孔和车牌。
