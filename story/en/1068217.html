UK's CMA launches an investigation into whether Amazon and Google are doing enough to crack down on fake reviews and deter sellers from manipulating listings
英国的CMA推出调查亚马逊和谷歌是否正在进行足以破解用于操纵上市的假审查和销售商

2021-06-25 19:14:52

The U.K.’s antitrust regulator launched an investigation into whether Amazon AMZN -1.56% com Inc. and Google are doing enough to crack down on fake reviews, adding a new layer to regulatory scrutiny of U.S. tech giants.

U.K.的反托拉斯监管机构调查了亚马逊Amzn -1.56％COM Inc.和Google是否正在进行足以破解虚假评论，为美国科技巨头的监管审查添加新层。

The Competition and Markets Authority said Friday the move stems from concerns Amazon and Alphabet GOOG 0.65% Google haven’t been doing enough to address fake reviews on their sites, but added that it hasn’t yet reached a view on whether the companies have broken the law.

竞争和市场管理局周五表示，担心亚马逊和字母谷歌的源于0.65％谷歌并没有做足够的事情来解决他们的网站的虚假评论，而是尚未达到这些公司是否已破坏法律。

The tech giants have for years been dogged by inauthentic reviews, whereby third-party sellers offer financial rewards for reviewing their products. But the practice is hard for the companies to police, with compensation coordinated out of their reach.

Tech Giants多年来一直受到了不认实的评价，其中第三方销售商提供财务奖励，用于审查其产品。但是，公司对警方的努力，赔偿协调。

The move comes amid recent investigations into Silicon Valley’s market power and privacy practices, turning the spotlight to misleading online reviews. The coronavirus pandemic has prompted a shift to online consumption, increasing consumers’ reliance on online reviews to decide on product purchases.

该举动在最近调查硅谷的市场力量和隐私实践中，转向误导在线评论。冠状病毒大流行促使转向在线消费，增加消费者对在线审查的依赖，以确定产品购买。

The CMA’s decision follows an initial investigation, opened in May 2020, assessing several platforms’ internal systems and processes for spotting and dealing with fake reviews. The work raised concerns that Amazon and Google weren’t doing enough to detect fake and misleading reviews and suspicious behavior, investigate and promptly remove fake reviews, or impose adequate sanctions on reviewers or businesses to deter them.

CMA的决定遵循了初步调查，于2020年5月开业，评估了几个平台的内部系统和流程，用于发现和处理假审查。这项工作提出了担忧，亚马逊和谷歌无法检测到审查和可疑行为，调查和及时删除假审查，或对审阅者或企业实施充分的制裁来阻止他们。

The CMA also said it was concerned Amazon’s systems were failing to adequately stop or deter sellers from manipulating product listings, like co-opting reviews from other products.

CMA还表示，它涉及亚马逊的系统未能充分停止或阻止卖方从操作产品列表，如其他产品的共同选择。

U.S. lawmakers in 2019 questioned Amazon’s efforts to fight fake reviews in a letter to Chief Executive Jeff Bezos. The Federal Trade Commission also in 2019 fined a retail website that paid a third party to post reviews on Amazon, in its first case against the use of fake paid reviews.

美国立法者在2019年质疑亚马逊的努力在一封信中为首席执行官杰夫贝罗斯进行打击假审查。联邦贸易委员会也在2019年被罚款，零售网站支付了第三方，以便在亚马逊的审查中发布审查，这是第一次抵御使用假收薪评论。

Amazon said it devotes significant resources to preventing fake or incentivized reviews from appearing in its store and to ensure reviews accurately reflect customers’ experience.

亚马逊表示，它致力于预防假冒或激励的审查，从出现储存并确保评论准确反映客户的经验。

“We will continue to assist the CMA with its enquiries and we note its confirmation that no findings have been made against our business,” an Amazon spokesman said.

亚马逊发言人表示，“我们将继续询问CMA，并记录其确认没有对我们的业务进行的发现，”亚马逊发言人表示。

Amazon says it stopped more than 200 million suspected fake reviews last year before they were seen by customers, and that it is taking action against reviews and customer accounts sold on external social-media sites. In the first quarter of 2021, it reported more than 1,000 social-media groups.

亚马逊表示，去年在客户看到之前停止了超过2亿疑似的假假审查，并采取了在外部社交媒体网站上销售的评论和客户账户采取行动。在2021年的第一季度，它报告了超过1,000个社交媒体群体。

A Google spokesperson said the company takes action when it finds violations of its policy, from removing abusive content to disabling user accounts, and that it looks forward to continuing its work with the CMA.

谷歌发言人表示，该公司在发现违反其政策时采取行动，从删除滥用内容到禁用用户账户，并期待继续与CMA合作。

Facebook and eBay last year committed to combating trading in fake and misleading reviews on their sites, in response to concerns raised by the CMA. In April, the regulator said Facebook had removed 16,000 groups that sold fake reviews on both Facebook and Instagram.

去年Facebook和eBay致力于打击对其网站的假冒和误导性评论的交易，以回应CMA提出的担忧。 4月，监管机构表示，Facebook已删除了16,000个组，这些组在Facebook和Instagram上销售了假审查。

The regulator said its probe into fake reviews is part of a broader program to establish a new regulatory regime for digital markets. Last week, the CMA said it was launching a study into Apple and Google’s mobile ecosystems and earlier in June said it would have oversight over Google’s removal of user-tracking technology from its Chrome browser under a set of commitments from the company to settle an antitrust investigation.

该监管机构表示，其探讨为假审查是建立数字市场新监管制度的更广泛方案的一部分。上周，CMA表示，它向Apple启动了一项研究，谷歌的移动生态系统和谷歌的移动生态系统，早些时候在歌谷谷谷谷谷谷谷谷谷谷物中删除了来自其Chrome浏览器的删除了公司的一组承诺来解决反垄断的承诺调查。

European Union regulators are also ramping up scrutiny for U.S. tech giants. The European Commission, the EU’s top antitrust enforcer, on Tuesday opened an antitrust investigation into allegations that Google is abusing its leading role in the advertising-technology sector. Google said it would continue to engage with the commission.

欧盟监管机构也对美国科技巨头进行了审查。欧盟委员会欧盟的顶级反托拉斯卡队在周二开启了反垄断调查，谷歌正在滥用广告技术领域的主导作用。谷歌表示，它将继续与委员会聘用。

Early in June, the EU and the U.K. opened formal antitrust investigations into Facebook Inc.’s classified-ads service, Marketplace. Facebook said it would cooperate with the probes to “demonstrate that they are without merit.”

6月初，欧盟和U.K.将正式的反托拉斯调查开放成Facebook Inc.的分类广告服务市场。 Facebook表示将与探针合作“证明他们没有优点”。
