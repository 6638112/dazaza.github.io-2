The Sovereign Military Order of Malta ( SMOM), officially the Sovereign Military Hospitaller Order of Saint John of Jerusalem, of Rhodes and of Malta ( Italian: Sovrano Militare Ordine Ospedaliero di San Giovanni di Gerusalemme di Rodi e di Malta; Latin: Supremus Militaris Ordo Hospitalarius Sancti Ioannis Hierosolymitani Rhodiensis et Melitensis), commonly known as the Order of Malta, Malta Order or Knights of Malta, is a Catholic lay religious order, traditionally of military, chivalric and noble nature. [4] Though it possesses no territory, the order is a sovereign entity of international law and maintains diplomatic relations with many countries.

马耳他主权军事勋章（SMOM），正式是耶路撒冷圣约翰，罗得岛和马耳他的主权军事医务员勋章（意大利语：Sovrano Militare Ordine Ospedaliero di San Giovanni di Gerusalemme di Rodi e di Malta；拉丁语：Supremus Militaris Ordo Hospitalarius Sancti Ioannis Hierosolymitani Rhodiensis et Melitensis）通常被称为马耳他教团，马耳他教团或马耳他骑士团，是天主教的信奉宗教秩序，传统上具有军事，骑士和贵族性质。 [4]该命令虽然没有领土，但它是国际法的主权实体，并与许多国家保持外交关系。

SMOM claims continuity with the Knights Hospitaller, a chivalric order that was founded c.  1099 by the Blessed Gerard in the Kingdom of Jerusalem. [5] The order is led by an elected Prince and Grand Master. [6] Its motto is Tuitio fidei et obsequium pauperum ('defence of the faith and assistance to the poor'). The order venerates the Virgin Mary as its patroness, under the title of Our Lady of Philermos. Its modern-day role is largely focused on providing humanitarian assistance and assisting with international humanitarian relations, for which purpose it has had permanent observer status at the United Nations General Assembly since 1994. [7]

The order has a large number of local priories and associations around the world, but there also exist a number of organizations with similar-sounding names that are unrelated, including numerous fraudulent (self-styled) orders seeking to capitalize on the name. [8]

In order to protect its heritage against fraud, the order has legally registered 16 versions of its names and emblems in some 100 countries. [10]

The headquarters of the Order of Saint John or the Knights Hospitaller was located in Malta from 1530 until 1798. It was technically a vassal of the Kingdom of Sicily, holding Malta in exchange for a nominal fee, but declared independence in 1753.

The Order of Saint John was expelled from Malta under the French occupation in 1798 and, from 1805 to 1812, many of its possessions in Protestant Europe were confiscated, resulting in the fragmentation of the order into a number of Protestant branches, since 1961 united under the umbrella of the Alliance of the Orders of Saint John of Jerusalem.The Congress of Vienna of 1815 confirmed the loss of Malta. The seat of the order was moved to Ferrara in 1826 and to Rome in 1834, the interior of Palazzo Malta being considered extraterritorial sovereign territory of the order. The grand priories of Lombardy-Venetia and of Sicily were restored from 1839 to 1841. The office of Grand Master was restored by Pope Leo XIII in 1879, after a vacancy of 75 years, confirming Giovanni Battista Ceschi a Santa Croce as the first Grand Master of the restored Order of Malta.

The Order has 13,500 Knights, Dames and auxiliary members. A few dozen of these are professed religious. Until the 1990s, the highest classes of membership, including officers, required proof of noble lineage. More recently, a path was created for Knights and Dames of the lowest class (of whom proof of aristocratic lineage is not required) to be specially elevated to the highest class, making them eligible for office in the order.

The order employs about 42,000 doctors, nurses, auxiliaries and paramedics assisted by 80,000 volunteers in more than 120 countries, [note 1] assisting children, homeless, handicapped, elderly, and terminally ill people, refugees, and lepers around the world without distinction of ethnicity or religion. The birth of the Knights Hospitaller dates back to around 1048. Merchants from the ancient Marine Republic of Amalfi obtained from the Caliph of Egypt the authorisation to build a church, convent, and hospital in Jerusalem, to care for pilgrims of any religious faith or race. The Order of St. John of Jerusalem – the monastic community that ran the hospital for the pilgrims in the Holy Land – became independent under the guidance of its founder, the religious brother Gerard.

骑士医院的诞生可以追溯到1048年左右。古代的阿马尔菲海洋共和国的商人从埃及的哈里发获得了在耶路撒冷建造教堂，修道院和医院的权利，以照顾任何宗教信仰或种族的朝圣者。 。耶路撒冷圣约翰骑士团（St. John of Jerusalem）–在圣地为朝圣者经营医院的修道院社区–在其创始人宗教兄弟杰拉德（Gerard）的指导下独立。

With the Papal bull Pie postulatio voluntatis dated 15 February 1113, Pope Paschal II approved the foundation of the Hospital and placed it under the aegis of the Holy See, granting it the right to freely elect its superiors without interference from other secular or religious authorities. By virtue of the Papal Bull, the hospital became an order exempt from the control of the local church. All the Knights were religious, bound by the three monastic vows of poverty, chastity and obedience.

随着1113年2月15日的罗马教皇义卖义令的达成，教皇帕夏尔二世批准了医院的成立，并将其置于罗马教廷的主持下，赋予其自由选举其上级的权利，而不受其他世俗或宗教当局的干扰。凭借教皇公牛，该医院成为免于当地教会控制的命令。所有的骑士都是宗教的，受到贫穷，贞操和服从的三个修道院誓言的约束。

The constitution of the Christian Kingdom of Jerusalem during the Crusades obliged the order to take on the military defence of the sick, the pilgrims, and the captured territories. The order thus added the task of defending the faith to that of its hospitaller mission.

在十字军东征期间，耶路撒冷基督教王国的宪法有义务对士兵，朝圣者和被占领的领土进行军事防御。因此，该命令将捍卫信仰的任务添加到了其接待员任务中。

As time went on, the order adopted the white, eight-pointed Cross that is still its symbol today. The eight points represent the eight beatitudes that Jesus pronounced in his Sermon on the Mount.

随着时间的流逝，该命令采用了白色的八角十字架，至今仍然是它的象征。这八点代表耶稣在山上的讲道中宣告的八种福气。

When the last Christian stronghold in the Holy Land fell after the Siege of Acre in 1291, the order settled first in Cyprus.

当1291年对英亩的围攻行动之后，圣地的最后一个基督徒据点沦陷时，该命令首先在塞浦路斯定居。

In 1310, led by Grand Master Fra' Foulques de Villaret, the knights regrouped on the island of Rhodes. From there, the defense of the Christian world required the organization of a naval force; so the Order built a powerful fleet and sailed the eastern Mediterranean, fighting battles for the sake of Christendom, including Crusades in Syria and Egypt.

在1310年由大师弗拉＆＃39; Foulques de Villaret，骑士们在罗得岛上重新集结。从那里开始，捍卫基督教世界需要一支海军部队。因此，该司令部建立了一支强大的舰队，并航行了地中海东部，为基督教世界而战，包括叙利亚和埃及的十字军东征。

In the early 14th century, the institutions of the Order and the knights who came to Rhodes from every corner of Europe were grouped according to the languages they spoke. The first seven such groups, or Langues (Tongues) – from Provence, Auvergne, France, Italy, Aragon ( Navarre), England (with Scotland and Ireland), and Germany – became eight in 1492, when Castile and Portugal were separated from the Langue of Aragon. Each Langue included Priories or Grand Priories, Bailiwicks, and Commanderies.

在14世纪初期，从欧洲各个角落来到罗得岛的骑士团和骑士的组织根据他们所讲的语言进行了分组。从普罗旺斯，奥弗涅，法国，意大利，阿拉贡（纳瓦拉），英格兰（与苏格兰和爱尔兰）和德国来的前七个类似的语言族（朗格语）在1492年成为八个，当时卡斯蒂利亚和葡萄牙从阿拉贡的语言。每个语言都包括优先级或大优先级，Bailiwicks和Commanderies。

The Order was governed by its Grand Master, the Prince of Rhodes, and its Council. From its beginning, independence from other nations granted by pontifical charter and the universally recognised right to maintain and deploy armed forces constituted grounds for the international sovereignty of the Order, which minted its own coins and maintained diplomatic relations with other states. The senior positions of the Order were given to representatives of different Langues.

该命令由其大师，罗得王子亲王及其理事会管理。从一开始，就通过了《宪章宪章》赋予的与其他国家独立的地位以及得到普遍承认的维持和部署武装部队的权利，为该勋章的国际主权奠定了基础，该勋章铸造了自己的硬币并与其他国家保持外交关系。该命令的高级职位授予了不同语言的代表。

In 1523, after six months of siege and fierce combat against the fleet and army of Sultan Suleiman the Magnificent, the Knights were forced to surrender, and left Rhodes with military honours.

1523年，在对壮丽的苏丹苏莱曼苏丹的舰队和军队进行了长达6个月的攻城和激烈战斗之后，骑士被迫投降，并以军事荣誉离开了罗得岛。

The order remained without a territory of its own until 1530, when Grand Master Fra' Philippe de Villiers de l'Isle Adam took possession of the island of Malta, granted to the order by Emperor Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor and his mother Queen Joanna of Castile as monarchs of Sicily, with the approval of Pope Clement VII, for which the order had to honour the conditions of the Tribute of the Maltese Falcon.

直到1530年弗拉大师（Fra Master）订单一直没有领土。菲利普·德·维利耶斯·德·艾勒·亚当·菲利普·德·艾利·亚当拥有罗马帝国的皇帝查理五世和他的母亲卡斯蒂利亚的皇后乔安娜的命令，并由教皇克莱门特七世批准，占领了马耳他岛，为此，该命令必须尊重马耳他猎鹰进贡的条件。

The Reformation which split Western Europe into Protestant and Catholic states affected the knights as well. In several countries, including England, Scotland and Sweden, the order was dissolved. In others, including the Netherlands and Germany, entire bailiwicks or commanderies (administrative divisions of the order) experienced religious conversions; these " Johanniter orders" survive in Germany, the Netherlands, and Sweden and many other countries, including the United States and South Africa. It was established that the order should remain neutral in any war between Christian nations. [ citation needed]

将西欧分裂为新教徒和天主教国家的宗教改革也影响了骑士。在包括英格兰，苏格兰和瑞典在内的几个国家，该命令被解散。在包括荷兰和德国在内的其他国家，整个百利威克人或司令部（该命令的行政区划）经历了宗教religious依；这些＆＃34; Johanniter订单在德国，荷兰，瑞典和其他许多国家（包括美国和南非）生存。已经确定，在基督教国家之间的任何战争中，该秩序应保持中立。 [需要引用]

From 1651 to 1665, the Order of Saint John ruled four islands in the Caribbean. On 21 May 1651 it acquired the islands of Saint Barthélemy, Saint Christopher, Saint Croix and Saint Martin. These were purchased from the French Compagnie des Îles de l'Amérique which had just been dissolved. In 1665, the four islands were sold to the French West India Company.

从1651年到1665年，圣约翰勋章统治了加勒比海的四个岛屿。 1651年5月21日，它获得了圣巴托洛缪岛，圣克里斯托弗岛，圣克鲁瓦岛和圣马丁岛。这些是从刚刚解散的法国Companie desÎlesde l'Amérique购买的。 1665年，这四个岛屿被卖给了法属西印度公司。

In 1565, the Knights, led by Grand Master Fra' Jean de Vallette (after whom the capital of Malta, Valletta, was named), defended the island for more than three months during the Great Siege by the Ottomans.

1565年，骑士由弗拉格大师（Fra Master）领导。让·德·瓦莱特（Jean de Vallette）（后以马耳他的首都瓦莱塔（Valletta）命名）在奥斯曼帝国大围困期间保卫了该岛三个多月。

The fleet of the order contributed to the ultimate destruction of the Ottoman naval power in the Battle of Lepanto in 1571, led by John of Austria, half brother of King Philip II of Spain.

该命令的舰队在1571年的勒潘托战役中最终破坏了奥斯曼帝国的海军力量，该战役由西班牙国王菲利普二世的同父兄弟奥地利约翰·约翰率领。

Their Mediterranean stronghold of Malta was captured by the French First Republic under Napoleon in 1798 during his expedition to Egypt, following the French Revolution and the subsequent French Revolutionary Wars. Napoleon demanded from Grand Master Ferdinand von Hompesch zu Bolheim that his ships be allowed to enter the port and to take on water and supplies. The Grand Master replied that only two foreign ships could be allowed to enter the port at a time. Bonaparte, aware that such a procedure would take a very long time and would leave his forces vulnerable to British Admiral Horatio Nelson, immediately ordered a cannon fusillade against Malta. [16] The French soldiers disembarked in Malta at seven points on the morning of 11 June and attacked. After several hours of fierce fighting, the Maltese in the west were forced to surrender. [17]

法国大革命和随后的法国独立战争之后，他们的地中海据点马耳他在1798年被拿破仑率领的法兰西第一共和国占领，当时他是埃及人。拿破仑向费迪南德·冯·汉普斯·祖·博尔海姆大师提出要求，允许他的船只进入港口并取水和补给。大师回答说一次只能允许两艘外国船进入港口。 Bonaparte意识到这样的程序将花费很长时间，并使他的部队容易受到英国海军上将Horatio Nelson的攻击，因此立即下令对马耳他实行加农炮大炮。 [16]法国士兵于6月11日上午7点在马耳他下船并发动了进攻。经过数小时的激烈战斗，西方的马耳他人被迫投降。 [17]

Napoleon opened negotiations with the fortress capital of Valletta. Faced with vastly superior French forces and the loss of western Malta, the Grand Master negotiated a surrender to the invasion. [18] Hompesch left Malta for Trieste on 18 June. [19] He resigned as Grand Master on 6 July 1799.

拿破仑开始与瓦莱塔要塞进行谈判。面对强大的法军和马耳他西部的沦陷，宗师商讨了对入侵的投降。 [18]翁佩施（Hompesch）于6月18日离开马耳他前往的里雅斯特（Trieste）。 [19]他于1799年7月6日辞职为大师。

The knights were dispersed, though the order continued to exist in a diminished form and negotiated with European governments for a return to power as part of the agreement between France and Holy Roman Empire during the German mediatisation. The Russian Emperor, Paul I, gave the largest number of knights shelter in Saint Petersburg, an action which gave rise to the Russian tradition of the Knights Hospitaller and the Order's recognition among the Russian Imperial Orders. [20] The refugee knights in Saint Petersburg proceeded to elect Tsar Paul as their Grand Master – a rival to Grand Master von Hompesch until the latter's abdication left Paul as the sole Grand Master. Grand Master Paul I created, in addition to the Roman Catholic Grand Priory, a "Russian Grand Priory" of no fewer than 118 Commanderies, dwarfing the rest of the Order and open to all Christians. Paul's election as Grand Master was, however, never ratified under Roman Catholic canon law, and he was the de facto rather than de jure Grand Master of the Order.

骑士分散了，尽管该命令以减少的形式继续存在，并与欧洲各国政府谈判以恢复权力，这是在法国调解期间法国与神圣罗马帝国之间达成的协议的一部分。俄罗斯皇帝保禄一世在圣彼得堡为骑士团提供了最多的庇护所，这一举动引起了俄罗斯骑士医院骑士团的传承，并获得了俄罗斯帝国勋章的认可。 [20]圣彼得堡的难民骑士继续选举沙皇保罗为大师，与冯·本皮施大师竞争，直到后者的退位使保罗成为唯一的大师。除罗马天主教大修道院外，保罗一世大师还创建了“俄罗斯大修道院”。不少于118个司令官兵，使该教团的其余成员相形见and，并向所有基督徒开放。然而，根据罗马天主教的正典法，从未批准保罗为大师的选举，他是事实上的而不是法律上的教父。

By the early 19th century, the order had been severely weakened by the loss of its priories throughout Europe. Only 10% of the order's income came from traditional sources in Europe, with the remaining 90% being generated by the Russian Grand Priory until 1810. This was partly reflected in the government of the Order being under Lieutenants, rather than Grand Masters, in the period 1805 to 1879, when Pope Leo XIII restored a Grand Master to the order. This signaled the renewal of the order's fortunes as a humanitarian and religious organization.

到19世纪初，由于整个欧洲失去优先次序，该命令已被严重削弱。该命令的收入只有10％来自欧洲的传统来源，其余90％是由俄罗斯大修道院修道院产生的，直到1810年。这在一定程度上反映了该命令的政府是由中尉而不是大总统领导的。大师，在1805年至1879年期间，教皇利奥十三世恢复了一位大师的统治。这标志着作为人道主义和宗教组织的命令的命运得以更新。

On 19 September 1806, the Swedish government offered the sovereignty of the island of Gotland to the Order. The offer was rejected since it would have meant the Order renouncing their claim to Malta. [21]

1806年9月19日，瑞典政府将哥特兰岛的主权授予了该命令。这项提议被拒绝了，因为这意味着该命令放弃了他们对马耳他的要求。 [21]

During the seventeen years that separated the seizure of Malta and the General Peace, "the formality of electing a brother Chief to discharge the office of Grand Master, and thus to preserve the vitality of the Sovereign Institute, was duty attended to". [23] [24] The office of Lieutenant of the Magistery and ad interim of Grand Master was held by the Grand Baillies Field Marshal Counto Soltikoff, Giovanni Tommasi, De Gaevera, Giovanni y Centelles, De Candida and the Count Colloredo. [24] Their mandates complexively covered the period until the death of the Emperor Paul in 1801. [24]

在没收马耳他和全面和平的17年间，选举兄弟首长卸任大师的职务以维护主权研究所的活力的手续是当务之急。 34;。 [23] [24]大君子中尉Counto Soltikoff，Giovanni Tommasi，De Gaevera，Giovanni y Centelles，De Candida和Count Colloredo担任治安中尉和临时大师的职务。 [24]他们的任务比较复杂，涵盖了直到1801年保罗皇帝去世的时期。[24]

The Treaty of Amiens (1802) obliged the United Kingdom to evacuate Malta which was to be restored to a recreated Order of St. John, whose sovereignty was to be guaranteed by all of the major European powers, to be determined at the final peace. However, this was not to be because objections to the treaty quickly grew in the UK.

《亚眠条约》（1802年）要求联合王国撤离马耳他，马耳他将被恢复为重新建立的圣约翰勋章，该勋章的主权将由所有欧洲主要大国保证，并在最终和平时确定。但是，这并不是因为反对条约在英国迅速增加。

Bonaparte's rejection of a British offer involving a ten-year lease of Malta prompted the reactivation of the British blockade of the French coast; Britain declared war on France on 18 May. [25]

Bonaparte拒绝了英国的一项涉及马耳他的十年租约的提议，这促使英国对法国海岸的封锁重新开始。英国于5月18日对法国宣战。 [25]

The 1802 treaty was never implemented. The UK gave its official reasons for resuming hostilities as France's imperialist policies in the West Indies, Italy, and Switzerland. [26]

1802年条约从未得到执行。作为法国在西印度群岛，意大利和瑞士的帝国主义政策，英国给出了恢复敌对行动的正式理由。 [26]

After having temporarily resided in Messina, Catania, and Ferrara, in 1834 the precursor of the Sovereign Military Order of Malta settled definitively in Rome, where it owns, with extraterritorial status, the Magistral Palace in Via Condotti 68 and the Magistral Villa on the Aventine Hill.

在暂时居住在墨西拿，卡塔尼亚和费拉拉之后，马耳他君主军事勋章的前身最终定居在罗马，在罗马，它拥有域外地位，位于康多提大街68号的治安官邸和阿文廷的治安官邸爬坡道。

The original hospitaller mission became the main activity of the order, growing ever stronger during the 20th century, most especially because of the contribution of the activities carried out by the Grand Priories and National Associations in many countries around the world. Large-scale hospitaller and charitable activities were carried out during World Wars I and II under Grand Master Fra' Ludovico Chigi Albani della Rovere (1931–1951). Under the Grand Masters Fra' Angelo de Mojana di Cologna (1962–88) and Fra' Andrew Bertie (1988–2008), the projects expanded.

最初的接待员任务成为该命令的主要活动，在20世纪变得越来越强大，尤其是由于世界上许多国家的“大修道院”和“全国同盟”所开展的活动所做出的贡献。在第一次世界大战和第二次世界大战期间，弗拉格大师（Fra＆＃39）进行了大规模的接待和慈善活动。 Ludovico Chigi Albani della Rovere（1931-1951）。在大师傅下Angelo de Mojana di Cologna（1962–88）和Fra＆＃39;安德鲁·伯蒂（Andrew Bertie，1988–2008年），项目得到了扩展。

Two bilateral treaties were concluded with the Republic of Malta. The first treaty is dated 21 June 1991 and is now no longer in force. [27] The second treaty was signed on 5 December 1998 and ratified on 1 November 2001. [28]

与马耳他共和国缔结了两项双边条约。第一项条约的日期为1991年6月21日，现已不再生效。 [27]第二项条约于1998年12月5日签署，并于2001年11月1日批准。[28]

This agreement grants the Order the use with limited extraterritoriality of the upper portion of Fort St. Angelo in the city of Birgu. Its stated purpose is "to give the Order the opportunity to be better enabled to carry out its humanitarian activities as Knights Hospitallers from Saint Angelo, as well as to better define the legal status of Saint Angelo subject to the sovereignty of Malta over it". [29]

该协议授予该命令在比尔古（Birgu）市圣安吉洛堡（Fort St. Angelo）上半部有限的域外使用权。它的既定目的是使该命令有机会更好地能够以圣安吉洛骑士医院的人员身份开展其人道主义活动，并更好地确定圣安吉洛的法律地位，但马耳他对此具有主权。它。 [29]

The agreement has a duration of 99 years, but the document allows the Maltese Government to terminate it at any time after 50 years. [30] Under the terms of the agreement, the flag of Malta is to be flown together with the flag of the Order in a prominent position over Saint Angelo. No asylum may be granted by the Order and generally the Maltese courts have full jurisdiction and Maltese law shall apply. The second bilateral treaty mentions a number of immunities and privileges, none of which appeared in the earlier treaty. [27] [28]

该协议的有效期为99年，但该文件允许马耳他政府在50年后随时终止该协议。 [30]根据协定的规定，马耳他的旗帜应与圣安杰洛上方显着位置的命令的旗帜一同悬挂。该命令不得给予任何庇护，一般而言，马耳他法院具有完全管辖权，应适用马耳他法律。第二项双边条约提到了许多豁免和特权，在先前的条约中都没有出现。 [27] [28]

In February 2013, the order celebrated the 900th anniversary of its papal recognition with a general audience with Pope Benedict XVI and a Mass celebrated by Cardinal Tarcisio Bertone in Saint Peter's Basilica. [31]

2013年2月，该命令与教皇本尼迪克特十六世一起庆祝了教皇的认可900周年，并由圣彼得大教堂的红衣主教塔西西奥·伯通尼（Tarcisio Bertone）庆祝了弥撒。 [31]

The Order experienced a leadership crisis beginning in December 2016, when Albrecht von Boeselager protested his removal as Grand Chancellor by Grand Master Matthew Festing.In January 2017 Pope Francis ordered von Boeselager reinstated and required Festing's resignation. [32] [33] [34] [35] [36] Francis also named Archbishop Giovanni Becciu [37] as his personal representative to the Order – sidelining the Order's Cardinal Patron Raymond Burke – until the election of a new Grand Master. [38] [39] [40]

该命令从2016年12月开始经历了领导危机，当时阿尔布雷希特·冯·博斯拉格（Albrecht von Boeselager）抗议将他辞去大法官马修·费斯廷（Matthew Festing）的大臣职务.2017年1月，教皇方济各下令冯·博斯拉格恢复原职，并要求辞职。 [32] [33] [34] [35] [36]弗朗西斯还任命乔万尼·比丘（Giovanni Becciu）大主教[37]作为该命令的私人代表– lining视该命令的红衣主教雷蒙·伯克（Raymond Burke）–直到选举新的大师。 [38] [39] [40]

The Pope effectively taking control over the order was seen by some as a break with tradition and the independence of the order. [41] In May 2017, the Order named Mauro Bertero Gutiérrez, a Bolivian member of the Government Council, to lead its constitutional reform process. [42] [43] [44] And in May 2018 when a new Grand Master was elected, Francis extended Becciu's mandate indefinitely. [45] [46] In June 2017, in a departure from tradition, the leadership of the Order wore informal attire rather than formal wear full dress uniforms to their annual papal audience. [47] When the Order's General Chapter met in May 2019, as it does every five years, the participants included women for the first time, three of the 62 participants. [48]

教皇有效地控制了命令，在某些情况下被视为对传统和命令独立性的破坏。 [41] 2017年5月，该命令任命玻利维亚政府理事会成员毛罗·贝特罗·古铁雷斯（Mauro BerteroGutiérrez）领导宪法改革进程。 [42] [43] [44]在2018年5月，当一位新的大师获得选举时，弗朗西斯无限期延长了Becciu的任期。 [45] [46]在2017年6月，与传统背道而驰的是，该教团的领导者向每年的教皇观众穿着非正式的着装，而不是正式的正装。 [47]当该勋章总章节于2019年5月举行时（每五年一次），参与者首次包括女性，这是62名参与者中的三名。 [48]

On 1 November 2020, Pope Francis named Silvano Tomasi to replace Becciu as his Special Delegate to the Order, reiterating the responsibilities of that office as his sole representative. [49]

2020年11月1日，弗朗西斯教皇任命西尔瓦诺·托马西（Silvano Tomasi）取代贝乔（Becciu）成为该命令的特别代表，重申该办公室作为其唯一代表的职责。 [49]

The proceedings of the Order are governed by its Constitutional Charter and the Order's Code. It is divided internationally into six territorial Grand Priories, six Sub-Priories and 47 national associations.

该法令的程序受其宪法宪章和该法令的约束。它在国际上分为六个领土大优先事项，六个次要优先事项和47个国家协会。

The supreme head of the Order is the Prince and Grand Master, who is elected for life by the Council Complete of State, holds the precedence of a cardinal of the Church since 1630 and received the rank of Prince of the Holy Roman Empire in 1607.
