Google makes GameSnacks, a collection of 100+ lightweight HTML5 games, more accessible in Chrome, Pay, Discover, and Assistant in some countries, like India
谷歌制作GamesNacks，一系列100+轻量级的HTML5游戏，在一些国家的Chrome，Pay，Discover和Assistant中更易于使用，如印度

2021-02-24 22:19:36

A year ago, Google’s Area 120 incubator launched an HTML5 gaming platform to “test whether lightweight, casual games would resonate with people who use the internet via low memory devices on 2G and 3G networks.” Proving popular, these HTML5 games are now being coming to Discover, Assistant, and Chrome for Next Billion Users (NBU).

一年前，谷歌的Area 120孵化器启动了HTML5游戏平台，以“测试轻巧，休闲的游戏是否会与通过2G和3G网络上的低内存设备使用互联网的人们产生共鸣。”事实证明，这些HTML5游戏已经广受欢迎，并面向下十亿用户（NBU）推出了Discover，Assistant和Chrome。

Since last February, Google says early development partners have built more than 100 games for GameSnacks with millions of people around the world playing them. These titles are small in size and load near instantaneously even on varying network conditions that are common in NBU markets (2G) and day-to-day life everywhere else (intermittent subway connections).

谷歌表示，自去年2月以来，早期的开发合作伙伴已经为GameSnacks制作了100多种游戏，全世界有数百万人在玩这些游戏。即使在NBU市场（2G）中常见的变化网络条件下以及其他地方的日常生活中（间歇性的地铁连接），这些标题的大小和负载都几乎瞬间达到。

These games span multiple genres: classics (e.g.  Chess), racing games (e.g.  Retro Drift), puzzle games (e.g.  Element Blocks), and hypercasual games (e.g.  Cake Slice Ninja) to list a few. You can check out the full catalog by visiting  gamesnacks.com.

这些游戏涵盖多种类型：经典游戏（例如国际象棋），赛车游戏（例如Retro Drift），益智游戏（例如Element Blocks）和休闲游戏（例如Cake Slice Ninja）等。您可以访问gamenacks.com查看完整目录。

Besides optimizing to work on any device, Google wants to tackle the discovery problem for HTML5 games. It’s leveraging existing products to achieve this, starting in Chrome. The New Tab page in India, Indonesia, Nigeria, and Kenya features a “Game” section that has become “one of the most frequently visited sections of the page.” Meanwhile, it’s also available for Indian Google Pay users as part of a “Games” carousel.

除了优化在任何设备上均可使用外，Google还希望解决HTML5游戏的发现问题。从Chrome开始，它将利用现有产品来实现这一目标。印度，印度尼西亚，尼日利亚和肯尼亚的“新标签页”页面具有“游戏”部分，该部分已成为“页面中访问量最大的部分之一”。同时，作为“游戏”轮播的一部分，印度Google Pay用户也可以使用它。

Google is experimenting with bringing GameSnacks to Assistant on phones with titles launching via voice commands. There’s also testing in Google Discover for Indian users where titles appear alongside article cards.

Google正在尝试将GameSnacks引入通过语音命令启动游戏的手机助手。 Google Discover还针对印度用户进行了测试，标题与文章卡一起显示。

The company says it will “look for more opportunities to bring GameSnacks games to more Google products” over the coming months. Meanwhile, it’s exploring “next-generation” AdSense for Games formats to “help developers build meaningful gaming businesses.”

该公司表示，未来几个月将“寻找更多将GameSnacks游戏带入更多Google产品的机会”。同时，它正在探索“下一代” AdSense游戏广告格式，以“帮助开发人员建立有意义的游戏业务。”
