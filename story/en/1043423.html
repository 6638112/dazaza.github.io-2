Amazon is working on a new Alexa-powered device that can track and detect sleep apnea, Business Insider has learned.

据《商业内幕》了解到，亚马逊正在开发一种新的Alexa驱动的设备，该设备可以跟踪和检测睡眠呼吸暂停。

The palm-sized device is designed to sit on a nightstand and use contactless technology to monitor the sleeping disorder, people familiar with the matter said. It will primarily use millimeter-wave radar to track sleeping and breathing patterns of individual users and detect subtleties in their movements that could come from stoppages of breathing associated with sleep apnea, these people said.

知情人士说，这种掌上大小的设备被设计为可以放在床头柜上，并使用非接触式技术来监测睡眠障碍。这些人说，它将主要使用毫米波雷达来跟踪单个用户的睡眠和呼吸模式，并检测其运动中的细微变化，这些细微变化可能源于与睡眠呼吸暂停相关的呼吸停止。

The project is internally code-named "Brahms," according to the people and an internal document viewed by Business Insider — likely in reference to Johannes Brahms, the German composer best known for writing "Lullaby" who is suspected to have suffered from sleep apnea.

该项目的内部代号为＆＃34; Brahms，＆＃34;根据知情人士和Business Insider查看的一份内部文件，这可能是参考德国作曲家约翰内斯·勃拉姆斯（Johannes Brahms）所著，他最著名的作品是写《摇篮曲》被怀疑患有睡眠呼吸暂停的人。

The previously unreported plans for the sleep-tracking device show how Amazon is continuing to take a scattershot approach to its personal-hardware business, as part of an ambition to make its Alexa voice assistant more ubiquitous. While Amazon has launched a number of successful products like the Echo home speaker, it's also dabbled in oddball ideas in recent years, including an Alexa-enabled microwave and a flying home drone that can be controlled with voice commands.

此前尚未公布的睡眠跟踪设备计划表明，亚马逊将继续采取分散的方式处理其个人硬件业务，以使其Alexa语音助手更加普及。亚马逊推出了许多成功的产品，例如Echo家用扬声器，但近年来，它也涉足了奇怪的想法，包括支持Alexa的微波炉和可以通过语音命令控制的家用无人机。

The new sleep-tracking device will likely be able to connect to other devices and send notifications through a separate app, people familiar with the matter said. The prototype looks like a standing hexagonal pad connected to a metal wire base, one of the people said.

知情人士说，新的睡眠跟踪设备很可能能够连接到其他设备，并通过单独的应用发送通知。一位知情人士说，该原型看起来像一个固定的六角形焊盘，与金属线基座相连。

Over the past year, Amazon has grown its team in charge of building the sleep-tracking device. It has increased its scope to bring application- and cloud-development capabilities in-house, instead of relying on external teams within Amazon, an internal document seen by Business Insider said. It has also expanded its team in Mumbai, hiring a group of software and infrastructure developers in India.

在过去的一年中，亚马逊增加了负责构建睡眠跟踪设备的团队。 Business Insider看到的一份内部文件称，它扩大了将内部应用程序和云开发功能引入内部的范围，而不是依靠亚马逊内部的团队。它还扩大了在孟买的团队，在印度雇用了一组软件和基础架构开发人员。

Amazon expects to leverage its advanced machine-learning capabilities and cloud infrastructure to build out a deeper sleep-analysis program that covers other sleeping disorders, one of the people said.

其中一位知情人士说，亚马逊希望利用其先进的机器学习功能和云基础架构来构建更深入的睡眠分析程序，以涵盖其他睡眠障碍。

Plans for "Brahms" also shed light on Amazon's intention to get deeper into the health space, even after shutting down Haven on Monday, its joint venture with JPMorgan and Berkshire Hathaway that was intended to tackle the rising costs for employee healthcare.

＆＃34;布拉姆斯＆＃34;的计划亚马逊周一关闭了其与摩根大通（JPMorgan）和伯克希尔哈撒韦公司（Berkshire Hathaway）成立的合资企业，旨在解决员工医疗保健成本不断上涨的问题，即使这家公司周一关闭后，亚马逊也希望进一步深入健康领域。

In the past year alone, Amazon launched its first wearable device, Halo, and a new online prescription service called Amazon Pharmacy, while rolling out fitness-tracking features to its Echo Buds wireless headphones. It has also started providing online and in-person primary care to its employees in 2019 through Amazon Care, a medical service it's trying to sell to other companies, Business Insider previously reported.

仅在过去的一年中，亚马逊就推出了其首款可穿戴设备Halo和一项名为Amazon Pharmacy的新在线处方服务，同时在其Echo Buds无线耳机中推出了健身追踪功能。据《商业内幕》（Business Insider）先前报道，该公司还于2019年开始通过亚马逊医疗（Amazon Care）向其员工提供在线和亲自初级保健，该医疗服务正试图向其他公司出售。

Amazon would join an increasingly competitive sleep-tracking-device market. Apple acquired the sleep-tracking startup Beddit in 2017, while other hardware companies, from Samsung and Xiaomi to startups like Oura and Sleepscore Labs, have made inroads in the space. The global market for smart sleep-tracking devices is projected to grow almost 16% annually, reaching $43.5 billion in size by 2026, according to 360 Research Reports.

亚马逊将加入竞争日益激烈的睡眠跟踪设备市场。苹果于2017年收购了睡眠追踪初创公司Beddit，而其他硬件公司（从三星和小米到Oura和Sleepscore Labs等初创公司）已涉足这一领域。根据360 Research Reports的预测，智能睡眠跟踪设备的全球市场每年将以近16％的速度增长，到2026年将达到435亿美元的规模。
