Katherine Wu leaves Coinbase Ventures to join crypto investment firm Archetype
Katherine Wu离开Coinbase Ventures加入加密投资公司Archetype

2022-02-26 05:52:42

“[T]oday is my last day at coinbase sometimes you take that leap of faith and it just doesn’t land quite right- that’s ok!” Wu wrote in a tweet on Friday announcing her departure.

“今天是我在coinbase的最后一天，有时候你的信仰发生了飞跃，但它并没有完全正确落地——没关系！”吴在周五的推文中宣布了她的离开。

Wu will work as a venture partner at Archetype, an early-stage VC founded by Ash Egan, who is an investor in a number of prominent crypto startups including Chainalysis, BlockFi, and FalconX, according to the firm’s website. Egan, who previously co-founded ConsenSys Ventures, has invested in renewable bitcoin miner US BTC Corp. and proof-of-attendance startup POAP through Archetype.

根据Archetype公司的网站，吴将在Archetype公司担任风险合伙人。Archetype是由Ash Egan创立的早期风险投资公司，他是ChainAnalysis、BlockFi和FalconX等知名加密初创公司的投资者。伊根之前与人共同创立了ConsenSys Ventures，他通过原型投资了可再生比特币矿商美国BTC公司和出席证明初创公司POAP。

Wu told TechCrunch in a written message that she was “really excited to be back doing what [I] do best — lead investment in founders in crypto looking to raise their first round.” She said she hopes to continue to find ways to make crypto more accessible, citing the example of the “Around The Block” podcast she co-hosted for Coinbase on industry trends.

Wu在一封书面信息中告诉TechCrunch，她“非常高兴能回来做我最擅长的事情——领导对加密技术创始人的投资，希望提高他们的第一轮融资。”她说，她希望继续找到让加密更容易访问的方法，并引用了她为Coinbase联合主持的关于行业趋势的“全方位”播客的例子。

Coinbase Ventures had its most active year yet in 2021, investing in nearly 150 deals.

Cin Basic创投在2021年度最活跃的一年，投资了近150笔交易。

Archetype, formerly known as Acrylic, raised $55 million last summer from limited partners including Cendana Capital, as well as Accolade Partners, Accomplice, and Digital Currency Group. The Brooklyn, NY-based fund’s mandate is to invest between $500,000 and $1.5 million into early-stage crypto startups, the Wall Street Journal reported in July.

Archetype（原名丙烯酸树脂）去年夏天从包括Cendana Capital在内的有限合伙人，以及Acclade partners、同谋和数字货币集团（Digital Currency Group）筹集了5500万美元。据《华尔街日报》7月报道，这家总部位于纽约布鲁克林的基金的任务是向早期加密初创公司投资50万至150万美元。

Wu will invest alongside Egan and principal Danny Sursock, the only two Archetype employees currently listed on the firm’s website. Archetype and Coinbase could not be reached for comment.

Wu将与Egan和负责人Danny Sursock一起投资，他们是目前该公司网站上仅有的两名原型员工。记者未能联系到Archetype和Coinbase置评。

Before Coinbase, Wu worked at pre-seed venture capital firm Notation and crypto data provider Messari.

在Coinbase之前，吴曾在种子期之前的风险投资公司Notation和加密数据提供商Messari工作。
