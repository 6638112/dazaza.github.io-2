流感在2020年几乎消失了

2020-10-30 23:02:03

National Influenza Centres (NICs) and other national influenza laboratories from 69 countries, areas or territories reported data to FluNet for the time period from 28 September 2020 to 11 October 2020 a (data as of 2020-10-23 04:13:16 UTC).The WHO GISRS laboratories tested more than 81257 specimens 1 during that time period. A total of 172 specimens were positive for influenza viruses, of which 108 (62.8%) were typed as influenza A and 64 (37.2%) as influenza B. Of the sub-typed influenza A viruses, 4 (6.1%) were influenza A(H1N1)pdm09 and 62 (93.9%) were influenza A(H3N2). Of the characterized B viruses, 2 (8%) belonged to the B-Yamagata lineage and 23 (92%) to the B-Victoria lineage.

来自69个国家、地区或地区的国家流感中心和其他国家流感实验室向FLUNET报告了2020年9月28日至2020年10月11日的数据(世界协调时2020年9月28日至10月11日的数据)。世界卫生组织地理信息系统实验室在此期间检测了81257多个样本1。共检出流感病毒172份，其中甲型流感108份(62.8%)，乙型64份(37.2%)。甲型流感病毒亚型中，甲型H1N1流感pdm09 4份(6.1%)，甲型H3N2流感62份(93.9%)。在特征性B病毒中，2株(8%)属于B-Yamagata系，23株(92%)属于B-Victoria系。
