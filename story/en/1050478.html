Epic continues its studio-acquisition spree, buys Fall Guys devs
Epic继续其工作室收购热潮，收购了Fall Guys开发人员

2021-03-03 03:15:02

On Tuesday, Epic Games, the maker of Unreal Engine and studio behind  Fortnite, continued its push into owning a corner of the game- and software-development ecosystem by acquiring Mediatonic, the makers of popular video game series Fall Guys.

周二，虚幻引擎和Fortnite背后的工作室的制造商Epic Games通过收购流行视频游戏系列Fall Guys的开发商Mediatonic继续推动拥有游戏和软件开发生态系统的一角。

The studio, headquartered in London with satellite operations throughout Europe, was formed in 2005 and is mostly known for contract work on licensed games (along with a significant stint making "web" games for platforms like Facebook), only to explode last year with Fall Guys, a quirky, family-friendly twist on the burgeoning "battle royale" genre. The game's 2020 success was propelled in part by a PlayStation Plus giveaway and high viewership on Twitch. This studio acquisition means Devolver Digital has been relieved as Fall Guys' publisher. This comparison to Rocket League is an immediate reminder that Epic has been bullish on the studio-acquisition front, since it purchased game maker Psyonix in May 2019. Within a year of that acquisition, Epic turned the game Rocket League (which originally launched with a $19.99 MSRP) into a free-to-play product, and Mediatonic's FAQ on their own acquisition acknowledges this possibility by saying they have "nothing to announce right now" about going F2P. In Psyonix's transition, Rocket League was eventually wrested away from Steam for PC players, and Mediatonic has, for now, pledged that their own game "will remain purchasable on Steam and PlayStation" and will soon get ports to Xbox and Nintendo Switch. (That language about "will remain purchasable" gives Mediatonic and Epic Games plenty of wiggle room to change the deal going forward.)

与Rocket League的比较立即提醒我们，Epic自2019年5月收购游戏制造商Psyonix以来一直在工作室收购方面看好。在收购之后的一年内，Epic推出了Rocket League游戏（最初以建议零售价$ 19.99美元）作为免费游戏产品，Mediatonic的常见问题解答（FAQ）就承认了这种可能性，称他们目前没有任何消息要宣布。关于F2P。在Psyonix的过渡过程中，火箭联盟最终从Steam手中夺走了PC玩家的支持，而Mediatonic到目前为止已承诺，他们自己的游戏将继续在Steam和PlayStation上可购买。并将很快获得Xbox和Nintendo Switch的端口。 （有关“ ##的语言将仍然是可购买的”，这为Mediatonic和Epic Games留出了很大的回旋余地，以改变未来的交易。）

Epic Games's development-acquisition tear has largely revolved around software and tools, not game studios, as evidenced by their acquisition of hugely popular RAD compression and development suite in January. (If you've played console or PC games in the past decade, you've seen the RAD logo in at  least one game's opening crawl, if not dozens of them.) And two animation-minded acquisitions, of  Hyprsense in November 2020 and Cubic Motion in March 2020, have been paid forward in Epic's "digital humans" initiative, which revolves around impressive real-time human animations for Unreal Engine games and software alike. These acquisitions have come alongside continued rounds of funding in Epic's favor, with summer 2020 seeing the studio getting an injection of $1.78 billion.

Epic Games的开发收购眼泪主要围绕软件和工具而不是游戏工作室展开，这一点在1月份收购了极为流行的RAD压缩和开发套件便证明了这一点。 （如果您在过去十年中玩过主机游戏或PC游戏，则至少在一场游戏的开幕式中就看到了RAD徽标，即使不是数十种）。 2020年11月对Hyprsense的收购以及2020年3月对Cubic Motion的收购已通过Epic的数字人支付了该计划围绕虚幻引擎游戏和软件等令人印象深刻的实时人类动画展开。这些收购与Epic的支持下的持续几轮融资一起，到2020年夏季，该工作室将获得17.8亿美元的注资。
