Frankfurt, Germany-based CLARK, which searches for the best insurance rates for its users, raises €69M Series C led by Tencent

Clark, a Frankfurt, Germany-based digital insurance manager, raised €69m in Series ​C funding.

总部位于德国法兰克福的数字保险经理克拉克（Clark）筹集了6,900万欧元的C轮融资。

The round was led by Tencent with participation from existing investors Portag3 Ventures, White Star Capital and Yabeo.

该轮融资由腾讯牵头，现有投资者Portag3 Ventures，White Star Capital和Yabeo参与。

The company intends to use the funds to accelerate its growth through further investments in its product and user acquisition.

该公司打算利用这笔资金通过对产品和用户获取的进一步投资来加速其增长。

Founded in June 2015 by Dr. Christopher Oster, Steffen Glomb, Dr. Marco Adelt and Chris Lodde, Clark is an insurance manager that offers its customers a digital way to improve their policies. After a quick registration process, customers can view the status of their insurance situation in the app (iOS and Android) and on the website. Algorithm-based, The system provides an analysis of all the customer’s rates. Clark searches the entire market’s offerings to identify the best rates. The company is present in Germany and Austria (led by Dr. Philip Steiner from Salzburg), has a staff of more than 270 and has a customer base of over 300,000 individuals,
