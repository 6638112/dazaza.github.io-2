谷歌停止销售Nest Secure，仍提供支持

2020-10-29 12:04:34

We want to provide an update on the Nest Secure alarm system. We sold out of Nest Secure and won’t be making the full system available for sale any longer. We are committed to giving existing users the same feature and software support they have always had with Nest Secure.

我们想提供Nest安全警报系统的最新情况。我们的Nest Secure销售一空，不再提供完整的系统可供销售。我们致力于为现有用户提供他们在Nest Secure中一直拥有的相同功能和软件支持。

We are committed to bringing our users the same feature and software support they’ve always had with Nest Secure, including existing cross-product integrations within the Nest ecosystem. We will also continue to deliver critical security updates and software fixes. Nest Secure owners shouldn’t notice any difference with their service.

我们致力于为我们的用户提供他们在Nest Secure中一直拥有的相同功能和软件支持，包括Nest生态系统内现有的跨产品集成。我们还将继续提供重要的安全更新和软件修复。Nest安全所有者应该不会注意到他们的服务有什么不同。

Nest Tag is currently available on the Google Store and we’re working to have more Nest Detect sensors available for sale by mid-December 2020 on the Google Store.

Nest标签目前在Google Store上可用，我们正在努力让更多的Nest Detect传感器在2020年12月中旬之前在Google Store上出售。

If you have a question or problem, you can get answers in our Nest Secure online help center, or ask other Nest Secure owners in the Google Nest Community. You can also contact our support team for Nest Secure issues not covered by our online resources.

如果您有问题或问题，您可以在我们的Nest安全在线帮助中心获得答案，或者向Google Nest社区中的其他Nest安全所有者咨询。您也可以联系我们的支持团队，了解我们的在线资源未涵盖的Nest安全问题。

We sold out of the Nest Secure starter pack and won’t be making the full system available any longer. You can still expand your alarm system with additional Nest Tags and Nest Detects.

Nest安全入门包已经售罄，不会再提供完整的系统。您仍然可以使用附加的Nest标签和Nest检测器来扩展您的警报系统。
