Apple Exec Indicted for Allegedly Trying to Bribe Officers with iPads
Apple Exec被指控涉嫌试图贿赂iPad人员

2020-11-24 12:04:07

Apple’s head of global security was indicted by a grand jury Monday for attempting to bribe law enforcement officers with iPads for concealed firearm permits, the highest-profile tech executive so far roped into the widening Santa Clara Sheriff’s Office scandal.

周一，陪审团起诉苹果全球安全负责人，罪名是企图用iPad贿赂执法人员以获取隐藏的枪支许可证。

A grand jury alleges that Thomas Moyer promised to donate 200 iPads worth $70,000 to the Santa Clara Sheriff’s Office in exchange for four concealed firearm licenses for Apple employees.

大陪审团称，托马斯·莫耶（Thomas Moyer）承诺向圣克拉拉警长办公室捐赠200台iPad，价值7万美元，以换取苹果公司员工的四张暗藏枪支许可证。

Santa Clara District Attorney Jeff Rosen alleges the scheme was orchestrated by Undersheriff Rick Sung and Captain James Jensen, who were also indicted.

圣塔克拉拉地区检察官杰夫·罗森（Jeff Rosen）声称，该计划是由副警长里克·桑（Rick Sung）和詹姆斯·詹森（James Jensen）上尉精心策划的，他们也被起诉。

The donation never came to fruition, though, because the district attorney’s office began to investigate in August 2019.

不过，由于地区检察官办公室于2019年8月开始调查，因此该捐赠从未实现。

Moyer’s lawyer Ed Swanson said in a statement to Forbes his client is innocent and collateral damage in a dispute between the Santa Clara County sheriff and the district attorney.

莫耶（Moyer）的律师埃德·斯旺森（Ed Swanson）在向《福布斯》发表的声明中说，在圣塔克拉拉县警长与地方检察官之间的纠纷中，他的委托人无辜和附带损害。

Moyer’s indictment is part of a larger scandal involving alleged bribes that went to Sheriff Laurie Smith’s reelection campaign in 2018, which has seen four other people, though not Smith, indicted this year.

穆耶（Moyer）的起诉书是更大的丑闻的一部分，该丑闻涉及涉嫌贿赂，后者于2018年参加了警长劳里·史密斯（Lariff Smith Smith）的竞选连任，该运动今年还起诉了另外四人，尽管不是史密斯。

“In the case of four CCW licenses withheld from Apple employees, Undersheriff Sung and Cpt. Jensen managed to extract from Thomas Moyer a promise that Apple would donate iPads to the Sheriff’s Office,” a press release from the Santa Clara District Attorney reads.

“如果苹果公司的雇员Undersheriff Sung和Cpt扣留了四个CCW许可证，詹森设法向托马斯·莫耶（Thomas Moyer）提取了一个承诺，即苹果将向警长办公室捐赠iPad，”圣塔克拉拉地方检察官在一份新闻稿中写道。
