Apple's AirTags Are a Gift to Stalkers
Apple的Airtags是追踪者的礼物

2021-05-14 04:12:10

When Apple first unveiled the iPod, Steve Jobs promised to put a “thousand songs in your pocket.” Twenty years later, when Apple unveiled its newest device, AirTags, it promised to put a global tracking network in your pocket, purse, or any place else the tiny device can be hidden. AirTags are a Bluetooth-enabled homing beacon about the size of a quarter. They are marketed as a great way to track lost luggage or keys, but they may become the latest way for abusers to stalk survivors of intimate partner violence.

当Apple首先揭开iPod时，史蒂夫乔布斯承诺将一个“口袋里的千歌”。二十年后，当Apple推出最新的设备，Airtags时，它承诺将一个全球跟踪网络放入口袋，钱包或任何地方可以隐藏微小设备。 Airtags是一个有一个蓝牙的归位信标，其大小为四分之一。它们作为跟踪丢失行李或钥匙的好方法，但它们可能成为滥用虐待者的虐待者的最新方法。

Yes, stalkerware already exists on phones, and compromised accounts can give away your location. But there are also known defenses against these threats, whether it’s two-factor authentication, antivirus software for phones, or even Apple’s own guide to securing your accounts and settings when your safety is at risk.

是的，手机上已经存在的STAMKERWARE，妥协的帐户可以放弃您的位置。但是，还有已知的防御这些威胁，无论是双重认证，手机的防病毒软件，甚至是Apple自己的指南，以确保您的账户和设置，当您的安全处于风险时。

The threat from AirTags is different. AirTags are easy to hide in a target’s bag or car, giving an abuser an easy way to track their location. This is similar to the threat posed by other trackers like Tile, but on a far larger scale. Tiles are roughly the same weight and size as AirTags, with one key difference: the sheer size of the network. When a Tile is lost or stolen, it tries to communicate with nearby Tile users, piggybacking on their cell or Wi-Fi connection to communicate with the owner. You may go all day without coming within Bluetooth range (approximately 30 feet) of a Tile user, but try getting through the day without coming within 30 feet of an iPhone or iPad. Tile measures its users in the tens of thousands. Apple has more than a billion.

Airtags的威胁是不同的。 Airtags易于隐藏在目标的包或汽车中，给施虐者一个简单的方式来跟踪他们的位置。这类似于其他跟踪器这样的威胁，如瓷砖，但在更大的范围内。瓷砖的重量和大小与空中特拉斯大致相同，具有一个关键差异：网络的纯粹大小。当瓦片丢失或被盗时，它试图与附近的瓷砖用户进行沟通，捎带他们的电池或Wi-Fi连接以与所有者通信。您可以整天都在没有瓷砖用户的蓝牙范围内（大约30英尺），但尝试在不超过iPhone或iPad的30英尺之内完成当天。瓷砖衡量其数万的用户。苹果有超过十亿。

Apple has conscripted nearly every iOS device into its global tracking network by default. If you want to opt out, you must navigate a labyrinth of menu options that will prove completely inaccessible to all but the most technically proficient. Apple is offering the illusion of choice, of consent, but nothing more.

默认情况下，Apple将几乎将每个IOS设备引用到其全局跟踪网络中。如果您想选择退出，您必须导航一个菜单选项的迷宫，这些选项将完全无法进入所有技术熟练。 Apple提供了选择的幻觉，同意，但没有。

For people who use an iPhone, Apple has provided new software alerts to let them know about potential stalking. While the feature is fairly inaccessible, users can get some peace of mind by going to the settings menu and looking for unfamiliar AirTags if they suspect they’re being spied on. But if the survivor has no phone or has an Android device, they’re out of luck. Only after being separated from its owner for 72 hours will the AirTag alert people with a 60-decibel tone, about the same volume as a dishwasher or casual conversation. The easily muffled or circumvented beep will only go off if the AirTag has been out of range of the phone it is paired to for three consecutive days, meaning that abusers who live with survivors (which is quite common) can frequently reset the clock.

对于使用iPhone的人来说，Apple提供了新的软件警报，让他们了解潜在的跟踪。虽然该功能相当不可思议，但如果他们怀疑他们被屏蔽，用户可以通过寻找不熟悉的空中信料来实现一些安心。但如果幸存者没有手机或有一个Android设备，他们就会运气。只有在其所有者分开72小时后，Airtag可以提醒人们用60分贝的音调，大约与洗碗机或随意的谈话相同。如果航空公司已经超出了连续三天的手机的范围，那么易受痛苦或脾气暴躁的哔声只会关闭它，这意味着与幸存者（这是相当普遍）一起生活的滥用者可以经常重置时钟。

Even when abusers don’t live with survivors, that's still three free days of stalking, followed by a warning sound that may be easily missed and which is useless for the hard of hearing. Apple’s failure to take seriously the safety of people who exist outside of the Apple ecosystem is inexcusable. It’s not enough for Apple to just protect iOS users. The billions of Android users deserve to be protected from stalking too. The single most important step that Apple should take is to create an Android app that alerts users to nearby trackers. You shouldn’t have to own an Apple device to know if you are safe from Apple’s products. Additionally, those of us with Apple devices shouldn’t be added to the tracking network without consent. Apple should only add those users who opt in. There is a long and painful history of seeing stalkers and abusers co-opt seemingly innocuous technologies. The location-tracking services built into many family cell phone plans have been used by abusers so frequently that Congress is moving forward with legislation to mitigate the threat.

即使滥用者不用幸存者，那个＆＃39;仍然是踩踏的三天，其次是一个可能很容易错过的警告声，这对于艰难的听力是无用的。苹果未能认真对待苹果生态系统以外的人的安全性不可原谅。 Apple不足以保护IOS用户是不够的。数十亿个Android用户也值得免受跟踪的保护。 Apple应该采取的单一最重要的一步是创建一个Android应用程序，警告用户附近的跟踪器。您不应该拥有一个Apple设备来了解您是否安全的Apple产品。此外，未经同意，我们不应将我们与Apple设备的苹果设备添加到跟踪网络中。 Apple应该只添加那些选择选择的用户。看到偷偷手和滥用者的悠闲痛苦历史，看似无害的技术。在许多家庭手机计划中建立的位置跟踪服务已被滥用者使用，因此大会正在通过立法向前迈进以减轻威胁。

Apple needs to take domestic abuse and stalking seriously. More than 10 million Americans have likely faced stalking in their lifetimes, with more than a million facing this threat every year. The rates for intimate partner violence is even starker, with more than a quarter of women and 10 percent of men reporting abuse. These are not outliers, this is an epidemic of violence touching nearly every corner of our globe. When Apple fails to protect survivors, the consequences can be fatal. Apple leadership needs to give abuse survivors and experts a central place in its development process, incorporating their feedback from the start. Otherwise, the company will continue to make products that endanger people more than they help.

Apple需要滥用国内虐待并认真跟踪。超过1000万美国人在他们的寿命中面临缠扰，每年面临超过百万的威胁。亲密合作伙伴暴力的利率甚至是诽谤，超过四分之一的妇女和10％的人报告滥用。这些不是异常值，这是一种暴力疫情，几乎是我们地球的每个角落。当Apple未能保护幸存者时，后果可能是致命的。 Apple领导力需要滥用幸存者和专家在其开发过程中进行中央处，从一开始就融合了他们的反馈。否则，公司将继续制造危害人民的产品而不是他们的帮助。
