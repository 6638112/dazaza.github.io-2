My bank sent me 64 copies of the same debit card
我的银行给了我64份同一个借记卡

2021-03-12 05:27:19

Some years back (before this was a super easy thing to do) I posted a guide on setting up android phones with a low minute, high data plan that T-Mobile had at the time and using Google Voice to call over data through a free PBX system, so you could basically get unlimited voice and text as long as you had data coverage for like $20/month. I included the regular link to sign up for the plan and also a "if you guys want to use, this is my referral link; if you sign up that way we both get a $15 Visa card after your first month or whatever, but up to you." My understanding was that this was a limited thing like you could only refer three people that way in a month, or something like that.

几年后（在这是一个超级容易做的事情之前）我发布了一个关于建立Android手机的指南，其中T-Mobile当时的高数据计划，并使用Google语音通过免费调用数据PBX系统，因此您可以基本上获得无限的语音和文本，只要您拥有20美元/月的数据覆盖率。我包括定期链接，以报名参加计划，也是一个＆＃34;如果你们想使用，这是我的推荐链接;如果您在那种方式注册，我们都会在您的第一个月或其他任何内容之后获得15美元的签证卡，但到你。＆＃34;我的理解是，这是一个有限的东西，就像你在一个月内那样引用三个人，或者这样的东西。

I forgot all about it, and a couple weeks later I was super annoyed to find like 6 T-Mobile letters in my mailbox, until I opened them and realized they were $15 prepaid debit cards. Neat!

我忘记了它，还有几周后，我非常恼火，在我的邮箱中找到6个T-Mobile字母，直到我打开它们并意识到他们是15美元的预付借记卡。整洁的！

But the next day they kept coming. I got like 12 more the next day, and another 10 or 12 the day after that, and another 10 the day after that, for like 5 or six days. Hundreds and hundreds of dollars worth of prepaid cards, $15 at a time.

但第二天他们一直到来。第二天，我的第二天和第二天的另一个或12日之后，我的第二天就在这一天的另一天，就像5或六天一样。数百和数百美元的预付卡，一次15美元。

I'm assuming at some point they caught that they were sending me way more than I was supposed to get and it dried up after a week or so and I didn't get any more the next month or anything, but I had a couple months of free lunches out of those, $15 at a time.

我在某些时候假设他们抓住了他们的方式比我应该得到更多的方式，并且在一周左右的情况下又来了，我没有再去下个月或任何东西，但是我有几个月的免费午餐，一次15美元。
