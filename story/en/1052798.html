Intel Launches 11th-Gen Rocket Lake-S CPUs
英特尔推出11- reg火箭湖-s cpus

2021-03-17 02:31:30

The new generation of Intel Core CPUs is here. Intel is using a new architecture on its ancient 14nm process to power the 11th-generation Rocket Lake-S processors. That results in some significant power improvements, but it also means that Intel can only fit 8 cores on its flagship Core i9-11900K. That sacrifice to the number of cores looks bad compared to the 12-core AMD Ryzen 9 5900X or even the last-gen 10-core i9-10900K. But Intel is also promising massive improvements to efficiency that should keep the Rocket Lake-S parts competitive — especially in gaming.

新一代英特尔核心CPU就在这里。英特尔正在使用它的古代14nm过程中的新架构，为第11代火箭湖的处理器供电。这导致了一些显着的功率改进，但它也意味着英特尔只能在其旗舰核心I9-11900K上安装8个核心。与12-Core AMD Ryzen 9 5900x甚至是最后的10-Core I9-10900K相比，牺牲核心数量看起来很糟糕。但英特尔也有希望造成巨大的改进，以保持火箭湖的零件竞争力 - 特别是在游戏中。

Rocket Lake-S CPUs launch March 30. The $539 Core i9-11900K has 8 cores and 16 threads with a single-core Thermal Velocity boost of 5.3GHz and 4.8GHz all-core boost. The slightly more affordable $399 i7-11700K boosts up to 5GHz, and the i5-11600K is $262 with 6 cores at a 4.9GHz boost.

Rocket Lake-S CPU推出3月30日。$ 539核心I9-11900K拥有8个核心和16个线程，具有5.3GHz和4.8GHz的单核热速度升压。略高于399 I7-11700K的稍微经济高达5GHz，I5-11600K为262美元，达到4.9GHz的核心。

While the lack of cores is going to hurt Rocket Lake-S CPUs in multi-threaded applications, Intel claims that its 19% improvement to instructions per clock (IPC) will make up much of the difference. The UHD graphics processor in the CPUs also deliver 50% better performance than last generation.

虽然缺乏核心将在多线程应用中伤害火箭湖-S CPU，但英特尔声称其对每时钟（IPC）指令的19％的提高将构成大部分差异。 CPU中的UHD图形处理器还提供比上一代更好的50％。

Of course, Intel is focusing on games because that is where its processors remain the most competitive versus AMD. And that should continue with its Rocket Lake-S chips. These high-clocked parts with improved performance should keep up and even exceed AMD’s Zen 3 chips in certain games, like Microsoft’s Flight Simulator (according to Intel).

当然，英特尔专注于游戏，因为这是其处理器仍然是最具竞争力的与AMD。这应该继续其火箭湖-s芯片。这些具有改进性能的高钟部件应在某些游戏中保持甚至超过AMD的Zen 3芯片，如微软的飞行模拟器（根据英特尔）。

This new generation of CPUs is also finally pushing Intel’s desktop products into PCIe 4.0. This means you can get some of the benefits of cutting-edge connectivity between the processor, GPU, and storage.

这一新一代CPU还在最终将英特尔的桌面产品推向PCIe 4.0。这意味着您可以获得处理器，GPU和存储之间的尖端连接的一些好处。

In a briefing with press, Intel claimed that its PCIe 4.0 implementation is already 11% faster than AMD Ryzen.

在简介中，英特尔声称其PCIe 4.0实施比AMD Ryzen快11％。

So is this new Intel chip for you? It might be — especially if you still cannot find a Ryzen later this month.

这款新的英特尔芯片是给你的吗？它可能是 - 特别是如果你仍然无法在本月晚些时候找到一个ryzen。
