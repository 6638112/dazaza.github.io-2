Interplay, which develops enterprise focused VR training tools, raises $18M Series B led by Owl Ventures and S3 Ventures
Interplay开发以企业为中心的VR培训工具，由Owl Ventures和S3 Ventures领投了1800万美元的B轮融资

2021-02-17 21:22:48

Interplay, an Austin, Texas-based startup developing a 3D and virtual reality platform for trade workers, today announced that it raised $18 million. The company says it’ll use the proceeds to move into new markets and further develop its existing products.

Interplay是一家为贸易工作者开发3D和虚拟现实平台的总部位于德克萨斯州奥斯汀的初创公司，今天宣布筹集了1800万美元。该公司表示，将使用所得款项进入新市场并进一步开发其现有产品。

According to an ABI Research report, before the pandemic, the virtual reality market was forecasted to grow at a 45.7% compound annual rate, surpassing $24.5 billion in revenue by 2024. But even amid the health crisis, virtual reality is booming. A PricewaterhouseCoopers survey predicts that nearly 23.5 million jobs worldwide will use augmented and virtual reality by 2030 for training, work meetings, or customer service.

根据ABI Research的一份报告，在大流行之前，虚拟现实市场预计将以45.7％的复合年增长率增长，到2024年将超过245亿美元的收入。但是即使在健康危机期间，虚拟现实仍在蓬勃发展。普华永道（PricewaterhouseCoopers）的一项调查预测，到2030年，全球将有2350万个工作岗位将使用增强和虚拟现实技术进行培训，工作会议或客户服务。

Founded in 2016 by Doug Donovan and Steve Quirk, Interplay provides online and virtual reality training for a range of skilled trades. Its digital learning simulations span HVAC, plumbing, electrical, facilities maintenance, and solar subject matter, letting customers practice hands-on learning and train to be job-ready.

Interplay由Doug Donovan和Steve Quirk于2016年创立，为各种技术行业提供在线和虚拟现实培训。它的数字学习模拟涵盖了HVAC，管道，电气，设施维护和太阳能主题，使客户可以练习动手学习并进行培训以准备工作。

Interplay’s platform delivers a catalog of courses and measurement and engagement tools, as well as knowledge checks, 3D simulations, and more. Sim walkthrough videos feature experts who lead users through procedure guides step-by-step, with a short quiz and supplemental learning material including wiring diagrams, equations, example system configs, and charts following each lesson.

Interplay的平台提供了课程，测量和参与工具的目录，以及知识检查，3D模拟等。 Sim演练视频由专家组成，他们带领用户逐步完成过程指南，并提供简短的测验和补充学习材料，包括接线图，方程式，示例系统配置和每节课后的图表。

With each course, Interplay guides users through the diagnostic process. The platform then presents a randomly generated fault in a system with some guardrails removed. In the last stage, users aren’t told what fault they’re working on. Instead, they have to rely on earlier training, employing the diagnostic process established throughout the course.

对于每门课程，Interplay都会指导用户完成诊断过程。然后，该平台会在系统中随机删除一些护栏，从而产生随机生成的故障。在最后阶段，不会告诉用户正在处理的故障。相反，他们必须依靠早期培训，并采用整个课程中建立的诊断过程。

Interplay, whose platform supports smartphones, tablets, and PCs in addition to virtual reality headsets, offers learning paths, or roadmaps created to help teams learn skills in the right order. It also allows managers to view the top users based on education credits earned in a timeframe and to administer tests that assess skills and abilities, with an automated system that instantly generates a report.

Interplay的平台除了支持虚拟现实耳机外，还支持智能手机，平板电脑和PC，它提供了学习路径或路线图，旨在帮助团队以正确的顺序学习技能。它还可以使管理人员根据在一定时间范围内获得的教育学分查看最高用户，并通过自动生成报告的自动化系统来管理评估技能和能力的测试。

Interplay competes with a number of startups leveraging virtual reality and simulation to upskill workers. Strivr, a company behind a platform that companies use to train employees, recently raised $30 million in venture funding. There’s also Talespin, which nabbed $15 million last March to develop what it calls “extended reality” using technologies like virtual reality and augmented reality.

Interplay与许多利用虚拟现实和模拟来提升员工技能的初创公司竞争。 Strivr是一家公司用来培训员工的平台背后的公司，最近筹集了3000万美元的风险资金。还有Talespin，去年3月获得了1500万美元的投资，用于使用虚拟现实和增强现实技术开发所谓的“扩展现实”。

But Donovan says the pandemic is acting as an adoption catalyst for his company’s solutions. In 2020, its usage was up 445%, exceeding 300% year-over-year growth. And now, Interplay has more than 2,000 customers including Rheem Manufacturing and Tesla, which combined have 15,000 monthly users on Interplay’s training platform.

但多诺万（Donovan）表示，大流行病正在成为其公司解决方案的采用催化剂。到2020年，其使用量增长了445％，超过了同比300％的增长。现在，Interplay拥有Rheem Manufacturing和Tesla在内的2,000多个客户，在Interplay的培训平台上，每月总共有15,000个用户。

“Skilled workers have been overlooked by edtech innovation and the venture community,” Donovan told VentureBeat via email. “Most investment has been in digital skills or knowledge skills companies.  Interplay Learning is in unique position to serve the electrical, mechanical and industrial market at scale.”

“技术熟练的工人被edtech创新和风险社区所忽视，” Donovan通过电子邮件告诉VentureBeat。 “大部分投资都投资于数字技能或知识技能公司。 Interplay Learning在为电气，机械和工业市场提供大规模服务方面处于独特的位置。”

Owl Ventures and S3 Ventures led Interplay’s series B funding round announced today. Strada Education Network and The Venture Reality Fund also participated, as well as existing investors SJF Ventures, Sierra Ventures, Holt Ventures, Wild Basin Investments, and Shelter Capital Partners. It brings the 60-person startup’s total raised to date to $25 million.

Owl Ventures和S3 Ventures今天宣布了Interplay的B轮融资。 Strada Education Network和The Venture Reality Fund以及现有投资者SJF Ventures，Sierra Ventures，Holt Ventures，Wild Basin Investments和Shelter Capital Partners也参加了会议。迄今为止，这家拥有60名员工的创业公司的总融资额达到2500万美元。
