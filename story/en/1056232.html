Make It Your Own: Why I Laser Engraved My Camera
让它自己：为什么我激光刻有我的相机

2021-04-05 16:12:19

As photographers, we are immersed in a world of creativity, expression, and individuality. Our craft is one that we pursue in hopes of creating something that evokes emotion and thought, insight and so much more. So why are our cameras so… boring?

作为摄影师，我们沉浸在一个创造力，表达和个性世界中。我们的工艺是我们追求创造一些引起情绪和思想，洞察力等等的人的工艺。那么为什么我们的相机如此......无聊？

And as we continue on our journey as artists — because that is what I believe we are — we find that outside of the actual art itself, there is little in the way of individuality and expression when it comes to our equipment. Sure, some of the more adventurous among us may venture into the world of film if we only shoot digital and some of us may start adapting vintage glass to use on our digital cameras, but at the end of the day, we are still left with equipment that looks like every other photographer’s gear.

随着我们作为艺术家的旅程继续，因为这就是我相信的所在 - 我们发现在实际艺术本身之外，在我们的设备方面时，个性和表情都很少。当然，如果我们只拍摄数字，我们中的一些人可能会开始适应我们的数码相机，但在我们的数码相机上使用，但在我们的数码相机上使用，但在一天结束时，我们可能会冒险进入电影世界，但在一天结束时，我们仍然留下来看起来像其他所有摄影师的装备的设备。

But what if you get bored of the same black box with the same familiar lens attached? I personally have spent more money than I’d care to admit on things like custom leather straps, colorful shutter release buttons, thumb grips, leatherette half-cases, and so on. These were all purchased in my quest to individualize my camera gear. Some will read this and scoff at the thought of dressing up their gear while others will completely understand my pursuit of making my gear distinctly mine. For those looking to move beyond the temporary embellishments like straps and shutter release buttons, those who are like me and willing to commit to something more extreme (this is coming from someone whose arms are covered in lifelong commitment: tattoos), I offer you the ultimate in personalization:

但是，如果你厌倦了与相同熟悉的镜头相同的黑匣子厌倦了什么？我个人花费了更多的钱，而不是我关心定制皮革肩带，彩色快门释放按钮，拇指手柄，人造皮革半箱等东西。这些都在我寻求中购买我的相机装备。有些人会读到这个和嘲笑他们的装备，而其他人会完全了解我追求我的装备明显地。对于那些希望超越临时点缀的人，如皮带和快门释放按钮，那些像我一样，愿意犯有更极端的东西（这来自终身承诺的人的人：纹身），我为你提供了终极个​​人化：

Recently an artist friend of mine, Jakob Soper reached out to tell me about his latest purchases to create art with. Lasers! These aren’t the ones you see in ads on Facebook or on Kickstarter, these lasers are much higher powered and capable of not just lightly etching the finish on the surface of your MacBook or the like, but actually cutting through it. Now don’t let that power scare you away! Because these lasers — when controlled by someone like my friend who has years of experience — are capable of the most intricate details.

最近艺术家的朋友，Jakob Soper伸出告诉我他的最新购买，以创造艺术。激光！这些不能在Facebook或Kickstarter上看到的不是那些，这些激光器的动力要高，并且不仅能轻松地在MacBook的表面上轻松蚀刻，而且实际上通过它。现在不要让电力吓到你！因为这些激光器 - 当由像我的朋友那样受到多年经验的人控制 - 有能力最复杂的细节。

I immediately responded to his message with a question, “Do you want to engrave some cameras?”

我立即回复了他的留言，“你想雕刻一些相机吗？”

At first, he wasn’t sure why I’d suggested I have him engrave my cameras versus something more pedestrian like, say, a keychain, but after I told him my vision for what I wanted to be done he got excited and we began planning.

起初，他不确定为什么我建议我让他雕刻我的相机与更多的行人一样，说，一个钥匙扣，但在我告诉他我对我想做的事情的愿景之后，他开始兴奋，我们开始了规划。

Our first order of business was my favorite street camera, the Olympus PEN-F. The Olympus PEN-F is a beautiful camera designed to look similar to a rangefinder with an offset electronic viewfinder and classic step-down top plate. But more importantly, this camera was created to be a digital homage to the original Olympus PEN-F 35mm half-frame camera released in 1963.

我们的第一阶业务是我最喜欢的街头摄像头，奥林巴斯笔-f。 Olympus Pen-F是一款漂亮的相机，旨在看起来类似于具有偏移电子取景器和经典降压顶板的测距仪。但更重要的是，该相机被创建为1963年发布的原始Olympus Pen-F 35mm半帧摄像头的数字致敬。

That camera was small and perfect for carrying anywhere, especially in the streets. The front of the camera was engraved with the words Olympus-PEN and immediately to the right of that was a large Gothic letter F. This Gothic F is an eye-catching branding statement and somehow never made it onto the 2016 digital homage PEN-F. In my mind, this was a terrible omission I was about to correct it!

那台相机很小，非常适合携带任何地方，特别是在街道上。相机的前部刻有奥林巴斯 - 笔，并立即到达那个大型哥特式字母F.这位哥特式F是醒目的品牌声明，不知何故从未成功于2016年数字致敬笔-F 。在我的脑海里，这是一个可怕的遗漏我即将纠正它！

When I delivered my cameras to Jakob I told him the first camera I wanted us to tackle was the PEN-F and I showed him what he’d be engraving, and where it would go. This was by far the easiest of the two cameras to customize thanks to the big empty space for engraving and for the relatively simple design of the letter F.

当我把我的相机送到jakob时，我告诉他我希望我们解决的第一台相机是笔 - 我向他展示了他的雕刻，它会去哪里。这是迄今为止，由于雕刻的大空空间和字母F的相对简单的设计，这是两个相机的最简单。

Jakob started the etching process and sent me some cell phone videos of the laser at work. After five passes, he sent me photos to show the depth of the engraving and we decided to run the laser for five more passes to get the engraving as deep as the original camera markings.

Jakob开始了蚀刻过程并在工作中发出了一些激光器的手机视频。经过五次通行证，他发给了我的照片来展示雕刻的深度，我们决定运行激光5次传球，以使雕刻深入作为原始相机标记。

Upon completion, I was blown away at how crisp the laser cut vs what I assume is a cast-in process for the lettering done by Olympus from the factory. Every corner is very well defined, the curves fluid and perfect — laser perfect.

完成后，我被吹走了激光切割的脆弱与奥林巴斯从工厂完成的刻字的演员过程。每个角落都很明确，曲线流体和完善 - 激光完美。

All that was left was for me to do was blacken the letter to match the original paint on the camera. That was done using acrylic model paint and syringe. Once you put a single drop of paint into the engraving, the capillary effect draws the paint throughout every curve and fills the space evenly.

剩下的一切都是让我这样做的是将字母变黑，以匹配相机上的原始油漆。这是使用丙烯酸模型涂料和注射器完成的。一旦将一滴涂料放入雕刻，毛细管效果将在整个曲线中绘制涂料并均匀地填充空间。

With one camera down, it was on to what I felt was going to be the real head turner out of the bunch: my Fuji X-E3. Having spent nearly a decade only ever shooting Olympus equipment, one of the first non-Olympus cameras that I bought was the Fuji. I loved the form factor of the PEN-F but was dying to play with the Fuji color science. With the Fuji, I had settled on a design that was surely going to test the capabilities of the laser — and Jakob.

通过一个相机向下，它令我觉得将成为真正的头部特纳：我的富士X-E3。花了几年只有射击奥林巴斯设备，是我买的第一个非奥林巴的相机之一是富士。我喜欢笔-f的形状因素，但随着富士色彩科学致死。随着富士，我已经确定了一个肯定会测试激光和雅各布的能力的设计。

The pattern is a floral Damask pattern that has a decidedly Victorian feel to it but with a little bit of my personality mixed in. Comprised of ornate, floral sprigs and embellishments, the central design in the pattern is skulls. Yeah, I’m that guy. If I can get something that takes the delicate beauty of a floral pattern and can pop a skull in there for contrast, I will.

这些模式是一种花卉锦缎模式，对此有一个明确的维多利亚时代的感觉，但我的个性混合了。由华丽，花香和装饰组成，图案中的中央设计是头骨。是的，我是那个人。如果我能得到一些花卉图案的精致美丽的东西，可以在那里弹出一个头骨，但我会的。

“See if you can put it on the bottom and work around the battery door first,” I said to Jakob.

“看看你是否可以把它放在底部并首先在电池门上工作，”我对雅各布说。

He got ahold of me later that with a photo showing that he had no problem working around the battery door and proceeded to tell me that he could work around anything on the camera, really. This was very exciting news! Jakob proceeded to map out the entire top plate and face of the camera so he could engrave around all the dials, buttons, lettering, and lens mount.

他稍后会看到我的照片显示，一张照片显示他在电池门上没有问题，并继续告诉我，他可以在相机上绕过任何东西。这是非常令人兴奋的消息！ Jakob继续映射到相机的整个顶板和面部，因此他可以围绕所有拨号，按钮，刻字和镜头安装座。

He once again sent me a video of the process to ask how deep I wanted the engraving to go and I decided to keep this one shallower than I went on the PEN-F, since there would be more physical contact with the engraved surfaces on the Fuji. If we’d gone too deep, the camera would feel abrasive and may end up not being much fun to handle and that is not how a camera should feel in your hands.

他再次发给我一个过程的视频，问我有多深，我有多深，我决定让这个比我走在笔-f浅的时候，因为与雕刻的表面会有更大的身体接触富士。如果我们走得太深，相机会感到磨蚀，可能最终不得不处理，这不是相机应该在你手中感受到的。

The end result was for me much better than I could have imagined! The intricate design flows around the camera and is noticeable from a distance. I have decided to keep this engraving bare metal and not blacken it the way I did on the PEN-F since I think the blackening process might make everything look too messy.

最终结果对我来说比我想象的要好得多！错综复杂的设计在相机周围流动，从距离中明显。我决定保持这种雕刻裸露的金属，而不是像我在笔-f上所做的方式那样变黑，因为我认为黑化过程可能会使一切看起来太乱了。

I am now working with Jakob on design ideas for my lenses and other pieces of camera gear. Not everything will end up as wild as the Fuji though. Other ideas we have been kicking around are putting contact information on equipment in a discreet way or branding with people’s social handles or logos. The sky is really the limit with what he can do and it is now up to us to figure out what else we can do to make our gear uniquely our own.

我现在正在使用Jakob对我的镜头和其他相机齿轮的设计理念。不是一切都会尽快成为富士的。我们一直在踢的其他想法正在以谨慎的方式或品牌与人们的社交句柄或徽标进行联系信息。天空真的是他能做什么的极限，现在已经取决于我们弄清楚我们可以做些什么来使我们的装备独特自己。
