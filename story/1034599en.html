PlayStation5有一个隐藏的网络浏览器，下面是如何找到它的

2020-11-12 02:14:19

Officially, the PlayStation 5 doesn't include a Web browser, marking an apparent software downgrade from the PlayStation 4. But Ars has discovered the PS5 does actually include a limited, hidden Web browsing interface that you can use to load up generic websites on your TV in a pinch.

按照官方说法，PlayStation 5没有网络浏览器，这明显标志着PlayStation 4软件的降级。但ARS发现，PS5实际上包含一个有限的、隐藏的网络浏览界面，你可以在必要时用它在电视上加载通用网站。

The easiest way to get into the PS5 Web browser is to go to the system settings and load up the User's Guide. Unlike most other informational menus on the system, this one loads up inside a Web browser that points to the live URL at manuals.playstation.net.

进入PS5网络浏览器的最简单方法是进入系统设置并加载用户指南。与系统上的大多数其他信息菜单不同，该菜单加载在Web浏览器中，该浏览器指向manuals.playsit.net上的实时URL。

If you visit that address from a standard PC or mobile Web browser, the site gives an error message. But if you access it from the PS5 menu, you see a picture explaining "how to navigate this user's guide" with the DualSense controller, as seen above.

如果你通过标准的个人电脑或移动网络浏览器访问该地址，该网站会给出一条错误信息。但如果你从PS5菜单访问它，你会看到一张图片，解释如何使用DualSense控制器导航这个用户指南，如上所示。

While this user's guide is fun (and informative) to play around in, it doesn't offer any external links that could provide a hook to the wider Web. And without any option to just type in a generalized URL via the PS5 interface, the User's Guide is of limited use for surfing the information superhighway (as all the hippest '90s kids would say).

虽然这本用户指南很有趣(而且信息量也很大)，但它并没有提供任何可以提供更广泛网络链接的外部链接。而且，由于没有任何选项可以只通过PS5界面输入一个通用的URL，用户指南在浏览信息高速公路方面的用处有限(正如所有最时髦的90后孩子都会说的那样)。

To get that kind of wider access, you have to scroll down to the Users and Accounts menu on the PS5 and head to the "Link with Other Services" option. There, you can choose to link to a Twitter account, which brings up a Web page with a Twitter login prompt.

要获得更广泛的访问权限，你必须向下滚动到PS5上的用户和账户菜单，然后转到链接到其他服务选项。在那里，您可以选择链接到Twitter帐户，这将打开一个带有Twitter登录提示的网页。

As designed, this page is meant to log you in to Twitter, link your account to the PS5, and spit you back to the system menu. However, if you click on the small Twitter icon in the top corner, you go instead to Twitter's standard Web interface, just as it would look on any other browser. Log in from there and you get full access to Twitter through the PS5's Web browser.

按照设计，这个页面是为了让你登录Twitter，将你的账号链接到PS5，然后让你回到系统菜单。然而，如果你点击上角的小Twitter图标，你就会进入Twitter的标准Web界面，就像它在任何其他浏览器上一样。从那里登录，你就可以通过PS5的个人网页浏览器完全访问Twitter。

Though you still can't type into the URL bar in this interface, Twitter.com offers (slightly cumbersome) access to the wider Web through clickable links in tweets and profile descriptions. If the site you're interested in has a Twitter account (or is simply discussed on social media at all), finding those links is usually as simple as typing your intended target into the Twitter search bar.

虽然你仍然不能在这个界面的地址栏中输入，但Twitter.com通过推文和个人资料描述中的可点击链接提供了更广泛的网络访问(略显麻烦)。如果你感兴趣的网站有Twitter账号(或者只是在社交媒体上被讨论)，找到这些链接通常就像在Twitter搜索栏中输入你想要的目标一样简单。

You can even plug in a USB keyboard to make the searching simpler and/or hop through links with the arrow and Enter keys. USB mice don't seem to work with the PS5 browser, though—you still need to use the DualSense joysticks to scroll or move the on-screen pointer.

你甚至可以插入USB键盘来简化搜索和/或使用箭头和Enter键跳过链接。不过，USB鼠标似乎无法与PS5浏览器配合使用--你仍然需要使用DualSense操纵杆来滚动或移动屏幕上的指针。

After a bit of spot testing, we found that the PS5 Web browser seems to work just fine for pages that are primarily text and images. Multimedia and/or interactive pages were a bit more hit and miss, though.

经过一些现场测试后，我们发现PS5的Web浏览器对于主要是文本和图像的页面似乎工作得很好。不过，多媒体和/或互动页面的点击率更高一些。

Video sites like YouTube, Vimeo, and Twitch worked without much issue on the PS5, complete with sound, though trying to go "full screen" with a video failed on each one. Music streaming sites like Spotify, Soundcloud, and Bandcamp, on the other hand, could generally load the interface but couldn't actually play any music through the PS5 in our tests.

YouTube、Vimeo和Twitch等视频网站在PS5上运行时没有出现太多问题，而且都有声音，不过每台都试图全屏播放，但都没有播放视频。另一方面，Spotify、SoundCloud和BandCamp等音乐流媒体网站一般都可以加载界面，但在我们的测试中无法真正通过PS5播放任何音乐。

Web-based games were also pretty spotty in our PS5 browser tests. Games and/or emulators that are coded in pure HTML and Javascript seem to load, though controls were an issue unless they're designed to work with just a keyboard. Games that require WebGL, Flash, or other fancier Web-based libraries seemed to fail, though. That includes the Internet Archive's massive library of emulated software; the site's Web-based implementations of DOSBox and MESS/MAME seemed to freeze immediately after loading assets on the PS5.

在我们的PS5浏览器测试中，基于网络的游戏也相当参差不齐。用纯HTML和Javascript编写的游戏和/或模拟器似乎可以加载，尽管控件是一个问题，除非它们被设计成只与键盘一起工作。不过，那些需要WebGL、Flash或其他更时髦的基于Web的库的游戏似乎都失败了。这包括互联网档案馆庞大的仿真软件库；在PS5上加载资源后，该网站基于网络的DOSbox和Mess/Mame的实现似乎立即冻结。

When it comes to productivity sites, I was surprised that I could log in to Ars' Slack Web interface and even type to my co-workers from the PS5. Ars Technica's WordPress-based Content Management System loaded too, though I decided to actually compose this post on a PC (after struggling to write a similar post on an Xbox One seven years ago).

说到工作效率网站，我很惊讶我可以登录Ars&#Slack网络界面，甚至可以通过PS5与我的同事打字。Ars Technica基于WordPress的内容管理系统也被加载了，不过我决定在PC上撰写这篇文章(七年前我很难在Xbox One上写一篇类似的文章)。

Trying to write on Google Docs, though, led to an error asking me to upgrade my browser (if only that were possible) and an inability to type in the main composition window. The site's top menu worked just fine, though, so I could make a complete document by inserting web images and "special characters" one by one on the PS5 if I really wanted to.

然而，试图在Google Docs上写东西时，出现了一个错误，要求我升级浏览器(如果可能的话)，并且无法在主撰写窗口中输入内容。不过，该网站的顶层菜单运行良好，所以如果我真的想要的话，我可以通过在PS5上一个接一个地插入网页图片和特殊字符来制作一份完整的文档。

It might seem odd for Sony to build a decently functional Web browser into the PS5 and then hide it away where most users will never really use it. It might seem especially odd since the PS4 featured a fully supported Web browser complete with URL bar and quick access to "frequently used pages." It seems odder still when you notice the PS5 system menu actually includes a "Web Browser" settings section where you can disable JavaScript, delete cookies, and "prevent cross-site tracking."

索尼在PS5中内置了一个功能良好的网络浏览器，然后将其隐藏在大多数用户永远不会真正使用的地方，这似乎有些奇怪。这似乎特别奇怪，因为PS4有一个完全受支持的网络浏览器，带有地址栏，可以快速访问常用页面。当你注意到PS5系统菜单实际上包括一个网络浏览器设置部分时，似乎更奇怪，你可以在这个部分禁用JavaScript，删除Cookie，并阻止跨站点跟踪。"；。

That said, for Sony to upgrade the PS5 browser from its current "ramshackle interface for the User's Guide and Web service logins" to "fully featured browser that works reliably across the Web" would probably take a decent amount of effort (and customer support). And Sony has recently suggested that putting in that effort just isn't a priority at the moment.

话虽如此，索尼要将PS5浏览器从目前用于用户指南和网络服务登录的摇摇欲坠的界面升级为功能齐全、能在网络上可靠运行的浏览器，可能需要付出相当大的努力(以及客户支持)。索尼最近表示，目前投入这一努力并不是当务之急。

"Currently, we don't intend to include a Web browser in PS5," Sony Senior Vice President Hideaki Nishino said in a recent interview with Japanese site AV Watch (as translated by Twitter used Nibel). "We have doubts about whether a Web browser is necessary for a game console as an app, so we'll have to wait and see."

索尼高级副总裁西野英明(Hideaki Nishino)最近在接受日本网站AV Watch采访时表示，目前，我们不打算在PS5中加入网络浏览器。对于游戏机作为应用程序是否需要网络浏览器，我们持怀疑态度，所以我们还得拭目以待。

(On the Xbox One and Series S/X, third-party browsers like Monument can serve your browsing needs nicely).

(在Xbox One和Series S/X上，Monument等第三方浏览器可以很好地满足你的浏览需求)。

For now, if you're desperate to load up a webpage through your PS5 for some reason, the workaround discussed above will let you do so. But don't be surprised if that's the closest we get to official support for PS5 Web browsing in the near future.

目前，如果你出于某种原因不顾一切地想通过PS5加载网页，上面讨论的解决办法可以让你做到这一点。但是，如果这是我们在不久的将来最接近官方对PS5网络浏览的支持，不要感到惊讶。
