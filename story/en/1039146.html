Zodiac ‘340 Cypher’ Cracked
十二生肖“ 340密码”破解

2020-12-12 03:27:09

The solution to what’s known as the 340 Cipher, one of the most vexing mysteries of the Zodiac Killer’s murderous saga, has been found by a code-breaking team from the United States, Australia and Belgium.

美国，澳大利亚和比利时的密码破译团队已经找到了所谓的340密码解决方案，这是十二生肖杀手的杀人传奇中最令人困扰的谜团之一。

The cipher, sent in a letter to The Chronicle in November 1969, has been puzzling authorities and amateur sleuths since it arrived 51 years ago. Investigators hoped the Zodiac, who killed five people in the Bay Area in 1968 and 1969, would reveal his name in one of his many ciphers, but the 340 does not do that.

该密码于1969年11月寄给《纪事报》，自51年前问世以来，一直困扰着当局和业余侦探。调查人员希望在1968年和1969年在湾区杀死5人的黄道十二宫能够用他的多种密码之一透露自己的名字，但是340并不能做到这一点。

According to code-breaking expert David Oranchak, the cipher’s text includes: “I hope you are having lots of fun in trying to catch me. ... I am not afraid of the gas chamber because it will send me to paradise all the sooner because I now have enough slaves to work for me.”

根据密码破解专家David Oranchak的说法，密文包括：“我希望您在抓住我的过程中感到很开心。 ……我不惧怕毒气室，因为它现在会让我早日成为天堂，因为我现在有足够的奴隶为我工作。”

“Last weekend, a team I’m on solved the 340 and submitted it to the FBI,” he said in an email to The Chronicle Friday. “They have confirmed the solution. No joke! This is the real deal.”

“在上周末，我所在的团队解决了340，并将其提交给FBI，”他在周五给《纪事报》的电子邮件中说。 “他们已经确认了解决方案。不是开玩笑！这是实打实的。”

Cameron Polan, spokeswoman for the FBI’s San Francisco office, confirmed Oranchak’s claim Friday. In a statement emailed to The Chronicle, she said:

FBI旧金山办事处女发言人卡梅伦·波兰（Cameron Polan）周五证实了奥兰查克（Oranchak）的要求。在通过电子邮件发送给《纪事报》的声明中，她说：

“The FBI is aware that a cipher attributed to the Zodiac Killer was recently solved by private citizens. The Zodiac Killer case remains an ongoing investigation for the FBI San Francisco division and our local law enforcement partners.

“联邦调查局知道，一个属于黄道十二宫杀手的密码最近被私人公民解决了。十二生肖杀手案仍在对FBI旧金山分部和我们当地的执法合作伙伴进行调查。

“The Zodiac Killer terrorized multiple communities across Northern California and even though decades have gone by, we continue to seek justice for the victims of these brutal crimes,” she continued. “Due to the ongoing nature of the investigation, and out of respect for the victims and their families, we will not be providing further comment at this time.”

她继续说：“十二生肖杀手使整个北加州的多个社区遭受了恐怖袭击，即使过去了几十年，我们仍在为这些残酷罪行的受害者寻求正义。” “由于调查的持续性质，出于对受害者及其家人的尊重，我们目前暂不提供进一步的评论。”

This is the second time a Zodiac cipher has been cracked. The first, one long cipher sent in pieces to The Chronicle, San Francisco Examiner and Vallejo Times-Herald papers in 1969, was solved by a Salinas schoolteacher and his wife.

这是十二生肖密码第二次被破解。最早的一个长密码是在1969年寄给《纪事报》，《旧金山审查员》和《瓦列霍时代报》的论文的，后来由萨利纳斯州的一名老师和他的妻子解决了。

It said little beyond: “I like killing because it is so much fun.”

它几乎没有说：“我喜欢杀人，因为它是如此有趣。”
