CDPR CEO blames “in-game streaming” for Cyberpunk’s console problems
CDPR首席执行官将“游戏内流媒体”归咎于Cyberpunk的控制台问题

2021-01-15 21:04:05

CD Projekt Red is still trying to contain the damage from widespread reports of major technical problems in the versions of Cyberpunk 2077 released for the PS4 and Xbox One last month. To that end, studio co-founder and CEO Marcin Iwinski today tweeted a video message seeking to explain the internal situation leading up to the problematic launch. "Despite good reviews on PC, the console version of Cyberpunk 2077 did not meet the quality standard we wanted it to meet," Iwinski said in the message. "I, and the entire leadership team, are deeply sorry for this, and this video is me publicly owning up to that."

The core of the problem, Iwinski said, was the "in-game streaming system" that Cyberpunk 2077 used to "feed" content and game mechanics to the engine without frequent breaks for loading. That system had to be "constantly improved" for last-gen consoles during development, Iwinski said, in order to keep up with the "epic" look of the PC version (which saw its graphics and other assets scaled down to work on more limited, older console hardware).

伊温斯基说，问题的核心是“游戏中的流媒体系统”。赛博朋克2077曾经用来“喂养”内容和游戏机制，无需频繁中断加载。该系统必须“不断改进”。 Iwinski表示，在开发过程中使用的是最新一代的游戏机，以跟上＆＃34; epic＆＃34; PC版本的外观（它的图形和其他资产按比例缩小以在更有限的旧控制台硬件上工作）。

"Things did not look super difficult at first, [but] I think that time has proven we underestimated the task," Iwinski said. "Because the city is so packed and the disk bandwidth of old-gen consoles is what it is, it constantly challenged us."

“一开始看起来并不困难，但是，我认为时间证明了我们低估了这个任务，”伊温斯基说。 ＆＃34;因为城市是如此拥挤，而老式控制台的磁盘带宽正是它的本钱，所以它不断挑战着我们。

Iwinski was less direct about why these problems with the console versions weren't discovered and either fixed or delayed before launch. "Every change and improvement needed to be tested, and as it turned out, our testing did not show a big part of the issues you experienced while playing the game," Iwinski said. He added that communication problems caused by the team working from home amid COVID-related restrictions meant some issues got lost over video calls or emails. "We saw significant improvements each and every day leading up to release [and] really thought we'd deliver in the day zero [version on consoles]," he said.

对于为什么未发现控制台版本的这些问题，以及在启动之前已解决或延迟的问题，Iwinski不太直接。 ＆＃34;每项变更和改进都需要进行测试，事实证明，我们的测试并未显示您在玩游戏时遇到的大部分问题，＆＃34;伊温斯基说。他补充说，由于团队在COVID相关的限制下在家工作导致的通信问题，这意味着一些问题由于视频通话或电子邮件而丢失了。 ＆＃34;我们每天都看到重大改进，从而导致发布[并且]真的以为我们将在零[控制台版本]交付的那一天交付，＆＃34;他说。

Iwinski said these late-in-development improvements and constant testing on consoles were the reason review code for the console versions of the game was not offered to reviewers alongside PC code in early December. While Iwinski said console code was sent out December 8 (later than was originally planned), Ars Technica was not provided with any console version to test before the game's launch on December 10 (much less ahead of the initial PC review embargo on December 7).

Iwinski说，这些开发后期的改进和对游戏机的不断测试是12月初未与PC代码一起提供给游戏者的游戏版本的评论代码的原因。虽然Iwinski说控制台代码是在12月8日发出的（比原计划的要晚），但Ars Technica在12月10日游戏发布之前没有提供任何控制台版本来进行测试（比起最初的PC审查禁运要少得多）在12月7日）。

Iwinski's statement also doesn't address the significant (but less-than-game-breaking) bugs initially found in the PC version of the game. As we stated in our review of the PC version, "non-hostile characters will stand motionless in midair, stuck in a ridiculous T-pose, or pace back and forth in tight corridors for no reason. People phase in and out of existence before my eyes. A crowded table at a club will feature one drink hovering in the hands of an invisible patron."

Iwinski的声明也没有解决最初在PC版本的游戏中发现的重大（但不足游戏性的）错误。正如我们在PC版本的评论中所指出的那样，非敌对角色将在空中一动不动，卡在一个荒谬的T型姿势中，或者无缘无故地在狭窄的走廊中来回走动。人们在我眼前逐步淘汰存在。俱乐部拥挤的桌子上将有一种饮料悬停在一个看不见的顾客手中。＆＃34;

In addition to three hotfixes already released, Iwinski reiterated a promise for a major update dropping "within 10 days" and another significant update "in the following weeks." After that, the development team will focus on previously promised free DLC and free updates for PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series S/X-native versions of the game, now expected in the second half of 2021.

除了已经发布的三个修补程序外，Iwinski重申了对重大更新的承诺，即在10天内删除该更新。并在接下来的几周内进行了另一项重大更新。之后，开发团队将专注于先前承诺的免费PlayStation 5和Xbox Series S / X-native版本的免费DLC和免费更新，现在预计将在2021年下半年发布。

Iwinski's latest message follows a December conference call in which he admitted the CDPR leadership team "ignored the signals about the need for additional time to refine the game on the base last-gen consoles." Since that call, the game has been delisted from both the PlayStation and Xbox online stores, and CD Projekt Red has been faced with a lawsuit from unhappy shareholders.

伊温斯基（Iwinski）的最新消息是在12月的一次电话会议上发表的，在那次电话会议中，他承认CDPR领导团队忽略了需要额外时间在基本的上一代游戏机上改进游戏的信号。 自该电话以来，该游戏已从PlayStation和Xbox在线商店中除名，而CD Projekt Red则面临着股东不满的诉讼。
