New model F, ultracompact F77 keyboard impressions
新型F，超紧凑F77键盘印象

2020-12-07 03:18:12

I was in a need of a new keyboard, and spending at least a third (often much more) of every day typing, I decided to get the keyboard with the best typing experience. I read and watched plenty of keyboard reviews and switch descriptions out there, and determined the Ultracompact F77 keyboard from modelfkeyboards.com should have the best typing experience. So I ordered one. It arrived in perfect condition and worked perfectly well from the start, even though I was given warnings that some tweaks might be necessary at first. Below, I will try to avoid talking about the technical aspects of the keyboard, and will focus on the user experience.

我需要一个新的键盘，并且每天花至少三分之一（通常是更多的钱）来打字，所以我决定让键盘具有最佳的打字体验。我阅读并观看了许多键盘评论和开关说明，并确定来自modelfkeyboards.com的Ultracompact F77键盘应该具有最佳的打字体验。所以我点了一个。尽管我收到警告，一开始可能需要进行一些调整，但它的状态良好，并且从一开始就运行良好。下面，我将尽量避免谈论键盘的技术方面，而是着重于用户体验。

It greatly surpassed my expectations. I haven't used an original model F before, and I barely remember the key feel of model Ms. Prior to this, I was very satisfied with Cherry MX blue switches. I couldn't understand people calling them bad, mushy etc. and stating how they can't even compare to buckling springs. I expected buckling springs to be better, I just didn't believe the difference would be significant, considering how good I found cherry mx blue. Now I get it, and I'll try to make a comparison so others can understand too. Even though actuation force of the model F switch is slightly higher, they feel lighter to press, and the actuation feedback is cleaner. For buckling springs, the force required to press the key increases steadily until it reaches the actuation point, when it drops significantly at once, and after the drop it's much lower for a while, steadily increasing until the end of the key travel. While on the cherry mx blue, the force increases linearly, then it sharply increases more at the actuation point and drops back to the previous level and continues to increase. So while the cherry mx blue tactile feedback feels like hitting and breaking a barrier, the buckling springs tactile feedback feels like a sudden relief. Even though the actuation force on the model F is higher, the total energy required to press the key is much lower. Every key press feels like a bigger accomplishment done with less effort. So typing in general feels like an effortless and more satisfying activity. Since the key travel before the actuation point is higher on the buckling springs, I expected to feel a long key travel before it actuates. But because keys are pressed fast during typing, the only things I feel are instant, sharp and satisfying "snap snap snap"

它大大超出了我的期望。我以前从未使用过原始的F型，我几乎不记得M型女士的按键感觉。在此之前，我对Cherry MX蓝色开关非常满意。我无法理解人们称他们为劣质，糊状等的说法，甚至无法与屈曲的弹簧相提并论。考虑到我发现樱桃Mx蓝色有多好，我希望屈曲弹簧会更好，但我只是不相信这种差异会很大。现在我明白了，我将尝试进行比较，以便其他人也能理解。即使F型开关的致动力稍高，它们的按动感觉也更轻，并且致动反馈更干净。对于翘曲弹簧，按动琴键所需的力会稳定增加，直到到达驱动点为止，当它立即显着下降时，在下降之后，它会暂时降低一小段时间，并稳步增大直到琴键的末端旅行。在樱桃mx蓝色上，力线性增加，然后在驱动点急剧增加，然后又下降到以前的水平并继续增加。因此，当樱桃mx蓝色触觉反馈感觉像是撞破障碍物时，屈曲弹簧触觉反馈感觉像是突然的缓解。即使模型F上的致动力更高，按下按键所需的总能量也要低得多。每次按键都感觉是事半功倍的成就。因此，总体而言，打字感觉像是轻松而又令人满意的活动。由于屈曲弹簧上的促动点之前的键行程较高，因此我预计在促动之前会有较长的键行程。但是，因为在键入过程中快速按下了按键，所以我感觉到的只是瞬间，清晰而又令人满意的快照。

I have a strong opinion that all designs should be as simple as possible as long as they don't sacrifice functionality. Every feature of a design, every decoration, is only good if it serves a practical purpose. Anything else is trend that will come and go, just like cars with fins. With that in mind, this is one of the best designs for a keyboard I have seen.

我坚决认为，只要不牺牲功能性，所有设计都应尽可能简单。设计的每个功能和装饰都只有在达到实际目的时才是好的。就像有鳍的汽车一样，其他任何趋势都是会流行的。考虑到这一点，这是我所见过的最好的键盘设计之一。

Aside from the simple and sturdy design of capacitive buckling springs, every other detail of the keyboard is built to last and withstand as much damage as possible. Full metal casing, thick metal boards inside, hard plastic keycaps and barrels, etc. Every part is replaceable, and it arrived with a small plastic bag with spare parts. Its only vulnerability is water, but other than that, it's nearly indestructible. With all the metal parts, it weights ~1.8 kg. And this is the ultra compact version, made as small and light as possible without sacrificing quality. The paint work is done amazingly well, as if black is the natural color of the metal used. It would be really hard to make scratches on it. But even if you somehow manage to make scratches through the years, this is the kind of product where they would only add to its character. With the small size of the project, I expected to see some imperfections in the final product (after all, I was in it for the typing experience only), but there are none. This is a perfectly executed product and thing of beauty.

除了电容式屈曲弹簧的简单而坚固的设计外，键盘的其他所有部件均经久耐用，并能承受尽可能多的损坏。全金属外壳，内部较厚的金属板，坚硬的塑料键帽和枪管等。每个零件都是可更换的，它带有一个带备用零件的小塑料袋。它唯一的脆弱性是水，但除此之外，它几乎坚不可摧。使用所有金属零件，重量约为1.8千克。这是超紧凑的版本，在不牺牲质量的情况下尽可能减小体积和重量。油漆工作非常出色，就好像黑色是所用金属的自然色一样。真的很难在上面划痕。但是，即使您多年来设法以某种方式进行划痕，这种产品也只会增加其性能。由于项目规模较小，我希望最终产品会出现一些瑕疵（毕竟，我只是出于打字经验而参与其中），但是没有瑕疵。这是完美执行的产品和美丽的事物。
