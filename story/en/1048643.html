Metallica's BlizzCon Performance Ruined by Twitch
Twitch破坏了Metallica的BlizzCon性能

2021-02-21 07:43:46

BlizzCon veterans Metallica returned for this year's only-only BlizzConline festivities, but the thrash metal band's performance was ruined by a most unlikely culprit: Twitch. Right in the middle of one of its songs, the audio was replaced by some easy listening bell music, presumably to avoid having Blizzard banned for a false DMCA notice.

BlizzCon退伍军人Metallica参加了今年唯一的BlizzConline庆祝活动，但the打金属乐队的表演被最不可能的罪魁祸首Twitch破坏了。就在其中一首歌曲的中间，音频被一些易于听的铃铛音乐所取代，以免暴雪禁止虚假的DMCA通知。

The clip, which esports expert Rod Breslau shared on Twitter, shows the band rocking out, with lead guitarist Kirk Hammett shredding, completely unaware that people were hearing what sounded like the main menu theme for a Teletubbies game. We didn't have to hear that tinny snare drum, however, which is a bonus.

电子竞技专家Rod Breslau在Twitter上分享的剪辑显示，乐队摇摇欲坠，首席吉他手Kirk Hammett撕碎，完全没有意识到人们听到的声音听起来像是Teletubbies游戏的主菜单主题。但是，我们不必听到那细小的军鼓。

Several commenters online have pointed out the irony of this happening to Metallica. The band, particularly drummer Lars Ulrich, has been heavily critical of online music sharing, and the band had a high-profile case with Napster back in 2000.

网上有几位评论者指出了Metallica的讽刺意味。乐队，特别是鼓手拉斯·乌尔里希（Lars Ulrich）对在线音乐共享提出了严厉的批评，乐队在2000年与纳普斯特（Napster）一起备受瞩目。

Still, we have to imagine the band is "madly in anger" with Twitch over the fiasco. The performance was one of the only semi-normal BlizzCon elements that could continue during the pandemic, as Blizzard has hosted big-name musicians for its celebrations going back to The Offspring in 2005. No one could "come out and play" this year, so the virtual show was all fans could get. BlizzCon continued on February 20 with additional panels.

At the very least, this year's BlizzCon event was free for everyone, rather than the $50 Virtual Ticket price Blizzard usually charges. During the opening ceremony, Blizzard unveiled a remastered Diablo 2, the rogue class in Diablo 4, and World of Warcraft: Burning Crusade Classic. We also learned more about abilities, modes, and updated character designs in Overwatch 2.

至少，今年的BlizzCon活动对所有人都是免费的，而不是Blizzard通常收取的$ 50虚拟门票价格。在开幕式上，暴雪发布了重新制作的暗黑破坏神2（暗黑破坏神4中的流氓类）和《魔兽世界：燃烧的远征经典》。我们还通过《守望先锋2》了解了有关能力，模式和更新角色设计的更多信息。
