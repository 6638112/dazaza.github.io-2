Sources: Salesforce has held talks about acquiring Slack, although a deal is not guaranteed; Slack stock jumps 20%+ while Salesforce falls ~4%
资料来源：Salesforce已就收购Slack进行过谈判，尽管并不能保证达成交易。疲弱的股票上涨20％以上，而Salesforce下跌〜4％

2020-11-26 02:22:10

Salesforce.com CRM -2.93% has recently been in talks to buy Slack Technologies WORK 23.50% according to people familiar with the matter, a deal that would mark a big move by the corporate-software maker into office communications.

据知情人士称，Salesforce.com CRM -2.93％最近正在商讨收购Slack Technologies WORK 23.50％，这笔交易标志着公司软件制造商向办公通信领域迈出了一大步。

Any deal would likely value Slack at more than its current market capitalization of $17 billion and represent Salesforce’s largest acquisition ever. There is no guarantee the talks will lead to a deal and Salesforce could ultimately settle on another target, some of the people said.

Slack的任何交易都可能使Slack的市值超过其目前的170亿美元，并且是Salesforce有史以来最大的一笔收购。某些人说，不能保证谈判将达成协议，Salesforce最终可能会达成另一个目标。
