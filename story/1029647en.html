你可能用错了Mastodon

2020-10-19 02:35:23

I will try to convince you that the word alternative doesn't mean here what you think it means,and why you may be using Mastodon wrong if you find it boring.

我会试着说服你，“另类”这个词在这里的意思并不是你所想的那样，如果你觉得它很无聊，那么为什么你可能会用错Mastodon呢？

Forget about the privacy angle for now. Mastodon is an alternative community, where people behavedifferently.

现在先别管隐私问题了。乳齿象是一个另类群落，在那里人们的行为方式不同。

There may be some people for whom Mastodon is a safe haven. Yes, some users really do migratethere to avoid censorship or bullying but, for most of us, that will not be the case.

对某些人来说，乳齿象是一个安全的避风港。是的，一些用户确实迁移到那里是为了避免审查或欺凌，但对我们大多数人来说，情况并非如此。

Linux is libre software. But that's not why most people use it. Linux users mostly want to get their work done, and Linux is an excellent platform.There is no Microsoft Word, no Adobe Photoshop, no Starcraft. If you need to use these tools, honestly,you'd better stick with Windows. You can use emulation, in the same way that there areutilities to post to Twitter from Mastodon, but that would miss the point.

Linux是libre软件。但这并不是大多数人使用它的原因。Linux用户大多想把工作做完，Linux是一个很好的平台，没有Microsoft Word，没有Adobe Photoshop，没有星际争霸。如果你需要使用这些工具，老实说，你最好还是使用Windows。您可以使用仿真，就像有从Mastodon发布到Twitter的实用程序一样，但这没有抓住重点。

The bottom line is, you can perform the same tasks, but the process will be different.You can post toots on Mastodon, upload gifs, send DMs... but it's not Twitter, and that is fine.

底线是，你可以执行相同的任务，但过程会有所不同。你可以在Mastodon上发布Toot，上传gif，发送DM...。但它不是推特，这没问题。

The problem most people have with Mastodon is that they "get bored" with it quickly. I've seen it a lot, andit means one thing: the person created their account on the wrong server.

大多数人对乳齿象有一个问题，那就是他们很快就会对它感到厌烦。我看过很多次了，这意味着一件事：这个人在错误的服务器上创建了他们的帐户。

"But," they say, "isn't Mastodon federated? Can't I chat with everybody, regardless of their server?"Yes, of course. But discoverability works differently on Mastodon.

但是，他们说，乳齿象不是联盟的吗？我不能和每个人聊天吗，不管他们的服务器是什么？当然可以。但可发现性对乳齿象的作用不同。

Twitter has only two discoverability layers: your network and the whole world. Either a small group ofcontacts, or everybody in the whole world. That's crazy.

Twitter只有两个可发现层：你的网络和整个世界。要么是一小群联系人，要么是全世界的每个人。这太疯狂了。

They try very hard to show you tweets from outside your network so you can discover new people.And, at the same time, they show your tweets to third parties, so you can get new followers.This is the way thatthey try to keep you engaged once your network is more or less stable and starts getting stale.

他们非常努力地向你显示来自网络外部的推文，这样你就可以发现新人。同时，他们会将你的推文显示给第三方，这样你就可以获得新的追随者。这是在你的网络或多或少稳定并开始变得陈旧时，他们试图让你参与进来的方式。

Mastodon, instead, has an extra layer between your network and the whole world:messages from people on your server. This is called the local timeline.

相反，乳齿象在你的网络和整个世界之间有一个额外的层：来自你服务器上的人的消息。这称为本地时间线。

If you're of a certain age you may remember BBSs, Usenet, the IRC, or early internet forums.Do you recall how exciting it was to log into the unknown and realize that there were peopleall around the world who shared your interests?

如果你到了一定的年龄，你可能还记得BBS、Usenet、IRC或早期的互联网论坛。你还记得登录未知世界并意识到世界各地都有和你有共同兴趣的人是多么令人兴奋吗？

It was an amazing feeling which got lost on the modern internet. Now you have a chance to relive it.

这是一种在现代互联网上迷失的奇妙感觉。现在你有机会重温一下了。

The local timeline dynamics are very different. There is a lot of respectful interactions among total strangers,because there is this feeling of community, of being in a neighborhood. Twitter is just the opposite, strangersshouting at each other.

当地的时间线动态是非常不同的。在完全陌生的人之间有很多相互尊重的互动，因为有一种社区的感觉，就像在一个社区里一样。推特正好相反，陌生人互相大喊大叫。

Furthermore, since the local timeline is more or less limited in the amount of users, you have the chanceto recognize usernames, and being recognized. You start interacting with strangers, mentioning them, sending themlinks they may like. You discover new websites, rabbit holes, new approaches to your hobbies.

此外，由于本地时间线或多或少地限制了用户数量，因此您有机会识别用户名，并被识别。你开始与陌生人互动，提到他们，给他们发送他们可能喜欢的链接。你会发现新的网站、兔子洞、新的业余爱好方法。

I've made quite a few new internet friends on my Mastodon server, and I don't mean followers or contacts.I'm talking about human beings who I have never met in person but feel close to.

我在我的Mastodon服务器上结识了相当多的新网友，我不是指粉丝或联系人。我说的是那些我从未见过面，但感觉很亲近的人。

People are humble and respectful. And, for less nice users, admins enforce codes of conduct and, on extreme cases, users may get kicked off a server. But they are not being banned by a faceless corporationdue to mass reports, everybody is given a chance.

人们是谦逊的，有敬意的。而且，对于不太友好的用户，管理员会强制执行行为准则，在极端情况下，用户可能会被踢出服务器。但他们并没有因为大量报道而被一个不知名的公司禁止，每个人都有机会。

The problem with "generalist" Mastodon servers like mastodon.socialis that users have just too diverse interests and backgrounds.Therefore, there is no community feeling. For some people, that may be exactly what they're looking for. But, for most of us, there is more value on the smaller servers.

像mastodon.Socialis这样的多面手Mastodon服务器的问题是，用户的兴趣和背景太不同了，因此没有社区感。对于一些人来说，这可能正是他们正在寻找的。但是，对于我们大多数人来说，小型服务器的价值更高。

So, how can you choose the right server? Fortunately, you can do a bit of research. There is an official directory of Mastodon servers categorized byinterests and regions.

那么，您如何才能选择正确的服务器呢？幸运的是，你可以做一点研究。有一个按兴趣和地区分类的Mastodon服务器的官方目录。

There are many more. Simply search online for "mastodon server MY_FAVORITE_HOBBY." And believe me, serversbetween 500 and 5,000 people are the best.

还有更多。只需在网上搜索乳齿象服务器My_Favorest_Hobby。相信我，500到5000人的服务器是最好的。

Before clicking on "sign up", always browse the local timeline,the about page, and the most active users list. You will get a pretty good idea of the kind of peoplewho chat there.Once you feel right at home you can continue your adventure and start following users from other servers.

在单击注册之前，请始终浏览当地时间表、关于页面和最活跃用户列表。你会对在那里聊天的人有一个很好的了解，一旦你感到宾至如归，你就可以继续你的冒险，开始关注来自其他服务器的用户。

Mastodon has an option to only display toots in specific languages. It can be very useful to avoid beingflooded by toots that you just have no chance of understanding or even getting what they're about.

乳齿象可以选择只显示特定语言的嘟嘟声。避免被孩子淹没是非常有用的，因为你根本没有机会理解甚至理解他们在说什么。

You can also filter your notifications by types: replies, mentions, favorites, reposts, and more.This makes catching up much more manageable than on Twitter.

您还可以按类型过滤通知：回复、提及、收藏、转发等。这使得追赶比在Twitter上更易于管理。

Finally, Mastodon has a built-in "Content Warning" feature. It allows you to hidetext behind a short explanation, in case you want to talk about sensible topics or just about spoilinga recent movie.

最后，Mastodon有一个内置的内容警告功能。它允许你在简短的解释后面隐藏文字，以防你想谈论明智的话题或只是想毁了最近的一部电影。
