英国在线超市Ocado表示，它以2.62亿美元现金收购了两家机器人公司：Kindred Systems以2.62亿美元现金收购，Haddington Dynamics以2500万美元现金加股票收购

2020-11-02 22:48:11

Online supermarket Ocado has acquired San Francisco-headquartered robotics firm Kindred Systems for $262 million and Las Vegas firm Haddington Dynamics for $25 million.

在线超市Ocado以2.62亿美元收购了总部位于旧金山的机器人公司Kindred Systems，以2500万美元收购了拉斯维加斯公司Haddington Dynamics。

Ocado Chief Executive Tim Steiner said the acquisitions will allow the company to "accelerate delivery, innovate more and grow faster."

Ocado首席执行官蒂姆·施泰纳(Tim Steiner)表示，这些收购将使公司加快交付，创新更多，增长更快。

LONDON — Ocado, a British online supermarket, announced Monday that it has acquired two robotics companies in the U.S. as it seeks to make its warehouses more efficient.

伦敦-英国在线超市Ocado周一宣布，它已经在美国收购了两家机器人公司，以求提高其仓库的效率。

The company, which also raised its full-year profit forecast, said it has agreed to buy San Francisco-headquartered robotics firm Kindred Systems for $262 million in cash and Las Vegas firm Haddington Dynamics for $25 million in cash and shares.

该公司还上调了全年利润预期，表示已同意以2.62亿美元现金收购总部位于旧金山的机器人公司Kindred Systems，以2500万美元现金加股票收购拉斯维加斯公司Haddington Dynamics。

"In our mission to solve one of the world's hardest challenges in robotics, the picking and packing of groceries, we're acquiring two leading robotics companies," Ocado wrote in an announcement.

Ocado在一份声明中写道，我们的使命是解决世界上最严峻的机器人挑战之一，即食品杂货的拣选和包装，我们再次收购了两家领先的机器人公司。

Kindred Systems, which has around 45 engineers and 90 staff in total, builds robots that are used to pick and pack online orders, while Haddington Dynamics creates robotic arms.

Kindred Systems总共拥有约45名工程师和90名员工，该公司制造用于挑选和打包在线订单的机器人，而哈丁顿动力公司(Haddington Dynamics)则制造机械臂。

Ocado Chief Executive Tim Steiner said the acquisitions will allow the company to "accelerate delivery, innovate more and grow faster."

Ocado首席执行官蒂姆·施泰纳(Tim Steiner)表示，这些收购将使公司加快交付，创新更多，增长更快。

The purchases will have a small hit on earnings but they'll also increase revenues in the next financial year by around £30 million ($38.7 million), Ocado said.

Ocado表示，这些收购将对收益造成小幅影响，但它们还将使下一财年的收入增加约3000万GB(3870万美元)。

While many businesses have been hurt by the pandemic, Ocado's business has benefited as swathes of people turn to online shopping.

虽然许多企业都受到了疫情的影响，但随着大量人转向网购，Ocado的业务也从中受益。

In an update on trading, Ocado said its joint venture with British retail chain Marks & Spencer remained strong through its fiscal fourth quarter.

Ocado在一份最新的交易报告中表示，其与英国零售连锁店玛莎百货(Marks&；Spencer)的合资企业在整个第四财季都保持强劲。

The retailer said that it now expects earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortization to be more than £60 million in the year leading up to the end of November, up from previous guidance of more than £40 million.

该零售商表示，目前预计截至11月底的一年，息税折旧及摊销前利润(EBITDA)将超过6000万GB，高于此前超过4000万GB的指引。

Shares of Ocado, which is valued at around £18 billion, were up over 9% on Monday.

Ocado的估值约为180亿英镑，其股价周一上涨超过9%。
