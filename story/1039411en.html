The Practice of Change – How I survived being interested in everything
变革实践–对一切感兴趣的生存方式

2020-12-13 15:53:17

A post-Internet framework for understanding and intervening in complex adaptive systems. Drawing on systems dynamics, evolutionary dynamics and theory of change based on causal networks, I describe a way to understand and suggest ways to intervene in complex systems. I argue that an anti-disciplinary approach and paradigm shifts are required to achieve the outcomes we desire.

后互联网框架，用于理解和干预复杂的自适应系统。基于因果网络，利用系统动力学，进化动力学和变化理论，我描述了一种理解和建议干预复杂系统的方法。我认为，需要反学科的方法和范式转变才能实现我们期望的结果。

Learnings from the creation and management of post-Internet organizations that can be applied to designing and deploying interventions. I propose an architecture of layers of interoperability to unbundle complex, inflexible, and monolithic systems and increase competition, cooperation, generativity, and flexibility. I argue that the Internet is the best example of this architecture and that the Internet has provided an opportunity to deploy this architecture in other domains. I demonstrate how the Internet has has made the world more complex but through lowering the cost of communication and collaboration has enabled new forms of organization and production. This has changed the nature of our interventions.

从互联网后组织的创建和管理中学到的经验教训可用于设计和部署干预措施。我提出了一种互操作性层的体系结构，以解开复杂，僵化和单一的系统，并增加了竞争，合作，生成性和灵活性。我认为Internet是此体系结构的最佳示例，并且Internet提供了在其他域中部署此体系结构的机会。我演示了互联网如何使世界变得更加复杂，但是通过降低通信和协作成本，可以实现新的组织和生产形式。这改变了我们干预的性质。

How and why we must change the values of society from one based on the measurement of financial value to flourishing and robustness. The paradigm determines what we measure and generates the values and the goals of a system. Measuring value financially has created a competitive market-based system that has provided many societal benefits but has produced complex problems not solvable through competitive market-based solutions. In order to address these challenges, we must shift the paradigm across our systems to focus on a more complex measure of flourishing and robustness. In order to transcend  our current economic paradigm, the transformation will require a movement that includes arts and culture to transform strongly held beliefs. I propose a framework of values based on the pursuit of flourishing and a method for transforming ourselves.

我们如何以及为什么必须将社会的价值从基于对金融价值的衡量的一种转变为繁荣和健壮。该范式确定我们要衡量的内容，并生成系统的价值和目标。从财务上衡量价值创造了一种竞争性的基于市场的系统，该系统提供了许多社会效益，但产生了无法通过竞争性的基于市场的解决方案解决的复杂问题。为了应对这些挑战，我们必须在我们的系统中转移范式，以专注于衡量繁荣和健壮性的更复杂方法。为了超越我们目前的经济范式，这种转变将需要一场包括艺术和文化的运动来转变坚定的信念。我提出了一个基于追求繁荣的价值观框架和一种自我转变的方法。
