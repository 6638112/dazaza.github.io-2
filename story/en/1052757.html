CurseForge Core, based on technology that Overwolf acquired in June 2020, provides a software development kit (SDK) for game developers that integrates into the game itself and functions as a gateway for users to discover and install mods in-game. It works across all platforms and storefronts, and enables publishers to easily integrate mods safely into existing or new games.

“We think gamer creators are going to be profound contributors to the ecosystem of triple-A games,” said Overwolf CEO Uri Marchand, in an interview with GamesBeat. “As we move towards the metaverse, and the more complex game experiences, we see them as built with the creator tools and communities to allow for sort of an open source system for the gamers that are passionate about that world.”

“我们认为游戏玩家创作者将成为三重游戏生态系统的贡献者，”Overwolf Ceo Uri Marchand说，在一次游戏博格采访时说。 “随着我们走向偏见，以及更复杂的游戏体验，我们将它们视为与创建者工具和社区建造，以便为对该世界充满热情的游戏玩家进行一种开源系统。”

Griffin Gaming Partners and Insight Partners jointly led the round, with participation from investors including Ubisoft, Warner Music Group, Meg Whitman, and Gen.G Co-Founder, Kevin Chou. Existing investors Marker, Intel Capital and Liberty Media also participated.

格里芬博彩合作伙伴和洞察力合作伙伴共同领导，参加投资者，包括Ubisoft，华纳音乐集团，Meg Whitman和Gen.G联合创始人Kevin Chou。现有的投资者标记，英特尔资本和自由媒体也参加了。

“It was a therapeutic experience for me doing this round because previously I really struggled raising money,” Marchand said. “The narrative was difficult for me to explain about how big this would be. I didn’t have any proof. But today it is very favorable.”

“这对我来说，这是一个治疗经验，因为之前我真的挣扎着筹集资金，”马克兰说。 “叙述很难解释这是多大的。我没有任何证据。但今天它非常有利。“

Marchand believes user-generated content is at an inflection point, poised to grow dramatically as in-game creators thrive by making their own modifications to games. Overwolf believes in a future in which the community can participate in the creation and enhancement of triple-A games. This benefits game developers, as game development costs continue to rise. By adding community firepower, the developers can get reinforcements.

Marchand相信用户生成的内容处于拐点，因为通过对游戏的修改而茁壮成长，茁壮成长。奥普尔斯通相信社区可以参与创造和增强三重游戏的未来。这是游戏开发人员，因为游戏开发成本继续上升。通过添加社区Firepower，开发人员可以获得增强。

The funding will be used to boost development dedicated to creating services for creators. The money will also enable Overwolf itself to invest in more projects built by in-game creators.

资金将用于提高致力于为创造者创造服务的发展。这笔钱也将使渗透本身能够投资于游戏内创作者建造的更多项目。

“The key reason is for the round is to be able to scale this operation, and to do more meaningful investments with mod authors and app creators, and kind of help them turn their passion into a living,” Marchand said. “Apart from that, it’s going to help us scale our marketing efforts.”

“关键原因是四轮的，是能够扩展这项操作，并为Mod作者和应用程序创造者做更有意义的投资，以及他们对谋生的帮助，”Marchand说。 “除此之外，它将帮助我们扩展我们的营销努力。”

When Overwolf started in 2011, it did not have an open framework. It offered features like communications inside a game, Facebook integration, capture capabilities, and other things. Overwolf created everything itself, with an overlay that allowed players to access services while they were playing games.

当奥克斯卫星在2011年开始时，它没有开放的框架。它提供了游戏内的通信等功能，Facebook集成，捕获功能和其他事物。奥普敦创造了一切本身，覆盖着允许玩家在玩游戏时访问服务。

“It didn’t work out so well,” Marchand said. “Fast forward a couple of years, and nothing was scaling. So we had to ask ourselves hard questions about what we would do next. Then we realized that user-generated content around games has a place in the world but we cannot build everything ourselves. And so we pivoted to build an open framework instead of building everything ourselves.”

“它没有很好地锻炼身体，”马克兰说。 “快进了几年，没有什么是缩放。所以我们不得不问自己难题我们接下来会做什么。然后，我们意识到游戏周围的用户生成的内容在世界上有一个地方，但我们无法建立自己的一切。因此，我们枢转来建立一个开放的框架而不是建立自己的一切。“

By 2015, Overwolf’s platform drew the first creator apps, with an app for League of Legends. That snowballed. A funding round from Intel helped Overwolf invest in the community, and by 2019 the creators hit a critical mass. Then the company picked up big followings with Minecraft and other games with the Curse deal in 2020.

到2015年，Overwolf的平台吸引了第一个创造者应用程序，其中包含联盟的应用程序。那雪球。来自英特尔的资金一轮帮助奥克斯州投资于社区，到2019年，创作者袭击了临界质量。然后公司在2020年与诅咒交易拾取了与Minecraft和其他游戏的大次。

“We weren’t sure where the world was going to go, as it was after COVID hit,” Marchand said. “There was a lot of anxiety, in the markets and with our company. But it felt like a strategic move and it helped us  grow.”

“我们不确定世界要去哪里，因为它在Covid打击之后，”Marchand说。 “市场和我们公司在市场上有很多焦虑。但它觉得自己的战略举动，它帮助我们成长。“

In 2020, Overwolf acquired CurseForge from Twitch. CurseForge had the biggest repository of mods for video games including top titles like World of Warcraft and Minecraft. Now the platform has 30,000 creators, 90,000 mods and add-ons, and 18 million monthly users. Overwolf has experienced 300% growth in the last year as the market for content around games rises.

在2020年，奥洪尔夫从抽搐中获取了CURSEFFORGE。 CuleSforge拥有视频游戏的最大存储库，包括魔兽世界和MINECRAFT世界的顶级标题。现在该平台拥有30,000名创作者，90,000名Mods和附加组，每月1800万。奥普尔卫星在比赛中崛起的环境中的市场升级时，去年经历了300％的增长。

With CurseForge Core, Overwolf manages content moderation, UGC-related live ops, and creator payment. That lets game developers  access community-created content easily without incurring additional costs. CurseForge Core enables cross-platform modding, an analytics dashboard, safe and secure moderation, creator relations and payment, and events like contests and hackathons.

凭借Cuseforge Core，Overwolf管理内容审核，UGC相关的直播OPS和创造者付款。这让游戏开发人员轻松访问社区创建的内容，而不会产生额外的成本。 CURSEFORGE CORE支持跨平台修改，分析仪表板，安全和安全的审核，创作者关系和付款，以及比赛和哈克萨斯等事件。

“This is all about existing intellectual property,” said Nick Tuosto, cofounder of Griffin Gaming Partners and managing director at LionTree, said in an interview with GamesBeat. “You can see the content created on Overwolf is great for the ecosystem because it deepens the community engagement and ultimately makes the gaming IP more valuable. This model takes some of the burden off of the developer and publisher to come up with extensions of the core product by leveraging developer community’s creative capacities to enhance the user experience.”

“这都是关于现有知识产权的全部，”李佛州格里芬博彩合作伙伴的Cofounder和Liontree董事总经理的尼克布努托说，在一次采访博马特采访时说。 “你可以看到奥普尔卫星上创建的内容非常适合生态系统，因为它加深了社区参与，最终使游戏IP更有价值。该模型采用开发人员和出版商的一些负担，通过利用开发人员社区的创新能力来提高用户体验，提出核心产品的扩展。“

Oscar Navarro, head of corporate development for Ubisoft, said in a statement that Overwolf is developing the holy grail of frameworks for UGC for both publishers and in-game creators, and that’s a game-changer for intellectual property holders. Such IP holders often have trouble with user-generated content when it comes to piracy or copycats. But Overwolf has a curated marketplace.

奥斯卡·纳沃罗（Ubisoft）首都奥斯卡·纳瓦罗表示，奥巴斯福特的一份声明在一份声明中表示，渗透卫星为出版商和游戏内创作者开发了UGC框架的圣杯，这是知识产权持有人的游戏变更者。此类IP持有者通常在涉及到盗版或模仿时的用户生成的内容。但渗透的市场有一个策划的市场。

As for the amount of money raised, Tuosto said, “The capital markets are very supportive of businesses that have explosive growth and have platform positions in an ecosystem like gaming. Platforms have accumulated enormous value over time in this space. And this is very rare that you see both sides of the marketplace exploding — both the number of developers and applications as well users on the platform.”

对于所提出的金额，Tuosto表示，“资本市场非常支持具有爆炸性增长的企业，并在游戏中具有平台职位。平台在这个空间中随着时间的推移积累了巨大的价值。这是非常罕见的，你看到市场的两侧爆炸 - 在平台上的用户也是开发人员和应用程序的数量。“

The company has 67 employees and Marchand hopes to grow that to 120 by next year.
