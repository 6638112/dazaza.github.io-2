Bitcoin hits $1T in market value
比特币市值达到1T

2021-02-20 03:34:57

The digital currency was trading at just under $54,000 per coin as it hit the new level.

当数字货币达到新水平时，其交易价格接近每枚硬币54,000美元。

The price of bitcoin crossed another major milestone Friday, as the cryptocurrency's market value surpassed $1 trillion, according to Coindesk.

根据Coindesk的数据，由于加密货币的市场价值超过1万亿美元，比特币的价格在周五突破了另一个主要里程碑。

The digital currency was trading at just under $54,000 per coin Friday as it hit the new level, and rose to a high of $54,880 later in the session, according to Coin Metrics. The price of bitcoin has now gained about 350% over the past six months. Before the recent surge, the digital asset has never traded above $20,000.

根据Coin Metrics的数据，上周五，该数字货币的交易价格接近每枚硬币54,000美元，创下新高，并在盘中晚些时候升至54,880美元的高位。在过去的六个月中，比特币的价格现在已经上涨了约350％。在最近的飙升之前，数字资产的交易价格从未超过20,000美元。

The move has been fueled in part by increased adoption of bitcoin by major investors and companies. The oldest bank in the United States, the Bank of New York Mellon, announced earlier this month that it was moving into the space. Elon Musk's Tesla converted some of its balance sheet cash into bitcoin earlier this year and said it would start accepting the digital tokens as payment.

主要投资者和公司增加了对比特币的采用，在一定程度上推动了这一举措。美国最古老的银行纽约梅隆银行本月初宣布将进军这一领域。埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）的特斯拉（Telon）今年早些时候将其资产负债表中的部分现金转换为比特币，并表示将开始接受数字代币作为付款。

Bitcoin "has started to get so big that it arguably creates its own demand as companies and institutions begin to make forays into a field they wouldn't have touched a few months previously," Deutsche Bank research strategist Jim Reid said in a note. "Ironically it is turning itself into a credible asset class to many by rallying so much of late and also by getting increasing institutional buy-in."

比特币已经开始变得如此庞大，以至于可以说它会产生自己的需求，因为公司和机构开始涉足几个月前没有接触过的领域，＆＃34;德意志银行研究策略师吉姆·里德在报告中称。具有讽刺意味的是，它通过聚集这么多后期投资者并通过增加机构支持来将自己变成对许多人来说都是可靠的资产类别。＆＃34;

Market value is calculated by multiplying the price of bitcoin by the number created. While not a perfect comparison, the $1 trillion market value would make bitcoin's value greater than all but a handful of the world's stocks. Tesla, for instance, has a market cap of around $700 billion, while Apple is valued at more than $2 trillion.

市场价值是通过将比特币的价格乘以创建的数字来计算的。虽然不是一个完美的比较，但1万亿美元的市值将使比特币的价值大于除少数几只全球其他股票以外的所有比特币。例如，特斯拉的市值约为7000亿美元，而苹果的市值超过2万亿美元。

"From white paper to $1 Trillion. #Bitcoin is eating gold alive," Gemini's Cameron Winklevoss said on Twitter.

＆＃34;从白皮书到1万亿美元。 #Bitcoin正在吞食黄金，＆＃34; Gemini的Cameron Winklevoss在Twitter上说。

To be sure, not everyone on Wall Street has been convinced of bitcoin's future prospects. Citadel Securities founder Ken Griffin said Friday that he was not interested in cryptocurrency, while researchers at JPMorgan have said bitcoin's rally is unsustainable.

可以肯定的是，并不是每个华尔街人士都对比特币的未来前景深信不疑。 Citadel Securities创始人肯·格里芬（Ken Griffin）周五表示，他对加密货币不感兴趣，而摩根大通（JPMorgan）的研究人员则表示，比特币的反弹是不可持续的。
