Don’t look now, but GameStop’s stock price is surging again
现在不要看，但GameStop的股价又在飙升

2021-02-26 00:07:41

GameStop's stock price saw yet another sudden surge in late trading hours on Wednesday. After opening at $44.70 that morning, the price shot up from $52.41 at 3pm to $91.71 just before the market closed at 4pm.

GameStop的股价在周三尾盘交易时段又出现了突然的上涨。当天早上开盘价为44.70美元后，价格从下午3点的52.41美元飙升至下午4点收盘之前的91.71美元。

That 75 percent increase in a single hour was followed shortly after by a peak price of nearly $200 in post-market trading Wednesday evening. As of this writing, the stock is currently selling at about $132 in highly volatile early market trading.

在一个小时内上涨了75％之后，不久之后，周三晚上的盘后交易价格达到了近200美元的峰值。在撰写本文时，该股票目前在高度波动的早期市场交易中的售价约为132美元。

The sudden surge obviously brings to mind GameStop's similarly quick stock price run-up in late January. Since peaking at over $400 during that extremely volatile week, though, GameStop's stock price had settled to something resembling calm, closing between $40 and $60 every day since February 8. The recent jump came approximately 24 hours after GameStop announced the sudden resignation of CFO Jim Bell, effective next month. Business Insider's sources suggest Bell was forced out after the board lost faith in his ability to turn the troubled retailer around, and GameStop's announcement of that move mentioned the company is looking for a replacement "to help accelerate GameStop’s transformation."

突如其来的激增显然使我想到了GameStop在1月下旬股价同样迅速上涨。不过，自从在这个极为动荡的一周中超过400美元的峰值以来，GameStop的股价一直稳定在类似的水平，自2月8日以来每天收盘于40至60美元之间。最近一次上涨是在GameStop宣布突然首席财务官吉姆·贝尔辞职，下个月生效。据《商业内幕》消息人士称，贝尔在董事会对扭转困境的零售商的能力失去信心后被迫下台，而GameStop宣布这一举动时提到，该公司正在寻找替代者。帮助加速GameStop的转型。＆＃34;

The movement also comes days after reports that Keith "DeepFuckingValue" Gill—who recently testified to a House committee on his massive gains via early GameStop stock purchases—had doubled his position in the stock by buying 50,000 more shares sometime in February. And early in the afternoon Wednesday, activist investor and GameStop board member Ryan Cohen made one of his rare cryptic tweets featuring a frog emoji and a picture of a soft-serve ice cream cone.

报道称Keith＆＃34; DeepFuckingValue＆＃34;吉尔（他最近通过早期购买GameStop向内务委员会作证）向内务委员会作证。他在二月份的某个时候又购买了50,000股股票，从而使自己在股票中的头寸增加了一倍。周三下午早些时候，激进投资者和GameStop董事会成员Ryan Cohen制作了他稀有的神秘推文之一，该推文带有青蛙表情符号和一张冰淇淋甜筒图片。

That all might seem like thin reason for a stock's value to more than triple overnight. But amid the memes of Reddit's now-famous WallStreetBets subreddit are a number of posts reading the tea leaves to predict a gamma squeeze. That's a complicated process where market makers are forced to buy stock to hedge their bets on call options they previously offered, sending the stock price shooting quickly higher.

似乎所有这些似乎都使股票的价值在一夜之间翻了三倍还不多。但是，在Reddit现在著名的WallStreetBets subreddit的模因中，有许多帖子都在阅读茶叶以预测伽玛挤压。这是一个复杂的过程，做市商被迫购买股票以对冲他们先前提供的看涨期权的押注，从而使股价迅速上涨。

Others on the subreddit are airing their grievances against mainstream financial reporters or encouraging their fellow betters to continue holding the stock as its price goes up.

该次级债券的其他人士正在对主流财务记者发表不满，或鼓励他们的同伴更好地在股价上涨时继续持有该股票。

The last time GameStop stock showed this much volatility, popular commission-free trading platform Robinhood was forced to temporarily limit purchases of GameStop and a few other stocks due to clearinghouse collateral requirements. Robinhood CEO Vlad Tenev recently testified that the company has raised an additional $3.4 billion since then to prevent the need for such restrictions going forward. Meanwhile, the Depository Trust and Clearing Corporation, a major stock sales processor, has come out in favor of reducing the time it takes to settle a stock trade from two days to one. That would help cut brokerage costs and liquidity requirements during times of volatility, the company said.

上一次GameStop股票表现出如此大的波动性时，由于票据交换所的抵押要求，受欢迎的免佣金交易平台Robinhood被迫暂时限制GameStop和其他几只股票的购买。 Robinhood首席执行官Vlad Tenev最近作证说，从那时起，该公司又筹集了34亿美元，以防止今后需要这种限制。同时，主要的股票销售处理商美国存托证券和清算公司已经宣布，将结算股票交易所需的时间从两天减少到一天。该公司表示，这将有助于在波动时期降低经纪成本和流动性要求。
