Lord of the Rings, Hobbit 4K Blu-ray sets: Must-own home-theater stunners
《指环王》，霍比特人4K蓝光碟机：必备的家庭影院震撼者

2020-11-26 02:44:29

Ever since its early '00s conclusion, The Lord of the Rings film trilogy has remained the ultimate collision of nerd scrutiny and filmmaking excellence. It's the exact kind of series that fans have hoped to one day see on 4K UHD Blu-ray for pristine color reproduction and utmost image quality.

自从00年代初得出结论以来，《指环王》电影三部曲就一直是书呆子审查和电影摄制的最终碰撞。粉丝们希望有一天能在4K UHD Blu-ray上看到这一系列，以实现原始色彩再现和最高图像质量。

This year, after countless DVD and Blu-ray releases, box sets, and special editions, Peter Jackson's acclaimed trilogy has finally gotten a home version at four times the previous pixel resolution—and it's as beautiful as I'd hoped for. If previous Blu-ray releases had you anxious about Jackson and Weta Workshop's color-correction philosophy, rest assured that 4K Blu-ray's full HDR canvas has done wonders for all three Tolkien classics.

今年，在无数DVD和Blu-ray发行，盒装和特别版发行之后，彼得·杰克逊（Peter Jackson）广受赞誉的三部曲终于获得了家用版本，其原始分辨率是以前的像素分辨率的四倍-就像我所希望的那样美丽。如果以前的Blu-ray版本让您担心Jackson和Weta Workshop的色彩校正哲学，请放心，4K Blu-ray完整的HDR画布为Tolkien的所有三个经典作品带来了奇迹。

Oh, and the three Hobbit films have gotten the same treatment, if you're into that sort of thing. (Should image quality be your jam, you might even want to  start with the newer trilogy in your 4K Blu-ray player of choice.)

哦，如果您喜欢这种东西，那三部霍比特人电影也得到了相同的待遇。 （如果图像质量成为您的难题，您甚至可能想要从所选4K蓝光播放器中的较新三部曲开始）。

Just ahead of the United States' Thanksgiving break, I received both trilogies' standard 4K boxed sets, each with a gargantuan $90 price tag. Pay more, and you can get the same discs in slightly fancier packaging, but not with any additional discs or video content. All of the sets (launching December 1 at retailers) include each film's theatrical and extended edits as separate discs, along with a streaming unlock code to access the films' 4K versions via Movies Anywhere. The LOTR set weighs in at nine discs, with each theatrical run fitting on a single disc, and each extended edition being split into two discs. The Hobbit set is six discs in all: three theatrical film versions, and three extended cuts.

就在美国的感恩节假期来临之前，我收到了这两个三部曲的标准4K盒装套装，每套价格都高达90美元。支付更多的费用，您可以得到稍贵一些的相同光盘，但没有其他光盘或视频内容。所有这些装置（将于12月1日在零售商处推出）都将每部电影的舞台表演和扩展剪辑作为单独的光盘，以及流媒体解锁代码，可通过Movies Anywhere访问电影的4K版本。 LOTR装置的重量为9张碟片，每个戏剧放映装置都装在一张碟片上，每个扩展版都分成两张碟片。霍比特人套装总共有6张唱片：3个戏剧电影版本和3个扩展剪辑。

The past few years have seen more classic and acclaimed films receive 4K Blu-ray launches—and these are arguably the finest way to reproduce a director's original vision in home theaters. But newcomers to the format should know what they're in for. 4K Blu-ray often doesn't guarantee that every pixel is filled with raw, filmed imagery, as fueled by modern filmmaking equipment like Red 8K cameras. Rather, LOTR's live-action footage was shot on a variety of 35mm cameras, then immediately transferred to digital formats for the sake of preservation and digital VFX work. The completed, combined product was then transferred to 35mm. That final digital-to-analog step remains intact in this year's 4K re-release. Jackson's production crew has not gone back with the full pixel depth of 4K Blu-ray to insert highest-resolution renders of massive virtual armies, or manually paint super-zoomed detail that was never originally captured. Rather, each frame benefits from a tasteful amount of digital noise reduction (DNR). Many of the films' original filmstock artifacts remain, like mild dithering to break up otherwise solid blocks of color—yet these are set off with absolutely handsome reproductions of dynamic, color-filled moments. A flicker of light off a sword's blade shines with the maximum luminance afforded by an HDR panel. A wave of heat blurs an indoor pyre with striking resolution and depth, making its blur effect look that much more like a real fire on your screen.

在过去的几年中，更多经典和备受赞誉的电影获得了4K Blu-ray发行，这可以说是在家庭影院中再现导演最初构想的最好方法。但是格式的新手应该知道它们的用途。 4K蓝光通常不能保证每个像素都充满原始的，已拍摄的图像，这是由Red 8K相机等现代电影制作设备所推动的。相反，LOTR的真实镜头是在各种35mm摄像机上拍摄的，然后为了保护和数字VFX工作而立即转换为数字格式。然后将完成的合并产品转移到35mm。最后的数模转换步骤在今年的4K重新发布中保持不变。杰克逊（Jackson）的制作团队并没有采用4K蓝光的全像素深度插入高分辨率的大型虚拟军队渲染，也没有手动绘制从未被捕获的超缩放细节。而是，每个帧都受益于高雅的数字降噪（DNR）。电影的许多原始胶片伪像仍然存在，例如柔和的抖动会破坏原本固定的色块，但这些色块却以动态的，充满色彩的瞬间的绝对漂亮的复制品开始出现。剑刃上的闪光闪烁着HDR面板提供的最大亮度。一股热浪使室内柴堆具有惊人的分辨率和深度，使其模糊效果看起来更像是屏幕上的真实火焰。

The results look cleaner and more striking than many classic film re-releases, which may be because Jackson and co. already leaned heavily on digital archival systems when filming began. However they pulled it off, the resulting balance between film stock artifacts and gentle DNR application is some of the finest work I've ever seen in the 4K Blu-ray universe.

与许多经典电影的重新发行相比，结果看上去更干净，更醒目，这可能是因为Jackson和co。拍摄开始时，已经严重依赖数字档案系统。无论他们如何努力，在胶片原件和温和的DNR应用之间取得的平衡都是我在4K蓝光世界中见过的最出色的作品。
