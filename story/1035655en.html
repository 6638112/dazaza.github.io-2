I Miss Working from the Office
我想念在办公室工作

2020-11-21 22:46:03

I went into my office yesterday for the first time in a few months to pick some stuff up. We got notifieda couple of days ago to get any personal property out of the office before Thanksgiving or else it'd bethrown out, so I guess we're moving out of the office 1. It was a pretty eerie place to be; even now, 8months later, most people haven't been back and it kind of looks like the entire office was abducted by aliens inearly March.

昨天，我几个月来第一次去办公室收拾东西。几天前，我们接到通知，要在感恩节之前将任何个人财产带出办公室，否则将被扔掉，所以我想我们要离开办公室了。1。即使到了8个月后的今天，大多数人还没有回来，3月初整个办公室似乎都被外星人绑架了。

I miss the 48" TVs on the wall showing Grafana dashboards with key metrics so there was always ambient knowledge ofwhat was going on with the product.

我想念墙上的48英寸电视，其中显示了具有关键指标的Grafana仪表板，因此始终了解该产品的运行情况。

I miss having meetings in conference rooms where you can see other people and have a giant whiteboard to sketch on.

我想念在会议室开会的地方，在那里您可以看到其他人，并有一个巨大的白板可以画画。

I miss casual collaboration. Video chat is a poor substitute and Slack is a deeply irritating tool that combines theworst aspects of synchronous and asynchronous communication.

我想念随意的合作。视频聊天是一种较差的替代方法，而Slack是一种令人讨厌的工具，它结合了同步和异步通信的最坏方面。

I miss productivity! Our engineering productivity has fallen through the floor since COVID despite everyoneworking longer and harder; I fundamentally do not think remote teams can ever be as productive as in-person teams.

我想念生产力！自从COVID以来，我们的工程生产率就已经下降到最低水平，尽管每个人的工作时间都越来越长。从根本上说，我认为远程团队不可能像面对面的团队那样高效。

I miss having my employer pay for electricity and heat and fast symmetric multi-gigabit Internet access instead ofshifting those costs to me.

我想念我的雇主为电，热和快速对称的数千兆位互联网访问付费，而不是将这些费用转移给我。

I miss having a plethora of convenient and delicious options for take-out lunch! I miss the gyros and french friesat Ayola. I miss the chicken pesto sandwich at Working Girls'Cafe. I miss the chow mein and pork buns from Yank Sing. Imiss the katsu curry from Muracci's. I miss the curry burritos from Curry UpNow. I miss the croque monsieur from Cafe Madeline. Imiss the $5 lunch specials from Mehfil.

我想吃外带午餐有很多方便又美味的选择！我想念陀螺仪和法式炸薯条阿约拉。我想念Working Girls'Cafe的鸡肉香蒜酱三明治。我想念扬克星的炒面和猪肉包子。忽略了Muracci的katsu咖喱。我想念来自Curry UpNow的咖喱墨西哥卷饼。我想念来自马德琳咖啡馆（Cafe Madeline）的魔术师。忽略了Mehfil提供的5美元午餐特价。

I miss the succession of friendly baristas at our favorite coffee shop who always knew our orders and put up with our banter.

我想念我们最喜欢的咖啡店里的友好咖啡师接班人，他们总是知道我们的订单并忍受我们的玩笑。

I miss occasional after-work drinks at the sky bar with the $18 well drinks that we always convinced management to pay for.

我总是在空中酒吧怀念偶尔下班后喝的饮料，而我们总是说服管理层为这笔酒支付18美元。

Look, I know, it's not all bad. It's a lot easier taking care of a baby while working from home than it would be ifI were commuting 45 minutes each way every day. I'm spending a lot less money on food and coffee and BART. And I'mnot arguing that we should be in the office now; not a single one of those things I listed above is worth the healthrisk of jamming 150 people 3 into an open-plan office with a quasi-functional ventilation system. But Ireally hope that office-work isn't a permanent victim of this pandemic, that all the companies pushing forpermanent WFH to save a few bucks on rent reconsider their stances, and that there comes a time when I can go back towork again.

看，我知道，这还不是全部。与在家每天上下班通勤45分钟相比，在家照顾婴儿要容易得多。我在食品，咖啡和BART上的花费少得多。我不是在争论我们现在应该在办公室里。我上面列出的这些事情中，没有一件值得冒着将150人3挤入具有准功能通风系统的开放式办公室的健康风险。但是我真的希望办公室工作不会成为这种流行病的永久受害者，所有推动永久性WFH以节省几美元租金的公司都重新考虑他们的立场，并且希望有一段时间我可以再次上班。

I am pretty sure this is an euphemism for "we haven't paid rent and are getting kicked out" but who knows…  ↩

我敢肯定这是委婉的说法，因为“我们还没有付房租，就被踢出去”，但是谁知道呢？

I'm lucky I have any desk at all; if we still lived in the apartment in SF I'd be working with my laptop on my lap every day and probably have a permanent crick in my neck. The "Work From Home" requirement strongly favors people rich enough to own spacious houses.  ↩

我很幸运，我根本没有办公桌。如果我们仍然住在SF的公寓里，那么我每天都会在膝上使用笔记本电脑，并且脖子上可能会永久性弯曲。 “在家工作”的要求强烈地鼓励有钱的人拥有宽敞的房屋。 ↩
