MagSafe Duo Charger和iPhone12机型的MagSafe皮套现在都被苹果官网列为“即将推出”，每套售价129美元

2020-11-07 06:58:54

As preorders for the iPhone 12 Mini and iPhone Pro Max kicked off on Friday, Apple also revealed pricing for its MagSafe Duo Charger as well as a Leather Sleeve with MagSafe for the iPhone 12 and 12 Pro. Both accessories will cost $129, or $10.75 a month for 12 months. Apple's site lists both as "coming soon" but doesn't offer a specific release date.

周五，随着iPhone 12 Mini和iPhone Pro Max的预订开始，苹果公司还公布了MagSafe二人组充电器的定价，以及iPhone 12和12 Pro的MagSafe皮套。这两种配件的价格都是129美元，或者12个月每月10.75美元。苹果官网将这两款产品都列为即将推出的产品，但并未提供具体发布日期。

Apple announced the MagSafe Duo charger along with the iPhone 12, HomePod Mini and other MagSafe accessories during an event in October, but it didn't offer details on pricing or release date. The pad features two chargers, one for the iPhone 12 and the other for an Apple Watch. The charging pad may be considered the spiritual successor to the infamously  canceled AirPower charging mat.

苹果在10月份的一次活动中宣布了MagSafe Duo充电器以及iPhone 12、HomePod Mini和其他MagSafe配件，但没有提供定价和发布日期的细节。这款平板电脑配备了两个充电器，一个用于iPhone 12，另一个用于Apple Watch。充电垫可以被认为是臭名昭著的空中动力充电垫的精神继承者。

The new Leather Sleeve, which comes in four color options, joins a slew of  magnetic MagSafe accessories that snap onto the back of the iPhone 12, such as cases, chargers, mounts and a wallet. Apple says the Leather Sleeve offers protection without adding bulk, and also includes a pocket for a credit card or ID. It comes with a matching wrist strap.

新的皮革袖子有四种颜色可供选择，加入了一系列磁性MagSafe配件，这些配件可以卡在iPhone 12的背面，如保护套、充电器、底座和钱包。苹果公司表示，皮制袖子在不增加体积的情况下提供保护，还包括一个可存放信用卡或身份证的口袋。它还配有配套的手腕带。

The iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro were released in October, shortly after Apple announced the four new iPhone models. Preorders for Apple's  iPhone 12 Mini and  iPhone 12 Pro Max started today, with deliveries and in-store availability set for Nov. 13. All  four new iPhone 12 models come with  5G,  better cameras and MagSafe.

IPhone 12和iPhone 12 Pro是在苹果宣布推出四款新iPhone后不久于10月发布的。苹果iPhone 12 Mini和iPhone 12 Pro Max今天开始预订，预计11月13日发货和店内供应。这四款新iPhone 12 Mini和MagSafe都配备了5G、更好的摄像头和MagSafe。
