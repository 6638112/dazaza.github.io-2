Apple’s new AirPods Max over-ear headphones cost $549
Apple新款AirPods Max入耳式耳机售价549美元

2020-12-08 23:35:36

This morning, Apple made a surprise announcement: it begins taking orders today for its long-rumored over-ear headphones bearing the AirPods brand. Called AirPods Max, they take design cues from existing Apple products and use several internal sensors and microphones to deliver "computational audio" features.

今天早上，苹果公司发布了一个令人惊讶的公告：它今天开始接受传闻已久的带有AirPods品牌耳罩式耳机的订单。他们被称为AirPods Max，它们从现有的Apple产品中汲取设计灵感，并使用多个内部传感器和麦克风来提供“计算音频”。特征。

Priced at $549, AirPods Max are fully wireless headphones, and they use Bluetooth aided by Apple's custom tech to connect to audio sources like an iPhone or a computer. They come with a "Smart Case" that protects the headphones for storage, and puts the headphones in a low-power sleep mode. Additionally, the case has a Lightning port, allowing users to charge the headphones with an included Lightning to USB-C cable. The above-mentioned sensors include an optical sensor, a position sensor, a case-detect sensor, and an accelerometer in each ear cup, and a gyroscope in just the left ear cup. There are also nine microphones. One is devoted exclusively to voice pickup, while the other eight are used for active noise cancellation; two of the mics perform double duty.

AirPods Max售价549美元，是完全无线的耳机，它们使用Apple定制技术辅助的蓝牙连接到iPhone或计算机等音频源。它们带有＆＃34; Smart Case＆＃34;保护耳机以进行存储，并使耳机进入低功耗睡眠模式。此外，外壳还具有Lightning端口，允许用户使用随附的Lightning至USB-C电缆为耳机充电。上述传感器包括在每个耳罩中的光学传感器，位置传感器，壳体检测传感器和加速度计，以及仅在左耳罩中的陀螺仪。还有9个麦克风。其中一个专门用于语音拾音，而另外八个则用于主动降噪。其中两个麦克风执行双重职责。

In addition to the active noise cancellation that is standard in many headphones at or below this price point, computational audio features also include transparency mode (as seen in AirPods Pro previously), spatial audio, and adaptive EQ. The built-in sensors can also be used to detect when and how the headphones are being worn, driving some convenience features like automatically pausing music playback when you lift one of the ear cups.

除了在此价位或低于此价位的许多耳机中都标配有的主动降噪功能之外，计算音频功能还包括透明模式（如之前在AirPods Pro中所见），空间音频和自适应均衡器。内置传感器还可用于检测耳机的佩戴时间和佩戴方式，并提供一些便利功能，例如提起一个耳罩时会自动暂停音乐播放。

As for audio quality, we'll have to wait and see reviews to see where it lands, but Apple is of course effusive in its own press release explaining why it believes these headphones should offer top-notch audio:

至于音频质量，我们必须等待并查看评论以了解其到达的位置，但是苹果公​​司当然在自己的新闻稿中大肆宣传，解释了为什么它认为这些耳机应该提供一流的音频：

AirPods Max feature a 40-mm Apple-designed dynamic driver that provides rich, deep bass, accurate mid-ranges, and crisp, clean high-frequency extension so every note can be heard. A unique dual neodymium ring magnet motor allows AirPods Max to maintain total harmonic distortion of less than 1 percent across the entire audible range, even at maximum volume. Equipped with an Apple-designed H1 chip in each ear cup, a custom acoustic design, and advanced software, AirPods Max use computational audio to deliver the highest quality listening experience possible. Utilizing each of the chips’ 10 audio cores — capable of 9 billion operations per second — computational audio powers a breakthrough listening experience that includes Adaptive EQ, Active Noise Cancellation, Transparency mode, and spatial audio.

AirPods Max具有Apple设计的40毫米动态驱动器，可提供丰富，深沉的低音，准确的中音范围以及清晰，干净的高频扩展，因此可以听到每个音符。独特的双钕环形磁铁电动机使AirPods Max在整个可听范围内，即使在最大音量下，也能将总谐波失真保持在小于1％。每个耳罩均配有Apple设计的H1芯片，自定义的声学设计和高级软件，AirPods Max使用计算音频来提供最高质量的聆听体验。利用音频的10个音频内核中的每一个（每秒可进行90亿次运算），计算音频为突破性的聆听体验提供了支持，包括自适应均衡器，主动降噪，透明模式和空间音频。

Apple says AirPods Max will get up to 20 hours of battery life with active noise cancellation and spatial audio enabled, and that five minutes of charge time can deliver 1.5 hours of playback.

苹果表示，AirPods Max将具有长达20小时的电池续航时间，同时具有主动降噪功能和启用的空间音频功能，而五分钟的充电时间可以提供1.5小时的播放时间。

AirPods Max are controlled using a single button and a digital crown knob located at the top of one of the ear cups—an interface that draws inspiration from the Apple Watch, minus the touchscreen. The crown can be used to adjust volume, skip forward or backward, answer incoming calls, or invoke Siri.

AirPods Max是通过一个按钮和位于一个耳罩顶部的数字冠状旋钮控制的，该接口从Apple Watch上汲取了灵感，而没有触摸屏。表冠可用于调节音量，向前或向后跳过，接听来电或调用Siri。

The headphones come in five colors—space gray, silver, green, sky blue, and pink—and can optionally be engraved at the time the order is placed. Apple is taking orders for AirPods Max in its online store starting today, and the headphones are expected to reach their first buyers on December 15.

耳机有五种颜色-灰色，银色，绿色，天蓝色和粉红色-可以在下订单时进行雕刻。 苹果将从今天开始在其在线商店中接受AirPods Max的订单，预计耳机将于12月15日到达第一批购买者。
