“Mass Effect Will Return:” Teaser trailer sets a new (old) direction for the sequel
“大众效应将重现：”预告片为续集设定了新的（旧的）方向

2020-12-12 02:29:43

We knew it was coming, and now we have confirmation: a new Mass Effect game is on the horizon. It was announced with a teaser trailer during last night's Game Awards broadcast.

我们知道它即将到来，现在我们得到了确认：即将推出新的《质量效应》游戏。它是在昨晚的Game Awards广播中用预告片宣布的。

What we have is a short trailer with no gameplay but one that still makes a few things clear about the direction of the series. The new game will be made by BioWare under the EA umbrella, and the foremost revelations are that it seems to be set in the same galaxy as the original trilogy of games, it occurs immediately after the conclusion of Mass Effect 3, and it appears to feature at least one returning character (Liara T'Soni). As for what gameplay looks like or where the story goes from here, it's anyone's guess. We don't want to spoil anything here, but each of the Mass Effect 3 endings left the game's setting unrecognizable compared to how it appeared during the course of the trilogy—hence BioWare's decision to set the poorly received follow-up Mass Effect: Andromeda in a completely different galaxy with a new cast of characters.

我们所拥有的是一部简短的预告片，没有任何游戏玩法，但仍然可以清楚地说明本系列的发展方向。新游戏将由BioWare在EA的保护下制造，最重要的启示是它似乎与原始游戏三部曲处于同一个星系中，它在《质量效应3》结束后立即出现，并且似乎至少要有一个返回字符（Liara T＆＃39; Soni）。至于游戏玩法是什么样的，或者故事从何而来，这是任何人的猜测。我们不想在这里破坏任何东西，但是与《三部曲》的出现相比，《质量效应3》的每个结局都无法识别游戏的设置，因此BioWare决定设置效果不佳的后续《质量效应：仙女座》在一个完全不同的银河系中出现了新角色。

It's not clear from the new trailer which of the Mass Effect 3 endings ended up canon (given the scope of some endings' differences, it seems unlikely that the player will get to import saves or choose), but in any case, the trailer suggests that much of the galaxy is in ruin.

从新预告片中尚不清楚质量效应3的哪些结局以佳能结尾（考虑到某些结局的范围不同，玩家似乎不太可能导入保存或选择），但是在无论如何，预告片表明许多银河系已经毁了。

It's worth noting that several key creative team members responsible for the original Mass Effect trilogy have moved on from BioWare, which has seen a troubled few years with a rocky track record, to say the least. A select few, however, returned for this project, according to tweets from Project Director Mike Gamble, including Narrative Designer Dusty Everman, Art Director Derek Watts, and Cinematics Director Parrish Lay.

值得一提的是，负责原始《质量效应》三部曲的几位关键创意团队成员已经从BioWare转移过来了，至少可以说，BioWare经历了几年的艰难历程。然而，根据项目总监Mike Gamble的推文，包括该叙事设计师Dusty Everman，艺术总监Derek Watts和电影导演Parrish Lay在内的少数人返回了该项目。

Regardless of its makeup of both new and old members, the team making the new Mass Effect game has a difficult challenge ahead, given both the uncertain ending of Mass Effect 3 and the harsh backlash to that game's conclusion, which was a flashpoint in the ongoing conversation about today's persistent problems of online toxicity and developer harassment in the gaming industry and reportedly a major factor in the original founders of BioWare leaving the industry. The trailer did not reveal a release date for the game or which platforms it will arrive on. It didn't even give the game a name yet, so we'll likely be waiting a while for more information.

无论新老成员的组成如何，考虑到《质量效应3》的不确定终结和对该游戏结论的强烈反对，制作新《质量效应》游戏的团队面临着艰巨的挑战。关于当今游戏行业在线毒性和开发人员骚扰的持续问题的持续讨论中出现了闪点，而且据报道，这是BioWare最初创始人离开该行业的主要因素。预告片没有透露游戏的发布日期或发布的平台。它甚至还没有给游戏起个名字，所以我们可能要等待一段时间才能获得更多信息。
