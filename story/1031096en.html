GAP：组、算法、编程：计算离散代数系统

2020-10-25 23:02:51

GAP is a system for computational discrete algebra, with particular emphasis on Computational Group Theory. GAP provides a programming language, a library of thousands of functions implementing algebraic algorithms written in the GAP language as well as large data libraries of algebraic objects. See also the overview and the description of the mathematical capabilities. GAP is used in research and teaching for studying groups and their representations, rings, vector spaces, algebras, combinatorial structures, and more. The system, including source, is distributed freely. You can study and easily modify or extend it for your special use.

GAP是一个计算离散代数系统，特别强调计算群理论。GAP提供了一种编程语言，一个包含数千个实现用GAP语言编写的代数算法的函数库的库，以及大量的代数对象库数据。另请参阅数学运算功能的概述和说明。GAP用于研究和教学学习群及其表示、环、向量空间、代数、组合结构等。该系统(包括源代码)是自由分布的。您可以根据您的特殊用途对其进行研究和轻松修改或扩展。

In July 2008, GAP was awarded the ACM/SIGSAMRichard Dimick Jenks Memorial Prize for Excellence inSoftware Engineering applied to Computer Algebra.

2008年7月，GAP被授予ACM/SIGSAMRichard Dimick Jenks计算机代数应用软件工程杰出纪念奖。

The current release is GAP 4.11.0 and it can be obtained from our downloads page. This website describes this release if not stated otherwise. Changes from earlier versions are described in the Release history.

当前版本是GAP 4.11.0，可以从我们的下载页面获得。如果未另行说明，本网站将对本新闻稿进行说明。与早期版本相比的更改在发布历史中进行了描述。

We welcome contributions to GAP. The GAP development repository is hosted on GitHub. You may find some guidance on contributing via GitHub here. If you have any questions, or suggestions for GAP, the repository, or documentation, feel free to contact us via the open GAP development mailing list or submit an issue or a pull request on GitHub.

我们欢迎为GAP做出贡献。GAP开发存储库托管在GitHub上。你可以在这里找到一些关于通过GitHub做出贡献的指导。如果您对GAP、存储库或文档有任何问题或建议，请随时通过开放的GAP开发邮件列表与我们联系，或在GitHub上提交问题或请求。

There is an extensive documentation advising how to write a GAP code. Also there is a guidance on developing a GAP package and its submission to GAP.

有大量文档建议如何编写GAP代码。此外，还有关于制定GAP一揽子计划及其提交给GAP的指导。

The GAP Group welcomes contacts with the GAP users and offers support for them. To keep up to date on GAP news (discussion of problems, release announcements, bug fixes), we suggest you to subscribe to the email-based GAP Forum. You may also follow GAP on Twitter.

GAP集团欢迎与GAP用户接触，并为他们提供支持。要及时了解GAP新闻(问题讨论、发布公告、错误修复)，我们建议您订阅基于电子邮件的GAP论坛。你也可以在推特上关注GAP。

Please tell us about your use of GAP in research or teaching. We may well want to provide a link to your work. If your work is published then we ask you to cite GAP like a journal article or book. We maintain a Bibliography of publications citing GAP. Please help us keeping it up to date.

请告诉我们您在研究或教学中使用GAP的情况。我们很可能希望提供您工作的链接。如果你的作品已经出版，我们会要求你引用GAP，比如一篇期刊、一篇文章或一本书。我们有引用GAP的出版物书目。请帮助我们使其保持最新。

GAP has been and is developed by international cooperation of many people, including user contributions. We gratefully acknowledge all this help as well as some funding. GAP was started at Lehrstuhl D für Mathematik, RWTH Aachen in 1986. After 1997 the development of GAP was coordinated in St Andrews. At present, the GAP Centers in Aachen, Braunschweig, Fort Collins, Kaiserslautern and St Andrews are jointly coordinating the further development and maintenance of GAP.

GAP已经并正在通过包括用户贡献在内的许多人的国际合作来发展。我们感谢所有这些帮助以及一些资金。GAP成立于1986年，位于亚琛RWTH的Lehrstuhl Dür Mahematik。1997年后，圣安德鲁斯的GAP得到了协调发展。目前，亚琛、布伦斯韦格、柯林斯堡、凯泽斯劳滕和圣安德鲁斯的GAP中心正在共同协调GAP的进一步发展和维护。
