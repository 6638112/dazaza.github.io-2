The Battle of Snake Island
蛇岛之战

Jump to navigation Jump to search The Battle of Snake Island took place on 24 February 2022 on Snake Island ( Ukrainian: Острів Зміїний, Ostriv Zmiinyi) during the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine.

Snake Island is a small rocky island off the southern coast of Ukraine, which is strategically beneficial to Ukraine due to its location near Romania and at the edge of Ukrainian territorial waters in the Black Sea. [2] [3] In August 2021, Ukrainian president Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a press event on Snake Island, during which he said "This island, like the rest of our territory, is Ukrainian land, and we will defend it with all our might". [3]

斯内克岛是乌克兰南部海岸附近的一个岩石小岛，由于其位置靠近罗马尼亚，位于黑海乌克兰领海的边缘，因此在战略上对乌克兰有利。〔2〕〔3〕2021年8月，乌克兰总统Volodymyr Zelenskyy在蛇岛举行新闻发布会，期间他说“34”；这个岛和我们的其他领土一样，是乌克兰的领土，我们将竭尽全力保卫它；。[3]

At around 18:00 local time on 24 February 2022, Ukrainian State Border Guards announced that Snake Island, located in Ukrainian territorial waters in the Black Sea, had come under attack from Russian ships, during the first day of the 2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine. [4] [5] The cruiser Moskva and patrol boat Vasily Bykov were involved in the attack, using their deck guns. [6]

2022年2月24日当地时间18点左右，乌克兰国家边防军宣布，在2022年俄罗斯入侵乌克兰的第一天，位于黑海乌克兰领海的斯内克岛遭到俄罗斯船只的袭击。[4] [5]莫斯科巡洋舰和瓦西里·别科夫巡逻艇使用甲板炮参与了袭击。[6]

According to audio shared by the Ukrayinska Pravda, when the Russian warship identified itself and instructed the Ukrainian soldiers stationed on the island to surrender, their response was "Russian warship, go fuck yourself" ( Russian: 'Русский военный корабль, иди на хуй', Russkiy voyennyy korabl', idi na khuy). [7] [8]

根据Ukrayinska Pravda分享的音频，当俄罗斯军舰表明身份并指示驻扎在该岛的乌克兰士兵投降时，他们的反应是"；俄罗斯战舰，去你妈的"；（俄语：'；####39；######；#####。[7] [8]

Later in the evening, the State Border Guard Service stated that communication with the island had been lost [9] and at 22:00 (01:00 Moscow Time, UTC+2), announced that Russian forces had captured the island following a naval and air bombardment that destroyed all infrastructure on the island. [10] [11] Thirteen border guards, representing the entirety of the Ukrainian military presence on the island, were killed during the battle after refusing to surrender. [12] [13] One of the Ukrainian soldiers live-streamed the moment that the Russian warship opened fire. [14]

晚上晚些时候，国家边防局表示，与该岛的通讯已经中断[9]，并于22:00（莫斯科时间1:00，UTC+2）宣布，俄罗斯军队在海空轰炸摧毁该岛所有基础设施后占领了该岛。[10] [11]13名边防警卫在拒绝投降后在战斗中丧生，他们代表了乌克兰在该岛的全部军事存在。[12] [13]俄罗斯军舰开火时，一名乌克兰士兵现场直播。[14]

On the same day, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy announced that all thirteen guards will be posthumously awarded the title of Hero of Ukraine, the highest Ukrainian military honor. [8]

同一天，总统沃洛蒂米尔·泽伦斯基宣布，所有13名卫兵将被追授乌克兰英雄称号，这是乌克兰最高军事荣誉。[8]

The Ukrainian border guards' defiant last words "Russian warship, go fuck yourself" went viral and became a rallying cry for Ukrainians and their supporters around the world. [15] The Week compared the phrase to " Remember the Alamo" from the 19th century Republic of Texas . [16]

乌克兰边防卫队'；挑衅的临终遗言"；俄罗斯战舰，去你妈的"；病毒性传播，成为乌克兰人及其世界各地支持者的集会口号。[15] 本周将该短语与"；记住阿拉莫"；来自19世纪的德克萨斯共和国。[16]
