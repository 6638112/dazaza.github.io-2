Supernormal Stimulus
超常刺激

2021-01-02 08:18:52

Jump to navigation Jump to search A supernormal stimulus or superstimulus is an exaggerated version of a stimulus to which there is an existing response tendency, or any stimulus that elicits a response more strongly than the stimulus for which it evolved.

跳转至导航跳转至搜索超常刺激或超刺激是存在响应趋势的刺激的放大版本，或者是比其演化的刺激更强烈地引起响应的任何刺激。

For example, when it comes to bird eggs, they can evolve to prefer the artificial versions to their own [1], particularly evident in brood parasitism, and humans can be similarly exploited by junk food [2] and pornography. [3] Organisms tend to show a preference for the stimulus properties (i.e. size, colour, etc.) that have evolved in nature; but when offered an artificial exaggerated stimulus, animals will show behaviour in favour of the artificial stimulus, over the naturally occurring stimulus. [4] A variety of organisms that display or are susceptible to supernormal stimuli include insects, birds, and humans.

例如，当涉及到鸟蛋时，它们可以进化为偏爱人造蛋而不是自己的蛋[1]，特别是在雏鸟寄生中很明显，而垃圾食品[2]和色情也可以类似地剥削人类。 [3]生物倾向于对自然界已发展的刺激特性（即大小，颜色等）表现出偏爱；但是当提供人工夸大刺激时，动物会表现出比自然刺激更有利于人工刺激的行为。 [4]表现出或易受超常刺激的各种生物包括昆虫，鸟类和人类。

Supernormal stimuli are present in areas of biology and psychology, but are also studied within other fields like sociology and art.

超常刺激存在于生物学和心理学领域，但也在社会学和艺术等其他领域进行研究。

British academic Nigel Spivey demonstrates the effect in the first episode of the 2005 BBC documentary series How Art Made the World to illustrate neuroscientist Vilayanur S. Ramachandran's speculation that this might be the reason for the grossly exaggerated body image demonstrated in works of art from the Venus of Willendorf right up to the present day.

英国学者奈杰尔·斯皮维（Nigel Spivey）在2005年英国广播公司（BBC）纪录片系列的第一集中展示了这种效果，以说明神经科学家Vilayanur S. Ramachandran的推测，这可能是在直到今天，维伦多夫维纳斯的艺术。

Animals exhibiting, or responding to, characteristics that represent a supernormal stimulus usually display them as a result of selective pressures. Co-evolution between animals displaying supernormal stimuli, and the organisms responding to the supernormal stimuli, rely on evolution and propagation of genetics, behavioral patterns, and other biological factors. [5] Supernormal stimuli such as emphasized color, size, patterns, or shapes, are often successful because an organism that exhibits them will often be selected by an organism that favors it. This will ensure survival and increased reproductive fitness of current and latter generations.

表现出或响应代表超常刺激的特征的动物通常会由于选择压力而显示它们。在显示超常刺激的动物与对超常刺激作出反应的生物之间的共同进化取决于遗传学，行为方式和其他生物学因素的进化和传播。 [5]超常刺激（例如强调的颜色，大小，样式或形状）通常是成功的，因为表现出它们的生物通常会被喜欢它的生物选择。这将确保今世后代的生存并提高其生殖适应性。

In the 1950s, Konrad Lorenz observed that birds would select brooding eggs that resembled those of their own species but which were larger. Niko Tinbergen, coined this term through his research, which discovered that experimenters could display an alternate target that attract an organism's attention more so than the naturally occurring target. [5] Tinbergen studied herring gulls, and found the chicks peck at the red spot located on their parent's bill. The offspring targets the red spot due to the contrast of color (stimulus). [5] They do this in order to receive food through regurgitation from the parent. [5] Tinbergen and colleagues developed an experiment that presented different models to chicks and determined their pecking rates. [5] They used different models including an adult herring gull's natural head, a standard wooden model of its head, the bill only, and a red stick with smaller white markings on it. [5] The pecking rate of the chicks were consistent with the natural head, standard head model, and the bill only model. [5] The pecking rate of the chicks increased when presented with the stick model. [5] This suggests that the chicks preferred the dramatic contrast of the red stick with the yellow markings, therefore the artificial stimulus of the stick model was favored over the basic herring gull head and bill models, proving that the artificial stimuli was favored over the naturally occurring stimuli. Following his extensive analysis of the stimulus features that elicited food-begging in the chick of the herring gull, constructed an artificial stimulus consisting of a red knitting needle with three white bands painted around it; this elicited a stronger response than an accurate three-dimensional model of the parent's head (white) and bill (yellow with a red spot). [5]

在1950年代，康拉德·洛伦兹（Konrad Lorenz）观察到鸟类会选择与自己物种相似但更大的育卵。 Niko Tinbergen在他的研究中创造了这个名词，他发现实验者可以展示出一个替代性的靶标，它比自然存在的靶标更能吸引生物体的注意力。 [5] Tinbergen研究了鲱鸥，并发现小鸡在其父母帐单上的红色斑点处啄食。后代由于颜色（刺激）的对比而将红点作为目标。 [5]他们这样做是为了通过父母反胃来获得食物。 [5] Tinbergen及其同事开发了一项实验，为雏鸡提供了不同的模型，并确定了它们的啄食率。 [5]他们使用了不同的模型，包括成年鲱鸥的自然头部，头部的标准木制模型，仅钞票和上面带有较小白色标记的红色棍子。 [5]雏鸡的啄食率与自然头，标准头模型和仅账单模型一致。 [5]当用棒状模型出现时，雏鸡的啄食率增加。 [5]这表明小鸡更喜欢红色棍棒与黄色标记的戏剧性对比，因此棍棒模型的人工刺激优于基本的鲱鸥头和比尔模型，证明了人工刺激比人工鲱鱼更受青睐。自然发生的刺激。在他对引起鲱鱼食的乞讨行为的刺激特征进行了广泛的分析之后，他构造了一个人工刺激，该刺激由一根红色的织针和周围画有三个白色带子组成；与正确的父母头（白色）和比尔（黄色，带红色斑点）的三维模型相比，这引起了更强的响应。 [5]

Tinbergen and his students studied other variations of this effect. He experimented with dummy plaster eggs of various sizes and markings finding that most birds preferred ones with more exaggerated markings than their own, more saturated versions of their color, and a larger size than their own. Small songbirds which laid light blue grey-dappled eggs preferred to sit on a bright blue black polka-dotted dummy so large they slid off repeatedly. Territorial male stickleback fish would attack wooden floats with red undersides—attacking them more vigorously than invading male sticklebacks if the underside were redder. [1]

Tinbergen和他的学生研究了这种效应的其他变化。他对各种大小和标记的假石膏鸡蛋进行了实验，发现大多数鸟类都喜欢那些比自己的鸟更夸张的标记，颜色更饱和的鸟和更大的鸟。会产下浅蓝灰色斑驳鸡蛋的小型鸣禽更喜欢坐在明亮的蓝黑色波尔卡圆点假人上，以至于它们会反复滑落。领土雄性棘背鱼会攻击带有红色底面的木质浮标，如果其底面较红，则比入侵雄性棘背鱼要更有力。 [1]

Lorenz and Tinbergen accounted for the supernormal stimulus effect in terms of the concept of the innate releasing mechanism; however this concept is no longer widely used. [ citation needed] The core observation that simple features of stimuli may be sufficient to trigger a complex response remains valid, however.

Lorenz和Tinbergen用先天释放机制的概念解释了超常刺激效应。但是这个概念不再被广泛使用。然而，关于刺激的简单特征可能足以触发复杂反应的核心观察仍然有效。

In 1979, the term supernormal stimulus was used by Richard Dawkins and John Krebs to refer to the exaggeration of pre-existing signs induced by social parasites, noting the manipulation of baby birds (hosts) from these, to illustrate the effectiveness of those signals. [6]

1979年，理查德·道金斯（Richard Dawkins）和约翰·克雷布斯（John Krebs）使用了“超常刺激”一词来指称由社交寄生虫引起的先有迹象的夸大，并指出了对这些幼鸟（宿主）的操纵，以说明这些信号的有效性。 [6]

In 1983, entomologists Darryl Gwynne and David Rentz reported on the beetle Julodimorpha bakewelli attempting to copulate with discarded brown stubbies (a type of beer bottles) studded with tubercules (flattened glass beads). [7] This work won them the 2011 Ig Nobel Prize in biology. [8]

1983年，昆虫学家Darryl Gwynne和David Rentz报道了甲虫Julodimorpha bakewelli试图与堆满结节（扁平的玻璃珠）的废弃的棕色短茎（一种啤酒瓶）交配。 [7]这项工作为他们赢得了2011年伊格诺贝尔生物学奖。 [8]

Another example of this is the study made by Mauck and colleagues, where they evaluated the effects of a plant pathogen named cucumber mosaic virus or CMV. This study showed that the aphids preferred the healthy plants but are still attracted by the infected plants, because of the manipulation of volatile compounds used by plants to attract them. [9]

另一个例子是Mauck及其同事进行的研究，他们评估了名为黄瓜花叶病毒或CMV的植物病原体的作用。这项研究表明，蚜虫更喜欢健康的植物，但由于操纵植物用来吸引它们的挥发性化合物，它们仍被被感染的植物吸引。 [9]

Pollinators, like butterflies, show behavioral response(s) to supernormal stimuli through intersexual communication. [10] Butterflies use olfactory cues, but primarily rely on visual forms of communication, due to wind and temperature affecting their sense of smell. [10] Sexually active butterflies will perch and wait for a mate. Once an object is detected, the butterfly can determine if the color and movement patterns are of a sexually receptive butterfly. [10] In certain species, like the silver-washed fritillary butterfly ( Argynnis paphia), the male butterfly will exhibit high contrast sensitivity and well-developed visual acuity. [10] A more dramatic contrast of color (or movement pattern) resembling, but further emphasizing the traits of the female butterfly, could alter this usual behavior in males. [10] Studies show that the male silver-washed fritillary butterfly select a monochromatic orange paper model, over receptive female butterflies who portrayed their usual coloration/markings. [10]

授粉者像蝴蝶一样，通过性交交流表现出对超常刺激的行为反应。 [10]蝴蝶会使用嗅觉提示，但由于风和温度会影响它们的嗅觉，因此它们主要依靠视觉交流。 [10]性活跃的蝴蝶会栖息并等待伴侣。一旦检测到物体，蝴蝶就可以确定颜色和运动模式是否为性爱蝴蝶。 [10]在某些物种中，例如洗银的贝母蝴蝶（Argynnis paphia），雄性蝴蝶将表现出高对比度敏感度和发达的视敏度。 [10]相似的颜色（或运动模式）戏剧性对比，但进一步强调雌性蝴蝶的特征，可能会改变雄性这种通常的行为。 [10]研究表明，雄性洗银的贝母蝴蝶会选择单色的橙色纸质模型，而不是刻画其通常的颜色/标记的雌性雌性蝴蝶。 [10]

In 2001, Holen et al., analyzed the evolutionary stability of hosts manipulation through exaggerated signals. Their model indicated that intensity of parasitic signals must be below a threshold to ensure acceptance from host. This threshold depends directly on the range of parasitism. [11]

Holen等人在2001年通过夸大的信号分析了宿主操纵的进化稳定性。他们的模型表明，寄生信号的强度必须低于阈值，以确保被宿主接受。该阈值直接取决于寄生虫的范围。 [11]

Supernormal stimuli can be exhibited by brood parasites, for example, the parasitic cuckoo chick and parental care by reed warblers. [12] Brood parasites have evolved more dramatic colors, sizes, patterns, and/or shapes that lead to the parasite being interpreted as healthier or more preferable, in contrast to neighboring offspring. Cuckoo chicks are often successful because their begging calls, the supernormal stimulus, are representative of an entire reed warbler brood. [12] Due to the host parent's evolutionary instinct, elicited by selective pressures, they will select this exaggerated form of the stimulus. These calls will cause the host parent to primarily invest energy into the parasitic chick and provide it with additional food resources. [12] Studies show that the supernormal stimuli in cuckoo chicks alter the foraging behavior in parental reed warblers. [12]

巢寄生虫（例如，布谷鸟雏鸡）和芦苇莺对父母的照顾可以表现出超常刺激。 [12]与邻近的后代相比，育成的寄生虫已进化出更加引人注目的颜色，大小，样式和/或形状，导致该寄生虫被解释为更健康或更可取。杜鹃小鸡通常是成功的，因为它们的乞calls叫“超常刺激”代表了整个芦苇莺群。 [12]由于寄主父母的进化本能，是由选择性压力引起的，他们会选择这种夸大的刺激形式。这些电话将使寄主父母主要将能量投入到寄生小鸡中并为其提供额外的食物资源。 [12]研究表明，布谷鸟雏鸡的超常刺激会改变父母芦苇莺的觅食行为。 [12]

For them, the only evolutionary stable strategy is when the host accepts all signs of the parasite with optimal intensity, which must be below the threshold; if this is not the case, the host can use these signals to identify the parasite. [11]

对他们而言，唯一的进化稳定策略是当宿主以最佳强度接受寄生虫的所有体征时，这些体征必须低于阈值。如果不是这种情况，则宿主可以使用这些信号来识别寄生虫。 [11]

Maladaptive behaviors are shown by organisms that display a preference for supernormal stimuli over naturally occurring stimuli. This is often based on instinct to gather as many resources as possible in a resource-sparse environment. [13] It can also be instinctual for certain species to select the supernormal stimuli that will suggest the best energy investment of the individual, often parental investment. [14] The selection of the supernormal stimuli must also simultaneously outweigh the cost of the behavior in order for it to evolve. This is shown in the cuckoo chick and the effects on host reed warblers. [14] These parasitic chicks exhibit irresistible begging calls toward the parental host. [14] This occurs as a result of selective pressures. The reed warbler increases foraging efforts to feed the parasitic chick and its own offspring. [14] As a result, this shows a maladaptive behaviour of the host reed warbler as it is investing into a chick that is not biologically related, which does not provide reproductive fitness gain.

适应不良的行为表现为有机体，其对超自然刺激的偏好要高于自然刺激。这通常是基于本能来在资源稀疏的环境中收集尽可能多的资源。 [13]对于某些物种而言，选择超自然刺激也将是本能，这将暗示个体的最佳能源投资，通常是父母的投资。 [14]超常刺激的选择还必须同时超过行为的成本，以使其得以发展。这在布谷鸟雏鸡中以及对宿主芦苇莺的影响中都可以看出。 [14]这些寄生小鸡对父母宿主表现出无法抗拒的乞求。 [14]这是选择性压力的结果。芦苇莺增加了觅食工作，以喂养寄生小鸡及其后代。 [14]结果，这表明宿主芦苇莺在向与生物学无关的雏鸡投资时不适应，这不提供生殖适应性的提高。

The significance of supernormal stimuli and brood parasitism or in various other species susceptible to environmental manipulation, is that this can drastically reduce the population numbers of the respective species. Brood parasitism can cause host parents to ignore their own offspring or display a higher parental investment into the parasite. [15] Animals that are at risk of extinction, extirpation, or vulnerability will be impacted by supernormal stimuli present in brood parasites.

超常刺激和巢寄生或在其他易受环境操纵的其他物种中的意义在于，这可以大大减少各个物种的种群数量。亲子寄生可能会导致寄主父母无视自己的后代或父母对寄生虫的投资更高。 [15]处于灭绝，灭绝或脆弱状态的动物会受到育成寄生虫中超正常刺激的影响。

Harvard psychologist Deirdre Barrett argues that supernormal stimulation governs the behavior of humans as powerfully as that of other animals. In her 2010 book, Supernormal Stimuli: How Primal Urges Overran Their Evolutionary Purpose, [16] she examines the impact of supernormal stimuli on the diversion of impulses for nurturing, sexuality, romance, territoriality, defense, and the entertainment industry's hijacking of our social instincts. In her earlier book Waistland, [2] she explains junk food as an exaggerated stimulus to cravings for salt, sugar, and fats and television as an exaggeration of social cues of laughter, smiling faces and attention-grabbing action. Modern artifacts may activate instinctive responses which evolved prior to the modern world, where breast development was a sign of health and fertility in a prospective mate, and fat was a rare and vital nutrient.

哈佛大学心理学家Deirdre Barrett认为，超常刺激与其他动物一样有力地支配着人类的行为。在她的2010年著作《超常刺激：原始的刺激如何超越其进化目的》中，[16]她研究了超常刺激对刺激冲动的冲动的影响，这些冲动包括性，性，浪漫，领土性，防御和娱乐业。劫持我们的社会本能。她在较早的《 Waistland》一书中[2]将垃圾食品解释为对盐，糖，脂肪和电视渴望的过度刺激，并夸大了社交笑声，笑脸和引人注目的动作的线索。现代人工制品可能会激活本能反应，这种本能反应是在现代世界发生之前发展的，在该世界中，乳房发育是准配偶健康和生育的标志，而脂肪则是稀有且至关重要的营养素。

In a cross-cultural study, Doyle and Pazhoohi showed that surgically augmented breasts are supernormal stimuli, and they are more attractive than natural breasts, regardless of their size. [17] Also in a theoretical paper, Doyle proposed that how women walk creates supernormal stimuli through continuously alternating motion of the waist and hips causing peak shifts in perceptions of physical attractiveness involving women's waist-to-hip ratio (WHR). [18] Furthermore, Pazhoohi et al. (2019) using eye tracking confirmed that lower than optimal WHRs are supernormal stimuli and they may generate peak shifts in responding. [19]

在一项跨文化研究中，道尔（Doyle）和帕兹（Pazhoohi）指出，外科隆胸是超常刺激，无论大小如何，它们都比天然乳房更具吸引力。 [17]另外，在一次理论论文中，道尔（Doyle）提出，妇女的行走方式是通过持续交替的腰部和臀部运动来产生超常刺激，从而引起涉及女性腰臀比（WHR）的身体吸引力感知的峰值移动。 [18]此外，Pazhoohi等人。 （2019）使用眼动追踪证实，低于最佳WHRs是超常刺激，它们可能会在响应中产生峰值偏移。 [19]

Costa and Corazza (2006), [20] examining 776 artistic portraits covering the whole history of art, showed that eye roundness, lip roundness, eye height, eye width, and lip height were significantly enhanced in artistic portraits compared to photographic ones matched for sex and age. In a second study, forty-two art academy students were requested to draw two self-portraits, one with a mirror and one without (from memory). Eye and lip size and roundness were greater in artistic self-portraits. These results show that the exaggeration and "supernormalization" of key features linked to attractiveness, such as eye and lip size, are frequently found in art. Pazhoohi et al. (2019) showed that classic contrapposto pose is considered more attractive and provided evidence and insight as to why, in artistic presentation, goddesses of beauty and love are often depicted in contrapposto pose. [21]

Costa and Corazza（2006）[20]对涵盖整个艺术史的776幅艺术肖像进行了研究，结果表明，与相匹配的摄影肖像相比，艺术肖像中的眼睛圆度，嘴唇圆度，眼睛高度，眼睛宽度和嘴唇高度得到了显着提高。性别和年龄。在第二项研究中，四十二名艺术学院的学生被要求画两幅自画像，一幅有镜子，一幅没有（自记忆）。在艺术自画像中，眼睛和嘴唇的大小和圆度更大。这些结果表明，夸张和“超标准化”。与吸引力相关的关键特征之一，例如眼睛和嘴唇的大小，在艺术中很常见。 Pazhoohi等。 （2019）表明，经典的骗子姿势被认为更具吸引力，并提供了证据和见解，说明为什么在艺术表现形式中，常常在骗子姿势中描绘出美丽和爱的女神。 [21]
