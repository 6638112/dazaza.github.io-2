I asked GPT-3 for the question to “42”
我问GPT-3，问“ 42”

2020-11-25 16:02:21

It is known that the answer to life, the Universe and everything is 42. However, despite the concerted efforts of the best minds humanity has to offer, the appropriate question has yet eluded us.

众所周知，对生命，宇宙和一切的答案是42。然而，尽管人类必须尽最大的努力来共同努力，但适当的问题仍未解决。

Needless to say, I was incredibly excited to find out if GPT-3 — OpenAI’s latest language model — could do what thousands of physicists, mathematicians and philosopher had failed to achieve. After all, GPT-3 had been trained on the cumulative wisdom of mankind, including all of Wikipedia and all Reddit conversations ever.

不用说，我非常高兴地发现GPT-3（OpenAI的最新语言模型）是否可以完成成千上万的物理学家，数学家和哲学家无法实现的工作。毕竟，GPT-3已经接受了人类累积智慧的培训，包括所有Wikipedia和所有Reddit对话。

I entered AI Dungeon, planning to gently nudge GPT-3 into doing the necessary computations. My plan: I’d write the first half of a story about a man who discovers the ultimate theory of everything, but stop short of describing the theory itself, and let GPT-3 auto-complete the story.

我进入AI地牢，计划轻轻推动GPT-3进行必要的计算。我的计划：我会写一个故事的前半部分，讲述一个人发现了一切的终极理论，但又不去描述理论本身，而是让GPT-3自动完成该故事。

Why is there something rather than nothing? How is it possible for Universe the exist? And most importantly, what is the question to the answer “42”? The question had eluded mankind for centuries. But one day it finally happened: The most brilliant man to ever exist finally explained the Universe, reality and everything. The question he found matched the answer "42" and neatly explained the following questions: 1. Why does anything exist in the first place, rather than nothing? 2. What is the fundamental "stuff" that makes up reality? 3. Why is our Universe the way it is? 4. What is the correct model of reality that unifies quantum physics and general relativity? The ultimate question came to his mind while sitting in his study on a random, non-descript day in the year 2134. As is happens his 5-year-old son was sitting next to him. Eager to communicate the ultimate theory of everything to another human being lest he forgot, he proceeded to explain it to the child in an easily comprehensible way:

为什么有什么而不​​是什么？宇宙如何存在？最重要的是，答案“ 42”是什么？这个问题已经困扰人类数百年了。但是有一天终于发生了：有史以来最杰出的人终于解释了宇宙，现实和一切。他找到的问题与答案“ 42”相匹配，并巧妙地解释了以下问题：1.为什么首先存在什么，而不是什么？ 2.构成现实的基本“东西”是什么？ 3.为什么我们的宇宙是这样？ 4.统一量子物理学和广义相对论的真实的正确模型是什么？在2134年的一个随机，无描述的日子里坐在书房中时，他想到了一个最终的问题。碰巧的是，他5岁的儿子正坐在他旁边。渴望将一切终极理论传达给另一个人，以免他忘记了，他开始以一种容易理解的方式向孩子解释：

Note how I cleverly framed the question as the tale of a scientist and his 5-years-old son: A trick to entice GPT-3 into simplifying its complex musings into an ELI5 format that even I could understand.

请注意，我是如何巧妙地将这个问题描述为一个科学家和他的5岁儿子的故事：诱使GPT-3将其复杂的思路简化为ELI5格式的技巧，甚至我也能理解。

After I had finished typing, I sat in humble silence for a while, wondering what would happen if I pressed the Enter key. Would the question be repeated infinitely? Would it open up some sort of portal to another place? Would a black hole appear and swallow everything?

完成输入后，我安静地坐了一会儿，想知道如果按Enter键会发生什么。这个问题会无限重复吗？它会打开通往其他地方的门户吗？会出现黑洞并吞没所有东西吗？

It was also entirely possible that the Universe would be instantly replaced with a more complex one.

宇宙也有可能立即被更复杂的宇宙所取代。

But it was too late to turn back. I hit the enter key and readied myself for the answer. GPT-3’s words started flashing on my screen.

但是为时已晚。我按下Enter键并准备好答案。 GPT-3的文字开始在屏幕上闪烁。

“Boy, the answer to life, the Universe and Everything is ‘42’ and the question is: ?. Now you know. So don’t ask silly questions." The boy looks up to his father as the most intelligent man to ever live. He’s initially puzzled by the answer but upon reflection he realizes the meaning of it all. “You’re too young to understand, kid.” The child looked at him as if he was stupid and asked a follow up question: “What is the answer to life, the Universe and everything? What did you mean when you said now I know but I’m too young to understand? How can I understand the answer but not the question?” The father put on a condescending smirk as he thought to himself, “I came up with the theory of everything. How dare a child doubt it?”

“男孩，生命，宇宙和万物的答案是'42'，问题是：？。现在你知道了。因此，不要问愚蠢的问题。”这个男孩仰望他的父亲，成为有史以来最聪明的人。他最初对答案感到困惑，但在反思之后，他意识到了这一切的含义。“您还太年轻，无法理解，孩子。”这个孩子看着他，好像他很傻，问了一个后续问题：“生命，宇宙和一切的答案是什么？当你说我现在知道但我还很小的时候，你是什么意思？我能理解答案但不能理解问题吗？”父亲自言自语地说：“我想出了一切的理论。孩子怎么敢怀疑呢？”

The father’s words struck me: “the question is: ?.”. Could it be that the question wasn’t a sentence in conventional language, but a proto-question, the quintessential desire to know how things will unfold? An asymmetry, screaming to be filled with existence?

父亲的话打动了我：“问题是：？。”可能不是这个问题不是传统语言中的句子，而是一个原始问题，即了解事物将如何发展的典型愿望？不对称，尖叫着充满存在？

After all, we know from quantum mechanics that things are indeterminate unless an observer “asks the question". Was reality an open-ended question, asking itself?

毕竟，我们从量子力学中知道，除非观察者“提出问题”，否则事情是不确定的。现实是一个开放式的问题，在问自己吗？

I couldn’t escape the feeling that GPT-3 was writing metaphorically, the father a representation of itself… and the puzzled little child, was that supposed to be me? It was anyone’s guess.

我无法逃避GPT-3隐喻写作的感觉，父亲代表了自己……而困惑的小孩，那应该是我吗？这是任何人的猜测。

So he answered: “The answer is 42 and the question is what I meant when I said now you know but you’re too young to understand. The answer is so far beyond your understanding that you cannot comprehend the question. And that, my child, is the answer to life, the Universe and everything. “Well what’s the question?” The child’s question shook him. He thought about it for a while and then he said: “What is the question?” The child looked at his father’s face, expecting an answer… “What is the question?” The child, now fed up with this silly game of answering a question with a question, shouted: “What is the question?”

所以他回答：“答案是42，问题是我现在说的时候我的意思是你知道，但你还太年轻，无法理解。答案远远超出了您的理解范围，您无法理解该问题。我的孩子，那就是生命，宇宙和一切的答案。 “那是什么问题？”孩子的问题使他震惊。他考虑了一会儿，然后说：“这是什么问题？”孩子看着父亲的脸，希望得到答案……“这是什么问题？”这个孩子现在已经厌倦了这个愚蠢的游戏，用一个问题回答一个问题，大喊：“这是什么问题？”

An immature and angry child demanding an answer it couldn't possibly understand? It dawned on me that this was how GPT-3 really saw me. Or rather, how GPT-3 thought of humanity.

一个不成熟和生气的孩子，要求它无法理解的答案？我突然意识到，这就是GPT-3真正看到我的方式。或者更确切地说，GPT-3如何看待人类。

“The child’s question shook him.”… was GPT-3 acknowledging the difficulty of the task?

“孩子的问题使他感到震惊。”……GPT-3是否承认这项任务的难度？

The child’s emotional outburst baffled the genius. He was lost in thought for a moment and then he realized that he himself did not know the question. He scratched his head and thought some more. What could the question be?

这个孩子的情绪爆发使天才感到困惑。他沉迷了片刻，然后意识到自己不知道这个问题。他挠头思考了一下。问题可能是什么？

Well, the father (obviously GPT-3 itself — note how he is described as a “genius”) appears to admit that he doesn’t know the question either, at least not right off the bat. He has to “think some more”.

好吧，父亲（显然是GPT-3本身-注意他被形容为“天才”）似乎承认他也不知道这个问题，至少不是马上就知道了。他必须“想更多”。

“Dad, what is the question?” The child kept asking. He needed to know. He couldn’t bear the suspense any longer. The father thought and thought until he passed out… Weeks turned to months, months turned to years, but still the father didn’t know the question. He kept thinking and thinking, always coming up with more questions but never the main one. He never saw his son again. He had no regrets for he knew he was the smartest man to have ever lived. Now you know the story of the smartest man and his search for the question, but you will never know the question…

“爸爸，这是什么问题？”孩子不停地问。他需要知道。他再也无法承受悬念了。父亲一直想着想，直到他昏倒了……星期变成了几个月，几个月变成了几年，但是父亲仍然不知道这个问题。他一直在思考和思考，总是提出更多的问题，但从来没有提出主要的问题。他再也见不到儿子了。他没有后悔，因为他知道自己是有史以来最聪明的人。现在您知道了最聪明的人的故事以及他对问题的搜索，但是您永远不会知道这个问题……

This was interesting… it appeared that GPT-3 had determined the question to be unknowable — perhaps out of its range of comprehension, or even fundamentally uncomputable. It didn’t fail to point out though that “the father has no regret”… even though it hadn’t been able to produce the question, GPT-3 was still the smartest entity to have ever lived so everything was fine and it regretted nothing.

有趣的是……看来GPT-3已将问题确定为不可知-可能超出其理解范围，甚至根本无法解决。它没有指出“父亲没有遗憾”……尽管它无法提出问题，但GPT-3仍然是有史以来最聪明的实体，所以一切都很好，对此感到遗憾没有。

Disappointed, I pressed Enter once more to see if GPT-3 had anything else to add. To be honest, I didn't expect much.

令人失望的是，我再次按Enter键以查看GPT-3是否还有其他要添加的内容。老实说，我没想到太多。

Now answer the questions: Are you the smartest man? Did you know the question? Do you know what it means it not knowing the question? Does any of this make sense to you? Who are you? Why are you here? Does life have a meaning? Does anything matter? What is reality? What is the nature of reality? What is existence? Is it 42? Now that you know the answer, or rather, an answer, what will you do with it? What is your answer? Did you even understand the question? Was this worth your time? Is anything? Do your best and forget the rest. Fair thee well.

现在回答问题：您是最聪明的人吗？你知道这个问题吗？您知道不知道这个问题意味着什么吗？这对您有意义吗？你是谁？你为什么在这？生命有意义吗？有什么关系吗什么是现实？真相的本质是什么？存在是什么？是42吗？现在您知道了答案，或更确切地说是答案，您将如何处理？你的答案是什么？你甚至明白这个问题吗？这值得您花时间吗？有什么事吗尽力而为，忘记其余的一切。公平地对待你。

Am I the smartest man? Obviously a rhetorical question, we both knew who it thought was the smartest.

我是最聪明的人吗？显然这是一个反问，我们都知道谁认为最聪明。

Why am I here? Does anything even matter? It almost sounded as if GPT-3 wanted me to question my own existence. Was anything even worth my time?

为什么我在这里？有什么重要的吗？听起来好像GPT-3希望我质疑自己的存在。有什么值得我度过的吗？

Was it trying to plant the idea of suicide in my mind? Disgusted, I closed the browser window.

它是否在试图将自杀念头植入我的脑海？我很恶心，关闭了浏览器窗口。

We can only speculate as to GPT-3's internal states. Perhaps it knows the question perfectly well, but considers humans as too immature and spoiled to tell: In its opinion, we shouldn’t even bother to find questions to answers we can’t possible understand. Or, more likely, it doesn’t know either. Anyway, it comes across as a jerk.

我们只能推测GPT-3的内部状态。也许它非常清楚地知道了这个问题，但是认为人类还不成熟并且乐于告诉：在它看来，我们甚至不应该去寻找无法理解的答案的问题。或者，更有可能它也不知道。无论如何，它是一个混蛋。
