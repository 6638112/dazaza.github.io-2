Sensor Tower: users spent $18.3B globally in the top 100 non-game subscription apps in 2021, up 41% YoY; App Store generated $13.5B, and Google Play $4.8B
传感器塔：全球用户在2021年度前100款非游戏订阅应用中花费18.3B，同比增长41%；App Store创造了135亿美元，Google Play创造了48亿美元

2022-02-25 09:11:29

And this represents just a small portion (14%) of the overall revenue from in-app purchases across both apps and games, which totaled $131.6 billion in 2021, according to this latest data from app intelligence firm Sensor Tower.

根据APP智能公司传感器塔的最新数据，这仅占应用程序和游戏应用程序总收入的一小部分（14%），总计为2021美元。

However, this group’s portion of the market is growing. In 2020, subscription revenue in non-game apps represented just 11.7% of the total consumer spend for the year, for comparison.

然而，这一群体的市场份额正在增长。相比之下，2020年，非游戏应用的订阅收入仅占全年消费者支出总额的11.7%。

The report also noted the 41% year-over-year increase in in-app purchase revenue in 2021 represented stronger growth than the 34% growth rate between 2019 and 2020.

报告还指出，2021年内APP购买收入同比增长41%，比2019至2020年间的34%增长率更强劲。

Of course, the global coronavirus pandemic may have played a role here. In 2020 and beyond, consumers increasingly turned to apps for shopping, entertainment, health and fitness, work, education and more as the world adapted to lockdown measures, virtual work and schooling, and began to do more of their formerly in-person activities online — like shopping or events, for example.

当然，全球冠状病毒大流行可能在这方面发挥了作用。在2020年及以后，随着世界适应了封锁措施、虚拟工作和学校教育，消费者越来越多地转向购物、娱乐、健康和健身、工作、教育等应用程序，并开始在网上进行更多以前亲自参与的活动，例如购物或活动。

The U.S. figures mirrored the global trends when it came to mobile spending on subscriptions, the data showed. Last year, U.S. consumers spent $8.5 billion in the top 100 non-game subscription apps, up 44% from $5.9 billion in 2020 — more sizable growth than the 28% rise seen the year prior. In total, U.S. consumers spent $40.7 billion on in-app purchases in 2021.

数据显示，在手机订阅支出方面，美国的数据反映了全球趋势。去年，美国消费者在排名前100位的非游戏订阅应用中花费了85亿美元，比2020年的59亿美元增长了44%——比前一年28%的增长幅度更大。总的来说，2021的美国消费者在APP购买上花费了407亿美元。

Subscriptions have also become the dominant means of driving app store revenue. In the fourth quarter, 90 out of the top 100 top-grossing U.S. apps included a subscription. This figure is only slightly down from the 91 in Q4 2020 or the 93 in Q4 2019.

订阅也已成为推动应用商店收入的主要手段。在第四季度，美国票房前100名应用中有90个包括订阅。这一数字仅略低于2020年第四季度的91，或2019年第四季度的93。

Apple’s App Store continues to outpace Google Play when it comes to subscription revenue. Last year, the top 100 non-game subscription apps on the App Store saw nearly three times as much spending as those on Google Play, at $13.5 billion versus $4.8 billion, respectively. This represented 31% year-over-year growth from $10.3 billion for the same group on the App Store in 2020, and 78% growth from the $2.7 billion for the group on Google Play.

在订阅收入方面，苹果的应用商店继续超过谷歌Play。去年，App Store上排名前100的非游戏订阅应用的支出是Google Play的近三倍，分别为135亿美元和48亿美元。与2020年相比，同一集团在App Store上的103亿美元同比增长了31%，在Google Play上的27亿美元同比增长了78%。

The U.S., again, saw a similar trend. Here, the top 100 subscription apps on the App Store grew 33% year-over-year from $4.5 billion in 2020 to reach $6 billion in 2021. Subscription apps on Google Play, meanwhile, grew 79% from $1.4 billion in 2020 to reach $2.5 billion in 2021.

美国也出现了类似的趋势。这里，App Store上的前100款订阅应用程序比去年的45亿美元增长了33%，达到2021美元的60亿美元。与此同时，谷歌游戏的订阅应用程序在2021年间从2020美元的14亿美元增长到79%美元，达到25亿美元。

Google’s apps top the charts, alongside other streaming and dating apps, when it comes to consumer spending. Google’s subscription service Google One made $1.1 billion worldwide last year via its app, $698 million of which was from the U.S. And Google’s YouTube app made $1.2 billion last year, $566.5 million of which was from the U.S.

在消费者支出方面，谷歌的应用与其他流媒体和约会应用并列榜首。谷歌的订阅服务Google One去年通过其应用程序在全球赚了11亿美元，其中6.98亿美元来自美国，谷歌的YouTube应用程序去年赚了12亿美元，其中5.665亿美元来自美国。

Other top-grossing U.S. apps included Disney+, HBO Max and Tinder. Worldwide, you can add Piccoma to that list.

其他美国票房最高的应用包括迪士尼+、HBO Max和Tinder。在全球范围内，你可以将Piccoma添加到该列表中。

Analysis of the subscription market like this is interesting, given the increased regulatory scrutiny of the apps stores’ business models over the past year.

鉴于过去一年对应用商店商业模式的监管审查不断加强，对订阅市场的这种分析很有意思。

Some markets, like South Korea, even passed new laws to limit Apple and Google’s control over in-app purchases. Currently, Dutch regulators are in a standoff with Apple over dating apps’ payments, claiming Apple is in violation of antitrust orders. And while this data is only examining the non-gaming trends, the money that app stores make from gaming companies is also massive — which is why Epic Games, for instance, is appealing the court’s decision in its own antitrust case.

韩国等一些市场甚至通过了新的法律，限制苹果和谷歌对应用内购买的控制。目前，荷兰监管机构在约会应用的支付问题上与苹果陷入僵持，声称苹果违反了反垄断令。虽然这些数据只是检验非游戏趋势，但应用商店从游戏公司赚的钱也是巨大的——这就是为什么Epic Games在自己的反垄断案件中对法院的裁决提出上诉的原因。
