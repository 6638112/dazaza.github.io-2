Why did it take me so long? One big reason: tutorials. At the time (2004), the blender community was small and so there weren't many resources for learning. There was a few text tutorials floating around the web, but they were hard to find. So because of this, I had to do most of my learning through trial and error, which was  incredibly inefficient. If there's one thing I hope you learn from my experience it's that tutorials are like gold when you're learning. You can learn more in one day with tutorials, than 2 months without them. Seriously. Because I struggled to learn without tutorials, I know how important they are. So I've spent the last 8 years creating the set of tutorials I wish I had when I first started learning Blender.
