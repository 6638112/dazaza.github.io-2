In a blog post, Twitter said global tests of the feature indicated the tool helped people feel more comfortable joining public conversations on the service.

Twitter在博客中表示，对该功能进行了全球测试，表明该工具使人们在加入该服务的公共对话时感到更加自在。

"Those new to Twitter found Fleets to be an easier way to share what's on their mind,"  the company said. "Because they disappear from view after a day, Fleets helped people feel more comfortable sharing personal and casual thoughts, opinions and feelings."

该公司表示：``Twitter的新手发现Fleets是一种更轻松的方式分享他们的想法。'' “由于他们一天后就消失了，舰队使人们在分享个人和随意的想法，见解和感觉时感到更加自在。”

The feature's debut Tuesday brought its share of complaints about the product, with some people saying the Fleets froze, lagged or  made their Twitter crash. "We're aware of some issues people may be having and are working to fix them," a Twitter spokesperson said. Twitter didn't have numbers to share for the first day of Fleeting but suggested that it's watching the response closely.  "Some of you hating... but we see you Fleeting," the company tweeted.

该功能的首次亮相周二引起了对该产品的抱怨，有人说舰队冻结，落后或使他们的Twitter崩溃。 Twitter发言人说：“我们知道人们可能遇到的一些问题，并正在努力解决。” Twitter在Fleeting上市的第一天就没有数字可分享，但表示正在密切关注回应。该公司在推特上写道：“你们当中有些人讨厌……但我们看到您在流浪。”
