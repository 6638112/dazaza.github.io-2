Jump to navigation Jump to search The following table shows the aspect ratio (height to width ratio) of national flags used by countries and dependencies. Variant flags such as ensigns are listed in the "Alternative flags" column if they have different proportions from the national flag. Territories without an official flag distinct from that of their controlling country are excluded.

跳转到导航跳转以搜索下表显示了国家和属地使用的国旗的纵横比(高宽比)。不同的国旗，如国旗，如果它们与国旗的比例不同，则列在备选国旗列中。没有与其控制国不同的官方旗帜的领土被排除在外。

The ratios most commonly used are 2:3, used by 85 of 195 sovereign states, followed by 1:2, used by 54 sovereign states. Most dependencies and former colonies use the same proportions as their mother countries: all British Overseas Territories use 1:2 ratios, while the flags of most former and current Dutch and French areas have 2:3 proportions.

最常用的比例是2：3,195个主权国家中有85个使用，其次是1：2，54个主权国家使用。大多数属地和前殖民地使用与其母国相同的比例：所有英国海外领土使用1：2的比例，而大多数以前和现在的荷兰和法国地区的国旗使用2：3的比例。

For comparison, the ratios are also given as a decimal number, which is the flag width divided by its height (e.g. a 2:3 flag has a decimal ratio of 3 / 2 = 1.5). Flags with irrational ratios have only a decimal approximation, and have the exact form given in the "Notes" column.

为便于比较，这些比率也以十进制数字给出，即旗帜宽度除以其高度(例如，2：3旗帜的十进制比率为3/2=1.5)。具有无理比率的标志只有一个小数近似值，其格式与注释一栏中给出的格式完全相同。

None of the thirteen overseas regions and territories of France except French Polynesia, New Caledonia and the French Southern and Antarctic Lands have official flags other than the French tricolor. A flag for the department of French Guiana has been officially adopted, though this flag has not been approved by the regional council. Most of the other territories however have unofficial flags, and some of these flags are used locally. See Flags of the regions of France.

除法属波利尼西亚、新喀里多尼亚和法属南极洲外，法国的13个海外地区和领地中，除法国三色旗外，没有其他地区和领地有官方旗帜。法属圭亚那的一面旗帜已经被正式采用，尽管这面旗帜还没有得到地区议会的批准。然而，大多数其他地区都有非官方旗帜，其中一些是当地使用的。请参见法国各地区的国旗。

Akrotiri and Dhekelia, Sovereign Base Areas of the United Kingdom, don't have an official flag other than the Union Jack. A reported green flag defaced with two golden lions is actually the flag of the Dhekelia Garrison, and is not an official flag of the entire territory. [103]

阿克罗蒂里(Akrotiri)和迪凯利亚(Dhekelia)是英国的主权根据地，除了英国国旗，没有其他的官方旗帜。据报道，一面印有两只金狮的绿色旗帜实际上是Dhekelia驻军的旗帜，而不是整个领土的官方旗帜。[103][font=宋体]。

The British Overseas Territory of Saint Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha has no separate flag and uses the Union Jack. The three constituent parts have their own flags, which all follow the "blue ensign with territorial arms" pattern of most other BOTs. See Flag of Saint Helena, Flag of Ascension Island and Flag of Tristan da Cunha.

英国海外领地圣赫勒拿、阿森松和特里斯坦-达库尼亚没有单独的国旗，使用英国国旗。这三个组成部分都有自己的旗帜，它们都遵循着大多数其他机器人的蓝色旗帜和领地武器的图案。见圣赫勒拿国旗、阿森松岛国旗和特里斯坦-达库尼亚国旗。

The nine United States Minor Outlying Islands don't have official flags, though there are unofficial local flags for five of the islands and island groups ( Johnston Atoll, the Midway Islands, Palmyra Atoll, Wake Island and Navassa Island).

美国九个较小的离岛没有官方旗帜，但其中五个岛屿和岛群(约翰斯顿环礁、中途岛、帕尔米拉环礁、维克岛和纳瓦萨岛)有非官方旗帜。

Antarctica has no government and as such can't officially adopt a flag, though there are two proposed flags widely used to represent the continent: a blue flag by Graham Bartram and an orange flag by Whitney Smith. See Flags of Antarctica.

南极洲没有政府，因此不能正式采用国旗，尽管有两面国旗被广泛用于代表南极洲：格雷厄姆·巴特伦(Graham Bartram)设计的蓝色国旗和惠特尼·史密斯(Whitney Smith)设计的橙色国旗。参见南极洲旗帜。
