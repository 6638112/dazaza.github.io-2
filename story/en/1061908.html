Engageli nabs $33M more for its collaborative video-based teaching platform
Engageli Nabs为其协同视频教学平台提供3300万美元

2021-05-11 18:26:46

Engageli, which has built an online teaching platform from the ground up — providing not just its own built-in-house video technology to deliver lectures and enable conversations, but tools to enable students to “sit” in study groups to work together; and features to share and annotate lecture notes, take quizzes and more — has picked up $33 million in funding.

Engageli，它已经建立了一个从地上建立了一个在线教学平台 - 不仅提供了自己的内置视频技术，可以提供讲座和启用对话，而是使学生在学习团体中“坐下”的工具;分享和注释讲座的功能，采取测验和更多 - 已经挑选了3300万美元的资金。

CEO Dan Avida — who co-founded the company with his wife Daphne Koller (the Coursera co-founder) and Serge Plotkin — said the startup will be using to continue building more tools and scaling its platform and opening it up to more schools, specifically the bracket of higher learning institutions that it targets as customers, as they continue to embrace the promise of better video tools both for delivering live lessons and also bringing in more features to improve how to use Engageli’s video and other tools on demand.

CEO DAN AVIDA - 与他的妻子Daphne Koller（Coursera联合创始人）和Serge Plotkin共同创立了公司 - 表示，启动将使用该公司将继续建立更多的工具并缩放其平台并将其缩小到更多学校，具体地将其开放到更多学校高等学校机构的括号，它以客户为目标，因为他们继续接受更好的视频工具的承诺，都是为了提供现场课程，并带来更多功能以改善如何使用Engageli的视频和其他工具。

“At first the priority was on the best synchronous experience,” he said in an interview. “Now everyone is much more focused on multi-modality.”

“首先，优先事项是最好的同步体验，”他在接受采访时说。 “现在每个人都更专注于多种方式。”

The funding, a Series A, is being co-led by Maveron and another (unnamed) investor and also includes participation from Corner Ventures, Good Friends, Educapital, and what Engageli describes as several “prominent individual technology executives.” The funding is coming only seven months after the startup first emerged from stealth in October 2020, and investors from the $14.5 million seed round that it announced at the same time are also participating. The startup has now raised over $47 million and is not disclosing its valuation.

资金是一个系列A，由Maveron和另一个（未命名）的投资者联合领导，并包括来自角落企业，好朋友，教育和Engageli的参与描述为几个“突出的个人技术高管”。在2020年10月，初创公司首次出现的初创物中只有七个月，投资者来自1450万美元的种子，它同时宣布的投资者也在参加。创业公司现已筹集超过4700万美元，并未披露其估值。

While there are now dozens, maybe even hundreds, of tools to help students learn things without being inside a traditional classroom, Engageli has taken a slightly different approach from the pack by building its video-based platform from the ground up with educational aims in mind. This is already a step change when you think about it from the likes of Teams from Microsoft, Google Classroom, or Zoom, probably three of the most commonly used video platforms in educational settings, but technology that was, essentially, originally built with more enterprise and generic purposes in mind.

虽然现在有几十个，但甚至有数百种工具，帮助学生在没有传统教室内部学习的工具，但Engageli通过在与教育目标的基础上建立基于视频的平台，从包装中取出了略微不同的方法。当您从Microsoft，Google教室或缩放的团队中思考时，这已经是一个步骤变化，可能是教育设置中最常用的视频平台中的三个，但基本上是最初的企业的技术和普遍用途的思想。

From this, Engageli has worked on expanding the platform with tools that enhance not just the video experience, but enhance it in ways that make sense for educators and learners. Up to now, these have focused on both the kinds of conversations that students can have with each other and the teacher, and ways for the teacher to keep their students engaged, by way of quizzes, notes that they can download and annotate and Q&A channels. Over time you can see how these can become the building blocks for linking in with bigger learning management systems and the other tools that institutions might already be using regularly.

从这一点来看，Engageli在扩展平台上，用工具来增强不仅仅是视频体验，而是以一种对教育者和学习者有意义的方式增强它。到目前为止，这些都集中在学生互相拥有的谈话中，以及教师让学生通过测验来遵守学生的方式，注意他们可以下载和注释和Q＆amp;渠道。随着时间的推移，您可以看出这些可以成为与更大的学习管理系统相关联的构建块以及机构可能已经定期使用的其他工具。

Avida said that Engageli is not yet disclosing any metrics on engagement time, customer or user numbers or any other figures. For now, he said the startup is picking up university customers across the U.S., in the UK and Israel (where the founders hail from and all originally cut their tech teeth in the country’s military units), and that classes are handling up to “hundreds” of concurrent students. He also added that even as more schools return to in-person learning, he is expecting a boost of new users in the fall quarter because the genie, so to speak, is out of the bottle with remote learning and the fact that it can continue to be effective.

Avida表示，Engageli还没有披露参与时间，客户或用户号码或任何其他数据的任何指标。目前，他表示，启动是在英国和以色列的大学客户上挑选大学客户（那里的创始人们在该国的军事单位中抚养了他们的技术牙齿），并且该课程正在处理“数百人” “并发学生。他还补充说，随着更多的学校回归人员学习，他期待在秋季的新用户提高新用户，因为Genie，所以要说的是远程学习的瓶子，而且它可以继续有效。

“Even before the pandemic there were tens of millions of students online, half of all students were taking some form of online course, and we expect this to go much further, not unlike online grocery or telemedicine,” he said. “One professor described it to me like this: the trough of disillusionment” — a reference to the Gartner Magic Quadrant visualization — “is very shallow here. We ain’t going back.”

“甚至在大流行前有数百万学生在线，所有学生的一半都在占地某种形式的在线课程，我们希望这进一步走得更远，而不是与在线杂货或远程医疗不同，”他说。 “一个教授给我描述了这一点：幻灭的低谷” - 对Gartner魔法象限可视化的引用 - “在这里非常浅。我们不会回来。“

“It’s hard to go back once you raise the bar on engagement. With Engageli, I felt the experience was the most like a real classroom. Students are sitting at tables, I can quickly see what they are doing, they can ask others at their table questions, they are chatting and interacting,” said Dr. Theodora Christou, a professor at Queen Mary University of London, in a statement. “I finally have an easy way to lead meaningful group work and case studies online. I would choose Engageli over any other existing tool that my university offers.”

一旦你在订婚吧，就很难回去。与Engageli，我觉得这一体验最像真正的教室。学生们正坐在桌前，我可以快速看看他们在做什么，他们可以在他们的表格问题上询问别人，他们正在聊天和互动，“伦敦女王玛丽大学教授在一份声明中说道。 “我终于有一种简单的方法来领导有意义的群体工作和在线案例研究。我会在大学提供的任何其他现有工具上选择Engageli。“

The company’s funding and growth come at a time when we’re indeed seeing a wider wholesale adoption and development of more tools to accommodate digital modes of learning, in many cases also to complement what is happening offline, and also in younger age groups, too. Just in the last week, Kahoot acquired Clever in the U.S. to bring on a popular platform used by many K-12 schools to manage their online learning interctions, and yesterday StuDocu raised funding for a platform that crowdsources, rates and shares university class notes, a platform that has now passed 15 million students and is growing very fast. All of these spell higher expectations for better technology in the future, something Engageli will also be hoping to engage.
