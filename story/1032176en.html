确定性整数因式分解的指数五分之一算法

2020-10-30 07:01:02

Download PDF Abstract: Hittmeir recently presented a deterministic algorithm that provably computesthe prime factorisation of a positive integer $N$ in $N^{2/9+o(1)}$ bitoperations. Prior to this breakthrough, the best known complexity bound forthis problem was $N^{1/4+o(1)}$, a result going back to the 1970s. In thispaper we push Hittmeir's techniques further, obtaining a rigorous,deterministic factoring algorithm with complexity $N^{1/5+o(1)}$.

下载PDF摘要：Hittmeir最近提出了一个确定性算法，该算法可证明在$N^{2/9+o(1)}$位运算中计算正整数$N$的素因式分解。在这一突破之前，这个问题最广为人知的复杂性界限是$N^{1/4+o(1)}$，这一结果可以追溯到20世纪70年代。在这篇文章中，我们进一步推广了Hittmeir的技术，得到了一个严格的、确定性的分解算法，其复杂度为$N^{1/5+o(1)}$。
