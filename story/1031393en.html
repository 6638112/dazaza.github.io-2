获得15000美元，搬到密歇根州并远程工作

2020-10-27 10:28:13

Amid the pandemic, buyers have been flocking to the lakefront crescent in southwest Michigan and northwest Indiana often called Harbor Country. The Move to Michigan deal offers incentives to look slightly farther north up the Lake Michigan coastline. The southernmost ZIP code it covers, 49106 in Bridgman, is 86 miles from downtown Chicago.

在大流行期间，买家一直蜂拥至密歇根州西南部和印第安纳州西北部的湖滨新月，通常被称为海港之乡(Harbor Country)。搬到密歇根州的交易提供了激励人们将目光投向密歇根湖海岸线上稍微靠北的地方。它覆盖的最南端的邮政编码是布里奇曼的49106，距离芝加哥市中心86英里。

While farther from Chicago than, say, New Buffalo (71 miles from downtown Chicago), these towns have restaurant districts and nice beaches, including the spectacular sand dunes at Warren Dunes State Park near Bridgman and Silver Beach County Park in St. Joseph. The area also includes some economically depressed areas, such as in Benton Harbor.

虽然这些城镇距离芝加哥比新布法罗(距离芝加哥市中心71英里)更远，但有餐饮区和美丽的海滩，包括布里奇曼附近的沃伦沙丘州立公园和圣约瑟夫的银滩县公园壮观的沙丘。该地区还包括一些经济不景气的地区，比如本顿港。

The $15,000 grant is forgivable at the rate of $5,000 per year—which means for each full year the recipient stays in Michigan, the debt goes down by $5,000, until it’s gone after three years.

这笔15000美元的赠款以每年5000美元的速度是可以免除的-这意味着受助人每在密歇根州呆一整年，债务就会减少5000美元，直到三年后债务就会消失。

The money comes with some stipulations: It's available only to people who: • are not already living in Michigan; • pledge to get a Michigan driver's license and become permanent Michigan residents, and • certify that they work for a company outside southwest Michigan.

这笔钱有一些规定：它只适用于以下人员：·尚未居住在密歇根州；·承诺获得密歇根州驾照并成为密歇根州永久居民；以及·证明他们在密歇根州西南部以外的一家公司工作。

“We have thousands of people who come over here from Chicago on weekends. Why not come over and live in a beautiful place?” said Rob Cleveland, president and CEO of Cornerstone Alliance.

“我们有数以千计的人周末从芝加哥来到这里。为什么不过来住在一个美丽的地方呢？“基石联盟总裁兼首席执行官罗布·克利夫兰说。

Cleveland said that while his group ordinarily recruits businesses to locate in the region, the remote-working phenomenon pointed toward a different approach: “If you can’t recruit Google, go recruit a Google engineer.”

克利夫兰说，虽然他的团队通常会招募企业在该地区开展业务，但远程工作现象指向了一种不同的方式：“如果你招不到谷歌，那就去招聘一名谷歌工程师。”

Along with the cash, the Move to Michigan incentive package includes a menu of other offers, from which buyers choose options worth $5,000. They include use of a discount program for Whirlpool appliances, a one-year free pass on the South Shore Line that runs from Chicago to South Bend, Ind., a free membership in a Benton Harbor fitness facility, free use of the driving range at Harbor Shores in Benton Harbor, and car service to local airports for a year.

除了现金，搬到密歇根州的激励方案还包括一份其他优惠的菜单，买家可以从菜单中选择价值5000美元的选项。这些措施包括使用惠而浦电器的折扣计划，从芝加哥到印第安纳州南本德的南岸线一年免费通行证，本顿港健身设施的免费会员资格，免费使用本顿港港口海岸公园的练习场，以及为期一年的当地机场用车服务。

Those incentives are designed to “make moving here easier,” Cleveland said. The idea is to help newcomers get settled into using local amenities, like the Welcome Wagon of old.

克利夫兰说，这些激励措施旨在“让搬到这里更容易”。这个想法是为了帮助新来的人适应使用当地的便利设施，比如旧的欢迎旅行车。

People who move to the area might remain employed elsewhere, Cleveland said, “but they’ll have kids they put in the schools, they’ll spend time at the breweries. That’s how we build community.”

克利夫兰说，搬到这个地区的人可能会继续在其他地方工作，“但他们会把孩子送到学校，他们会在酿酒厂度过一段时间。这就是我们建立社区的方式。“。

At not far over the $200,000 minimum, a buyer could get t his four-bedroom, 1,700-square-foot house in St. Joseph, priced at $209,900, or in Bridgman, this new-construction three-bedroom, 1,460-square-footer priced at a little under $231,000.

以略高于20万美元的最低价格，购房者就可以买到他在圣约瑟夫(St.Joseph)的一套四居室1700平方英尺(约合2700平方米)的房子，售价为20.99万美元(约合人民币217万元)；或者在布里奇曼(Bridgman)，这套新建的三居室1460平方英尺(约合人民币227万平方米)的房子的售价略低于231,000美元(约合人民币23万元)
