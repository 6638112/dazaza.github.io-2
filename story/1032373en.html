YC创业学校，面向尚未做好创业准备的未来创业者

2020-10-31 04:14:51

We launched Startup School as an online program in 2017 to support founders actively working on building their companies. On November 9th, we’re also opening Startup School to potential founders who are interested in starting a company in the future. You can register for the new course today.

2017年，我们推出了创业学校(Startup School)在线项目，支持创始人积极创建自己的公司。11月9日，我们还将向未来有兴趣创办公司的潜在创始人开放创业学校(Startup School)。你今天可以报名参加新课程。

Startup School has been extremely successful at helping active founders grow their companies, serving over 140,000 founders to date! That said, our online program has never been a great fit for individuals who hadn’t yet decided what to work on — in fact, you couldn’t even complete registration if you weren’t actively working on a startup.

创业学校在帮助活跃的创始人发展他们的公司方面取得了极大的成功，到目前为止已经为超过14万名创始人提供了服务！这就是说，我们的在线项目从来都不太适合那些还没有决定要做什么的人-事实上，如果你不积极地从事一家初创公司的工作，你甚至无法完成注册。

So starting November 9th, we’re pleased to announce a new Startup School course, which we’re calling Startup School for Future Founders. This course will run for 6 weeks and is designed for students, engineers, creatives and anyone who is thinking about founding a startup at some point, but not actively working on one right now.

因此，从11月9日开始，我们很高兴地宣布一门新的创业学校课程，我们称之为面向未来创建者的创业学校(Startup School For Future Founders)。这门课程将持续6周，面向学生、工程师、创意人士和任何正在考虑在某个时候创建一家初创公司，但目前并不积极从事初创公司的人。

We’ve developed a new curriculum for this track targeted at future founders, including YC classics like How to Talk to Users and Before the Startup, as well as new lectures by YC partners like How to Find Incredible Startup Ideas. We’ve also built high-impact exercises to put those learnings into practice, covering key early topics such as generating good startup ideas, finding/vetting a co-founder and validating your idea with potential customers.

我们为这条赛道开发了面向未来创始人的新课程，包括YC经典课程，如如何与用户交谈和创业之前，以及YC合作伙伴的新讲座，如如何找到令人难以置信的创业想法。我们还建立了高影响力的练习来将这些知识付诸实践，涵盖了一些关键的早期主题，比如产生好的创业想法，寻找/审查联合创始人，以及向潜在客户验证你的想法。

When you’re ready to take the next step and actually start working on a company, Startup School can help there too. Future Founder participants can switch to an active founder profile at any time to take advantage of our partner deals, weekly updates, group sessions and expanded curriculum. We’re also very excited to launch a co-founder matching service for our community soon. There are thousands of founders on Startup School actively looking for a co-founder right now, so talented prospective founders won’t even need to come up with their own idea — they can just look for an existing team that they mesh with!

当你准备好迈出下一步并真正开始在一家公司工作时，创业学校也可以提供帮助。未来的创始人参与者可以随时切换到活跃的创始人简介，以利用我们的合作伙伴交易、每周更新、小组会议和扩展课程。我们也非常高兴能很快为我们的社区推出联合创始人配对服务。创业学校现在有成千上万的创始人在积极寻找联合创始人，所以有才华的潜在创始人甚至不需要想出自己的想法-他们只需寻找一个与他们契合的现有团队！

Finally, we’ve also been contacted by universities, incubators and entrepreneurship centers interested in incorporating Startup School content into their curriculum. The Startup School for Future Founders content and exercises are a great fit for universities looking to share market-tested entrepreneurship advice. Feel free to incorporate them into your curriculum! If you’re a professor or advisor interested in using our content, please let us know! We’d love to support you however we can.

最后，大学、孵化器和创业中心也与我们联系，希望将创业学校的内容纳入他们的课程。未来创业者创业学校的内容和练习非常适合希望分享经过市场检验的创业建议的大学。请随意将它们纳入您的课程！如果您是对使用我们的内容感兴趣的教授或顾问，请让我们知道！我们愿意尽我们所能支持你。

If you’re interested in starting a startup someday, or know someone who is, sign up for Startup School now.

如果你有兴趣在某一天创办一家初创公司，或者认识一些正在创业的人，现在就报名参加创业学校吧。
