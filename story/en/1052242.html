More San Francisco Tech Companies Cancel Leases Due to Remote Work
更多旧金山科技公司因偏远工作而取消租约

2021-03-14 05:49:38

Salesforce, the city’s largest private employer, has canceled its 325,000-square-foot lease at the unbuilt Parcel F tower in San Francisco’s Transbay neighborhood, as first reported by the San Francisco Business Times.

这座城市最大的私营者Salesforce取消了旧金山的跨国街区Unbuilt Parcel F Trays的325,000平方英尺的租约，如旧金山的营业时间首次报道。

The company announced in February that more than half of its workforce will continue working remotely or on a flexible schedule after the pandemic is over. It is also subleasing part of its offices at 350 Mission St., according to the San Francisco Chronicle. (SFGATE and The San Francisco Chronicle are both owned by Hearst but operate independently of one another.)

该公司于二月宣布，超过一半的劳动力将在流行结束后继续远程或灵活的时间表继续工作。根据旧金山纪念碑的说法，它还在350名任务中延迟其一部分办事处。 （SFGATE和旧金山编年史都被赫斯特所拥有，但彼此独立运作。）

The tower planned for 542-550 Howard St. has faced many challenges in development, with city delays going on five years and Chinatown activists opposing the project because of the shadow it would cast on Willie “Woo Woo” Wong Playground.

该塔规划542-550霍华德圣的发展面临着许多发展挑战，城市延误发生了五年，而唐人街活动家因影子而反对该项目，它将在Willie“Woo”Wong Playground上。

The lease termination is just the latest blow to San Francisco’s downtown office footprint as more companies shrink or offload leases because of the persistence of remote work. The lease on Yelp’s 161,876-square-foot office space at 140 New Montgomery St. is up in October 2021 and the entire space has been listed for rent. WeWork confirmed it would be scaling back its Bay Area locations and is closing five downtown locations.

租赁终止只是对旧金山的市中心办公室足迹的最新打击，因为由于远程工作的持久性，更多公司缩小或卸载租赁。在10月2021年10月的140岁新蒙哥马利圣耶尔普的161,876平方英尺的办公空间租赁，整个空间已被列入租金。 Wework确认它将缩放其湾区地区，并截止五个市中心。

Just this week, the Mission Bay headquarters once leased by Dropbox is being sold for $1.08 billion. The company adopted a remote work policy in October 2020. Old Navy also announced in February it would be shutting down its Mission Bay offices and moving into parent company Gap Inc.’s workspace.

就在本周，Mission Bay总部一旦被Dropbox租赁，售价为120.8亿美元。该公司于10月2020年10月通过了遥控工作政策。老海军还宣布于2月份，它将在其使命湾办事处关闭并进入母公司Gap Inc.的工作区。

Salesforce has more than 9,000 employees in the Bay Area, according to LinkedIn, and leases more than two-thirds of its namesake Salesforce Tower.

根据LinkedIn的说法，Salesforce在湾区拥有超过9,000名员工员工，并租用超过三分之二的名称Salesforce Tower。

SFGATE reached out to Salesforce and did not hear back as of publication. This story was updated at March 10 at 2:18 p.m. to include additional information on Salesforce subleasing offices in its Salesforce East building.

SFGATE达到Salesforce，并没有听到出版物。这个故事在3月10日在下午2:18更新。包括关于其Salesforce东部建筑的Salesforce扬声器的其他信息。
