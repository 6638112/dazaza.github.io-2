Samsung making Windows laptop with Exynos chip
三星使用Exynos芯片制造Windows笔记本电脑

2021-02-26 03:14:10

Samsung is reportedly gearing up to launch a Windows 10 laptop equipped with an Exynos processor and AMD graphics. Exynos are ARM-based chips, so this PC will likely run Windows 10 on ARM and compete with Qualcomm’s Snapdragon 8cx.

据报道，三星正准备推出配备Exynos处理器和AMD显卡的Windows 10笔记本电脑。 Exynos是基于ARM的芯片，因此该PC可能会在ARM上运行Windows 10，并可以与高通的Snapdragon 8cx竞争。

According to a ZDNet Korea report, the Samsung device is expected to launch in the second half of this year, potentially after the release of the next Galaxy Note. Incidentally, there are still conflicting reports about whether the Galaxy Note line will have a part to play in Samsung’s future.

根据ZDNet Korea的报告，三星设备有望在今年下半年推出，可能是在下一个Galaxy Note发行之后。顺便说一句，关于Galaxy Note系列是否会在三星的未来中扮演重要角色的报道仍然存在矛盾。

Samsung has released Windows 10 on ARM devices in the past, including the Galaxy Book S and Galaxy Book 2. But those devices were equipped with Qualcomm processors. ZDNet Korea claims Samsung’s upcoming laptop will feature an Exynos 2200, which would be the successor to its Exynos 2100 processor launched at Unpacked 2021.

三星过去曾在ARM设备上发布过Windows 10，包括Galaxy Book S和Galaxy Book2。但是这些设备都配备了高通处理器。 ZDNet Korea称三星即将推出的笔记本电脑将配备Exynos 2200，这将是其在Unpacked 2021上推出的Exynos 2100处理器的继任者。

Samsung and AMD have been collaborating on a mobile GPU since 2019, so the rumored laptop has been a long time coming. Samsung previously teased that the component would make its way into a “flagship product.” Reports previously speculated the component would first appear in a next-generation foldable, but that might not be the case.

自2019年以来，三星和AMD就一直在移动GPU上进行合作，因此传闻中的笔记本电脑问世已经有很长时间了。三星此前曾嘲笑该组件将成为“旗舰产品”。先前的报道推测该组件将首先出现在下一代可折叠设备中，但事实并非如此。

A high-end Windows 10 on ARM PC made by Samsung could be the boost the platform needs. The platform hasn’t really taken off, but it’s gotten a lot better since its launch. These days, more Windows 10 apps support ARM devices, while Microsoft is working on x64 emulation. The development should go a considerable way toward closing the app gap.

三星生产的ARM PC上的高端Windows 10可能会推动平台需求的增长。该平台尚未真正起步，但自发布以来已经变得更好了。如今，当Microsoft正在研究x64仿真时，更多Windows 10应用程序支持ARM设备。开发应该以相当大的方式缩小应用程序差距。

Companies like Intel and Qualcomm have long had a monopoly on the market, but some OEMs are taking matters into their own hands. Apple designed its M1 chip to power the new MacBook Air and MacBook Pro, and the Cupertino company is looking to power its entire Mac lineup with M series chips. Samsung could be the next company to follow this approach, starting with a Windows 10 PC powered by an Exynos chip.

像英特尔和高通这样的公司长期以来一直在市场上处于垄断地位，但是一些OEM却将事情掌握在自己手中。苹果公司设计了其M1芯片来为新款MacBook Air和MacBook Pro供电，而库比蒂诺公司则希望通过M系列芯片为整个Mac系列提供动力。三星可能是下一个采用这种方法的公司，首先是采用Exynos芯片的Windows 10 PC。
