The remainder of these notes cover either more advanced aspects of topics from the book, or other topics that appear only in our more advanced algorithms class CS 473. Don't be fooled by the fancy typesetting; these notes are considerably less polished than the textbook. Extended Dance Remix: These are notes on more advanced material directly related to the textbook. The notes are ordered roughly to match the textbook chapters. Director's Cut: These are notes on topics not covered in the textbook. The numbering is completely independent os the textbook; I just started over at 1.
