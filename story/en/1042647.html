Ticketmaster employees accessed a competitor's computers using stolen passwords
Ticketmaster员工使用被盗的密码访问了竞争对手的计算机

2020-12-31 21:22:37

New York (CNN)Ticketmaster entered into a plea agreement with federal prosecutors in Brooklyn, New York and will pay $10 million in fines to resolve charges that it accessed the computer systems of a competitor without authorization.

纽约（CNN）票务官与纽约布鲁克林的联邦检察官达成了认罪协议，并将支付1000万美元的罚款，以解决其未经授权访问竞争对手计算机系统的指控。

"Ticketmaster employees repeatedly -- and illegally -- accessed a competitor's computers without authorization using stolen passwords to unlawfully collect business intelligence," Acting U.S. Attorney Seth DuCharme said Wednesday in a press release.

“ Ticketmaster”员工反复（非法）未经授权使用竞争对手的计算机使用盗窃的密码非法收集商业情报，从而访问了竞争对手的计算机，＆＃34;代理美国检察官塞斯·杜查姆（Seth DuCharme）周三在新闻稿中表示。

Ticketmaster is a subsidiary of LiveNation ( LYV). In 2019, Democratic senators called for a federal antitrust investigation of LiveNation for what they called "nefarious practices" and "sky-high fees" levied on consumers.

Ticketmaster是LiveNation（LYV）的子公司。 2019年，民主党参议员呼吁对LiveNation进行联邦反托拉斯调查，以调查其所谓的``邪恶行为''。和“天价”费用向消费者征收。

The company will pay the fine to resolve a five-count criminal information filed Wednesday with counts including conspiracy to commit computer intrusion, computer intrusion of a protected computer, computer intrusion in furtherance of fraud, wire fraud conspiracy and wire fraud.

该公司将支付罚款，以解决周三提交的五项刑事信息，其中包括串谋实施计算机入侵，对受保护计算机的计算机入侵，助长欺诈的计算机入侵，电汇欺诈阴谋和电汇欺诈。

According to the deferred prosecution agreement unsealed Wednesday, a former Ticketmaster employee named Zeeshan Zaidi went to work for Live Nation in 2013 as a consultant, and was hired full time to work for Ticketmaster in 2014 after leaving a competing company, which is unnamed in the court filing.

根据周三公布的延期起诉协议，前Ticketmaster雇员Zeeshan Zaidi于2013年在Live Nation工作，担任顾问，并在离开竞争对手的公司后于2014年被聘用为Ticketmaster全职工作，该公司未在法院备案。

Zaidi allegedly had access to usernames and passwords for the unnamed competitor, and used them without authorization to access that company's systems while working for Ticketmaster between 2013 and 2015. The deferred prosecution agreement states that the information obtained from accessing the systems was used, for among other things, to prepare "strategy presentations for senior Live Nation and Ticketmaster executives that benchmarked competitor products and services," including ones offered by the competitor company.

据称Zaidi可以访问未命名竞争对手的用户名和密码，并在2013年至2015年期间为Ticketmaster工作期间未经授权使用它们来访问该公司的系统。除其他外，还用于为Live Nation和Ticketmaster高管准备以竞争对手产品和服务为基准的战略演示，＆＃34;包括竞争对手公司提供的产品。

A press release by federal prosecutors said Ticketmaster employees held a division-wide summit during which the stolen passwords were used to access the victim company's computers "as if that were an appropriate business tactic."

联邦检察官在一份新闻稿中说，Ticketmaster员工举行了一次全公司范围的峰会，在此期间，被盗的密码被用来访问受害公司的计算机，这似乎是一种适当的商业策略。

According to the deferred prosecution agreement, a Ticketmaster executive described that the goal was to "choke off" the victim company and "steal back" one of its clients.

根据延后的起诉协议，Ticketmaster高管描述说，目标是“阻止”诉讼。受害者公司和“偷回”其客户之一。

Zaidi was terminated from Ticketmaster in 2017 and pleaded guilty in Brooklyn federal court in 2019 to one count of conspiring to access protected computers without authorization and to commit wire fraud, according to the deferred prosecution agreement. Zaidi's sentencing has been delayed, according to court filings. CNN has contacted his attorneys for comment.

根据推迟的起诉协议，Zaidi于2017年被Ticketmaster终止，并于2019年在布鲁克林联邦法院认罪，其中一项共谋罪是密谋未经授权访问受保护的计算机并进行电汇欺诈。根据法庭文件，Zaidi的判决已被推迟。 CNN已联系其律师发表评论。

"When employees walk out of one company and into another, it's illegal for them to take proprietary information with them," said FBI Assistant Director-in-Charge William Sweeney, whose office is conducting the investigation. "Ticketmaster used stolen information to gain an advantage over its competition, and then promoted the employees who broke the law."

当员工走出一家公司而进入另一家公司时，让他们随身携带专有信息是非法的，＆＃34;联邦调查局助理局长威廉·斯威尼（William Sweeney）说，他的办公室正在进行调查。 ＆nbsp; Ticketmaster使用窃取的信息来获得竞争优势，然后提拔了违反法律的员工。＆＃34;

In a remote court proceeding Wednesday, Ticketmaster General Counsel Michael Rowles, a subsidiary of LiveNation, entered the deferred prosecution agreement and waived the company's right to have indictment proceedings on behalf of Ticketmaster, with authorization of the board, he said in the remote proceeding Wednesday.

他在周三的远程法院程序中，LiveNation的子公司票务总法律顾问迈克尔·罗尔斯（Michael Rowles）进入了延期起诉协议，并放弃了公司在获得董事会授权的情况下代表票务大师提起诉讼的权利。星期三进行远程程序。
