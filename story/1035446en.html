WarnerMedia says HBO Max will be available on Amazon Fire devices from November 17
WarnerMedia表示，从11月17日起，HBO Max将在亚马逊Fire设备上提供

2020-11-16 22:32:15

Almost six months after ’s launch, the streaming service will finally be landing on Fire TV and Fire tablets.

在推出近六个月后，这项流媒体服务终于将登陆Fire TV和Fire平板电脑。

The HBO Max app for Amazon’s devices will be available Tuesday, Nov. 17. The launch will bring WarnerMedia to Amazon’s estimated 40 million-plus active Fire TV users as well as the company’s Fire tablet customers.

适用于亚马逊设备的HBO Max应用程序将于11月17日(星期二)上市。此次发布将把WarnerMedia带给亚马逊估计超过4000万的Fire电视活跃用户以及该公司Fire平板电脑的客户。

For now, HBO Max remains unavailable on the Roku platform, which counts 46 million active accounts for its devices.

目前，HBO Max仍无法在Roku平台上使用，Roku平台的设备拥有4600万活跃账户。

Terms of WarnerMedia’s deal with Amazon for the Fire devices are not being disclosed.

WarnerMedia与亚马逊就Fire设备达成的协议条款尚未披露。

Starting Nov. 17, current subscribers of HBO through Amazon’s Prime Video Channels will be able to log in to the HBO Max app with their Amazon credentials — at no additional cost. Earlier this year, Amazon said it had nearly 5 million HBO subscribers through Prime Video Channels.

从11月17日开始，目前通过亚马逊Prime视频频道订阅HBO的用户将可以用他们的亚马逊凭证登录HBO Max应用程序--不需要额外付费。今年早些时候，亚马逊表示，它通过Prime Video频道拥有近500万HBO订户。

As of the end of the third quarter, WarnerMedia said 28.7 million customers were eligible to get HBO Max. The Amazon deal will immediately boost that number to at least 33 million.

截至第三季度末，WarnerMedia表示，有2870万客户有资格获得HBO Max。与亚马逊的交易将立即将这一数字提高到至少3300万。

The HBO app on Fire TV and Fire tablets will automatically update to become the HBO Max app; customers will be able to log in using their existing HBO credentials. New customers can also subscribe to HBO Max directly in the app, for the $14.99 per month price. Additionally, all existing HBO Max customers (regardless of how they subscribe to the platform) will be able to access HBO Max via supported Fire TV and Fire tablet devices using their existing provider credentials.

Fire TV和Fire平板电脑上的HBO应用程序将自动更新为HBO Max应用程序；客户将能够使用现有的HBO凭据登录。新客户也可以在应用程序中直接订阅HBO Max，每月14.99美元。此外，所有现有的HBO Max客户(无论他们如何订阅该平台)都将能够使用其现有的提供商凭证通过受支持的Fire TV和Fire平板设备访问HBO Max。

“We are very excited that Amazon customers will now be able to enjoy the best-in-class content that lives within HBO Max,” Tony Goncalves, head of sales and distribution for WarnerMedia, said in announcing the deal. “Our continued goal is to make HBO Max and its unparalleled content available to customers across all the devices they love. Fire TV is a favorite among customers and we look forward to working with the Amazon team to engage and grow our existing subscriber base by showcasing all that HBO Max has to offer.”

WarnerMedia的销售和分销主管托尼·贡卡尔维斯(Tony Goncalves)在宣布这笔交易时表示：“亚马逊的客户现在可以享受HBO Max内最好的内容，我们对此感到非常兴奋。”我们的持续目标是使HBO Max及其无与伦比的内容能够通过客户喜爱的所有设备提供给他们。Fire TV是客户的最爱，我们期待着与亚马逊团队合作，通过展示HBO Max提供的所有服务来吸引和扩大我们现有的订户基础。“。

HBO Max provides about 10,000 hours of premium content, including programming from HBO, Warner Bros., DC, Cartoon Network, Adult Swim, Turner Classic Movies and more.

HBO Max提供约10,000小时的优质内容，包括来自HBO、华纳兄弟、DC、卡通网络、成人游泳、特纳经典电影等的节目。

“We’ve worked closely with HBO for many years to bring their great content to Fire TV and to make it easier to discover and enjoy with features like search integration, Alexa and personalized recommendations,” Marc Whitten, Amazon’s VP of entertainment devices and services. “We are excited to continue that partnership with the launch of HBO Max to bring even more incredible content to customers on Fire TV. Alexa, play Raised by Wolves.”

亚马逊娱乐设备和服务副总裁马克·惠顿表示：“多年来，我们一直与HBO密切合作，将他们的精彩内容带到Fire TV上，并通过搜索整合、Alexa和个性化推荐等功能，让人们更容易发现和享受这些内容。”我们很高兴通过推出HBO Max继续这种合作关系，为Fire TV的客户带来更多令人难以置信的内容。亚历克莎，狼群养的戏。“
