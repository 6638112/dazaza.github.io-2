Q&A: CEO Jensen Huang on the antitrust environment as Nvidia attempts to acquire Arm, PC vs. next-gen consoles, and Nvidia's gaming and datacenter businesses
问答：英伟达试图收购Arm，PC与下一代游戏机以及英伟达游戏和数据中心业务时，首席执行官黄仁勋在反托拉斯环境中

2020-11-24 19:46:10

Nvidia had another stellar quarter, reporting revenues of $4.73 billion for its third fiscal quarter ended October 25, up 57% from a year earlier.

英伟达的业绩再创辉煌，截至10月25日的第三财季收入为47.3亿美元，同比增长57％。

We talked to CEO Jensen Huang about those results, but we also strayed into other issues, such as the antitrust environment. Tech giants like Apple, Google, Amazon, and Facebook are facing more scrutiny on antitrust these days, and I asked Huang if that affected Nvidia, especially as it attempts to acquire Arm for $40 billion. Nvidia also said its GeForce Now will debut on iOS via the web, with Fortnite coming soon, in a way that was constraining because of Apple’s rules about cloud gaming.

我们与首席执行官黄仁勋（Jensen Huang）讨论了这些结果，但同时也误入了其他问题，例如反托拉斯环境。如今，诸如苹果，谷歌，亚马逊和Facebook之类的科技巨头在反托拉斯方面正面临更多审查，我问黄光裕是否影响了英伟达，特别是在它试图以400亿美元收购Arm的时候。英伟达还表示，其GeForce Now将通过网络在iOS上首次亮相，Fortnite即将面世，但由于苹果关于云游戏的规定，这种方式受到了限制。

I also asked Huang if he thinks the Arm deal will also make Nvidia become more open than it otherwise might be. And we chatted about the PC versus next-generation consoles and how Nvidia’s two big businesses, gaming graphics and AI/datacenter chips, are contending with each other to become the company’s biggest source of revenues.

我还问过黄仁勋，他是否认为与Arm的交易还会使Nvidia变得比以前更加开放。我们聊了聊PC与下一代游戏机的竞争，以及Nvidia的两大业务游戏图形和AI /数据中心芯片如何相互竞争，成为该公司最大的收入来源。

GamesBeat: Congratulations on another good quarter. On anti-trust, I wondered — the regulators and Congress are all going after the tech giants now. Is that something that might affect Nvidia in some way, like with the Arm acquisition? What do you think about the environment for antitrust?

GamesBeat：恭喜您再创佳绩。关于反托拉斯，我想知道-监管机构和国会现在都在追赶科技巨头。这是否会在某种程度上影响Nvidia，例如收购Arm？您如何看待反托拉斯环境？

Jensen Huang: There’s nothing on my radar. All the businesses we’re in are super-competitive. Arm has giant customers. The new markets they want to grow into, they’re the severe underdog. There’s every reason to believe that by adding more horsepower to Arm, we’re going to increase customer choice and increase innovation in the marketplace. Our assessment is that regulatory is going to be quite favorable to this transaction.

黄仁勋：我的雷达没什么。我们从事的所有业务都是极具竞争力的。阿姆拥有大量客户。他们想要发展的新市场是他们的劣势。完全有理由相信，通过为Arm增加更多功能，我们将增加客户选择并增加市场创新。我们的评估是，监管将对该交易非常有利。

GamesBeat: Do you think Arm can help Nvidia become more open than it ordinarily might be?

GamesBeat：您认为Arm可以帮助Nvidia变得比以往更加开放吗？

Huang: Arm and Nvidia are very similar. Nvidia’s architecture is available in every cloud, in every computer maker, in every shape and size. You can buy chips. You can buy systems. You can rent it for a dollar an hour. The reason why our platform is so well-adopted is because it’s open. People talk about even reverse-engineering our architecture, and that’s fine. The architecture is probably the most abundantly available architecture outside of the x86 in the world, for general-purpose programming.

黄：Arm和Nvidia非常相似。 Nvidia的体系结构可在每个云中，每个计算机制造商中以任何形状和大小使用。您可以购买筹码。您可以购买系统。您可以以每小时一美元的价格租用它。我们的平台之所以被广泛采用的原因是因为它是开放的。人们甚至谈论对我们的架构进行逆向工程，这很好。对于通用编程而言，该体系结构可能是x86之外最丰富的可用体系结构。

Arm is the same way. Their architecture is available to anybody who wants to come and get it. Those are very similar, and we have very similar attitudes about the availability of our architecture to customers.

手臂是一样的。他们的体系结构可供任何想要获得它的人使用。这些非常相似，我们对客户的体系结构可用性也持非常相似的态度。

GamesBeat: The other side of this is, do you want other companies to be more open and allow you to do more? For example, Apple and GeForce Now. That cloud gaming app can’t really happen except on the web. How do you feel about others being open?

GamesBeat：另一方面，您是否希望其他公司更加开放并允许您做更多的事情？例如，Apple和GeForce Now。除非在网络上，否则该云游戏应用程序不可能真正实现。您如何看待别人开放？

Huang: Our strategy is to be open, to have an open platform for everybody to use however they’d like to use it. But everybody has their own strategy. Ours just happens to be an open platform strategy.

黄：我们的策略是开放，为每个人都可以使用的开放平台提供使用权。但是每个人都有自己的策略。我们的恰好是一个开放平台策略。

GamesBeat: We have another console season here. What do you think of the PC versus console right now? There’s an interesting competition shaping up.

GamesBeat：我们在这里还有另一个游戏机季节。您现在对PC和控制台有何看法？一场有趣的比赛即将开始。

Huang: I don’t really think they’re competitors. The things you can do on a PC, you can’t do them on a console. But the thing that’s great is that all the content developers are having to raise the bar because the consoles are so powerful. Everyone is moving to ray tracing, which is fantastic. All of that is good for gaming.

黄：我不是真的认为他们是竞争对手。您可以在PC上执行的操作，而不能在控制台上执行。但是很棒的事情是所有内容开发人员都必须提高标准，因为控制台是如此强大。每个人都在转向光线追踪，这太棒了。所有这些对游戏都有好处。

If you look at the way that people use PCs these days, as you know, gaming has gone well beyond just gaming. It’s used for art. It’s used for sport. It’s used for sharing and influencers. The PC is the best platform for doing all of that. Not to mention, you need a PC anyway for video conferencing and things like that. You might as well get an Nvidia GeForce with AI broadcasting and all that stuff. It’s good bang for the buck.

如您所知，如果您看看当今人们使用PC的方式，那么游戏不仅限于游戏。它用于艺术。它用于运动。用于分享和影响者。 PC是完成所有这些操作的最佳平台。更不用说，您仍然需要一台PC来进行视频会议等。您还可能会获得具有AI广播和所有功能的Nvidia GeForce。物超所值。

GamesBeat: I see gaming and the datacenter are trading the lead in your revenues back and forth. Do you expect that to keep happening in the foreseeable future?

GamesBeat：我看到游戏和数据中心在您的收益中来回交易。您是否希望在可预见的将来会继续发生这种情况？

Huang: I hope the two businesses will continue to trade leads in the size of the business. It’s a foregone conclusion that everybody in the world will be a gamer someday. There are only a billion active gamers today. Someday there will be 7 billion, 8 billion active gamers. The growth opportunity for gaming is still well ahead of us. Gaming is the only entertainment that can be any entertainment. You and I both know that when the metaverse [arrives], we’re going to spend a lot more time in game worlds, not just to game, but just to hang out, to be with people, to interact with people. The gaming market has a great future ahead of it.

黄：我希望这两家公司将继续在公司规模上进行交易。可以肯定的是，世界上每个人都有一天会成为游戏玩家。今天只有十亿活跃玩家。有一天，将有70亿，80亿活跃游戏玩家。游戏的增长机会仍然遥遥领先。游戏是唯一可以作为娱乐的娱乐方式。您和我俩都知道，当元宇宙[到达]时，我们将在游戏世界中花费更多的时间，不仅是在游戏上，而且还只是为了和人们在一起，与人们互动。游戏市场的发展前景广阔。

On the other hand, I also know that AI is a new way of writing software, and this way of writing software is going to impact every industry. Because we can now write software that we otherwise could not before. Computing could reach more places that we otherwise could not before. For example, who would have thought that on the roads of the future, a billion computers could be just driving around? Who would have thought that, in the future, there will be thousands of computers roaming around warehouses and factories? Those are all new applications that otherwise wouldn’t have been possible without AI.

另一方面，我也知道AI是一种新的软件编写方式，这种软件编写方式将影响每个行业。因为我们现在可以编写以前没有的软件。计算可以到达我们以前无法到达的更多地方。例如，谁会想到在未来的道路上，可能会有十亿台计算机在行驶呢？谁会想到，将来会有数千台计算机在仓库和工厂中漫游？这些都是全新的应用程序，否则，如果没有AI，这些应用程序将无法实现。

Every building will be an AI. Everything will be an AI. That’s going to generate a lot of data, a lot more computing. The computer industry is going to be gigantic because of AI. It’s the catalyst that’s been missing. It’s the final piece of the puzzle, to write the software. If someone can write the software, we can sell a computer. Now we can have computers write software. Those two businesses are long-term secular opportunities.

每座建筑物都是AI。一切都会成为AI。这将产生大量数据，更多计算量。由于人工智能，计算机行业将变得巨大。这是缺少的催化剂。编写软件，这是难题的最后一部分。如果有人可以编写软件，我们可以出售计算机。现在我们可以让计算机编写软件了。这两个企业是长期的长期机遇。
