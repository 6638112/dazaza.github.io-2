Snapchat现在允许创建者显示他们的订户数量，这是Snapchat首次向用户提供任何面向公众的受众或追随者指标

2020-11-04 02:38:09

Snapchat is now allowing creators to publicize how many subscribers they have. This is the first time Snapchat has given its users any sort of public-facing metric about their followings or audiences, and it represents a substantial change to the platform, as first reported by Tubefilter. In a statement to The Verge, a Snap spokesperson said: “We’ve listened to feedback from our creator community and many of them expressed interest in having the option to show that their community on Snapchat is growing,” which is why it’s making subscriber counts visible. Creators can decide to turn this feature on or off from their settings.

Snapchat现在允许创建者公布他们有多少订户。这是Snapchat首次向其用户提供关于他们的追随者或受众的任何形式的面向公众的指标，正如Tubefilter最先报道的那样，这代表着该平台的重大变化。在给The Verge的一份声明中，Snap的一位发言人表示：“我们听取了创作者社区的反馈，他们中的许多人都表示有兴趣可以选择展示他们在Snapchat上的社区正在增长。”这就是为什么Snapchat的订阅量是可见的。创建者可以决定从其设置中打开或关闭此功能。

The company introduced creator profiles in September, which gives users on the platform more insights on their audience and helps them coordinate with brands. The fact that Snap’s saying its creators wanted to show off their growing community with public subscriber counts, suggests that they want a public-facing metric to demonstrate how successful they are for brands. On Instagram, for example, brands often look at follower counts and engagement rates to gauge how big a creator is on the platform. On Snapchat, brands would have had to ask creators to share those details before committing to a deal. Making those metrics public streamlines that conversation and helps creators and brands determine whether a deal might work between them. This then could lead to Snapchat becoming an overall more creator-friendly platform where more influencers see an opportunity for business.

该公司在9月份推出了创作者个人资料，这让平台上的用户对他们的受众有了更多的洞察力，并帮助他们与品牌进行协调。Snap表示，其创建者希望通过公共订户数量来炫耀他们不断增长的社区，这一事实表明，他们想要一个面向公众的衡量标准，以展示他们对品牌的成功程度。例如，在Instagram上，品牌通常会通过粉丝数量和参与率来衡量创作者在平台上的知名度。在Snapchat上，品牌在承诺交易之前必须要求创作者分享这些细节。将这些指标公之于众可以简化对话，并帮助创作者和品牌确定他们之间的交易是否可行。这可能会导致Snapchat成为一个总体上对创建者更友好的平台，在那里更多的有影响力的人看到了商机。
