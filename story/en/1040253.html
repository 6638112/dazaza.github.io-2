Qualcomm promises three years of Android updates for its entire SoC lineup
高通承诺为其整个SoC系列提供三年的Android更新

2020-12-17 09:42:22

Google and Qualcomm are teaming up to enable a longer support window for flagship Android smartphones. Qualcomm, with Google's help, will now support its chipsets for three years of major OS updates and four years of security updates, enabling a better-than-Pixel level for all future Android phones, provided your OEM is willing to cooperate. This policy is starting with the flagship Snapdragon 888, but even lower-end chips will be supported. Qualcomm PR tells us "the plan is to roll this out to all Snapdragon chipsets, including lower-tier ones, but starting the new Snapdragon 888 platform."

谷歌和高通正在合作，为旗舰Android智能手机提供更长的支持时间。高通公司现在将在Google的帮助下，为芯片组提供三年的主要操作系统更新和四年的安全更新，只要您的OEM愿意合作，它就能为所有未来的Android手机提供优于Pixel的水平。 。此政策始于旗舰产品Snapdragon 888，但将支持更低端的芯片。 Qualcomm PR告诉我们＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆nbsp;＆quot;

Part of the challenge of Android updates is the continuous chain of software custody that has to be maintained, across several companies, from the Android repository to your phone. Google and Qualcomm now say they are willing to pass the update baton to OEMs for three major updates and four years of security updates, but OEMs will actually need to update their Android skins and ship working builds to each of their devices. If they don't, we at least know who to blame now.

Android更新面临的挑战之一是，从Android存储库到您的手机，必须在多家公司之间维持连续的软件托管链。谷歌和高通现在表示，他们愿意将更新指挥棒传递给OEM进行三项主要更新和四年的安全更新，但OEM实际上需要更新其Android外观并将可运行的版本交付给他们的每台设备。如果他们不这样做，我们至少知道现在应该归咎于谁。

Qualcomm and Google's blog posts both contain the same phrasing, that they will "support 4 Android OS versions and 4 years of security updates." Read that quote closely and you'll spot two different units of measurement happening there, which some people have misinterpreted. While there are four years of security updates, the two companies are counting the initial release of Android in their quote of "4 Android OS versions," so it's three years of major Android updates, not four years. We double-checked with Qualcomm and got back "Qualcomm will support the launch version + 3 OS upgrades, for a total of 4 major Android OS versions. Snapdragon 888 will support Android 11, 12, 13, and 14."

高通公司和Google的博客文章都包含相同的措词，它们将支持4个Android OS版本和4年的安全更新。仔细阅读该报价，您会发现那里发生的两个不同的度量单位，有些人误解了。在进行了四年的安全更新后，两家公司都在其“＃4 Android OS版本”（＆＃34;）中引用了Android的初始版本。因此它是Android重大更新的三年，而不是四年。我们与Qualcomm进行了两次核对，并得出结论，＆＃34; Qualcomm将支持启动版本+ 3操作系统升级，总共支持4个主要的Android OS版本。 Snapdragon 888将支持Android 11、12、13和14。＆＃34;

This is the same update plan Pixels have gotten and what Samsung has promised, but with one more year of security updates. Keep in mind, Qualcomm is also bringing this level of support to low-end devices, so while this is only a baby step for flagship phones, lower-end phones could see greatly increased support windows.

这与Pixels已经获得的更新计划和Samsung承诺的更新计划相同，但是还有一年的安全更新。请记住，高通还为低端设备带来了这种级别的支持，因此虽然这只是旗舰手机的一大步，但低端手机的支持窗口可能会大大增加。

Google's blog post goes into detail about how it has made updating easier for SoC manufacturers like Qualcomm. Android's Project Treble re-architecture split the OS in half, separating the OS from the hardware with a modular interface. This makes it easy to run the same build of Android across multiple pieces of hardware (it's called a Generic System Image, or GSI). While that makes things easier if you're an OEM building an Android skin, Google was apparently heaping update requirements on SoC vendors.

Google的博客文章详细介绍了它如何使Qualcomm等SoC制造商更容易进行更新。 Android的Project Treble重新架构将操作系统分成了一半，并通过模块化接口将操作系统与硬件分离。这使得跨多个硬件（称为通用系统映像，即GSI）轻松运行相同版本的Android。如果您是制造Android外观的OEM厂商，这会使事情变得容易，但Google显然在SoC供应商上堆积了更新要求。

SoC vendors are partly responsible for the "vendor" implementation in Project Treble—the bottom half of OS split that contains the hardware support. While things above the Project Treble split (the software) were guaranteed backward compatibility, the hardware support was not. For each SoC, Qualcomm would need to maintain a vendor implementation for each software history permutation. That means one for phones that launched with Android 10, another for Android 11, and a third for devices that launched with Android 10 and were upgraded to Android 11.

SoC供应商部分负责＆＃34; vendor＆＃34;。 Project Treble中的实施-操作系统拆分的下半部分，包含硬件支持。虽然可以保证向后兼容Project Treble组件（软件），但不能提供硬件支持。对于每个SoC，高通都需要为每个软件历史记录排列维护一个供应商实施。这意味着一种适用于运行Android 10的手机，另一种适用于Android 11的手机，第三种适用于使用Android 10发行并升级到Android 11的设备。

This system did not scale well. Today, Google is vaguely announcing changes to Project Treble that will let Qualcomm support new and upgrading devices with a single vendor implementation. It's also (again, vaguely) cooked up some kind of scheme to let Qualcomm use the same vendor implementation across multiple SoCs, which will cut down on its update work even more.

该系统的伸缩性不好。今天，Google隐约宣布对Project Treble进行更改，这将使高通通过单个供应商实施来支持新设备和升级设备。它还（再次，含糊地）制定了某种方案，以使高通公司在多个SoC中使用相同的供应商实现，这将进一步减少其更新工作。

Google is also taking this time to update us on the state of the Android update situation. Android 11 is ever-so-slightly seeing the fastest adoption rate ever and is outpacing the previous release (previously the fastest ever), at least in terms of raw users (I doubt the percentage would be much different since I doubt the total number of active Android users has changed in a year.) Thanks to Project Treble, the Android 10 chart turned into a wicked hockey stick about 100 days after launch, and we're not at that stage yet with Android 11.

Google还将花时间向我们更新Android更新情况的状态。 Android 11的采用率一直是有史以来最快的，并且至少在原始用户方面超过了以前的版本（以前是最快的）（我怀疑百分比会大不相同，因为我怀疑总数活跃的Android用户一年来发生了变化。）感谢Project Treble，Android 10图表在发布后约100天变成了邪恶的曲棍球棒，而我们还没有使用Android 11。

Of course, this still doesn't bring Android in line with what Apple has been doing, which is five years of major OS updates and seven years of security updates for iPhones. Apple is the SoC vendor, the OS developer, and the device manufacturers though, so it has fewer logistics to work out, and it doesn't have to deal with preserving profit margins in each step of the process.

当然，这仍无法使Android与Apple一直在进行的工作保持一致，这是五年的主要OS更新和七年的iPhone安全更新。苹果虽然是SoC供应商，操作系统开发商和设备制造商，所以需要的后勤工作量较少，并且不必在过程的每个步骤中都要保留利润。

Every Android update announcement feels like one small step toward making the situation better, and nothing will be a silver bullet aside from blowing up the whole system. Just about every sentence in this article could end with the caveat of "if your OEM wants to cooperate," and for many, that last link in the update chain will be the critical one. If your OEM doesn't want to play ball, well, you know what to do, right? Vote with your wallet!
