Rocky Linux gets a parent company, with $4m Series A funding
Rocky Linux获400万美元A轮融资的母公司

2021-01-29 05:14:49

Gregory Kurtzer, co-founder of the now-defunct CentOS Linux distribution, has founded a new startup company called Ctrl IQ which will serve in part as a sponsoring company for the upcoming Rocky Linux distribution.

现在已经不复存在的CentOS Linux发行版的联合创始人Gregory Kurtzer已经成立了一家名为Ctrl IQ的新启动公司，它将部分作为即将到来的Rocky Linux发行版的赞助公司。

Rocky Linux is to be a benefactor of Ctrl IQ's revenue, not its source—the company describes itself in its announcement as the suppliers of a "full technology stack integrating key capabilities of enterprise, hyper-scale, cloud and high-performance computing."

Rocky Linux将成为Ctrl IQ收入的受益者，而不是其来源-该公司在公告中将自己描述为集成了企业，超大规模，云的关键功能的完整技术堆栈的供应商和高性能计算。＆＃34;

If you've been hiding under a Linux rock for the last few months, CentOS Linux was the most widely known and used clone of Red Hat Enterprise Linux. Kurtzer co-founded CentOS Linux in 2004 with mentor Rocky McGaugh, and it operated independently for 10 years until being acquired by Red Hat in 2014. When Red Hat killed off CentOS Linux in a highly controversial December 2020 announcement, Kurtzer immediately announced his intention to recreate CentOS with a new distribution, named after his deceased mentor. The Rocky Linux concept got immediate, positive community reaction—but there's an awful lot of work and expense that goes into creating and maintaining a Linux distribution. The CentOS Linux project itself made that clear when it went for the Red Hat acquisition in 2014; without its own source of funding, the odds of Rocky Linux becoming a complete 1:1 replacement—serving the same massive volume of users that CentOS did—seemed dicey at best.

如果您在过去的几个月中一直躲在Linux的岩石下，那么CentOS Linux是Red Hat Enterprise Linux最为广泛使用的克隆。 Kurtzer在2004年与导师Rocky McGaugh共同创立了CentOS Linux，并独立运营了10年，直到2014年被Red Hat收购。当Red Hat在一个备受争议的2020年12月公告中杀死了CentOS Linux时，Kurtzer立即宣布了他的意图。用新的发行版重新创建CentOS，以他的已故导师的名字命名。 Rocky Linux的概念引起了社区的积极响应，但是在创建和维护Linux发行版方面却花费了大量的工作和金钱。 CentOS Linux项目本身在2014年收购Red Hat时就明确表示了这一点。没有自己的资金来源，Rocky Linux成为完全1：1替代产品（为CentOS所服务的大量用户提供服务）的几率似乎最好。

Wading through the buzzword bingo, Ctrl IQ's real business seems to be in supplying relatively turn-key infrastructure for high-performance computing (HPC) workloads, capable of running distributed across multiple sites and/or cloud providers.

通过流行语宾果游戏，Ctrl IQ的真正业务似乎是为高性能计算（HPC）工作负载提供相对完整的基础架构，这些基础架构能够跨多个站点和/或云提供商运行。

Ctrl IQ's fully supported technology stack was created to accelerate speed and efficiency of multi-prem, multi-cloud, multi-architecture orchestration of workflows. The solution fits within existing enterprise architecture and infrastructures, and will include:

创建了Ctrl IQ完全支持的技术堆栈，以加快工作流程的多Prem，多云，多体系结构编排的速度和效率。该解决方案适合现有的企业架构和基础架构，并将包括：

Rocky Linux: enterprise Linux supported for cloud and enterprise created by Kurtzer, Ctrl IQ CEO and founder of CentOS Warewulf: scalable systems management suite designed to manage clusters of compute resources. Ctrl Computing Stacks: fully supported computational stacks designed for all performance intensive workflows like, HPC, AI, ML and scientific workflows. Fuzzball: provides the intelligent, secure and performant orchestration of workflows and data in a managed or hosted on-premise solution. Ctrl IQ Cloud: provides a secure hybrid platform to execute and orchestrate both performance critical workflows and services.

Rocky Linux：由Ctrl IQ首席执行官兼CentOS Warewulf创始人Kurtzer创建的，支持云和企业的企业Linux：旨在管理计算资源集群的可扩展系统管理套件。 Ctrl计算堆栈：完全受支持的计算堆栈，适用于所有性能密集型工作流程，例如HPC，AI，ML和科学工作流程。 Fuzzball：在托管或托管的内部部署解决方案中提供智能，安全且高效的业务流程和数据编排。 Ctrl IQ Cloud：提供一个安全的混合平台，以执行和协调对性能至关重要的工作流和服务。

Rocky Linux itself is still more dream than download, as are Ctrl Computing Stacks, Fuzzball, and Ctrl IQ Cloud—but not all of Ctrl IQ's offerings are theoretical. Warewulf, also founded by Kurtzer, is currently developed and maintained by the US Department of Energy. Anyone can freely download and use Warewulf, but it's not difficult to imagine value added in consulting with one of its founders.

Rocky Linux本身，Ctrl Computing Stacks，Fuzzball和Ctrl IQ Cloud仍然比下载更富梦想，但并不是所有Ctrl IQ产品都是理论上的。同样由Kurtzer创立的Warewulf，目前由美国能源部开发和维护。任何人都可以自由下载和使用Warewulf，但不难想象在与其中一位创始人进行咨询时会带来增值。

Kurtzer and his new company received accolades from Sandia National Laboratories—the current home of Warewulf—as well as from investment firm IAG, which also funds (and sees Ctrl IQ's mission as critical to) NAS company OpenDrives.

Kurtzer和他的新公司获得了桑迪亚国家实验室（Warewulf的现址）以及投资公司IAG的赞誉，后者也为NAS公司OpenDrives提供资金（并把Ctrl IQ的使命视为至关重要）。

We asked Ctrl IQ what the relationship between itself and Rocky Linux would be—specifically, whether the Rocky Linux project would operate separately and independently or as a wholly owned property of Ctrl IQ. A Ctrl IQ representative confirmed that Rocky Linux is and will remain a community project.

我们询问Ctrl IQ自身与Rocky Linux之间的关系是什么-具体来说，Rocky Linux项目是将独立运行还是作为Ctrl IQ的全资拥有。 Ctrl IQ代表确认Rocky Linux是并且将继续是社区项目。

No, it is not wholly owned and is still community-based. Ctrl IQ is supporting the initial capital for expenses, legal, etc. and [Rocky Linux] is an OS option for the full tech stack.

不，它不是全资拥有，仍然基于社区。 Ctrl IQ支持费用，法律费用等的初始资金，[Rocky Linux]是完整技术堆栈的OS选项。

Ctrl IQ is one of three Tier 1 sponsors identified by the Rocky Linux project, along with Amazon Web Services (which provides core build infrastructure) and Mattermost, which is providing enterprise collaboration services—think "Slack, but not Slack."
