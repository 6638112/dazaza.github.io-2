Apple debuts Apple Card Family to let spouses build credit together and extend access to family members over 13, with optional controls, coming to US in May
Apple首次亮相Apple Card Family让配偶将信用共同建立，并在13岁以上的家庭成员延长可选控制，可选

2021-04-21 08:55:08

Apple Card Family will launch first in the U.S. in May after users update to the latest version of iOS, Apple said today.

Apple今天说，Apple Card Family将于5月首次推出美国5月份的最新版本。

The timing of the announcement is interesting. Last month, Apple and partner Goldman Sachs were cleared of any gender bias in how they build credit scoring in the Apple Card, a long simmering investigation that had been going for nearly as long as the Apple Card itself.

公告的时间很有趣。上个月，Apple和Partner Goldman Sachs被清除了他们如何在Apple卡中建立信用评分的任何性别偏见，这是一个漫长的酝酿，几乎只要Apple卡本身就会出现。

At the time of the ruling, even as Apple was cleared, the regulator noted that what the case did highlight was the more endemic gender and other disparities in the credit scoring system, particularly as they pertain to spouses and partners.

在裁决的时候，即使苹果被清除，监管机构也指出，案件突出的是，信用评分制度中更为的性别和其他差异，特别是他们对配偶和合作伙伴都有津津有伙伴。

In light of that, some might say the announcement today is not coming as a surprise, and others might say it is long overdue.

鉴于此，有些人可能会说今天的宣布并不是一个惊喜，其他人可能会说它姗姗来迟。

This service announcement feels like it’s the company’s effort to seal the deal and draw a line under that story, and more formally lay out its intentions to be equitable even if regulations have yet to catch up.

这项服务公告感觉就像公司努力封印交易并在该故事下画出一条线，即使法规尚未赶上法规，即使规定尚未赶上，也可以正式奠定了公平的意图。

“One of the things that became apparent to us in the beginning [of launching Apple Card] was a lack of fairness in the way the industry calculated credit scores when there were two holders of a credit card,” said CEO Tim Cook today. “One of you got the benefit of building a good credit history, and the other did not. We want to reinvent the way this works.”

“在发射Apple卡的开始时，我们显而易见的事情之一是在行业计算的信用卡时缺乏公平性，当时今天首席执行官蒂姆师傅说。 “你们之一有利于建立一个良好的信用历史，另一个没有。我们想重新发明这项工作的方式。“

(It’s also not a surprise on another level: developer previews of iOS 14.5 revealed that Apple was building in multiuser support for Apple Cards, laying the groundwork for joint accounts for adults and wider family usage.)

（这也不令人惊讶于另一个级别：iOS 14.5的开发人员预览显示Apple正在为Apple卡的多用户支持建立，为成人和更广泛的家庭使用奠定了联合账户的基础。）

As Cook described it, spouses and partners will be allowed to share and merge their credit lines have equal rights on their account, in order to “build credit equally.”

作为厨师描述了它，将允许配偶和合作伙伴分享并合并其信用额度在其账户上具有平等的权利，以“平等地建立信贷”。

“This solution helps deliver financial equity, and it’s a game-changer,” he said. Indeed, in cases where either one partner has outstanding debts, or has defaulted on some of that debt, or doesn’t have the same earning power, this move is a way to confer the stronger spending power of one partner on another, in cases where partners are actually combining their finances anyway. It makes sense and frankly is long overdue.

“此解决方案有助于提供金融股权，这是一款游戏更换者，”他说。事实上，如果一个合作伙伴具有杰出债务的情况下，或违反了一些债务，或者没有同样的收入权，这一举措就是一种赋予另一个合作伙伴的强大支出能力的方法无论如何，合作伙伴实际上的财务状况。它有意义，坦率地逾期。

The ability to give Apple Card access to over-13s, meanwhile, will come with spending limits if you want to put them in, along with other controls in terms of how it can be used.

如果您想将它们放入，则将Apple卡访问超过13秒的Apple卡访问超过13秒的能力，以及在如何使用它的情况下以及其他控件。

This is also smart, an extension of how Apple has built a role for itself as the “responsible” technology company with privacy controls for all users, and parental controls for devices, and now it’s adding on a new angle, becoming an educative tool.

这也是聪明的，苹果公司如何成为所有用户的“负责的”技术公司为所有用户的隐私控制，以及设备的父母控制，现在正在添加新角度，成为一个新角度，成为一个新角度，成为一个教育工具。

Many parents are turning to technology and apps to build more financial nous among their children, and so it makes sense for Apple to position itself as a partner in this effort.

许多父母正在转向技术和应用程序，以在他们的孩子中建立更多的金融别的，因此苹果将自己定位为这项努力的伴侣是有道理的。

While letting families run their finances in a more equitable way, there is of course another, more business-minded strategy here: It’s clearly giving Apple a much, much bigger pool of potential consumers using its Apple Card, using services like Daily Cash (which gives up to 3% of every purchase as cash on users’ Apple Cash card each day) and access to using the secure titanium Apple Card that comes without a visible card number, CVV security code, expiration date or signature.

虽然让家庭以更公平的方式运行他们的财务状况，但在这里还有更多的商业思维策略：它清楚地向苹果提供了许多更大的潜在消费者，使用其苹果卡，使用日常现金（哪种）每天提供高达3％的购买量作为现金，并每天获得现金，并使用无可见卡号，CVV安全码，到期日或签名的安全钛苹果卡。

On that subject, we don’t quite know how many people are using that Apple Card today. Cook simply called Apple Card, which was estimated to be used by over 3.1 million people as of March 2020, “the most successful credit card launch ever.”

在这个主题上，我们不太了解今天有多少人使用该苹果卡。厨师简单地称为Apple卡，估计超过31万人截至3月20日超过310万人，“最成功的信用卡推出。”
