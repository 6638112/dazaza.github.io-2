Facebook Banned Our SaaS Website
Facebook禁止我们的SaaS网站

2020-12-27 07:33:14

As if 2020 had not been exciting enough, Facebook decided to ban our website 8 months ago. No links to it could be shared any more, the old posts and comments linking to our domain were nowhere to be found. Triggered by some kind of machine learning moderation algorithm, our harmless little SaaS, which spits out one boring article a month (and gets three "likes"), was suddenly labeled as "violating community standards"

好像2020年还不够令人兴奋，Facebook决定在8个月前禁止我们的网站。无法再共享与它的链接，找不到与我们的域链接的旧帖子和评论。由某种机器学习审核算法触发后，我们的无害小SaaS突然被标记为“违反社区标准”，该SaaS每月吐出一篇无聊的文章（并得到3个“赞”）。 ;

And they didn't even bother to let me know. I noticed this accidentally - in my Buffer dashboard.

他们甚至都不想让我知道。我在Buffer仪表板中意外地注意到了这一点。

The worst part is that you cannot even contact Facebook. Like, at all. There are literally no feedback channels.

最糟糕的是，您甚至无法联系Facebook。都一样实际上没有反馈渠道。

You can check the status of your website using this debugging tool and there's even a "dispute" link there, but I tried it and there was no response in 8 months. Many people complain they have been waiting for years. Some bloggers suggest getting a Facebook advertising account, which opens up a "live chat" support option but this has stopped working apparently. Good thing our business is not at all dependent on Facebook, but this still bugged me.

您可以使用此调试工具检查网站的状态，甚至还会有争议。链接到那里，但我尝试了一下，但在8个月内没有任何反应。许多人抱怨他们已经等待了多年。一些博客作者建议获得一个Facebook广告帐户，该帐户可以打开＆＃34;实时聊天＆＃34;支持选项，但这显然已停止工作。好事，我们的业务完全不依赖于Facebook，但这仍然困扰着我。

As it turns out there are companies out there, that sell a "we'll contact an actual support person at Facebook" service.

事实证明，那里有一些公司在出售产品，我们会与Facebook的实际支持人员联系。服务。

The price starts at $500. That's for creating a freaking support ticket. The companies are mostly SMM/marketing agencies who consult big clients with large ad-spending budgets and, because of that, FB assigns an actual "human" manager to them or whatever. Which has enabled them to offer this shady little "service" on the side.

价格从500美元起。这就是创建怪异的支持票证的过程。这些公司大多是SMM /营销机构，他们会向广告预算较高的大客户进行咨询，因此，FB会分配实际的“人力”。他们的经理或其他。这使他们能够提供这种可屏蔽的小型＆＃34; service＆＃34;服务。在一边。

So after we hired a "mediator" company to file the appeal for us, the website has been instantly unbanned. And they confirmed the ML glitch: most likely caused by an attack - a botnet was sending fake abuse reports to ban our website.

因此，在我们雇用了＆＃34;调解员＆＃34;公司向我们提出上诉，该网站已立即被禁止。他们证实了ML错误：最有可能是由攻击引起的-僵尸网络正在发送虚假的滥用报告以禁止我们的网站。

Now, we are in a lucky position to almost not give a damn, our clients don't come to us from Facebook. But there are businesses that are much, much more dependent on Facebook (or Instagram, Youtube, etc). And if your business is built entirely around piggybacking one platform, it can go belly up overnight.

现在，我们处于一个幸运的位置，几乎不该死，我们的客户不会从Facebook来找我们。但是有些企业越来越依赖于Facebook（或Instagram，Youtube等）。而且，如果您的业务完全建立在搭载一个平台上，那么它可能会在一夜之间崩溃。

It can happen by accident - this summer, for example, YouTube copyright algorithms have blocked NASA videos of SpaceX Falcon-9 launch because the bots "discovered" National Geographic licensed content in it.

这可能是偶然发生的-例如，今年夏天，YouTube的版权算法已阻止“发现”机器人的SpaceX Falcon-9发射的NASA视频。国家地理许可内容。

It can happen on purpose - there is a whole industry of copyright blackmail on YouTube and Instagram. Using fake copyright claims and shady legal entities one can take down and steal almost any content (here's the infamous example) and this loophole has been working for years (I'm not even sure if it has been addressed).

这可能是有意发生的-YouTube和Instagram上存在着整个版权勒索行业。使用伪造的版权声明和可疑的法人实体，您可以删除并窃取几乎所有内容（此处是臭名昭著的示例），并且这种漏洞已经存在多年了（我不确定是否已解决） 。

One way or another, your business can be shut down - but not necessarily because the platform decides to change its algorithms or sunset their API, but because of an attack from other users on the platform.

您可以通过某种方式关闭您的业务-但这不一定是因为平台决定更改其算法或停用其API，而是因为平台上其他用户的攻击。
