Australian startup Morse Micro, which is developing ultra low-power, long-range Wi-Fi HaLow chips for IoT environments, extends its Series A by $13M to $30M
澳大利亚初创公司Morse Micro正在为物联网环境开发超低功耗，远程Wi-Fi HaLow芯片，并将其A轮融资增加1300万美元至3000万美元

2020-11-28 19:40:55

Morse Micro, the Australian IoT WiFi start-up,  has secured $13 million (AU $18) in additional funding.

澳大利亚物联网WiFi初创公司Morse Micro已获得1300万美元（18澳元）的额外资金。

New and existing investors, including Blackbird Ventures, Main Sequence Ventures, Clean Energy Innovation Fund, Skip Capital, and Ray Stata  participated in the funding, taking the total Series A funding round to $30 million (AU $42).

包括Blackbird Ventures，Main Sequence Ventures，Clean Energy Innovation Fund，Skip Capital和Ray Stata在内的新投资者和现有投资者都参与了此次融资，使A轮融资总额达到3,000万美元（42澳元）。

This additional investment enables Morse Micro to expand its product and technology development teams, both in Sydney and internationally. It will also allow the company to expand into emerging applications and continue developing its innovative wireless solutions.

这项额外的投资使Morse Micro能够在悉尼和国际上扩展其产品和技术开发团队。它还将使该公司能够扩展到新兴应用中，并继续开发其创新的无线解决方案。

Morse Micro is an Australian semiconductor company and creator of the Wi-Fi HaLow chip; an ultra-low-power, long-range and secure Wi-Fi chip specifically designed for IoT environments.

Morse Micro是一家澳大利亚半导体公司，并且是Wi-Fi HaLow芯片的创建者。专为物联网环境设计的超低功耗，远程安全的Wi-Fi芯片。

The company says that its  team includes the original inventors of Wi-Fi and designers of the world’s first Wi-Fi chipset.

该公司表示，其团队包括Wi-Fi的原始发明者和世界上第一个Wi-Fi芯片组的设计师。
