US Panel Asks FBI To Review Role of Parler In Capitol Attack
美国小组要求联邦调查局审查Parler在国会大厦袭击中的作用

2021-01-22 05:46:20

WASHINGTON (Reuters) - The House Oversight and Reform Committee on Thursday asked the FBI to investigate the role Parler, a social media website and app popular with the American far right, played a role in the violence at the U.S. Capitol.

华盛顿（路透社）-众议院监督和改革委员会周四要求联邦调查局调查Parler这个社交媒体网站和受美国人欢迎的应用程序在美国最右端的暴力行为中扮演的角色。

Representative Carolyn Maloney, who chairs the panel, cited press reports that detailed violent threats on Parler against state elected officials for their role in certifying the election results before the Jan. 6 attack that left five dead. She also noted numerous Parler users have been arrested and charged with threatening violence against elected officials or for their role in participating in the attack.

小组主席卡洛琳·马洛尼（Carolyn Maloney）引用了新闻报道，详细报道了在1月6日袭击造成5人死亡之前，帕勒对州选举产生的官员在证明选举结果中所起的暴力威胁。她还指出，Parler的许多用户已被逮捕，并被指控威胁对民选官员的暴力行为或他们参与攻击的作用。

Reuters reported this week that Parler partially resumed online operations with the help of a Russian-owned technology company after being shut down by Amazon Web Services, which said it had failed to moderate violent content effectively.

路透社本周报道说，在被亚马逊网络服务公司关闭后，帕勒在一家俄罗斯国有技术公司的帮助下部分恢复了在线运营。亚马逊网络服务公司称未能有效缓解暴力内容。

Maloney asked the FBI to review Parler’s role “as a potential facilitator of planning and incitement related to the violence, as a repository of key evidence posted by users on its site, and as a potential conduit for foreign governments who may be financing civil unrest in the United States.”

马洛尼（Maloney）要求联邦调查局（FBI）审查帕勒（Parler）的角色：“作为与暴力相关的计划和煽动行为的潜在促进者，用户在其站点上发布的主要证据的存储库，以及为可能为内乱提供资金的外国政府的潜在渠道”美国。”

Maloney asked the FBI to review Parler’s financing and its ties to Russia after she noted the company had re-emerged.

马洛尼（Maloney）注意到帕勒（Parler）重新出现后，要求联邦调查局（FBI）审查帕勒（Parler）的融资及其与俄罗斯的关系。

Maloney cited Justice Department charges against a Texas man who used a Parler account to post threats regarding the riots that he would return to the Capitol on Jan. 19 “carrying weapons and massing in numbers so large that no army could match them.”

马洛尼援引司法部对一名得克萨斯州男子的指控，该男子使用帕勒的账户发布了骚乱的威胁，称他将于1月19日返回国会大厦，“携带的武器和数量庞大，以至于没有军队可以与他们匹敌”。

The Justice Department said the threats were viewed by other social media users tens of thousands of times.

司法部表示，其他社交媒体用户已经查看了数以万计的威胁。

More than 25,000 National Guard troops and new fencing ringed with razor wire were among the unprecedented security steps put in place ahead of Wednesday’s inauguration of President Joe Biden.

在周三总统拜登就职典礼之前，采取了前所未有的安全措施，其中包括25,000多名国民警卫队部队和用剃刀钢丝打成圈的新围栏。
