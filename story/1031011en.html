PineCube相机套件售价30美元-linuxgizmos.com

2020-10-25 05:14:45

Pine64’s $30 PineCube camera dev kit runs Linux on an Allwinner S3 and offers a 5MP, OmniVision OV5640 based M12 camera with IR night-vision plus audio I/O, WiFi, 10/100 LAN with PoE, USB, 26-pin GPIO, and optional battery and display. The open-spec PineCube was first revealed by Pine64 in early 2019 as a device called The CUBE with an 8-megapixel Sony iMX179 CMOS sensor. By early this year, it was recast as the CUBE IP Camera, which was promised for a delayed 2Q release due to issues with the Sony camera implementation. Since then, Pine64 switched to a 5MP OmniVision OV5640 sensor and a new PineCube name. The camera dev kit has now shipped for $30. PineCube, front and back (click images to enlarge) The 55 x 51.5 x 51mm, double-board PineCube runs on an 800MHz, Cortex-A7 based Allwinner S3, a camera-focused SoC co-developed with Allwinner by Sochip that appeared on Sochip’s Quanzhi S3 dev board. Last November, Olimex teased an S3-based S3-OLinuXino board, inviting its community to comment on the specs, but the SBC has yet to arrive. The Allwinner S3 offers [email protected] decode and encode with support for up to two HD cameras.

Pine64售价30美元的PineCube相机开发套件在AllWinner S3上运行Linux，并提供基于500万像素、OmniVision OV5640的M12相机，具有红外夜视和音频I/O、WiFi、10/100 LAN、PoE、USB、26针GPIO以及可选的电池和显示器。2019年初，Pine64首次发布了开放规格的PineCube，当时它是一款名为Cube的设备，配备了800万像素的索尼iMX179 CMOS传感器。到今年初，它被重新塑造为Cube IP相机，由于索尼相机实现的问题，承诺推迟第二季度的发布。从那时起，Pine64换成了500万像素的OmniVision OV5640传感器和一个新的PineCube名称。相机开发套件现已发货，售价为30美元。PineCube正面和背面(点击图像放大)55 x 51.5 x 51 mm双板PineCube运行在800 MHz、基于Cortex-A7的AllWinner S3上，Allwinner S3是与Allwinner by SoChip共同开发的一款相机SoC，出现在SoChip的全志S3开发板上。去年11月，Olimex取笑了一个基于S3的S3-OLinuXino董事会，邀请其社区对规格发表评论，但SBC尚未到达。Allwinner S3提供[受电子邮件保护的]解码和编码功能，最多可支持两个高清摄像头。

The OmniVision OV5640 is used in embedded devices such as Arrow’s 96Boards compatible DragonBoard 410c Camera Kit, which combines the DragonBoard 410c with the DesignCore Camera Mezzanine Board OV5640. The OV5640 is a 1/4″ CMOS sensor that supports 5-megapixel @ 15fps and 1080p @ 30fps video. It has a 3.5-inch focal length and 10cm-to-infinity focus distance, as well as a 65-degree field of view. PineCube and upcoming M12 lens selection (click images to enlarge) The PineCube specs have shifted slightly since the Feb. 2019 announcement. In addition to the new sensor, there is only a single RAM option for 128MB DDR3 and the GPIO has shrunk from 32 to 26 pins.

OmniVision OV5640用于嵌入式设备，例如Arrow的96Boards兼容DragonBoard 410c相机套件，它将DragonBoard 410c与DesignCore相机夹层板OV5640结合在一起。OV5640是1/4英寸CMOS传感器，支持500万像素@15fps和1080p@30fps视频。它有3.5英寸的焦距和10厘米到无限远的焦距，以及65度的视场。PineCube和即将推出的M12镜头选择(点击图片放大)自2019年2月发布以来，PineCube的规格略有变化。除了新的传感器，128MB DDR3只有一个RAM选项，GPIO从32针缩小到26针。

Other features include Fast Ethernet with passive PoE, 2.4GHz WiFi, micro-USB power and USB 2.0 host ports, mic and speaker, and a microSD slot. A battery pack and 4.5-inch display are optional. The PineCube offers an M12-compatible attachment. A variety of lenses, including a long-range zoom and a 185 degree fisheye, will soon be offered in the Pine Store.

其他功能包括带无源PoE的快速以太网、2.4 GHz WiFi、微型USB电源和USB 2.0主机端口、麦克风和扬声器以及microSD插槽。电池组和4.5英寸显示屏是可选的。PineCube提供与M12兼容的附件。各种镜头，包括远程变焦和185度鱼眼，很快就会在Pine Store提供。

A Linux SDK and schematics are available for the kit, which is designed for developers with “extensive embedded Linux OS experience and an interest in open source IP camera or embedded computer development.” Linux Mainline development has begun, but the CNXSoft post that first covered the launch noted that mainline may take a while and will be dependent on developer interest.

该工具包提供了Linux SDK和原理图，专为具有“丰富的嵌入式Linux OS经验和对开源IP摄像机或嵌入式计算机开发感兴趣的开发人员”而设计。Linux主线开发已经开始，但CNXSoft最先报道此次发布的帖子指出，主线可能需要一段时间，并将取决于开发者的兴趣。

Power — 5V/1A via micro-USB or 4-18V via PoE; optional 950-1600mAh Lithium Polymer Ion Battery Pack (model 903048)

电源-5V/1A(通过微型通用串行总线)或4-18V(通过PoE)；可选的950mAh-1600mAh锂聚合物离子电池组(型号903048)。

The PineCube is available now for $29.99. More information may be found on Pine64’s shopping and wiki pages.

PineCube现在售价29.99美元。更多信息可以在Pine64的购物和维基页面上找到。
