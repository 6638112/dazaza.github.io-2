Electric autonomous vehicle startup Cruise raises $2B at a $30B valuation from Microsoft, GM, Honda, and others
电动自动驾驶汽车初创公司Cruise以30亿美元的估值从Microsoft，GM，Honda等公司筹集了2B

2021-01-19 22:21:57

GM, Honda and other institutional investors have also put more capital into Cruise as the autonomous vehicle company inches closer to commercializing its technology.

随着自动驾驶汽车公司将其技术商业化的步伐越来越近，通用汽车，本田和其他机构投资者也向克鲁斯投入了更多资金。

While Microsoft’s capital is important, the partnership might provide equal and longer term value for Cruise, at least in the two companies’ views. Under the long-term strategic partnership, Cruise will leverage Azure, Microsoft’s cloud and edge computing platform, to commercialize its autonomous vehicle solutions at scale.

尽管微软的资本很重要，但这种合作关系至少可以在两家公司的观点中为Cruise提供平等和长期的价值。在长期的战略合作伙伴关系下，Cruise将利用Microsoft的云和边缘计算平台Azure来将其自动驾驶解决方案大规模商业化。

Any autonomous vehicle company aiming to commercialize — meaning bring their tech to the public at scale — needs a robust cloud computing platform. Operating fleets of self-driving vehicles that will shuttle people and even packages generates a massive amount of data, making cloud services one of the bigger costs for an AV company.

任何旨在商业化的自动驾驶汽车公司（即将其技术大规模地公开上市）都需要一个强大的云计算平台。运行无人驾驶甚至包裹的自动驾驶车队会产生大量数据，这使云服务成为视听公司的更大成本之一。

Cruise’s partnership with Microsoft aims to provide benefits for both companies. Cruise will be able to lock in lower prices for cloud services and Microsoft will be able to test some of its bleeding edge systems that can handle workloads needed to bring machine learning and robotics — like autonomous vehicles — to life and at scale.

Cruise与Microsoft的合作伙伴关系旨在为两家公司提供收益。 Cruise能够锁定较低的云服务价格，微软将能够测试其一些前沿系统，这些系统可以处理将机器学习和机器人技术（如自动驾驶汽车）实现并大规模实现所需的工作量。

“Advances in digital technology are redefining every aspect of our work and life, including how we move people and goods,” Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella said in a statement. “As Cruise and GM’s preferred cloud, we will apply the power of Azure to help them scale and make autonomous transportation mainstream.”

微软首席执行官萨蒂亚·纳德拉（Satya Nadella）在一份声明中说：“数字技术的进步正在重新定义我们工作和生活的方方面面，包括我们如何移动人员和货物。” “作为Cruise和GM首选的云，我们将利用Azure的力量来帮助他们扩展规模，并使自动驾驶成为主流。”

The partnership extends to GM as well, according to Tuesday’s announcement. Microsoft will be GM’s preferred public cloud provider to help the automaker accelerate several of it digitization initiatives as well as streamline operations across digital supply chains.

根据周二的公告，该合作伙伴关系也扩展到了通用汽车。微软将成为通用汽车的首选公共云提供商，以帮助该汽车制造商加快其数位化计划并简化数字供应链中的运营。

The partnership will not only allow Cruise to accelerate the commercialization of its all-electric, self-driving vehicles, it help “GM realize even more benefits from cloud computing as we launch 30 new electric vehicles globally by 2025 and create new businesses and services to drive growth, GM Chairman and CEO Mary Barra said.

这项合作伙伴关系不仅将使Cruise加速其全电动无人驾驶汽车的商业化，而且还将帮助“通用汽车从云计算中获得更多收益，因为我们到2025年在全球推出30款新型电动汽车，并创造新的业务和服务，通用汽车董事长兼首席执行官玛丽·巴拉（Mary Barra）表示：
