JummBox-一个绘制和分享Chiptune旋律的在线工具

2020-11-13 23:50:16

This is an online tool for sketching and sharing chiptune melodies. It is a modification of the original BeepBox by John Nesky.

这是一个绘制和分享Chiptune旋律的在线工具。这是由约翰·尼斯基(John Nesky)设计的原版蜂鸣盒的改装。

If you're familiar with BeepBox and just want to learn what JummBox does differently, check out this overview video. You can also find the latest patch notes here.

如果你对BeepBox很熟悉，只想了解JummBox有什么不同之处，请看这段概述视频。您还可以在此处找到最新的补丁说明。

All song data is contained in the URL at the top of your browser. When you make changes to the song, the URL is updated to reflect your changes. When you are satisfied with your song, just copy and paste the URL to save and share your song!

所有歌曲数据都包含在浏览器顶部的URL中。当您对歌曲进行更改时，URL会更新以反映您的更改。当您对您的歌曲感到满意时，只需复制并粘贴URL即可保存并共享您的歌曲！

You can add or remove notes by clicking on the gray rows at the top. JummBox automatically plays the notes out loud for you. Try it!

您可以通过单击顶部的灰色行来添加或删除备注。JummBox会自动为您大声播放音符。试试看！

Notes go into patterns, and you can edit one pattern at a time. Those numbered boxes at the bottom of the editor are the different patterns you can edit. Click the other boxes to move to a different part of the song, or click the arrows on the currently selected box to swap which pattern is played during that part of the song.

便笺可以放入图案中，您可以一次编辑一个图案。编辑器底部的那些编号框是您可以编辑的不同图案。单击其他框可移动到歌曲的其他部分，或单击当前选定框上的箭头以交换歌曲该部分期间播放的模式。

JummBox can play several rows of patterns simultaneously, and each row has its own set of patterns. Most rows can play melodies or harmonies, but the bottom row is for drums.

JummBox可以同时播放多行图案，每行都有自己的一组图案。大多数排可以演奏旋律或和声，但最下面的一排是鼓。

All song data is contained in the URL at the top of your browser. When you make changes to the song, the URL is updated to reflect your changes. When you are satisfied with your song, just copy and paste the URL to save and share your song!

所有歌曲数据都包含在浏览器顶部的URL中。当您对歌曲进行更改时，URL会更新以反映您的更改。当您对您的歌曲感到满意时，只需复制并粘贴URL即可保存并共享您的歌曲！

When JummBox has focus (click on its interface above), you can use these keyboard shortcuts:

当JummBox获得焦点时(单击上面的界面)，您可以使用以下键盘快捷键：

- +: move the notes in the pattern up or down (and hold Shift to move an octave at a time!)

+：向上或向下移动图案中的音符(按住Shift键一次移动一个八度！)。

Shift M: Mute/unmute all channels N: set pattern to the lowest-numbered one not used anywhere in the song (This can help you find gaps in patterns that you missed).

Shift M：静音/取消静音所有声道N：将Pattern设置为歌曲中未使用的编号最低的声道(这可以帮助您找到遗漏的声道中的间隙)。

In the note pattern editor, you can click and drag horizontally on a note to adjust its duration. You can also click above or below an existing note to add more notes to be played simultaneously, which is called a chord.

在注解模式编辑器中，您可以在注解上单击并水平拖动以调整其持续时间。您也可以在现有音符的上方或下方单击以添加要同时播放的更多音符，这称为和弦。

ADVANCED: Drag vertically from an existing note to bend its pitch, or drag vertically from above or below the note to adjust its volume. You can perform fine volume adjustment if you hold Control while doing this!

高级：从现有便笺垂直拖动以降低其音调，或从便笺上方或下方垂直拖动以调整其音量。如果您在执行此操作时按住Ctrl键，则可以执行微调音量！

ADVANCED: Click and drag on the pattern grid to make a selection. You can then press C/V to mass copy/paste parts of your song.

高级：在图案网格上单击并拖动以进行选择。然后，您可以按C/V批量复制/粘贴部分歌曲。

JummBox has many more features. Try playing with the buttons and menus on the right side to find out what it can do! You can also click on the label next to each option for a description of what it does.

JummBox还有更多功能。试着玩一下右边的按钮和菜单，看看它能做什么！您还可以单击每个选项旁边的标签，查看其功能说明。

Check out some of these songs that other people have posted on Twitter! If you find something you like, you should let the creator know! And if you see any beginners asking for advice, maybe you can help them!

看看其他人在推特上发布的一些歌曲吧！如果你找到了你喜欢的东西，你应该让创造者知道！如果你看到任何初学者寻求建议，也许你可以帮助他们！

JummBox is developed by Jummbus. You can find him sporadically at twitter.com/jummbus. BeepBox was originally developed by John Nesky, also known as @shaktool.

JummBox是由Jummbus开发的。你可以偶尔在twitter.com/jumbus上找到他。BeepBox最初是由John Nesky(也被称为@shaktool)开发的。

You can download an offline version of JummBox to use when you don't have access to the internet. The features are otherwise the same.

您可以下载JummBox的离线版本，以便在无法访问互联网时使用。在其他方面，这些功能是相同的。
