Hydroxycloroquine reduces odds of Covid death by 73%
羟基氯喹将Covid死亡的几率降低了73％

2021-06-07 05:10:22

The role of hydroxychloroquine (HCQ) in early outpatient management of mild coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) needs further investigation.

羟基氯喹（HCQ）在早期门诊病治疗Mild Coronavirus疾病中的作用（Covid-19）需要进一步调查。

This study was a multicenter, population-based national retrospective-cohort investigation of 28,759 adults with mild COVID-19 seen at the network of Comprehensive Healthcare Centers (CHC) between March and September 2020 throughout Iran. The baseline characteristics and outcome variables were extracted from the national integrated health system database.

本研究是一家基于人口的人口的国家回顾 - 队列 - 28,759名成年人，在伊朗三月和9月20日在伊朗综合医疗中心（CHC）网络中看到了温和的Covid-19。从国家综合卫生系统数据库中提取基线特征和结果变量。

A total of 7295 (25.37%) patients who presented with mild COVID-19 within 3–7 days of symptoms onset received HCQ (400 mg twice daily on day 1 followed by 200 mg twice daily for the next four days and were then followed for 14 days).

总共7295名（25.37％）患有轻度Covid-19的患者在症状的3-7天内发作，接受HCQ（每日每天两次，每天两次，接下来的四天每天两次，然后进行14天）。

The main outcome measures were hospitalization or death for six months follow-up. COVID-19-related hospitalizations or deaths occurred in 523 (7.17%) and 27 (0.37%) respectively, in HCQ recipients and 2382 (11.10%) and 287 (1.34%) respectively, in non-recipients. The odds of hospitalization or death was reduced by 38% (odds ratio [OR] = 0.62; 95% confidence interval [CI]: 0.56–0.68, p = < 0.001) and 73% (OR = 0.27; 95% CI: 0.18–0.41, p = < 0.001) in HCQ recipients and non-recipients. These effects were maintained after adjusting for age, comorbidities, and diagnostic modality. No serious HCQ-related adverse drug reactions were reported.

主要的结果措施六个月随访时为住院或死亡。与非接受者分别在523（7.17％）和2782（11.10％）和2382（11.10％）和287（1.34％）中分别发生在523（7.17％）和27（0.37％）中发生的Covid-19相关住院或死亡。住院或死亡的几率降低了38％（差距[或] = 0.62; 95％置信区间[CI]：0.56-0.68，P = <0.001）和73％（或= 0.27; 95％CI： 0.18-0.41，p = <0.001）在HCQ收件人和非接受者中。调整年龄，组用和诊断方式后，维持这些效果。没有报告任何严重的HCQ相关的不良药物反应。

In our large outpatient national cohort of adults with mild COVID-19 disease who were given HCQ early in the course of the disease, the odds of hospitalization or death was reduced significantly regardless of age or comorbidities.

在我们在疾病过程早期获得HCQ的温和Covid-19疾病的大型门诊国家队列中，无论年龄或合并症如何，住院或死亡的几率明显减少。
