PeerJS – Simple peer-to-peer with WebRTC

PeerJS wraps the browser's WebRTC implementation to provide a complete, configurable, and easy-to-use peer-to-peer connection API. Equipped with nothing but an ID, a peer can create a P2P data or media stream connection to a remote peer. var conn = peer.connect('another-peers-id');// on open will be launch when you successfully connect to PeerServerconn.on('open', function(){ // here you have conn.id conn.send('hi!');});

To broker connections, PeerJS connects to a PeerServer. Note that no peer-to-peer data goes through the server; The server acts only as a connection broker.

为了代理连接，PeerJS连接到PeerServer。请注意，没有对等数据通过服务器。该服务器仅充当连接代理。

If you don't want to run your own PeerServer, we offer a free cloud-hosted version of PeerServer. Official PeerServer!
