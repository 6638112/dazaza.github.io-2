Pocket migration to Firefox accounts
袖珍迁移到Firefox帐户

2022-02-14 22:50:18

Pocket, a product from Mozilla, is your home and guide for great content around the Web. Pocket lets you save things from anywhere on the Web and find suggestions from our editors for what to read next. When you use Pocket, you can access saved articles and videos in a private and secure account, and read or watch on your computer or mobile device.

Pocket是Mozilla的一款产品，是您的家，是您在网络上获取精彩内容的指南。Pocket可以让你在网上的任何地方保存东西，并从我们的编辑那里找到下一步阅读的建议。当你使用Pocket时，你可以通过一个私人且安全的帐户访问保存的文章和视频，并在电脑或移动设备上阅读或观看。

Pocket has been part of the Mozilla family of products since 2017, and up to this year, we’ve maintained a separate account system. We’re now migrating all accounts to use Firefox accounts instead.

Pocket自2017年以来一直是Mozilla系列产品的一部分，截至今年，我们一直保持一个独立的账户系统。我们现在将所有帐户迁移到使用Firefox帐户。

Pocket, which previously allowed users to log in with their email address and password, is migrating all accounts to use Firefox accounts instead. Products from Mozilla use Firefox accounts as a centralized and secure account system. With this migration, Pocket is taking advantage of the many benefits that this system has to offer.

Pocket以前允许用户使用电子邮件地址和密码登录，现在正在将所有帐户迁移到使用Firefox帐户。Mozilla的产品使用Firefox帐户作为一个集中且安全的帐户系统。通过这种迁移，Pocket充分利用了该系统提供的许多好处。

With this migration, we’re adopting a more secure account system developed by security experts at Mozilla. Not only will this make Pocket more secure, but it allows you to opt-in to enhanced security functionality, like two-factor authentication (2FA) and account recovery keys, to make your account even more secure.

通过这次迁移，我们采用了由Mozilla安全专家开发的更安全的帐户系统。这不仅会使Pocket更加安全，还允许您选择增强的安全功能，如双因素身份验证（2FA）和帐户恢复密钥，以使您的帐户更加安全。

In addition to enhanced security, the same Firefox account can also be used for other products from Mozilla, including Firefox, Mozilla VPN and more.

除了增强的安全性，同一个Firefox帐户还可以用于Mozilla的其他产品，包括Firefox、Mozilla VPN等。

New users signing up for Pocket will be prompted to create a new Firefox account or use their existing account to get started.

注册Pocket的新用户将被提示创建新的Firefox帐户或使用其现有帐户开始使用。

Existing users of Pocket will be asked to migrate their account the next time they log in. At that time, they’ll have the option to create a new Firefox account or use an existing account. They can also skip the migration step for a limited time.

Pocket的现有用户将在下次登录时被要求迁移其帐户。届时，他们可以选择创建新的Firefox帐户或使用现有帐户。他们还可以在有限的时间内跳过迁移步骤。

We’re requesting all existing users to migrate their accounts at their earliest convenience. The deadline for migrating is June 30th, 2022. After this date, Pocket users will be required to migrate to a Firefox account in order to login and access their list.

我们要求所有现有用户尽早迁移他们的帐户。迁移的最后期限是2022年6月30日。在此日期之后，Pocket用户需要迁移到Firefox帐户才能登录并访问他们的列表。

Aside from changing how you log in to Pocket, this does not affect your saved items. For Pocket Premium subscribers, migrating your account will not impact your subscription.

除了更改登录Pocket的方式外，这不会影响您保存的物品。对于Pocket Premium订户，迁移您的帐户不会影响您的订阅。

Open Pocket’s Login page. If you’re already logged in, simply log out to be redirected to the Login page.

打开Pocket的登录页面。如果您已经登录，只需注销即可重定向到登录页面。

Log into Pocket. Afterwards, you’ll see a message with instructions for migrating your account.

登录口袋。之后，您将看到一条消息，其中包含迁移帐户的说明。

Select Yes to proceed then select Continue (If you select No, you will be prompted to migrate the next time you log in).

选择“是”继续，然后选择“继续”（如果选择“否”，下次登录时将提示您进行迁移）。

You’ll be redirected to your Pocket account. From now on, simply use your Firefox account to log into Pocket.

您将被重定向到您的Pocket帐户。从现在起，只需使用Firefox帐户登录Pocket即可。

What is your relationship with Firefox? Pocket is part of the Mozilla family of products, which are all connected via Firefox accounts.

你和Firefox是什么关系？Pocket是Mozilla系列产品的一部分，所有产品都通过Firefox帐户连接。

Do I need to switch to the Firefox browser to keep using Pocket? While Pocket now requires a Firefox account to sign up or log in, you can continue using your preferred web browser to access Pocket. Our browser extensions for many popular browsers and apps for iOS and Android will continue to be available as well.

我需要切换到Firefox浏览器才能继续使用Pocket吗？虽然Pocket现在需要Firefox帐户才能注册或登录，但您可以继续使用首选web浏览器访问Pocket。我们针对许多流行浏览器的浏览器扩展，以及针对iOS和Android的应用程序也将继续提供。

I already have a Firefox account and a Pocket account. What do I need to do to migrate my account?

我已经有了一个Firefox账户和一个Pocket账户。我需要做什么来迁移我的帐户？

To migrate your account, log in to Pocket using your Firefox account. Then, your Pocket account email address will change to the address you’re using for your Firefox account.

要迁移您的帐户，请使用Firefox帐户登录Pocket。然后，您的Pocket帐户电子邮件地址将更改为您用于Firefox帐户的地址。

What happens if I don’t migrate by the deadline? After June 30th, 2022 you’ll be required to migrate your account the next time you attempt to access Pocket’s app or website. You will not be able to proceed to your account until you complete the migration.

如果我在截止日期前不迁移会发生什么？2022年6月30日之后，下次尝试访问Pocket的应用程序或网站时，您将被要求迁移您的帐户。在完成迁移之前，您将无法继续使用您的帐户。

Is there any cost associated with this migration to Firefox accounts? There is no cost to sign up for Pocket, and there is no cost to migrate your existing Pocket account to a Firefox account.

迁移到Firefox账户会产生任何费用吗？注册Pocket无需支付任何费用，将现有Pocket帐户迁移到Firefox帐户也无需支付任何费用。

I have a subscription to Pocket Premium. Do I need to migrate? All Pocket users are being asked to migrate their account. If you subscribe to Pocket Premium, there will be no impact on your subscription or the available features.

我订阅了Pocket Premium。我需要迁移吗？所有Pocket用户都被要求迁移他们的帐户。如果您订阅Pocket Premium，将不会影响您的订阅或可用功能。

How does this change impact my privacy? It doesn’t. Pocket is a part of the Mozilla family of products. By using a Firefox account to access Pocket, your account is subject to Mozilla and Pocket’s terms of service and privacy policies. To learn how to manage your Firefox account and the data associated with it, click here.

这一变化如何影响我的隐私？Pocket是Mozilla系列产品的一部分。通过使用Firefox帐户访问Pocket，您的帐户将遵守Mozilla和Pocket的服务条款和隐私政策。要了解如何管理Firefox帐户及其相关数据，请单击此处。

I no longer want to use Pocket. What should I do? If you no longer wish to use Pocket, you can click here to export your account data (optional) and access the account deletion page here.

我再也不想用口袋了。我该怎么办？如果您不想再使用Pocket，可以单击此处导出您的帐户数据（可选）并访问此处的帐户删除页面。

Can I delete my Pocket account without deleting my Firefox account? Yes, you can delete your Pocket account without impacting your Firefox account. In order to make changes or delete your Pocket account, click here.

我可以在不删除Firefox帐户的情况下删除我的Pocket帐户吗？是的，你可以删除你的Pocket帐户而不影响你的Firefox帐户。要更改或删除您的Pocket帐户，请单击此处。

Before deleting your account, we recommend exporting your data. Deleting an account is permanent and cannot be undone.

在删除您的帐户之前，我们建议导出您的数据。删除帐户是永久性的，无法撤消。

How can I get help with an account-related issue? If you have a question regarding your Pocket account, such as password reset, merging two Pocket accounts, or another issue, click here to contact Pocket Support.

如何在与帐户相关的问题上获得帮助？如果您有关于Pocket帐户的问题，如密码重置、合并两个Pocket帐户或其他问题，请单击此处联系Pocket Support。

If you have a question regarding your Firefox account, click here to visit Firefox’s help center or ask a question in the support forum.

如果您对Firefox帐户有任何疑问，请单击此处访问Firefox的帮助中心或在支持论坛上提问。

I have a question that isn’t answered here. How can I get in touch? Click here to contact Pocket Support, and we’ll be glad to help you.

我有一个问题在这里没有回答。我怎么联系？单击此处联系Pocket Support，我们将很乐意为您提供帮助。
