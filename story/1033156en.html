第二体系效应

2020-11-04 18:56:38

Jump to navigation Jump to search The second-system effect (also known as second-system syndrome) is the tendency of small, elegant, and successful systems to be succeeded by over-engineered, bloated systems, due to inflated expectations and overconfidence. [1]

跳转到导航跳跃搜索第二系统效应(也称为第二系统综合症)是指由于过度期望和过度自信，小的、优雅的、成功的系统被过度设计、臃肿的系统所取代的趋势。[1]。

The phrase was first used by Fred Brooks in his book The Mythical Man-Month, first published in 1975. It described the jump from a set of simple operating systems on the IBM 700/7000 series to OS/360 on the 360 series, which happened in 1964. [2]

弗雷德·布鲁克斯(Fred Brooks)在1975年首次出版的《神话中的人月》(The Mythical Man-Month)一书中首次使用了这一短语。它描述了从IBM 700/7000系列上的一组简单操作系统到360系列上的OS/360的飞跃，这发生在1964年。[2]。
