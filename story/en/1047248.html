Pandemic Drove Sales of 4G and 5G-Enabled PCs To New Record In 2020
大流行带动4G和5G支持的PC的销售在2020年再创新高

2021-02-06 20:43:35

View all news BOSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Global sales of cellular-enabled mobile PCs reached more than 10 million units for the first time in 2020 as home workers sought improved connectivity in response to the closure of office facilities during the COVID-19 pandemic. According to the latest analysis from Strategy Analytics’ Connected Computing Devices program, global shipments increased by 70% to 10.1 million, the highest ever annual total. North America accounted for nearly half of 3G-, 4G- and 5G-enabled PC shipments, while Europe and Asia-Pacific accounted for 45%. The report, Notebook PC Cellular Connectivity Shipment and Installed Base Forecast, estimates that more than 26 million cellular-enabled PCs are now in use worldwide, an increase of 25% in twelve months.

查看所有新闻波士顿-（美国商业资讯）-蜂窝式移动PC的全球销量在2020年首次超过1000万台，这是由于在COVID- 19大流行。根据Strategy Analytics的“连接计算设备”计划的最新分析，全球出货量增长了70％，达到1,010万，是有史以来最高的年度总出货量。北美占启用3G，4G和5G的PC出货量的近一半，而欧洲和亚太地区则占45％。该报告《笔记本电脑蜂窝连接出货量和安装基础预测》估计，全球目前有超过2600万台支持蜂窝功能的PC正在使用，在十二个月内增长了25％。

While 4G/LTE standards dominated the market in 2020, accounting for 97% of cellular-enabled PC shipments, 5G notebook launches in 2021 are showing a greater diversity in price points, form factors, and vendor participation, and Strategy Analytics expects 5G to build its share towards 69% by 2025. The report indicates that this growth will depend on improvements in customer education by vendors, carriers and retailers.

尽管2020年4G / LTE标准在市场中占据主导地位，占蜂窝移动PC出货量的97％，但2021年推出的5G笔记本电脑在价格点，外形尺寸和供应商参与方面将显示出更大的多样性，Strategy Analytics预计5G将会建立到2025年，其市场份额将达到69％。报告指出，这一增长将取决于供应商，运营商和零售商对客户教育水平的提高。

“What form new pricing plans take in the 5G world must be informed by a holistic view of the consumer, which devices they use where, and what they use them for,” says Chirag Upadhyay, Industry Analyst. “In the enterprise space, vendors, carriers and resellers must be able to explain how connected notebooks help save companies money in the field and help reduce security breaches compared to mobile hotspots or dongles.”

“在5G世界中，新的定价计划应采取什么样的形式，必须从整体角度了解消费者，他们在何处使用哪些设备以及将它们用于什么目的，”行业分析师Chirag Upadhyay说。 “在企业领域，与移动热点或加密狗相比，供应商，运营商和转售商必须能够说明联网笔记本如何帮助公司在现场节省资金并减少安全漏洞。”

Eric Smith, Director, Connected Computing Devices, added: “We see this is a ‘when’ problem, not an ‘if’ problem. Cellular connected notebooks will become more commonplace over the next decade but the key to when that happens lies in how industry players introduce the idea to consumers. A clear view into how users choose cellular plans is crucial for vendors, carriers, and even retailers to understand how to better educate consumer segments of more cellular-embedded options.”

互联计算设备总监Eric Smith补充说：“我们看到这是一个'when'问题，而不是'if'问题。蜂窝式笔记本电脑在未来十年将变得越来越普遍，但何时才能实现这一目标的关键在于行业参与者如何将这种想法介绍给消费者。清楚地了解用户如何选择蜂窝计划对于供应商，运营商甚至零售商了解如何更好地教育更多的蜂窝选项用户是至关重要的。”
