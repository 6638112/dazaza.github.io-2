Metadata for Apple's WWDC keynote YouTube video includes terms such as “m1x” and “M1X MacBook Pro”
Apple的WWDC主题演讲元数据包括“M1X”和“M1X MacBook Pro”等术语

2021-06-10 07:24:22

Apple’s Worldwide Developers Conference keynote came and went this year without a new MacBook Pro — but it looks like that wasn’t the original intent! Intriguingly, Apple quietly included the phrases “M1X MacBook Pro” and “M1X” as tags on its YouTube video of the live keynote, as spotted by Max Balzer (via 9to5Mac).

Apple的全球开发人员会议主题演讲是今年来了，没有一个新的MacBook Pro - 但它看起来不是原始的意图！有趣的是，Apple悄悄地将“M1X MacBook Pro”和“M1X”的短语作为其YouTube视频的标签，如Max Balzer（通过9To5Mac）所察觉。

Not only does that sound like tacit confirmation of at least one new Arm-powered MacBook Pro, it also corroborates the rumors that it’ll include a new M1 chip, and that Apple will likely market it as an enhanced “X” variant, like it used to do with its high-end iPads, rather than going straight to M2. (Or, as some are pointing out, it could mean that someone on the social media team decided to try for an SEO boost and made it up.)

这听起来不仅像默认确认至少一个新的ARM-Powered MacBook Pro，它也证实了它将它包含一个新的M1芯片的谣言，并且苹果可能会将其作为增强的“X”变量推销，如它曾与其高端iPad一起做，而不是直接到M2。 （或者，有些人指出，它可能意味着社交媒体团队中的某人决定尝试SEO提升并使其成为。）

Three weeks ago, Bloomberg’s Mark Gurman reported that we’d see redesigned 14- and 16-inch MacBook Pros “as early as this summer,” ones that would bring back the SD card slot, HDMI port, and some form of the beloved trip-friendly MagSafe charging connector; offer twice as many high-performance CPU and GPU cores; and support up to 64GB of RAM. I really thought announcing them at WWDC would be the perfect way to distract from recent developer turmoil — you know, by announcing a MacBook Pro that’s actually for pros?

三周前，布隆伯格的标记古尔曼报告说，我们会看到重新设计的14和16英寸MacBook专业书“早在今年夏天，那将带回SD卡插槽，HDMI港，以及某种形式的心爱的旅行 - 友好的Magsafe充电连接器;提供两倍的高性能CPU和GPU核心;并支持高达64GB的RAM。我真的想在WWDC宣布他们是分散最近开发商动荡的完美方式 - 你知道，通过宣布实际用于专业人士的MacBook Pro吗？

Perhaps that was the original idea, but we got an absolutely jam-packed keynote filled with rapid-fire software announcements instead. Check out our supercut below for the condensed video version, or read our list of the 15 biggest WWDC 2021 announcements if you only want the highlights.

也许这是原始的想法，但我们得到了一个绝对众所周的众多众所周知的主题演讲，更换了快速消防软件通知。在下面查看浓缩视频版本的SuperCut，或者阅读我们的15个最大的WWDC 2021公告列表，如果您只希望亮点。
