Yaw String
偏航线

2022-02-15 18:26:35

Jump to navigation Jump to search The yaw string, also known as a slip string, is a simple device for indicating a slip or skid in an aircraft in flight. It performs the same function as the slip-skid indicator ball, but is more sensitive, and does not require the pilot to look down at the instrument panel. [1] Technically, it measures sideslip angle, not yaw angle, [2] but this indicates how the aircraft must be yawed to return the sideslip angle to zero.

跳转到导航跳转搜索偏航线，也称为滑线，是一种简单的设备，用于指示飞行中飞机的滑转或打滑。它的功能与滑动滑动指示器球相同，但更灵敏，不需要飞行员俯视仪表板。[1] 从技术上讲，它测量的是侧滑角，而不是偏航角[2]，但这表明飞机必须如何偏航才能将侧滑角恢复为零。

It is typically constructed from a short piece or tuft of yarn placed in the free air stream where it is visible to the pilot. [3] In closed-cockpit aircraft, it is usually taped to the aircraft canopy. It may also be mounted on the aircraft's nose, either directly on the skin, or elevated on a mast, in which case it may also be fitted with a small paper cone at the trailing end. [4] They are commonly used on gliders, but may also be found on jet aircraft (especially fighters), ultralight aircraft, light-sport aircraft, autogyros, [5] airplanes and helicopters. Its usefulness on airplanes with a tractor configuration (single propeller at the nose) is limited because the propeller creates turbulence and the spiral slipstream displaces the string to one side. [6]

它通常由放置在飞行员可见的自由气流中的一小片或一簇纱线构成。[3] 在封闭式驾驶舱飞机上，它通常用胶带固定在飞机舱盖上。它也可以安装在飞机上#39；s型机头，可直接放在皮肤上，也可放在桅杆上，在这种情况下，它的尾端还可安装一个小纸锥。[4] 它们通常用于滑翔机，但也可能用于喷气式飞机（尤其是战斗机）、超轻型飞机、轻型运动飞机、自动陀螺仪、[5]飞机和直升机。它在拖拉机配置的飞机上（机头只有一个螺旋桨）的用途是有限的，因为螺旋桨会产生湍流，螺旋滑流会将管柱移向一侧。[6]

The yaw string is considered a primary flight reference instrument on gliders, which must be flown with near zero sideslip angle to reduce drag as much as possible. It is valued for its high sensitivity, and the fact that it is presented in a head-up display. Even the most sophisticated modern racing sailplanes are fitted with yaw strings by their pilots, who reference them constantly throughout the flight.

偏航管柱被认为是滑翔机上的主要飞行参考仪器，滑翔机必须以接近零的侧滑角飞行，以尽可能减少阻力。它的价值在于它的高灵敏度，以及在平视显示器上显示的事实。即使是最先进的现代赛艇，飞行员也会为其安装偏航弦，在整个飞行过程中，飞行员都会不断参考这些弦。

The yaw string dates from the earliest days of aviation, and actually was the first flight instrument. The Wright Brothers used a yaw string on their 1902 glider tied on their front mounted elevator. [7] Wilbur Wright is credited with its invention, having applied it concurrently with the movable rudder invented by his brother Orville in October 1902, [8] although others may have used it before. Glenn Curtiss also used it on his early airplanes.

偏航弦可以追溯到最早的航空时代，实际上是第一个飞行仪器。莱特兄弟在1902年的滑翔机上使用了一根偏航绳，该滑翔机系在前面安装的电梯上。[7] 威尔伯·赖特（Wilbur Wright）因其发明而备受赞誉，他在1902年10月将其与他的兄弟奥维尔（Orville）发明的可移动舵同时应用，尽管其他人以前可能也使用过它。格伦·柯蒂斯（Glenn Curtiss）也在他早期的飞机上使用过它。

In flight, the rule to remember is simple: step on the head of the yaw string, the head is the front of the string, where the string is taped to the canopy. If the head of the yaw string is to the right of the yaw string tail, then the pilot needs to apply right rudder pressure. If the head of the yaw string is to the left of the yaw string tail, then the pilot should apply left rudder pressure. Or think of the attached point of the yaw string as an arrow head pointing to the rudder pedal needing the application of pressure. According to Helmut Reichmann, "...rudder against the string and/or aileron toward the string. In a spin the string always points to the inside." To center the ball on an inclinometer, apply rudder pressure on the side to which the ball is deflected. Use the simple rule, “step on the ball,” to remember which rudder pedal to press. [9] [10] [11] [12] [13]

在飞行中，要记住的规则很简单：踩在偏航绳的头部，头部是绳子的前部，绳子用胶带固定在伞罩上。如果偏航管柱的头部位于偏航管柱尾部的右侧，则飞行员需要施加右舵压力。如果偏航管柱的头部位于偏航管柱尾部的左侧，则飞行员应施加左舵压力。或者把偏航弦的附加点想象成一个箭头，指向需要施加压力的方向舵踏板。据赫尔穆特·赖克曼说，"；。。。方向舵紧靠弦和/或副翼朝向弦。旋转时，绳子总是指向内部" 要使球在倾斜仪上居中，请在球偏转的一侧施加方向舵压力。使用简单的规则“踩球”，记住要踩哪个方向舵踏板。[9] [10] [11] [12] [13]

Yaw strings are also fitted to the Lockheed U-2 high-altitude surveillance aircraft. [14] A flat spin, caused by excessive sideslip even in level flight, happens much more easily at high altitudes. Some light twin-engine airplane pilots place yaw strings on their aircraft to help maintain control in the event of an engine failure, because the slip-skid indicator ball is not accurate in this case. [15]In a multiengine airplane with an inoperative engine,the centered ball is no longer the indicator of zerosideslip due to asymmetrical thrust. The yaw string is the onlyflight instrument that will directly tell the pilot theflight conditions for zero sideslip. [16]

洛克希德U-2高空侦察机也安装了偏航管柱。[14] 即使在水平飞行中，由于过度侧滑而导致的平转在高海拔地区也更容易发生。一些轻型双引擎飞机飞行员在飞机上放置偏航线，以帮助在发动机故障时保持控制，因为在这种情况下，打滑指示器球不准确。[15] 在发动机不工作的多引擎飞机上，由于不对称推力，中心球不再是零侧滑的指示器。偏航管柱是唯一能直接告诉飞行员零侧滑起飞条件的飞行仪表。[16]

A variation of the yaw string is the side string, used in gliders for a determination of the angle of attack. In this way the best L/D speed, the best thermalling speed and the stall speed can be measured independently of all parameters like air speed, all up weight, acceleration due to turning or stick movements, and gusts. [17]

偏航弦的一种变体是侧弦，用于滑翔机中确定迎角。通过这种方式，最佳L/D速度、最佳热转速和失速速度可以独立于所有参数进行测量，如空气速度、所有上升重量、由于转向或斗杆运动而产生的加速度以及阵风。[17]

Investigations of the side string and on its use in glider flight were performed from the Akaflieg Cologne. [18]

从阿卡弗利格科隆号上对侧线及其在滑翔机飞行中的使用进行了调查。[18]
