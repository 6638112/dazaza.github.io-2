Perseverance Has Landed
毅力降落

2021-02-19 05:22:24

On Feb. 18, 2021, NASA's Mars Perseverance rover makes its final descent to the Red Planet. Here are some of the ways you can take part in this landing.

2021年2月18日，美国国家航空航天局（NASA）的火星毅力漫游者号首次降落到红色星球。您可以通过以下几种方式参与这次登陆。

Watch Online Tune in to landing on Feb. 18 and get our guide to the TV programs coming up you can watch online.

在线观看收看2月18日收看的电视节目，并获得我们的电视节目指南，您可以在网上观看。

Get Your Landing Resources If you are hosting a landing event, download and share the virtual landing packet. Download posters, stickers, fact sheets, mission patches and more.

获取您的着陆资源如果您要主持着陆事件，请下载并共享虚拟着陆数据包。下载海报，贴纸，情况说明书，任务补丁等。

Ways To Participate Try the Photo Booth to virtually put yourself in mission control, Send Your Name on the next mission to Mars, and check out other interactive experiences.

参与方式尝试使用照相亭来虚拟地控制自己的任务，在下一次前往火星的任务中发送自己的名字，并查看其他交互式体验。

Mission to Mars Student Challenge Get Mars-related webcasts for learners of all ages, along with lessons and activities for students.

挑战火星学生挑战赛获取适合所有年龄段学习者的火星相关网络广播，以及针对学生的课程和活动。

Register for a Virtual Landing Event Get notifications about landing opportunities, programming, and other mission information, plus a landing stamp for your virtual passport.

注册虚拟着陆事件获取有关着陆机会，编程和其他任务信息的通知，以及虚拟护照的着陆图章。

Virtual NASA Social Connect online with other space enthusiasts, ask questions and get answers from NASA experts. Get a special badge to share online or print at home.

与其他太空爱好者在线上虚拟的NASA Social Connect，提出问题并从NASA专家那里获得答案。获取特殊徽章以在线共享或在家打印。

If your city lights up red for landing, tag and share your photos using #CountdownToMars. Feb. 16: Empire State Building The iconic Empire State Building in New York City lit the tower red on Tuesday, Feb. 16 starting at sunset until 2 a.m. Feb. 17: Chicago Skyline Various buildings in Chicago light up red on Wednesday night, including the Adler planetarium. Feb. 17-18: Terminal Tower in Cleveland, Ohio Cleveland's landmark skyscraper will be lit up Mars red Wednesday and Thursday. Feb. 17-19: Los Angeles International Airport The light pillars at LAX will glow red from sundown on Wednesday, Feb. 17 through sunrise on Friday, Feb. 19. Feb. 17-20: St. Louis Science Center The McDonnell planetarium at the St. Louis Science Center in Missouri will feature a Martian glow through Feb. 20.
