Unity partners with Snap to extend the reach of Unity's ads and integrate SnapKit in Unity's game engine, including Bitmoji in 2021
Unity与Snap合作扩展了广告的覆盖面，并将SnapKit集成到Unity的游戏引擎中，包括2021年的Bitmoji

2020-12-18 16:31:31

Game engine maker Unity has teamed up with Snap to extend the reach of its ads and technology platform.

游戏引擎制造商Unity已与Snap合作，以扩展其广告和技术平台的覆盖范围。

The deal will extend the reach of Unity’s Ads supply to Snapchat advertisers, and it brings Snap technology to game developers through Snap Kit tools. It’s another sign that gaming is more mainstream today than ever and part of a shared social consumer experiences.

该交易将把Unity的广告供应范围扩展到Snapchat广告客户，并通过Snap Kit工具将Snap技术带给游戏开发人员。这是另一个迹象，表明游戏在当今比以往任何时候都更为主流，并且是共享的社会消费者体验的一部分。

The alliance has been in the works during the second half of 2020, and it’s a partnership of giants. More than 50% of all mobile games use Unity, and Snapchat has advertising and game integrations that reach its audience of 249 million people every day.

该联盟于2020年下半年开始运作，是巨人的伙伴关系。超过50％的手机游戏使用Unity，Snapchat的广告和游戏集成每天达到2.49亿用户。

Julie Shumaker is the vice president of revenue and operate solutions at Unity, and she said in an email to GamesBeat that Snap’s big advertising community on its messaging service will be a good fit with Unity’s highly engaged and valuable player audiences. The alliance should drive social sharing and game discovery.

朱莉·舒马克（Julie Shumaker）是Unity收入和运营解决方案副总裁，她在给GamesBeat的电子邮件中说，Snap消息传递服务的大型广告社区将非常适合Unity的高度参与和有价值的玩家观众。联盟应推动社交共享和游戏发现。

“We both focus on what’s best for users, and in this partnership, users are both developers and players,” Shumaker said. “Developers will benefit from both increased ads demand with SAN bidding on Unity supply, and the Snap Kit integration provides them added benefit with social game discovery between players.”

Shumaker说：“我们俩都专注于最适合用户的东西，在这种合作关系中，用户既是开发人员又是玩家。” “开发人员将受益于SAN竞标Unity时增加的广告需求，并且Snap Kit集成为他们带来了玩家之间社交游戏发现的更多好处。”

Starting today, Unity Ads is now included in the Snap Audience Network (SAN). Snapchat’s SAN advertiser campaigns will now include video inventory from Unity’s extensive network of mobile gaming titles, helping advertisers extend beyond Snapchat. Unity Ads reports 22.9 billion-plus monthly global ad impressions, and it reaches more than two billion monthly active users through game developers who use its engine.

从今天开始，Unity Ads现在已包含在Snap Audience Network（SAN）中。 Snapchat的SAN广告客户活动现在将包括Unity广泛的移动游戏标题网络中的视频广告资源，从而帮助广告客户扩展到Snapchat之外。 Unity Ads报告的全球每月广告展示量超过229亿，通过使用其引擎的游戏开发人员，每月的活跃用户超过20亿。

In 2020, mobile ad viewers have converted at higher frequencies, with install conversion rates up by 23%, and mobile gamers installing 84% more apps, thanks to the pandemic. Snap launched games in April 2019, and it has reached more than 100 million game downloads as gaming becomes a shared activity that people increasingly engage in on Snapchat, said Snap’s Ben Schwerin in an email to GamesBeat.

到2020年，由于大流行，移动广告查看器的转换率提高了，安装转换率提高了23％，移动游戏安装量增加了84％。 Snap的Ben Schwerin在发给GamesBeat的电子邮件中表示，Snap于2019年4月推出了游戏，由于游戏已成为人们越来越多地在Snapchat上参与的共享活动，因此下载量已超过1亿。

“With Snap Kit, we’ve integrated with hundreds of apps, but we haven’t paid attention to the game market directly yet — same with Snap Audience Network (SAN),” Schwerin said. “We took this opportunity to go to the biggest platform in games — Unity.”

Schwerin说：“借助Snap Kit，我们已经集成了数百种应用程序，但我们还没有直接关注游戏市场-与Snap Audience Network（SAN）一样。” “我们借此机会进入了游戏中最大的平台Unity。”

Available today in the Unity Asset Store, mobile game developers can also now leverage select features of Snap Kit to enhance gameplay and the game discovery experience. Developers can use Snap Kit’s Login Kit to allows gamer to use their Snapchat account as a quick way to sign up and log in to games. And Snap Kit’s Creative Kit extends the experience by allowing users to share their gameplay, decorating still shots or 15-second videos with branded stickers, or attaching an AR lens that has been created with game branding to share with their Snapchat friends.

现在，Unity Asset Store中提供了移动游戏开发人员，他们现在还可以利用Snap Kit的精选功能来增强游戏玩法和游戏发现体验。开发人员可以使用Snap Kit的Login Kit来允许游戏玩家使用其Snapchat帐户作为注册和登录游戏的快速方法。 Snap Kit的Creative Kit扩展了体验，它允许用户共享他们的游戏玩法，使用品牌贴纸装饰静态照片或15秒视频，或者附加使用游戏品牌创建的AR镜头，以便与Snapchat朋友共享。

Snap’s Bitmoji avatars will be integrated with Unity in early 2021 and will add a new level of personalization to gameplay via cartoon-like avatars. With Bitmoji for Game’s Unity SDK, developers will be able to use 3D Bitmoji to create a more immersive experience in games made with Unity.

Snap的Bitmoji头像将于2021年初与Unity集成，并将通过类似于卡通的头像将游戏的个性化水平提高到新水平。借助Bitmoji for Game的Unity SDK，开发人员将能够使用3D Bitmoji在Unity制作的游戏中创造更身临其境的体验。
