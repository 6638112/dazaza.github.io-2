Twitter Selects AWS and Graviton Arm Instances to Serve Timelines
Twitter选择AWS和Graviton Arm实例服务时间表

2020-12-19 01:56:12

SEATTLE, Dec. 15, 2020 — Today, Amazon Web Services, Inc. (AWS), an Amazon.com, Inc. company , announced that Twitter has selected AWS to provide global cloud infrastructure to deliver Twitter timelines. Under the multi-year deal, Twitter will leverage AWS’s proven infrastructure and portfolio of services to support delivery of millions of daily Tweets. This expansion onto AWS marks the first time that Twitter is leveraging the public cloud to scale their real-time service. Twitter will rely on the breadth and depth of AWS, including capabilities in compute, containers, storage, and security, to reliably deliver the real-time service with the lowest latency, while continuing to develop and deploy new features to improve how people use Twitter. The new agreement builds on the companies’ more than decade-long collaboration, where AWS continues to provide Twitter with storage, compute, database, and content delivery services to support its distribution of images, videos and ad content.

西雅图，2020年12月15日—今天，Amazon.com，Inc.公司Amazon Web Services，Inc.（AWS）宣布Twitter已选择AWS提供全球云基础设施来交付Twitter时间表。根据多年协议，Twitter将利用AWS久经考验的基础架构和服务组合来支持数百万条每日Tweet的交付。这种向AWS的扩展标志着Twitter首次利用公共云扩展其实时服务。 Twitter将依靠AWS的广度和深度，包括计算，容器，存储和安全性方面的功能，以最低的延迟可靠地交付实时服务，同时继续开发和部署新功能以改善人们使用Twitter的方式。这项新协议建立在两家公司超过十年的合作基础上，AWS继续为Twitter提供存储，计算，数据库和内容交付服务，以支持其图像，视频和广告内容的分发。

Millions of people and organizations use Twitter to share and react to what’s happening and what people are talking about right now, and Twitter will leverage AWS infrastructure and services to continue improving performance and security for them. Twitter and AWS will create an architecture that extends Twitter’s on-premises infrastructure to enable them to seamlessly run and scale the real-time service globally, increase its reliability using AWS’s fault-tolerant infrastructure, and rapidly move new features into production around the world. Twitter will take advantage of AWS Graviton2-based instances on Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2) to power its cloud-based workloads, and use AWS container services to develop and deploy new features and applications consistently across its hybrid infrastructure.

数以百万计的人和组织使用Twitter来共享和响应正在发生的事情以及人们现在在谈论什么，Twitter将利用AWS基础设施和服务来继续改善其性能和安全性。 Twitter和AWS将创建一个架构，以扩展Twitter的本地基础结构，使它们能够在全球无缝运行和扩展实时服务，使用AWS的容错基础结构来提高其可靠性，并将新功能快速移至全球生产环境。 Twitter将利用Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud（Amazon EC2）上基于AWS Graviton2的实例来为其基于云的工作负载提供支持，并使用AWS容器服务在其混合基础架构中一致地开发和部署新功能和应用程序。

In addition, Twitter will continue to use AWS services such as Amazon CloudFront (AWS’s fast content delivery network service that securely delivers data, videos, applications, and APIs with low latency and high transfer speeds to customers globally) and Amazon DynamoDB (AWS’s key-value database that delivers single-digit millisecond performance at any scale).

此外，Twitter将继续使用AWS服务，例如Amazon CloudFront（AWS的快速内容交付网络服务，以低延迟和高传输速度安全地向全球客户交付数据，视频，应用程序和API）和Amazon DynamoDB（AWS的关键-价值数据库，可在任何规模上提供单位毫秒级的性能）。

“We are excited to work with AWS to expand the infrastructure Twitter uses to serve the public conversation as we grow globally,” said Parag Agrawal, Chief Technology Officer, Twitter. “The collaboration with AWS will improve performance for people who use Twitter by enabling us to serve Tweets from data centers closer to our customers at the same time as we leverage the Arm-based architecture of AWS Graviton2 instances. In addition to helping us scale our infrastructure, this work with AWS enables us to ship features faster as we apply AWS’s diverse and growing portfolio of services.

Twitter首席技术官Parag Agrawal说：“随着我们在全球范围内的发展，我们很高兴与AWS一起扩展Twitter用于服务于公众对话的基础架构。” “与AWS的协作将使我们能够在利用AWS Graviton2实例的基于Arm的架构的同时，从更靠近客户的数据中心提供推文，从而提高使用Twitter的用户的性能。除了帮助我们扩展基础架构之外，与AWS的合作还使我们能够在应用AWS多样化且不断增长的服务组合时更快地发布功能。

“Twitter’s decision to rely on AWS infrastructure and services for its real-time workloads will help them instantly scale their global footprint up and down without ever compromising the experience for people who use Twitter,” said Matt Garman, Vice President, Sales and Marketing at Amazon Web Services, Inc. “By using AWS container services to create a seamless hybrid on-premises and cloud environment, Twitter can innovate and deliver new experiences quickly and cost-effectively.”

“ Twitter决定依靠AWS基础架构和服务来处理其实时工作负载，这将帮助他们立即在全球范围内上下扩展，而丝毫不会影响使用Twitter的用户的体验，” Twitter销售和营销副总裁Matt Garman说。 Amazon Web Services，Inc.“通过使用AWS容器服务创建无缝的本地和云混合混合环境，Twitter可以快速，经济高效地创新并提供新的体验。”
