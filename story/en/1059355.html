Microsoft disables Google's FLoC tracking in Microsoft Edge, for now
Microsoft在Microsoft Edge中禁用Google的Floc跟踪，现在

2021-04-20 15:46:56

Microsoft has disabled Google's controversial FLoC browser-based tracking feature in their Chromium-based Microsoft Edge browser.

Microsoft在其基于铬的Microsoft Edge浏览器中禁用了Google＆＃39;基于争议的Floc浏览器的跟踪功能。

This month, Google began testing a new tracking platform called Federated Learning of Cohorts, or FLoC, that places users in anonymous buckets, or cohorts, based on their interest and browsing behavior.

本月，谷歌开始测试一个名为联合学习的新的跟踪平台，或絮凝物的联合学习，以匿名桶中的用户基于他们的兴趣和浏览行为。

Unlike third-party cookies used by advertisers to track your behavior and interests across different sites, FLoC  is built into the web browser, which assigns you to behavior cohorts and shares that information with websites and advertisers.

与广告商使用的第三方cookie不同，以跟踪不同网站上的行为和兴趣，Floc内置于Web浏览器中，这为您分配了与网站和广告商的信息和共享信息。

As FLoC is a Google browser-based feature, it is currently only supported on Google Chrome and Chromium-based browsers through a component named 'Federated Learning of Cohorts.'

由于Floc是基于Google浏览器的功能，它目前仅支持Google Chrome和基于铬的浏览器通过名为＆＃39的组件;联邦学习队列。＆＃39;

When building Chromium-based browsers, Google has enabled support for FLoC by default, as shown in Chromium's source code.

在构建基于铬的浏览器时，谷歌已启用默认支持Floc，如Chromium＆＃39; s源代码所示。

This means that other companies who develop Chromium-based browsers, such as Microsoft Edge, will automatically have the component installed as well unless they specifically disable it.

这意味着其他开发基于铬的浏览器（如Microsoft Edge）的公司将自动安装组件，除非他们专门禁用它。

Such is the case with Microsoft Edge, where FLoC has not been enabled, and the component is not available in the browser even if enabled via command-line arguments.

这样的情况是Microsoft Edge，尚未启用Floc的情况，即使通过命令行参数启用，浏览器中的组件也不可用。

When we reached out to Microsoft to confirm if FLoC was intentionally disabled, we were not given a clear answer. Microsoft instead pivoted to their favoring of a Microsoft-led proposal called PARAKEET.

当我们向Microsoft达到确认Floc被故意禁用时，我们没有得到明确的答案。 Microsoft反而追溯了他们对叫做agakeet的微软LED建议。

"We believe in a future where the web can provide people with privacy, transparency and control while also supporting responsible business models to create a vibrant, open and diverse ecosystem. Like Google, we support solutions that give users clear consent, and do not bypass consumer choice. That’s also why we do not support solutions that leverage non-consented user identity signals, such as fingerprinting. The industry is on a journey and there will be browser-based proposals that do not need individual user ids and ID-based proposals that are based on consent and first party relationships. We will continue to explore these approaches with the community. Recently, for example, we were pleased to introduce one possible approach, as described in our PARAKEET proposal. This proposal is not the final iteration but is an evolving document." - Microsoft.

＆＃34;我们相信Web的未来可以为人们提供隐私，透明度和控制的人，同时支持负责任的商业模式，以创造充满活力，开放和多样化的生态系统。与谷歌一样，我们支持提供用户明确同意的解决方案，并无法绕过消费者选择。这也是为什么我们不支持利用非同意的用户身份信号（例如指纹）的解决方案。该行业正在进行旅程，将有基于浏览器的建议，不需要基于同意和第一方关系的个人用户ID和基于ID的建议。我们将继续与社区探索这些方法。例如，最近，我们很高兴地介绍一种可能的方法，如我们的滞留提案中所述。这个提议不是最终的迭代，而是一个不断发展的文件。＆＃34; - 微软。

However, in conversations with people familiar with Microsoft's plans, we were told that they plan to wait and watch how FLoC evolves before committing to any particular platform.

但是，在与熟悉微软的人的谈话中，我们被告知他们计划等待并观察Floc在致力于任何特定平台之前的发展。

Other browser developers are also holding off on implementing FLoC in their browsers to see how the tests go and how people react to it's test.

其他浏览器开发人员也在浏览器中实施Floc，以了解测试如何以及人们如何对其作出反应。

In a statement shared with BleepingComputer, Mozilla states that they are currently evaluating various advertising proposals but have not implemented any of the platforms in Mozilla Firefox.

在与BleepingComputer共享的声明中，Mozilla指出，他们目前正在评估各种广告建议，但尚未实施Mozilla Firefox中的任何平台。

"We are currently evaluating many of the privacy preserving advertising proposals, including those put forward by Google, but have no current plans to implement any of them at this time.

＆＃34;我们目前正在评估许多保护广告提案的隐私，包括谷歌提出的那些，但目前没有计划在此时实施任何一个。

We don’t buy into the assumption that the industry needs billions of data points about people, that are collected and shared without their understanding, to serve relevant advertising. That is why we’ve implemented Enhanced Tracking Protection by default to block more than ten billion trackers a day, and continue to innovate on new ways to protect people who use Firefox.

我们不购买该行业需要数十亿数据点的有关人员的数据，而不会在没有理解的情况下收集和分享，以满足相关的广告。这就是为什么我们通过默认实现增强的跟踪保护，以阻止每天超过100亿个跟踪器，并继续创新新的方式来保护使用Firefox的人。

Advertising and privacy can co-exist. And the advertising industry can operate differently than it has in past years. We look forward to playing a role in finding solutions that build a better web." - Mozilla.

广告和隐私可以共存。而广告业可能与过去几年的运作方式不同。我们期待着在寻找建立更好网络的解决方案中发挥作用。＆＃34; - Mozilla。

While Apple has not made a public statement about whether they support FLoC, Safari browser engineer John Wilander, who works the Tracking Prevention feature, has stated that they will wait and see as well.

虽然Apple尚未发出关于它们是否支持Floc，Safari浏览器工程师John Wilander的公开陈述，但曾表示他们将等待和看看。

We have not said we will implement and we have our tracking prevention policy. That’s it for the time being. Serious standards proposals deserve thinking and I appreciate Brave sharing theirs.

我们还没有说我们将实施，我们有我们的跟踪预防政策。这是暂时的。严重的标准提案值得思考，我很欣赏勇敢分享他们的分享。

However, privacy advocates and browser developers, including Brave, Vivaldi, the EFF, and DuckDuckGo, have already stated that they believe FLoC is a bad idea and will not be supporting it.

然而，隐私倡导者和浏览器开发人员（包括勇敢），Vivaldi，Eff和DuckDuckgo已经表示，他们认为幸福是一个坏主意，不会支持它。

WordPress is proposing adding code to their blogging platform that automatically disables FLoC on WordPress sites.

WordPress建议将代码添加到其博客平台，该平台自动禁用WordPress站点上的Floc。

DuckDuckGo is now blocking FLoC in their ' DuckDuckGo Privacy Essentials' Chrome browser extension used by over 5 million people.

Duckduckgo现在封锁他们的＆＃39; Duckduckgo隐私福利＆＃39;超过500万人使用的Chrome浏览器扩展。

Even with the lack of support from browser developers and privacy advocates, with Google holding close to a 70% web browser market share, it may be challenging to get rid of FLoC at this point for those who use Google Chrome.

即使浏览器开发人员和隐私倡导者的支持缺乏支持，谷歌持有近70％的Web浏览器市场份额，对于在此时拿走谷歌浏览器的人来说，可能会挑战絮凝物。
