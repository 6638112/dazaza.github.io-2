Ukrainian government calls on hackers to help defend against Russia
乌克兰政府呼吁黑客帮助防御俄罗斯

2022-02-25 13:28:40

Cybersecurity firm seeks volunteers to help nation’s military conduct espionage operations against Russian forces

网络安全公司寻求志愿者帮助国家军队对俄罗斯军队进行间谍活动

The government of Ukraine is asking for volunteers from the country’s hacker underground to help protect critical infrastructure and conduct cyber spying missions against Russian troops, according two people involved in the project.

据两名参与该项目的人士透露，乌克兰政府要求该国地下黑客提供志愿者，帮助保护关键基础设施，并对俄罗斯军队执行网络间谍任务。

As Russian forces attacked cities across Ukraine, requests for volunteers began to appear on hacker forums on Thursday morning, as many residents fled the capital Kyiv.

随着俄罗斯军队袭击乌克兰各地的城市，周四上午，随着许多居民逃离首都基辅，黑客论坛上开始出现招募志愿者的请求。

“Ukrainian cybercommunity! It’s time to get involved in the cyber defense of our country,” the post read, asking hackers and cybersecurity experts to submit an application via Google docs, listing their specialties, such as malware development, and professional references.

“乌克兰网络社区！是时候参与我们国家的网络防御了，”帖子写道，要求黑客和网络安全专家通过谷歌文档提交申请，列出他们的专长，如恶意软件开发和专业参考。

Yegor Aushev, the co-founder of a cybersecurity company in Kyiv, told Reuters he wrote the post at the request of a senior Defense Ministry official who contacted him on Thursday. Aushev’s firm Cyber Unit Technologies is known for working with Ukraine’s government on the defense of critical infrastructure.

基辅一家网络安全公司的联合创始人耶戈尔·奥舍夫告诉路透社，他是应周四与他联系的国防部高级官员的要求写这篇文章的。Aushev的公司Cyber Unit Technologies因与乌克兰政府合作防御关键基础设施而闻名。

Another person directly involved in the effort confirmed that the request came from the Defense Ministry on Thursday morning.

另一位直接参与这项工作的人士证实，这项请求是国防部在周四上午提出的。

Ukraine Defense Ministry representatives did not respond to a request for comment. A defense attache at Ukraine’s embassy in Washington said he “cannot confirm or deny information from Telegram channels” referring to the mobile messaging platform, and declined further comment.

乌克兰国防部代表没有回应置评请求。乌克兰驻华盛顿大使馆的一名国防武官表示，他“无法确认或否认来自移动通讯平台的电报渠道的信息”，并拒绝进一步置评。

Aushev said the volunteers would be divided into defensive and offensive cyber units. The defensive unit would be employed to defend infrastructure such as power plants and water systems. In a 2015 cyber-attack, widely attributed to Russia state hackers, 225,000 Ukrainians lost electricity.

奥舍夫说，志愿者将分为防御和进攻网络部队。防御部队将被用于防御发电厂和供水系统等基础设施。在2015年的一次网络攻击中，22.5万乌克兰人失去了电力，这一攻击被广泛归因于俄罗斯国家黑客。

The offensive volunteer unit Aushev said he is organizing would help Ukraine’s military conduct digital espionage operations against invading Russian forces.

进攻性志愿部队奥舍夫说，他正在组织一支将帮助乌克兰军队对入侵的俄罗斯军队进行数字间谍行动的部队。

“We have an army inside our country,” Aushev said. “We need to know what they are doing.” On Wednesday, a newly discovered piece of destructive software was found circulating in Ukraine, hitting hundreds of computers, according to researchers at the cybersecurity firm ESET. Suspicion fell on Russia, which has repeatedly been accused of hacks against Ukraine and other countries. The victims included government agencies and a financial institution, Reuters previously reported.

“我们在国内有一支军队，”奥舍夫说。“我们需要知道他们在做什么。”据网络安全公司ESET的研究人员称，周三，一个新发现的破坏性软件在乌克兰流通，击中了数百台计算机。怀疑落到了俄罗斯身上，俄罗斯曾多次被指控对乌克兰和其他国家进行黑客攻击。路透社此前报道称，受害者包括政府机构和一家金融机构。

The effort to build a cyber military force is coming late in the game, Aushev acknowledged.

奥舍夫承认，建立网络军事力量的努力来得晚。

A Ukrainian security official said earlier this month that the country had no dedicated military cyber force, the Washington Post reported. “It’s our task to create them this year,” he told the Washington Post.

据《华盛顿邮报》报道，乌克兰一名安全官员本月早些时候表示，该国没有专门的军事网络部队。“我们今年的任务是创建它们，”他告诉《华盛顿邮报》。

Reached late Thursday night in Ukraine, Aushev said he already had received hundreds of applicants and was going to begin vetting to ensure that none of them were Russian agents.

周四深夜抵达乌克兰的奥舍夫说，他已经收到数百名申请者，并将开始审查，以确保他们中没有一人是俄罗斯特工。
