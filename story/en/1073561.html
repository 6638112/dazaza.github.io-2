Finland-based Swappie, which refurbishes and resells used iPhones through its marketplace, raises a €108M Series C, bringing its total funding to €149M
总部位于芬兰的Swappie通过其市场翻新和转售二手iPhone，筹集了1.08亿欧元的C系列，使其总资金达到1.49亿欧元

2022-02-16 19:40:33

It’s now raised a $124 million (€108 million) Series C funding round led by growth equity firm Verdane, alongside existing investors Lifeline Ventures, Inventure, Reaktor Ventures and TESI. This latest round brings the company’s total funding to over $171 million (€149 million).

现在，它与现有投资者Lifeline Ventures、Inventure、Reaktor Ventures和TESI一起，筹集了一轮1.24亿美元（1.08亿欧元）的C系列融资，由增长型股权公司Verdane牵头。最新一轮融资使该公司的总资金超过1.71亿美元（1.49亿欧元）。

In a statement, Sami Marttinen, co-founder and CEO of Swappie, said: “We truly believe that Swappie can drive consumer awareness and trust in the sector by standardizing quality, championing the role of sustainability, and making buying a refurbished smartphone as common as buying a used car.”

Swappie的联合创始人兼首席执行官萨米·马蒂宁（Sami Marttinen）在一份声明中说：“我们真的相信，Swappie可以通过标准化质量、倡导可持续发展的作用，以及让购买翻新智能手机和购买二手车一样常见，来提高消费者对该行业的认识和信任。”

The overall market for used and refurbished smartphones is forecast to grow at an annual growth rate of 10.23% between 2022 and 2027, with smartphones predicted to generate 146 million tons of CO 2 emissions in 2022. If people keep their mobiles for longer it saves around 2 million tonnes of emissions, according to Swappie.

2022年至2027年间，二手和翻新智能手机的整体市场预计将以10.23%的年增长率增长，智能手机预计将在2022年产生1.46亿吨二氧化碳排放。根据Swappie的数据，如果人们将手机保留更长时间，可以节省大约200万吨的排放量。

Janne Holmia, partner at Verdane added: “As people and companies progressively move towards a more sustainable lifestyle, the iPhone refurbishment and re-commerce space has grown considerably, and with it Swappie, which has demonstrated the potential to become Europe’s market leader in refurbished smartphones.”

Verdane的合伙人Janne Holmia补充道：“随着人们和公司逐步走向更可持续的生活方式，iPhone的翻新和再贸易空间已经大幅增长，随着它的出现，Swappie已经显示出成为欧洲翻新智能手机市场领军者的潜力。”

However, the race to conquer Europe’s electronics refreshment market is not quite finished just yet.

然而，征服欧洲电子点心市场的竞赛还没有结束。

Last year, Refurbed, a European marketplace for refurbished electronics, raised a $54 million Series B funding led by Evli Growth Partners and Almaz Capital. It takes a slightly different approach, with 130 refurbishers selling through its marketplace.

去年，欧洲翻新电子产品市场Refurbed筹集了5400万美元的B系列资金，由Evli Growth Partners和Almaz Capital牵头。它采取了一种稍有不同的方式，130家翻新厂在其市场上销售。

Operating in Germany, Austria, Ireland, France, Italy and Poland, the startup plans to expand to three other countries.

这家初创公司在德国、奥地利、爱尔兰、法国、意大利和波兰运营，计划将业务扩展到其他三个国家。

Other players in this space include Back Market, Swappa (U.S.) and Amazon Renew. Then there’s Rebuy in Germany. So plenty more competition to chew on.

该领域的其他参与者包括Back Market、Swappa（美国）和亚马逊续订。还有德国的Rebuy。所以还有很多竞争要考虑。
