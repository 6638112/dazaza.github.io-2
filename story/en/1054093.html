OVH writes off another data centre – SBG1 – and reveals new battery incident
OVH写下另一个数据中心 - SBG1 - 并揭示了新的电池事件

2021-03-23 06:51:26

OVH has written off a second data centre because of the March 10th fire that destroyed its SBG2 facility.

OVH已经写完了第二个数据中心，因为3月10日火灾摧毁了其SBG2设施。

In a Saturday update, OVH founder and CTO Octave Klaba wrote: “We don’t plan to restart SBG1. Ever.” Previous guidance on SBG1 suggested only a few racks were destroyed, while others were considered recoverable. The fire has already destroyed the SBG2 data centre.

在星期六更新中，OVH创始人和CTO Octave Klaba写道：“我们不打算重新启动SBG1。曾经。”以前关于SBG1的指导建议只销毁了几个机架，而其他机架则被认为是可恢复的。火已经摧毁了SBG2数据中心。

Update March,19 9:30pm The batteries were in unused room, not used, not connected. We didn’t have the fire, but we had lot of smoke. The situation is under control now. Everybody is safe. We stop the operation in SBG for this night. We will take the decision tomorrow.

更新3月19日9:30 PM电池在未使用的房间内，未使用，未连接。我们没有火，但我们有很多烟。现在正在控制这种情况。每个人都很安全。我们今晚停止了SBG的操作。我们将明天作出决定。

— Octave Klaba (@olesovhcom) March 19, 2021

Klaba has also detailed further delays to restoration of the Strasbourg site as new conduits are dug to bring power to SBG3. The CTO offered further information in a pair of Sunday tweets detailing restoration efforts.

Klaba还详细延迟了恢复斯特拉斯堡网站，因为新的导管挖掘以为SBG3带来电力。 CTO在一对周日推文中提供了详细恢复工作的进一步信息。

OVH customers, meanwhile, have picked up on a post to the OVH forums by Klaba from November 2017 that discussed a power outage that the CTO described as “the worst incident that can happen to us” - one which The Register referred to on the day of the fire.

同时，ovh客户从2017年11月开始了Klaba的OVH论坛的帖子，讨论了CTO被描述为“最糟糕的事件，这可能发生在我们”中 - 登记当天所提到的火灾。

The post describes how the Strasbourg site initially used shipping containers and Klaba wrote that decision meant “we have not prepared the site for large scale.” He then said the company also made two design errors:

该帖子描述了Strasbourg网站最初使用的航运容器和klaba写道，该决定意味着“我们没有为大规模准备了该网站”。然后他说该公司还制作了两个设计错误：

We did not bring the SBG site up to internal standards which provide for 2 independent 20KV electrical inlets, like all our DC sites which have several double electrical inlets. This is a significant investment of around 2 to 3 million euros per electrical arrival, but we believe that this is part of our internal standard.

我们没有将SBG站点达到内部标准，为2个独立的20kV电气入口提供，如我们的所有直流网站，具有几个双电入口。这是每个电气到达约2至300万欧元的重要投资，但我们认为这是我们内部标准的一部分。

We built the SBG2 electrical network by placing it on the SBG1 electrical network, instead of making them independent of each other, as in all our data centers. At OVH, each datacenter number means that the electrical network is independent of another datacenter. Everywhere except on the SBG site.

我们通过将SBG2电网构建了SBG2电网，而不是在所有数据中心中彼此独立地制作它们。在OVH，每个数据中心号码表示电网与另一个数据中心无关。除了SBG网站外的各处。

The 2017 post was made in the wake of a power outage that took down the data centres, a scenario Klaba described as “the worst incident that can happen to us.”

2017年帖子是在停电的推迟数据中心的帖子之后，这是一个被描述为“可能发生在我们的最糟糕的事件”的场景Klaba。

The Register asked OVH weeks ago how far along it was with the power grid upgrade it referred to in the post, which was said to involve "de-installation of maritime containers" (shipping containers) and major electrical work. It has yet to respond. ®

寄存器问OVH周前沿着电网升级它在帖子中提到的电网升级有多远，这据说涉及＆＃34;去安装海上容器＆＃34; （运输集装箱）和主要电气工作。它尚未回应。 ®
