意大利警方利用兰博基尼在两小时内运送捐赠者肾脏300英里

2020-11-16 13:22:34

In what may be one of the most Italian things that has ever happened, the Italian State Police rushed a donor kidney from Padua to Rome for a transplant in a Lamborghini Huracan. Last week’s journey is around 300 miles, but with the help of a specially-outfitted supercar, the police made it happen in just about two hours at an average speed of 143 mph—and that’s a journey that normally takes around six.

意大利国家警察将捐献的肾脏从帕多瓦紧急送往罗马，在兰博基尼·胡拉坎(Lamborghini Huracan)轿车上进行移植，这可能是有史以来最具意大利特色的事情之一。上周的路程约为300英里，但在一辆特别配备的超级跑车的帮助下，警方在短短两个小时内以143英里的平均时速完成了这一旅程-这通常需要大约6英里的路程。

The police posted a video of the Lambo after the completed journey, and it is just delightful:

警方在完成旅程后上传了一段兰博的视频，简直是令人欣喜：

Yes, the Italian Police own a Lamborghini and use it as a regular ol’ patrol vehicle most of the time. It’s outfitted with lights, a police computer, and other equipment for traffic stops and arrests. That said, though, the machine isn’t exactly ideal for the day-to-day (where, exactly, do you intend to put someone that you’ve arrested?). It’s still cool as hell for these more extreme circumstances, though.

是的，意大利警方拥有一辆兰博基尼，大部分时间都把它当作普通的老式巡逻车使用。它配备了灯光、警方电脑和其他交通拦截和逮捕设备。尽管如此，这台机器在日常工作中并不是很理想(你到底打算把你逮捕的人放在哪里？)。然而，对于这些更极端的情况来说，它仍然凉爽得要命。

But for this specific instance, the frunk came in handy. The police force turned it into a refrigerated compartment for organ transport or the delivery of other temperature-sensitive medical supplies.

但在这个特定的例子中，Frunk派上了用场。警察部队把它变成了一个冷藏室，用于器官运输或其他对温度敏感的医疗用品的运送。

With a 202 mph top speed and a 0-60 time of 2.8 seconds, it’s one hell of an efficient vehicle for these high-speed runs.

它的最高时速为202英里，0-60秒的时间为2.8秒，对于这些高速运行来说，这是一辆效率极高的汽车。

The Italian police actually own a few different Lambos, with this specific one joining the force back in 2017. It’s a pretty solid use of a supercar, although folks on Twitter have wondered why Italian officials didn’t just use a helicopter to transport the kidney. A Google Maps view of the Policlinico Universitario in Padua, the starting hospital, doesn’t seem to show a helipad or an easily accessible flat area nearby, so a Lamborghini likely made the most sense here.

意大利警方实际上拥有几种不同的兰博，这只兰博是在2017年加入警队的。这是超级跑车的一个相当可靠的用途，尽管推特上的人想知道为什么意大利官员不直接用直升机来运送肾脏。在谷歌地图上看到的帕多瓦警察大学(Policlinico University)的起始医院，似乎没有显示直升机停机坪或附近容易到达的平坦区域，所以兰博基尼在这里可能是最有意义的。

I hope the receiver of the kidney knows how it was transported. I know that, personally, I’d work to make sure that kidney is well-appreciated for as long as it lasts.

我希望肾脏的接收者知道它是如何被运送的。我知道，就我个人而言，我会努力确保肾脏在存活期间得到充分的重视。

In order to average 143 mph, they had to spend a fair amount of time up near the 200 top speed. That’s before also realizing the Huracan only has a 21.9 gallon fuel tank and gets between 13-18 mpg fuel economy (at normal speeds). Drag increases exponentially with speed, so I’d be surprised if it did better than 10 mpg at 140+ speeds, which would only give it a 200 mile range running the tank dry (and it’s likely far worse).

为了达到平均时速143英里，他们不得不在接近200英里的最高时速上花费相当长的时间。这也是在意识到Huracan只有21.9加仑的油箱和13-18英里的燃油经济性(在正常速度下)之前。阻力随速度呈指数增长，所以如果它在140+速度下跑得比每加仑10英里还好，那只会让它在干涸的情况下行驶200英里(而且可能要糟糕得多)。

Also, they blur the tags out on the car in some shots but not others. I’m thinking it’s far more likely one Lambo picked it up, took to a helo and then another Lambo met the helicopter to finish the trip.

此外，他们在一些镜头中模糊了汽车上的标签，但在另一些镜头中却没有。我认为更有可能是一辆兰博把它捡起来，带上直升机，然后另一辆兰博与直升机相遇，完成了旅程。
