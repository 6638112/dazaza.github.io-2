Cheap Router Table and Lift
便宜的路由器表和升降机

2020-11-21 20:08:49

A router is an awesome tool in every wood working shop. I am using a cheap Makita clone and am really happy with it. But once you started enjoying the versatility of this tool, you will also feel the need to have a stationary version: a router table.

路由器是每个木工车间的绝妙工具。我正在使用便宜的牧田克隆，并且对此感到非常满意。但是，一旦开始享受该工具的多功能性，您还将感到需要一个固定的版本：路由器表。

Some people opt for a way where they can switch their machine between being handheld and mounted into a table, but I wanted to have both without the need to do complicated mounting. So I needed a second router for the table to be.

有些人选择了一种方法，可以在手持和安装到桌子之间切换机器，但是我希望两者都不需要复杂的安装。所以我需要第二个路由器来放置桌子。

Once a router is built into a table, you want an easy way to lift it up and down from the top of the table. Unfortunately there are only a handful known models that support this out of the box. The Triton TRA001 seems to be the most popular one. But that thing costs a whopping 350 EUR!

将路由器内置到桌子中后，您需要一种简单的方法来将其从桌子顶部上下移动。不幸的是，只有少数已知的模型可以立即使用。 Triton TRA001似乎是最受欢迎的一款。但是那东西要花350欧元！

Once you have the router you also need an insert plate for your table and a lifting mechanism. When you go for something like the Kreg Panel you're looking at another 100 EUR and still have no lifting hardware.

有了router刨机后，您还需要为工作台准备一块插入板和一个升降机构。当您购买Kreg Panel之类的产品时，您要价100欧元，却仍然没有起重硬件。

So a professional setup may cost you about 500 EUR. Here is how to do something similar for less than 100 EUR by buying the cheap Einhell TC-RO 1155 E, some 3D printing and a bit of wood working.

因此，专业设置可能会花费您大约500欧元。以下是通过购买便宜的Einhell TC-RO 1155 E，一些3D打印和一些木制品来做不到100欧元的类似操作的方法。

All the needed parts are available at PrusaPrinters. While you can print the provided STLs as is, you probably want to use the included Fusion 360 files to adjust the insert plate measurements at least.

PrusaPrinters提供了所有必需的零件。虽然您可以按原样打印提供的STL，但您可能希望至少使用随附的Fusion 360文件来调整插入板的尺寸。

You can also easily adjust this system to just about any cheap router that has handles that can be unscrewed. Just adjust the provided Fusion 360 file for the handle attachment and the rest will be exactly the same.

您也可以轻松地将此系统调整为几乎任何带有可松开手柄的廉价路由器。只需调整提供的Fusion 360文件以处理手柄附件，其余的将完全相同。

You need a hole in your table and a plate to fit in. I chose to put it into my work bench right next to the table saw - this way I can reuse the table saw fence as a router fence.

您的桌子上需要有一个孔，并需要一块板子才能放进去。我选择将其放入台锯旁边的工作台中-这样，我就可以将台锯围栏用作router刨机围栏。

I actually did this step at the very end, but in hindsight I recommend doing it first. This way you can adjust the base plate size to your cutout instead the other way round – much easier than how I did it.

实际上，我实际上是在最后完成此步骤的，但事后看来，我建议先执行此步骤。这样，您可以根据切口调整基板尺寸，而不必反过来调整，这比我做起来要容易得多。

For size I went with 220x278mm, but the exact size doesn't matter too much. It should be large enough to cover the size of the router and be small enough to fit under your drill press. For thickness I used 12mm coated plywood. I would rather recommend going for 8mm – it brings the collet higher up.

对于尺寸，我选择了220x278mm，但是确切的尺寸并不太重要。它应足够大以覆盖the刨机的尺寸，并且应足够小以适合钻床。对于厚度，我使用了12mm涂层胶合板。我宁愿建议使用8毫米-它会使筒夹升高。

Initially I tried to route an exactly sized lip into the work bench to hold up the base plate, but in the end I settled for better method that allows more control to make the plate exactly flush with the bench.

最初，我尝试将尺寸正确的唇缘放入工作台中以支撑底板，但最后我决定采用更好的方法，该方法允许更多控制以使工作台与工作台完全平齐。

To do so cut all the way through. Either with your router or a jig saw. Regardless of method, be sure to use a guide so you cut straight.

为此，请一路过关斩将。使用路由器或曲线锯。不管使用哪种方法，请务必使用导板，以使您笔直。

If you use a router, you will end up with round corners. Your base plate needs round corners too then. I used the radius_10mm.stl from this router template model 1) and a flush trim bit to do so.

如果使用路由器，则最终会出现圆角。您的底板也需要圆角。我使用了来自此路由器模板模型1）的radius_10mm.stl和平齐修剪位来执行此操作。

Once the cutout is done and your plate fits perfectly, mount some strips of woods under it, making sure there is enough clearance for the router handles. You only really need some support in the four corners.

切口完成后，您的盘子完全适合，在下面安装一些木条，确保router刨机手柄有足够的间隙。您实际上只需要在四个角落提供一些支持。

Finally drive some wood screws into these supports. By carefully adjusting how far they are sticking out you can perfectly align the base plate. Super simple and accurate.

最后，将一些木螺钉拧入这些支架中。通过仔细调整它们伸出的距离，您可以完美地对准基板。超级简单准确。

The base plate will hold the router and accept insert plates to vary the hole diameter.

基板将固定the刨机并接受插入板以改变孔的直径。

At the center we want a large hole that will take the insert plates later. On a Kreg plate those inserts are locked with a twist mechanism. In our version we'll use magnets and a notch/bump mechanism to keep them from rotating.

在中心，我们需要一个大孔，稍后再将插入板取下。在Kreg板上，这些刀片通过扭转机构锁定。在我们的版本中，我们将使用磁铁和缺口/凸块机制来防止它们旋转。

Note: for all drilling work use a drill press if you have one. The usual precautions of waste boards or drilling from both sides for clean edges etc. apply.

注意：对于所有钻孔作业，如果有，请使用钻床。废物板或从两侧钻孔以清洁边缘等通常的预防措施均适用。

First mark the center of the plate and drill a small guide hole. Next use a 82mm hole saw to start on cutting out the large circle, but do not drill all the way through, just do a few millimeters at first.

首先标记板的中心并钻一个小导向孔。接下来，使用82毫米孔锯开始切出较大的圆，但不要一直钻孔，首先要钻几毫米。

Next use a square and mark a point on the circle where your bump goes. Again use a small drill to make a guide hole 2).

接下来，使用一个正方形，在圆上标记凹凸点。再次使用小钻头制作一个导向孔2）。

Using a 16mm Forstner bit drill out the notch. Once you have the notch, continue to drill out the large hole with the hole saw.

使用16毫米Forstner钻头钻出缺口。一旦有缺口，继续用孔锯钻出大孔。

Next we need something the insert's magnets can attach to. I used some pieces of 1.5mm steel which I cut to length. The steel plates go on the long edge sides and should overlap the large hole by about 10mm.

接下来，我们需要插入件的磁铁可以附着的东西。我用了一些1.5mm的钢，将它们切成一定长度。钢板位于长边侧，应与大孔重叠约10mm。

Because we need the plate to be flush for attaching the router, the steel needs to be sunken into the wood. I used my router and chisel to carefully cut the 1.5mm. The holes in the steel need to be countersunk so that the screw heads are flush, too.

因为我们需要平整板以安装the刨机，所以需要将钢沉入木材中。我用router刨机和凿子小心地切开了1.5mm。钢中的孔需要埋头，以便螺钉头也齐平。

Finally we need the holes to attach the router to the plate. Dismount the plastic cover from the router's plunge base and align it to match your hole.

最后，我们需要孔将路由器连接到板上。从路由器的插入式底座上卸下塑料盖，然后将其对齐以匹配孔。

Note: this is where I messed up. I only aligned it by eye. But ideally it should be perfectly center on the hole. I would recommend making a jig using the inserts model, but if you miss the center slightly you can adjust for that in the insert model later.

注意：这是我搞砸的地方。我只用眼睛对齐。但是理想情况下，它应该完全位于孔的中心。我建议使用插入模型制作夹具，但是如果您稍稍错过中心，可以稍后在插入模型中进行调整。

Once you marked the screw holes, drill them with a 4mm drill bit and counter sink them.

标记螺丝孔后，用4mm的钻头钻孔，然后将其沉没。

Start by removing all accessories like the depth gauge, revolver stop, dust shield and various thumb screws. Just keep the depth fixing lever for now.

首先卸下所有配件，例如深度计，左轮手枪挡块，防尘罩和各种翼形螺钉。暂时保持深度固定杆。

The springs are super strong, we need to make it a little easier to move the router up and down the plunge base. Removing them will also give us about 5mm more travel, compensating for the thick base plate.

弹簧非常坚固，我们需要使the刨机在柱塞底座上上下移动变得更容易一些。卸下它们还将使我们增加大约5mm的行程，以补偿较厚的基板。

First unscrew the top (4 screws). This gives you access to the motor brushes which have to come out. They are spring loaded and can easily be clipped out.

首先松开顶部（4个螺丝）。这使您可以访问必须伸出的电刷。它们是弹簧加载的，可以很容易地剪掉。

Next unscrew the lower part (4 very deep screws). No need to disassemble the spindle part.

接下来松开下部（4个非常深的螺钉）。无需拆卸主轴零件。

Now carefully pull the motor apart. The slack in the internal cables should give you three to four centimetres to work with. That should be enough - no need for further disassembly.

现在，小心地将电动机拉开。内部电缆的松弛应该使您可以工作三到四厘米。这样就足够了-无需进一步拆卸。

The two columns of the plunge base are held in place with steel rings. Before you use needle nose pliers to remove them, take a note which way the plunge base is oriented relative to the body 3). Use the depth lever to fixate the columns at a comfortable height.

柱塞基座的两根圆柱通过钢环固定在适当的位置。在使用尖嘴钳取下尖嘴钳之前，请记下柱塞基座相对于阀体的方向3）。使用深度杆将立柱固定在舒适的高度。

Once the rings are off you can pull out the whole base and access the springs. Remove them both then reinsert the columns again and make sure the base is oriented the same way as before.

一旦环脱下，您就可以拉出整个基座并接触弹簧。将它们都移除，然后再次重新插入色谱柱，并确保基座的方向与以前相同。

Re-add the steel rings, put the lower part back together, reinsert the motor brushes and close the top.

重新添加钢环，将下部放回去，重新插入电动机电刷，然后关闭顶部。

The scary part is done. Give the machine a spin to check it's still working.

可怕的部分完成了。旋转机器以检查其是否仍在工作。

Time to start on the first printed part. Print the handle.stl - if you're using a different router than the TC-RO 1155, you will probably need to modify the model.

是时候开始第一个印刷零件了。打印handle.stl-如果您使用的路由器与TC-RO 1155不同，则可能需要修改模型。

I used 0.3mm layer height and told PrusaSlicer to generate supports from the bed only. After printing remove the supports and insert two M6 nuts in both ends - they should press fit with little force.

我使用0.3mm的层高，并告诉PrusaSlicer仅从床上产生支撑。打印后，取下支架，并在两端插入两个M6螺母-它们应以很小的压力压入配合。

Disconnect the speed control board by removing the heat shrink wrap and undoing the crimping. Put the board aside for later.

通过移除热缩包装并取消压接来断开速度控制板的连接。将板放在一边以备后用。

Use the screws to attach the 3D printed part. Be sure to pull the speed control cable through the little hole on the side. Make sure you use the right orientation, the two nuts should be in a straight vertical line above each other.

使用螺钉固定3D打印的零件。确保将速度控制电缆穿过侧面的小孔。确保使用正确的方向，两个螺母应在彼此垂直的垂直线上。

Next we need a M6x200 hex head bolt. They are super hard to find, but you can order them from AliExpress. If you're impatient like me, you can try epoxying a nut to a piece of threaded rod while you wait.

接下来，我们需要一个M6x200六角头螺栓。它们很难找到，但是您可以从速卖通订购它们。如果您像我这样不耐烦，可以在等待时尝试将螺母涂在螺杆上。

Attach the router to the base plate (using M4x20 screws), then adjust the rod so it touches your base plate - mark where it does so. De-attach the router and remove the bolt again.

将router刨机安装到基板上（使用M4x20螺钉），然后调节杆使其与您的基板接触-在此标记位置。拆下路由器，然后再次卸下螺栓。

Drill a small guide hole on the mark you just made. Then use a 20mm forstner bit to make a pocket at the top about 7 to 8mm deep 4). Use a 7mm bit to drill a through hole.

在您刚做的标记上钻一个小导向孔。然后使用20毫米福斯特钻头在顶部形成约7至8毫米深的凹处4）。使用7毫米钻头钻通孔。

Now attach the router again, put a M6 washer in the hole and thread the bolt in again from the top.

现在再次安装attach刨机，将M6垫圈放入孔中，然后从顶部再次拧入螺栓。

Turning the bolt will now heighten or lower the router. Use a battery powered drill if you want to do it fast.

现在转动螺栓将升高或降低路由器。如果要快速进行，请使用电池供电的电钻。

Thanks to the M6 thread, one rotation will lift exactly 1mm so you can be pretty precise.

多亏了M6螺纹，旋转一圈即可精确提升1毫米，因此您可以非常精确。

Remember the speed control board from earlier? Time to put it into an enclosure. Print the box and lid ( switch.stl), thread the cables through the holes and close it. For me it held together nicely with a press fit, but you could also super glue it.

还记得以前的速度控制板吗？是时候将其放入机箱了。打印包装盒和盖子（switch.stl），将电缆穿过孔，然后将其关闭。对我来说，它与压配合很好地结合在一起，但是您也可以将其超级粘合。

To control speed and power from the front of the table, we need to have a cord from the machine to a new switch. I bought this one. The cable I harvested from an old electrical appliance.

要从桌子的前面控制速度和功率，我们需要从机器到新开关的电源线连接在一起。我买了这个。我从旧电器中收集的电缆。

With double sided tape (or super glue) attach the speed controller to the switch, drill holes into the switch and then connect the cables to one side of the switch terminals. Attach your cable to the opposite terminals.

用双面胶带（或超级胶水）将速度控制器连接到开关，在开关上钻孔，然后将电缆连接到开关端子的一侧。将电缆连接到相反的端子。

Once the switch is mounted to your table, connect the cable to the two wires sticking out on the side of the height adjustment handle using WAGO clamps.

将开关安装到桌面后，使用WAGO夹将电缆连接到伸出高度调节手柄侧面的两根电线。

The inserts make sure that there is as little space between your router bit and the router table. They can easily be changed for different bit sizes and be removed to change the bit.

插入内容可确保路由器位和路由器表之间的空间尽可能小。可以轻松地将它们更改为不同的位大小，然后将其删除以更改位。

The model for the inserts is adjustable via user parameters, so you can easily make it fit perfectly into your base plate. To do so, open the model in Fusion 360 and select Modify → Change Parameters. Each parameter has a description that should help you to identify what to tune. The description also tells you if you should add or substract clearances.

刀片的型号可通过用户参数进行调整，因此您可以轻松地使其完美地适合您的底板。为此，请在Fusion 360中打开模型，然后选择“修改”→“更改参数”。每个参数都有一个描述，可以帮助您确定要调整的内容。该说明还告诉您是应该添加还是减去间隙。

If your router is not mounted perfectly centered over the base plate hole, you can offset the hole in the insert using the offx and offy parameters to compensate.

如果路由器的安装位置没有完全对准基板孔的中心，则可以使用offx和offy参数进行补偿，以使插入件中的孔偏移。

The easiest and fastest way to operate the lifting mechanism is your power drill with the proper nut.

操作提升机构的最简单，最快的方法是使用适当的螺母安装电钻。

But you can also operate it manually with the “dial” I included with the 3D files. It can simply be put on the lift mechanism and operated with a finger, just like those old rotary dial phones.

但是，您也可以使用3D文件随附的“拨号”手动操作它。就像那些旧的旋转拨号电话一样，它可以简单地放在升降机构上并用手指操作。

It looks a bit silly but actually works just fine. Optionally you can add another magnet in the top so you can store it together with the other inserts by magnetically attaching it to a strip of metal.
