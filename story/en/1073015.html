Jon Stewart asks, ‘who gets to decide’ what is misinformation
乔恩·斯图尔特问，“谁来决定”什么是错误信息

2022-02-14 10:47:50

Former Daily Show host is taking on one of the bigger questions of our time: “Who gets to decide” what information constitutes “misinformation.”

前《每日秀》主持人正在回答我们这个时代一个更大的问题：“谁来决定”哪些信息构成“错误信息”

Speaking on his + podcast, Stewart cast a cynical eye at so-called “fact-checkers” and the legacy media labeling certain narratives as bad information and casting them out.

斯图尔特在他的+播客上发表讲话时，对所谓的“事实核查者”和传统媒体进行了愤世嫉俗的审视，这些媒体将某些叙事标记为不良信息，并将其逐出。

“The New York Times,  right, was a giant purveyor of misinformation, and disinformation,” Stewart said, citing the media outlet’s support of the reasons for the Iraq war. “And that’s as vaunted a media organization as you can find, but there was no accountability for them.”

斯图尔特说：“没错，《纽约时报》是错误信息和虚假信息的巨大传播者。”他引用了媒体对伊拉克战争原因的支持。“这是你能找到的最自夸的媒体组织，但他们没有责任。”

Stewart pointed out that he was “very vocal” about his opposition to the Iraq war in 2003, a minority opinion at the time.

斯图尔特指出，他在2003年“非常直言不讳”地反对伊拉克战争，这在当时只是少数人的意见。

“In the Iraq war, I was on the side of what you would think, on the mainstream is misinformation, I was promoting what they would call misinformation, but it turned out to be right years later and the establishment media was wrong,” Stewart said. “But my point is, these are shifting sands, and I think I get concerned with, well, who gets to decide?”

斯图尔特说：“在伊拉克战争中，我站在你认为的那一边，站在主流是错误信息的一边，我在宣传他们所说的错误信息，但几年后，结果证明这是正确的，建制派媒体是错误的。”。“但我的观点是，这些都是流动的沙子，我想我关心的是，好吧，谁来决定？”
