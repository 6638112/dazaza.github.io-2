Homebrew's Satya Patel and Hunter Walk team up with 8 VCs to launch Screendoor, a $50M fund to back up to 15 underrepresented investors raising their first fund
Homebrew的Satya Patel和Hunter Walk团队与8 vcs一起推出屏幕，获得50万元的基金，返回最多15个不足的投资者提出他们的第一个基金

2021-06-11 00:08:16

Would-be venture capitalists looking to raise a fund for the first time often face a chicken-and-egg problem: to raise money, they’re expected to demonstrate a track record of promising startup investments; to build such a portfolio, they need to raise money.

威胁的资本家希望首次筹集基金经常面临鸡蛋和蛋问题：筹集资金，他们希望展示有前途的启动投资的轨道记录;建立这样的投资组合，他们需要筹集资金。

It’s a challenge felt even more by emerging fund managers from underrepresented backgrounds, say Homebrew partners Satya Patel and Hunter Walk. “What we heard over the last few years as we got approached by new managers hoping to raise funds was that were plenty of opportunities for them to get counsel or mentorship, but what they really needed was capital,” Patel says.

Satya Patel和Hunter Walk说，来自非代表性的背景的新兴基金经理更多，这是一个挑战。 “我们在过去几年中听到了什么，因为我们被筹集资金的新经理接近，这对他们来说是有很多机会来获得律师或指导，但他们真正需要的是资本，”Patel说。

Now, Patel, Walk and eight other general partners at established VC firms are trying an experiment: raise their own fund together, across firms, to bring the resources of some of the industry’s elite limited partners into emerging funds faster. Called Screendoor, the fund is a $50 million-plus investment vehicle that will look to back up to 15 underrepresented investors raising their first institutional fund, all while providing formal and informal training and support without drawing carry or fees.

现在，帕尔，步行和八个在成立的VC公司的八个普通伙伴正在尝试一个实验：将自己的基金跨越公司，将一些行业精英有限合作伙伴带入新兴资金的资源更快。该基金称为屏幕，该基金是5000万美元的投资车，将备份最多15个不足的投资者培养了他们的第一个机构基金，同时提供了不绘制或费用的正式和非正式培训和支持。

The ten partners behind Screendoor include Aileen Lee of Cowboy Ventures; Eva Ho of Fika Ventures; Kirsten Green of Forerunner Ventures; Chris Howard and Leah Solivan of Fuel Capital; Patel and Walk at Homebrew; Kanyi Maqubela of Kindred Ventures; Charles Hudson of Precursor Ventures; and Shauntel Garvey of Reach Capital. The institutions investing in Screendoor include the investment arms of Davidson, Duke, Harvard, Michigan, Princeton and Virginia; The James Irvine Foundation; and investment firms Hall Capital and Sapphire Partners.

斯克雷德托的十个合作伙伴包括牛仔企业的Aileen Lee;菲克的eva ho;先行者帝国帝国风险投资;克里斯霍华德和燃料资金的莱夫帕特尔和散步在家; Kanyi Maqubela的Kindred Ventures;查尔斯哈德森的前体风险投资;和川川公园的到达资本。投资Screendoor的机构包括戴维森，杜克，哈佛，密歇根州，普林斯顿和弗吉尼亚州的投资武装;詹姆斯欧文基金会;和投资公司大厅资本和蓝宝石合作伙伴。

By pooling the capital in a fund-of-funds like Screendoor, such institutions can invest and build relationships with new fund managers they wouldn’t otherwise be able to back, Walk says, as first-time funds often raise amounts too small for a university endowment or large foundation’s VC strategies. Every limited partner in Screendoor is an investor in at least one of the firms represented by Screendoor’s founders, meaning Screendoor’s partners can use their own reputations as a bridge. “This can be a training ground for emerging managers to scale ahead of direct relationships with some of these larger institutions,” Walk says.

通过汇集资本在基金基金会，如斯克雷登托，此类机构可以与新基金经理的建立关系，他们将无法回答，走路说，作为第一次资金经常为a筹集太小的资金大学捐赠或大型基金会的VC策略。斯克雷维特的每个有限伙伴都是斯克雷登托的创始人代表的至少一家公司的投资者，这意味着斯凯内的合作伙伴可以使用自己的声誉作为桥梁。 “这可以是新兴管理人员在与这些较大的一些机构的直接关系领先地位的培训基础，”走路说。

And while Screendoor’s own general partners are VCs themselves and thus potential co-investors and peers, the investors they back will not be asked to share deal flow or more information than they would with a typical LP. Screendoor will make investments through a rotating investment committee of three, starting with Patel, Hudson and a representative of its limited partner The James Irvine Foundation. “Screendoor promises access to talented emerging managers and allows us to begin a relationship with these investors ahead of their first institutional raises.

虽然屏幕客人自己的一般合作伙伴都是VCS本身，因此潜在的共同投资者和同龄人，但他们回来的投资者不会被要求分享流量或更多信息，而不是与典型的LP。屏幕入口将通过旋转投资委员会进行投资，以康格尔，哈德森和其有限合伙人的代表詹姆斯·欧文基金会。 “屏幕托偶承诺访问有才华的新兴经理，并让我们在第一次制度提升之前与这些投资者开始关系。

“We’re participating not just as capital partners but will contribute time as well, alongside the hands-on mentorship Screendoor managers will receive,” Jesús Argüelles, the foundation’s director of investments, said in a statement. “Over time I hope we’ll build direct relationships with many of these firms.”

“我们不仅仅是作为资本合作伙伴而且会贡献时间，也可以贡献一下，德国投资总监Jesús阿格莱斯·耶和华·阿格拉斯在一份声明中表示。 “随着时间的推移，我希望我们能与许多公司建立直接关系。”

Screendoor is launching as a wave of new funds and initiatives are picking up in the venture industry, historically slow to major change but galvanized over recent months since the death of George Floyd. Those include the launch of funds from Zane Venture Fund and Collab Capital in Atlanta; a new fund from Harlem Capital in New York; MaC Venture Capital in Los Angeles; and Sixty8 Capital in Indianapolis, among others. And other programs, such as a new scout program launched by BLCK VC with Lightspeed and Sequoia, have looked to inject more investors into the ecosystem.

屏幕托克斯作为一股新的基金和倡议在风险投入的措施推动，历史上慢慢变化，但乔治弗洛伊德去世以来近几个月镀锌。那些包括从Zane风险基金和亚特兰大的合作资本推出资金;来自纽约哈莱姆首都的新基金;洛杉矶的Mac风险投资;和印第安纳波利斯的第六十八首都等。其他程序，例如Black VC与LightSpeed和SequoIa发布的新侦察程序，我们希望将更多的投资者注入生态系统中。

But such progress has been incremental at best, still a relative drop in the bucket compared to billions raised by traditional funds. That has shut such investors out of what Patel calls Silicon Valley’s “virtuous cycle,” in which investors back tech companies that in turn churn out more successful angel and venture investors as they exit. “The unfortunate part is that virtuous cycle has been inaccessible for large portions of the population,” Patel says.

但此类进度充其量是令人难以置信的，仍然是传统资金提出的数十亿的相对下降。这已经将这些投资者关闭了Patel呼叫Silicon Valley的“良性周期”，其中投资者回到科技公司，反过来又在他们退出时搅拌了更成功的天使和风险投资者。 “不幸的部分是，良性循环对于大部分人口来说已经无法进入，”Patel说。

Screendoor hopes to help set up its own virtuous cycle of underrepresented investors backing underrepresented founders who elevate underrepresented employees. But it doesn’t presume to have all the answers. Screendoor’s organizers will “open-source” their process for any other groups looking to emulate or improve upon it, Patel and Walk say; they also plan to expand Screendoor’s investor group with future funds.

屏幕客人希望帮助建立自己的善良的投资者繁琐的投资者循环支持，这是一个高度推出经验位置的员工的代表性创始人。但它并没有假设有所有的答案。屏幕托纳的组织者将“开源”他们的进程，以便寻求模仿或改善它，髌骨和步行说;他们还计划将斯凯托的投资者组扩展，未来的资金。

Screendoor is in talks with some prospective fund managers already, but the group haven’t made any commitments yet. Interested investors will be able to apply via open application on the fund’s website; what constitutes an underrepresented background or first institutional fund is flexible. “Anybody who believes that they are bringing something to the ecosystem that is currently underrepresented by the way capital has flown within venture, they have a chance to tell us about that,” says Walk.

Screendoor已经与一些潜在基金经理进行了会谈，但本集团还没有提出任何承诺。有兴趣的投资者将能够通过基金网站的公开申请申请;什么构成了不足的背景或第一个机构基金是灵活的。 “任何人认为他们正在向资本在风险投资中飞过的方式为目前推出的生态系统带来一些东西，他们有机会告诉我们这一点，”走路说。

And while Screendoor’s partners aren’t taking fees or carry in the fund, it’s not intended as charity. The fund will hire a full-time analyst or similar role to manage its portfolio and structured training and advice offered by its partners. And with those partners’ own LPs backing Screendoor, everyone involved expects a financial success. “The investors in the fund aren’t investing out of charity, they expect industry-beating returns here,” Patel says.

虽然斯凯内的合作伙伴没有收取费用或携带基金，但它并不是慈善机构。该基金将聘请全日制分析师或类似的角色来管理其合作伙伴提供的投资组合和结构化培训和建议。与那些合作伙伴自己的LPS背衬屏幕，每个人都会期望财务成功。 “该基金的投资者不投资慈善机构，他们期待在这里的行业殴打返回，”Patel说。
