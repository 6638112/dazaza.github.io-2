Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)
完全同态加密（FHE）

2021-06-15 11:56:51

This repository contains open-source libraries and tools to perform fullyhomomorphic encryption (FHE) operations on an encrypted data set.

此存储库包含开源库和工具，用于在加密数据集上执行Qualhomorphic加密（FHE）操作。

Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) is an emerging data processing paradigm thatallows developers to perform transformations on encrypted data. FHE can changethe way computations are performed by preserving privacy end-to-end, therebygiving users even greater confidence that their information will remain privateand secure.

完全同性恋加密（FHE）是一个新兴数据处理范例，它已使开发人员在加密数据上执行转换。可以通过保留隐私结束来执行计算来计算计算，从而提高用户甚至更有信心，以至于他们的信息将保持privateand安全。

The FHE C++ Transpiler is a general purpose library that converts C++ intoFHE-C++ that works on encrypted input. The code, examples, and more informationis in the transpiler subdirectory.

FHE C ++ Transpiler是一个通用库，它将C ++ Intofhe-C ++转换为加密输入。 Transpiler子目录中的代码，示例和更多信息。
