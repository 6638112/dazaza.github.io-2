Virgin Hyperloop fired almost half of its employees on Friday as it pivots away from passenger travel to freight.

Virgin Hyperloop周五解雇了近一半的员工，因为它从客运转向货运。

The futuristic transportation company confirmed with the Financial Times that 111 people were laid off. The company told the FT the layoffs would help the company focus on delivering cargo through its hyperloop pods, which are jetted through vacuum tubes at more than 600 mph.

这家未来派运输公司向英国《金融时报》证实，111人被解雇。该公司告诉英国《金融时报》，裁员将帮助该公司专注于通过超环型吊舱运送货物，超环型吊舱通过真空管以超过600英里/小时的速度喷射。
