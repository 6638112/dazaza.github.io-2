More than 1,200 Google workers condemn firing of AI scientist Timnit Gebru
超过1,200名Google员工谴责解雇AI科学家Timnit Gebru

2020-12-05 08:29:14

More than 1,200 Google employees and more than 1,500 academic researchers are speaking out in protest after a prominent Black scientist studying the ethics of artificial intelligence said she was fired by Google after the company attempted to suppress her research and she criticized its diversity efforts.

超过1200名Google员工和1,500多名学术研究人员在抗议活动中表示抗议，此前一位研究人工智能伦理的著名黑人科学家表示，在公司试图压制她的研究后，她被Google解雇，并批评了其多元化努力。

Timnit Gebru, who was the technical co-lead of Google’s Ethical AI team, wrote on Twitter on Wednesday that she had been fired after sending an email to an internal group for women and allies working in the company’s AI unit.

Google Ethical AI团队的技术联席主管Timnit Gebru周三在Twitter上写道，在向内部AI小组的女性和同盟发送电子邮件后，她被解雇了。

The email, which was first published by the tech newsletter Platformer, referenced a dispute over a research paper, but more broadly expressed frustration at Google’s diversity programs. In it, Gebru argued that “there is zero accountability” or real incentive for Google leadership to change. “Your life gets worse when you start advocating for underrepresented people, you start making the other leaders upset,” Gebru wrote. “There is no way more documents or more conversations will achieve anything.”

该电子邮件最初是由技术通讯Platformer发布的，其中提到了有关某研究论文的争议，但更广泛地表达了对Google多元化计划的不满。在其中，Gebru认为“零责任制”或Google领导改变的真正动力。 “当您开始倡导代表性不足的人时，您的生活会变得越来越糟，您开始使其他领导人感到沮丧，” Gebru写道。 “没有更多的文档或更多的对话将无法达成任何目标。”

Gebru is one of the most well-known and respected Black female scientists working in AI. Before joining Google, she co-authored a widely cited 2018 paper that found higher error rates in facial analysis technology for women with darker skin tones. She also co-founded the non-profit Black in AI that aims to increase representation of people of color in the field.

Gebru是从事AI工作的最著名和最受尊敬的黑人女性科学家之一。在加入Google之前，她与人合着了2018年被广泛引用的论文，发现肤色较深的女性面部分析技术的错误率更高。她还与他人共同创立了非营利性组织AI领域的黑人组织，该组织旨在增加有色人种在该领域的代表性。

The dispute over Gebru’s research arose in November, when a senior manager at Google told Gebru that she would have to either retract or remove her name from a paper she had co-authored, Gebru told Wired. The paper, co-authored by researchers inside and outside Google, contended that technology companies could do more to ensure AI systems aimed at mimicking human writing and speech do not exacerbate historical gender biases and use of offensive language, according to a draft copy seen by Reuters.

Gebru告诉Wired，有关Gebru的研究的争议始于11月，当时Google的一位高级经理告诉Gebru，她将不得不撤回或从她合着的论文中删除自己的名字。该论文由Google内部和外部的研究人员共同撰写，认为技术公司可以做更多的工作，以确保旨在模仿人类写作和语音的AI系统不会加剧历史性别偏见和令人反感的语言的使用。路透社

“I felt like we were being censored and thought this had implications for all of ethical AI research,” she told Wired. “You’re not going to have papers that make the company happy all the time and don’t point out problems. That’s antithetical to what it means to be that kind of researcher.”

她告诉《连线》杂志：“我觉得我们正在受到审查，并认为这对所有道德AI研究都有影响。” “您不会拥有使公司始终保持快乐并没有指出问题的文件。那与成为那种研究者意味着什么相反。”

Gebru told Wired that she attempted to negotiate with Google, offering to remove her name from the paper in exchange for a full explanation of the company’s objections, as well as a discussion of a better process for handling such matters in the future. If the company declined, she would arrange to leave at a later date.

Gebru告诉《连线》杂志，她试图与Google进行谈判，提出将她的名字从报纸上删除，以换取对该公司反对意见的完整解释，并讨论了将来处理此类问题的更好程序。如果公司拒绝，她将安排在以后离开。

Google rejected her request, and – after Gebru sent the frustrated email about diversity efforts – the company emailed her team to say it had accepted her resignation. They also cut off her access to company email, Gebru said. Google has maintained that Gebru resigned.

Google拒绝了她的请求，在Gebru发送了有关多元化工作的沮丧电子邮件之后，该公司给她的团队发送电子邮件说接受了她的辞职。他们还切断了她对公司电子邮件的访问权限。 Google坚持认为Gebru辞职。

Gebru’s abrupt departure sent shockwaves through Google and the broader AI research community. Sherrilyn Ifill, president of the NAACP’s Legal Defense and Educational Fund, wrote on Twitter that Gebru’s firing was “absolutely infuriating” and “a disaster”. More than 1,200 Google employees and more than 1,500 supporters from academia and civil society signed a letter of protest.

Gebru的突然离职，在Google和更广泛的AI研究社区中掀起了一阵冲击。 NAACP法律辩护和教育基金会主席Sherrilyn Ifill在推特上写道，格布鲁的开枪“绝对令人生气”和“一场灾难”。超过1,200名Google员工和来自学术界和民间社会的1,500多名支持者签署了抗议信。

“The termination is an act of retaliation against Dr Gebru, and it heralds danger for people working for ethical and just AI – especially Black people and People of Color – across Google,” read the letter, published by the group that organized a walkout at Google over the company’s treatment of sexual harassment and gender discrimination.

该组织在组织罢工的小组发表的信中说：“解雇是对格布鲁博士的报复行动，它预示着道德操守的人以及在整个Google从事AI工作的人们（尤其是黑人和有色人种）的危险。” Google对公司对性骚扰和性别歧视的待遇。

“Research integrity can no longer be taken for granted in Google’s corporate research environment, and Dr Gebru’s firing has overthrown a working understanding of what kind of research Google will permit. This is also a moment of reckoning beyond Google.”

“在Google的公司研究环境中，研究完整性不再是理所当然的，而Gebru博士的解雇已经推翻了对Google允许进行何种研究的有效理解。这也是Google之外无法估量的时刻。”

Gebru’s abrupt departure adds to years of angst, including several resignations and firings, in the AI department and other organizations at Google over diversity and whether the company’s efforts to minimize the potential harms of its services are sufficient.

Gebru的突然离职加剧了多年来对AI部门和Google其他组织的多样性的焦虑，包括多次辞职和解雇，以及公司为减少其服务的潜在危害所做的努力是否足够。

Just Wednesday, the National Labor Relations Board issued a complaint accusing Google of unlawfully monitoring and questioning several workers who were then fired for protesting against company policies and trying to organize a union.

就在星期三，美国国家劳工关系委员会（National Labour Relations Board）发出投诉，指控Google非法监视和质疑几名工人，然后这些工人因抗议公司政策和试图组织工会而被解雇。

Jeff Dean, head of Google’s AI unit, told staff in an email obtained by Platformer that the research paper “didn’t meet our bar for publication”. Dean, who is white and male, also addressed Gebru’s email to the women and allies group, writing: “I also feel badly that hundreds of you received an email just this week from Timnit telling you to stop work on critical DEI programs. Please don’t.”

Google AI部门负责人Jeff Dean在Platformer获得的电子邮件中告诉员工，该研究论文“没有达到我们的出版标准”。白人和男性的迪安（Dean）也向格布鲁（Gebru）的男女同盟小组致信，写道：“我也感到非常遗憾的是，你们中的数百人仅在本周收到蒂姆尼特（Timnit）的一封电子邮件，告诉您停止重要的DEI计划。请不要。”

On Friday, Dean released a longer statement about Gebru’s termination, which he continued to call a resignation. In it, he reiterated his argument that the paper did not include enough references to efforts Google has made to lessen the environmental impacts of computing or its attempts to mitigate bias in AI.

周五，迪恩（Dean）发布了一份关于格布鲁（Gebru）辞职的更长的声明，他继续称辞职。 在文章中，他重申了自己的论点，即该论文未充分提及Google为减轻计算对环境的影响或其减轻AI偏见的努力。
