Tech groups sue Maryland to block new digital-ad tax from going into effect
科技团体苏马里兰阻止新的数字广告税业

2021-02-19 05:50:15

A group of business organizations led by the US Chamber of Commerce is suing the state of Maryland, seeking to block implementation of the state's brand-new, first-of-its-kind tax on digital-advertising revenue.

由美国商会牵头的一组商业组织正在起诉马里兰州，试图阻止该州对数字广告收入实行全新的同类之先税。

Maryland's tax bill is "deeply flawed" and "illegal in myriad ways," the suit ( PDF) alleges, claiming that act will "harm Marylanders and small businesses and reduce the overall quality of Internet content."

马里兰州的税单有严重缺陷。和以各种方式非法的，＆＃34;该诉讼（PDF）声称，该行为将损害马里兰人和小型企业并降低互联网内容的整体质量。

"This is a case of legislative overreach, punishing an industry that supports over one hundred thousand jobs in Maryland and contributes tens of billions of dollars to its economy each year," the Internet Association, one of the plaintiffs, said in a statement about the suit. "Internet services and companies are proud to play a role in creating opportunities for Maryland’s small businesses and citizens."

＆＃34;这是一例立法超支的案例，对一个在马里兰州提供十万个工作岗位并每年为其经济贡献数百亿美元的行业进行惩罚。原告之一互联网协会在有关诉讼的声明中表示。 ＆＃34;互联网服务和公司为在马里兰州的小型企业和公民创造机会中发挥作用而感到自豪。

Maryland's Digital Advertising Gross Revenues Tax does what it sounds like: it imposes a tax on the gross revenues companies make from digital advertising inside Maryland. Digital-advertising services, as defined in the bill, include "advertisements in the form of banner advertising, search advertising, interstitial advertising, and other comparable advertising services."

马里兰州的数字广告总收入税听起来像：对公司从马里兰州内部的数字广告所得的总收入征税。法案中定义的数字广告服务包括＆＃34;标语广告，搜索广告，非页内广告和其他类似广告服务形式的广告。

Businesses that make less than $100 million in a calendar year from global digital-advertising revenues are exempt from the tax, after which it scales up. Businesses with global annual gross advertising revenues higher than $100 million, up to $1 billion, owe 2.5 percent. For businesses between $1 billion and $5 billion, it's 5 percent, then 7.5 percent for businesses with global revenue up to $15 billion, and 10 percent for anything larger than that.

在一个日历年中，全球数字广告收入少于1亿美元的企业可以免税，之后再扩大。全球年度广告总收入超过1亿美元（至10亿美元）的企业所占比例为2.5％。对于收入在10亿美元至50亿美元之间的企业，该比例为5％，然后是全球收入不超过150亿美元的企业为7.5％，大于10％的企业则为10％。

The Act uses global revenue to calculate what tax bracket a company falls into, but it specifically only imposes that tax on digital advertising in Maryland, aimed at Marylanders. If 2 percent of a company's US digital ad revenue in 2021 comes from advertising in Maryland, then the company would owe tax on just that 2 percent.

该法案使用全球收入来计算公司应缴纳的税种，但它仅专门针对马里兰州针对马里兰州的数字广告征税。如果公司2021年在美国的数字广告收入中有2％来自马里兰州的广告，则该公司仅需为此2％缴税。

Lawmakers in the Old Line State first approved the bill back in May, but Republican Gov. Larry Hogan vetoed it. Both chambers of the state legislature voted last week in favor of overriding the veto and implementing the law.

旧线州的议员于5月首次批准了该法案，但共和党州长拉里·霍根（Larry Hogan）否决了该法案。州议会两院上周投票赞成压倒否决权和执行法律。

Several Maryland businesses joined with large trade groups last year to launch a coalition, Marylanders for Tax Fairness, to lobby against the tax. The major groups representing almost all of the large US tech firms—including the Internet Association, the IAB, the NCTA, and TechNet—are among the coalition's membership.

去年，马里兰州的几家企业与大型贸易组织一起发起了一个联盟，名为“税收公平的马里兰人”，以游说反对税收。代表几乎所有大型美国科技公司的主要团体（包括互联网协会，IAB，NCTA和TechNet）都加入了该联盟。

The coalition in a statement last week all but promised to sue when the legislature overrode Hogan's veto on the bill, and three of its members, plus another organization, are now making good on that threat.

联盟在上周的一份声明中几乎承诺要在立法机关推翻霍根对法案的否决权时提起诉讼，该党的三名成员以及另外一个组织现在已对这一威胁作出了回应。

The bill is not actually a tax but is instead a "punitive fee, penalty, or fine" under US law, the trade groups argue in the suit. Because of the revenue-based scale, the Act targets "extra-territorial conduct," i.e., behavior outside the state of Maryland, and "is a highly unusual and extraordinarily severe form of exaction."

该帐单实际上不是税款，而是＆quot;惩罚性费用，罚则或罚款＆＃34;根据美国法律，贸易团体对此提起诉讼。由于基于收入的规模，该法案针对的是“域外行为”，即行为是马里兰州以外的行为，并且是一种非常不寻常且极为严厉的勒索行为。＆＃34;

Additionally, the plaintiffs argue, the tax violates the Internet Tax Freedom Act. Congress first passed the ITFA in 1998 and subsequently renewed it several times before President Barack Obama signed a permanent extension of the bill into law in 2016.

此外，原告认为，该税收违反了《互联网税收自由法案》。国会于1998年首先通过了ITFA，随后又对其进行了数次更新，直到总统巴拉克·奥巴马（Barack Obama）于2016年将法案永久性延长为法律。

The ITFA is designed to stop states from taxing Internet access services, which looked in the 1990s like they might be about to become a new potential line of state revenue. In short, your state, county, or city cannot impose a 5% tax on your monthly Comcast or Verizon bill. A handful of states had enacted such taxes prior to the 1998 law, but were required to phase those taxes out by 2020.

ITFA旨在阻止各州对互联网接入服务征税，在1990年代，互联网接入服务看起来像它们可能将成为州财政收入的新潜力。简而言之，您所在的州，县或市不能对您的每月Comcast或Verizon帐单征收5％的税。少数几个州在1998年法律颁布之前就已经颁布了此类税收，但必须在2020年之前逐步淘汰。

The IFTA does not prohibit other sales and use taxes related to transactions that take place over the Internet, which is why most of us pay sales tax on our online orders by now. What will be left to the court to determine is if the ad sales tax is discriminatory because the bill does not include other advertisers, such as print and television, and instead only includes online ads.

IFTA并不禁止与通过互联网进行的交易相关的其他销售和使用税，这就是为什么我们大多数人现在要对在线订单支付销售税的原因。由法院决定的是广告销售税是否具有歧视性，因为该账单不包括印刷和电视等其他广告商，而仅包括在线广告。
