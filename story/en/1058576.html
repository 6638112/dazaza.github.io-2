Gathering under a New Umbrella — We,maintainers and contributors of well-known free softwareprojects, are making this place our new home, fightingfor the freedom of computer users and hacking the good hack.

在新的伞下聚集 - 我们，知名的自由软件商品商品的维护者和贡献者，正在为这个地方制作我们的新家，为计算机用户的自由而战士打捞。

Governance, Not Unilateralism — This new home is governed by thosewho build it following common goals and sharing the samevalues. The Assemblyis where we discuss day-to-day operation andthe general direction of our umbrella project, including its governance, transparently.

治理，而不是单侧主义 - 这个新的家庭受到普通目标和分享凝视之后的人的管辖。我们讨论了日常运营和我们伞项目的一般方向，包括其治理，众所周知。

This Group’s Not Uniform — We have common goals but also differentbackgrounds, and we view it as a strength. Our interactions in thisproject are subject to its code ofconduct.

这个小组不统一 - 我们有共同的目标，也有不同的背景，我们将其视为一种力量。我们在该项目中的交互符合其代码的代码。

GNU’s Novelty is Unequaled — We contribute to projects that go backto the roots of free software.These projects and newer ones areleading in their own domain and we plan to keep it that way.

GNU的新奇是不平等的 - 我们贡献了返回自由软件根源的项目。这些项目和较新的项目在他们自己的领域中，我们计划将其保留。

GNU Needs U! — It’s about building a software commons to empowercomputer users. We need you — as a user, developer, speaker,coordinator, translator, designer, system administrator: if you share ourgoals, bring your owncontributions!

GNU需要你！ - 它是关于构建一个软件共享到授权计算机用户。我们需要您 - 作为用户，开发人员，扬声器，协调员，翻译，设计师，系统管理员：如果您共享OURGOALS，请带上自己的信件！

If you’re a GNU maintainer or contributor and you share that vision, join us now!

如果您是GNU维护者或撰稿人，并且您分享该愿景，请立即加入我们！
