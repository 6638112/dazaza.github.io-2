停止部分捕捞将增加整体渔获量

2020-11-09 00:51:23

I most recent year for which relevant data are available, people consumed more fish than they did either pork or beef or poultry. Humanity’s appetite for the sea’s bounty has more than doubled since 1990. Fish, whether wild caught or farmed, now make up nearly a fifth of the animal protein that human beings eat.

在有相关数据的最近一年，人们吃的鱼比猪肉、牛肉或家禽都多。自1990年以来，人类对海洋资源的需求增加了一倍多。鱼，无论是野生捕获的还是养殖的，现在占人类食用的动物蛋白的近五分之一。

In this context, running the world’s fisheries efficiently might seem a sensible idea. In practice, that rarely happens. Even well-governed coastal countries often pander to their fishing lobbies by setting quotas which give little respite to battered piscine populations. Those with weak or corrupt governments may not even bother with this. Deals abound that permit outsiders legal but often badly monitored access to such countries’ waters. And many rogue vessels simply enter other people’s fishing grounds and steal their contents.

在这种背景下，有效地管理世界渔业似乎是一个明智的想法。在实践中，这种情况很少发生。即使是治理良好的沿海国家，也经常通过设定配额来迎合他们的渔业游说团体，这让遭受重创的鱼类种群几乎没有得到喘息的机会。那些政府软弱或腐败的人可能甚至不会为此而烦恼。允许外来者合法进入这些国家水域的交易比比皆是，但往往监管不力。许多流氓船只干脆进入别人的渔场，偷走他们的东西。

The demand side of the equation, then, has many problems. But there may be a way to improve the supply side: increase the area where fishing is forbidden altogether.

因此，等式中的需求方面存在很多问题。但或许有一种方法可以改善供应面：增加完全禁止捕鱼的区域。

This paradoxical approach, which involves the creation of so-called marine protected areas ( s), has already been demonstrated on several occasions to work locally. Valuable fish stocks off Apo island in the Philippines increased significantly after a no-take reserve was created in 1982, and similar results have been forthcoming off the coasts of Florida, South Africa and St Lucia. Extrapolating from these examples, Reniel Cabral of the University of California, Santa Barbara and his colleagues have, as they describe in the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences, built a model which explores the idea of extending s elsewhere. If the right extensions are picked, their model suggests, designating a mere 5% more of the world’s oceans as s—which would triple the area protected—could increase the future global catch of the 811 species they looked at by more than 20%. That corresponds to an extra 10m tonnes of food a year.

这种自相矛盾的方法，包括建立所谓的海洋保护区，已经在几个场合证明在当地有效。在1982年设立禁捕保护区后，菲律宾阿波岛附近有价值的鱼类资源显著增加，佛罗里达、南非和圣卢西亚海岸也即将出现类似的结果。加州大学圣巴巴拉分校的雷尼尔·卡布拉尔(Reniel Cabral)和他的同事们从这些例子中推断出，正如他们在《美国国家科学院院刊》上所描述的那样，他们建立了一个模型，探索在其他地方推广科学的想法。他们的模型表明，如果选择了正确的延伸，仅将世界上5%的海洋指定为海洋保护区-这将是受保护面积的三倍-可能会使他们研究的811个物种未来的全球捕获量增加20%以上。这相当于每年额外增加1000万吨粮食。

The idea that restricting fishing would permit more fish to be caught may seem counterintuitive, but the logic is simple. Fish in s can grow larger than those at constant risk of being pulled from the ocean. Larger fish produce more eggs. More eggs mean more fry. Many of these youngsters then grow up and move out of the safe zone, thus becoming available to catch in adjoining areas where fishing is permitted.

限制捕鱼将允许捕获更多鱼的想法似乎有违直觉，但逻辑很简单。S区的鱼可以长得比那些经常有可能被从海洋中打捞上来的鱼更大。体型较大的鱼产卵较多。更多的鸡蛋意味着更多的油炸。这些幼鱼中的许多人后来长大并搬出了安全区，因此可以在邻近允许捕鱼的地区捕鱼。

Dr Cabral and his team based their calculations on data they compiled for 1,338 stocks of fish and marine invertebrates. For each they mapped the geographical range, mobility and population growth of the local species, and assessed the sustainability of present-day fishing levels. That let them create a model of the ocean from which the impact of changes in fishable area could be assessed. Working at a fairly coarse resolution—each pixel corresponding to a square of ocean 55km across—they discovered that a small, well-aimed increase in the size of s brought substantial benefit.

卡布拉尔博士和他的团队基于他们收集的1338种鱼类和海洋无脊椎动物的数据进行了计算。他们绘制了每种鱼的地理范围、流动性和当地物种的种群增长情况，并评估了目前捕捞水平的可持续性。这让他们创建了一个海洋模型，从这个模型可以评估可捕捞区域变化的影响。在相当粗的分辨率下工作--每个像素对应一个55公里宽的海洋正方形--他们发现，小幅、有针对性地增加s的大小会带来实质性的好处。

Though s work their magic on all sorts of fishing grounds, Dr Cabral’s model suggests they are especially beneficial for the worst-managed areas, most of which are tropical—and in particular for overfished species such as Atlantic horse mackerels, Japanese anchovies and Argentine hake (the catch pictured on the previous page).

卡布拉尔博士的模型表明，尽管它们在所有类型的渔场都发挥了它们的魔力，但它们对管理最差的区域特别有益，这些区域大多是热带地区，特别是对过度捕捞的物种，如大西洋马鱼、日本凤尾鱼和阿根廷鳕鱼(上一页的捕获物)。

They also have the virtue of simplicity. The setting of quotas is open to pressure to overestimate of how many fish can safely be caught. And different species need different arrangements, making management harder. The breeding habits of the fish concerned must be monitored, and good estimates made of their numbers. This is difficult enough for countries with well-developed fisheries-research establishments. For those without such it is little more than guesswork. And the problem is made yet more complicated by the fact that tropical ecosystems are more speciose and thus harder to understand and manage than those in temperate climes.

它们还具有简单的优点。配额的设定面临着高估能安全捕捞多少鱼的压力。不同的物种需要不同的安排，这使得管理变得更加困难。必须监测有关鱼类的繁殖习性，并对其数量作出准确估计。对于渔业研究机构发达的国家来说，这已经够困难的了。对于那些没有这样做的人来说，这只是一种猜测。而且，热带生态系统比温带生态系统更特殊，因此更难理解和管理，这一事实使问题变得更加复杂。

Setting the rules for an is, by contrast, easy. You stick up a metaphorical sign that says, “No fishing”. Knowing who is breaking the rules is easy, too. If your gear is in the water, you are fishing illegally.

相比之下，为一家银行制定规则则很容易。你竖起一个比喻的牌子，上面写着“禁止钓鱼”。知道谁在违反规则也很容易。如果你的渔具在水里，你就是非法捕鱼。

There are, Dr Cabral and his team readily admit, limitations to their model. Not all fish stocks are equally well recorded, meaning many pertinent species escaped the analysis. Including these would more than double the underlying tonnage of catch being looked at, and might reasonably be presumed to add to the increase in yield that the model already predicts. Also, though good data exist about the movement of fish around the world’s oceans, where they spawn is less well understood. As spawning grounds are prime sites for s, this uncertainty muddies the waters.

卡布拉尔博士和他的团队欣然承认，他们的模型存在局限性。并不是所有的鱼类种群都有同样好的记录，这意味着许多相关物种逃脱了分析。如果计入这些因素，将使正在研究的潜在捕捞量增加一倍以上，并可能被合理地推定为增加了模型已经预测的产量增长。此外，尽管有关于鱼类在世界海洋中活动的良好数据，但人们对它们在哪里产卵知之甚少。由于产卵场是鱼类的主要栖息地，这种不确定性搅乱了局面。

The researchers’ hope, though, is that—perhaps inspired by this study—some governments will now think it worthwhile to conduct more detailed investigations of their own. If a bit more protection results in a lot more fish, everybody wins. ■

不过，研究人员的希望是--或许是受到这项研究的启发--一些政府现在会认为，自己进行更详细的调查是值得的。如果更多的保护措施导致更多的鱼，那么所有人都是赢家。。
