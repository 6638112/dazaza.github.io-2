Shilling Founders Fund is Portugal’s newest VC, with $35.6M to spend on early-stage startups
先令的创始人基金是葡萄牙最新的VC，在早期初创公司上花费35.6米

2021-04-08 16:06:23

The fund says it has already invested in seven companies: Rows (spreadsheet for app creators), Vawlt (secure and resilient multi-cloud platform), Promptly (SaaS platform for health outcomes analytics), Modatta (decentralized marketplace for consented personal data), Biocol Labs (DTC post-chemical pharmacy), Decipad (low-code notebook) and Detech.ai (AI-powered application and infrastructure monitoring platform).

该基金表示已经投资于七家公司：行（App Creators的电子表格），VIALLT（安全和弹性多云平台），及时（SaaS平台，用于健康成果分析），Modatta（分散的市场个人数据）， BIOCOL LABS（DTC后化工药房），DECIPAD（低码笔记本）和DESTECH.AI（AI供电和基础设施监控平台）。

The fund is also launching what it dubs the “Shilling Platform” – a pool of learnings and resources for startups.

该基金也在推出它配给“先令平台” - 初创公司的学习和资源池。

In a statement, Pedro Santos Vieira, managing partner at Shilling said: “We call it experience-based acceleration. Additionally, we run on a profit-sharing model. Each portfolio founder will receive a share of our returns. This twofold approach fully aligns incentives between Shilling, LPs, and portfolio founders.”

在Shileding的伙伴管理合作伙伴佩德罗桑托斯维埃拉的声明中说：“我们称之为基于体验的加速。此外，我们在利润共享模型上运行。每个投资组合创始人都会收到我们的回报份额。这种双重的方法完全对准先令，LPS和投资组合创始人之间的激励。“

Founded by Hugo Gonçalves Pereira, António Casanova, Diogo da Silveira, João Coelho Borges, Juan Alvarez and Pedro Rutkowski in 2011, Shilling was later joined by tech founders Ricardo Jacinto (Elecctro), Miguel Santo Amaro (Uniplaces), Pedro Ramalho Carlos (IP) and Pedro Santos Vieira (GoodGuide) in the last five years. Since 2011, it has invested in a number of breakout hits from the country, including Unbabel; Bizay; Uniplaces; and Best Tables, acquired by TripADvisor.

公司成立于2011年，AntónioCasanova，Diogo da Silveira，Juango To Silveira，Juan Alvarez和Pedro Rutkowski，后来加入Tech创始人Ricardo Jacinto（Elecctro），Miguel Santo Amaro（Uniplace），Pedro Ramalho Carlos（IP ）在过去的五年中，佩德罗桑托斯维埃拉（Goodguide）。自2011年以来，它投资了来自该国的一些突破点击，包括Unbabel; bizay; uniplace;和TripAdvisor收购的最佳表格。

Hugo Gonçalves Pereira, founder of Shilling, added: “We are a Portugal-based, globally ambitious, VC fund, with a founder-friendly approach to early-stage investing… and when we say founder-friendly we truly mean it: in our pre-seed program, ventures go from first call, to money in the bank, in less than 30 days.”

HugoGonçalvesPereira，Shileding的创始人补充说：“我们是一家位于葡萄牙，全球雄心勃勃的VC基金，具有创始人友好的方法，即早期投资的方法......当我们说创始人友好的时候，我们真的是指它：在我们的预先种子计划，企业从第一次打电话，到银行的钱，不到30天。“

As we noted earlier this year in our ExtraCrunch survey of Lisbon, Portugal, the city is gearing up to join other significant tech hubs.

正如我们今年早些时候在葡萄牙里斯本的额外调查中指出的那样，这座城市正在加入其他重要的技术中心。

Other leading VCs in the country include  Indico Capital Partners,   Faber, Armilar Venture Partners, Tocha, and   Portugal Ventures.

该国其他领先的VCS包括印度资本合作伙伴，Faber，Armilar Venture Partners，Tocha和葡萄牙企业。
