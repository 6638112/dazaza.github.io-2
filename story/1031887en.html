1961年，南极洲的列昂尼德·罗戈佐夫医生为自己做阑尾切除手术。

2020-10-29 10:33:19

Leonid Rogozov was a Soviet general practitioner who took part in the sixth Soviet Antarctic Expedition in 1960–1961. He was the only doctor stationed at the Novolazarevskaya Station and, while there, developed appendicitis, which meant he had to perform an appendectomy on himself, a famous case of self-surgery.

列昂尼德·罗戈佐夫(Leonid Rogozov)是一名苏联全科医生，曾在1960-1961年参加过第六次苏联南极探险队。他是唯一一名驻扎在Novolazarevskaya车站的医生，在那里期间，他患上了阑尾炎，这意味着他不得不给自己做阑尾切除术，这是一个著名的自我手术案例。

In 1961, Rogozov was stationed at a newly constructed Russian base in Antarctica. The 12 men inside were cut off from the outside world by the polar winter by March of that year. On the morning of 29 April 1961, Rogozov experienced general weakness, nausea, and moderate fever, and later pain in the lower right portion of the abdomen.

1961年，罗戈佐夫驻扎在南极洲新建的俄罗斯基地。到当年3月，由于极地的冬天，里面的12个人与外界隔绝了。1961年4月29日早晨，罗戈佐夫出现全身虚弱、恶心和中度发烧，后来右下腹疼痛。

His symptoms were classic: he had acute appendicitis. “He knew that if he was to survive he had to undergo an operation”, the British Medical Journal recounted. “But he was in the frontier conditions of a newly founded Antarctic colony on the brink of the polar night. Transportation was impossible. Flying was out of the question, because of the snowstorms. And there was one further problem: he was the only physician on the base”. Rogozov wrote in his diary:

他的症状很典型：他患了急性阑尾炎。“英国医学杂志”报道说：“他知道如果他想活下来，他必须接受手术。”“但他当时处在一个新建立的南极殖民地的边界条件下，即将进入极夜。交通是不可能的。因为暴风雪，坐飞机是不可能的。还有一个问题：他是基地里唯一的医生。罗戈佐夫在日记中写道：

“It seems that I have appendicitis. I am keeping quiet about it, even smiling. Why frighten my friends? Who could be of help? A polar explorer’s only encounter with medicine is likely to have been in a dentist’s chair”.

“我好像得了阑尾炎。我对此保持沉默，甚至微笑。为什么要吓唬我的朋友？谁能帮上忙呢？极地探险家与医学的唯一邂逅很可能是坐在牙医的椅子上“。

All the available conservative treatment was applied (antibiotics, local cooling), but the patient’s general condition was getting worse: his body temperature rose, vomiting became more frequent.

所有可用的保守治疗都得到了应用(抗生素，局部降温)，但患者的一般情况越来越差：体温上升，呕吐变得更加频繁。

“I did not sleep at all last night. It hurts like the devil! A snowstorm whipping through my soul, wailing like a hundred jackals. Still no obvious symptoms that perforation is imminent, but an oppressive feeling of foreboding hangs over me… This is it… I have to think through the only possible way out: to operate on myself…It’s almost impossible…but I can’t just fold my arms and give up”.

“我昨晚一夜没睡。疼得要命！暴风雪席卷我的灵魂，像一百只狼一样哀号。仍然没有明显的症状表明穿孔迫在眉睫，但一种不祥的压迫感笼罩着我…。就是这个，…。我必须想清楚唯一可能的出路：给自己动手术…。这几乎是不可能的，…。但我不能袖手旁观，放弃。“。

Rogozov had no option but to perform the operation on himself. The operation started at 02:00 local time on the first day of May with the help of a driver and meteorologist, who were providing instruments and holding a mirror to observe areas not directly visible, while Rogozov was in a semi-reclining position, half-turned to his left side.

罗戈佐夫别无选择，只能自己做手术。行动于当地时间5月1日凌晨2点在一名司机和气象学家的帮助下开始，他们正在提供仪器，手持镜子观察不可直接看到的区域，而罗戈佐夫则处于半卧位，半转向左侧。

After 30-40 minutes Rogozov started to take short breaks because of general weakness and vertigo. Finally he removed the severely affected appendix. He applied antibiotics in the peritoneal cavity and closed the wound. The operation itself lasted an hour and 45 minutes. Partway through, the helping team took photographs of the operation.

30-40分钟后，由于全身虚弱和眩晕，罗戈佐夫开始短暂休息。最后，他切除了受严重影响的阑尾。他在腹腔内使用了抗生素，并缝合了伤口。手术本身持续了1小时45分钟。中途，帮助小组拍摄了行动的照片。

“I worked without gloves. It was hard to see. The mirror helps, but it also hinders—after all, it’s showing things backwards. I work mainly by touch. The bleeding is quite heavy, but I take my time—I try to work surely. Opening the peritoneum, I injured the blind gut and had to sew it up. Suddenly it flashed through my mind: there are more injuries here and I didn’t notice them… I grow weaker and weaker, my head starts to spin. Every 4-5 minutes I rest for 20-25 seconds.

“我工作时没有戴手套。很难看清。镜子有帮助，但也有阻碍--毕竟，它是在倒着看东西。我主要靠触摸来工作。出血相当严重，但我不着急--我努力工作。打开腹膜，我弄伤了盲肠，不得不缝合。突然，我的脑海里闪过这样一个念头：这里有更多的伤员，我没有注意到。…。我变得越来越虚弱，我的头开始旋转。每隔4-5分钟，我休息20-25秒。

Finally, here it is, the cursed appendage! With horror I notice the dark stain at its base. That means just a day longer and it would have burst and… At the worst moment of removing the appendix I flagged: my heart seized up and noticeably slowed; my hands felt like rubber. Well, I thought, it’s going to end badly. And all that was left was removing the appendix… And then I realized that, basically, I was already saved”.

终于到了，被诅咒的附属物！我惊恐地注意到它底部的深色污渍。这意味着只要再多一天，它就会破裂和…。在切除阑尾的最糟糕时刻，我发了一句话：我的心脏停止跳动，明显放慢了速度；我的手摸起来像橡胶一样。嗯，我想，结局会很糟糕。剩下的就是切除阑尾…。然后我意识到，基本上，我已经得救了。“。

After the operation gradual improvement occurred in the signs of peritonitis and in the general condition of Rogozov. Body temperature returned to normal after five days, and the stitches were removed seven days after the operation. He resumed his regular duties in about two weeks.The self-surgery captured the imagination of the Soviet public at the time. In 1961 he was awarded Order of the Red Banner of Labour.

术后腹膜炎体征及Rogozov一般情况逐渐改善。5天后体温恢复正常，7天后拆线。大约两周后，他恢复了正常的工作。这次自我手术激发了当时苏联公众的想象力。1961年，他被授予劳动红旗勋章。

Rogozov’s self operation remains an example of determination and the human will for life. In later years Rogozov himself rejected all glorification of his deed. When thoughts like these were put to him, he usually answered with a smile and the words: “A job like any other, a life like any other”.

罗戈佐夫的自我操作仍然是决心和人类生命意志的典范。在后来的几年里，罗戈佐夫本人拒绝一切美化他的行为。当有人向他提出这样的想法时，他通常会微笑着回答说：“工作和其他工作一样，生活和其他工作一样。”

In October 1962 Rogozov returned to Leningrad and started working on an MD at his alma mater. In September 1966 he defended an MD thesis titled “Resection of the esophagus for treating esophageal cancer”. He later worked as a doctor in various hospitals in Saint Petersburg.

1962年10月，罗戈佐夫回到列宁格勒，开始在他的母校攻读MD学位。1966年9月，他为一篇题为“切除食管治疗食管癌”的MD论文辩护。后来，他在圣彼得堡的多家医院担任医生。

From 1986 to 2000 he served as the head of the surgery department of Saint Petersburg Research Institute for Tubercular Pulmonology. Rogozov died in 2000, aged 66, in Saint Petersburg, Russia, from lung cancer.

1986年至2000年，他担任圣彼得堡肺结核病研究所外科主任，罗戈佐夫于2000年因肺癌在俄罗斯圣彼得堡去世，享年66岁。
