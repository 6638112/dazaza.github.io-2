Blender-OSM Add-on: Current state and ongoing development
Blender-OSM加载项：当前状态和正在进行的开发

2021-06-10 21:24:28

In this monthly meetup the keynote presentation will be given by the developer of the Blender-OSM Add-on, Vladimir Elistratov ( @prochitecture ), on its current state and ongoing development. The ultimate goal of the add-on is to generate an environment in Blender out of OpenStreetMap data as realistically as possible. A part of the talk will be dedicated to the textual description of the building style.

在此月份聚会中，主题演示文稿将由Blender-OSM加载项，vladimir Elistratov（@ProChitecture）的开发人员在其当前状态和正在进行的开发中给出。附加组件的最终目标是尽可能实际地在OpenStreetMap数据中生成搅拌机的环境。谈判的一部分将致力于建筑风格的文本描述。
