苹果新款M1 Mac不能与外部GPU配合使用

2020-11-11 09:01:58

Today Apple showed off the first Macs powered by its new M1 CPU, with a new Mac mini, MacBook Air and 13-inch MacBook Pro. It touted impressive performance marks for the new machines and promised excellent battery life, however they won’t come without any compromises. As Paul Gerhardt mentioned in a tweet, tech spec pages for the new machines reveal that none of them are compatible with external GPUs that connect via Thunderbolt. According to Apple Insider, it has confirmed none of the machines will support eGPUs.

今天，苹果公司展示了首批搭载其新的M1处理器的Mac电脑，配备了新的Mac mini、MacBook Air和13英寸的MacBook Pro。它吹捧新机器令人印象深刻的性能标志，并承诺卓越的电池续航能力，然而，它们不会在没有任何妥协的情况下出现。正如Paul Gerhardt在推特上提到的那样，新机器的技术规格页面显示，它们中没有一个与通过迅雷连接的外部GPU兼容。据Apple Insider透露，该公司已确认所有机器都不会支持eGPU。

Only some people would require add-on oomph in any case, but Apple’s support for external graphics cards gave it some extra gaming cachet and informed creative professionals their needs would continue to be met. Now, they’ll have to wait and see if things change for higher-end models as Apple Silicon spreads throughout the company’s PC lineup.

在任何情况下，只有一些人需要外置显卡，但苹果对外部显卡的支持给它带来了一些额外的游戏声望，并告诉创意专业人士，他们的需求将继续得到满足。现在，他们将不得不拭目以待，看看高端机型的情况是否会发生变化，因为苹果硅片已经遍布整个公司的个人电脑产品阵容。

There’s also been some focus on the fact that the 13-inch MacBook Pro M1 models only include two USB-C ports onboard instead of four, but whether or not you think that’s enough ports, it’s consistent with the cheaper Intel models it replaces. A more striking limitation is the one we’ve already noted, that the MBP is limited to 16GB of RAM — if you think you’ll need 32GB then you’ll have to opt for an Intel-powered model.

13英寸的MacBook Pro M1机型只有两个USB-C接口，而不是四个，这一事实也引起了一些关注，但无论你是否认为这些端口足够，这与它所取代的更便宜的英特尔机型是一致的。一个更引人注目的限制是我们已经注意到的，MBP的内存被限制在16 GB--如果你认为你需要32 GB，那么你将不得不选择英特尔驱动的机型。
