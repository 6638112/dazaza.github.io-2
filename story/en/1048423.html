The Land Rover Defender—rugged, charming, but drinks like a fish
路虎卫士（Land Rover Defender）-坚固，迷人，但喝起来像鱼

2021-02-19 07:13:03

I wasn't expecting to be quite as charmed as I was by the Land Rover Defender. We first saw the new Defender at 2019's Frankfurt auto show, where it proved to be a hit by marrying rugged looks and off-road skills with a healthy helping of tech. Even though I fell for its looks, I was trepidatious about spending a week with the vehicle, having failed to gel with most Land Rovers in the past. As I found out, those fears were mostly misplaced, as the Defender was not at all agricultural in behavior, unlike its 20th-century forerunners.

我没想到自己会像陆虎卫士一样魅力无限。我们首先在2019年的法兰克福车展上看到了这款新的Defender，在坚固的外观和越野技能与健康的技术帮助之间取得了巨大的成功。即使我爱上了它的外观，我还是对在车上呆了一个星期感到恐惧，过去我没有与大多数Land Rovers融为一体。正如我所发现的那样，这些恐惧大多被放错了位置，因为与20世纪的先驱者不同，捍卫者的行为根本不属于农业。

But before we go any further, an apology of sorts—or at least an explanation. Last September, Land Rover held a media first-drive event for the SUV, where journalists got to spend a couple days driving up and down Mount Equinox in the Taconics. Alas, Ars couldn't attend because of scheduling conflicts—instead, we used that time to test a couple of electric vehicles. So although the Defender is built to go off-road, the best I managed during my week with it was some radical parking, as seen in the gallery above.

但是，在我们进行进一步讨论之前，先进行道歉，或者至少是一种解释。去年9月，陆虎（Land Rover）为SUV举办了媒体首次试驾活动，记者不得不花几天时间在Taconics上上下骑Equinox。 scheduling，由于日程安排冲突，Ars无法参加–相反，我们用这段时间测试了几辆电动汽车。因此，尽管“后卫”是专为越野而设计的，但正如我在上一个画廊中所看到的那样，我一周内最好的停车方式是停车。

Therefore, I don't know how well it fords water up to 35.4 inches (900mm) deep. I don't know how well it approaches breaks-over or departs from obstacles (at angles of 38˚, 28˚, and 40˚, respectively). I can't really tell you how good the permanent four-wheel-drive system, with a twin-speed transfer case and optional locking center and rear differentials, works on rough ground. I can't opine on how well the various software systems—Terrain Response 2, All Terrain Progress Control, Hill Descent Control—manage the task of keeping you right-side up and moving in the intended direction. Which makes this review somewhat lacking, given that stuff is basically the Defender's raison d'être. Mea culpa.

因此，我不知道它能淹没深度达35.4英寸（900毫米）的水有多好。我不知道它接近障碍物或偏离障碍物的角度（分别以38°，28°和40°的角度）。我真的无法告诉您带有双速分动箱和可选的锁止中心和后差速器的永久四轮驱动系统在崎ground不平的地面上的性能如何。我无法确定各种软件系统（地形响应2，全地形进度控制，山坡下降控制）的性能如何，使您保持正确的姿势并朝预期的方向移动。考虑到主要是Defender的存在理由，这使得本次评测有些缺乏。 Mea culpa。

But enough about what I can't tell you—let's talk about the stuff I can. Our test Land Rover was the range-topping Defender 110 X. At $83,000, this particular Defender is no steel wheel'd farm vehicle or aid-worker transport—those are available, with the cheapest Defender 90 starting at $46,100. (Expensive though it undoubtedly is, this Defender 110 X is still $9,000 cheaper than the cheapest Range Rover.)

但是，关于我不能告诉您的信息已经足够了，让我们来谈一谈我能做到的事情。我们测试的Land Rover是最顶级的Defender 110X。这款售价为$ 83,000的Defender没有钢制轮式农用车或救援人员运输工具，最便宜的Defender 90售价为$ 46,100。 （尽管无疑是昂贵的，但这款Defender 110 X仍比最便宜的揽胜便宜9,000美元。）

Unlike earlier Land Rovers, the new Defender eschews body-on-frame construction for an aluminum monocoque chassis, which JLR says is three times stiffer than the old-fashioned approach. This design is related to the platforms used by the Jaguar I-Pace (called D7e)—as well as the Range Rover, Range Rover Sport, and Discovery (D7u)—but is its own thing (called D7x) that shares no body panels with those other vehicles.

与早期的Land Rovers不同，新的Defender避开了铝制单体壳底盘的车架结构，JLR称其刚度是老式方法的三倍。此设计与Jaguar I-Pace（称为D7e）以及Range Rover，Range Rover Sport和Discovery（D7u）所使用的平台有关，但它是自己的东西（称为D7x），不共享车身面板与其他车辆。

In the old days, a Defender 110 meant it had a 110-inch (2,794mm) wheelbase; now it just signifies that you're in the bigger of the two Defender bodies. The 21st-century Defender 110 actually has a wheelbase of 119 inches (3,022mm), although the short front and rear overhangs help disguise some of its 198-inch (5,018mm) length. (That's with the rear tire included; take it off and the length is 187 inches/4,758mm.) The vehicle is also fairly wide at 79 inches (2,008mm) and rather tall at 77 inches (1,967mm). Given its size, the 110 is no featherweight, but at 5,035lbs (2,284kg), it's a little lighter than I was expecting.

过去，Defender 110意味着它的轴距为110英寸（2,794毫米）。现在，它只是表明您在两个Defender机构中的较大者。 21世纪的Defender 110的轴距实际上为119英寸（3022毫米），尽管前后悬垂较短，有助于掩饰其198英寸（5018毫米）的某些长度。 （包括后轮胎在内；将其取下，长度为187英寸/ 4,758毫米。）车辆的宽度也相当宽，为79英寸（2,008毫米），而高度则为77英寸（1,967毫米）。考虑到它的大小，它110并不轻巧，但重量为5,035磅（2,284公斤），比我预期的要轻一些。

Under the hood—tastefully accessorized with some patches of plastic diamond plate—is a 3.0L turbocharged straight-six engine, which produces 395hp (295kW) and 406lb-ft (550Nm). Per Jaguar Land Rover's 2017 pledge to electrify its entire lineup, the 110 is also a 48V "mild" hybrid; Land Rover has added a plug-in hybrid option to the Defender range, but there are no plans to bring it to the United States. Oh well.

引擎盖下是3.0L涡轮增压直六缸发动机，装饰有一些塑料菱形板，装饰高雅，可产生395hp（295kW）和406lb-ft（550Nm）。根据捷豹路虎2017年的承诺，将其整个产品系列电气化，110也是48V轻度的;杂交种;路虎在Defender系列中增加了一个插电式混合动力选件，但没有计划将其引入美国。那好吧。

There's only a single choice of transmission, but it's ZF's excellent 8HP eight-speed automatic. And as already mentioned, there's a two-speed transfer case and a bunch of other clever-sounding off-road gubbins, both electronic and mechanical.

变速箱只有一个选择，但是ZF的出色的8HP 8速自动变速器。正如已经提到的，这里有一个两速分动箱和一堆其他听起来不错的非公路用电子齿轮箱和机械齿轮箱。

The chunky utilitarian theme—Land Rover calls it "constructivist"—continues on the inside. A powder-coated magnesium crossbar forms part of the structure of the dash as well as providing grab handles to make the vehicle easier to get into (as well as providing something to hold on to during strenuous off-roading). Trim pieces on the doors and elsewhere have exposed hex bolts, and the mix of materials is interesting both to look at and touch. I adored it, but my wife was less impressed; she also found the cabin a little hard to climb up into, despite the grab handle.

厚重的功利主义主题（陆虎称其为“建构主义者”）在内部继续存在。涂有粉末涂层的镁制横杆构成了仪表板结构的一部分，并提供了把手，使车辆更易于进入（以及在剧烈的越野行驶过程中提供了保持力）。门和其他地方的装饰件都露出了六角螺栓，而且材料的混合在外观和触感上都很有趣。我崇拜它，但我的妻子印象深刻。她也发现尽管有把手，但船舱还是有些难以爬上。

As befits a 21st-century Land Rover, there's a decent array of tech onboard. The Pivi Pro infotainment system has a 10-inch screen mounted into that magnesium crossrail, and while there was a little input lag at times, it never did anything to really annoy me. Apple CarPlay and Android Auto are both present and correct, and a multitude of USB ports can be found in the front and back.

为适应21世纪的路虎，车上还配备了许多不错的技术。 Pivi Pro信息娱乐系统的镁制横梁上装有10英寸的屏幕，尽管有时输入延迟很小，但它从没有做过任何让我烦恼的事情。 Apple CarPlay和Android Auto都存在且正确，并且前后都有多个USB端口。

My most pleasant surprise came from the Defender's on-road manners. I was expecting plenty of body roll and generally ponderous handling, but in fact it rode well. The 110 insulated the occupants from bumps and jolts, and its steering was direct. Sitting so high up provides an excellent view of the road, and the onboard cameras make the Defender easy to park.

我最令人惊喜的是来自后卫的公路举止。我原本希望身体滚动很多，而且操作起来很麻烦，但实际上骑起来很好。 110可以使乘员免受颠簸和颠簸的影响，其转向是直接的。如此高的坐姿可提供绝佳的道路视野，并且内置摄像头使Defender易于停车。

So far, this all sounds pretty great, but the 110 is not all gravy, unfortunately. Officially, the EPA rates the Defender 110 X at 19mpg combined, 17mpg in the city, and 22mpg on the highway. Yet, I never averaged more than about 13mpg, which is unacceptable. The early January weather was almost certainly a factor, as the cold was often too much for the engine's stop-start function to operate, and the optional off-road tires couldn't have helped. Whatever the cause, the resulting lack of efficiency is still hard to stomach.

到目前为止，这一切听起来都不错，但是不幸的是110并非全部都是肉汁。根据官方的说法，EPA对Defender 110 X的总和为19mpg，城市为17mpg，高速公路为22mpg。但是，我从未平均超过13mpg，这是不可接受的。 1月初的天气几乎可以肯定是一个因素，因为寒冷常常使发动机的启停功能无法正常工作，而选装的越野轮胎也无济于事。无论是什么原因，导致的效率不足仍然令人难以忍受。
