Xbox cloud gaming service hits iOS, Windows PCs in spring 2021
Xbox云游戏服务将于2021年春季登陆iOS和Windows PC

2020-12-10 02:57:48

In a blog post today outlining everything from upcoming games to plans for Xbox Series X/S, Microsoft announced that Xbox cloud gaming will come to iOS mobile devices and Windows PCs in spring of 2021.

微软在今天的博客文章中概述了从即将推出的游戏到Xbox Series X / S计划的所有内容，微软宣布Xbox云游戏将在2021年春季登陆iOS移动设备和Windows PC。

On Windows PCs, the games will stream through the Xbox app or a Web browser, whereas the service will be limited to the mobile Web browser on iOS devices.

在Windows PC上，游戏将通过Xbox应用程序或Web浏览器流式传输，而该服务将仅限于iOS设备上的移动Web浏览器。

Microsoft's game-streaming features require an Xbox Game Pass Ultimate subscription, which also includes an on-demand library of downloadable games for both Xbox platforms and Windows PCs, the EA Play downloadable game library, and Xbox Live Gold, Microsoft's online multiplayer service. Xbox Game Pass Ultimate costs $14.95 per month in the US, but Microsoft is currently offering a discounted three-month trial at $1 for new users.

微软的游戏流功能需要Xbox Game Pass Ultimate订阅，该订阅还包括一个Xbox平台和Windows PC的按需下载游戏的库，EA Play可下载游戏库以及Xbox Live Gold，Microsoft 39; s的在线多人游戏服务。 Xbox Game Pass Ultimate在美国的价格为每月14.95美元，但微软目前为新用户提供1美元的3个月折扣试用期。

Additionally, Microsoft says that both cloud gaming and Xbox Game Pass Ultimate will come to new markets in the spring, including Australia, Brazil, Japan, and Mexico.

此外，微软表示云游戏和Xbox Game Pass Ultimate将在春季进入新市场，包括澳大利亚，巴西，日本和墨西哥。

Xbox game streaming has been available on Android devices for a few months, and it was expected to arrive on Windows PCs eventually. But its presence on iOS was far from certain.

Xbox游戏流式传输已在Android设备上使用了几个月，并且有望最终在Windows PC上实现。但是它在iOS上的存在还远远不能确定。

Apple's App Store guidelines at first prohibited any kind of game-streaming service, with the argument that curation for quality and non-obscenity are important principles of how the App Store is run. Apple argued that the inability for its internal reviewers to assess individual games on a wide-ranging streaming service precluded the option to offer services like Microsoft's, or Google's competing Stadia service, on the App Store.

苹果公司的App Store指南最初禁止使用任何类型的游戏流媒体服务，理由是对质量和非淫秽行为的策划是App Store运行方式的重要原则。苹果公司辩称，其内部审核员无法评估各种流媒体服务上的单个游戏，因此无法在App Store上提供诸如Microsoft或Google的竞争对手Stadia服务之类的服务。

Microsoft and others pointed out that Apple seems to treat games differently from other forms of media; Cupertino has not balked at the presence of TV shows, books, music, magazines, or other media in similar subscription-based apps on its App Store. It has only applied these restrictions to games.

微软和其他公司指出，苹果公司对待游戏的态度似乎与其他形式的媒体有所不同。库比蒂诺在其App Store上基于订阅的类似应用程序中并未出现电视节目，书籍，音乐，杂志或其他媒体的出现。它仅将这些限制应用于游戏。

After a spirited PR battle and debates in the press, Apple slightly loosened the restrictions, saying that game subscription services like Xbox cloud streaming or Stadia could live on the iOS App Store provided each game offered in such a service is listed as an individual app on the App Store, and thus is subject to Apple's review process. Google's Stadia announced plans to forgo that hassle entirely and instead offer its games through a browser-based experience, and now (as previously rumored) Microsoft has taken the same path.

经过激烈的公关斗争和新闻界的辩论后，苹果略微放松了限制，称诸如Xbox云流或Stadia之类的游戏订阅服务可以在iOS App Store上使用，前提是该服务中提供的每个游戏都列为单独的应用。 App Store，因此需要经过Apple的审查程序。 Google的Stadia宣布了完全放弃这一麻烦的计划，而是通过基于浏览器的体验来提供其游戏，而现在（如先前所传闻），微软已经走了同样的道路。
