Countries build 9 mile wide tree wall across Africa to prevent Sahara advancing
各国在非洲各地修建9英里宽的树墙，以防止撒哈拉沙漠前进

2020-12-19 18:50:26

12 African nations have come together pledging to build a 9 mile wide band of trees that will stretch all the way across Africa, 4750 miles, in order to stop the progressive advancement of the Sahara.

12个非洲国家共同承诺建立9英里宽的树木带，这些树木将遍及非洲4750英里，以阻止撒哈拉沙漠的逐步发展。

Senegal is leading a 12 African nations to build a living defense system appropriately named the Great Green Wall of Africa.

塞内加尔正在领导一个12个非洲国家建立一个活生生的防御系统，适当地称为非洲绿色长城。

The Sahara is currently the 2nd largest desert in size, only smaller than Antarctica. However, unlike its frozen relative, the Sahara is actually expanding.

撒哈拉沙漠目前是第二大沙漠，仅比南极洲小。但是，撒哈拉沙漠与其冻结的亲戚不同，它实际上正在扩张。

The United Nations estimates that, by 2025, 60 % of Africa’s arable land will be covered in Saharan sand, significantly expanding the current 9 million square km’s. Even if these estimations prove aggressive, the effects of farmland exploitation on a region already hard-pressed for food would be devastating on any level. With this danger in sight, the leaders of Senegal, Mauritania, Mali, Burkina Faso, Niger, Nigeria, Chad, Sudan, Eritrea, Ethiopia, and Djibouti have banded together on an remarkable effort to prevent approaching disaster.

联合国估计，到2025年，非洲60％的耕​​地将被撒哈拉沙漠覆盖，这将大大扩大目前的900万平方公里的土地。即使这些估计证明是激进的，但对已经难以获得食物的地区来说，农田开发的影响在任何程度上都是毁灭性的。眼见着这种危险，塞内加尔，毛里塔尼亚，马里，布基纳法索，尼日尔，尼日利亚，乍得，苏丹，厄立特里亚，埃塞俄比亚和吉布提的领导人齐心协力，为防止即将来临的灾难作出了巨大努力。

Once complete, Africa’s Green Wall will be a manmade forest of drought-resistant trees (principally acacia) extending across the entire region. 9 miles wide and 4,750 miles long, the vision for the project is as committed as it is necessary. Thus far, only 330 miles of greenery stands guard in Northern Senegal, and has cost the Sengalese government over $6 million since the start of digging in 2008. International organizations have pledged over $3 billion to the enormous defense system.

一旦建成，非洲的绿墙将是覆盖整个地区的人工抗旱树木（主要是金合欢）森林。宽9英里，长4750英里，因此该项目的愿景已达到必要的承诺。到目前为止，塞内加尔北部仅守卫着330英里的绿地，自2008年开始挖掘以来，已使塞纳河政府损失了600万美元。国际组织已为庞大的国防系统认捐了30亿美元。

Leaders point out that the Great Green Wall is about more than just defense from windblown sand. The project will bring thousands of jobs to poor communities, and has already transformed usually useless land into gardens scattered with tree nurseries.

领导人指出，绿色长城不仅仅是防风沙。该项目将为贫困社区带来成千上万的就业机会，并且已经将通常无用的土地变成散布有苗圃的花园。

The increase of tourists, scientists, and medical professionals has also brought attention and resources to a neglected region in which aid is scarce and doctors are not readily available to needy people.

游客，科学家和医务人员的增加也使人们对一个被忽视的地区的关注和资源增加了，那里的援助稀少，有需要的人也不容易获得医生。
