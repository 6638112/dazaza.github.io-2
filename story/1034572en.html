拜登过渡团队中科技高管多于科技批评者

2020-11-12 00:50:19

WASHINGTON (Reuters) - Far more executives from technology companies than outspoken tech critics were named to U.S. President-elect Joe Biden’s transition team on Tuesday, offering clues on who will decide on filling key roles and ultimately influence his administration’s thinking in coming years.

华盛顿(路透社)-周二，被任命为美国当选总统乔·拜登(Joe Biden)过渡团队成员的科技公司高管远远多于直言不讳的科技批评者，这为谁将决定填补关键职位并最终影响他的政府在未来几年的想法提供了线索。

Tech companies have been trying to strengthen their relationship with a future Biden administration to ensure they have a voice in an onslaught of federal and state investigations of their business practices.

科技公司一直在努力加强与未来拜登(Biden)政府的关系，以确保它们在联邦和州政府对其商业行为的猛烈调查中拥有发言权。

Amazon.com Inc's AMZN.O Tom Sullivan, an executive on the public policy team, will be part of Biden's review team overseeing the Department of State. Similarly, Mark Schwartz from Amazon's cloud computing arm, who is also a former Obama administration official, will be weighing in on decisions for the Office of Management and Budget.

亚马逊公司(Amazon.com Inc.)的AMZN.O汤姆·沙利文(Tom Sullivan)是公共政策团队的一名高管，他将成为拜登负责监督国务院的审查小组的成员。同样，亚马逊云计算部门的马克·施瓦茨(Mark Schwartz)也是前奥巴马政府官员，他将参与管理与预算办公室(Office Of Management And Budget)的决策。

Microsoft-owned MSFT.O LinkedIn's senior director for North America policy, Nicole Isaac, is part of the team overseeing the Department of Treasury.

微软旗下的微软领英(MSFT.O LinkedIn)北美政策高级总监妮可·艾萨克(Nicole Isaac)是负责监督财政部的团队成员。

Nicole Wong, a former deputy chief technology officer under the administration of Democratic President Barack Obama and a vice president and general counsel for Alphabet-owned Google GOOGL.O found a spot in the review team for the National Security Council.

妮可·王(Nicole Wong)曾在民主党总统巴拉克·奥巴马(Barack Obama)政府担任副首席技术官，现在是Alphabet旗下谷歌GOOGL.O的副总裁兼总法律顾问，她在国家安全委员会(National Security Council)的审查小组中找到了一席之地。

By contrast, tech critics such as Gene Kimmelman, senior adviser with Washington-based Public Knowledge, which focuses on areas such as antitrust policy, will be on the review team for the Department of Justice and Sarah Miller from the American Economic Liberties Project was chosen for the group weighing in on decisions about the Department of Treasury.

相比之下，总部位于华盛顿、专注于反垄断政策等领域的Public Knowledge高级顾问吉恩·基梅尔曼(Gene Kimmelman)等科技批评人士将加入美国司法部(DoJ)的审查小组，美国经济自由项目(American Economic Liberties Project)的萨拉·米勒(Sarah Miller)将被选为小组成员，就有关财政部的决定发表意见。

Both Kimmelman and Miller have pushed for higher antitrust scrutiny of Big Tech’s business practices.

金梅尔曼和米勒都在推动对大型科技公司的商业行为进行更严格的反垄断审查。

Executives from relatively smaller tech companies such as AirBnB, Uber UBER.N , Lyft LYFT.O , and Stripe were also appointed into these agency review groups.

Airbnb、优步(Uber UBER.N)、Lyft LYFT.O和Strip等规模相对较小的科技公司的高管也被任命为这些机构审查小组的成员。

Amazon declined comment. The other companies did not immediately respond to a request for comment.

亚马逊拒绝置评。其他公司没有立即回复记者的置评请求。

A Biden transition team spokesman said agency review teams are responsible for evaluating the operations of the federal agencies and making sure the Biden-Harris administration can achieve policy goals discussed during the campaign.

拜登过渡团队发言人说，机构审查小组负责评估联邦机构的运作，并确保拜登-哈里斯政府能够实现竞选期间讨论的政策目标。

There will also be a team reviewing the Federal Trade Commission, which includes Bill Baer, a former director of the FTC’s Bureau of Competition and ex-head of the Justice Department’s Antitrust Division, who has recently called for tougher antitrust law.

还将有一个审查联邦贸易委员会(Federal Trade Commission)的小组，其中包括联邦贸易委员会(FTC)竞争局前局长、司法部反垄断部门前负责人比尔·贝尔(Bill Baer)，他最近呼吁制定更严格的反垄断法。

Baer’s DOJ sued to stop two insurance company mergers on the same day in 2016.

2016年，贝尔的美国司法部在同一天提起诉讼，要求阻止两家保险公司的合并。

The FTC team also includes Laura Moy, who teaches at Georgetown Law and is an expert on consumer privacy, data security and net neutrality. The lead in the team is Heather Hippsley, a three-decade veteran of the agency.

联邦贸易委员会的团队还包括劳拉·莫伊(Laura Moy)，她在乔治敦大学法学院任教，是消费者隐私、数据安全和网络中立方面的专家。团队的领头人是在该机构工作了30年的希瑟·希普斯利(Heather Hippsley)。
