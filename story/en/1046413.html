Robinhood, the fee-free investment app that has helped Redditors and other retail investors pump dark horse stocks like GameStop, AMC, BlackBerry, and Nokia, has stopped allowing users to buy those stocks and others YOLO picks.

Robinhood是一款免费的投资应用程序，已帮助Redditors和其他散户投资者抽出GameStop，AMC，BlackBerry和Nokia等黑马股票，该公司已停止允许用户购买这些股票和其他YOLO精选股票。

According to screenshots shared on social media, on Thursday morning a notification appeared on Robinhood telling users that they could close their position on GameStop's stock, but not buy any additional shares. Redditors are currently panicking, looking for ways to transfer their shares of GameStop off of Robinhood to other platforms, and are generally furious at the platform. In a blog post, Robinhood confirmed that it has placed restrictions on several stocks due to volatility. “We continuously monitor the markets and make changes where necessary. In light of recent volatility, we are restricting transactions for certain securities to position closing only, including $AMC, $BB, $BBBY, $EXPR, $GME, $KOSS, $NAKD and $NOK. We also raised margin requirements for certain securities,” the blog post read. Robinhood did not respond to a request for comment.

根据社交媒体上分享的屏幕截图，在周四上午，Robinhood上出现一条通知，告知用户他们可以平仓GameStop股票，但不能购买任何其他股票。 Redditors目前感到恐慌，正在寻找将他们的GameStop股份从Robinhood转移到其他平台的方式，并且通常对该平台感到愤怒。罗宾汉（Robinhood）在博客中证实，由于波动性，它对数只股票设置了限制。 “我们不断监控市场，并在必要时进行更改。鉴于最近的波动，我们将某些证券的交易限制为仅平仓，包括$ AMC，$ BB，$ BBBY，$ EXPR，$ GME，$ KOSS，$ NAKD和$ NOK。我们还提高了某些证券的保证金要求。” Robinhood没有回复置评请求。

This is likely to have a massive impact on Robinhood users and ultimately the company. According to a popup on the app's homepage, 56 percent of all Robinhood users own at least some GameStop stock. They are now unable to freely trade it; the app is only allowing users to close out their positions, meaning they can sell it but not buy more. This is potentially devastating for novice investors or those who simply want to follow the general marching orders of the r/WallStreetBets subreddit, which is to hold (and buy more) GameStop stock until further notice.

这可能会对Robinhood用户以及最终对公司产生巨大影响。根据该应用程序首页上的弹出窗口，所有Robinhood用户中有56％至少拥有一些GameStop股票。他们现在无法自由交易。该应用仅允许用户平仓，这意味着他们可以出售但不能购买更多。对于新手投资者或仅想遵循r / WallStreetBets subreddit的一般行情指令的投资者而言，这可能是灾难性的，后者将持有（并购买更多）GameStop股票，直至另行通知。

Over the past week, the stock market has gone haywire as retail investors on the /r/WallStreetBets subreddit decided to buy the stock of the video game retailer GameStop en masse. The idea was that GameStop's stock was extremely undervalued, and that hedge funds had overextended themselves by betting against it, or "shorting" it. Now that the meme has caught on and been encouraged by Elon Musk (who famously despises short sellers and whose company Tesla is another retail investor darling) GameStop's stock is worth roughly $350, up from $4 in December. BlackBerry, AMC, and Nokia, other WallStreetBets favorites, are also up.

在过去一周中，/ r / WallStreetBets subreddit上的散户投资者决定大规模购买视频游戏零售商GameStop的股票，股市一团糟。当时的想法是，GameStop的股票被严重低估，对冲基金通过押注或“卖空”来过度扩张自己。它。现在，模因流行起来并受到埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）的鼓舞（埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）着迷于卖空者，特斯拉是另一位零售投资者的宠儿），GameStop的股价从12月份的4美元升至350美元左右。黑莓（BlackBerry），AMC和诺基亚（Nokia）等WallStreetBets热门产品也出现了。

The result has been chaos for the funds that shorted GameStop, with Melvin Capital needing to call in a multi-billion dollar backstop investment to cover its losses. In response, several large trading platforms temporarily halted trading on the affected stocks. On Wednesday, the New York Stock Exchange halted trading of GameStop and AMC, and Canada's trading regulator halted BlackBerry. TD Ameritrade restricted users from trading GameStop, AMC, and other stocks, but was not specific about the timeline.

结果导致做空GameStop的资金陷入混乱，梅尔文资本（Melvin Capital）需要召集数十亿美元的支持投资来弥补其亏损。作为响应，几个大型交易平台暂时停止了受影响股票的交易。周三，纽约证券交易所停止了GameStop和AMC的交易，加拿大的交易监管机构也停止了黑莓手机。 TD Ameritrade限制用户交易GameStop，AMC和其他股票，但并未具体说明时间表。

A trading halt is usually implemented to protect investors from volatility or manipulation, usually lasts just a few minutes, and is relatively common. In its announcement on BlackBerry, the Canadian regulator noted that halts are enacted "to ensure a fair and orderly market." TD Ameritrade, meanwhile, says it restricted trading "out of an abundance of caution amid unprecedented market conditions."

为了保护投资者免受波动或操纵的影响，通常会暂停交易，通常只持续几分钟，这是相对常见的。加拿大监管机构在黑莓手机上的公告中指出，暂停交易是为了确保市场公平有序。同时，TD Ameritrade表示，在史无前例的市场环境下，出于谨慎考虑，限制了交易。

To many retail investors, these justifications seemed like large platforms running cover for institutional investors being buffeted by the chaos. Chaos, notably, that large institutions often foment and exploit for profit themselves. Indeed, establishment-types are very, very mad at retail investors right now. On Wednesday, Secretary of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts William Galvin even suggested that platforms should halt trading for an entire month for the affected stocks.

对于许多散户投资者而言，这些理由似乎是大型平台掩盖了机构投资者被混乱所困扰的情况。尤其是，混乱的是，大型机构经常煽动和利用自身牟利。的确，目前，机构类型对散户投资者非常非常生气。周三，马萨诸塞州联邦国务卿威廉·加尔文（William Galvin）甚至建议平台应暂停受影响股票的整整一个月的交易。

With trading platforms, regulators, and politicians all in the mix now, it seems like the GameStop saga is still far from over.

现在有了交易平台，监管机构和政界人士的混战，GameStop传奇似乎还远远没有结束。
