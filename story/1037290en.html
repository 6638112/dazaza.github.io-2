Salesforce's deal to buy Slack is expected to be announced Tuesday after markets close, sources told CNBC's David Faber.

消息人士告诉CNBC的David Faber，预计Salesforce收购Slack的交易将于周二市场关闭后宣布。

The deal is expected to be about half cash and half stock, the sources said, and will price Slack at a premium to its current price.

消息人士称，预计这笔交易将以现金加一半股票进行，并以高于其当前价格的价格对Slack进行定价。

消息人士告诉CNBC的David Faber，预计Salesforce收购Slack的交易将于周二市场关闭后宣布。

The deal is expected to be about half cash and half stock, the sources said, and will price Slack at a premium to its current price. Salesforce is set to report quarterly earnings on Tuesday.

消息人士称，这笔交易预计将有一半现金和一半股票，并将以高于当前价格的价格对Slack定价。 Salesforce将于周二报告季度收益。

Shares of Slack rose about 8% Monday on the news. Salesforce shares fell about 1.5%.

消息传出，Slack的股票周一上涨了约8％。 Salesforce股票下跌约1.5％。

Talks between the two companies were first reported last week by The Wall Street Journal and confirmed by CNBC, sending Slack's stock soaring 38%.

上周《华尔街日报》首先报道了两家公司之间的谈判，并得到了CNBC的证实，这使Slack的股价飙升了38％。

Salesforce has made several large acquisitions in recent years, including its $6.5 billion deal for MuleSoft in 2018 and its $15.3 billion deal with data visualization company Tableau.

Salesforce近年来进行了几项大型收购，包括2018年以65亿美元收购MuleSoft以及以153亿美元收购数据可视化公司Tableau的交易。

The deal with Slack could become one of the largest software deals. IBM bought Red Hat for $34 billion in 2019 and Microsoft bought LinkedIn for $27 billion in 2016.

与Slack的交易可能成为最大的软件交易之一。 IBM在2019年以340亿美元的价格收购了Red Hat，并在2016年以270亿美元的价格收购了LinkedIn。

It could also make Slack a more formidable competitor to Microsoft's Teams communication product. Slack filed a complaint against Microsoft with the European Commission this summer, alleging it abused its dominant market position to eliminate competition for Teams. Microsoft has said Teams has benefited during the pandemic from video-conferencing capabilities that Slack lacks.

这也可能使Slack成为Microsoft Teams通讯产品的更强大竞争对手。 Slack于今年夏天向欧洲委员会提起了针对微软的投诉，指控其滥用市场支配地位来消除Teams的竞争。微软表示，在大流行期间，Teams从Slack缺乏的视频会议功能中受益。
