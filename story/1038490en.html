Pornhub will no longer let users download videos or unverified users upload videos, after a NYT report alleged that it was hosting videos of rape, underage sex
在《纽约时报》报道称该网站托管了强奸，未成年人性行为的视频之后，Pornhub将不再允许用户下载视频或未经验证的用户上传视频

2020-12-09 06:39:43

NEW YORK (AP) — Pornhub said Tuesday it was halting unverified users from uploading video material after a report alleged that the pornographic website was showing videos of rape and underage sex.

纽约（美联社）-Pornhub周二表示，在有报道称该色情网站正在播放强奸和未成年人性行为的视频后，正在暂停未经验证的用户上传视频材料。

Visa and Mastercard said over the weekend that they are investigating their business relationship with Pornhub after Nicholas Kristof, opinion columnist at The New York Times, wrote that the site also carries revenge pornography and other explicit video taken without consent of the participants.

Visa和万事达卡周末表示，他们正在调查与Pornhub的业务关系，此前《纽约时报》的舆论专栏作家尼古拉斯·克里斯托夫（Nicholas Kristof）写道，该网站还载有报仇色情和其他未经参与者同意而拍摄的露骨视频。

Pornhub said that it doesn’t knowingly allow images of sexual abuse of children. But, in a blog post on Tuesday, the company listed steps it was taking to further protect against images of abuse and nonconsensual activity on its site.

Pornhub说，它故意禁止发布对儿童进行性虐待的图片。但是，在周二的博客文章中，该公司列出了正在采取的措施，以进一步防止网站上出现滥用和未经许可的活动的图像。

Pornhub said next year it will announce ways in which individuals can become verified users. It will still allow new material from partners it knows, like porn production companies. The website has a huge volume of material: 6.8 million new videos were uploaded last year. The company could not say Tuesday what percentage of that was from unverified users.

Pornhub表示，明年将宣布个人可以成为经过验证的用户的方式。它将仍然允许来自其认识的合作伙伴的新资料，例如色情制作公司。该网站内容丰富：去年上传了680万个新视频。该公司周二无法透露来自未验证用户的百分比。

Pornhub said it has hired a law firm to conduct an independent review of its content, has moderators that examine each video that is posted to the site and works with 40 organizations with a focus on child safety to monitor what it posts.

Pornhub说，它已经聘请了一家律师事务所对其内容进行独立审查，并拥有主持人来检查发布到该网站的每个视频，并与40个致力于儿童安全的组织合作，以监控其发布的内容。

The company said that “every online platform has the moral responsibility” to join in the fight against depictions of child sexual abuse or non-consensual activity.

该公司表示，“每个在线平台都有道义上的责任”，以参与描绘儿童性虐待或非自愿活动的斗争。

The online payment service PayPal last year stopped processing payments to Pornhub, which is owned by the pornography conglomerate MindGeek. In his column, Kristof criticized other card issuers for working with the site.

在线支付服务PayPal去年停止了对Pornhub的付款处理，该公司由色情集团MindGeek拥有。在他的专栏中，克里斯托夫（Kristof）批评其他发卡机构与该网站合作。

Pornhub said it had more than 42 billion visits to its website last year, an average of 115 million a day.

Pornhub表示，去年对其网站的访问超过420亿次，平均每天访问量为1.15亿次。
