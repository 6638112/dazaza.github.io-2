Aluminum-gallium composite for clean efficient hydrogen fuel
清洁高效氢燃料用铝镓复合材料

2022-02-21 01:22:45

Aluminum is a highly reactive metal that can strip oxygen from water molecules to generate hydrogen gas. Its widespread use in products that get wet poses no danger because aluminum instantly reacts with air to acquire a coating of aluminum oxide, which blocks further reactions. For years, researchers have tried to find efficient and cost-effective ways to use 's reactivity to generate clean fuel. A new study by researchers at UC Santa Cruz shows that an easily produced composite of gallium and aluminum creates aluminum nanoparticles that react rapidly with water at room temperature to yield large amounts of hydrogen. The gallium was easily recovered for reuse after the reaction, which yields 90% of the hydrogen that could theoretically be produced from reaction of all the aluminum in the composite.

铝是一种高活性金属，可以从水分子中去除氧气，生成氢气。它在潮湿产品中的广泛使用不会造成危险，因为铝会立即与空气发生反应，形成氧化铝涂层，从而阻止进一步的反应。多年来，研究人员一直试图找到高效、经济的方法来使用'；它的反应性可以产生清洁的燃料。加州大学圣克鲁斯分校（UC Santa Cruz）研究人员的一项新研究表明，一种容易制备的镓和铝的复合材料可以生成铝纳米颗粒，在室温下与水快速反应，生成大量氢气。镓在反应后很容易回收再利用，理论上可以从复合材料中所有铝的反应中产生90%的氢气。

"We don't need any energy input, and it bubbles hydrogen like crazy. I've never seen anything like it," said UCSC Chemistry Professor Scott Oliver.

"我们没有'；它不需要任何能量输入，而且会疯狂地使氢产生气泡。我'；我从来没见过这样的事，"；加州大学化学系教授斯科特·奥利弗说。

Oliver and Bakthan Singaram, professor of chemistry and biochemistry, are corresponding authors of a paper on the new findings, published February 14 in Applied Nano Materials.

Oliver和化学与生物化学教授Baktan Singaram是一篇关于新发现的论文的通讯作者，该论文发表在2月14日的《应用纳米材料》上。

The reaction of aluminum and gallium with water has been known since the 1970s, and videos of it are easy to find online. It works because gallium, a liquid at just above room temperature, removes the passive aluminum oxide coating, allowing direct contact of aluminum with water. The new study, however, includes several innovations and novel findings that could lead to practical applications.

铝和镓与水的反应早在20世纪70年代就已为人所知，网上很容易找到相关视频。它之所以有效，是因为镓是一种略高于室温的液体，可以去除钝化氧化铝涂层，使铝与水直接接触。然而，这项新研究包含了一些创新和新发现，这些创新和新发现可能会带来实际应用。

Singaram said the study grew out of a conversation he had with a student, coauthor Isai Lopez, who had seen some videos and started experimenting with aluminum-gallium hydrogen generation in his home kitchen.

Singaram说，这项研究源于他与一名学生、合著者Isai Lopez的一次对话，后者曾看过一些视频，并开始在家中的厨房里试验铝镓氢发电。

"He wasn't doing it in a scientific way, so I set him up with a graduate student to do a systematic study. I thought it would make a good senior thesis for him to measure the hydrogen output from different ratios of gallium and aluminum," Singaram said.

"他没有39岁；我没有用科学的方法做这件事，所以我安排他和一名研究生一起做一项系统的研究。我认为，对他来说，测量不同比例的镓和铝的氢气输出将是一篇很好的高级论文；辛格拉姆说。

Previous studies had mostly used aluminum-rich mixtures of aluminum and gallium, or in some cases more complex alloys. But Singaram's lab found that increased with a gallium-rich composite. In fact, the rate of hydrogen production was so unexpectedly high the researchers thought there must be something fundamentally different about this gallium-rich alloy.

之前的研究大多使用铝和镓的富铝混合物，或者在某些情况下使用更复杂的合金。但新加坡'；s的实验室发现，使用富含镓的复合材料时，这一比例会增加。事实上，氢的生成率高得出人意料，以至于研究人员认为这种富镓合金肯定有本质上的不同。

Oliver suggested that the formation of aluminum nanoparticles could account for the increased hydrogen production, and his lab had the equipment needed for nanoscale characterization of the alloy. Using scanning and X-ray diffraction, the researchers showed the formation of aluminum nanoparticles in a 3:1 gallium-aluminum composite, which they found to be the optimal ratio for hydrogen production.

奥利弗提出，铝纳米颗粒的形成可以解释氢气产量的增加，他的实验室拥有对合金进行纳米级表征所需的设备。通过扫描和X射线衍射，研究人员发现在3:1的镓-铝复合材料中形成了铝纳米颗粒，他们发现这是制氢的最佳比例。

In this gallium-rich composite, the gallium serves both to dissolve the aluminum oxide coating and to separate the aluminum into nanoparticles. "The gallium separates the nanoparticles and keeps them from aggregating into larger particles," Singaram said. "People have struggled to make aluminum nanoparticles, and here we are producing them under normal atmospheric pressure and conditions."

在这种富含镓的复合材料中，镓既能溶解氧化铝涂层，又能将铝分离成纳米颗粒"镓分离纳米颗粒，防止它们聚集成更大的颗粒；辛格拉姆说"人们一直在努力制造铝纳米颗粒，现在我们正在正常大气压和条件下生产它们"

"Our method uses a small amount of aluminum, which ensures it all dissolves into the majority gallium as discrete nanoparticles," Oliver said. "This generates a much larger amount of hydrogen, almost complete compared to the theoretical value based on the amount of aluminum. It also makes gallium recovery easier for reuse."

"我们的方法使用了少量的铝，这确保了铝以分散的纳米颗粒形式溶解到大部分镓中；奥利弗说"这会产生更多的氢，与基于铝含量的理论值相比几乎完全。它还使镓回收更容易重复使用"

The composite can be made with readily available sources of aluminum, including used foil or cans, and the composite can be stored for long periods by covering it with cyclohexane to protect it from moisture.

该复合材料可以用现成的铝源制成，包括用过的箔或罐，并且该复合材料可以通过用环己烷覆盖以防止受潮而长期储存。

Although is not abundant and is relatively expensive, it can be recovered and reused multiple times without losing effectiveness, Singaram said. It remains to be seen, however, if this process can be scaled up to be practical for commercial hydrogen production.
