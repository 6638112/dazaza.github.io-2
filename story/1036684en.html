Many jokes have been made about the true nature of Emacs, such as itbeing a fully-fledged operating system. This talk will demonstrateits suitability for playing retro games, then explore the innerworkings of a CHIP-8 emulator capable of smooth video game emulation.

关于Emacs的真实本质，已经有很多笑话，例如它是一个成熟的操作系统。本演讲将展示其适合玩复古游戏，然后探讨能够平滑视频游戏仿真的CHIP-8仿真器的内部工作原理。
