Russia anticipating a major medical event in which doctors have to be drafted
俄罗斯期待着一场必须征召医生的重大医疗活动

2022-02-26 04:24:13

I have been given a copy of document issued today by the Russian Ministry of Health which shows it is anticipating a major medical event in which doctors have to be drafted from across the country.

我收到了俄罗斯卫生部今天发布的一份文件副本，该文件显示，俄罗斯卫生部预计将举行一次重大医疗活动，必须从全国各地征召医生。

It indicates Russia is anticipating a massive medical emergency and has ordered health organisations to immediately identify medical staff ready to relocate and work. The document from the Russian Ministry of Health is signed by the deputy health minister and dated today - February 25.

它表明，俄罗斯预计将发生大规模医疗紧急情况，并已命令卫生组织立即确定准备搬迁和工作的医务人员。

It asks medical organisations to be ready "to be promptly involved in activities aimed at saving lives and preserving the health of people in Russia". Russian medical institutions have been ordered by the Health Ministry to send a list no later than 6pm to the deputy health minister of "medical specialists and medical workers… indicating their full name, place of work, positions & contact details". The document makes it clear these medics will be deployed somewhere and will be offered a "reimbursement of travel and accommodation expenses, as well as payment of labour is expected from funds from the Federal Centre of Medical Disasters".

它要求医疗机构做好准备#34；迅速参与旨在拯救生命和保护俄罗斯人民健康的活动；。

The letter has a long list of medics it is looking for: trauma, heart, maxilofacial and paediatric surgeons, anaesthetists, radiologists, nurses (including for operating rooms) and infectious disease specialists. The letter shows the Russian Ministry of Health is clearly anticipating a major medical event in which doctors have to be drafted from across the country. The letter is signed by Deputy Health Minister Plutnitsky. This order from the Russian Ministry of Health has been issued as Russian forces reportedly advance on Kyiv and other cities in Ukraine.

这封信中列出了一长串需要的医生：创伤、心脏、面部和儿科外科医生、麻醉师、放射科医生、护士（包括手术室）和传染病专家。

I understand doctors were gathered together yesterday and told to ‘get ready.’ Today, the document from the Russian Health Ministry arrived.

我知道医生们昨天聚集在一起，被告知要“做好准备”今天，俄罗斯卫生部的文件送达。

I am told this is highly unusual and almost unprecedented. After viewing the document, tonight a Ukrainian military official told me it could indicate that the medics are being drafted to assist with casualties from Russia’s invasion in Ukraine.

我被告知这是非常不寻常的，几乎是前所未有的。

The military official indicated that one interpretation of the document is that Russia did not expect a level of resistance from Ukraine.

这名军方官员表示，对该文件的一种解释是，俄罗斯没有预料到乌克兰会有一定程度的抵抗。

This would tally with reports from a senior US defence official who said today "there is greater resistance by Ukrainians than the Russians expected," and that Russia is not moving on Kyiv as fast as they anticipated and that Russia may have lost a "little bit of their momentum".

这与一位美国国防部高级官员今天的报告相符"；乌克兰人的抵抗比俄罗斯人预期的要大；而且俄罗斯在基辅的行动没有他们预期的那么快，俄罗斯可能已经失去了"；他们的一点点动力"；。

Tonight I have asked Russian officials if it is true Russia is preparing for a mass casualty event and if it is because Russia has met more resistance than it anticipated in its operation against Ukraine.

今晚，我问俄罗斯官员，俄罗斯是否真的在准备一场大规模伤亡事件，是否因为俄罗斯在对乌克兰的行动中遇到了比预期更多的阻力。
