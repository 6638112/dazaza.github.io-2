Motorola’s 5G-equipped Razr is being sold unlocked, which means you can buy it straight from Amazon. (It’s even on sale for $400 off the usual $1,399.99 price.) But if you do order the foldable flip phone from Amazon, you might have been greeted by an unusual sight when you open the box: someone else might have already unboxed your new phone and folded it for you.

摩托罗拉搭载5G的Razr正在解锁销售，这意味着你可以直接从亚马逊购买。(它甚至以400美元的价格打折，通常售价为1399.99美元。)。但如果你真的从亚马逊订购了这款可折叠翻盖手机，当你打开盒子时，你可能会看到一个不同寻常的景象：可能已经有人打开了你的新手机的盒子，为你折叠了它。

(That deal is also available from Best Buy and B&H, which will presumably not get their grubby mitts all over your new phone before shipping.)

(百思买(Best Buy)和百安居(B&；H)也提供这一优惠，这两家公司大概不会在发货前把肮脏的手套弄得到处都是你的新手机。)。

RAZR was meant to be shipped in the unfolded position. However, in order to better protect the display we have folded your RAZR – it’s safer, but may not look as elegant as we hoped. We apologize if you see fingerprints on your device, but we assure you your RAZR is brand new.

Razr原本是以展开的位置装运的。然而，为了更好地保护显示器，我们折叠了RAZR-它更安全，但可能看起来不像我们希望的那样优雅。如果您在您的设备上看到指纹，我们深表歉意，但我们向您保证，您的RAZR是全新的。

Motorola is packaging the new razr in its closed form at the factory level. Once the device leaves our facilities it is not reopened. Motorola places strict requirements on handling with gloves and sanitation procedures along with shipping protocols aimed at providing a great out of box experience. As with all products, we’ll continue to closely monitor and make additional adjustments as needed to give the best consumer experience.

摩托罗拉正在工厂层面对新款Razr进行封闭式包装。一旦设备离开我们的设施，就不会重新打开。摩托罗拉对处理手套和卫生程序以及运输协议提出了严格的要求，旨在提供良好的开箱即用体验。与所有产品一样，我们将继续密切监控，并根据需要进行额外调整，以提供最佳的消费者体验。

Both the original Razr and the updated model come in fancy display boxes meant to showcase your phone to its fullest degree. (It also functions as a passive speaker, and its shape is designed to amplify the sound from the phone’s speaker.) The Razr itself was originally shipped in an unfolded configuration to better show off the flexible 6.2-inch OLED panel.

最初的Razr和升级版的Razr都装在花哨的展示盒中，旨在最大限度地展示你的手机。(它还具有被动扬声器的功能，其形状旨在放大手机扬声器发出的声音。)。Razr本身最初是以展开的配置发货的，以更好地展示灵活的6.2英寸OLED面板。

But it seems that either Amazon or Motorola is now concerned about the safety of shipping the $1,399 phone with that massive (and relatively fragile) display exposed to the elements. Hence what appears to be the shift towards folding the phone before shipping.

但似乎亚马逊或摩托罗拉现在都在担心这款售价1,399美元的手机发货时的安全性，这款手机带有暴露在空气中的巨大(且相对脆弱)的显示屏。因此，似乎是在发货前将手机折叠起来。

The whole situation is very weird — Motorola’s statement implies that the phones are folded before leaving the factory, but Amazon’s warning seemed to make it clear that it was manually folding the phones (complete with a fingerprint warning.) And all of this isn’t helped by the fact that even the best foldable phones are fragile.

整个情况非常奇怪--摩托罗拉的声明暗示手机在出厂前是折叠好的，但亚马逊的警告似乎表明它是手动折叠手机(并带有指纹警告)。而且，即使是最好的折叠式手机也是易碎的，这对所有这些都没有帮助。

Is this a casual indictment of the fragility of Motorola’s foldable phone? Amazon taking aggressively cautious measures to safely ship a product? Who can say.

这是对摩托罗拉可折叠手机脆弱性的不经意的控诉吗？亚马逊采取了积极谨慎的措施来安全发货吗？谁能说呢。

Update, Friday October 30th, 2:36pm: Updated article to note that Amazon has removed its fingerprint warning and added Motorola’s response.

更新，10月30日星期五下午2点36分：更新文章指出，亚马逊已经取消了指纹警告，并增加了摩托罗拉的回应。
