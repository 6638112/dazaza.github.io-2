Vaccinated? Krispy Kreme will give you free doughnuts for rest of 2021
接种疫苗？ Krispy Kreme将为您提供2021年休息的免费甜甜圈

2021-03-23 11:04:41

Being protected from COVID-19 is the most obvious benefit of getting vaccinated, but here's another one: free doughnuts. Starting Monday, anyone who shows proof of vaccination at Krispy Kreme will receive a free glazed doughnut — and if you're really into Krispy Kreme, you can do this every single day for the rest of 2021.

受到Covid-19的保护是接种疫苗的最明显的益处，但这里的另一个是：免费甜甜圈。从星期一开始，任何显示Krispy Kreme疫苗疫情证明的人都会收到一个免费的釉面甜甜圈 - 如果你真的进入Krispy Kreme，你可以每天在2021年的其余部分来做这一天。

“We all want to get COVID-19 behind us as fast as possible and we want to support everyone doing their part to make the country safe by getting vaccinated as soon as the vaccine is available to them,” said Krispy Kreme representative Dave Skena in a press release.

“我们都希望尽可能快地在我们身后获得Covid-19，我们希望通过疫苗可供选择，通过接种疫苗，支持所有人，以便通过接种疫苗来支持国家安全，”Krispy Kreme代表Dave Skena说新闻稿。

For doughnut-obtaining purposes, it doesn't matter if you haven't snagged your second shot yet — anyone who has received at least one of the two shots of the Moderna or Pfizer vaccine or one shot of the Johnson & Johnson vaccine is eligible. But make sure you bring your actual COVID-19 Vaccination Record Card, because vaccine stickers won't be accepted.

According to Krispy Kreme, all Bay Area locations will be participating in the promotion. While the only San Francisco location of the doughnut chain recently closed permanently, you can still get your free vaccine doughnuts in Daly City, Concord, Union City, Fremont, Mountain View and Santa Clara.

根据Krispy Kreme的说法，所有湾区地区都将参加促销活动。虽然甜甜圈连锁的唯一旧金山位置最近永久关闭，但您仍然可以在Daly City，Concord，Union City，Fremont，山景和圣克拉拉的免费疫苗甜甜圈。
