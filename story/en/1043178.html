Bitcoin Surges 25% In One Week. Warren Buffett Still Won't Buy It
比特币在一周内上涨了25％。 沃伦·巴菲特仍然不会购买

2021-01-04 21:09:41

Bitcoin soared to $34,000 yesterday— but here’s why Warren Buffett will never own Bitcoin.

比特币昨天飙升至34,000美元，但这就是沃伦·巴菲特（Warren Buffett）永远不会拥有比特币的原因。

The price of Bitcoin, which is the world’s largest cryptocurrency, has increased approximately 23% in the past five days. That’s good news for Bitcoin investors and speculators. For Warren Buffett, however, it’s completely irrelevant. Why? Buffett has called Bitcoin, among other names, “rat poison squared” and has said he won’t ever buy the cryptocurrency. “I don’t have any cryptocurrency and I never will,” Buffett told CNBC in February, when Bitcoin was trading at about $10,000. Here are 3 reasons why Buffett will never own Bitcoin, no matter how high the price of Bitcoin soars:

比特币是世界上最大的加密货币，在过去五天内价格上涨了约23％。对于比特币投资者和投机者来说，这是个好消息。但是，对于沃伦·巴菲特来说，这完全无关紧要。为什么？巴菲特称比特币为“鼠药平方”，并表示他永远不会购买这种加密货币。巴菲特在二月份告诉CNBC，当时比特币的交易价格约为10,000美元，“我没有任何加密货币，我永远也不会。”无论比特币价格飙升多少，巴菲特都不拥有比特币的原因有以下三个：

Buffett believes that Bitcoin has no underlying value. As a value investor, Buffett invests in companies that are undervalued, produce stable and recurring cash flow and have the ability to increase in book value. To Buffett, Bitcoin doesn’t produce earnings or dividends. Rather, the value of Bitcoin is simply what one person is willing to pay for it. In this regard, Bitcoin is no different than the tulip craze of 1637. Therefore, Buffett believes that Bitcoin has no inherent value.

巴菲特认为，比特币没有任何潜在价值。作为价值投资者，巴菲特投资于那些被低估，产生稳定且经常性现金流量并能够增加账面价值的公司。对巴菲特而言，比特币不会产生收益或股息。相反，比特币的价值仅仅是一个人愿意为此付出的代价。在这方面，比特币与1637年的郁金香热潮没有什么不同。因此，巴菲特认为比特币没有内在价值。

Should you buy Bitcoin: If you currently own, or intend to own, Bitcoin, do you have an underlying thesis to support your investment? Whether you think Bitcoin has value, make sure you have a clear thesis to support your decision.

您是否应该购买比特币：如果您当前拥有或打算拥有比特币，您是否有支持其投资的基础论点？无论您认为比特币具有价值，请确保您有清晰的论据来支持您的决定。

This may sound obvious to some: only invest in things you understand. However, too many investors, especially retail investors, chase investments based on headlines and stock tips from friends. They don’t do their research or conduct due diligence before investing. Rather, they hear about this “hot technology” and simply buy the stock. Or, they hear about that stock that soared 20% and automatically conclude it will jump another 20%. Sometimes, stocks go up for all kinds of reasons, and this “strategy” may work. Over time, and across an entire investment portfolio, however, it won’t work. In contrast, Buffett has a set of investment principles that he follows. He prefers to invest in stable consumer goods companies like Coca-Cola and financial services companies like American Express AXP . If a potential investment falls outside his area of expertise, he rarely, if ever, buys it. Sometimes, there are exceptions, but mostly Buffett invests only in sectors and business models that he understands.

对于某些人来说，这听起来似乎很明显：仅投资于您了解的事情。但是，太多的投资者，尤其是散户投资者，根据标题和朋友的股票提示追逐投资。他们在投资前不会进行研究或进行尽职调查。相反，他们听说了这种“热门技术”，只是购买了股票。或者，他们听说那支股票飙升了20％，并自动得出结论，它将再跳20％。有时，由于各种原因，股票上涨，这种“策略”可能起作用。随着时间的流逝，在整个投资组合中，它都将失效。相反，巴菲特有一套他遵循的投资原则。他更喜欢投资可口可乐等稳定的消费品公司和美国运通AXP等金融服务公司。如果潜在投资超出了他的专业领域，那么他很少（如果有的话）会购买它。有时也有例外，但巴菲特大多只投资于他了解的行业和商业模式。

Should you buy Bitcoin: Do you have a set of investment principles? Or, do you simply buy what you read or hear about it without conducting any independent research? Couldn’t you simply buy Bitcoin to own it even if it doesn’t fit your typical investment criteria? Sure. When you think about portfolio strategy, however, you should understand why you own every position (and if you’re comfortable buying a position that you may not fully understand).

您是否应该购买比特币：您有一套投资原则吗？或者，您是否只是在不进行任何独立研究的情况下购买了您所阅读或听到的东西？即使不符合您的典型投资条件，您是否也可以简单地购买比特币来拥有它？当然。但是，在考虑投资组合策略时，您应该了解为什么拥有所有头寸（以及如果您愿意购买可能不完全了解的头寸）。

For some, Bitcoin is the single greatest investment in their lifetime. For others, it’s a speculative bubble that will crash to zero. Buffett doesn’t play in this sandbox. Why? Buffett isn’t a speculative investor; instead, he invests in companies that have a wide economic moat. While all investing involves some degree of speculation, Buffett’s background is in insurance and risk mitigation. Buffett doesn’t invest in “high fliers”—that’s not his game. His game is “buy and hold”—forever. He invests in companies that grow over time, steadily and consistently.

对于某些人来说，比特币是他们一生中最大的一笔投资。对于其他人来说，这是一个投机泡沫，它将崩溃为零。巴菲特不在此沙盒中玩。为什么？巴菲特不是投机性投资者。相反，他投资于经济前景广阔的公司。尽管所有投资都涉及某种程度的投机活动，但巴菲​​特的背景是保险和减轻风险。巴菲特不投资于“高手”，那不是他的游戏。他的游戏永远是“买入并持有”。他投资于随着时间推移而稳定，持续增长的公司。

Should you buy Bitcoin: Here is a helpful way to approach a potentially speculative investment. The upside is easy: you make a lot of money. As I explain in The Lemonade Life, focus on the downside. Are you comfortable with Bitcoin being worth zero? Are you willing to lose 100% of your investment?

您是否应该购买比特币：这是一种有助于进行潜在投机性投资的有用方法。好处很简单：您赚了很多钱。正如我在《柠檬水生活》中所解释的那样，着重于不利方面。您对比特币的价值为零感到满意吗？您愿意损失100％的投资吗？

You need to decide for yourself, and with the help of your financial advisor, if Bitcoin is right for you. Warren Buffett is only one investor. Critics say he doesn’t understand cryptocurrency or blockchain technology, and he’s already missed some of Bitcoin’s meteoric rise. After all, regardless of the underlying reason, each Bitcoin is now worth about $34,000. Isn’t that proof enough that it’s a good investment? Maybe. It’s possible, although unlikely, that Buffett ultimately changes his mind. Buffett said for years he wouldn’t buy technology stocks, only later to buy Apple AAPL . Until then, however, Buffett is not a fan of Bitcoin and says he never will be.

如果比特币适合您，您需要自己决定，并在财务顾问的帮助下进行。沃伦·巴菲特只是一位投资者。批评人士说，他不了解加密货币或区块链技术，他已经错过了比特币的飞速发展。毕竟，不管根本原因是什么，每个比特币现在的价值约为34,000美元。那还不够证明这是一笔很好的投资吗？也许。巴菲特有可能（虽然不太可能）最终改变主意。巴菲特说，多年来他不会购买科技股，只是后来才购买苹果APL。然而，在那之前，巴菲特并不喜欢比特币，并表示他永远不会。
