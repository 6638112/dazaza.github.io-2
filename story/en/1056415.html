According to another security expert, Alon Gal, the data was leaked in 2020 because of a vulnerability that enabled seeing the phone number linked to every Facebook account. Facebook noted that the vulnerability was discovered in 2019 that allowed phone numbers of millions of users to be scraped from Facebook servers. The social media giant said that the vulnerability was patched in August 2019. However, according to Gal, despite that flaw being fixed, it could provide valuable information to cyber criminals.

根据另一个安全专家，Alon Gal，数据在2020年泄露，因为漏洞使能看到与每个Facebook帐户相关联的电话号码。 Facebook指出，该漏洞是在2019年发现的，允许从Facebook服务器刮擦数百万用户的电话号码。社交媒体巨人表示，脆弱性在2019年8月修补。但是，据GAL表示，尽管缺陷固定，但它可以向网络犯罪分子提供有价值的信息。
