At GTC 2021, Nividia this morning unveiled Drive Atlan, an AI-enabled system-on-a-chip for autonomous vehicles that will target automakers’ 2025 models. Capable of delivering just over 1,000 trillion operations per second (TOPs), Atlan includes Nvidia’s next-generation GPU architecture, ARM CPU cores, and deep learning and computer vision accelerators.

在GTC 2021，Nividia今天早上推出了Atlan，一个支持Autia的Automous车辆的AiS的Aim-on-A-in-aim。能够每秒提供超过1000万亿辆业务（上衣），包括NVIDIA的下一代GPU架构，ARM CPU核心和深度学习和计算机视觉加速器。

An MIT  report estimates truly autonomous vehicles might not hit the streets for a decade. Still, some experts predict the pandemic will hasten the adoption of autonomous transportation technologies. Despite needing disinfection, driverless cars can potentially minimize the risk of spreading disease. Allied Market Research predicts that the the global autonomous vehicle market will be valued at $556.67 billion by 2026.

麻省理工学院报告估计真正的自治车辆可能不会达到街道十年。尽管如此，一些专家仍然预测大流行将加速采用自治运输技术。尽管需要消毒，但无人驾驶汽车可能会使蔓延疾病的风险最大限度地降低。盟军市场研究预测，全球自主车辆市场将以2026年的价值为5566.6亿美元。

Atlan, which integrates an Nvidia BlueField data processing unit for networking, storage, and security functions, is 4 to 33 times as powerful in terms of compute as Nvidia’s other autonomous driving-focused chips, Drive Xavier and Drive Orin. Xavier can reach up to 30 TOPs, while Orin tops out at 254.

atlan集成了NVIDIA Bluefield数据处理单元进行网络，存储和安全功能，与Compute的Compule As为NVIDIA的其他专注的驾驶芯片，驱动Xavier和Drive Orin等方面是强大的4至33倍。 Xavier最多可以达到30个上衣，而奥林在254次上面。

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang says Atlan is designed to handle the large number of AI applications that run simultaneously in driverless vehicles.

Nvidia首席执行官Jensen Huang表示，亚特兰旨在处理大量在无人驾驶车辆中同时运行的AI应用。

“Our new Atlan is truly a technical marvel, fusing all of Nvidia’s strengths in AI, auto, robotics, safety and BlueField-secure datacenters to deliver safe, autonomous-driving fleets,” Huang said in a press release. “The transportation industry needs a computing platform that it can rely on for decades. The software investment is too immense to repeat for each car.”

“我们的新atlan是真正的技术奇迹，融合了AI，汽车，机器人，安全和蓝田安全的数据中心的所有NVIDIA的优势，以提供安全，自主驾驶车队，”黄先生在新闻稿中说。 “交通行业需要一个计算平台，可以依赖几十年。软件投资太大，无法重复每辆车。“

Nvidia also announced it would expand its partnership with Volvo to use Orin in upcoming Volvo models. The first car featuring the chip will be the XC90, scheduled to be revealed next year, which will leverage software developed by Volvo’s Zenseact subsidiary, as well as backup systems for steering and braking. A Xavier-powered server will manage core functionalities inside the car, like energy management and driver assistance, while Orin will handle vision and lidar sensor data processing.

NVIDIA还宣布它将扩展其与沃尔沃的伙伴关系在即将推出的沃尔沃型号中使用orin。为芯片为特色的第一辆车将是明年透露的XC90，这将利用沃尔沃ZenseaCT子公司开发的软件，以及用于转向和制动的备份系统。 XAVIER-Powered Server将管理汽车内的核心功能，如能量管理和驾驶员援助，而orin将处理视觉和LIDAR传感器数据处理。

Nvidia and Volvo began collaborating in 2018, when Volvo agreed to use Xavier for the computers in cars built on its scalable product architecture (SPA2) platform. SPA2 ships with an autonomous driving feature, Highway Pilot, that can be activated in supported locations and conditions.

NVIDIA和沃尔沃在2018年开始合作，当沃尔沃同意为其可扩展产品架构（SPA2）平台上的汽车中的计算机使用Xavier。 SPA2船舶具有自动驾驶功能，高速公路飞行员，可在支持的位置和条件下激活。

Nvidia also launched its newest Hyperion platform, which includes sensors, high-performance compute, and software for level 4 autonomous vehicle development. The 8th-gen Hyperion employs two Orin chips to process data from 12 exterior cameras, three interior cameras, nine radars, and one lidar sensor. Calibrated for 3D data collection and compatibility with Nvidia’s existing Drive AV and Drive IX software stacks, the platform supports real-time record and capture for driving data processing.

NVIDIA还推出了最新的Hyperion平台，包括传感器，高性能计算和4级自主车辆开发的软件。第8 -Gen Hyperion采用两个欧丁芯片来处理来自12个外部相机，三个内部摄像机，九个雷达和一个激光雷达传感器的数据。校准3D数据收集和与NVIDIA现有驱动AV和Drive IX软件堆栈的兼容性，该平台支持实时记录和捕获来驱动数据处理。
