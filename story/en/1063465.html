Inflation hits levels last seen in May 2008
2008年5月，通货膨胀率次达到水平

2021-06-04 15:06:55

The annual inflation rate for the United States is 4.2% for the 12 months ended April 2021 after rising 2.6% previously, according to U.S. Labor Department data published May 12. The next inflation update is scheduled for release on June 10 at 8:30 a.m. ET. It will offer the rate of inflation over the 12 months ended May 2021.

截至5月12日之前，美国劳工部数据提高2.6％后，美国的年度通胀率为4.2％.12。下一个通货膨胀更新，安排在6月10日上午8:30发布等。它将提供2021年5月12日的通货膨胀率。

The chart and table below display annual US inflation rates for calendar years from 2000 and 2011 to 2021. (For prior years, see historical inflation rates.) If you would like to calculate accumulated rates between two different dates, use the US Inflation Calculator.

*For 2021, the most recent monthly inflation data (12-month based) is displayed in the chart.

*对于2021，最近的月充气数据（基于12个月的）显示在图表中。

Since figures below are 12-month periods, look to the December column to find inflation rates by calendar year. For example, the rate of inflation in 2020 was 1.4%.

由于下面的数字是12个月的时间，因此向12月列栏查找日历年的通货膨胀率。例如，2020年的通货膨胀率为1.4％。

The last column, “Ave,” shows the average inflation rate for each year using CPI data, which was 1.2% in 2020. They are published by the BLS but are rarely discussed in news media, taking a back seat to a calendar year’s actual rate of inflation.

最后一列“Ave”显示了每年使用CPI数据的平均通货膨胀率，即2020年的1.2％。它们由BLS发布，但在新闻媒体中很少讨论，返回历时到日历年的实际情况通货膨胀率。

Annual rates of inflation are calculated using 12-month selections of the Consumer Price Index which is published monthly by the Labor Department’s Bureau of Labor Statistics ( BLS).

使用12个月选择的消费者价格指数计算每月由劳工处的劳工统计局（BLS）的每月发布的12个月选择的年度通货膨胀率计算。

For example, to calculate the inflation rate for January 2017, subtract the January 2016 CPI of "236.916" from the January 2017 CPI of "242.839." The result is "5.923." Divide this number by the January 2016 CPI and then multiply by 100 and add a % sign.
