Toyota Mirai sets new distance record for hydrogen-powered vehicles 1003 km
丰田Mirai为1003公里为氢气动力车辆设置了新的距离记录

2021-06-05 06:17:57

Toyota successfully set a new world record, the longest distance traveled by a mass-produced hydrogen-powered car future It can travel 1,003 kilometers with a single charge of hydrogen. This exceeds the previous record holder, Hyundai Lux, Ran 887.5 kilometers in Australia a few weeks ago.

丰田成功地设定了新的世界纪录，距离大规模生产的氢气动力汽车未来的最长距离，它可以在1,003公里，单笔氢气行驶。这超过了以前的历史持有人，现代勒克斯，几周前在澳大利亚运行887.5公里。

According to the Japanese automaker, Mirai departed from the HysetCo hydrogen refueling station in Orly, France, at 5:43 a.m. on May 26, and then traveled to the public areas south of Paris, Loire-Cher and Indre-Loire. Driving field on the road.

据日本汽车制造商称，Mirai于5月26日在5月26日左右离开了Hysetco氢气加油站，然后前往巴黎南部的公共区域，卢瓦尔雪雪群岛和印度卢瓦尔德。在路上的驾驶场。

Four drivers were responsible for the record-setting attempt, including Victorien Erussard, the founder and captain of Energy Observer (a hydrogen-powered ship); James Olden, engineer at Toyota Motor Europe; Maxime le Hir, Mirai product manager; and Toyota French PR Manager Marie Gadd (Marie Gadd). Although the company stated that “there is no special technology that daily drivers cannot use”, they all adopt an “eco-driving” style.

Under the supervision of an independent authority that certifies vehicle driving distance and fuel consumption, Mirai’s average fuel consumption is 0.55 kg/100 km, and the remaining mileage on the trip computer is 9 km. A fuel cell electric vehicle (FCEV) can store up to 5.6 kg of hydrogen, which can be filled in less than 5 minutes at a 700 bar gas station.

根据认证车辆驾驶距离和燃料消耗的独立权力的监督，Mirai的平均燃料消耗量为0.55公斤/ 100公里，距离旅行电脑上的剩余里程为9公里。燃料电池电动车（FCEV）可存储高达5.6千克的氢气，可在700巴的加油站在不到5分钟内填充。
