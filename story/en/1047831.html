Atlassian updates Trello with a visual overhaul, new card customization, and new board views; Trello says it has 50M+ users
Atlassian通过视觉大修，新卡定制和新板面视图更新了Trello； Trello说它拥有5000万以上的用户

2021-02-17 18:42:14

Productivity app Trello, a virtual whiteboard-style platform for organizing and managing projects, is announcing a major redesign today in addition to new features for helping businesses manage third-party integrations. The platform is getting a visual overhaul, both to its logo and the illustrations it deploys across its website and apps. It’s also getting multiple new ways to both customize cards and view your workload beyond the core whiteboard column view.

生产力应用程序Trello是用于组织和管理项目的虚拟白板样式平台，除用于帮助企业管理第三方集成的新功能外，今天还宣布进行重大重新设计。该平台正在对其徽标以及在其网站和应用程序中部署的插图进行视觉检查。除了核心白板列视图之外，它还提供了多种新方法来自定义名片和查看工作量。

“During the pandemic, Trello became many people’s new offices in a remote-first world. The overnight shift to distributed work coupled with the frantic adoption of digital tools led to an exponential increase in digital work artifacts scattered across apps, some of which now resemble a graveyard of ‘tried-and-tossed’ tools,” explains Trello co-founder Michael Pryor, who leads up the platform at enterprise software giant Atlassian following its acquisition in 2017, in a blog post published on Tuesday.

“在大流行期间，Trello成为了偏远第一世界中许多人的新办公室。一夜之间转移到分布式工作，再加上对数字工具的疯狂使用，导致分散在各个应用程序中的数字工作工件呈指数级增长，其中一些现在类似于“反复无常”的工具的墓地，” Trello联合创始人解释说。迈克尔·普赖尔（Michael Pryor）在周二发布的博客文章中说，他在2017年被企业软件巨头Atlassian收购后负责该平台。

Pryor says Trello has accumulated well over 50 million users, and the company wants to expand the way Trello works with third-party services like Google Drive, ticketing platform Jira, and Slack. “Today, we are unveiling the beginning of a whole new Trello, built specifically to support teams as they usher in a new era of work. These features, along with our plans for the future, will give users a central vantage point from which to view, plan and tackle their work.”

普赖尔说，Trello已经积累了超过5000万用户，该公司希望扩大Trello与第三方服务（例如Google云端硬盘，票务平台Jira和Slack）的合作方式。 “今天，我们将揭开全新Trello的开端，这是专门为支持团队迎接新时代而设计的。这些功能以及我们对未来的计划，将为用户提供一个中心位置，从中可以查看，计划和解决他们的工作。”

The new cards include both mirror cards and link cards, which the company hopes will make it easier to manage other apps and services from within Trello. Link cards, for instance, can now display previews if they contain a link to a third-party service like Dropbox, Google Docs, and YouTube. Another new card type, mirror cards, will let you pair together cards across multiple boards, so changes to one are reflected on all the others. Trello says this should help cut down on confusion and unnecessary interoffice updates, because more basic information about the status of a project will be automatically available instead of having to be manually communicated.

新卡包括镜像卡和链接卡，该公司希望这将使从Trello内部管理其他应用程序和服务变得更加容易。例如，如果链接卡包含指向Dropbox，Google Docs和YouTube等第三方服务的链接，则现在可以显示预览。另一种新的卡类型，即镜像卡，可让您跨多个板将卡配对在一起，因此对一个卡的更改会反映在所有其他卡上。 Trello说，这应该有助于减少混乱和不必要的部门间更新，因为有关项目状态的更多基本信息将自动提供，而不必手动进行传达。

Trello is also introducing five new board views that deviate from the standard column layout the platform helped popularize. These include team table view for turning multiple Trello boards into something more akin to a spreadsheet for easy viewing; timeline view for organizing a complex project by upcoming deadlines; calendar view for tracking projects and even simply individual to-do list items on a monthly basis; map view for organizing projects that incorporate information (like real estate); and dashboard view for visualizations of data and other project information into bar, line, and pie charts.

Trello还引入了五种新的董事会观点，这些观点偏离了该平台帮助普及的标准列布局。其中包括团队表视图，用于将多个Trello板变成类似于电子表格的形式，以便于查看；在即将到来的截止日期之前组织复杂项目的时间表视图；日历视图，用于跟踪项目，甚至每月仅跟踪单个待办事项列表项；地图视图，用于组织包含信息的项目（例如房地产）；仪表板视图可将数据和其他项目信息可视化为条形图，折线图和饼图。

These changes may help Trello, which is celebrating its 10th anniversary this year, stay competitive against alternatives like Asana, as well as the scores of smaller project management apps all vying for a coveted spot among the ever-growing list of software tools used by small startups and large corporations alike. Key to that, Pryor says, is acknowledging that Trello can’t be the one app that does everything you want it to do, but rather that it needs to be flexible enough to let companies and small teams connect to the tools they do need and have it all be accessible.

这些变化可能会帮助Trello（今年正庆祝其成立10周年）保持与Asana等替代产品的竞争能力，以及数十种小型项目管理应用程序，这些应用程序都在争夺不断增长的小型软件工具列表中争夺一席之地初创公司和大型公司。 Pryor说，关键是要承认Trello不能成为一个可以完成您想要做的所有事情的应用程序，而是需要足够灵活以使公司和小型团队能够连接到他们需要的工具，并且一切都可以访问。

“‘Hey, we’re just going to get everyone to use our tool, we will be the project management tool to rule them all.’ When you look at how people work, that strategy is a little myopic,” Pryror told The Verge in an interview last week. “There’s a lot of different tools people use to get their job done and they’re not all the same thing to different people.”

“'嘿，我们只是要让所有人使用我们的工具，我们将成为统治所有人的项目管理工具。'当您查看人们的工作方式时，这种策略有点近视，” Pryror告诉The Verge在上周的一次采访中。 “人们使用很多不同的工具来完成工作，而对于不同的人来说，它们并不是一回事。”

The goal for Trello going forward, he added, is figuring out “how can we expand the card metaphor to different tools and to give you a perspective on all the work that’s happening.” New types of cards, and new ways of visualizing Trello’s signature whiteboard, could help expand those metaphors and, Pryor hopes, make people more productive in the process.

他补充说，Trello前进的目标是弄清楚“我们如何将卡的隐喻扩展到不同的工具，并为您提供正在发生的所有工作的视角。” 新型卡片和可视化Trello签名白板的新方法可以帮助扩大这些隐喻，并且Pryor希望可以使人们在此过程中提高工作效率。
