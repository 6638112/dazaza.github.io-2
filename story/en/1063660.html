Cross-border payments startup dLocal's shares closed up 54% in its Nasdaq debut on Thursday, after raising $617.6M in its IPO, valuing the company at ~$10B
跨境付款启动Dlocal的股票于周四在其首次公开募股中筹集617.6亿美元后，在其纳斯达克举行的纳斯达克股票上涨了54％，以〜10亿美元重视公司

Shares of soared as much as 59% Thursday after the company completed an initial public offering that raised more than expected.

公司周四飙升至59％的股份完成了募集的首次公开发行，以上超过预期。

The cross-border payments company collected $617.6 million after selling 29,411,765 shares at $21 each, above the range of $16 to $18 it had told investors to expect.

跨境支付公司收集了617.6百万美元，售价29,411,765股，每股21美元，超过16至18美元，它告诉投资者预期。

Shares of dLocal (ticker: DLO) opened at $31, hit a high of $33.50, and closed at $32.39, up 54% from its offer price. At that price, dLocal’s valuation hit $10 billion in its first day of trading.

Dlocal（Ticker：DLO）的股票以31美元开机，达到33.50美元，截至32.39美元，距离提供的价格为54％。此价格，Dlocal的估值达到了10亿美元的交易。

DLocal is the only IPO this week due to the Memorial Day holiday. Next week, several companies are scheduled to go public, including TaskUs, LifeStance Health Group, and monday.com.

Dlocal是本周唯一由于阵亡将士纪念日假期的IPO。下周，一些公司计划公开，包括Taskus，LifeStance Health Group和Monday.com。

Founded in 2016, dLocal provides a platform that helps over 330 merchants connect with billions of consumers in emerging markets. Customers include Microsoft (MSFT), Amazon.com (AMZN), Spotify Technology (SPOT), and MailChimp. The Montevideo, Uruguay, company earns revenue from the fees it charges for processing cross-border and local payment transactions.

Dlocal成立于2016年，提供了一个平台，有助于330多家商家与新兴市场的数十亿个消费者联系。客户包括Microsoft（MSFT），Amazon.com（AMZN），Spotify技术（点）和MailChimp。 Montevideo，乌拉圭，公司从收取收费的费用中获得收入，以处理跨境和本地付款交易。

DLocal is the latest payments company to go public. Flywire (FLYW) and Paymentus (PAY) listed their shares last week while Marqeta is making its public equities debut next week.

Dlocal是公众的最新付款公司。 Flywire（Flyw）和Paypeal（Pay）上周列出了他们的股票，而马克塔在下周首次亮相公共股票。

The payments market is huge, exciting and full of opportunities, Sebastián Kanovich, dLocal’s CEO, told Barron’s from the Nasdaq. Payments companies are also better suited to being public entities, he said. “The level of transparency we will welcome,” he said. “It’s all about trust. We want to make sure that merchants have full visibility of what we do.”

支付市场是巨大的，令人兴奋和充满机会，Dlocal的首席执行官SebastiánKanovich告诉Barron's来自纳斯达克。他说，付款公司也更适合作为公共实体。 “我们欢迎的透明度水平，”他说。 “这都是关于信任的。我们希望确保商家能够完全了解我们所做的事情。“

The IPO wasn’t a capital raise for dLocal, which is profitable and doesn’t have long-term debt. DLocal sold about 4.4 million shares in the offering and will receive roughly $92.6 million. The other 25 million shares are coming from investors like General Atlantic, which sold roughly 6.6 million shares and made about $139 million. GA will have the biggest stake in dLocal and will own 21.3% after the offering. At dLocal’s closing price, GA’s remaining stake is valued at $2 billion. DLocal’s stockholders didn’t want to sell, Kanovich said. “We had to get shareholders to sell in order for us to have a public float,” he said.

Going public is something dLocal wanted to do but didn’t have to do, he said. “We are fully funded. This is an event we are proud of going for but it’s not a capital [raising] event for us. It’s more about reputation, transparency and visibility, and [building] trust for us,” he said.

他说，去公众是Dlocal想要做的事情，但没有必要做。 “我们完全资助了。这是我们为此感到自豪的活动，但这不是我们的资本[筹集]活动。他说，它更加关于声誉，透明度和可见性，以及[建立]信任，“他说。

DLocal will continue with its goal of connecting global companies with emerging markets customers, he said. Its platform lets merchants transact in 29 markets using 600 payment methods.

他说，Dlocal将继续将全球公司连接到新兴市场客户的目标。它的平台让商家使用600个付款方式在29个市场中交易。

Right now, dLocal doesn’t allow Bitcoin payments, Kanovich said. “If in the future, if the regulatory framework becomes more mature, and there is demand for it, we will continue to explore the option …Right now, there are no crypto payments,” he said.

现在，Dlocal不允许比特币支付，Kanovich说。 “如果在将来，如果监管框架变得更加成熟，而且需要它，我们将继续探索选项......现在，没有收音机支付，”他说。

DLocal plans to continue growing organically but may consider taking part in M&A, he said. “We will keep our options open and see what is out there in the markets,” he said.

他说，Dlocal计划继续生长，但可以考虑参加M＆amp; A。 “我们将保持我们的选择打开，看看市场上的内容，”他说。

The IPO was an important milestone, Kanovich said. “Tomorrow we are back to work and we will continue executing,” he said.

卡诺维奇说，IPO是一个重要的里程碑。 “明天我们回去工作，我们将继续执行，”他说。
