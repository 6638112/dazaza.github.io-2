Italian Artist Auctions Off an ‘Invisible Sculpture’ for $18,300
意大利艺术家拍卖一个“无形雕塑”，以18,300美元

2021-06-04 03:24:29

From the department of “They sold that for how much ?!” comes today’s story, about an Italian artist who, for the cool price of €15,000 ($18,300), recently auctioned an artwork that is…  well, nothing.

从“他们卖得这件事？”来自今天的故事，关于一个意大利艺术家，因为一个意大利艺术家，为了达15,000欧元（18,300美元），最近拍卖了一个......好吧的艺术品。

Last month, the 67-year-old artist Salvatore Garau sold an “immaterial sculpture”—which is to say that it doesn’t exist.

上个月，这位67岁的艺术家萨尔瓦特Garau出售了一个“非物质雕塑” - 这是说它不存在。

To be fair, the artist might disagree on conceptual grounds. For Garau, the artwork, titled Io Sono (which translates to “I am”), finds form in its own nothingness. “The vacuum is nothing more than a space full of energy, and even if we empty it and there is nothing left, according to the Heisenberg uncertainty principle, that nothing has a weight,” he told the Spanish news outlet Diario AS . “Therefore, it has energy that is condensed and transformed into particles, that is, into us.”

要公平，艺术家可能对概念理由不同意。对于Garau，艺术品，标题为Io Sono（翻译为“我是”），在自己的虚无地发现表格。 “真空只不过是一个充满活力的空间，即使我们清空它，没有什么，根据海森伯格的不确定性原则，没有任何重量，”他告诉西班牙新闻出口迪里奥。 “因此，它具有浓缩和转化为颗粒的能量，即进入我们。”

Lo Sono went up for sale in May at the Italian auction house Art-Rite. The pre-sale estimate valued the piece between €6,000-9,000, according to AS , but competing bidders pushed the price tag to €15,000.

罗索诺在5月份出售，在意大利拍卖宾馆艺术仪式。根据售前的估计，估价值为6,000-9,000欧元，据此，但竞争的投标人将价格标签推到15,000欧元。

The lucky buyer went home with a certificate of authenticity and a set of instructions: the work, per Garau, must be exhibited in a private house in a roughly five-by-five-foot space free of obstruction.

幸运买家带着真实性证明和一套指示：每只Garau的工作必须在一个私人住宅中展出，以大约五脚空的空间没有阻碍。

“When I decide to ‘exhibit’ an immaterial sculpture in a given space, that space will concentrate a certain amount and density of thoughts at a precise point, creating a sculpture that, from my title, will only take the most varied forms,” the artist went on.

“当我决定在一个特定的空间中”展示“一个非物质雕塑时，那个空间将在一个精确的点中集中一定数量和密度的思想，创造一个雕塑，从我的标题中只采取最多形式，”艺术家继续前进。

If you thought that was artsy claptrap, he goes on to draw a rather lofty comparison to the work: “After all, don’t we shape a God we’ve never seen?” he added.

如果你认为这是艺术Claptrap，他继续与工作相当崇高的比较：“毕竟，我们造成了我们从未见过的上帝？”他加了。

Lo Sono isn’t the only artwork of its kind in Garau’s oeuvre. In February of this year, at the Piazza Della Scala in Milan, the artist exhibited BUDDHA IN CONTEMPLATION , a similarly invisible sculpture demarcated by a square of tape on a cobble-stoned walkway. Meanwhile, this week, he installed A FRODITE CRIES in front of the New York City stock exchange. The effort, evidenced by an empty white circle, was supported by the Italian Cultural Institute.

Lo Sono不是Garau的Oeuvre中唯一的艺术品。今年2月，在米兰的迪拉斯斯卡拉广场，艺术家在沉思中展出了佛陀，一个类似地看不见的雕塑在一个鹅卵石扔石头上的磁带正方形。与此同时，本周，他在纽约市证券交易所前安装了一臂哭泣。意大利文化研究所的空白圈子所证明的努力得到了支持。

“You don’t see it but it exists; it is made of air and spirit,” he explained in a video documenting the Milan piece. “It is a work that asks you to activate the power of the imagination, a power that anyone has, even those who don’t believe they have it.”

“你没有看到它，但它存在;它是由空气和精神制成的，“他在一个记录米兰片的视频中解释道。 “这是一个要求你激活想象力的力量，任何人都有的权力，甚至那些不相信他们拥有的力量。”

Indeed, many people on the internet seem to be having trouble tapping into that power. “So you really just taped a square and called that a sculpture?” reads the most-liked comment on the video page .

事实上，互联网上的许多人似乎都遇到了攻击这种权力。 “所以你真的只是录制了一个广场，并称为雕塑？”在视频页面上读取最受欢迎的评论。
