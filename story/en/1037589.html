Reddit Reveals Daily Active User Count For the First Time: 52 Million
Reddit首次显示每日活跃用户数：5200万

2020-12-04 21:27:25

Reddit revealed that it now has 52 million daily users, and the number appears to be growing quickly. Reddit told The Wall Street Journal that daily usage grew 44 percent year over year for October, a sizable increase on a metric that speaks to just how essential a product it is to its users.

Reddit透露它现在每天有5200万用户，并且这个数字似乎正在迅速增长。 Reddit告诉《华尔街日报》，10月份的每日使用量同比增长了44％，这一指标的大幅增长表明该产品对其用户的重要性。

The number is small compared to other social media rivals, though. Twitter has 187 million daily users, Snap has 249 million, and Facebook has 1.82 billion. But at their larger sizes, none of those services are seeing daily usage grow as rapidly as Reddit. In their most recent quarters, Twitter reported 29 percent year-over-year growth, Snap reported 18 percent, and Facebook reported 12 percent.

不过，与其他社交媒体竞争对手相比，这个数字很小。 Twitter每天有1.87亿用户，Snap有2.49亿，Facebook有18.2亿。但是，在规模更大的情况下，这些服务中没有一个能看到每日使用量像Reddit一样快。在最近的几个季度中，Twitter报告同比增长29％，Snap报告18％，Facebook报告12％。

Daily usage of Reddit is being shared for the first time “as a more accurate reflection of our user growth and to be more in-line with industry reporting,” the company told the Journal. (It is true that social media companies tend to use daily users as their preferred metric; though Twitter, for instance, only switched away from reporting monthly users because that number was dipping, while daily users was growing.) The other reason for the change was to focus on a number that would better help to grow Reddit’s advertising business.

该公司告诉《华尔街日报》，这是首次首次共享Reddit的每日使用情况，“这是对我们用户增长的更准确反映，并且与行业报告更加一致”。 （的确，社交媒体公司倾向于将每日用户作为首选指标；尽管例如，Twitter只是放弃报告每月用户，因为该数字正在下降，而每日用户却在增长。）发生这一变化的另一个原因专注于可以更好地帮助Reddit发展广告业务的业务。

Reddit has focused on monthly usage in the past. This time last year, the company said it had 430 million monthly users, with 30 percent year-over-year growth. The quick growth has come during a period that Reddit began to iterate more rapidly on its service. It rolled out a significant redesign in 2018 and this year has added features like galleries and polls. The company has also gotten more serious about cracking down on some of the problematic communities hosted on its site, which should help to make the service friendlier to both advertisers and users overall.

Reddit过去专注于每月使用。去年的这个时候，该公司表示每月有4.3亿用户，同比增长30％。在Reddit开始快速迭代其服务的时期内，出现了快速增长。它在2018年进行了重大的重新设计，今年又增加了画廊和民意调查等功能。该公司还更加认真地打击了其网站上托管的一些有问题的社区，这应该有助于使该服务对广告商和用户整体而言更加友好。
