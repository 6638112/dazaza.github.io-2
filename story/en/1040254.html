Twitch bans words “simp” and “virgin”
Twitch禁止使用“简单”和“处女”一词

2020-12-17 09:59:07

As part of an ongoing process of retooling its policies around harassment and hateful conduct, Twitch announced on Wednesday that the words “simp,” “incel,” and “virgin” will no longer be allowed on the platform as long as they are used by streamers and commenters as insults. If they are used in a friendly way, the words are, presumably, still okay to use.

Twitch星期三宣布，在围绕骚扰和仇恨行为重新制定政策的过程中，Twitch宣布平台上将不再允许使用“ simp”，“ incel”和“ virgin”一词。彩带和评论者为侮辱。如果以友好的方式使用它们，这些单词大概仍然可以使用。

In a statement, a Twitch spokesperson told The Verge that the platform plans to take action against the use of those terms “specifically when they are being used to negatively refer to another person’s sexual practices.” Using the terms on their own in a way that doesn’t actively insult other users, the spokesperson added, “wouldn’t lead to an enforcement, but we would take action if they were used repeatedly in a harassing manner.”

Twitch发言人在一份声明中告诉The Verge，该平台计划采取行动，反对“特别是当这些术语被用来否定地指代他人的性行为时”使用这些术语。发言人补充说，自己使用这些条款不会主动侮辱其他用户，“不会导致强制执行，但是如果以骚扰的方式反复使用这些条款，我们将采取行动。”

The new rules will officially go into effect on January 22nd, and will also include stricter policies around sexual harassment. Among the other new banned behaviors are “making derogatory statements about another person’s perceived sexual practices or sexual morality” and “repeatedly negatively targeting another person with sexually-focused terms, such as ‘whore’ or ‘virgin.’”

新规定将于1月22日正式生效，还将包括有关性骚扰的更严格政策。在其他新的被禁止的行为中，有“对他人的性行为或性道德的贬损言论”和“反复地以性别为中心的术语（例如，“妓女”或“处女”）否定目标。

During live-streamed town hall on Wednesday afternoon, Twitch COO Sara Clemens said that terms like “simp,” incel,” and “virgin” are specifically not allowed because they are often deployed as “insults to negatively refer to another person’s sexual activity.” Clemens also noted that the platform will monitor emotes, a Twitch-specific set of emoticons, for the banned terms, and will remove them when reported.

Twitch的首席运营官Sara Clemens在周三下午直播的市政厅中说，特别禁止使用“简单”，“内含”和“处女”等字眼，因为它们经常被用作“侮辱他人的性行为的侮辱”。 ” Clemens还指出，该平台将监控表情符号（一组Twitch特定的表情符号）中的禁忌条款，并在报告后将其删除。

In June, Twitch said that it was “committed to continuing our efforts to make Twitch a safer environment” after the platform finally began reckoning with the wave of sexual harassment and hateful content complaints that had long plagued it.

6月，Twitch表示，在平台终于开始考虑长期困扰它的一波性骚扰和仇恨内容投诉之后，“致力于继续努力使Twitch成为一个更安全的环境”。

While there’s no word on how the moderators will enforce the new ban, they’ll have their work cut out for them; there are a lot of people calling each other simps on Twitch.

虽然没有人说主持人将如何执行新的禁令，但他们会为他们剪裁工作； Twitch上有很多人互相呼。
