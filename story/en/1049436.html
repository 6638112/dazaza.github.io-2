Twitter reveals 'Super Follow' paid service, new groups feature, more
Twitter揭示了“超级关注”付费服务，新的群组功能以及更多

2021-02-26 02:36:17

During an Analyst event today, Twitter revealed at least part of the paid subscription service it’s been working on for some time. It’s called “Super Follow” and will allow users to get exclusive content, deals, and community access to creators for $4.99/month. Other announcements include the testing of a new groups feature, company growth goals, and more.

在今天的Analyst活动中，Twitter透露了至少一部分已在使用一段时间的付费订阅服务。它被称为“超级关注”，它将使用户能够以每月4.99美元的价格获得创作者的独家内容，交易和社区访问权限。其他公告包括对新组功能的测试，公司增长目标等。

The most interesting announcement today may be the new “Super Follow” feature that will give an opportunity for creators and more the option to directly monetize their audience on Twitter. It will compete with existing services like Patreon and OnlyFans.

今天最有趣的公告可能是新的“超级关注”功能，该功能将为创作者提供机会，并提供直接在Twitter上通过受众获利的选项。它将与Patreon和OnlyFans等现有服务竞争。

Twitter shared several screenshots of the new feature at its Analyst Day. Perks will include “Supporter badge, Subscriber-only Newletters, Exclusive content, Deals & discounts, Community access.”

Twitter在其“分析日”上分享了该新功能的多个屏幕截图。特权将包括“支持者徽章，仅订阅者的Newletters，独家内容，Deals＆amp;折扣，社区访问。”

The Super Follow subscriptions will go for $4.99/month with the option to “Cancel anytime.”

Super Follow订阅的价格为每月$ 4.99，可以选择“随时取消”。

“$4,99/month – Cancel anytime”: Twitter arbeitet an einer “Super Follow”-Funktion, mit der man direkt auf der Plattform Geld verdienen kann. Hier sind die ersten Screenshots, via @TwitterIR: pic.twitter.com/hPiq27Ov5U

“ $ 4,99 /月–随时取消”：Twitter推荐了一个超级“追随者” -Funktion，作者是Plattform Geld verdienen kann。 Hier sind die ersten屏幕截图，通过@TwitterIR：pic.twitter.com/hPiq27Ov5U

Noted by The Information’s Alex Heath, one big change beyond the wide-ranging creators, public figures, businesses, and more that would likely use Super Follow is that reporters using the feature to charge for content could significantly impact publications around the world.

《资讯》的亚历克斯·希思（Alex Heath）指出，除了广泛的创作者，公众人物，企业以及可能使用超级追随者的其他功能以外，这是一项重大变化，那就是记者使用该功能收费的内容可能会对全球出版物产生重大影响。

Reporters being about to paywall their tweets is going to shake up so many newsrooms

记者即将支付的推文将动摇许多新闻编辑室

Twitter also shared some new goals it’s looking to hit by 2023. These include the aim to “at least double total annual revenue from $3.7 billion in 2020 to $7.5 billion or more in 2023,” get to “315M or more mDAU in Q4 2023.”

Twitter还分享了一些其希望在2023年实现的新目标。这些目标包括“至少使年总收入从2020年的37亿美元翻倍到2023年的75亿美元或更多，并在2023年第四季度达到3.15亿或更多的mDAU。 ”
