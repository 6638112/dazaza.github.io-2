SpaceX raises $1.16B in equity financing
Spacex在股权融资中提高1.66亿美元

2021-04-15 22:19:37

(Reuters) -Billionaire Elon Musk’s SpaceX has raised about $1.16 billion in equity financing over the last two months, the private rocket company said in an amended regulatory filing on Wednesday.

（路透社） - 私人火箭公司在周三修订的监管申请中表示，在过去两个月的股权融资中筹集了约11.16亿美元的股票筹资。

The filing comes after SpaceX, whose investors include Alphabet Inc and Fidelity Investments, in February disclosed that it raised about $850 million, which had reportedly lifted its valuation to about $74 billion. ( bit.ly/3g8wK8S)

该申请率为Spacex后，其投资者包括Alphabet Inc和Fidelity Investments，于2月份披露其筹集了约8.5亿美元，据报道，据报道，估值将其估值提升至约740亿美元。 （bit.ly/3g8wk8s）

The private space company had raised $1.9 billion in August in its largest single fundraising round, according to financial software and data provider PitchBook.

根据金融软件和数据提供者的节奏，私人空间公司在8月份在其最大的单一筹款中筹集了19亿美元。

In February, a prototype of SpaceX’s Starship rocket, SN9, blew up on its final descent, like SN8 before it. The SN9 was a test model being developed to carry humans and 100 tons of cargo on future missions to the moon and Mars.

2月，Spacex Starship火箭的原型，SN9，最终下降，如SN8之前爆发。 SN9是一种测试模型，用于携带人类和100吨货物对月球和火星的未来任务。

Following this, the U.S. House of Representatives Transportation and Infrastructure Committee said last month it was investigating a SpaceX commercial space launch that regulators determined violated U.S. safety requirements and its test license. ( reut.rs/2Rvy7UV)

在此之后，美国代表的房屋交通和基础设施委员会上个月表示，它正在调查SPACEX商业空间推出，该监管机构确定违反美国的安全要求及其测试许可证。 （REUT.RS/2RVY7UV）

Musk, who also leads several futuristic companies, including Tesla Inc, Neuralink and Boring Co, said last month SpaceX will be landing its Starship rockets on Mars well before 2030.

麝香，他还领导了几家未来派公司，包括Tesla Inc，Neuralink和Boring Co，上个月的Spacex将在2030年之前将其星舰火箭放在火星上。
