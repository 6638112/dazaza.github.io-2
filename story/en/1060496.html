Mozilla Firefox update is making me abandon Chrome – Here's why
Mozilla Firefox Update让我放弃Chrome - 这就是为什么

2021-05-05 20:22:27

In the monopolistic world of browsers, Mozilla Firefox has competed while at a constant disadvantage. While Apple’s Safari, Microsoft Edge, and Google Chrome have thrived on the back of their own computing platforms, Firefox’s market share has nosedived from double-digits ages ago to a measly 2 to 3% today.

在众多浏览器世界中，Mozilla Firefox在不断缺点的同时竞争。虽然Apple的Safari，Microsoft Edge和Google Chrome在自己的计算平台的背面繁荣，但Firefox的市场份额从Agenes Agay Agay Agay达到了今天2％至3％。

But as companies like Google strive for greater control and begin dictating their own rules for the open web, the need for independent and open-source browsers is greater than ever. And Mozilla has been beavering away, with a slate of compelling privacy-first updates to capture that growing niche of people.

但是，由于谷歌这样的公司争取更大的控制并开始对开放网络的自己的规则决定，因此对独立和开源浏览器的需求比以往任何时候都大。和Mozilla一直拍摄，一个令人信服的隐私 - 首先更新的石板，以捕捉人们不断增长的人。

For its latest attempt to persuade users, Mozilla Firefox is betting on simplicity. Its upcoming update materializes the long-awaited Proton redesign that cleans up the many visual transitions Firefox has undergone in the last couple of years and underpins the browser’s efforts to put an end to invasive third-party trackers as well as cookies.

为其最新尝试说服用户，Mozilla Firefox正在简化赌注。它即将到来的更新估计了期待已久的质子重新设计，清理了许多视觉过渡Firefox在过去几年中经历过的许多视觉过渡，并且基于浏览器的努力，将结束进入侵入性的第三方跟踪器以及Cookie。

One of the foremost objectives of the new Firefox look, Mozilla tells us, is to promote a calmer web experience. After spending a week with it, I found that to be true in more ways than one.

新的Firefox外观的最重要目标之一，Mozilla告诉我们，是推动一个平静的网络体验。在花一个星期之后，我发现这是比一个更多样的。

No longer do the prompts hijack what you’re doing at any moment to ask if you’d like to turn on the website’s notifications, and the alerts have been tweaked to take up as little screen space as possible. Extra menus and lines that could be omitted have been omitted. This expands to nearly every annoying aspect of the web. Auto-playing sites, for instance, are silenced in the background and a quiet indicator appears over the tab to let you know of the action.

不再劫持你在任何时候劫持你在做什么，要求你想打开网站的通知，并且警惕被调整为尽可能少的屏幕空间。省略了额外的菜单和线条。这将扩展到几乎所有令人讨厌的方面。例如，自动播放站点在后台静音，在选项卡上显示安静指示器，以通知您的操作。

Mozilla Firefox’s refreshed design language echoes this theme as well. The stiff and boxy toolbars and tabs have been swapped out for floating elements with ever-so-slightly curved edges. The result is an app that feels more inviting and one that eliminates the overwhelming sense users face when they switch to a new browser app.

Mozilla Firefox的刷新设计语言也会回应这个主题。刚刚和柔软的工具栏和标签已被交换出浮动元素，具有较为略微略微弯曲的边缘。结果是一个应用程序，该应用程序感觉更多的邀请，并且在切换到新的浏览器应用程序时消除压倒性的感觉用户的一个应用程序。

The browser’s theme now seeps into more corners and crevices of its interface to emphasize these visual updates. In earlier versions, for example, the tabs were clustered together with no space in between and only their top edge was accented. Firefox 89 frees up these spaces. Under the new design, the tabs are a lot more highlighted and easier to navigate, especially when you have dozens of them lined up.

浏览器的主题现在渗入更多的角落和缝隙，以强调这些视觉更新。例如，在早期版本中，选项卡在群集群集，不会在其间，只有其顶部边缘的空间。 Firefox 89释放了这些空间。在新的设计下，标签更加突出显示，更容易导航，特别是当您有几十个人排列时。

On paper, these additions seem too trivial to matter but viewed together, they add the subtle modern touch Mozilla Firefox sorely needed. In addition, the extra spacing, lighter typography, and simpler aesthetic offer comfort in an increasingly cluttered web experience.

在纸上，这些补充似乎太琐事了，但在一起看起来过于琐碎，他们加入了纯粹需要的微妙现代触摸Mozilla Firefox。此外，在越来越杂乱的网络体验中，额外的间距，打火图和更简单的美学提供舒适性。

What has most convinced me to set Firefox as my default is its extensive suite of privacy tools. Over the last two years, Firefox has bolstered its commitment to user privacy to tackle new emerging threats known to slip through traditional browser protections.

最确信我将Firefox设置为默认设置的是它的广泛隐私工具套件。在过去的两年里，Firefox已经促使其对用户隐私的承诺，以解决通过传统浏览器保护而闻名的新兴的新兴威胁。

Take third-party trackers, for example. These little pieces of code keep tabs on all your online moves and allow advertisers to essentially build a dossier on you. Every browser has safeguards to block such activities but some websites refuse to function without their tracker scripts, which often leads to rendering and performance hiccups. With its SmartBlock feature, Firefox generates local dummies that act just like a regular tracker script and tricks websites into believing their built-in trackers haven’t been blocked.

例如，参加第三方跟踪器。这些小型代码可以在所有在线移动上保留选项卡，并允许广告商基本上构建您的档案。每个浏览器都有保护措施来阻止此类活动，但某些网站拒绝在没有跟踪器脚本的情况下删除功能，这通常会导致渲染和性能打嗝。通过SmartBlock功能，Firefox会生成当地的假人，就像常规跟踪器脚本一样，并欺骗网站相信其内置的跟踪器尚未被阻止。

Similarly, Firefox’s Total Cookie Protection maintains a separate space for each website’s cookies so that they can’t cross-share the data they’ve collected on you. Platforms such as Facebook take advantage of cross-site cookies to spy on you even when you’re not actually browsing within Facebook’s own websites.

同样，Firefox的总cookie Protection为每个网站的cookie维护一个单独的空间，以便他们不能交叉共享他们收集的数据。诸如Facebook等平台利用横向网站Cookie来窥探您，即使您实际上不在Facebook自己的网站内浏览。

There are a handful of additional Firefox privacy tools I’ve grown used to in the past week. The HTTPS-only mode ensures websites built with outdated and insecure technologies exclusively communicate with their servers through an encrypted channel.

我已经在过去的一周里习惯了额外的额外Firefox隐私工具。仅限HTTPS模式可确保使用过时的和不安全技术构建的网站专门通过加密通道与其服务器通信。

With “Container Tabs,” you can run multiple, isolated sessions of a website in the same window. This allows you to multitask between your work and personal accounts, for instance, without launching a new private window. Since container tabs are entirely isolated from the rest of your browser’s data, they also come in handy to surf websites you don’t trust and hence, don’t want to risk compromising any of your existing browsing information to them.

使用“容器选项卡”，您可以在同一窗口中运行多个网站的孤立的会话。这允许您在工作和个人帐户之间进行多任务，例如，在不启动新的私有窗口。由于容器选项卡完全从浏览器的数据中分离出来，因此他们也可以帮助您不信任的冲浪网站，因此，不希望冒险将任何现有的浏览信息妥协到它们。

Plus, in my cursory tests, I found Mozilla Firefox to be easier on my computer’s 8GB of memory, consuming at least 1GB less than Google Chrome no matter the task. Overall, though, there’s little difference between Firefox and Chrome performance which wasn’t the case a year ago. When I last tried, I discovered the former severely falling behind from the competition.

此外，在我的练习序测试中，我发现Mozilla Firefox在我的电脑的8GB内存中更容易，而不是谷歌Chrome的少量少于1GB，无论任务都要。但总的来说，Firefox和Chrome表现之间的差异很大，而不是一年前的情况。当我上次尝试时，我发现前者落后于竞争对手。

Another factor driving me to switch to Firefox is Google’s recent Chrome updates. Late last month, the search engine giant began testing its controversial proprietary replacement for third-party cookies -- a move that lets Google exert a monopoly on an uncomfortably large portion of the web and the advertising industry, which it already dominates. Mozilla Firefox, among several other browsers like Safari, says it doesn’t plan to adopt it.

让我切换到Firefox的另一个因素是谷歌最近的Chrome更新。上个月末，搜索引擎巨头开始测试其对第三方饼干的有争议的专有替代品 - 这一举措让谷歌在令人不安的大部分网络和广告行业上占据了一部分占主导地位的垄断。 Mozilla Firefox，其中几个像Safari这样的其他浏览器，说它不计划采用它。

“Advertising and privacy can co-exist,” a Mozilla spokesperson told Laptopmag, “And the advertising industry can operate differently than it has in past years. We look forward to playing a role in finding solutions that build a better web.”

“广告和隐私可以共存，”一位Mozilla发言人告诉笔记本电脑，“和广告行业可以不同于过去几年的方式。我们期待着在寻找建立更好网络的解决方案中发挥作用。“

Mozilla Firefox has always been the browser I wanted to switch to but couldn’t due to reasons ranging from performance shortcomings to dull visuals. However, with the Firefox Proton update and the rest of Mozilla’s privacy-first services such as the VPN app and the disposable email generator, it has never been in a better position to be the go-to browser for just about everyone. It’s secure, its in-house (read non-Chromium) engine has caught up to Google Chrome and Apple’s Safari, and its new design overhaul, which is expected to publicly ship on June 1st, is a refreshing take on the modern browser experience.

Mozilla Firefox一直是我想要切换的浏览器，但不能因为从性能缺点到沉闷的视觉效果而导致的原因。然而，通过Firefox Proton更新和Mozilla的其余部分，例如VPN应用程序和一次性电子邮件生成器，从来没有成为一个更好的位置，以便成为每个人的Go-to Browser。它是安全的，它的内部（读非铬）发动机赶上了谷歌铬和苹果的野生动物园，其新的设计大修，预计将于6月1日公开发货，是现代浏览器体验的令人耳目一新。
