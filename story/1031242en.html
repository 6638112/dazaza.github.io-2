总部位于伦敦的PrimaryBid开发连接散户投资者和上市公司的软件，筹集4230万欧元的B系列资金

2020-10-26 22:02:10

London-based fintech PrimaryBid has today announced raising approx. €42.3 million Series B funding, co-led by OMERS, London Stock Exchange Group and Draper Esprit, alongside Fidelity, and existing investors Pentech and Outward VC among others.

总部设在伦敦的金融科技今天宣布提高大约。4230万欧元的B系列融资，由OMERS、伦敦证券交易所集团(London Stock Exchange Group)和法国德雷珀ESPRIT(Draper ESPRIT)以及富达(Fidelity)以及现有投资者Pentech和Outside VC等共同牵头。

Founded in 2015, PrimaryBid seamlessly connects everyday investors with public companies. Through a long-term agreement with the London Stock Exchange, it ensures that retail investors transact at the same time and at the same price as institutional investors, creating a more democratised access to public markets. To date it has been involved in 90 issuances on London markets, including Compass Group, Ocado, Taylor Wimpey and William Hill.

成立于2015年的PrimaryBid将日常投资者与上市公司无缝连接。通过与伦敦证券交易所(London Stock Exchange)达成的一项长期协议，它确保散户投资者与机构投资者在同一时间、以相同的价格进行交易，从而创造了更民主的公开市场准入。到目前为止，它已经参与了伦敦市场上的90只股票发行，包括Compass Group、Ocado、泰勒·温佩比和威廉·希尔(William Hill)。

So far, PrimaryBid has completed over 41 capital raisings for UK listed companies and investment trusts since April 2020, working alongside global investment banks to broaden investor access as companies recapitalised their balance sheets and raised growth capital. Retail investors directly own 13.5% of the UK market, while additionally holding approximately €296.7 billion (£269 billion) in cash ISAs, making them an important constituency in any capital raising activity (sources: ONS, 2018; HMRC, 2020).

自2020年4月以来，PrimaryBid已为英国上市公司和投资信托基金完成了逾41笔融资，随着企业对资产负债表进行资本重组并筹集增长资本，PrimaryBid与全球投行合作，拓宽了投资者渠道。散户投资者直接拥有英国市场13.5%的份额，同时额外持有约2967亿欧元(2690亿英镑)的现金ISA，使他们成为任何融资活动的重要支持者(资料来源：ONS，2018年；HMRC，2020年)。

Anand Sambasivan, co-founder and CEO at PrimaryBid, commented: “We are delighted to count Draper Esprit as both an investor and a partner as we expand internationally, and look toward North America. Our simple, inclusive technology has global applicability. It allows corporate issuers to access the widest possible set of investors, improving capital formation and putting the public back into public markets.”

PrimaryBid联合创始人兼首席执行官阿南德·桑巴西文评论说：“在我们向国际扩张并将目光投向北美的过程中，我们很高兴将德雷珀ESPRIT作为投资者和合作伙伴。我们简单、包容的技术具有全球适用性。它使公司发行人能够接触到尽可能广泛的投资者，改善了资本形成，并使公众重新进入公开市场。“。

Vinoth Jayakumar, Partner at Draper Esprit, commented: “Our investment in PrimaryBid aligns with part of our wider investment thesis to democratise retail investors’ access to public markets as well as modernise market infrastructure software. For us, both our companies are anticipating the direction  of travel of the future of finance.”

Draper ESPRIT合伙人Vinoth Jayakumar评论说：“我们对PrimaryBid的投资符合我们更广泛的投资理念的一部分，即让散户投资者进入公开市场的民主化，以及市场基础设施软件的现代化。对我们来说，我们两家公司都在预判金融未来的行进方向。“。

Charlie Walker, Head of Equity and Fixed Income, Primary Markets at London Stock Exchange plc said: “This investment builds on our collaboration with PrimaryBid and is part of London Stock Exchange Group’s commitment to broadening retail investor access to public equity markets. Through PrimaryBid’s innovative offering, retail investors have been able to access capital raisings on the same terms as institutional investors, supporting the U.K.’s public companies by providing additional capital and liquidity. PrimaryBid has become an important part of the U.K.’s capital raising ecosystem and we look forward to working with them to further enhance retail investor access to capital markets within the U.K. and globally.”

伦敦证券交易所公司股票和固定收益初级市场部主管查理·沃克说：“这项投资建立在我们与PrimaryBid合作的基础上，是伦敦证券交易所集团扩大散户投资者进入公开股票市场的承诺的一部分。通过PrimaryBid的创新产品，散户投资者能够以与机构投资者相同的条件获得融资，通过提供额外的资本和流动性来支持英国的上市公司。PrimaryBid已经成为英国融资生态系统的重要组成部分，我们期待着与他们合作，进一步加强散户投资者进入英国国内和全球资本市场的机会。“。

Tara Reeves, Partner at OMERS Ventures said:  “As fintech specialists it’s been impossible to ignore the rise of PrimaryBid in 2020 and its success championing retail investors in the capital markets.PrimaryBid’s technology sits at the intersection of powerful trends in financial services – regulation, digitalisation and democratisation – and OMERS Ventures is delighted to support the team’s mission to put individual investors on equal terms with institutions. PrimaryBid is now well integrated at the highest levels of the U.K.’s capital raising ecosystem, and we look forward to helping the team realize their ambitions internationally.”

OMERS Ventures合伙人塔拉·里夫斯(Tara Reeves)表示：“作为金融科技的专家，我们不可能忽视PrimaryBid在2020年的崛起及其在资本市场上倡导散户投资者的成功。PrimaryBid的技术处于金融服务领域强大趋势-监管、数字化和民主化-的交汇点，OMERS Ventures很高兴支持团队的使命，即让个人投资者与机构处于平等地位。”PrimaryBid现在已经很好地融入了英国融资生态系统的最高级别，我们期待着帮助这个团队实现他们在国际上的雄心壮志。“
