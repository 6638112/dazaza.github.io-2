Great Britain's Electricity System Has Greenest Day Ever Over Easter
英国的电力系统在复活节中有最温馨的一天

2021-04-07 12:05:45

Great Britain’s electricity system recorded its greenest ever day over the Easter bank holiday as sunshine and windy weather led to a surge in renewable energy.

英国的电力系统在复活节银行假期中记录了最近的一天，因为阳光和刮风的天气导致可再生能源激增。

The power plants generating electricity in England, Scotland and Wales produced only 39g of carbon dioxide for every kilowatt-hour of electricity on Monday, according to National Grid’s electricity system operator, the lowest carbon intensity recorded since National Grid records began in 1935.

根据国家电网的电力系统运营商，苏格兰和威尔士发电仅生产电力，苏格兰和威尔士仅生产了每千瓦的电量的二氧化碳二氧化碳，这是1935年全国网格记录开始以来的最低碳强度。

The new low smashed the grid’s previous record of 46g on 24 May last year, during the country’s greenest ever month for electricity generation.

新的低粉碎了该网格以前的46克历史，于去年5月24日，在该国最温馨的电力发电期间。

On Easter Monday, wind turbines and solar farms generated 60% of all electricity as households enjoyed a bank holiday lunch. At the same time the UK’s nuclear reactors provided 16% of the electricity mix, meaning almost 80% of the grid was powered from low-carbon sources.

在复活节星期一，风力涡轮机和太阳能电场产生了60％的电力，因为家庭享受了银行假日午餐。与此同时，英国的核反应堆提供了16％的电力组合，这意味着近80％的网格来自低碳来源。

The low-carbon power surge, combined with lower than average demand for electricity over the bank holiday, kept gas-fired power in Great Britain to 10% of the electricity mix and caused the “carbon intensity” of the electricity system to plummet to its lowest on record.

低碳动力浪涌，结合银行假期的电力低于平均需求，将英国的燃气电量保持在电力混合的10％，并导致电力系统的“碳强度”暴跌记录最低。

The UK’s electricity emissions have fallen sharply over the last year owing to the slump in demand for electricity from office blocks, restaurants and hotels which have stood empty for much of the last year due to the Covid-19 pandemic.

由于Covid-19大流行，在去年的电力需求下降，英国的电力排放量在过去的一年中，由于来自办公室街区，餐馆和宾馆的电力下降，这是由于Covid-19大流行的大部分时间。

The electricity system operator said demand for electricity remained 5% lower than usual last month despite the gradual reopening of the economy, from around 20% below normal levels in April last year.

电力系统运营商表示，尽管经济逐步重新开放，但去年4月低于正常水平的大约20％，仍比上个月低于平常的电力需求持续5％。

Last month, wind and solar power made up 24% and 4% of the electricity mix respectively, while gas-fired power plants made up the bulk of Britain’s electricity at 39% of the electricity mix. The carbon intensity was 185g of CO 2/kWh for March, but is expected to fall through the summer months as solar power plays a larger role in meeting the country’s electricity demand.

上个月，风和太阳能分别占22％和4％的电力组合，而燃气发电厂则以39％的电力混合组成了英国电力的大部分电力。 3月份的碳强度为185克二氧化碳/千瓦时，但预计将落下夏季，因为太阳能在满足国家的电力需求方面发挥着更大的作用。

Fintan Slye, a director at the system operator at National Grid, said the latest record was another example of how Britain’s energy system was transforming “at an astonishing rate” as the industry moves away from fossil fuels and begins to harness renewable energy sources.

国家电网系统运营商的董事Fintan Slye表示，最新纪录是英国能源系统如何以惊人的能源改变为单位，因为该行业远离化石燃料并开始利用可再生能源。

Over 2020 as a whole, carbon intensity fell to 181g of CO₂/kWh in part due to the collapse in demand for electricity during the coronavirus lockdowns. But under the UK’s climate targets the electricity system will need to cut its carbon intensity to around 50g of CO 2/kWh in 2030, 10g of CO 2/kWh in 2035, and 2g of CO2/kWh by 2050.

由于冠状病毒锁定期间电力需求崩溃，碳强度超过2020年，碳强度落到181克的二氧化碳/千瓦时。但根据英国的气候目标，电力系统需要将其碳强度降至2030，10g的二氧化碳2 / kWh在2035年，2050℃，2g CO 2 / kWh的Co 2 / kWh约为2050。

“It’s an exciting time, and the progress we’re seeing with these records underlines the significant strides we’re taking towards our ambition of being able to operate the system carbon free by 2025,” Slye said.

“这是一个令人兴奋的时刻，我们通过这些记录所看到的进展强调了我们采取的重要进步，我们朝着我们能够在2025年免费运营系统碳的野心，”Slye说。
