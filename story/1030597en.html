PS5在发布时将缺少一些PS4视频流应用程序

2020-10-23 00:24:09

It seems that many of the video-streaming apps on the PS4 won't be available on the PlayStation 5 at launch. That's a marked contrast to the Xbox Series X, where media apps designed for the Xbox One work seamlessly on the new hardware.

看来PS4上的许多视频流媒体应用程序在发布时并不能在PlayStation5上使用。这与Xbox Series X形成了鲜明对比，Xbox Series X为Xbox One设计的媒体应用程序可以在新硬件上无缝运行。

In a blog post this morning, Sony listed "some of the entertainment apps scheduled to hit the PS5 console on day one." Those include:

在今天早上的一篇博客文章中，索尼列出了一些计划在PS5游戏机首日上市的娱乐应用程序。这些应用程序包括：

The post also lists a few "additional streaming apps" that are simply "coming to PS5" with no mention of timing, including Amazon Prime Video, MyCanal, Hulu, Peacock "and more." While the wording is a little vague, the implication seems clear that these apps, and others not listed, are part of the promised "more apps to come in the future" and are not expected to be ready for the PS5's Nov. 12 launch. (Sony has yet to respond to a request for comment from Ars Technica.)

这篇帖子还列出了几个其他的流媒体应用，它们只是简单地推出了PS5，没有提到时间，包括Amazon Prime Video、myCANAL、Hulu、Peacock&34；等等。虽然措辞有点含糊，但言下之意似乎很清楚，这些应用和其他没有列出的应用，都是PS5&34；未来承诺的更多应用的一部分，预计不会为PS5&12的发布做好准备。(索尼尚未回复Ars Technica的置评请求。)。

The PS4 currently hosts versions of 21 different media-streaming apps. Among the ones not even mentioned in today's PS5 blog post: CBS All Access, CBS News, HBO Max, Crackle, CrunchyRoll, Funimation, NBA, NFL, PlutoTV, Plex, VRV, and Vudu.

PS4目前托管着21个不同版本的流媒体应用。在今天PS5的博客帖子中甚至没有提到的包括：CBS All Access，CBS News，HBO Max，Crackle，Crunchyroll，Funimation，NBA，NFL，PlutoTV，Plex，VRV和Vudu。

By contrast, existing Xbox One media apps just work on the Xbox Series X. According to pre-release testing from Ars' own Sam Machkovech, those apps even load faster on the upcoming console. And Microsoft has recently confirmed that all apps developed under the Universal Windows Platform system on Xbox should "run at parity" on the Series S and Series X without any work on the developer's part.

相比之下，现有的Xbox One媒体应用只能在Xbox Series X上运行。根据Ars&39；Owner Sam Machkovech发布前的测试，这些应用在即将推出的游戏机上的加载速度甚至更快。微软最近确认，在Xbox的通用Windows平台系统下开发的所有应用程序都应该在Series S和Series X上同等运行，而不需要开发者做任何工作。

In the long run, this probably won't be a huge difference between the two consoles. If a media-streaming app was popular enough to attract an audience on the PS4, that app will likely be ported to the PS5 in relatively short order. But in the early days, people who use their PS4 in part as a video-streaming set-top box may find their new PS5 a little lacking.

从长远来看，这可能不会是两款游戏机之间的巨大差异。如果一个流媒体应用程序足够受欢迎，足以在PS4上吸引观众，那么该应用程序很可能会在相对较短的时间内移植到PS5上。但在早期，将PS4部分用作视频流机顶盒的人可能会发现他们的新PS5有点欠缺。

Sony also used today's blog post to announce a specially designed Media Remote for the PS5, currently listed for $29.99 at Best Buy and Target. In addition to basic media and TV control and menu navigation functions, the remote includes quick-launch buttons for Disney+, Netflix, Spotify, and YouTube, as well as a microphone button for voice commands.

索尼还利用今天的博客发布了一款专为PS5设计的媒体遥控器，目前在百思买(Best Buy)和塔吉特(Target)的标价为29.99美元。除了基本的媒体和电视控制以及菜单导航功能外，该遥控器还包括用于Disney+、Netflix、Spotify和YouTube的快速启动按钮，以及用于语音命令的麦克风按钮。
