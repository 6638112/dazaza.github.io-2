Outer Orbit – old school Spacewar-for-one game
外轨道–老派太空战一局游戏

2020-12-26 02:33:43

About Outer Orbit Outer Orbit is an attempt to create a “Spacewar!-for-one” as a tribute to the line of heritage of digital video games. It combines elements of the most classic shooter games, Spacewar! (1961/62) [1], the first known video game on a digital computer, Computer Space (1971) [2] [3] [4], the first coin-operated digital video game, and Asteroids (1979) [5]:

关于外轨道外轨道是一种尝试创造“太空大战！一对一”的形式，以向数字视频游戏的传统路线致敬。它结合了最经典的射击游戏太空战的元素！ （1961/62）[1]，第一个已知的数字计算机视频游戏，《 Computer Space》（1971）[2] [3] [4]，第一个投币式数字视频游戏，以及Asteroids（1979）[5] ]：

From “ Spacewar!” it borrows the comic book spaceship, the central star, gravity and orbital mechanics,

摘自《太空大战！》它借用了漫画书的宇宙飞船，中央恒星，重力和轨道力学，

from “ Computer Space” the game mechanics of the saucer opponents and the time-based gameplay,

来自“计算机空间”中飞碟对手的游戏机制和基于时间的游戏玩法，

from “ Asteroids”, well, the asteroids, which serve here just as additional obstacles to make the game a bit trickier.

来自“小行星”，好吧，小行星，在这里充当了其他障碍，使游戏变得有些棘手。

Note: A wave has a fixed duration of 80 seconds. You may progress to the next wave/level, if you manage to outscore the saucers in a given level, i.e. shoot the saucers more often than your ship crashes, by this maintaining a positive balance. Mind that the balance is reset to zero at the start of each level.

注意：波浪的固定持续时间为80秒。如果您设法在给定水平上超越飞碟，则可以前进到下一个波浪/等级，即通过保持正平衡，射击飞碟的次数多于船舶坠毁的次数。请注意，在每个级别开始时，余额都会重置为零。

Tip: Like in Spacewar! enter an orbit around the gravitational star by aligning your spaceship perpendicular to its center and firing your thruster for two or three seconds. — Do not attempt to destroy asteroids, dodge them.

提示：就像在太空战争中一样！通过使飞船垂直于其中心对准引力星进入轨道，并发射推进器两到三秒钟。 —不要试图消灭小行星，躲避它们。

Implementation Notes: The sound-scape is for the most a homage to Computer Space, less the background hum. The display font is based on definitions for a PDP-1 font renderer (early 1960s) and implemented in software. In order to bring the iterative machanics of a gravity based game to the modern age of asynchronous rendering, the game is computed internally in fixed time slices of an 1/100 th of a second and the display rendered on demand.

实施说明：音景是对计算机空间的最大敬意，而不是背景嗡嗡声。显示字体基于PDP-1字体渲染器（1960年代初期）的定义，并以软件实现。为了将基于重力的游戏的迭代机制带到异步渲染的现代时代，在内部以1/100秒的固定时间片计算游戏，并按需渲染显示。

Setting an Initial Level: Experienced players may want to select the initial level by specifying a wave as an URL-parameter, as in https://www.masswerk.at/outerorbit/?wave=30 for a game starting at wave #30.

设置初始级别：有经验的玩家可能希望通过将wave指定为URL参数来选择初始级别，如https://www.masswerk.at/outerorbit/?wave=30，对于从Wave＃30开始的游戏 。
