Terray Therapeutics, which specializes in AI-enabled drug discovery, emerges from stealth with a $60M Series A led by Madrona Venture Group
特瑞治疗公司（Terray Therapeutics）是一家专门从事人工智能药物研发的公司，该公司在马德罗纳风险投资集团（Madrona Venture Group）的带领下，以6000万美元的a系列产品秘密脱颖而出

2022-02-17 06:00:43

After three years in stealth mode, a Pasadena biotech startup has emerged with $60 million and, it believes, a new way to create drugs. Terray Therapeutics launched on Tuesday with $60 million in Series A funding led by Madrona Venture Group. A handful of other venture firms also participated in the round, including Pasadena-based Alexandria Venture Investments. Terray had previously raised an unannounced $20 million seed round led by Digitalis Ventures and Two Sigma Ventures—two firms that also invested in the Series A.

经过三年的秘密运作，帕萨迪纳的一家生物技术初创公司以6000万美元的资金脱颖而出，并相信这是一种新的药物研发方式。Terray Therapeutics于周二推出，由Madrona Venture Group牵头，获得6000万美元的A系列资金。其他几家风险投资公司也参与了这轮投资，包括总部位于帕萨迪纳的亚历山大风险投资公司。Terray之前曾在未经宣布的情况下筹集了2000万美元的种子资金，由Digitalis Ventures和两家Sigma Ventures牵头，两家公司也投资了A系列。

The company specializes in AI-enabled drug discovery—a massive emerging market not only for pharmaceutical giants like AstraZeneca, but also the likes of IBM and Google. The area is promising; AI can cut down the time it takes to create a drug, enabling more rapid innovation and potentially faster turnaround times to get therapeutics to the people who need them. As well as AstraZeneca, major pharma companies like Pfizer and Genentech have also partnered with smaller companies deploying AI-enabled techniques, with the aim of creating successful drugs that are less likely to fail in clinical trials.

该公司专门从事人工智能药物发现，这不仅是阿斯利康等制药巨头的巨大新兴市场，也是IBM和谷歌等公司的巨大新兴市场。该地区前景看好；人工智能可以缩短药物研发所需的时间，从而实现更快的创新，并有可能加快周转时间，为需要药物的人提供治疗。除了阿斯利康，辉瑞和基因泰克等大型制药公司也与部署人工智能技术的小型公司合作，目的是开发成功的药物，减少临床试验失败的可能性。

Terray Therapeutics’ computational discovery platform analyzes chemical compounds, puts together different combinations, and assesses which compounds are promising for a specific drug candidate. Most importantly, the platform continually reassesses these compounds based on how well they perform.

Terray Therapeutics的计算发现平台分析化合物，组合不同的组合，并评估哪些化合物有望成为特定候选药物。最重要的是，该平台根据这些化合物的性能不断重新评估它们。

“Even the most powerful artificial intelligence can’t get past insufficient or unclear data,” Terray CEO Jacob Berlin said in a statement. “Current chemical data lacks the scale and quality to enable AI-driven drug development. We’re building a future where the speed, precision and scale of our engine enable us to predictably create drugs that meet urgent patient needs.”

Terray首席执行官雅各布·柏林（Jacob Berlin）在一份声明中说：“即使是最强大的人工智能也无法通过不足或不清楚的数据。”。“目前的化学数据缺乏规模和质量，无法实现人工智能驱动的药物开发。我们正在建设一个未来，在这个未来，我们的引擎的速度、精度和规模使我们能够以可预测的方式创造出满足患者紧急需求的药物。”

The biotech firm said the new funding will go toward advancing its preclinical pipeline and its partnerships.

这家生物技术公司表示，新的资金将用于推进其临床前项目和合作伙伴关系。
