The report also mentions a January launch date for the S21 series, which Android Central can independently confirm.

报道还提到了S21系列的1月发布日期，Android Central可以独立证实这一点。

Leaks around the upcoming Samsung Galaxy S21 (a.k.a. Galaxy S30) are coming thick and fast. Following recent rumors of the phones entering mass-production earlier than ever, leaker Steve Hemmerstoffer has revealed the design of the Galaxy S21 on his Voice page. Hemmerstoffer, who also goes by @OnLeaks, published CAD renders of an S21 with a 6.2-inch display, hole-punch selfie camera and more uniform bezels than the Galaxy S20. These kinds of renders have proven to be accurate in the past, and Hemmerstoffer has a solid track record.

即将推出的三星Galaxy S21(又名。(Galaxy S30)正纷至沓来。最近有传言称Galaxy S21比以往任何时候都更早进入批量生产，泄密者史蒂夫·海默斯托弗在他的语音页面上透露了Galaxy S21的设计。Hemmerstoffer也被称为@OnLeaks，他发表了一份S21的CAD渲染图，它有6.2英寸的显示屏，打孔式自拍摄像头，比Galaxy S20的边框更均匀。这些类型的渲染在过去已经被证明是准确的，Hemmerstoffer有着坚实的记录。

Around the back of the Galaxy S21, there's a new-style camera module that seems to extend from the phone's outer frame. Dimensions are quoted as being 151.7 x 71.2 x 7.9mm (or 9.0mm with the camera bump), making the S30 slightly wider than the S20, but otherwise very close in size to its predecessor.

在Galaxy S21的背面，有一个新型的相机模块，似乎是从手机的外框延伸出来的。尺寸为151.7 x 71.2x 7.9 mm(或相机凸块为9.0 mm)，这使得S30比S20略宽，但在其他尺寸上与其前身非常接近。

In a separate leak, Hemmerstoffer released purported renders of the larger flagship Galaxy S21 Ultra. Like the S20 and Note 20 Ultras, this one has a larger display and a honking camera bump on the back, along with four sensors, a laser autofocus module, and a flash.

在另一起泄密事件中，Hemmerstoffer发布了据称是更大的旗舰机型Galaxy S21 Ultra的渲染。与S20和Note 20 Ultras一样，这款手机的显示屏更大，背面有一个按喇叭的摄像头凸块，还有四个传感器，一个激光自动对焦模块和一个闪光灯。

He suggests that unlike the regular S21, the Ultra has a slightly curved display, though not nearly as severe as the company's 2020 lineup. As for dimensions, he's got numbers: 165.1 x 75.6 x 8.9mm, or 10.8mm thick when the camera bump is included. For comparison, the S20 Ultra measures 166.9 x 76 x 8.8 mm, so expect the S21 ultra to be ever-so-smaller.

他表示，与普通的S21不同，Ultra的显示屏略有弯曲，尽管没有该公司2020年的产品阵容那么严酷。至于尺寸，他得到的数字是：165.1 x 75.6x 8.9 mm，如果算上相机凸块，厚度是10.8 mm。相比之下，S20Ultra的尺寸是166.9 x 76 x 8.8 mm，所以预计S21 Ultra会变得更小。

Finally, the leak suggests that the S21 doesn't have a compartment for the S Pen as previous rumors have suggested, but that it may support Samsung's S Pen, which could be purchased separately or, ideally, come in the box for people who want it.

最后，泄露的消息表明，S21没有像之前的传言所说的那样为S Pen设置隔间，但它可能支持三星的S Pen，可以单独购买，或者最好是盒装的，供想要的人购买。

Hemmerstoffer says the phones are due to arrive earlier than ever for a Galaxy S flagship, specifically in January of 2021. Android Central can independently confirm from our own sources that the next Galaxy S flagships are currently slated to launch in January. We're unclear at this point if there's going to be a middle-of-the-line Galaxy S21 Plus.

Hemmerstoffer表示，Galaxy S旗舰手机的到货时间比以往任何时候都要早，特别是在2021年1月。Android Central可以从我们自己的消息来源独立确认，下一代Galaxy S旗舰目前计划在1月份发布。我们目前还不清楚是否会有一款中端的Galaxy S21 Plus。

A January arrival for the Galaxy S21, combined with a mid-November debut for Apple's top-end iPhone 12 Pro Max, would see the Apple and Samsung flagships launching closer together than ever before. While the immediate post-holiday period isn't typically seen as a great time to launch new products in the West, Samsung may be looking to capitalize on delays to its rival's 2020 handsets with this earlier launch.

Galaxy S21将于明年1月上市，苹果高端iPhone 12 Pro Max将于11月中旬首次亮相，苹果和三星的旗舰产品将比以往任何时候都更加紧密地联系在一起。虽然节后这段时间通常不被认为是在西方发布新产品的好时机，但三星可能希望利用竞争对手2020年的手机延迟发布的机会，提前发布这款手机。
