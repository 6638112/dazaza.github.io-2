“Demand will probably exceed supply”: Nvidia explains RTX 30 shortages
“需求可能会超过供应”：Nvidia解释了RTX 30短缺

2020-12-05 02:38:10

Speaking at the Credit Suisse digital financial services conference, Nvidia Chief Financial Officer Colette Kress addressed and partially explained the recent shortages of the company's new RTX 30-series graphics cards like the GeForce RTX 3080 and  3070.

Nvidia首席财务官Colette Kress在瑞士信贷数字金融服务会议上发表讲话，并部分解释了该公司新的RTX 30系列图形卡如GeForce RTX 3080和3070的近期短缺。

She confirmed that wafer shortages at chip supplier Samsung Foundry are a factor, as many have speculated, but also suggested there is more to it than that. Said Kress:

她证实了芯片供应商三星晶圆厂的晶圆短缺是一个因素，正如许多人所推测的那样，但也暗示了更多的原因。克雷斯说：

We do have supply constraints, and our supply constraints do expand past what we're seeing in terms of wafers and silicon, but yes, some constraints are in substrates and components. We continue to work during the quarter on our supply, and we believe, though, that demand will probably exceed supply in Q4 for overall gaming.

我们确实有供应限制，而且我们的供应限制确实超出了我们在晶片和硅方面看到的范围，但是是的，某些限制在基板和组件中。我们将在本季度继续进行供应方面的工作，尽管如此，但我们相信，第四季度整体游戏的需求可能会超过供应。

She also said that it might be "a couple months" before Nvidia can catch up to demand, but qualified even that by saying "at this time, it's really difficult for us to quantify."

她还说可能要几个月了。在Nvidia赶不上需求之前，但即使说了这一点，现在我们仍然很难量化。

Interest and demand across almost every part of the gaming industry has soared in 2020, presumably as people spend more time at home pursuing hobbies, when they would have previously (in the pre-pandemic world) been out and about. But at the same time, COVID-19 has impacted supply lines for electronics companies. These two factors together have led to many situations where demand has outstripped supply for gaming products like video cards or consoles. Even early in the pandemic, long before the new 30-series RTX cards launched, Nintendo Switch consoles became scarce at retailers around the world because Nintendo struggled to keep up with a sudden, unexpected surge in demand this spring. And like Nvidia's RTX cards, the newly launched PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S game consoles have sold out near-instantly any time they've become available from online retailers, leaving many would-be buyers frustrated.

到2020年，几乎整个游戏行业的兴趣和需求都飙升了，大概是因为人们以前（在大流行前的世界中）早已出没了，而将更多的时间花在家里追求业余爱好。但是同时，COVID-19影响了电子公司的供应线。这两个因素共同导致了许多情况，供不应求的游戏产品如视频卡或游戏机。甚至在大流行初期，就在新的30系列RTX卡推出之前很久，任天堂Switch控制台在全球零售商中就变得稀缺，因为任天堂在今年春天一直在努力应对需求的突然突如其来的激增。与Nvidia的RTX卡一样，新推出的PlayStation 5和Xbox Series X | S游戏机在可以从在线零售商处购买到的任何时候都几乎立即售罄，这使许多潜在的购买者感到沮丧。

"We would characterize the launch and what we're seeing as overwhelming demand," Kress said. "Each day, things continue to improve. But before the end of the quarter, we'll be able to provide some more information."
