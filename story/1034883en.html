欧莱雅推出“虚拟化妆”系列

2020-11-13 19:08:21

The cosmetics world has also seen some rapid change in the past several years as makeup has proven particularly ripe for up-and-coming direct-to-consumer and influencer-endorsed brands to take hold. Plenty of legacy brands have seen their revenues decimated, while others have proven resilient by leaning into new tech and sales channel trends.

在过去的几年里，化妆品世界也经历了一些快速的变化，事实证明，对于新兴的直接面向消费者和有影响力的品牌来说，化妆已经特别成熟。许多传统品牌的收入大幅下降，而其他品牌则通过采用新的技术和销售渠道趋势证明了自己的韧性。

Back in 2018, L’Oréal made the interesting decision to buy an augmented reality filter company called Modiface. Fast-forward to 2020 and they’ve opted to roll out a line of “virtual makeup” selfie filters. The “Signature Face” filters show off eye makeup, lipsticks and hair products from the company.

早在2018年，欧莱雅就做出了一个有趣的决定，收购了一家名为ModiFaces的增强现实过滤器公司。快进到2020年，他们选择推出一系列“虚拟化妆”自拍滤镜。“招牌脸”滤镜展示了该公司的眼妆、口红和美发产品。

They’ve gone fairly wide, with the rollout supporting Instagram, Snapchat, Snap Camera and Google Duo. Snap Camera support in particular enables the selfies to be used across plenty of video chat services like Houseparty and Zoom; L’Oréal is marketing these selfies as a way to spice up your look on video calls specifically. You can check our more details on where you can use the filters on their site.

它们的应用范围相当广，推出的产品支持Instagram、Snapchat、Snap Camera和谷歌二人组(Google Duo)。尤其是Snap摄像头的支持，使得这些自拍可以在Houseparty和Zoom等大量视频聊天服务中使用；欧莱雅将这些自拍作为一种特别的方式在视频通话中为你的外表增添情趣。你可以在他们的网站上查看我们关于在哪里可以使用过滤器的更多详细信息。

In terms of the filters themselves, there’s nothing terribly more advanced about them than the makeup-centric selfie filters that have been floating around Snapchat for years, but it is interesting to see such a substantial brand leaning in so heavily and pitching this idea where people use selfie filters during video calls in a non-gimmicky way. It’s not clear whether the technology or consumer habits are there yet but it’s certainly plausible that things could move in that direction, especially as social media apps begin a more focused drive toward becoming commerce platforms.

就滤镜本身而言，没有什么比Snapchat上流行多年的以化妆为中心的自拍滤镜更先进的了，但有趣的是，看到一个如此强大的品牌如此大力地倾斜，并提出这样的想法，即人们在视频通话中以一种不花哨的方式使用自拍滤镜。目前还不清楚技术或消费者习惯是否已经存在，但事情肯定会朝着这个方向发展，特别是在社交媒体应用开始更专注地成为商务平台的情况下。
