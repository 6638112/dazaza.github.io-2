Every organization has implicit cultural norms and working practices. How HashiCorp Works started as an internal resource to document these practices to help new employees be successful. This encouraged us to make the implicit explicit and turn siloed knowledge into knowledge that could be used by anyone across our organization.

每个组织都有隐含的文化规范和工作实践。 Hishicorp工作如何开始作为内部资源来记录这些做法，以帮助新员工成功。这鼓励我们使隐含的明确明确并将恐怖知识转变为我们组织中的任何人可以使用的知识。

Within How HashiCorp Works you'll find documentation on everything from setting up a successful at-home work environment to participating in HashiCorp's writing culture for making decisions. With over 1,100 HashiCorp employees working together all over the world, we are committed to helping all team members learn our individual, team, and company-wide best practices.

We open sourced How HashiCorp Works in March 2021. We look to other companies’ systems and processes as inspiration material for our own, and open sourcing our own various practices is our way of paying it forward. We recommend that other organizations use How HashiCorp Works as inspiration to find the aspects that make sense for their settings. Every organization is different.

我们开设了2021年3月的Hashicorp如何工作。我们将其他公司的系统和流程视为我们自己的灵感材料，开放我们自己的各种实践是我们向前推进的方式。我们建议其他组织使用Hashicorp如何作为灵感来查找对其设置有意义的方面。每个组织都不同。

Readers will notice that some of the guides reference internal documents; we decided to keep those references to maintain the same document for both internal and external readers. Those internal documents don't provide much additional information, just more prescription, so external readers aren't missing key content by not having access.

读者会注意到一些指南参考内部文件;我们决定将这些参考文献保留以维护内部和外部读者的相同文件。那些内部文件不提供更多的附加信息，只需更具处方，所以外部读者而不是没有访问权限。

We update the guides in How HashiCorp Works every year, as our practices are always evolving. If you have feedback, questions, or want to learn more — you can reach at hhw@hashicorp.com.
