NASA fears SpaceX plan for 30k satellites could hamper space missions
美国国家航空航天局担心SpaceX计划的3万颗卫星可能会妨碍太空任务

2022-02-13 14:25:16

The US agency says Elon Musk’s expansion could flood Earth’s orbit with objects, making flights and observations more difficult

该美国机构表示，埃隆·马斯克的扩张可能会使地球轨道上的物体泛滥，使飞行和观测更加困难

Nasa has raised concerns about SpaceX’s plan to deploy about 30,000 satellites for its Starlink, as have some major companies.

Nasa对SpaceX计划为其Starlink部署约30000颗卫星表示担忧，一些大公司也是如此。

Elon Musk’s SpaceX previously received authorisation for about 12,000 satellites to offer broadband internet and has requested the go-ahead for a second-generation constellation of 30,000 satellites.

埃隆·马斯克（Elon Musk）的SpaceX此前获得了约12000颗卫星提供宽带互联网的授权，并已请求批准第二代30000颗卫星星座。

“Nasa has concerns with the potential for a significant increase in the frequency of conjunction events and possible impacts to Nasa’s science and human spaceflight missions,” the agency said in a submission to the Federal Communications Commission (FCC).

该机构在提交给联邦通信委员会（FCC）的文件中表示：“Nasa担心会合事件的频率可能大幅增加，并可能对Nasa的科学和载人航天任务造成影响。”。

Nasa noted there was currently a total of 25,000 objects being tracked on-orbit – and about 6,100 below 600km. SpaceX’s Gen2 expansion “would more than double the number of tracked objects in orbit and increase the number of objects below 600km over five-fold”, it added.

美国国家航空航天局指出，目前共有25000个物体在轨道上被跟踪，在600公里以下约有6100个。SpaceX的第二代扩展“将使轨道上被跟踪物体的数量增加一倍多，并将600公里以下的物体数量增加五倍以上”，它补充道。

A Harvard-Smithsonian astrophysicist, Jonathan McDowell, part of the American Astronomical Society panel examining the impacts of satellites on astronomy, said: “We’ve been concerned with having these large numbers of satellites that interfere with astronomical observations ... I think we need a little more experience with the several thousand operating satellites before we can ramp up to the tens of thousands.”

哈佛史密森天体物理学家乔纳森·麦克道尔是美国天文学会研究卫星对天文学影响小组的成员，赛义德说：“我们一直担心有这么多干扰天文观测的卫星……我认为我们需要对几千颗运行中的卫星多一点经验，然后才能升级到数万颗。”

Musk tweeted on 15 January that SpaceX had 1,469 Starlink satellites active and 272 moving to operational orbits soon.

马斯克在1月15日发推文称，SpaceX有1469颗Starlink卫星处于活动状态，272颗卫星即将进入运行轨道。

Amazon.com, which has pledged to spend at least $10bn to build 3,236 such satellites through its Project Kuiper program, separately raised concerns with the FCC about SpaceX’s plan, as did the US direct-broadcast satellite provider, Dish Network.

亚马逊。com已承诺通过其“柯伊伯计划”（Project Kuiper program）投入至少100亿美元建造3236颗此类卫星，该公司与美国直播卫星提供商Dish Network一样，分别向FCC提出了对SpaceX计划的担忧。

Amazon said under SpaceX’s application “at least hundreds, and potentially more than 10,000” SpaceX satellites could operate at the same altitudes as the Kuiper system.

亚马逊表示，在SpaceX的应用下，“至少有数百颗，甚至可能超过10000颗”SpaceX卫星可以在与柯伊伯系统相同的高度运行。

It warned “the effect of this orbital overlap would be a dramatic increase in risks and other burdens on the Kuiper system” and asked the FCC to impose “reasonable conditions”.

它警告说，“这种轨道重叠的影响将大幅增加柯伊伯系统的风险和其他负担”，并要求FCC施加“合理条件”。

While extremely costly to deploy, satellite technology can provide high-speed internet for people who live in rural or hard-to-serve places where fiber optic cables and cell towers do not reach. The technology could also be a critical backstop when hurricanes or other natural disasters disrupt communication.

虽然部署成本极高，但卫星技术可以为生活在农村或难以服务的地方的人们提供高速互联网，而这些地方的光缆和手机发射塔无法到达。当飓风或其他自然灾害扰乱通讯时，这项技术也可能成为重要的后盾。
