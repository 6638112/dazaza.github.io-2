Jump to navigation Jump to search Enola Holmes is a 2020 American mystery film based on the first book in the young adult fiction series of the same name by Nancy Springer. The story is about the teenage sister of the already-famous Sherlock Holmes, who goes to London in search of her mother who has disappeared. The film is directed by Harry Bradbeer, from a screenplay by Jack Thorne. Millie Bobby Brown stars as the title character, while also serving as a producer on the film. Henry Cavill, Sam Claflin, Adeel Akhtar, Fiona Shaw, Frances de la Tour, Louis Partridge, Susie Wokoma and Helena Bonham Carter appear in supporting roles.

Originally planned for a theatrical release by Warner Bros. Pictures, the distribution rights to the film were sold to Netflix due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Enola Holmes was released on September 23, 2020. The film received generally positive reviews from critics, who praised Brown's performance.

Enola Holmes is the youngest sibling in the famous Holmes family. She is extremely intelligent, observant, and insightful, and defies the social norms for women of the time. Her mother, Eudoria, has taught her everything from chess to jujitsu and encouraged her to be a strong-willed and independent thinking young woman.

埃诺拉·霍姆斯是著名的福尔摩斯家族中最小的兄弟姐妹。她非常聪明，观察敏锐，富有洞察力，违反了当时女性的社会规范。她的母亲尤多利亚(Eudoria)教会了她从国际象棋到柔术的一切，并鼓励她成为一个意志坚强、独立思考的年轻女子。

On the day of her sixteenth birthday, Enola awakens to find that her mother has disappeared, leaving behind only some birthday gifts. She rushes to the train station to meet her brothers, Mycroft and Sherlock, who fail to recognize her at first, not having seen her in many years. Sherlock finds her to be an intelligent girl, whereas Mycroft finds her troublesome. As her legal guardian, Mycroft intends to send her away to a finishing school run by the stern Miss Harrison. The flowers left by her mother reveal secret messages and lead to hidden money, which Enola uses to escape disguised as a boy. On the train, she finds the young Viscount Tewkesbury hidden in a travel bag. She thinks he is a nincompoop but warns him that a man in a brown bowler hat (named Linthorn) is on the train searching for him. They jump off the train to escape. Neither having any food, Tewkesbury forages for edible plants. They travel to London, where they part ways.

在她16岁生日的那天，埃诺拉醒来发现她的母亲不见了，只留下了一些生日礼物。她冲到火车站去接她的哥哥麦克罗夫特和夏洛克，他们一开始都认不出她来，因为很多年没见过她了。夏洛克觉得她是个聪明的女孩，而麦克罗夫特则觉得她很麻烦。作为她的法定监护人，麦克罗夫特打算把她送到一所由严厉的哈里森小姐开办的精修学校。她母亲留下的鲜花揭示了秘密信息，导致了隐藏的钱，埃诺拉用这些钱伪装成男孩逃脱。在火车上，她发现年轻的图克斯伯里子爵藏在一个旅行袋里。她认为他是个笨蛋，但警告他，一个戴着棕色圆顶圆顶帽子的男人(名叫林松)正在火车上寻找他。他们跳下火车逃跑。图克斯伯里既没有食物，也在寻找可食用的植物。他们前往伦敦，在那里他们分道扬镳。

Disguised as a proper Victorian lady, she continues to trace Eudoria and leaves cryptic messages in the newspaper personal advertisements. Enola discovers pamphlets and a safehouse containing explosives and learns that Eudoria is part of a radical group of suffragettes. She is attacked by Linthorn, who tortures her for information about Tewkesbury, attempting to drown her. They fight, but she is able to ignite the explosives and escape. Enola decides to pause the search for her mother and instead find Tewkesbury again to save him because he is not capable of defending himself. Enola visits the Tewkesbury estate at Basilwether Hall to learn more. Mycroft has Inspector Lestrade of Scotland Yard search for Enola.

她伪装成维多利亚时代的淑女，继续追踪尤多利亚，并在报纸上的个人广告中留下隐晦的信息。埃诺拉发现了小册子和一个藏有炸药的藏身之处，并得知尤多利亚是一个激进的女权主义者组织的一部分。她被林肯袭击，谁折磨她关于图克斯伯里的信息，试图淹死她。他们打了起来，但她点燃了炸药，逃脱了。埃诺拉决定暂停寻找她的母亲，而是再次找到图克斯伯里来救他，因为他没有能力为自己辩护。埃诺拉参观了位于巴西尔韦瑟大厅的图克斯伯里庄园，以了解更多信息。麦克罗夫特让苏格兰场的莱斯特雷德探长寻找埃诺拉。

Enola finds Tewkesbury selling flowers in Covent Garden and warns him of the danger. She helps him escape but is caught by Lestrade and imprisoned in Miss Harrison's finishing school by Mycroft. Sherlock visits her and admits he is impressed by her detective work. Tewkesbury sneaks into the school, and they escape together, stealing Miss Harrison's motor car. They reach a fork in the road and, rather than returning to London, Enola decides they must go to Basilwether Hall and face Tewkesbury's uncle, who she has deduced was trying to kill him. The estate is seemingly deserted, but Linthorn ambushes them, firing a shotgun. Enola trips him using a jujitsu move, causing a fatal head injury. Tewkesbury's grandmother is revealed as the real villain; a traditionalist, she did not want him to take his father's place in the House of Lords and vote for the Reform Bill. She shoots her grandson in the chest, but he survives thanks to a plate of armor he had hidden under his clothes. Sherlock arrives at Scotland Yard and Lestrade asks him two questions: first, how he managed to solve the case, and second, how his sister solved it first.

埃诺拉发现图克斯伯里在考文特花园卖花，并警告他有危险。她帮助哈里森逃跑，但被莱斯特拉德抓住，并被麦克罗夫特囚禁在哈里森小姐的精修学校。夏洛克拜访了她，并承认她的侦探工作给他留下了深刻的印象。图克斯伯里偷偷溜进学校，他们一起逃跑，偷走了哈里森小姐的汽车。他们来到了一个岔路口，埃诺拉没有返回伦敦，而是决定他们必须去巴西韦瑟庄园，面对图克斯伯里的叔叔，她推测图克斯伯里的叔叔试图杀死图克斯伯里。庄园看似空荡荡的，但林桑伏击了他们，开了一把猎枪。埃诺拉用柔术绊倒了他，导致头部致命受伤。图克斯伯里的祖母被揭露为真正的恶棍，她是一个传统主义者，她不想让他接替父亲在上议院的位置，投票支持改革法案。她朝孙子的胸部开了一枪，但孙子幸免于难，这要归功于他藏在衣服下面的一盘盔甲。夏洛克来到伦敦警察厅，莱斯特雷德问了他两个问题：第一，他是如何破案的；第二，他姐姐是如何先破案的。

Enola finds and deciphers a message in a newspaper but deduces that it was not sent by her mother. At the meeting point, Sherlock and Mycroft discuss Enola, and Sherlock suggests becoming her guardian. They decide to leave, and Sherlock notices a clue, but chooses not to look for Enola. All the while Enola has been watching disguised as a newsboy. Returning to her lodgings, Enola finds her mother waiting there. They embrace, and Eudoria explains why she had to leave, and why she must leave again, but she is impressed by what Enola has become. Enola has found her freedom and her purpose—she is a detective and a finder of lost souls.

埃诺拉发现并破译了报纸上的一条信息，但推断这不是她母亲发来的。在会面的地方，夏洛克和麦克罗夫特讨论了埃诺拉，夏洛克建议成为她的监护人。他们决定离开，夏洛克注意到了一条线索，但选择不去找埃诺拉。在此期间，埃诺拉一直伪装成报童在看电视。回到她的住所，埃诺拉发现她的母亲在那里等着。他们拥抱在一起，尤多利亚解释了为什么她必须离开，为什么她必须再次离开，但她对埃诺拉变成的样子印象深刻。伊诺拉找到了她的自由和她的目标--她是一名侦探，也是迷失灵魂的寻求者。

By February 2019, a film adaptation of the Nancy Springer book series The Enola Holmes Mysteries was in development at Legendary Pictures, with Millie Bobby Brown producing and starring in the title role and Harry Bradbeer set to direct. [3] Brown had read the books with her older sister Paige and immediately wanted to play the role of Enola, but wasn't yet old enough to play the character. She later told her father they should make it into a film and partnered with Legendary Pictures, the same company she had previously worked with on Godzilla: King of the Monsters. Brown and Bradbeer discussed the film and wanted energy, emotion and eccentricity as key elements of the film. [4] [5] Brown discussed with writer Jack Thorne how she wanted to break the fourth wall. [5] [6] Bradbeer was enthusiastic about the script as it combined his interest in stories about "dysfunctional families coming to terms with each other" with his love of Sherlock Holmes. [4] Brown took the opportunity to improvise, something she would not do on Stranger Things, which is heavily scripted and because the character of Enola Holmes allowed for it. Capturing those moments and keeping up with Brown proved challenging for the focus puller. [5]

截至2019年2月，传奇影业正在开发改编自南希·斯普林格(Nancy Springer)系列小说《埃诺拉·福尔摩斯之谜》(The Enola Holmes Mysteries)的电影，米莉·鲍比·布朗(Millie Bobby Brown)担任片名角色并主演，哈里·布拉德比尔(Harry Bradmer)将执导。[3]布朗和姐姐佩吉一起读了这本书，她马上就想要扮演埃诺拉这个角色，但她还没有长大到可以扮演这个角色的年龄。后来，她告诉父亲，他们应该把这部电影拍成电影，并与传奇影业(Legendary Pictures)合作，这家公司之前曾与她合作过《哥斯拉：怪兽之王》(Godzilla：King of the Monsters)。布朗和布拉德比尔讨论了这部电影，并希望能量、情感和古怪成为这部电影的关键元素。[4][5]布朗与作家杰克·索恩(Jack Thorne)讨论了她想如何打破第四堵墙。[5][6][6]布拉德比尔对这部剧本非常感兴趣，因为它将他对病态家庭相互妥协故事的兴趣与他对夏洛克·福尔摩斯的热爱结合在了一起。[4]布朗利用这个机会即兴发挥，这是她在《奇怪的事情》中不会做的事情，因为这部剧有大量的剧本，因为埃诺拉·福尔摩斯(Enola Holmes)这个角色允许这样做。事实证明，捕捉到这些时刻并跟上布朗的脚步对吸引焦点的人来说是具有挑战性的。[5]。

Consolata Boyle was responsible for creating the costumes. Boyle and her team custom-designed, dyed and made the costumes for the main cast and background actors. Approximately thirty costumes were created for Enola, and costumes such as the red powderpuff dress had to be repeated several more times because of the physicality of the role and any damage that might occur during fight scenes. The powderpuff dress was based on theatrical costumes of the period, and Boyle wanted to use red, the color of courage. Boyle's favorite was Enola's final dress, it used the same shape as the first dress Enola wore while riding the bicycle bringing the costumes back full circle, but also the natural untreated silk used in the final dress has no strong color and "it allows for anything to happen next." [18] [19] The colors violet, green and ivory were associated with the suffragette movement so Boyle consciously made use of those colors in the costumes. [20]

康索拉塔·博伊尔(Consolata Boyle)负责制作这些服装。博伊尔和她的团队为主要演员和背景演员定制设计、染色和制作服装。大约有30套服装是为埃诺拉制作的，而像红色粉扑连衣裙这样的服装不得不重复几次，因为角色的肉体和在打斗场景中可能出现的任何损害。粉扑连衣裙是以那个时期的戏剧服装为基础的，而博伊尔想要使用红色，这是勇气的颜色。博伊尔最喜欢的是埃诺拉的最后一件连衣裙，它与埃诺拉骑自行车时穿的第一件连衣裙的形状一样，使服装绕了一圈，而且最后一件连衣裙中使用的未经处理的天然丝绸没有浓重的颜色，它允许任何事情发生。紫罗兰、绿色和象牙的颜色与女权主义运动联系在一起，所以博伊尔有意识地在服装中使用了这些颜色。[18][18][18][18][18][18][19]紫罗兰、绿色和象牙的颜色与女权运动联系在一起，所以博伊尔有意识地在服装中使用了这些颜色。[20]。

The Conan Doyle Estate filed a lawsuit against Netflix over the film, claiming it violates copyright by depicting Sherlock Holmes as having emotions. They argue this aspect of the character does not fall under the public domain as he was only described as having emotions in stories published between 1923 and 1927, and the copyright for the stories published in that period still have not expired under copyright law in the United States. [24] [25]Cavill said that his portrayal of Sherlock "a lot more emotional to begin with, so we pared it back, and we said, 'alright, let's not make it too emotional'." On the lawsuit he said "It's a character from a page which we worked out from the screenplay. The legal stuff is above my pay grade." [26]On October 30, 2020, lawyers for the defendants filed a motion to dismiss, saying the estate was unfairly attempting to prevent the fair use of characters that are "undeniably in the public domain". [27]

柯南·道尔遗产管理公司(Conan Doyle Estate)就这部电影对Netflix提起诉讼，称该片将夏洛克·福尔摩斯(Sherlock Holmes)描绘成有感情的人，侵犯了版权。他们认为，这个角色的这一方面不属于公共领域，因为他只被描述为在1923年至1927年之间出版的故事中有情感，根据美国版权法，在那个时期出版的故事的版权仍然没有到期。[24][25]卡维尔说，他对夏洛克的刻画从一开始就更加情绪化，所以我们削减了它，我们说，好的，让我们不要让它太情绪化。在诉讼中，他说这是我们从剧本中找出的一页中的一个人物。(编者注：原文为《神探夏洛克》，原文为《神探夏洛克》。)。法律方面的事情超出了我的能力范围。2020年10月30日，被告的律师提交了驳回诉讼的动议，称遗产管理公司不公平地试图阻止那些不可否认属于公共领域的人物的合理使用。[26]2020年10月30日，被告的律师提交了驳回申请的动议，称该遗产不公平地试图阻止对不可否认属于公共领域的人物的合理使用。[27][27]。

Originally intended to be a theatrical release by Warner Bros. Pictures, in April 2020, Netflix acquired distribution rights to the film due to the COVID-19 pandemic. [30] It was digitally released on September 23, 2020. [31]

Netflix原本打算由华纳兄弟影业公司在影院上映，但在2020年4月，由于新冠肺炎疫情的蔓延，Netflix获得了该片的发行权。[30]它于2020年9月23日以数字形式发布。[31][31]。

To promote the film in the United Kingdom, Netflix installed a series of statues of famous sisters next to existing statues of their famous brothers. [32] They also released a free play at home adventure game in collaboration with Escape Hunt UK called An Enola Holmes Adventure. [33]

为了在英国宣传这部电影，Netflix在她们著名兄弟的现有雕像旁边安装了一系列著名姐妹的雕像。[32]他们还与Escape Hunt UK合作推出了一款免费的家庭探险游戏，名为Enola Holmes Adenture。[33][33]。

In its debut weekend Enola Holmes was the second most-watched item on Netflix, behind the TV series Ratched. [34] Over its first five days it was the most watched film on Netflix, [35] [36] as the film would go on to become the biggest first day opening Netflix title in 2020 as well as dominating the largest number of Netflix's monitored countries. [37] In October 2020, Netflix reported the film was on-track to have 76 million households watch the film over its first four weeks of release. [38] In November, Variety reported that according to data by ScreenEngine/ASI the film was the 13th-most watched straight-to-streaming title of 2020 up to that date. [39]

在首映周末，埃诺拉·福尔摩斯是Netflix上收视率第二高的节目，仅次于电视剧《Ratch》。[34]在上映的头五天里，它是Netflix上观看人数最多的电影，[35][36]因为这部电影将成为Netflix 2020年首日上映的最大影片，同时也占据了Netflix监控的国家数量最多的国家。[37]2020年10月，Netflix报道称，这部电影上映后的前四周将有7600万家庭观看。[38]去年11月，《综艺》报道称，根据ScreenEngine/ASI的数据，截至目前，这部电影在2020年收视率最高的直播剧标题中排名第13位。[39][font=宋体]。

On Rotten Tomatoes, the film has an approval rating of 91% based on 179 reviews, with an average rating of 7.04/10. The website's critics consensus reads: " Enola Holmes brings a breath of fresh air to Baker Street – and leaves plenty of room for Millie Bobby Brown to put her effervescent stamp on a franchise in waiting." [40] On Metacritic, it has a weighted average score of 68 out of 100, based on reviews from 30 critics, indicating "generally favorable reviews". [41]

在烂番茄上，这部电影在179条评论的基础上获得了91%的好评率，平均评分为7.04/10。该网站的评论家一致认为：埃诺拉·霍姆斯(Enola Holmes)给贝克街带来了一丝新鲜空气--给米莉·鲍比·布朗(Millie Bobby Brown)留下了很大的空间，让她在等待中的系列电影上打上她热情洋溢的印记。[40]在Metacritic上，根据30位评论家的评论，该片的加权平均得分为68分(满分100分)，这表明&。[41][font=宋体]。

Peter Debruge of Variety called the film an "entertaining franchise starter" and praised Brown's performance, stating that "[her] acting style recalls the effusive spontaneity Keira Knightley brought to Pride and Prejudice, shattering the straitlaced propriety of so many Jane Austen adaptations before it." Debruge found the film "more tasteful in its high-energy storytelling than Guy Ritchie's recent Sherlock Holmes movies, and considerably more fun than [2019]'s Nancy Drew reboot." [42] John DeFore of The Hollywood Reporter gave the film a positive review and wrote: "It successfully imagines a place for its heroine in Holmes' world, then convinces young viewers that Enola needn't be constrained by that world's borders." [43] Ann Hornaday of The Washington Post wrote: " Enola Holmes offers brisk and exuberant escape from the heaviness of modern times, with its leading actress lending her own appealing touches to the journey. When the game is afoot, she's more than capable, not just of keeping up, but winning the day." [44] Ella Kemp of Empire magazine wrote: "Well-intentioned if sometimes lacking in subtlety, Enola Holmes offers a fine, spirited reminder that a traditional story can always be retold – although it might need more refined teachings on feminism next time." [45] Peter Bradshaw of The Guardian gave a mixed review, saying: "It all rattles along amiably enough. Enola Holmes is the kind of all-star production that might once have been made by the BBC" but had some criticism for the mystery elements of the story "there should have been more specifically ingenious deducting and solving from Enola – codebreaking isn't the same thing." [46]

《综艺》杂志的彼得·德布鲁奇称这部电影是一部有趣的系列电影，并赞扬了布朗的表演，称她的表演风格让人想起凯拉·奈特莉给《傲慢与偏见》带来的热情自发性，打破了之前许多简·奥斯汀改编的电影那种拘谨的礼仪。德布鲁热认为这部电影在讲述故事时比《盖伊》更有品味。[42]《好莱坞报道》的约翰·德福尔对这部电影给予了肯定的评价，他写道：《福尔摩斯》成功地想象了女主角在福尔摩斯世界中的一席之地，然后让年轻观众相信，埃诺拉不需要被那个世界的边界所束缚。《华盛顿邮报》的安·霍纳迪写道：埃诺拉·霍姆斯与女主角埃诺拉·霍尔摩斯一起，轻快而充满活力地逃离了现代的繁重。当比赛进行时，她的能力更强，不仅能跟上形势，还能赢得胜利。《帝国》杂志的埃拉·坎普写道：她的用意是好的，但有时缺乏微妙之处，埃诺拉·霍姆斯精辟地提醒人们，传统故事总是可以重述的--尽管下一次可能需要更精致的女权主义教义。《卫报》的彼得·布拉德肖给出了褒贬不一的评价。埃诺拉·福尔摩斯是那种曾经可能由BBC制作的全明星作品，但由于故事中的神秘元素受到了一些批评，应该有更巧妙的演绎和从埃诺拉解密不是一回事中更巧妙地解决这一问题。(译者注：埃诺拉·福尔摩斯是一部全明星制作的电影，但因故事中的神秘元素而受到一些批评，因此应该更巧妙地演绎和解决埃诺拉的密码破译不是一回事。)。

In a negative review, Mick LaSalle of the San Francisco Chronicle wrote: "A bright young actress, a movie-star actor and a potentially interesting concept gets smothered in 128 minutes of colorful, empty nonsense." [47]

在一篇负面评论中，《旧金山纪事报》的米克·拉萨勒写道：一个聪明的年轻女演员，一个电影明星演员，一个潜在的有趣的概念，在128分钟的时间里被扼杀了。

In September 2020, producer and star Millie Bobby Brown and director Harry Bradbeer acknowledged their intentions to develop a sequel. [5] Brown thought only about making the first film but loved playing the character and said it would be a dream to do it again. [48]
