GitLab封锁了伊朗人的访问

2020-10-25 23:04:52

On 3rd Oct. 2020 GitLab blocked Iranians’ access (based on IP) without any prior notice! and five days later (8th Oct.) my friend’s account blocked and still he doesn’t have any access to his projects! even after creating a ticket and asks for a temporary access to only export his projects! GitLab refused to unblock him! (screenshot in appendix). My friend is not the only one who blocked by GitLab, with a simple search on the web you can find a growing list of blocked accounts. So I decided to move from GtiLab and EVERY Free Software based/hosted/managed on/in USA.

2020年10月3日，GitLab在没有任何事先通知的情况下屏蔽了伊朗人的访问(基于IP)！五天后(10月8日)。我朋友的账户被封锁了，但他仍然无法访问他的项目！即使在创建了票证并要求临时访问后也只能导出他的项目！GitLab拒绝解锁他！(附录中的屏幕截图)。我的朋友并不是唯一一个被GitLab屏蔽的人，只要在网上简单地搜索一下，你就会发现越来越多的被屏蔽的账户。所以我决定离开GtiLab和所有基于/托管/管理在美国的自由软件。

GitLab is not the only actor in this discrimination against Persian/Iranian people, we also blocked by GitHub, Docker, NPM, Google Developer, Android, AWS, Go, Kubernetes and etc.

GitLab并不是这种歧视波斯/伊朗人的唯一参与者，我们也被GitHub、Docker、NPM、Google Developer、Android、AWS、Go、Kubernetes等屏蔽。

But I believe in Freedom and Free Software. I believe Free Software is a SOCIAL MOVEMENT and I hope …

但我相信自由和自由软件。我相信自由软件是一场社会运动，我希望…。
