Linus Torvalds wants Apple’s new M1-powered Macs to run Linux
Linus Torvalds希望苹果的新型M1驱动的Mac运行Linux

2020-11-24 23:56:34

Earlier this month,  Apple revealed its own ARM-based M1 processor, along with new MacBooks and a desktop Mac Mini powered by this chip. Reviewers across the globe have been praising Apple‘s first attempt, giving it high marks for performance and battery life.

本月初，苹果发布了自己的基于ARM的M1处理器，新的MacBook和由该芯片提供动力的台式Mac Mini。全球的评论家一直赞扬Apple的首次尝试，为其在性能和电池寿命方面给予了很高的评价。

All this positive coverage has tempted many to take the plunge and buy one of the new machines — even if some apps are not running natively at the moment. Even Linus Torvalds, the principal developer of the Linux kernel,  wants one. However, he wants it to run Linux:

所有这些积极的覆盖面吸引了许多人尝试购买一台新机器，即使某些应用当前未在本地运行。甚至Linux内核的主要开发人员Linus Torvalds都想要一个。但是，他希望它运行Linux：

I’d absolutely love to have one, if it just ran Linux.. I have fairly fond memories of the 11″ Macbook Air (I think 4,1) that I used about a decade ago (but moved away from because it took Apple too long to fix the screen – and by the time they did, I’d moved on to better laptops, and Apple had moved on to make Linux less convenient).

如果它只运行Linux，我绝对会喜欢的。.我对大约十年前使用的11英寸Macbook Air（我认为是4,1）记忆犹新（但后来因为它花了苹果而放弃了）修复屏幕的时间太长了-到那时为止，我已经转向了更好的笔记本电脑，而苹果已经开始使Linux变得不那么方便了。

Answering to question in the Real World Tech forum, Torvalds said that while Apple runs its servers and cloud services on Linux, its computers don’t run it natively. He said that he’s been wanting an ARM-based Linux laptop for a while, but  he doesn’t want to have to mess with Apple’s new laptop for it to run Linux:

在现实世界技术论坛中回答问题时，托瓦尔兹说，尽管苹果公司在Linux上运行服务器和云服务，但其计算机并不是在本地运行的。他说，一段时间以来，他一直在想要一台基于ARM的Linux笔记本电脑，但是他不想因为要运行Linux而不得不与苹果的新笔记本电脑搞混：

I’ve been waiting for an ARM laptop that can run Linux for a long time. The new Air would be almost perfect, except for the OS. And I don’t have the time to tinker with it, or the inclination to fight companies that don’t want to help.

我一直在等待可以长时间运行Linux的ARM笔记本电脑。除了操作系统外，新版Air几乎是完美的。而且我没有时间去修补它，也没有时间去与那些不想提供帮助的公司抗争。

Linux support on MacBooks would’ve made it a more attractive bet for programmers.  However, I don’t think any engineers at the Cupertino campus plan to make that happen anytime soon. Sorry, Linus.

MacBook对Linux的支持使它对程序员来说更具吸引力。但是，我认为Cupertino园区的任何工程师都不会计划很快实现这一目标。抱歉，莱纳斯。
