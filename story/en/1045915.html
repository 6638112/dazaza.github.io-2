California has the most vaccines of any US state; it has used only 27% of them
加利福尼亚拥有美国所有州中最多的疫苗； 它只使用了其中的27％

2021-01-23 06:57:52

California has more doses of the COVID-19 vaccine than any other U.S. state, with 2.8 million received since Jan. 11, according to data from the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.

根据美国疾病控制和预防中心的数据，自1月11日以来，加利福尼亚州接种COVID-19疫苗的剂量比美国其他任何州都要多。

This makes sense, as California is the most populous state in the country with about 40 million residents.

这是有道理的，因为加利福尼亚州是该国人口最多的州，约有4000万居民。

But what doesn't make sense is the Golden State is getting its allotment into the arms of citizens more slowly than other states.

但是没有道理的是，金州比其他州更慢地将其分配给公民。

Other states — including ones with massive populations such as Texas and New York — are moving more quickly, and their vaccine rates (number of doses administered per 100,000 people) are much higher. As of Monday, California ranks 42nd in terms of administering the vaccine.

其他州-包括得克萨斯州和纽约州等人口众多的州-迁移速度更快，其疫苗接种率（每10万人施用的疫苗数量）要高得多。截至周一，加利福尼亚在接种疫苗方面排名第42位。

South Dakota has the highest vaccine rate with 5,451 doses administered per 100,000. West Virginia is second with 5,376, and North Dakota third with 5,100. By comparison, California is administering 1,981 doses per 100,000.

南达科他州疫苗接种率最高，每100,000人注射5,451剂疫苗。西弗吉尼亚州以5,376位居第二，北达科他州以5,100位居第三。相比之下，加利福尼亚州每100,000人服用1,981剂。

Here's a look at the vaccine rates in the 10 most populations states, listed in order of highest to lowest rates.

这是在人口最多的10个州中疫苗接种率的排名，按最高到最低的顺序列出。

California Gov. Gavin Newsom vowed in a press briefing Monday to speed up the state's vaccine rollout and pledged 1 million shots will be administered by Sunday, Jan. 17, more than doubling what’s been done so far.

加利福尼亚州州长加文·纽瑟姆（Gavin Newsom）周一在新闻发布会上发誓要加快该州疫苗的推出速度，并承诺将在1月17日（周日）实施100万支疫苗注射，是迄今为止的一倍。

That effort will require what Newsom called an “all-hands-on-deck approach,” including having vaccinations dispensed by pharmacists and pharmacy technicians, dentists, paramedics and emergency medical technicians, and members of the California National Guard.

这项工作将需要Newsom所谓的“全力以赴”的方法，包括由药剂师和药学技术人员，牙医，医护人员和紧急医疗技术人员以及加利福尼亚国民警卫队的成员进行疫苗接种。

The state is transforming baseball stadiums, fairgrounds and even a Disneyland Resort parking lot into mass vaccination sites.

该州正在将棒球场，露天市场，甚至迪士尼乐园度假区的停车场改造成大规模的疫苗接种场所。

It took six months for the nation's most populous state to reach 10,000 deaths but barely a month to jump from 20,000 to 30,000 deaths. California ranks third nationally for COVID-19-related deaths, behind Texas and New York, which is No. 1 with nearly 40,000.

美国人口最多的州花了六个月的时间才达到10,000例死亡，但是从20,000例死亡增加到30,000例仅用了一个月的时间。加利福尼亚州与COVID-19相关的死亡人数在全国排名第三，仅次于德克萨斯州和纽约，排名第一，有近40,000人。

Public health officials have estimated about 12% of those who catch the virus will require hospital care, usually several weeks after infection as they get sicker.

公共卫生官员估计，感染这种病毒的人中约有12％需要住院治疗，通常是在感染后几周，因为他们病了。

Editor's note: This story has been updated to reflect that California has administered 27% of its vaccine doses and ranks 42nd in vaccine distribution.

编者注：这个故事已经更新，以反映加利福尼亚已经接种了27％的疫苗剂量，在疫苗分配中排名第42位。
