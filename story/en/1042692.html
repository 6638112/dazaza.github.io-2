ESPN+ is raising its annual fee for new subscribers from $49.99 to $59.99; renewals for existing subscribers will remain $49.99 through March 2, 2021
ESPN +将新订户的年费从49.99美元提高到59.99美元； 到2021年3月2日，现有用户的续订费用仍为49.99美元

2021-01-01 08:15:28

fans are going to pay more to catch the promoter’s premiere fights on Disney’s Plus streaming sports package next year.

明年，粉丝们将花更多的钱在迪士尼Plus视频流体育节目中观看主持人的首映式。

As of Jan. 8, 2021, the price of UFC pay-per-view events on ESPN Plus — the exclusive home of the mixed martial arts fights in the U.S. — will increase by $5 apiece, from $64.99 to $69.99. In addition, the price of an annual subscription for new subscribers will go up 20%, rising from $49.99 to $59.99 per year. According to ESPN, renewals of annual subscriptions for existing subscribers will remain at $49.99 until at least March 2, 2021.

自2021年1月8日起，ESPN Plus上的UFC按次观看比赛的价格（美国混合武术格斗的独家所在地）的价格将每次上涨5美元，从64.99美元增至69.99美元。此外，新订阅者的年度订阅价格将上涨20％，从每年49.99美元上涨至59.99美元。根据ESPN的数据，现有用户的年度订阅续费将保持在49.99美元，直到至少2021年3月2日。

It’s the second time ESPN Plus has raised the price of UFC PPVs: When the service first launched in April 2018, the UFC PPV events cost $59.99. That went up to $64.99 each at the beginning of 2020.

这是ESPN Plus第二次提高UFC PPV的价格：当该服务于2018年4月首次推出时，UFC PPV活动的费用为59.99美元。到2020年初，每个价格上涨至64.99美元。

The moves signal that ESPN feels it has more pricing power with ESPN Plus, after the sports world suffered through pandemic cancelations and postponements in 2020. The price hikes come after ESPN Plus’ monthly price plan increased to $5.99 (from $4.99 previously) this summer. (The annual plan, even at the higher $60 rate, represents a 17% savings vs. the monthly plan.) ESPN Plus will remain $5.99/month, and the price of Disney’s triple-play bundle of Disney Plus, ESPN Plus and Hulu will stay at $12.99/month for now. However, as of March 26, 2021, Disney Plus pricing in the U.S. will increase by a dollar to $7.99 per month, when the bundle will go up to $13.99 per month.

此举表明，在体育界在2020年因大流行取消和推迟而遭受苦难之后，ESPN认为它具有ESPN Plus的更多定价权。价格上涨是在ESPN Plus的每月价格计划从今年夏天的4.99美元（从之前的4.99美元）提高到此之后。 （即使按每月60美元的较高费率计算，年度计划也比每月计划节省了17％。）ESPN Plus将保持每月5.99美元的价格，迪士尼的三重播放迪士尼Plus，ESPN Plus和Hulu的价格将暂时保持每月$ 12.99。但是，从2021年3月26日起，迪士尼在美国的定价将增加一个美元，达到每月7.99美元，而当捆绑销售价格将上升到每月13.99美元时。

As of Dec. 2, ESPN Plus had 11.5 millions subscribers, nearly doubling in the past year, Disney announced at its investor day conference this month.

迪士尼在本月的投资者日会议上宣布，截至12月2日，ESPN Plus拥有1150万订户，在过去一年中几乎翻了一番。

ESPN Plus has advertised that it offers more than 12,000 live events annually, although the COVID-19 pandemic diminished the schedule for 2020. Live sports programming on ESPN Plus includes UFC Fight Nights and PPV events; MLS; NHL; MLB; Top Rank Boxing; Bundesliga, Serie A, FA Cup and English Premier League soccer; PGA Tour; and U.S. Open Tennis.

ESPN Plus广告宣传其每年提供超过12,000个现场赛事，尽管COVID-19大流行减少了2020年的时间表。ESPNPlus上的现场体育节目包括UFC搏击之夜和PPV活动； MLS； NHL；美国职业棒球大联盟顶级拳击；德甲，意甲，足总杯和英超足球；美巡赛和美国网球公开赛

In addition to live sports, ESPN Plus includes thousands of hours of on-demand content including original shows and series, documentaries, features, classic events and select replays.

除现场体育节目外，ESPN Plus还包括数千小时的点播内容，包括原创节目和系列，纪录片，功能，经典事件和精选重播。
