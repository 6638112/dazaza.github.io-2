G2, which crowdsources enterprise software reviews and helps paying customers glean insights, raises $157M Series D at a $1B+ valuation
G2，众群企业软件审查和帮助客户收藏洞察力，为1亿美元+估价提高157米系列D.

2021-06-22 20:57:49

Business software intelligence marketplace G2 (formerly known as G2 Crowd) has raised $157 million in a series D round of funding.

商业软件智能市场G2（以前称为G2人群）在D系列资金中筹集了15700万美元。

Founded out of Chicago in 2012, G2 is probably better known as a sort of “ Yelp for business software,” serving as a public-facing portal to garner insights, feedback, and reviews spanning thousands of popular applications. This includes the ability to compare-and-contrast similar software to help reach a buying decision.

在2012年建立出芝加哥，G2可能被称为“商业软件的eLELP”，作为一个朝向Garner Insights，反馈，反馈和跨越数千家流行应用程序的Portal的门户网站。这包括比较和对比类似软件来帮助达到购买决定的能力。

According to G2 cofounder and CEO Godard Abel, a recent survey it carried out revealed that 94% of enterprise software buyers “don’t trust vendor sales and marketing people,” a statistic that could probably apply to just about any software vertical across the business and consumer spheres.

根据G2 Cofounder and Ceo Godard Abel，它进行了最近进行的，揭示了94％的企业软件买家“不相信供应商销售和营销人员”，这是一个可能适用于所有业务垂直的软件的统计数据和消费球体。

“G2 brings trust and transparency to the enterprise software buying process,” Abel told VentureBeat. “Software buyers now rely on trusted peer reviews for everything they shop for as consumers — this is increasingly true for B2B software purchases.”

“G2为企业软件购买过程带来了信任和透明度，”Abel告诉Venturebeat。 “软件买家现在依赖于他们作为消费者购物的所有东西的可信同行评审 - 这对B2B软件购买越来越真实。”

Today, G2 claims more than 1.3 million reviews and ratings, with 60 million enterprise software buyers visiting its platform each year.

如今，G2声称超过130万的评论和评级，每年有6000万企业软件买家访问其平台。

On the other side, G2 enables paying companies such as Adobe, Autodesk, HubSpot, Salesforce, and Zoom to manage their online product profile and glean insights such as buyer intent, which helps them identify potential customers and target them with tailored reachout campaigns at the right time. This is enabled by myriad integrations such as LinkedIn, Salesforce, HubSpot, Terminus, Salesloft, and Outreach

另一方面，G2使得Adobe，Autodesk，Hubspot，Salesforce和Zoom等支付公司，以管理他们的在线产品配置文件和更加买家意图的收集洞察力，这有助于他们识别潜在客户，并针对他们的定制覆盖范围适当的时候。这是由LinkedIn，Salesforce，Hubspot，Terminus，Salesloft和OutReach等的Myriad Integation

Internally, companies can also use G2 Track — a result of its 2018 Siftery acquisition — to monitor and manage their SaaS spend, with granular analytics detailing how each piece of software is used by employees and by departments, enabling businesses to cut their spend on underused products.

在内部，公司也可以使用G2曲目 - 其2018年Siftery采集的结果 - 监控和管理他们的SaaS花费，细粒度分析细节员工和各部门如何使用各个软件，使企业能够将其花费削减所削减的业务产品。

It also allows them to track all their various vendor contracts in a centralized hub, which may also help identify duplicate SaaS subscriptions for the same product taken out by different teams.

它还允许他们跟踪集中式集线器中的所有各种供应商合同，这也可能有助于识别不同团队所采取的同一产品的重复的SaaS订阅。

Prior to now, G2 had raised around $100 million, and its latest cash injection was led by Permira’s growth fund, with participation from HubSpot Ventures, Salesforce Ventures, Accel, Emergence, and IVP. Additionally, the company also confirmed that it is now valued at more than $1 billion.

此之前，G2筹集了约1亿美元，最新的现金注入由Permira的增长基金领导，参与Hubspot Ventures，Salesforce Ventures，Excorence和IVP。此外，该公司还确认现已重视10亿美元。

While online reviews have existed since the advent of the web, large businesses looking for software insights and recommendations to power their buying decisions have not always been served in the same way as consumers researching the best restaurants or hotels have been.

虽然在线评论已经存在自网的出现以来，但寻找软件洞察力和向购买决策的建议的大型企业并不总是与研究最佳餐馆或酒店所在的消费者一样。

Traditionally, businesses have often had to pay to access third-party research and reports (e.g. from Gartner), something that G2 helps circumvent with real-time reviews that can be filtered and segmented by company size. This means that a potential enterprise buyer can choose to view ratings purely from other enterprise buyers — this is important, because companies can have entirely different experiences based on their needs and use-cases.

传统上，企业往往不得不支付访问第三方的研究和报告（例如，来自Gartner），G2有助于规避可以通过公司规模过滤和分割的实时评测。这意味着潜在的企业买家可以选择纯粹从其他企业买家查看评级 - 这很重要，因为公司可以根据他们的需求和使用情况完全不同的经验。

“Unlike analyst models, G2’s platform provides real-time data sourced from actual, verified software users,” Abel said. “There is no need to purchase subscriptions for tens of thousands of dollars to access research, or to pay thousands of dollars for access to analysts.”

“与分析师模型不同，G2的平台提供了从实际验证的软件用户提供的实时数据，”Abel说。 “无需购买数万美元的订阅，以访问研究，或支付数千美元以获取分析师。”
