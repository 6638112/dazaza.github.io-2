Toxic admin drives away funding/donations. I attended a university where they severely restricted student organizations from directly getting donations. The university policy was that donors can either donate to the university and get the tax deduction, or donate to the student organizations and not get the tax deductions, but they can't donate to both and still get the university's tax deduction.

The amount of student organizations at that university tumbled down hard because the university was also tight-fisted with giving money to those organizations, especially the more cash-heavy engineering organizations where they can't run enough bake sales and other cutesy stuff to cover their expenses. I'm pretty sure the only ones that got their full needed funding were the ones that could run on minimal budget or generated lots of positive publicity.

这所大学的学生组织数量艰难地跌倒了，因为大学也紧紧闪过这些组织，尤其是他们可以赚钱的最大现金重型工程组织＆＃39; t运行足够的烘烤销售和其他可爱的东西涵盖他们的费用。我很确定唯一一个拥有完整资金的唯一一个可以在最小预算中运行或产生许多积极宣传的那些。

The biggest joke I've seen was when that university gave a brand new SAE Formula Hybrid collegiate team about $3000, and stipulated that they would only get more funding if that team successfully competes that same year's national competition.

最大的笑话我看到的是，当该大学给予了一个全新的SAE公式混合大学团队时，大约3000美元，并规定如果该团队成功竞争同一年，他们只会获得更多资金＆＃39;国家竞争。

They also gave a few hundred dollars to a solar car team. That team of course ceased to exist when they ran out of money just building the frame, which other student engineering organizations promptly cannibalized the parts for their own projects.

他们也给太阳能汽车队给了几百美元。当然，当他们耗尽框架时，该团队不再存在，其他学生工程组织迅速蚕食其自己的项目。
