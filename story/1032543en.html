Dieter Rams：优秀设计的十大原则

2020-11-01 05:22:38

Here at Vitsœ we stand for the inordinate power of good design in everything we do: designing thoughtfully, responsibly and intelligently for our company, our furniture and the many people who share a profound interest in all of our tomorrows.

在VITSœ，我们代表着优秀设计在我们所做的一切中的巨大力量：为我们的公司、我们的家具和许多对我们的未来有着深厚兴趣的人进行深思熟虑、负责任和智慧的设计。

For over 60 years, Vitsœ has worked continuously with Dieter Rams. He still improves his designs to this day and is often cited by a new generation as a key influence on their work that is shaping the 21st century.

60多年来，VITSœ一直与迪特尔·拉姆斯合作。直到今天，他仍然在改进他的设计，并经常被新一代人引用为对他们正在塑造21世纪的工作的关键影响。

“In 1957 I began to develop a storage system that formed the basis of the company Vitsœ, which was founded in 1959. Thus the ideology behind my design is engrained within the company.”

1957年，我开始开发一种存储系统，它构成了VITSœ公司的基础，该公司成立于1959年。因此，我设计背后的思想在公司内部根深蒂固。“。

Back in the late 1970s, Dieter Rams was becoming increasingly concerned by the state of the world around him: “An impenetrable confusion of forms, colours and noises.”

早在20世纪70年代末，迪特尔·拉姆斯(Dieter Rams)就越来越担心他周围的世界状况：“形状、颜色和噪音令人费解的混乱。”

Aware that he was a significant contributor to that world, he asked himself an important question: is my design good design?

意识到他是那个世界的重要贡献者，他问了自己一个重要的问题：我的设计是好的设计吗？

The possibilities for innovation are not, by any means, exhausted. Technological development is always offering new opportunities for innovative design. But innovative design always develops in tandem with innovative technology, and can never be an end in itself.

无论如何，创新的可能性并没有耗尽。技术的发展总是为创新设计提供新的机遇。但是，创新设计总是与创新技术同步发展的，它本身永远不会是目的。

A product is bought to be used. It has to satisfy certain criteria, not only functional, but also psychological and aesthetic. Good design emphasises the usefulness of a product whilst disregarding anything that could possibly detract from it.

产品是买来用的。它必须满足一定的标准，不仅是功能性的，而且是心理的和审美的。好的设计强调产品的实用性，而忽略任何可能减损它的东西。

The aesthetic quality of a product is integral to its usefulness because products we use every day affect our person and our well-being. But only well-executed objects can be beautiful.

产品的审美质量对其实用性是不可或缺的，因为我们每天使用的产品会影响我们的人和我们的福祉。但只有制作精良的物品才能美观。

It clarifies the product’s structure. Better still, it can make the product talk. At best, it is self-explanatory.

它澄清了产品的结构。更好的是，它可以让产品畅所欲言。充其量，它是不言而喻的。

Products fulfilling a purpose are like tools. They are neither decorative objects nor works of art. Their design should therefore be both neutral and restrained, to leave room for the user’s self-expression.

实现目标的产品就像工具。它们既不是装饰品，也不是艺术品。因此，他们的设计应该既中立又克制，为用户的自我表达留出空间。

It does not make a product more innovative, powerful or valuable than it really is. It does not attempt to manipulate the consumer with promises that cannot be kept.

它不会让一个产品比它的实际价值更具创新性、更强大或更有价值。它不会试图用无法兑现的承诺来操纵消费者。

It avoids being fashionable and therefore never appears antiquated. Unlike fashionable design, it lasts many years – even in today’s throwaway society.

它避免了时尚，因此永远不会显得过时。与时尚设计不同的是，它持续了很多年-即使在今天的一次性社会也是如此。

Nothing must be arbitrary or left to chance. Care and accuracy in the design process show respect towards the user.

任何事情都不能武断或听天由命。设计过程中的细心和准确性体现了对用户的尊重。

Design makes an important contribution to the preservation of the environment. It conserves resources and minimises physical and visual pollution throughout the lifecycle of the product.

设计对保护环境做出了重要贡献。在产品的整个生命周期中，它节约了资源，并将物理和视觉污染降至最低。

Less, but better – because it concentrates on the essential aspects, and the products are not burdened with non-essentials.

更少，但更好-因为它集中在基本方面，产品没有非必需品的负担。
