FTP is 50 years old
FTP是50岁

2021-04-16 22:49:50

16 of April 1971 is not only the date where the Rolling Stone first released Brown Sugar, it is also marked with the publication of RFC 114 which mark the birthday of FTP.

1971年4月16日不仅是滚石首次发布的红糖的日期，也标志着FTP生日的RFC 114的出版物。

Back in those days, the Vietnam war is on the forefront of the news, TCP/IP didn’t exist yet, Jimmy Hendrix died 6 months ago, telnet was the new cool kid and some of the most influential rock n roll artists were about to release masterpieces while FTP was using a network protocol called NCP.

回到那些日子里，越南战争是关于新闻的最前沿，TCP / IP不存在，Jimmy Hendrix 6个月前去世，Telnet是新的酷孩子，一些最具影响力的摇滚乐艺术家在FTP使用名为NCP的网络协议时释放杰作。

Over the years, the FTP protocol got refined with 16 different revisions (*1) adding support with TCP/IP, a secure extension also known as FTPS which is leveraging the same tech as HTTPS and more recent addition like IPv6 support.

多年来，FTP协议通过16种不同的修订（* 1）添加了TCP / IP的支持，这是一种安全的扩展，也称为FTPS，它利用与HTTPS相同的技术，更近似的IPv6支持。

50 years after its inception, FTP is still going very strong with millions of FTP server still being exposed on the internet which is fairly amazing considering the bad press it gets from so many people and companies like Dropbox writing on how bad FTP is conflating the protocol to a full-fledge product when the closest alternative to the protocol they have is their proprietary API which is far less flashy than its UI and only usable if Dropbox is kind enough to grant you a key.

成立50年后，FTP仍然非常强大，仍然在互联网上暴露了数百万的FTP服务器，这是考虑到的坏消息，这是从这么多人和像Dropbox写作中的那样糟糕的FTP，因为FTP如何与FTP混淆协议的情况在全氟化物产品中，当他们拥有的协议的最接近的替代品是他们的专有API，它比其UI更不那么柔软，只有在Dropbox是足够友好授予您的键的情况下只有使用。

In 2021, what seems to be acknowledged as progress take the form of proprietary protocols made behind closed doors and without any RFC whatsoever. Instead, vendors wanting to create competing servers are left reverse engineering SDKs as Minio did with S3.

在2021年，似乎被承认的进步采取了封闭门后的专有议定书的形式，没有任何RFC。相反，希望创建竞争服务器的供应商留下倒车工程SDK，因为Minio用S3做了。

Also, how could we touch the topic of FTP without pointing at the most infamous comment on hacker news which has been a major inspiration source when creating Filestash. Indeed, I believe it shouldn’t matter which protocol my mum’s tool is using, as soon as this tool is easy to use, she can transfer those photos she wants to share, open up videos and do all the other things that shouldn’t require her to know about a protocol as our job as engineers is to abstract away all those complicated stuff so that by the magics of abstraction, someone accessing their bank account from the comfort of their browser isn’t expected to pick a cipher when negotiating SSL.

此外，我们如何触及FTP的主题，而不指向黑客新闻中最臭名昭着的评论，这是创建Filestash时的主要灵感源。的确，我相信我的妈妈工具使用哪种协议是不可能使用的，一旦这个工具很容易使用，她就可以转移她想要分享的照片，打开视频并完成所有不应该的东西要求她知道作为我们作为工程师的工作的议定书，以便将所有这些复杂的东西抽象出来，以便通过抽象的魔法，访问他们的银行账户的人从浏览器中的舒适性时，没有预计会在协商SSL时挑选密码。
