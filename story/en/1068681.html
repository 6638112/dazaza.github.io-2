Neeva Announces Public Availability of Its Ads Free, Private Search Engine
Neeva宣布公共可用性自由私人搜索引擎

2021-06-30 01:40:50

Today, we are pleased to announce the availability of our ad-free, private subscription search engine in the United States. Sign up and get three months free, then only $4.95 per month.

今天，我们很高兴地宣布在美国的无广告私人订阅搜索引擎的可用性。注册并免费获得三个月，然后每月只需4.95美元。

The current state of search is broken. Mainstream search engines no longer primarily serve the user. They are littered with advertisements, burying organic search results far below the fold. What started as a well-intentioned way to organize the world’s information has turned into a business focusing most of its resources on monetizing clicks to support advertisers.

当前搜索状态被打破。主流搜索引擎不再主要为用户提供服务。它们具有广告，将有机搜索结果掩盖远低于折叠。以善意的方式组织世界信息的良好方式已经成为一项专注于货币化点击以支持广告商的大部分资源的业务。

Neeva is search reimagined -- ads-free, private, and customizable. This all starts with flipping the traditional business model from an ads-based to a subscription-based service that is 100 percent focused on providing the best search experience for the user. We deliver more relevant results and higher quality content. We block third-party trackers. And by connecting Neeva to email accounts, calendars, and cloud storage platforms, users can surface information that is most valuable to them without sacrificing privacy.

NEEVA是搜索Reimagined - 免费广告，私人和可定制。这一切都开始从广告中的传统商业模式从基于订阅的服务翻转到基于订阅的服务，这是100％的重点是为用户提供最佳搜索体验。我们提供更相关的结果和更高质量的内容。我们阻止了第三方跟踪器。并通过将NEEVA连接到电子邮件帐户，日历和云存储平台，用户可以在不牺牲隐私的情况下对其进行最有价值的表面信息。

Moreover, we support the content ecosystem. Rather than being a toll collector for the web’s content, we are strongly pro-publisher and are committed to creating revenue opportunities for content creators. We will share at least 20% of our topline revenue with content creator partners when their content is used to directly answer a Neeva user’s query.

此外，我们支持内容生态系统。我们不是网络内容的收费者，我们是强烈的Pro-Publisher，并致力于为内容创建者创建收入机会。当他们的内容用于直接回答NEEVA用户查询时，我们将在内容创建者合作伙伴中分享至少20％的Topline收入。

“Neeva was built on the premise that search should focus on the consumer, and only the consumer, not advertisers,” said Sridhar Ramaswamy, CEO, and Co-Founder, Neeva. “Search results should always prioritize finding the best answer to a consumer’s query — not on selling ads or tracking behavior online. Today’s launch of our subscription-based model is the first step in providing a viable search alternative for consumers, built on trust and transparency.

“Neeva建于寻求搜索应该专注于消费者，而且只有消费者，而不是广告商，”Sridhar Ramaswamy，CEO和联合创始人Neeva表示。 “搜索结果应始终优先考虑找到消费者查询的最佳答案 - 而不是在网上销售广告或跟踪行为。今天推出了我们的订阅的模型是为消费者提供可行的搜索替代品的第一步，基于信任和透明度。

We put the user at the center of everything. From online shopping to searching old emails, every feature is designed with the user in mind.

我们把用户放在一切的中心。从网上购物到搜索旧电子邮件，每个功能都与用户设计。

Ad-free: Up to 40% of search results on the major search engine are advertisements. We deliver 100% ad-free, real search results, allowing users to find what they want, without ads getting in the way.

无广告：主要搜索引擎上最多40％的搜索结果是广告。我们提供100％的广告，真实的搜索结果，允许用户找到他们想要的东西，没有广告妨碍了。

Browse safely and privately: Our browser extension, compatible with all major browsers, prevents trackers from following consumers. We never sell or share user data with anyone. It’s part of our privacy promise. And users can search anonymously in incognito mode.

安全私下浏览：我们的浏览器扩展名，与所有主要浏览器兼容，可防止跟踪器以下消费者。我们永远不会向任何人销售或与用户数据分享。这是我们隐私承诺的一部分。用户可以在隐身模式中匿名搜索。

Customize search results: Users can choose the retailers they love and news sources they want to see results from. Neeva users can stay up-to-date on everything from stock picks to the status of packages for delivery on their homepage.

自定义搜索结果：用户可以选择他们喜欢的零售商和他们想要查看结果的新闻来源。 NEEVA用户可以在其主页上提供股票选择的最新信息，以便在其主页上交付的包裹状态。

Get expert recommended shopping results: We allow users to discover the very best products aggregated in one place, and easily read through reviews from reputable sites and verified customers.

获取专家推荐的购物结果：我们允许用户在一个地方发现汇总的最佳产品，并通过信誉良好的网站和验证客户轻松阅读评论。

Search the web — and personal accounts: To make searching even more convenient, Neeva users can sync personal email, calendar, and documents so they can find everything they need from one search bar.

搜索Web - 和个人帐户：为了更方便地进行搜索，NEEVA用户可以同步个人电子邮件，日历和文档，以便他们可以从一个搜索栏中找到所需的一切。

Support content creators with search queries: We are committed to sharing at least 20% of our topline revenue with content creator partners when their content is used to directly answer a Neeva user’s query. Not only are we committed to paying content creator partners, but we are also working on a set of features to make it easy for content creators to establish direct relationships with Neeva users. For example, Neeva users will be able to easily subscribe to email newsletters directly from a search results page. We are already working with Medium and Quora and in the process of establishing robust and diverse partnerships from news to food to travel and beyond.

支持具有搜索查询的内容创建者：当他们的内容用于直接回答NEEVA用户的查询时，我们致力于使用内容创建者合作伙伴共享至少20％的Copline收入。我们不仅致力于支付内容创建者合作伙伴，但我们还在研究一组功能，使内容创建者能够容易地与NEEVA用户建立直接关系。例如，NEEVA用户将能够直接从搜索结果页面订阅电子邮件新闻通讯。我们已经在中等和Quora工作，并在从新闻中建立了强大和多样化的伙伴关系，以便为食物带来旅行和超越。

We provide a browser-based extension, compatible with all major browsers, Chrome, Firefox, Brave, Edge, and Safari. It runs across all devices and is available via an iOS app.

我们提供基于浏览器的扩展，与所有主要浏览器，Chrome，Firefox，勇敢，Edge和Safari兼容。它遍历所有设备，可通过iOS应用程序提供。

Neeva is the world’s first private, ad-free search engine, committed to showing you the best result for every search. We will never sell or share your data with anyone, especially advertisers. Go to neeva.com for a free three-month trial. Following the free trial, you can subscribe for $4.95 per month.

NEEVA是世界上第一个私人广告搜索引擎，致力于向您展示每个搜索的最佳结果。我们永远不会与任何人，尤其是广告商销售或与您的数据分享。转到Neeva.com获得免费的三个月试用。在免费试用后，您可以每月订阅4.95美元。
