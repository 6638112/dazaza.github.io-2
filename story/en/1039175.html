The Zodiac Killer's most uncrackable cipher has, at last, been solved
十二生肖杀手最坚不可摧的密码终于被解决了

2020-12-12 05:29:28

After millions of attempts, thousands of bad solutions and 51 years, the Zodiac Killer’s most infamous cipher has been solved.

经过数百万次尝试，成千上万个错误的解决方案和51年的努力，十二生肖杀手最臭名昭著的密码已经解决。

If your skepticism is tingling, you are not alone. In the decades since the Bay Area’s most infamous serial killer fired off his mysterious letter to the San Francisco Chronicle, countless people have come forward saying they’ve cracked it. But the solutions were all a little off and almost always required some illogical gymnastics, like ignoring whole chunks or omitting certain inconvenient characters. This solution is different. It references an event that just occurred and has several key phrases that Zodiac used in other letters. It reads:

如果您的怀疑感刺痛，那么您并不孤单。自从湾区最臭名昭著的连环杀手发射了他写给《旧金山纪事报》的那封神秘信件以来的几十年来，无数人挺身而出表示已经破解了它。但是解决方案有点差，几乎总是需要一些不合逻辑的体操，例如忽略整个块或忽略某些不便的字符。该解决方案是不同的。它引用了刚刚发生的一个事件，并且具有十二生肖在其他字母中使用的几个关键短语。内容为：

I HOPE YOU ARE HAVING LOTS OF FUN IN TRYING TO CATCH ME  THAT WASNT ME ON THE TV SHOW  WHICH BRINGS UP A POINT ABOUT ME  I AM NOT AFRAID OF THE GAS CHAMBER  BECAUSE IT WILL SEND ME TO PARADICE ALL THE SOONER  BECAUSE I NOW HAVE ENOUGH SLAVES TO WORK FOR ME  WHERE EVERYONE ELSE HAS NOTHING WHEN THEY REACH PARADICE  SO THEY ARE AFRAID OF DEATH  I AM NOT AFRAID BECAUSE I KNOW THAT MY NEW LIFE IS LIFE WILL BE AN EASY ONE IN PARADICE DEATH

我希望您有很多乐趣可以尝试吸引我，这在电视节目中是不是对我有帮助，所以我对气体室一无所知，因为它将使我早日RAD然，因为我已经无足轻重了从属于我的奴隶使每个人在到达天堂时都没有，所以他们对死亡感到恐惧，因为我知道我的新生活会很容易

Before going into how this cipher fits into Zodiac’s known writings and activities, here’s a little background on the methodology. The 340 cipher — so-named for its number of characters — was solved by an international three-person team of codebreakers: American computer programmer David Oranchak, Belgian computer programmer Jarl Van Eycke and Australian applied mathematician Sam Blake. Using Van Eycke’s codebreaking software, Blake was able to run over 650,000 simulations to determine the correct reading direction of the characters.

在探讨此密码如何适合黄道十二宫的著述和活动之前，请先介绍一下这种方法。 340个密码（因其字符数而得名）由一个由三人组成的国际密码破解团队解决：美国计算机程序员David Oranchak，比利时计算机程序员Jarl Van Eycke和澳大利亚应用数学家Sam Blake。通过使用Van Eycke的密码破解软件，Blake能够进行650,000多次模拟，以确定字符的正确读取方向。

On December 3, the men had their first breakthrough. By breaking the cipher into three chunks and reading the characters diagonally, like in a word search puzzle, they started to see phrases take shape. “Trying to catch me” and “the gas chamber” appeared. As they continued to solve, however, words started to jumble. Oranchak and Van Eycke realized Zodiac made a mistake while constructing his complex cipher — once they shifted a character over, the misspellings cleared up and the cipher worked.

他们于12月3日取得了第一个突破。通过将密码分成三个部分并按对角线读取字符，就像在单词搜索难题中一样，他们开始看到短语已成形。出现“试图抓住我”和“毒气室”。然而，随着他们继续解决，言语开始混乱。 Oranchak和Van Eycke意识到Zodiac在构造其复杂的密码时犯了一个错误-一旦他们将字符移开，便清除了拼写错误并且密码起作用了。

The cipher solved, the men sent the solution to the FBI, which confirmed to the Chronicle Friday that "a cipher attributed to the Zodiac Killer was recently solved by private citizens."

密码解决后，这些人将解决方案发送给了联邦调查局，联邦调查局在周五的《纪事》上确认，“黄道十二宫杀手”的密码最近被私人公民解决了。

Beyond its solid methodology, there is contextual reason to believe these are Zodiac’s words. The most glaring is the reference to slaves in "paradice." Zodiac regularly and almost certainly intentionally misspelled paradise in his correspondence with the media. He correctly spelled more complex words and paraphrased operas like “The Mikado” with ease, suggesting his glaring misspellings of simple words were either stylistic or meant as red herrings.

除了扎实的方法论之外，还有上下文方面的理由相信这些是十二生肖的话。最明显的是＆＃34; paradice中对奴隶的引用。黄道带在与媒体的往来中经常且几乎可以肯定是故意拼写错误的天堂。他正确地拼写了更复杂的单词，并轻松地改写了类似“天皇”之类的歌剧，这表明他对简单单词的明显拼写错误在风格上或意味深长。
