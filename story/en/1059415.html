Clearbanc rebrands its way into a unicorn
Clearbanc Rebrand将其进入独角兽

2021-04-20 21:37:53

Clearbanc has renamed itself Clearco, a move that is more in line with the company’s long-term vision of providing data-driven solutions for founders, say co-founders Michele Romanow and Andrew D’Souza.

Clearbanc已经重命名了Clearco，这一举措更加符合公司的长期愿景，为创始人提供数据驱动的解决方案，例如联合创始人Michele Romanow和Andrew d'Souza。

“We’re moving from just being a capital provider and [having] sort of a transactional relationship with our customers to really using data, our network, guidance, [and] capital to be a long-term partner,” D’Souza said. In other words, Clearco wants founders to think of the company as more than a check-writing machine.

“我们正从作为一个资本提供者而言，以及与我们的客户进行一系列交易关系，以真正使用数据，我们的网络，指导，[和]资本成为一个长期合作伙伴，”D'Souza说。换句话说，Clearco希望创始人认为该公司与支票写作机一样。

Today’s news a step away from what Clearco framed itself around just two years ago: the 20-minute term-sheet. The product, perhaps its most well-known in tech, allowed e-commerce companies to raise non-dilutive marketing growth capital between $10,000 to $10 million based on its revenue and ad spend. The founders then flexed rapid capital deployment based on data – and to date, Clearco has put over $2 billion in over 4,600 companies.

今天的新闻是距离克利科队在两年前陷入困境的新闻：20分钟的术语表。该产品，也许是最着名的技术，允许电子商务公司在收入和广告支出的收入和广告支出的基础上筹集非稀释型营销增长资金。然后，创始人根据数据展示了快速的资本部署 - 迄今为止，Clearco已经超过了超过4,600家公司的20亿美元。

“We can provide you the capital, really efficiently but then we can also help you figure out what to do with that capital to grow your business, and increase the value, and that was a big part of the motivation around the rebrand,” D’Souza said.

“我们可以为您提供资本，真正有效地，但随后我们也可以帮助您弄清楚与该资本一起成长为您的业务，并增加价值，并且这是重新建戒中动机的重要组成部分，”D 'Souza说。

Clearco has been on a tear of new product launches in the past year. In April 2020, Clearco launched ClearRunway to help SaaS founders secure non-dilutive capital repaid through revenue-share agreements. A few months later, in July, it launched a way for founders to figure out how to value their companies based on benchmarking data and internal metrics. In October, Clearco launched a tool that would purchase a company’s inventory upfront directly from suppliers, and is then paid back as products sell. And in February, the company announced that it had created Clearangel, a product similar to its 20-minute term sheet, but focused on founders who bring in less revenue.

Clearco在过去一年中一直在新产品的推出。 4月2020年4月，Clearco推出了ClearRunway来帮助SaaS创始人通过收入股份协议偿还非稀释型资本。几个月后，7月份，它为创始人推出了一种方法来弄清楚如何根据基准数据和内部指标来重视他们的公司。 10月，Clearco推出了一个工具，可以直接从供应商那里购买公司库存，然后随后作为产品销售而退款。在2月份，该公司宣布它创建了Clearangel，该产品类似于其20分钟的术语表，但专注于带来收入较少的创始人。

The growth hasn’t come without challenges. In April 2020, the same month it launched ClearRunway, Clearco laid off 8% of its workforce, or 17 people. The company said it would be writing more conservative checks, more frequently. At that time, it gave $1 billion to 2,200 companies. By now, it has sent $2 billion to  4,500 companies, a mix between startups and online businesses.

增长没有冒险。在2020年4月，它同月推出ClearRunway，Clearco占据了8％的员工，或17人。该公司表示，它将写更多的保守派检查，更频繁。当时，它给了10亿美元到2,200家公司。到目前为止，它已经向初创公司和在线企业之间发送了20亿美元到4,500家公司。

The company, still unprofitable, declined to close ARR, but instead pointed to another proxy: With all of its capital products, Clearco makes 6% in fees when it is repaid. Last year, Clearco spent $1 billion on its companies. This means that Clearco brought in around $60 million in sales last year.

本公司仍然无利可图，拒绝关闭ARR，而是指出另一个代理：通过其所有资本产品，CLEARCO在偿还时的费用为6％。去年，Clearco在其公司中花了10亿美元。这意味着Clearco去年销售额约为6000万美元。

“We were very naive when we started the business around the complexity around how fast you could lose a lot of money if you don’t get things right,” he said. Romanow added that in the beginning, Clearco had “very, very high loss rates” and it has gotten better with more data over time. The company is doubling down on different channels to get and shape and convey that data thus feels like a logical next step.

“当我们在周围开始围绕的复杂性时，我们很天真，如果你没有得到了很多钱，那么如果你没有得到了很多钱，”他说。 Romanow补充说，在开始，Clearco已经“非常高的损失率”，并且随着时间的推移，更多的数据变得更好。该公司在不同的渠道上倍增，以获得和形状，传达数据因此感觉像一个逻辑的下一步。

Since inception in 2015, Clearco’s biggest challenge was scaling the capital market side of its business and making sure it consistently had funds available. With the new product suite, Clearco’s new biggest challenge will be providing valuable services, consistently, to companies and balancing that mentorship with these checks.

自2015年成立以来，Clearco的最大挑战是缩放其业务的资本市场方面，并确保它一直有资金。通过新产品套件，Clearco的新最大挑战将始终如一地为公司和平衡提供有价值的服务，并对这些支票进行指导。

Many of Clearco’s newest products are still in their infancy, but the potential success of the startup could nearly be tied to the general growth of startups looking for alternatives to venture capital when financing their startups. Similar to how AngelList’s growth is neatly tied to the growth of emerging fund managers, Clearco’s growth is cleanly related to the growth of founders who see financing as beyond a seed check from Y Combinator.

许多ClearCo的最新产品仍处于起步阶段，但初创公司的潜在成功可能与在为其初创公司提供资金时寻找备选资本的初创公司的初始增长。类似于鞍市的增长与新兴基金经理的增长完全依赖于新兴基金经理的增长，Clearco的增长与教育家的增长干净地有关，他们认为融资超出了y组合者的种子检查。

“I don’t believe anyone becomes a founder because they’re like, ‘I can’t wait to fundraise right now and can’t wait to like figure out which software to use’,” Romanow said. “They just want to build products and market incredible products [and] we’re just trying to make it easier.”
