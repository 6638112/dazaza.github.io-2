How to justify your technology choices, illustrated using PostgreSQL
如何证明您的技术选择，使用PostgreSQL进行说明

2022-02-14 12:49:23

As a programmer and developer, sometimes you need (should?) to justify your technology choices, even if that is in front of the mirror in the morning. A core reason for that is that technologies embody cognition, so the reasoning behind your job is not only reflected in your tools, in a sense they are your tools.

作为程序员和开发人员，有时你需要（应该？）为了证明你的技术选择是正确的，哪怕是早上在镜子前。其中一个核心原因是，技术体现了认知，所以你工作背后的理由不仅体现在你的工具上，从某种意义上说，它们是你的工具。

This question becomes heavier (or should, at least) when you are talking about a Database Management System (DBMS), since it is probably the most crucial piece of technology you run. Why? It is what manages what you believe to be true of the universe (see the Closed-world assumption).

当您谈论数据库管理系统（DBMS）时，这个问题会变得更加严重（或者至少应该如此），因为它可能是您运行的最关键的技术。为什么？它管理着你所相信的宇宙真实性（参见封闭世界假设）。

This article uses PostgreSQL to illustrate how to justify your choice of a DBMS. More abstractly, it helps you see how to justify your reasons for any technology. These are fundamentally based on Aristotle’s rhetoric (Rapp, Christof, 2010).

本文使用PostgreSQL来说明如何证明选择DBMS的合理性。更抽象地说，它帮助你了解如何证明你对任何技术的理由。这些基本上是基于亚里士多德的修辞（拉普，克里斯托夫，2010）。

Dr Stonebreaker HS one of the core people behind PostgreSQL. He is not only the recipient of the highest recognition in computing, but he really gets the Relational Model (Michael Stonebraker, 2010). His is not only an intellectual grasp of the model since he has lived thought the cycles of hypes of models (Michael Stonebraker and Joseph M. Hellerstein, 2004).

Stonebreaker博士是PostgreSQL背后的核心人物之一。他不仅在计算领域获得了最高的认可，而且真正获得了关系模型（Michael Stonebraker，2010）。自从他经历了模型炒作的周期以来，他不仅在智力上掌握了模型（Michael Stonebraker和Joseph M.Hellerstein，2004）。

Fact: PostgreSQL’s history illustrates the power of its philosophy applied to numerous cases.

事实：PostgreSQL的历史说明了其哲学适用于无数案例的力量。

PostgreSQL has, since its conception, the view of supporting a broad range of scenarios (Stonebraker, M. and Rowe, L.A. and Hirohama, M., 1990). It also has a story of:

PostgreSQL自提出以来，一直支持广泛的场景（Stonebraker，M.和Rowe，L.a.和Hirohama，M.，1990）。它还有一个故事：

The relational model, conceived as an algebra of relations, has proven theoretically its superiority over its 2 rivals, the hierarchical and the graph (or network) models.

关系模型被认为是一种关系代数，从理论上证明了其优于两个竞争对手：层次模型和图（或网络）模型。

You can find out why in more in-depth sources than this article (I recommend (C. J. Date, 1995) for an in-depth discussion on these alternatives). Suffice to say that the relational model is simpler, while keeping the same expressivity.

你可以在比本文更深入的资料中找到原因（我建议（C.J.Date，1995）对这些替代方案进行深入讨论）。可以说，关系模型更简单，同时保持了相同的表达能力。

Tools are embodiments of cognition, both good and bad cognition. PostgreSQL embodies a lot of great cognition, and now you know some of them.

工具是认知的化身，无论是好的认知还是坏的认知。PostgreSQL体现了很多伟大的认知，现在你知道了其中的一些。

More importantly, you now have a framework to justify any technology choice, to yourself and others.

更重要的是，你现在有了一个框架，可以向自己和他人证明任何技术选择的合理性。
