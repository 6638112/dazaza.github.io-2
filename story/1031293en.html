IPhone 12 Pro相机评论

2020-10-27 01:47:49

I’ve been exploring this area, based out of our Airstream, testing the camera of the new iPhone 12 Pro in all kinds of conditions from bright and sunny to dark and snowy. (And all very cold!)

我一直在探索这一领域，基于我们的Airstream，测试新iPhone12Pro在各种条件下的摄像头，从阳光明媚到黑暗和下雪。(而且都很冷！)。

As always, Apple delivered a presentation with a punch and a lot of the focus was on the camera, especially the iPhone 12 Pro Max Camera. (Spoiler alert: that review will be coming in a few weeks and I can’t wait to see what the hardware in that thing can do.)

像往常一样，苹果公司用拳头做演示，很多焦点都在相机上，特别是iPhone12 Pro Max相机。(剧透提醒：审查将在几周内进行，我迫不及待地想看看那台机器的硬件能做些什么。)。

The iPhone 12 Pro’s upgrades really rely on software, whereas the iPhone 12 Pro Max gets all the software upgrades and a major hardware upgrade. With the iPhone 12 Pro in hand this week, a lot of my focus has been on Ultra Wide Night mode, LiDAR autofocus, and exploring new capabilities in the software.

IPhone12Pro的升级实际上依赖于软件，而iPhone12Pro Max获得了所有的软件升级和重大的硬件升级。随着本周iPhone12Pro的问世，我的大部分注意力都放在了超宽夜间模式、LiDAR自动对焦以及探索该软件的新功能上。

Big thanks to my friends at Airstream, ProDPI, and Halide for supporting this review and for making great products for the creative pro community at large.

非常感谢我在Airstream、ProDPI和Halide的朋友们支持这篇评论，并为广大创意专业社区制作了伟大的产品。

For the first time in several generations, we got an upgraded Wide (26 mm) lens this year (now f/1.6 instead of f/1.8). This means the lens hardware will perform slightly better in low light, and it’s also a 7-element lens, which Apple claims offers better edge-to-edge sharpness.

几代人以来，我们今年第一次升级了宽镜头(26毫米)(现在是f/1.6，而不是f/1.8)。这意味着镜头硬件在暗光下的表现会稍微好一些，而且它也是一个7元素镜头，苹果公司声称这提供了更好的边缘到边缘的清晰度。

Last year, we experienced a huge leap forward in low-light performance with the release of Night mode (software), and this year in the iPhone 12 Pro we’re getting better low-light performance from a faster Wide lens (hardware).

去年，随着夜间模式(软件)的发布，我们在微光性能方面经历了巨大的飞跃，而今年在iPhone12 Pro中，我们通过更快的广角镜头(硬件)获得了更好的微光性能。

Below is a 30-second exposure shot on a tripod with nearly no visible light. For context, I couldn't see the blue or green colors with my naked eye, so I found this shot to a great demonstration of how Night mode and the new Wide lens work together. Notice the cloud movement over the 30-second exposure.

下面是一张在几乎没有可见光的三脚架上拍摄的30秒曝光照片。至于背景，我用肉眼看不到蓝色或绿色，所以我觉得这张照片很好地展示了夜间模式和新的广角镜头是如何协同工作的。注意30秒曝光时云层的移动。

Here are a couple more shots using Night Mode with the new f/1.6 Wide (26 mm) lens:

以下是使用新的f/1.6宽(26 Mm)镜头使用夜间模式拍摄的更多照片：

Beyond low-light performance, I was also curious about the edge-to-edge sharpness of the new 7-element lens, so I shot a few tests with lots of detail at the corners of the frame and found the sharpness to be about the same as the iPhone 11 Pro.

除了微光性能，我还对新的7元素镜头的边缘到边缘的清晰度感到好奇，所以我在帧的角落拍摄了几个非常详细的测试，发现清晰度与iPhone 11 Pro大致相同。

I love shooting super wide with any camera, so when the iPhone 11 Pro introduced the Ultra Wide lens last year, I was stoked. While I really love the perspective, I found the quality of the Ultra Wide wasn’t up to my standards when shooting in medium- to low-light conditions, so I only used the Ultra Wide in bright, daylight conditions.

我喜欢用任何相机拍摄超宽镜头，所以当iPhone11Pro去年推出超宽镜头时，我很兴奋。虽然我真的很喜欢它的透视效果，但我发现在中微光条件下拍摄时，超宽相机的质量达不到我的标准，所以我只在明亮的日光条件下使用了超宽相机。

Now that Night mode is available with the Ultra Wide lens, shooting in low light has significantly improved. We got caught in a snowstorm just before sunrise and I hopped out to snap a couple shots with the Ultra Wide in Night mode.

现在，超宽镜头可以使用夜间模式，因此在低光下拍摄的效果有了很大改善。我们在日出前遇到了暴风雪，我跳出来在夜间模式下用超宽相机拍了几张照片。

Just like last year, when I first compared non-Night mode shots to Night mode shots, the results are not even in the same league. What software is doing with Night mode is truly a night and day difference. In one test, I found the Ultra Wide with Night mode on the iPhone 12 Pro captured a beautiful image where the Ultra Wide without Night mode on the iPhone 11 Pro rendered a mostly black frame full of noise.

就像去年，当我第一次比较非夜间模式的镜头和夜间模式的镜头时，结果甚至不在同一级别。软件在夜间模式下所做的事情确实是昼夜不同的。在一次测试中，我发现iPhone12 Pro上的有夜超宽模式捕捉到了一张美丽的图像，iPhone11 Pro上的超宽无夜模式呈现出一个充满噪音的黑色画面。

I thought the iPhone 12 Pro version of this shot turned out gorgeous and I can’t wait to see how it looks as a B&W fine art print on the wall. Stop and consider that for a moment… because of Night mode and computational algorithms we went from “completely unusable dark frame” to “I want that on my wall.”

我觉得这张照片的iPhone12 Pro版本很漂亮，我迫不及待地想看看它作为一张挂在墙上的黑白精美照片会是什么样子。停下来想一想，…。由于夜间模式和计算算法，我们从“完全不能用的暗框”变成了“我想要挂在墙上”。

Another place a super-wide-angle lens is often helpful is tight, indoor spaces, but it was tough to use the previous Ultra Wide in these scenarios because they often come with lower light levels. With Night mode, we can now use the Ultra Wide indoors at night and still produce strong frames. Here’s an Ultra Wide Night mode shot of my Airstream workstation (where I wrote this review!) — while not quite as strong as the Wide (26 mm), this shot is crisp from edge to edge and definitely usable.

另一个超广角镜头通常有用的地方是狭小的室内空间，但在这些情况下很难使用以前的超广角镜头，因为它们通常光线较低。在夜间模式下，我们现在可以在室内夜间使用超宽镜片，同时仍能产生坚固的镜框。这是我的Airstream工作站的一张超宽夜间模式照片(我就是在这里写这篇评论的！)-虽然没有宽(26毫米)那么强烈，但这张照片从边缘到边缘都很清晰，绝对可以使用。

This year’s upgrade to Smart HDR yields some nice improvements. There’s so much involved in Smart HDR but at the end of the day, the aim of this technology is to help the iPhone camera capture what the eye saw. In the image below, note the detail attained in the dark shadows of the rock, while also (mostly) retaining the detail in the bright, snow-capped peaks in the distance.

今年升级到Smart HDR带来了一些不错的改进。智能HDR涉及到很多东西，但归根结底，这项技术的目的是帮助iPhone摄像头捕捉眼睛所看到的。在下图中，请注意在岩石的暗影中获得的细节，同时(主要)保留远处白雪覆盖的明亮山峰中的细节。

You may remember me writing about how Smart HDR was so good at keeping shadow detail it actually made it difficult for me to shoot a silhouette. (This was in my iPhone XS review.) This has gotten a ton better, and when I pulled the exposure slider down to underexpose my subject, the subject got much darker as expected (thank you Smart HDR team.)

你可能还记得我写过，Smart HDR是如何如此擅长保持阴影细节，以至于实际上让我很难拍摄剪影。(这是在我的iPhone XS评论中。)。这已经变得更好了，当我拉下曝光滑块以曝光不足时，拍摄对象如预期的那样变暗了(谢谢Smart HDR团队)。

Here’s a shot with the Ultra Wide — again, notice how the underwater rocks in the foreground are properly exposed, but we still haven’t lost any detail in the sun-lit snowy peaks in the distance.

这是一张超宽镜头的照片，再次注意前景中的水下岩石是如何适当暴露的，但我们仍然没有丢失远处阳光照亮的雪峰的任何细节。

I like to push my gear to the extremes… and knowing others would likely be testing Night mode in urban environments with some available light, I decided to really push the capabilities of Night mode portraits. I shot this portrait of Esther about forty-five minutes after sundown, and it was overcast, so there was very little available light. (To give you an idea, we had to use a flashlight to see our path.)

我喜欢把我的装备推到极致…。知道其他人可能会用一些可用的光线在城市环境中测试夜间模式，我决定真正推动夜间模式肖像的功能。我在日落后大约45分钟拍摄了埃丝特的这张肖像，天气阴沉，所以几乎没有可用的光线。(为了给你一个概念，我们不得不用手电筒来看我们的路。)。

This Portrait mode w/ Night mode 3-second exposure was shot with the iPhone 12 Pro in my right hand. Simultaneously, in my left hand, I held my iPhone 11 Pro and used the light emitting from the screen (not the flash) as an off-camera fill light for Esther’s face.

这张带有夜间模式的肖像模式3秒曝光是用我右手中的iPhone12Pro拍摄的。与此同时，我左手拿着iPhone11Pro，用屏幕发出的光(而不是闪光灯)为埃丝特的脸提供相机外的补光。

As you can imagine, there was definitely some camera shake and movement with this unstable setup but thanks to a little Night mode computational wizardry, better OIS, faster ISO, and of course LiDAR, Esther is still sharp and surprisingly color accurate.

正如你可以想象的那样，在这种不稳定的设置下，肯定会有一些相机抖动和移动，但多亏了一点夜间模式计算魔法，更好的OIS，更快的ISO，当然还有LiDAR，Esther仍然很清晰，颜色准确得令人惊讶。

The LiDAR worked great here — I realized later I never once messed with the focus it was just locked in on her face the whole time. Also, creating an accurate depth map around the furry hood seems like it would be really complex, but the iPhone 12 Pro did a great job.

激光雷达在这里工作得很好-后来我意识到我从来没有弄乱过焦点，它只是一直锁定在她的脸上。此外，在毛茸茸的引擎盖周围制作精确的深度图似乎真的很复杂，但iPhone12Pro做得很好。

The blurring and noise reduction is a bit less realistic and a bit more… dreamy? But it’s sharp enough where it matters, and overall I found this image to be just lovely. (And so did Esther, which is what matters most!)

模糊和降噪不那么逼真，更具…。梦幻般的？但它在重要的地方足够锐利，总的来说，我觉得这张照片很可爱。(埃丝特也是如此，这才是最重要的！)。

There are a handful of new camera settings for photographers in this year’s iPhone 12 Pro (many of which are for anyone on iOS 14). Each of these controls have their own perks, but overall I’m excited to see how the iPhone camera software team is really listening to the pro community and continuing to find ways to implement their pleas for more control and customization.

今年的iPhone12Pro为摄影师提供了几个新的相机设置(其中许多都是为iOS14上的任何人设置的)。这些控件都有自己的好处，但总的来说，我很兴奋地看到iPhone摄像头软件团队是如何真正听取专业社区的意见，并继续寻找方法来实现他们对更多控件和定制的请求。

I’m absolutely thrilled about this new (and hardly talked about) feature. We now have true exposure adjustment that doesn’t revert back to auto mode every time a picture is captured.

我对这个新的(几乎没有被谈论过的)功能感到非常兴奋。我们现在有了真正的曝光调整，不会在每次拍摄照片时都恢复到自动模式。

This adjustment remains even when you switch between .5x, 1x, and 2x lenses, or when switching modes. Even if you lock your iPhone and come back to it later, it still remembers your exposure settings. This is much more like working with a traditional manual camera and I love it.

即使您在0.5x、1x和2x镜头之间切换，或者在切换模式时，此调整仍保持不变。即使您锁定了iPhone并在稍后返回，它仍会记住您的曝光设置。这更像是使用传统的手动相机，我很喜欢。

An example of a perfect use for something like this is shooting in snow. Most cameras determine their auto exposure based on averaging the light in a scene, which means if you have a ton of bright light in your frame (like from snow), the meter will average this out and make the bright parts more gray by default. To compensate for this, most photographers adjust their exposure up 1 to 2 stops to get closer to true white in the frame.

一个完美的例子就是在雪地里射击。大多数摄影机根据平均场景中的灯光来确定其自动曝光，这意味着如果您的帧中有大量明亮的灯光(如来自雪的灯光)，则计量器会将其平均，默认情况下会使明亮部分变得更灰。为了弥补这一点，大多数摄影师会将曝光调高1到2个光圈，以接近画面中的真白。

I went out into the snow with my wife, Esther, and our pup, Mac, set my exposure adjustment to +1.3 stops, and shot with these settings for an hour! I love being able to set it and forget it — usually in a scene like this on iPhone, I’m re-adjusting the exposure almost every shot to compensate.

我和我的妻子埃丝特，还有我们的小狗麦克一起走进雪地，将我的曝光调整设置为+1.3个停靠点，并用这些设置拍摄了一个小时！我喜欢能够设定然后忘记它-通常在iPhone上这样的场景中，我几乎每拍一张照片都会重新调整曝光，以弥补这一点。

Note: This is different than the tap-and-hold AE/AF lock that we’ve had for years. This is exposure adjustment only and doesn’t lock focus.

注意：这不同于我们多年来一直使用的点击式AE/AF锁。这只是曝光调整，不会锁定焦点。

To toggle this setting, go to Settings > Camera > Preserve Settings > Exposure Adjustment. (I leave mine ON.)

要切换此设置，请转至设置>；相机&>保留设置>；曝光调整。(我把我的留着。)。

After you turn this feature on, you’ll notice a new exposure adjustment meter in the top left of your native iPhone Camera app. You can tap this meter to make adjustments to your exposure.

打开这一功能后，你会注意到在你原生的iPhone相机应用程序的左上角有一个新的曝光调节仪。你可以轻触这个计量器来调整你的曝光量。

One of the slick camera features added last year was the ability to “Capture Outside the Frame” — basically when shooting with the Wide (26 mm) lens, the iPhone is also capturing the perspective of the Ultra Wide (13 mm) lens so you can make cropping and geometry adjustments later. This is a really cool feature and it plays to the strengths of the iPhone lens set, but my challenge with it in practice has been the in-camera preview.

去年增加的一项巧妙的相机功能是“在框架外捕捉”-基本上当用宽(26毫米)镜头拍摄时，iPhone还捕捉到了超宽(13毫米)镜头的视角，这样你以后就可以进行裁剪和几何调整了。这是一个非常酷的功能，它发挥了iPhone镜头组的优势，但我在实践中遇到的挑战是相机内预览。

Sometimes seeing objects outside of your frame can be distracting. When composing an image, I’d have trouble finding the perfect visual balance because I’d be seeing major elements just outside the edge of my frame. Now we have the choice to turn off this preview.

有时，看到框架外的物体可能会让人分心。当构图时，我很难找到完美的视觉平衡，因为我会看到画面边缘之外的主要元素。现在我们可以选择关闭此预览。

To toggle this setting, go to Settings > Camera > View Outside the Frame. (I leave mine OFF.)

要切换此设置，请转至设置>；相机&>框架外的视图。(我把我的关了。)。

Also in iOS 14, you can now choose to use the Volume Up button to trigger Burst mode in the native Camera app. With this setting on, press-and-hold Volume Up to trigger Burst mode and Volume Down to record video.

同样在iOS 14中，你现在可以选择使用音量调高按钮在本地相机应用程序中触发连拍模式。在启用此设置的情况下，按住Volume Up可触发连拍模式，按住Volume Down可录制视频。

I use Burst mode frequently because perfectly timing an image capture is often one of the most important variables in creating a photograph (sometimes more so than perfect exposure or composition).

我经常使用连拍模式，因为完美的图像捕捉时间通常是创建照片时最重要的变量之一(有时比完美的曝光或构图更重要)。

To toggle this setting, go to Settings > Camera > Volume Up for Burst. (I leave mine ON.)

要切换此设置，请转至设置>；相机&>提高连拍音量。(我把我的留着。)。

Ever try to shoot a bunch of pictures in a few seconds and miss the moment because the camera wasn’t responsive enough? I’m not talking about Burst mode, I’m talking about tapping the shutter button as fast as possible. The iPhone camera is making lots (billions?) of exposure calculations each time you take a picture, and sometimes shooting in fast succession can bog it down.

有没有试过在几秒钟内拍摄一大堆照片，但因为相机反应不够灵敏而错过了那一刻？我说的不是连拍模式，我说的是尽可能快地按快门按钮。IPhone摄像头赚得盆满钵满(数十亿美元？)。你每次拍照都要计算曝光的次数，有时连续快速拍摄会让你的工作停滞不前。

For me, I’d gladly give a little image quality to get the perfect moment.

对我来说，我很乐意提供一点图像质量来获得完美的时刻。

To toggle this setting, go to Settings > Camera > Prioritize Faster Shooting. (I leave mine ON.)

要切换此设置，请转至设置>；相机&>优先设置更快的拍摄速度。(我把我的留着。)。

I didn’t test this extensively, but it’s cool to see how well Night mode works for a time-lapse. I started this time-lapse well after sunset and Night mode kicked on when it was needed. Note how fluid the transition from day to night is — no flickering, just smooth exposure ramping into the darkness.

我没有对此进行过广泛的测试，但是很高兴看到夜间模式对于延时效果有多好。我在日落之后很久才开始这个延时，在需要的时候夜里模式开始了。注意从白天到晚上的过渡是多么流畅-没有闪烁，只有平滑的曝光进入黑暗。

Up until now, I haven’t been a huge fan of wireless charging. I’ve mainly been annoyed at the slower speeds and the difficulty positioning the device properly on the charger, but it sounds like MagSafe may improve both of these issues.

到目前为止，我并不是无线充电的铁杆粉丝。我主要是对较慢的速度和在充电器上正确定位设备的困难感到恼火，但听起来MagSafe可能会改善这两个问题。

It’s exciting to dream about an ecosystem of MagSafe products, especially accessories for photographers. I could imagine having a sweet LED flash, external batteries, quick-release tripod mounts, and a ton more. I can’t wait to see what the third-party community does with the new MagSafe interface.

梦见MagSafe产品的生态系统是令人兴奋的，特别是摄影师的配件。我可以想象有一个甜美的LED闪光灯，外部电池，快速释放的三脚架支架，还有更多。我迫不及待地想看看第三方社区如何使用新的MagSafe界面。

I realize many feel this is overhyped, and maybe it is, but the potential speeds of 5G for a constantly-moving, media-heavy creative professional like myself is huge. You wouldn’t believe the hoops I’ve jumped through to get the speeds needed to upload photos for client deliveries or important Zoom calls. Right now, I count on very little uploading from the road, but I’m rethinking my creative workflow and backup solutions with 5G speeds on the brink. (I’ve written and uploaded a good chunk of this post working remotely in my Airstream, connected via LTE personal hotspot.)

我意识到许多人觉得这是夸大了，也许是这样，但对于像我这样不断移动、以媒体为主的创意专业人士来说，5G的潜在速度是巨大的。你不会相信，为了达到上传照片以供客户交付或重要的Zoom电话所需的速度，我经历了多大的困难。目前，我几乎不指望从路上上传，但在5G速度即将到来的情况下，我正在重新考虑我的创造性工作流和备份解决方案。(我已经撰写并上传了这篇文章的很大一部分内容，在我的Airstream上远程工作，通过LTE个人热点连接。)。

Another place 5G impacts the photographer is pulling down images from iCloud Photo Library. I have my whole library stored in the cloud, and sometimes when I go to edit an image it takes awhile to pull the image. This little blip of waiting often has a surprisingly disruptive effect on my creative workflow. With faster speeds, the images will feel more local.

5G影响摄影师的另一个地方是从iCloud图片库下载图像。我将我的整个库存储在云中，有时当我去编辑图像时，需要一段时间才能拉出该图像。这种短暂的等待经常会对我的创造性工作流程产生令人惊讶的破坏效果。随着速度的加快，图像会给人以更本土化的感觉。

The team at Halide continues to evolve their best-in-class third-party camera app, and they’ve had Smart RAW (very similar to ProRAW in theory) for quite some time. One feature built into Halide is the Technical Readout, which basically gives you visibility of the hardware inside your iPhone camera.

Halide的团队在继续开发他们同类中最好的第三方相机应用程序，他们使用Smart RAW(理论上与ProRAW非常相似)已经有很长一段时间了。Halide内置的一个功能是技术读数，它基本上可以让你看到iPhone摄像头内部的硬件。

The biggest revelation here is the dramatic increase of Max ISO on the Wide camera, which is now 5808 (just about double the iPhone 11 Pro’s maximum ISO of 3072). This means the sensor in the iPhone 12 Pro is substantially more sensitive to light (though image grain increases at higher ISO levels).

这里最大的启示是宽屏上的最大ISO急剧增加，现在是5808(大约是iPhone11Pro最大ISO 3072的两倍)。这意味着iPhone12Pro中的传感器对光的敏感度大大提高(尽管图像颗粒在较高的ISO水平下会增加)。

It looks like ProRAW will offer the meticulous control creative pros demand without sacrificing the greatest strengths of the iPhone as a largely computational camera. 看起来，ProRAW将在不牺牲iPhone作为主要计算型相机的最大优势的情况下，提供创意专业人士所需的细致控制。这是一个令人兴奋的前景，我急切地想看看这些文件在实践中是如何工作的，但我也很感兴趣地想看看ProRAW是否会改变专业摄影师在他们的工作流程中利用iPhone相机的方式。

Traditionally, RAW files themselves can’t be edited. When adjustments are made, they’re stored in a reference file instead of destructively changing the original image file.

传统上，原始文件本身不能编辑。进行调整时，它们存储在引用文件中，而不是破坏性地更改原始图像文件。

With this in mind, many publications require the submission of RAW files for the images sent in by photographers. This allows them to examine the original, untouched data and helps protect their legitimacy as a news source.

考虑到这一点，许多出版物要求提交摄影师发送的图像的原始文件。这使他们能够检查原始的、未接触的数据，并有助于保护其作为新闻源的合法性。

If the ProRAW format really does work this way, it marks an important step forward in the validation of the iPhone camera as a tool photographers can rely on to deliver client work, particularly in the editorial space.

如果ProRAW格式真的以这种方式工作，它标志着在验证iPhone相机作为摄影师交付客户工作的工具方面向前迈出了重要的一步，特别是在编辑领域。
