Tehtris, a Paris-based startup that offers an XDR service that monitors IP addresses in 60 countries to stop cyber attacks, raises €20M Series A

2020-11-27 11:03:26

Tehtris, a Paris, France-based provider of cybersecurity solutions, raised €20M in Series A funding.

总部位于法国巴黎的网络安全解决方案提供商Tehtris筹集了2000万欧元的A轮融资。

Backers included Ace Management, Open CNP, the corporate venture fund of CNP Assurances, Nouvelle-Aquitaine Co-Investissement (NACO) and several business angels.

支持者包括Ace Management，Open CNP，CNP Assurances的企业风险基金，Nouvelle-Aquitaine Co-Investissement（NACO）和几位商业天使。

Founded in 2010 by Eléna Poincet and Laurent Oudot, Tehtris develops and delivers an XDR platform (eXtended Detection and Response) enabling real-time, automated detection and neutralization of known and unknown digital threats in corporate infrastructures. In response to new threats that bypass traditional security solutions, the products, algorithms, software robots, and artificial intelligence created by the company’s engineers are the core of its platform. With this technology, Tehtris and its international partners monitor millions of IP addresses in over 60 countries and analyze billions of events every week. Customers are major players in industry, engineering and services. The company has also established work collaborations with Capgemini Sogeti, Orange CyberDefense and Expleo, among others.

Tehtris由ElénaPoincet和Laurent Oudot于2010年创立，开发并提供了XDR平台（扩展的检测和响应），可对企业基础架构中的已知和未知数字威胁进行实时，自动检测和消除。为了应对绕过传统安全解决方案的新威胁，由公司工程师创建的产品，算法，软件机器人和人工智能是其平台的核心。借助这项技术，Tehtris及其国际合作伙伴可以监控60多个国家/地区的数百万个IP地址，并每周分析数十亿个事件。客户是行业，工程和服务的主要参与者。该公司还与Capgemini Sogeti，Orange Cyber​​Defense和Expleo等建立了合作关系。
