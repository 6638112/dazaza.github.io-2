Human Taste Buds Can Tell the Difference Between Normal and 'Heavy' Water
人类味蕾可以讲述正常和“重”水之间的差异

2021-04-12 23:39:13

This may come as a bit of a shocker, but technically speaking, not all water on Earth is made up of H 2O molecules.

这可能是一点令人震惊，但在技术上讲，不是地球上的所有水都是由H 2O分子组成的。

Less than a century ago, the discovery of the hydrogen isotope deuterium –  2H, but often simplified to D – revealed the existence of another kind of water with the chemical formula 2H 2O or simply D 2O.

少于一个世纪前，发现氢同位素氘 - 2h，但经常被简化为d - 揭示了另一种水与化学式2H 2 O或简单的D 2O的存在。

Here's how they differ. A typical hydrogen atom contains one proton within its nucleus. The deuterium isotope, however, has a neutron in addition to the proton, giving the hydrogen atom a greater mass. Hence, water formed with this type of heavy hydrogen is usually called... heavy water.

在这里＆＃39;他们如何不同。典型的氢原子含有在其核中的一个质子。然而，除了质子之外，氘同位素具有中子，使氢原子具有更大的质量。因此，用这种重氢形成的水通常被称为...重水。

Aside from that one key difference between H 2O and D 2O – which gives heavy water about 10 percent greater density than regular water – these two water types are chemically the same, although deuterium does exhibit slightly different bonding behavior to regular hydrogen (which is also known as protium, by the way).

除了H 2O和D 2O之间的一个关键差异 - 这使得重水的密度大约10％的密度大约在常规水中大约10％ - 这两种水类型是化学相同的，尽管氘确实表现出略微不同的粘合行为至规则氢气（也是如此顺便说一句，被称为蛋白酶）。

Because of that altered bonding behavior – which can affect bodily chemistry if you ingest deuterium in D 2O – scientists generally say it's not a great idea to drink heavy water, at least not in high doses.

由于这种改变的粘接行为 - 如果你在D 2O中摄取氘 - 科学家们一般来说，这可能会影响身体化学，这通常不会喝重水，至少不是高剂量。

Small amounts are considered harmless for humans, however, and are in fact often administered to participants in scientific experiments.

然而，对于人类而言，少量被认为是无害的，并且实际上经常给科学实验的参与者管理。

Due to such incidental consumption, now going back almost a century, there's been a longstanding question over whether heavy water tastes the same as regular drinking water – or whether its subtle isotopic variation yields a different taste that people may be able to perceive.

由于这种偶然的消费，现在越来越几个世纪，那里有一个长期的繁重水味道与正常饮用水相同 - 或者其微妙的同位素变异是否会产生不同的味道，以至于人们可能能够提供不同的味道感知。

"There is anecdotal evidence from the 1930s that the taste of pure D 2O is distinct from the neutral one of pure H 2O, being described mostly as 'sweet'," an international team of researchers led by first authors and biochemists Natalie Ben Abu and Philip E. Mason explains in a new study.

＆＃34;从20世纪30年代存在轶事证据，纯D 2o的味道与纯H 2O中的中性之一不同，主要描述为＆＃39;甜蜜＆＃39;由第一名作者和生物化学家NaTalie Ben Abu和Philip E. Mason领导的国际研究人员在一项新的研究中解释道。

"However, Urey and Failla [the former being Harold Urey, the scientist who discovered deuterium] addressed this question in 1935 concluding authoritatively that upon tasting 'neither of us could detect the slightest difference between the taste of ordinary distilled water and the taste of pure heavy water'."

＆＃34;然而，Urey和Failla [前者是被发现的科学家哈罗德·乌雷，被发现氘的科学家]在1935年颁发了这个问题，授权在品尝＆＃39;我们都不能发现普通蒸馏的味道之间的最轻微的差异水和纯净水的味道＆＃39;。＆＃34;

But was that conclusion a tad premature? Ben Abu and Mason say that Urey and Failla's unequivocal opinion on the topic effectively stifled further research in this area for much of the next century, at least in terms of human taste-testing.

但结论是一个早产权吗？本阿布和梅森说，乌雷迪和粮农组织＆＃39;在下个世纪的大部分内部有效地扼杀了该专题的难以有效地扼杀了这一领域的进一步研究。

Tests in rats have shown that too much heavy water consumption can be fatal to the animals, but evidence for whether rats can taste the difference remains unclear.

大鼠的测试表明，对动物的含水量太大可能是致命的，但对大鼠是否可以味道的证据仍然不清楚。

In the last two decades or so, advancements in our understanding of human taste receptors have prompted a reopening of old cases like this – and in their new research, Ben Abu, Mason, and their team can finally confirm that there really is something a bit different about the taste of heavy water.

在过去的二十年左右，我们对人类口味受体的理解的进步促使像这样的旧案件重新开放 - 在他们的新研究中，本·阿布，梅森和他们的团队最终能够确认有点有所了解关于重水的味道不同。

"Despite the fact that the two isotopes are nominally chemically identical, we have shown conclusively that humans can distinguish by taste (which is based on chemical sensing) between H 2O and D 2O, with the latter having a distinct sweet taste," explains senior author and physical chemist Pavel Jungwirth from the Czech Academy of Sciences.

＆＃34;尽管两位同位素是名义上的化学上相同的事实，我们已经表明，人类可以通过H 2 O和D 2o之间的味道（基于化学感测）来区分，后者具有不同的甜味， ＆＃34;从捷克科学院解释高级作者和物理化学家Pavel Jungwirth。

In a taste-testing experiment with 28 participants, most people were able to distinguish between H 2O and D 2O, and tests with mixed amounts of the waters revealed that greater proportions of heavy water were perceived as tasting sweeter.

在具有28名参与者的味道测试实验中，大多数人能够区分H 2O和D 2O，并且与水的混合量的测试显示，将更加比例的重水被认为是品尝更甜的甜味。

In tests with mice, however, the animals did not seem to prefer drinking heavy water over regular water, although they did show a preference for sugared water – suggesting that in mice, D 2O does not elicit the same sweet taste that people can perceive.

然而，在用小鼠的测试中，这些动物似乎没有常规水喝重水，尽管他们确实表现出糖水的偏好 - 表明在小鼠中，D 2o并不引起人们可以感知的相同甜味。

Other taste tests conducted by the team suggest why this is so, indicating that human taste receptivity to D 2O is mediated by the taste receptor TAS1R2/TAS1R3, which is known to respond to sweetness in both natural sugars and artificial sweeteners.

该团队进行的其他味道测试表明为什么这是这样的，表明人类口味接受性对D 2O的味道受体TAS1R2 / TAS1R3介导，已知其响应天然糖和人造甜味剂中的甜味。

Experiments in the lab with HEK 293 cells confirmed the same thing, showing robust responses in TAS1R2/TAS1R3 expressing cells when exposed to D 2O.

具有HEK 293细胞的实验室中的实验证实了相同的事情，在暴露于D 2O时表达TAS1R2 / TAS1R3表达细胞的稳健反应。

In addition, computational modeling with molecular dynamics simulations revealed slight differences in the interactions between proteins and H 2O versus D 2O, which the team says needs further study to fully explain, but accords with previous research, and provides another example of nuclear quantum effects in chemical systems, including that of water.

此外，具有分子动力学模拟的计算建模揭示了蛋白质和H 2O之间的相互作用的轻微差异，而团队所说的需要进一步研究以充分解释，但符合以前的研究，并提供另一个核量子效应的例子化学系统，包括水的化学系统。

"Our findings point to the human sweet taste receptor TAS1R2/TAS1R3 as being essential for sweetness of D 2O," the authors conclude.

＆＃34;我们的发现指向人类甜味受体Tas1R2 / TAS1R3，对D 2O的甜度至关重要，＆＃34;作者得出结论。
