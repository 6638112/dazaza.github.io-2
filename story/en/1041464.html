The beautiful Silent ThunderBolt-3 PC
美丽的静音ThunderBolt-3 PC

2020-12-23 21:28:55

When I placed the last screw on the DB4 chassis of the Beautiful Silent PC [1], I thought I would not have to open it up again for years. Perhaps, I hypothesized, it would be a decade before an upgrade. The imperative of working from home proved me wrong when the seemingly easy problem of sharing peripherals between a desktop and a laptop turned out to be more complicated than expected. It had the positive outcome of teaching me about the marvelous world of cables, USB, DisplayPort, DisplayLink and Thunderbolt.

当我将最后一颗螺丝钉在Beautiful Silent PC [1]的DB4机箱上时，我以为多年不用再打开它了。我猜想，也许升级还需要十年时间。当在台式机和笔记本电脑之间共享外围设备看似简单的问题变得比预期的要复杂时，在家工作的当务之急证明了我的错。它的积极成果使我了解了电缆，USB，DisplayPort，DisplayLink和Thunderbolt的奇妙世界。

My first solution was based on a USB stack with a USB 3 hub supporting 4k60Hz over DisplayPort. Plenty of such devices are advertised on Amazon. Sadly it turned out that USB 3.1 and Display Port 1.2 hardware could not do that. As Steve Chamberlin detailed [2], there is just not enough bandwidth. The best one can hope for while relying soly on USB is 4k30Hz with 3.1 ports, 4k60Hz with 2.0 ports, or an exotic hub that will use compression via DisplayLink. Steve's article mentioned how DP 1.4's DSC would help but none of my hardware supported it. Ditto USB-4.The workaround was a less elegant system of two cables per machine with one to drive the monitor and one for the peripherals. It kind of worked but with sporadic issues such as refusal to wake up when typing on the keyboard. Other times the same Ergodox EZ would find itself inexplicably unpowered. At that point it became painfully obvious that USB hub quality varied greatly. If I was going to spend more on a quality hub, why not take it to the next level?

我的第一个解决方案基于具有USB 3集线器的USB堆栈，该集线器通过DisplayPort支持4k60Hz。在亚马逊上刊登了大量此类设备的广告。不幸的是，事实证明USB 3.1和Display Port 1.2硬件无法做到这一点。正如史蒂夫·张伯林（Steve Chamberlin）所详述的[2]，带宽不足。仅依靠USB时，最好的选择是带有3.1端口的4k30Hz，带有2.0端口的4k60Hz或将通过DisplayLink使用压缩的特殊集线器。史蒂夫（Steve）的文章提到了DP 1.4的DSC如何提供帮助，但我的硬件都不支持它。同上USB-4。解决方法是一种不太优雅的系统，每台机器使用两根电缆，一根用于驱动显示器，另一根用于外围设备。这种方法行之有效，但偶尔会出现一些问题，例如在键盘上打字时拒绝唤醒。其他时候，同一个Ergodox EZ会发现自己无法供电。那时，很明显，USB集线器的质量变化很大。如果我要花更多钱在质量中心上，为什么不将其提升到一个新水平？

According to Steve's article, Thunderbolt-3 can provide display, power, and peripherals on a single cable. I looked for hubs supporting 4k60 and USB-3 and read many good things about the CalDigit TS3 Plus. All I had to do was to find an ITX motherboard with Thunderbolt-3 capability. A quick search revealed that the X570 Phantom Gaming-ITX/TB3 [3] by ASRock would be a match.

根据Steve的文章，Thunderbolt-3可以在单根电缆上提供显示，电源和外围设备。我寻找了支持4k60和USB-3的集线器，并阅读了有关CalDigit TS3 Plus的许多优点。我要做的就是找到具有Thunderbolt-3功能的ITX主板。快速搜索显示，华擎的X570 Phantom Gaming-ITX / TB3 [3]将是匹配的。

The Phantom Gaming ITX TB-3 has massive heat-sinks. The Southbridge even has a dedicated fan with a heat pipe (left of CPU). There is a secondary huge heat-sink with yet-to-be-determined purpose on the right of the CPU socket. The consequences of these radiators is that there is not enough clearance for the 105W capable LH6 heat pipes. The standard SH6 providing 65W dissipation were used instead. Funky cables are involved in this build. The 2m (6ft) Thunderbolt-3 must be active in order to be 40Gb/s capable. Another weirdness is the 1 foot DP to DP cable required to connect the GPU DP-output to the Mobo DP-input. This is required to get the video stream on the Thunderbolt port. No doubt it will bring warm memories to those who had to do the same thing with VGA pass-through on their 3dFX.

幻影游戏ITX TB-3具有大型散热器。南桥甚至有一个带有热管的专用风扇（CPU左侧）。 CPU插槽右侧有一个次级散热器，用途尚待确定。这些散热器的后果是，对于支持105W的LH6热管没有足够的间隙。替代使用提供65W功耗的标准SH6。时髦的电缆参与了此构建。 2m（6英尺）Thunderbolt-3必须处于活动状态才能具备40Gb / s的能力。另一个怪异之处是将GPU DP输出连接到Mobo DP输入所需的1英尺DP到DP电缆。这是在Thunderbolt端口上获取视频流所必需的。毫无疑问，它将为那些在其3dFX上通过VGA直通进行相同操作的人带来美好的回忆。

Going from 105W to 65W dissipation was a concern. Pinning all 12 cores for 15mn showed a steady increase whereas 10 cores were stable around 7X oC. Combined to the temperature cliff once load diminishes, I don't expect to see any throttling. A new thermal sensor appeared in HWiNFO64.EXE. SB, sanding for "SouthBridge", seems to be doing a lot regardless of the load on the CPU or GPU. This component stabilizes at 75 oC which, at rest, is the hottest component in the machine (CPU = 37 oC, GPU = 39 oC, and Mobo = 46 oC). Even actively cooled build see the same kind of numbers so it does not look like the DB4 is at fault here. Seeing such temperature on an IO chipset was a total surprise. But observing Caldigit TB3's large heat-sink makes me wonder if performing Thunderbolt-3 IO is simply just an order of magnitude more intensive than USB 3.2. Maybe ASRock motherboard is doing the best it can. But if that is the case, how does Apple cools down its fanless laptop? That chipset has a fan which does not play along with the fan-less-ness of the machine. Surprisingly, running it at 100% or disabling it with a "FAN-Tastic-Tuning" custom policy does not change the operating temperature. Setting the threshold to 80 o is a bold move but that is how you get where no one has gone before. With this setting, I have never heard the fan run.

从105W到65W的功耗是一个问题。将所有12个内核固定1500万表明稳定增长，而10个内核在7X oC附近稳定。一旦负载减少，再加上温度悬崖，我不希望看到任何节流现象。 HWiNFO64.EXE中出现了一个新的热传感器。 SB，为SouthBridge磨砂，似乎在做很多事情，无论CPU或GPU上的负载如何。该组件稳定在75 oC，这是机器中最热的组件（CPU = 37 oC，GPU = 39 oC，Mobo = 46 oC）。即使是主动冷却的构建，也会看到相同类型的数字，因此看起来DB4不会出错。在IO芯片组上看到如此高的温度真是令人惊讶。但是观察Caldigit TB3的大型散热器会让我怀疑执行Thunderbolt-3 IO是否仅比USB 3.2强度高一个数量级。也许华擎主板正在尽力而为。但是，如果真是这样，苹果如何为无风扇笔记本电脑降温？该芯片组的风扇不会随着机器的无风扇运转。令人惊讶的是，以100％的速度运行它或通过＆＃34; FAN-Tastic-Tuning＆＃34;禁用它自定义策略不会更改工作温度。将阈值设置为80 o是一项大胆的举措，但这就是您到达以前没有人去过的地方的方式。使用此设置，我从未听见风扇运转的声音。

The BSTB3-PC is definitely on the edge of the DB4 thermal frontier but it works reliably. I have been using it to browse the web, write this article, VC, program, and play lose at Starcraft 2 without issues so far. Ironically, as I finished putting it together, Apple announced a fanless M1 Macbook Air which would have performed just as well, if not better. Nonetheless, I am proud of the result. It is a damn good looking silent computer. And this one is "Designed in Sunnyvale" baby!

BSTB3-PC绝对位于DB4热边界的边缘，但工作可靠。到目前为止，我一直在使用它来浏览Web，编写本文，VC，程序以及玩《星际争霸2》时的失败。具有讽刺意味的是，当我完成组装后，苹果公司宣布了无风扇M1 Macbook Air，即使性能更好，它的表现也一样好。尽管如此，我为结果感到骄傲。这是该死的好看的静音计算机。而这是在桑尼维尔设计的。宝宝！
